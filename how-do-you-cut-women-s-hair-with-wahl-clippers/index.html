How Do You Cut Women s Hair With Wahl Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting women's hair with clippers has become a popular trend in recent years, thanks to its convenience and ease of use. Wahl clippers are an excellent choice for women's haircuts as they are designed to provide a clean and precise cut. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of how to cut women's hair with Wahl clippers.

How to Cut Women's Hair with Wahl Clippers

Step 1: Choose the right clipper guard

The first step in cutting women's hair with Wahl clippers is to choose the right clipper guard. The clipper guard determines the length of hair that will be cut, so it's essential to choose the right one based on the desired length.

For shorter haircuts, use a smaller clipper guard, and for longer haircuts, use a larger clipper guard. It's always better to start with a larger clipper guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 2: Prepare the hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is washed and dried. Comb the hair in the direction of growth to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Start cutting

Once you have chosen the right clipper guard and prepared the hair, it's time to start cutting. Start from the bottom of the head and work your way up, using the clipper guard to trim the hair to the desired length.

Make sure to keep the clippers at a 45-degree angle, and move them in a smooth, steady motion. Don't rush the process, and take your time to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Blend the hair

Once the hair is cut to the desired length, it's time to blend the hair using a blending shear or scissors. Blend the hair by cutting small sections of hair at a time, using a comb to guide the scissors.

Step 5: Finish the cut

Once you have blended the hair, it's time to finish the cut. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs and create a clean, polished look.

Tips for Cutting Women's Hair with Wahl Clippers

  • Always start with a larger clipper guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut
  • Keep the clippers at a 45-degree angle
  • Move the clippers in a smooth, steady motion
  • Take your time and don't rush the process
  • Use a blending shear or scissors to blend the hair
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs and create a clean, polished look

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Wahl clippers be used on women's hair?

Yes, Wahl clippers can be used on women's hair. They are designed to provide a clean and precise cut, making them an excellent choice for women's haircuts.

What clipper guard should I use for women's hair?

The clipper guard you should use for women's hair depends on the desired length. For shorter haircuts, use a smaller clipper guard, and for longer haircuts, use a larger clipper guard.

Can I use Wahl clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, Wahl clippers can be used to cut your own hair. However, it's always better to have someone else cut your hair to ensure an even cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Women's Hair with Wahl Clippers

After cutting women's hair with Wahl clippers, it's essential to take care of the hair to maintain the cut. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for the hair type, and avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often.

Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help maintain the cut and keep the hair healthy.

Conclusion

Cutting women's hair with Wahl clippers is a convenient and easy way to achieve a clean and precise cut. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
