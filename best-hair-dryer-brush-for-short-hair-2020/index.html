If you have short hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. From choosing the right products to finding the perfect tool, it can take a lot of trial and error. One tool that can make your life easier is a hair dryer brush. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the best hair dryer brush for short hair in 2020.

Description

A hair dryer brush is a versatile tool that combines a hair dryer and a brush in one. It allows you to dry and style your hair at the same time, making it a time-saving solution for busy mornings. The brush can add volume, smoothness, and shine to your hair, while the heat helps to set the style. Hair dryer brushes come in various sizes and shapes, so you can choose the one that works best for your short hair.

How to Use a Hair Dryer Brush on Short Hair

Using a hair dryer brush on short hair is simple, but it does require some practice. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry it until it's damp but not dripping. Comb your hair to remove tangles.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Spray a heat protectant on your hair to prevent damage from the heat. This is especially important if you use the hair dryer brush on a regular basis.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and place the brush underneath it, near the roots. Use the brush to pull the hair taut and turn on the hair dryer. Move the brush downwards, following the brush with the dryer. Repeat until the section is dry and styled to your liking. Repeat with the other sections until your hair is completely dry and styled.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to use a hair dryer brush for short hair:

Step 1: Choose the right hair dryer brush

Not all hair dryer brushes are created equal. Choose a brush that is specifically designed for short hair. Look for a brush with a smaller barrel and bristles that are close together.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry it until it's damp but not dripping. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and place the brush underneath it, near the roots. Use the brush to pull the hair taut and turn on the hair dryer. Move the brush downwards, following the brush with the dryer. Repeat until the section is dry and styled to your liking. Repeat with the other sections until your hair is completely dry and styled.

Step 5: Finish with a cool shot

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a cool shot of air from the hair dryer. This will help to set the style and add shine.

Tips for Using a Hair Dryer Brush on Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hair dryer brush:

  • Choose the right brush for your hair type and length.
  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage from the heat.
  • Divide your hair into sections for easier styling.
  • Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.
  • Use the brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair dryer brush on wet hair?

A: No, it's best to use a hair dryer brush on damp hair. Wet hair is more prone to damage from the heat.

Q: How often should I use a hair dryer brush?

A: It's best to use a hair dryer brush no more than twice a week to prevent damage from the heat.

Q: How do I clean my hair dryer brush?

A: Clean your hair dryer brush regularly by removing any hair or debris from the bristles. You can also use a mild detergent and warm water to clean the brush.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, a hair dryer brush can be a game-changer for short hair. With the right tool and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy look in the comfort of your own home. Use these tips and tricks to make the most of your hair dryer brush and keep your short hair looking its best.
