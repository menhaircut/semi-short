Mid Length Hair For Round Face 2019

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't flatter your round face? Well, fear not! In this article, we'll be discussing the perfect mid length haircuts for round faces that were popular in 2019 and still are in 2023. From how to achieve the perfect style to hair care tips, we've got you covered.

Description

Mid length haircuts have been a popular choice for many women over the years. Not only do they allow for versatility in styling, but they also complement a round face shape. The length of the hair helps to elongate the face, making it appear slimmer and more balanced. In 2019, mid length haircuts for round faces were all about adding layers and texture to create movement and volume.

How to

The key to achieving the perfect mid length haircut for a round face is to add layers and texture. This will help to create movement and volume, which will in turn make the face appear slimmer. When it comes to styling, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Blow Dry

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to lift the hair and create volume.

2. Use Product

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair. Apply the product to the roots and work it through the lengths of your hair.

3. Curl or Straighten

Curling or straightening your hair can help to add even more movement and texture. Use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide on how to achieve the perfect mid length haircut for a round face:

1. Consultation

Before getting your haircut, have a consultation with your stylist to discuss the best cut for your face shape.

2. Layers

Ask your stylist to add layers to your mid length haircut. This will help to create movement and volume.

3. Texture

Ask your stylist to add texture to your hair, either through cutting techniques or by using a razor.

4. Styling

When styling your hair, use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add volume. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. Curl or straighten your hair to create movement and texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling mid length hair for a round face:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Avoid heavy bangs that sit straight across your forehead. These can make your face appear even rounder.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Instead, opt for side swept bangs that are longer on the sides than in the middle. This will help to create a more balanced look.

3. Face Framing Layers

Ask your stylist to add face framing layers to your haircut. This will help to draw attention to your features and create a more flattering look.

FAQ

What other hairstyles are good for round faces?

In addition to mid length haircuts, long layered haircuts and pixie cuts with side swept bangs can also be flattering for round faces.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining the health of your hair is important for achieving the perfect mid length haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using hot tools on your hair, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

2. Deep Condition

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Protect from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mid length haircut for your round face shape.
