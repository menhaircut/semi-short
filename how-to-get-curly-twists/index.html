How To Get Curly Twists The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly twists are a popular hairstyle among women. Not only do they look stylish, but they are also low maintenance and can last for several weeks. In this article, we will guide you on how to get curly twists on relaxed hair. Follow our step-by-step guide, and you will have beautiful curly twists in no time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Twists
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Twists
  • FAQs

How to Get Curly Twists

Curly twists are achieved by twisting hair extensions or your natural hair into small coils, then curling them using perm rods or flexi rods. The result is a beautiful, defined curl pattern that lasts for weeks.

Materials Required

  • Hair extensions or natural hair
  • Perm rods or flexi rods
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair clips
  • Styling gel or mousse
  • Hair oil or serum

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start, ensure that your hair is clean, moisturized, and detangled. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair extensions or natural hair to keep them moisturized. Then, part your hair into small sections using a rat-tail comb and secure each section with a clip.

Step 2: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and apply a styling gel or mousse to it. Then, twist the hair around itself tightly, starting from the root and working your way down to the end. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Once you have twisted all your hair, it's time to curl it. Take a perm rod or flexi rod and wrap the twisted hair around it tightly. Secure the rod in place by bending the ends of the rod. Repeat this process for each twisted section of hair.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Allow your hair to air dry or use a hooded dryer to speed up the drying process. Once your hair is completely dry, carefully remove the rods from each section of hair. You should have beautiful, defined curls.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Twists

  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or using a satin pillowcase.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your curls.
  • Refresh your curls by lightly misting them with water and applying a curl defining cream or gel.
  • Do not wash your hair too often, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy and lose their definition.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Can I get curly twists on my natural hair?

Yes, you can achieve curly twists on your natural hair. Follow the same steps outlined in this article, but instead of using hair extensions, use your natural hair.

How long can I keep curly twists in my hair?

You can keep curly twists in your hair for up to six weeks, but it's recommended that you remove them after four weeks to prevent tangling and matting.

Hair Care for Curly Twists

To keep your curly twists looking fresh, you need to take care of them properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly using a water-based leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.

Now that you know how to get curly twists on relaxed hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. Follow our step-by-step guide, and you will have beautiful, defined curls that will last for weeks. Remember to take care of your hair properly, and you'll be able to enjoy your curly twists for even longer.
