Would Curtain Bangs Look Good On A Round Face

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering whether curtain bangs would look good on you. The good news is that curtain bangs can be flattering on almost any face shape, including a round face. In this article, we'll explore how to wear curtain bangs on a round face, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Description

Curtain bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. They are characterized by long, wispy strands that frame the face and can be parted in the middle. Curtain bangs can add softness and dimension to a round face, creating a more balanced and flattering look. However, it's important to choose the right length and style of curtain bangs to suit your face shape.

How to Wear Curtain Bangs on a Round Face

If you have a round face, there are a few things to consider when choosing curtain bangs. First, you'll want to avoid bangs that are too short or blunt, as these can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for longer, wispy bangs that are angled or layered to create a more flattering shape.

You should also consider the placement of your curtain bangs. If you have a high forehead, you can place your bangs slightly higher up on your head to balance your features. If you have a smaller forehead, you can place your bangs slightly lower to create the illusion of a longer face.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing curtain bangs on a round face:

  1. Choose a length and style of curtain bangs that flatters your face shape.
  2. Part your hair in the middle to create a curtain effect.
  3. Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Apply a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add definition and hold.

Tips for Wearing Curtain Bangs on a Round Face

Here are some additional tips for wearing curtain bangs on a round face:

  • Avoid straight-across bangs, as these can make your face look wider.
  • Consider getting longer bangs that blend into the rest of your hair, rather than shorter bangs that stop at your eyebrows.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to maintain their shape and prevent them from becoming too long or heavy.
  • Use a high-quality hair care routine to keep your hair healthy, shiny, and strong.

FAQ

Can curtain bangs make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, curtain bangs can create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding height and dimension to the forehead area.

What face shapes do curtain bangs look best on?

Curtain bangs can look great on almost any face shape, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

How do I style curtain bangs?

You can style your curtain bangs using a variety of techniques, including blow drying, flat ironing, and using styling products such as texturizing sprays and pomades.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from becoming too long or heavy.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to use a high-quality hair care routine. This should include regular shampooing and conditioning, as well as the use of styling products such as texturizing sprays and pomades. You should also avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Finally, be sure to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking their best.
