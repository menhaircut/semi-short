What Does A Box Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you curious about what a box haircut looks like? This trendy haircut was popularized in the 90s and has made a comeback in recent years. It’s a versatile haircut that works for all hair types and lengths. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about the box haircut, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

What is a Box Haircut?

A box haircut is a hairstyle that features a defined, square shape with sharp lines and angles. The sides are cut short, while the top is left longer and cut into a box shape. This creates a clean and polished look that’s perfect for those who want a stylish yet classic haircut.

How to Get a Box Haircut

Getting a box haircut is easy, but it’s important to go to a professional stylist who has experience cutting this style. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide how short you want the sides and back to be. The length will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to go shorter on the sides to create a more balanced look.

Step 2: Define the Box Shape

The top of the hair should be left longer and cut into a square shape. The stylist will use clippers or scissors to create a defined line across the top of the hair. The sides will be cut shorter, creating a clean and polished look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair using pomade, gel, or wax. Use a comb to create a defined part and slick back the sides. You can also add texture to the top of your hair by using a texturizing spray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Box Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your box haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

FAQ About Box Haircuts

What face shape is best for a box haircut?

A box haircut works well for most face shapes, but it’s particularly flattering for those with a square or angular face.

Can I get a box haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, a box haircut can work for curly hair. The stylist will need to cut your hair when it’s dry to ensure that the shape is defined.

How long does a box haircut last?

A box haircut can last up to 6 weeks with proper maintenance and regular trims.

Final Thoughts

A box haircut is a classic and stylish haircut that works for all hair types and lengths. It’s easy to maintain and looks great on everyone. If you’re looking for a new haircut, consider trying out the box haircut.

Remember to go to a professional stylist who has experience cutting this style to ensure that you get the best results. With proper maintenance and regular trims, your box haircut will look great for weeks to come.
