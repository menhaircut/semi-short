The Best Fringe For Thick Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect fringe to suit your thick wavy hair? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide to help you choose the best fringe for your hair type. In this article, we will cover everything from the different types of fringes to how to style them, so you can find the perfect look for you.

Description: What is Thick Wavy Hair?

Thick wavy hair is a hair type that is characterized by its thickness and its natural waves. This hair type can be difficult to manage, but with the right cut and style, it can look stunning. Thick wavy hair often requires more maintenance than other hair types, but the results are worth it.

How to Choose the Best Fringe for Thick Wavy Hair

When choosing a fringe for thick wavy hair, it is important to consider the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the shape of your face. Here are some tips to help you choose the best fringe:

1. Consider the Length of Your Hair

If you have long hair, a long fringe can look stunning. If you have shorter hair, a shorter fringe may be more suitable. It is important to choose a fringe that complements the length of your hair.

2. Consider the Thickness of Your Hair

If you have thick hair, a heavy fringe may be too much. Consider a lighter fringe that will not overwhelm your hair. If you have thin hair, a heavier fringe can add volume and texture.

3. Consider the Shape of Your Face

The shape of your face can also play a role in choosing the best fringe. If you have a round face, a side-swept fringe can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a soft, rounded fringe can help to soften your features.

Step by Step: How to Style the Best Fringe for Thick Wavy Hair

Once you have chosen the best fringe for your thick wavy hair, it is important to know how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling the perfect fringe:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick wavy hair. This will help to nourish your hair and make it easier to manage.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Style Your Fringe

Use a small round brush to style your fringe. Blow dry your fringe in the direction that you want it to sit. Use a little bit of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe

Maintaining your fringe is important to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your fringe:

1. Trim Your Fringe Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your fringe looking neat and tidy. Aim to trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your fringe looking fresh between washes.

3. Avoid Touching Your Fringe

Avoid touching your fringe too much, as this can cause it to become greasy and lose its shape.

FAQs

What is the Best Fringe for Thick Wavy Hair?

The best fringe for thick wavy hair is one that complements the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the shape of your face. Consider a long, side-swept fringe for a dramatic look, or a shorter, choppy fringe for a more casual look.

How Do I Style a Side-Swept Fringe?

To style a side-swept fringe, blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to sit. Use a small round brush to create volume and texture. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

How Do I Maintain My Fringe?

To maintain your fringe, trim it regularly, use a dry shampoo to keep it looking fresh between washes, and avoid touching it too much.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

Thick wavy hair requires more maintenance than other hair types. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use a Shampoo and Conditioner Designed for Thick Wavy Hair

Using a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick wavy hair can help to nourish your hair and make it easier to manage.

2. Use a Hair Mask Once a Week

Using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it more manageable and less prone to frizz.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

By following these tips, you can find the best fringe for your thick wavy hair and keep it looking its best. Remember to choose a fringe that complements the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the shape of your face. With a little bit of maintenance and styling, you can achieve the perfect look for you.
