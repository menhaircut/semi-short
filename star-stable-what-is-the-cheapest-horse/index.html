Star Stable What Is The Cheapest Horse

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a new player in Star Stable and looking for the cheapest horse? Look no further! We’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to find the cheapest horse in Star Stable.

Description

Star Stable is a horse simulation game where players get to ride, care for, and compete with their horses. Players can choose from different breeds of horses and customize them with various accessories such as saddles, reins, and blankets.

However, owning a horse in Star Stable can be expensive. The cost of buying a horse and its accessories can quickly add up. That’s why it’s essential to know which horse is the cheapest in the game.

How to Find the Cheapest Horse in Star Stable

Here are the steps to find the cheapest horse in Star Stable:

Step 1: Go to the Horse Market

The Horse Market is where players can buy and sell horses. To access the Horse Market, click on the map icon and select the Horse Market option.

Step 2: Filter by Breed and Price

In the Horse Market, you can filter the horses by breed, gender, level, and price. To find the cheapest horse, select the breed that has the lowest price.

For example, the cheapest horse breed in Star Stable is the Jorvik Pony. You can filter the horses by breed and select the Jorvik Pony. Then, sort the horses by price, and the cheapest Jorvik Pony will appear first.

Step 3: Buy the Horse

Once you’ve found the cheapest horse, click on the Buy Horse button to purchase it. The horse will be added to your stable.

Tips for Buying a Cheap Horse in Star Stable

Here are some tips to keep in mind when buying a cheap horse in Star Stable:

  • Consider the horse’s stats: The cheapest horse may not have the best stats, so make sure to check its speed, stamina, and jumping ability.
  • Look for discounts: Star Stable often has sales and discounts on horses and accessories, so keep an eye out for those.
  • Consider buying a foal: Foals are cheaper than adult horses, but they require more care and training.

FAQ

What is the cheapest horse breed in Star Stable?

The cheapest horse breed in Star Stable is the Jorvik Pony.

How much does the cheapest horse cost in Star Stable?

The price of the cheapest horse in Star Stable varies depending on its breed, level, and stats. However, the cheapest Jorvik Pony costs around 450 Star Coins.

Can I customize my cheap horse in Star Stable?

Yes, you can customize your cheap horse in Star Stable by adding accessories such as saddles, reins, and blankets.

Hair Care for Your Cheap Horse in Star Stable

Just because your horse is cheap doesn’t mean you can neglect its hair care. Here are some tips to keep your horse’s mane and tail healthy:

  • Brush its hair regularly: Use a soft brush to remove tangles and debris from your horse’s mane and tail.
  • Use a detangler spray: Spray a detangler product on your horse’s hair to make it easier to comb through.
  • Trim its hair: Trim the ends of your horse’s mane and tail to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your cheap horse’s hair looking healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, finding the cheapest horse in Star Stable is easy if you know where to look. By following the steps we’ve provided and keeping our tips in mind, you can save money while still enjoying the game.
