How To Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs For Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? Curtain bangs may just be the answer you've been looking for. They are a trending hairstyle that suits all face shapes, and the best part is that you can easily cut them yourself at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own curtain bangs for wavy hair in relaxed English language.

Description

Curtain bangs are long, wispy bangs that frame your face on both sides, creating a curtain-like effect. They are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. They are also low maintenance, making them perfect for those who want a trendy look without the hassle of frequent salon visits.

How to Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs for Wavy Hair

Cutting your own curtain bangs for wavy hair may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it's actually quite simple. Follow these steps to get the perfect curtain bangs:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a hair clip.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into a triangle shape, starting from the top of your head and ending at your eyebrows. Clip the rest of your hair away so that it's out of the way.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the section of hair straight up and comb it forward. Cut your bangs in a straight line, starting from the middle and working your way outwards. Be sure to keep the scissors at an angle to create a soft, wispy effect.

Step 4: Check the Length

After you've cut your bangs, check the length. If they're too long, you can always go back and trim them. Remember, it's better to start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter than to cut them too short right away.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Now that you've cut your curtain bangs, it's time to style them. You can style them to the side or wear them parted in the middle. Use a curling iron to add some waves or leave them straight for a sleek look.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Start with dry hair so that you can see exactly how your bangs will fall.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Be sure to cut your bangs while they're dry. Wet hair can shrink when it dries, which can result in shorter bangs than you intended.
  • When in doubt, cut less. You can always go back and trim more if needed, but it's difficult to fix bangs that are too short.

FAQ

Can I Cut Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair?

Yes, you can cut curtain bangs with curly hair. However, it's important to note that curly hair can shrink when it dries, so be sure to cut your bangs longer than you want them to be.

How Often Should I Trim My Curtain Bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, be sure to wash and condition them regularly. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry as much as possible.

Conclusion

Cutting your own curtain bangs for wavy hair is a great way to update your hairstyle and stay on-trend without breaking the bank. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for the best results. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to try again if your first attempt doesn't turn out as expected. Good luck!
