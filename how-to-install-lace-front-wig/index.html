How To Install Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Installing a lace front wig can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and a little practice, it can be done easily in the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of installing a lace front wig, as well as tips for maintaining and caring for your wig.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing Your Hair and Wig
  • Applying Your Wig
  • Styling Your Wig
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Wig

Tools You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Lace front wig
  • Wig cap
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Adhesive (optional)

Preparing Your Hair and Wig

Before applying your wig, it's important to prepare both your hair and the wig itself.

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Put on a wig cap to keep your hair flat and in place.
  3. Trim the lace on your wig with scissors to fit your hairline.
  4. Apply adhesive (optional) to the perimeter of the wig for extra hold.

Applying Your Wig

Now that your hair and wig are ready, it's time to apply the wig.

  1. Place the wig on your head, starting at the front hairline.
  2. Adjust the wig so that it fits snugly and comfortably.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the wig in place.
  4. Blend your natural hair with the wig using a comb.
  5. Apply hairspray to keep the wig in place.

Styling Your Wig

Once your wig is in place, you can style it to your liking. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a curling iron or straightener to style your wig.
  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your wig to prevent damage.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

To keep your wig looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for wigs.
  • Avoid brushing your wig when it's wet to prevent damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about lace front wigs:

  • How long do lace front wigs last? With proper care and maintenance, a lace front wig can last up to six months to a year.
  • Can I swim or shower with my lace front wig? It's not recommended to wear your wig in the shower or while swimming, as this can damage the wig and its adhesive.
  • How do I remove my lace front wig? To remove your wig, simply lift the wig off your head from the back to the front.

Hair Care for Your Wig

To keep your wig looking and feeling its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for wigs.
  • Avoid brushing your wig when it's wet to prevent damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to confidently install and care for your lace front wig. Happy styling!
