How To Make A Square Face Look Pretty

Written by Robby Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, people have become more open to experimenting with different looks and styles. One of the biggest challenges for those with a square face is finding ways to enhance their features and create a pretty look. If you have a square-shaped face, don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing how to make a square face look pretty with some tips and tricks.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a wide forehead. It is a symmetrical face shape, and the length and width of the face are almost equal. This face shape can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques, you can create a pretty look that enhances your features.

How to

Step 1: Contouring

Contouring is a great technique to create an illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Using a contouring powder or cream, apply it to the sides of your forehead, temples, and under your cheekbones. Blend it well to create a natural look. This will help to soften your jawline and make your face look more feminine.

Step 2: Highlighting

Highlighting is another technique that can help to enhance your features. Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the tip of your nose. This will help to create a more defined look and bring attention to these areas.

Step 3: Soft Waves

Soft waves can help to soften your features and create a more feminine look. Use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair. This will help to frame your face and draw attention away from your jawline.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your square face look pretty:

  • Choose haircuts that are soft and feminine, such as long layers or a bob with soft curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sharp or angular, such as a blunt bob or a severe updo.
  • Wear earrings that are long and dangling, as they can help to elongate your face.
  • Avoid earrings that are too short or wide, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Choose makeup shades that are warm and soft, such as peach or coral shades.
  • Avoid makeup shades that are too dark or cool, such as black or blue shades.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make a square face look pretty:

What kind of hairstyles look good on a square face?

Hairstyles that are soft and feminine look good on a square face. Long layers or a bob with soft curls are great options.

What kind of earrings should I wear with a square face?

Long and dangling earrings are great options for a square face, as they can help to elongate your face.

What kind of makeup should I wear with a square face?

Warm and soft makeup shades, such as peach or coral shades, look great on a square face. Avoid makeup shades that are too dark or cool.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important to enhance your features. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your square face look pretty and enhance your features. Remember to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
