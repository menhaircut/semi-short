How To Wear Turban Headband With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that wearing a turban headband is out of the question. But fear not! With the right technique, you can rock a turban headband with short hair and look fabulous. In this guide, we’ll show you how to wear a turban headband with short hair, including tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Turban Headband with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Turban Headband with Short Hair

The first step to wearing a turban headband with short hair is choosing the right headband. Look for one that is stretchy and can be adjusted to fit your head. You can choose a headband with a knot in the front or a plain one, depending on your preferences.

Once you have your headband, it’s time to start styling your hair. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and help your turban headband stay in place.

2. Create volume

If you have short hair, you might be worried that a turban headband will make your hair look flat. To avoid this, create some volume by blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing product.

3. Section your hair

Section your hair into two or three parts, depending on the thickness of your hair. This will make it easier to wrap your hair around the headband.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your headband and have prepped your hair, it’s time to start wrapping!

1. Place the headband on your head

Place the headband on your head, making sure it’s centered and covers your ears.

2. Take a section of hair

Take a section of hair from the front of your head and wrap it around the headband, tucking the ends in at the back.

3. Repeat on the other side

Repeat on the other side, taking a section of hair and wrapping it around the headband.

4. Continue wrapping

Continue wrapping sections of hair around the headband, going from the front to the back of your head.

5. Tuck in the ends

Once you’ve wrapped all of your hair around the headband, tuck the ends in at the back. You can use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect turban headband look:

1. Experiment with different styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. You can try wrapping your hair in a spiral pattern or twisting it before wrapping it around the headband.

2. Use a scarf

If you don’t have a turban headband, you can use a scarf instead. Just fold it into a long strip and wrap it around your head, tying it at the back.

3. Add accessories

You can add accessories like earrings or a necklace to complete your turban headband look. Just make sure they don’t clash with your headband.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a turban headband with curly hair?

A: Yes! A turban headband can look great with curly hair. Just make sure to create some volume before wrapping your hair around the headband.

Q: How do I keep my turban headband in place?

A: Make sure your headband is snug but not too tight. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Q: Can I wear a turban headband with bangs?

A: Yes! You can either tuck your bangs into the headband or leave them out for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for one that is sulfate-free and contains natural ingredients.

2. Condition regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a turban headband with short hair and look fabulous. So go ahead and try it out – you might just discover your new favorite hairstyle!
