How To Get Rid Of Frizzy Hair Fast Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 27, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to tame those unruly locks. Whether you have naturally curly hair or your hair tends to frizz up in humid weather, there are plenty of ways to get rid of frizzy hair fast. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you get smooth, shiny hair in no time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair Fast
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Frizzy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair Fast

Frizzy hair is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair shaft. When the hair is dry, it tends to frizz up and become more difficult to manage. Here are some tips to help you get rid of frizzy hair fast:

1. Use a Hair Mask

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that can help to moisturize and nourish your hair. Look for a hair mask that is specifically designed for frizzy hair, and use it once a week to help keep your hair smooth and shiny.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to detangle your hair and keep it hydrated throughout the day. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it, and comb it through to distribute evenly.

3. Use a Serum or Oil

A serum or oil can help to smooth down the hair cuticle and keep your hair looking shiny and healthy. Apply a small amount of serum or oil to your hair after styling it, and avoid applying too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

4. Use a Microfiber Towel

A microfiber towel is gentler on your hair than a regular towel, and can help to prevent breakage and frizz. Instead of rubbing your hair dry with a towel, gently blot it to remove excess water.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get rid of frizzy hair fast:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo.

Look for a shampoo that is specifically designed for dry or frizzy hair, and avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a hair mask.

Apply a hair mask to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time, and then rinse it out thoroughly.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner.

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair, and comb it through to distribute evenly.

4. Apply a serum or oil.

Apply a small amount of serum or oil to your hair, focusing on the ends. Avoid applying too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

5. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Use a low heat setting on your blow dryer, and use a diffuser attachment to help distribute the heat evenly.

6. Finish with a blast of cool air.

Finish blow-drying your hair with a blast of cool air to help seal the cuticle and keep your hair smooth and shiny.

Tips to Prevent Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips to help prevent frizzy hair:

1. Avoid over-washing your hair.

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try washing your hair every other day, or even every third day, to help keep it hydrated.

2. Use a heat protectant spray.

If you use hot tools on your hair, such as a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage and frizz.

3. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase.

A silk or satin pillowcase is gentler on your hair than a cotton pillowcase, and can help prevent breakage and frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair mask every day?

A: No, it’s not necessary to use a hair mask every day. Use a hair mask once a week or as needed to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Q: Can I use a leave-in conditioner on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can use a leave-in conditioner on dry hair to help detangle and moisturize your hair throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated:

1. Eat a healthy diet.

A healthy diet can help to nourish your hair from the inside out. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits and vegetables, and drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Protect your hair from the sun.

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to help protect your hair from the sun.

3. Get regular trims.

Getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to frizz. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of frizzy hair fast and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and avoid using harsh chemicals or hot tools that can damage your hair and lead to frizz.
