What Product Should I Use To Slick My Hair Back

Written by Petter Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that will make you look sharp and sophisticated? Slicking your hair back is a classic and timeless hairstyle that has been popular for decades. However, finding the right product to use to achieve this look can be challenging. In this article, we will discuss the best product to use to slick your hair back and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Slick Your Hair Back
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

Slicking your hair back involves using a hair product to smooth your hair down and style it so that it stays in place. The product you choose will depend on your hair type, the length of your hair, and the level of hold and shine you want to achieve. There are various products available on the market, including pomades, gels, waxes, and styling creams.

How To Slick Your Hair Back

Before you start, it's important to have clean and dry hair. You can either wash your hair with shampoo or wet your hair with water to dampen it. Once your hair is ready, you can apply the product of your choice. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. Then, use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and moving towards the back of your head.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to slick your hair back:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo or wet your hair with water to dampen it.
  2. Choose the product you want to use, such as a pomade, gel, wax, or styling cream.
  3. Take a small amount of product and apply it to your hair, starting from the front and working towards the back.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and moving towards the back of your head.
  6. Once your hair is slicked back, use your fingers to smooth down any stray hairs or flyaways.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a product that suits your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, use a stronger product that will hold your hair in place.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more if needed. You can always add more product, but it's harder to remove it once it's been applied.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to slick your hair back. A comb with wider teeth may not be able to smooth down your hair as effectively.
  • Don't apply too much product to your hair. This can make your hair greasy and heavy, and it may not hold the style as well.
  • Use a hairspray to set the style in place and prevent flyaways.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking your hair back:

  • What is the best product to use to slick my hair back? The best product to use will depend on your hair type and the level of hold and shine you want to achieve. Pomades, gels, waxes, and styling creams are all good options.
  • Can I slick my hair back if I have short hair? Yes, you can slick your hair back if you have short hair. Use a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down and slick your hair back with a comb.
  • How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy? Use a small amount of product and avoid applying too much. You can also use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking clean and fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what product to use to slick your hair back, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look with ease. Remember to choose a product that suits your hair type and texture, start with a small amount of product, and use a comb to slick your hair back. With these tips and tricks, you'll have the perfect slicked-back hairstyle in no time!
