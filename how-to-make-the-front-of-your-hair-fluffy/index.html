How To Make The Front Of Your Hair Fluffy

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling flat in the front? Do you want to add some volume and fluffiness to your locks? Well, you're in luck because we have some tips and tricks that will help you achieve that desired fluffiness in the front of your hair.

Description

Having fluffy hair has been a popular trend for many years. But achieving that look can be a bit tricky, especially when it comes to the front of your hair. The front of your hair is one of the most noticeable parts of your hair, and if it falls flat, it can ruin your entire hairstyle. That's why we've put together this guide to help you achieve that fluffy front look.

How to

Here are some steps you can follow to make the front of your hair fluffy:

Step 1: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Start by blow-drying your hair, focusing on the front section. Use a round brush to lift the hair up and away from your face. This will help add volume and create a fluffy look.

Step 2: Use a Texturizing Spray

Next, apply a texturizing spray to the front section of your hair. This will add texture and help create a tousled, fluffy look. Spray the product onto your hair, then scrunch it with your fingers to create volume.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is a great way to add volume and create a fluffy look. Use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair, focusing on the front section. This will create a lot of volume and give you that fluffy look you're after.

Step 4: Use a Hair Dryer and Round Brush

Use a hair dryer and round brush to create even more volume in the front section of your hair. Start by blow-drying your hair with the round brush, lifting it up and away from your face. Then, use the hair dryer to add heat to the roots of your hair, which will help set the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve that fluffy front look:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or loose waves, to add even more volume to your hair.
  • Consider getting a haircut with layers, as this can help add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making the front of your hair fluffy:

What products should I use to make the front of my hair fluffy?

You can use a texturizing spray, volumizing mousse, or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to the front of your hair.

How often should I tease my hair?

You should only tease your hair when it's absolutely necessary, as it can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit teasing to once or twice a week.

What hairstyles work best with fluffy hair?

Hairstyles that work best with fluffy hair include messy buns, loose waves, and half-up, half-down styles.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, fluffy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to keep your hair strong and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve that fluffy front look you've always wanted. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
