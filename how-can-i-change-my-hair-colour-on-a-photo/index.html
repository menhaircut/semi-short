How Can I Change My Hair Colour On A Photo A Complete Guide

Changing your hair colour can be a fun way to experiment with different looks. However, if you're not ready to commit to a new hair colour just yet, there's a way to change your hair colour without any risk. In this article, we'll be discussing how can I change my hair colour on a photo using simple techniques and tools.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Changing your hair colour on a photo means that you can see what you would look like with a different hair colour without actually dyeing your hair. There are several ways to change your hair colour on a photo, such as using photo editing software or apps.

How To

To change your hair colour on a photo, you can use photo editing software such as Adobe Photoshop or GIMP. Alternatively, you can use a hair colour changing app such as Hair Color Booth or YouCam Makeup.

Step by Step

Here's how you can change your hair colour on a photo using Adobe Photoshop:

  1. Open the photo you want to edit in Adobe Photoshop.
  2. Select the "Lasso" tool from the toolbar.
  3. Use the Lasso tool to carefully select the hair you want to change the colour of. Be sure to select only the hair and not any other parts of the photo.
  4. Once you've selected the hair, go to "Image"> "Adjustments"> "Hue/Saturation".
  5. Adjust the Hue slider to change the hair colour. You can also adjust the Saturation and Lightness sliders to fine-tune the colour.
  6. When you're happy with the new hair colour, save the edited photo.

Here's how you can change your hair colour on a photo using Hair Color Booth:

  1. Install Hair Color Booth on your phone.
  2. Select the photo you want to edit from your phone's gallery.
  3. Use the app's tools to select the hair you want to change the colour of. You can use the brush tool to paint over the hair or the smart tool to automatically detect the hair.
  4. Once you've selected the hair, choose a new hair colour from the app's colour palette.
  5. Adjust the hair colour intensity and brightness as desired.
  6. When you're happy with the new hair colour, save the edited photo.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair colour on a photo:

  • Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.
  • Experiment with different hair colours to find your perfect match.
  • Use high-quality photos for best results.
  • Be patient and take your time when selecting the hair to edit.

FAQ

Can I change my hair colour on any photo?

You can change your hair colour on any photo as long as the hair is visible and not obscured by anything else.

Do I need to have any photo editing skills to change my hair colour on a photo?

No, you don't need any photo editing skills to change your hair colour on a photo. You can use a hair colour changing app that does all the work for you.

Hair Care

Remember that changing your hair colour on a photo is just for fun and experimentation. If you decide to dye your hair, make sure to take proper care of it to keep it healthy and vibrant. Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for coloured hair, avoid excessive heat styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Changing your hair colour on a photo can be a fun way to try out new looks without any commitment. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily change your hair colour on a photo and see what you would look like with a different hair colour. Happy experimenting!
