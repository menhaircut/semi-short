How To Make Your Long Hair Wavy Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're a guy with long hair who wants to add some waves to your locks, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll show you how to make your long hair wavy guys with a simple, step-by-step guide that's easy to follow. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or you just want to switch up your look, these tips will help you achieve the wavy hairstyle you've been dreaming of.

  2. Tips for Getting the Perfect Waves

How to Make Your Long Hair Wavy Guys

The first step in making your long hair wavy guys is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and make sure to rinse thoroughly. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the styling tools you'll be using. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Now it's time to start creating your waves. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use a curling iron or wand to curl each section of hair, holding the iron or wand vertically and wrapping the hair around it. Hold for a few seconds, then release the curl.

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently tousle your waves and break them up a bit. You can also use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add some volume and texture to your waves.

Alternative Method: Braids

If you don't have access to a curling iron or wand, you can also create waves using braids. Simply divide your damp hair into sections and braid each section tightly. Once your hair is dry, undo the braids and use your fingers to tousle your waves.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Waves

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect waves:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a barrel that's at least 1 inch in diameter for loose, beachy waves.
  • Don't wrap your hair too tightly around the curling iron or wand, as this can create tight, ringlet-like curls instead of waves.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions and alternate the size of the sections you're curling.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold to your waves.

FAQs

How long will my waves last?

Your waves should last for several hours, or even all day if you use a strong-hold texturizing spray. However, they may start to fall out or lose their shape if your hair gets wet or if you're sweating heavily.

Can I create waves without using heat?

Yes, you can create waves using braids or twists and letting your hair air dry. However, this method may not result in as defined or long-lasting waves as using a curling iron or wand.

Do I need to use a heat protectant spray?

Yes, using a heat protectant spray is essential to prevent damage to your hair from the high temperatures of the curling iron or wand.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

If you want to keep your wavy hair looking its best, here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair to keep your waves looking defined and frizz-free.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your waves. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of curling irons and other styling tools to once or twice a week.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

By following these tips and using the techniques outlined in this post, you'll be able to make your long hair wavy guys in no time. Whether you're going for a casual, beachy look or a more polished style, wavy hair is a versatile and stylish option that's sure to turn heads.
