How To Have A More Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to have a more oval face? Having an oval-shaped face is often considered the most desirable face shape as it is believed to be the most balanced and symmetrical. If you have a round or square face shape, don't worry, you can still achieve an oval-shaped face by following these tips.

How to Have a More Oval Face

There are several ways to have a more oval face, including:

1. Contouring

Contouring is an easy and effective way to create the illusion of an oval face. You can use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face that you want to minimize, such as your cheeks and jawline. Use a brush to blend the contouring product well into your skin to create a natural look.

2. Hairstyling

Your hairstyle can greatly affect the shape of your face. If you have a round face, opt for a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face, such as a voluminous updo or a long layered haircut. A side-swept fringe can also help to create the illusion of an oval face.

3. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can lead to a more defined and contoured face shape. Some effective facial exercises include smiling and holding the smile for a few seconds, puckering your lips and holding for a few seconds, and making a fish face by sucking in your cheeks.

4. Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool for creating the illusion of an oval face. Use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone, to create dimension and draw attention away from the areas you want to minimize. You can also use a darker foundation or powder to contour your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving an Oval Face Shape

Follow these steps to achieve an oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can begin to work towards an oval face shape, you need to determine your current face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and examine the shape of your face. Is it round, square, or heart-shaped?

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Once you have determined your face shape, use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face that you want to minimize. Blend well using a brush.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face, such as a voluminous updo or a long layered haircut. A side-swept fringe can also help to create the illusion of an oval face.

Step 4: Do Facial Exercises

Perform facial exercises regularly to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can lead to a more defined and contoured face shape.

Step 5: Use Makeup Techniques

Apply highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone, to create dimension and draw attention away from the areas you want to minimize. Use a darker foundation or powder to contour your face.

Tips for Achieving an Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional tips for achieving an oval face shape:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid sugary and salty foods, which can cause bloating and puffiness in the face.
  • Get enough sleep to prevent under-eye bags and puffiness.
  • Wear sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage.

FAQ

Can I achieve an oval face shape without surgery?

Yes, you can achieve an oval face shape without surgery by following the tips outlined in this article.

How long does it take to achieve an oval face shape?

The time it takes to achieve an oval face shape depends on several factors, including your current face shape and the methods you use to achieve the desired shape. Consistency is key, so stick to a routine and be patient.

Can facial exercises cause wrinkles?

No, facial exercises should not cause wrinkles if performed correctly. However, excessive facial movements can lead to the formation of fine lines and wrinkles over time.

Hair Care for an Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you have a lot of versatility when it comes to hairstyles. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your features.

Choose a haircut that adds volume and lift to your hair, such as a layered or textured bob. A side-swept fringe can also help to create the illusion of an oval face. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat, as they can make your face appear longer and narrower.

When styling your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and lift. Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

By following these tips, you can achieve a more oval face shape and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to be patient and consistent, and always choose hairstyles and makeup techniques that complement your unique features.
