Introduction

Knowing your face shape is important when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories. However, many women are unsure of their face shape and how to determine it. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to tell your face shape female, along with tips and hair care advice for each shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you will need to measure your face using a tape measure or ruler. You will need to measure the following areas:

  • Forehead
  • Cheekbones
  • Jawline
  • Face length

Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Round: Your face length and cheekbone width are similar, and your forehead and jawline are also similar in width.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your jawline tapers to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower and roughly the same width.
  • Oblong: Your face length is greater than your cheekbone and forehead width, and your jawline is rounded.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to tell your face shape female:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead at the widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point.
  5. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  6. Compare your measurements to the guidelines for each face shape.
  7. Determine which face shape matches your measurements.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips for determining your face shape:

  • If your measurements are within a quarter of an inch of each other, you may have a combination of two face shapes.
  • If you have a prominent chin or forehead, this can affect the appearance of your face shape.
  • Look at photos of yourself from different angles to get a better idea of your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can weight loss or gain affect my face shape?

A: Yes, significant weight loss or gain can affect the appearance of your face shape.

Q: Can plastic surgery change my face shape?

A: Yes, certain types of plastic surgery can alter the shape of your face.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will flatter an oval face shape.
  • Round: Long, layered hairstyles can help elongate a round face shape.
  • Square: Soft, layered hairstyles can help soften the angles of a square face shape.
  • Heart: Chin-length or layered hairstyles can balance a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: Short hairstyles or hairstyles with volume at the crown can flatter a diamond-shaped face.
  • Oblong: Wispy bangs and layered hairstyles can help soften the length of an oblong face shape.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can determine your face shape and choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to experiment with different styles and consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect look for you.
