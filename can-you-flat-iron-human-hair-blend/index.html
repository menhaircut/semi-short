Can You Flat Iron Human Hair Blend

Written by Luffy Oct 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to straighten your hair, flat ironing is a great option. But what if you have a human hair blend? Can you flat iron it? The answer is yes, you can!

Description

A human hair blend is a combination of human hair and synthetic fibers. It's a popular choice for wigs, weaves, and extensions because it's more affordable than 100% human hair. However, it's important to note that not all human hair blends can be flat ironed.

How to Flat Iron Human Hair Blend

Before you start flat ironing your human hair blend, it's important to make sure that it's not a heat-sensitive blend. Some blends can't handle high heat, so it's best to check the label or ask the manufacturer. If your blend can handle heat, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup or dirt that may be on your hair.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before flat ironing. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and make it easier to straighten.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair straight with a round brush before flat ironing. This will make it easier to get a smooth, straight finish.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to flat iron your hair and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 5: Flat Iron Your Hair

Starting at the roots, glide the flat iron down the section of hair. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are straightened.

Step 6: Finish with a Serum or Oil

Finish your look with a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Tips for Flat Ironing Human Hair Blend

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when flat ironing your human hair blend:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates
  • Don't use too much heat
  • Don't flat iron wet or damp hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before flat ironing
  • Don't flat iron the same section of hair multiple times
  • Don't tug or pull on your hair when flat ironing

FAQ

Can You Curl Human Hair Blend?

Yes, you can curl human hair blend. Follow the same steps as you would for flat ironing, but use a curling iron instead.

How Often Can You Flat Iron Human Hair Blend?

You can flat iron human hair blend as often as you would like, but it's important to use heat protectant and not exceed the recommended heat settings for your hair blend.

Hair Care for Human Hair Blend

Proper hair care is key to keeping your human hair blend looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Keep your hair clean and well-moisturized
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind

With the right care and techniques, you can flat iron your human hair blend and achieve a sleek, straight look. Just remember to use heat protectant, don't use too much heat, and follow the steps outlined above.
