Mens Hairstyles High Top Fade The Ultimate Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the high top fade! This classic hairstyle has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect high top fade, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve a High Top Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade

Description

The high top fade is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the hair on top longer. The hair on top is then cut and shaped into a flat, rectangular shape that stands upright. The style is often paired with a line-up or edge-up around the hairline and a beard or goatee for a complete look.

The high top fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. It can be worn with straight, curly, or wavy hair, and can be done in a variety of lengths and shapes.

How to Achieve a High Top Fade

The high top fade is a complex haircut that requires precision and skill. It is recommended that you go to a professional barber or stylist to achieve the perfect high top fade. However, if you are feeling brave and want to try it yourself, here is a step-by-step guide:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is damp.
  2. Section off the hair on top of your head and clip it up and out of the way.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short. The length will depend on your personal preference, but typically ranges from a #1 to a #3 guard.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top using a comb and scissors or clippers.
  5. Unclip the hair on top of your head and comb it straight up.
  6. Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair on top into a rectangular shape. The length and shape will depend on your personal preference, but typically ranges from 2-4 inches.
  7. Blend the hair on top into the sides and back using a comb and scissors or clippers.
  8. Create a line-up or edge-up around the hairline using clippers or a razor. This will give your haircut a clean, polished look.
  9. Style your high top fade using a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax.
  10. Maintain your high top fade by getting regular trims every 2-4 weeks.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade

Here are a few tips to help you keep your high top fade looking fresh and stylish:

  • Use a high-quality hair product to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.
  • Brush your hair regularly to maintain the rectangular shape on top.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid touching or messing with your hair throughout the day to prevent it from losing its shape.
  • Consider adding a beard or goatee to complete your high top fade look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about high top fades:

Q: What type of hair works best for a high top fade?

A: The high top fade works best with thick, coarse hair. However, it can also be done with straight, curly, or wavy hair.

Q: How long does a high top fade last?

A: A high top fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Q: Can I achieve a high top fade at home?

A: While it is possible to achieve a high top fade at home, it is recommended that you go to a professional barber or stylist to ensure that it is done correctly.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your high top fade looking healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can weaken your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect high top fade and keep it looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come. So why wait? Book your appointment with a professional barber or stylist today and rock the high top fade with confidence!
