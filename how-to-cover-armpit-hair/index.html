How To Cover Armpit Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who prefers to keep their armpit hair covered, you might be wondering how to do so effectively. Whether you're wearing a sleeveless top or just want to feel more comfortable, covering up armpit hair is easier than you might think. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to do it, as well as some tips and FAQs you might find helpful.

How to Cover Armpit Hair

There are a few different methods you can use to cover up your armpit hair depending on your preference. Here are some of the most common:

1. Shave

Shaving your armpits is one of the most popular ways to get rid of hair. If you prefer this method, make sure to use a sharp razor and shaving cream to prevent irritation.

2. Wax

Waxing is another popular method that removes hair from the root. You can either go to a salon or do it yourself at home with a waxing kit. Just be prepared for some pain!

3. Use a Depilatory Cream

Depilatory creams are a less painful alternative to waxing. They dissolve hair at the skin's surface, making it easy to wipe away. Be sure to read the instructions carefully and do a patch test before using it all over your armpits.

4. Wear Clothing that Covers Your Armpits

If you don't want to remove your armpit hair, you can always wear clothing that covers it up. This might include long-sleeved shirts, jackets, or cardigans.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cover your armpit hair:

1. Clean and Dry Your Armpits

Make sure your armpits are clean and dry before attempting to cover them. This will help the adhesive stick better and prevent any irritation.

2. Apply Deodorant

Applying deodorant before covering your armpits can help control sweat and prevent odor.

3. Apply Adhesive Tape

Apply adhesive tape to the areas where you want to cover your armpit hair. Make sure the tape is secure but not too tight.

4. Wear Clothing that Covers Your Armpits

Wear clothing that covers the adhesive tape and your armpits. This might include a sleeveless top with a cardigan or a long-sleeved shirt.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when covering your armpit hair:

1. Use High-Quality Adhesive Tape

Using high-quality adhesive tape will ensure it stays in place and doesn't irritate your skin.

2. Avoid Tight Clothing

Tight clothing can cause irritation and make it difficult for the adhesive tape to stick. Opt for looser clothing that covers your armpits comfortably.

3. Be Gentle When Removing Tape

When removing the adhesive tape, be gentle to avoid irritating your skin. You can use baby oil or coconut oil to help remove any residue.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering armpit hair:

1. Is it safe to use adhesive tape on my skin?

Yes, as long as you're using high-quality adhesive tape and not applying it too tightly. If you have sensitive skin, do a patch test first.

2. Can I swim or exercise with adhesive tape on my armpits?

It's not recommended to swim or exercise with adhesive tape on your armpits as it can come loose and irritate your skin.

3. How often do I need to replace the adhesive tape?

It depends on how often you wear it and how much you sweat. Generally, you should replace the adhesive tape after a few uses.

Hair Care

If you choose to remove your armpit hair, make sure to take care of your skin afterwards. Here are some tips:

1. Moisturize

After shaving or waxing, make sure to moisturize your armpits to prevent dryness and irritation.

2. Exfoliate

Exfoliating your armpits once a week can help prevent ingrown hairs and keep your skin smooth.

3. Avoid Heavy Antiperspirants

Heavy antiperspirants can clog your pores and cause irritation after hair removal. Opt for a lighter formula instead.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cover your armpit hair and feel comfortable in any outfit.
