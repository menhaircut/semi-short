What Is The Hairstyle Called With One Side Shaved

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, you might be interested in the one-side-shaved hairstyle. This trendy look features a shaved or undercut section on one side of the head, with long hair on the other side. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It's a great way to show off your personality, and it can be customized to suit your individual style.

How to Achieve the One-Side-Shaved Look

If you're interested in trying out the one-side-shaved look, you'll need to visit a hairstylist who is familiar with this style. The process involves shaving or undercutting one side of the head, while leaving the other side long. The length of the long side can vary, depending on your preference. Some people opt for a dramatic contrast, with a very short shaved side and very long hair on the other side. Others prefer a more subtle look, with a longer shaved side and shorter hair on the other side.

Once the hair has been shaved or undercut, you'll need to style it to achieve the desired look. This can involve using hair gel, mousse, or other styling products to create volume and hold. You can also experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, ponytails, or buns, to show off your new look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the one-side-shaved look:

  1. Choose a hairstylist who is familiar with this style.
  2. Decide on the length of the shaved side and the long side.
  3. Sit back and let the hairstylist work their magic!
  4. Once the hair has been shaved or undercut, style it using your preferred products.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your One-Side-Shaved Style

If you've decided to try out the one-side-shaved look, here are some tips for maintaining your new style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and exciting.
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some extra flair to your style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the one-side-shaved hairstyle:

  • Is the one-side-shaved look suitable for all hair types?
  • Can I still wear my hair up with a one-side-shaved style?
  • How often do I need to get my hair shaved or undercut?
  • Can I still dye my hair with a one-side-shaved style?

Hair Care for One-Side-Shaved Style

It's important to take good care of your hair, especially if you've opted for the one-side-shaved style. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair oil or serum to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

The one-side-shaved hairstyle is a bold and edgy look that can be customized to suit your individual style. Whether you opt for a dramatic contrast or a more subtle look, this style is sure to turn heads. With the right care and maintenance, you can enjoy your new look for months to come.
