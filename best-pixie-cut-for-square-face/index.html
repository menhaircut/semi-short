Best Pixie Cut For Square Face

If you have a square face shape, you may feel like your options for haircuts are limited. However, a pixie cut can be a great choice for those with square faces, as it can help soften the angles of the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of a pixie cut for square faces is to balance out these features and soften the angles of the face. This can be achieved by incorporating layers, texture, and volume into the cut.

How to Get the Best Pixie Cut for Square Face

Before getting your pixie cut, it's important to consult with your stylist to determine which style will work best for your face shape and hair type. Some of the best pixie cuts for square faces include:

1. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can help soften the angles of a square face shape. This cut typically features shorter layers on top and longer layers on the sides, which can create volume and movement in the hair.

2. Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut can also be a great choice for square faces. This cut features choppy layers and lots of texture, which can help add dimension to the hair and soften the face shape.

3. Side-Swept Pixie Cut

A side-swept pixie cut can help draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while also balancing out a square jawline. This style typically features longer bangs that are swept to one side of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect pixie cut for square faces:

1. Consult with Your Stylist

Before getting your pixie cut, be sure to consult with your stylist to determine which style will work best for your face shape and hair type. They may also be able to recommend products and styling techniques to help you maintain your new cut.

2. Wash Your Hair

Before your appointment, be sure to wash your hair and avoid using any heavy styling products, as this can make it more difficult for your stylist to work with your hair.

3. Discuss Length and Layers

During your appointment, discuss the desired length and layers for your pixie cut. Your stylist may also be able to recommend a specific style based on your face shape and hair type.

4. Cut and Style Your Hair

Your stylist will then cut and style your hair to achieve the desired look. They may also use styling products and tools to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are a few tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help extend the time between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as using a flat iron or curling wand, to create different looks with your pixie cut.

FAQs

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can work well for a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your pixie cut and prevent split ends.

Can I style my pixie cut in different ways?

Absolutely! There are many ways to style a pixie cut, including adding volume, texture, and waves. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut

Here are a few tips for keeping your pixie cut healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right cut and styling techniques, a pixie cut can be a great choice for those with square faces. Consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair type, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
