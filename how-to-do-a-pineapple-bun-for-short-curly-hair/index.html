How To Do A Pineapple Bun For Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a quick and easy hairstyle for your short curly hair? Look no further than the pineapple bun! This trendy hairstyle is perfect for those with short, curly hair who want to keep their curls intact while still looking stylish. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a perfect pineapple bun.

Table of Contents

  • How To Do a Pineapple Bun
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Pineapple Bun

How To Do a Pineapple Bun

A pineapple bun is a high bun that sits on the top of your head, resembling the shape of a pineapple. It's a simple and easy hairstyle that only takes a few minutes to create. Here's what you'll need:

  • Elastic hairband
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with dry, curly hair. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your curls and smooth out any bumps. If your curls are particularly frizzy or dry, you may want to apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner or oil to help smooth them out.

Step 2: Create a High Ponytail

Gather your hair on the top of your head and create a high ponytail. Use an elastic hairband to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 3: Pull Your Hair Through the Hairband

Take the ponytail and pull it through the hairband, creating a loop. Make sure the hairband sits at the base of the loop.

Step 4: Fluff Your Curls

Fluff your curls by gently pulling them outwards, creating volume and height. You'll want to make sure your curls are evenly distributed around the bun.

Step 5: Secure the Bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. You may need to use several pins to make sure the bun stays put.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place. You can also use bobby pins to smooth down any flyaways or to add extra volume to your bun.

Tips for a Perfect Pineapple Bun

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before creating the pineapple bun. Wet hair can cause the bun to sag or lose its shape.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a more natural look.
  • If you have shorter hair, you can still create a pineapple bun by using a small hairband and pulling your hair through the band multiple times to create a smaller loop.
  • If you want extra volume, you can tease your hair before creating the pineapple bun.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do a pineapple bun with straight hair?

While the pineapple bun is traditionally done with curly hair, you can still create a similar look with straight hair. Simply use a curling iron or rollers to add some curls or waves to your hair before creating the bun.

How long does a pineapple bun last?

A well-done pineapple bun can last all day, although you may need to use a few bobby pins to keep it in place throughout the day.

Can I wear a pineapple bun to a formal event?

While the pineapple bun is a more casual hairstyle, you can dress it up by adding some hair accessories, such as a headband or sparkly pins.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

If you have short curly hair, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking healthy and defined. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, detangle your curls with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers when your hair is wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to help keep your curls moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your curls. If you need to use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls.

With these tips, you'll be able to create a perfect pineapple bun for your short curly hair in no time. Give it a try and see how it transforms your look!
