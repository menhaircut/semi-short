How To Know Which Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Written by Frank Jun 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair colour and looking to switch things up? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you have never dyed your hair before. With so many colours and shades available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will suit you best. But don't worry, we're here to help you figure out how to know which hair colour will suit you.

How to Determine Which Hair Colour Will Suit You

Before you start browsing hair colour options, it's important to determine which colours will suit your skin tone, eye colour, and overall style. Here are some tips to help you decide:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour, as it can greatly impact how the colour looks on you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have warm-toned skin (yellow or golden undertones), you'll look best with warm hair colours like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper.
  • If you have cool-toned skin (pink or blue undertones), you'll look best with cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or deep brown.
  • If you have neutral-toned skin (a mix of warm and cool tones), you'll look good with most hair colours, but try to avoid extremes like very light or very dark shades.

Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in which hair colour will suit you best. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have blue eyes, you'll look best with cool hair colours like platinum blonde or ash brown.
  • If you have green eyes, you'll look best with warm hair colours like golden blonde or caramel brown.
  • If you have brown eyes, you can pull off a wide range of hair colours, but warm shades like honey or chestnut will look particularly good.

Consider Your Style

Your personal style should also be taken into account when choosing a hair colour. If you have a classic, conservative style, you may want to stick with natural-looking shades like brown or blonde. If you have a more edgy or trendy style, you may be able to pull off bolder colours like bright pink or blue.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Now that you have a better idea of which hair colours may suit you, here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right one:

  1. Start by considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.
  2. Look at pictures of people with similar features to yours who have dyed their hair the colour you're considering.
  3. Talk to your hair stylist about your options.
  4. Consider doing a temporary or semi-permanent colour before committing to a permanent change.
  5. Take good care of your hair after dyeing it to ensure the colour lasts as long as possible.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle - if you have a busy schedule or don't want to spend a lot of time maintaining your hair, choose a low-maintenance colour.
  • Be realistic about the upkeep - some colours require more maintenance than others, so make sure you're prepared to commit to regular touch-ups and salon visits.
  • Think about the season - darker colours may be more appropriate for fall and winter, while lighter colours are perfect for spring and summer.
  • Ask for professional advice - your hair stylist can provide valuable insight into which colours will suit you best.

FAQ About Choosing the Right Hair Colour

How can I tell if a hair colour will look good on me?

The best way to tell if a hair colour will look good on you is to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. Look at pictures of people with similar features to yours who have dyed their hair the colour you're considering, and talk to your hair stylist about your options.

What's the best way to maintain a new hair colour?

To maintain a new hair colour, be sure to use products specifically designed for coloured hair, avoid excessive heat styling, and protect your hair from the sun. Regular touch-ups at the salon will also help keep your colour looking fresh.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's generally recommended to have it done professionally, especially if you're making a drastic change. A hair stylist can help ensure that the colour is applied evenly and that your hair stays healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you've chosen a new hair colour, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure that the colour lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner formulated for coloured hair to prevent fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can cause damage and fade the colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular touch-ups at the salon to maintain your colour.

Choosing a new hair colour can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style, and following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect colour to suit you.
