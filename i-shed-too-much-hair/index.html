I Shed Too Much Hair Understanding The Causes And Solutions

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you worried about the amount of hair you're shedding lately? If so, you're not alone. Many people, both men and women, experience excessive hair shedding at some point in their lives. In this article, we'll explore the causes of hair shedding and provide some tips on how to reduce it.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Shedding
  • How to Reduce Hair Shedding
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Reducing Hair Shedding
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Shedding

Causes of Hair Shedding

There are many different causes of hair shedding, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications
  • Hair treatments

It's important to identify the underlying cause of your hair shedding in order to determine the best course of action.

How to Reduce Hair Shedding

Reducing hair shedding involves identifying the underlying cause and making lifestyle changes to address it. Here are some general tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, iron, and vitamins
  • Manage stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques
  • Avoid harsh hair treatments like perms, relaxers, and excessive heat styling
  • Use gentle hair products that don't contain sulfates or other harsh chemicals
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage

Step-by-Step Guide to Reducing Hair Shedding

If you're shedding too much hair, here's a step-by-step guide to reducing it:

  1. Identify the underlying cause of your hair shedding
  2. Make lifestyle changes to address the cause (e.g. eat a balanced diet, manage stress)
  3. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that don't contain sulfates or other harsh chemicals
  4. Avoid excessive heat styling and harsh hair treatments
  5. Consider using a hair growth supplement or topical treatment
  6. Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage

Tips for Preventing Hair Shedding

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair shedding:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Don't brush or comb your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or saltwater
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it normal to shed hair every day?

A: Yes, it's normal to shed 50-100 hairs per day. However, if you're shedding more than that, it could be a sign of a problem.

Q: Can stress cause hair shedding?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair shedding. When you're stressed, your body produces more cortisol, which can lead to hair loss.

Q: Can hair products cause hair shedding?

A: Yes, hair products that contain harsh chemicals like sulfates can cause hair shedding.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

In addition to preventing hair shedding, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental damage

By following these tips and making lifestyle changes, you can reduce hair shedding and promote healthy hair growth. If you're still experiencing excessive shedding, it's important to talk to a healthcare professional to rule out any underlying medical conditions.
