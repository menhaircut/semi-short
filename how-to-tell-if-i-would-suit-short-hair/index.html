How To Tell If You Would Suit Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks and going for a short hairstyle? Short hair can be liberating, low maintenance, and stylish. However, not everyone can pull off a short haircut. If you're wondering whether you would suit short hair, keep reading. In this guide, we'll provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you make an informed decision.

How to Tell if You Would Suit Short Hair

Before you make the chop, you need to determine whether short hair suits you. Here are some factors to consider:

1. Face Shape

Short haircuts tend to look best on oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. However, if you have a round face, you can still wear short hair as long as you choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head.

2. Hair Texture

If you have straight or wavy hair, short hair will suit you. However, if you have curly hair, you'll need to choose a style that works with your natural texture. Pixie cuts, for instance, may not be the best choice for curly hair, as they require a lot of maintenance.

3. Lifestyle

Short haircuts are perfect for busy women who don't have time for styling. If you're always on the go, a short hairstyle will save you time and effort. However, if you're someone who loves experimenting with different hairstyles, you may find short hair limiting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to take the plunge and chop off your locks? Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Research

Before you visit the salon, do some research to determine the style you want. Look for inspiration on social media platforms like Pinterest and Instagram.

2. Consult with Your Stylist

Once you've decided on a style, consult with your stylist. They will advise you on whether the style will suit your face shape and hair texture.

3. Make the Chop

When you're ready, sit back and let your stylist work their magic. They will cut your hair to the desired length and style.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, style it using a styling product that works for your hair type. Short haircuts require minimal styling, which makes them perfect for busy women.

Tips for Choosing a Short Haircut

If you're still unsure whether short hair suits you, here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

1. Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a style that suits your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance style like a bob or a pixie cut will work best.

2. Take Your Face Shape into Account

Your face shape will determine the type of short haircut that suits you. Consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape.

3. Choose a Style That Works with Your Hair Texture

If you have curly hair, choose a style that works with your natural texture. If you have straight or wavy hair, you can experiment with different styles.

FAQs

1. Will short hair make me look older?

No, short hair doesn't make you look older. In fact, short hair can make you look younger and more vibrant.

2. Can I still style my short hair?

Yes, you can still style your short hair. Short haircuts require minimal styling, which makes them perfect for busy women.

3. Will a short haircut suit my round face?

Yes, a short haircut can suit a round face as long as you choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but that doesn't mean you should neglect your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo that works for your hair type.

2. Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and rinse thoroughly.

3. Style with a Product

Use a styling product that works for your hair type to style your hair. Apply the product to damp hair and style as desired.

Now that you know how to tell if you would suit short hair, it's time to make the chop. Remember to choose a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. With the right style, you'll look and feel amazing.
