How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly For Guys

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to try something new? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair naturally curly for guys. From how to prep your hair to tips for maintaining your curls, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Prepping Your Hair

Before we begin, it's important to prep your hair properly. Here's what you'll need:

  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair gel or mousse
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer

Now that you have your supplies, let's get started.

How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

There are a few different ways to make your hair naturally curly, but we'll focus on the most common method, which is using hair gel or mousse and a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair naturally curly:

  1. Shampoo your hair and condition it as you normally would.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Flip your hair upside down and use the diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Be sure to use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair while drying.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair again to create more curls.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair gel or mousse to your hair again, focusing on the ends.
  7. Flip your hair upside down again and use the diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair one more time.
  8. Once your hair is completely dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair one final time to create more curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Now that your hair is curly, you'll want to maintain your curls for as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it every day can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from tangling while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair naturally curly:

Can I make my hair naturally curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use methods like twist-outs or braid-outs to create curls without using heat.

How can I make my curls last longer?

Apply a small amount of hair gel or mousse to your hair every day and scrunch your hair to redefine your curls.

How long will it take for my hair to become naturally curly?

It depends on your hair type and texture, but it can take several months to a year for your hair to become naturally curly.

Hair Care

Maintaining curly hair requires a bit more effort than straight hair, but it's worth it to have those luscious curls. Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

And there you have it! Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll have natural, healthy-looking curls in no time.
