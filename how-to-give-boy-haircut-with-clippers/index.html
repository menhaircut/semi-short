How To Give Boy Haircut With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on your son's haircuts? Or maybe you want to learn how to give your child a haircut at home? Whatever your reason may be, using clippers to give a boy a haircut is an easy and cost-effective way to keep your child's hair neat and tidy. In this guide, we will walk you through how to give a boy a haircut with clippers.

How to

Before we begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a good quality set of clippers with different guards, a comb, scissors, and a towel. You may also need a spray bottle to wet the hair.

Step 1: Preparation

Start by washing your child's hair and towel-drying it. Comb out any tangles and make sure the hair is dry before starting the haircut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the right guard for the length of hair you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if necessary.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the clippers to cut the hair evenly, moving against the direction of hair growth. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven cuts.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair with the first guard, switch to a shorter one and blend the hair. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any longer strands.

Step 5: Trim the Sides

Use a shorter guard to trim the sides of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer strands.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of the head. Comb the hair forward and trim any longer strands. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 7: Clean Up

Once you have finished cutting, use a towel to wipe away any excess hair. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs that you may have missed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you give a boy a haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any longer strands.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  • Trim the hair when it is dry.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving a boy a haircut with clippers:

How often should I cut my son's hair?

It depends on your son's hair type and how fast it grows. On average, boys should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry if you make a mistake. Hair grows back quickly, and you can always go to a professional if you need help fixing a bad haircut.

Hair Care

After cutting your son's hair, make sure to wash and condition it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and shiny.

So there you have it, a step-by-step guide on how to give a boy a haircut with clippers. With a little practice, you'll be able to save money and give your child a great haircut at home.
