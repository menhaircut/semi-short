Does Short Hair Suit Chubby Faces

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It’s easy to maintain, versatile, and looks great on everyone! However, if you have a chubby face, you might be wondering if short hair will suit you. The good news is that short hair can look amazing on people with chubby faces. In this article, we’ll explore how to choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape and how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Chubby Faces
  • Step-by-Step: How to Style Short Hair for Chubby Faces

  • FAQs about Short Hair for Chubby Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Chubby Faces

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, it’s important to consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a slimmer jawline.

One of the best short hairstyles for round faces is a bob. A bob that hits just below the chin can help to elongate your face and create a more defined jawline. Another great option is a pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs. This style draws attention to your eyes and away from your cheeks, creating a more balanced look.

For those with square faces, a short hairstyle with layers can help to soften the angles of the face. A layered bob or shaggy pixie cut can work well for this face shape. If you have an oval face, you’re in luck – almost any short hairstyle will suit you!

Step-by-Step: How to Style Short Hair for Chubby Faces

Styling short hair is easy, and there are many different ways to wear it. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to styling short hair for chubby faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hair gel, to add texture and hold
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting the roots for added volume
  4. If desired, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to set the style

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Chubby Faces

Maintaining short hair is simple, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs about Short Hair for Chubby Faces

Q: Will short hair make my face look even rounder?

A: No, not necessarily. The right short hairstyle can actually help to elongate your face and create a more defined jawline.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if it’s short?

A: Yes! While a traditional ponytail might not be possible, you can still create a cute and stylish ponytail with a short haircut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Q: Is short hair high maintenance?

A: No, short hair is actually very low maintenance. It requires less shampoo and conditioner, dries faster, and is easier to style than longer hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but there are still a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your style looking fresh

Overall, short hair can look amazing on people with chubby faces. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and maintaining it properly, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance look that’s perfect for any occasion.
