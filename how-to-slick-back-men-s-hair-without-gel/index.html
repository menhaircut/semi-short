How To Slick Back Men s Hair Without Gel

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of using hair gel to slick back your hair, you're in luck! There are other products and techniques you can use to get the same sleek look without the sticky, crunchy feeling of gel. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back men's hair without gel and provide tips for keeping your hair healthy and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Men's Hair Without Gel
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Slick Back Men's Hair Without Gel

The key to slicking back your hair without gel is to use a product that provides hold and shine without the sticky residue of gel. Here are some products you can use:

  • Pomade: Pomade is a popular alternative to gel because it provides a similar hold and shine without the crunchy feeling. Look for pomades that are water-based, as they are easier to wash out.
  • Wax: Hair wax is another option for slicking back your hair. It provides a strong hold and a natural-looking finish.
  • Hair Spray: If you don't want to use any product in your hair, you can use hairspray to slick it back. Look for a hairspray with a strong hold and a natural finish.

Once you've chosen your product, follow these steps to slick back your hair:

Step by Step

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to style.
  2. Apply the product of your choice to your hair. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  3. Use a comb to slick back your hair. Start at the front and work your way back. Use the comb to create a part, if desired.
  4. Smooth down any flyaways with your hands or a brush.
  5. Finish with a hairspray, if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the best results when slicking back your hair without gel:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to get a sleek, polished look.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles, such as a side part or a slicked-back undercut.
  • Keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly and using a conditioner to keep it moisturized.

FAQ

Can I slick back my hair without using any product?

Yes, you can use hairspray to slick back your hair without using any product. Look for a hairspray with a strong hold and a natural finish.

Can I use gel occasionally?

Yes, you can use gel occasionally if you want to achieve a specific look. Just make sure to wash it out thoroughly to avoid buildup and damage to your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors, such as wind and pollution, by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can slick back your hair without gel and maintain healthy, stylish hair.
