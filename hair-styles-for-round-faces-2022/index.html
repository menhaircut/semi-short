Hair Styles For Round Faces 2022

Written by Petter Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to find the right one. In this article, we will be discussing the best hair styles for round faces in 2022.

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and height, making the face appear more oval-shaped. This can be achieved through a variety of haircuts and styling techniques.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are relatively equal, you likely have a round face.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to start exploring different hairstyles. Here are some of the best haircuts and styles for round faces in 2022:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for round faces, as they can create the illusion of length and height. A pixie cut, for example, can help elongate the neck and make the face appear more oval-shaped. A bob with layers can also be a flattering option, as it can add volume to the crown of the head and elongate the face.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts can also be a great option for round faces. A shoulder-length cut with layers can help create movement and texture, which can add dimension to the face. A lob (long bob) can also be a flattering choice, as it can elongate the face and create a more angular shape.

Long Haircuts

If you have a round face, you may be hesitant to try long haircuts. However, with the right styling techniques, long hair can be a great option. A layered cut with face-framing layers can help create the illusion of length and height. Soft, loose waves can also be a flattering choice, as they can add texture and dimension to the hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for round faces in 2022:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume at the crown of the head.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and definition.

Lob

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron to create loose waves.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the best hair styles for round faces in 2022:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Consider getting bangs, as they can help elongate the face and create a more angular shape.
  • Experiment with different partings, as they can help create the illusion of length and height.
  • Use styling products, such as volumizing mousse and hair wax, to add texture and definition to the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair styles for round faces in 2022:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and height. Short haircuts, such as a pixie cut, can be a great option, as can medium-length haircuts like a lob. Long haircuts can also work, as long as they are layered and styled properly.

Should I get bangs if I have a round face?

Bangs can be a great option for round faces, as they can help elongate the face and create a more angular shape. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can be a flattering choice.

How can I add volume to my hair if I have a round face?

Adding volume to the crown of the head can help elongate the face and create the illusion of height. You can do this by blow-drying your hair with a round brush or using a volumizing mousse.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face in 2022. Whether you opt for a short pixie cut or long layered locks, the key is to create the illusion of length and height. With a little experimentation and the right styling techniques, you can achieve a look that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features.
