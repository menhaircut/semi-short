How To Dye Asian Hair Blonde At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your black or brown hair and looking for a change? If you're an Asian with dark hair, you might think it's impossible to go blonde. But, with the right products and techniques, you can dye your hair blonde at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step, including tips and hair care instructions.

Description

Dyeing Asian hair blonde can be a challenging task because of the hair's natural pigmentation. Asian hair tends to have warm undertones, which can make the blonde hair look brassy or orange. However, with the right preparation and products, you can achieve a beautiful blonde shade that complements your skin tone.

How to Dye Asian Hair Blonde at Home

Step 1: Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

The first step in dyeing your hair blonde is to choose the right shade. Asians tend to have warm skin tones, so it's best to choose a warm or neutral blonde shade. Avoid cool or ashy tones, as they can clash with your skin tone and make your hair look unnatural.

Step 2: Bleach Your Hair

Before you can dye your hair blonde, you need to bleach it to remove the natural pigmentation. Bleaching can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use a high-quality bleach and follow the instructions carefully. Start with a small section of hair to test the bleach before applying it to your entire head.

Step 3: Apply the Blonde Dye

Once your hair is bleached, you can apply the blonde dye. Follow the instructions on the box carefully and apply the dye evenly to your hair. It's essential to leave the dye on for the recommended time to ensure that the color develops properly.

Step 4: Rinse Your Hair

After the recommended time, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized after the bleaching and dyeing process.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect blonde shade:

  • Use a high-quality bleach and blonde dye to ensure that the color comes out evenly.
  • Do a strand test before applying the bleach and dye to your entire head.
  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo before bleaching to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for the bleach.
  • Use a toner to remove any brassy or orange tones that may appear after bleaching.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently after dyeing, as this can strip the color and damage your hair.

FAQ

Can I Dye My Hair Blonde Without Bleaching?

No, it's not possible to dye your hair blonde without bleaching it first. Bleaching removes the natural pigmentation from your hair, allowing the blonde dye to take hold.

Can I Dye My Hair Blonde at Home?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde at home. However, it's essential to use high-quality products and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

How Often Should I Touch Up My Roots?

It's best to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color and prevent your natural hair color from showing through.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair blonde, it's important to take proper care of it to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and saltwater, which can cause the color to fade.

With these tips and instructions, you can dye your Asian hair blonde at home and achieve a beautiful, natural-looking color. Remember to always take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
