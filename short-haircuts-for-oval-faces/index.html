Short Haircuts For Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide 2023

Written by Luffy Oct 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! You have one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to choosing a short haircut. With a balanced forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for oval faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining them.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a balanced forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. It's longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and no sharp angles. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, but short haircuts are perfect for showcasing the balanced proportions of an oval face. Plus, short hair is low-maintenance and can make you look younger and more modern.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Oval Face

When choosing a short haircut for your oval face, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some tips:

Consider Your Hair Type

Short haircuts work best on hair that is thick, fine, or medium in texture. If you have curly hair, you may want to opt for a longer haircut to avoid a triangular shape. If you have thin hair, a layered haircut can add volume and movement.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some styling. If you're always on the go, choose a haircut that can be styled quickly and easily. If you have a more formal job or occasion, consider a more polished and structured haircut.

Consider Your Personal Style

When choosing a short haircut, consider your personal style and what makes you feel confident. Do you prefer a classic, timeless look or a more trendy and edgy style? Are you comfortable with a bold color or do you prefer to keep it natural?

Step by Step: How to Style Short Haircuts for Oval Faces

Now that you've chosen the right short haircut for your oval face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products formulated for your hair type. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to your damp hair. This will help to define your hair's natural texture and add volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift the roots and create volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement. You can also use a styling cream to define your hair's natural texture and add shine.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Oval Faces

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep to look their best. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

To maintain the shape and structure of your short haircut, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products formulated for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What are the best short haircuts for oval faces?

The best short haircuts for oval faces are pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and layered haircuts. These styles showcase the balanced proportions of an oval face.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, choose a style that complements your face shape, such as side-swept or blunt bangs.

Can oval faces pull off short haircuts with curls?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short haircuts with curls. However, choose a style that is layered and textured to avoid a triangular shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Oval Faces

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some TLC to look their best. Here are some hair care tips:

Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly with products formulated for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Avoid Overstyling

Avoid overstyling your hair with heat tools or harsh chemicals. This can cause damage and breakage.

Protect Your Hair from the Elements

When spending time outdoors, protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather with a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, short haircuts are perfect for oval faces due to their balanced proportions. Choose a style that complements your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style, and be sure to maintain it with regular trims and quality hair products. With the right short haircut, you can look modern, youthful, and confident.
