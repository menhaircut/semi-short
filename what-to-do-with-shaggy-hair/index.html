What To Do With Shaggy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Shaggy hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can turn your unruly locks into a stylish mane. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and tricks to help you manage your shaggy hair like a pro.

Description

Shaggy hair is characterized by its messy and unkempt appearance. This hairstyle is popular among both men and women and can be worn in different lengths and styles. However, shaggy hair can be challenging to style and maintain, especially if you have thick, curly, or coarse hair.

How to Manage Shaggy Hair

If you want to tame your shaggy hair, here are some tips and tricks to help you:

1. Get a Haircut

The first step to managing shaggy hair is to get a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right haircut that will complement your features and make your hair more manageable.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in managing shaggy hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for curly, thick, or fine hair. Also, use styling products like hair gel or mousse to help you control your hair and keep it in place.

3. Comb and Brush Your Hair

Combing and brushing your hair regularly help to detangle it and reduce frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, gently brushing or combing your hair in sections.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help to smooth out the frizz and give your hair a more polished look. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair while blow-drying it. However, avoid using high heat settings as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage your shaggy hair:

Step 1:

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2:

Apply a styling product like hair gel, mousse, or leave-in conditioner to help you control your hair.

Step 3:

Comb or brush your hair in sections, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4:

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Avoid using high heat settings to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5:

Style your hair according to your preference. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to add more texture and definition to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you manage your shaggy hair:

1. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help to hydrate and nourish your hair, making it more manageable and healthy-looking.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks can help to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Shaggy hair can be worn in different styles, such as messy buns, loose braids, or half-up hairstyles. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can I manage shaggy hair without cutting it?

A: While getting a haircut can help to manage shaggy hair, you can also use styling products and techniques to make your hair more manageable.

Q: What hair products should I use for shaggy hair?

A: Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for curly, thick, or fine hair. Also, use styling products like hair gel or mousse to help you control your hair and keep it in place.

Q: How often should I wash my shaggy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often. However, washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to manage. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days and use dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein can help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, making it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage. If you must use these tools, use them on low heat settings and apply a heat protectant product to your hair first.

Managing shaggy hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can turn your unruly locks into a stylish and manageable mane. Follow the tips and tricks outlined in this article to help you achieve the perfect shaggy hairstyle.
