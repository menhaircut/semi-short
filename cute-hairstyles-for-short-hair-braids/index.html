Cute Hairstyles For Short Hair Braids

If you have short hair, you might think that braids are not an option for you. But that's not true! There are plenty of cute hairstyles for short hair braids that you can try. In this article, we'll go over some of the best options for braids on short hair.

How to Braid Short Hair

Braiding short hair can be a bit tricky, but with a little practice, you can master it. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start by parting your hair on one side or in the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Continue to cross the right and left sections over the middle section, adding a small amount of hair to each section as you go.
  5. When you reach the nape of your neck, you can either continue to braid normally or switch to a different type of braid, such as a fishtail or a Dutch braid.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or bobby pins.

Cute Hairstyles for Short Hair Braids

Now that you know how to braid short hair, let's look at some cute hairstyles you can create:

1. Crown braid

A crown braid is a great option for short hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle, then braid each side of your hair into a Dutch braid. Once you reach the nape of your neck, continue to braid the rest of your hair regularly. Pin the braids up at the top of your head to create a crown-like effect.

2. Double braids

Divide your hair down the middle and create two regular braids. You can leave them as they are or wrap them around your head like a headband.

3. French braid

A French braid is a classic hairstyle that works well on short hair. Start at the top of your head and braid your hair down to the nape of your neck, then continue to braid normally to the end of your hair. You can either leave the braid as it is or tuck it under and pin it up for a more polished look.

4. Braided bangs

If you have bangs, try braiding them into a small braid and pinning them back to the side of your head. This adds a cute and unique touch to any hairstyle.

Tips for Braiding Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding short hair:

  • Use smaller sections of hair to make the braid more manageable.
  • Secure the end of the braid with a clear elastic band or bobby pins.
  • Add hairspray or texturizing spray to help the braid stay in place.
  • Experiment with different types of braids, such as fishtail or Dutch braids.

FAQs About Braids on Short Hair

Can you braid short hair?

Yes, you can braid short hair. It may take a bit more practice than braiding longer hair, but it's definitely doable.

What are some cute hairstyles for short hair braids?

Some cute hairstyles for short hair braids include crown braids, double braids, French braids, and braided bangs.

Hair Care for Braids on Short Hair

When you have braids on short hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when braiding.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair while it's braided.

With these cute hairstyles for short hair braids and tips for braiding and hair care, you'll be able to create a variety of looks that are both stylish and practical. Have fun experimenting with different braiding techniques and finding the perfect style for you!
