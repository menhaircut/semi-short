What Hairstyle Compliments My Face

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial when it comes to enhancing facial features. A hairstyle that compliments your face shape can bring out the best in you, while a wrong one can make you look unattractive. In this article, we will discuss the different face shapes and the hairstyles that best suit each face shape.

How to

The first step in determining which hairstyle will compliment your face is to identify your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can then choose a hairstyle that will enhance your features and bring out your best.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape by measuring your face with a measuring tape or ruler. Measure from the hairline to the chin, across the cheekbones, and across the jawline. Note down the measurements.

Step 2: Identify your face shape by comparing the measurements with the following guide:

  • Oval: The length of the face is about one and a half times the width. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin.
  • Round: The length and width of the face are almost equal. The chin is rounded, and the cheeks are full.
  • Square: The length and width of the face are almost equal. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: The forehead is wider than the cheeks, and the cheeks are wider than the chin.
  • Diamond: The face is widest at the cheekbones, and the forehead and chin are narrow.
  • Oblong: The face is longer than it is wide, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.

Step 3: Choose a hairstyle that compliments your face shape. Here are some recommendations:

Tips

Oval Face: Almost any hairstyle will compliment an oval face, but avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead completely.

Round Face: Long hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown area or hairstyles with layers that fall below the chin are great for round faces. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin.

Square Face: Soften the sharp angles of a square face with a layered hairstyle that falls below the jawline or a wavy hairstyle that adds volume to the sides.

Heart Face: Long hairstyles with layers that fall below the chin or shoulder-length hairstyles with soft curls are great for heart-shaped faces. Avoid short hairstyles that add volume to the crown area.

Diamond Face: A chin-length bob or shoulder-length hairstyle with layers that frame the face are great for diamond-shaped faces. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Oblong Face: Short hairstyles with volume on top or chin-length hairstyles with soft waves are great for oblong faces. Avoid long hairstyles that add length to the face.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that does not compliment my face shape?
A: Yes, you can wear any hairstyle you like, but it may not bring out the best in you. It is always best to choose a hairstyle that compliments your face shape.

Q: Can I change my face shape with a different hairstyle?
A: No, you cannot change your face shape with a different hairstyle, but you can enhance your features with a hairstyle that compliments your face shape.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen a hairstyle that compliments your face shape, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyle compliments your face, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that will enhance your features and bring out your best.
