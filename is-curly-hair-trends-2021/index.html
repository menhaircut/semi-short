Is Curly Hair Trends 2021: A Complete Guide

Curly hair is always in trend, and 2021 is no exception. If you have curly hair, it's time to embrace your natural texture and make the most of it. In this article, we'll discuss the latest curly hair trends, how to achieve them, and essential hair care tips to keep your curls healthy and bouncy.

  • How to Achieve the Trendy Curly Look

Curly Hair Trends 2021

This year, curly hair is all about embracing volume, texture, and natural movement. Here are some of the top curly hair trends you can try:

  • Big, Bouncy Curls: Think 80s curls with a modern twist. This trend is all about embracing your natural curls and adding volume and bounce to them.
  • Curly Bangs: Bangs are back in style, and curly bangs are a great way to add some edge to your look.
  • Curly Lob: The long bob is still going strong, and this year, it's all about adding some curls to it for a playful, flirty look.
  • Natural Hair Texture: The no-fuss, natural hair look is also trendy this year. Embrace your natural texture and add some styling products to enhance it.

Whatever trend you choose, make sure it works with your hair type and face shape.

How to Achieve the Trendy Curly Look

The key to achieving the trendy curly look is to work with your natural texture and enhance it. Here are some tips:

  • Start with a Good Haircut: A good haircut is the foundation of any great hairstyle. Make sure your stylist knows how to cut curly hair.
  • Hydrate Your Hair: Curly hair tends to be dry, so make sure you use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep it moisturized.
  • Apply Styling Products: Use styling products that enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume and definition.
  • Use Diffuser Attachment: A diffuser attachment on your hairdryer will help enhance your curls without causing frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair with hydrating products.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply curl-enhancing product to your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your natural curl pattern.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.
  6. Scrunch your hair again to add volume and bounce.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Essential Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some essential curly hair care tips to keep your curls healthy and bouncy:

  • Avoid Heat Styling: Heat styling can damage your curls, so try to avoid it as much as possible.
  • Deep Condition Regularly: Deep conditioning treatments can help restore moisture to your curls and keep them healthy.
  • Protect Your Hair While Sleeping: Use a silk pillowcase or wrap your hair in a silk scarf to prevent friction and frizz while you sleep.
  • Don't Overwash Your Hair: Curly hair doesn't need to be washed as often as straight hair. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.

Curly Hair FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

  • Is it okay to brush curly hair? It's best to avoid brushing curly hair as it can cause frizz and disrupt your natural curl pattern. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.
  • How often should I trim my curly hair? It's recommended to trim your curly hair every 3-4 months to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Can I wear my curly hair straight? Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but make sure you use a heat protectant and avoid doing it too often to prevent damage.
  • How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair? Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, avoid overwashing your hair, and use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the latest curly hair trends in 2021 and beyond. Embrace your natural texture and have fun with your curls!
