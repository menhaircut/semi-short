How To Make Your Hair Look Short Like A Boy Without Cutting It

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you ever feel like switching up your hairstyle without committing to a drastic cut? If you're looking for a way to make your hair look short like a boy without actually cutting it, you're in luck. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to achieve the look you want without grabbing the scissors.

Description

The "short hair, don't care" mentality is becoming increasingly popular among women. The idea of having a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and stylish is appealing to many. However, not everyone is ready to chop off their locks. If you're looking for ways to make your hair look short without cutting it, read on.

How to

There are several ways to make your hair look short without actually cutting it. Here are some methods you can try:

1. Tuck and Pin

One of the easiest ways to make your hair look shorter is to tuck and pin it. Start by pulling your hair back as if you were going to put it in a ponytail. Then, roll the ends under and pin them in place at the nape of your neck. This will create the illusion of a shorter haircut.

2. Use Hair Wax

Hair wax is a great product to use if you want to create a messy, textured look. Apply a small amount of hair wax to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends. Use your fingers to style your hair in a tousled, choppy way to make it look shorter.

3. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another way to make it look shorter. Start by creating a deep side part and then French braid your hair to the side. Secure the braid with a hair tie and then tuck the ends under at the nape of your neck. This will give you a cute, faux-bob look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair look short like a boy without actually cutting it:

Step 1: Tuck and Pin

Start by pulling your hair back as if you were going to put it in a ponytail.

Step 2: Roll the Ends Under

Roll the ends of your hair under and pin them in place at the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Apply Hair Wax

Apply a small amount of hair wax to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers to style your hair in a tousled, choppy way to make it look shorter.

Step 5: French Braid Your Hair

Create a deep side part and then French braid your hair to the side.

Step 6: Secure the Braid

Secure the braid with a hair tie and then tuck the ends under at the nape of your neck.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short hair look:

  • Invest in a good quality hair wax for the best results.
  • Experiment with different braiding techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place when tucking and pinning.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one.

FAQ

Can I make my hair look short without cutting it?

Yes, there are several ways to make your hair look shorter without actually cutting it. You can try tucking and pinning your hair, using hair wax to create a choppy, textured look, or braiding your hair to create a faux-bob.

What products do I need to make my hair look short?

You'll need a good quality hair wax to create a messy, textured look. You may also want to use bobby pins to secure your hair in place when tucking and pinning.

How can I make my hair look thicker when it's shorter?

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help make your hair look thicker and fuller. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair, even if you're not cutting it. Regular washing and conditioning, as well as occasional deep conditioning treatments, can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
