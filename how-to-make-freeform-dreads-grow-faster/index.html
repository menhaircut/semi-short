How To Make Freeform Dreads Grow Faster Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to achieve long and healthy freeform dreads, you need to give your hair the proper care and attention it deserves. Growing freeform dreads requires patience, time, and dedication - but there are some tips and tricks that can help you speed up the process. In this article, we’ll be sharing with you some effective ways to make your freeform dreads grow faster. Let’s dive in!

How to Make Freeform Dreads Grow Faster

Freeform dreads, also known as neglect dreads, are formed naturally without the use of any tools or products. The process of growing freeform dreads involves allowing your hair to mat and tangle over time. While this may seem like a simple process, there are some things you can do to encourage faster growth and healthier hair.

Step 1: Keep Your Scalp Clean

A clean and healthy scalp is essential for growing freeform dreads. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. A clean scalp promotes hair growth and prevents dandruff and other scalp infections.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturizing your hair is essential for preventing dryness and breakage. Use a natural oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Apply the product to your hair and scalp regularly to promote growth and prevent damage.

Step 3: Avoid Heat and Chemical Treatments

Heat and chemical treatments can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. Also, avoid chemical treatments like relaxers and dyes that can weaken your hair and cause breakage.

Step 4: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp helps to increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which promotes growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day.

Step 5: Protect Your Hair at Night

Protecting your hair at night can prevent breakage and promote growth. Use a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. This will prevent friction and moisture loss, which can cause damage to your hair.

Tips for Growing Freeform Dreads

Here are some additional tips to help you grow healthy and strong freeform dreads:

  • Be patient - freeform dreads take time to form and grow
  • Avoid using products with harsh chemicals
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage

FAQs About Freeform Dreads

Q: How long does it take to grow freeform dreads?

A: The time it takes to grow freeform dreads varies depending on your hair type and length. It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow freeform dreads.

Q: Can I wash my freeform dreads?

A: Yes, it’s important to wash your freeform dreads regularly to keep them clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid scrubbing your scalp too hard.

Q: Do I need to use products to grow freeform dreads?

A: No, you don’t need to use any products to grow freeform dreads. However, using natural oils or leave-in conditioners can help to keep your hair healthy and promote growth.

How to Take Care of Your Freeform Dreads

Once you’ve grown your freeform dreads, it’s important to take care of them properly to keep them healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of your freeform dreads:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a natural oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair at night with a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage

By following these tips and tricks, you can speed up the growth process of your freeform dreads and achieve long and healthy hair. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you’ll have beautiful freeform dreads in no time!
