What is a Triangle Shaped Face?

A triangle shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and wide jawline. The face narrows from the forehead to the jawline, creating a triangular shape. Women with triangle shaped faces usually have prominent cheekbones and a pointed chin. The key to finding the best haircuts for triangle shaped face female is to balance out the width of the jawline with the narrowness of the forehead.

How to Determine if You Have a Triangle Shaped Face

The easiest way to determine if you have a triangle shaped face is to measure your face. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your jawline is wider than your cheekbones and forehead, then you have a triangle shaped face. Another way to determine your face shape is to look in the mirror and outline your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Stand back and look at the outline. If it forms a triangle shape, then you have a triangle shaped face.

Best Haircuts for Triangle Shaped Face Female

The best haircuts for triangle shaped face female are those that add volume to the narrow forehead and balance out the wide jawline. Here are some of the best haircuts for triangle shaped face female:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for triangle shaped face female as they add volume to the narrow forehead and balance out the wide jawline. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at the cheekbone and go all the way down to the ends of your hair.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for a triangle shaped face female. A bob that ends at the jawline helps to balance out the width of the jawline. You can also add layers to the bob to create volume at the crown.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice for a triangle shaped face female, but it can look amazing if done right. A short pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides can balance out the wide jawline. You can also add volume at the crown to create a more balanced look.

4. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to add volume to the narrow forehead of a triangle shaped face female. They also help to balance out the wide jawline. Ask your stylist for long, side swept bangs that start at the crown and go all the way down to the cheekbone.

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut for Triangle Shaped Face Female

When choosing the best haircut for triangle shaped face female, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the narrow forehead.
  • Choose a haircut that balances out the wide jawline.
  • Avoid haircuts that accentuate the narrowness of the forehead.
  • Avoid haircuts that make the jawline look wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create volume at the crown.

FAQ

1. Can a triangle shaped face female have bangs?

Yes, a triangle shaped face female can have bangs. Side swept bangs are a great option for a triangle shaped face female as they add volume to the narrow forehead and balance out the wide jawline. Avoid blunt bangs as they can accentuate the narrowness of the forehead.

2. Can a triangle shaped face female have a short haircut?

Yes, a triangle shaped face female can have a short haircut. A short pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides can balance out the wide jawline. You can also add volume at the crown to create a more balanced look.

3. Can a triangle shaped face female have a bob haircut?

Yes, a bob haircut is a great option for a triangle shaped face female. A bob that ends at the jawline helps to balance out the width of the jawline. You can also add layers to the bob to create volume at the crown.

Hair Care Tips for Triangle Shaped Face Female

Here are some hair care tips for triangle shaped face female:

  • Avoid hairstyles that pull the hair back tightly as they can accentuate the narrowness of the forehead.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the narrow forehead.
  • Avoid heavy, flat hairstyles that can make the jawline look wider.
  • Consider using a round brush when blow drying your hair to create volume at the crown.

With these tips and haircuts, you can easily flatter your triangle shaped face and look amazing. So, go ahead and try out these haircuts to find the one that suits you the best!
