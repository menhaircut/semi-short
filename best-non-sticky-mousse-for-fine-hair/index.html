The Best Non Sticky Mousse For Fine Hair In 2023

Written by Frank Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using hair mousses that leave your hair feeling sticky and weighed down? If you have fine hair, finding the right hair mousse can be a challenge. You want a product that can add volume and hold without leaving your hair feeling heavy or sticky. In this article, we'll be discussing the best non-sticky mousse for fine hair in 2023.

Description

The best non-sticky mousse for fine hair is one that provides volume and hold without leaving any sticky residue. A good non-sticky mousse should also be lightweight and easy to apply. Here are some of the best non-sticky mousses for fine hair that you can try in 2023.

1. Moroccanoil Volumizing Mousse

The Moroccanoil Volumizing Mousse is a lightweight formula that adds volume and lift to fine hair without leaving any sticky residue. It contains argan oil and antioxidants that nourish and protect the hair, leaving it looking healthy and shiny. This mousse is also free from sulfates, parabens, and phthalates, making it a great option for those with sensitive scalps.

2. Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse

The Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse is a non-sticky formula that provides long-lasting volume and hold to fine hair. It contains a patented Thickening Molecule that creates a flexible, invisible layer on the hair, adding volume without any crunchiness or stickiness. This mousse is also safe for color-treated hair and is free from sulfates, parabens, and phthalates.

3. TRESemmé Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse

The TRESemmé Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse is a budget-friendly option that provides volume and hold to fine hair. Its lightweight formula contains heat-activated polymers that work with your hair dryer to provide long-lasting volume and hold. This mousse is also safe for color-treated hair and is free from sulfates and parabens.

How to Apply Non-Sticky Mousse to Fine Hair

Applying non-sticky mousse to fine hair is easy. Here's how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove any excess water.
  3. Shake the mousse can well and dispense a small amount into your hand.
  4. Spread the mousse evenly through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  5. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

Tips for Using Non-Sticky Mousse

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your non-sticky mousse:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to prepare your hair for the mousse.
  • Apply the mousse to damp hair for best results.
  • Start with a small amount of mousse and add more as needed.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Avoid applying mousse to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after applying the mousse.

FAQs

Q. Can I use non-sticky mousse on dry hair?

A. Yes, you can use non-sticky mousse on dry hair. However, it may not provide as much volume and hold as it would on damp hair.

Q. Can non-sticky mousse be used on color-treated hair?

A. Yes, most non-sticky mousses are safe for use on color-treated hair. However, it's always best to check the label first to make sure.

Q. How often should I use non-sticky mousse?

A. It's best to use non-sticky mousse every time you wash your hair. However, if you have oily hair, you may want to skip using mousse on the days that you don't wash your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, finding the right non-sticky mousse for fine hair can make a big difference in the way your hair looks and feels. By following the tips and recommendations in this article, you can achieve voluminous, healthy-looking hair that you'll love.
