Homemade Volumizer For Fine Hair Get Fuller Hair Now

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how difficult it can be to get the volume you want. No matter how many products you try or how much you tease your hair, it always seems to fall flat. Fortunately, there's a solution: homemade volumizer for fine hair! In this article, we'll show you how to make your own volumizer using ingredients you probably already have in your kitchen.

What is a Volumizer?

A volumizer is a product that's designed to give your hair more body and fullness. It can be a mousse, spray, or powder that you apply to your hair before styling. Volumizers work by coating your hair strands and lifting them away from your scalp, creating the illusion of thicker hair.

Why Use Homemade Volumizer?

Homemade volumizer is a great option for those who want to avoid chemicals and save money. Store-bought volumizers can be expensive and contain harsh ingredients that can damage your hair. Making your own volumizer is a natural, affordable way to get fuller hair without any negative side effects.

How to Make Homemade Volumizer for Fine Hair

Here's how to make your own volumizer using just a few simple ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Steps:

  1. Combine the cornstarch and water in a small bowl, mixing until smooth.
  2. Beat the egg white in a separate bowl until it becomes frothy.
  3. Add the cornstarch mixture to the egg white, stirring until well combined.
  4. Stir in the olive oil.
  5. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  6. Massage your scalp to distribute the mixture evenly.
  7. Style your hair as usual.

This homemade volumizer is easy to make and can be customized to suit your individual needs. If you have dry hair, you can add more olive oil to the mixture. If you prefer a stronger hold, you can add more cornstarch.

Tips for Using Homemade Volumizer

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your homemade volumizer:

  • Use a small amount of volumizer to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Apply the volumizer to damp hair for best results.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Experiment with different ingredients to find the perfect recipe for your hair.

FAQ

Is homemade volumizer safe for all hair types?

Yes, homemade volumizer is safe for all hair types. However, if you have sensitive skin or allergies, it's always a good idea to do a patch test before using any new product on your hair.

How often should I use homemade volumizer?

You can use homemade volumizer as often as you like. However, it's important to avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care

In addition to using homemade volumizer, there are a few other things you can do to keep your fine hair looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you get the most out of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for fine hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and your homemade volumizer, you'll be able to achieve the full, voluminous hair you've always wanted.
