How To Cut Pixie Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that's both stylish and practical, a pixie cut might be the perfect choice for you. But if you're hesitant to go to a salon or don't want to spend money on a professional cut, you can easily cut your pixie hair at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut pixie hair yourself in a few easy steps.

How to Cut Pixie Hair Yourself

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a great pixie cut at home. Follow these steps to cut your pixie hair yourself:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. It's important to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see the exact length and shape of your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all the hair in the back section.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair to your desired length, making sure to match the length with the back section. Repeat on the other side.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the back section as a guide and cut the hair to the same length. Repeat on all sections of the top.

Tips for Cutting Pixie Hair Yourself

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your pixie hair yourself:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated.
  • Don't hesitate to ask for help if you're having trouble.

FAQ About Cutting Pixie Hair Yourself

Do I Need to Cut My Hair When It's Wet or Dry?

You should always cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see the exact length and shape of your hair.

How Often Should I Cut My Pixie Hair?

How often you cut your pixie hair depends on how fast it grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Can I Cut My Pixie Hair Shorter?

Yes, you can cut your pixie hair shorter, but be careful not to cut too much at once. Start with small sections of hair and cut gradually until you've reached your desired length.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your pixie hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Now that you know how to cut pixie hair yourself, you can save money and achieve a great hairstyle at home. Just remember to be patient, use the right tools, and take your time to get the perfect cut.
