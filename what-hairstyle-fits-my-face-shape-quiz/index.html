What Hairstyle Fits My Face Shape Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to suit you? Are you looking for a more personalized approach to choosing the perfect hairstyle? Look no further than the "what hairstyle fits my face shape quiz!" This quiz is designed to help you determine your face shape and suggest hairstyles that will flatter your features. In this article, we will guide you through taking the quiz, give you some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle, and provide some hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

How to Take the Quiz

The "what hairstyle fits my face shape quiz" is easy to take and only takes a few minutes. To get started, make sure you have a camera or phone with a camera handy. Here are the steps:

  1. First, pull your hair back away from your face and take a well-lit, straight-on photo of your face.
  2. Upload the photo to the quiz website or app.
  3. Answer a few questions about your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.
  4. Get your personalized hairstyle recommendations!

It's that simple! Now let's take a closer look at how to choose the right hairstyle based on your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Once you've taken the quiz and received your personalized recommendations, it's time to choose the right hairstyle for you. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the four main face shapes and their characteristics:

  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the length and width of the face are roughly equal.
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, and the angle of the jaw is sharp.
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the cheeks and jawline, and the chin is pointed.

You can also have a combination of face shapes, so don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their opinion as well.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will help to control the volume. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will add volume and texture.

Step 3: Look at Your Personal Style

You should also consider your personal style when choosing a hairstyle. If you prefer a more natural, effortless look, you may want to choose a simple, low-maintenance hairstyle. If you prefer a bolder, edgier look, you may want to choose a more dramatic hairstyle.

Step 4: Try Before You Buy

Finally, before you commit to a new hairstyle, try it out first. You can use a wig or temporary hair extensions to get a better idea of how the hairstyle will look on you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that will accentuate your best features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that will draw attention to features you don't like.
  • Consider your hair's natural part when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that will complement your wardrobe and personal style.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their opinion.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective product.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and silky.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "what hairstyle fits my face shape quiz":

Q: Is the quiz accurate?

A: Yes! The quiz is based on scientific principles and has been tested on thousands of people.

Q: Can I take the quiz more than once?

A: Yes! You can take the quiz as many times as you like to get different recommendations.

Q: Can I share my results with my stylist?

A: Absolutely! Your stylist will appreciate the extra information and will be able to use it to give you the best possible haircut.

Conclusion

The "what hairstyle fits my face shape quiz" is a great way to get personalized recommendations for your next haircut. By following our step-by-step guide and using some of our tips and hair care advice, you'll be sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and personal style. So what are you waiting for? Take the quiz and get ready for a new, fresh look!
