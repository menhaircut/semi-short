Description

Mens hairstyle with long hair on top has become a popular trend in recent years. This hairstyle is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. It provides a versatile look that can be styled in many ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured look.

How To

Before getting a mens hairstyle with long hair on top, it's important to consider your hair type and face shape. Long hair on top can be challenging to manage, especially for those with thick or curly hair. It's also important to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Grow out the hair on top of your head.

Step 3: Trim the sides and back to a shorter length.

Step 4: Style your hair using products such as pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips

To maintain a mens hairstyle with long hair on top, it's important to keep it clean and well-groomed. Regular shampooing and conditioning can help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage. Using a leave-in conditioner can also help keep your hair hydrated and smooth. Additionally, getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Q: How long does my hair need to be on top to get a mens hairstyle with long hair on top?

A: Your hair should be at least 3-4 inches long on top to achieve the desired look.

Q: What face shapes suit a mens hairstyle with long hair on top?

A: This hairstyle suits oval and square face shapes.

Hair Care

Maintaining a mens hairstyle with long hair on top requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

Wash your hair regularly

Regular shampooing and conditioning can help keep your hair clean and healthy.

Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and smooth.

Get regular trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Avoid excessive heat styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause breakage. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can promote healthy hair growth. Eat foods rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
