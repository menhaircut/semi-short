How Do I Find Out What Hairstyle Suits Me

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to switch things up? Or are you looking for a new hairstyle that suits your face shape and personality? Finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to find out what hairstyle suits you best.

How to Find Out What Hairstyle Suits You

There are several factors to consider when finding a hairstyle that suits you. These include your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Here's how to find out what hairstyle suits you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that enhances your features and balances your proportions. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in finding a hairstyle that suits you. If you have naturally curly hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that embraces your curls. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer hairstyle that showcases your hair's thickness.

Step 3: Assess Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in finding a hairstyle that suits you. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling time. If you're more adventurous, you may want to consider a bolder hairstyle that makes a statement.

Step 4: Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style also plays a role in finding a hairstyle that suits you. If you have a classic style, you may want to consider a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. If you have a bohemian style, you may want to opt for a more relaxed hairstyle that reflects your carefree spirit.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know how to find a hairstyle that suits you, here are some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

Tip 1: Bring Pictures to Your Stylist

If you have a specific hairstyle in mind, bring pictures to your stylist. This will help them understand exactly what you're looking for and make any necessary adjustments based on your face shape and hair texture.

Tip 2: Try on Wigs

If you're unsure about a particular hairstyle, try on wigs to see how it looks on you. This will give you a better idea of how the hairstyle will look with your face shape and hair texture.

Tip 3: Consider Hair Accessories

If you want to switch up your hairstyle without making a drastic change, consider hair accessories. A simple headband or hair clip can transform your look and add a touch of personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about finding a hairstyle that suits you:

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

A: You can use online virtual hairstyle tools or try on wigs to see how a hairstyle will look on you. You can also consult with your hairstylist, who can suggest hairstyles based on your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I pull off short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can pull off short hair if you have a round face. However, you may want to consider a shorter hairstyle that adds height and lengthens your face.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can change your hairstyle if you have curly hair. However, you may want to consider a hairstyle that embraces your curls and minimizes frizz.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

Tip 1: Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant to minimize damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Invest in Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your hair healthy and strong. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Your hairstylist can recommend a schedule based on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

Finding a hairstyle that suits you can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style, you can find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your features and reflects your personality. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality, and don't be afraid to switch things up and try something new!
