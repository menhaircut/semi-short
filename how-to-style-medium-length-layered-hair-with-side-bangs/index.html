How To Style Medium Length Layered Hair With Side Bangs

If you have medium length layered hair with side bangs, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that is both stylish and practical. Fortunately, there are a variety of ways to style this type of hair that can work for both casual and formal occasions. Description

Medium length layered hair with side bangs is a popular hairstyle that can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. The layers in this style can add volume and movement to your hair, while the side bangs can frame your face and give you a more youthful appearance. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn in many different ways, depending on your personal preference and the occasion.

How to

Here are some steps you can follow to style your medium length layered hair with side bangs:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and will help your style last longer. You can use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type to achieve the best results.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

To help your hair hold its shape, apply a styling product such as mousse or gel. This will also help to protect your hair from heat damage if you plan to use hot tools such as a curling iron or straightener.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up to the top. This will help to give your hair volume and shape. Use the brush to create a slight curl at the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

Step 4: Style your bangs

Once your hair is dry, style your side bangs. You can sweep them to the side or pin them back if you prefer. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add some texture.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To help your style last throughout the day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place without making it feel stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length layered hair with side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length layered hair with side bangs:

Q: Can I wear my hair up with side bangs?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up with side bangs. You can pin your bangs back or leave them loose to frame your face.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.

Q: How can I add more volume to my hair?

A: To add more volume to your hair, try blow drying it upside down or using a volumizing spray or mousse before styling.

  • Use a conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
