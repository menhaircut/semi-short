How To Change Oval Face Shape

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of having an oval face shape and want to try something new, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll discuss how to change oval face shape, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a gently rounded hairline and jawline, with the widest point being at the cheekbones. While some people may love their oval face shape, others may want to change it for a more angular or square appearance.

How to Change Oval Face Shape

If you want to change your oval face shape, there are a few things you can do:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using light and dark shades to create the illusion of different facial shapes. To make an oval face shape appear more angular or square, use a darker shade of foundation or contour powder along the jawline, temples, and forehead. Then, use a lighter shade to highlight the cheekbones and center of the forehead.

2. Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in changing the appearance of your face shape. To make an oval face shape appear more angular or square, try a hairstyle with layers or choppy ends that create volume at the sides. A side-swept fringe can also help to balance out the face shape.

3. Facial Hair

If you're a man with an oval face shape, facial hair can help to create a more angular or square appearance. A beard or goatee can help to add definition to the jawline, while a mustache can help to balance out the proportions of the face.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you're ready to change your oval face shape, follow these step-by-step instructions:

1. Determine your face shape

Before you can change your face shape, you need to know what you're working with. Use a measuring tape to measure the widest point of your face, from cheekbone to cheekbone, and the length of your face from hairline to chin. If the measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape.

2. Contour your face

Use a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to create shadows along the jawline, temples, and forehead. Use a lighter shade to highlight the cheekbones and center of the forehead.

3. Choose a hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle with layers or choppy ends that create volume at the sides. A side-swept fringe can also help to balance out the face shape.

4. Consider facial hair

If you're a man, consider growing a beard or mustache to add definition to the jawline or balance out the proportions of the face.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you change your oval face shape:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and makeup techniques until you find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Changing your face shape can be a fun way to switch up your look.
  • Consult with a hairstylist or makeup artist if you need help finding the right style or technique.
  • Be patient. It may take some trial and error to find the right look for you.

FAQ

Can I permanently change my face shape?

No, you cannot permanently change your face shape without surgery. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and other techniques to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Will changing my face shape make me more attractive?

Beauty is subjective, and what one person finds attractive may not be the same for someone else. However, changing your face shape can help you feel more confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Whether you're trying to change your face shape or just looking for the right hairstyle for your current shape, here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, try a hairstyle with layers or choppy ends that create volume at the top of the head. A side-swept fringe can also help to elongate the face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, try a hairstyle with soft layers that frame the face. A side-swept fringe or gentle waves can help to soften the angles of the face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, try a hairstyle with layers that frame the face and add volume at the chin. A side-swept fringe can help to balance out the proportions of the face.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, try a hairstyle with layers or choppy ends that create volume at the sides. A side-swept fringe can also help to balance out the face shape.

Changing your face shape can be a fun way to switch up your look and feel more confident in your own skin. Whether you're using makeup, hairstyles, or other techniques, experiment with different styles and be patient as you find what works best for you.
