How To Know What Hair Colours Suit You

Written by Frank Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your usual hair colour and want to try something new? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which colours will suit you best. However, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair colour for you. Read on to learn how to know what hair colours suit you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining which hair colours will suit you is to identify your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a significant role in which hair colours will look best on you. There are two main skin tones - cool and warm. Cool skin tones have blue or pink undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones.

Once you've identified your skin tone, you can start selecting hair colours that will complement it. For cool skin tones, choose hair colours with cool undertones like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or deep burgundy. For warm skin tones, go for hair colours with warm undertones like honey blonde, chestnut brown, or auburn.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow to determine your skin tone:

  1. Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.
  2. Check the colour of your eyes. If you have blue, grey, or green eyes, you have a cool skin tone. If you have brown, hazel, or amber eyes, you have a warm skin tone.
  3. Examine your natural hair colour. If your hair is black, brown, or blonde with ashy undertones, you have a cool skin tone. If your hair is red, golden blonde, or brown with golden undertones, you have a warm skin tone.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a hair colour that requires minimal upkeep. If you have more time to spare, try experimenting with more complex hair colours.
  • Be realistic about what will suit you. While it's fun to experiment with bold hair colours, not every colour will look good on everyone.
  • Consider the season. Darker hair colours like brown and black are better suited for colder months, while lighter colours like blonde and platinum are perfect for summer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone and the colour you choose. If you have a warm skin tone, a drastic change to a cool hair colour like platinum blonde may not suit you. Similarly, if you have a cool skin tone, a drastic change to a warm hair colour like auburn may not work.

Q: How do I maintain my hair colour?

A: Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the colour to fade faster. Consider using a hair mask or treatment to help keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair after colouring is just as important as choosing the right colour. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for coloured hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair colour for you. Remember to consider your skin tone, lifestyle, and season when selecting a colour, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
