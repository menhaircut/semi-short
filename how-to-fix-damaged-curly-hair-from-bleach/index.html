How To Fix Damaged Curly Hair From Bleach

Written by Bardi Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and have gone through a bleaching process, you may have noticed that your hair has become dry, brittle, and prone to breakage. Damaged curly hair from bleach can be a nightmare to manage, but there are ways to bring it back to life. In this post, we will discuss how you can fix damaged curly hair from bleach.

Description

Bleaching is a chemical process that can lighten your hair color. However, it can also damage your hair, especially if you have curly hair. Curly hair is naturally dry and prone to breakage, and bleaching can make it worse. The bleach can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry, brittle, and prone to breakage.

How to Fix Damaged Curly Hair from Bleach

If you have damaged curly hair from bleach, there are a few things you can do to bring it back to life.

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

The first step in fixing damaged curly hair from bleach is to trim your hair. This will get rid of the damaged ends and make your hair look healthier.

Step 2: Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair is crucial to restoring its moisture and preventing breakage. Use a deep conditioner that contains natural oils such as coconut oil, argan oil, or avocado oil. Apply the conditioner to your hair, cover it with a plastic cap, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes. Rinse it off with cool water.

Step 3: Use a Protein Treatment

Protein treatments can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage. Look for a protein treatment that contains hydrolyzed keratin, which can penetrate the hair shaft and strengthen it from within.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can further damage your hair, so it's best to avoid it for a while. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you fix damaged curly hair from bleach:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.
  • Wear protective styles such as braids or twists to give your hair a break from manipulation.

FAQ

How long does it take to fix damaged curly hair from bleach?

It can take several months to fix damaged curly hair from bleach. However, with proper care and treatment, you should start to see an improvement in your hair's health within a few weeks.

Can damaged curly hair from bleach be fixed?

Yes, damaged curly hair from bleach can be fixed. However, it will take time, patience, and proper care.

Is it safe to bleach curly hair?

Bleaching curly hair can be risky as it can cause damage and breakage. It's best to consult a professional stylist before bleaching your curly hair.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and prevent damage, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to detangle your hair and protect it from breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Wear protective styles to give your hair a break from manipulation.

In conclusion, damaged curly hair from bleach can be fixed with proper care and treatment. Trim your hair, deep condition it, use a protein treatment, and avoid heat styling. Follow these tips, and you'll have healthy, beautiful curly hair in no time.
