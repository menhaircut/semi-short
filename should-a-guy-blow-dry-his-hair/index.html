Should A Guy Blow Dry His Hair

Written by Petter Nov 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our blog post about the age-old question: should a guy blow dry his hair? Many men wonder if they should take the time to blow dry their hair, or if it's better to let it air dry. In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of blow drying, as well as provide tips for how to do it properly.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Blow Dry Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Blow Drying
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Blow Dried Hair

Description

Blow drying your hair can have many benefits. For one, it can help you achieve a more polished and professional look. Blow drying can also add volume and body to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker. However, blow drying can also damage your hair if not done properly. Overheating your hair can cause split ends and breakage, which can lead to hair loss over time.

How to Blow Dry Your Hair

Before you start blow drying your hair, make sure it's clean and towel-dried. Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage. Then, follow these steps:

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into 2-3 inch sections using hair clips or elastics. This will make it easier to blow dry each section and ensure that every part of your head is evenly dried.

Step 2: Start blow drying from the roots

Hold your blow dryer about 6 inches away from your hair and start blow drying from the roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair up and away from your scalp, directing the blow dryer towards your roots. This will add volume and body to your hair.

Step 3: Work your way down the length of your hair

Once you've dried the roots of each section, work your way down the length of your hair. Use the round brush to smooth out any tangles or knots, and direct the blow dryer down towards the ends of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to blow drying your hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair, then towel dry it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into 2-3 inch sections using hair clips or elastics.
  4. Start blow drying from the roots, holding your blow dryer about 6 inches away from your hair.
  5. Use a round brush to lift your hair up and away from your scalp, directing the blow dryer towards your roots.
  6. Once you've dried the roots of each section, work your way down the length of your hair.
  7. Use the round brush to smooth out any tangles or knots, and direct the blow dryer down towards the ends of your hair.
  8. When you're finished blow drying, apply a finishing product such as hairspray or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Blow Drying

Here are some tips to keep in mind when blow drying your hair:

  • Use a low heat setting to prevent overheating your hair.
  • Don't hold the blow dryer too close to your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and body to your hair.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage.
  • Blow dry your hair in a well-ventilated area to prevent overheating.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying:

Is it bad to blow dry your hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage over time. It's best to let your hair air dry whenever possible, and limit blow drying to once or twice a week.

What's the best type of blow dryer to use?

Look for a blow dryer with multiple heat and speed settings, as well as a cool shot button. A ceramic or tourmaline blow dryer can also help reduce heat damage to your hair.

How long should I blow dry my hair?

It's best to blow dry your hair in 10-15 minute intervals, taking breaks in between to prevent overheating. The total amount of time will depend on your hair length and thickness.

Hair Care for Blow Dried Hair

Blow drying your hair can cause damage over time, so it's important to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and split ends. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can cause further damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, blow drying your hair can be a great way to achieve a polished and professional look. However, it's important to take precautions to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Follow our tips and tricks for blow drying your hair, and be sure to take care of your hair with proper hair care techniques.
