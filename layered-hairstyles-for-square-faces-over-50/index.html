Description

As we age, our hairstyle choices may change to better suit our features and lifestyle. For women over 50 with square faces, a layered hairstyle can accentuate their facial structure and add volume and movement to their hair. Layers can also help soften harsh lines and angles, making the face appear more youthful and feminine.

Layered hairstyles for square faces over 50 can come in different lengths and styles, from short pixie cuts to longer wavy layers. The key is to find a style that flatters your face shape and hair type, and that you feel confident and comfortable wearing.

How to Achieve Layered Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Before committing to a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair texture, density, and face shape. Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead, with a width that is roughly equal to the length of the face. Layered hairstyles can help soften these features and add dimension to the hair.

Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with mature women and who can recommend a few options for layered hairstyles. Look for inspiration in magazines or online, and bring pictures of styles you like to your appointment. Consider factors such as maintenance, styling time, and color options when selecting a hairstyle.

Best Layered Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Some popular layered hairstyles for square faces over 50 include:

  • Short pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob with face-framing layers
  • Long layered haircut with subtle waves
  • Shoulder-length layered cut with blunt ends
  • Layered shag haircut with choppy layers

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a general guide to achieving a layered hairstyle for square faces over 50:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into 2-3 parts, depending on the thickness of your hair.
  3. Using a sharp pair of scissors, cut your hair in a straight line at the desired length. This will be your baseline for the layers.
  4. Starting at the crown of your head, take a section of hair and comb it straight up. Using your scissors, cut the hair at an angle to create a diagonal section of hair.
  5. Repeat this process, working your way around the head and cutting the hair at different angles to create layers of varying lengths.
  6. Once you have finished cutting the layers, use a razor or thinning shears to soften any harsh lines or edges.
  7. Style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten the hair.
  8. Apply a styling product such as mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

To keep your layered hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, try these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Avoid over-styling or using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a square face over 50?

A layered haircut can be a flattering option for women over 50 with square faces. Look for styles that add volume and movement to the hair, and that soften harsh lines and angles.

How often should I get my hair cut if I have a layered hairstyle?

It's generally recommended to get a trim or touch-up every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your layered haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

In addition to regular trims and touch-ups, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry out the scalp and hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a well-balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right haircut and proper care, women over 50 with square faces can look and feel confident and beautiful. Experiment with different layered hairstyles and find the one that best suits your personality and lifestyle.
