How To Cut Your Hair Pixie Style At Home

Written by Daniel Sep 29, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you ready for a change? A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that can make you feel confident and stylish. But going to a salon can be expensive, and doing it at home may seem daunting. Don't worry, with these step-by-step instructions, you can cut your hair pixie style at home with ease. Here's what you need to know.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for a Successful Pixie Cut

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start, here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers or trimmers
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to protect your clothes

Now, let's get to the cutting:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use sectioning clips to keep each part separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Comb it straight down and cut it to your desired length. If you want a shorter pixie cut, use clippers or trimmers to create a tapered effect at the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Moving on to the sides, comb the hair straight down and cut it to the same length as the back. Use your fingers to create a guide for the length if you prefer a more textured look.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Now, it's time to tackle the top section. Use your comb to lift the hair straight up and cut it to your desired length. You can also use your fingers to create texture and volume.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Once you've cut all sections, use a mirror to check for balance. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even on both sides.

Tips for a Successful Pixie Cut

Cutting your hair pixie style at home can be intimidating, but these tips will help you achieve the look you want:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length.
  • Don't cut too much at once. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check for balance and make sure your hair is even on both sides.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring and you don't want to rush the process.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair pixie style if it's curly?

Yes! Pixie cuts look great on curly hair. Just make sure to cut it when it's dry so you can see the natural shape of your curls.

What if I don't like how it turns out?

Hair grows back! If you don't like your pixie cut, there are plenty of styles you can try while it grows out.

Do I need to use special scissors?

No, any sharp scissors will do. Just make sure they're clean and sanitized before you start cutting.

Hair Care after the Cut

Now that you have your new pixie cut, it's important to take care of it:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in styling products like gel or mousse to help create your desired look.
  • Visit a salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your pixie cut.

Cutting your hair pixie style at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you'll be able to achieve the look you want without breaking the bank.
