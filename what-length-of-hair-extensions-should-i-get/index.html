What Length Of Hair Extensions Should I Get

Written by Oliver Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about what length of hair extensions you should get. Hair extensions have been a popular choice for women who want to add length and volume to their hair without waiting for their natural hair to grow. But with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what length of hair extensions is right for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect length of hair extensions for your hair.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Hair extensions come in a variety of lengths, ranging from 12 inches to 30 inches. The most popular lengths are usually between 16 inches to 22 inches. Choosing the right length of hair extensions will depend on your desired look and your natural hair length.

How to

To determine what length of hair extensions you should get, follow these steps:

  1. Measure your natural hair length.
  2. Decide on the length you want to add to your hair.
  3. Choose the length of hair extensions that will achieve your desired look.

Step by step

Step 1: Measure your natural hair length. To do this, measure from the root of your hair to the tips. This will give you an idea of how much length you need to add to your hair.

Step 2: Decide on the length you want to add to your hair. Do you want to add just a few inches, or do you want to go for a dramatic change? Keep in mind that the longer the extensions, the heavier they will be on your head.

Step 3: Choose the length of hair extensions that will achieve your desired look. If you want to add just a few inches, 16-18 inch extensions may be enough. If you want a more dramatic change, you may want to consider 20-24 inch extensions. Keep in mind that longer extensions will require more maintenance and care.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the length of your hair extensions:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Longer extensions may not be practical if you lead an active lifestyle or work in a profession that requires a lot of physical activity.
  • Choose a length that complements your face shape. Longer extensions can elongate your face, while shorter extensions can make your face appear wider.
  • Take into account the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, longer extensions may be too heavy and may cause damage to your natural hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair extensions to a shorter length?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair extensions to a shorter length if you find that they are too long. However, keep in mind that once you cut them, you cannot add the length back.

Q: Can I style my hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can style your hair extensions just like you would your natural hair. However, be sure to use heat protectant products to prevent damage to the extensions.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your hair extensions looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair extensions.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your extensions, or use a heat protectant product.
  • Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair extensions.

Now that you know how to choose the perfect length of hair extensions, you can confidently shop for the right extensions to give you the look you desire. Remember to take into account your natural hair length, your lifestyle, and your personal style when making your decision. Happy shopping!
