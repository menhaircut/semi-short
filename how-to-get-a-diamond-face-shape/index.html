How To Get A Diamond Face Shape

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you dream of having a diamond-shaped face? This facial shape is highly sought after because it is associated with beauty and elegance. If you have a round or square face shape, don't worry! With a few simple tips and tricks, you can achieve a diamond face shape in no time. In this article, we will discuss how to get a diamond face shape, step by step.

Description

A diamond face shape is characterized by wide cheekbones, a narrow forehead, and a pointed chin. This facial shape is often seen as the ideal because it is well-balanced and symmetrical. However, not everyone is born with this facial shape. If you have a round or square face shape, you can still achieve a diamond face shape with the right techniques.

How to

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in getting a diamond face shape is to determine your current face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and examine the shape of your face. If you have a round face shape, your cheekbones and jawline will be the same width. If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will be the same width. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start using techniques to achieve a diamond face shape.

Step 2: Contour Your Cheekbones

One of the key features of a diamond face shape is wide cheekbones. To achieve this look, you can use contouring techniques to create the illusion of higher cheekbones. Use a contour powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it along the hollows of your cheeks. Blend it outwards towards your temples to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 3: Highlight Your Forehead

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead, so you can use highlighting techniques to draw attention to this area. Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead and blend it outwards towards your hairline. This will create the illusion of a wider forehead and a more balanced facial shape.

Step 4: Define Your Jawline

A pointed chin is another key feature of a diamond face shape. To define your jawline, use a contour powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it along your jawline. Blend it downwards towards your neck to create a natural-looking shadow. This will make your chin appear more pointed and create the illusion of a diamond face shape.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

The right hairstyle can also help you achieve a diamond face shape. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and tapers towards the ends. This will create the illusion of a narrower forehead and a more pointed chin, which are key features of a diamond face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a diamond face shape:

  • Use a matte bronzer to contour your cheekbones for a more natural-looking shadow.
  • Use a light hand when applying contouring products to avoid a heavy or unnatural look.
  • Choose a highlighter that complements your skin tone for a natural-looking glow.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that best suits your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve a diamond face shape without makeup?

A: Yes, you can achieve a diamond face shape without makeup by focusing on your hairstyle and choosing one that complements your face shape. You can also use facial exercises to tone and define your facial muscles.

Q: How long does it take to achieve a diamond face shape?

A: The time it takes to achieve a diamond face shape depends on your current face shape and the techniques you use. It may take some time to perfect your contouring and highlighting techniques, but with practice, you can achieve a diamond face shape in no time.

Hair Care

In addition to using makeup techniques to achieve a diamond face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and use products that nourish and protect your hair. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

Getting a diamond face shape is easier than you think. With the right techniques and a little practice, you can achieve a well-balanced and symmetrical facial shape that is associated with beauty and elegance. Remember to focus on contouring your cheekbones, highlighting your forehead, defining your jawline, and choosing the right hairstyle to complement your face shape.
