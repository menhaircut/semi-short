How To Do Self Hair Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon for a simple haircut? Well, why not try doing it yourself? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of doing a self-haircut. From the preparation to the finishing touches, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started.

How to Prepare for a Self-Haircut

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to prepare yourself and your tools. Here are some things you need to do:

1. Choose the Right Tools

You will need a good pair of scissors, hair clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a spray bottle filled with water. Make sure your scissors are sharp and your clippers have different length guards.

2. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely. Wet hair is harder to cut, and it can also lead to uneven cuts.

3. Choose Your Style

Decide on the style and length you want. Look up pictures online or in magazines for inspiration. Also, make sure the style you choose is achievable with the tools you have.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Do Self-Haircut

Now that you have everything you need and have prepared yourself, it’s time to start cutting your hair. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into different parts. Start at the back and work your way to the front. Clip each section you’re not cutting to keep it out of the way.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

Using your clippers, start trimming the sides and back of your hair. Use a longer guard at first, and then switch to a shorter one to fade the sides. Blend the sides and back to avoid harsh lines.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure you cut it evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Check for Unevenness

After cutting your hair, check for any unevenness. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs or sections that are longer than others.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

After trimming and cutting your hair, it’s time to style it. Use your comb and a styling product to achieve the look you want. If you have any bald spots or uneven areas, use a concealer to cover them up.

Tips for a Successful Self-Haircut

To ensure your self-haircut is a success, follow these tips:

1. Start with a Longer Guard

Start with a longer guard and work your way to a shorter one. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.

2. Cut Small Sections at a Time

Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much hair at once. This will also help you achieve a more even cut.

3. Use a Mirror

Use a mirror to check the back of your head. Place a mirror in front of you and another behind you to get a better view.

4. Be Patient

Be patient and take your time. Rushing the process can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.

FAQs About Self-Haircut

1. Can I do a self-haircut with curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry to avoid uneven cuts. Use a diffuser to dry your hair after washing it.

2. Can I cut my own bangs?

Yes, you can. Use your scissors to cut your bangs to the desired length. Start with small sections and cut vertically to avoid a blunt cut.

3. What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always fix it by trimming the area or covering it up with a styling product.

How to Care for Your Hair After a Self-Haircut

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.

2. Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturize your hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain your haircut, trim your hair regularly. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.

And that’s it! Follow these steps, tips, and tricks, and you’ll be able to achieve a professional-looking self-haircut at home. Happy cutting!
