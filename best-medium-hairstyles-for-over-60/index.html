Best Medium Hairstyles For Over 60

Written by Luffy Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture, thickness, and color. Finding a hairstyle that not only flatters our features but also suits our lifestyle can be challenging. However, there are plenty of stylish and manageable medium hairstyles for women over 60. In this article, we will discuss the best medium hairstyles for over 60, including how to style and care for them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium hairstyles are versatile and practical for women over 60. A medium haircut falls between the chin and shoulders, making it easy to manage and style. It's also a great length for those who want to keep some length but still have a manageable look. The best medium hairstyles for over 60 are those that flatter your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Below are some of the best medium hairstyles for women over 60.

How to Style

The best medium hairstyles for over 60 are those that are easy to style and maintain. Here are some tips for styling your medium-length hair:

  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and body.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create loose waves.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to add texture and hold.
  • Use a hair spray to set your style and keep it in place throughout the day.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style some of the best medium hairstyles for over 60:

Layered Bob

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and body.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a smooth finish.
  3. Part your hair to the side and create layers around your face for a flattering look.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to add texture and hold.
  5. Use a hair spray to set your style and keep it in place throughout the day.

Shoulder-Length Waves

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and body.
  2. Use a flat iron to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  3. Part your hair down the middle and create layers around your face for a flattering look.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to add texture and hold.
  5. Use a hair spray to set your style and keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best medium hairstyles for over 60:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that is easy to style and maintain.
  • Consult with a stylist to get advice on the best hairstyle for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium hairstyles for over 60:

What is the best length for medium hairstyles for over 60?

The best length for medium hairstyles for over 60 falls between the chin and shoulders.

What face shape is best for a layered bob?

A layered bob flatters most face shapes, but it looks especially good on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

What hair texture is best for shoulder-length waves?

Shoulder-length waves look best on those with medium to thick hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your medium-length hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the best medium hairstyles for over 60 is all about finding a style that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. With the right styling and hair care, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful at any age!
