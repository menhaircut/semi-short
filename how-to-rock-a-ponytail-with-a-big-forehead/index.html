How To Rock A Ponytail With A Big Forehead Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a big forehead, you may feel self-conscious about wearing your hair up in a ponytail. But fear not! With a few tips and tricks, you can rock a ponytail like a pro and feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to rock a ponytail with a big forehead, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Rock a Ponytail with a Big Forehead
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Rock a Ponytail with a Big Forehead

First things first: it’s important to choose the right ponytail style for your face shape. If you have a big forehead, you’ll want to avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this will only accentuate your forehead. Instead, opt for a loose, messy ponytail that sits lower on your head. This will help to balance out your features and create a more flattering look.

Another important factor to consider is the placement of your ponytail. If you have a big forehead, you’ll want to avoid pulling your ponytail back too far on your head. Instead, try placing your ponytail slightly to the side, or even lower down on your head. This will help to create a more balanced look and draw attention away from your forehead.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know what to keep in mind when styling your ponytail, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you create the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may want to apply a smoothing serum or cream to help tame frizz.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair back away from your face. If you have a side part, you may want to comb your hair over to one side.
  3. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure to leave a few strands of hair loose around your face to create a soft, feminine look.
  4. If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, gently tease the hair at the crown of your head with a fine-tooth comb. This will help to create a more voluminous, glamorous look.
  5. Finish by spritzing your hair with a light hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you rock a ponytail with a big forehead:

  • Experiment with different ponytail styles to find the one that works best for you. Try a high ponytail, a low ponytail, or even a side ponytail.
  • Consider adding some accessories to your ponytail, such as a headband, scarf, or barrette. This can help to add some interest and texture to your look.
  • If you have long hair, consider wearing your hair in a braid or a bun instead of a ponytail. This can help to create a more sophisticated, elegant look.
  • Don’t be afraid to play around with different hair textures, such as curls or waves. This can help to create a more playful, youthful look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to rock a ponytail with a big forehead:

  • Can I wear a high ponytail if I have a big forehead?
    While a high ponytail can be a great look, it may not be the most flattering style for someone with a big forehead. Instead, try a lower ponytail or a side ponytail.
  • What kind of hair tie should I use?
    Opt for a soft, fabric hair tie instead of a tight elastic band. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • How can I prevent my ponytail from slipping?
    Try using two hair ties instead of one. First, gather your hair in a loose ponytail with one hair tie. Then, add a second hair tie a few inches down from the first one to help hold your hair in place.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair soft and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a ponytail with a big forehead like a pro. So go ahead and try out some new styles – you might be surprised at how beautiful you look!
