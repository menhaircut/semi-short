Good Short Haircuts For Thick Hair A Guide To The Best Styles

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts are a great option for those with thick hair as they can help to reduce the bulk and create a more manageable style. However, finding the right haircut for your hair type can be challenging. This guide will provide you with some of the best short haircuts for thick hair, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thick Hair

When choosing a short haircut for thick hair, it's important to consider your face shape and personal style. Some haircuts work better for certain face shapes, while others may not be as flattering. It's also important to think about how much time and effort you want to put into styling your hair each day.

Consulting with a professional hairstylist is a great way to get personalized recommendations for your hair type and face shape. They can also provide tips on how to care for and style your hair to keep it looking its best.

Top Short Haircuts for Thick Hair

Here are some of the best short haircuts for those with thick hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for those with thick hair. It's a low-maintenance style that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. The hair is cut short all around, with longer pieces left on top to create a textured look.

Bob

The bob is another great option for those with thick hair. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to wavy and tousled. The hair is cut to just above the shoulders, with layers added to reduce the bulk.

Short Shag

The short shag is a layered haircut that works well for those with thick hair. It's a textured style that can be tousled for a casual look or styled sleek and smooth for a more polished appearance. The hair is cut in layers of varying lengths to create movement and reduce the bulk.

Tips for Styling Short Thick Hair

Styling short thick hair can be a challenge, but there are some tips you can follow to make it easier:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to add volume
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition to your hair
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick hair:

Can I still wear a ponytail with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with a short haircut. However, you may need to use hair extensions or a clip-in ponytail to achieve the length you want.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best. This will help to remove split ends and keep your hair healthy.

What can I do if my hair is too thick?

If your hair is too thick, you can ask your hairstylist to thin it out using a razor or thinning shears. This will help to reduce the bulk and make your hair more manageable.

Hair Care for Short Thick Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your short thick hair looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut for your hair type and face shape, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look that you'll love.
