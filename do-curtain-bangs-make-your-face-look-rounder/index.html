Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Rounder Tips How To And Hair Care

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but worried it might make your face look rounder? You're not alone. Many women are hesitant to try this trendy hairstyle because they fear it won't flatter their face shape. But the truth is, curtain bangs can look great on anyone, regardless of their face shape, as long as they're done right. In this blog post, we'll explore everything you need to know about curtain bangs, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs, also known as Bardot bangs, are a type of fringe that's parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They're called curtain bangs because they resemble the drapery in a window.

Curtain bangs have become increasingly popular in recent years, with celebrities like Alexa Chung, Dakota Johnson, and Taylor Swift sporting this look.

Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Rounder?

The short answer is no, curtain bangs do not make your face look rounder. In fact, they can actually make your face appear slimmer and more angular.

The reason for this is that curtain bangs create a vertical line down the center of your face, which helps to elongate it. Additionally, the soft, wispy texture of curtain bangs helps to soften harsh angles and draw attention to your eyes.

Of course, as with any hairstyle, the way you wear and style your curtain bangs can affect how they flatter your face shape. We'll explore this in more detail in the next section.

How to Style Curtain Bangs?

Styling curtain bangs is relatively easy, and there are many different ways to wear them. Here are some of our favorite styles:

1. Middle Part

The classic curtain bangs style is parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. To achieve this look, simply blow-dry your bangs straight down, using a round brush to add volume at the roots. Then, use a flat iron to create a slight bend at the ends for a soft, natural look.

2. Side Part

If you prefer a more asymmetrical look, you can also try parting your curtain bangs to one side. This style is great for adding volume to one side of your face and drawing attention to your eyes.

3. Pin Back

If you're having a bad hair day or want to switch up your look, you can also pin back your curtain bangs. Simply twist the sides of your bangs back and secure them with bobby pins for an effortless, chic look.

Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your curtain bangs:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

While curtain bangs can look great on any face shape, certain styles may be more flattering than others. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for longer, more tapered bangs to create a slimming effect.

2. Use the Right Products

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots to add lift and texture. You can also use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.

3. Trim Regularly

To maintain your curtain bangs, it's important to trim them regularly. This will help prevent split ends and keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

FAQ about Curtain Bangs

1. How long should curtain bangs be?

Curtain bangs can be any length, but they typically fall between the eyebrows and cheekbones. If you have a round face, longer bangs may be more flattering.

2. Can I wear curtain bangs with curly hair?

Yes! Curtain bangs look great on curly hair, and can help to frame your face and define your curls. Just be sure to keep your curls moisturized and use a diffuser when blow-drying your bangs.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

To prevent oil buildup and keep your bangs looking fresh, wash your hair regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair, so try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling wands. If you must use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

3. Moisturize Regularly

To keep your hair and bangs healthy and hydrated, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture.

Overall, curtain bangs are a versatile and flattering hairstyle that can look great on anyone. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock this trendy look with confidence!
