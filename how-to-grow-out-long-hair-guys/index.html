How To Grow Out Long Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair and want to grow it out to achieve a stylish and long hairstyle? Growing out long hair can be a challenging process, especially for guys who are not used to having long hair. But with the right hair care routine and some patience, you can achieve the long locks of your dreams. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to grow out long hair guys and answer some of the most frequently asked questions related to hair growth.

How to Grow Out Long Hair Guys

Here are the steps that you need to follow to grow out your hair:

Step 1: Set Realistic Goals

Before you start growing out your hair, you need to set realistic goals. Know what kind of hairstyle you want, how long you want your hair to be, and how much time it will take to achieve your desired length. Keep in mind that hair grows about half an inch per month, so don't expect to have long hair overnight.

Step 2: Be Patient

Growing out long hair requires patience, so don't give up if you don't see results immediately. It may take several months or even a year to achieve your desired length, so be prepared to wait.

Step 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims may sound counterintuitive when you're trying to grow out your hair, but it's essential to maintain the health of your hair. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks will prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down your hair growth.

Step 4: Use the Right Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for long hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Apply a leave-in conditioner to prevent tangles and breakage, and use a heat protectant if you use heat styling tools.

Step 5: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and slow down your hair growth. If you must use heat styling tools, use a low heat setting and a heat protectant.

Step 6: Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet plays a crucial role in the health of your hair. Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods such as eggs, nuts, fish, and leafy greens are excellent for promoting hair growth.

Step 7: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the elements, such as the sun, wind, and cold weather. Wear a hat or a scarf to prevent your hair from drying out and breaking. Also, avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.

Tips for Growing Out Long Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips that can help you grow out your hair:

  • Massage your scalp to stimulate hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair, such as ponytails and braids.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Get enough sleep to promote hair growth.

FAQ about Growing Out Long Hair Guys

Here are some frequently asked questions and answers about growing out long hair guys:

How long does it take to grow out long hair?

Hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months or even a year to grow out long hair.

Should I use hair growth supplements?

Hair growth supplements may help promote hair growth, but it's best to consult a doctor before taking any supplements.

Can I dye my hair while growing it out?

You can dye your hair while growing it out, but it's best to avoid harsh chemicals and opt for a semi-permanent or temporary hair color.

Hair Care for Long Hair Guys

Here are some additional hair care tips for long hair guys:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and steps, you can grow out your hair and achieve the long hairstyle that you've always wanted. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you'll see results in no time.
