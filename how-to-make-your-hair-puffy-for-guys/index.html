How To Make Your Hair Puffy For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, making it puffy is a great way to achieve that look. While it may seem like a difficult task, there are actually several simple techniques you can use to create puffy hair. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair puffy for guys, along with some helpful tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Your Hair Puffy for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Making Your Hair Puffy
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Puffy Hair

How to Make Your Hair Puffy for Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand what "puffy" hair actually means. Puffy hair refers to hair that has been styled to have volume and texture, often achieved through teasing or backcombing. There are several ways to make your hair puffy, including:

  • Teasing or backcombing
  • Using volumizing hair products
  • Blow-drying your hair in a certain way
  • Using a curling iron or straightener

Keep in mind that some of these methods may work better for certain hair types or lengths, so it may take some trial and error to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair puffy for guys:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it first.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair. This will help add texture and hold to your hair.
  3. Section off the top portion of your hair, leaving the sides and back down.
  4. Using a fine-toothed comb, tease the hair at the crown of your head. Start by holding a section of hair straight up and combing it downwards towards your scalp. Repeat this motion a few times until the hair is teased and puffy.
  5. Repeat this teasing process on the rest of the top section of your hair, working your way towards the front.
  6. Once the top section is teased to your liking, release the sides and back of your hair and use the comb to smooth down the top layer of teased hair.
  7. Spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair to help hold the style.

And there you have it – puffy hair! You can adjust the amount of teasing or backcombing depending on how much volume you want to achieve.

Tips for Making Your Hair Puffy

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your hair puffy:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add thickness to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to make your hair puffy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I make my hair puffy if I have short hair?

A: Yes! While it may be easier to achieve puffy hair with longer hair, you can still create volume and texture with shorter hair by using a volumizing product and teasing your hair at the crown.

Q: Will teasing my hair damage it?

A: Teasing your hair can cause some damage, especially if done frequently or aggressively. To minimize damage, be gentle when combing through your hair and use a lightweight hairspray to help hold the style.

Q: Can I make my hair puffy without using any products?

A: It may be more difficult to achieve puffy hair without using any products, as they help add texture and hold to your hair. However, you can try teasing your hair with a comb or backcombing it with your fingers to create some volume.

Hair Care Tips for Puffy Hair

Once you've achieved puffy hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for puffy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it more difficult to style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your strands than a brush.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to make your hair puffy and full of volume. Happy styling!
