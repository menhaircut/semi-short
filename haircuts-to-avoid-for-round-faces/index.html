Haircuts To Avoid For Round Faces A Guide To Flattering Styles

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't flatter your round face? Look no further! We've compiled a list of haircuts to avoid for round faces and some tips on how to choose a style that complements your unique features. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Haircuts to Avoid
  • How to Choose a Flattering Haircut

  • Tips for Styling Round Faces

Haircuts to Avoid

There are certain haircuts that don't flatter round faces. Here are some styles to avoid:

Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can make your face appear shorter and wider. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that angle down towards your cheekbones.

Chin-Length Bobs

Chin-length bobs can make your face appear wider. Instead, go for a longer, layered bob that hits just below your chin.

One-Length Cuts

One-length cuts can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for layers that frame your face and add dimension.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts can make your face appear rounder. Instead, go for a longer, layered cut that hits just below your chin.

How to Choose a Flattering Haircut

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider the shape of your face. For round faces, the goal is to create the illusion of length and height. Here are some tips:

Opt for Layers

Layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, which can help elongate your face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs that angle down towards your cheekbones can help create the illusion of length.

Longer Hair

Longer hair can help elongate your face. If you're not ready to commit to long hair, consider a longer bob or lob.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Flattering Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a flattering haircut:

Step 1: Research

Research different haircuts that flatter round faces, and bring pictures to your stylist.

Step 2: Consultation

During your consultation, discuss your hair goals and concerns with your stylist. They can recommend a style that will complement your unique features.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you've agreed on a style, your stylist will cut and style your hair. Be sure to ask for styling tips and product recommendations.

Tips for Styling Round Faces

Here are some tips for styling round faces:

Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands and hair clips can help create the illusion of length.

Style Your Hair Off Your Face

Styling your hair off your face can help create the illusion of height.

Try a Side Part

A side part can help create the illusion of length.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add volume to your hair, which can help elongate your face.

Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can make it appear shorter and wider.

Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and height. Opt for layers, side-swept bangs, and longer hair.

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, one-length cuts, and pixie cuts if you have a round face.

How often should I get a haircut if I have a round face?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you'll be able to choose a flattering haircut that complements your round face. Remember to consult with your stylist and bring pictures to ensure you get the look you want. Happy styling!
