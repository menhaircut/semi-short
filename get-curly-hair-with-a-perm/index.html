Get Curly Hair With A Perm How To Achieve Beautiful Curls In 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get curly hair with a perm. If you’re tired of using curling irons or hot rollers to achieve curly locks, a perm might be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about getting a perm, including the process, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair with a Perm
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting the Best Results
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Getting a Perm

How to Get Curly Hair with a Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly or wavy. The process involves wrapping your hair around perm rods, applying a perming solution, and then neutralizing the hair to set the curls in place. The result is long-lasting curls that can last up to six months.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you get a perm, it’s important to do your research and find a reputable salon or stylist. Here’s a step-by-step guide on what to expect during the perm process:

  1. Consultation: Your stylist will discuss your desired curl pattern and assess the health of your hair to determine if a perm is right for you.
  2. Preparation: Your hair will be shampooed and conditioned to remove any product buildup and prepare it for perming.
  3. Wrapping: Your stylist will section your hair and wrap it around perm rods of various sizes to create your desired curl pattern.
  4. Perming Solution: A perming solution will be applied to your hair, which will break down the bonds that give your hair its natural shape.
  5. Neutralization: Your hair will be rinsed with a neutralizing solution to rebuild the broken bonds and set the curls in place.
  6. Rinse and Style: Your stylist will rinse your hair again, apply a conditioning treatment, and style your hair to perfection.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

Getting a perm can be a great way to achieve beautiful curls, but there are a few things to keep in mind to get the best results:

  • Choose the right size perm rods: The size of the rods will determine the size of your curls, so choose wisely. Smaller rods will create tighter curls, while larger rods will create looser curls.
  • Be patient: Perming takes time, so be prepared to spend a few hours at the salon. The results are worth it!
  • Follow aftercare instructions: Your stylist will give you instructions on how to care for your newly permed hair to ensure the curls last as long as possible.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a perm:

  • How long does a perm last? Perms can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.
  • Can you get a perm if you have color-treated hair? Yes, but it’s important to let your stylist know so they can choose the right perming solution for your hair type.
  • Will a perm damage my hair? Perming can cause some damage to your hair, but a good stylist will take steps to minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care After Getting a Perm

After you get a perm, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep the curls looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm to give the curls time to set.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the curls and cause them to fall out.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

That’s it! We hope this guide has been helpful in teaching you how to get curly hair with a perm. Remember to choose a reputable stylist, be patient, and follow aftercare instructions to get the best results.
