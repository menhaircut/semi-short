How To Do A Fade Haircut With Clippers Step By Step

Written by Bardi Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will show you how to do a fade haircut with clippers step by step. We will also provide you with some tips to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about this type of haircut.

Description

A fade haircut is a popular style that involves gradually fading the hair from short to long. This type of haircut is often used by men, but it can also be used by women. Clippers are the best tool to achieve this look, as they allow you to easily control the length of the hair.

How to

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle of water.

Step 1:

Start by washing and drying the hair. Wet hair is easier to work with, so lightly spray the hair with water.

Step 2:

Decide on the length of the fade. The longer the hair on top, the lower you will want to start the fade. Use the appropriate guard on the clippers to achieve the desired length.

Step 3:

Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up. Use the clippers without a guard to create a bald line. This is where the fade will start.

Step 4:

Use the guard one size higher to create the next section of the fade. Blend the hair between the two sections by using a flicking motion with the clippers.

Step 5:

Repeat step 4 with each guard size until you reach the desired length on top.

Step 6:

Use scissors to trim any longer pieces of hair on top.

Step 7:

Use the clippers without a guard to trim the hairline around the ears and neck.

Step 8:

Finish the haircut by using the comb to blend any remaining lines.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

- Use a mirror to check the back of the head.

- Use a spray bottle of water to keep the hair wet and easier to work with.

- Take your time and be patient. It's better to go slow and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to do a fade haircut with clippers:

Q: How long does a fade haircut last?

A: A fade haircut typically lasts 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast the hair grows.

Q: Can you do a fade haircut on curly hair?

A: Yes, a fade haircut can be done on curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a smooth blend.

Q: What is the difference between a fade and a taper?

A: A taper is a gradual shortening of the hair, while a fade involves a more dramatic change in length.

Hair Care

To maintain your fade haircut, you should trim the hair every 2-4 weeks. Use a comb and clippers to blend any lines. You should also wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to achieve a perfect fade haircut with clippers. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you will be rewarded with a great looking haircut.
