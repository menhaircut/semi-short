How To Choose A Hair Color That Suits Your Skin Tone

Written by William Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's hard to know where to start. The key is to find a hair color that complements your skin tone. This guide will help you determine your skin tone and choose a hair color that will enhance your natural beauty.

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tone categories: warm, cool, and neutral.

If you have warm skin, your undertones will be yellow, peach, or golden. If you have cool skin, your undertones will be pink, blue, or red. If you have neutral skin, you will have a balance of both cool and warm undertones.

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If your veins appear blue-green, you have neutral undertones.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm skin tones, you should choose hair colors that are warm and rich. Some good options include golden blonde, honey blonde, caramel, copper, and auburn. These colors will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural warmth.

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool skin tones, you should choose hair colors that are cool and ashy. Some good options include platinum blonde, ash blonde, ash brown, and black. These colors will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural coolness.

Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have neutral skin tones, you are lucky because you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. Some good options include chocolate brown, golden brown, and red. These colors will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Think about your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may want to choose a hair color that is close to your natural color.
  • Consult with a professional. A hairstylist can help you choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone.
  • Consider the season. In the summer, you may want to choose a lighter hair color, while in the winter, you may want to choose a darker hair color.

FAQs

What if I have a warm skin tone but want to try a cool hair color?

You can still try a cool hair color, but make sure it has warm undertones. For example, if you want to try ash blonde, choose a shade with a hint of gold.

What if I have a cool skin tone but want to try a warm hair color?

You can still try a warm hair color, but make sure it has cool undertones. For example, if you want to try auburn, choose a shade with a hint of red.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's important to take care of your colored hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid hot showers and styling tools, as heat can fade hair color.
  • Avoid chlorine and salt water, as they can also fade hair color.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to nourish your hair.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips, you can find a hair color that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful.
