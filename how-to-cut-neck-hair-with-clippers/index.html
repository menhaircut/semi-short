How To Cut Neck Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting your own neck hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done easily and quickly. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps on how to cut neck hair with clippers. Follow these steps for a clean and professional-looking cut.

How to Cut Neck Hair with Clippers

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you will need.

Tools needed:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Barbers comb
  • Scissors
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the clippers can easily glide through your hair.

2. Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers. If you are unsure which guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve your desired length.

3. Begin by trimming the hair at the base of your neck. Use the clippers to trim the hair in an upward motion, following the natural curve of your neck. Be sure to use a light touch to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

4. Next, move to the sides of your neck. Use the same technique as before, trimming the hair in an upward motion, following the natural curve of your neck.

5. Once you have trimmed the hair at the base and sides of your neck, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs that were missed by the clippers.

6. Use the barbers comb to comb through your hair, ensuring that all the hair is evenly trimmed.

7. Finally, use the mirror to check the back of your neck. If there are any uneven spots or missed hairs, trim them with the clippers or scissors.

Tips:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve your desired length.
  • Use a light touch with the clippers to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Check the back of your neck in the mirror to ensure that all the hair is evenly trimmed.

FAQ:

Q: What if I mess up?

A: If you accidentally cut too much hair or create an uneven cut, don't panic. You can always trim your hair shorter or visit a professional barber for help.

Q: How often should I cut my neck hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to trim your neck hair every 2-3 weeks.

Hair Care:

After cutting your neck hair, be sure to clean your clippers and store them in a safe place. Additionally, be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.


With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut your own neck hair with clippers. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll be left with a clean and professional-looking cut.
