Types Of Hats For Round Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Nov 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hat that complements your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the different types of hats that will flatter your face shape. From fedoras to beanies, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Round faces have a width that is almost equal to the length, and they tend to have soft features with a curved jawline. When it comes to choosing the perfect hat, it’s important to consider the shape of your face to achieve a balanced and proportional look. A hat that is too small or too big can make your face look even rounder, while the right hat can draw attention to your best features and make you look stylish and confident.

How to Choose the Right Hat for Your Round Face

When shopping for a hat, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose Hats with Angular Shapes

Hats with sharp angles, such as fedoras and trilbies, can create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. These hats have a brim that is wider at the front and narrower at the back, which helps to elongate your face and balance out your features.

2. Avoid Hats with a Rounded Shape

Round hats, such as beanies and berets, can accentuate the roundness of your face, making it look wider and shorter. These hats also tend to sit low on the forehead, which can make your face appear shorter. If you do decide to wear a beanie or beret, make sure to wear it further back on your head to create more height.

3. Opt for Hats with a High Crown

Hats with a high crown, such as a fedora or a top hat, can add height to your face and create the illusion of a longer, slimmer shape. These hats also tend to have a brim that is wider at the front, which helps to balance out the roundness of your face.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Ultimately, the best way to find the perfect hat for your round face is to experiment with different styles and see what works best for you. Don’t be afraid to try on different hats and take some selfies to see how they look. You might be surprised at what you like!

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hat

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hat for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start shopping for hats, it’s important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and comparing them to the length of your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Style

Your hair style can also play a role in choosing the right hat. If you have long hair, you might want to opt for a hat with a wider brim to balance out the length of your hair. If you have short hair, you can experiment with different styles and shapes.

Step 3: Choose a Hat with Angular Shapes

As mentioned earlier, hats with angular shapes, such as fedoras and trilbies, can help to elongate your face and balance out your features. These hats also tend to have a brim that is wider at the front, which can help to create more height and balance out the roundness of your face.

Step 4: Avoid Rounded Hats

Avoid hats with a rounded shape, such as beanies and berets, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face and make it look wider and shorter. If you do decide to wear a beanie or beret, make sure to wear it further back on your head to create more height.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

Finally, don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the right hat for your round face. Take your time, try on different hats, and see what works best for you.

Tips for Wearing Hats with a Round Face

Here are some additional tips for wearing hats with a round face:

1. Avoid Tight-Fitting Hats

Avoid hats that are too tight or snug, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face and make it look wider. Instead, opt for hats that fit comfortably and have a bit of room.

2. Wear Your Hat Further Back on Your Head

Wearing your hat further back on your head can create more height and balance out the roundness of your face.

3. Choose Hats with Vertical Details

Hats with vertical details, such as a ribbon or a feather, can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a longer shape.

FAQs

What Hats Should I Avoid if I Have a Round Face?

Avoid hats with a rounded shape, such as beanies and berets, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face and make it look wider and shorter.

What Hats Should I Choose if I Have a Round Face?

Choose hats with angular shapes, such as fedoras and trilbies, as they can help to elongate your face and balance out your features.

Can I Wear a Baseball Cap if I Have a Round Face?

Yes, you can wear a baseball cap if you have a round face. However, make sure to wear it further back on your head to create more height.

Hair Care for Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right hat, there are also some hair care tips that can help to flatter your round face:

1. Add Layers to Your Hair

Adding layers to your hair can help to create more volume and movement, which can balance out the roundness of your face.

2. Avoid Haircuts with Blunt Ends

Avoid haircuts with blunt ends, as they can make your face look wider and shorter. Instead, opt for haircuts with layers and texture.

3. Try a Side-Swept Fringe

A side-swept fringe can help to create more height and balance out the roundness of your face.

With these tips, you’ll be able to find the perfect hat that complements your round face and makes you look stylish and confident. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your look!
