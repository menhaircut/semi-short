How To Tell If You Would Look Good With Shoulder Length Hair

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a new hairstyle and wondering if you would look good with shoulder length hair? It can be difficult to predict how a certain hairstyle will look on you, but there are a few factors you can consider before making the chop. In this article, we’ll go over how to tell if you would look good with shoulder length hair, including some tips and tricks to help you make the decision.

Table of Contents

  2. Face Shape
  3. Hair Type
  4. Neck Length
  5. Confidence

Factors to Consider

Before you decide to go for a shoulder length hairstyle, there are a few factors to consider. These include your face shape, hair type, neck length, and confidence level.

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If you have a round face, a shoulder length hairstyle can help elongate your face and create a more angular look. If you have a square face, a shoulder length hairstyle can soften your features and add some femininity. If you have an oval face, you’re in luck – you can pull off just about any hairstyle!

Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. If you have fine, straight hair, a shoulder length hairstyle can add some volume and body to your hair. If you have thick, curly hair, a shoulder length hairstyle can help tame your curls and make your hair more manageable. If you have wavy hair, a shoulder length hairstyle can enhance your natural texture and create a beachy, effortless look.

Neck Length

The length of your neck can also play a role in whether or not a shoulder length hairstyle will look good on you. If you have a short neck, a shoulder length hairstyle can make your neck appear shorter and your head appear larger. If you have a long neck, a shoulder length hairstyle can emphasize your neck and create a more elegant look.

Confidence

Finally, your confidence level is important when choosing a new hairstyle. If you’re confident in your appearance and don’t mind trying out new things, a shoulder length hairstyle can be a great option. However, if you’re hesitant or unsure about a new hairstyle, it may not be the best choice for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’ve considered the factors above and have decided that a shoulder length hairstyle is right for you, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you make the chop:

  1. Find a reputable stylist who has experience cutting shoulder length hair.
  2. Bring in pictures of shoulder length hairstyles that you like and discuss them with your stylist.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair gradually, starting with a longer length and trimming as needed.
  4. Be prepared for some maintenance – shoulder length hair requires regular trims to keep it looking healthy and fresh.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you make the most of your new shoulder length hairstyle:

  • Add layers to create movement and texture.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to fine hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to switch up your style.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

FAQs

Here are some common questions and answers about shoulder length hair:

  • Will shoulder length hair look good on me if I have a double chin?

    • Yes! A shoulder length hairstyle can actually help create a more defined jawline and draw attention away from a double chin.

  • Is shoulder length hair easy to style?

    • Yes and no – it depends on your hair type and the style you’re going for. However, in general, shoulder length hair is fairly easy to style and maintain.

  • Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length hairstyle?

    • Yes! There are plenty of cute and easy updos that you can do with shoulder length hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your shoulder length hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

Hopefully, this article has helped you determine whether or not a shoulder length hairstyle is right for you. Remember, the most important factor when choosing a new hairstyle is your own personal preference and confidence level. As long as you feel great in your new hairstyle, that’s all that matters!
