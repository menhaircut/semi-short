Best Short Hairstyles For Long Oval Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect short hairstyle that suits your face shape. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on the best short hairstyles for long oval faces, including how to style them, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a lengthened shape that's slightly narrower at the forehead and jaw. This face shape is ideal for short hairstyles, as it provides a lot of room for experimentation. The key is to choose styles that balance out the length of your face, while highlighting your best features. In general, short hairstyles that feature layers or volume around the crown area work best for long oval faces.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle for your long oval face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. From pixie cuts to blunt bobs, there are plenty of options to choose from. Here are some of the best short hairstyles for long oval faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for long oval faces. This style features short layers that create volume around the crown area, while keeping the sides and back of the hair on the shorter side. This cut draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones, making it a great choice if you want to highlight your best features.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is another great short hairstyle for long oval faces. This cut features a straight, blunt edge that falls just above or below the jawline. The key to pulling off this style is to keep the hair sleek and straight, which helps to balance out the length of your face.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option if you want to add volume and texture to your hair. This style features short layers that create movement and bounce around the face. This cut works well for long oval faces because it helps to add width and balance out the length of the face.

Step by Step

Here's how to style some of the best short hairstyles for long oval faces:

Pixie Cut

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair, and blow dry using a round brush.
  2. Use a small amount of pomade to define the layers around the crown area.
  3. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Blunt Bob

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to damp hair, and blow dry using a round brush.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten the hair, creating a sleek and smooth finish.
  3. Finish with shine spray to add a glossy finish.

Layered Bob

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair, and blow dry using a round brush.
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout the hair.
  3. Finish with texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling short hairstyles for long oval faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear longer.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to your short hairstyle, as this can help to balance out the length of the face.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair, which helps to create the illusion of width.
  • Experiment with different parting options to find the one that works best for your face shape.

FAQ

What other short hairstyles work well for long oval faces?

In addition to the styles mentioned above, other short hairstyles that work well for long oval faces include the asymmetrical bob, the choppy pixie cut, and the layered pixie cut.

How often should I get my hair cut if I have a short hairstyle?

It's recommended that you get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

What hair care products should I use for my short hairstyle?

For short hairstyles, it's important to use products that add volume and texture to the hair. This includes volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo. It's also important to use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for short hair, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. This includes using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and applying a hair mask once a week. It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive heat styling.

Now that you have a complete guide on the best short hairstyles for long oval faces, it's time to experiment and find the perfect style for you. Remember to consider your hair texture, personal style, and face shape when choosing a short hairstyle, and don't be afraid to try something new!
