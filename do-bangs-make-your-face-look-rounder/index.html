Do Bangs Make Your Face Look Rounder

Description

Many people wonder if bangs make their face look rounder or not. Bangs can completely change your look, but it's important to consider your face shape before getting them. In general, bangs can make your face look rounder, but it depends on the type of bangs you get and how you style them. If you have a round face shape, you should be careful when choosing bangs because they can make your face look even rounder. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right bangs for your face shape and how to style them to avoid making your face look rounder.

How to

Choose the right bangs for your face shape

The first step to avoiding making your face look rounder with bangs is to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have a round face shape, avoid blunt bangs that end at your eyebrows. Instead, opt for longer, layered bangs that start at the crown of your head.
  • If you have a square face shape, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side and shorter on the other.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, choose wispy bangs that are longer on the sides.
  • If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any type of bangs.

Style your bangs to avoid making your face look rounder

Once you've chosen the right type of bangs for your face shape, it's important to style them in a way that flatters your face. Here are some tips:

  • Style your bangs to the side to create more angles and make your face look less round.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. Short bangs can make your face look rounder.
  • Keep your bangs long and layered to create movement and texture.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.

Step by step

If you're planning on cutting bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the right type of bangs for your face shape (see above).
  2. Part your hair where you want your bangs to start.
  3. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  4. Start by cutting a small amount of hair at a time. You can always cut more later.
  5. Use a comb to make sure your bangs are even.
  6. Style your bangs using the tips above.

Tips

  • Consider getting clip-in bangs before committing to a permanent cut.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on what type of bangs would look best on you.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Do bangs make your face look rounder?

In general, bangs can make your face look rounder. However, if you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and style them correctly, you can avoid this.

What type of bangs should I get if I have a round face shape?

If you have a round face shape, you should opt for longer, layered bangs that start at the crown of your head. Avoid blunt bangs that end at your eyebrows.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs more frequently than the rest of your hair.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and lift.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

In conclusion, bangs can make your face look rounder, but it depends on the type of bangs you get and how you style them. By choosing the right type of bangs for your face shape and styling them correctly, you can avoid making your face look rounder. Remember to take good care of your bangs to keep them looking their best.
