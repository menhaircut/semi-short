Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to our guide on the “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz.” Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure which shade will complement your skin tone and features. Fortunately, we’ve got you covered with this quiz that will help you find your perfect hair colour match.

  • Description
  • How to Take the Quiz

  • Tips for Choosing Your Hair Colour

Description

The “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz” is a simple online quiz that takes into consideration your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to determine which hair colour will suit you best. The quiz will ask you a series of questions about your preferences, lifestyle, and hair care routine to help you find your perfect match.

How to Take the Quiz

Taking the “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz” is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. You’ll need a device that’s connected to the internet and a few minutes of your time.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open your web browser and go to the “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz” website.

Step 2: Click on the “Start Quiz” button to begin.

Step 3: Answer the questions honestly and to the best of your ability.

Step 4: Once you’ve completed the quiz, you’ll receive your results.

Tips for Choosing Your Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour can be challenging, but these tips can help make the process a little easier:

  • Consider your skin tone: Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours like ash blonde, while warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours like honey blonde.
  • Choose a shade that suits your lifestyle: If you’re low-maintenance, choose a hair colour that won’t require a lot of upkeep.
  • Take inspiration from your wardrobe: Your wardrobe can be a great source of inspiration when it comes to choosing a hair colour. If you wear a lot of warm colours, like reds and oranges, consider a warm hair colour like auburn.

FAQs

Q: Is the “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz” accurate?

A: While the quiz is not foolproof, it’s a great starting point for choosing your hair colour.

Q: Can I take the quiz more than once?

A: Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like.

Q: Can the quiz be taken on a mobile device?

A: Yes, the quiz is mobile-friendly and can be taken on any device that’s connected to the internet.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Once you’ve found your perfect hair colour, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

That’s it for our guide on the “Which Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz.” We hope this quiz helps you find your perfect hair colour match. Good luck!
