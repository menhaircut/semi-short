How To Do A Ponytail With A Bump On Top

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Are you tired of the same old boring ponytail every day? Why not add a little flair to your hairstyle by creating a ponytail with a bump on top? This trendy hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and can be done in just a few simple steps. In this article, we will show you how to do a ponytail with a bump on top, along with some tips and tricks to make it look its best.

Description

A ponytail with a bump on top, also known as a "pouf," is a popular hairstyle that adds volume and height to your ponytail. It involves creating a small bump or hump on the crown of your head before securing your hair in a ponytail. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their hair without spending a lot of time or effort.

How to

Here are the steps to create a ponytail with a bump on top: 1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. 2. Using a comb, section off the top layer of your hair from the crown of your head to your temples. 3. Take the section of hair that you have separated and tease it by backcombing it towards your scalp. This will create volume and texture. 4. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section to create a bump on top of your head. 5. Gather the rest of your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. 6. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or kinks in your ponytail. 7. Finish the look by spraying a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step-by-Step

Finish the look by spraying a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

- Use a thin-toothed comb to tease your hair for a more natural look. - Avoid teasing your hair too much to prevent damage or breakage. - Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair around the bump. - Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still create a ponytail with a bump on top if I have short hair? A: Yes, you can still create a bump with short hair by using a hair piece or clip-in extension. Q: How do I prevent my bump from flattening throughout the day? A: Use a strong hold hairspray and bobby pins to keep your bump in place. Q: Can I create a ponytail with a bump on top if I have curly hair? A: Yes, you can still create a bump with curly hair by straightening the section of hair that will be teased.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind: - Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. - Limit the use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray before using them. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. - Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. In conclusion, a ponytail with a bump on top is an easy and trendy hairstyle that can be done in just a few simple steps. With a little bit of teasing and some hairspray, you can add volume and height to your ponytail for a stylish look. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair accessories and styles to make this look your own.
