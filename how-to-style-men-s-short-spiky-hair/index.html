How To Style Men s Short Spiky Hair

Written by William Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, short spiky hair might be the perfect choice for you. Not only is it low-maintenance, but it's also versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your individual style. In this article, we'll walk you through how to style men's short spiky hair, step-by-step. We'll also provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice to keep your short spiky hair looking its best.

How to Style Men's Short Spiky Hair

Before you start styling your short spiky hair, it's important to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from drying out. Once your hair is clean, follow these steps to create the perfect spiky hairstyle:

Step 1: Apply A Styling Product

The first step in styling short spiky hair is to apply a styling product. There are many different types of styling products to choose from, including pomades, waxes, and gels. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and provides the hold and texture you need to create the perfect spikes.

Step 2: Create the Spikes

Once you've applied your styling product, it's time to create the spikes. Use your fingers or a comb to work the product through your hair, creating spikes as you go. You can create spikes all over your head or focus on specific areas, such as the top of your head or the front of your hairline.

Step 3: Add Texture

To give your spiky hairstyle some extra texture, use a texturizing spray or powder. This will help to add volume and definition to your spikes, making them look more natural and effortless.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your spiky hairstyle with a light layer of hairspray. This will help to hold your spikes in place throughout the day and prevent them from falling flat.

Tips for Styling Men's Short Spiky Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short spiky hair:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and provides the hold and texture you need.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.
  • Use a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and definition to your spikes.
  • Finish your hairstyle with a light layer of hairspray to hold your spikes in place.
  • Don't overdo it with the product. Too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short spiky hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.

Q: Can I style my short spiky hair without using product?

A: Yes, you can style your short spiky hair without using product, but it will be harder to create and maintain the spikes. Using a quality styling product will help to provide the hold and texture you need to create the perfect spiky hairstyle.

Q: What is the best styling product for short spiky hair?

A: The best styling product for short spiky hair depends on your hair type and the look you're trying to achieve. Pomades, waxes, and gels are all popular choices, but it's important to choose a product that works well with your hair and provides the hold and texture you need.

Hair Care for Men's Short Spiky Hair

To keep your short spiky hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

By following these tips and using the right styling products, you can create a stylish and low-maintenance spiky hairstyle that looks great and is easy to maintain. So why not give it a try?
