How To Style Long Curly Hair Guys

Written by Bardi Nov 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a guy with long curly hair, you might be wondering how to style it. Long curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a stylish and manageable look. In this article, we'll show you how to style long curly hair guys, with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Style Long Curly Hair Guys

There are many ways to style long curly hair for guys, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Here are some popular options:

  • Curly Afro
  • Long Curly Ponytail
  • Half-Up Half-Down Style
  • Curly Top Knot
  • Curly Bun

Each of these styles can be achieved with the right techniques and products. In the following sections, we'll show you how to style each of these looks.

Curly Afro

A curly afro is a popular hairstyle for guys with long curly hair. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  4. Use your fingers to fluff out your curls and create volume.

You can also use a pick or wide-tooth comb to create more volume in your afro.

Long Curly Ponytail

A long curly ponytail is a stylish and simple hairstyle for guys with long curly hair. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create a smooth finish.
  4. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.
  5. Use your fingers to fluff out your curls and create volume.

You can also add accessories, such as a headband or scarf, to your long curly ponytail for a more stylish look.

Half-Up Half-Down Style

A half-up half-down style is a versatile hairstyle that works well for guys with long curly hair. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create a smooth finish.
  4. Gather the top section of your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.
  5. Use your fingers to fluff out the curls in the ponytail and create volume.

You can leave the rest of your hair down or create a second ponytail with the remaining hair.

Curly Top Knot

A curly top knot is a trendy hairstyle that works well for guys with long curly hair. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create a smooth finish.
  4. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.
  5. Wrap the ponytail around the base to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  6. Use your fingers to fluff out the curls in the bun and create volume.

You can also leave some curls loose around your face for a softer look.

Curly Bun

A curly bun is a classic hairstyle that works well for guys with long curly hair. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create a smooth finish.
  4. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail.
  5. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  6. Use your fingers to fluff out the curls in the bun and create volume.

You can also add accessories, such as a headband or scarf, to your curly bun for a more stylish look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long curly hair guys:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and create a smooth finish.
  4. Choose your desired hairstyle and follow the steps outlined above.
  5. Use your fingers to fluff out your curls and create volume.
  6. Add accessories, such as a headband or scarf, if desired.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips for styling long curly hair guys:

  • Use products designed for curly hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, as this can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for your hair type and personal style.

FAQs

How often should I wash my long curly hair?

You should wash your long curly hair every 2-3 days, as over-washing can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.

What products should I use to style my long curly hair?

You should use products designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. Look for products that are moisturizing and provide hold without causing stiffness or crunchiness.

How can I prevent frizz in my long curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your long curly hair, use products designed for curly hair, avoid over-washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair Guys

Here are some additional hair care tips for long curly hair guys:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
