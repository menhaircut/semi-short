Long Hair Face Shape Male How To Make It Work

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair on men has been a trend for years now, and it’s not going away anytime soon. However, not all men have the same face shape, and it’s essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. In this article, we’ll guide you through how to make long hair work for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Long hair can be a great look for men, but it’s crucial to choose a style that suits your face shape. The most common face shapes for men are oval, square, round, and heart-shaped. Each of these shapes requires a different approach to styling long hair.

How to

The first step in making long hair work for your face shape is to identify your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features. For example:

  • Oval face shape: Men with an oval face shape can pull off almost any long hairstyle. Consider a messy, layered look or a slicked-back style.
  • Square face shape: For men with a square face shape, a long, layered cut can soften the angles of the face. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.
  • Round face shape: Men with a round face shape should opt for longer hair on top and shorter sides. This will create the illusion of length and balance out the face shape.
  • Heart-shaped face: For men with a heart-shaped face, a long, layered cut with a side part can soften the forehead and create balance.

Step by step

Here are some general steps to follow when styling long hair for men:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as wax, pomade, or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired look.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling long hair:

  • Regularly trim your hair to avoid split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Consider the occasion when choosing a hairstyle. A messy, casual style may be appropriate for everyday wear, but a sleek, polished look may be better for a formal event.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on what hairstyle would work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hair on men:

Can any man pull off long hair?

Long hair can work for many men, but it’s essential to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

How often should I trim my long hair?

It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy look and avoid split ends.

What styling products work best for long hair?

The best styling product for long hair depends on your hair type and style. Wax, pomade, and gel are all popular options.

Hair Care

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Regularly trim your hair to avoid split ends.

With these tips in mind, you can make long hair work for your face shape and hair type. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find the perfect look for you.
