What Length Hair Suits A Long Face

Written by Daniel Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, one of the most important factors to consider is the shape of your face. If you have a long face, you may be wondering what length hair suits your face shape best. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your long face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Length
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheeks. To balance out the proportions of your face, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds width and volume to the sides of your face. The right length of hair will also help to create a more oval-shaped face, which is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hair Length

When it comes to choosing the right hair length for a long face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a length that falls just below your chin or at your shoulders. This will help to create the illusion of width and balance out your face shape.

Another important factor to consider is the amount of layers in your hairstyle. Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to create the illusion of width. However, be careful not to add too many layers, as this can make your hair look too thin and stringy.

Best Hair Lengths for a Long Face

  • Shoulder-length hair
  • Chin-length hair
  • Bob hairstyles
  • Medium-length hair with layers

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hair length for a long face:

  1. Determine the shape of your face
  2. Choose a length that falls just below your chin or at your shoulders
  3. Add layers to add volume and movement to your hair
  4. Consider getting a bob hairstyle
  5. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect length and style for your face shape

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling your hair if you have a long face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long
  • Choose a length that falls just below your chin or at your shoulders
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers to add volume and movement
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe to add width to your face
  • Try out different hairstyles to see what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I have long hair if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can have long hair if you have a long face. However, you should avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer. Opt for a length that falls just below your chin or at your shoulders.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that are too long, as well as hairstyles that are too short. Avoid styles that add height to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or top knots. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as this can make your face appear even longer.

Hair Care for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect length hair that suits your long face and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
