How To Use Dyson Airwrap On Fine Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have fine hair, it can be challenging to style it without causing damage or breakage. However, the Dyson Airwrap is a game-changer for those with fine hair, as it uses air instead of extreme heat to style your hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to use Dyson Airwrap on fine hair, step-by-step.

Table of Content

  • How to Use Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair
  • Tips for Using Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

How to Use Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair

Step 1: Begin by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Step 2: Once your hair is damp, start by detangling it.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections, depending on the thickness and length of your hair.

Step 4: Attach the appropriate styling attachment to the Dyson Airwrap, depending on the style you want to achieve.

Step 5: Turn on the Dyson Airwrap and select the desired heat and speed setting.

Step 6: Bring the Dyson Airwrap close to your hair, and let it suck your hair into the barrel.

Step 7: Hold the barrel in place for a few seconds and then release the hair.

Step 8: Repeat the process until you have styled all the sections of your hair.

Step 9: Finish off by using the cool shot feature to set your hair in place.

Tips for Using Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair

Tip 1: Use the lower heat settings to avoid damaging your fine hair.

Tip 2: Start with the lower speed setting and gradually increase it as you get used to the device.

Tip 3: Use the smaller barrels for tighter curls and the larger barrels for loose waves.

Tip 4: Use the pre-styling dryer attachment to dry your hair gently before styling.

Tip 5: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using the Dyson Airwrap.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use the Dyson Airwrap on wet hair?

A: No, it is recommended to use the Dyson Airwrap on damp hair.

Q: Can I use the Dyson Airwrap on all hair types?

A: Yes, the Dyson Airwrap is suitable for all hair types, including fine hair.

Q: How long does it take to style hair using the Dyson Airwrap?

A: The time it takes to style your hair depends on the thickness and length of your hair, as well as the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are free of sulfates and parabens.

Tip 2: Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid breakage when detangling your hair.

Tip 4: Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Tip 5: Limit the use of heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant spray.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve beautiful and healthy-looking curls with the Dyson Airwrap, without causing damage to your fine hair.
