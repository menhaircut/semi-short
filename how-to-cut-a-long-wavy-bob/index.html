How To Cut A Long Wavy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a long wavy bob haircut but do not want to spend a fortune at the salon? Cutting your hair at home could be a fun and cost-effective way to achieve the look you want. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting a long wavy bob.

Description

A long wavy bob, also known as a lob, is a haircut that falls between the chin and the shoulders, with subtle waves that add volume and texture to the hair. This style is versatile and suitable for any face shape and hair texture. A long wavy bob can be styled in many ways, from messy beach waves to sleek and straight.

How to Cut a Long Wavy Bob

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Follow these steps to cut a long wavy bob:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with your usual shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel dry your hair and blow-dry it until it is completely dry. Make sure your hair is free of tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Secure each section with hair clips.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the bottom and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your neck. Work your way up, taking small sections of hair and cutting them at the same angle. Repeat this process for the other back section.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Unclip one of the front sections of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the front and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your chin. Work your way up, taking small sections of hair and cutting them at the same angle. Repeat this process for the other front section.

Step 5: Add Layers (Optional)

If you want to add layers to your long wavy bob, take small sections of hair from the top and cut them at an angle, following the shape of your head. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or straighten your hair. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little at a time, as you can always cut more later.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and precise cut.
  • Do not cut your hair when it is wet, as it will appear shorter when it dries.
  • If you are unsure about cutting your hair, seek advice from a professional hairdresser.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair when it is wet?

No, it is not recommended to cut your hair when it is wet, as it will appear shorter when it dries. Always cut your hair when it is dry.

How often should I cut my long wavy bob?

You should trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I add highlights to my long wavy bob?

Yes, you can add highlights or lowlights to your long wavy bob to add dimension and depth to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long wavy bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to cut a long wavy bob, you can experiment with different styles and make this haircut your own. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
