Can I Put Makeup On My Underarms

Description

Makeup is not just for the face, you can use it to enhance other parts of your body too, including your underarms. While it may seem strange to apply makeup to your underarms, it can be helpful if you have hyperpigmentation or want to cover up scars or blemishes. However, it's important to use makeup that is safe and won't irritate your skin.

How to

If you want to try putting makeup on your underarms, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Clean Your Underarms

Before applying makeup, make sure your underarms are clean and dry. Use a gentle cleanser and pat dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply Moisturizer

Next, apply a moisturizer to your underarms to help create a smooth base for your makeup. Allow the moisturizer to absorb before applying makeup.

Step 3: Choose the Right Makeup

When it comes to makeup for your underarms, you want to choose products that are safe and won't irritate your skin. Look for products that are labeled as hypoallergenic and free of fragrances, parabens, and sulfates. You can use a concealer or foundation that matches your skin tone to cover up any imperfections.

Step 4: Apply Makeup

Using a makeup brush or sponge, apply a thin layer of concealer or foundation to your underarms. Make sure to blend well so that the makeup looks natural.

Step 5: Set Your Makeup

To make sure your makeup lasts all day, you can set it with a translucent powder. Use a large powder brush to apply the powder to your underarms.

Step by Step

  1. Clean your underarms
  2. Apply moisturizer
  3. Choose the right makeup
  4. Apply makeup
  5. Set your makeup

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting makeup on your underarms:

  • Use a gentle cleanser to avoid irritation
  • Make sure your underarms are dry before applying makeup
  • Blend well to avoid a patchy look
  • Choose products that are labeled as hypoallergenic
  • Set your makeup with a translucent powder to make it last longer

FAQ

Is it safe to put makeup on my underarms?

Yes, it is safe to put makeup on your underarms as long as you use products that are safe and won't irritate your skin.

What type of makeup should I use on my underarms?

You can use a concealer or foundation that matches your skin tone to cover up any imperfections. Look for products that are labeled as hypoallergenic and free of fragrances, parabens, and sulfates.

How do I make sure my underarm makeup lasts all day?

You can set your makeup with a translucent powder to make it last longer.

Hair Care

While makeup can help cover up imperfections on your underarms, it's important to also take care of the skin in this area. Here are some tips for underarm hair care:

  • Shave or wax regularly to avoid ingrown hairs
  • Moisturize your underarms to avoid dryness
  • Avoid using antiperspirants with alcohol, which can dry out your skin

Putting makeup on your underarms can be a helpful way to cover up imperfections and enhance your appearance. Just make sure to use safe products and take care of your skin in this area.
