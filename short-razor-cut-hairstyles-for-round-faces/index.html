Short Razor Cut Hairstyles For Round Faces A Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle, it can be a challenge for those with round faces. But fear not, short razor cut hairstyles are an excellent option for those looking for a fresh, modern look that flatters their face shape. In this guide, we'll explore everything you need to know about short razor cut hairstyles for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Short razor cut hairstyles are characterized by choppy, textured layers that give the hair a more edgy and modern look. This style works well for those with round faces because it adds volume and height at the crown, elongating the face.

When it comes to styling, there are a variety of options for short razor cut hairstyles. You can wear it straight and sleek for a polished look, or add some waves or curls for a more playful vibe.

How to Achieve

To achieve a short razor cut hairstyle, it's best to visit a professional stylist who has experience with this cutting technique. They will use a razor blade to create textured layers throughout the hair, giving it a choppy, edgy look.

When it comes to choosing the right length for your face shape, it's important to consider your individual features. For those with round faces, it's best to opt for a length that falls just below the chin or above the shoulders. This will help elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a short razor cut hairstyle:

  1. Visit a professional stylist who has experience with razor cutting.
  2. Discuss your desired length and style with your stylist.
  3. Have your hair cut with a razor blade to create textured layers.
  4. Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining

To maintain your short razor cut hairstyle, it's important to keep up with regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help keep the layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

When it comes to styling, it's best to use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and hold to your hair. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down the hair and make it look flat.

FAQs

Q: Will a short razor cut hairstyle work for all hair types?

A: Short razor cut hairstyles can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to discuss your hair type with your stylist to ensure that this cutting technique is the right fit for you.

Q: How do I know if a short razor cut hairstyle is right for my face shape?

A: Short razor cut hairstyles can work well for those with round faces, as they add volume and height at the crown, elongating the face. However, it's always best to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hairstyle for your individual features.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short razor cut hairstyle looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips and maintaining regular trims, your short razor cut hairstyle is sure to look fabulous for months to come!
