How To Make Your Straight Hair Curly Without Heat

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally straight hair, you might be envious of those with bouncy curls. However, heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can cause damage to your hair over time. The good news is that there are ways to achieve curly hair without using heat. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to make your straight hair curly without heat.

  • Step by Step Guide

How to Make Your Straight Hair Curly Without Heat

There are several methods you can use to make your straight hair curly without heat. Some of the easiest ones include:

Method 1: Braiding

Braiding your hair is a simple and easy way to get curly hair without using heat. Here are the steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Take out the braids and run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

Method 2: Twist Out

A twist-out is another way to get curly hair without heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Leave the twists in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Take out the twists and run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for making your straight hair curly without heat:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.
  4. Choose your preferred method for getting curly hair without heat (braiding, twist-out, etc.)
  5. Divide your hair into sections and follow the steps for your chosen method.
  6. Leave your hair to dry overnight or for several hours until it's completely dry.
  7. Take out the braids or twists and run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair Without Heat

Here are some additional tips to help you get curly hair without using heat:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product like mousse, gel, or cream to help define your curls.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz and protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting a curly haircut or layers to enhance your natural curls.

FAQs

Can I use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to make my hair curly?

While a blow dryer on a low heat setting can help enhance your curls, it's still considered heat styling and can cause damage over time. We recommend avoiding heat altogether and using one of the methods mentioned above to get curly hair without heat.

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on several factors, including the method you used, the products you used, and your hair type. Generally, braids and twist-outs can last for several days or until your next wash day.

Can I use these methods if I have chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can still use these methods if you have chemically treated hair. However, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for chemically treated hair to avoid damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Once you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners on a regular basis.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair without using heat. Give it a try and see how you like your new look!
