Boy Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Stylish Look For Your Little Man

Written by William Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut for your little man, boy haircuts long on top short on sides could be the perfect choice. This haircut is characterized by longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides, giving a stylish and modern look to your child. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to achieve this haircut, hair care tips, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve Boy Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides?

The first step to achieving this haircut is to choose the right length. The top should be at least 2-3 inches long, while the sides should be buzzed or trimmed short. You can use clippers or scissors to trim the hair, depending on your preference. Once you have the right length, you can start styling the hair.

To style the hair, you'll need some hair products like pomade, wax, or gel. Apply a small amount of product on your child's hair and use your fingers or comb to style it. You can go for a messy, textured look or a sleek, neat style.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Start by washing your child's hair and towel drying it.

2. Use a comb or scissors to trim the hair on the sides, leaving it short.

3. Use clippers to fade the hair from the sides to the top.

4. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top to the desired length.

5. Apply hair product and style the hair using your fingers or comb.

Tips for Maintaining Boy Haircuts Long on Top Short on Sides

To keep your child's haircut looking fresh and stylish, you'll need to maintain it regularly. Here are some tips:

  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to keep the length and shape.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Encourage your child to brush or comb his hair every day to prevent tangles and matting.

FAQs

1. Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, boy haircuts long on top short on sides can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. Can my child style his hair differently every day?

Yes, this haircut is versatile, and your child can experiment with different styles every day. You can also take inspiration from celebrity hairstyles or ask your hairstylist for suggestions.

3. Can I achieve this haircut at home, or do I need to visit a salon?

You can achieve this haircut at home, but it's recommended to visit a salon or hairstylist, especially if it's your first time. A professional can help you achieve the right length and style and give you some tips on maintaining the haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from regular haircuts, there are other ways to take care of your child's hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangler brush to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dye on your child's hair.
  • Encourage your child to wear a hat or cap when playing outside to protect the hair from the sun and wind.

Conclusion

Boy haircuts long on top short on sides are a stylish and versatile option for your little man. Whether you go for a messy or neat style, this haircut can make your child look trendy and modern. By following the tips and guide mentioned above, you can achieve and maintain this haircut at home or with the help of a professional hairstylist.
