What Hair Color Fits Me Best A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but unsure which one will suit you best? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about finding the perfect hair color for your skin tone, eye color, and personality. So, without further ado, let's dive in!

What is Your Skin Tone?

The first step in finding the right hair color for you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have a yellow or golden undertone, while cool skin tones have a pink or blue undertone. Neutral skin tones are a combination of both.

If you're not sure which category you fall into, here's a quick test. Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they look blue, you have a cool skin tone. If you can't tell, you probably have a neutral skin tone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color Based on Your Skin Tone

Now that you know your skin tone let's find out the hair color that suits you best!

Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm skin tones, colors with warm undertones like gold, honey, caramel, copper, and auburn will suit you best. Avoid cool colors like ash blonde or platinum as they may wash you out. Instead, opt for warm shades like golden blonde, strawberry blonde, or chestnut brown.

Cool Skin Tones

For those with cool skin tones, cool colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, and black will complement your skin best. Avoid warm shades like golden blonde or auburn as they may clash with your skin tone. Instead, go for cool shades like icy blonde, espresso brown, or jet black.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you're lucky! You can pull off almost any hair color, both warm and cool shades. Some great options for you are caramel, chocolate brown, or champagne blonde.

Other Factors to Consider

Aside from your skin tone, there are other factors you need to consider when choosing the right hair color. These include your eye color, natural hair color, and personality.

Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the right hair color for you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm colors like copper, honey, and golden blonde will make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, cool colors like ash brown or black will make your eyes stand out.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you determine the right shade for you. If you have naturally dark hair, going too light can be a shock to your system. Instead, opt for shades that are no more than two shades lighter than your natural color. If you have naturally light hair, you can experiment with more dramatic changes.

Personality

Your hair color should also reflect your personality. If you're adventurous and outgoing, bold colors like purple or red may suit you best. If you're more laid back and prefer a classic look, natural shades like caramel or blonde may be more your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair color looking fresh:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about finding the right hair color:

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. On average, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can, but it's best to leave it to the professionals. If you're not careful, you can damage your hair or end up with an uneven color.

What if I don't like my new hair color?

If you're not happy with your new hair color, don't panic. Wait a few days and see if it grows on you. If not, visit your stylist and they can help you make any necessary changes.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but with this guide, we hope we've made it a little easier for you. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personality when choosing your new shade. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
