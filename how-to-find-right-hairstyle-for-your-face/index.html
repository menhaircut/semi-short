How To Find The Right Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

As we all know, your hairstyle can make or break your look. But with so many different styles and trends out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your face shape. In this guide, we’ll go through the steps to finding the perfect hairstyle for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Find the Right Hairstyle for Your Face
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is important because it can enhance your best features and balance out any flaws. It’s also important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a new hairstyle.

How to Find the Right Hairstyle for Your Face

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck because almost any hairstyle will look good on you. Consider a pixie cut, long layers, or a bob.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face. Consider a long layered cut, a side-swept bang, or a lob.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you’ll want to soften your angles with a layered cut or a side-swept bang. Consider a shoulder-length cut, a bob, or a pixie cut.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you’ll want to balance out your forehead and chin. Consider a long layered cut, a side-swept bang, or a bob.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you’ll want to soften your angles with a layered cut or a side-swept bang. Consider a shoulder-length cut, a bob, or a pixie cut.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you’ll want to add width to your face with a hairstyle that has volume on the sides. Consider a long layered cut, a bob, or a pixie cut with bangs.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle
  3. Find inspiration from magazines or online
  4. Consult with a hairstylist
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools
  6. Choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features and balances out any flaws
  7. Experiment with different styling techniques and products

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on styling your hair each day
  • Find inspiration from celebrities or people with a similar face shape and hair texture
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can give you personalized recommendations
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hairstyles will look on you

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I pull off a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts look great on oval, heart, and diamond face shapes. However, if you have a round or square face shape, you’ll want to avoid a pixie cut because it can make your face look rounder.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture. Consider a layered cut or a bob with choppy layers.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

By following these steps and tips, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Remember to consider your personal style and lifestyle when choosing a new hairstyle, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques.
