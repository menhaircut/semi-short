How To Style Long Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long thick hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with and can create a variety of styles. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you're short on time. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling long thick hair, so you can look and feel your best.

How to

Before we dive into styling techniques, it's important to have a good foundation. This means taking care of your hair by washing it regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step by Step

Now that you have a good foundation, let's talk about how to style long thick hair. Here are some step-by-step instructions for several popular hairstyles:

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular and effortless-looking style that's perfect for long thick hair. Here's how to create them:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, and clip each section up.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand, starting from the middle of the hair.
  4. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat with the rest of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a tousled effect.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a classic look that's easy to achieve. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and pull it back.
  3. Secure the section with a clip or hair tie.
  4. Optional: tease the crown section for more volume.
  5. Leave the rest of your hair down, or add soft waves for more texture.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

3. Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a chic and sophisticated style that's perfect for a night out. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair back and gather it into a high ponytail.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  4. Optional: wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin for a more polished look.
  5. Finish with a high-shine hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling long thick hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, and scrunchies.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long thick hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every third day is sufficient. However, if you have an oily scalp or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to make the process easier.

How can I add volume to my hair?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair. You can also tease your hair at the crown for extra lift.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, and let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your long thick hair with ease and confidence. Remember to take care of your hair, experiment with new styles, and have fun with your look.
