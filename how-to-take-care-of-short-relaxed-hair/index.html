How To Take Care Of Short Relaxed Hair Tips And Tricks

Having short relaxed hair can be a great look for many people. It's easy to maintain, and it can be styled in a variety of ways. However, it's important to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to take care of short relaxed hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Take Care of Short Relaxed Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Take Care of Short Relaxed Hair

Short relaxed hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some steps you can take to take care of your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every week or every two weeks, depending on how oily your scalp is. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Leave the conditioner on for at least 20 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use Heat Protection

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take care of short relaxed hair:

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Wet your hair with warm water and apply a quarter-sized amount of sulfate-free shampoo. Massage your scalp for a few minutes, then rinse out the shampoo.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a quarter-sized amount of deep conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the conditioner on for at least 20 minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any heat styling tools.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using heat styling tools or air-drying.

Step 5: Trim Your Hair

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep your short relaxed hair looking great:

1. Avoid Overprocessing Your Hair

Overprocessing your hair can cause damage and breakage. Avoid using relaxers and other chemical treatments too frequently.

2. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent breakage and frizz.

3. Use Oils to Moisturize Your Hair

Use natural oils like coconut oil or argan oil to moisturize your hair and scalp.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short relaxed hair?

A: Wash your hair every week or every two weeks, depending on how oily your scalp is.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my short relaxed hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Hair Care Products

Here are some hair care products that can help you take care of your short relaxed hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep conditioner
  • Heat protectant
  • Natural oils like coconut oil or argan oil
  • Silk or satin scarf

By following these tips and tricks, you can maintain healthy and beautiful short relaxed hair. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and avoid overprocessing or using too many styling products. With proper care, your short relaxed hair can be your best accessory!
