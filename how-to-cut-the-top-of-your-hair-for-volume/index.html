How To Cut The Top Of Your Hair For Volume

Written by Aprilia Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume to your hair, one of the best ways to do so is by cutting the top of your hair. This technique involves shortening the length of your hair at the crown, which can help create the illusion of more volume. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut the top of your hair for volume, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and more.

Description

Cutting the top of your hair for volume is a simple yet effective way to add some oomph to your locks. This technique involves trimming the hair at the crown of your head, which is the highest point on your head. By shortening the length of your hair at this area, you create the illusion of more volume and height, which can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

How to Cut the Top of Your Hair for Volume

If you're ready to try cutting the top of your hair for volume, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate idea of how your hair lays and where the crown of your head is located.

Step 2: Locate the Crown of Your Head

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle and then comb it straight back. The highest point on your head is the crown, which is where you'll want to focus your cutting.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using a pair of sharp scissors, start by cutting a small amount of hair from the crown. You can always cut more, so it's better to start small and work your way up. Hold the hair up with your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process, cutting small sections at a time, until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments until you're happy with the length and volume of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the top of your hair for volume:

  • Start with dry, clean hair to get a more accurate idea of where the crown of your head is.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure you're achieving the desired length and volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the top of your hair for volume:

Is cutting the top of your hair for volume suitable for all hair types?

Yes! Cutting the top of your hair for volume can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I cut the top of my hair for volume?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how much volume you want to maintain. Generally, you can cut the top of your hair for volume every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut the top of my hair for volume myself, or should I go to a professional?

You can definitely cut the top of your hair for volume yourself, but if you're not confident in your cutting abilities, it's always a good idea to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care

In addition to cutting the top of your hair for volume, there are other things you can do to keep your hair looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and cutting the top of your hair for volume, you can achieve a fuller, more voluminous look that's sure to turn heads.
