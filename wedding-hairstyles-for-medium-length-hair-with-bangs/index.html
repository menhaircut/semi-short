Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Bangs

Written by Luffy Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect wedding hairstyle for your medium length hair with bangs? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with various hairstyle ideas along with step-by-step instructions and tips to help you achieve your dream bridal look.

Description

Medium length hair with bangs is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways for your wedding day. Whether you want a classic updo or a bohemian half-up hairstyle, there are many options to choose from. Adding bangs to your medium length hair can also enhance your facial features and add a touch of elegance to your overall look.

How to

Before diving into the hairstyles, it is important to prep your hair properly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type. Follow up with a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage. Blow dry your hair and use a flat iron or curling wand to create any desired curls or waves.

Classic Updo

The classic updo is a timeless hairstyle that exudes elegance and sophistication. To achieve this look, start by backcombing the crown section of your hair to create volume. Next, gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. Twist and pin your hair into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins. Finish off the look with hairspray to keep it in place.

Bohemian Half-Up

If you're looking for a more relaxed and bohemian look, the half-up hairstyle is perfect for you. Start by creating loose waves in your hair. Next, take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist it towards the back. Secure them together with an elastic band and wrap a small section of hair around the band to hide it. Finish off the look with hairspray to keep it in place.

Step by Step

1. Prep your hair by washing, conditioning, and using a heat protectant spray or serum.

2. Blow dry and style your hair with a flat iron or curling wand.

3. Choose your desired hairstyle.

4. Follow the step-by-step instructions provided above for your chosen hairstyle.

5. Finish off the look with hairspray.

Tips

- Choose a hairstyle that complements your wedding dress and overall bridal look.

- Do a trial run of your chosen hairstyle before the wedding day to ensure it is perfect.

- Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them better.

- Bring extra hairpins and hairspray with you on the wedding day for touch-ups.

FAQ

Can I wear a veil with a hairstyle that has bangs?

Yes, you can wear a veil with a hairstyle that has bangs. You can either position the veil above or below the bangs, depending on your preference.

Can I add hair accessories to my wedding hairstyle?

Yes, you can add hair accessories to your wedding hairstyle. From hairpins to floral crowns, there are many options to choose from that can add a touch of personality and flair to your overall look.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, it is important to take care of your hair to ensure it is healthy and radiant. Incorporate a hair care routine that includes regular washing and conditioning, deep conditioning treatments, and minimal heat styling to prevent damage. On the wedding day, use a heat protectant spray or serum before styling, and finish off with hairspray to keep your hair in place.

With these wedding hairstyle ideas and tips, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that showcases your unique style and personality. Remember to practice your chosen hairstyle before the big day and take proper care of your hair leading up to the wedding day.
