Best Haircuts For Oblong Faces Female

Are you looking for the perfect haircut to flatter your oblong face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for oblong faces female, how to achieve them, and provide tips and hair care advice to keep your new style looking fresh and fabulous.

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best haircuts for oblong faces female are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face, while minimizing length. The goal is to create balance and proportion, making the face appear shorter and wider.

How To

The first step in finding the best haircut for your oblong face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face from the hairline to the chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If the length is greater than the width, you have an oblong face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to consider the best haircuts for oblong faces female. The most flattering haircuts for this face shape include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add volume and movement to the hair, while creating softness around the face. This cut is especially flattering when paired with side-swept bangs, which help to break up the length of the face.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic haircut that can be adjusted to flatter any face shape. For an oblong face, a chin-length bob with layers at the ends can help to widen the face and create balance. Pair with a side part and soft waves for added volume.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for an oblong face. This short, cropped style helps to create the illusion of width, while showing off your features. Ask your stylist for a cut that adds volume to the crown and sides of the head.

Step By Step

Now that you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the best haircuts for oblong faces female:

1. Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry hair, then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots for added volume.
  4. Once hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep hair in place.

2. Bob

  1. Wash and condition hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry hair, then apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry hair with a paddle brush, focusing on the roots for added volume.
  4. Once hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten ends and add texture.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

3. Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry hair, then apply a styling cream or gel.
  3. Blow dry hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown for added volume.
  4. Once hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the best haircuts for oblong faces female:

  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make the face appear even longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and texture to the hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to fine hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help create volume and movement in the hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should be avoided for oblong faces?

Straight, blunt cuts and center parts should be avoided for oblong faces. These styles can make the face appear even longer.

What is the best haircut for a long face with a big forehead?

The best haircut for a long face with a big forehead is one with side-swept bangs. This helps to break up the length of the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

What is the best way to style a bob?

The best way to style a bob is to blow dry with a round brush, focusing on the roots for added volume. Finish with a texturizing spray for added hold and texture.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the best haircut for your oblong face and keeping your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
