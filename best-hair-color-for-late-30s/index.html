Best Hair Color For Late 30S A Guide For Women

Written by Aprilia Nov 16, 2023 · 4 min read

As we move into our late 30s, many of us start to consider updating our hair color. Whether it’s to cover grays, add dimension, or simply change up our look, choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. In this guide, we’ll explore the best hair color options for women in their late 30s, as well as tips for maintaining your new color.

Description

Choosing a hair color in your late 30s can be tricky. You want something that flatters your skin tone and features, while also being age-appropriate. This means opting for colors that are more natural-looking and subtle, rather than bold or dramatic.

Some of the best hair colors for women in their late 30s include warm browns, rich reds, and natural-looking blondes. These colors tend to complement a variety of skin tones and can be easily customized to suit your individual style.

How To

When choosing a hair color in your late 30s, it’s important to consider your skin tone and natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, opt for hair colors with golden or honey tones, such as caramel or chestnut. If you have cool undertones, consider hair colors with ashy or cool tones, such as platinum blonde or burgundy.

It’s also important to consider the maintenance required for your new hair color. If you’re not willing to commit to regular touch-ups, opt for a low-maintenance color such as balayage or highlights. These techniques create a natural-looking, sun-kissed effect that grows out gracefully.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone and natural hair color.

2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and features.

3. Consider the maintenance required for your new hair color.

4. Book a consultation with a professional stylist to discuss your options and ensure your new color is applied correctly.

5. Follow your stylist’s recommendations for at-home hair care, including using color-safe products and scheduling regular touch-ups.

Tips

1. Avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light, as these can be aging.

2. Consider adding subtle highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

3. Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for recommendations or to bring in photos of hair colors you like.

4. If you’re nervous about making a drastic change, start with a subtle hair color update such as adding highlights or lowlights.

FAQ

Q: What is the best hair color for women in their late 30s?

A: Some of the best hair colors for women in their late 30s include warm browns, rich reds, and natural-looking blondes.

Q: How do I choose a hair color that complements my skin tone?

A: If you have warm undertones, opt for hair colors with golden or honey tones, such as caramel or chestnut. If you have cool undertones, consider hair colors with ashy or cool tones, such as platinum blonde or burgundy.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it’s important to use color-safe products to prolong the life of your new color. This includes using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, as well as a weekly hair mask to nourish and strengthen your strands.

It’s also important to schedule regular touch-ups with your stylist to keep your new color looking fresh and vibrant. This typically involves visiting the salon every 6-8 weeks for a color refresh.

By following these tips and choosing a hair color that flatters your skin tone and features, you can update your look and feel confident in your new color.
