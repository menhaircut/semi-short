What Color Hair Do Guys Prefer

Written by Oliver Jun 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you curious about what color hair guys prefer? Whether you’re looking to change up your look or just curious about what’s popular, this guide will give you all the information you need. We’ll cover everything from natural hair colors to bold, statement-making shades, and provide tips on how to care for your hair no matter what color you choose.

Table of Contents

  • Natural Hair Colors
  • Blonde Hair
  • Brunette Hair
  • Red Hair
  • Bold Hair Colors
  • Hair Care Tips
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Natural Hair Colors

When it comes to natural hair colors, guys tend to prefer shades that are close to your original hair color. That means if you’re a natural blonde, going darker might not be as popular with guys as going lighter. Similarly, if you’re a natural brunette, most guys will be attracted to shades of brown that are close to your natural hair color.

If you’re looking to highlight your natural hair color, you can try adding some subtle highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair. This can help make your hair look more interesting and can highlight your natural beauty.

Blonde Hair

Blonde hair is a classic look that has been popular with guys for decades. Whether you’re a natural blonde or you’re thinking about going blonde, this is a color that is sure to turn heads.

When it comes to shades of blonde, guys tend to prefer lighter shades like platinum or honey blonde. These shades can make you look more youthful and can help to brighten up your features. However, if you have darker skin or features, a darker shade of blonde like ash blonde might be more flattering.

Brunette Hair

Brunette hair is another classic look that has been popular with guys for years. Whether you’re a natural brunette or you’re thinking about going darker, this is a color that is always in style.

When it comes to shades of brown, guys tend to prefer warmer, richer shades like chestnut or chocolate brown. These shades can give your hair a luxurious, sophisticated look and can help to bring out your natural beauty.

Red Hair

Red hair is a bold, statement-making color that is sure to turn heads. Whether you’re a natural redhead or you’re thinking about going red, this is a color that is sure to get you noticed.

When it comes to shades of red, guys tend to prefer deeper, richer shades like auburn or burgundy. These shades can give your hair a sultry, mysterious look that is sure to catch the eye.

Bold Hair Colors

If you’re feeling daring, you might want to try a bold, statement-making hair color. Whether you’re thinking about going pink, blue, or even green, there are plenty of options to choose from.

When it comes to bold hair colors, guys tend to have mixed opinions. Some guys love the confidence and individuality that comes with a bold hair color, while others prefer more natural looks. Ultimately, the most important thing is to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hair color you choose, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair and fade your color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colors do guys find most attractive?

Guys tend to prefer natural hair colors that complement your skin tone and features. However, some guys also find bold, statement-making hair colors attractive as well.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it’s important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and non-irritating. Look for hair dyes that are free of harsh chemicals like ammonia and peroxide, and always do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

How often should I wash my colored hair?

It’s best to wash colored hair no more than three times a week to help preserve your hair color. Using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner can also help to maintain your hair color for longer.

Can I change my hair color if I have natural curls?

Yes, you can still change your hair color if you have natural curls. However, it’s important to choose a hair color that won’t damage your curls or alter their natural texture. A professional stylist can help you choose a hair color that is safe for your curls and will complement your natural beauty.

Now that you know what color hair guys prefer, it’s time to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you opt for a natural shade or a bold, statement-making color, remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
