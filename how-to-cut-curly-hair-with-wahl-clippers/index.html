How To Cut Curly Hair With Wahl Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how tough it can be to keep it looking neat and tidy. Going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, and it can be hard to find a stylist who really knows how to work with curly locks. That's where Wahl clippers come in. With these powerful tools, you can easily trim your own hair at home, saving you time and money in the process. Here's what you need to know about how to cut curly hair with Wahl clippers.

Why Use Wahl Clippers for Curly Hair

When it comes to cutting curly hair, clippers are a great option because they allow you to create clean, even lines that are difficult to achieve with scissors. Clippers also allow you to work quickly and efficiently, making it easier to trim your hair on a regular basis. Plus, with the right attachments and blade guards, you can customize your cut to suit your specific hair type and style.

How to Cut Curly Hair with Wahl Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, you'll need a few things:

  • Wahl clippers with adjustable blade guards
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror
  • Hair clips or elastics (optional)

Make sure your clippers are charged and your blade guards are adjusted to the desired length.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel-dry it gently. You want your hair to be slightly damp, but not soaking wet. Use a comb to detangle your curls, then section your hair into manageable pieces using clips or elastics if necessary.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Begin by using the longest blade guard to trim the ends of your hair. Work your way around your head, using the comb to guide the clippers and create an even line. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much off at once.

Once you've trimmed the ends, switch to a shorter blade guard to create more texture and shape. Work in small sections, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim away any excess.

Step 4: Refine Your Style

Once you've finished the bulk of your trimming, take a step back and assess your hair. Are there any areas that need more attention? Do you want to create more layers or texture? Use the clippers and comb to refine your style, making small adjustments as needed.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you're happy with your cut, it's time to clean up any stray hairs. Use the clippers and comb to trim away any stray hairs around your ears, neck, and hairline. Use the spray bottle to dampen your hair and rinse away any loose hairs.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair with Wahl Clippers

  • Start with a longer blade guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once
  • Use the comb to lift and separate your curls, making it easier to see what you're cutting
  • Work in small sections to ensure an even cut
  • Take breaks as needed to rest your arms and neck
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it

FAQ

Q: Can I use Wahl clippers on wet hair?

A: It's best to use clippers on slightly damp hair, as this makes it easier to see what you're cutting and prevents the clippers from getting clogged with hair.

Q: Can I use Wahl clippers to create a specific hairstyle, like a fade or undercut?

A: Yes, with the right attachments and blade guards, you can use Wahl clippers to create a variety of hairstyles. However, it's best to start with a simple trim to get the hang of using the clippers before attempting more complex styles.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Cutting your hair with Wahl clippers is just one part of maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your shape

By following these tips and using Wahl clippers to trim your hair, you can keep your curls looking their best. Happy cutting!
