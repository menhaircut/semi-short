How To Make My Hair Big And Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 · 5 min read
How to Make My Hair Big and Curly: A Step-By-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

If you're looking to add some volume and bounce to your hair, there's no better way to do it than by making it big and curly. Whether you have naturally straight hair or you're looking to enhance your curls, there are plenty of techniques and products you can use to achieve the look you want. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps you need to follow to get big, curly hair, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

  • How to Make My Hair Big and Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for getting Big, Curly Hair
  • FAQ About Big, Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Big, Curly Hair

How to Make My Hair Big and Curly

There are many ways to make your hair big and curly, but here are some of the most effective:

1. Use a curling iron or wand

The easiest way to create curls is to use a curling iron or wand. Choose a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want, and wrap sections of hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing. For big, loose curls, use a larger barrel, and for tighter, more defined curls, use a smaller barrel.

2. Try hot rollers

Hot rollers are another great way to add curls to your hair. Roll sections of hair onto the rollers and leave them in until they cool. Remove the rollers and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create volume.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help enhance your natural curls and add volume. Use it on a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

4. Use a volumizing mousse or spray

Volumizing products can help create lift and body in your hair. Apply a mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting big, curly hair:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create lift and body.

2. Apply a volumizing product

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer on a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

4. Use a curling iron or wand

Use a curling iron or wand to create loose, bouncy curls. Wrap sections of hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Getting Big, Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

1. Use the right products

Using the right shampoo, conditioner, and styling products can make all the difference in achieving big, curly hair. Look for products that are designed to add volume and boost curls.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage

Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Don't overdo it with product

Using too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Use just enough to get the desired effect.

FAQ About Big, Curly Hair

1. Can I get big, curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can. You'll just need to use some hot tools and styling products to create the curls and volume you want.

2. How long will my big, curly hair last?

It depends on the products you use and how well you care for your hair. With the right products and care, your curls should last all day.

3. How often should I wash my big, curly hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to keep it clean without stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Big, Curly Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your big, curly hair looking its best:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Look for products that are sulfate-free.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Use one at least once a week.

3. Avoid heat styling as much as possible

Heat styling can damage your hair over time. Try to air-dry your hair as often as possible, and use hot tools sparingly.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve big, bouncy curls that are sure to turn heads. Have fun experimenting with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you!
