Haircut For Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly dealing with unruly, thick hair? Do you find yourself struggling to manage it every day? If so, you may want to consider getting a haircut for thick hair. Not only can it make your hair more manageable, but it can also help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about getting a haircut for thick hair, including how to choose the right style, what to expect during the process, and how to care for your hair afterward.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Thick Hair

Choosing the right haircut for thick hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not familiar with the different styles and techniques. However, there are a few things you can keep in mind to help you make the right choice.

Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a haircut for thick hair is your face shape. Certain styles may complement your features better than others, so it's important to choose a cut that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a layered cut with longer pieces around the face can help elongate your features.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Another important consideration when choosing a haircut for thick hair is your hair texture. Some styles may work better with thick, coarse hair, while others may be more suited for fine, straight hair. Talk to your stylist about your hair texture and ask for their recommendations on which styles will work best for you.

Look for Inspiration

If you're not sure what type of haircut you want, try looking for inspiration online or in magazines. You can also bring in pictures to show your stylist, which can help give them a better idea of the look you're going for.

What to Expect During the Haircut Process

Once you've chosen a haircut for thick hair, it's time to schedule your appointment. Here's what you can expect during the process:

Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they'll likely ask you a few questions about your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. This will help them determine the best way to approach your haircut and ensure you're happy with the final result.

Shampoo and Conditioner

After the consultation, your stylist will likely shampoo and condition your hair to ensure it's clean and tangle-free.

Cutting and Styling

Once your hair is prepped, your stylist will start cutting and styling your hair. They may use various techniques, such as layering, thinning, or texturizing, to achieve the desired look.

Finishing Touches

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will likely add some finishing touches, such as blow-drying, flat ironing, or curling your hair. They may also apply products to help protect and style your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

After getting a haircut for thick hair, it's important to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products specifically designed for thick hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners.

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat can damage your hair, so try to avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Talk to your stylist about your preferences and ask for their recommendations.

How often should I get my hair cut if I have thick hair?

You should aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and maintain your haircut.

Can I still color my hair if it's thick?

Yes, you can still color your hair if it's thick. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair type, and to use products specifically designed for color-treated hair.

Getting a haircut for thick hair can be a great way to achieve the look you want while also making your hair more manageable. By choosing the right style, taking proper care of your hair, and getting regular trims, you can keep your thick hair looking healthy and beautiful.
