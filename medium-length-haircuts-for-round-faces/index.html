Medium Length Haircuts For Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best medium length haircuts for round faces. Whether you're looking for a sleek bob or voluminous curls, we've got you covered.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheeks, and a softer jawline. The goal when choosing a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and definition. Medium length haircuts provide the perfect balance between short and long hair, making them a great option for those with round faces.

How to

When choosing a medium length haircut for a round face, it's important to consider a few key factors. First, you want to choose a haircut that elongates the face. This can be achieved by adding layers or volume to the top of the head, which draws the eye upward. Additionally, you want to avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear even rounder.

Step by Step

1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.

2. Choose a haircut that elongates the face, such as a layered bob or a shaggy cut.

3. Avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or a one-length cut.

4. Consider adding volume to the top of the head to draw the eye upward.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium length haircut for a round face:

- Opt for side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create definition

- Don't be afraid to play with texture, such as beachy waves or a messy bun

FAQ

Q: What are the best medium length haircuts for round faces?

A: The best haircuts for round faces are those that elongate the face and add definition. This can be achieved with a layered bob, a shaggy cut, or a long bob with layers.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: While blunt bangs can make a round face appear even rounder, side-swept bangs can actually help to elongate the face.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect medium length haircut for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness

- Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing the right medium length haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning look that flatters your round face.
