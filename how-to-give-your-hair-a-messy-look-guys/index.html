How To Give Your Hair A Messy Look Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to achieve a laid-back, effortless hairstyle, a messy look might be just what you need. Not only is it trendy and versatile, but it’s also easy to style and maintain. In this article, we’ll show you how to give your hair a messy look guys in just a few simple steps. From choosing the right products to styling techniques, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Give Your Hair a Messy Look Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Messy Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Messy Hair

How to Give Your Hair a Messy Look Guys

The first step in achieving a messy look is to choose the right haircut. A messy hairstyle typically features layers and texture, so ask your barber for a cut that will add volume and movement to your hair. Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your hair for that messy, undone look.

One of the easiest ways to create a messy look is by using the right products. A texturizing spray or sea salt spray can help add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a hair wax or pomade to create separation and definition. Just be sure to choose a product that’s appropriate for your hair type and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to give your hair a messy look guys:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using your hands to create a tousled, messy look.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to create separation and definition.
  5. Ruffle your hair with your hands to create a messy, undone look.
  6. Use a comb or your fingers to create additional texture or volume as needed.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Messy Look

Once you’ve achieved your desired messy look, you’ll want to maintain it throughout the day. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid touching or running your fingers through your hair too often, as this can flatten your style.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving your hair a messy look:

Q: How often should I wash my hair for a messy style?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. You can use a dry shampoo on the days in between to keep your hair looking fresh.

Q: Can I achieve a messy look with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a messy, textured way using the right products and techniques.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving and maintaining a messy look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a messy, effortless look that’s perfect for any occasion. Remember to choose the right products for your hair type and texture, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques until you find what works best for you.
