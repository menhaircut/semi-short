Is It Haram To Get A Fade Haircut

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a Muslim, you may be wondering whether it's haram to get a fade haircut. There seems to be a lot of confusion surrounding this topic, so we've put together this article to answer all your questions. In this post, we'll explore the Islamic perspective on getting a fade haircut, how to get one, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Description

A fade haircut is a popular hairstyle among men that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back of the head, while leaving the hair on top longer. The hair is trimmed in such a way that it gradually fades from longer to shorter, hence the name 'fade' haircut. This hairstyle can be achieved using clippers or scissors and is often paired with a beard or mustache.

How to Get a Fade Haircut?

If you've decided to get a fade haircut, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, find a barber who has experience with this type of haircut, as it requires precision and skill. Second, decide on the type of fade you want - low, mid, or high fade. Third, make sure you communicate clearly with your barber about how short you want your hair to be and what kind of style you're looking for.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a fade haircut:

  1. Choose a reputable barber who has experience with fade haircuts.
  2. Decide on the type of fade you want - low, mid, or high fade.
  3. Communicate clearly with your barber about how short you want your hair to be and what kind of style you're looking for.
  4. The barber will start trimming your hair with clippers or scissors and gradually fade it from longer to shorter.
  5. Once the fade is complete, the barber will trim the hair on top of your head to your desired length.
  6. Finally, the barber will shape your hairline and neckline to give you a clean, polished look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a fade haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your barber.
  • Be specific about how short you want your hair and what kind of fade you're looking for.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice on what would look good on you.
  • Make sure you're comfortable with the barber's technique before they begin cutting your hair.
  • Consider getting a beard or mustache to complement your new haircut.

FAQ

Is it haram to get a fade haircut?

There is no clear consensus on whether getting a fade haircut is haram or not. Some Muslim scholars argue that it is permissible as long as it does not resemble the hairstyle of non-Muslims or violate any other Islamic principles. Others believe that it is haram because it is a form of imitating non-Muslims. Ultimately, it is up to each individual to make their own decision based on their personal beliefs and values.

How can I take care of my hair after getting a fade?

After getting a fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks good. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Apply conditioner to keep your hair soft and easy to style.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  • Consider using hair products like pomade or gel to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, regardless of what kind of haircut you have. Here are a few general tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Apply conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your hair and scalp moisturized.

Conclusion

So, is it haram to get a fade haircut? The answer is not clear-cut, and it ultimately depends on your personal beliefs and values. However, if you do decide to get a fade, make sure you choose a reputable barber, communicate clearly about what you want, and take care of your hair afterwards. By following these tips, you can look and feel your best with your new haircut!
