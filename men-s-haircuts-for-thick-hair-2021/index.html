Men s Haircuts For Thick Hair 2021 How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect haircut that complements your hair type. Thick hair is often unruly and can be difficult to manage, but with the right haircut, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look. In this article, we'll discuss the top men's haircuts for thick hair in 2021, how to achieve them, and tips for hair care to maintain your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Men's Haircuts for Thick Hair
  • Tips for Hair Care to Maintain Your Look
  • FAQs about Men's Haircuts for Thick Hair 2021

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Thick Hair

When choosing a haircut for thick hair, you'll want to consider several factors, including the length, texture, and shape of your hair. A shorter haircut may be easier to manage for thick hair, while longer hairstyles can add weight and volume to your hair.

Some popular haircuts for thick hair include the crew cut, buzz cut, and undercut. These styles are low-maintenance and can help you achieve a clean and polished look. If you prefer longer hair, consider a layered cut or a textured style to add dimension and movement to your hair.

Tips for Choosing a Haircut for Thick Hair

  • Look for hairstyles that complement the natural texture of your hair
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a haircut
  • Consult with a professional stylist for advice on the best haircut for your hair type

Step-by-Step Guide for Men's Haircuts for Thick Hair

If you're looking to achieve a men's haircut for thick hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Determine the length and shape you want your hair to be.
  3. Use a comb to section off your hair and trim the sides and back of your hair with clippers.
  4. Use scissors to cut the top of your hair to your desired length.
  5. Blend the sides and top of your hair together to create a seamless look.
  6. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair as desired.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Men's Haircut for Thick Hair

  • Invest in quality hair clippers and scissors for a clean and precise cut.
  • Take your time when cutting your hair and make small adjustments as needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair for any uneven areas.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Tips for Hair Care to Maintain Your Look

To maintain your men's haircut for thick hair, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

FAQs about Men's Haircuts for Thick Hair 2021

Q: Can I still get a long haircut if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a long haircut if you have thick hair. However, it's important to choose a style that complements the natural texture of your hair and is easy to manage. Consider a layered cut or a textured style to add dimension to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair to maintain my men's haircut for thick hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and polished.

Q: What styling products work best for men's haircuts for thick hair?

A: Pomade, wax, and clay are popular styling products for men's haircuts for thick hair. These products provide hold and texture to your hair without making it look greasy or weighed down. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

In conclusion, there are many men's haircuts for thick hair to choose from in 2021. Whether you prefer a short and low-maintenance style or a longer, textured look, there's a haircut that will complement your hair type and face shape. By following a regular hair care routine and experimenting with different styling products, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look that will turn heads.
