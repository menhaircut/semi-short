How To Tame Frizzy Hair On Top Of Head

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Frizzy hair on top of the head can be a real styling challenge. Whether you have curly or straight hair, frizz can make your hair look unkempt and difficult to manage. Fortunately, there are several ways to tame frizzy hair on top of the head. In this article, we will explore some of the best tips and tricks to keep your hair smooth and silky.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQs

Description

Frizzy hair on top of the head is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. This lack of moisture causes the hair to become dry and brittle, which leads to frizz. Other factors that can contribute to frizz include humidity, heat damage, and chemical treatments. Taming frizzy hair is all about restoring moisture to the hair and protecting it from damage.

How to

There are several ways to tame frizzy hair on top of the head:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to restore moisture to your hair, which will reduce frizz. Look for shampoos and conditioners that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Look for leave-in conditioners that contain ingredients like glycerin or aloe vera.

3. Use a Hair Serum

Hair serums are excellent for taming frizz. They coat the hair shaft and help to smooth down any flyaways. Apply a hair serum to your hair after styling to keep your hair smooth and shiny.

4. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try to limit washing your hair to two or three times per week. If you need to freshen up your hair between washes, use a dry shampoo.

5. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb. This will help to prevent breakage and minimize frizz. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, gently detangling as you go.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to taming frizzy hair on top of the head:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to minimize frizz.

Step 4: Apply a Hair Serum

Apply a hair serum to your hair to smooth down any flyaways.

Step 5: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a heat protectant spray if using heat styling tools.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help tame frizzy hair on top of the head:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on high heat, as this can damage your hair and lead to frizz.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth down your hair and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair oil to tame frizzy hair?

A: Yes, hair oils can be very effective at taming frizz. Look for oils like coconut oil, argan oil, or jojoba oil.

Q: Can I use a hair mask to tame frizzy hair?

A: Yes, hair masks are excellent for restoring moisture to the hair and reducing frizz. Look for masks that contain ingredients like avocado oil, honey, or shea butter.

Hair Care

Taming frizzy hair on top of the head is all about proper hair care. Be sure to use moisturizing products, avoid overwashing your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle. With the right care, you can keep your hair looking smooth and silky.
