How To Get Long Lasting Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your waves falling flat after just a few hours? Do you want to achieve long lasting wavy hair that will last all day? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve that long lasting wavy hair you desire.

How to Get Long Lasting Wavy Hair

There are a few key steps that you should follow to get long lasting wavy hair:

  1. Prep your hair
  2. Curl your hair
  3. Finish with hairspray

Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it is important to prep your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for wavy hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and provide your hair with the necessary moisture to prevent frizz.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the curling iron. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Curl Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to start curling! Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Take each section and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds and then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Pro tip: If you want looser waves, use a larger curling iron. If you want tighter waves, use a smaller curling iron.

Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is curled, it's time to finish with hairspray. Choose a hairspray that provides a strong hold but still allows your hair to move naturally. Spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair, making sure to cover all of the curls.

Pro tip: Don't overdo it with the hairspray! Too much hairspray can make your hair stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Long Lasting Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve long lasting wavy hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much. The more you touch your hair, the more likely your waves will fall flat.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your waves.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to prevent frizz and add shine.

FAQ

How long will my waves last?

If you follow the proper steps and use the right products, your waves should last all day.

What if my hair is naturally straight?

If your hair is naturally straight, you can still achieve waves by using a curling iron. Follow the steps above to achieve long lasting waves.

What if my hair is naturally curly?

If your hair is naturally curly, you may not need to use a curling iron to achieve waves. Follow the steps above to enhance your natural waves and make them last longer.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve long lasting wavy hair that will turn heads all day long!
