How To Know If You Look Better With Blonde Or Brown Hair

Written by Eveline Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but unsure if you would look better with blonde or brown hair? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, as it can completely transform your look. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to determine which hair color suits you better.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Undertone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Analyze Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 4: Try on Wigs or Hair Extensions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Undertone

The first step in deciding whether blonde or brown hair suits you better is to determine your skin undertone. Your skin undertone can either be cool, warm or neutral. If you have a cool undertone, you will look better with blonde hair as it will complement your skin. On the other hand, if you have a warm undertone, brown hair will suit you better. If you have a neutral undertone, you can pull off both blonde and brown hair.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider when choosing between blonde or brown hair is your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, blonde hair will make your eyes pop. If you have brown or hazel eyes, brown hair will bring out the richness in your eye color. However, if you have light brown or amber eyes, both colors will suit you equally well.

Step 3: Analyze Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you determine which color would suit you better. If you have naturally blonde hair, you may want to go for a darker shade of blonde or light brown. If you have naturally brown hair, you can either go for a lighter shade of brown or blonde. It’s always advisable to stay within two shades of your natural hair color to avoid looking too drastic.

Step 4: Try on Wigs or Hair Extensions

If you’re still unsure which hair color suits you better, you can always try on wigs or hair extensions. This will give you an idea of how you would look with blonde or brown hair without committing to a permanent change. You can also seek the advice of a professional hairstylist who can help you choose the best color for your skin tone and features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you’ve chosen your hair color, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot showers and use lukewarm water instead
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight for prolonged periods
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner at least once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I dye my hair from blonde to brown or vice versa?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair from blonde to brown or vice versa. However, it’s important to seek the advice of a professional hairstylist to ensure that the color change is done properly.

Q: Will blonde or brown hair make me look younger?

A: Blonde hair tends to give a younger and more youthful appearance while brown hair is more mature and sophisticated. However, this also depends on your skin tone, eye color and natural hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires extra care and attention to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently

Choosing between blonde or brown hair can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you can make an informed decision. Remember to always seek the advice of a professional hairstylist and maintain your hair color to keep it looking its best.
