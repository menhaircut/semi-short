How To Cut Pixie Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the salon for a pixie haircut? Why not try cutting it yourself? With a little bit of practice and patience, you can learn how to cut your own pixie haircut at home. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps on how to cut a pixie haircut yourself.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A pixie haircut is a short, cropped haircut that is typically cut close to the head. It is a popular hairstyle that can be customized to fit your individual style. Cutting a pixie haircut yourself can be a challenging task, but it is possible with the right tools and techniques.

How to

Before starting to cut your hair, make sure you have the necessary tools such as scissors, clippers, and a comb. It is also recommended to have a mirror and a spray bottle of water nearby.

Step by Step

Here are the steps on how to cut a pixie haircut yourself:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Use the clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually move to a shorter guard until you reach your desired length.
  3. Use the scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start by taking small sections of hair and cutting it to the desired length.
  4. Blend the sides and back of your hair with the top using the scissors.
  5. Use the clippers to clean up the nape of your neck and around your ears.
  6. Use the scissors to cut any remaining stray hairs.
  7. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it is even and balanced.
  8. Style your hair as desired using a styling product.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a pixie haircut yourself:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed.
  • Use a comb to section off your hair and cut one section at a time.
  • Cut your hair when it is dry to ensure an even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a pixie haircut yourself:

Can I cut my own hair if I have no experience?

Yes, but it will take practice and patience to get it right. Start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You will need scissors, clippers, a comb, and a mirror. It is also recommended to have a spray bottle of water nearby.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It is recommended to cut your hair when it is dry to ensure an even cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and apply a styling product as desired.

Cutting a pixie haircut yourself can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, it is possible. Remember to start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed. Practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect. Happy cutting!
