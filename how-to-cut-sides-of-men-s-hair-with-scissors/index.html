How To Cut Sides Of Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

In this article, we will be discussing the process of cutting the sides of men's hair with scissors. This is a basic haircutting technique that can be done at home with the right tools and a little bit of practice. Whether you're looking to save money on haircuts or just prefer to style your hair at home, this guide will provide you with everything you need to know.

How to Cut Sides of Men's Hair with Scissors

The first step in cutting the sides of men's hair with scissors is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Once you have these tools, you can begin the haircutting process.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure that the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be difficult to cut and may result in uneven or choppy cuts. Use the spray bottle to mist the hair lightly and comb through it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Next, use the comb to section the hair. Start by dividing the hair at the temple and combing it straight back. Use the comb to create a straight, vertical part on each side of the head, about 1 inch behind the hairline. This will create two sections of hair on the sides of the head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Be sure to trim the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut. Work from the bottom up, cutting the hair in vertical sections. Repeat the process on the other side of the head.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you have finished cutting both sides, check for evenness. Use the comb to comb through the hair and look for any areas that may need additional trimming. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until it is even.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting the sides of men's hair with scissors:

  1. Gather the necessary tools
  2. Start with clean, dry hair
  3. Section the hair
  4. Start cutting
  5. Check for evenness

Tips for Cutting Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness
  • Hold the hair between your fingers for better control
  • Use a comb to check for evenness and adjust as needed

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a healthy length and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, with the right tools and practice, you can cut your own hair. However, it is always recommended to seek the help of a professional if you are unsure or inexperienced.

Hair Care After Cutting

After you have finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Now that you know how to cut the sides of men's hair with scissors, you can save money and achieve the perfect cut at home. Remember to use sharp scissors, work in small sections, and check for evenness to achieve the best results. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to master this basic haircutting technique in no time!
