Is It Good To Relax Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering relaxing your short hair but wondering if it's a good idea? You're not alone. Many women with short hair struggle with whether to relax their hair or leave it natural. In this guide, we'll explore the pros and cons of relaxing short hair, how to do it, and tips for maintaining healthy, relaxed hair.

Table of Contents

  • Pros and Cons of Relaxing Short Hair
  • How to Relax Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Relaxed Short Hair
  • FAQ

Pros and Cons of Relaxing Short Hair

Before deciding whether to relax your short hair, it's important to understand the advantages and disadvantages. Here are some of the pros and cons:

Pros

  • Smoother, straighter hair
  • Easier to manage and style
  • Less frizz and flyaways

Cons

  • Dry, damaged hair if not done properly
  • Frequent touch-ups required
  • Possible hair breakage

Overall, relaxing short hair can be a good option if you're looking for a sleek, polished look. However, it's important to weigh the pros and cons and make an informed decision.

How to Relax Short Hair

If you've decided to relax your short hair, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Preparation

Before relaxing your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any styling products. You should also do a strand test to determine how long to leave the relaxer on your hair.

Step 2: Apply the Relaxer

Using a relaxer kit, apply the relaxer to your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and avoid getting the relaxer on your scalp.

Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize

After the recommended time, rinse the relaxer out thoroughly and apply the neutralizing shampoo. Leave the shampoo on for a few minutes before rinsing out.

Step 4: Condition

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair to restore moisture and protect against damage.

Remember to always follow the instructions carefully and use a relaxer specifically designed for your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Relaxed Short Hair

Once you've relaxed your short hair, it's important to take proper care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent further damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

1. Can I relax my hair at home?

Yes, you can relax your hair at home using a relaxer kit. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a strand test first to avoid damage.

2. How often should I touch up my relaxer?

You should touch up your relaxer every 6-8 weeks to maintain the straight, smooth appearance.

3. Can I color my relaxed hair?

Yes, you can color your relaxed hair. However, it's important to wait at least two weeks after relaxing to avoid damage.

Hair Care for Relaxed Short Hair

Maintaining healthy, relaxed short hair requires proper care and attention. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a satin pillowcase or wearing a satin scarf.
  • Consider getting professional treatments, such as protein or moisture treatments, to keep your hair strong and healthy.

With proper care and attention, relaxed short hair can be a great option for a sleek, polished look. However, it's important to weigh the pros and cons and make an informed decision before taking the plunge.
