How To Get A Longer Face Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your round or square face? Do you want to achieve a longer-looking face? Then, you have come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to get a longer face. From simple exercises to haircuts, we got you covered. So, sit back, relax, and read on!

Exercises

One of the best ways to get a longer face is by doing facial exercises. These exercises target the muscles in your face and help to stretch them out. Here are some exercises you can try:

The Fish Face

This exercise is great for toning the muscles around your cheeks and lips. Here's how to do it:

  1. Suck your cheeks and lips inwards, as if you are trying to make a fish face.
  2. Hold the position for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Relax and repeat the exercise 10-15 times.

The Jaw Jut

This exercise is perfect for toning the muscles in your jawline. Here's how to do it:

  1. Jut your lower jaw out as far as you can.
  2. Hold the position for 5-10 seconds.
  3. Relax and repeat the exercise 5-10 times.

Haircuts

The right haircut can also help to create the illusion of a longer face. Here are some haircuts you can try:

The Long Bob

A long bob, or lob, is a great haircut for creating the illusion of a longer face. The length of the bob should hit just below your chin, and the layers should be kept long and subtle.

The Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can also help to elongate your face. The bangs should be cut diagonally, starting from the crown of your head and ending at your cheekbones.

Makeup

Makeup can also be used to create the illusion of a longer face. Here are some makeup tips you can try:

The Contour

Contouring can help to create shadows that make your face appear longer. Use a contour powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline.

The Highlight

Highlighting can also help to elongate your face. Use a highlighter that is one shade lighter than your skin tone, and apply it to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on your cheekbones.

Fashion

The right clothing can also help to create the illusion of a longer face. Here are some fashion tips you can try:

The V-Neck

A V-neck top or dress can help to elongate your face. The V-shape draws the eye downward, creating the illusion of length.

The Vertical Stripes

Vertical stripes can also help to make your face appear longer. The stripes draw the eye up and down, creating a lengthening effect.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a longer face:

Can I get a longer face naturally?

Yes, you can get a longer face naturally by doing facial exercises and maintaining good posture.

What is the best exercise for a longer face?

The fish face and the jaw jut are both great exercises for a longer face.

What is the best haircut for a longer face?

The long bob and side-swept bangs are both great haircuts for a longer face.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking good care of your hair can also help to make your face appear longer. Here are some hair care tips you can try:

Avoid Heavy Products

Using heavy hair products, such as waxes and gels, can weigh your hair down and make it appear shorter. Opt for lighter products, such as mousses and sprays, instead.

Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush when blow-drying your hair can help to create volume and length.

There you have it! Some tips and tricks on how to get a longer face. Remember, these are just suggestions, and everyone's face is unique. Embrace your natural beauty and love the skin you're in!
