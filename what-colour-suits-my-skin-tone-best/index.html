What Colour Suits My Skin Tone Best

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right color that complements your skin tone can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. Picking the perfect hue can make your skin glow and your eyes pop, while wearing the wrong shades can make you look dull and washed out. If you're not sure what color suits your skin tone best, don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your skin tone and the colors that will flatter it the most.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect color for your skin tone is to determine what your skin tone actually is. Here's a simple way to do it:

Step 1: Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they look blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you might have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Check Your Skin Reaction to the Sun

If your skin turns pink or burns easily in the sun, you have cool undertones. If your skin tans easily, you have warm undertones. If you're somewhere in between, you might have neutral undertones.

Step 3: Look at Your Skin

If your skin looks pink, rosy or blueish-red, you have cool undertones. If your skin looks yellow, peachy or golden, you have warm undertones. If you're somewhere in between, you might have neutral undertones.

Colors for Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, you'll look best in colors that have blue or pink undertones, such as:

  • Navy blue
  • Bright blue
  • Emerald green
  • Lavender
  • Pink
  • Purple
  • Ruby red
  • Silver

These colors will complement your skin tone and make you look bright and fresh. Avoid colors that have yellow or orange undertones, like mustard yellow or peach, as they can make you look sallow.

Colors for Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in colors that have yellow or orange undertones, such as:

  • Coral
  • Cream
  • Gold
  • Peach
  • Rust
  • Terracotta
  • Warm red
  • Yellow

These colors will complement your skin tone and make you look radiant. Avoid colors that have blue or pink undertones, like icy blue or lavender, as they can make you look washed out.

Colors for Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, you're lucky because you can wear most colors! However, some shades might look better on you than others. Here are some colors that will complement your skin tone the most:

  • Black
  • Brown
  • Gray
  • Green
  • Navy blue
  • Red
  • White

These colors will complement your skin tone and make you look polished and put together.

Tips for Choosing the Right Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right color:

  • Consider the occasion: Some colors might be more appropriate for certain occasions. For example, you might want to wear brighter colors for a summer wedding, but stick to darker hues for a job interview.
  • Experiment with different shades: Just because you have warm undertones doesn't mean you can't wear blue. Experiment with different shades to see what suits you best.
  • Consider your hair color: Your hair color can also affect what colors look best on you. For example, if you have blonde hair, you might want to avoid wearing pastels as they can make you look too washed out.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear black if I have warm undertones?

A: Yes, you can wear black if you have warm undertones. However, you might want to pair it with a warm-colored accessory, like a gold necklace, to balance it out.

Q: Can I wear white if I have cool undertones?

A: Yes, you can wear white if you have cool undertones. However, you might want to pair it with a cool-colored accessory, like a silver watch, to balance it out.

Hair Care

Choosing the right hair color can also make a big difference in how your skin looks. Here are some tips for choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your skin tone: Just like how you choose clothing colors based on your skin tone, you should also choose hair colors based on it. If you have cool undertones, you might want to go for ashy blonde or cool brown. If you have warm undertones, you might want to go for golden blonde or warm brown.
  • Consider your eye color: Your eye color can also affect what hair color looks best on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, you might want to go for a warm, honey blonde. If you have green eyes, you might want to go for a rich, auburn brown.
  • Consult a professional: If you're not sure what hair color would suit you best, it's always a good idea to consult a professional stylist. They can recommend colors that will flatter your skin tone and eye color the most.

By following these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect color that suits your skin tone the best, whether it's for clothing or hair. Remember, it's all about experimenting and finding what works for you.
