Long On Top Short On Sides Haircut A Timeless Style

Written by Bardi Oct 22, 2023 · 5 min read

A long on top, short on sides haircut is a versatile and timeless style that has been popular for decades. This haircut involves leaving the hair on the top of the head long, while the sides and back are trimmed short. This creates a sharp contrast between the longer and shorter sections of hair, giving the style a unique and stylish look.

Description

The long on top, short on sides haircut is a classic style that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile haircut that can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes and hair textures. This style is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head, which can be styled in various ways, while the sides and back are trimmed short. This creates a sharp contrast between the longer and shorter sections of hair, giving the style a unique and stylish look.

How to Get the Long on Top, Short on Sides Haircut

If you're interested in getting a long on top, short on sides haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience and specializes in men's haircuts. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have a similar style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the long on top, short on sides haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it is clean and easy to work with.
  2. Discuss the style you want with your barber or hairstylist. Show them pictures of the style you like and ask for their advice on whether it will suit your face shape and hair texture.
  3. The barber or hairstylist will start by trimming the sides and back of your hair with clippers, leaving the hair on the top of your head longer.
  4. They will then use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. This can be left longer for a more dramatic look, or shorter for a more subtle style.
  5. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. Your barber or hairstylist can recommend the best styling products for your hair type and desired look.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Once you have your long on top, short on sides haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking sharp and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides and back short and tidy.
  • Use a good quality styling product to keep the hair on top looking neat and styled. This can be a wax, pomade, or gel.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you. You can try slicking the hair back for a more formal look, or creating a messy, textured style for a more casual look.

FAQs

What face shape suits the long on top, short on sides haircut?

The long on top, short on sides haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond. It can help to elongate a round face, while adding width to a narrow face.

What hair type is best for the long on top, short on sides haircut?

The long on top, short on sides haircut can work well with most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. It's important to choose a styling product that is suitable for your hair type to keep the hair looking neat and styled.

How do I style the hair on top?

The hair on top can be styled in various ways, depending on your desired look. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a slicked-back or textured style, or use a blow dryer to add volume and height to the hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your long on top, short on sides haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a good quality styling product that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap when swimming.

In conclusion, the long on top, short on sides haircut is a timeless and versatile style that can suit a variety of face shapes and hair textures. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your style looking sharp and stylish for years to come.
