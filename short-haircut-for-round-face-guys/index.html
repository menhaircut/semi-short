Short Haircut For Round Face Guys A Guide To The Best Styles In 2023

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are plenty of options out there that will flatter your face shape and have you looking your best. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short haircuts for round face guys in 2023.

Description: What Makes a Good Short Haircut for Round Face Guys

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length in your face. This means avoiding styles that add too much volume on the sides or top of your head. Instead, you want to focus on styles that are more streamlined and tapered.

Another key consideration is the shape of your hairline. If you have a high hairline, you may want to avoid styles that expose too much of your forehead. On the other hand, if you have a receding hairline, you may want to consider a style that incorporates a fringe or bangs to help conceal it.

How To: Choosing the Right Short Haircut for Round Face Guys

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to work with a stylist who has experience working with this face shape. They can help you choose a style that will flatter your features and bring out your best qualities.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to do some research and gather some inspiration photos. Look for styles that feature shorter sides and longer lengths on top, as well as styles that incorporate texture and movement.

Step By Step: Getting the Perfect Short Haircut for Round Face Guys

Step 1: Consult with your stylist. Discuss your face shape, hair texture, and personal style preferences. Bring along any inspiration photos you have.

Step 2: Choose a style that flatters your face shape. Consider tapered sides, longer lengths on top, and texture.

Step 3: Have your hair cut to the desired length and shape. Your stylist may use clippers or scissors, depending on the style you've chosen.

Step 4: Style your hair at home using a product that works well with your hair type and texture. Experiment with different techniques to find a look that works for you.

Tips: Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Round Face Guys

Once you've gotten your new short haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQs: Short Haircut for Round Face Guys

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Some short haircuts can make a round face look even rounder, while others can help to create the illusion of length and balance.

Q: Can I still have a fringe or bangs with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can! A fringe or bangs can be a great way to add interest and texture to a short haircut, and can help to conceal a receding hairline.

Q: What kind of products should I use to style my short haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Generally, a lightweight styling product like a pomade or wax can help to add texture and hold without weighing your hair down.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Short Haircut for Round Face Guys Looking Great

To keep your short haircut looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means using high-quality products, avoiding too much heat, and getting regular trims and touch-ups.

It's also important to pay attention to your diet and overall health. Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth, while getting enough sleep and exercise can help to reduce stress and promote overall wellness.

With the right care and styling, a short haircut can be a great choice for guys with round faces. So why not give it a try and see how it looks on you?
