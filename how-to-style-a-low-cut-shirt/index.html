How To Style A Low Cut Shirt Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Low cut shirts can be tricky to style, but when done right, they can be a stylish addition to any wardrobe. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to style a low cut shirt to make you look fashionable and confident.

How to Style a Low Cut Shirt

Low cut shirts are perfect for any casual occasion, but they can also be dressed up for a night out. Here are some tips on how to style a low cut shirt:

1. Layer with a Camisole

If you're not comfortable showing too much skin, layer your low cut shirt with a camisole. Choose a camisole that matches the color of your shirt or go for a contrasting color for a pop of color.

2. Accessorize with Jewelry

Low cut shirts are perfect for showing off your favorite necklace or earrings. Choose a statement piece of jewelry that complements your outfit. Avoid wearing too much jewelry that can overwhelm your outfit.

3. Pair with High-Waisted Bottoms

High-waisted bottoms such as jeans, skirts, or shorts are the perfect pairing for low cut shirts. High-waisted bottoms balance out the amount of skin that you're showing and make you look put together.

4. Wear a Blazer

If you're going for a professional look, wear a blazer with your low cut shirt. A blazer adds structure to your outfit and makes you look polished.

5. Tuck into a Skirt

Tucking your low cut shirt into a skirt is a great way to show off your curves while still looking classy. Choose a skirt that complements your shirt and adds interest to your outfit.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style a low cut shirt:

Step 1: Choose your Low Cut Shirt

Choose a low cut shirt that you feel comfortable in. Consider the occasion and the weather when picking out your shirt.

Step 2: Pick your Bottoms

Choose high-waisted bottoms that complement your low cut shirt. Consider the color and texture of your bottoms.

Step 3: Accessorize with Jewelry

Pick a statement piece of jewelry that complements your outfit. Avoid wearing too much jewelry that can overwhelm your outfit.

Step 4: Layer with a Camisole

If you're not comfortable showing too much skin, layer your low cut shirt with a camisole. Choose a camisole that matches the color of your shirt or go for a contrasting color for a pop of color.

Step 5: Wear a Blazer

If you're going for a professional look, wear a blazer with your low cut shirt. A blazer adds structure to your outfit and makes you look polished.

Tips for Styling a Low Cut Shirt

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling a low cut shirt:

1. Don't Wear a Bra

If you're wearing a low cut shirt, avoid wearing a bra. Instead, choose a sticky bra or bra tape to avoid any visible bra straps.

2. Don't Wear Too Much Jewelry

Avoid wearing too much jewelry that can overwhelm your outfit. Choose one statement piece of jewelry that complements your outfit.

3. Choose the Right Shoes

Choose shoes that complement your outfit. If you're wearing a casual outfit, wear sneakers or sandals. If you're going for a dressier look, wear heels or dress shoes.

FAQ

Q: What should I wear under a low cut shirt?

A: You can wear a camisole or bralette under a low cut shirt.

Q: Can I wear a blazer with a low cut shirt?

A: Yes, a blazer adds structure to your outfit and makes you look polished.

Q: Can I wear a low cut shirt to work?

A: It depends on your workplace's dress code. If your workplace allows casual attire, you can wear a low cut shirt with a blazer or cardigan.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a low cut shirt, your hair can make a big impact on your overall look. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Style your hair based on the occasion

Choose a hairstyle that complements your outfit and the occasion. If you're going for a casual look, wear your hair in a messy bun or ponytail. If you're going for a dressier look, wear your hair in loose waves or a sleek bun.

2. Use hair products

Use hair products such as hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair in place. Avoid using too much product that can make your hair look greasy.

3. Keep your hair clean

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Conclusion

Styling a low cut shirt can be easy when you follow these tips and tricks. Remember to choose high-waisted bottoms, accessorize with jewelry, and layer with a camisole if needed. With these tips, you can rock a low cut shirt with confidence and style.
