How To Disguise Grey Hair With Highlights A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of seeing grey hairs peeking through your locks? Don't worry; you're not alone. Grey hair is a natural part of the aging process, but that doesn't mean you have to embrace it fully. You can easily disguise grey hair with highlights, and we're here to show you how.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Highlights are a great way to disguise grey hair because they draw attention away from the roots. By adding lighter pieces of hair throughout your locks, the grey hairs blend in and become less noticeable. Plus, highlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, giving you a more youthful appearance.

How To

Before you start highlighting your hair, it's essential to decide on the type of highlights you want. There are several options, including:

  • Full highlights: This technique involves highlighting your entire head of hair.
  • Partial highlights: This technique involves highlighting specific sections of your hair, such as the crown or the sides.
  • Balayage highlights: This technique involves hand-painting the highlights onto your hair for a more natural look.

Once you've chosen your highlighting technique, it's time to select the color. To disguise grey hair, choose a color that's no more than two shades lighter than your natural hair color. This will ensure that the highlights blend in seamlessly with your hair.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to disguise grey hair with highlights:

  1. Separate your hair into sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  2. Apply the highlighting color to the sections of hair you want to highlight.
  3. Wrap each highlighted section in foil to keep the color from bleeding into other sections.
  4. Let the color sit for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse out the color and apply a color-safe conditioner.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when highlighting your hair:

  • Start with a small amount of highlights and gradually add more if you want a more dramatic look.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your highlights looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the highlights.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your highlights looking fresh and to prevent grey hairs from showing through.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my highlights touched up?

A: It's best to get your highlights touched up every 8-12 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I highlight my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to highlight your hair at home, we recommend going to a professional to ensure that the highlights are applied evenly and that the color is the right shade.

Hair Care

After highlighting your hair, it's essential to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your highlights from fading.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair and your highlights.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips, you'll be able to disguise grey hair with highlights and maintain healthy, vibrant locks. So why not give it a try and embrace a more youthful appearance?
