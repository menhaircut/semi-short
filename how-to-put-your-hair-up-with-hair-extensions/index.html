How To Put Your Hair Up With Hair Extensions

Written by Eveline Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you want to create a new hairstyle with hair extensions, you are in the right place. In this article, we will show you how to put your hair up with hair extensions. Whether you have clip-in or tape-in hair extensions, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will make you look stunning.

Description

Hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your hair. They come in different types and textures, so you can find the one that suits your hair type and style. If you want to put your hair up with hair extensions, you need to know how to do it properly. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to put your hair up with hair extensions.

How To

Step 1: Brush your hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and tangle-free. Use a brush or a comb to detangle your hair from the roots to the ends. This will make it easier to style your hair and prevent any damage to your hair extensions.

Step 2: Prepare your hair extensions

If you have clip-in hair extensions, open the clips and lay them flat on a surface. If you have tape-in hair extensions, separate your hair into sections and remove the tape backing from the wefts.

Step 3: Section your hair

Use a comb to section your hair into the desired style. For a high ponytail, section the hair from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck. For a half-up half-down style, section the hair from the temples to the crown of your head.

Step 4: Clip in your extensions

If you have clip-in hair extensions, clip them in one by one, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure the clips are secure and hidden under your hair. If you have tape-in hair extensions, sandwich your natural hair between two wefts and press them firmly together.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once your hair extensions are clipped in, you can style your hair as desired. For a high ponytail, gather your hair and extensions and secure them with a hair tie. For a half-up half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a clip or a hair tie.

Tips

- Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair extensions as it can damage them.

- Don't sleep with your hair extensions in as it can cause tangling and breakage.

FAQ

Can I put my hair up with tape-in hair extensions?

Yes, you can put your hair up with tape-in hair extensions. Just make sure to sandwich your natural hair between two wefts and press them firmly together.

Can I put my hair up with clip-in hair extensions?

Yes, you can put your hair up with clip-in hair extensions. Just clip them in one by one, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

How long can I wear my hair extensions?

You can wear your hair extensions for up to 8 weeks, depending on the type and quality of the extensions. However, it's best to remove them every 6 weeks to prevent any damage to your natural hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair extensions looking great, you need to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

- Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangling.

- Wash your hair extensions every 2-3 weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair extensions as it can damage them.

- Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing them.

With these simple tips, you can keep your hair extensions looking great for months to come.
