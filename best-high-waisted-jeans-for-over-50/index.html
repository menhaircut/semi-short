Best High Waisted Jeans for Over 50

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of high waisted jeans, it's all about comfort and style. Here are our top picks:

1. Levi's High Rise Straight Jeans

Levi's is a classic denim brand that's been around for over 150 years. Their High Rise Straight Jeans are perfect for over 50 women who want a comfortable and flattering fit. These jeans have a high waist and straight leg, which elongates the body and gives a slimming effect. Plus, they come in a range of washes to suit any style.

2. NYDJ Marilyn Straight Jeans

NYDJ is a brand that's dedicated to creating jeans that flatter every body type. Their Marilyn Straight Jeans are no exception. These high waisted jeans have a straight leg and patented Lift Tuck Technology that slims from within. Plus, they come in a variety of sizes, so you're sure to find the perfect fit.

3. Madewell The Perfect Vintage Jean

If you're looking for a more relaxed fit, Madewell's The Perfect Vintage Jean is a great option. These high waisted jeans have a straight leg and a slightly cropped hem, which gives them a casual and stylish look. Plus, they're made with a stretchy denim that's comfortable to wear all day.

How to Style High Waisted Jeans

High waisted jeans are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Here are some tips for how to style them:

  • For a casual look, pair your high waisted jeans with a simple t-shirt and sneakers.
  • To dress up your jeans, wear a blouse or button-down shirt tucked into the waistband and add a pair of heels or ankle boots.
  • A denim jacket or blazer can add a stylish touch to your high waisted jeans outfit.
  • Accessorize with a statement necklace, scarf, or belt to add some personality to your outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Pair

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect pair of high waisted jeans:

  1. Know your body type: Are you apple-shaped, pear-shaped, or hourglass-shaped? Knowing your body type will help you find a pair of jeans that flatters your figure.
  2. Measure your waist: Measure your waist at the narrowest point to find the right size for your high waisted jeans.
  3. Try on different styles: High waisted jeans come in a variety of styles, including straight leg, skinny, and wide leg. Try on different styles to find the one that suits your body type and personal style.
  4. Check the rise: Make sure the rise of the jeans hits at the right point on your waist to ensure a comfortable fit.
  5. Check the length: The length of the jeans should be just right â not too long or too short.
  6. Check the fabric: Look for high quality denim that's stretchy and comfortable to wear all day.

Tips for Caring for Your High Waisted Jeans

Here are some tips for caring for your high waisted jeans:

  • Wash your jeans inside out to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using fabric softener, which can break down the fibers of the denim.
  • Hang your jeans to dry to prevent shrinking.
  • Iron your jeans on a low setting to remove wrinkles.

FAQs About High Waisted Jeans for Over 50

Q: Are high waisted jeans comfortable?

A: Yes, high waisted jeans can be very comfortable, especially when made with stretchy denim.

Q: What body type looks best in high waisted jeans?

A: High waisted jeans can flatter any body type, but they are especially flattering on women with an hourglass figure.

Q: How do I know if high waisted jeans are right for me?

A: Try on different styles of high waisted jeans to see which ones flatter your figure and suit your personal style.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing High Waisted Jeans

When wearing high waisted jeans, it's important to consider your hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • If you're wearing your hair down, add volume at the roots for a flattering look.
  • If you're wearing your hair up, consider a messy bun or ponytail for a casual and stylish look.
  • A statement hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, can add some personality to your outfit.

With these tips and our top picks for the best high waisted jeans for over 50, you'll be ready to rock this stylish and comfortable denim trend!
