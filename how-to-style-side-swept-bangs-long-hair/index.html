How To Style Side Swept Bangs Long Hair

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair and side swept bangs, you're in luck! This hairstyle is not only versatile, but it's also easy to style. In this article, we'll show you how to style side swept bangs long hair in a few simple steps.

  • How to Style Side Swept Bangs Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs Long Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Side Swept Bangs Long Hair

Side swept bangs can be styled in many different ways, depending on your mood and the occasion. Here are a few popular styles:

1. Side Swept Bangs with Loose Waves

This style is perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip them to the side.
  3. Curl the rest of your hair using a curling iron or wand.
  4. Once you've curled all of your hair, release your bangs and style them to the side.
  5. Finish off the look with hairspray.

2. Side Swept Bangs with a High Ponytail

This style is great for a more formal occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing all of your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip them to the side.
  3. Smooth out any bumps in your ponytail using a brush.
  4. Release your bangs and style them to the side.
  5. Finish off the look with hairspray.

3. Side Swept Bangs with a Bun

This style is perfect for a day when you don't want your hair in your face. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by brushing all of your hair back into a low bun.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip them to the side.
  3. Smooth out any bumps in your bun using a brush.
  4. Release your bangs and style them to the side.
  5. Finish off the look with hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style side swept bangs long hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip them to the side.
  3. Decide on the style you want to achieve (loose waves, high ponytail, or bun).
  4. Style your hair according to the style you chose.
  5. Release your bangs and style them to the side.
  6. Finish off the look with hairspray.

Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs Long Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your side swept bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heated styling tools.
  • Don't overuse hairspray, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your bangs.

FAQs

1. How do I keep my side swept bangs from falling in my face?

You can use bobby pins or hair clips to keep your bangs out of your face. You can also use hairspray to keep them in place.

2. How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs Long Hair

Here are a few tips for taking care of your side swept bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your side swept bangs long hair in a variety of different ways. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great!
