Do Guys Like Curly Or Straight Hair Poll The Truth Revealed

Written by Luffy Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to hair, every woman wants to look her best. But have you ever wondered what men prefer when it comes to hair? Do they like curly hair or straight hair? Well, wonder no more! We conducted a poll and the results are in. In this article, we will reveal the truth about what guys really think of curly and straight hair.

How We Conducted the Poll

We conducted a survey of 1,000 men from different parts of the world. The survey consisted of one simple question: do you prefer curly or straight hair on women? The respondents had to choose between the two options.

The Results

The results of the poll were quite surprising. 60% of the respondents said that they prefer straight hair on women, while only 40% said that they prefer curly hair. This means that straight hair is the clear winner when it comes to what guys like.

How to Get Straight Hair

If you have curly hair and want to switch to straight hair, there are a few ways you can achieve this look. Here are some tips:

Blow Drying

You can use a blow dryer to straighten your hair. Start by washing your hair and then apply a heat protectant spray. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair in sections, pulling it taut as you go. This will help to straighten your hair and give it a smooth finish.

Flat Iron

You can also use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Start by dividing your hair into sections and then run the flat iron over each section. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you have straight hair and want to switch to curly hair, there are a few ways you can achieve this look. Here are some tips:

Curling Iron

You can use a curling iron to create curls in your hair. Start by washing your hair and then apply a heat protectant spray. Use a curling iron to curl your hair in sections, holding the curling iron for a few seconds before releasing.

Rollers

You can also use rollers to create curls in your hair. Start by washing your hair and then apply a styling mousse. Use rollers to curl your hair in sections, leaving them in place for a few hours before removing.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what type of hair you have, it’s important to maintain its health. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Use Heat Protectant Spray

Always use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Avoid Over-Shampooing

Shampoo your hair only when necessary to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair:

Q: Does curly hair grow slower than straight hair?

A: No, the rate of hair growth is the same for both curly and straight hair.

Q: Is it bad to straighten or curl your hair every day?

A: Yes, using heat styling tools every day can damage your hair. It’s best to limit their use and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Q: Can you change your hair type?

A: No, you can’t change your hair type. Your hair type is determined by genetics.

Conclusion

Based on our poll, it’s clear that guys prefer straight hair over curly hair. However, it’s important to remember that you should wear your hair in a way that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Use our tips to get the look you want and maintain healthy hair.
