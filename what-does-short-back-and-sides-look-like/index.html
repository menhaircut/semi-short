What Does Short Back And Sides Look Like

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short back and sides is a classic men's haircut that has been around for decades. It's a style that's still popular today, and for good reason. It's a versatile cut that works well for most hair types, face shapes, and lifestyles. In this article, we'll go over what short back and sides looks like, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

Short back and sides is a haircut that involves trimming the hair short on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it's usually around 2-3 inches. The sides and back are usually cut with clippers, and a fade or taper is often incorporated to create a gradual transition from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Short back and sides is a clean, classic look that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. It's a versatile cut that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. It's also a great option for men who want to keep their hair short but don't want to go for a completely shaved look.

How to Get Short Back and Sides

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides haircut, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Make sure to bring a picture of the style you want, so your stylist can get a clear idea of what you're looking for.

During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. They will then use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. They may also use a razor to create a clean, sharp line around the edges of the haircut.

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist may apply some styling product to help you achieve the desired look. This could be a pomade, gel, or wax, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for.

Step by Step

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides haircut, here are the steps you can expect:

  1. Find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want.
  3. Sit in the chair and let the stylist get to work.
  4. They will use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  5. They will use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length.
  6. They may use a razor to create a clean, sharp line around the edges of the haircut.
  7. Once the cut is complete, they may apply styling product to help you achieve the desired look.

Tips

If you're thinking about getting a short back and sides haircut, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  • Bring a picture of the style you want to help your stylist get a clear idea of what you're looking for.
  • Consider the length of the hair on top - it can vary depending on your personal preference and face shape.
  • Think about the styling product you want to use to achieve the desired look.
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance - short back and sides requires frequent trims to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short back and sides:

Is short back and sides a good haircut for me?

Short back and sides is a versatile cut that works well for most hair types and face shapes. It's a clean, classic look that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish.

How often do I need to get a short back and sides haircut?

Short back and sides requires regular maintenance to keep the shape and length of the haircut. You should plan to get a trim every 3-4 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

What styling product should I use for short back and sides?

The styling product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you're going for. A pomade or wax can help create a sleek, polished look, while a gel can provide more hold and volume.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Short back and sides is a classic men's haircut that's still popular today. It's a versatile cut that works well for most hair types, face shapes, and lifestyles. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style or something more polished, short back and sides is a great option.
