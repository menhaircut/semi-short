Hairstyles To Suit Fat Round Faces Tips And Ideas

Written by Robby Dec 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face with chubby cheeks, you might struggle to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. But fear not! With the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best angles and create a look that's both chic and slimming. In this article, we'll share some of our top tips and ideas for hairstyles to suit fat round faces.

  • How to Choose a Hairstyle for a Fat Round Face
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair to Suit a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Slimming Hairstyle

How to Choose a Hairstyle for a Fat Round Face

The first step in finding a flattering hairstyle for a round face is to consider your face shape and features. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, soft jawline, and full cheeks. To create a more balanced look, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face, while also slimming your cheeks and jawline.

Some key factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle for a fat round face include:

  • Length: Longer hairstyles can add length to your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid short styles that end at the chin or jawline, as these can emphasize your roundness.
  • Layers: Layered hairstyles can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Focus on layers that start at the jawline or below.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Avoid blunt bangs, which can make your face appear rounder.
  • Texture: Adding waves or curls to your hair can create movement and dimension, which can help slim your face. Avoid straight styles, which can make your face appear wider.

Tips for Styling Your Hair to Suit a Round Face

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your features, it's important to style it in a way that accentuates your best angles. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Part your hair off-center: A deep side part can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Try parting your hair to one side and sweeping it across your forehead.
  • Add volume at the crown: Teasing the hair at the crown of your head can add height and elongate your face. Just be sure not to overdo it, as too much teasing can look dated.
  • Avoid tight updos: Tight buns or ponytails can emphasize your roundness. Instead, opt for looser styles that add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Experiment with accessories: Adding a headband, scarf, or hair clip can draw attention to your best features and add some personality to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Slimming Hairstyle

Ready to create a slimming hairstyle that flatters your round face? Follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds length and height to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept bob.
  2. Part your hair to one side and sweep it across your forehead. If you have bangs, style them to the side as well.
  3. Add volume at the crown by teasing your hair with a comb or brush. Be sure to tease gently, as too much teasing can damage your hair.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls to your hair. Focus on the ends of your hair to create movement and dimension.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles and Round Faces

Q: Can short hair work for a round face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a short style that adds height and volume to your hair. A short layered bob or pixie cut can be flattering for a round face if styled properly.

Q: Should I avoid center parts if I have a round face?

A: Center parts can emphasize the roundness of your face, so it's generally best to avoid them. However, if you prefer a center part, try adding volume at the crown to balance out your features.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but straight hair can make your face appear wider. If you prefer straight styles, try adding layers or waves to create dimension and movement.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Healthy Locks

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle to suit your fat round face and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect fit!
