What Unnatural Hair Color Is Best For My Cool Skin Tone

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair color and looking to switch things up? If you have a cool skin tone, there are certain colors that will complement your complexion better than others. In this article, we will discuss what unnatural hair color is best for your cool skin tone and provide tips on how to maintain your new color.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Cool skin tones have blue or pink undertones, and they look best with hair colors that have cool undertones as well. Some good options include:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Silver
  • Light blue
  • Lavender
  • Dark blue
  • Green

These colors will complement your cool skin tone and make your features pop. However, it's important to keep in mind that these colors may require more maintenance than traditional hair colors, so be prepared to invest time and money into upkeep.

How to

When it comes to coloring your hair an unnatural color, it's best to leave it to the professionals. They can help you choose the right shade for your skin tone and ensure that the color is applied evenly.

Before your appointment, it's important to do your research and find a stylist who has experience with unnatural hair colors. You can also bring in pictures of the color you want to achieve and ask for their advice on how to achieve it.

Step by Step

The process of coloring your hair an unnatural color will vary depending on the shade and your natural hair color. However, here is a general step-by-step guide:

  1. Consult with your stylist to choose the right shade for your skin tone.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it and avoiding any products that may interfere with the coloring process.
  3. Your stylist will apply the color to your hair, section by section.
  4. After the color has been applied, you will need to wait for it to process. This can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours depending on the color and your natural hair color.
  5. Your stylist will then rinse the color out of your hair and apply a conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking fresh.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as these can also cause the color to fade.
  • Consider using a color-depositing conditioner to refresh your color between appointments.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about unnatural hair colors and cool skin tones:

Can I dye my hair an unnatural color at home?

We do not recommend dyeing your hair an unnatural color at home. It's best to leave it to the professionals to ensure that the color is applied evenly and looks its best.

Will an unnatural hair color damage my hair?

Any type of hair color has the potential to damage your hair, especially if it involves bleaching. However, if you take proper care of your hair and use quality products, you can minimize damage.

How often will I need to touch up my hair color?

This will depend on the color and your natural hair color. Some colors may require touch-ups every few weeks, while others may last several months.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair after you dye it an unnatural color. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a weekly hair mask to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Overall, choosing an unnatural hair color is a fun way to switch up your look. By choosing the right shade for your skin tone and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a bold and beautiful new look.
