What Does A Short Layered Bob Haircut Look Like

Written by Petter Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, then a short layered bob might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut can be customized to suit your individual style and face shape, and it is suitable for both straight and curly hair. In this article, we will explain what a short layered bob looks like, how to get it, and how to care for it.

Description

A short layered bob is a haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It is typically cut at chin-length, although it can be shorter or longer depending on your preference. The layers in the haircut give it volume and texture, making it a great choice for thin or fine hair. The bob can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to messy and tousled.

How to Get a Short Layered Bob

If you're interested in getting a short layered bob, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced with this type of cut. You can bring in pictures of the style you like, and your stylist can help you determine the best length and layering for your face shape and hair texture.

During the haircut, your stylist will typically start by cutting the back of your hair shorter than the front. They will then add layers to the sides and top of your hair to create volume and texture. The length of the bob can be adjusted to suit your individual style, and you can choose to have bangs or leave them out.

Step by Step

Getting a short layered bob is a process that involves several steps. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you understand what to expect:

Step 1: Consultation

Your stylist will start by talking to you about the style you want and your hair texture. They may also ask you about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Your hair will be shampooed and conditioned to prepare it for the cut.

Step 3: Cutting the Back

Your stylist will start by cutting the back of your hair shorter than the front. This creates the classic bob shape.

Step 4: Adding Layers

Next, your stylist will add layers to the sides and top of your hair to create volume and texture. The length of the layers can be adjusted to suit your individual style.

Step 5: Styling

Finally, your stylist will style your hair to complete the look. They may use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create your desired style.

Tips

If you're considering a short layered bob, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a length and layering that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider adding bangs to frame your face.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob.

FAQ

Is a short layered bob suitable for all face shapes?

A short layered bob can be customized to suit different face shapes. However, it is important to choose a length and layering that flatters your individual features.

Can a short layered bob be styled in different ways?

Yes, a short layered bob can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to messy and tousled. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

How often should I get my short layered bob trimmed?

You should get your short layered bob trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered bob looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair mask.

A short layered bob is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your individual style and face shape. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your bob looking beautiful and healthy.
