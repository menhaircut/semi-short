How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers At Home

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barber shop or salon to get your hair cut? With a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can learn how to cut men's hair with clippers at home. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps and provide some helpful tips to make the process as smooth as possible.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Helpful Tips
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of clippers with various guard sizes, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting men's hair with clippers at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural texture and make it easier to cut. Use a towel to dry the hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Start with the longest guard size and work your way down. Use the comb to lift the hair and clip it with the clippers. If you're not sure which guard size to use, start with a larger one and work your way down until you find the right length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start at the sides and work your way to the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it with the clippers. Make sure to follow the natural hairline and use the mirror to check your progress.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Cut the hair to the desired length and use the comb to blend it with the sides and back.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline and Around the Ears

Use scissors or clippers without a guard to clean up the hair around the neckline and ears. Use the mirror to make sure everything is even and symmetrical.

Helpful Tips

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair with clippers at home:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress and make sure everything is even.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to take small amounts off at a time than to cut too much off at once.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the sides and back.
  • Ask the person whose hair you are cutting for feedback and adjust accordingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have when cutting men's hair with clippers at home:

Do I need any special training to cut hair with clippers?

No, you don't need any special training. With a little bit of practice and the right tools, anyone can learn how to cut men's hair with clippers at home.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back and mistakes can be fixed. If you're not sure how to fix it, ask a professional for help.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the person and their hair type. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've finished cutting the hair, make sure to clean up any loose hairs and give the person a quick rinse to remove any hair clippings. You can also apply some hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep the hair healthy and soft. Remember to clean and oil your clippers after each use to keep them in good condition.

Congratulations, you've successfully learned how to cut men's hair with clippers at home. With a little bit of practice and patience, you can save yourself time and money by cutting hair at home.
