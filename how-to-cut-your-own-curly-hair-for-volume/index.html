How To Cut Your Own Curly Hair For Volume

Written by William Nov 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your curly hair trimmed by a professional? Do you want to add more volume to your hair without spending a lot of time and money at a salon? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own curly hair for volume.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to cut, but with the right techniques, you can add more volume to your hair and save money on salon visits. The key to cutting curly hair is to avoid cutting too much at once and to cut your hair while it is dry. Cutting your hair when it is wet can lead to uneven results.

How To

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a wide-tooth comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry completely. Cutting your hair when it is wet can lead to uneven results.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Separate your hair into sections and clip each section up using hair clips.
  4. Starting with the bottom section, take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Cut off the ends of the twisted section, being careful not to cut too much at once.
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to cut evenly and not to cut too much at once.
  6. Once you have finished cutting the bottom section, move on to the middle section and repeat the process.
  7. Finally, cut the top section of your hair, again taking small sections and cutting evenly.
  8. After you have finished cutting your hair, shake your head to let your curls fall naturally.
  9. Use the wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your hair and remove any loose hairs.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting curly hair can be time-consuming, but the results are worth it.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to cut small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Don't cut your hair too often. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so cutting too often can lead to shorter hair than you intended.
  • Experiment with different cutting techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

  • Can I cut my curly hair when it is wet?

    • No, cutting your curly hair when it is wet can lead to uneven results. It is best to cut your hair when it is dry.

  • How often should I cut my curly hair?

    • It is recommended to cut curly hair every 3-6 months to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

  • Can I use regular scissors to cut my curly hair?

    • No, it is best to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your curly hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain healthy hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant when you do. Finally, be gentle when combing your hair to avoid causing breakage.

Now that you know how to cut your own curly hair for volume, you can save money and achieve the perfect look at home. Happy cutting!
