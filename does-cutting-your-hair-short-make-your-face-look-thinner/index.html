Does Cutting Your Hair Short Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Frank Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your hair short can be a daunting decision, especially if you're not sure how it will look on your face shape. One of the most common questions people ask is whether cutting your hair short makes your face look thinner. In this blog post, we'll explore the answer to this question along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Having a round or chubby face is not always a bad thing, but some people prefer a slimmer or sharper look. If you're one of those people, then you might want to consider cutting your hair short. Short hair can help create an illusion of a slimmer face by drawing attention to your cheekbones and jawline, making them more prominent.

How To

To achieve a slimmer-looking face with short hair, here's what you can do:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before deciding to cut your hair short, you need to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you choose the right haircut that will complement your features. Some of the most common face shapes include round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.

2. Choose the Right Haircut

Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right haircut that will flatter your features. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for a short bob with layers that frame your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you might want to choose a pixie cut that softens your features.

3. Add Layers

Adding layers to your short hair can help create a sense of movement and texture, making your face look slimmer. Layers can also help create volume, which can balance out a round or chubby face.

4. Play with Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to create a slimmer-looking face. Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face, while blunt bangs can add more structure to your features.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step tips to help you achieve a slimmer-looking face with short hair:

1. Consult with Your Stylist

Before cutting your hair short, it's important to consult with your stylist. Your stylist can help you choose the right haircut that will flatter your face shape and features. They can also give you tips on how to style your hair at home.

2. Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash it with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to style.

3. Use the Right Products

Using the right styling products can help create a slimmer-looking face. For example, volumizing mousse can help add volume to your hair, while a texturizing spray can help create movement and texture.

4. Style Your Hair

Once you've washed your hair and applied the right products, it's time to style it. Use a round brush to create volume and add curls or waves to create movement. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a slimmer-looking face with short hair:

- Avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead, as they can make your face look shorter and wider. - Experiment with different hairstyles and haircuts to find the one that works best for you. - Use a contouring powder or bronzer to create more definition on your cheekbones and jawline. - Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and create a slimmer-looking face.

FAQ

Q: Will cutting my hair short make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, cutting your hair short can create an illusion of a slimmer face by drawing attention to your cheekbones and jawline.

Q: What haircut should I choose for a round face?

A: For a round face, you might want to opt for a short bob with layers that frame your face.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair short can be a great way to achieve a slimmer-looking face, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. - Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

In conclusion, cutting your hair short can help create an illusion of a slimmer face, but it's important to choose the right haircut that flatters your features. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the look you desire.
