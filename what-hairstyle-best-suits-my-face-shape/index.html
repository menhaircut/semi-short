What Hairstyle Best Suits My Face Shape

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your face shape? It's time to put an end to that struggle and find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing what hairstyle best suits your face shape, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to maintain it.

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure what suits your face shape. It's essential to select a hairstyle that complements your face shape to enhance your overall look. Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the hairstyle that best suits you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six different face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's how you can determine your face shape:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is circular and has soft curves. Your forehead and jawline are usually the same width, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your face is angular and has a strong jawline. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are usually the same width.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your chin. Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin is pointed.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrow.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are usually the same width.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle that suits you. Here's a step by step guide to help you out:

  1. Round Face Shape - Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown. Long layers and side-swept bangs also work well for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and chin-length bobs.
  2. Square Face Shape - Soften your angular features with hairstyles that have soft layers and wispy bangs. Long bobs and asymmetrical cuts also work well for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs.
  3. Oval Face Shape - Almost any hairstyle works well for an oval face shape, so experiment with different styles.
  4. Heart Face Shape - Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown and have side-swept bangs. Long layers and chin-length bobs also work well for this face shape. Avoid short hairstyles and blunt cuts.
  5. Diamond Face Shape - Opt for hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin. Soft layers and side-swept bangs also work well for this face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the cheekbones.
  6. Oblong Face Shape - Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown and have side-swept bangs. Shoulder-length cuts and soft layers also work well for this face shape. Avoid long hairstyles and blunt cuts.

Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle best suits your face shape:

  • Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a round face shape?
  • A: Yes, you can. Opt for a short pixie cut or a bob with volume at the crown to add height to your face.
  • Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face shape?
  • A: Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs as they will accentuate your angular features.
  • Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a heart face shape?
  • A: Yes, you can. A middle part works well for a heart face shape as it creates the illusion of a narrower forehead.
  • Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have an oval face shape?
  • A: Yes, you can. An oval face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, including a high ponytail.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from maintaining your hairstyle, it's also essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and nourished:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as it can weigh down your hair.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make a significant difference in enhancing your overall look. Follow these tips and tricks to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and nourished.
