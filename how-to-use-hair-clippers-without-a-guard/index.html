How To Use Hair Clippers Without A Guard

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a fan of short haircuts, you may have thought about using hair clippers without a guard. This technique can give you a closer cut, but it requires some practice and patience. In this article, we will show you how to use hair clippers without a guard, step by step.

How to Use Hair Clippers Without a Guard

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You will also need a pair of hair clippers, a mirror, and a comb.

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Not all clippers are created equal. If you want to use them without a guard, you need a pair that is powerful enough to cut through thick hair. Look for clippers with a high motor speed and sharp blades.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Without a guard, your clippers will cut your hair very short. You need to decide on the length you want before you start. If you are not sure, start with a longer length and work your way down.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Clip the top sections of your hair out of the way.

Step 4: Start at the Nape

Begin at the nape of your neck, where your hair is the thickest. Hold the clippers flush against your head and move them upwards in a smooth, steady motion.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Once you have finished the back, move to the sides of your head. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and moving upwards.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Unclip the top sections of your hair and trim them using the same technique. Be careful not to cut too much off, as the top of your head is more visible.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head. If you missed any spots, go back and trim them.

Step 8: Clean Your Clippers

After you are finished, clean your clippers with a brush or a cloth. This will prevent them from getting clogged with hair and keep them in good condition.

Tips for Using Hair Clippers Without a Guard

- Start with a longer length and work your way down

- Use a comb to section your hair

- Hold the clippers flush against your head

- Move them in a smooth, steady motion

- Check your work with a mirror

FAQ:

What length should I start with?

It depends on how short you want your hair. If you are not sure, start with a longer length and work your way down.

Can I use regular clippers without a guard?

Yes, but make sure they are powerful enough to cut through thick hair. Look for clippers with a high motor speed and sharp blades.

How do I clean my clippers?

Use a brush or a cloth to remove hair from the blades. You can also use a small amount of oil to lubricate them.

Hair Care

Using hair clippers without a guard can be a great way to get a close, even cut. However, it is important to take care of your hair afterwards. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to use hair clippers without a guard, you can experiment with different styles and lengths. Remember to start with a longer length and work your way down, and to check your work with a mirror. With practice, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
