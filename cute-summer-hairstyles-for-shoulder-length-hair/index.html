Cute Summer Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Summer is the perfect time to experiment with different hairstyles, especially if you have shoulder length hair. Not too short and not too long, shoulder length hair is versatile and allows for a variety of styles. Whether you're going to the beach or attending a summer party, there's a cute summer hairstyle that will suit your shoulder length hair. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the latest cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair, as well as tips on how to achieve them.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a great length for summer, as it's not too short and not too long. It's versatile, meaning you can create a variety of hairstyles for different occasions. From beachy waves to braids, there are plenty of cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair.

How to

If you're wondering how to achieve cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair, it's easier than you might think. Many styles can be achieved with a few simple tools, such as a curling iron, hair straightener, or hair elastics. You can also add accessories such as headbands or hair clips to add an extra touch of style.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a classic summer hairstyle that looks great on shoulder length hair. To achieve this look, start by applying a heat protectant to your hair. Then, use a curling iron to curl your hair in sections, wrapping the hair around the iron away from your face. Once all the hair is curled, run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural, beachy look.

Braids

Braids are a great summer hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. For shoulder length hair, try a side braid or a fishtail braid. To create a side braid, start by parting your hair to one side. Then, braid your hair to one side, securing the end with a hair elastic. To create a fishtail braid, divide your hair into two sections. Take a small section from the left side and cross it over to the right side. Then, take a small section from the right side and cross it over to the left side. Continue crossing sections over until you reach the end of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What are some cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

Some cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair include beachy waves, braids, low ponytails, and messy buns.

Can I create cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair without using heat styling tools?

Yes, you can create cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair without using heat styling tools. For example, you can create beachy waves by braiding your hair while it's damp and letting it dry naturally.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair during the summer months, as the sun, saltwater, and chlorine can all cause damage. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

Cute summer hairstyles for shoulder length hair are easy to achieve and can help you look and feel your best during the summer months. Whether you're going to a party or spending the day at the beach, there's a cute summer hairstyle that will suit your shoulder length hair. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can create a variety of styles that will keep you looking stylish and cool all summer long.
