What Size Curling Iron For Beach Waves

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve effortless, beachy waves, using a curling iron is a great way to do it. But the question is, what size curling iron should you use?

Description

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that can be achieved with a curling iron. This hairstyle is great for both casual and formal occasions and can be worn by people with different hair types and lengths. However, to achieve the perfect beach waves, it's essential to use the right size curling iron.

How To

The first step in achieving beach waves is to select the right size curling iron. A curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 1.5 inches is ideal for creating loose, beachy waves. This size of the barrel is perfect for those who have medium to long hair.

If you have shorter hair, you should opt for a curling iron with a smaller barrel size between 0.5 and 1 inches. This size of the barrel will help you create tighter curls that will eventually loosen up and give you the perfect beachy waves.

Step by Step

Now that you have selected the right curling iron, it's time to get started. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's essential to prepare it. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair. Once you've washed it, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier for you to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of your hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold it in place for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, finish the look with a light hold hairspray. This will help your beach waves last longer.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray before curling your hair to add texture and volume.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose curls will give you a more natural beachy look.
  • After curling your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what size curling iron to use for beach waves:

What size curling iron should I use for short hair?

If you have short hair, you should use a curling iron with a barrel size between 0.5 and 1 inches. This size of the barrel will help you create tighter curls that will eventually loosen up and give you the perfect beachy waves.

What size curling iron should I use for long hair?

If you have long hair, you should use a curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 1.5 inches. This size of the barrel is perfect for creating loose, beachy waves.

Can I achieve beach waves with a straightener?

Yes, you can achieve beach waves with a straightener. To do this, twist your hair and run the straightener over it. This will create loose, beachy waves.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what size curling iron to use for beach waves, it's time to get started. Follow these tips and steps, and you'll have perfect beach waves in no time.
