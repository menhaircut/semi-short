Best Haircut For Round Face Medium Length Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut for your round face and medium length hair? Look no further! In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyle options that flatter a round face shape and work well with medium length hair. From bobs to layers, we’ve got you covered. Let’s dive in!

Description

A round face shape is characterized by wider cheekbones and a rounded chin. The goal of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles, which can help to balance out the face shape. Medium length hair is a great option for those who want versatility with their hair. It’s long enough to style in a variety of ways, but not so long that it becomes difficult to manage.

How To

The first step in finding the best haircut for your round face shape and medium length hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best length and style based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. Here are some popular options to consider:

Bob Haircut

The classic bob is a great option for those with a round face and medium length hair. This haircut can be customized to fit your unique features, with options for a blunt cut or layers. A bob that falls just below the chin can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of angles.

Layered Haircut

Layers are a great way to add texture and movement to medium length hair. For those with a round face, layers that start just below the chin can help to create the illusion of length. This haircut can be styled straight or with loose waves for a chic look.

Long Pixie Cut

The long pixie cut is a popular option for those who want a shorter hairstyle. This cut features longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides and back. It can be styled with a side part or swept back for a more edgy look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps you can follow to get the best haircut for your round face and medium length hair:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style.
  3. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for your round face and medium length hair:

  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting options to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face and medium length hair?

A: Yes! Bangs can still work with a round face shape and medium length hair. Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer in the front to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid blunt cuts if I have a round face?

A: It depends on your personal preferences and the advice of your stylist. A blunt cut can work well with a round face shape if it falls just below the chin and is angled slightly.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and options, you’re well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your round face and medium length hair. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that suits you best. Good luck!
