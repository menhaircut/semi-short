Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top Ladies The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and edgy hairstyle, then hairstyles with short sides and long tops for ladies may be just what you need. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It's a versatile look that can be adapted to suit your individual style, and it's perfect for women who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this trend and offer some tips on how to achieve the perfect short sides long top look.

Description

Hairstyles with short sides and long tops for ladies are characterized by shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This style can be worn with a variety of hair textures, from straight to curly, and it can be adapted to suit any face shape. The short sides can be tapered or shaved, while the top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, tousled, or even braided.

How to Achieve the Look

There are a few different ways to achieve the short sides long top look for ladies. One option is to have your hair cut by a professional stylist. They will be able to create the perfect shape and texture for your face and hair type. Alternatively, you can try cutting your hair at home using clippers or scissors. This can be a more affordable option, but it's important to be careful and take your time to ensure that you achieve the desired result.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the short sides long top look:

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.

2. Use a comb to create a parting on the top of your head.

3. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.

4. Blend the sides into the top using scissors or clippers.

5. Style the top of your hair using your fingers, a brush, or a comb to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short sides long top look:

- Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

- Use hair products such as wax, pomade, or gel to add texture and hold to your hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension.

- If you're not sure about cutting your hair short, start with a longer style and gradually work your way up to a shorter look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short sides long top hairstyles for ladies:

Q: Will this style suit me?

A: Short sides long top hairstyles can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type, so it's worth giving it a try.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways?

A: Yes, the beauty of this hairstyle is that it's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Q: How often will I need to get my hair cut?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the shape of your style. Typically, you'll need to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and advice, you should be able to achieve the perfect short sides long top hairstyle for ladies. Whether you want to make a bold statement or simply try something new, this look is sure to turn heads.
