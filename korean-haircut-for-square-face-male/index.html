Korean Haircut For Square Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a square-faced male looking for a new hairstyle? Look no further than the Korean haircut! Korean hairstyles have been gaining popularity in recent years due to their stylish and unique designs. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of getting a Korean haircut that complements your square face shape. Let's get started!

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and angular features. To balance out these features, a Korean haircut can soften the angles and add volume to the top of the head. This type of haircut typically involves longer layers on top and shorter sides, resulting in a tapered look that flatters the face shape.

How To

The first step in getting a Korean haircut for a square face male is to find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in this type of cut. Once you've found a stylist, discuss your hair goals and preferences with them. Bring in pictures of Korean hairstyles that you like and ask for their professional opinion on what would work best for your face shape.

From there, the stylist will typically start by cutting the hair shorter on the sides and gradually longer towards the top. This will create a tapered effect that adds volume to the top of the head. They may also add layers to the top of the hair to create texture and movement.

Once the haircut is complete, the stylist will likely style your hair with products such as pomade or wax. This will help to hold the shape of the cut and add definition to the hair.

Step by Step

1. Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in Korean haircuts for square-faced males.

2. Discuss your hair goals and preferences with the stylist.

3. Bring in pictures of Korean hairstyles that you like.

4. The stylist will typically start by cutting the hair shorter on the sides and gradually longer towards the top.

5. They may also add layers to the top of the hair to create texture and movement.

6. Once the haircut is complete, the stylist will likely style your hair with products such as pomade or wax.

Tips

- Make sure to find a hairstylist who specializes in Korean haircuts for square-faced males.

- Bring in pictures of Korean hairstyles that you like to help guide the stylist.

- Consider adding some color or highlights to add depth and dimension to the hair.

FAQ

What if I don't like my Korean haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, speak to your stylist about what changes can be made. They may be able to make adjustments to the cut or offer suggestions for styling the hair differently.

How often do I need to get a Korean haircut?

This can vary depending on the individual and their hair growth rate. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the cut.

What products should I use to style my Korean haircut?

Pomade or wax are popular choices for styling Korean haircuts. These products provide hold and definition without weighing down the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your Korean haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using products that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow-drying or straightening, as this can damage the hair and affect the style of the cut.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and flattering Korean haircut for your square face shape. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
