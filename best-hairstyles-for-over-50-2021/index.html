Best Hairstyles For Over 50 2021 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that's age-appropriate, but still stylish and easy to manage. Fortunately, there are plenty of options out there to choose from. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hairstyles for over 50 in 2021, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, you want to find a style that flatters your face shape and complements your features. You also want to think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling.

Some of the best hairstyles for over 50 in 2021 include:

Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a great option for women over 50. This style is easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with wispy bangs. Use a round brush to blow-dry and style.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding some long layers to give your locks some movement and texture. This style works well for all face shapes and can be styled straight or with loose waves. Use a curling iron or flat iron to achieve your desired look.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice, but it can be incredibly flattering for women over 50. This style is low-maintenance and can be styled with a bit of texturizing cream for added volume and texture. Ask your stylist for a choppy, layered pixie cut for a modern and edgy look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen your hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling some of the best hairstyles for over 50 in 2021:

Bob with Bangs

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Add a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front.
  4. When you get to the front, use the brush to create a slight curl under.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Using a curling iron or flat iron, create loose waves throughout your hair.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing cream to your hair.
  3. Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create volume and texture.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you know how to style your hair, it's important to keep it looking its best between salon visits. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or damaged.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

What's the best hairstyle for thin, fine hair?

A short bob or pixie cut can be a great option for women with thin, fine hair. These styles can help to add volume and texture to your locks.

What's the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered bob or a long, side-swept fringe can be flattering for women with round faces. These styles help to elongate the face and create a more angular look.

Hair Care Tips for Over 50

As we age, our hair can become more dry and brittle. Here are some hair care tips for women over 50:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help moisturize and nourish your locks.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Consider taking a hair, skin, and nail supplement to help support healthy hair growth.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, women over 50 can look and feel their best. Whether you prefer a sleek bob or an edgy pixie cut, there's a style out there for everyone. Use these tips and tricks to achieve your desired look and keep your locks looking fabulous.
