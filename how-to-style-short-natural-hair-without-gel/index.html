How To Style Short Natural Hair Without Gel

Written by Bardi Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Styling short natural hair can be a challenge, especially when you don't want to use gel. Whether you want to avoid the crunchiness that comes with gel or you simply don't have any on hand, there are plenty of ways to style short natural hair without it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling short natural hair without gel.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Short Natural Hair Without Gel
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair Without Gel
  4. FAQs About Styling Short Natural Hair Without Gel
  5. Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

How to Style Short Natural Hair Without Gel

When it comes to styling short natural hair without gel, there are a few things you can do to make the process easier. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean hair: Make sure your hair is clean and freshly washed before you start styling. This will help your hair hold its shape better.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner: A leave-in conditioner can help moisturize your hair and make it easier to style.
  • Use a natural oil: A natural oil like coconut oil or jojoba oil can help add shine and hold to your hair.
  • Use a styling cream: A styling cream can help define your curls and add hold without the crunchiness of gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling short natural hair without gel:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Apply a natural oil to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use a styling cream to define your curls and add hold.
  5. Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a natural oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair Without Gel

Here are some additional tips for styling short natural hair without gel:

  • Experiment with different products: Everyone's hair is different, so it may take some trial and error to find the right products for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers: Your fingers can be a great styling tool when it comes to short natural hair.
  • Try different styling techniques: You can experiment with twist-outs, braid-outs, and other techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs About Styling Short Natural Hair Without Gel

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short natural hair without gel:

Can I use hairspray instead of gel?

Yes, you can use hairspray instead of gel. However, hairspray can be drying to your hair, so it's best to use it sparingly.

What's the best natural oil to use for styling short natural hair?

Coconut oil, jojoba oil, and argan oil are all great options for styling short natural hair.

How often should I wash my short natural hair?

It's best to wash your short natural hair once a week to avoid drying it out.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short natural hair:

  • Keep your hair moisturized: Moisture is key when it comes to natural hair, so make sure to use a leave-in conditioner and natural oils to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair at night: Wear a satin or silk bonnet or sleep on a satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat can be damaging to natural hair, so try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short natural hair without gel in no time. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and always prioritize hair care to keep your hair healthy and strong.
