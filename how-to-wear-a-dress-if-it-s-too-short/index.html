How To Wear A Dress If It s Too Short The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have a favorite dress that you love to wear, but it's just a little too short? Don't worry, you don't have to give up on your favorite dress just yet. With a few simple styling tricks and hacks, you can easily make your short dress look chic and stylish. In this post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to wear a dress if it's too short.

Description

Wearing a short dress can be a tricky business, especially if you don't want to look too revealing. But with the right styling techniques, you can make your dress look modest and stylish at the same time. In this post, we will share some tips on how to wear a dress if it's too short, so you can confidently rock your favorite dress without feeling self-conscious.

How to Wear a Dress If It's Too Short:

1. Wear Tights or Leggings

If your dress is too short, wearing tights or leggings is an easy way to make it look more modest. Choose sheer or opaque tights, depending on the occasion, and pair them with your dress for a chic and stylish look. Leggings can also be worn under your dress for added coverage, and you can even mix and match different patterns and textures to create a unique look.

2. Layer with a Long Top or Cardigan

Another way to make your dress look longer is to layer it with a long top or cardigan. This works especially well with sleeveless dresses, as you can wear a long-sleeved top underneath for added coverage. A long cardigan can also be worn over your dress, creating a stylish and comfortable look that's perfect for cooler weather.

3. Add a Belt

If your dress is too short, adding a belt can help to create the illusion of a longer hemline. Choose a wide belt to cinch your waist, and position it at the narrowest part of your torso. This will help to elongate your legs and make your dress look longer.

4. Wear Knee-High Boots

Knee-high boots are a perfect way to make your dress look longer, as they cover up a large portion of your legs. Choose a pair of boots that match the color of your dress, or go for a contrasting color for a bold look. Knee-high boots are also great for cooler weather, as they keep your legs warm and cozy.

5. Try a Pencil Skirt

If your dress is too short, you can also wear it as a top with a pencil skirt. Choose a skirt that's the same color as your dress, or go for a contrasting color for a bold look. This will add length to your outfit and create a chic and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion.

Step by Step: How to Wear a Dress If It's Too Short

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a dress if it's too short:

Step 1: Choose Your Dress

Choose a dress that you love, but that's just a little too short. Make sure the dress is in good condition and fits you well.

Step 2: Wear Tights or Leggings

Put on a pair of tights or leggings to add coverage and length to your dress.

Step 3: Layer with a Long Top or Cardigan

Layer your dress with a long top or cardigan to create a stylish and comfortable look.

Step 4: Add a Belt

Add a belt to cinch your waist and create the illusion of a longer hemline.

Step 5: Wear Knee-High Boots

Wear knee-high boots to cover up a large portion of your legs and add length to your outfit.

Tips for Wearing a Dress If It's Too Short

Here are some tips for wearing a dress if it's too short:

1. Keep it Simple

When wearing a short dress, keep the rest of your outfit simple to avoid looking too busy or overwhelming. Choose simple accessories and shoes to let your dress take center stage.

2. Know Your Body Type

Choose a dress that flatters your body type, and use styling tricks to accentuate your best features. For example, if you have long legs, wear knee-high boots to show them off.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and trends to find what works best for you. Try different layering techniques, accessories, and shoes to create a unique look that's all your own.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a formal event?

A: It depends on the dress code of the event. If the event is black-tie or formal, it's best to wear a longer dress or gown. If the dress code is semi-formal, you can wear a shorter dress as long as it's dressy and appropriate for the occasion.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a short dress?

A: Yes, sandals can be worn with a short dress for a casual and comfortable look. Choose sandals that match the style and color of your dress for a cohesive look.

Q: Can I wear a short dress in the winter?

A: Yes, you can wear a short dress in the winter by layering it with tights, boots, and a warm coat. Choose fabrics that are warm and cozy, such as wool or cashmere, and accessorize with a scarf and gloves to stay warm.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a short dress, your hair can be an important part of your overall look. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose a Hairstyle that Complements Your Dress

Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and enhances your best features. For example, if you're wearing a high-neck dress, wear your hair up to show off your neckline.

2. Use Heat Protectant Products

If you're using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help your hair look its best and complement your short dress.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a short dress without feeling self-conscious. Whether you're dressing for a casual or formal occasion, you can make your dress look chic and stylish by using these simple techniques. So go ahead and rock that short dress with confidence!
