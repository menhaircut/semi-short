Best Hair Dryer Brush For Short Hair Uk

Written by Eveline Sep 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best hair dryer brush for short hair UK. If you are looking for an efficient and time-saving way to style your short hair, then a hair dryer brush is the perfect tool for you. It not only dries your hair but also adds volume and texture to it. We have researched and tested various hair dryer brushes available in the UK market and have shortlisted the top picks for you. So, let's get started.

Description

A hair dryer brush is a combination of a hairbrush and a blow dryer. It has bristles on the surface that help detangle and style your hair while the hot air from the dryer dries your hair quickly. It comes in different shapes and sizes, and you can choose the one that suits your hair type and length.

How to Choose the Best Hair Dryer Brush for Short Hair UK

Choosing the right hair dryer brush for your short hair can be overwhelming. Here are some factors that you should consider before making a purchase:

Bristles

The bristles of the hair dryer brush should be soft and flexible to prevent hair breakage. For short hair, you can opt for shorter bristles as they will be easier to manipulate and style.

Size

The size of the hair dryer brush should be proportional to your hair length. For short hair, a smaller brush will be more manageable and will give you more control over styling.

Heat Settings

Make sure that the hair dryer brush has multiple heat settings to cater to different hair types. For short hair, low or medium heat will be sufficient to dry and style your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Use a Hair Dryer Brush for Short Hair UK

Here's how you can use a hair dryer brush to style your short hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Pat dry your hair with a towel to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent heat damage.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 4: Start Styling

Take a section of your hair and place the hair dryer brush at the roots. Gently glide the brush down to the ends while rotating it inwards or outwards depending on the style you want. Repeat this process for all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Using a Hair Dryer Brush for Short Hair UK

Here are some tips that will help you get the most out of your hair dryer brush:

Tip 1: Use a Round Brush

A round brush will give you more volume and bounce than a flat brush. Choose a brush with a smaller diameter for short hair.

Tip 2: Start with Low Heat

Always start with low heat and increase the temperature gradually if needed. High heat can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Tip 3: Dry Your Hair Completely

Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you finish styling. Wet hair can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Q1. Can I use a hair dryer brush on wet hair?

A1. No, it is not recommended to use a hair dryer brush on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Always pat dry your hair with a towel before using a hair dryer brush.

Q2. Can I use a hair dryer brush on all hair types?

A2. Yes, you can use a hair dryer brush on all hair types. However, make sure to choose the right brush and heat setting according to your hair type.

Q3. How often should I clean my hair dryer brush?

A3. You should clean your hair dryer brush at least once a month to remove any buildup of hair products and dirt.

Hair Care for Short Hair UK

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful short hair:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair tends to grow faster than long hair, so it is essential to get regular trims to maintain the shape and style.

Tip 2: Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that does not contain harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 3: Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair at least once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.

In conclusion, a hair dryer brush is a versatile tool that can help you achieve salon-quality hairstyles at home. Choose the best hair dryer brush for short hair UK that suits your hair type and length, and follow the tips and steps mentioned in this article for the best results.
