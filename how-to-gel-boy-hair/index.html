How To Gel Boy Hair A Complete Guide For 2023

Gel is a classic hair styling product that is used by both men and women to achieve a variety of hairstyles. For boys, gel can be used to create a slick, polished look or a messy, textured style. In this guide, we'll show you how to gel boy hair step by step, with tips and tricks for achieving the perfect hairstyle every time.

  • How to Gel Boy Hair

How to Gel Boy Hair

Gel is a versatile hair styling product that can be used on wet or dry hair. It is perfect for creating sleek, polished styles or textured, messy looks. Here are the steps to follow when using gel to style boy hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Gel

When it comes to choosing a gel for boy hair, there are a few things to consider. Look for a gel that:

  • Is specifically formulated for men's hair
  • Provides a strong hold
  • Doesn't leave a greasy residue
  • Is easy to wash out

Some popular brands of gel for boy hair include American Crew, Axe, and Redken.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying gel to your boy hair, make sure it is clean and dry. You can either wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, or simply rinse it with water. Towel dry your hair so that it is slightly damp but not dripping wet.

Step 3: Apply the Gel

Squeeze a small amount of gel onto your fingertips and rub them together. Then, apply the gel to your boy hair using your fingers or a comb. Start at the roots and work your way up to the tips, making sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the gel is applied, you can style your boy hair in any way you like. Use a comb or your fingers to create a slick, polished look, or use your fingers to create a messy, textured style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray (Optional)

If you want your boy hair to stay in place all day, you can finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will provide extra hold and help to prevent your hair from frizzing or falling out of place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide for how to gel boy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Squeeze a small amount of gel onto your fingertips
  3. Rub your fingertips together to distribute the gel
  4. Apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips
  5. Distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair using your fingers or a comb
  6. Style your hair in any way you like, using a comb or your fingers
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray (optional)

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect boy hair style with gel:

  • Use a small amount of gel to avoid a greasy, weighed-down look
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best
  • Use a comb for a sleek, polished look, and your fingers for a messy, textured style
  • Apply gel to the front of your hairline to create a trendy "wet look"
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to keep your hair in place all day

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to gel boy hair:

Q: Can gel damage my hair?

A: Gel is generally safe for all hair types, but using too much or applying it too frequently can dry out your hair and lead to breakage. To avoid damage, be sure to wash your hair regularly and avoid using gel every day.

Q: How do I wash gel out of my hair?

A: Gel is water soluble, which means it can be easily washed out of your hair with water and shampoo. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all traces of gel.

Q: Can I use gel on curly hair?

A: Yes, gel can be used on curly hair to help define curls and reduce frizz. Use a small amount of gel and scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a textured, natural look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your boy hair looking healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

Now that you know how to gel boy hair, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best. Remember to choose the right gel for your hair type, prepare your hair properly, and style with confidence. With these tips and tricks, you'll have perfectly styled boy hair in no time!
