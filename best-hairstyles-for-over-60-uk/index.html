Best Hairstyles For Over 60 Uk

As we age, our hair changes, and sometimes it can be difficult to find a hairstyle that suits us. However, there are plenty of options available for women over 60 in the UK. In this article, we will share some of the best hairstyles for over 60 UK, along with tips on how to achieve them and how to care for your hair.

  • Short Hairstyles
  • Medium Hairstyles

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance look. A pixie cut is a popular choice, as it requires minimal styling and suits most face shapes. You can also opt for a bob or a layered cut to add texture and volume to your hair.

If you have thinning hair, a short hairstyle can help to make it appear fuller. A layered cut or a short shaggy style can add volume and texture to your hair. Alternatively, you can opt for a classic bob, which is always in style and can be tailored to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to Achieve a Short Hairstyle

To achieve a short hairstyle, visit a reputable hairdresser who specializes in cutting short hair. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and show you how to style it at home.

When styling a short hairstyle, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. You can also use a styling wax or pomade to add definition and separation to your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium length hairstyles are a great option for women over 60 who want a versatile look. A shoulder-length bob or a layered cut can help to frame your face and add volume to your hair.

If you have curly or wavy hair, a medium length cut can help to enhance your natural texture. Ask your hairdresser to add layers to your hair to create movement and volume.

How to Achieve a Medium Hairstyle

To achieve a medium length hairstyle, visit a reputable hairdresser who specializes in cutting medium length hair. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and show you how to style it at home.

When styling a medium length hairstyle, use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and hold. You can also use a curling or straightening iron to create waves or sleekness, depending on your preference. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be a great option for women over 60 who have thick hair or who want a more dramatic look. A long layered cut can help to add movement and texture to your hair, while a blunt cut can create a sleek and polished look.

If you have thinning hair, a long hairstyle may not be the best option, as it can make your hair appear flat and lifeless. However, if you have thick hair, a long hairstyle can be a great way to show off your luscious locks.

How to Achieve a Long Hairstyle

To achieve a long hairstyle, visit a reputable hairdresser who specializes in cutting long hair. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and show you how to style it at home.

When styling a long hairstyle, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. You can also use a curling or straightening iron to create waves or sleekness, depending on your preference. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hair Care Tips

As we age, our hair can become drier and more brittle, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for thinning hair?

A short layered cut or a bob can help to add volume and texture to thinning hair. Avoid long hairstyles, as they can make your hair appear flat and lifeless.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered cut or a bob can help to slim down a round face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear wider.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can feel confident and stylish at any age. Remember to take good care of your hair, and always consult with a reputable hairdresser before making any drastic changes to your hairstyle.
