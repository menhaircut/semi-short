Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces How To Look Your Best

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round or fat face, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. However, with the right long hairstyle, you can accentuate your features and look your best. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for finding the perfect long hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Having a fat face doesn't mean you can't look beautiful. By choosing the right long hairstyle, you can highlight your best features and downplay your least favorite. Not all long hairstyles will work for a round or fat face, but there are plenty of options that will. The key is to find a style that elongates your face and draws attention to your best features.

How to

When it comes to choosing a long hairstyle for a fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a style that adds height and length. If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with layers and waves. If you have an oval face, you're in luck as most hairstyles will work for you!

Choose the Right Length

When it comes to length, longer hairstyles tend to work best for fat faces. Long layers can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.

Consider Bangs

Adding bangs to your long hairstyle can help to balance out your face and draw attention to your eyes. Side-swept bangs work well for round faces, while blunt bangs can work for square faces.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating a long hairstyle for a fat face:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing your hair down.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before styling your hair, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from hot tools.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl sections of your hair away from your face. Start at the mid-lengths of your hair and work your way down. Leave the ends straight to avoid creating too much volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your long hairstyle by spritzing it with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a long hairstyle for a fat face:

  • Opt for loose waves instead of tight curls to avoid adding too much volume to your face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.
  • Try a half-up, half-down hairstyle to add height to the top of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek and straight, as they can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, too sleek and straight, or too voluminous. These styles can all make your face appear wider.

What long hairstyles work best for a round face?

Long layers, loose waves, and side-swept bangs all work well for round faces. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous.

Hair Care

To keep your long hairstyle looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools, and avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent dryness. Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing your hair down, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
