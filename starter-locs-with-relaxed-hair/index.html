Starter Locs With Relaxed Hair Everything You Need To Know

Are you considering starting locs but have relaxed hair? Don't worry, it's possible! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about starter locs with relaxed hair, including how to do it, tips, and hair care.

Description

Starter locs with relaxed hair are a popular hairstyle among those looking for a low-maintenance, natural look. However, starting locs with relaxed hair can be challenging and requires patience, dedication, and proper hair care.

How To Start Locs with Relaxed Hair

Before you start locs with relaxed hair, it's important to have healthy hair. You should also consider the length of your hair, as starter locs require at least 2-3 inches of hair. Here are the steps to start locs with relaxed hair:

Step-By-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Part your hair into small sections using a rat-tail comb.
  3. Apply a locking gel or wax to each section of hair.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly, starting from the root and working your way down to the end.
  5. Secure the end of each twist with a small rubber band or hair clip.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each section of hair.
  7. Cover your hair with a silk or satin scarf to protect it while you sleep.
  8. Avoid washing your hair for the first 4-6 weeks to allow the locs to form.

Tips for Starting Locs with Relaxed Hair

Starting locs with relaxed hair can be challenging, but here are some tips to help you along the way:

  • Be patient. It takes time for locs to form, especially with relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and inhibit loc formation.
  • Keep your hair moisturized to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and slow down loc formation.
  • Get regular trims to remove any split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about starter locs with relaxed hair:

Can I start locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, it's possible to start locs with relaxed hair. However, it requires patience, dedication, and proper hair care.

How long does it take for locs to form with relaxed hair?

It can take several months for locs to form with relaxed hair, depending on your hair type and how well you care for your hair.

What products should I use to start locs with relaxed hair?

You should use a locking gel or wax to start locs with relaxed hair. Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and inhibit loc formation.

Hair Care for Starter Locs with Relaxed Hair

Proper hair care is essential for starter locs with relaxed hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but avoid using harsh shampoos or conditioners.
  • Keep your hair moisturized using natural oils or a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and slow down loc formation.
  • Get regular trims to remove any split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and inhibit loc formation.

Starting locs with relaxed hair can be challenging, but with patience, dedication, and proper hair care, you can achieve the natural, low-maintenance look you desire. Remember to be patient, avoid heavy products, and keep your hair moisturized to ensure healthy, beautiful locs.
