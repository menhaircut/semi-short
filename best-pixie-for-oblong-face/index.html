The Best Pixie For Oblong Face Your Ultimate Guide

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the perfect haircut can be a bit of a challenge. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this guide, we will walk you through the best pixie for oblong face shape, how to style it, and everything you need to know about hair care. So, let's get started!

Best Pixie for Oblong Face

The best pixie for oblong face shape is one that adds volume and width to your face. This means you should go for a pixie cut that has layers and texture. Avoid blunt cuts that will make your face look longer.

Here are some of the best pixie cuts for oblong face shape:

1. Pixie Cut with Bangs

A pixie cut with bangs is a great option for oblong faces as it adds width to the forehead and draws attention away from the length of your face. The bangs should be wispy and textured.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut is perfect for oblong faces as it adds volume and movement to your hair. The layers should be choppy and textured to create a more natural look.

3. Side-Swept Pixie Cut

A side-swept pixie cut is another great option for oblong faces. The side-swept fringe adds volume to the forehead and creates a more balanced look. The rest of the hair should be layered and textured.

How to Style Your Pixie Cut

Styling your pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the ends of your hair to create definition and texture.
  5. Finish off with hairspray to set your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair into a pixie cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides.
  3. Start with the back section and cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Move on to the sides and cut the hair in a diagonal direction towards the back.
  5. Finally, cut the top section to the desired length and add layers and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining your pixie cut is easy if you follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Invest in a good quality hair wax or pomade to create texture and definition.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

1. Will a pixie cut suit an oblong face?

Yes, a pixie cut can look great on an oblong face shape. Just make sure to go for a cut that adds volume and width to your face.

2. Can I style my pixie cut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your pixie cut in different ways by using hair accessories, changing your parting, or adding volume and texture.

3. How often should I wash my hair with a pixie cut?

You can wash your hair with a pixie cut every two to three days, depending on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cut

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and shiny pixie cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.

That's it! Now you know everything there is to know about the best pixie for oblong face shape, how to style it, and how to take care of your hair. Go ahead and try out one of these pixie cuts and see how it transforms your look!
