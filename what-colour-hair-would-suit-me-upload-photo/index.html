What Colour Hair Would Suit Me Upload Photo A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not quite sure which shade will suit you best. Luckily, there's a simple solution: take a photo of yourself and upload it to a virtual hair color app. These apps use advanced technology to help you visualize different hair colors on your actual face, making it easier than ever to find your perfect shade.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in shades of red, gold, or copper. Cool undertones look great with ash, platinum, or violet shades. Neutral undertones can pull off just about any color.

To determine your skin tone, simply look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear somewhere in between, you likely have neutral undertones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Uploading Your Photo

Once you know your skin tone, it's time to upload your photo to a virtual hair color app. Follow these steps:

  1. Choose a virtual hair color app that suits your needs. Some popular options include L'Oreal's Style My Hair, Matrix's Virtual Hair Color Try-On, and Schwarzkopf's Hair Color App.
  2. Take a well-lit photo of yourself, making sure your hair is pulled back and your face is in focus.
  3. Upload the photo to the app and follow the prompts to get started.
  4. Browse through different hair color options and select the shades you'd like to try on.
  5. Adjust the color to suit your preferences and save the photo for future reference.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Color

Now that you've uploaded your photo and tried on different hair colors, how do you know which one is the best fit? Here are some tips:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're not willing to spend hours in the salon every few weeks, opt for a low-maintenance hair color that will grow out gracefully.
  • Think about your wardrobe. Your hair color should complement your clothing, not clash with it.
  • Take your eye color into account. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop, while others can make them appear dull.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. If you've always been a blonde, try going brunette for a change. You might be surprised at how much you love it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a virtual hair color app on my phone?

Yes, most virtual hair color apps are available for download on both iOS and Android devices.

Will the hair color in the app look exactly the same in real life?

No, the hair color in the app is just an approximation. Factors like lighting and your natural hair color can affect how the final result looks.

What if I don't like the hair color I chose?

No problem! Simply go back to the app and try on a different color until you find one you love.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

Once you've found your perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair and cause color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your color looking fresh.

Now that you know how to choose the right hair color for your skin tone and upload your photo to a virtual hair color app, you're well on your way to finding your perfect shade. Happy coloring!
