When blow drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to avoid disrupting your curls. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. To use a diffuser, simply attach it to your blow dryer and use it on a low heat setting.

Step by Step Guide

Hereâs a step-by-step guide on how to style super thick curly hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly.

3. Apply Curl Defining Cream

Apply a curl defining cream to your hair to enhance your curls. Use a quarter-sized amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

4. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it on a low heat setting. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

5. Finish with a Hair Spray

Finish off your look with a hair spray to hold your curls in place. Use a light hold spray to avoid weighing down your curls.

1. Donât Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage. Try to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

2. Use a Microfiber Towel

Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentle on your hair and can help reduce frizz.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
