What Hairstyles Are Best For Thick Hair

Written by Daniel Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's great for creating voluminous styles, but it can also be difficult to manage and can become heavy and bulky. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for thick hair, so you can rock your beautiful mane with confidence.

Description

Thick hair is characterized by a high density of hair follicles, which can make it challenging to style. It also tends to be coarser than fine hair, which can make it more prone to tangling and frizz. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, thick hair can look absolutely gorgeous!

How to

If you have thick hair, you may find that certain hairstyles work better than others. Here are some of the best hairstyles for thick hair:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick hair, as they help to remove some of the bulk and create movement and texture. This style works particularly well for those with wavy or curly hair, as it allows the curls to bounce and move freely.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for all hair types, including thick hair. A chin-length or shoulder-length bob can help to reduce the weight of your hair and create a sleek, sophisticated look. You can also add layers to a bob for added texture and movement.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for thick hair. This style is short and low-maintenance, making it perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. A pixie cut can also help to make your hair look thicker and more voluminous.

4. Half Up, Half Down

If you want to show off your luscious locks while keeping your hair out of your face, a half up, half down style is a great option. You can create a half up, half down style by pulling the top section of your hair back and securing it with a clip or hair tie. This style works particularly well for those with long, thick hair.

Step by Step

Here's how to create a half up, half down style:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair, from ear to ear.
  3. Smooth the top section of your hair with a brush or comb.
  4. Twist the top section of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie.
  5. Leave the bottom half of your hair down.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling thick hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create lift and body.
  • Avoid heavy products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to help detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help reduce the weight of your hair and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for thick hair:

1. Can I still get a bob if I have really thick hair?

Absolutely! A bob is a great option for thick hair, and can help to reduce the weight and bulk of your hair.

2. Will a pixie cut make my hair look thinner?

No, a pixie cut can actually make your hair look thicker and more voluminous, as it removes some of the weight and bulk.

3. Can I wear my hair straight if it's really thick?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if it's thick. However, you may need to use a flat iron or other heat styling tools to get the desired look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your thick hair with confidence and style. Whether you prefer long layers, a bob, a pixie cut, or a half up, half down style, there's a perfect hairstyle out there for you!
