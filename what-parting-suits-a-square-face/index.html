What Parting Suits A Square Face

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles and partings that don't seem to suit your face shape? If you have a square face, it's essential to choose a parting that flatters your features. In this article, we'll discuss what parting suits a square face, how to achieve it, and some essential hair care tips to keep your locks in perfect shape.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Part Hair for Square Faces
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Square Face

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, forehead, and cheekbones of almost the same width. The forehead is usually flat, and the chin is sharp. The best parting for a square face is one that softens the angles and adds some length to the face. The ideal parting for a square face is a side parting, which creates an illusion of a more extended face.

How to Part Hair for Square Faces

When it comes to parting your hair, there are different options to choose from, such as the middle part, side part, or zigzag part. However, for square faces, the side part is the most flattering. It balances out the face and adds softness to the pronounced angles.

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to achieve a side part for a square face:

  1. Start by using a tail comb to create a straight line from the top of your head to the nape of your neck.
  2. Use the end of the comb to part your hair on one side, starting from the highest point of your eyebrow arch.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and blow-dry it using a round brush.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it and add some shine.
  5. Finish off with some hairspray to keep your parting in place.

Tips for Styling a Square Face

Aside from choosing the right parting, here are some tips to help you style your hair if you have a square face:

  • Go for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a messy bun or a high ponytail.
  • Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs that emphasize your strong jawline.
  • Try out wavy or curly hairstyles that soften the angles of your face.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add some interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I still rock a middle part if I have a square face?

A: While a middle part is not the most flattering for a square face, you can still try it out. Keep in mind that it may emphasize your strong jawline and make your face look wider.

Q: Should I go for short or long hair if I have a square face?

A: Both short and long hair can work for a square face. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds some length to your face and softens the angles. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care for Square Face

Aside from styling your hair, it's crucial to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to get rid of product buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Invest in a good quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what parting suits a square face, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that flatters your features the most. Don't be afraid to try out new things and have fun with your hair!
