How to Style Short Naturally Curly Hair

Styling short naturally curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, it can be a breeze. Here are some popular hairstyles for short naturally curly hair:

Braids

Braids are a perfect hairstyle for short naturally curly hair as they can add texture and volume. French braids and Dutch braids are two of the most popular types of braids. To create a French braid, start at the crown of your head and braid downwards while adding hair sections as you go. For a Dutch braid, follow the same steps but braid the hair under instead of over. Once you've completed the braid, secure it with an elastic band and use bobby pins to tuck any loose strands.

Updos

Updos are an elegant and sophisticated hairstyle that can be achieved with short naturally curly hair. The classic bun is a popular updo that can be achieved by gathering your hair into a ponytail and twisting it into a bun. To add texture, use a curling iron to curl any loose strands. Another popular updo is the messy bun, which can be achieved by gathering your hair into a ponytail and twisting it while leaving some strands loose for a messy look.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, stylish haircut that can be perfect for naturally curly hair. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top slightly longer. This will give your curls a chance to shine while also enhancing the texture and volume of your hair.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short naturally curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product to enhance your curls' texture and volume.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while also enhancing your curls' shape.
  4. If you want to add more texture, use a curling iron to curl any loose strands.
  5. Style your hair into your desired hairstyle, such as braids or updos.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short naturally curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to avoid pulling and damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • When sleeping, wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet to avoid tangling and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Can I straighten my short naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your short naturally curly hair using a flat iron. However, keep in mind that heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause frizz, so it's best to use them sparingly.

What products should I use for short naturally curly hair?

You should use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, and a curl-defining cream or gel. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful short naturally curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short naturally curly hair with ease. Remember to take care of your hair and use products that are gentle and nourishing. Happy styling!
