How To Cut Your Own Short Hair In Layers With Scissors

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every time you need a haircut? Do you want to save money and time? Cutting your own short hair in layers with scissors is easier than you think. In this article, we will show you how to do it step by step.

Description

Cutting your own hair may seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more stylish and chic. Short hair is perfect for busy women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks great.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror

Here are the steps to cut your own short hair in layers with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting. Wet hair is harder to cut accurately.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use clips to secure the sections you are not working on.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair, comb it straight, and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to cut at an angle for a layered effect. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working from back to front.

Step 4: Check for Balance

Once you have finished cutting, check for balance. Use a handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them separately. Comb them straight and cut them in a straight line. You can cut them straight across or at an angle for a layered effect.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it is wet. It will look shorter when it dries.
  • Use a handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror to check for balance.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair in layers with scissors?

Yes, you can. It may take some practice, but it is possible to cut your own hair in layers with scissors.

What type of scissors should I use?

Use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Don't use regular household scissors.

Can I cut my hair when it is wet?

No, it is best to cut your hair when it is dry. Wet hair is harder to cut accurately.

Hair Care

After you have cut your hair, take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products.

Cutting your own short hair in layers with scissors can be a great way to save money and time. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a chic and stylish look.
