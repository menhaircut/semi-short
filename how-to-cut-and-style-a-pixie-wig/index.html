How To Cut And Style A Pixie Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are looking for a fresh and bold hairstyle, a pixie cut may be just what you need. However, if you are not ready to commit to such a drastic change, a pixie wig can be a great alternative. With a pixie wig, you can enjoy the benefits of a short and sassy haircut without the commitment. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of cutting and styling a pixie wig for a perfect look.

Description

A pixie wig is a short wig that typically features layers and texture. It is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and looks fabulous. Pixie wigs come in a variety of styles, colors, and textures, so you can choose the one that best suits your personality and preferences.

How to Cut a Pixie Wig

The first step in cutting a pixie wig is to choose the right wig for your face shape and style preferences. Once you have your wig, you can follow these steps to cut it:

Step 1: Prep the Wig

Before you start cutting the wig, you need to make sure it is clean and dry. Brush the hair thoroughly and remove any tangles. Then, use a wig cap to secure the wig in place and make sure it fits snugly on your head.

Step 2: Cut the Bangs

The bangs are an essential part of a pixie cut, so you need to cut them carefully. Use a comb to separate the bangs from the rest of the hair and hold them in place with your fingers. Then, use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the bangs to the desired length. Cut them slowly and gradually, so you do not cut too much hair at once.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

The sides and back of a pixie wig should be short and textured. Use your scissors to cut the hair in small sections, starting from the nape of your neck and moving upwards. Cut the hair to the desired length, but make sure you do not cut too much at once. Keep checking the length as you go, and adjust the cutting technique as necessary.

Step 4: Add Texture

Once you have cut the hair to the desired length, you can add texture by using thinning shears or a razor comb. These tools will help you create a choppy and textured look that is perfect for a pixie cut. Use them to thin out the hair and create layers that add volume and movement.

How to Style a Pixie Wig

Styling a pixie wig is easy and fun. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect look:

Tip 1: Use a Styling Product

A styling product can help you achieve the desired texture and hold for your pixie wig. Choose a product that suits your hair type and style preferences, such as a mousse, gel, or wax. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to style it as desired.

Tip 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help you add volume and texture to your pixie wig. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and blow-dry it in an upwards direction. This will create a voluminous and textured look that is perfect for a pixie cut.

Tip 3: Add Accessories

Accessories can help you add a personal touch to your pixie wig. Consider adding a headband, scarf, or hairpin to your hair to create a unique and stylish look.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my pixie wig?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your wig. Generally, you should cut your pixie wig every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q: Can I color my pixie wig?

A: Yes, you can color your pixie wig. However, it is important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color and texture of the wig.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie wig looking fabulous, you need to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Clean Your Wig

Clean your wig regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair soft and shiny.

Tip 2: Store Your Wig Properly

Store your wig in a cool and dry place to prevent damage from heat and humidity. Use a wig stand or mannequin head to keep the wig in shape.

Tip 3: Protect Your Wig

Protect your wig from the sun, wind, and rain by wearing a hat or scarf. Avoid using hot styling tools on your wig, as they can damage the fibers.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut and style a pixie wig that looks fabulous and suits your personality and style. Enjoy your new look and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and accessories!
