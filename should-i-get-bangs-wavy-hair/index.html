Should I Get Bangs With Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide to answering the question, "Should I get bangs with wavy hair?" If you're considering a hair change, whether it's a big chop, color, or cut, bangs are a great way to switch up your look. However, before diving in, it's important to consider how bangs will work with your hair texture. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting bangs with wavy hair, including how to style them and how to care for your new 'do.

How To Determine If Bangs Will Work With Your Wavy Hair

Before jumping into the world of bangs, it's important to determine if they'll work with your hair texture. Wavy hair can be tricky to work with, as it tends to have a mind of its own. However, the good news is that there are plenty of bang styles that work well with wavy hair. Here are some things to consider when deciding if bangs are right for you:

Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if bangs will work with your wavy hair is your face shape. While bangs can work with any face shape, certain styles may be more flattering on specific face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face, while blunt bangs may make your face appear shorter and wider.

Hair Texture

Another thing to consider when deciding if bangs will work with your wavy hair is your hair texture. While wavy hair can work well with bangs, it's important to choose a style that complements your natural texture. For example, if you have thick, coarse waves, heavy blunt bangs may be too much for your hair to handle. Instead, consider softer, side-swept bangs that will blend with your waves.

Lifestyle

The final thing to consider when deciding if bangs will work with your wavy hair is your lifestyle. Bangs require regular maintenance, so if you're not up for frequent salon visits or daily styling, bangs may not be the best option for you. However, if you're willing to put in the effort, bangs can be a great way to switch up your look.

How To Style Bangs With Wavy Hair

Once you've decided to take the plunge and get bangs with your wavy hair, the next step is to learn how to style them. Here are some tips for styling bangs with wavy hair:

Blow Dry Your Bangs

If you have naturally wavy hair, you may find that your bangs don't lay flat when air-dried. To combat this, blow dry your bangs with a round brush to smooth them out and create volume.

Use A Curling Iron

If you want to add some extra definition to your waves, use a curling iron to curl your bangs away from your face. This will create a flattering, face-framing effect.

Avoid Heavy Products

When styling your bangs, avoid using heavy products that can weigh them down. Instead, opt for lightweight products, like a texturizing spray or mousse, to add volume and hold without making your bangs look greasy.

Step-By-Step Guide To Getting Bangs With Wavy Hair

If you've decided that bangs are right for you, here's a step-by-step guide to getting them with your wavy hair:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before heading to the salon, find some inspiration photos of bangs that you like. This will help you to communicate your desired style to your stylist.

Step 2: Consult With Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the best bang style for your face shape and hair texture. They can help you to determine the best length, thickness, and shape for your bangs.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Once you and your stylist have decided on a bang style, it's time to cut them! Make sure to communicate any concerns or questions that you have with your stylist before they start cutting.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

After your bangs are cut, your stylist will likely style them for you. Pay attention to the products and techniques that they use so that you can recreate the look at home.

Tips For Caring For Bangs With Wavy Hair

Now that you have bangs with your wavy hair, it's important to care for them properly to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your new 'do:

Trim Them Regularly

Bangs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Make sure to schedule regular trims with your stylist to keep your bangs at the right length and shape.

Wash Them Frequently

Bangs can get greasy quickly, so make sure to wash them frequently to keep them looking fresh. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your waves.

Use A Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent your bangs from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.

FAQs About Getting Bangs With Wavy Hair

Q: Will bangs make my wavy hair look frizzy?

A: Bangs can make wavy hair look frizzy if they're not properly styled. It's important to blow dry your bangs and use lightweight products to keep them looking smooth and defined.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes! While bangs can be a bit tricky to style, they can still be worn in a ponytail. Simply pull your bangs back with the rest of your hair, or pin them to the side with bobby pins.

Q: How do I know if bangs will look good with my face shape?

A: Bangs can work with any face shape, but certain styles may be more flattering on specific face shapes. Consult with your stylist to determine the best bang style for your face shape.

Conclusion

So, should you get bangs with wavy hair? The answer is a resounding yes, as long as you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. With the right styling techniques and regular maintenance, bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some extra flair to your wavy locks.
