Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair To Do At Home

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for easy hairstyles for medium length hair to do at home, you're in luck! There are plenty of options that will have you looking chic and put-together in no time. In this article, we'll go over step-by-step instructions for some of the most popular and stylish medium length hairstyles, as well as tips for caring for your hair and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the specific hairstyles, it's important to understand some basic techniques that will make styling your medium length hair easier:

  • Invest in some quality hair tools, such as a good brush, hair ties, and bobby pins. These will make your hairstyles look more polished.
  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to style and may not hold the shape you want.
  • Use products sparingly. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Don't be afraid to practice! The more you try different hairstyles, the more comfortable you'll become with styling your own hair.

Step-by-Step

1. Low Ponytail

This classic hairstyle is easy to do and looks chic on medium length hair.

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  3. Use a small section of hair to wrap around the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. Optional: pull out a few face-framing pieces of hair for a more relaxed look.

2. Messy Bun

This effortless hairstyle is perfect for a casual day or even a night out.

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair to create a messy, undone look.

3. Half-up Half-down

This hairstyle is a great way to show off your hair's length while keeping it out of your face.

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it slightly for volume.
  2. Gather the teased section and a few pieces of hair from each side of your head and secure them with a hair tie.
  3. Optional: curl the remaining hair for a more polished look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest to your hairstyles.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair more body.
  • If you have thick hair, use a lightweight hair oil to tame frizz and add shine.

FAQ

What if my hair is too short for these hairstyles?

If your hair is shorter than medium length, you can still try some of these hairstyles with the help of hair extensions or by adapting the instructions to your hair length.

What if my hair is too thick or too thin?

These hairstyles can be adapted to different hair types. For thick hair, use hair ties and bobby pins that are designed for thicker hair. For thin hair, use volumizing products and try teasing your hair for more volume.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you have some easy hairstyles for medium length hair to do at home, you can experiment with different looks and find what works best for you. Remember to have fun and don't be afraid to try something new!
