Why Is The Underneath Of My Hair Curly But The Top Is Straight

Written by Eveline Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Many people have different textures of hair on different parts of their head. Some people have coarser hair at the top of their head, while others have finer hair underneath. But what about those who have curly hair underneath and straight hair at the top? This can be a frustrating hair type to manage, but understanding why this happens can help you better care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to hair texture, genetics plays a significant role. The genes that determine hair texture are passed down from parents to their children. Hair texture is determined by the shape of the hair follicle, which can be round, oval, or flat. People with flat hair follicles tend to have straight hair, while those with round or oval follicles tend to have wavy or curly hair.

In some cases, people may have a combination of different hair textures on their head. This can happen for several reasons:

  • Hormonal changes
  • Environmental factors
  • Chemical treatments
  • Heat styling
  • Age

How to

If you have curly hair underneath and straight hair at the top, there are a few things you can do to manage your hair:

Step by step

  1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help prevent frizz and keep your hair smooth.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible.
  4. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage to your hair.
  5. Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  6. Instead of brushing, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  7. Consider getting a haircut that is tailored to your hair type to help manage your curls and straight strands.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help manage your curly hair underneath and straight hair at the top:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as this can dry out your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

Why is the underneath of my hair curly but the top is straight?

The texture of your hair is determined by genetics, and it is possible to have different textures on different parts of your head. Hormonal changes, environmental factors, chemical treatments, heat styling, and age can also contribute to changes in hair texture.

Can I make my hair texture more uniform?

While you cannot change your hair texture permanently, there are things you can do to help manage your hair. Using moisturizing products, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting a haircut that is tailored to your hair type can all help.

Hair Care

Managing hair that has different textures can be challenging, but with the right care, you can keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Using moisturizing products, avoiding heat styling tools, and protecting your hair from the sun are all essential steps to keeping your hair in good condition.

Remember, everyone's hair is unique, and what works for one person may not work for another. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
