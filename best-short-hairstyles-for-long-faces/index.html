Best Short Hairstyles For Long Faces

Written by Luffy Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and balance out the length of your face. One solution is to go for a short hairstyle. Short haircuts can help to create the illusion of width and volume, which can be especially helpful for those with longer faces.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Long Face

When it comes to short hairstyles for long faces, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short.
  • Consider layers or a side-swept fringe to add volume.
  • Try a bob or a pixie cut for a sleek and stylish look.

Ultimately, the right hairstyle for you will depend on your personal style and preferences. Consult with a hairstylist to find a cut that flatters your features and works with your hair type and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Long Face

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle for your long face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Using a flat iron or curling iron, style your hair as desired.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

For a more textured look, you can also use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and definition to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on a Long Face

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair on a long face:

  • Visit your hairstylist for regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a long face?

A: Yes, adding bangs to a short hairstyle can help to balance out the length of your face. Consider a side-swept fringe or choppy bangs for a stylish look.

Q: Will a bob haircut flatter a long face?

A: Yes, a bob can be a great choice for those with long faces. Look for a bob that falls just below your chin to add width to your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a half-up hairstyle or a messy bun for a chic and effortless look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on a Long Face

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can make your hair look greasy or limp.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to refresh your hair.

With the right short hairstyle and hair care routine, you can flatter your long face and feel confident and stylish every day.
