How To Style A Skirt That Is Too Short

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you've ever purchased a skirt, only to realize that it's just a bit too short for your liking, don't fret! There are plenty of ways to style a skirt that is too short, and make it work for your personal taste and style. In this article, we'll go over the various ways you can style a shorter skirt, and make it look chic and stylish. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Skirt That is Too Short
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Short Skirt
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Look

How to Style a Skirt That is Too Short

There are a few ways you can style a shorter skirt, and make it work for your personal style. Here are some tips:

1. Pair it with Leggings or Tights

If you're not comfortable showing too much leg, you can always pair your shorter skirt with leggings or tights. This will not only make you feel more comfortable, but it will also add an extra layer of warmth during colder months.

2. Opt for Longer Tops

When wearing a shorter skirt, it's always a good idea to opt for longer tops. This will balance out the shorter length of the skirt, and create a more cohesive look. You can choose from a range of tops, such as a sweater, tunic, or a button-down shirt.

3. Wear High Boots

Another way to style a shorter skirt is to wear high boots. This will not only add some height to your look, but it will also cover up more of your legs. You can choose from a range of boots, such as ankle boots, knee-high boots, or even thigh-high boots.

4. Accessorize with a Belt

If you're wearing a shorter skirt, you can accessorize with a belt to create a more defined waistline. This will not only add some shape to your look, but it will also draw attention away from the shorter length of the skirt. You can choose from a range of belts, such as a thin belt, a thick belt, or even a statement belt.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style a skirt that is too short:

Step 1: Choose Your Skirt

Choose a skirt that you love, but wish was a bit longer. This could be a denim skirt, a mini skirt, or any other style you prefer.

Step 2: Choose Your Top

Choose a top that complements the style of your skirt. Opt for a longer top, such as a sweater, tunic, or a button-down shirt.

Step 3: Pair with Leggings or Tights

If you're not comfortable showing too much leg, pair your skirt with leggings or tights.

Step 4: Accessorize with a Belt

Add a belt to create a more defined waistline and draw attention away from the shorter length of the skirt.

Step 5: Wear High Boots

Finish off your look by wearing high boots. This will add some height to your look, and cover up more of your legs.

Tips for Styling a Short Skirt

Here are some additional tips for styling a shorter skirt:

1. Consider the Occasion

When styling a shorter skirt, it's important to consider the occasion. If you're going to a formal event, you may want to opt for a longer skirt. If you're going out with friends, a shorter skirt may be more appropriate.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles when styling a shorter skirt. Try pairing it with different tops, shoes, and accessories to create a range of looks.

3. Choose the Right Undergarments

When wearing a shorter skirt, it's important to choose the right undergarments. Opt for seamless underwear, and avoid anything too revealing.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a shorter skirt to work?

A: It depends on the dress code of your workplace. If you work in a more casual environment, a shorter skirt may be appropriate. If you work in a more formal environment, you may want to opt for a longer skirt.

Q: What shoes should I wear with a shorter skirt?

A: You can wear a range of shoes with a shorter skirt, such as high boots, ankle boots, flats, or even sneakers.

Q: How short is too short for a skirt?

A: This can vary depending on personal preference and the occasion. However, a good rule of thumb is to ensure that the skirt covers at least the upper half of your thighs.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Look

When styling a shorter skirt, it's important to also think about your hair. Here are some hair care tips to complement your look:

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that complements your look. You can opt for a sleek and simple style, or add some volume and texture for a more playful look.

2. Consider Your Hair Color

Your hair color can also play a role in your overall look. Choose a color that complements your skin tone, and consider adding highlights or lowlights for some added dimension.

3. Use the Right Products

Use the right hair products to keep your hair looking its best. This could include a volumizing mousse, a smoothing serum, or a texturizing spray.

Styling a skirt that is too short can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can create a chic and stylish look that you feel confident in. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles, and find the look that works best for you!
