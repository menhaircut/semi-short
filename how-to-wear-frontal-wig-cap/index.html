How To Wear Frontal Wig Cap Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to wear frontal wig cap. If you're new to wearing wigs, it can be a daunting task to put on a wig cap, let alone a frontal wig cap. But fear not, we're here to guide you through the process and help you achieve a natural and comfortable look. In this guide, we'll cover everything from how to prep your hair to how to secure the wig cap and wig. Let's get started!

Description

A frontal wig cap is a type of wig cap that covers the entire hairline from ear to ear, creating a natural-looking hairline. It's perfect for those who want to achieve a realistic and seamless look, especially when wearing lace front wigs. The frontal wig cap is made of a stretchy and breathable material that allows for maximum comfort and flexibility. It's easy to wear and can be styled in various ways to suit your preferences.

How to Wear Frontal Wig Cap

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step in wearing a frontal wig cap is to prep your hair. Make sure your hair is clean, dry, and free of any knots or tangles. If your hair is long, we recommend braiding it or putting it in a low ponytail to ensure a smooth and flat surface. You can also use a wig cap liner to keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Put on the Wig Cap

Next, it's time to put on the wig cap. Start by placing the wig cap over your head, making sure it covers your entire hairline. Adjust the cap so that it sits comfortably on your head, and make sure it's snug but not too tight. Tuck any loose hair or flyaways under the cap.

Step 3: Secure the Wig Cap

Once you've put on the wig cap, it's time to secure it in place. Use bobby pins to secure the cap around the perimeter of your hairline. Make sure the pins are hidden under the wig cap, so they don't show through the wig. You can also use a wig grip band to keep the wig cap in place, especially if you have a lot of hair.

Step 4: Put on the Wig

Now that the wig cap is securely in place, it's time to put on the wig. Place the wig on top of the wig cap, making sure it's centered and aligned with your natural hairline. Adjust the wig until it fits comfortably and looks natural. You can use wig clips or adhesive to secure the wig in place, but make sure they don't damage your wig or scalp.

Tips

  • Use a wig cap liner to keep your hair in place and protect your scalp from irritation.
  • Choose a wig cap that matches your skin tone to create a seamless and natural-looking hairline.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and wig accessories to achieve your desired look.
  • Take care of your wig by washing it regularly, storing it properly, and avoiding heat styling.

FAQ

Q: How do I choose the right wig cap size?

A: To choose the right wig cap size, measure the circumference of your head and compare it to the wig cap size chart. Most wig caps come in small, medium, and large sizes, so choose the one that fits your head snugly but not too tightly.

Q: Can I wear a frontal wig cap with natural hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a frontal wig cap with natural hair. Just make sure your hair is flat and smooth, and use a wig cap liner to keep your hair in place.

Q: How do I clean my wig cap?

A: You can clean your wig cap by hand washing it with mild shampoo and warm water, then air drying it. Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals that can damage the wig cap.

Hair Care

Wearing a frontal wig cap is a great way to achieve a natural and stylish look, but it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Make sure to wash your wig cap regularly and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your wig or scalp. Invest in high-quality wigs and wig accessories to ensure a comfortable and long-lasting wear. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear a frontal wig cap with confidence and style!
