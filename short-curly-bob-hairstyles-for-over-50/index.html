Short Curly Bob Hairstyles For Over 50 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a fresh new hairstyle? Look no further than the short curly bob! This versatile cut is perfect for those with naturally curly hair or those who want to add some texture to their locks. In this article, we'll dive into everything you need to know about short curly bobs for over 50, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintenance
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style

The beauty of the short curly bob is that it can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some popular options:

Blowout

A blowout is a great way to show off the natural volume of your curls. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair, then use a round brush and blow dryer to smooth out your hair while keeping the curls intact. Finish off with a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Beachy Waves

If you're looking for a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, try creating beachy waves. Apply a salt spray to damp hair, then scrunch your locks with your hands as you blow dry. Once dry, use a curling iron to enhance the waves and add some texture.

Sleek and Straight

If you want to switch things up, try straightening your short curly bob. Apply a heat protectant to your hair, then use a flat iron to smooth out your curls. Finish off with some anti-frizz serum to keep your locks looking sleek and shiny.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to get a short curly bob:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp locks to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to maintain the curl pattern.
  4. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need a bit more definition.
  5. Trim your hair to the desired length and shape.
  6. Finish off with a bit of hairspray or anti-frizz serum to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintenance

Here are some tips for keeping your short curly bob looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the curls.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bob.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still wear my hair up with a short curly bob?

Absolutely! You can still wear your hair in a ponytail or bun, or try half-up half-down styles for a fun twist.

Is a short curly bob suitable for all face shapes?

Short curly bobs can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's important to work with your stylist to find the right length and shape for you.

How often should I wash my short curly bob?

This can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle, but aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it looking and feeling its best.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short curly bob healthy and vibrant:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments on your hair, as these can damage the curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a fabulous short curly bob in no time!
