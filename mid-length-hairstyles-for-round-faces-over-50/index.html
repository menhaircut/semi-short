Mid Length Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Whether you prefer a classic look or something more modern, there's a hairstyle here for you. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Your Round Face

When choosing a mid length hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length and slimness. This means avoiding hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, like layered cuts or styles with height at the crown. You can also try hairstyles with side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a longer face.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat. Instead, try a layered cut with soft, feathery ends. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can work well to create a sleek, polished look.

Hairstyles to Consider:

  • Layered Shoulder-Length Cut
  • Long Bob with Side-Swept Bangs
  • Blunt Cut with Face-Framing Layers
  • Wavy Lob with Balayage Highlights
  • Asymmetrical Bob with Side Part

Step by Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect mid length hairstyle for your round face, it's time to style it. Here's a step by step guide to styling mid length hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume
  4. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse to add body
  5. If you have thick hair, use a straightening or smoothing serum to tame frizz
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls if desired
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Now that you have your new mid length hairstyle, you want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends looking fresh
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine during the summer months

FAQs About Mid Length Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Q: Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can work well for round faces.

Q: Will bangs look good with a mid length hairstyle?

A: Yes, side-swept or face-framing bangs can look great with a mid length hairstyle for round faces.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's important to add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length. Use a round brush when blow drying your hair and add volumizing products.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Mid length hairstyles for round faces over 50 require some extra care to keep them looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage
  • Protect your hair from the elements with a hat or scarf

Now that you have some ideas and tips for mid length hairstyles for round faces over 50, it's time to try something new! Whether you choose a classic bob or a modern lob, you're sure to find a style that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident.
