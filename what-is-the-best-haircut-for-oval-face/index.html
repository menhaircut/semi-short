What Is The Best Haircut For Oval Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the hair salon and not knowing what haircut suits your oval face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for oval face shape and everything you need to know about it.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the ideal shape as it has balanced proportions. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are high. This shape can pull off almost any hairstyle and is considered the most versatile face shape.

How to

When choosing a haircut for an oval face shape, it's important to consider the hair texture, length, and personal style. Below are some of the best haircuts for oval face shape:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great haircut for oval face shape as it adds volume and texture. It's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut.

2. Long Layers

If you have long hair, long layers can add movement and dimension to your hair. It's a great way to show off your beautiful face shape while keeping your hair long.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften the face and add a touch of glamour. It's perfect for those who want to add a little something extra to their haircut without going too drastic.

Step by Step

When going to the salon, make sure to communicate with your stylist about the haircut you want. Below are some steps to ensure you get the best haircut for oval face shape:

1. Research

Before going to the salon, do your research and find hairstyles that suit oval face shape. You can even bring pictures to show your stylist.

2. Consultation

During the consultation, communicate with your stylist about your hair texture, length, and personal style. Your stylist can then recommend a haircut that suits your face shape and preferences.

3. Maintenance

After getting your haircut, make sure to maintain it regularly. This includes trimming the ends every 6-8 weeks and using hair care products that suit your hair type.

Tips

Below are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for oval face shape:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can overpower the face and make it look smaller. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

2. Consider Face Shape

When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.

3. Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture when choosing a haircut. For example, if you have fine hair, a short haircut may not be the best option.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for oval face shape?

The most flattering haircut for oval face shape is a layered bob, long layers, or side-swept bangs.

Can oval face shape pull off any haircut?

Yes, oval face shape can pull off almost any haircut.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is just as important as choosing the right haircut. Below are some hair care tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Use hair care products that suit your hair type. For example, if you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a heat protectant spray and wearing a hat when outdoors.

3. Be Gentle

Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair. Avoid harsh pulling and tugging, which can cause breakage and damage.

Now that you know everything about the best haircut for oval face shape, it's time to book that salon appointment and rock your new hairstyle!
