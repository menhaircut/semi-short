What Is A Straight Haircut Called

Written by William Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

In this day and age, it’s important to know what you’re talking about when you go to the salon. You don’t want to be lost in a sea of technical jargon and leave with a haircut you didn’t even ask for. One of the most common types of haircuts is a straight haircut. But what is a straight haircut called in relaxed English language? Let’s find out!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A straight haircut, also known as a blunt cut, is a simple and classic style that is achieved by cutting the hair straight across. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and lengths, and is great for those who want a low maintenance look. A straight haircut can be worn with or without bangs, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to

If you’re considering getting a straight haircut, it’s important to communicate clearly with your stylist. Here are some tips on how to get the perfect straight haircut:

1. Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair will determine the overall look of your straight haircut. If you have long hair, you may want to consider a shoulder length cut. If you have short hair, a chin length cut may be more suitable. Your stylist can help you choose the right length for your face shape and hair type.

2. Decide on Bangs

If you’re considering bangs, it’s important to decide on the right type for your face shape. Side swept bangs are great for round faces, while blunt bangs are better for oval faces. Your stylist can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

3. Communicate Clearly

When you’re at the salon, it’s important to communicate clearly with your stylist. Bring in pictures of the style you’re looking for, and be specific about the length and type of bangs you want. This will help your stylist give you the perfect straight haircut.

Step by Step

Here’s a step by step guide to getting a straight haircut:

1. Wash and Dry

Wash and dry your hair before your appointment. This will make it easier for your stylist to cut your hair.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts – two on top and two on the bottom. Use clips to keep each section separate.

3. Cut the Bottom Section

Cut the bottom section of your hair straight across, making sure to cut all the hair at the same length.

4. Cut the Top Section

Cut the top section of your hair straight across, making sure to blend it with the bottom section.

5. Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a straightening iron or blow dryer to create a sleek, straight look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your straight haircut:

1. Use the Right Products

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Protect Your Hair

Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools, such as a straightening iron or blow dryer. This will help protect your hair from damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your straight haircut looking fresh and healthy. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

What is the difference between a straight haircut and a layered haircut?

A straight haircut is achieved by cutting the hair straight across, while a layered haircut involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create depth and movement.

Can a straight haircut be worn with curly hair?

Yes, a straight haircut can be worn with curly hair. However, it’s important to remember that the haircut will look different on curly hair than it does on straight hair.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

In conclusion, a straight haircut is a classic and versatile style that can be worn by anyone. Whether you’re looking for a low maintenance look or a sleek and sophisticated style, a straight haircut is a great option. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your straight haircut looking fresh and healthy.
