What Is A Medium Coat Size

Written by Daniel Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to buying a coat, one of the most important factors to consider is the size. However, with so many different size options out there, it can be difficult to know what size to go for. In this article, we will be focusing on what exactly a medium coat size is and how to determine if it is the right size for you.

Description

A medium coat size is typically considered to be a size that falls somewhere in the middle of the size spectrum. While exact measurements may vary depending on the brand, a medium coat size typically corresponds to a chest measurement of around 38-40 inches for men and a bust measurement of around 36-38 inches for women.

It is important to note that coat sizes can vary between brands and different styles of coats. Therefore, it is always best to refer to the specific sizing chart for the brand and style you are interested in to ensure the best fit.

How to

When determining your coat size, it is important to take accurate measurements of your body. For men, measure around the fullest part of your chest and for women, measure around the fullest part of your bust.

Once you have your measurements, refer to the sizing chart for the brand and coat style you are interested in. This will give you a better idea of which size is likely to fit you the best.

It is also important to keep in mind that different styles of coats may fit differently. For example, a fitted blazer may require a different size than a more relaxed winter coat. It is always best to try on different styles and sizes to find the best fit for you.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide to determining your coat size:

  1. Take accurate measurements of your chest or bust.
  2. Refer to the sizing chart for the brand and style of coat you are interested in.
  3. Try on different styles and sizes to find the best fit.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when determining your coat size:

  • Always refer to the sizing chart for the specific brand and style of coat you are interested in.
  • Be aware that different styles of coats may fit differently.
  • Try on different sizes and styles to find the best fit.
  • Consider the layering you will be doing under your coat when determining your size.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions related to medium coat sizes:

What is the average size for a medium coat?

The average size for a medium coat is around 38-40 inches for men and 36-38 inches for women. However, it is important to refer to the specific sizing chart for the brand and style you are interested in to ensure the best fit.

What should I do if I am between sizes?

If you are between sizes, it is generally recommended to size up. This will allow you to layer clothing underneath your coat and ensure a comfortable fit.

Can I alter a coat to fit better?

Yes, it is possible to alter a coat to fit better. However, it is generally best to try on different sizes and styles to find the best fit before resorting to alterations.

Hair Care

When trying on coats, it is important to keep in mind any hairstyles or hair accessories you may be wearing. For example, if you frequently wear your hair in a high bun or ponytail, you may want to consider a coat with a larger hood or collar to accommodate your hairstyle.

Additionally, hair accessories such as hats and headbands can add extra bulk to your head and affect the fit of your coat. It is always best to try on coats with and without any hair accessories you may be wearing to ensure the best fit.
