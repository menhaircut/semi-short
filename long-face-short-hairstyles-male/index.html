Long Face Short Hairstyles For Men The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing our top tips and tricks for finding the perfect short hairstyle for men with long faces. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right cut to how to style your hair for maximum impact. Let's get started!

Description

A long face can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's a very attractive and masculine feature. On the other hand, it can make finding the right hairstyle a challenge. The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for men with long faces is to choose a cut that balances out your features. This means finding a cut that adds width to your face without making it look too round.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right cut, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, you'll want to choose a cut that works with your hair's natural volume. If you have thin hair, you'll want to choose a cut that adds volume and texture.

Next, think about your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to avoid cuts that make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for cuts that add width to your face. This can be achieved through layers, texture, and volume.

Finally, consider your personal style. Your haircut should reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you're a busy guy who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance cut. If you're more adventurous, try a bold, edgy style.

Step by Step Guide

Once you've chosen the right cut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step by step guide to styling short hair for men with long faces:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a pomade, wax, or gel.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Remember, the key to styling short hair is to keep it simple. Don't overthink it, and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works for you.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling a short hairstyle for men with long faces:

  • Choose a cut that works with your hair's natural texture.
  • Try adding layers or texture to your hair to add width to your face.
  • Avoid cuts that are too short, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on choosing a cut that works for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for men with long faces:

What is the best short hairstyle for men with long faces?

The best short hairstyle for men with long faces is one that adds width and volume to your face. This can be achieved through layers, texture, and volume.

Should I use a comb or my fingers to style my hair?

It depends on your personal preference and the look you're going for. A comb will give you a more polished look, while using your fingers will give you a more natural, textured look.

What styling products work best for short hair?

The best styling products for short hair are pomades, waxes, and gels. Choose a product that works with your hair's texture and adds the right amount of hold and shine.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair in tip-top shape:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle for men with long faces and keep your hair looking healthy and strong for years to come.
