How To Style Very Soft Hair Tips And Techniques

Written by William Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Soft hair can be tricky to style as it often lacks volume and hold. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful hairstyles that showcase your natural texture. In this article, we will explore some tips and tricks on how to style very soft hair.

How to

Before we get into styling techniques, it's important to start with clean, well-moisturized hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for soft hair. Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip away natural oils and leave your hair dry and frizzy. Once your hair is clean and conditioned, towel-dry gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Next, apply a styling product that will help add body and hold to your hair. Mousse, gel, and styling cream are all excellent options for soft hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the roots and mid-lengths.

Now it's time to start styling! Here are some techniques that work well for soft hair:

Blow-Drying

Blow-drying is an excellent way to add volume and body to soft hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow-drying. This will help create volume and give your hair a bouncy, full look.

Curling

Soft hair can be difficult to curl, but with the right technique, it can be done. Use a curling iron with a small barrel and wrap small sections of hair around the iron. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release. Once you have curled your entire head, use a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Braiding

Braids are a great way to add texture and volume to soft hair. Create a few small braids throughout your hair and leave them in overnight. When you wake up, take out the braids and gently finger-comb your hair to create loose, beachy waves.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style very soft hair:

  1. Start with clean, well-moisturized hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that will add body and hold.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots.
  4. Curl your hair using a small-barrel curling iron.
  5. Use a light hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  6. Create a few small braids throughout your hair and leave them in overnight.
  7. Take out the braids in the morning and finger-comb your hair to create loose waves.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling very soft hair:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still straighten my soft hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your soft hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

Q: What kind of styling products should I use for soft hair?

A: Mousse, gel, and styling cream are all excellent options for soft hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for your hair type.

Hair Care

Taking care of soft hair requires a gentle touch. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for soft hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip away natural oils.
  • Towel-dry gently to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your soft hair with confidence and create beautiful, voluminous hairstyles that showcase your natural texture.
