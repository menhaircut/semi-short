How to Choose the Best Hair Color for Round Chubby Face

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you have a round chubby face. You want a hair color that will slim your face and make it look more oval. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair color for your round chubby face:

1. Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing a hair color, you need to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, then you should go for warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, then you should go for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or blue-black.

2. Avoid Dark Colors

Dark hair colors can make your face look even rounder. Instead, go for lighter shades that will create an illusion of length. You can try shades like honey blonde, light brown, or caramel.

3. Highlight Your Face

You can also use highlights to create an illusion of length. Ask your stylist to add highlights around your face to draw attention to your features.

4. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color for your round chubby face. If you have blue eyes, then you should go for blonde shades. If you have brown eyes, then you should go for warmer shades like chestnut brown or caramel.

Step by Step Guide on How to Color Your Hair

Now that you know how to choose the best hair color for your round chubby face, it's time to learn how to color your hair. Here is a step by step guide:

1. Choose the Right Hair Color

Choose a hair color that is one or two shades lighter than your natural hair color. This will create a more natural look. If you want a drastic change, then you can go for a completely different color.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Do not use conditioner as it can make it difficult for the color to penetrate your hair.

3. Apply the Hair Color

Wear gloves to protect your hands. Mix the hair color according to the instructions on the box. Apply the hair color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave it on for the recommended time.

4. Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair with cool water until the water runs clear. Do not shampoo your hair for at least 48 hours after coloring it.

Tips on How to Take Care of Your Colored Hair

Colored hair needs extra care to maintain its color and shine. Here are some tips on how to take care of your colored hair:

1. Use a Color-Protecting Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for colored hair. This will help to maintain the color and prevent it from fading.

2. Avoid Hot Water

Hot water can strip the color from your hair. Use cool water when washing your hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can fade your hair color. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQ

What hair color makes a round face look slimmer?

Lighter hair colors like honey blonde, light brown, or caramel can create an illusion of length and make your face look slimmer.

Can I color my hair if I have a chubby face?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a chubby face. Just make sure to choose the right hair color that will complement your face shape.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

If you have colored hair, then you need to take extra care of it to maintain its color and shine. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

1. Use a Color-Protecting Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for colored hair. This will help to maintain the color and prevent it from fading.

2. Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and fade the color. Avoid using them or use them sparingly.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

4. Get Regular Hair Trims

Get regular hair trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help to maintain the color and shine of your hair.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair color for round chubby face is not as hard as it seems. Just follow the tips we have given you and you will be on your way to having a beautiful hair color that will complement your face shape. Don't forget to take care of your colored hair to maintain its color and shine. Happy coloring!
