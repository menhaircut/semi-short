How To Style Medium Length Hair With Bangs

Written by Daniel Aug 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Medium length hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle that never goes out of style. It’s versatile, easy to maintain, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are countless ways to style your medium length hair with bangs that will enhance your natural features and give you a chic look.

Description

Medium length hair with bangs is a hairstyle that falls between the chin and the shoulders. Bangs are cut across the forehead, framing the face and drawing attention to the eyes. This hairstyle is suitable for any face shape and hair texture, making it a popular choice among women of all ages.

How to Style Medium Length Hair with Bangs

There are many ways to style medium length hair with bangs, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Straight and Sleek

If you have straight hair, a sleek and straight look is perfect for you. Start by blow-drying your hair straight using a round brush. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a glossy finish. For the bangs, use a straightening iron to make them straight and smooth.

2. Loose Waves

If you have wavy hair, loose waves are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. Apply some sea salt spray to your damp hair and scrunch it with your hands to create beachy waves. For the bangs, use a curling iron to create loose waves that frame your face.

3. Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, embrace your natural curls and create a bouncy look. Apply some curl cream to your damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the curls. For the bangs, use a curling iron to create loose curls that blend in with your natural curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you style your medium length hair with bangs:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse, gel, or cream, to your damp hair. This will help to define your hair and make it easier to style.

3. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture. Use a nozzle attachment on your blow dryer to direct the airflow down the hair shaft and create a smooth finish.

4. Style the Bangs

Use a straightening or curling iron to style your bangs, depending on the look you want to achieve. If you want straight bangs, use a straightening iron to make them smooth and sleek. If you want curly bangs, use a curling iron to create loose curls that blend in with your natural curls.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that provides hold without making your hair stiff or sticky.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length hair with bangs:

  • Use heat protectant spray before using a hot styling tool to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Try different hairstyles, such as updos, braids, and ponytails, to add variety to your look.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

1. Can I style my bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your bangs without heat by using a hair roller or hairpin to create curls or waves. You can also use a headband or hairband to create a sleek and stylish look.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair with bangs healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your medium length hair with bangs and achieve a chic and stylish look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
