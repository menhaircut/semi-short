How To Cut Hair For Big Forehead Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of hiding your big forehead with bangs or hats? It's time to embrace your forehead and learn how to cut your hair in a way that will flatter your features. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to cut hair for big forehead, so you can feel confident and stylish.

Description

Having a big forehead can be a challenge when it comes to styling your hair. Bangs can be a quick fix, but they require constant maintenance and can sometimes look too heavy or blunt. However, there are many other ways to cut your hair that can minimize the look of a big forehead and balance out your face shape.

How to Cut Hair for Big Forehead: Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when cutting your hair for a big forehead:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and balances out your forehead. If you have a big forehead, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that adds volume or width to the sides of your face.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that suits you. Some hairstyles that work well for big foreheads include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long, face-framing layers. These styles can help soften your features and draw attention away from your forehead.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

If you're cutting your hair at home, it's important to use sharp scissors and take your time. Start by sectioning your hair and cutting small amounts at a time. It's always better to cut less than you think you need, as you can always go back and trim more later.

Tips for Cutting Hair for Big Forehead

Here are some additional tips for cutting hair for big forehead:

  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you're unsure about cutting your hair at home.
  • Use a comb and scissors to create soft, layered edges.
  • Avoid blunt bangs that can make your forehead look even bigger.
  • Try a side-swept fringe that falls diagonally across your forehead.
  • Add volume to the sides of your hair with layers or curls.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ: Cutting Hair for Big Forehead

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a big forehead?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a big forehead, but choose a style that is soft and wispy rather than blunt and heavy. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can be a good option.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: What products should I use to style my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. Some products that can help add volume and texture to your hair include mousse, hairspray, and dry shampoo.

Hair Care for Big Forehead

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for big forehead:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and hydration to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut your hair to flatter your big forehead and feel stylish and confident. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
