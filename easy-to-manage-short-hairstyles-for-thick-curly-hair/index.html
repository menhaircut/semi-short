Easy To Manage Short Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair

Thick curly hair can be a beautiful and unique feature, but it can also be a challenge to manage. If you're tired of dealing with unruly curls, a short hairstyle may be the solution you're looking for. In this article, we'll explore some easy to manage short hairstyles for thick curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles for thick curly hair are a great way to tame your locks and make them more manageable. Not only do shorter hairstyles require less maintenance, but they also add volume and texture to your hair. The key is finding the right style that works for your hair type and face shape. Here are some easy to manage short hairstyles for thick curly hair that you can try.

How to

Before you decide on a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and face shape. If you have thick curly hair, you'll want to choose a style that works with your natural texture rather than against it. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Look for hairstyles that add volume and texture
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of styling or maintenance
  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some general steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product that works for your hair type
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls
  4. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls and add volume
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or styling cream

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your short curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible, as it can damage your hair
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thick curly hair:

What are the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair?

Some popular short hairstyles for thick curly hair include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag haircut.

Can short hairstyles work for all face shapes?

Yes, there are short hairstyles that work for all face shapes. It's important to choose a style that flatters your features and balances your proportions.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and manageable curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using hot tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray if you must
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz

With these easy to manage short hairstyles for thick curly hair and some proper hair care, you can embrace your natural texture and rock a stylish, low-maintenance look.
