Medium Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50

Written by Oliver Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes and so does our face shape. One of the most common face shapes for women over 50 is a square face. If you have a square face, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your facial features. But don't worry, we have got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best medium hairstyles for square faces over 50.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 50

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it is essential to know how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape. When you have a square face, you want to soften the angles and create a more feminine look. The best way to do this is by choosing hairstyles that add texture and volume to your hair. You also want to avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or severe, as they can make your face appear even more square.

Step by Step Guide to Medium Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

1. Layered Bob - A layered bob is an excellent option for square faces over 50. It adds texture and volume to your hair, and the layers soften the angles of your face. You can opt for a short or long bob, depending on your preference.

2. Shoulder-Length Waves - Soft, loose waves are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. If you have shoulder-length hair, consider adding some waves to create a more feminine look. You can use a curling iron or a flat iron to create these waves.

3. Side-Swept Bangs - Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften the angles of your face. They also add texture and volume to your hair. You can opt for long or short side-swept bangs, depending on your preference.

4. Layered Lob - If you prefer longer hair, consider a layered lob. A lob is a longer version of a bob, and it falls just above the shoulders. The layers add texture and volume to your hair, and the length softens the angles of your face.

Tips for Styling Medium Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

1. Use Texturizing Products - Texturizing products, such as mousse or sea salt spray, can add volume and texture to your hair. Apply these products to damp hair, and then blow-dry your hair to create the desired look.

2. Use a Round Brush - When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to add volume and texture. Wrap your hair around the brush and then blow-dry it in an upward motion to create volume.

3. Avoid Center Parts - Center parts can make your face appear even more square. Instead, opt for a side part, which will soften the angles of your face.

FAQs About Medium Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 50

Q. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a square face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a square face. However, make sure to add some texture and volume to your hair to soften the angles of your face.

Q. Are bangs a good option for square faces over 50?

A. Yes, bangs are a great option for square faces over 50. Side-swept bangs are a better option than blunt bangs, as they soften the angles of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Over 50

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner - As we age, our hair becomes drier, so it is essential to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage - When using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

3. Get Regular Haircuts - Regular haircuts will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a haircut every six to eight weeks.

Medium hairstyles for square faces over 50 can be flattering and feminine. By choosing the right hairstyle and using the right hair care products, you can create a look that flatters your facial features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
