Wavy Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to maintain, then the wavy top short sides might just be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to achieve it, what to expect, and how to take care of it. So, let's get started!

Description

The wavy top short sides is a hairstyle that features a longer, wavy top combined with short sides and back. The contrast between the two lengths creates a stylish and edgy look that's perfect for men who want to stand out from the crowd. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preferences and the occasion.

How to Achieve Wavy Top Short Sides

Getting the wavy top short sides is relatively easy, but it requires some effort and patience. Here's how to achieve this hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

The first step is to get a haircut that's suitable for the wavy top short sides. You'll need to have your hair cut short on the sides and back, while keeping the top longer. You can ask your barber or stylist for a textured crop or a disconnected undercut, which are two popular hairstyles that work well with wavy hair.

Step 2: Grow out your hair

If your hair is naturally straight or curly, you'll need to grow it out a bit to achieve the wavy top. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to create waves or use a sea salt spray to enhance your natural texture. You can also use a curling iron or rollers to create more defined waves.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once you have the right haircut and length, it's time to style your hair. You can use a hair wax, pomade, or clay to create a messy or slicked-back look. You can also experiment with different partings, such as a side part or a middle part, to see what suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Top Short Sides

Here are some tips to help you maintain your wavy top short sides:

- Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to moisturize and nourish your hair. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your natural waves. - Trim your hair regularly to keep it neat and tidy. - Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the wavy top short sides:

Q: Is the wavy top short sides suitable for all hair types?

A: The wavy top short sides is best suited for men with wavy or curly hair. If you have straight hair, you'll need to use styling products and techniques to create waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: Can I style my wavy top short sides in different ways?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks, such as a messy or slicked-back style.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your wavy top short sides looking its best:

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products that can damage your hair. - Eat a healthy and balanced diet to provide your hair with the nutrients it needs. - Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or a scarf. - Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the wavy top short sides with confidence and style. Remember to experiment with different looks and have fun with your hair!
