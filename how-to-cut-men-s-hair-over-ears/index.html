How To Cut Men s Hair Over Ears A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting hair can be a daunting task, especially for those who are new to it. However, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can give a great haircut. In this article, we will be discussing how to cut men's hair over ears. This is a popular hairstyle that many men prefer, and it's important to know how to achieve it. So, let's get started!

Description

The hairstyle we will be discussing is one where the hair is cut to a length of about an inch or two above the ear. This gives a clean and neat look, and the hair is not covering the ears. This style is popular among men of all ages and can be achieved with a little practice.

How to Cut Men's Hair Over Ears

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to cutting men's hair over ears is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Brush
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Make sure that all your tools are clean and in good condition.

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

The next step is to prepare the hair for cutting. Wet the hair with water and comb it straight. Use a clipper to trim the hair at the back and sides of the head. This will give you a good base to work with.

Step 3: Cut the Hair Over the Ears

Once you have prepared the hair, it's time to cut the hair over the ears. Use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Start at the top of the ear and work your way down. Use a comb to hold the hair in place and cut it neatly.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After cutting the hair over the ears, you need to blend it with the rest of the hair. Use scissors or a clipper to blend the hair so that it looks natural. You can also use a comb to blend the hair and make it look even.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting the hair over the ears, it's important to check your work. Use a mirror to check the back and sides of the head. Make sure that the hair is even and looks natural.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair over ears:

  • Use sharp scissors.
  • Trim the hair slowly and carefully.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Blend the hair to make it look natural.

FAQ

Q: How long should the hair be over the ears?

A: The hair should be about an inch or two above the ear.

Q: Should I wet the hair before cutting it?

A: Yes, it's best to wet the hair before cutting it. This makes it easier to work with and helps you get a more even cut.

Q: Can I cut my own hair over the ears?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair over the ears. However, it's best to have someone else do it for you if you're not experienced with cutting hair.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair over the ears, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. You can also use a styling product to keep the hair in place.

Cutting men's hair over ears is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can give a great haircut. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be able to achieve the perfect cut every time. Good luck!
