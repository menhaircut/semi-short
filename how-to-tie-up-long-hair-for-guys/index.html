How To Tie Up Long Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair has always been a statement for guys. It’s bold, daring and stylish. But it can get quite frustrating when it gets in your face all the time. The good news is that there are many ways to tie up long hair for guys that are both practical and fashionable. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the steps of tying up your long hair and give you some tips on how to take care of it.

How to Tie Up Long Hair for Guys

Tying up long hair for guys can be quite simple. The key is to find a style that suits your hair type and face shape. Here are some popular methods:

Bun

The bun is a classic style for both men and women. It’s easy to do and works for most hair types. Simply gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun. Use a hair tie to secure it in place. You can leave the bun loose or use bobby pins to secure it.

Ponytail

The ponytail is another classic style that works for most hair types. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and use a hair tie to secure it. You can leave the ponytail loose or use bobby pins to secure it.

Braid

The braid is a popular choice for guys with long hair. It’s a great way to keep your hair out of your face and can look quite stylish. Simply divide your hair into three sections and weave them together. Use a hair tie to secure the end.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to tie up long hair for guys:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to tie up.

Step 2: Decide on a Style

Decide on a style that suits your hair type and face shape. The bun, ponytail and braid are popular choices.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair into a ponytail or the base of the style you’ve chosen.

Step 4: Twist or Braid Your Hair

Twist or braid your hair depending on the style you’ve chosen.

Step 5: Secure with a Hair Tie

Use a hair tie to secure your hair in place. Make sure it’s tight enough to hold your hair securely.

Step 6: Use Bobby Pins (Optional)

If your hair is particularly long or thick, you may need to use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Tips for Tying Up Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you tie up your long hair:

Tip 1: Use a Hair Tie

Always use a hair tie when tying up your hair. This will help to keep it in place and prevent it from falling out.

Tip 2: Use Bobby Pins

If your hair is particularly long or thick, you may need to use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. Don’t be afraid to try something new.

Tip 4: Use Hair Products

Use hair products such as gel or wax to help keep your hair in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I tie up my long hair if it’s curly?

A: Yes, you can tie up your long curly hair. Just make sure to use a hair tie that won’t damage your hair.

Q: How long does my hair need to be to tie it up?

A: Your hair needs to be at least shoulder length to tie it up.

Q: Can I tie up my long hair if it’s thin?

A: Yes, you can tie up your long thin hair. Just be gentle when tying it up to avoid breakage.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your long hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons. These can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Tip 4: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to tie up your long hair in a way that’s both practical and stylish.
