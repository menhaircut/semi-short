How To Make Low Ponytail Look Good

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old high ponytail? Low ponytail is a great alternative that can give you a chic and sophisticated look. In this blog post, we will share with you how to make low ponytail look good step by step, along with some tips, FAQs and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Low Ponytail Look Good
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Make Your Low Ponytail Look Even Better
  • FAQs

How to Make Low Ponytail Look Good

A low ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. Here are the steps to make low ponytail look good:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow drying it. Apply a heat protectant to protect your hair from damage. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. If you have fine hair, you can apply a volumizing mousse to add some texture and volume.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and clip it away. This section will be used later to add height to your ponytail. Then, create a part in the middle of your head and separate your hair into two sections.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it with a hair tie. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it causes a headache. Use a fine-tooth comb to smooth any flyaways or bumps.

Step 4: Add Height

Take the section of hair that you clipped away earlier and tease it with a comb. Then, gently pull it back and secure it with bobby pins. This will add height to your ponytail and create a more elegant look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your low ponytail in place, spray it with a light hold hairspray. This will prevent any flyaways and keep your hair looking neat and polished.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to make low ponytail look good:

  • Prep your hair by washing and drying it.
  • Section your hair and tie it at the nape of your neck.
  • Add height to your ponytail by teasing and securing a section of hair at the crown of your head.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips to Make Your Low Ponytail Look Even Better

Here are some tips to make your low ponytail look even better:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color.
  • Apply a shine serum to add some gloss to your hair.
  • Add some accessories, such as a ribbon or a hair clip, to make your low ponytail look more stylish.
  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks.

FAQs

1. Can I make a low ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can make a low ponytail with short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and add a volumizing product to give your hair some texture.

2. How can I make my low ponytail look more formal?

To make your low ponytail look more formal, add some height to it by teasing the crown of your head. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a flower or a jewel, to make it look more elegant.

Hair Care Tips for Low Ponytail

Here are some hair care tips for low ponytail:

  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before tying it.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to make low ponytail look good, it's time to try it out for yourself. With these simple steps and tips, you can create a chic and sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion.
