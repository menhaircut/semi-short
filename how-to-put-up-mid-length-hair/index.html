How To Put Up Mid Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your mid length hair always getting in your face? Do you want to try out new hairstyles but don't know where to start? With this step-by-step guide, you'll learn how to put up mid length hair in a variety of styles, from a simple ponytail to a chic updo.

How to Put Up Mid Length Hair

Mid length hair can be tricky to style, especially if you're used to having longer or shorter hair. But with a few simple techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that will keep your hair off your face and looking great all day long.

Ponytail

A simple ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well for mid length hair. To create a ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic.
  4. If desired, use a hair tie to wrap around the base of the ponytail to conceal the hair elastic.

For a more polished look, you can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin.

Bun

A bun is another easy hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. Here's how to create a bun:

  1. Start with a ponytail, as described above.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

You can also create a messy bun by pulling out a few strands of hair around your face and teasing the bun for volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Whether you're looking for a casual or formal hairstyle, these step-by-step instructions will help you create a variety of looks for mid length hair.

Half Up Half Down

  1. Starting at the temples, take a section of hair from the front of your head on each side.
  2. Twist each section of hair and bring them to the back of your head.
  3. Secure the two sections of hair together with a hair elastic.

You can leave the remaining hair down or add curls or waves for a more formal look.

Fishtail Braid

  1. Divide your hair into two equal sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section.
  3. Repeat on the right side, taking a small section of hair from the outside of the right section and crossing it over to the left section.
  4. Continue crossing small sections of hair over from each side until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the braid with a hair elastic.

You can make the braid tighter or looser by pulling on the sections of hair as you go.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your mid length hair:

  • Invest in good quality hair ties and bobby pins to keep your hairstyles in place.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hair clips.
  • Try out different products like mousse, hairspray, and texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

How often should I wash my mid length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Can I still wear my hair down with mid length hair?

Of course! Mid length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including wearing it down. Try adding waves or curls for a more glamorous look.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Proper hair care is important for keeping your mid length hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create a variety of stylish and practical hairstyles for your mid length hair. Whether you prefer a sleek bun or a messy braid, there's a hairstyle that's perfect for you.
