How To Care For Permanent Straight Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently gotten a permanent straightening treatment on your hair, congratulations! You're now the proud owner of sleek, straight locks that will last for months. However, with great hair comes great responsibility. It's important to care for your straightened hair properly to ensure it stays healthy and looks its best. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to care for permanent straight hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • What is Permanent Straightening?
  • How to Care for Permanent Straight Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Final Thoughts

What is Permanent Straightening?

Permanent straightening is a chemical process that permanently alters the structure of your hair to make it straight. The process involves applying a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its natural shape and texture. Once the bonds are broken down, the hair is rinsed, dried, and flat ironed to create a straight, sleek look. The effects of permanent straightening can last for several months.

How to Care for Permanent Straight Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to help you care for your permanent straight hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. To keep your straight hair looking its best, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for straight hair, as these will help to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

2. Avoid heat styling

One of the benefits of permanent straightening is that you don't need to use heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons to achieve a sleek look. However, if you do choose to use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

3. Don't wash your hair too often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair hydrated and protected from environmental damage. Look for a lightweight, non-greasy formula that won't weigh your hair down.

5. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your straight hair:

1. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. Silk is also gentler on your hair than cotton, which can be rough and cause damage.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentle on your hair and won't cause breakage or damage. Use it to detangle your hair after washing, and avoid using a brush, which can be too harsh on your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, causing it to dry out and become brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair when you're out in the sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I color my hair after getting a permanent straightening treatment?

Yes, you can color your hair after getting a permanent straightening treatment, but it's important to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair to give it time to recover from the straightening process.

2. How often should I get a permanent straightening treatment?

You should wait at least six months between permanent straightening treatments to give your hair time to recover. However, the exact timing will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows.

3. Can I swim with permanent straightened hair?

Yes, you can swim with permanent straightened hair, but it's important to protect your hair from chlorine and salt water, which can be damaging. Wear a swim cap or apply a leave-in conditioner before swimming to protect your hair.

Final Thoughts

Caring for permanent straight hair requires a little bit of extra effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your straight hair looking sleek and shiny for months to come.
