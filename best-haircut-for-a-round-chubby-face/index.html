Best Haircut For A Round Chubby Face Flattering Styles To Try In 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your round and chubby face? One of the easiest ways to enhance your natural features and feel more confident is to choose a flattering haircut that complements your face shape. In this article, we’ll explore the best haircut for a round chubby face, along with some tips and tricks for styling and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Round Chubby Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut
  4. Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Haircut
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Round Chubby Faces

Description

A round and chubby face is characterized by soft curves and a lack of defined angles. This face shape can be beautiful and youthful, but it can also be challenging to style with the wrong haircut. The goal is to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. Below are some of the best haircuts for round and chubby faces.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Round Chubby Face

When selecting a haircut for a round chubby face, you want to aim for styles that add height and length to the top of the head. This helps to elongate the face and balance out the roundness. Avoid styles with blunt bangs or heavy layers that add volume to the sides of the face. Instead, opt for layers that start at the cheekbones or jawline, and choose a hairstyle that frames the face without adding bulk.

Best Haircuts for Round Chubby Faces

The following haircuts are best suited for round and chubby faces:

  • Long layered waves or curls
  • Shoulder-length blunt bob
  • Layered pixie cut
  • Side-swept bangs with layers
  • Face-framing layers with a center part

Worst Haircuts for Round Chubby Faces

Avoid the following haircuts if you have a round and chubby face:

  • Blunt bangs
  • Chin-length bob
  • Slicked-back ponytail
  • Short cropped cut
  • Bob with heavy layers

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Once you’ve chosen the best haircut for your round and chubby face, it’s essential to know how to style it correctly. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your haircut:

  1. Start with clean, freshly washed hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember to avoid adding too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear rounder. Instead, focus on adding height and length to the top of your head.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist for a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round chubby face?

The best haircut for a round and chubby face is one that adds height and length to the top of the head while framing the face without adding bulk. Some good options include long layered waves, a shoulder-length bob, or a layered pixie cut.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round chubby face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, or heavy layers. Instead, opt for styles that elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How often should I visit my hairstylist for a trim?

For best results, visit your hairstylist for a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care for Round Chubby Faces

Finally, it’s essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and choosing the best haircut for your round and chubby face, you can enhance your natural features and feel more confident in your appearance. Remember to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you!
