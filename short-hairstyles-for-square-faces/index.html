Short Hairstyles For Square Faces Tips And Ideas

Written by Robby Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, you might have noticed that some hairstyles just don't look as good on you as they do on others. That's because certain styles can accentuate the angles of your face, while others soften them. If you're considering a short haircut, you'll want to choose a style that complements your square face shape.

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and straight sides. If you have a square face, you can balance out these features by choosing a hairstyle that softens your angles and adds some roundness to your face. Short haircuts can work well for square faces, as long as they're styled in a way that flatters your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Square Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for square faces, keep these tips in mind:

  • Look for styles that add volume to the top of your head, which will balance out the width of your forehead.
  • Avoid styles that are too straight or angular, as they can make your face look even squarer.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut, which will soften your features and add movement to your hair.
  • Choose hairstyles that have some roundness to them, such as a bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Square Faces

Follow these steps to style short hair for square faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair, focusing on the top of your head.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume.
  4. If you have bangs, blow dry them to the side to create a soft, face-framing effect.
  5. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Square Faces

Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims, as short hair can start to look unkempt quickly.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washings to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace your texture and let it shine.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Square Faces

What are the best short hairstyles for square faces?

Some of the best short hairstyles for square faces include bobs, pixie cuts, and layered shags. Look for styles that add volume to the top of your head and have some roundness to them.

Can I wear bangs with a square face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a square face. Side-swept bangs can create a soft, face-framing effect that flatters square faces.

How do I know if a short hairstyle will look good on me?

The best way to know if a short hairstyle will look good on you is to try it out! Consult with your stylist to find a style that flatters your face shape and fits your lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Square Faces

Short hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage and damage.

10 Short Hairstyles for Square Faces

Here are 10 short hairstyles that flatter square faces:

  1. Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  2. Textured bob with layers
  3. Asymmetrical bob
  4. Short shag with bangs
  5. Curly pixie cut
  6. Chin-length bob with side-swept bangs
  7. Wavy bob
  8. Layered pixie cut
  9. Sleek bob with blunt bangs
  10. Mohawk-inspired pixie cut

Conclusion

If you have a square face, don't be afraid to try a short hairstyle! With the right cut and styling, you can accentuate your best features and create a look that's uniquely you. Keep these tips in mind when choosing and maintaining your short hairstyle, and consult with your stylist to find the perfect look for your face shape.
