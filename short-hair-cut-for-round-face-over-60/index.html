Short Hair Cut For Round Face Over 60

Written by Aprilia Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60 with a round face and looking for a short hair cut, you have come to the right place. Short hair cuts are a versatile option and can be a great choice for women over 60 with round faces. In this post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right short hair cut for your round face, tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A short hair cut for round face over 60 is a style that works well with a round face shape. It can help to make the face look slimmer and more elongated. The right short hair cut depends on the shape of your face, hair texture, and personal preferences. It is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and suits your lifestyle.

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Cut for Round Face Over 60

Choosing the right short hair cut for your round face over 60 involves considering several factors, including:

Face Shape

The first factor to consider is your face shape. Women with round faces should choose a hair cut that adds height and volume to the crown of the head. This will help to elongate the face and make it look slimmer. Avoid hair cuts that are too short or too long, as they can make the face look wider.

Hair Texture

The second factor to consider is your hair texture. Women with fine hair should choose a hair cut that adds volume and texture, while women with thick hair should choose a hair cut that thins out the hair and adds movement.

Personal Preferences

The third factor to consider is your personal preferences. Choose a hair cut that you feel comfortable with and suits your lifestyle. Consider the amount of time you are willing to spend styling your hair each day and whether you want a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hair cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the right short hair cut for your round face over 60:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are roughly the same, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Research different short hair cut styles that work well with a round face shape. Look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of the head, such as pixie cuts, bob cuts, or layered cuts.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine which short hair cut will work best for your face shape and hair texture. They can also provide you with tips on how to maintain your new hair cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair cut for round face over 60:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and volume to the crown of the head.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your hair cut.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair cuts for round face over 60:

Q: Are short hair cuts suitable for women over 60 with round faces?

A: Yes, short hair cuts can be a great choice for women over 60 with round faces. They can help to make the face look slimmer and more elongated.

Q: What are some popular short hair cut styles for round faces over 60?

A: Some popular short hair cut styles for round faces over 60 include pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair cut?

A: You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hair cut.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain the health and appearance of your short hair cut for round face over 60. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Now that you know how to choose the right short hair cut for your round face over 60, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look!
