How To Make A Chignon With Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for an elegant and chic hairstyle for your shoulder-length hair? A chignon might be the perfect solution! In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to make a chignon with shoulder length hair, and provide you with some useful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make a Chignon with Shoulder Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making a Chignon with Shoulder Length Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Chignon Looking Great

Description

A chignon is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for special occasions, such as weddings, proms, or formal dinners. It involves pulling the hair back into a bun or knot at the back of the head or nape of the neck. The chignon can be sleek and polished or tousled and messy, depending on your preference and the occasion.

How to Make a Chignon with Shoulder Length Hair

Making a chignon with shoulder length hair might seem challenging, but it’s actually quite simple. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. If you have fine hair, you might want to add some texture by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck, as if you were going to make a low ponytail.
  3. Twist your hair to create a rope-like strand.
  4. Curl the twisted strand into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. If you want a more polished look, you can use a hairnet to cover the bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  6. Use hairspray to keep your chignon in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips for Making a Chignon with Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you make a chignon with shoulder length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less visible.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as sleek and polished or messy and tousled, to find the look that suits you best.
  • If you have thin hair, you can add volume to your chignon by teasing the hair at the crown of your head before making the bun.
  • If you have curly hair, you can enhance your natural curls by using a curl-enhancing mousse or cream before making the chignon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about making a chignon with shoulder length hair:

  • Can I make a chignon with shoulder length hair? Yes, you can! The key is to twist your hair and create a bun at the nape of your neck.
  • Do I need any special tools or products to make a chignon with shoulder length hair? You don’t need any special tools, but you might want to use bobby pins, a hairnet, and hairspray to keep your chignon in place.
  • How long does it take to make a chignon with shoulder length hair? It shouldn’t take more than 10-15 minutes, depending on your skill level and the complexity of the style.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Chignon Looking Great

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your chignon looking great:

  • Don’t wash your hair on the day you’re planning to make a chignon, as freshly washed hair can be too slippery.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you’re using a curling iron or other heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make the chignon look greasy or messy.
  • If you’re going to be outside for an extended period of time, protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and the step-by-step guide, you should be able to make a beautiful chignon with your shoulder length hair. Whether you’re attending a wedding, prom, or formal dinner, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and elegant.
