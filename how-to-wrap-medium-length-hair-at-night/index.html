How To Wrap Medium Length Hair At Night A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 12, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how important it is to keep it looking great. One of the best ways to do this is by wrapping it at night. In this post, we’ll show you how to wrap medium length hair at night in a way that will keep it looking great all day long.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wrap Medium Length Hair at Night
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wrapping Medium Length Hair at Night
  4. FAQs

How to Wrap Medium Length Hair at Night

Wrapping your hair at night is a great way to protect it from breakage and keep it looking great. Here’s how to wrap medium length hair at night:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you wrap your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking great.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will help to remove tangles and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Step 4: Use a silk or satin scarf

Wrap your hair with a silk or satin scarf. This will help to protect your hair from breakage and keep it looking great.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to wrapping your medium length hair at night:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Comb your hair
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner
  4. Use a silk or satin scarf
  5. Wrap the scarf around your head
  6. Tuck the ends of the scarf under
  7. Secure the scarf with bobby pins
  8. Go to sleep
  9. In the morning, remove the scarf
  10. Gently comb your hair

Tips for Wrapping Medium Length Hair at Night

Here are some tips to help you wrap your medium length hair at night:

  • Use a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Wrap your hair loosely to prevent breakage
  • Secure the scarf with bobby pins to keep it in place
  • Comb your hair gently in the morning to prevent breakage

FAQs

How often should I wrap my medium length hair at night?

You should wrap your medium length hair at night every night to prevent breakage and keep it looking great.

Can I use a cotton scarf to wrap my hair at night?

No. Cotton scarves can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Use a silk or satin scarf instead.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your medium length hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-drying
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

By following these tips, you can keep your medium length hair looking great and protect it from breakage.
