How To Make Dreads With Short Hair And No Products

Written by Christine Jul 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your short hair and want to try something new? Have you always wanted to try dreadlocks but don't want to use any products? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will show you how to make dreads with short hair and no products. Follow these steps and achieve the look you've always wanted!

How to

The first step to making dreads with short hair and no products is to section your hair. Use a comb to create even sections of hair, about the width of your finger. The size of the sections will determine the thickness of your dreads. If you want thinner dreads, make smaller sections. If you want thicker dreads, make larger sections.

Once you have sectioned your hair, twist each section tightly in one direction until it starts to curl up on itself. This will create the dread. As you twist, keep the hair as close to the scalp as possible to create a tight dread.

Use a small elastic band to secure the end of the dread. Make sure the elastic band is tight, but not too tight that it cuts off circulation to your hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your head is full of dreads.

Step by Step

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to create even sections of hair, about the width of your finger. The size of the sections will determine the thickness of your dreads. If you want thinner dreads, make smaller sections. If you want thicker dreads, make larger sections.

Step 2: Twist Each Section

Once you have sectioned your hair, twist each section tightly in one direction until it starts to curl up on itself. This will create the dread. As you twist, keep the hair as close to the scalp as possible to create a tight dread.

Step 3: Secure with Elastic Bands

Use a small elastic band to secure the end of the dread. Make sure the elastic band is tight, but not too tight that it cuts off circulation to your hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your head is full of dreads.

Tips

  • Twist each section tightly to create a tight dread.
  • Use small elastic bands to secure the ends of each dread.
  • Be patient, creating dreads takes time.
  • Consider using a hair tool like a crochet hook to tighten your dreads over time.

FAQ

Can I make dreads with short hair?

Yes, you can make dreads with short hair. Follow the steps outlined in this article to achieve the look you want.

Do I need to use products to make dreads?

No, you don't need to use any products to make dreads. This article shows you how to make dreads with short hair and no products.

How long do dreads take to form?

Creating dreads takes time. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months for your dreads to fully form and mature.

Hair Care

After you have created your dreads, it's important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your dreads regularly to keep them clean.
  • Use a residue-free shampoo to prevent buildup.
  • Avoid using conditioner as it can loosen your dreads.
  • Use a hair tool like a crochet hook to tighten your dreads over time.

Follow these tips and enjoy your new look!
