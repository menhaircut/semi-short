How To Sleep When You Have Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have curly hair and struggle with getting a good night's sleep? Tossing and turning can cause your curls to become frizzy and tangled, making it difficult to style them the next day. Fortunately, there are ways to sleep with curly hair without ruining your locks. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you get a good night's sleep and keep your curls looking great.

How to Sleep When You Have Curly Hair

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair Before Bed

Before you hit the hay, make sure your hair is prepped for sleep. You can do this by applying a leave-in conditioner or a curl definer to your hair. This will help to keep your curls moisturized and defined while you sleep.

Step 2: Choose the Right Pillowcase

The type of pillowcase you use can make a big difference for your curls. Silk or satin pillowcases are recommended for curly hair as they are gentler on the hair and help to reduce friction. This can help to prevent tangles and frizz while you sleep.

Step 3: Use a Satin Bonnet or Scarf

Another option is to wear a satin bonnet or scarf to bed. This will help to protect your curls from rubbing against your pillow and causing friction. It will also help to keep your curls in place and prevent them from becoming tangled while you sleep.

Step 4: Pineapple Your Hair

The pineapple method is a popular technique for sleeping with curly hair. To pineapple your hair, gather your curls on the top of your head and secure them with a scrunchie or hair tie. This will help to keep your curls in place and prevent them from getting squished while you sleep.

Step 5: Refresh Your Curls in the Morning

In the morning, you may find that your curls have become a bit flattened or frizzy. To refresh your curls, you can spritz them with a bit of water or a curl refresher spray. You can also scrunch your hair to help define your curls and add volume.

Tips for Sleeping with Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get a good night's sleep and keep your curls looking great:

  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair as this can cause your curls to become flat and frizzy
  • Don't use hair elastics with metal parts as they can snag and damage your hair
  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases as they can cause friction and lead to tangles and frizz
  • If you have long hair, braid your hair before bed to help keep your curls in place

FAQ About Sleeping with Curly Hair

Can I sleep with my curly hair down?

While it's possible to sleep with your curly hair down, it's not recommended as it can lead to tangles and frizz. It's best to secure your curls in a pineapple or wear a satin bonnet or scarf to bed.

How often should I refresh my curls?

This will depend on your hair type and how well your curls hold their shape. Some people may need to refresh their curls every day, while others can go a few days between washes. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using products with sulfates as they can dry out your hair and strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your curls moisturized
  • Don't wash your hair every day as this can also dry out your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause breakage and frizz

By following these tips and tricks, you can sleep peacefully knowing that your curls are protected and will look great in the morning.
