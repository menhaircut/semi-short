Best Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

If you're over 50 and have a heart-shaped face, you may be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your features. Heart-shaped faces are typically characterized by a broader forehead, narrower jawline, and high cheekbones. In this article, we'll be sharing the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Description

Heart-shaped faces are a popular face shape among women over 50. This face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. This means that the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50 are those that balance out the wider forehead and accentuate the cheekbones.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we delve into the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to draw the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the outline.
  4. Identify which shape your face most closely resembles:
    • Round
    • Oval
    • Square
    • Heart
    • Diamond
    • Triangle

Best Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces Over 50

Now that you've determined that you have a heart-shaped face, let's take a look at the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces over 50:

Shoulder Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that flatters heart-shaped faces by adding volume to the chin area. This style is low maintenance and easy to style on a daily basis.

Long Layers

Long layers that fall around the face are a great way to soften the forehead and draw attention to the cheekbones. This style can be achieved with a simple blowout and is perfect for women who prefer longer hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to balance out a wider forehead and add volume to the chin area. This style is low maintenance and can be achieved with a simple blowout.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that can flatter heart-shaped faces. This style adds volume to the top of the head and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal style.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great way to add volume to the chin area and draw attention to the cheekbones. This style is low maintenance and can be achieved with a simple blowout.

Short Shag

A short shag is a great way to add volume to the crown of the head and balance out a wider forehead. This style is low maintenance and can be achieved with a simple blowout.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a bold and daring hairstyle that can flatter heart-shaped faces. This style adds volume to the chin area and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your heart-shaped face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can women over 50 wear long hair?

A: Yes, women over 50 can wear long hair. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Q: Is a pixie cut low maintenance?

A: Yes, a pixie cut is low maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal style.

Q: Can women over 50 wear bangs?

A: Yes, women over 50 can wear bangs. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some hair care tips specifically for women with heart-shaped faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead, as this can make your face appear top-heavy.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, as this can balance out a wider forehead.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hair products that weigh your hair down, as this can make your face appear wider.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you're sure to find a look that flatters your heart-shaped face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
