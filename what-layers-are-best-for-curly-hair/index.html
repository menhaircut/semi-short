What Layers Are Best For Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and voluminous, it can also be challenging to manage. One way to make curly hair more manageable is by adding layers. In this guide, we'll discuss what layers are best for curly hair, how to style them, and how to care for your curls.

  • Best Layers for Curly Hair
  • How to Style Layers

What are Layers?

Layers are different lengths of hair that are cut into your existing hair to add dimension, texture, and movement. Layers can be added to all different hair types, but they work especially well for curly hair. Layers can help reduce bulk, prevent triangle hair, and add bounce and volume to your curls.

Best Layers for Curly Hair

When it comes to curly hair, the best layers are long layers. Long layers will help keep the curls intact while adding volume and texture. Short layers can disrupt the curls and make them frizzy. It's also best to avoid choppy layers, as they can make the curls look uneven and choppy.

Another consideration is face shape. For oval or heart-shaped faces, layers that start at the chin or collarbone work well. For round faces, longer layers that start below the chin can help elongate the face.

Types of Layers for Curly Hair

There are a few different types of layers that work well for curly hair:

  • Long Layers: These layers start at the chin or collarbone and add volume and movement to your curls.
  • Face-Framing Layers: These layers start at the cheekbones and add definition to your curls.
  • Blunt Layers: These layers are cut straight across and add shape and structure to your curls.

How to Style Layers

Styling layered curly hair can seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disrupting them
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz
  • Scrunch your curls with a curl cream or gel to enhance their shape

Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid heat styling, as this can damage your curls
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

FAQ

Can layers make curly hair frizzy?

Short, choppy layers can disrupt the curl pattern and make curly hair look frizzy. Long layers, on the other hand, can help keep the curls intact while adding volume and texture.

How often should I get my curly hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your curly hair trimmed every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will dry your curls without disrupting them and help enhance their shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid heat styling, as this can damage your curls
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

Adding layers to your curly hair can help give it more dimension, texture, and movement. By choosing the right type of layer and following proper hair care techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.
