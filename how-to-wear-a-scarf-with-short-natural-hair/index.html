How To Wear A Scarf With Short Natural Hair

Written by Robby Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Scarves are a fantastic accessory that can add a pop of color and style to any outfit. However, for those with short natural hair, it can be challenging to figure out how to wear a scarf without it overwhelming your hair. In this post, we'll cover how to wear a scarf with short natural hair and provide some tips and tricks to make it work for you.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear a Scarf with Short Natural Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Wear a Scarf with Short Natural Hair

There are a few different ways to wear a scarf with short natural hair, but the most common method is to tie it around your head like a headband. This look is both stylish and functional, keeping your hair out of your face while adding a pop of color to your outfit.

To achieve this look, start by selecting a scarf that is the right size for your head. You want it to be long enough to tie comfortably, but not so long that it overwhelms your hair. A square scarf works well for this look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to tie a scarf around your head like a headband:

  1. Fold the scarf diagonally to form a triangle.
  2. Place the scarf on top of your head with the point facing towards your forehead.
  3. Tie the two ends of the scarf together at the back of your head.
  4. Tuck any loose ends of the scarf under the knot to create a neat finish.

Voila! You now have a stylish headband that will keep your hair out of your face and add a pop of color to your outfit.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make wearing a scarf with short natural hair even easier:

  • Choose a scarf that is made from a lightweight material, such as cotton or silk, to avoid adding too much bulk to your hair.
  • Experiment with different scarf tying techniques, such as twisting the scarf into a rope before tying it around your head or tying it in a knot at the top of your head.
  • Pair your scarf with a bold lipstick or statement earrings to draw attention away from your hair and add some extra style to your look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear a scarf with short natural hair if I have a tapered cut?

Absolutely! A scarf can add some extra style to a tapered cut, particularly if you choose a scarf with a bold print or bright color.

What if my scarf keeps slipping off?

If your scarf keeps slipping off, try tying it a little tighter or adding some bobby pins to hold it in place. You can also try wrapping the scarf around your head twice for a more secure fit.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a scarf can help protect your hair from damage, particularly if you choose a scarf made from a gentle material like silk. However, it's still important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

Here are some hair care tips for those with short natural hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear a scarf with short natural hair and look stylish and put-together every day.
