Hair Cuts To Suit Round Faces

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering which hair cuts would best suit your features. Round faces have a soft, circular shape with equal width and length, making it essential to choose a hairstyle that helps elongate and balance the face. In this post, we will discuss the best hair cuts to suit round faces, including How to, Step by step, Tips, FAQ, and Hair Care tips.

Description

There are several haircuts that can flatter round faces, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs. These haircuts can help create the illusion of a slimmer and longer face. Additionally, incorporating volume to the crown can also help elongate the face. However, it's essential to avoid blunt cuts and center parts, which can add width to the face.

How to

If you're looking for a haircut to suit your round face, here's how to get started:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.
  2. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Consider adding volume to the crown to elongate the face.
  4. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts, which can add width to the face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut that suits your round face:

  1. Research hairstyles that flatter round faces and choose one that you like.
  2. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if the hairstyle will work for your hair texture and face shape.
  3. Show your hairstylist photos of the hairstyle you want, and discuss any concerns or modifications you may need.
  4. During the haircut, make sure to communicate with your hairstylist and provide feedback on the length and shape of your hair.
  5. After the haircut, style your hair as desired and use hair products that suit your hair type.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for round faces:

  • Consider incorporating layers to add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Side-swept bangs can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks.
  • Opt for haircuts that fall below the chin to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts and bobs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs are all great options for round faces.

Should I avoid blunt cuts if I have a round face?

Yes, blunt cuts can add width to the face, making it appear rounder. It's best to opt for hairstyles that create volume at the crown and avoid adding volume to the sides.

Can I have short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have short hair if you have a round face. However, it's essential to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, such as a pixie cut with volume at the crown.

Hair Care

After getting a haircut that suits your round face, it's essential to take care of your hair properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

In conclusion, choosing a haircut that flatters your round face can be challenging, but it's essential to find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. By following the tips and guidelines in this post, you can find a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture and makes you feel your best.
