Why Does My Head Stick Up At The Back

Written by Petter Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you've ever looked in the mirror and wondered why your head always seems to stick up at the back, you're not alone. This is a common problem that affects many people, and there are several reasons why it might be happening to you. In this article, we'll explore some of the possible causes of this phenomenon, as well as some tips and tricks for how to address it.

Description

When we say that your head is "sticking up" at the back, what we mean is that the hair at the back of your head is standing up, making your head appear taller than it should be. This can be frustrating and embarrassing, especially if you're trying to maintain a neat and tidy appearance.

How to

If you're struggling with a head that sticks up at the back, there are several things you can try to address the issue:

1. Change Your Hairstyle

One of the most common causes of a head that sticks up at the back is an ill-suited hairstyle. If your hair is cut too short or is layered in a way that doesn't suit your natural hair texture, it can be difficult to get it to lie flat. Consider talking to a hair stylist to find a cut that works better for you.

2. Use Hair Products

Another way to combat a head that sticks up at the back is to use hair products that can help tame your hair. Pomade, wax, and hairspray can all be effective at keeping your hair in place and preventing it from standing up.

3. Change Your Pillowcase

Believe it or not, the type of pillowcase you use can also affect the way your hair lies. If you're using a rough or abrasive pillowcase, it can cause your hair to become frizzy and stand up. Switching to a satin or silk pillowcase can help smooth your hair down and prevent it from sticking up.

Step by step

If you're not sure where to start when it comes to addressing a head that sticks up at the back, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Assess Your Hairstyle

The first thing you should do is take a close look at your hairstyle. Is it too short? Is it layered in a way that doesn't suit your hair texture? If so, consider talking to a stylist to find a more suitable cut.

Step 2: Use Hair Products

Once you have a suitable hairstyle, try using hair products to help keep your hair in place. Pomade, wax, and hairspray can all be effective at taming unruly hair.

Step 3: Change Your Pillowcase

Finally, consider switching to a satin or silk pillowcase to help smooth your hair down and prevent it from standing up.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for preventing a head that sticks up at the back:

- Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to standing up. Try washing your hair every other day instead.

- Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush can help prevent your hair from becoming frizzy and standing up.

- Consider Getting a Keratin Treatment

A keratin treatment can help smooth down your hair and prevent it from standing up. Talk to a stylist to see if this might be a good option for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about a head that sticks up at the back:

Why does my head stick up at the back?

There are several reasons why your head might be sticking up at the back, including an ill-suited hairstyle, rough pillowcases, and overwashing your hair.

Can I prevent my head from sticking up at the back?

Yes, there are several steps you can take to prevent your head from sticking up at the back, including changing your hairstyle, using hair products, and switching to a satin or silk pillowcase.

Hair Care

A head that sticks up at the back can be frustrating, but with the right hair care routine, it's a problem that can be solved. By finding a suitable hairstyle, using hair products, and making small changes to your hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent it from standing up at the back.
