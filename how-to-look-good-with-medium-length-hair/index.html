How To Look Good With Medium Length Hair

Written by Frank Oct 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect balance between long and short hair. It is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for many people. However, styling medium length hair can be a challenge, especially if you are unsure of what looks good on you. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to look good with medium length hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Medium Length Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair
  3. Tips for Looking Good with Medium Length Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into how to style medium length hair, it is important to understand what medium length hair is. Generally, medium length hair falls between the chin and shoulders. It is longer than a bob but shorter than long hair.

When it comes to styling medium length hair, there are many options to choose from. You can opt for a sleek and straight look, add some waves or curls, or go for a more textured and messy look. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

1. Sleek and Straight

If you have naturally straight hair, a sleek and straight look can be a great option. This style is easy to achieve with a flat iron and some heat protectant spray. Start by applying the heat protectant spray to your hair and then use the flat iron to straighten your hair. You can also add some shine spray to give your hair a glossy finish.

2. Waves and Curls

If you have wavy or curly hair, you can enhance your natural texture by adding some waves or curls. You can use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves or tight curls. To keep your curls or waves in place, you can use a hairspray or a texturizing spray.

3. Textured and Messy

A textured and messy look can be achieved by using a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray. This style works well for those with fine hair as it adds volume and texture. To achieve this look, apply the texturizing spray to your hair and then scrunch your hair with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair

Now that you know the different ways to style medium length hair, let's take a look at a step-by-step guide to achieving these styles.

1. Sleek and Straight

  1. Wash your hair and apply heat protectant spray.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  4. Apply some shine spray to add some gloss to your hair.

2. Waves and Curls

  1. Wash your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.
  3. Section your hair and use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or tight curls.
  4. Apply some hairspray or texturizing spray to keep your curls or waves in place.

3. Textured and Messy

  1. Wash your hair and apply a texturizing or sea salt spray.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create a messy and textured look.
  4. Apply some hairspray or texturizing spray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Looking Good with Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you look good with medium length hair:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer, flat iron, and curling iron.
  • Use heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works for your hair.
  • Visit a salon regularly to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. However, if you have dry hair, washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. How do I prevent frizz in my medium length hair?

To prevent frizz, avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can rough up the hair cuticles. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water. Apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair while it is still damp and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful medium length hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair when wet to prevent breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can look good with medium length hair and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
