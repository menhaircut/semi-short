How To Cut Teenage Guy Hair At Home The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a parent or guardian of a teenage boy, you may be wondering how to cut his hair at home. With the pandemic forcing many salons to close their doors, cutting your son's hair at home has become a necessity. Not to worry, we have put together a comprehensive guide to help you achieve a great haircut for your teenage boy.

How to

Before you get started, make sure you have the appropriate tools such as a pair of shears, clippers, combs, and a mirror. Here's how to cut teenage guy hair at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash your son's hair with shampoo and conditioner, then towel-dry it. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back, leaving the top longer. Use the scissors to cut the top to the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start with the sides and back of the head. Use the clippers with the appropriate guard to achieve the desired length. Begin at the bottom of the head and work your way up, using an upward motion. Be sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the top to the desired length. Start at the front of the head and work your way back, using a comb to hold the hair in place. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

Blend the hair on top with the sides and back using the scissors. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed. Use the clippers to clean up the hair around the ears and the neckline.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head as you go.
  • Go slow and be patient.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always cut the hair shorter to fix it. If you're really unsure, consider watching some online tutorials or asking a friend or family member for help.

How often should I cut my son's hair?

This depends on how fast his hair grows and the style he wants. As a general rule, most boys need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your son's hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim the hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on the hair.

By following these tips, you can help your son maintain a great haircut and healthy hair.

Conclusion

Cutting teenage guy hair at home doesn't have to be difficult. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve a great haircut for your son. Remember to go slow, start with a longer length, and use a mirror to check your work. If you're still unsure, consider watching some online tutorials or asking for help. By following these tips and taking care of the hair, your son can maintain a great looking haircut for weeks to come.
