What Hair Colour Suits Me Best App The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through Pinterest and Instagram, trying to find the perfect hair colour that suits you? Have you ever wondered what you would look like with a different hair colour, but didn't want to commit to dyeing your hair? Well, you're in luck! There's an app for that - the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app.

Description

The "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app is a virtual hair colour simulation tool that allows you to try on different hair colours before committing to dyeing your hair. The app uses a photo of you to create a 3D model of your face and then applies different hair colours to see which one suits you best. It's like having a personal hairstylist in your pocket!

How to Use the App

Using the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app is easy and straightforward. Here's how:

  1. Download and install the app on your smartphone or tablet.
  2. Take a clear photo of your face, with your hair pulled back or away from your face.
  3. Upload the photo to the app and wait for it to create a 3D model of your face.
  4. Select the hair colour you want to try on and see how it looks on you.
  5. Save the photo or share it with your friends to get their opinion.

It's that simple!

Step by Step Guide

If you're still unsure about how to use the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app, here's a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Download and Install the App

Search for the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app on your smartphone's app store and download and install it. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices, and it's free to download.

Step 2: Take a Clear Photo of Your Face

Find a well-lit area and take a clear photo of your face, with your hair pulled back or away from your face. Make sure your face is in focus and that there are no shadows or glare on your face. The app needs a clear photo to create a 3D model of your face.

Step 3: Upload the Photo to the App

Open the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app and upload the photo you just took. The app will ask you to adjust the position of your face and crop the photo if necessary. Follow the instructions on the screen to ensure that the app can create a 3D model of your face accurately.

Step 4: Select the Hair Colour You Want to Try On

Once the app has created a 3D model of your face, you can start trying on different hair colours. The app has a wide range of hair colours to choose from, including natural colours like brown, blonde, and black, and more unconventional colours like pink, purple, and blue. Select the hair colour you want to try on and wait for the app to apply it to your photo.

Step 5: Save or Share the Photo

Once you've found a hair colour that suits you, you can save the photo to your phone or share it with your friends to get their opinion. The app also allows you to compare different hair colours side-by-side, so you can see which one looks best on you.

Tips for Using the App

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app:

  • Use a clear photo with good lighting and no shadows.
  • Pull your hair back or away from your face to ensure that your face is visible.
  • Try on different hair colours in different lighting conditions to see how they look in different settings.
  • Get a second opinion from a friend or family member before committing to dyeing your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app:

Is the App Accurate?

The app uses advanced 3D modelling technology to create a virtual representation of your face, which allows you to see how different hair colours look on you. While the app is not 100% accurate, it's a great way to get an idea of how a particular hair colour will look on you before committing to dyeing your hair.

Is the App Free?

Yes! The "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app is free to download and use. However, the app may contain ads or offer in-app purchases.

Can I Use the App on Different Devices?

Yes! You can use the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app on any device that supports it, as long as you have a clear photo of your face to upload.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Using the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Best" app is a great way to experiment with different hair colours and find the one that suits you best. So, what are you waiting for? Download the app and start trying on different hair colours today!
