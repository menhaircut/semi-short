How To Curl Hair In Front Of Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curling your hair in front of your face can add a touch of glamour to your look. Whether you're going for a casual or formal occasion, this style can make you look stunning. If you're wondering how to curl hair in front of face, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve this gorgeous hairstyle.

Description

Curling hair in front of the face involves using a curling iron to create loose or tight curls around the front section of your hair. This style frames your face, adds volume and movement to your hair, and gives you a beautiful, soft look. With the right tools, technique, and a few tips, you can create stunning curls in no time.

How to Curl Hair in Front of Face: Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to curl hair in front of face:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before curling your hair, prepare it by washing, conditioning, and drying it thoroughly. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Divide the front section of your hair from the rest of your hair. Clip the back section of your hair to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Heat Your Curling Iron

Heat your curling iron to the desired temperature. If you have fine hair, use a lower temperature, and if you have thick hair, use a higher temperature. Let the curling iron heat up for a few minutes before using it.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the front section and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, and then release the curl. Repeat the process until you've curled all the hair in the front section.

Step 5: Style Your Curls

Once you've curled all the hair in the front section, style your curls. You can loosen them for a softer look or leave them tight for a more defined look. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look by applying a light hold hairspray to keep the curls in place. Hold the hairspray can about six inches away from your hair and spray lightly.

Tips for Curling Hair in Front of Face

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect curl:

  • Choose the right curling iron size for your hair length and texture.
  • Section your hair properly to make it easier to work with.
  • Don't hold the curling iron in place for too long to avoid heat damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Apply hairspray lightly to avoid weighing down your curls.

FAQs

Q: What kind of curling iron should I use?

A: Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your hair length and texture. If you have short hair, use a smaller barrel size, and if you have long hair, use a larger barrel size. Ceramic or tourmaline curling irons are good options as they distribute heat evenly and protect your hair from damage.

Q: How long should I hold the curling iron in place?

A: Hold the curling iron in place for 5-10 seconds, depending on the thickness of your hair. Don't hold it in place for too long to avoid heat damage.

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

A: Use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place. Apply it lightly to avoid weighing down your curls.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after curling it. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent heat damage. After curling your hair, apply a serum to add shine and prevent frizz. Wash your hair regularly and use a deep conditioner once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
