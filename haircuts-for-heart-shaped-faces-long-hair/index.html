Description of Heart Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, and a narrower chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will balance out your features and create a more symmetrical look.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart Shaped Face

The easiest way to determine if you have a heart-shaped face is to stand in front of a mirror and examine your facial features. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your chin comes to a pointed shape, you have a heart-shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a heart-shaped face with long hair, you want to create volume around the chin area to balance out your facial features. Here are some of the best haircuts for heart-shaped faces with long hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for heart-shaped faces with long hair. They add volume and texture to the hair while balancing out the facial features. You can add face-framing layers around the chin area to create the illusion of a wider jawline.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for heart-shaped faces with long hair. They draw attention away from the forehead and create a more balanced look. Make sure to opt for longer bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair is perfect for heart-shaped faces with long hair. The waves add volume and texture to the hair while softening the facial features. You can create waves using a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for heart-shaped faces with long hair. It adds volume to the hair while drawing attention away from the forehead. You can add braids or twists to the hairstyle for a more intricate look.
