What Curling Iron Is Best For Beachy Waves

Written by William Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for the perfect beachy waves, you'll need the right curling iron. There are so many different curling irons on the market, so how do you know which one is best for beachy waves? In this article, we'll break down the different types of curling irons and share our top recommendations for achieving that perfect beachy look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Beachy waves are a popular hairstyle for a reason. They're effortless, casual, and perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, you'll need a curling iron that can create loose waves without making them too tight or defined. There are a few different types of curling irons that can create beachy waves, including:

  • Wand Curling Irons
  • Clipless Curling Irons
  • Triple Barrel Curling Irons

In general, curling irons with larger barrels will create looser waves, while smaller barrels will create tighter curls. For beachy waves, you'll want to use a curling iron with a barrel size of at least 1 inch.

How To

To achieve beachy waves with a curling iron, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage. You can also add a texturizing spray or mousse to help create more volume and hold.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process for all of your hair sections.

Step 4: Loosen Your Waves

Once you're finished curling your hair, use your fingers to loosen the waves and create a more natural look. You can also use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add more texture and hold.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, then blow dry it using a round brush to create volume.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

Spray a heat protectant spray over your hair to protect it from damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into 1-inch sections using hair clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 4: Choose Your Curling Iron

Choose a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel size. Wand curling irons, clipless curling irons, and triple barrel curling irons all work well for beachy waves.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold for 5-10 seconds, then release. Repeat this process for all of your hair sections.

Step 6: Loosen Your Waves

Once you're finished curling your hair, use your fingers to loosen the waves and create a more natural look. You can also use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add more texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beachy waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage
  • Choose a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel size
  • Section your hair into 1-inch sections for easier curling
  • Loosen your waves with your fingers for a more natural look
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added texture and hold

FAQ

What temperature should I use on my curling iron?

Most curling irons have adjustable temperatures. For beachy waves, you'll want to use a lower temperature (around 300 degrees) to avoid making your waves too tight or defined.

How long should I hold the curling iron on my hair?

You should hold the curling iron on your hair for 5-10 seconds. Holding it for too long can damage your hair and create overly tight curls.

What's the best way to protect my hair from heat damage?

Using a heat protectant spray is the best way to protect your hair from heat damage. You can also use a lower temperature setting on your curling iron and avoid using it too often.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a hair mask once a week to add extra moisture and repair damage.

Now that you know what curling iron is best for beachy waves, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right curling iron and a few styling tips, you'll be able to create perfect beachy waves in no time.
