Description

Curly hair needs extra care and attention, and using a serum can help in keeping your curls healthy and frizz-free. There are a lot of serums available in the market, but finding the right one for your curly hair can be a daunting task. To simplify your search, we have listed down the best serums for curly hair in India that you can try in 2023.

How to Use Serum for Curly Hair

If you're new to using serums for curly hair, it's important to know how to use them correctly. Here are some tips:

  • Start with a small amount of serum and apply it on damp hair.
  • Distribute the serum evenly on your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  • Avoid applying too much serum as it can weigh down your curls.
  • You can use a diffuser to dry your hair after applying the serum for defined curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use serum for curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curly hair shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Take a small amount of serum in your palm and rub your hands together to warm it up.
  4. Apply the serum on your damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  5. Distribute the serum evenly on your hair, avoiding the roots.
  6. Use a diffuser to dry your hair for defined curls or let it air dry for a natural look.

Tips for Using Serum for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for using serum for curly hair:

  • Use a serum that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using too much serum as it can weigh down your curls.
  • Apply the serum on damp hair for best results.
  • Use a diffuser to dry your hair for defined curls.
  • Don't touch your curls too much after applying the serum to avoid frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using serum for curly hair:

  • Q: Can I use serum on dry hair?

    • A: It's best to use serum on damp hair as it helps in distributing the product evenly.

  • Q: How often should I use serum on my curly hair?

    • A: It depends on your hair type and the serum you're using. Read the instructions on the product and follow them accordingly.

  • Q: Can serum make my hair greasy?

    • A: Using too much serum can make your hair greasy. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly on your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Using serum is just one part of curly hair care. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a curly hair shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
