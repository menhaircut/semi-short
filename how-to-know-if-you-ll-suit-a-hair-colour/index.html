How To Know If You ll Suit A Hair Colour

Written by William Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour, but not sure if it will suit you? Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to know if you'll suit a hair colour.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair colour is important as it can enhance your skin tone and make you look more radiant. However, not all hair colours will suit everyone. Your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour are some of the factors that can determine if a hair colour will suit you or not.

How to

There are different ways to determine if a hair colour will suit you or not. Here are some tips:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. There are warm and cool skin tones. Warm skin tones have yellow undertones, while cool skin tones have pink undertones. If you have warm skin tones, choose hair colours with warm undertones such as gold, honey, and copper. If you have cool skin tones, choose hair colours with cool undertones such as ash, platinum, and blue.

2. Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also influence the hair colour that will suit you. For instance, if you have blue eyes, hair colours with warm undertones such as golden blonde or caramel can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, hair colours with cool undertones such as ash brown or black can make your eyes stand out.

3. Know Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also affect the hair colour that will suit you. If you have dark hair, it may be difficult to go blonde without damaging your hair. On the other hand, if you have light hair, going for a darker hair colour may not look natural. Consider your natural hair colour when choosing a hair colour.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a hair colour:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Use a white cloth or paper to determine your skin tone. If your skin looks yellowish, you have warm skin tones. If it looks pinkish, you have cool skin tones.

2. Consider Your Eye Colour

Look at your eyes in natural light and determine the colour. Choose a hair colour that will complement your eye colour.

3. Know Your Natural Hair Colour

Determine your natural hair colour and choose a hair colour that will look natural on you.

4. Consult a Professional

If you're not sure which hair colour will suit you, consult a professional hairstylist. They can recommend the best hair colour for your skin tone and natural hair colour.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hair colour.
  • Choose a hair colour that will complement your wardrobe.
  • Try a temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent one.
  • Consider the maintenance required for the hair colour.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. However, it's important to do a patch test before dyeing your hair to avoid any allergic reactions.

2. Can I dye my hair if I have grey hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have grey hair. Choose a hair colour that will cover the grey hair and look natural.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for coloured hair. Avoid washing your hair too often and use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. Follow these tips to find the perfect hair colour for you.
