How To Make Curly Hair Air Dry Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that it can be challenging to achieve the perfect wavy look. While many people turn to heat styling tools to create waves, these methods can be damaging to your hair. Fortunately, there's a natural and easy way to make your curly hair air dry wavy. In this post, we'll guide you through the process with a step-by-step guide and share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect wavy look.

How to Make Curly Hair Air Dry Wavy

The key to making curly hair air dry wavy is to work with your natural texture. Instead of fighting your curls, embrace them and let them dry naturally. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then gently squeeze out any excess water with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still damp. This will help define your curls and create a wavy texture. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse, depending on your preference. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Next, scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your curls. This will also help create a wavy texture. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently scrunching your hair as you go. Don't be too rough with your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've applied your product and scrunched your hair, let it air dry. Avoid touching your hair as it dries, as this can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process, but be sure to use a low heat setting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to help you create wavy hair from curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then gently squeeze out any excess water with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still damp. This will help define your curls and create a wavy texture. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse, depending on your preference. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four equal parts: two in the front and two in the back. This will make it easier to apply your product and scrunch your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Starting with one section of your hair, gently scrunch your hair with your hands. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, using an upward motion. Repeat this process with each section of your hair.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've applied your product and scrunched your hair, let it air dry. Avoid touching your hair as it dries, as this can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process, but be sure to use a low heat setting.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect wavy look:

Tip 1: Use a Microfiber Towel

Instead of using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel. This will help reduce frizz and dry your hair more gently.

Tip 2: Sleep with Your Hair in a Pineapple

If you want to preserve your wavy hair overnight, sleep with your hair in a pineapple. To do this, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie. This will help keep your curls intact while you sleep.

Tip 3: Refresh Your Curls with a Spray Bottle

If your curls start to fall flat throughout the day, refresh them with a spray bottle filled with water and a little bit of curl enhancing product. Spray your hair lightly and scrunch with your hands to revive your curls.

FAQ

Can I use this method on all types of curly hair?

Yes, this method can be used on all types of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils. The key is to work with your natural texture and embrace your curls.

Will this method cause frizz?

While there's always a risk of frizz when air drying curly hair, following the steps outlined in this post will help minimize frizz. Be sure to use a curl enhancing product and avoid touching your hair as it dries.

How long will it take for my hair to air dry?

The amount of time it takes for your hair to air dry will depend on your hair type and the humidity in your environment. On average, it takes 2-4 hours for curly hair to air dry.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your wavy hair:

Tip 1: Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Using a sulfate-free shampoo will help prevent dryness and maintain the natural oils in your hair.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Your Hair Regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week will help keep it healthy and moisturized.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, so try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant and a low heat setting.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide outlined in this post, you can achieve the perfect wavy look without damaging your hair. Embrace your natural texture and enjoy your beautiful, wavy hair!
