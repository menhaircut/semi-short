Mens Short Hairstyles For Thinning Hair On Top

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are a man with thinning hair on top, you might be wondering what hairstyles will work best for you. The good news is that there are plenty of options out there that can help you look stylish and confident. In this article, we will explore some of the best mens short hairstyles for thinning hair on top, including how to achieve these looks and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Thinning hair on top is a common issue for men as they age. While this can be a frustrating experience, it doesn't mean that you have to sacrifice your style. Short hairstyles are a great option for men with thinning hair, as they can help to create the illusion of thickness and volume. By choosing the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and flattering.

How to

The first step in achieving a great short hairstyle for thinning hair is to choose the right cut. Some of the most popular options include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop. These styles all work well for men with thinning hair, as they are short enough to create the illusion of thickness while still being stylish and modern.

Once you have chosen your cut, it's important to invest in quality styling products. A lightweight pomade or wax can help to add texture and volume to your hair without weighing it down. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair a boost.

Step by Step

1. Choose your cut: The first step in achieving a great short hairstyle for thinning hair is to choose the right cut. Some popular options include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop.

2. Invest in quality styling products: Once you have chosen your cut, it's important to invest in quality styling products. A lightweight pomade or wax can help to add texture and volume to your hair without weighing it down. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair a boost.

3. Style your hair: To style your hair, start by applying a small amount of product to your fingertips and working it through your hair. Use your fingers to create texture and volume, and use a comb or brush to smooth out any areas that need it.

Tips

1. Avoid heavy styling products: Heavy styling products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Stick to lightweight options like pomades and waxes.

2. Keep your hair healthy: Regular haircuts and a healthy diet can help to keep your hair looking its best. Make sure to drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

FAQ

Q: What are the best short hairstyles for men with thinning hair?

A: Some of the best options include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop.

Q: What styling products should I use for thinning hair?

A: Lightweight pomades and waxes are a great option, as they can add texture and volume without weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes regular haircuts, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best even if you have thinning hair on top.
