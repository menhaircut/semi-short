How To Relax Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of dealing with tight curls and want to achieve a more relaxed look, you're in luck. Relaxing your curls can be a great way to switch up your style and achieve a more manageable look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to relax your curls at home.

What Does It Mean to Relax Curls?

Relaxing your curls involves using a chemical treatment to break down the protein bonds in your hair. This process loosens the curl pattern and makes your hair more manageable. It's important to note that relaxing your curls is a permanent process, so make sure you're ready for the change before you begin.

How to Relax Curls: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, make sure you have all the supplies you'll need. This includes:

  • Relaxer kit
  • Gloves
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Neutralizing shampoo
  • Deep conditioner

Once you have all your supplies, you're ready to get started.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you apply the relaxer, you'll need to prep your hair. This involves washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into four quadrants using sectioning clips. This will make it easier to apply the relaxer evenly.

Step 4: Apply the Relaxer

Put on your gloves and begin applying the relaxer to your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use the comb to distribute the relaxer evenly through each section of hair. Be sure to follow the instructions on the relaxer kit closely, as application times may vary.

Step 5: Rinse and Neutralize

Once the relaxer has been on your hair for the recommended amount of time, rinse it out thoroughly with warm water. Next, apply the neutralizing shampoo and work it through your hair. Rinse out the shampoo and repeat the process until the water runs clear.

Step 6: Deep Condition

After you've rinsed out the neutralizing shampoo, it's important to deep condition your hair. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and let it sit for the recommended amount of time. Rinse out the conditioner and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Relaxing Curls

  • Be sure to read and follow the instructions on your relaxer kit carefully.
  • Avoid relaxing your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage to relaxed hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to relax curls?

The process of relaxing your curls can take anywhere from 45 minutes to an hour, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Is it safe to relax your curls at home?

With the proper precautions and careful attention to the instructions on your relaxer kit, it is safe to relax your curls at home. However, if you're unsure or uncomfortable with the process, it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

How often should I relax my hair?

You should avoid relaxing your hair more than once every six to eight weeks, as frequent relaxing can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

Relaxed hair requires special care to stay healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your relaxed hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage to relaxed hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or bleach, on your relaxed hair.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair, as relaxed hair is more prone to breakage.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to relax your curls at home with confidence. Remember to always read and follow the instructions on your relaxer kit carefully, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
