Long On Top Short On Sides Women s Haircut A Trendy And Edgy Look

Written by Frank Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and fashionable haircut, then a long on top short on sides women's haircut might just be the perfect fit for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among women who want to add some edge to their overall look. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to style it, its maintenance, and some tips on how to keep it looking its best.

Description

A long on top short on sides women's haircut is a hairstyle where the top part of the hair is kept long, while the sides and back are cut short or shaved. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter sides and gives the haircut a unique and edgy look. This haircut is a popular choice among women who want to add some edge to their overall style and make a statement.

How to

If you're interested in getting a long on top short on sides women's haircut, the first step is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found a stylist, you can discuss your desired length and style for the top and sides of your hair. It's important to note that this haircut requires some maintenance and styling to keep it looking its best.

When styling your long on top short on sides women's haircut, start by applying a small amount of hair product to the top part of your hair. This will help to keep it in place and add some volume. You can then use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top in your desired direction. For the sides and back, you can use clippers or scissors to maintain the length and shape.

Step by Step

1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in long on top short on sides women's haircut.

2. Discuss your desired length and style with your hairstylist.

3. Apply a small amount of hair product to the top part of your hair.

4. Style the hair on top in your desired direction using a comb or your fingers.

5. Use clippers or scissors to maintain the length and shape of the sides and back.

Tips

1. Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair in place and add some volume.

2. Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to maintain the length and shape.

3. Experiment with different styles and directions for the hair on top to find what works best for you.

4. Consider getting regular touch-ups to keep your haircut looking its best.

FAQ

Q: Is a long on top short on sides women's haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a touch-up for my long on top short on sides women's haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Q: Can I style my long on top short on sides women's haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles and directions for the hair on top to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your long on top short on sides women's haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and regularly trimming the ends of your hair to prevent split ends. Additionally, you can use hair masks and oils to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

A long on top short on sides women's haircut is a trendy and edgy look that can add some personality and style to your overall look. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your haircut looking its best and make a statement with your style.
