How To Big Chop Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you ready to start fresh with a new hairstyle? One option is to big chop your hair. This means cutting off all of your relaxed or chemically-treated hair and starting with a short, natural style. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to big chop your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Big Chop Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Big Chopping Hair
  • FAQs About Big Chopping Hair
  • Hair Care After Big Chopping

How to Big Chop Hair

Big chopping your hair can be a scary decision, but it can also be liberating. Here are some things to consider before you begin:

  • What style do you want to have after big chopping?
  • How short are you willing to go?
  • Are you prepared for the maintenance that comes with a new natural style?

Once you’ve made the decision to big chop, it’s time to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to big chop your hair:

  1. Wash and detangle your hair.
  2. Section your hair into small parts.
  3. Cut each section of hair as close to the roots as possible.
  4. Use scissors or clippers to create an even cut.
  5. Once all the hair is cut off, wash your hair again.
  6. Moisturize your hair with a leave-in conditioner.
  7. Style your new hair.

It’s important to note that some people may choose to go to a professional stylist to big chop their hair, while others may prefer to do it themselves at home.

Tips for Big Chopping Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when big chopping your hair:

  • Be patient with the process.
  • Remember that hair grows back.
  • Invest in good hair care products.
  • Protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products.

FAQs About Big Chopping Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about big chopping hair:

  • How long does it take for hair to grow back after big chopping?
  • What are some good hairstyles for short, natural hair?
  • How often should I wash my hair after big chopping?
  • What are some good products to use on natural hair?

Hair Care After Big Chopping

After big chopping, it’s important to take care of your new hair. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently big chop your hair and start a new hair journey.
