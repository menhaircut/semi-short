Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Thick Straight Hair

If you're blessed with shoulder-length thick straight hair, then you know how versatile and easy to style your hair can be. From sleek and polished looks to effortless and natural styles, you'll never run out of options. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for shoulder length thick straight hair that you can try at home.

Description

Shoulder length thick straight hair is a hair type that sits just above or at the shoulders and has a dense and voluminous texture. It can be challenging to manage, but with the right styling techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit any occasion.

How to Style Shoulder Length Thick Straight Hair

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's essential to know how to style your shoulder length thick straight hair. Here are some tips:

  • Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Opt for lightweight styling products that won't weigh down your hair.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Thick Straight Hair

1. Half Up Half Down

This style is effortless to achieve and perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail with an elastic band.
  3. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Sleek and Straight

This style is perfect for a formal event or a day at the office. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by applying a smoothing serum to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair from roots to ends.
  4. Finish with a shine spray to add some extra shine to your locks.

3. Messy Bun

This style is perfect for a lazy day or when you're running late. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by tying your hair into a high ponytail with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the elastic band to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Thick Straight Hair

To keep your shoulder length thick straight hair looking healthy and beautiful, follow these tips:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for shoulder length thick straight hair?

The best haircut for shoulder length thick straight hair is a blunt cut with layers. It helps to add texture and movement to your hair.

Can I wear bangs with shoulder length thick straight hair?

Yes, you can wear bangs with shoulder length thick straight hair. Opt for side-swept bangs or blunt bangs that sit just above your eyebrows.

Conclusion

Shoulder length thick straight hair is a versatile hair type that offers endless styling possibilities. Whether you prefer sleek and polished looks or effortless and natural styles, you can try out different hairstyles and find the one that suits you the best. Follow the tips and steps mentioned in this article to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
