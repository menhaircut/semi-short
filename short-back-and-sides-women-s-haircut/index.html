Short Back And Sides Women s Haircut A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by Daniel Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The short back and sides women's haircut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides while leaving the top longer. It's a great way to add some edge to your look and make a statement.

Description

The short back and sides women's haircut is a modern take on a classic men's haircut. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type. The hair on the back and sides is usually cut very short, while the top is left longer and styled in a variety of ways.

How to

If you want to try the short back and sides women's haircut, here's how to get it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it.
  2. Section your hair into three parts: the top, the back, and the sides.
  3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the back and sides very short. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
  4. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top to the desired length. You can leave it longer for a more feminine look or shorter for a more edgy look.
  5. Style your hair using your favorite products.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the short back and sides women's haircut:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair so that it's damp but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, section your hair into three parts: the top, the back, and the sides. Clip the top section out of the way so that you can focus on the back and sides.

Step 3: Cut the back and sides

Using clippers, start cutting the hair on the back and sides. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length. Be sure to blend the hair so that it looks natural.

Step 4: Trim the top

Using scissors, trim the hair on the top to the desired length. You can leave it longer for a more feminine look or shorter for a more edgy look. Be sure to trim the hair evenly and blend it into the hair on the back and sides.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style your hair using your favorite products. You can use a hair dryer to add volume or a flat iron to straighten the hair. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short back and sides women's haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a hair dryer or flat iron to add volume or straighten your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

1. Will a short back and sides women's haircut suit me?

The short back and sides women's haircut can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type. Talk to your stylist to find out if this haircut is right for you.

2. How long does the short back and sides women's haircut last?

The short back and sides women's haircut can last for several weeks to several months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your look.

3. How do I style my short back and sides women's haircut?

You can style your short back and sides women's haircut in a variety of ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides women's haircut looking its best, be sure to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking sharp.
