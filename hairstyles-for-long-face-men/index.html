Hairstyles For Long Face Men Tips How To And Care Guide

Written by Frank Sep 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a long-faced man looking for the perfect hairstyle? The shape of your face can have a huge impact on how your hair looks, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. In this article, we'll cover the best hairstyles for long face men, how to style them, and tips for hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles for Long Face Men
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • FAQ

Hairstyles for Long Face Men

When it comes to hairstyles for long face men, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that make your face look even longer. This means steering clear of styles that are too slicked back or flat on top. Instead, go for styles that add volume and texture to the top of your head. Here are some great options:

1. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a great option for long face men because it adds volume to the top of your head and creates a flattering shape. To style this look, apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair and blow-dry it using your fingers to create texture. Then, use a comb to push the hair up and back, creating a messy quiff.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is another great option for long face men because it adds volume and texture to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. To style this look, use a styling cream to create texture and then use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

3. Side Swept Fringe

The side swept fringe is a classic hairstyle that works well for long face men. It adds volume to the top of your head and creates a flattering shape. To style this look, use a styling cream to create texture and then use a comb to sweep the hair to one side.

How to Style Your Hair

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for long face men, it's time to learn how to style them. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

The right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Look for products that add volume and texture, such as styling cream or sea salt spray.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help add volume and texture. Use your fingers to create texture as you blow dry, and don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques.

3. Use a Comb or Brush

When styling your hair, a comb or brush can be your best friend. Use a comb to create a side part or to sweep your hair to one side. Use a brush to smooth out any tangles or knots.

Tips for Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair over time. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking neat and healthy. Aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, go for a shorter hairstyle that adds volume and texture to the top of your head. A messy crop or textured quiff can work well.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, embrace it! A longer hairstyle that shows off your curls can look great on a long face.

How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

One way to get an idea of how a hairstyle will look on you is to use a virtual hairstyle app. You can upload a photo of yourself and try out different hairstyles to see which ones suit you best.

What if I don't want to style my hair every day?

If you don't want to style your hair every day, go for a shorter hairstyle that requires minimal styling, such as a buzz cut or crew cut.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for long face men, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair, it's time to go out there and try out some new looks. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Happy styling!
