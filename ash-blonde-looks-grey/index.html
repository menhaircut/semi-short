Ash Blonde Looks Grey Why It Happens And How To Get The Look

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Ash blonde is a popular hair color that is known for its cool, silver undertones. However, sometimes ash blonde can end up looking more grey than blonde. If you're struggling with this issue, don't worry - it's a common problem that can be easily fixed. In this article, we'll explain why ash blonde looks grey and provide tips and tricks for achieving the perfect ash blonde look.

Why Does Ash Blonde Look Grey?

Ash blonde looks grey when the hair is too light or when the dye used is too cool-toned. This means that there is too much silver or blue present in the hair, making it look more grey than blonde. This can happen when the hair is over-processed or when the dye is left on for too long. It can also be caused by using the wrong shade of dye or not toning the hair properly.

How to Achieve the Perfect Ash Blonde Look

Step 1: Choose the Right Shade of Dye

The first step in achieving the perfect ash blonde look is to choose the right shade of dye. Look for a dye that is labeled as "ash blonde" or "cool blonde" and avoid any shades with warm or golden undertones. It's also important to choose a dye that is the right level of lightness for your hair - if your hair is too dark, the dye won't take properly and you'll end up with a greyish color.

Step 2: Use a Toner

After dyeing your hair, it's important to use a toner to remove any unwanted brassy or yellow tones. Look for a toner that is specifically designed for ash blonde hair and follow the instructions carefully. Toning will help to neutralize any warm tones in the hair and give it a cooler, more silvery look.

Step 3: Take Care of Your Hair

Once you've achieved your perfect ash blonde look, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can cause damage and fade the color. If you're going to be spending time in the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to prevent fading.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Ash Blonde Look

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect ash blonde look:

  • Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure about dyeing your hair at home
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade
  • Consider adding lowlights or highlights to break up the color and add dimension
  • Use a purple shampoo once a week to maintain the cool tones in your hair

FAQ

Can I Achieve Ash Blonde Hair on Dark Hair?

Ash blonde can be achieved on dark hair, but it may require multiple sessions with a professional stylist to avoid damaging the hair. It's important to choose the right shade of dye and to use a toner to remove any unwanted warm tones.

Will Ash Blonde Look Grey on Everyone?

No, ash blonde will not look grey on everyone. The color can vary depending on the natural undertones in your hair and the dye used. It's important to choose a dye that is the right level of lightness for your hair and to use a toner to neutralize any warm tones.

Hair Care for Ash Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your ash blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for color-treated hair
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can cause damage and fade the color
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade

Conclusion

If you're struggling with ash blonde hair that looks more grey than blonde, don't worry - it's a common problem that can be easily fixed. By choosing the right shade of dye, using a toner, and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the perfect ash blonde look. Remember to consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure about dyeing your hair at home, and to take care of your hair to maintain the color.
