How To Have A Long Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a round or square face shape and want to achieve a longer, more oval shape? Don't worry, you're not alone. Having a long face shape is often associated with grace and elegance, and many people aspire to have this type of facial structure. In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks on how to have a long face shape, from simple haircuts to facial exercises.

Haircut Tips

Your haircut can drastically affect the appearance of your face shape. One of the easiest ways to create the illusion of a longer face is to add layers to your hair. Layers add volume and texture, which can elongate your face. You can also try a side-swept fringe or a middle part to draw attention to the center of your face.

Another trick is to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt. Short haircuts can make your face appear wider, while blunt cuts can create a boxy look. Instead, opt for longer hair or a graduated bob that falls just below your jawline.

Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool in creating the illusion of a longer face. Contouring is a popular technique that involves using a darker shade of foundation or powder to create shadows on your face. By placing the contour below your cheekbones and along your jawline, you can create a more defined and elongated look.

You can also use highlighter to draw attention to the center of your face. Apply a small amount of highlighter to the bridge of your nose, the inner corners of your eyes, and above your cheekbones. This will create a subtle glow that can make your face appear longer and more angular.

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help strengthen the muscles in your face and enhance your natural bone structure. One exercise that can help elongate your face is the chin lift. Simply tilt your head back and lift your chin towards the ceiling. Hold this position for a few seconds and repeat several times.

Another exercise is the cheek lift. Puff out your cheeks and hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat several times. You can also try the fish face, which involves sucking in your cheeks and lips to create a fish-like face.

Dietary Changes

Your diet can also play a role in the shape of your face. Eating a diet high in sodium can cause your face to retain water, which can make it appear bloated and round. Try to limit your sodium intake and drink plenty of water to flush out excess fluids.

Additionally, eating a diet that is high in fruits and vegetables can help improve your skin's elasticity and prevent sagging. Foods that are rich in collagen, such as bone broth and leafy greens, can also help promote healthy skin and prevent wrinkles.

Lifestyle Adjustments

Finally, making certain lifestyle adjustments can also help improve the appearance of your face shape. Getting enough sleep is crucial for skin health, as lack of sleep can cause dark circles and puffiness. Additionally, reducing stress through meditation or exercise can help prevent wrinkles and promote healthy skin.

FAQs

Q: Can facial massage help elongate my face?

A: Yes, facial massage can help stimulate blood flow and promote lymphatic drainage, which can help reduce puffiness and improve skin tone. Use gentle, upward strokes when massaging your face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid?

A: Yes, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bouffant or a curly bob. These styles can make your face appear wider and shorter.

Conclusion

There are many ways to achieve a longer face shape, from simple haircut tips to facial exercises and dietary changes. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for you. Remember, having a long face shape is not the only standard of beauty, and you should embrace and celebrate your unique features.

Bonus: Hair Care Tips

In addition to the above tips, here are some bonus hair care tips to promote healthy, shiny hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
