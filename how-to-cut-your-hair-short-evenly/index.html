How To Cut Your Hair Short Evenly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting your hair short can be a liberating experience, but it can also be nerve-wracking if you're doing it yourself. The key to achieving a great short cut is to cut it evenly, which can be a challenge if you're not a professional. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut your hair short evenly and answer some common questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Your Hair Short Evenly
  2. Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short
  3. FAQs
  4. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Cut Your Hair Short Evenly

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather some tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. Here's how to cut your hair short evenly:

  1. Start with dry hair. Wet hair can be deceptive and may cause you to cut too much off.
  2. Section off your hair. Use hair clips to separate your hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.
  3. Decide on your length. Use the comb to separate a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Decide on the length you want to cut and cut straight across.
  4. Repeat step 3. Work your way around your head, cutting each section to the desired length.
  5. Check for evenness. After you've cut all the sections, check for evenness. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them until you're satisfied.

Remember to take your time and be patient. It's better to cut less off at first and make adjustments later than to cut too much off and regret it.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a great short cut:

  • Invest in quality scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and make it harder to achieve an even cut.
  • Use a mirror. It's important to be able to see the back of your head while you're cutting.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're struggling to cut the back of your head, ask a friend or family member for assistance.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting your hair short:

  • How short should I cut my hair? The length you choose depends on your personal preference and face shape. It's best to consult with a stylist if you're unsure.
  • Should I cut my hair wet or dry? It's best to cut your hair dry to avoid cutting too much off.
  • How often should I trim my short hair? It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Keeping your short hair healthy and stylish requires some maintenance. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly. Short hair can get oily quickly, so it's important to wash it every 2-3 days.
  • Use a conditioner. Even short hair needs conditioner to keep it soft and healthy.
  • Invest in styling products. Short hair can be styled in many different ways with the help of products like gel, mousse, and pomade.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your hair short evenly and rock a stylish new look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and ask for help if you need it.
