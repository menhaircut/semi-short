Best Hair Style For Diamond Shaped Face A Comprehensive Guide

If you have a diamond shaped face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. You want to accentuate your unique features while also finding a style that flatters your face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best hair style for diamond shaped face, as well as provide tips and tricks on how to care for your hair.

Description: What is a Diamond Shaped Face?

A diamond shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones. This face shape is often considered to be the most dramatic and angular of all the face shapes. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create balance.

How to Determine if You Have a Diamond Shaped Face?

To determine if you have a diamond shaped face, take a close look at your face in the mirror. If you have wide cheekbones, a narrow forehead and jawline, and a pointed chin, then you have a diamond shaped face. This face shape is also sometimes referred to as a âtriangleâ face shape.

The Best Hair Style for Diamond Shaped Face

The best hair style for diamond shaped face is one that softens the angles of your face and creates balance. Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for this face shape:

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is an excellent choice for diamond shaped faces as it softens the angles and draws attention to the eyes. This hairstyle works best with a side-part, which helps to create balance.

2. Long Layers

Long layers are another great option for diamond shaped faces. They add volume to the hair and create a soft, feminine look. This hairstyle works well with a center-part or a deep side-part.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften the angles of a diamond shaped face. They draw attention to the eyes and create a more balanced look.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice, but it can work well with a diamond shaped face. The short hair draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating balance and softening the angles of the face.

5. Updos

When choosing an updo for a diamond shaped face, opt for a style that adds volume at the crown. This helps to create balance and soften the angles of the face.

Step by Step Guide for the Best Hair Style for Diamond Shaped Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a diamond shaped face, follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine if you have a diamond shaped face. Look closely at your face in the mirror to determine if you have wide cheekbones, a narrow forehead and jawline, and a pointed chin.

Step 2: Choose a Softening Style

Choose a hairstyle that softens the angles of your face and creates balance. You can choose from a shoulder-length bob, long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, or an updo.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick hair, a long layered style may work best. If you have fine hair, a shoulder-length bob may be a better option.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to determine the best hairstyle for your diamond shaped face. They will be able to make recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Your Diamond Shaped Face Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your diamond shaped face hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use heat protectant products when styling your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

4. Experiment with Accessories

Experiment with different hair accessories to add interest and flair to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with a diamond shaped face?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs are a great option for diamond shaped faces as they soften the angles of the face.

Q: Can I wear my hair short with a diamond shaped face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work well with a diamond shaped face as it draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Q: Can I wear an updo with a diamond shaped face?

A: Yes, an updo can work well with a diamond shaped face as long as it adds volume at the crown to create balance.

Hair Care Tips for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some additional hair care tips for diamond shaped face:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking and feeling clean and healthy.

2. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use hats or scarves to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Avoid over-styling your hair with heat styling tools as this can cause damage and breakage.

Choosing the best hair style for diamond shaped face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that accentuates your unique features and flatters your face shape. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the perfect hairstyle for you.
