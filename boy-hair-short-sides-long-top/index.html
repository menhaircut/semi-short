Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your boy? Look no further than the boy hair short sides long top style. This versatile cut has been popular for years and shows no signs of going out of fashion. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about this cut, including how to achieve it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The boy hair short sides long top style features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. The length and style of the top can vary, from a classic slicked-back look to messy and textured. This cut is versatile and works well with many face shapes and hair types. It’s also easy to maintain and style, making it a popular choice for boys of all ages.

How to Achieve the Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top Style

The boy hair short sides long top style can be achieved with a simple haircut. The sides are cut short with clippers or scissors, while the top is left longer and layered. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it’s typically at least 2-3 inches long. To achieve a more dramatic look, the top can be styled with product and swept back or to the side.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top

1. Start by sectioning the hair. Use clips or hair ties to separate the top from the sides.
2. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides short. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
3. Use scissors to cut the top. Start at the back and work your way forward, making sure to cut in layers for added texture.
4. Blend the sides and top with scissors or clippers, depending on the desired look.
5. Style the top with product if desired.

Styling Tips for Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top

The boy hair short sides long top style is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some tips for achieving different looks:

  • To achieve a classic look, slick the top back with gel or pomade.
  • For a messy, textured look, use a matte product and tousle the hair with your fingers.
  • To add volume, blow-dry the hair while using a round brush to lift the roots.
  • For a more dramatic look, style the top into a quiff by sweeping it back and up.

FAQ

What face shapes does the boy hair short sides long top style work best with?

The boy hair short sides long top style works well with many face shapes, including oval, square, and round.

Is this style easy to maintain?

Yes, the boy hair short sides long top style is easy to maintain. The short sides require minimal styling, and the top can be styled quickly with product.

What hair types work best with this style?

This style works well with many hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly.

Hair Care for Boy Hair Short Sides Long Top

To keep the boy hair short sides long top style looking fresh, it’s important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for caring for this style:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

With these tips and tricks, your boy will be rocking the boy hair short sides long top style in no time. Whether he prefers a classic or modern look, this versatile cut is sure to impress.
