Does Hair Up Make You Look Thinner

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a quick and easy way to make yourself look thinner, then putting your hair up may be the solution you are looking for. This simple hairstyle can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face and neck, and can also help to accentuate your best features. In this article, we will explore the question of whether or not hair up makes you look thinner, and provide you with some tips on how to achieve this look.

Description

Putting your hair up can help to create a more streamlined look by pulling the hair away from your face and neck. This can help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer neck, and can also help to highlight your facial features. Additionally, putting your hair up can help to draw attention to your shoulders and collarbone, which are often considered to be attractive features.

How to

If you are looking to put your hair up in a way that will help you to look thinner, there are a few tips you can follow. First, consider the style of updo you choose. A high ponytail or bun can help to elongate your neck and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Additionally, consider pulling your hair back tightly to create a more streamlined look.

Next, think about the placement of your updo. A topknot or high ponytail can help to draw attention to your cheekbones and eyes, while a low bun or chignon can help to lengthen your face and neck.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating an updo that will help you to look thinner:

  1. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, making sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  3. Secure the ponytail tightly with a hair tie.
  4. Twist the ponytail tightly and wrap it around the base, creating a bun.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the ends of the hair.
  6. Spray the updo with hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when trying to create an updo that will help you to look thinner:

  • Avoid adding too much volume to your hair, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider using a hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, to help create a more streamlined look.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for your face shape and features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether or not hair up makes you look thinner:

Does putting your hair up make your face look thinner?

Yes, putting your hair up can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face by elongating your neck and drawing attention to your facial features.

What is the best updo style to make your face look thinner?

A high ponytail or bun is often considered to be the best updo style for creating a slimmer look.

Should you add volume to your hair when putting it up?

No, adding too much volume to your hair can make your face appear wider. Instead, try to create a more streamlined look by pulling your hair back tightly.

Hair Care

When putting your hair up, it is important to take care to avoid damaging your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when creating an updo, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Putting your hair up can be a quick and easy way to create a slimmer, more streamlined look. By following these tips, you can create an updo that will help to accentuate your best features and create the illusion of a longer, slimmer neck and face. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
