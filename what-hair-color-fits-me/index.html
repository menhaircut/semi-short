What Hair Color Fits Me

Written by William Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our hair color guide for 2023! Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available in the market today. It's important to pick a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and overall features. In this post, we'll guide you on how to choose the perfect hair color that fits you.

Description

Choosing the right hair color is not just about following trends or choosing a color that looks good on someone else. It's about finding a color that suits your skin tone, eye color, and overall features. Your hair color should enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.

How to choose the perfect hair color

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

The first step to choosing the perfect hair color is determining your skin tone. There are three main skin tones â warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have a yellow, peachy, or golden undertone. Cool skin tones have a pink, red, or blue undertone. Neutral skin tones have a combination of warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear both green and blue, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider your eye color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the perfect hair color for you. Warm hair colors such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn complement warm eye colors such as brown and hazel. Cool hair colors such as platinum blonde, ash brown, and jet black complement cool eye colors such as blue and green.

Step 3: Assess your natural hair color

Your natural hair color can also provide a good indication of the hair colors that will suit you. If you have warm undertones, warm hair colors such as honey blonde and caramel will complement your skin. If you have cool undertones, cool hair colors such as ash blonde and cool brown will work well.

Step 4: Consider your lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the perfect hair color. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have time for frequent touch-ups, opt for a low-maintenance hair color that doesn't require a lot of upkeep. If you're adventurous and love trying new things, consider a bold hair color such as purple or blue.

Tips for choosing the perfect hair color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect hair color:

  • Consult a professional hairstylist for advice on the best hair color for you.
  • Consider your wardrobe and choose a hair color that complements your style.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities who have a similar skin tone and eye color as you.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors until you find the perfect one.

FAQ

What hair color suits warm skin tones?

Warm hair colors such as honey blonde, copper, and auburn complement warm skin tones.

What hair color suits cool skin tones?

Cool hair colors such as platinum blonde, ash brown, and jet black complement cool skin tones.

What hair color suits neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. Try experimenting with different shades to find the perfect one for you.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting a professional hairstylist for the best results.

Hair care tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use hair care products specifically designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners that can damage your hair.
  • Wash your hair with lukewarm water to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Choosing the perfect hair color can be a fun and exciting adventure. By considering your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and lifestyle, you can find a hair color that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and shine.
