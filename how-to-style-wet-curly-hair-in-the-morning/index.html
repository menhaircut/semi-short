How To Style Wet Curly Hair In The Morning Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a curly-haired person, you know how tricky it can be to style your hair in the morning. If you're not careful, you can end up with a frizzy mess that takes hours to tame. But fear not! With the right techniques and products, you can style your wet curly hair in the morning and have gorgeous curls all day long. In this article, we'll show you how.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Wet Curly Hair: Step-by-Step
  2. Tips for Styling Wet Curly Hair
  3. FAQs about Styling Wet Curly Hair
  4. Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Style Wet Curly Hair: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The key to styling wet curly hair is to start with clean, well-moisturized hair. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking their best.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

After you've washed and conditioned your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to wet hair. This will help moisturize your hair and prevent frizz. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. Depending on your hair type and the look you're going for, you can use a mousse, gel, cream, or serum. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

To enhance your curls, scrunch your hair with your hands. This will help define your curls and create volume. Be gentle when scrunching your hair to avoid causing frizz.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help prevent frizz and enhance your curls. Be sure to use a low heat setting and hold the diffuser close to your hair to dry it evenly.

Step 6: Finish with Oil or Serum

Once your hair is dry, finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz. Be sure to use a lightweight product that won't weigh down your curls.

Tips for Styling Wet Curly Hair

Tip 1: Use a Microfiber Towel

When drying your hair, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt instead of a regular towel. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.

Tip 2: Sleep with a Satin Pillowcase

To keep your curls looking their best overnight, sleep on a satin pillowcase. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.

Tip 3: Refresh Your Curls with Water and Product

If your curls start to look flat or frizzy during the day, refresh them with a spritz of water and a small amount of styling product. Scrunch your hair with your hands to revive your curls.

FAQs about Styling Wet Curly Hair

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair wash their hair every day, while others wash it once a week. Experiment to see what works best for you.

Q: What's the best way to dry curly hair?

A: The best way to dry curly hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help prevent frizz and enhance your curls.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage. Keep in mind that frequent heat styling can damage your hair over time.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Regularly

To keep your curls moisturized and healthy, deep condition your hair regularly. Use a deep conditioner once a week or as needed.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Protect your curls by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your wet curly hair in the morning and have beautiful curls all day long. Remember to be gentle with your hair and use products that are specifically formulated for curly hair. With a little patience and practice, you'll be a curly hair pro in no time!
