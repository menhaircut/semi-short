How To Know If Dark Brown Hair Will Suit You A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair dark brown but not sure if it will suit you? Choosing the right hair color can be challenging, especially if you're used to your natural hair color. However, with the right knowledge, you can make an informed decision and rock your new hair color with confidence. In this guide, we will provide you with tips on how to know if dark brown hair will suit you.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hair color suits you. If you have an oval face, you can rock any hair color, including dark brown. However, if you have a round face, a darker hair color can make your face appear even rounder. On the other hand, if you have a square face, dark brown hair can soften your features and create a more balanced look. For heart-shaped faces, a darker hair color can draw attention to your cheekbones and balance your forehead.

Check Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is another critical factor to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, with yellow or golden undertones, warm shades of dark brown will suit you best. If you have a cool skin tone, with pink or blue undertones, cool shades of dark brown will complement your skin tone. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can rock both warm and cool shades of dark brown.

Analyze Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help determine which hair color will suit you. If you have blue or green eyes, a dark brown hair color can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, dark brown hair can create a monochromatic look, but you can add dimension with highlights or lowlights.

Look at Your Wardrobe

When considering a new hair color, it's essential to look at your wardrobe. If you wear a lot of bold colors, a darker hair color can balance your overall look. If you prefer neutral colors, a darker hair color can make your outfits appear more sophisticated.

Evaluate Your Hair Type

Your hair type can also determine if dark brown hair will suit you. If you have fine hair, dark brown hair can make your hair appear fuller and thicker. If you have curly hair, a darker hair color can highlight your curls and add dimension. If you have damaged hair, a darker hair color can make your hair appear even more damaged, so it's essential to consider your hair's health before dyeing it.

Consider Your Personality

Your personality can also influence if dark brown hair will suit you. If you're outgoing and love to stand out, a darker hair color can make a bold statement. If you prefer a more natural look, a darker hair color can still complement your personality without attracting too much attention.

Consult with a Stylist

If you're still unsure if dark brown hair will suit you, it's always best to consult with a stylist. They can provide recommendations based on your face shape, skin tone, and hair type, and help you achieve the best results possible.

Think About Maintenance

Before dyeing your hair dark brown, it's essential to consider the maintenance required to keep your hair looking its best. Dark brown hair can fade quickly, so it's essential to use color-safe shampoos and conditioners and avoid excessive heat styling.

Tips for Maintaining Dark Brown Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining dark brown hair:

  • Use color-safe shampoos and conditioners
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as it can cause your hair color to fade

FAQs

Can I dye my hair dark brown if I have light hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair dark brown if you have light hair. However, it's essential to understand that the lighter your hair, the more challenging it is to achieve a dark brown color without causing damage.

Can dark brown hair make me look older?

No, dark brown hair does not necessarily make you look older. However, choosing the right shade of dark brown can complement your features and make you look more youthful.

How often do I need to touch up my dark brown hair color?

It depends on your hair's growth rate, but typically, you may need to touch up your roots every four to six weeks to maintain your hair color.

Can I dye my hair dark brown at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair dark brown at home, it's always best to consult with a professional stylist to ensure you achieve the desired results without causing damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to know if dark brown hair will suit you, you can make an informed decision and rock your new hair color with confidence. Remember to consider your face shape, skin tone, eye color, wardrobe, hair type, personality, and maintenance requirements before dyeing your hair. If you're still unsure, consult with a stylist for professional advice. With the right knowledge and care, you can achieve the perfect dark brown hair color and enjoy a new look that complements your unique features.
