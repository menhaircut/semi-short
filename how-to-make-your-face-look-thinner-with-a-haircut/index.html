How To Make Your Face Look Thinner With A Haircut

Written by Petter Jul 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Having a round face can sometimes be a challenge when trying to achieve a certain look. However, with the right haircut, you can make your face look thinner and more defined. In this article, we will discuss how to make your face look thinner with a haircut and provide you with tips and advice on how to achieve the look you desire.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with a Haircut
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Making Your Face Look Thinner
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with a Haircut

The first step in achieving a thinner-looking face is to choose the right haircut. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut for your face shape:

1. Go for a Long Bob

A long bob is a great haircut for those with a round face. The length of the bob should be just below the chin, which helps to elongate the face. You can also add some layers to the bob to create volume and texture, which will help to make your face look slimmer.

2. Try a Side-Swept Bang

A side-swept bang can also help to make your face look thinner. The bangs should be longer on one side and shorter on the other, which will help to create an asymmetrical look. This will draw the eye away from the roundness of your face and towards the longer side of your hair.

3. Add Some Layers

Adding layers to your hair can also help to make your face look thinner. Layers create movement and texture, which can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, be careful not to add too many layers, as this can create too much volume and make your face look rounder.

4. Go for a Pixie Cut

If you're feeling brave, a pixie cut can also help to make your face look thinner. A short, layered pixie cut can create volume and texture, which can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, make sure to choose a pixie cut that suits your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look thinner with a haircut:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Knowing your face shape will help you to choose the right haircut that will flatter your features and create balance.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose the right haircut. As mentioned earlier, a long bob, side-swept bang, layered haircut, or pixie cut can help to make your face look thinner.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Before getting your haircut, it's important to consult with your stylist. They can provide you with advice on which haircut will best suit your face shape and features. They can also give you tips on how to style your hair to make your face look thinner.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to style your hair correctly to make your face look thinner. You can use hair products such as mousse, gel, or hairspray to create volume and texture. You can also use a round brush to add volume to your hair.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Thinner

Here are some additional tips for making your face look thinner:

1. Contour Your Face

Contouring your face can help to create the illusion of a thinner face. You can use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows on your face, which will help to create definition.

2. Wear the Right Makeup

Wearing the right makeup can also help to make your face look thinner. Use a matte foundation and avoid shimmery or glittery makeup, as this can make your face look rounder.

3. Wear Statement Earrings

Wearing statement earrings can also help to make your face look thinner. Choose earrings that are long and thin, as this will elongate your face and draw attention away from its roundness.

4. Wear the Right Clothes

Wearing the right clothes can also help to make your face look thinner. Avoid wearing clothes that are too tight or too loose, as this can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for clothes that are fitted and have a V-neckline, as this will help to elongate your face.

FAQs

Q: Will cutting my hair make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, the right haircut can help to make your face look thinner. A long bob, side-swept bang, layered haircut, or pixie cut can all help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: Can contouring help to make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, contouring can help to create the illusion of a thinner face. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows on your face, which will help to create definition.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on a low heat setting.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks.

Now that you know how to make your face look thinner with a haircut, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look. With the right haircut and styling tips, you can achieve the look you desire and feel confident in your own skin.
