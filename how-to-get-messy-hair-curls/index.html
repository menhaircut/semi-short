How To Get Messy Hair Curls

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fun and effortless hairstyle that will make you look like you just rolled out of bed, then messy hair curls are the perfect choice. With a little bit of effort and some simple techniques, you can achieve a tousled, carefree look that's perfect for a day out with friends or a casual night on the town. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to get messy hair curls, as well as some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Messy hair curls are all about embracing your natural hair texture and creating a look that's relaxed and effortless. This style is perfect for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair without spending hours in front of the mirror. The key to getting messy hair curls is to use the right products and techniques that will help you achieve a tousled, carefree look.

How to

To get started with messy hair curls, you'll need a few essential tools and products, including a curling iron, heat protectant spray, and a texturizing spray. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect messy hair curls:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

To create the perfect messy hair curls, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. Make sure to use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your hair's natural oils and moisture.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant spray

Before you start curling your hair, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron, start curling your hair in small sections. Take each section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Use texturizing spray

Once you've curled your entire head of hair, use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture. This will help create that messy, tousled look that you're going for.

Step 5: Style your curls

Finally, use your fingers to style your curls and create the perfect messy look. You can also use a bit of hairspray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hair curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel to create loose, relaxed curls
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to help separate and define your curls
  • Use a sea salt spray to help enhance your hair's natural texture
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Q: What type of curling iron should I use for messy hair curls?

A: It's best to use a curling iron with a larger barrel (around 1.5 inches) to create loose, relaxed curls.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting too frizzy?

A: Using a heat protectant spray and a texturizing spray can help prevent frizziness. You can also try using a serum or oil to help smooth down any flyaways.

Q: Can I achieve messy hair curls without using heat?

A: Yes! You can try using braids or twists to create natural waves in your hair without using heat.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to practice good hair care habits. This includes using a good-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. It's also important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and to avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy hair curls in no time. Whether you're heading out for a night on the town or just want a fun and effortless look for a casual day out, messy hair curls are the perfect choice. So go ahead and embrace your natural hair texture, and have fun creating the perfect tousled look!
