How To Wear Your Hair Up After 50

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose its elasticity and shine, making it harder to style. However, just because we are over 50 does not mean we have to sacrifice style for convenience. Wearing your hair up is a great way to look polished while keeping your hair out of your face. In this article, we will give you tips on how to wear your hair up after 50.

How to

There are many ways to wear your hair up after 50. Some popular styles include:

  • Buns
  • Ponytails
  • Braids
  • Twists

When choosing a style, consider your hair type and face shape. For example, if you have thin hair, a messy bun may not be the best option as it can make your hair look even thinner. If you have a round face, a high ponytail can elongate your face.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create a simple bun:

  1. Brush your hair and gather it into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Tuck the ends of the ponytail under the bun and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you wear your hair up after 50:

  • Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and hairpins to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that suits you.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is easy to style up.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up after 50:

Q: Will wearing my hair up damage it?

A: Wearing your hair up can cause damage if done too tightly or too often. However, if done correctly and in moderation, it should not cause any damage.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair when wearing it up?

A: You can add volume by teasing your hair before creating the updo or using a volumizing product before styling.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have short hair?

A: Yes, there are many updo options for short hair such as a twisted bun or a half-up half-down style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy hair, especially as we age. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can wear your hair up after 50 and still look stylish and elegant. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect updo for you.
