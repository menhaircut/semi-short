Best Hair Colour For Over 60S A Guide For A Youthful Look

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair begins to lose its natural color and becomes gray. Many women find it challenging to maintain a youthful look with gray hair. Fortunately, there are many hair coloring options available that can enhance your natural beauty and give you a fresh and youthful appearance. In this article, we will discuss the best hair color for over 60s.

Description

The best hair color for over 60s is the one that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. The right hair color can make you look younger, more radiant, and more confident. It is essential to choose a color that suits your skin tone and eye color to avoid looking washed out or dull.

How To Choose The Best Hair Color For Over 60s

Choosing the right hair color can be challenging, especially if you have never dyed your hair before. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair color for over 60s:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider when choosing the best hair color for over 60s is your eye color. Certain hair colors can complement your eye color and make it stand out.

Step 3: Choose The Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone and eye color, it's time to choose the right shade. If you have a cool skin tone, look for hair colors with cool undertones, like ash blonde or platinum blonde. If you have a warm skin tone, look for hair colors with warm undertones, like golden blonde or copper. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors.

Step By Step Guide To Coloring Your Hair

Once you have chosen the best hair color for over 60s, it's time to color your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose The Right Hair Dye

Choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for mature hair. These dyes are formulated to be less harsh on your hair and scalp and provide better coverage for gray hair.

Step 2: Do A Patch Test

Before coloring your hair, do a patch test to make sure you are not allergic to the hair dye. Apply a small amount of the hair dye to your skin and wait 24 hours to see if you have any adverse reactions.

Step 3: Protect Your Skin

Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to protect your skin from the hair dye.

Step 4: Mix The Hair Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to mix the hair dye. Use a plastic bowl and brush to mix the hair dye.

Step 5: Apply The Hair Dye

Use the brush to apply the hair dye to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate your hair evenly.

Step 6: Wait

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to determine how long to leave the dye on your hair. Typically, it's between 20-40 minutes.

Step 7: Rinse

Rinse your hair with warm water until the water runs clear. Apply a conditioner to your hair to restore moisture.

Tips For Maintaining Your Hair Color

After coloring your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair every day
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: Is it safe to color your hair at 60?

A: Yes, it is safe to color your hair at 60. However, it's essential to choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for mature hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can cause damage if not done correctly. It's essential to choose a hair dye that is less harsh on your hair and to take care of your hair properly after coloring it.

Q: How often should I color my hair?

A: It's recommended to color your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60s

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and drier. Here are some hair care tips for over 60s:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote hair growth
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun

Overall, the best hair color for over 60s is the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Follow these tips to choose the right hair color for your skin tone and eye color and take care of your hair properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.
