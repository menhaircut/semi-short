How To Match Hair Color Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Matching your hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are doing it for the first time. But don't worry, we've got you covered with some tips and tricks that will make it easier for you to match your hair color. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of how to match hair color, step by step. We will also answer some common FAQ about hair color matching and provide some hair care tips.

  • How to Match Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Match Hair Color

Matching your hair color involves taking into consideration your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. The goal is to find a shade that complements your features and looks natural on you. Here are some tips for picking the right hair color:

  • Choose a shade that is within two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color to ensure a natural look.
  • Consider your skin tone. If you have warm-toned skin, go for warm-toned hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool-toned skin, opt for cool-toned hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.
  • Take into account your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, blonde, light brown, or red hair colors will complement your eyes. If you have brown or hazel eyes, darker hair colors will make your eyes pop.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to match your hair color:

  1. Identify your natural hair color. This will serve as a base for choosing your new hair color.
  2. Determine your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool-toned skin. If they appear green, you have warm-toned skin.
  3. Consider your eye color. This will help you decide which hair color will complement your eyes.
  4. Choose a hair color that is within two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.
  5. Try on a wig or use a virtual hair color app to see how the new hair color will look on you.
  6. Consult a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about the hair color you have chosen. They can help you select the perfect shade and provide tips on maintaining the color.

Tips for Matching Hair Color

Here are some additional tips for matching your hair color:

  • Consider the undertones of your natural hair color. For example, if you have warm-toned brown hair, a cool-toned hair color like ash blonde may not look natural on you.
  • Take into account the maintenance required for the hair color you choose. For example, blonde hair colors require more upkeep than darker hair colors.
  • If you are unsure about the hair color you have chosen, start with a temporary hair dye to see how it looks on you.

FAQ

Here are some common FAQ about matching hair color:

  • How often should I color my hair? It depends on the type of hair color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair colors should be touched up every six to eight weeks, while semi-permanent hair colors can last up to six weeks.
  • Can I color my hair at home? Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it is recommended that you consult a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about the process.
  • Can I dye my hair a different color after I have already dyed it? Yes, but it is recommended that you wait at least four weeks before coloring your hair again to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to maintain your new hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent the color from fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.

Matching your hair color can be a fun experience if you follow these tips and tricks. Remember to take into account your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a new hair color. And don't forget to maintain your hair with proper hair care to keep your new color looking vibrant and healthy.
