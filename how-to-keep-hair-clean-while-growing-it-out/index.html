How To Keep Hair Clean While Growing It Out

Written by Christine Aug 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you in the process of growing out your hair? It can be a frustrating journey, especially when it comes to keeping your hair clean. Longer hair tends to get greasier faster, and it can be difficult to maintain its cleanliness. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your hair clean and healthy while you're growing it out. In this article, we'll share some of the best ways to do just that.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some simple steps you can follow to keep your hair clean while growing it out:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

The first step to keeping your hair clean is to wash it regularly. This will help to remove any dirt and oil that has accumulated on your scalp and hair. However, be careful not to overwash your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

2. Use a Gentle Shampoo

When washing your hair, make sure to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that is free from sulfates and parabens, as these chemicals can be harsh on your hair and scalp.

3. Condition Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a conditioner to help keep it moisturized and healthy. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding your scalp, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

4. Use Dry Shampoo

If you don't have time to wash your hair, or if you want to extend the time between washes, use a dry shampoo. This will help to absorb any excess oil on your scalp and hair, leaving it looking and feeling clean.

5. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brushing your hair regularly will help to distribute the natural oils from your scalp throughout your hair, keeping it moisturized and healthy. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Keeping Hair Clean While Growing it Out

Here are some additional tips to help keep your hair clean and healthy while you're growing it out:

1. Avoid Touching Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more oil and dirt you transfer from your hands to your hair. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day, and use a hair tie or clip to keep it out of your face.

2. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

When you sleep, your hair can get tangled and matted, leading to breakage and damage. To protect your hair while you sleep, use a silk or satin pillowcase, or tie your hair up in a loose bun or braid.

3. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

If you're using a lot of styling products in your hair, they can build up over time and make your hair look dull and greasy. Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any product buildup and keep your hair looking fresh.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein to keep your hair strong and healthy.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and how quickly it gets greasy.

What type of shampoo should I use?

Look for a gentle shampoo that is free from sulfates and parabens. You may also want to use a shampoo that is specifically designed for your hair type.

Can I use dry shampoo every day?

While dry shampoo is a great option for extending the time between washes, you shouldn't use it every day. Overuse of dry shampoo can lead to product buildup and dryness.

Hair Care Products to Help Keep Hair Clean While Growing it Out

Look for a gentle shampoo that is free from sulfates and parabens. Some great options include the L'Oreal Paris EverPure Moisture Shampoo and the Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo.

With these tips and products, you can keep your hair clean and healthy while you're growing it out. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair, and you'll soon have the long, beautiful locks you've been dreaming of.
