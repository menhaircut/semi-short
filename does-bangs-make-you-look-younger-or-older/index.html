Does Bangs Make You Look Younger Or Older

Written by Aprilia Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but unsure if it will make you look younger or older? Many people believe that bangs can make you look more youthful, while others think it can age you. In this article, we will explore the question, "does bangs make you look younger or older?" and provide you with tips on how to choose the right bangs for your age and face shape.

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair in front of the forehead to create a fringe. Bangs come in different styles, such as blunt, side-swept, wispy, and choppy. Many celebrities and fashion icons have rocked bangs, including Audrey Hepburn, Brigitte Bardot, and Zooey Deschanel.

However, the question remains, does bangs make you look younger or older? The answer is not straightforward, as it depends on several factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and the type of bangs you choose.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Age

Here are some tips on how to choose the right bangs for your age:

20s and 30s

If you are in your 20s or 30s, you can experiment with different types of bangs, such as blunt or choppy. These styles can create a youthful and edgy look. You can also try side-swept bangs, which can frame your face and add dimension.

40s and 50s

As you get older, you may want to opt for softer, wispy bangs that can soften your features and create a more youthful appearance. Side-swept bangs are also a good option, as they can draw attention away from fine lines and wrinkles.

60s and Above

For those in their 60s and above, you may want to avoid heavy bangs that can weigh down your face. Instead, opt for light, wispy bangs that can add volume and frame your face. Side-swept bangs can also work well, as they can create a more youthful appearance.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Bangs

If you decide to cut your bangs at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely before cutting your bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by combing it straight down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Choose Your Bangs

Choose the type of bangs you want to cut, such as blunt, side-swept, or wispy.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using sharp scissors, start cutting your bangs from the middle and work your way outwards. Cut small sections at a time and check your progress in the mirror.

Step 5: Trim as Needed

Once you have cut your bangs, trim them as needed to achieve your desired length and shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs fresh between washes.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy and flat.

FAQs

Q: Can bangs make your face look longer?

A: Yes, if you have a round face, blunt bangs can make your face look longer and slimmer.

Q: How do I know if bangs will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing bangs. You can also use virtual try-on tools or consult with a hairstylist.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long your bangs are. As a general rule, trim your bangs every 4 to 6 weeks.

Hair Care for Bangs

Lastly, taking care of your hair and scalp is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful bangs. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, the question "does bangs make you look younger or older?" is subjective and depends on several factors. However, by choosing the right type of bangs for your age and face shape, maintaining them properly, and taking care of your hair and scalp, you can rock bangs at any age and look fabulous.
