Updos For Shoulder Length Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing your curly hair down all the time? If you have shoulder length curly hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, that’s far from the truth! In this guide, we’ll show you some stunning updos for shoulder length curly hair that you can try in 2023. Whether you’re attending a wedding, a formal event, or just want to switch up your everyday look, we’ve got you covered!

Description: What are Updos for Shoulder Length Curly Hair?

Updos refer to hairstyles where the hair is pulled up and away from the face and neck. They can be elegant, chic, or casual, depending on the occasion and the style you choose. Updos for shoulder length curly hair are hairstyles that are designed to work with curly hair that falls just above or at the shoulders. These styles can range from simple and effortless to intricate and elaborate.

How to: Prepping Your Hair for Updos

Before we get into the updos themselves, it’s essential to prepare your hair properly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair, then let it air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently. Avoid brushing or combing your curls too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

2. Apply a curl-defining product

Use a curl-enhancing cream, gel, or mousse to help define your curls and keep them looking bouncy and shiny. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use a heat protectant spray

If you plan on using heat tools like a curling iron or flat iron, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray first. This will help prevent damage and breakage.

Step by Step: Easy Updos for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Now that your hair is prepped and ready to go, it’s time to try some updos! Here are some easy styles to get you started:

1. Half Up Half Down

This style is effortless yet chic. Simply gather the top half of your hair, twist it, and secure it with bobby pins at the back of your head. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose and curly.

2. Braided Crown

Divide your hair into two sections – top and bottom. French braid the top section from one side of your head to the other, securing the end with a clear elastic band. Repeat on the other side. Take the two braids and cross them over your head, securing them with bobby pins.

3. Low Bun

Pull your hair back into a low ponytail, leaving a few strands loose around your face. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins. Gently tug at the bun to make it look fuller and messier.

Tips for Updos for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying updos for shoulder length curly hair:

1. Don’t be afraid to use accessories

Headbands, scarves, and hairpins can add a fun and stylish touch to your updo. Experiment with different accessories to see what works best for you.

2. Embrace the messiness

Updos for curly hair look best when they’re a little messy and undone. Don’t worry about making everything perfect – embrace the natural texture and volume of your curls.

3. Use hairspray to set the style

Once you’ve created your updo, use a light hold hairspray to keep it in place. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking polished all day.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I create updos for shoulder length curly hair on my own?

Yes! With a little practice, you can create beautiful updos for shoulder length curly hair on your own. Use mirrors and take your time to ensure that the style is secure and polished.

2. Do I need to straighten my hair before trying updos?

No! Updos for shoulder length curly hair are designed to work with your natural curls. Embrace the texture and volume of your hair – it will make your updo look even more stunning.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Shoulder Length Curly Hair Healthy

Finally, it’s essential to take care of your shoulder length curly hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Deep condition regularly

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s essential to deep condition regularly to keep it moisturized. Look for a deep conditioning treatment that contains nourishing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter.

3. Avoid heat styling as much as possible

Heat styling can damage your curls, causing them to become brittle and prone to breakage. If you must use heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

With these tips and updo ideas, you’ll be able to create stunning hairstyles for your shoulder length curly hair in no time. Whether you’re looking for a simple and casual style or an elegant updo for a special occasion, there’s something here for everyone. Remember to take care of your hair and embrace its natural texture and beauty!
