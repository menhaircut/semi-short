How To Cut Long Hair Straight Across At Home

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair at home can be a daunting task, especially if you have long hair that needs to be cut straight across. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a salon-worthy haircut without leaving your home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long hair straight across at home, as well as some tips on hair care and maintenance.

Description

Cutting long hair straight across is a classic and timeless hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. This hairstyle is ideal for those who want to maintain their hair length while adding a fresh and polished look. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, cutting your hair straight across can enhance your natural features and give you a more youthful appearance.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a comb, and a hair tie. Follow these steps to cut your long hair straight across:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair completely using a blow dryer or let it air dry. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to divide your hair into two equal sections, with one section in the front and one section in the back. Use the hair tie to secure the back section of your hair, leaving the front section loose.

Step 3: Measure the Length

Take the front section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to measure the length you want to cut. For a straight across cut, measure the length from the tip of your hair to the desired length. Make sure both sides are even.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

With the front section of your hair still combed straight down, use the hair scissors to cut across at the desired length. Make sure you cut straight across and not at an angle. Repeat this process on the other side of your hair, making sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Take down the back section of your hair and blend the hair from the front and back sections by combing them together. Use the hair scissors to trim any uneven ends. Make sure you blend the hair evenly to achieve a seamless look.

Step 6: Check Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, check it in the mirror to make sure it is even and straight. Make any necessary adjustments if needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect straight across haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a sharp pair of hair scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member to help you if you are not confident in cutting your own hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be time-consuming, but the end result is worth it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long hair straight across:

Can I cut my hair straight across with any type of hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across regardless of your hair type. However, the technique may vary depending on your hair texture and thickness.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it is recommended to use hair scissors as they are designed to cut hair without damaging it.

How often should I cut my hair straight across?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny after cutting it straight across, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often as they can cause heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Cutting your long hair straight across at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut without leaving your home. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect straight across cut, and don't forget to take care of your hair after cutting it to keep it healthy and shiny.
