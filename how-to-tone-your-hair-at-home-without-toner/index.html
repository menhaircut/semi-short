How To Tone Your Hair At Home Without Toner

Written by William Nov 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, toning can be a great option. Toning helps to neutralize unwanted brassy or yellow tones in your hair, leaving you with a more even and natural-looking color. But what if you don't have toner on hand? Don't worry, there are still ways to tone your hair at home without toner. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for achieving a toned look without the use of toner.

Description

Toning your hair at home without toner may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually easier than you might think. With a few simple ingredients and some patience, you can achieve beautiful, toned hair without ever leaving your house. Whether you're looking to neutralize brassy tones, add depth and dimension, or simply switch up your look, these tips will help you achieve the perfect tone.

How to Tone Your Hair at Home Without Toner

If you're ready to tone your hair at home without toner, here's what you'll need:

  • White vinegar
  • Lemon juice
  • Baking soda
  • Shampoo

Step by Step

Follow these steps to tone your hair at home without toner:

  1. Mix together equal parts white vinegar and water. Apply the mixture to your hair and let it sit for 15-20 minutes. Rinse thoroughly.
  2. Squeeze the juice of one lemon into a bowl. Add an equal amount of water and mix well. Apply the mixture to your hair and let it sit for 15-20 minutes. Rinse thoroughly.
  3. Mix together equal parts baking soda and shampoo. Apply the mixture to your hair and let it sit for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly.
  4. Condition your hair as usual.

Repeat these steps as often as needed to achieve your desired tone.

Tips

For best results, follow these tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup before toning.
  • Be patient. Toning can take several applications to achieve the desired result.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after toning to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Can I tone my hair without using any products?

Yes, you can tone your hair naturally by exposing it to sunlight. However, this method may take longer to achieve the desired result.

How often should I tone my hair?

This depends on your hair type and the desired result. Some people may need to tone their hair every few weeks, while others may only need to tone it once a month or less.

Can I use food coloring to tone my hair?

No, we do not recommend using food coloring to tone your hair. Food coloring can be damaging to your hair and may not provide the desired result.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair after toning. Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools, as they can damage your hair and cause color fading. And always use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, toned hair at home without toner. Try it out and see the difference for yourself!
