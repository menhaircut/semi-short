Best Beard Styles For Round Face How To Choose The Perfect Look

Choosing the right beard style for your face shape can make all the difference in your appearance. If you have a round face, you need to be careful when selecting a beard style. The wrong choice can make your face look even rounder, which is not what you want. So, what are the best beard styles for round face? In this article, we'll answer this question and more, so keep reading!

How to Choose the Right Beard Style for Your Round Face

When choosing a beard style for a round face, you need to consider the length and shape of the beard. The goal is to create a contrast between your beard and your face shape. This contrast will help to make your face appear slimmer and more defined. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a beard style that is longer on the chin and shorter on the sides
  • Avoid full beards that cover your cheeks and jawline
  • Consider a goatee or a soul patch
  • Try a beard with sharp angles to create definition
  • Avoid round beard styles that match the shape of your face

Now that you know what to look for in a beard style, let's take a look at some of the best options.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Beard Styles for Round Face

The Goatee

The goatee is a classic beard style that works well for round faces. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect goatee:

  1. Shave your cheeks and sideburns
  2. Trim your beard to create a small, triangular shape on your chin
  3. Maintain the length of your goatee with regular trimming
  4. Keep your mustache trimmed and separate from your goatee

The Soul Patch

The soul patch is a small patch of hair below the lower lip. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Shave your cheeks and sideburns
  2. Trim your beard to create a small patch of hair below your lower lip
  3. Maintain the length of your soul patch with regular trimming
  4. Keep the rest of your beard clean-shaven

The Extended Goatee

The extended goatee is a longer version of the classic goatee. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Shave your cheeks and sideburns
  2. Trim your beard to create a small, triangular shape on your chin
  3. Allow your beard to grow longer on the sides
  4. Keep your mustache trimmed and separate from your goatee
  5. Maintain the length of your extended goatee with regular trimming

Tips for Maintaining Your Round Face Beard Style

Maintaining your beard style is just as important as choosing the right one. Here are some tips to help you keep your beard looking its best:

  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized
  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo
  • Comb your beard to keep it neat and tidy
  • Avoid over-styling your beard with too much product

FAQs About Round Face Beard Styles

What beard styles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid beard styles that are too full or too round. These styles can make your face look even rounder. Also, avoid styles that cover your cheeks and jawline.

Can I still have a full beard with a round face?

If you have a round face, it's best to avoid full beards that cover your cheeks and jawline. However, you can still have a full beard if you keep it trimmed and angular.

How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard every 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast it grows. Regular trimming will help you maintain the shape of your beard and avoid split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Your Round Face Beard

When it comes to beard care, it's important to take care of the hair on your head as well. Here are some tips to keep your hair and beard looking their best:

  • Wash your hair and beard regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons
  • Use a conditioner on your hair and beard to keep them soft and moisturized
  • Avoid over-washing your hair and beard, as this can strip them of their natural oils
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know the best beard styles for round face, it's time to choose the perfect look for you. Remember to consider your face shape and the length and shape of your beard. With the right beard style and proper maintenance, you can look your best every day!
