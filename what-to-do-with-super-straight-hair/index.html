What To Do With Super Straight Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have super straight hair, you might feel like your locks are lacking in volume and texture. But don't worry – there are plenty of things you can do to add some oomph to your tresses. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks for styling super straight hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Super Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Super Straight Hair
  • Tips for Styling Super Straight Hair
  • FAQs about Styling Super Straight Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Super Straight Hair

How to Style Super Straight Hair

When it comes to styling super straight hair, the key is to add texture and volume. Here are some things you can do:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Add waves or curls to your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to create height and volume.
  • Use texturizing products, like sea salt spray or dry shampoo, to add texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like updos and braids, to add interest and dimension to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Super Straight Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling super straight hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls to your hair.
  4. Tease your hair at the crown to create height and volume.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture to your hair.

Tips for Styling Super Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling super straight hair:

  • Don't wash your hair every day – this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless.
  • Avoid using heavy products, like oils and serums, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Invest in a good quality curling iron or hot rollers – these tools can be a game changer for adding volume and texture to super straight hair.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for your super straight hair.

FAQs about Styling Super Straight Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling super straight hair:

Q: Can I use a straightener on super straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener on super straight hair to create a sleek, polished look. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my super straight hair?

A: You can add volume to your super straight hair by blow drying your hair upside down, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and using texturizing products.

Q: What hairstyles work best for super straight hair?

A: Updos, braids, and hairstyles with lots of layers work well for super straight hair, as they add interest and dimension to your locks.

Hair Care Tips for Super Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips for super straight hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, like straighteners and curling irons, every day – this can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush with gentle bristles to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can style and care for your super straight hair like a pro. Whether you want to add volume, texture, or interest to your locks, there's a solution for every hair woe. So go ahead and experiment – your super straight hair is a blank canvas just waiting for your creative touch!
