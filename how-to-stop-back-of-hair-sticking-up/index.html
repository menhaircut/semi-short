How To Stop Back Of Hair Sticking Up A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you ever wake up in the morning to find the back of your hair sticking up like a crazy afro? It's a common problem that many people face. The good news is that there are several ways to prevent this from happening. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to stop the back of your hair from sticking up. We'll also provide some helpful tips, answer some common questions, and give you some hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Stop Back of Hair Sticking Up

The first step in preventing the back of your hair from sticking up is to choose the right haircut. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to consider a shorter haircut that is layered to help control the curls. However, if you have straight hair, you can go for a longer cut that includes layers to add some volume and help prevent the hair from sticking up.

Another way to prevent the back of your hair from sticking up is to use the right styling products. A good hair gel or pomade can help keep your hair in place and prevent it from flying away. Make sure to choose a product that is specifically designed to control frizz and keep your hair looking neat.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to stop the back of your hair from sticking up:

  1. Choose the right haircut for your hair type.
  2. Use the right styling products to keep your hair in place.
  3. Blow dry your hair in the right direction using a round brush.
  4. Use a hairspray to set your style in place.

Following these steps can help you achieve a sleek and polished look that will last all day.

Helpful Tips

Here are some helpful tips to prevent the back of your hair from sticking up:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and minimize frizz.
  • Avoid using towels to dry your hair; instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to minimize frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about how to stop the back of your hair from sticking up:

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to prevent my hair from sticking up?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to help control the back of your hair. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Should I use a comb or a brush to style my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage. If you have straight hair, use a round brush to add volume and prevent the back of your hair from sticking up.

Q: Can I use a leave-in conditioner to prevent frizz?

A: Yes, a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. Look for a product that is specifically designed to tame flyaways and keep your hair smooth.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

Following these tips can help you achieve healthy and beautiful hair that is free of flyaways and frizz.

Now that you know how to stop the back of your hair from sticking up, you can say goodbye to bad hair days for good. Remember to choose the right haircut, use the right styling products, and follow our step-by-step guide for best results. With a little bit of effort and some TLC, you can achieve the sleek and polished look you've always wanted.
