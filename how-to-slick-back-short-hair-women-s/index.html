How To Slick Back Short Hair Women s A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Slicked back hairstyles have been a trend for both men and women for a while now. While it may seem like slicked back hair is only for long-haired women, short-haired women can also rock this look. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to slick back short hair women's style.

Description

Slicked back hair is a style where the hair is combed back and held in place with hair gel or pomade. This style can be tailored to suit different hair types and lengths. Slicked back hair is a versatile style that can be worn for a casual day out or a formal event.

How to

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a styling product such as hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Comb your hair back using a fine-tooth comb. Start from the front of your hairline and work your way back.

Step 4: Smooth out bumps

Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your comb or fingers.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to ensure a long-lasting hold.
  • Comb your hair back in sections to ensure a smooth finish.
  • Use a hairdryer on low heat to set the product in place.
  • Experiment with different styles such as a side part or a wet look.

FAQ

Is slicked back hair only for long hair?

No, slicked back hair can be achieved on short hair as well.

What type of product should I use for slicked back hair?

You can use hair gel, pomade, or wax for slicked back hair.

How long will the slicked back hairstyle last?

The slicked back hairstyle can last for a day or two if you use a high-quality styling product and hairspray.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask once a week, and trimming your hair every six to eight weeks.

In conclusion, slicked back hair is a versatile and stylish look that can be achieved on short hair. Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle.
