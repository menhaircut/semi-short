Are you looking for a classic and timeless haircut that will never go out of style? Look no further than the men's haircut 4 on sides. This cut has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this haircut, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The men's haircut 4 on sides is a short haircut that is achieved by cutting the hair to a length of 4 on the sides and back. The hair on top can be left longer or cut to a shorter length, depending on your preference. This cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a great choice for any occasion.

How to Get the Men's Haircut 4 on Sides

Getting the men's haircut 4 on sides is simple, but it's important to find a skilled barber or stylist who can execute the cut correctly. Here are the steps to getting the perfect men's haircut 4 on sides:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Before your barber or stylist begins cutting your hair, you'll need to decide how short you want the sides and back to be. A length of 4 is standard for this cut, but you can go shorter or slightly longer if you prefer.

Step 2: Communicate with Your Barber or Stylist

Be sure to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the length you want and any other details you'd like them to know. Depending on your hair type and face shape, your barber or stylist may suggest slight variations to the cut to ensure it looks its best on you.

Step 3: Sit Back and Relax

Once you've communicated with your barber or stylist, it's time to sit back and relax while they work their magic. They'll use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length on the sides and back, then blend it into the hair on top.

Tips for Styling the Men's Haircut 4 on Sides

The men's haircut 4 on sides is a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some tips for styling this classic cut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

To style the men's haircut 4 on sides, you'll need to use the right products. A matte pomade or wax is a great choice for a natural, textured look, while a high-shine pomade or gel can be used for a more polished look.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. This cut can be styled with a side part, slicked back, or messy and textured.

Tip 3: Consider Your Face Shape

The men's haircut 4 on sides can be flattering on many different face shapes, but it's important to consider your unique features when styling the cut. For example, a side part can help balance a round face, while a messy, textured look can add dimension to a square face.

Hair Care Tips for the Men's Haircut 4 on Sides

Here are some tips for taking care of the men's haircut 4 on sides:

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo

Use a quality shampoo to keep your hair clean and healthy. Look for a sulfate-free formula that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable. Look for a conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools like a blow dryer or flat iron. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.
