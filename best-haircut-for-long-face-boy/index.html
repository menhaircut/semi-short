Best Haircut For Long Face Boy The Ultimate Guide 2023

Written by Petter Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are a boy with a long face, finding the perfect haircut can be quite challenging. Your face shape requires a specific style that can balance your features and create a harmonious look. In this article, we will break down the best haircut for long face boy, providing you with tips, inspiration, and a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect style. Let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, an elongated chin, and a narrow jawline. The ideal haircut for this face shape is one that adds width to the face, balancing its proportions and creating a more oval shape. Generally, hairstyles that cover the forehead and add volume to the sides work best for long face boys. However, it's crucial to consider other factors such as hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

How to

To choose the best haircut for your long face shape, follow these easy steps:

  1. Identify your face shape: use a measuring tape to determine the length and width of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles for long face shape: look for inspiration online or in magazines. Pay attention to the haircuts that add volume to the sides and cover the forehead.
  3. Consider your hair texture: if you have thick hair, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume. If you have thin hair, go for haircuts that add texture and movement.
  4. Think about your lifestyle: choose a haircut that suits your daily routine and maintenance level. If you don't have much time to style your hair, opt for a low-maintenance cut.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist: a professional can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture. They can also provide you with styling tips and product recommendations.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best haircut for long face boy:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling your haircut:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head as they can elongate your face even more.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to shorten your forehead and balance your face proportions.
  • Avoid slicked-back hairstyles as they can make your face look longer.
  • Layered haircuts can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a fuller look.
  • Regular trims can help to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long face boy with curly hair?

The best haircut for a long face boy with curly hair is a layered cut that adds volume and texture. Avoid haircuts that are too short as they can make your face look longer. Instead, opt for a medium-length cut that falls around your shoulders.

Can I wear a buzz cut if I have a long face?

A buzz cut is not the best option for a long face shape as it can make your face look even longer. However, you can still wear a short haircut as long as it adds volume to the sides and covers the forehead. Consider a textured crop or a side-swept fringe.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of haircuts may vary depending on your hair texture, growth rate, and personal preference.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and choosing the best haircut for long face boy, you can enhance your features and create a balanced, harmonious look. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and consider your personal style and hair texture when choosing a haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and lifestyle.
