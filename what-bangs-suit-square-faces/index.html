What Bangs Suit Square Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a square-shaped face, you may find it challenging to choose the perfect bangs style that suits your facial features. But worry not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll discuss what bangs suit square faces and provide you with helpful tips and tricks to achieve that perfect look you’ve been dreaming of.

Description

Square faces have a broad forehead, strong jawline, and angular features. The goal of choosing the right bangs style is to soften the angles of your face and draw attention away from the strong jawline. The right bangs will help balance your facial features and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Square Faces

Choosing the right bangs style for square faces can be tricky, but it’s not impossible. Here are some tips you can follow to achieve the perfect look:

Consider the Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair is an essential factor in choosing the right bangs style. If you have long hair, you can opt for longer bangs that blend well with your hair. On the other hand, if you have short hair, shorter bangs will complement your hairstyle better.

Choose Soft and Rounded Bangs

Soft and rounded bangs are ideal for square faces as they help soften the angles of your face. You can opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that frame your face and draw attention away from your jawline.

Avoid Sharp and Angular Bangs

Avoid sharp and angular bangs as they can make your face appear more prominent and boxy. Instead, opt for soft and rounded bangs that balance your facial features and enhance your natural beauty.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Bangs for Square Faces

Now that you know what bangs suit square faces let’s dive into the step-by-step guide on how to achieve that perfect look:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Square faces have a broad forehead, strong jawline, and angular features. Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right bangs style that complements your facial features.

Step 2: Choose the Right Bangs Style

Choose the right bangs style that suits your face shape. Soft and rounded bangs such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal for square faces as they help soften the angles of your face.

Step 3: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bangs style that suits your hair type and facial features. Your hairstylist can also provide you with helpful tips and tricks to achieve that perfect look.

Step 4: Maintain Your Bangs

Maintain your bangs by trimming them regularly to keep them in shape. Use a round brush and blow-dry your bangs to achieve that perfect look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them in shape. This will prevent them from looking overgrown and unkempt.

Blow-Dry Your Bangs

Use a round brush and blow-dry your bangs to achieve that perfect look. This will also help keep your bangs in place throughout the day.

Avoid Touching Your Bangs

Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day as this can cause them to become greasy and lose their shape.

FAQ

What Bangs Style Suits Square Faces?

Soft and rounded bangs such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal for square faces as they help soften the angles of your face.

How Do I Achieve the Perfect Bangs for Square Faces?

You can achieve the perfect bangs for square faces by following these steps: determine your face shape, choose the right bangs style, consult with your hairstylist, and maintain your bangs regularly.

How Often Should I Trim My Bangs?

You should trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them in shape and prevent them from looking overgrown and unkempt.

Hair Care for Square Faces

To maintain healthy hair and enhance your natural beauty, follow these hair care tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair regularly as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. The sun’s UV rays can cause damage and dryness to your hair.

In conclusion, choosing the right bangs style for square faces can be challenging, but it’s not impossible. Soft and rounded bangs such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal for square faces as they help soften the angles of your face. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bangs style that suits your hair type and facial features. Maintain your bangs regularly by trimming them every four to six weeks and using a round brush and blow-drying them to achieve that perfect look.
