How To Cut Longer Hair Mens A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you know how important it is to keep your locks looking their best. But going to a salon can be expensive, and it's not always easy to find a stylist who understands how to cut longer hair mens. That's why we've put together this comprehensive guide to cutting your own hair at home. Whether you're looking for a simple trim or a more dramatic change, we've got you covered.

Description

This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own long hair. We will cover everything from choosing the right tools to creating the perfect style. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and provide tips for maintaining your hair after the cut.

How to Cut Longer Hair Mens

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather some tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, then blow dry until it's completely dry. This will make it easier to see what you're doing when you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Trim the Sides

Start with the sides of your hair. Take a small section of hair from one side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim the ends with your scissors. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Trim the Back

Next, move on to the back of your hair. Use a mirror to see the back of your head. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your head and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim the ends. Repeat this process, working your way down the back of your head.

Step 5: Trim the Front

Finally, it's time to trim the front of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim the ends. Repeat this process on both sides of your head.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've trimmed all four sections, it's time to blend the layers. Comb your hair straight down and use your scissors to blend any uneven sections.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid splitting the ends of your hair.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take small sections and trim gradually.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair in place while you're trimming.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting longer hair mens:

  • How often should I cut my hair? It's best to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy.
  • What if I make a mistake while cutting? Don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a salon to fix any mistakes.
  • Can I cut my hair wet or dry? It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you'll be able to cut your own longer hair mens like a pro. Remember to take your time and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Good luck!
