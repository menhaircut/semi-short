Best Styles For Round Faces

Written by Oliver Nov 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best styles for round faces? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the most flattering hairstyles for those with round faces. We will cover everything from how to pick the right style to hair care tips to keep your locks looking fabulous!

Description

When it comes to hairstyles, one size does not fit all. Different face shapes require different styles to flatter them. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a style that elongates your face and adds angles. The right cut can create the illusion of a slimmer, more oval-shaped face.

How to Choose the Right Style

The right style for a round face will depend on a few factors, including the length and thickness of your hair, your personal style, and your face shape. Here are some tips for choosing the perfect style:

Consider the Length

Long hair can be very flattering on a round face, especially if it's layered. Layers add movement and texture, which can help to break up the roundness of your face. If you prefer shorter hair, go for a bob that's longer in the front and shorter in the back. This will create a flattering angle that elongates your face.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in the best style for your face shape. If you have thick hair, you'll want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for a style that keeps your hair close to your head. If you have fine hair, you can add volume with layers and a bit of teasing at the crown.

Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also play a role in the best style for your face shape. If you love edgy, bold looks, go for a pixie cut with choppy layers. If you prefer a more classic look, try a sleek bob with blunt ends.

Step by Step: Flattering Styles for Round Faces

Now that you know what to consider when choosing the right style for your face shape, let's take a look at some of the most flattering styles for round faces.

1. Long Layers

If you have long hair, add some layers to create movement and texture. Keep your hair close to your head to avoid adding too much volume to the sides. This style will elongate your face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob that's longer in the front and shorter in the back is a great option for those with round faces. This style creates a flattering angle that elongates your face. Plus, it's easy to maintain and style!

3. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a bold, edgy look, a pixie cut with choppy layers is a great option. This style adds height to the top of your head, which elongates your face. Plus, it's low maintenance and easy to style!

4. Side-Swept Bangs

If you have long hair, add some side-swept bangs to create angles and break up the roundness of your face. This style is easy to maintain and looks great with a variety of hair textures.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

Once you've found the perfect style for your face shape, you'll want to keep it looking fabulous. Here are some tips for maintaining your style:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help to keep your hair looking great. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight styling product to add volume without weighing your hair down.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks can help to keep your style looking fresh and prevent split ends. Plus, it's a great way to maintain the shape of your cut.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. And always use a lower heat setting to avoid frying your locks.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your head, such as blunt bangs, one-length bobs, and blunt cuts.

Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just be sure to choose a style that elongates your face, such as a pixie cut with choppy layers or a bob that's longer in the front and shorter in the back.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Remember, the right style for your face shape is just one piece of the puzzle. Good hair care is also essential for keeping your locks looking healthy and fabulous. Be sure to:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove buildup and keep your locks looking shiny and healthy.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair looking and feeling soft and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.

Now that you know the best styles for round faces and how to maintain them, you're ready to rock your new look with confidence. Whether you prefer long layers, a shoulder-length bob, or a bold pixie cut, there's a style out there that will flatter your face shape and show off your unique style.
