Does Bangs Suit Oval Face A Guide To Rocking Bangs For Your Face Shape

Written by Christine Sep 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. But when it comes to bangs, you might be wondering if they suit your face shape. In this article, we’ll explore the answer to the question, “Does bangs suit oval face?” and give you tips on how to rock bangs for your face shape.

Description: Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced features, with a slightly curved jawline and a forehead that’s slightly wider than the chin. It’s considered the most versatile face shape because of its balanced proportions, making it suitable for almost any hairstyle. Oval faces also have a naturally high forehead and prominent cheekbones, making them perfect for bangs.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you’re unsure about your face shape, here’s a quick guide on how to determine it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you traced.
  4. Compare the shape to the common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Step by Step Guide: Does Bangs Suit Oval Face?

Yes, bangs do suit oval face. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to rock bangs for your face shape:

Step 1: Determine the Type of Bangs You Want

There are various types of bangs, such as blunt, side-swept, choppy, curtain, and baby bangs. Determine the type of bangs that you want and would suit your face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

Your hair texture and length play a significant role in determining the type of bangs that would suit you. For instance, if you have curly hair, you might want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept or curtain bangs instead.

Step 3: Consult Your Stylist

Consult your stylist and show them pictures of the type of bangs that you want. They can give you advice on the best type of bangs that would suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you have your bangs, it’s essential to style them correctly. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush, and use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your bangs.

Tips for Rocking Bangs with an Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips for rocking bangs with an oval face shape:

  • Opt for side-swept or curtain bangs to highlight your cheekbones and eyes.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear shorter.
  • Consider your hair texture and length when choosing the type of bangs to get.
  • Style your bangs correctly to avoid any kinks or frizz.

FAQ: Does Bangs Suit Oval Face?

What type of bangs suit oval face?

Side-swept and curtain bangs suit oval face the most, as they highlight the cheekbones and eyes.

Can oval face shape pull off blunt bangs?

Oval face shape can pull off blunt bangs, but it’s not the most flattering style for this face shape. Blunt bangs can make the face appear shorter, which doesn’t complement the balanced proportions of an oval face shape.

What should I consider before getting bangs?

Before getting bangs, consider your face shape, hair texture, and length. Consult your stylist and show them pictures of the type of bangs that you want. They can give you advice on the best type of bangs that would suit your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care for Bangs

Once you have bangs, it’s essential to take care of them correctly. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

  • Wash your bangs regularly, as they tend to get oily quickly.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to achieve a smooth finish.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as it can transfer oils and dirt from your hands to your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and avoid any split ends.

Now that you know the answer to the question, “Does bangs suit oval face?” you can confidently rock bangs that complement your face shape. Remember to consider your hair texture, length, and consult your stylist before getting bangs. With the right type of bangs and proper hair care, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look.
