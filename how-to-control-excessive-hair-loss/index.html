2. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair loss, so finding ways to manage stress is essential for controlling hair loss. Some stress-reducing techniques include:

These techniques can help reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to hair loss. Avoid using these tools, or use them sparingly. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
