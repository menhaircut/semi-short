How To Cut A Polo Shirt Into A Crop Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your old polo shirt? Do you want to give it a new look? If yes, then cutting it into a crop top is a great idea. It's easy, fun, and will save you some money. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a polo shirt into a crop top in a step-by-step guide. Let's get started!

How to Cut a Polo Shirt into a Crop Top

Before we start, here are some things you'll need:

  • A polo shirt
  • A pair of scissors
  • A ruler
  • A fabric marker

Step 1: Choose the Right Polo Shirt

Choose a polo shirt that fits you well and has a good length. If you choose a shirt that is too tight or too loose, it won't look good as a crop top.

Step 2: Mark the Length

Decide how short you want your crop top to be and mark the length with a fabric marker. Use a ruler to make sure the line is straight.

Step 3: Cut the Hem

Cut the hem of the shirt along the line you marked. Make sure you cut both the front and back of the shirt.

Step 4: Cut the Sleeves

Cut the sleeves of the shirt just above the seam. Make sure you cut both sleeves evenly.

Step 5: Try it On

Try the crop top on to see if it fits well. If it's too long, you can always cut it shorter.

Step 6: Finish the Edges

If you want a clean finish, you can fold the edges of the shirt and sew them. If you don't have a sewing machine, you can use fabric glue to secure the edges.

Step 7: Style it

You can style your new crop top in many ways. You can wear it with high-waisted jeans, shorts, or skirts. You can also accessorize it with a belt or a statement necklace.

Tips

  • Wash and iron the shirt before cutting it.
  • If you're not sure about the length, start with a longer shirt and cut it shorter gradually.
  • Use fabric scissors for a clean cut.

FAQs

Can I use any type of polo shirt?

Yes, you can use any type of polo shirt as long as it fits well and has a good length.

Do I need to sew the edges?

No, you don't need to sew the edges if you don't want to. You can use fabric glue or leave the edges raw for a more casual look.

Can I cut the sleeves shorter?

Yes, you can cut the sleeves shorter if you want. Just make sure you cut both sleeves evenly.

Hair Care

When wearing a crop top, you want to make sure your hair looks good too. Here are some tips:

  • Tie your hair up in a high ponytail for a chic look.
  • Wear your hair down in loose waves for a bohemian vibe.
  • Braid your hair for a cute and casual look.

Now that you know how to cut a polo shirt into a crop top, it's time to get creative. Have fun with it and experiment with different styles.
