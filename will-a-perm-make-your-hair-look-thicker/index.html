Will A Perm Make Your Hair Look Thicker

Description

Many people dream of having thick, luscious hair. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally thick hair. If you're looking for ways to make your hair look thicker, you may be considering getting a perm. But will a perm really make your hair look thicker? In this blog post, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide you with some tips for achieving thicker-looking hair.

How to

The first step in determining whether a perm will make your hair look thicker is to understand what a perm is. A perm involves chemically altering the structure of your hair to create curls or waves. This process can make your hair appear fuller and thicker.

If you're considering getting a perm, it's important to work with a professional stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Your stylist can recommend the best type of perm for your hair type and help you determine whether a perm will make your hair look thicker.

Step by step

If you decide to get a perm, here are the steps you can expect:

  1. Your stylist will wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Your hair will be wrapped around perm rods, which come in different sizes depending on the desired curl or wave.
  3. A perm solution will be applied to your hair, which will break down the bonds in your hair to create the curl or wave.
  4. Your hair will be rinsed thoroughly to remove the perm solution.
  5. A neutralizer will be applied to your hair to reform the bonds and set the curl or wave.
  6. Your hair will be rinsed again and styled as desired.

Tips

If you're looking to make your hair look thicker, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a perm size that will create the most volume and fullness for your hair type.
  • Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a lift.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Try blow-drying your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about perms and hair thickness:

Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can potentially damage your hair if not done properly. It's important to work with a professional stylist who can ensure that your hair is healthy enough for a perm and that the process is done correctly.

How long will a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your hair after the perm.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. Coloring your hair too soon after a perm can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care

If you decide to get a perm to make your hair look thicker, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the curl or wave.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage permed hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, a perm can make your hair look thicker by creating volume and fullness through curls or waves. However, it's important to work with a professional stylist and take good care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance.
