How To Texture Your Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking limp and lifeless? Do you want to add some texture to your locks? If so, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll teach you how to texture your hair guys in a few easy steps. From choosing the right products to styling techniques, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Choose the Right Products
  • Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair
  • Step 3: Apply the Texturizing Product
  • Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair
  • Step 5: Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Texturing Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Textured Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

The first step in texturizing your hair is to choose the right products. Look for a texturizing spray or pomade that is specifically designed for men’s hair. These products will help you create volume, definition, and texture without weighing your hair down. Some good options include:

  • Sea salt spray
  • Pomade
  • Mousse
  • Hair wax

Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you. Keep in mind that different hair types may require different products.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before applying any texturizing product, it’s important to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. If you have oily hair, look for a shampoo that is specifically formulated to control oil. If you have dry hair, look for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 3: Apply the Texturizing Product

Once your hair is clean and conditioned, it’s time to apply the texturizing product. Depending on the product you’re using, you may need to apply it to damp or dry hair. Follow the directions on the product label for best results.

Start by applying a small amount of product to your fingertips. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Then, work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair for the best results.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve applied the texturizing product, it’s time to blow dry your hair. Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to add volume and texture to your locks. Start by drying the roots of your hair, using your hands to lift and separate your hair as you go.

As you dry your hair, use your fingers to scrunch and shape your locks. This will help create a tousled, textured look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, it’s time to style your hair. Use your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style. You can also use a comb or a brush to create more definition.

Remember that texturizing your hair is all about creating a messy, undone look. Don’t worry if your hair looks a little disheveled – that’s the whole point!

Tips for Texturing Your Hair

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to add volume and texture to your locks.
  • Use your fingers to scrunch and shape your locks as you dry your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques until you find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

How often should I texturize my hair?

It’s up to you! Some people texturize their hair every day, while others only do it once a week. Experiment with different schedules to find the one that works best for you.

Can I texturize my hair if it’s already styled?

Yes! If you’ve already styled your hair, you can still add texture by using a texturizing spray or pomade. Simply apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.

Do I need to use a specific type of brush or comb?

No, you can use any brush or comb that you prefer. However, a wide-tooth comb may be helpful for detangling your hair before you apply the texturizing product.

Hair Care Tips for Textured Hair

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

And there you have it – a step-by-step guide on how to texture your hair guys! Follow these tips and tricks to add texture, volume, and definition to your locks. Remember to choose the right products, wash and condition your hair, and experiment with different styles until you find the look that works best for you. Happy styling!
