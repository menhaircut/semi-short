Can You Start Locs With Heat Damaged Hair

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Starting locs is a big decision that requires a lot of research and consideration. If you have heat damaged hair, you may be wondering if it is possible to start locs. The answer is yes, but there are some important things to keep in mind.

Description

Heat damaged hair is hair that has been exposed to high temperatures from styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. This can cause hair to become brittle, dry, and prone to breakage. Starting locs on heat damaged hair can be more challenging, but it is possible with the right techniques and care.

How to Start Locs with Heat Damaged Hair

Starting locs with heat damaged hair requires patience and a gentle approach. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before starting your locs, it is important to prepare your hair by doing a protein treatment to strengthen your strands. You should also deep condition your hair to add moisture and improve its elasticity.

Step 2: Choose a Method

There are several methods for starting locs, including the palm roll method, two-strand twist method, and interlocking. The method you choose will depend on your hair type, texture, and lifestyle.

Step 3: Be Gentle

When starting locs on heat damaged hair, it is important to be gentle to avoid further damage. This means avoiding tight styles and using a light touch when twisting or palm rolling your hair.

Step 4: Maintain Your Locs

Once your locs are established, you will need to maintain them by washing and conditioning them regularly. You should also avoid heat styling and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Tips for Starting Locs with Heat Damaged Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you start your locs on heat damaged hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent breakage
  • Be patient and allow your locs to form naturally

FAQ

Can you start locs on severely heat damaged hair?

It is possible to start locs on severely heat damaged hair, but it may take longer for your hair to form into locs. You will also need to be gentle and patient when starting your locs to avoid further damage.

Can you start locs on relaxed hair?

Yes, it is possible to start locs on relaxed hair. However, it is important to wait until your hair has grown out a bit to avoid damage to your scalp.

How often should you wash your locs?

You should wash your locs at least once a week to keep them clean and free from buildup.

Hair Care for Locs with Heat Damaged Hair

Once you have started your locs, it is important to take care of them to prevent further damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your locs
  • Wrap your hair at night to prevent breakage
  • Avoid heat styling and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your locs healthy and free from split ends

Starting locs on heat damaged hair may require more patience and care, but it is possible with the right techniques and maintenance. Be gentle with your hair, choose a method that works for you, and be patient as your locs form.
