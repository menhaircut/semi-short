How To Contour For Square Face A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having a square-shaped face and not knowing how to contour it properly? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to contour for square face and make it look more defined and slimmer. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour for Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring Square Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Faces

Description

A square face has a strong jawline, broad forehead, and wide cheekbones. The goal of contouring for square faces is to create a more oval or heart-shaped face by softening the angles and adding definition. Contouring helps to create shadows and highlights, which can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

How to Contour for Square Face

The key to contouring for square faces is to create the illusion of an oval or heart-shaped face. This can be achieved by contouring the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by applying foundation and concealer to your face.
  2. Using a contouring powder or cream that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone, apply it to the sides of your forehead and blend it in towards the center of your forehead.
  3. Apply the contouring powder or cream underneath your cheekbones and blend it upwards towards your temples.
  4. Apply the contouring powder or cream to your jawline and blend it downwards towards your neck.
  5. Apply highlighter to the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, your cheekbones, and your cupid's bow.
  6. Blend the highlighter in with a beauty blender or brush.
  7. Set your makeup with a translucent powder.

Tips for Contouring Square Faces

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when contouring for square faces:

  • Blend, blend, blend! This is the key to achieving a natural-looking contour.
  • Use a light hand when applying contouring products to avoid looking too harsh.
  • Choose a contouring product that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Apply highlighter to the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, your cheekbones, and your cupid's bow to add dimension to your face.
  • Use a matte bronzer to contour your face rather than a shimmery one.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about contouring for square faces:

Q: Can contouring make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, contouring can create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding shadows and highlights to your face.

Q: How often should I contour my face?

A: Contouring is a personal preference and can be done as often as you like. However, it's important to give your skin a break from heavy makeup every once in a while.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Your hair can also play a role in how your face looks. Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or bobs.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add length to your face, such as long layers or side-swept bangs.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters your face the most.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to contour for square face like a pro and create the illusion of a slimmer, more defined face. Remember to take your time and blend, blend, blend!
