Curly On Top Short On Sides Haircut

Written by Daniel Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that suits your curly hair? Look no further than the "curly on top short on sides" haircut. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their curls intact while still maintaining a neat and clean appearance. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, step by step, and provide some tips and FAQ for maintaining this hairstyle. Let's dive in!

Description

The curly on top short on sides haircut is a versatile haircut that features longer curly hair on top and shorter hair on the sides. This hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types and lengths, making it a popular choice for many men. The longer hair on top allows for more styling options, while the shorter sides create a clean and polished look.

How to

Here's how you can achieve the curly on top short on sides haircut:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is to determine the length of your hair. The hair on top should be long enough to create curls, while the sides should be short. The length of the hair on top can vary from person to person depending on their hair type and personal preference.

Step 2: Wash and condition your hair

Before styling your hair, it's important to wash and condition it to keep it healthy and hydrated. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Apply styling products

Once your hair is clean and conditioned, apply a styling product to enhance your curls and hold your hair in place. Use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to create defined curls on top, and a styling gel or pomade to keep the sides in place.

Step 4: Style your hair

With the right products in place, it's time to style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create defined curls on top, and use a clipper or trimmer to clean up the sides and back. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly on top short on sides haircut:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to keep your hair looking neat and clean.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your curls overnight.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the curly on top short on sides haircut:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

While this hairstyle works best for curly or wavy hair, it can be adapted for other hair types as well. Talk to your stylist to find the best haircut for your hair type.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I still style my hair differently with this haircut?

Absolutely! The longer hair on top allows for plenty of styling options, from messy curls to slicked-back styles.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and hydrated, it's important to use the right hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your curls. Instead, embrace your natural curls by using curl-enhancing products and diffusers to create defined, bouncy curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect curly on top short on sides haircut and maintain it for weeks to come. Embrace your curls and rock this stylish and trendy haircut!
