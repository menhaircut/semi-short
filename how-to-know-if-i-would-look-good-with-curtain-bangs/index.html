How To Know If You Would Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Oliver Jul 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but unsure if it would suit you? Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle in recent years, and it's no wonder why. They can frame your face beautifully, add texture to your hair, and give you a chic, effortless look. However, not everyone can rock the curtain bangs look. In this post, we'll guide you on how to know if you would look good with curtain bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Assessing Your Lifestyle
  • Evaluating Styling Time and Maintenance

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the most crucial factors to determine if you would look good with curtain bangs is your face shape. Curtain bangs are versatile and can work for any face shape, but some styles may suit certain shapes better than others. Here are some guidelines to follow:

  • Round Face: Opt for longer curtain bangs that are slightly parted in the middle.
  • Square Face: Soften your angles with wispy, textured curtain bangs.
  • Oval Face: Lucky you! You can try out any style of curtain bangs.
  • Heart Face: Go for wispy bangs that start from the middle of your forehead and taper down the sides.
  • Diamond Face: Try out heavy, blunt curtain bangs that hit below your eyebrows.

Remember that these are just guidelines, and you should choose the style that makes you feel most confident.


Considering Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture will also play a significant role in determining if you would look good with curtain bangs. If you have thin or fine hair, you might want to opt for shorter curtain bangs that add volume and texture to your hair. On the other hand, if you have thick or curly hair, you can go for longer curtain bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.


Assessing Your Lifestyle

Curtain bangs may require some maintenance, so it's essential to consider your lifestyle before getting them. Are you willing to spend time styling your bangs every day? Do you have a job that requires you to tie your hair back? If yes, then curtain bangs might not be the best option for you.


Evaluating Styling Time and Maintenance

Curtain bangs require some styling, but the amount of time you spend on them will depend on the style you choose. If you opt for shorter, wispy bangs, you might only need a few minutes to style them every morning. However, if you go for heavier, blunt bangs, you might need to spend more time blow-drying and styling them. Keep in mind that curtain bangs also require regular trims to maintain their shape.


Tips for Face-Framing Curtain Bangs

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs would suit you, try out face-framing curtain bangs first. These are shorter bangs that frame your face and add texture to your hair without committing to full-on curtain bangs. You can also experiment with clip-in bangs or faux bangs to see how they look on you before getting a haircut.


Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Since curtain bangs sit close to your forehead, they tend to get greasy faster than the rest of your hair. To keep your bangs looking fresh, use dry shampoo in between washes. You can also use a small amount of hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz. When blow-drying your bangs, use a round brush to create a soft, natural-looking curve.


FAQs about Curtain Bangs

Can I wear curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes! Curtain bangs can work well with curly hair, but you might need to trim them a little shorter than usual to prevent them from weighing down your curls.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

It's best to trim your curtain bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

It depends on the style you choose. Curtain bangs that are shorter and wispy require less maintenance than heavier, blunt bangs. However, all curtain bangs require regular trims to keep them looking fresh.

Can I style my curtain bangs differently?

Yes! Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can part them in the middle or to the side, sweep them to one side, or pin them back with bobby pins.


Now that you know how to determine if you would look good with curtain bangs, it's time to book that hair appointment and try them out! Remember to choose the style that makes you feel most confident and have fun experimenting with different ways to style your bangs.
