Do Curtain Bangs Suit Oblong Faces

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an oblong-faced beauty wondering if curtain bangs will suit you? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing whether curtain bangs are a good fit for oblong faces. We'll also provide you with a how-to guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to help you make your decision.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Oblong faces are characterized by their long and narrow shape. If you have an oblong face, you may have a high forehead, long nose, and pointed chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for an oblong face is to make it appear shorter and wider. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can achieve this effect, but do they suit oblong faces?

How to

If you're unsure whether curtain bangs will suit your oblong face, the best way to find out is to consult with a hairstylist. They can assess your face shape and recommend a hairstyle that suits you. However, if you're determined to try curtain bangs on your own, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The length of your curtain bangs depends on your preference and face shape. For an oblong face, it's best to keep the length longer and closer to the cheekbones. This will help to widen the face and balance out its length.

Step 2: Part your hair in the middle

Curtain bangs are characterized by their parted style. To achieve this look, part your hair in the middle and brush your hair forward. Your bangs should frame your face on both sides.

Step 3: Trim your bangs

Using sharp scissors, trim your bangs to the desired length. Start by trimming a little at a time and checking the length in the mirror. Remember, you can always trim more, but you can't add length back once it's gone.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving curtain bangs for oblong faces:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Part your hair in the middle
  3. Brush your hair forward
  4. Trim your bangs to the desired length
  5. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying curtain bangs on an oblong face:

  • Keep the bangs longer and closer to the cheekbones
  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and personal preference
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about the best length for your face shape
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid uneven cuts
  • Style your hair with a round brush for extra volume

FAQs

Do curtain bangs suit oblong faces?

Yes, curtain bangs can suit oblong faces. They can help to balance out the length of the face and create the illusion of a wider face shape.

What length should curtain bangs be for oblong faces?

Curtain bangs should be longer and closer to the cheekbones for oblong faces. This helps to widen the face and balance out its length.

Can I trim my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can trim your own curtain bangs at home. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about the best length for your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

So, there you have it - a complete guide to curtain bangs for oblong faces. Whether you decide to try them out or not, remember that the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. Happy hairstyling!
