Short Pixie Hairstyles For Round Faces And Thin Hair A Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face and thin hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and adds volume to your hair without overwhelming your features. That's where short pixie hairstyles come in. Pixie cuts are a versatile and stylish option for women with round faces and thin hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to rock a short pixie hairstyle with confidence and ease.

Description

Short pixie hairstyles are a popular choice for women of all ages and hair types. The style features short, textured layers that create volume and movement, while the tapered sides and back add definition to the face. Pixie cuts can be customized to suit any face shape, including round faces, and are a great way to show off your features.

How to

To achieve a short pixie hairstyle, you'll need to visit a hairstylist who is skilled in cutting short hair. Your stylist will work with you to determine the best length, texture, and shape for your face and hair type. They may also suggest adding layers or bangs to enhance your features.

Once you have your new cut, you'll need to style it at home. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair, focusing on the crown and sides to create volume. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step by step

1. Visit a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.

2. Work with your stylist to determine the best length, texture, and shape for your face and hair type.

3. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

4. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

5. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair, focusing on the crown and sides to create volume.

6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

1. Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

2. Add layers or bangs to your pixie cut to enhance your features.

3. Use a small amount of styling wax to create definition and texture.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is a pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can be customized to suit any face shape, including round faces.

Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

Q: Can I still style my hair in different ways with a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques, such as slicking your hair back or adding a side part.

Hair Care

To keep your short pixie hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools, and avoid using hot tools on wet hair. Finally, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf when outside.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short pixie hairstyle with confidence and ease. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you!
