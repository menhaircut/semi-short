Does Shorter Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're considering a shorter hairstyle but are worried that it will make your face look rounder or fuller, you're not alone. Many people believe that shorter haircuts can emphasize the face's width and make it appear fatter. But is this really true? In this blog post, we'll explore the myth and give you some tips on how to choose the right short haircut for your face shape.

Description

It's a common belief that shorter haircuts can make your face look fatter because they don't have the length to elongate and slim down the face. However, this is not entirely true. The right short hairstyle can actually enhance your facial features and make your face look more balanced and proportionate.

The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Different face shapes require different haircuts to create balance and harmony. A good hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and features.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a short haircut is to identify your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's a quick guide to help you determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Round: Your face is almost as wide as it is long, with a soft, circular hairline and full cheeks.
  • Square: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong, angular jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline.
  • Oblong: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight hairline and elongated chin.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Almost any short haircut will suit an oval face shape, as it's the most balanced and proportionate of all face shapes.
  • Round: A short haircut with layers and texture can help elongate the face and create a more angular look. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheeks.
  • Square: A short haircut with soft, wispy layers can soften the strong jawline and create a more feminine look. Avoid blunt, straight-across cuts.
  • Heart: A short pixie cut or bob can complement a heart-shaped face by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Avoid heavy bangs that can accentuate the forehead.
  • Diamond: A short haircut with volume at the crown can balance out the widest part of the face. Avoid styles that are too flat or slicked back.
  • Oblong: A short haircut with volume on the sides can create the illusion of width and balance out the face. Avoid styles that are too long or flat.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Short Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right short haircut for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape using the guidelines above.
  2. Research short hairstyles that complement your face shape. You can use online resources or consult with a hairstylist.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some short hairstyles require more maintenance and styling than others.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and daily routine. Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  5. Bring pictures of your desired hairstyle to your hairstylist. They can help you determine if it will work for your face shape and features.
  6. Communicate with your hairstylist about your desired look and any concerns you may have.
  7. Trust your hairstylist's professional opinion. They have the expertise to create a haircut that complements your face shape and features.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires more maintenance and styling than longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in quality styling products to create the look you want.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying or using a flat iron.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting a professional color treatment to enhance your haircut.
  • Keep your scalp healthy by washing your hair regularly and using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

FAQ

Do short haircuts make your face look fatter?

Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually enhance your facial features and create balance and proportion.

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A short haircut with layers and texture can help elongate a round face and create a more angular look.

Can anyone pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can look great on anyone, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires more maintenance and styling than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using a flat iron.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in quality styling products to create the look you want.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a professional color treatment to enhance your haircut.
  • Avoid over-styling or using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

Short haircuts can enhance your facial features and create balance and proportion. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features. By following the tips and guidelines in this blog post, you can find the perfect short haircut for your unique look.
