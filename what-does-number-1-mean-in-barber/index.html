What Does Number 1 Mean In Barber

Written by Christine Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In the world of barbers, numbers are used to indicate the length of the hair that is being cut. Number 1 is one of the most commonly used numbers in barbering, and it refers to the length of the hair that is left after the cut. In this article, we will discuss what does number 1 mean in barber and everything you need to know about it.

Description

The number 1 in barbering refers to a clipper guard size that is used to cut hair to a length of 1/8 inch or 3mm. It is the shortest guard size that is commonly used, and it is perfect for creating a very short buzz cut or a military-style haircut. It is also commonly used for creating fades and for trimming the hairline and around the ears.

How to

To achieve a number 1 haircut, you will need a set of clippers with a number 1 guard attached. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, it is important to prepare the hair. Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner to make it easier to cut. Dry the hair thoroughly with a towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Attach the Number 1 Guard

Attach the number 1 guard to your clippers. Make sure it is securely attached and facing the right way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of the head, run the clippers over the hair in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Move the clippers slowly and steadily, making sure to keep the guard flat against the head. Continue cutting until you have covered the entire head.

Step 4: Clean Up

Use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs that were missed by the clippers. Pay particular attention to the hairline and around the ears.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with a number 1 guard:

  • Make sure the hair is dry before cutting.
  • Start at the back of the head and work your way forward.
  • Move the clippers slowly and steadily.
  • Use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs.
  • Always cut against the direction of hair growth.

FAQ

Is a Number 1 Haircut the Same as a Buzz Cut?

Yes, a number 1 haircut is essentially a very short buzz cut. It is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain.

Can I Use a Number 1 Guard for Fades?

Yes, a number 1 guard is commonly used for creating fades. It is perfect for creating a low fade or a skin fade.

What Should I Do If I Make a Mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. Simply use a larger guard to even out the hair or ask a professional barber for help.

Hair Care

After getting a number 1 haircut, it is important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

Now that you know what does number 1 mean in barber, you can confidently ask for this haircut and maintain it properly. Remember to always consult a professional barber if you have any questions or concerns.
