How To Know What Hair Color Suits You

Written by Eveline Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking to switch things up? Maybe you're interested in a bold new look, or perhaps you simply want to try something different. Whatever your reason, changing your hair color can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your style. But with so many options out there, it can be tough to know which color will look best on you. In this article, we'll explore how to know what hair color suits you so you can confidently make a change.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining what hair color will suit you is to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone can have a big impact on how a particular hair color will look on you. Generally speaking, people with cool skin tones (those with pink or blue undertones) will look best with cool hair colors, such as ash blonde or cool brown. People with warm skin tones (those with yellow or golden undertones) will look best with warm hair colors, such as golden blonde or warm brown.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to stick with a darker shade to avoid looking washed out. On the other hand, if you have naturally light hair, you may be able to pull off a wider range of colors.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone. Look at the veins on your wrist - if they appear blue, you likely have a cool skin tone, while green veins indicate a warm skin tone.

2. Consider your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to stick with a darker shade to avoid looking washed out. If you have naturally light hair, you may be able to pull off a wider range of colors.

3. Decide on a color range that will suit your skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, consider cool hair colors such as ash blonde, cool brown, or black. If you have a warm skin tone, consider warm hair colors such as golden blonde, warm brown, or copper.

4. Consider your lifestyle and personality. Are you someone who likes to stand out and make a statement, or do you prefer a more subtle look? Do you have a job that requires a more conservative appearance? These factors can help you decide on a hair color that will suit your needs.

Tips

- If you're not sure what color will suit you, consider trying on wigs or using virtual try-on tools to get an idea of how different colors will look on you.

- If you're nervous about making a drastic change, consider starting with a subtle change such as adding highlights or lowlights.

- If you're planning on coloring your hair at home, be sure to read the instructions carefully and do a patch test to avoid any adverse reactions.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to be careful and do a patch test first. You may also want to consider using a hair dye specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

A: This will depend on the type of color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally speaking, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. This may include using color-safe shampoos and conditioners, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Changing your hair color can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your style. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal preferences, you can find a hair color that will suit you and make you feel confident and beautiful.
