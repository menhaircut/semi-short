Medium Length Hairstyles Long Bangs A Relaxed Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both chic and easy to maintain, you might want to consider medium length hairstyles with long bangs. This style is versatile and can work for any hair type, whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair. Plus, it's a great way to switch up your look without going too short or too long.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hairstyles with long bangs typically fall between the chin and shoulders. The long bangs can frame your face and add a touch of glamour to your look. They can also make your hair appear fuller and more voluminous.

One of the benefits of this hairstyle is that it's low maintenance. You can easily style it with a blow dryer or straightener or leave it to air dry for a more natural look. It's perfect for those who are always on the go and don't have much time to fuss over their hair.

How To

To achieve this hairstyle, you'll need to have medium length hair that's at least shoulder length. If your hair is shorter than that, you might want to consider growing it out or opting for a different style. Here's how to create medium length hairstyles with long bangs:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use lukewarm water to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently remove excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can lead to breakage.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start blow drying or straightening your hair, apply a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to dry your hair. Start from the roots and work your way to the tips. Make sure to dry your hair in sections to avoid tangling.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

If you want to achieve a sleek look, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Finally, style your long bangs by either sweeping them to the side or straight across your forehead. You can also use a little bit of hair spray to keep them in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle with long bangs:

  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or blow dryer to avoid damaging your hair.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Try using a volumizing mousse or spray to add more body and texture to your hair.
  • If you're not sure whether this hairstyle will suit you, try experimenting with clip-in bangs first.

FAQ

Q: Will this hairstyle work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can work for all face shapes. However, you might want to adjust the length and shape of your bangs depending on your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear this hairstyle if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear this hairstyle if you have thin hair. In fact, the long bangs can make your hair appear thicker and fuller.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or straightening your hair, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a stunning medium length hairstyle with long bangs. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
