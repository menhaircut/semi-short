Easy Ways To Tie Up Mid Length Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have mid length hair, you may sometimes struggle to find easy and quick ways to style it. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you some easy ways to tie up mid length hair that you can try at home. Whether you're running late for work or need a quick hairstyle for a casual day out, these hairstyles will save your day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Up Mid Length Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Tying Up Mid Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

How to Tie Up Mid Length Hair

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about some basic tools and products you'll need to tie up your mid length hair:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair ties or elastic bands
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Now, let's take a look at some easy ways to tie up mid length hair:

1. Half Up, Half Down

This is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Simply gather the top section of your hair and tie it up with an elastic band. You can leave the rest of your hair down or style it in loose waves for a more relaxed look.

2. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and chic hairstyle that you can wear for any occasion. Gather all your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it up with an elastic band. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a perfect hairstyle for those lazy days when you don't have much time to style your hair. Simply gather all your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with some bobby pins and pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

4. Top Knot

A top knot is a stylish and easy hairstyle that you can wear for work or play. Gather all your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a knot. Secure the knot with some bobby pins and pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

Tips for Tying Up Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when tying up mid length hair:

  • Use hair ties that won't damage your hair, such as ones made of fabric or silicone.
  • Don't tie your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use bobby pins to secure stray hairs and create a polished look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories, such as headbands and scarves, to add some variety to your look.

FAQs

Q: Can I tie up my mid length hair if it's thin?

A: Yes, you can still tie up your mid length hair if it's thin. Just be gentle when tying it up and use hair ties that won't damage your hair.

Q: How often should I tie up my mid length hair?

A: It's best to give your hair a break from tying it up every day. Try to alternate between tied up and down hairstyles to prevent breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep your mid length hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to untangle your hair gently.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these easy ways to tie up mid length hair, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products and avoiding heat styling too often. Happy hairstyling!
