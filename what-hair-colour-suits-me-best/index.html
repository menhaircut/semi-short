What Hair Colour Suits Me Best

Written by Bardi Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering changing your hair colour? Do you want to know what hair colour suits you best? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair colour that will complement your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

Description

Your hair colour is an essential aspect of your appearance, and it can have a significant impact on your overall look. The right hair colour can highlight your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful. However, choosing the perfect hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you have no idea where to start. This article will help you find your ideal hair colour based on your skin tone, eye colour and personal style.

How to Find Your Perfect Hair Colour

Finding your perfect hair colour is all about understanding your skin tone, eye colour and personal style. Here are the steps you can follow to find the best hair colour for you:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your perfect hair colour is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you cannot tell if they are blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a significant role in determining your perfect hair colour. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours such as golden blondes, caramel, honey or auburn will suit you best. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colours such as ash brown, platinum blonde or black will complement your eyes beautifully.

Step 3: Personal Style

Your personal style is also an essential factor to consider when choosing your perfect hair colour. If you prefer a natural look, choose a hair colour that is close to your natural hair colour. If you want to make a statement, choose a bold hair colour such as pink, purple or blue.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some tips for choosing the perfect hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone, eye colour and personal style when choosing your hair colour.
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.
  • Consider the maintenance required for your chosen hair colour.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colours before committing to a permanent hair colour.
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist to ensure the best results.

FAQ

What hair colour suits warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours such as golden blondes, caramel, honey or auburn.

What hair colour suits cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours such as ash brown, platinum blonde or black.

What hair colour is best for brown eyes?

Cool hair colours such as ash brown, platinum blonde or black complement brown eyes beautifully.

Can I change my hair colour if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it is best to consult with a professional hair stylist before changing your hair colour to avoid any adverse reactions.

Hair Care

Once you have found your perfect hair colour, taking care of your hair is essential to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners excessively.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can strip the colour from your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair colour that suits you best and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
