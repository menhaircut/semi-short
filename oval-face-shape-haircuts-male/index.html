Oval Face Shape Haircuts For Men The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a man with an oval face shape looking for the perfect haircut to enhance your features? Look no further! In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about oval face shape haircuts for men, including how to determine your face shape, the best haircuts for oval faces, and how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Oval Faces
  • Tips for Styling Oval Face Shape Haircuts
  • FAQs about Oval Face Shape Haircuts for Men
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your oval face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your face is longer than it is wide, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you likely have an oval face shape.

Other common face shapes include round, square, heart, and diamond. Knowing your face shape can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

Best Haircuts for Oval Faces

Men with oval face shapes are lucky because they can pull off a wide range of haircuts. Some of the best haircuts for oval faces include:

  • Short buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Side swept fringe
  • Textured crop
  • Slicked back undercut

These haircuts are versatile and can be adapted to suit your personal style and preferences. For example, if you prefer a more classic look, you may opt for a crew cut or side swept fringe. If you're more adventurous, a textured crop or slicked back undercut may be more your style.

Tips for Styling Oval Face Shape Haircuts

Once you've chosen a haircut that suits your oval face shape, it's important to know how to style it. Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your haircut:

  • Use a high-quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create your desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • If you have thick hair, consider getting it thinned out to make styling easier.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on styling your haircut.

FAQs about Oval Face Shape Haircuts for Men

Here are some common questions and answers about oval face shape haircuts for men:

Q: Can men with oval face shapes wear long hair?

A: Yes, men with oval face shapes can wear long hair, but it's important to choose a style that doesn't add too much volume to the sides of the face.

Q: What is the most flattering haircut for oval faces?

A: The most flattering haircut for oval faces is one that balances the length of the face and enhances your natural features.

Q: Should men with oval face shapes avoid certain haircuts?

A: Men with oval face shapes can wear almost any haircut, but it's best to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • If you have long hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep it soft and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for your oval face shape can be a daunting task, but with the right information, it can be easy and fun. By determining your face shape, choosing a flattering haircut, and taking good care of your hair, you can look and feel your best. So go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best!
