How To Short Layer Your Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Layered hair has been a popular hairstyle for many years, and with good reason. It adds volume, dimension, and texture to any hair length or texture. Short layered hair, in particular, can be a great way to refresh your look and add some excitement to your everyday style. If you're interested in learning how to short layer your own hair, keep reading for a step-by-step guide.

How to Short Layer Your Own Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Once you have your tools ready, you're ready to start short layering your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to short layer your own hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair, making sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Clip each section of hair up and out of the way, except for the bottom section.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom section, and comb it straight down. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut the hair at a 45-degree angle. This will create the first layer of your short layered hair.
  4. Continue to take small sections of hair from the bottom section, and cut them at a 45-degree angle until you've finished the whole section.
  5. Release the next section of hair, and repeat the same process. Make sure to use the first layer as a guide to create even layers throughout your hair.
  6. Once you've finished all sections, check your layers in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Once you're happy with your new short layered hair, style it as desired.

Tips for Short Layering Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when short layering your hair:

  • Make sure to use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Keep your cuts small and go slow to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Don't cut too close to the scalp, as this can create uneven layers.
  • Use the first layer as a guide to create even layers throughout your hair.
  • When in doubt, seek the help of a professional hair stylist.

FAQs About Short Layered Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layered hair:

Q: How often should I get my short layered hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy, even layers.

Q: Will short layered hair work with my hair type?

A: Short layered hair can work with any hair type, but it's important to consult with a professional stylist to find the best layering technique for your specific hair type.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with short layers?

A: Yes, short layered hair can still be styled in a variety of updos, including ponytails and buns.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

Now that you have your new short layered hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors.

Congratulations, you've successfully short layered your own hair! With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to maintain your new style and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
