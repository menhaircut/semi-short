How To Curl The Left Side Of Your Hair With A Flat Iron

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you want to add some extra bounce and volume to your hair, curling it with a flat iron is a great option. However, many people struggle with curling the left side of their hair, especially if they are right-handed. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to curl the left side of your hair with a flat iron. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair with a Flat Iron
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs About Curling Your Hair with a Flat Iron

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl the left side of your hair using a flat iron:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, you need to prepare it. Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start by dividing your hair in half, separating the left side from the right side. Then, divide the left side into smaller sections.

Step 3: Heat the Flat Iron

Plug in your flat iron and let it heat up to the desired temperature. If you have thin or fine hair, you should use a lower temperature setting. For thick or coarse hair, use a higher temperature setting.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair from the left side and clamp it between the flat iron plates. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down the length of your hair, holding it for a few seconds. Release the hair from the flat iron and let it cool down before moving on to the next section.

Step 5: Repeat

Continue curling the left side of your hair, taking small sections and curling them away from your face. Make sure to alternate the direction of the curls to create a more natural look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have finished curling your hair, spray it with some hairspray to set the curls in place. You can also use a texturizing spray or hair oil to add some shine and definition to your curls.

Tips for Curling Your Hair with a Flat Iron

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Start with a lower temperature setting and gradually increase it as needed.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to make it easier to curl.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or hold the flat iron on your hair for too long to prevent damage.

FAQs About Curling Your Hair with a Flat Iron

Can I curl my hair with a flat iron if it's already curly?

Yes, you can use a flat iron to touch up your curls or create more defined curls.

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, the curls should last for a few hours to a day.

Can I curl my hair with a flat iron without using hairspray?

While hairspray can help set the curls in place, it's not necessary. If you prefer a more natural look, you can skip the hairspray.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

By following these tips and using the techniques outlined in this blog post, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with a flat iron. Happy styling!
