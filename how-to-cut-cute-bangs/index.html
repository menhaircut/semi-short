How To Cut Cute Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new look that doesn't require a complete haircut? Cutting cute bangs is a great way to switch things up without committing to a drastic change. But, where do you start? Follow this step-by-step guide on how to cut cute bangs to achieve the perfect look.

Description: What Are Cute Bangs?

Cute bangs are a shorter section of hair that falls across the forehead, framing the face. They come in different styles, such as blunt, side-swept, wispy, and choppy. Depending on your face shape and hair type, you can select the best bangs that suit you best.

How to Cut Cute Bangs?

Before you start cutting your bangs, gather your tools, which include a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Bangs Style

Determine the type of bangs you want, such as blunt, side-swept, or wispy. Look up inspirations online or consult with a hairstylist.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or blow-dry it. Comb your hair straight to remove any tangles.

Step 3: Separate the Bangs Section

Divide the hair into a triangular section at the top of your head. Ensure that the base of the triangle aligns with the center of your forehead.

Step 4: Wet the Bangs Section

Spray water onto the bangs section to make the hair easier to cut.

Step 5: Cut the Bangs

Trim the hair in small sections, starting from the middle and working your way outward. Cut at an angle for a more natural look. Continuously comb the hair to check if the length and thickness are even on both sides.

Step 6: Style the Bangs

Blow-dry the bangs and use a round brush to add volume or a straightener for a sleek look. Apply some hair serum or mousse to prevent frizz and keep the style in place.

Tips for Cutting Cute Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting cute bangs:

  • Do not cut your hair when it's wet, as it may shrink when it dries.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Take breaks and step back to see the overall look before you make the final cut.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Cutting Cute Bangs

1. How do I know which bangs style suits me best?

You can consult with a hairstylist or use online apps that allow you to try different hairstyles virtually. Also, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences.

2. Can I cut bangs on curly hair?

Yes, but it may be trickier than cutting straight hair. Ensure that you cut the bangs slightly longer than intended, as curly hair tends to bounce up when it dries.

3. What should I do if I mess up my bangs?

If you cut too much or unevenly, don't panic. You can either style it differently or go to a professional hair salon to fix it.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Now that you've cut your cute bangs, you want to maintain their shape and health. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to prevent split ends and ensure the length remains even.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your hair and keep it shiny.

Cutting cute bangs may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look. Follow this guide on how to cut cute bangs, and don't forget to maintain them with proper hair care.
