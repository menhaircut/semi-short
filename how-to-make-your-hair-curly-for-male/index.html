How To Make Your Hair Curly For Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a male looking to add some curls to your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men want to achieve curly hair, but don't know where to start. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair curly for male with some easy steps.

How to Make Your Hair Curly for Male

Before we dive into the steps, let's first understand what makes hair curly. Hair gets its shape from the shape of the hair follicle. If the follicle is round, the hair will be straight. If it's oval or asymmetrical, the hair will be curly. So how can you make your hair curly? Here are some ways:

1. Use a Curling Iron

A curling iron is the easiest way to make your hair curly. It's a hot tool that heats up your hair and changes its shape. To use a curling iron, follow these steps:

  • Preheat your curling iron to the desired temperature.
  • Section your hair and start from the bottom.
  • Wrap a small section of hair around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds.
  • Release the hair and move on to the next section.
  • Once you're done, run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up.

Tip: Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage to your hair.

2. Use Hair Rollers

Hair rollers are another great way to make your hair curly. They come in different sizes and shapes, and they work by wrapping your hair around them. Here's how to use hair rollers:

  • Wash and dry your hair.
  • Section your hair and start from the bottom.
  • Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the roller.
  • Secure the roller with a clip or pin.
  • Repeat until all your hair is in rollers.
  • Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight.
  • Remove the rollers and run your fingers through the curls.

Tip: Apply a hair mousse or gel before using rollers to enhance the curls.

3. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is a natural way to make it curly. It works by creating waves in your hair when you undo the braids. Here's how to braid your hair:

  • Wash and dry your hair.
  • Section your hair and start from the bottom.
  • Braid each section of hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  • Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight.
  • Undo the braids and run your fingers through the waves.

Tip: Apply a hair oil before braiding your hair to avoid frizz.

FAQ: How to Make Your Hair Curly for Male

1. How long does it take to make hair curly?

It depends on the method you choose. Using a curling iron takes about 30 minutes, while using hair rollers or braiding your hair takes a few hours or overnight.

2. How long do the curls last?

The duration of the curls depends on the method you choose and your hair type. Using a curling iron or hair rollers can give you curls that last for a day or two. Braiding your hair can give you curls that last for a few days.

3. Can I make my hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without heat by using hair rollers, braiding your hair, or using a hair curling cream.

Hair Care: How to Maintain Your Curly Hair

Now that you have curly hair, you need to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to avoid damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

Tip: Avoid touching your hair too often to avoid frizz.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make your hair curly for male, you can choose the method that works best for you. Remember to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!
