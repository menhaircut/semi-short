What Does A 2 Back And Sides Look Like

Written by Frank Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic and versatile haircut, the 2 back and sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut involves shaving the back and sides of your head with a number 2 clipper guard, leaving your hair about ¼ inch long. The top of your hair is left longer, typically about 2-3 inches. In this blog post, we'll go over what a 2 back and sides looks like, how to get it, tips for styling and maintaining it, and more.

Description

A 2 back and sides is a short and tidy haircut that's easy to maintain. It's also known as a buzz cut or crew cut, and it can be styled in various ways. The number 2 clipper guard leaves your hair short enough to keep it manageable, but not too short that it looks completely shaved. The longer hair on top can be styled into a classic side part, a messy fringe, or anything in between.

How to Get a 2 Back and Sides

If you're interested in getting a 2 back and sides, the first step is to find a good hairstylist or barber who has experience with this type of haircut. Once you're at the salon, here's what you can expect:

Step 1: Consultation

Your stylist will talk to you about what you want and assess your hair type and face shape to determine the best way to cut your hair. They may use a mirror to show you different options and ask for your input.

Step 2: Clippers

The stylist will use clippers with a number 2 guard to shave the back and sides of your head. They will start at the bottom and work their way up, ensuring that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 3: Scissors

After the back and sides are done, your stylist will use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length and style. They may also use thinning shears to remove bulk and create texture.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Finally, your stylist will use a comb and scissors to blend the top and sides together and make any necessary adjustments. They may also use a trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around your hairline and neck.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining a 2 Back and Sides

Here are some tips for keeping your 2 back and sides looking sharp:

  • Use a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style the top of your hair. Avoid using too much, as it can weigh your hair down.
  • If you're going for a messy look, use your fingers to style your hair instead of a comb or brush.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Clean your clippers and scissors after each use to keep them in good condition.
  • Protect your hair and scalp from the sun with a hat or sunscreen.

FAQ

Is a 2 back and sides suitable for all face shapes?

A 2 back and sides can work for most face shapes, but it may not be the best choice for those with very round faces. In that case, a longer haircut that adds height to the top of the head may be more flattering.

Can I style a 2 back and sides in different ways?

Yes, you can style a 2 back and sides in various ways depending on your personal taste and hair type. You can create a side part, spike it up, slick it back, or leave it messy.

Is a 2 back and sides easy to maintain?

Yes, a 2 back and sides is easy to maintain. You can use clippers to touch up the back and sides between haircuts, and the longer hair on top can be styled with minimal effort.

Hair Care

While a 2 back and sides is a low-maintenance haircut, it's still important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Condition your hair once or twice a week to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, a 2 back and sides is a classic and versatile haircut that can suit many different styles and preferences. Whether you prefer a clean and polished look or a more relaxed and messy style, a 2 back and sides can be tailored to your needs. With proper care and maintenance, your 2 back and sides can look great for weeks to come.
