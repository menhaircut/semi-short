Best Low Cut Hairstyles For Guys

Written by Bardi Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Low cut hairstyles for guys are a great way to show off your masculine features while keeping your hair looking neat and tidy. Whether you're looking for a classic buzz cut, a modern fade, or something in between, there are plenty of low cut hairstyles to choose from. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best low cut hairstyles for guys, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

Description

Low cut hairstyles are characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. These hairstyles are popular among men of all ages and can be worn in a variety of ways. They are easy to maintain, require minimal styling, and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

To achieve a low cut hairstyle, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who specializes in men's hair. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and will use clippers to create a uniform length on the sides and back of your head. The hair on top will be left slightly longer, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step by Step

1. Choose a low cut hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
2. Visit a barber or stylist who specializes in men's hair.
3. Ask for a uniform length on the sides and back of your head, and slightly longer hair on top.
4. Style your hair on top using a comb, brush, or your fingers.
5. Use a styling product such as gel, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold.
6. Maintain your low cut hairstyle by visiting your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Tips

1. Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you.
2. Use a quality styling product to keep your hair looking its best.
3. Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice.
4. Keep your hair clean by washing it regularly.
5. Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or using a scarf in cold weather.

FAQ

Q: What face shapes suit low cut hairstyles?

A: Low cut hairstyles can suit a variety of face shapes, including round, square, and oval. Your barber or stylist will be able to recommend a style that flatters your face shape.

Q: How often should I visit my barber or stylist?

A: It's recommended that you visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim, to keep your low cut hairstyle looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my hair on top in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your hair on top in a variety of ways, including slicking it back, combing it to the side, or leaving it messy and textured.

Hair Care

To keep your low cut hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes washing it regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner, and using a styling product to add texture and hold. Avoid using hot styling tools such as hairdryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair. Protect your hair from the sun and cold weather by wearing a hat or using a scarf, and visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims.

Overall, low cut hairstyles for guys are a great way to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, while showing off your masculine features. Whether you prefer a classic buzz cut or a modern fade, there's a low cut hairstyle to suit your style and personality. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your low cut hairstyle looking its best for years to come.
