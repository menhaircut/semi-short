How to Determine Your Hair Type and Texture

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for thinning hair on top is to determine your hair type and texture. This will help you choose a hairstyle that works best for your hair and will help conceal any thinning areas.

There are four basic hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Hair texture refers to the thickness of each individual strand of hair, which can range from fine to coarse. Knowing your hair type and texture will help you choose a hairstyle that works best for your hair type and will help conceal any thinning areas.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

To determine your hair type, start by looking at the shape of your hair follicle. Straight hair has round follicles, while wavy and curly hair has oval-shaped follicles. Coily hair has flat, elliptical-shaped follicles.

Next, consider the natural movement of your hair. If your hair hangs straight down, you likely have straight hair. If your hair has a slight bend or wave, you likely have wavy hair. If your hair has a distinct curl pattern, you likely have curly or coily hair.

How to Determine Your Hair Texture

To determine your hair texture, take a single strand of hair and hold it up to the light. If the strand is barely visible, you likely have fine hair. If the strand is easily visible, you likely have medium or coarse hair.

You can also run your fingers through your hair to feel the thickness of each strand. If your hair feels thin, you likely have fine hair. If your hair feels thick, you likely have medium or coarse hair.

Best Hairstyles for Thinning Hair on Top

Now that you've determined your hair type and texture, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for thinning hair on top:

1. Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is a great option for men with thinning hair on top. This low-maintenance haircut is easy to style and can help create the illusion of thicker hair.

2. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a great option for men with wavy or curly hair. This haircut is short on the sides and longer on top, with plenty of texture and movement to help conceal any thinning areas.

3. Side Part

A classic side part is a great option for men with fine or thinning hair. This hairstyle works best with straight or wavy hair, and can be styled with a bit of product to create volume and texture.

4. Crew Cut

A crew cut is a timeless hairstyle that works well for men with all hair types. This short, clean-cut style is easy to maintain and can help create the illusion of thicker hair.

5. Shaggy Layers

For men with longer hair, shaggy layers can be a great option for concealing thinning areas. This hairstyle features choppy layers that add texture and movement to the hair, creating the illusion of thicker, fuller hair.

Tips for Caring for Thinning Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, there are a few tips you can follow to care for your thinning hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Avoid using hot water, which can also dry out your hair and scalp.

2. Don't Over-Style Your Hair

Avoid using too much product or over-styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

What causes thinning hair on top?

Thinning hair on top can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, age, and hormonal changes.

Can hair loss be reversed?

While there's no cure for hair loss, there are treatments available that can help slow down the process and promote hair growth.

What are some natural remedies for thinning hair?

Some natural remedies for thinning hair include using essential oils, taking supplements, and massaging the scalp to increase blood flow.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can help conceal thinning hair on top and promote healthy hair growth.
