How To Thin Men s Hair With Thinning Shears A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to get rid of some of the bulk in your hair, thinning shears can be a great solution. This tool is specifically designed to remove some of the hair's weight, making it easier to manage and style. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to thin men's hair with thinning shears, as well as some tips and tricks to get the best results.

How to Use Thinning Shears

Thinning shears are scissors with one serrated edge and one straight edge. They work by cutting some of the hair while leaving some of it untouched, creating a textured, layered look. Here's how to use them:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see where you need to thin your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Hold the Thinning Shears Properly

Hold the thinning shears with the serrated edge pointing towards the hair and the straight edge pointing outwards.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Starting from the top of the section, use the thinning shears to cut the hair in a zigzag motion. Make sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time, and to keep the shears at an angle to avoid a blunt cut.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat the process on all sections of your hair, making sure to blend the cut sections with the uncut sections to create a natural look.

Tips for Thinning Men's Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears on men's hair:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't undo a cut that's already been made.
  • Use the thinning shears sparingly, as overuse can create a choppy, uneven look.
  • Blend the cut sections with the uncut sections to create a natural look.
  • Don't use thinning shears on wet hair, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Consider using a comb to guide the shears and create a more even cut.

FAQ

Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

Yes, thinning shears can be used on all hair types, including curly, wavy, and straight hair. However, the amount of hair that needs to be thinned can vary depending on the thickness and texture of the hair.

How often should I use thinning shears on my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it becomes too thick. As a general rule, you should use thinning shears every 3-4 months to maintain your desired level of thickness.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy after using thinning shears, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and to avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

Now that you know how to thin men's hair with thinning shears, you can achieve a more manageable, stylish look in no time. Remember to take it slow, use the shears sparingly, and blend the cut sections with the uncut sections for a natural look. Happy styling!
