Do Side Fringes Suit Round Faces

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting a side fringe but have a round face? One common question people with round faces often ask is whether side fringes will suit them. In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide tips on how to style and care for your side fringe.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Get a Side Fringe
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Side Fringe
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Side Fringe
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Side Fringe

Description

Side fringes, also known as side bangs, are a popular hairstyle for women that can add an edge to any look. They work particularly well for those with long hair as they help to frame the face and add dimension to the hair. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering whether a side fringe will suit you.

The good news is that side fringes can work well for round faces if styled correctly. They can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. However, it's important to get the right type of fringe for your face shape and to style it in a way that suits you.

How to Get a Side Fringe

If you're considering getting a side fringe, it's important to find a style that will suit your face shape. For round faces, a side fringe that is longer at the sides and shorter in the middle can work well. This will help to create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention away from the roundness.

When getting your fringe cut, it's important to communicate with your stylist about the type of fringe you want and the look you're going for. They can help you to choose a style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Side Fringe

Styling your side fringe can be easy with the right tools and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush out your fringe to remove any tangles.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your fringe to make it smooth and shiny.
  4. Using a round brush, blow-dry your fringe in the direction you want it to fall.
  5. Once your fringe is dry, use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or curls.
  6. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep your fringe in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Fringe

Keeping your side fringe looking its best requires some maintenance. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your fringe regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair serum to keep your fringe smooth and shiny.
  • Avoid touching your fringe too much as this can make it look greasy.
  • If your fringe starts to look oily, use a dry shampoo to freshen it up.

FAQs

Q: Can a side fringe make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, a side fringe can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

Q: Will a side fringe suit my round face?

A: Yes, a side fringe can work well for round faces if styled correctly. It's important to choose a style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

Q: How often should I trim my side fringe?

A: It's a good idea to trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Your Side Fringe

Keeping your hair healthy and nourished is important for maintaining a great-looking side fringe. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, side fringes can work well for round faces if styled correctly. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock a fabulous side fringe that will make you look and feel amazing!
