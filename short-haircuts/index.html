Short Haircuts A Guide To Rocking The Look In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're thinking about chopping off your locks and going for a short haircut, you're not alone. Short haircuts have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They're low maintenance, versatile, and can be incredibly chic. But before you make the cut, it's important to know what you're getting into. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about short haircuts, from how to choose the right style to how to care for your new 'do.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for You

Choosing the right short haircut can be daunting, especially if you're used to having long hair. But with the right guidance, you can find a style that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short haircut:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right short haircut is to consider your face shape. Certain styles look better on certain face shapes, so it's important to choose a cut that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, opt for a short haircut with layers and volume at the crown to elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, go for a short bob or pixie cut to soften your features.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, try a layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut to balance out your features.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pull off just about any short haircut.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a short haircut. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a blunt cut or a layered bob to avoid a triangular shape. If you have fine hair, a pixie cut or a layered bob can add volume and texture. If you have curly hair, a short haircut can help define your curls and make them more manageable.

Consider Your Personal Style

Finally, consider your personal style when choosing a short haircut. Do you prefer a classic, polished look? A sleek bob may be perfect for you. Do you like to experiment with edgy styles? A choppy pixie cut could be just what you need. Your short haircut should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair Short

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, you may be wondering how to do it yourself. While we always recommend visiting a professional stylist for the best results, it is possible to cut your own hair at home. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, use hair clippers or scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move to the side sections, cutting the hair slightly longer than the back section to create layers.
  5. Finally, cut the top section to the desired length and style as desired.
  6. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Remember, cutting your own hair can be risky, so proceed with caution and always have a backup plan (like a cute hat!) just in case.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you have your short haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, like a good flat iron or curling wand.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent bedhead.
  • Try different accessories, like headbands or hair clips, to change up your look.

FAQs About Short Haircuts

Still have questions about short haircuts? Here are some FAQs:

Will a short haircut look good on me?

Short haircuts can look good on just about anyone, as long as you choose a style that flatters your features and suits your personal style.

Will a short haircut be easy to maintain?

Short haircuts are generally low maintenance, but you'll still need to visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups. You'll also need to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type.

Can I still style my short haircut in different ways?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can be incredibly versatile, and you can experiment with different styling techniques to create a variety of looks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Finally, it's important to take good care of your short hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling whenever possible, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage or damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your new short haircut like a pro in no time. Good luck!
