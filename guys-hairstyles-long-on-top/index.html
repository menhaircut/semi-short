Description

Guys hairstyles long on top have been popular for several years now, and it doesn't seem like they'll be going away anytime soon. This style is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter sides and back. It can be styled in a variety of ways, from a messy, tousled look to a more sleek and polished style.

If you're thinking of trying out this hairstyle, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to choose a style that works for your face shape and hair type. Second, you'll need to learn how to style your hair properly to achieve the look you want. And finally, you'll need to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

How to

The first step in getting a guys hairstyle long on top is to find a good barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. They'll be able to recommend a style that works for your face shape and hair type, and they can also give you tips on how to style your hair at home.

Once you've found a stylist, the next step is to choose a specific style. Some popular options include the slicked-back look, the quiff, the messy fringe, and the textured crop. Each of these styles has its own unique look and requires a slightly different approach to styling.

Choosing the Right Products

To style your hair long on top, you'll need to invest in some good styling products. Depending on the style you choose, you may need a pomade, a wax, a gel, or a styling cream. Your stylist can recommend the best products for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Blow-Drying Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to make sure it's dry. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely, using a round brush to create volume and shape. This will help you achieve the desired look and make it easier to style your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a guys hairstyle long on top:

  1. Choose a style that works for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look you want.
  3. Invest in some good styling products.
  4. Dry your hair completely with a blow dryer and round brush.
  5. Apply the styling product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  6. Style your hair using your fingers or a comb, creating the desired shape and texture.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your guys hairstyle long on top:

  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Be patient and take your time when styling your hair, especially if you're new to this look.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice or tips on how to style your hair at home.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys hairstyles long on top:

What face shapes work best with this style?

Guys hairstyles long on top can work with a variety of face shapes, but they tend to look best on those with oval or square faces.

How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

What kind of styling product should I use?

The type of styling product you use will depend on the style you choose and your hair type. Pomades and waxes work well for slicked-back looks, while gels and creams are better for messy, tousled styles.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when you have a guys hairstyle long on top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy a guys hairstyle long on top that looks great and stays healthy and vibrant.
