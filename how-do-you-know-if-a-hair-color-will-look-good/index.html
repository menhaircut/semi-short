How Do You Know If A Hair Color Will Look Good

Written by William Nov 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be an exciting adventure, but it can also be daunting. How do you know if a hair color will look good on you? There are several factors to consider, including your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. In this article, we will discuss how to determine if a hair color will look good on you and provide some tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine if a Hair Color Will Look Good on You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine if a Hair Color Will Look Good on You

One of the best ways to determine if a hair color will look good on you is to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone is the underlying color of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. Determining your skin tone can help you choose a hair color that complements your natural complexion.

If you have warm undertones, you may want to consider hair colors with warm tones, such as golden blondes, copper reds, and rich browns. Cool undertones tend to look best with cool hair colors, such as ash blondes, platinum blondes, and cool browns. If you have neutral undertones, you can generally wear any hair color.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Your eye color can help determine what shades will look best on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors, such as strawberry blonde or honey blonde, can bring out the blue in your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colors, such as ash brown or cool blonde, can make your eyes pop.

Your natural hair color can also play a role in determining what hair colors will look good on you. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to stick with darker shades or gradually lighten your hair over time. If you have naturally light hair, you may be able to experiment with a wider range of colors.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for determining if a hair color will look good on you:

  1. Determine your skin tone. This can be done by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.
  2. Consider your eye color. Determine if your eyes are warm or cool. If they are warm, go for warm hair colors. If they are cool, go for cool hair colors.
  3. Take into account your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, stick with darker shades. If you have naturally light hair, you may be able to experiment with a wider range of colors.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine what hair colors will look good on you and provide recommendations based on your complexion, eye color, and natural hair color.
  5. Consider trying a temporary hair color first. This can help you get a feel for the new color and determine if it's the right shade for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you've chosen a new hair color, it's important to take steps to maintain your new look. Here are some tips:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your new color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause your color to fade.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner to help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new hair color:

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and use a high-quality hair dye.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. As a general rule, it's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have bleached hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to be cautious and avoid over-processing your hair. It's recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist before dyeing bleached hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help keep your locks looking their best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair soft and manageable.

By considering your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can determine what hair colors will look good on you. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist and take steps to maintain your new look. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the hair color of your dreams!
