Does Long Hair Suit Big Faces

Written by Luffy Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has always been a popular hairstyle option for women, but it's not always easy to decide whether it will suit your face shape. For women with big faces, this can be an even more challenging choice. In this article, we'll explore whether long hair suits big faces and provide tips on how to make the most of your long locks.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Big faces are generally characterized by strong jawlines, high cheekbones, and wide foreheads. Women with big faces often feel self-conscious about their facial features and may struggle to find hairstyles that flatter their face shape. When it comes to long hair, there's no one-size-fits-all answer to whether it will suit a big face. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to help you make the best decision.

How to

The first step in deciding whether long hair will suit your big face is to consider your hair type. If you have thick, voluminous hair, long hair can help balance out your facial features and create a more proportional look. However, if your hair is fine and limp, long hair can make your face appear even bigger and overwhelm your features.

Another factor to consider is your lifestyle. Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair, so you'll need to be prepared to invest time and effort into styling and caring for your locks. If you're a busy woman who doesn't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, long hair may not be the best choice for you.

Step by Step

If you've decided that you want to try long hair with your big face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with big faces.
  2. Ask for layers and texture to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  3. Consider adding bangs to soften your forehead and balance out your facial features.
  4. Invest in quality hair care products to keep your locks healthy and shiny.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and ponytails, to find the look that flatters your face shape the most.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when deciding whether long hair suits your big face:

  • Consider your personal style and fashion sense. Long hair may not suit everyone's aesthetic.
  • Take care of your hair by using heat protectant products and avoiding excessive heat styling.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors and highlights to add depth and dimension to your locks.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories to add interest and style to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether long hair suits big faces:

Q: Will long hair make my face look even bigger?

A: It depends on your hair type and how you style it. If you have fine, limp hair, long hair may not be the best choice as it can make your face appear bigger. However, if you have thick, voluminous hair, long hair can help balance out your facial features.

Q: Should I get bangs if I have a big face?

A: Bangs can be a great way to soften your forehead and balance out your features. However, not all types of bangs will suit every face shape, so it's best to consult with a hairstylist to find the right style for you.

Hair Care

Long hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your long locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and guidelines in mind, you can make an informed decision about whether long hair suits your big face. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
