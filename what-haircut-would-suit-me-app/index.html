How to Use a What Haircut Would Suit Me App

Using a haircut app is simple and straightforward. Here are the steps to take:

  1. Download and install the app on your mobile device.
  2. Upload a clear photo of yourself, facing forward with your hair pulled back.
  3. Follow the prompts to input your hair type, face shape, and other features.
  4. Browse the recommended hairstyles and try them on virtually to see how they would look on you.
  5. Choose your favorite hairstyle and take it to your hairdresser for a professional cut.

Step 2: Upload a Clear Photo of Yourself

Step 3: Input Your Hair Type and Face Shape

The app will prompt you to input your hair type and face shape. This information helps the app narrow down the recommended hairstyles that will flatter your features.

The app will show you various hairstyles that will suit your face shape and features. You can try them on virtually to see how they look on you. You can also save your favorite hairstyles for future reference.
