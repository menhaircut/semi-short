How to Use Finasteride

Finasteride is a prescription medication that comes in tablet form. It works by blocking the conversion of testosterone into dihydrotestosterone (DHT), which is a hormone that causes hair loss in men. Finasteride is typically taken once a day, with or without food.

It is important to take finasteride as directed by your healthcare provider. Do not take more than the recommended dose or stop taking the medication without consulting your doctor.

Who Should Use Finasteride?

Finasteride is primarily used to treat male pattern baldness, which is a common condition that affects approximately 50% of men over the age of 50. It is not recommended for use by women or children.

Benefits of Finasteride

Finasteride has been shown to be an effective treatment for male pattern baldness. In clinical trials, men who took finasteride experienced a significant increase in hair growth and a decrease in hair loss compared to those who took a placebo.

Additionally, finasteride is a safe and well-tolerated medication. The most common side effects are mild and include decreased libido, erectile dysfunction, and decreased ejaculate volume. These side effects typically resolve on their own once the medication is discontinued.

Side Effects of Finasteride

While finasteride is generally safe and well-tolerated, it can cause some side effects in rare cases. These side effects may include:

  • Depression
  • Breast tenderness or enlargement
  • Allergic reactions
  • Testicular pain
  • Decreased semen volume

If you experience any of these side effects while taking finasteride, contact your healthcare provider immediately.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

While finasteride is an effective treatment for male pattern baldness, there are some things you can do to prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause hair breakage and damage
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid excessive brushing or combing that can cause hair breakage

FAQs About Finasteride and Hair Loss

Q: Does finasteride stop hair loss?

A: Yes, finasteride has been shown to effectively treat male pattern baldness by blocking the hormone that causes hair loss.

Q: How long does it take for finasteride to work?

A: Finasteride can take up to three months to show results. It is important to continue taking the medication as directed by your healthcare provider to see the full benefits.

Q: What are the side effects of finasteride?

A: The most common side effects of finasteride are mild and include decreased libido, erectile dysfunction, and decreased ejaculate volume. These side effects typically resolve on their own once the medication is discontinued.

Q: Can women use finasteride?

A: No, finasteride is not recommended for use by women. It can cause birth defects in a developing male fetus if it is taken during pregnancy.


Overall, finasteride is an effective treatment for male pattern baldness, but it is important to use it as directed by your healthcare provider. Additionally, incorporating hair care tips into your daily routine can help prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
