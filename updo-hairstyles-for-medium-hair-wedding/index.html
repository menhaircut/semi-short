Updo Hairstyles For Medium Hair Wedding

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to wedding hairstyles, updos are a classic choice that never goes out of style. Updo hairstyles for medium hair wedding are perfect for brides who want a chic and elegant look. Whether you are looking for a braided updo, a messy bun, or a sleek chignon, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we will be discussing how to create the perfect updo hairstyles for medium hair wedding, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you achieve your dream look.

Description

Updo hairstyles for medium hair wedding are versatile and can be customized to suit your individual style. They are perfect for outdoor weddings, as they keep your hair off your face and prevent it from getting tangled in the wind. Updos can also be dressed up or down, depending on the formality of the event. They are a great choice for bridesmaids, as they can be worn with a variety of dresses and accessories.

How to Create Updo Hairstyles for Medium Hair Wedding

Creating the perfect updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done in no time. Here's how:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair the night before the wedding and blow dry it straight. This will make it easier to work with and will help your updo stay in place.

Step 2: Prep your hair with a heat protectant

Spray your hair with a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools. This will protect your hair from damage and help your updo last all day.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron, curl small sections of your hair away from your face. This will give your hair some texture and make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Using a teasing comb, tease the crown of your hair to create volume. This will make your updo look fuller and more glamorous.

Step 5: Gather your hair into a ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. This will be the base of your updo.

Step 6: Twist and pin your hair

Take small sections of hair and twist them around the base of the ponytail. Secure each section with bobby pins, making sure to hide the pins in the hair.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to keep your updo in place all day.

Tips for Updo Hairstyles for Medium Hair Wedding

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and accessories.
  • Practice your hairstyle before the wedding to make sure you are comfortable with it.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them in the updo.
  • Consider adding a hair accessory, such as a flower or a headband, to your updo for a touch of glamour.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for medium hair wedding:

What is the best updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding?

The best updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding is one that complements your dress and accessories. Some popular options include braided updos, messy buns, and sleek chignons.

How long does it take to create an updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding?

It typically takes about an hour to create an updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding. However, this can vary depending on the complexity of the hairstyle and the skill level of the stylist.

Can I create an updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding on my own?

It is possible to create an updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding on your own, but it can be difficult. We recommend working with a professional stylist to ensure that your updo looks perfect on your big day.

Hair Care for Updo Hairstyles for Medium Hair Wedding

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair before and after your updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding:

  • Wash your hair the night before the wedding to give it time to settle.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it difficult to style.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much on the wedding day, as this can cause frizz and flyaways.
  • Remove any hair accessories gently to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to create the perfect updo hairstyle for medium hair wedding, it's time to start practicing! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and elegant look that will make you feel like a princess on your big day.
