Balding Hairstyles 2021 The Best Looks For Men

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss and looking for a new hairstyle that will suit your balding head? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with the best balding hairstyles for 2021. Whether you prefer a clean shave or a buzz cut, there is a hairstyle for everyone. Keep reading to learn more.

Description

Balding hairstyles are becoming increasingly popular in 2021. Men who experience hair loss or baldness are embracing their bald heads and experimenting with new haircuts. Balding hairstyles can help you look younger, more confident, and stylish. In this article, we will provide you with a guide on how to choose the best balding hairstyle for your head shape, face shape, and personal style.

How To Choose the Right Balding Hairstyle

Choosing the right balding hairstyle can be a challenging task. However, by following these simple steps, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your balding head:

  • Determine your head shape: The first step in choosing the right balding hairstyle is to determine your head shape. Common head shapes include round, oval, square, and heart-shaped.
  • Determine your face shape: Once you have determined your head shape, you should consider your face shape. Common face shapes include round, square, oval, and triangular.
  • Consider your personal style: Your personal style should also be considered when choosing a balding hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic or modern look? Are you looking for a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hairstyle?

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the best balding hairstyles for men:

The Buzz Cut

  1. Start by using a clipper with no guard, and buzz the entire head.
  2. Next, use a guard to trim the hair shorter on the sides and back.
  3. Finally, use scissors to trim the hair on the top of the head.

The Shaved Head

  1. Start by using a clipper with no guard, and buzz the entire head.
  2. Next, use a razor to shave the head completely.
  3. Finally, apply a moisturizing lotion to prevent razor burn and irritation.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your balding hairstyle:

  • Moisturize your scalp daily to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Use a sunscreen to protect your scalp from sunburn.
  • Consider wearing a hat or headscarf when exposed to the sun or cold.
  • Visit a barber or stylist regularly to maintain your balding hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about balding hairstyles:

Q: Will shaving my head make my hair grow back thicker?

A: No, shaving your head will not make your hair grow back thicker. Hair thickness is determined by genetics and hormones.

Q: Can I still style my hair even if I am balding?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair even if you are balding. A buzz cut or shaved head can still be styled with hair products such as pomade or gel.

Hair Care

Even though you may not have a lot of hair, it is still important to take care of your scalp. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy scalp:

  • Wash your scalp with a gentle shampoo at least once a week.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your scalp.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve circulation and stimulate hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have learned about the best balding hairstyles for 2021, you can confidently embrace your bald head and rock a new hairstyle. Remember to consider your head shape, face shape, and personal style when choosing a balding hairstyle, and maintain a healthy scalp for optimal hair growth.
