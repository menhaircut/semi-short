Mens Slicked Back Fade Hairstyles A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic yet trendy hairstyle, then the mens slicked back fade is the perfect choice. This hairstyle has been around for decades but has undergone several updates, making it one of the most popular hairstyles among men today. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect slicked back fade look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Look
  • FAQ

Description

The slicked back fade hairstyle is a combination of two different haircuts: the slicked back and the fade. The slicked back style involves combing the hair back and away from the face while the fade is a gradual transition from short hair to longer hair. Combining these two styles results in a sophisticated and edgy look that suits any occasion.

The slicked back fade hairstyle is versatile and can be worn in different ways. You can go for a high or low fade, depending on your preference, and you can also choose how long or short you want your hair to be on top. This hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes and hair types, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.

How to Achieve the Look

Before you start styling your hair, you need to make sure that you have the right tools. You will need a comb, a hair clipper or trimmer, and hair products such as pomade or gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt and oil buildup on your scalp and hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and use a hair clipper or trimmer to fade the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a gradual fade.
  3. Use a comb to slick back the hair on top of your head. Apply pomade or gel to your hair to help hold the style and add shine.
  4. Comb your hair back, making sure to distribute the product evenly. Use your fingers to create a natural-looking part.
  5. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair and set the style in place.
  6. Finish the look by applying a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair and combing it back.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips to help you maintain your slicked back fade:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to keep the fade looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality pomade or gel to prevent flaking and maintain the shine of your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • If you have long hair on top, consider tying it up in a man bun or ponytail to change up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back fade hairstyle:

What hair type is best suited for a slicked back fade?

The slicked back fade is suitable for all hair types, but it looks best on thick and straight hair.

How long does it take to style a slicked back fade?

It takes about 10-15 minutes to style a slicked back fade, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can I wear a hat with a slicked back fade?

Yes, you can wear a hat with a slicked back fade, but it may mess up the style. If you need to wear a hat, make sure to re-style your hair when you take it off.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Fade Hairstyles

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products that contain alcohol and sulfates as they can dry out your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Now that you know how to achieve the perfect slicked back fade, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sophisticated and edgy look that suits any occasion.
