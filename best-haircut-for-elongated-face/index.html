How to Determine if You Have an Elongated Face

An elongated face is characterized by having a longer and narrower shape than the average face. To determine if you have an elongated face, you can measure your face's length and width using a tape measure. Measure from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin to determine the length, and measure from one side of your face to the other to determine the width. If the length of your face is significantly longer than the width, then you have an elongated face shape.

Best Haircut Styles for Elongated Faces

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for elongated face, it's important to choose a style that adds width to your face and balances out the length. Here are some of the best haircut styles for elongated faces:

1. Long Bob

The long bob, also known as the lob, is a popular haircut style that works well for elongated faces. This style adds volume and width to the sides of your face, which helps to balance out the length. You can add layers to the hair to create more texture and movement, which also helps to add volume.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for elongated faces. They help to soften the face's length and add volume to the sides of the face. You can pair side-swept bangs with a long bob or a layered haircut to create a balanced look.

3. Shoulder-Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut with layers is another great option for elongated faces. This style helps to add volume and width to the sides of the face, which balances out the length. You can also add side-swept bangs to this style for an extra flattering look.

4. Pixie Cut

For those with short hair, a pixie cut can be a flattering option for elongated faces. This style helps to create volume and width at the top of the head, which balances out the length. You can add layers to the hair to create more texture and movement.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Your Haircut

Once you have chosen the best haircut for your elongated face shape, it's important to maintain and style it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products is essential for maintaining and styling your haircut. Choose products that work well for your hair type and help to add volume and texture.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and texture to your haircut. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the root while blow drying for maximum volume.

3. Avoid Over-Styling

Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.

4. Schedule Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and health of your haircut. Schedule a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking its best.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have an elongated face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an elongated face. However, it's important to add volume and texture to your hair to balance out the length.

2. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an elongated face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have an elongated face. However, it's best to avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate the length of your face. Instead, opt for a loose, messy ponytail.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Choose a hair mask that works well for your hair type and apply it to your hair for 15-20 minutes before rinsing.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray before styling. Avoid using heat styling tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.

That concludes our comprehensive guide on the best haircut for elongated face. We hope you found it helpful and informative. Remember, the key to choosing the best haircut for your face shape is to add volume and width to balance out the length. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that works for you!
