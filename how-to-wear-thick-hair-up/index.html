How To Wear Thick Hair Up A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick hair, you know the struggle of trying to style it every day. It can be difficult to manage and can often feel heavy and overwhelming. One solution to this problem is wearing your hair up. Not only does it take the weight off your shoulders, but it also gives you a polished and put-together look. In this guide, we will show you how to wear thick hair up in a relaxed and effortless way.

How to Wear Thick Hair Up

There are many ways to wear thick hair up, but the most popular styles are the messy bun, the high ponytail, and the braided crown. These styles are easy to achieve and can be done in a matter of minutes.

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for those days when you want to look effortless and stylish. To achieve this look, follow these simple steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some pieces of hair loose for a messy look.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

This style works best with second-day hair, as it gives the bun more volume and texture. You can also add a hair accessory like a scrunchie or a headband to jazz up the look.

High Ponytail

The high ponytail is a simple yet chic hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Tease the ponytail to add volume and texture.
  4. Wrap a small piece of hair around the elastic band to hide it.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

This style works best with straight or slightly wavy hair. You can also add some hair accessories like a scarf or a bow to make the look more interesting.

Braided Crown

The braided crown is a romantic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for weddings or special occasions. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and braid two small sections of hair on each side.
  2. Wrap the braids around your head, tucking the ends under the opposite braid.
  3. Pin the braids in place with bobby pins.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

This style works best with long hair, as it requires a lot of volume to create the braids. You can also add some flowers or jewels to the braids to make the look more glamorous.

Tips for Styling Thick Hair

Styling thick hair can be a challenge, but with these tips, you can make it easier:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume and texture.
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories like bobby pins and elastic bands.
  • Try different hairstyles to see what works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair up if it's short?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up even if it's short. You can try a half-up, half-down style or a mini messy bun.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from falling out of a ponytail?

A: To prevent your hair from falling out of a ponytail, use an elastic band that has a good grip and make sure to wrap it around your hair at least twice.

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair or don't sweat much, you can wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools like a straightener or curling iron.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to wear thick hair up, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your hairstyles!
