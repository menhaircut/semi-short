How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair With Scissors A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair is an art that requires precision, patience, and skill. The top of the head is the most important part of men's hair, and it needs to be cut perfectly to create a stylish look. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut the top of men's hair with scissors in a relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from how to section the hair to the best techniques to use.

Table of Contents

  • Sectioning the hair
  • Cutting the hair
  • Blending the hair
  • Tips for cutting the top of men's hair with scissors
  • FAQs
  • Hair care after cutting

Sectioning the hair

The first step to cutting the top of men's hair with scissors is to section the hair. This will help you to control the hair better and ensure that you cut it evenly. Here's how to do it:

  • Start by combing the hair straight back.
  • Use the comb to make a parting from the center of the forehead to the back of the head.
  • Clip one half of the hair out of the way using hair clips.
  • Take a section of hair from the top of the head, starting from the hairline and running back to the crown. The section should be about an inch wide.
  • Clip the rest of the hair out of the way and comb the section straight up. This will be your guide for cutting the rest of the hair.

Cutting the hair

Now that you have sectioned the hair, it's time to start cutting. Here's how to cut the top of men's hair with scissors:

  • Start at the front of the section and hold the hair between your fingers.
  • Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start with a little bit first until you get the hang of it.
  • Move to the back of the section and cut the hair to the same length as the front.
  • Continue this process until you have cut all the hair in the section.
  • Repeat the process on the other side of the head.

Blending the hair

Once you have cut the top of the hair, it's time to blend it with the sides and back. Here's how to do it:

  • Take a comb and comb the sides and back of the hair up.
  • Using the scissors, blend the top with the sides and back by cutting a small amount of hair at a time.
  • Blend until the hair looks even.

Tips for Cutting the Top of Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you cut the top of men's hair with scissors:

  • Make sure you have sharp scissors.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go along.
  • Cut the hair a little bit at a time until you get the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as you may end up cutting more than you intended.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut the top of my hair with clippers?

A: Yes, but using scissors will give you more control and precision.

Q: Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it?

A: No, but wetting the hair can make it easier to control and cut.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting the top of men's hair with scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking stylish and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great after cutting the top of men's hair with scissors.

Now that you know how to cut the top of men's hair with scissors, you can create a stylish look that will make you stand out from the crowd. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll be sure to achieve great results.
