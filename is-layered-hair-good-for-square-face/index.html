Is Layered Hair Good For Square Face

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what hairstyle will suit you best. One of the most popular hairstyles for square faces is layered hair. But is layered hair really good for square faces? In this blog post, we will explore this question and give you some tips on how to style your layered hair if you have a square face.

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of any hairstyle for a square face is to soften the angles of the face and make it appear more oval. Layered hair is a great option for square faces because it adds volume and movement to the hair, which can create a more balanced look.

Layered hair can be styled in many different ways to suit your preferences and face shape. You can opt for long layers or shorter layers, depending on the length of your hair and the look you want to achieve. Layers can be added to any haircut, including bobs, pixie cuts, and even long hair.

How to Style Layered Hair for Square Face

When styling layered hair for a square face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of your face, such as straight blunt bangs or straight hair that falls flat against your face.

Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and movement to your hair, such as loose curls or waves. You can also try a side-swept bang or a layered fringe that falls diagonally across your forehead to soften your features.

If you have long hair, consider adding layers that start at your chin or collarbone. This will help to create a more balanced look and draw attention away from your jawline. If you have short hair, try adding layers around your crown to add height and volume to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Style Layered Hair for Square Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling layered hair for a square face:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  3. If you have long hair, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves.
  4. If you have short hair, use a styling wax or pomade to add texture and height to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Layered Hair for Square Face

Here are some tips for styling layered hair for a square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face.
  • Try a side-swept bang or a layered fringe to soften your features.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Consider adding layers around your face to create a more balanced look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: Can I still have layered hair if I have a square face?

A: Yes, layered hair is a great option for square faces. It can help to soften your features and create a more balanced look.

Q: What kind of layers should I get if I have a square face?

A: You can opt for long layers or shorter layers, depending on the length of your hair and the look you want to achieve. Layers can be added to any haircut, including bobs, pixie cuts, and even long hair.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of your face, such as straight blunt bangs or straight hair that falls flat against your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

Layered hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for taking care of your layered hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layers.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your layers and make them look frizzy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh down your layers and make them look flat.

Overall, layered hair can be a great option for square faces. It can help to soften your features and create a more balanced look. With the right styling and hair care techniques, you can rock a beautiful layered hairstyle that complements your square face.
