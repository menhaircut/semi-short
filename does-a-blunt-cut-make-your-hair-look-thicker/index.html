Description

A blunt cut is a haircut in which all of the hair is cut to the same length. This creates a sharp, clean line at the ends of the hair, which can give the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. Blunt cuts are often used to create a sleek, modern look, but they can also be used to add volume and body to fine or thin hair.
