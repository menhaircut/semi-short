Long Hairstyles For Over 50 With Double Chin Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we get older, our faces and bodies change. One common change that many women experience is the development of a double chin. But don't worry - there are plenty of long hairstyles that can flatter your face and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for finding the perfect long hairstyle for women over 50 with a double chin.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a long hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face or a double chin, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt. Instead, opt for a hairstyle with layers that can help to slim down your face and draw attention away from your chin.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look flat and lifeless. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for more layers to help control the volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect long hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls. Avoid curling your hair too tightly, as this can make your face look wider.
  4. If you have bangs, use a round brush and a blow dryer to style them. Sweep them to the side to help elongate your face.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Now that you've styled your long hair, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining long hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about long hairstyles for women over 50 with a double chin:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, but be sure to keep it loose and low to help elongate your face.

Q: Is it better to have my hair parted in the middle or on the side?

A: It depends on the shape of your face. If you have a round face or a double chin, a side part can help to slim down your face.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a double chin?

A: No, you can still wear bangs if you have a double chin. Just be sure to style them to the side to help elongate your face.

Conclusion

There are plenty of long hairstyles that can flatter women over 50 with a double chin. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture, and maintaining it with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, you can look and feel your best at any age.
