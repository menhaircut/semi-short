Short Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our skin loses elasticity and our facial features may become more round. This can make finding the right hairstyle a challenge for women over 50 with round faces. However, with the right cut and styling, short hairstyles can be a great option! In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for round faces over 50.

Description

When selecting a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. Round faces have a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle, opt for cuts that are above or just below the chin. This length will help elongate the face and create angles. Layers are also a great option, as they can add volume and movement.

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for round faces over 50:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be tailored to suit a round face. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to create volume on top and around the crown. This will help elongate the face and create a more angular shape.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for women over 50 with round faces. Opt for a chin-length or slightly longer bob, and ask your stylist to add layers to create volume at the crown. You can also add side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length.

Layered Cut

A layered cut can add volume and movement to a short hairstyle, making it a great option for round faces. Ask your stylist to add layers throughout, focusing on the crown and sides. This will help create volume and movement, while elongating the face.

Step by Step

1. Choose a short hairstyle that is above or just below the chin.

2. Ask your stylist to add layers to create volume and movement.

3. Consider adding side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length.

4. Use styling products to add volume and texture.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

2. Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and angles. Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts are all great options.

What should I avoid when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face. Also avoid hairstyles that are too flat or lack volume.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right cut and styling, short hairstyles can be a great option for women over 50 with round faces. Consider trying one of the styles mentioned above, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and textures!
