How To Part The Back Of Your Hair By Yourself

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Parting the back of your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have someone to help you. But fear not, with a few simple steps, you can part the back of your hair by yourself and achieve the perfect look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of parting the back of your hair by yourself, step by step.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Parting the Back of Your Hair

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to part the back of your hair by yourself:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to part.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair down the middle from the back of your head to the nape of your neck. Make sure the part is straight.
  3. Clip one side of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  4. Using the comb, part the other side of your hair into two sections, creating a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be at the nape of your neck.
  5. Clip the bottom section of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  6. Part the top section of your hair into two more sections, creating another triangle shape. The point of this triangle should be at the top of your head.
  7. Clip the top section of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  8. Unclip the bottom section of your hair and part it down the middle. Make sure the part is straight.
  9. Unclip the top section of your hair and part it down the middle, joining it with the bottom section.
  10. Adjust the part as needed until you achieve the desired look.

That's it! You have successfully parted the back of your hair by yourself.

Tips for Parting the Back of Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect part:

  • Use a rat-tail comb to create a straight part.
  • Clip each section of your hair as you go to keep it out of the way.
  • Take your time and be patient. Parting the back of your hair can be tricky, but with practice, you will get better.
  • If you have trouble seeing the back of your head, use a handheld mirror to check your part.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about parting the back of your hair:

1. Can I use a hair clipper to part the back of my hair?

No, it is not recommended to use a hair clipper to part the back of your hair. A comb is a better tool for creating a straight part.

2. How often should I part the back of my hair?

That depends on your hairstyle and personal preference. Some people prefer to part their hair every day, while others only do it when they are styling their hair for a special occasion.

3. What if my part is not straight?

If your part is not straight, don't worry. You can use the comb to adjust it until you achieve the desired look.

Hair Care Tips

Parting the back of your hair can put stress on your hair, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to part the back of your hair by yourself, you can achieve the perfect look every time. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at parting your hair in no time!
