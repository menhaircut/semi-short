How To Know If A Hairstyle Suits You A Guide For 2023

In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about your hairstyle. We'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to tips on how to care for your hair. So, let's get started!

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether a hairstyle suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has a different set of rules when it comes to choosing a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length is about one and a half times the width
  • Round: cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, and the forehead and jawline are also similar in width
  • Square: all measurements are fairly similar, with a strong jawline
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin
  • Diamond: widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and jawline
  • Oblong: face is longer than it is wide, with a long, straight jawline

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use this information to find hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Consider Your Hair Type

In addition to your face shape, your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hair type can influence the texture, volume, and overall look of your hair. Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

Depending on your hair type, certain hairstyles may work better than others. For example, if you have curly hair, you might want to choose a style that showcases your curls, rather than trying to straighten them out.

Look at Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Are you someone who is always on-the-go, or do you have a more relaxed schedule? Do you work in a professional environment, or are you a student?

Your lifestyle can influence the type of hairstyle that will work best for you. If you're someone who is always on-the-go, you might want to choose a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you work in a professional environment, you might want to choose a more polished look.

Think About Your Personality

Your personality can also influence the type of hairstyle that will work best for you. Are you someone who likes to stand out, or do you prefer a more understated look?

If you're someone who likes to stand out, you might want to choose a bold, edgy style. On the other hand, if you prefer a more understated look, you might want to choose a classic, timeless style.

Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure about what type of hairstyle will work best for you, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist. A hairstylist can help you determine your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and can make recommendations based on your preferences.

When choosing a hairstylist, be sure to choose someone who has experience working with your hair type and who has a portfolio of styles that you like.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Some tips for maintaining your hairstyle include:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to know if a hairstyle suits you:

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will look good on me?

A: It's important to consider your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and personality when choosing a hairstyle. Consulting with a professional hairstylist can also help you make an informed decision.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy.

Hair Care

In addition to maintaining your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Some tips for maintaining healthy hair include:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and personality, you can find a hairstyle that suits you and helps you look and feel your best.
