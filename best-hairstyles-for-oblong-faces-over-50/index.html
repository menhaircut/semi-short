Best Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Over 50

Are you over 50 and have an oblong face? Finding the right hairstyle can be challenging, especially when you have to consider factors such as hair texture, face shape, and age. However, there are plenty of options that can help you look fabulous and confident.

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The goal is to create balance and soften the angular features of the face. Hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face and volume on top are ideal for oblong faces over 50.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oblong Faces Over 50

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preference. Short haircuts can be low maintenance and easy to style, while long hair can be versatile and give you more styling options. Layers can add volume and texture, while bangs can soften your features.

Step by Step: Best Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Over 50

1. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can be a great option for oblong faces over 50. The short length can add volume and width to the sides of the face, while the top can be styled to add texture and height. This style is low maintenance and easy to style.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a flattering option for oblong faces over 50. A chin-length or shoulder-length bob can add volume to the sides of the face and create a more balanced look. Layers can add texture and movement, while side-swept bangs can soften the features.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can add volume and texture to an oblong face over 50. Layers can be added to any length of hair and can be styled to add volume and movement. Side-swept bangs can also soften the features and add balance to the face.

4. Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair can be a versatile option for oblong faces over 50. Layers can be added for volume and movement, while side-swept bangs can soften the features. This length can be styled in different ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly.

Tips for Styling Hair for Oblong Faces Over 50

  • Add volume to the sides of the face to create balance.
  • Layers can add texture and movement to any length of hair.
  • Side-swept bangs can soften the features and add balance to the face.
  • Avoid styles that add height to the top of the head, as they can elongate the face.
  • Consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preference when choosing a hairstyle.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for oblong faces over 50?

Short pixie cuts, bob haircuts, layered haircuts, and shoulder-length hair can be great options for oblong faces over 50. These styles can add volume and width to the sides of the face and create a more balanced look.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face over 50?

Avoid styles that add height to the top of the head, such as tight curls, bouncy blowouts, and high ponytails. These styles can elongate the face and make it look even longer.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Faces Over 50

  • Use products that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain a healthy and fresh look.
  • Use heat protectant products when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes and add volume to your hair.

In conclusion, there are plenty of options when it comes to finding the best hairstyle for oblong faces over 50. The key is to create balance and soften the angular features of the face. Consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preference when choosing a hairstyle, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and fresh look.
