How To Choose A Hair Colour To Suit Your Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair colour but not sure what shade will suit you best? Choosing the right hair colour can make all the difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we will give you tips on how to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone is important because it can enhance your natural features and make you look more radiant. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and the right hair colour can help bring out the best in your complexion.

How to

The first step in choosing a hair colour that suits your skin tone is to determine what your skin tone is. There are a few different ways to do this:

  • Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.
  • Try on clothing in different colours. If you look best in cool colours like blues and greens, you have a cool skin tone. If you look best in warm colours like reds and yellows, you have a warm skin tone.
  • Look at your natural hair and eye colour. If you have dark hair and brown or hazel eyes, you likely have a warm skin tone. If you have blonde or light brown hair and blue or green eyes, you likely have a cool skin tone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can start to choose a hair colour that complements it.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Choose a hair colour based on your skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, choose a hair colour with cool undertones like ash blonde or cool brown. If you have a warm skin tone, choose a hair colour with warm undertones like golden blonde or warm brown.
  3. If you want to try something bold, consider a colour that is opposite your skin tone on the colour wheel. For example, if you have a cool skin tone, try a warm red or copper colour.
  4. Consider your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, a cool hair colour can help make them pop. If you have brown eyes, a warm hair colour can bring out the richness in your eye colour.
  5. Think about your lifestyle. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a hair colour that requires minimal upkeep.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider the season. In the summer, lighter hair colours can be more flattering. In the winter, darker hair colours can complement your skin tone better.
  • Consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and fits your lifestyle.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colour. If you're unsure about a hair colour, try a temporary dye first to see how it looks on you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

What is the best hair colour for cool skin tones?

The best hair colours for cool skin tones are ash blonde, cool brown, and platinum blonde.

What is the best hair colour for warm skin tones?

The best hair colours for warm skin tones are golden blonde, warm brown, and copper.

Can I dye my hair a different colour if I have a neutral skin tone?

Yes, if you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with a variety of hair colours.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after colouring it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for coloured hair, and avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone can be a fun way to change up your look. By following these tips, you can find a hair colour that makes you look and feel your best.
