How To Choose A New Haircut Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're tired of your old hairstyle and want to try something new, choosing a new haircut can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which style will suit you best. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to choose a new haircut male that will make you look and feel your best.

  • How to Choose a New Haircut Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

How to Choose a New Haircut Male

Choosing a new haircut male is not just about picking a style that looks cool or trendy. It's about finding a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some things to consider when choosing a new haircut:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you best. There are four basic face shapes: oval, round, square, and heart. Each shape has its own set of hairstyles that will complement it. For example, a round face looks best with short haircuts, while a square face looks great with longer hairstyles.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a new haircut. If you have thick hair, you'll want a style that allows you to manage it easily. If you have thin hair, you'll want a style that adds volume and texture.

Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into account when choosing a new haircut. If you have a laid-back, casual style, you may prefer a more relaxed haircut. If you have a more formal style, you may want a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a new haircut, let's go through the step-by-step process of finding the right style:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Face is almost as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and no defined angles.
  • Square: Face is as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and square forehead.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair texture and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. If you have thick hair, you may prefer a shorter style that's easy to manage. If you have thin hair, you may want a longer style that adds volume and texture.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Check out magazines or online resources for inspiration. Look for hairstyles that complement your face shape and hair texture. Save pictures of styles you like and bring them with you to your stylist.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Once you have an idea of what you want, schedule a consultation with your stylist. They can provide you with recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Be open to their suggestions and ask for their professional opinion.

Step 5: Try it Out

Once you've chosen a new haircut, try it out for a few weeks. Give yourself time to adjust to the new style and experiment with different ways of styling it.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're active or have a job that requires you to wear a hat, you'll want a style that's easy to manage.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. Your stylist is there to help you find a style that suits you best.
  • Be realistic. Choose a style that you can manage and maintain on a daily basis.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new haircut male:

What haircut is best for a round face?

A short, textured haircut is best for a round face. This will help to slim the face and add some definition.

What haircut is best for thinning hair?

A shorter haircut with layers is best for thinning hair. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing a new haircut male can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can find a style that will make you look and feel your best. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and ask for help from your stylist. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can have a look that's both stylish and easy to maintain.
