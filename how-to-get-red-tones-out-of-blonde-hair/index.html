How to Get Red Tones Out of Blonde Hair - Tips and Tricks

Are you tired of having red tones in your blonde hair? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to get rid of those pesky red tones and achieve the perfect blonde shade you've been dreaming of.

How to

There are a few methods to get rid of red tones in blonde hair. Some of them include:

Using a purple shampoo

Purple shampoos are designed to neutralize yellow and orange tones in blonde hair. But they can also help to get rid of red tones as well. Apply the shampoo to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off. Repeat this process until you achieve the desired result.

Using a toner

A toner is a hair product that is used to neutralize unwanted tones in the hair. To get rid of red tones in blonde hair, you can use a toner that has a blue or green base. Mix the toner with a developer and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for the recommended time and rinse it off.

Using a hair color remover

If you want a more drastic change, you can use a hair color remover to strip the red tones from your hair. However, this method can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to follow the instructions carefully.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of red tones in blonde hair:

  1. Choose a method that best suits your needs.
  2. Apply the product to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  3. Leave the product on for the recommended time.
  4. Rinse off the product and apply a conditioner.
  5. Repeat the process if necessary.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo before applying any product to your hair. This will help to remove any buildup that may interfere with the process.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair for a few days after applying any product. Heat can cause further damage to your hair.
  • Be patient. It may take a few tries to achieve the desired result.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of red tones in blonde hair:

Can I use lemon juice to get rid of red tones in my hair?

No, lemon juice is not recommended as it can cause further damage to your hair. Stick to the methods mentioned above.

How often should I use a purple shampoo?

You can use a purple shampoo once a week or as needed. Overuse can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after any chemical treatment. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get rid of red tones in blonde hair, you can achieve the perfect shade you've been dreaming of. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid any unnecessary damage. Good luck!
