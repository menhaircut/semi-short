Best Low Maintenance Cat Water Fountain A 2023 Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a loving pet owner, you want to ensure that your feline friend is always hydrated and healthy. One way to achieve this is by investing in the best low maintenance cat water fountain. Not only do these fountains provide a constant source of fresh water for your cat, but they also require minimal upkeep. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the best low maintenance cat water fountain, how to set it up, and some tips for maintaining it.

How to Choose the Best Low Maintenance Cat Water Fountain

When selecting a low maintenance cat water fountain, there are a few factors to consider:

Size and Capacity

The size and capacity of the fountain should be appropriate for the number of cats you have. If you have multiple cats, you'll need a larger fountain with a higher water capacity.

Material

Choose a fountain made of high-quality, durable materials that are safe for your cat to drink from. Stainless steel and ceramic are great options as they're easy to clean and don't contain harmful chemicals.

Filters

Look for a fountain with a filter that removes impurities and keeps the water fresh. Replaceable filters are ideal, as they're easy to replace and maintain.

Noise Level

Cats are sensitive to noise, so choose a fountain that operates quietly. A noisy fountain can scare your cat away and discourage them from drinking enough water.

How to Set Up a Low Maintenance Cat Water Fountain

Once you've chosen the best low maintenance cat water fountain, it's time to set it up. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Clean the Fountain

Before setting up the fountain, clean it thoroughly with warm water and soap. Rinse it well to remove any soap residue.

Step 2: Install the Filter

If your fountain comes with a filter, install it according to the manufacturer's instructions. Replaceable filters should be changed every 2-4 weeks.

Step 3: Fill the Fountain with Water

Fill the fountain with fresh, clean water. Be sure not to fill it above the recommended water level.

Step 4: Plug in the Fountain

Plug in the fountain and turn it on. Monitor it for the first few hours to ensure it's working properly and that your cat is comfortable with it.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Maintenance Cat Water Fountain

While low maintenance cat water fountains require minimal upkeep, there are a few things you can do to keep them in good condition:

Change the Water Regularly

Change the water in the fountain every 2-3 days to prevent bacteria growth and keep the water fresh.

Clean the Fountain Regularly

Clean the fountain every week to prevent buildup of dirt, hair, and other debris. Use warm water and soap to clean the fountain, and rinse it well.

Replace the Filter

Replace the filter every 2-4 weeks, depending on the manufacturer's instructions.

FAQ

Q: Can I use tap water in my cat water fountain?

A: Yes, you can use tap water in your cat water fountain. However, make sure to change the water regularly to prevent bacteria growth.

Q: How often should I clean my cat water fountain?

A: You should clean your cat water fountain at least once a week to prevent buildup of dirt, hair, and other debris.

Q: How often should I replace the filter in my cat water fountain?

A: You should replace the filter in your cat water fountain every 2-4 weeks, depending on the manufacturer's instructions.

Hair Care

Cats are known for shedding a lot of hair, and this can be a problem if the hair gets into the water fountain. To prevent this, regularly brush your cat to remove loose hair. You can also place the fountain away from areas where your cat likes to groom itself.

In conclusion, a low maintenance cat water fountain is a great investment for any pet owner. By choosing the right fountain, setting it up properly, and maintaining it regularly, you can ensure that your cat always has access to fresh, clean water.
