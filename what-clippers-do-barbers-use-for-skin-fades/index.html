What Clippers Do Barbers Use For Skin Fades A Complete Guide

Are you tired of getting messy haircuts with uneven fades? Do you want to achieve a flawless skin fade like the ones you see in magazines? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about what clippers do barbers use for skin fades. So, let's get started!

Description

Skin fade is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair gradually from long to short, with the shortest hair length at the skin level. This hairstyle requires a skilled barber who can use the right tools, especially clippers, to achieve a perfect fade. Clippers are an essential tool for barbers when it comes to skin fades. They help to create a smooth transition between different hair lengths, and the right clippers can make a significant difference in the final result.

How To Achieve Skin Fade

To achieve a skin fade, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Haircut Style

You need to decide the style of the haircut you want. There are different types of skin fades, such as high fade, low fade, bald fade, and taper fade. Choose the style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose Your Barber

You need to choose a skilled barber who has experience with skin fades. Make sure you do some research and find the best barber in your area.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how your barber will achieve a perfect skin fade:

Step 1: Cut The Top Hair

The barber will start by cutting the top hair to the desired length using scissors or clippers.

Step 2: Create The Guideline

The barber will create a guideline around the head using clippers. This guideline will be the starting point for the fade.

Step 3: Fade The Hair

The barber will use different clipper guards to fade the hair gradually from the guideline to the skin level. The barber will use a higher guard for longer hair and a lower guard for shorter hair.

Step 4: Blend The Hair

The barber will use a blending technique to create a smooth transition between different hair lengths. The barber will use a comb and scissors to blend the hair.

Step 5: Detail The Hair

The barber will use trimmers and a razor to detail the hairline and make sure the skin fade looks neat and clean.

Tips for Perfect Skin Fade

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect skin fade:

  • Choose a skilled barber who has experience with skin fades.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber about the style you want.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before your haircut.
  • Use the right clippers with the right guard size to achieve the desired hair length.
  • Use scissors and a comb to blend the hair and create a smooth transition between different hair lengths.
  • Use a trimmer and a razor to detail the hairline and create a neat and clean look.

FAQs

What clippers do barbers use for skin fades?

Barbers use different types of clippers for skin fades, such as Andis, Wahl, Oster, and Babyliss clippers. The type of clipper depends on the barber's preference and the needs of the client.

Can I achieve a skin fade at home?

You can achieve a skin fade at home, but it requires some skills and the right tools. We recommend that you go to a skilled barber who can give you a perfect skin fade.

How often should I get a skin fade?

You should get a skin fade every 2-4 weeks to maintain the hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care After Skin Fade

After getting a skin fade, you need to take care of your hair to maintain the hairstyle and keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair product, such as pomade or gel, to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your skin fade.

Now that you know everything about what clippers do barbers use for skin fades, you can confidently go to a skilled barber and get the perfect skin fade. Remember to follow our tips and take care of your hair after the haircut to maintain the hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.
