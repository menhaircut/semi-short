How To Cut A Mullet Curly Hair Girl A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's edgy, trendy, and bold, why not try a mullet? This classic 80's look has made a comeback in recent years, and it's not just for straight-haired individuals. With the right technique, you can create a beautiful mullet on curly hair girls too. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting a mullet on curly hair girls, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut a Mullet Curly Hair Girl
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A mullet is a haircut that's shorter on the top and sides, and longer in the back. It's a bold and edgy look that's perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. While the mullet is traditionally associated with straight hair, it can also look great on curly hair girls with the right technique.

How to Cut a Mullet Curly Hair Girl

Cutting a mullet on curly hair girls requires a different approach than cutting a mullet on straight hair. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle the Hair

Before you start cutting, wash and detangle the hair thoroughly. Use a conditioner to help detangle the curls and make them easier to work with.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Start with the bottom section. Comb out the curls and cut the hair to the desired length. Remember, the back should be longer than the front.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Section

Next, move on to the middle section. Comb out the curls and cut the hair to the desired length. This section should be a bit shorter than the bottom section.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section. Comb out the curls and cut the hair to the desired length. This section should be the shortest of the three.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers together by cutting in a diagonal line from the top to the bottom. This will create a seamless transition between the layers.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet on curly hair girls:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a hair-cutting cape to protect clothing from hair clippings.

FAQ

Q: Will a mullet look good on me if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, a mullet can look great on curly hair girls with the right technique. Just make sure to follow the steps outlined in this article.

Q: Is a mullet easy to maintain?

A: Yes, a mullet is easy to maintain. You can trim the back yourself or visit a salon every few months to keep the length and shape in check.

Hair Care

After cutting your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair and avoid breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a beautiful mullet on curly hair girls. So, what are you waiting for? Give it a try and rock this bold and edgy look!
