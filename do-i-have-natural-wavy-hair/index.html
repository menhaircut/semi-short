Do I Have Natural Wavy Hair Find Out Now

Written by Aprilia Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering if you have natural wavy hair? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about wavy hair, how to determine if you have it, and how to care for it. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine If You Have Natural Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Check Your Hair Type
  • Tips for Styling Natural Wavy Hair
  • FAQs

Description

Wavy hair is a hair type that falls between straight and curly hair. It has a natural wave pattern that may be loose or tight, and it can vary in texture from fine to coarse. Wavy hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right styling products and techniques, you can enhance your waves and create a beautiful, effortless look.

How to Determine If You Have Natural Wavy Hair

The easiest way to determine if you have natural wavy hair is to look at the shape of your hair strands. If your hair strands are slightly curved or have an “S” shape, then you likely have wavy hair. Another way to tell is to look at how your hair behaves when it dries naturally. If it forms loose waves, then you probably have wavy hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Check Your Hair Type

If you’re still unsure about your hair type, follow these steps to check:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Let your hair air dry without brushing or combing it.
  4. Observe the natural shape of your hair strands. If they have a slight curve or “S” shape, you likely have wavy hair.

If your hair dries straight, then you have straight hair. If it dries in tight ringlets or coils, then you have curly hair.

Tips for Styling Natural Wavy Hair

If you have natural wavy hair, here are some tips to enhance your waves:

  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair, scrunching it as you go.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the wave pattern.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling and frizz.

FAQs

Can I have wavy hair if I’ve been relaxing or straightening my hair?

Yes, it’s possible to have natural wavy hair even if you’ve been relaxing or straightening it. However, these treatments can change the texture of your hair, so it may be more difficult to tell your natural hair type.

Can I make my hair more wavy?

Yes, you can enhance your natural waves with the right styling techniques and products. Try using a curl-enhancing cream or mousse, and avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry.

What’s the best way to care for natural wavy hair?

The best way to care for natural wavy hair is to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry, and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the wave pattern.

Hair Care for Natural Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your natural wavy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

By following these tips and techniques, you can enhance your natural wavy hair and achieve a beautiful, effortless look. So, embrace your waves and enjoy your gorgeous locks!
