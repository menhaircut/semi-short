How To Test New Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Testing out new hairstyles can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. In this guide, we'll show you how to test new hairstyles and provide tips for taking care of your hair along the way.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Test New Hairstyles
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Testing New Hairstyles

How to Test New Hairstyles

Testing out new hairstyles can be intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. Follow these steps to make the process easier:

1. Find Inspiration

Before you start testing out new hairstyles, it's important to have an idea of what you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines. Save pictures of hairstyles that catch your eye and keep them in a folder or on your phone for reference.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Not all hairstyles work for every hair type. If you have thick, curly hair, a sleek, straight hairstyle may not be feasible. Consider your hair type when looking for inspiration and choosing a new hairstyle to test.

3. Use Virtual Try-On Tools

Virtual try-on tools are a great way to test out new hairstyles without committing to a cut or color. Many hair care brands and salons offer these tools on their websites. Simply upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles to see how they look.

4. Try Temporary Products

If you want to test out a new color or style without making a permanent change, try temporary products like hair chalk or spray-on color. These products wash out easily and allow you to test out a new look without commitment.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to start testing new hairstyles, follow this step-by-step guide to make the process easier:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean, conditioned hair. This will help your hair look its best and make it easier to style.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair straight or curly, depending on the style you want to test. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

3. Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to create the look you want. Remember to use heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Evaluate Your Look

Take a few pictures of your new hairstyle from different angles. Evaluate the look and decide if it's something you want to keep or change.

5. Make Adjustments

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, make adjustments. Use a different styling tool, add more volume or texture, or try a different product.

Tips for Testing New Hairstyles

Follow these tips to make testing out new hairstyles easier:

1. Take Your Time

Testing out new hairstyles can take time. Don't rush the process and give yourself time to experiment with different looks.

2. Start Small

If you're nervous about testing out a new hairstyle, start small. Try a different part or add a few highlights to see how you feel about the change.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hairstyle to test, consider your lifestyle. If you're constantly on the go, a low-maintenance style may be more practical.

FAQs

Q: How often can I test out new hairstyles?

A: You can test out new hairstyles as often as you like. Just be sure to take care of your hair and limit heat styling to prevent damage.

Q: What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

A: If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't worry. Hair grows back and you can always try something different.

Q: How can I prevent damage to my hair while testing new hairstyles?

A: Use heat protectant when styling your hair and limit heat styling to prevent damage. Also, be sure to use quality products and condition your hair regularly.

Hair Care

While testing out new hairstyles, it's important to take care of your hair. Use quality products and condition your hair regularly to prevent damage. Also, limit heat styling and use heat protectant when styling your hair.

Testing out new hairstyles can be fun and exciting. Follow these tips and steps to make the process easier and find a look you love.
