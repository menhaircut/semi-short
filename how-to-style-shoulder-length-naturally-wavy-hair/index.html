How To Style Shoulder Length Naturally Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have shoulder length naturally wavy hair, you may sometimes find it difficult to style it in a way that makes you feel confident and put-together. Fortunately, there are many ways to enhance your natural waves and create a look that works for you. In this article, we'll explore some tips, tricks, and techniques for styling shoulder length naturally wavy hair.

Description

Shoulder length naturally wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's easy to maintain and style, and it has a natural texture that looks great with minimal effort. On the other hand, it can be difficult to achieve a polished look, and it may require some extra care and attention to keep it healthy and shiny. Whether you're looking for a casual, everyday style or something more formal, there are plenty of options for styling your shoulder length waves.

How to

Here are some steps to follow when styling your shoulder length naturally wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for wavy hair, as they will help to enhance your natural texture and reduce frizz. Rinse your hair thoroughly, and gently towel-dry it to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair to help define your waves and reduce frizz. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, and avoid applying too much, as this can weigh your hair down.

Step 3: Blow Dry or Air Dry

Once you've applied your styling product, you can either blow dry your hair or let it air dry. If you want to add more volume and definition to your waves, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. If you prefer a more natural look, let your hair air dry and avoid touching it too much while it dries.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Finally, add any finishing touches to your hair that you like. You can use a curling iron or wand to add more definition to your waves, or you can use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold. You can also add accessories like headbands or clips to create a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your shoulder length naturally wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as this will help to preserve your natural waves.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can create frizz and break up your waves.
  • If you have fine hair, use a lightweight styling product to avoid weighing your hair down.
  • If you have thick hair, use a stronger hold product to help keep your waves in place.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling shoulder length naturally wavy hair:

What is the best way to enhance my natural waves?

The best way to enhance your natural waves is to use a styling product that is designed for wavy hair, and to avoid using a brush on your hair. You can also scrunch your hair while it's drying to help create more defined waves.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with naturally wavy hair can go several days without washing, while others may prefer to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

What can I do if my waves fall flat during the day?

If your waves fall flat during the day, you can use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and hold. You can also scrunch your hair with your hands to help revive your waves.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length naturally wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of styles that showcase your shoulder length naturally wavy hair. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance look or something more elaborate, there are plenty of options to choose from. With a little care and attention, you can have healthy, shiny hair that looks great every day.
