How to Style Short Hair While Growing It Out | Tips & Tricks

Are you tired of your short hair but want to grow it out? The awkward in-between stage can be frustrating, but don't worry! With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can make the most of your growing locks without sacrificing style. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style short hair while growing it out.

How to Style Short Hair While Growing It Out

When growing out short hair, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy, while also preventing split ends. To style your hair while it's growing out, consider the following techniques:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray is a great way to add volume and texture to short hair. Simply spray it onto dry hair and scrunch with your fingers for a tousled look.

2. Try a Side Part

A side part can help create the illusion of length in short hair. Use a comb to create a defined part, or simply use your fingers to part your hair and let it fall naturally.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Headbands, hair clips, and bobby pins can all be used to style short hair while growing it out. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step by Step: Styling Short Hair While Growing It Out

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair while growing it out:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and strong.

2. Apply Texturizing Spray

Spray a texturizing spray onto your dry hair and scrunch with your fingers to add volume and texture.

3. Create a Side Part

Use a comb to create a defined side part, or simply use your fingers to part your hair and let it fall naturally.

4. Add Hair Accessories

Play around with headbands, hair clips, and bobby pins to create different looks and styles.

Tips for Styling Short Hair While Growing It Out

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short hair while it's growing out:

1. Experiment with Different Hair Products

Try out different hair products, such as mousse or hair wax, to see what works best for your hair type.

2. Be Patient

Growing out short hair takes time, so be patient and don't get discouraged if it doesn't look the way you want it to right away.

3. Consider Hair Extensions

If you're really struggling with the awkward in-between stage, consider getting hair extensions to add length and volume to your hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed while growing it out?

A: It's best to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools on my short hair while it's growing out?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Short Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind while growing out short hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, so try to wash it every other day instead.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on hair than a brush, which can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your short hair while growing it out and make the most of your in-between stage. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
