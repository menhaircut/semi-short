Long Hair Straight Across Haircut Everything You Need To Know

Written by William Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair looking lifeless and dull? A long hair straight across haircut might be the solution you've been looking for. This classic haircut has been popular for decades and continues to be a go-to for those who want a chic and low-maintenance style. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the long hair straight across haircut, from how to get it to how to care for it.

What is a Long Hair Straight Across Haircut?

A long hair straight across haircut is exactly what it sounds like: a haircut where the hair is cut straight across at the ends. This creates a blunt, even look that is both simple and stylish. This haircut can be done on any length of hair, but it is most commonly seen on long hair.

How to Get a Long Hair Straight Across Haircut

If you're interested in getting a long hair straight across haircut, the first step is to find a reputable hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. Once you've found a hairstylist you trust, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Make sure to wash your hair before your appointment. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any oils or products that could make it difficult for your hairstylist to work with.

Step 2: Discuss Your Desired Length

Before your hairstylist starts cutting, make sure to discuss the length you want. It's important to be clear about your desired length so that your hairstylist can give you the exact look you're going for.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair Straight Across

Once you've discussed your desired length, your hairstylist will start cutting your hair straight across. They will use scissors to create a blunt, even line at the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your haircut is complete, your hairstylist will likely blow-dry and style your hair to give you a finished look. You can also style your hair at home using a straightener or curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining a Long Hair Straight Across Haircut

Once you've gotten your long hair straight across haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a good shampoo and conditioner is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type and avoid using products with harsh chemicals.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Damage

Heat damage can cause your hair to become dry and brittle. To avoid this, try to limit your use of heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons. If you do use these tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your long hair straight across haircut looking fresh, it's important to get regular trims. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions About Long Hair Straight Across Haircuts

Q: Will a long hair straight across haircut work for my face shape?

A: Yes! This haircut is versatile and can work for any face shape. Your hairstylist can adjust the length and shape to flatter your specific features.

Q: Is a long hair straight across haircut easy to maintain?

A: Yes! This haircut is low-maintenance and requires minimal styling. It's perfect for those who want a chic, effortless look.

Q: Can I still style my hair with a long hair straight across haircut?

A: Absolutely! You can still use styling tools like straighteners and curling irons to create different looks with your long hair straight across haircut.

Conclusion

A long hair straight across haircut is a classic and versatile style that can work for anyone. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance look or a chic and sophisticated style, this haircut is a great option. Use these tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant, and enjoy your new, stylish haircut!
