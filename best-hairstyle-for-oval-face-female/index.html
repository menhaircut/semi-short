The Best Hairstyles For Oval Face Females A Guide For 2023

Are you an oval face female wondering what hairstyles would suit you best? Look no further! In this guide, we'll go over the best hairstyles for oval face females, as well as how to achieve them, tips for maintaining them, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

Oval face females have a face shape that is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded jaw and forehead. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles with ease. However, there are some hairstyles that are particularly flattering for oval face females, and we'll be going over those in this guide.

How To

The first step to finding the best hairstyle for your oval face is to identify your face shape. If you're unsure, take a photo of yourself facing straight ahead and trace the outline of your face. Once you've determined that you have an oval face, you can start exploring different hairstyles.

Consider Your Hair Type

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type. For example, if you have thin, fine hair, a short, choppy bob might be the best option to add volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a long, layered cut might be more suitable to help manage your curls.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in your hairstyle choice. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut like a pixie or a lob might be the best option. If you have more time to devote to your hair, a longer, more intricate style might be more suitable.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for oval face females:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Starting with the back sections, take a 1-inch section of hair and hold it straight up from your head.
  4. Using scissors, cut into the hair at a slight angle, creating a soft, feathered effect.
  5. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair to the desired length, leaving the hair longer on top and shorter on the sides and back.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray or wax to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your oval face female hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage.

FAQ

What hairstyles should oval face females avoid?

Oval face females should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear longer. They should also avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can elongate the face.

Can oval face females pull off bangs?

Yes, oval face females can pull off bangs. However, they should avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make the face appear wider. Instead, they should opt for side-swept or layered bangs.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, beautiful hairstyle. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to prevent damage.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when styling or brushing.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face and keep it looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
