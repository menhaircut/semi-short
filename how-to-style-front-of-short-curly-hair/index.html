How To Style Front Of Short Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short curly hair can be tricky to style, especially when it comes to the front of your hair. Whether you have natural curls or you've styled your hair to have curls, getting the front of your hair to look just right can be a challenge. Here are some tips and tricks to help you style the front of your short curly hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style the Front of Short Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

How to Style the Front of Short Curly Hair

The front of your hair is the first thing people will notice about your hairstyle, so it's important to get it right. Here are some steps to help you style the front of your short curly hair:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair to help define your curls.
  • Apply a curl-defining styling product to the front of your hair. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry the front of your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting to avoid frizz.
  • Finger comb your curls to shape them into the style you want. Don't use a brush or comb, as this can break up your curls and create frizz.
  • Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define the curls at the front of your hair. This will help keep them in place and add shine.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style the front of your short curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a curl-defining styling product to the front of your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry the front of your hair.
  5. Finger comb your curls into the style you want.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your curls.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Don't use a brush on your curls, as this can cause them to break up and create frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your curls looking smooth.
  • Use a curl-refreshing spray or mist to revive your curls throughout the day.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls and cause them to lose their shape.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling the front of short curly hair:

  • How often should I wash my curly hair? It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Curly hair tends to be drier, so you may be able to wash it less frequently than straight hair.
  • How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair? Use a curl-defining styling product, and avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry.
  • What should I do if my curls lose their shape? Use a curl-refreshing spray or mist, or wet your hair and restyle it.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style the front of your short curly hair with ease. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
