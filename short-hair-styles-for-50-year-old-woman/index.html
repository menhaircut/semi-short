Short Hair Styles For 50 Year Old Woman

Written by Petter Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a 50 year old woman looking for a new hairstyle? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we will discuss the best short hair styles for women over 50. Whether you're looking for a low maintenance cut or something more edgy, we've got you covered. Read on to learn more!

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its natural shine. This is why many women over 50 choose to go for shorter hair styles. Not only are they easier to maintain, but they can also make you look younger and more vibrant. Short hair styles can range from a classic bob to a trendy pixie cut. It all depends on your personal style and preferences.

How to

Before you head to the salon, it's important to do your research and find a style that will work for you. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and bring pictures with you to your appointment. This will give your stylist a better idea of what you're looking for.

When it comes to short hair styles, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure the cut complements your face shape. For example, a round face may benefit from a layered bob, while a square face may look better with a short, angled cut.

Second, consider your hair type. If you have thin hair, a layered bob can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a shorter cut can help tame your locks and make them more manageable.

Step by Step

Step 1: Research short hair styles online or in magazines

Step 2: Bring pictures to your salon appointment

Step 3: Discuss your face shape and hair type with your stylist

Step 4: Get the cut!

Tips

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair

- Invest in a good quality hair dryer and straightener to style your hair at home

- Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Q: Will a short hair style make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! A well-executed short hair style can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Will a short hair style be harder to maintain?

A: It depends on the cut you choose. Some short hair styles are very low maintenance, while others may require more upkeep.

Hair Care

Short hair styles require different hair care techniques than longer styles. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

- Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair healthy

- Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a fabulous short hair style in no time! Remember, it's all about finding a cut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
