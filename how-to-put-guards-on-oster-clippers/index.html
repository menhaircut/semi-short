How To Put Guards On Oster Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to attach guards to your Oster clippers? If so, you're not alone. Many people find the process confusing, but with a little guidance, it's actually quite simple. In this article, we'll show you how to put guards on Oster clippers in relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the steps you'll need to follow. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers
  • Step 2: Choose the Right Guard
  • Step 3: Attach the Guard
  • Tips for Using Oster Clippers

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Oster clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Cleaning brush
  • Lubricating oil

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers

Before you attach a guard, you should clean and oil your clippers. This will help them run smoothly and prevent hair from getting trapped in the blades. Here's how:

  1. Turn off your clippers and unplug them from the wall.
  2. Remove any hair or debris from the blades using the cleaning brush.
  3. Apply a few drops of lubricating oil to the blades.
  4. Turn your clippers on for a few seconds to distribute the oil.
  5. Wipe off any excess oil with a clean cloth.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, so you'll need to choose the right one for the length of hair you want to cut. The guards are usually labeled with numbers that correspond to the length of the hair in millimeters. Here's a quick guide:

  • #1: 3mm
  • #2: 6mm
  • #3: 10mm
  • #4: 13mm
  • #5: 16mm
  • #6: 19mm

Choose the guard that corresponds to the length of hair you want to cut.

Step 3: Attach the Guard

Now it's time to attach the guard to your clippers. Here's how:

  1. Turn off your clippers and unplug them from the wall.
  2. Choose the guard you want to use and slide it onto the blade. Make sure it clicks into place.
  3. Turn your clippers on and test the guard to make sure it's securely attached.

That's it! You're ready to start cutting hair.

Tips for Using Oster Clippers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using Oster clippers:

  • Always clean and oil your clippers before and after use.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want to go shorter.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers through the hair.
  • Don't press too hard or you may cause nicks or cuts.
  • Take your time and go slowly to ensure a smooth and even cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting guards on Oster clippers:

What if the guard won't click into place?

Make sure the blade and guard are clean and free of debris. Try sliding the guard on from a different angle or with a bit more force.

Can I use Oster guards on other brands of clippers?

It depends on the brand and the size of the guard. Oster guards are designed to fit Oster clippers, but some may work with other brands as well. Check the size and compatibility before using.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and blow dryers too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide to putting guards on Oster clippers, you'll be able to cut hair like a pro in no time. Happy clipping!
