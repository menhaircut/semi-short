How To Wear A Face Mask Correctly In Australia A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

With the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, wearing a face mask has become mandatory in many parts of Australia. However, simply wearing a mask is not enough. It is essential to wear it correctly to ensure maximum protection. In this article, we will guide you on how to wear a face mask correctly in Australia.

  • How to Wear a Face Mask Correctly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Face Mask
  • FAQs

How to Wear a Face Mask Correctly

Wearing a face mask correctly involves the following:

  • Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use a hand sanitizer before touching the mask.
  • Ensure that the mask covers your nose and mouth. It should be snug but comfortable.
  • Adjust the mask to fit your face properly. There should be no gaps between the mask and your face.
  • Avoid touching the mask while wearing it. If you do, wash your hands or use hand sanitizer immediately.
  • When removing the mask, avoid touching the front of the mask. Remove it from behind, and discard it immediately in a closed bin.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use a hand sanitizer after removing the mask.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a face mask correctly:

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use a hand sanitizer.
  2. Pick up the mask by the ear loops or ties.
  3. Place the mask over your nose and mouth, ensuring it covers both.
  4. Loop the ear loops or ties around your ears or head, depending on the type of mask.
  5. Adjust the mask to fit your face properly. There should be no gaps between the mask and your face.
  6. Pinch the metal strip or bend the mask over your nose to ensure it fits snugly.
  7. When removing the mask, avoid touching the front of the mask. Remove it from behind, and discard it immediately in a closed bin.
  8. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water or use a hand sanitizer.

Tips for Wearing a Face Mask

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a face mask:

  • Avoid touching the mask while wearing it.
  • Do not wear the mask below your nose or on your chin.
  • Replace your mask if it becomes wet or dirty.
  • Wash reusable masks after each use.
  • Do not share your mask with others.

FAQs

1. Who should wear a face mask in Australia?

Currently, wearing a face mask is mandatory in some parts of Australia, such as Victoria and New South Wales. However, it is recommended that everyone wear a mask, especially if they are in a crowded or indoor setting.

2. What kind of mask should I wear?

You should wear a mask that covers both your nose and mouth, such as a surgical mask or a cloth mask. It is essential to ensure that the mask fits snugly and is comfortable to wear.

3. How often should I replace my mask?

If you are using a disposable mask, you should replace it after each use. If you are using a reusable mask, you should wash it after each use and replace it if it becomes damaged or dirty.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a mask for an extended period can cause irritation and breakouts on the skin. It can also cause hair breakage and tangling. Here are some tips to take care of your hair while wearing a mask:

  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as a ponytail or a bun, as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or a hair serum to protect your hair from friction.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup and breakouts on the skin.

By following these tips, you can wear a face mask correctly and protect yourself and others from the spread of COVID-19. Stay safe!
