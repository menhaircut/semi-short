What To Do With A Horrible Haircut Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever walked out of the salon or barbershop with a haircut that you absolutely despise? It can be a huge blow to your confidence, but don't worry, there are things you can do to fix it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on what to do with a horrible haircut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Accessorize
  • Wait It Out
  • Get a Fix
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

How to Style Your Hair

If you're not happy with your haircut, one of the easiest things you can do is try styling it differently. This can be as simple as changing the part in your hair or using different hair products. Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

If you have short hair, try using a texturizing product to add some volume and movement to your hair. If you have long hair, try braiding or twisting your hair into a chic updo. There are plenty of tutorials online that can help you with styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant product.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Style your hair using a texturizing product, gel, or hairspray.

Remember, practice makes perfect. It may take a few tries to get the hang of styling your hair differently, but keep at it until you find a style that you love.

Accessorize

If you're not happy with your haircut, try accessorizing with hats, headbands, or scarves. This can be a fun way to add some personality to your look while you wait for your hair to grow back.

Another option is to try out some hair accessories like clips, barrettes, or hair ties. These can be used to create different looks and styles with your hair.

Wait It Out

If you're not happy with your haircut, sometimes the best thing to do is wait it out. Hair grows back, and in a few weeks or months, your hair will be back to its normal length.

In the meantime, try to be patient and take care of your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair, as this can cause further damage.

Get a Fix

If you're really unhappy with your haircut, consider going back to the salon or barbershop to get it fixed. Explain what you don't like about your haircut and ask the stylist to make some changes.

Most salons and barbershops offer free touch-up services within a certain timeframe after your initial haircut, so take advantage of this if you can.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're waiting for your hair to grow back or trying to style it differently, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Don't wash your hair too often
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth

FAQs

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back?

A: Hair typically grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months for your hair to grow back to its normal length.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my haircut?

A: Try styling your hair differently, accessorizing, or waiting for it to grow back. If you're really unhappy, consider going back to the salon or barbershop to get it fixed.

Q: What can I do to promote hair growth?

A: Eating a healthy diet, drinking plenty of water, and taking care of your hair can all promote hair growth. There are also hair growth supplements and treatments available.

Remember, a bad haircut is not the end of the world. There are plenty of things you can do to fix it or make the most of it. Try out some of these tips and tricks until you find a solution that works for you.
