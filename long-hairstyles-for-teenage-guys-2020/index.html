Long Hairstyles For Teenage Guys 2020 A Guide To The Best Styles

Written by William Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a teenage guy with long hair, you're probably looking for some new hairstyles to try out in 2020. From messy man buns to sleek ponytails, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for teenage guys in 2020, and give you some tips on how to style them.

Description

Long hair isn't just for girls anymore. More and more teenage guys are growing out their locks and experimenting with different hairstyles. Whether you have curly or straight hair, thick or thin, there's a long hairstyle out there that will suit you perfectly.

How to

The first step in styling long hair is to make sure it's healthy and well-maintained. This means washing it regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner, and using a leave-in conditioner to keep it moisturized. You should also trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends.

Once your hair is healthy, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. One popular option is the messy man bun. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head. You can leave some strands loose for a more relaxed look, or slick your hair back for a sleeker style.

Another great option is the ponytail. This classic style works well for guys with straight or wavy hair, and can be worn high or low depending on your preference. To create a ponytail, simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles

3. Decide on your desired style (messy bun, sleek ponytail, etc.)

4. Gather your hair into the appropriate style

5. Secure with an elastic band or hair tie

6. Use a styling product (such as gel or pomade) to add texture or hold

7. Finish with hairspray to set the style in place

Tips

- Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

- Don't be afraid to use styling products to add texture or hold

- Keep your hair healthy and well-maintained with regular trims and conditioning treatments

- Use a comb or brush to help distribute product evenly throughout your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a long hairstyle if my hair is thin or curly?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of long hairstyles that work well for guys with thin or curly hair. Talk to your stylist about what styles would work best for your hair type.

Q: Do I need to use a lot of product to style my long hair?

A: It depends on the style you're going for. Some styles, like the messy bun, require very little product, while others, like a sleek ponytail, may require more. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for long hair, it's important to use the right products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for long hair, as these will help keep your locks healthy and shiny. You should also use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair, and a detangler to make brushing easier.

Regular trims are also important to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, or whenever you notice your ends starting to look frayed.

Finally, be gentle when brushing or styling your long hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle, and avoid using hot tools (like curling irons or straighteners) too often, as these can damage your hair over time.
