How To Find Hairstyle For My Face

Written by Bardi Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that does not suit your face shape? Do you want to find a hairstyle that flatters your features? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will share some tips on how to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape.

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror. Pay attention to the length and width of your face, as well as the shape of your jawline and forehead.

Step 2: Research hairstyles for your face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to do some research on hairstyles that flatter your features. You can use search engines like Google or Pinterest to find inspiration. Look for celebrities or models with a similar face shape and see what hairstyles they are wearing.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist.

If you are still unsure about what hairstyle suits your face shape, it's best to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can give you personalized advice based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Be sure to bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.

Tips

Tip 1: Consider your hair texture.

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a short haircut may not be the best option. Similarly, if you have fine, straight hair, a long hairstyle may look limp and lifeless. Talk to your hairstylist about what hairstyles work best for your hair texture.

Tip 2: Don't be afraid to experiment.

If you're feeling adventurous, don't be afraid to try out a new hairstyle. You never know, you may end up loving it. Just be sure to take your face shape and hair texture into consideration before making any drastic changes.

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can pull off a pixie cut if you have a round face. Just make sure to add some volume on top to elongate your face.

Q: What hairstyles suit heart-shaped faces?

A: Heart-shaped faces look great with hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline. Some examples include side-swept bangs, layered haircuts, and chin-length bobs.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

- Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
