What Colour Hair Suits Me App A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who's always wanted to experiment with different hair colours but isn't quite sure which one would suit you best, then you're in luck! With the rise of technology, there are now several apps available that can help you determine which hair colour would look best on you. In this article, we will talk about the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App" and how it can help you achieve your hair goals.

Description

The "What Colour Hair Suits Me App" is an application that uses augmented reality technology to help users try out different hair colours virtually. The app works by using your phone's camera to scan your face and then overlays different hair colours on top of your current hair. This way, you can see how you would look with blonde, brunette, red, or any other hair colour you've been eyeing.

How to Use the App

Using the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App" is easy and straightforward. Here's how:

  1. Download the app from your app store.
  2. Allow the app to access your phone's camera.
  3. Take a selfie or upload a photo of yourself.
  4. Select the hair colour you want to try out.
  5. The app will then show you the hair colour you selected on your photo.
  6. You can adjust the colour and intensity of the hair colour to suit your preferences.
  7. Save the photo or share it on social media to get feedback from your friends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App" to find the perfect hair colour:

Step 1: Download the App

The first step is to download the app from your app store. The app is free and available for both iOS and Android devices.

Step 2: Allow Camera Access

Once you've downloaded the app, you'll need to allow the app to access your phone's camera. This is necessary for the app to scan your face and overlay the hair colour on top of your current hair.

Step 3: Take a Selfie or Upload a Photo

Next, you'll need to take a selfie or upload a photo of yourself. Make sure that your face is well-lit and that your hair is pulled back or away from your face to get the best results.

Step 4: Select the Hair Colour

Select the hair colour you want to try out from the app's library of colours. You can choose from blonde, brunette, red, black, and even pastel colours like pink and purple.

Step 5: Adjust the Colour and Intensity

You can adjust the colour and intensity of the hair colour using the app's tools. This way, you can see how you would look with a subtle or bold change.

Step 6: Save or Share Your Photo

Once you're happy with the results, you can save the photo or share it on social media to get feedback from your friends.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App":

  • Make sure that your face is well-lit and that your hair is pulled back or away from your face to get the best results.
  • Try out different colours and intensities to see which one suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with bold colours like pink, purple, or blue.
  • Consider your skin tone and eye colour when choosing a hair colour.
  • Take your time and have fun with it!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App":

Q: Is the app free?

A: Yes, the app is free to download and use.

Q: Does the app work on all devices?

A: The app works on both iOS and Android devices.

Q: Can I save or share my photos?

A: Yes, you can save your photos or share them on social media.

Q: Can the app help me choose the right hair colour for my skin tone?

A: Yes, the app takes into account your skin tone and eye colour when suggesting hair colours.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair colour for you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent colour fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Overall, the "What Colour Hair Suits Me App" is a fun and easy way to experiment with different hair colours and find the perfect one for you. So go ahead, download the app, and see how you would look with a new hair colour!
