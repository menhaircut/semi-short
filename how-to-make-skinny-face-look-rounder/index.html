How To Make Your Skinny Face Look Rounder

Written by Luffy Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

In today’s world, many people are obsessed with having a round face. However, not everyone is blessed with a natural round face. If you have a skinny face, don't worry! There are a few tricks that can help you make your face look rounder without any surgery or makeup. In this article, we will discuss how to make your skinny face look rounder.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Skinny Face Look Rounder
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make Your Skinny Face Look Rounder

There are several ways to make your skinny face look rounder. Here are some of the most effective ones:

1. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help you tone your facial muscles and make your face look rounder. Some of the most effective facial exercises include:

  • The fish face
  • The jaw release
  • The cheek puff
  • The mouthwash move

These exercises can help you tone your facial muscles and make your face look rounder.

2. Change Your Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can have a big impact on the appearance of your face. To make your face look rounder, try changing your hairstyle. A shorter hairstyle with layers can help add volume to your hair and make your face look rounder.

3. Wear Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are wider than your face. This will help create the illusion of a rounder face.

4. Highlight Your Cheekbones

Highlighting your cheekbones can help create the illusion of a rounder face. Use a highlighter or bronzer to accentuate your cheekbones and make them stand out.

5. Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume. This will help make your face look rounder.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to make your skinny face look rounder:

  1. Do facial exercises daily to tone your facial muscles.
  2. Change your hairstyle to a shorter style with layers.
  3. Choose glasses frames that are wider than your face.
  4. Use a highlighter or bronzer to accentuate your cheekbones.
  5. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your skinny face look rounder:

  • Avoid wearing your hair in a tight ponytail or bun, as this can make your face look even skinnier.
  • Choose clothing that has a round neckline, as this can help create the illusion of a rounder face.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid smoking, as this can damage your skin and make your face look older and skinnier.

FAQ

Q: Can makeup help make my skinny face look rounder?

A: Yes, you can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a rounder face. However, if you want a more natural look, try using the tips mentioned above.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It can take several weeks or even months to see results from facial exercises. However, if you do them consistently, you should start to see a difference in the appearance of your face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining a healthy and attractive appearance. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can make your skinny face look rounder and achieve the look you desire. Remember, it's important to love yourself and embrace your natural beauty, no matter what shape your face is!
