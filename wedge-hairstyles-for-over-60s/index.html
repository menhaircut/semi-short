Wedge Hairstyles For Over 60S A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle? Wedge hairstyles are a popular option for mature women, as they are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. In this guide, we will explore everything you need to know about wedge hairstyles for over 60s, including how to style them, tips for choosing the right cut, and how to care for your hair.

  • What is a Wedge Hairstyle?
  • How to Style a Wedge Hairstyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Wedge Hairstyle

What is a Wedge Hairstyle?

A wedge hairstyle is a short haircut that is tapered at the back and layered at the top. It is called a "wedge" because the hair forms a triangular shape, with the back being shorter than the front. The style was first popularized in the 1970s by celebrities like Dorothy Hamill and Farrah Fawcett and has since become a classic look for women of all ages.

How to Style a Wedge Hairstyle

Styling a wedge hairstyle is easy and requires minimal effort. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten the ends and create a sleek look.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to add shine and hold.

For a more casual look, you can also try air-drying your hair and scrunching it with your hands to create soft waves and texture.

Tips for Choosing the Right Wedge Hairstyle

When choosing a wedge hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure you get the right cut for your face shape and hair texture:

  • Consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a longer, more angled wedge will help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shorter, more textured wedge will soften your features.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a shorter, layered wedge will add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer, more blunt wedge will help control the weight and create a sleek look.
  • Consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right wedge hairstyle based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a wedge and a bob?

A wedge hairstyle is similar to a bob, but it is tapered at the back and layered at the top, while a bob is a blunt cut that is the same length all around. The wedge can also be shorter at the back than a bob.

Can I color my hair if I have a wedge hairstyle?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a wedge hairstyle. However, it is important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

How often do I need to get my hair cut with a wedge hairstyle?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume of your wedge hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Wedge Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep your wedge hairstyle looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance wedge hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful at any age.
