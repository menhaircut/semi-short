How To Style Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy, frizzy, thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. But don’t worry, there are ways to tame your unruly locks and create beautiful hairstyles that you’ll love. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style wavy frizzy thick hair, including step-by-step instructions, hair care tips, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

How to Style Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

Styling wavy, frizzy, thick hair can be a challenge, but it’s not impossible. Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  • Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves and reduce frizz.
  • Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose waves.
  • Straighten your hair with a flat iron for a sleek and smooth look.
  • Create braids or twists to add texture to your hair.
  • Pull your hair up into a messy bun or ponytail for a casual look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s how you can style wavy, frizzy, thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for wavy, frizzy, thick hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to protect your hair from heat damage and reduce frizz.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on low heat to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose waves. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Straighten your hair with a flat iron for a sleek and smooth look. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  6. Create braids or twists to add texture to your hair. Braid your hair when it’s wet and leave it in overnight for beachy waves.
  7. Pull your hair up into a messy bun or ponytail for a casual look. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling wavy frizzy thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Try using natural oils, such as coconut oil or argan oil, to nourish and moisturize your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wavy frizzy thick hair:

Q: What’s the best way to reduce frizz in wavy frizzy thick hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to protect your hair from humidity and reduce frizz. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as it can cause friction and frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my wavy frizzy thick hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair with a flat iron. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy frizzy thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Frizzy Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for wavy frizzy thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for wavy, frizzy, thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as it can cause friction and frizz.
  • Try using natural oils, such as coconut oil or argan oil, to nourish and moisturize your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your wavy frizzy thick hair with confidence and ease. Remember to take care of your hair with the right products and techniques to keep it healthy and beautiful.
