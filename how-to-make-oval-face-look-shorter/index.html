How To Make Oval Face Look Shorter Tips And Tricks

If you have an oval face shape and want to make it look shorter, don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with oval faces struggle with finding the right hairstyle or makeup that can help balance out their facial features. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to make your oval face appear shorter and rounder. Let's get started!

How to Make Oval Face Look Shorter

There are different ways to make an oval face look shorter. The key is to create the illusion of a rounder face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Experiment with Bangs

Bangs can help shorten the length of your face. Opt for thick or side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows. This will help draw attention to your eyes and balance out your facial features.

2. Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to hairstyles, avoid long, straight hair as it can elongate your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered haircuts, bobs, or curls. These styles will make your face appear wider and rounder.

3. Contour Your Face

Contouring can help create the illusion of a shorter face. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to create shadows along your jawline and temples. This will help create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

4. Play with Colors

Experiment with makeup colors that can help balance out your facial features. For example, use a darker shade of blush on the apples of your cheeks to create the illusion of a shorter face. You can also try using darker eyeshadows to create depth and dimension on your eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your oval face appear shorter:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in making your oval face look shorter is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face. If the length of your face is longer than the width, then you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Experiment with Bangs

Try different types of bangs that hit just below your eyebrows. Thick or side-swept bangs can help balance out your facial features.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Tips for Making Your Oval Face Look Shorter

Here are some additional tips that can help you make your oval face look shorter:

1. Avoid Long, Straight Hair

Straight hair can elongate your face, so avoid it if you want to make your face look shorter. Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

2. Use Accessories

Accessories can help balance out your facial features. Try wearing earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your neck and shoulders.

3. Experiment with Makeup

Makeup can help create the illusion of a shorter face. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to create shadows along your jawline and temples.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have an oval face. However, make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out your facial features.

Q: Can contouring make my face look shorter?

A: Yes, contouring can help create the illusion of a shorter face. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to create shadows along your jawline and temples.

Q: Can I wear bold makeup if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear bold makeup if you have an oval face. Experiment with makeup colors that can help balance out your facial features.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips that can help you make the most of your oval face shape:

1. Use Hair Products Wisely

Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid products that weigh your hair down, as this can make your face look longer.

2. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Opt for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat and environmental damage by using heat protectant sprays and wearing a hat when you're outside.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your oval face appear shorter and rounder. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and makeup looks to find what works best for you. Have fun!
