Best High Waisted Jeans For Plus Size Women In 2023

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect pair of high waisted jeans that will flatter your curves and make you feel confident and comfortable? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best high waisted jeans for plus size women in 2023. From classic styles to trendy designs, these jeans are sure to become your go-to favorites for any occasion. In this article, we will share how to style them, step by step guide, tips, FAQs and hair care tips to complete your look.

Description

High waisted jeans have become a staple in every woman's wardrobe, regardless of their size or body shape. They are versatile, chic, and flattering, and have the power to instantly elevate any outfit. However, finding the perfect pair of high waisted jeans can be a daunting task, especially for plus size women.

That's why we have done the research for you and selected the best high waisted jeans for plus size women in 2023. Our list includes jeans from various brands, styles, and price ranges, so you can find the perfect pair that suits your taste and budget.

How to Choose the Best High Waisted Jeans for Plus Size Women

When shopping for high waisted jeans, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that you find the perfect pair:

1. Fit

The fit is crucial when it comes to high waisted jeans. Look for jeans that fit well around your waist and hips without being too tight or too loose. You should be able to move and sit comfortably in them.

2. Rise

The rise of the jeans is also important. High waisted jeans should sit above your belly button to create a slimming effect and accentuate your curves.

3. Length

The length of the jeans should be just right. Look for jeans that hit at your ankle or slightly above to elongate your legs and create a streamlined silhouette.

4. Fabric

The fabric of the jeans should be high-quality and stretchy. Look for jeans that contain spandex or elastane to ensure that they hug your curves in all the right places without losing their shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your high waisted jeans:

Step 1: Choose the right top

Pair your high waisted jeans with a flowy blouse or a tucked-in t-shirt to create a balanced silhouette.

Step 2: Add some accessories

Accessorize your outfit with a statement necklace, a pair of earrings, or a scarf to add some personality and flair.

Step 3: Choose the right shoes

Choose shoes that complement your outfit and your personal style. You can opt for sneakers, sandals, flats, or heels, depending on the occasion.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing high waisted jeans:

1. Tuck in your top

Tucking in your top can help create a streamlined silhouette and accentuate your waist.

2. Play with proportions

High waisted jeans can be styled with cropped tops, oversized sweaters, or fitted blazers to create different looks and play with proportions.

3. Experiment with colors and patterns

Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns to add some fun and personality to your outfit.

FAQs

Q: Can plus size women wear high waisted jeans?

A: Yes, absolutely! High waisted jeans are flattering and comfortable for women of all sizes and body shapes.

Q: What is the best fabric for high waisted jeans?

A: The best fabric for high waisted jeans is a blend of cotton, spandex, and elastane. This combination ensures that the jeans are stretchy, comfortable, and retain their shape after multiple wears.

Q: How do I know if high waisted jeans fit me well?

A: High waisted jeans should fit well around your waist and hips without being too tight or too loose. You should be able to move and sit comfortably in them.

Hair Care Tips

Complete your look with some hair care tips:

1. Experiment with hairstyles

High waisted jeans can be styled with various hairstyles, from sleek ponytails to messy buns. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

2. Keep your hair healthy

Use high-quality hair products and avoid using too much heat on your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

3. Accessorize your hair

Add some flair to your hair by accessorizing it with headbands, hair clips, or scrunchies.

With these tips and recommendations, you can confidently choose the best high waisted jeans for plus size women in 2023 and style them to perfection. Happy shopping and styling!
