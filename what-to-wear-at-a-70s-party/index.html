What To Wear At A 70S Party A Complete Guide

Written by William Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you excited to attend a 70s party but clueless about what to wear? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about dressing up for a 70s-themed event. From disco balls to platform shoes, this era was all about fun and fashion. So, let's get started!

Description

A 70s party is a themed event where guests are encouraged to dress up in the fashion of the 1970s. This era was known for its bold colors, funky patterns, and disco-inspired outfits. The fashion of the 70s was all about self-expression and individuality, so don't be afraid to have some fun with your outfit!

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dress up for a 70s party:

Step 1: Do Some Research

Before you start shopping for your 70s outfit, do some research on the fashion of the era. Look for inspiration from iconic celebrities like Farrah Fawcett, Cher, and John Travolta. You can also watch movies and TV shows from the 70s to get a better idea of the fashion trends of the time.

Step 2: Choose Your Outfit

Once you have an idea of the fashion of the era, it's time to choose your outfit. There are plenty of options when it comes to 70s fashion, so choose something that reflects your personal style. Some popular options include disco jumpsuits, bell-bottom jeans, and maxi dresses.

Step 3: Accessorize

No 70s outfit is complete without some fun accessories. Think big sunglasses, platform shoes, and statement jewelry. Don't be afraid to mix and match patterns and colors to add some extra flair to your outfit.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when dressing up for a 70s party:

  • Don't be afraid to go bold with your outfit. The fashion of the 70s was all about standing out.
  • Accessorize, accessorize, accessorize! Fun accessories are a key part of any 70s outfit.
  • Have fun with your outfit and don't take yourself too seriously.
  • Consider doing your hair and makeup in a 70s-inspired style to complete your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dressing up for a 70s party:

What colors were popular in the 70s?

The 70s were known for their bold, bright colors. Some popular colors of the era include burnt orange, mustard yellow, and avocado green.

What kind of shoes should I wear to a 70s party?

Platform shoes were a popular footwear choice in the 70s. You could also opt for some classic sneakers or sandals.

Can I wear a wig to a 70s party?

Absolutely! Wigs were a popular accessory in the 70s, so feel free to rock a fun wig to complete your outfit.

Hair Care

If you really want to get into the 70s spirit, consider doing your hair in a 70s-inspired style. Here are a few ideas:

  • Farrah Fawcett-inspired feathered hair
  • Afro
  • Long, straight hair with a center part
  • Cornrows

Remember to have fun with your hair and don't be afraid to try something new!

So there you have it, a complete guide on what to wear at a 70s party. With these tips and ideas, you're sure to stand out and have a great time at your next themed event. Happy shopping!
