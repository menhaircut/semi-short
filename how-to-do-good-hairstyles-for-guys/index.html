How To Do Good Hairstyles For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Hairstyles are an essential part of a guy's appearance. With the right hairstyle, you can look stylish and fashionable. However, choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. In this post, we will give you a complete guide on how to do good hairstyles for guys. We will cover everything from how to choose the right hairstyle to how to maintain it. So, let's get started.

Table of Content

  1. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  2. Tools Needed for Styling
  3. How to Style Your Hair
  4. Tips for Hairstyling

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in achieving a good hairstyle is choosing the right one. There are many factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, such as face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Determine your face shape. There are different hairstyles for different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides. Instead, go for a hairstyle that adds height to the top.
  • Consider your hair type. If you have thick hair, you can go for a hairstyle with more volume. If you have thin hair, you should avoid hairstyles with too much volume.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you should choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you can go for a hairstyle that requires more maintenance.

Tools Needed for Styling

Before you start styling your hair, you need to have the right tools. Here are the tools you will need:

  • A comb or a brush
  • A hair dryer
  • A styling product, such as mousse or gel

How to Style Your Hair

Now that you have the right tools, it's time to style your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair and make it easier to style.
  2. Towel dry your hair. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel as this can cause damage to your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair. This will help hold your hairstyle in place. The amount of product you use depends on the length and thickness of your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair. Use a comb or a brush to style your hair as you dry it. This will help create volume and shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or a pomade. This will help hold your hairstyle in place and add shine.

Tips for Hairstyling

Here are some tips to help you achieve a good hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Try different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Be patient. Styling your hair takes time and practice. Don't get frustrated if you don't get it right the first time.
  • Use the right products. Different hair types require different products. Make sure you choose the right one for your hair type.
  • Get a good haircut. A good haircut is the foundation of a good hairstyle. Make sure you go to a reputable stylist.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every other day or every two days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or a quiff.

What is the best hairstyle for thin hair?

The best hairstyle for thin hair is one that adds volume, such as a messy crop or a textured fringe.

Hair Care

Maintaining a good hairstyle is not just about styling your hair. You also need to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can cause build-up and damage to your hair.

By following these tips, you can achieve a good hairstyle that looks great and is easy to maintain. Remember, choosing the right hairstyle is the first step, but taking care of your hair is just as important.
