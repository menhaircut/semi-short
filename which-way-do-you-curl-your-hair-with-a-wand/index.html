Which Way Do You Curl Your Hair With A Wand

Written by Oliver Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read
Which Way Do You Curl Your Hair with a Wand? | Hair Care Tips

If you're looking to achieve gorgeous curls with a wand, you might be wondering which way to curl your hair. In this article, we'll go over the best techniques to achieve the perfect curls, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your curls looking great all day long.

How To Curl Your Hair With A Wand

Before we dive into which way to curl your hair with a wand, it's important to make sure you're using the right wand for your hair type. If you have fine hair, you should use a wand with a smaller barrel, while thicker hair types can handle a larger barrel.

When it comes to which way to curl your hair with a wand, there are two main techniques you can use: curling away from your face or curling towards your face. Both techniques can create beautiful curls, it just depends on the look you're going for.

Curling Away From Your Face

Curling away from your face is a great technique to use if you're looking for more volume and bounce in your curls. To achieve this look, start at the root of your hair and wrap a small section of hair around the wand, curling away from your face. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Repeat this process, working your way around your head until all of your hair is curled. Once you've curled all of your hair, you can use a brush or your fingers to separate the curls and create more volume.

Curling Towards Your Face

Curling towards your face is a great technique to use if you're looking for more defined curls that frame your face. To achieve this look, start at the root of your hair and wrap a small section of hair around the wand, curling towards your face. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Repeat this process, working your way around your head until all of your hair is curled. Once you've curled all of your hair, you can use a brush or your fingers to separate the curls and create more definition.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to curl your hair with a wand:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into small sections.
  3. Starting at the root of your hair, wrap a small section of hair around the wand, curling away from or towards your face.
  4. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat this process, working your way around your head until all of your hair is curled.
  6. Use a brush or your fingers to separate the curls and create more volume or definition.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips For Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect curls:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before curling to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don't hold the wand on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes to achieve different curl sizes.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to give your curls more texture and volume.

FAQ

How long does it take to curl your hair with a wand?

The time it takes to curl your hair with a wand depends on your hair length and thickness, as well as the size of the wand barrel you're using. On average, it takes about 20-30 minutes to curl medium length hair with a wand.

How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

To prevent your curls from falling out, use a light hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much. You can also use a curl-defining product to help your curls hold their shape.

Hair Care Tips

Curling your hair with a wand can cause damage if you're not careful. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can damage your hair.

Now that you know which way to curl your hair with a wand, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls in no time. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
