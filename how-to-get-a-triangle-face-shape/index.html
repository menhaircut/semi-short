How To Get A Triangle Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to get a triangle face shape? This facial shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and cheekbones with a wider jawline. If you want to achieve this look, there are some things you can do to enhance your bone structure and create the illusion of a triangle face shape. In this post, we'll go through the steps you need to take to get a triangle face shape, as well as some tips and hair care advice to help you maintain it.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get a Triangle Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs

How to Get a Triangle Face Shape

Getting a triangle face shape is all about creating balance between the upper and lower parts of your face. Here are some things you can do to achieve this:

  • Contour your jawline: Use a contouring product to create a shadow under your jawline, which will make it appear more prominent. This will help to balance out the width of your jawline with the narrowness of your forehead.
  • Highlight your cheekbones: Use a highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones to draw attention to this area. This will help to create the illusion of a wider upper face.
  • Grow out your hair: If you have short hair, consider growing it out to chin-length or longer. This will help to balance out the width of your jawline with the narrowness of your forehead.
  • Try a side part: A side part can help to balance out the width of your jawline with the narrowness of your forehead. Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a triangle face shape:

  1. Contour your jawline using a contouring product.
  2. Apply a highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones.
  3. Grow out your hair to chin-length or longer.
  4. Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.
  5. Practice good posture to help elongate your neck and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you get a triangle face shape:

  • Invest in a good contouring product: Look for a product that's easy to blend and matches your skin tone.
  • Use a highlighter sparingly: While highlighter can help to create the illusion of a wider upper face, too much of it can make your face look shiny and oily.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant.
  • Try different hairstyles: Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for your face shape. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting a professional makeup or hair consultation: A makeup artist or hair stylist can help you achieve the perfect triangle face shape.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a triangle face shape:

Can I get a triangle face shape without contouring?

Yes, you can still achieve a triangle face shape without contouring. Growing out your hair and experimenting with different hairstyles can help to balance out your face shape.

What hairstyles should I avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as big curls or waves.

How long does it take to get a triangle face shape?

Getting a triangle face shape is not an overnight process. It may take several weeks or months of consistent effort to achieve the look you want.

Hair Care for Triangle Face Shape

Here are some tips for maintaining your triangle face shape:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo: This can help to add volume to the top of your head and balance out the width of your jawline.
  • Avoid heavy hair products: Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make your face look wider.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider getting layers: Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair without adding width to your face.

Getting a triangle face shape takes time and effort, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want. Remember to take care of your hair and skin to maintain your new look.
