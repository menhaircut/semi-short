How To Style Permed Hair Without Washing A Complete Guide

Written by William Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have permed hair, you know that washing it too often can cause the curls to lose their shape and become frizzy. However, that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style for the sake of preserving your curls. In this guide, we'll show you how to style permed hair without washing it, so you can keep your curls looking fresh and bouncy for longer.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Permed Hair Without Washing
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Permed Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Permed Hair Without Washing

The key to styling permed hair without washing it is to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a lifesaver when it comes to styling permed hair without washing it. It helps to absorb excess oil and sweat, leaving your hair looking fresh and voluminous. Simply spray the dry shampoo onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

2. Use a Curl Refreshing Spray

Curl refreshing sprays are designed to revitalize curls that have lost their shape. Simply spritz the spray onto your hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers to bring them back to life.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Leave-in conditioners are great for adding moisture and shine to permed hair. Apply a small amount to your hair, focusing on the ends, and comb it through with a wide-toothed comb.

4. Use a Curling Iron

If your curls are looking limp and lifeless, a curling iron can help to give them a boost. Use a small curling iron to curl small sections of your hair, wrapping the hair around the barrel and holding it for a few seconds.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling permed hair without washing it:

Step 1: Apply Dry Shampoo

Start by applying dry shampoo to your roots, using your fingers to massage it in.

Step 2: Apply Curl Refreshing Spray

Next, spritz a curl refreshing spray onto your hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends, and comb it through with a wide-toothed comb.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If your curls need a boost, use a small curling iron to curl small sections of your hair.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling permed hair without washing it:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage, keeping your curls looking smooth and bouncy.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to become frizzy. Try to avoid touching it as much as possible, and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle it instead of a brush.

3. Use a Diffuser

When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help maintain your curls' shape.

FAQs

How often should I wash my permed hair?

It's best to avoid washing your permed hair too often, as this can cause the curls to lose their shape and become frizzy. Try to wash your hair no more than once a week, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep it looking fresh.

Can I straighten my permed hair?

Yes, you can straighten your permed hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid applying too much heat, as this can damage your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips to help keep your permed hair looking healthy and vibrant:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Look for a shampoo that's specifically designed for permed hair, as this will help to keep your curls hydrated and bouncy.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid Using Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your permed hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your curls and cause them to become dry and brittle.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your permed hair without washing it, keeping your curls looking fresh and bouncy for longer.
