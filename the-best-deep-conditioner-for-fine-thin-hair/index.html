Step-by-Step Guide to Deep Conditioning

If you want to get the most out of your deep conditioner, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply the deep conditioner to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  4. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Cover your hair with a shower cap or plastic wrap to trap in heat and help the conditioner penetrate your hair.
  6. Leave the conditioner in for at least 30 minutes (or as directed on the product label).
  7. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water.

In addition to using a deep conditioner, there are other steps you can take to care for your fine thin hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
