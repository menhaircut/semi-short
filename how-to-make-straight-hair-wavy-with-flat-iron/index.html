How To Make Straight Hair Wavy With Flat Iron

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you want to change your straight hair into wavy hair without using any hair products? Well, you can do that by using a flat iron. In this blog post, we will teach you how to make straight hair wavy with flat iron in easy steps. But before we proceed, let us first understand what a flat iron is.

Description

A flat iron is a hair tool used to straighten hair. But it can also be used to create waves and curls. It has two flat plates that heat up and clamp down on the hair to straighten it. By using a flat iron to make your hair wavy, you can save money and time compared to using a curling iron or other hair products.

How to Make Straight Hair Wavy with Flat Iron

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before using a flat iron, you need to prepare your hair by washing it with shampoo and conditioner. Dry it completely using a blow dryer or air dry it. Apply a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair from the heat of the flat iron.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips. The smaller the sections, the more defined your waves will be.

Step 3: Heat up your flat iron

Turn on your flat iron and set it to the desired temperature. For fine hair, use a lower temperature while for thick hair, use a higher temperature.

Step 4: Start with the bottom section

Start with the bottom section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron near the roots. Twist the flat iron away from your face and move it down to the end of your hair.

Step 5: Repeat on the other sections

Repeat step four on the other sections of your hair. Make sure to twist the flat iron away from your face to create waves.

Step 6: Finish with a hairspray

After you finish creating waves on your hair, apply a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your wavy hair last longer:

  • Apply a volumizing mousse before creating waves to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a sea salt spray on your hair to add texture to your waves.
  • Don't brush your hair after creating waves to avoid frizz.
  • Apply a hair serum to the ends of your hair to avoid dryness.

FAQ

Can I make my hair wavy with a straightener?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy with a straightener or flat iron. Just follow the steps mentioned above.

How long will my wavy hair last?

Your wavy hair can last up to two days depending on how you take care of it.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using a flat iron or any heat styling tool every day to avoid damage.
  • Apply a hair mask at least once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products with harsh chemicals.

Now that you know how to make straight hair wavy with flat iron, you can experiment and have fun with your hair. Just remember to take care of it to avoid damage.
