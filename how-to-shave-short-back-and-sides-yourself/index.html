How To Shave Short Back And Sides Yourself

Written by Bardi Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh haircut, shaving the back and sides of your hair is a classic style that will never go out of fashion. But if you're on a budget or just prefer to DIY, you can learn how to shave short back and sides yourself. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care instructions to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a timeless style that is popular among men. It's a versatile look that can be worn in a variety of ways, from a classic taper fade to a modern undercut. The style is achieved by shaving the hair on the back and sides of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a sleek and clean-cut appearance that is perfect for any occasion.

How to

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You'll need a set of hair clippers, a comb, scissors, a mirror, and a towel. If you have long hair, you'll also need hair ties to keep your hair out of the way.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

Using your clippers, start shaving the hair on the sides of your head. Start at the bottom, near your ear, and work your way up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create an even length. Repeat on the other side.

Step 3: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your head. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the same technique as you did on the sides, using a comb to guide the clippers and create an even length.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once you've shaved the back and sides, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length. Use the comb to blend the top with the sides and back.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you've finished shaving, use a towel to wipe away any loose hairs. Use a mirror to check for any missed spots or unevenness. If you notice any areas that need touch-ups, use your clippers or scissors to make adjustments.

Tips

  • Start with a higher guard on your clippers and work your way down to achieve your desired length.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to go slow and get it right than rush and make mistakes.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help if you're having trouble reaching certain areas.

FAQ

Q: How often should I shave my back and sides?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the style. Generally, you should shave every 2-3 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Q: Can I use a razor instead of clippers?

A: It's not recommended to use a razor as it can be difficult to control and may cause irritation or razor burn. Clippers are designed specifically for hair cutting and are much easier to use.

Hair Care

Once you've shaved your back and sides, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Visit a barber every few months to trim your hair and maintain the style.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily shave short back and sides yourself and achieve a stylish and timeless look.
