Medium Length Hairstyles For Diamond Shaped Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you have a unique facial structure with a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. It's a beautiful shape that looks great with a variety of hairstyles. In this article, we'll be sharing some medium-length hairstyles that will flatter your diamond-shaped face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hairstyles for Diamond Shaped Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Description

Medium length hairstyles are perfect for diamond-shaped faces because they balance out the wider cheekbones and narrow forehead. They also give you the flexibility to play around with different styling options, from sleek and straight to textured and wavy.

When choosing a medium length hairstyle, you want to look for styles that create volume around the chin and jawline. This will help soften the sharp angles of your face and create a more balanced look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that add volume around the chin and jawline
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and texture
  • Experiment with different partings to find what works best for you

Some great medium length hairstyles for diamond-shaped faces include:

Shoulder Length Bob

A shoulder length bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for diamond-shaped faces. The length hits just below the jawline, creating volume and balancing out the wider cheekbones. You can add layers to create movement and texture, or keep it sleek and straight for a more polished look.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding long layers to create movement and volume around the chin and jawline. This will help balance out your face shape and create a more flattering look.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to soften the sharp angles of a diamond-shaped face. They create volume around the forehead and balance out the wider cheekbones. You can pair them with a shoulder length bob or longer layers for a chic and stylish look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hairstyles for Diamond Shaped Face

Styling medium length hairstyles for diamond-shaped faces is relatively easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your medium length hairstyle looking its best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for diamond-shaped faces:

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to create volume around the chin and jawline, so your face doesn't appear too narrow.

2. Should I avoid bangs?

No, you don't have to avoid bangs. Side swept bangs are a great way to soften the sharp angles of a diamond-shaped face.

Hair Care Tips for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips for diamond-shaped faces:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair to prevent damage
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find a medium length hairstyle that flatters your diamond-shaped face. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
