How To Wear Fedora With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who loves wearing fedoras but have been hesitant to do so because of your short hair? Worry not! In this blog post, we will give you tips and tricks on how to wear fedoras with short hair. From choosing the right fedora to styling it, we've got you covered.

Description

A fedora is a timeless accessory that can add a touch of sophistication to any outfit. It can be paired with both casual and formal attire, making it a versatile accessory. However, some people are hesitant to wear fedoras because they believe that it only looks good on people with long hair. In this blog post, we will debunk that myth and show you how to wear fedoras with short hair.

How to Choose the Right Fedora?

The first step in wearing a fedora with short hair is choosing the right one. Here are some tips:

1. Choose the Right Size

Make sure that the fedora fits your head perfectly. It should not be too big or too small. A well-fitting fedora will enhance your overall look.

2. Choose the Right Color

Choose a fedora that complements your skin tone and hair color. If you have light hair, opt for a darker-colored fedora. If you have dark hair, go for a lighter-colored fedora.

3. Choose the Right Material

The material of the fedora is also important. If you're going for a casual look, choose a straw fedora. If you're going for a formal look, choose a wool or felt fedora.

Step by Step Guide on How to Wear Fedora with Short Hair

Follow these steps to wear a fedora with short hair:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before putting on the fedora, brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair once you put on the fedora.

Step 2: Position the Fedora

Place the fedora on your head and position it slightly towards the back of your head. This will leave some of your hair visible at the front.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair at the front so that it frames your face nicely. You can leave your hair down or tie it up in a bun or ponytail. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Step 4: Adjust the Fedora

Adjust the fedora so that it sits comfortably on your head. Make sure that it's not too tight or too loose.

Tips for Wearing Fedora with Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing a fedora with short hair:

1. Keep it Simple

When it comes to wearing a fedora, less is more. Keep your outfit simple and let the fedora be the statement piece.

2. Pay Attention to Details

Make sure that the rest of your outfit complements the fedora. Pay attention to details such as the color of your shoes and accessories.

3. Be Confident

The most important tip when wearing a fedora is to be confident. Own your style and wear the fedora with pride.

FAQ

Can I wear a fedora with any outfit?

Yes, a fedora can be paired with both casual and formal outfits. However, make sure that the rest of your outfit complements the fedora.

Can I wear a fedora with long hair?

Yes, a fedora can be worn with both short and long hair. The styling tips are similar for both.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing a fedora:

1. Don't Wear a Tight Fedora

A tight fedora can cause hair breakage and damage. Make sure that the fedora fits comfortably on your head.

2. Use a Silk Scarf

Wrap a silk scarf around your hair before wearing the fedora. This will protect your hair from damage and breakage.

3. Don't Wear the Fedora for Too Long

Avoid wearing the fedora for too long as it can cause scalp irritation and hair loss.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a fedora with short hair. Remember to choose the right fedora, style your hair, and be confident. Happy styling!
