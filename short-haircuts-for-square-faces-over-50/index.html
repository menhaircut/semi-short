Short Haircuts for Square Faces Over 50

As we age, our face shape changes and so does our hair. If you have a square face and are over 50, you may be looking for a short haircut that flatters your face shape. In this article, we will give you some ideas and tips on short haircuts for square faces over 50.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, with the width of the face being almost equal to the length. Short haircuts can help soften the angles of a square face and highlight the cheekbones. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a square face over 50, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. You may want to go for a layered bob, a pixie cut, or a shaggy haircut. The length of the haircut should be around chin-length or shorter, to draw attention to the cheekbones and away from the jawline.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short haircut for a square face over 50:

  1. Consider your hair texture - if you have fine hair, a layered bob would add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, a pixie cut would be a good option.
  2. Choose a length that flatters your face shape - chin-length or shorter is ideal for a square face.
  3. Consider your personal style - do you want a classic or edgy look?
  4. Consult with your hairstylist - they can help you choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for a square face over 50:

  • Avoid blunt cuts - they can make the jawline look even more pronounced.
  • Add layers to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Consider adding bangs to soften the forehead.
  • Experiment with different styles - you may find that a shaggy haircut or a layered bob flatters your face shape the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for square faces over 50:

What is the best short haircut for a square face over 50?

A layered bob or a pixie cut are good options for a square face over 50.

What length should a short haircut be for a square face over 50?

The length should be around chin-length or shorter.

Should I add bangs to a short haircut for a square face over 50?

Adding bangs can soften the forehead and complement a square face.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will help maintain the shape of your haircut.

Overall, there are many short haircuts that can flatter a square face over 50. Consider your hair texture, personal style, and face shape when choosing a haircut, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. With the right haircut and hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
