Hairstyles For Long Face Small Forehead The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Sep 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face and small forehead? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for your unique face shape. From short to long, curly to straight, there is a style that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Having a long face with a small forehead can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that doesn't accentuate your forehead while still complementing your face shape. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that balances your facial features and enhances your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a long face and small forehead is to create the illusion of a shorter face and a larger forehead. The right haircut can elongate your features and make your forehead appear larger. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

There are several face shapes, and each one requires a different hairstyle. If you have a long face, you want to create the illusion of width. A hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face can help achieve this effect.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair can also make a significant difference in how your face looks. If you have a long face and a small forehead, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that are too short. A longer hairstyle can help balance your features.

Think About Layers

Layers can be your best friend when it comes to creating the perfect hairstyle for a long face and small forehead. Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance your facial features.

Step-by-Step Hairstyles for Long Face Small Forehead

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle let's dive into some of the best hairstyles for a long face and small forehead. Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite hairstyles:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, edgy hairstyle that is perfect for a long face and small forehead. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by cutting your hair short on the sides and back of your head.
  2. Leave the hair on the top of your head longer, about 2-3 inches.
  3. Use a texturizing spray to create volume and texture in your hair.
  4. Style your hair with your fingers, using a bit of pomade or wax to create a messy, tousled look.

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that can flatter a long face and small forehead. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Cut your hair into a chin-length bob.
  2. Add layers to create volume and texture in your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, creating volume at the roots.
  4. Finish the look with a bit of hairspray to hold your style in place.

The Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a long face and small forehead. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Cut your bangs so that they are longer on one side of your face.
  2. Use a flat iron to smooth your hair and create a sleek look.
  3. Sweep your bangs to one side of your face, using a bit of hairspray to hold them in place.
  4. Style the rest of your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have the perfect hairstyle, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face and small forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too sleek and smooth, as they can make your forehead appear even smaller.

Can I still wear my hair up?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features. You can also add some bangs or layers to frame your face.

Hair Care for Long Face Small Forehead

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining any hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and hairstyles in mind, you can find the perfect look for your long face and small forehead. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks.
