Best Hairstyles For Rectangular Face Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read
Are you a rectangular face female struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for rectangular face females, how to achieve them, and tips on how to take care of your hair. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Rectangular Face Female
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A rectangular face female has a longer face shape with a strong jawline and a forehead that is the same width as the cheekbones. The goal when choosing a hairstyle is to balance out the length of the face and soften the sharp angles of the jawline.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for rectangular face females, let's first determine if you have a rectangular face shape. Here's how you can do it: 1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera straight on with your hair pulled back. 2. Using a ruler, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. 3. Compare the measurements. A rectangular face shape will have a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all about the same width.

Best Hairstyles for Rectangular Face Female

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's take a look at the best hairstyles for rectangular face females: 1. Long Layers - Long layers can add movement and volume to your hair, which can help balance out the length of your face. 2. Side-Swept Bangs - Bangs that are swept to the side can help soften the sharp angles of the jawline and balance out the length of the face. 3. Pixie Cut - A short pixie cut can add volume to the crown of the head, which can help balance out the length of the face. 4. Bob - A chin-length bob can add volume to the sides of the face, which can help balance out a rectangular face shape. 5. Beach Waves - Loose, beachy waves can add volume and movement to the hair, which can help balance out the length of the face.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair: 1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate the face even more. 2. Avoid center parts, as they can also elongate the face. Opt for a side part instead. 3. Adding volume to the sides of the hair can help balance out a rectangular face shape. 4. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the hair. 5. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail if I have a rectangular face shape?
A: Yes, you can still wear a ponytail. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of the hair to balance out the length of the face. Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a rectangular face shape?
A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of the hair to balance out the length of the face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind: 1. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. 2. Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. 3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. 4. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay. 5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to avoid breakage. In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a rectangular face female can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that flatters your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair by following the hair care tips mentioned above. Happy styling!
