Which Colour Is Best For Medium Skin Tone

Written by William Sep 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you have a medium skin tone. Your skin colour plays a vital role in determining which hair colour will suit you the best. In this article, we will discuss the best hair colours that complement medium skin tone. So, let's get started!

Description

Medium skin tone is a versatile skin colour that falls between fair and dark skin. It has a warm undertone that makes it perfect for warm and rich hair colours. However, it can be challenging to find the right hair colour that complements your skin tone without making you look washed out or dull.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for Medium Skin Tone?

Choosing the right hair colour for medium skin tone requires a bit of research and understanding of your skin's undertone. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin's Undertone

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin's undertone. Medium skin tone has a warm undertone, which means it has yellow or golden hues. You can determine your skin's undertone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear greenish, you have a warm undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining the right hair colour for you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colours like caramel, honey, or golden brown will complement your skin tone. If you have green or blue eyes, cool hair colours like ash brown, platinum blonde, or burgundy will look best on you.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also help you determine the right hair colour for your skin tone. If you have warm-toned hair, warm hair colours like copper or auburn will look great on you. If you have cool-toned hair, cool hair colours like ash or platinum blonde will suit you the best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour for Medium Skin Tone

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair colour for medium skin tone:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin's Undertone

As mentioned earlier, determining your skin's undertone is the first step in choosing the right hair colour. Look at the veins on your wrist to determine your skin's undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Take a look at your eye colour and determine whether it is warm or cool. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colours will complement your skin tone. If you have green or blue eyes, cool hair colours will suit you the best.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Consider your natural hair colour to determine whether it is warm or cool. If you have warm-toned hair, warm hair colours will look great on you. If you have cool-toned hair, cool hair colours will suit you the best.

Step 4: Choose the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin's undertone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, it's time to choose the right shade. Warm hair colours like caramel, honey, and golden brown will look great on medium skin tone. Cool hair colours like ash brown, platinum blonde, and burgundy will also complement medium skin tone.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you have chosen the right hair colour, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your hair colour to fade.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair and cause your hair colour to fade.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQs

What are the best hair colours for medium skin tone?

The best hair colours for medium skin tone are warm hair colours like caramel, honey, and golden brown. Cool hair colours like ash brown, platinum blonde, and burgundy also complement medium skin tone.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the hair dye to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your hair from drying out and to keep your hair colour looking fresh.

Hair Care for Medium Skin Tone

Medium skin tone can be prone to dryness, so it's essential to use hair products that hydrate and nourish your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

Now that you know which hair colour is best for medium skin tone, it's time to choose the right shade and rock your new look with confidence!
