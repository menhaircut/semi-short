Best Men s Hairstyles For Narrow Faces

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a narrow face, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements your facial features. However, there are many styles that can suit your face shape and enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will discuss the best men's hairstyles for narrow faces, including how to achieve them, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Narrow faces are characterized by a long and slim appearance, with a prominent chin and high cheekbones. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that create width and volume around the temples and cheekbones, while also balancing the proportions of the face. This can be achieved through various techniques, including layering, texturizing, and adding volume to the crown or sides of the head.

How to

To achieve the best men's hairstyles for narrow faces, you should consider your hair type, length, and personal style. Some of the most popular hairstyles for this face shape include:

1. Short Textured Crop

This hairstyle involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top section slightly longer and textured. This creates a balanced look that adds volume to the crown and temples, while also framing the face. To style, apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the top section and use your fingers to create a tousled, textured look.

2. Side-Parted Pompadour

The side-parted pompadour is a classic hairstyle that can work well for narrow faces. This involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving the top section longer and styled into a pompadour. The side parting helps to add width to the face, while the volume at the top adds balance and proportion. To style, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to the top section and use a comb to create the pompadour shape.

3. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different face shapes. For narrow faces, the key is to create volume and texture at the top, while keeping the sides short and tapered. This helps to add width to the face and balance the proportions. To style, apply a small amount of texturizing spray or mousse to the top section and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Step by Step

To achieve these hairstyles, you may need to follow some basic steps:

1. Choose the Right Haircut

Start by choosing a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with a professional stylist to get advice on the best styles for narrow faces.

2. Use the Right Products

Use the right hair products for your hair type and style. For example, if you have thick hair, you may need a stronger hold product like hair wax or pomade. If you have fine hair, you may need a lighter product like mousse or texturizing spray.

3. Style with Fingers

Use your fingers to style your hair, rather than a comb or brush. This helps to create a more natural, textured look that adds volume and dimension to the hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best men's hairstyles for narrow faces:

  • Choose a style that adds width and volume around the temples and cheekbones.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as these can make the face appear even narrower.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair type and personal style.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a narrow face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as these can make the face appear even narrower. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short or cropped, as these can accentuate the high cheekbones and prominent chin.

Can I still wear a beard if I have a narrow face?

Yes, you can still wear a beard if you have a narrow face. However, it's important to choose a beard style that complements your facial features. For example, a short, well-groomed beard can help to add width to the face, while a longer, fuller beard may accentuate the narrowness.

How often should I get my hair cut?

The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding over-styling or using too much product. Additionally, getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

In conclusion, there are plenty of options for men with narrow faces when it comes to choosing the best hairstyles. By considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find a style that complements your features and enhances your overall appearance.
