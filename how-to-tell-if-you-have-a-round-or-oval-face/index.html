How To Tell If You Have A Round Or Oval Face

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering whether you have a round or oval face? Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape and give you some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Content

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Enhancing Your Features
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in identifying your face shape is to pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • The length of your face compared to the width
  • The shape of your jawline
  • The width of your forehead
  • The shape of your cheekbones

Based on these features, you can determine whether you have a round or oval face.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face will be approximately as wide as it is long. Your jawline will be soft and rounded, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face. Your forehead and chin may be slightly smaller in proportion to your cheeks.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face will be longer than it is wide, and your jawline will be slightly curved. Your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face, but your forehead and chin will be more in proportion to your cheeks.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to determine your face shape:

  1. Pull your hair back away from your face.
  2. Look in the mirror and examine your face shape, paying attention to the length and width of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the width of your forehead.
  3. Compare your face to the descriptions of round and oval face shapes.
  4. Determine which face shape best describes your features.

Remember that face shapes can vary, and you may have a combination of features from both round and oval face shapes.

Tips for Enhancing Your Features

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use the following tips to enhance your natural beauty:

Round Face Shape

  • Choose hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob.
  • Wear makeup that contours your face to create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a blunt bob or a middle part.
  • Wear earrings that elongate your face, such as drop or dangle earrings.

Oval Face Shape

  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face, such as a side-swept bang or a long layered cut.
  • Wear makeup that highlights your cheekbones and defines your jawline.
  • Experiment with different styles of sunglasses, as most shapes will flatter your face.
  • Wear earrings that add width to your face, such as hoops or chandelier earrings.

FAQs

Q: Can face shape change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and will not change over time.

Q: How many face shapes are there?

A: There are several common face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond.

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, it's possible to have a combination of features from different face shapes.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make a big difference in how you look and feel. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

Round Face Shape

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height to the top of your head and create a more defined jawline.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.

Oval Face Shape

  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face and show off your cheekbones.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles, as most hairstyles will flatter your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover up your face, such as heavy bangs.

By following these tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Remember to embrace your unique features and celebrate your individuality!
