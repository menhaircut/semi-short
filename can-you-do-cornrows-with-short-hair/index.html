Can You Do Cornrows With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle that involves braiding the hair close to the scalp in a series of rows. While cornrows are typically associated with long hair, many people wonder if it is possible to achieve this look with shorter hair. The answer is yes! In this article, we will discuss how to do cornrows with short hair, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice.

Description

Cornrows are a protective hairstyle that can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. This style typically involves braiding the hair close to the scalp in rows that run parallel to each other. Cornrows can be styled in a variety of patterns and designs, making them a versatile and fashionable choice.

How to Do Cornrows with Short Hair

While cornrows are typically associated with long hair, it is possible to achieve this look with shorter hair. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start braiding, it is important to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair and applying a leave-in conditioner. This will help to detangle your hair and make it easier to braid. If your hair is too short to braid, you can try using hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small sections using a comb. The size of each section will depend on the length of your hair and the desired size of your cornrows. Make sure to section your hair evenly and secure each section with a clip or hair tie.

Step 3: Start Braiding

Starting at the front of your head, take a small section of hair and divide it into three equal parts. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the left section over the middle section, then crossing the right section over the new middle section. Continue braiding in this way, adding small sections of hair to each section as you go.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue braiding each section of hair until you have completed all of the rows. Make sure to keep each braid tight and close to the scalp. If you have any loose strands or flyaways, you can use a gel or hairspray to keep them in place.

Tips for Doing Cornrows with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when doing cornrows with short hair:

  • Use a hairpiece or extensions to add length if your hair is too short to braid.
  • Start with clean, detangled hair to make the braiding process easier.
  • Use a gel or hairspray to keep the braids in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Don't braid the hair too tightly, as this can cause discomfort and damage to the scalp.
  • Take breaks if you need to, as the braiding process can be time-consuming and tiring.

FAQ

Can you do cornrows with really short hair?

Yes, you can do cornrows with really short hair. However, it may be easier to add some length using a hairpiece or extensions.

How long does it take to do cornrows on short hair?

The amount of time it takes to do cornrows on short hair will depend on the length of your hair and the size of the braids. It can take anywhere from 1-3 hours to complete a full head of cornrows.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

Cornrows can be a great way to protect your hair and promote growth, but it is important to take care of your hair while it is in this style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and maintain scalp health.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause damage to the scalp and hair follicles.
  • Cover your hair with a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent friction and breakage.
  • Take breaks between hairstyles to give your hair a chance to rest and recover.

Conclusion

In conclusion, you absolutely can do cornrows with short hair. With the right techniques and some practice, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish look that is both practical and fashionable. Just remember to take care of your hair while it is in this style, and enjoy your new look!
