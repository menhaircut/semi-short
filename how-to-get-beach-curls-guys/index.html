How To Get Beach Curls Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Beach curls are a popular hairstyle for both men and women. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to achieve a relaxed, effortless look. In this post, we will cover how to get beach curls guys can rock with confidence. We will cover everything from what tools and products to use, how to style your hair, and tips for maintaining your beach curls.

How to Get Beach Curls Guys: What You'll Need

The first step to achieving beach curls is to gather the necessary tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Sea salt spray
  • Texturizing spray or cream
  • Hair tie or clip

Step 1: Protect Your Hair

Before you begin curling your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray evenly throughout your hair. This will help prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle due to the high heat of the curling iron.

Step 2: Create Beach Waves

To create beach curls, start by dividing your hair into small sections. Take each section and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the hair in place for 5-10 seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 3: Add Texture

Once you've created your beach waves, it's time to add texture. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers. This will help create a tousled, beachy look.

Step 4: Define Your Curls

To further define your beach curls, apply a texturizing spray or cream to the ends of your hair. This will help create more separation and definition in your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beach Curls

Now that you've achieved your beach curls, it's important to know how to maintain them. Here are some tips to keep your curls looking fresh:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause them to lose their shape
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your curls between washes

FAQ:

How long do beach curls last?

Beach curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use. To help your curls last longer, avoid touching them too much and use a hairspray to set them in place.

Can guys rock beach curls?

Absolutely! Beach curls are a versatile hairstyle that look great on both men and women. They are perfect for those who want a relaxed, effortless look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling great.

Conclusion

Beach curls are a great hairstyle for guys who want to achieve a relaxed, effortless look. By following the steps and tips outlined in this post, you can achieve perfect beach curls every time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
