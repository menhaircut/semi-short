Hairstyles For Oval Face Male Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Frank Jul 30, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're lucky! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, finding the perfect hairstyle for your oval face male can be a bit challenging. In this article, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval face male, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of the same width, you have an oval face shape.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

As mentioned earlier, oval face males can pull off almost any hairstyle. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face male:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are perfect for oval face males as they show off the facial features without overwhelming the face shape. Some of the best short haircuts for oval face males include:

  • Crew Cut
  • Buzz Cut
  • Short Textured Crop
  • Side Parted Short Hair

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts are also a great option for oval face males. They provide enough length to style the hair while still maintaining the balance of the face shape. Some of the best medium haircuts for oval face males include:

  • Side-Swept Undercut
  • Quiff
  • Side Parted Hairstyle
  • Slicked Back Hair

Long Haircuts

If you have an oval face shape and long hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles. Some of the best long haircuts for oval face males include:

  • Man Bun
  • Long Shaggy Hair
  • Layered Haircuts
  • Wavy or Curly Hair

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing the right hairstyle for your oval face male, here are some tips to consider:

  • Consider your hair type and texture.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and lifestyle.
  • Consider the length and volume of the hairstyle.
  • Consult a hairstylist to help you find the perfect hairstyle.

FAQ about Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

What hairstyles should oval face males avoid?

Oval face males should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top or sides of the head. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too long or cover the forehead.

Can oval face males pull off a beard?

Yes, oval face males can pull off a beard. However, avoid beards that are too long or cover the jawline.

How often should oval face males cut their hair?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. Short haircuts require frequent trimming, while longer haircuts can be trimmed every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Male

Here are some hair care tips for oval face males:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a hairbrush to style your hair gently.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly for trimming and styling.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval face male, it's time to experiment and find the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny!
