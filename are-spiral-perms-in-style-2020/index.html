Description

Are spiral perms in style 2020? The answer is yes! Spiral perms are a popular hairstyle that can add volume, bounce, and texture to your hair. They have been around for decades and have made a comeback in recent years. Spiral perms are a type of permanent wave that creates tight, springy curls that last for several months. They are ideal for people with straight or slightly wavy hair who want to add more texture and volume to their hair.

How to Get Spiral Perms

The first step in getting a spiral perm is to find a hair stylist who specializes in perms. You should also do your research and look for reviews and recommendations online. Once you have found a stylist, you can schedule a consultation to discuss your hair goals and determine if a spiral perm is right for you.

Factors to Consider Before Getting a Spiral Perm:

  • Your hair type: Spiral perms work best on straight or slightly wavy hair.
  • Your hair length: Spiral perms are ideal for medium to long hair.
  • Your hair condition: If your hair is damaged or over-processed, a spiral perm may not be recommended.
  • Your lifestyle: Spiral perms require some maintenance and styling, so it's important to consider if it fits into your daily routine.

Step-by-Step Process

The process of getting a spiral perm involves several steps:

  1. Your stylist will start by washing your hair to remove any dirt, oil, or styling products.
  2. Your hair will be sectioned into small, even sections and wrapped tightly around perm rods.
  3. A perm solution will be applied to your hair, which will break down the hair's natural bonds and reform them into a new shape.
  4. Your hair will be covered with a plastic cap and left to process for about 20-30 minutes, depending on your hair type and desired curl pattern.
  5. The perm solution will be rinsed out of your hair, and a neutralizing solution will be applied to set the curls.
  6. Your hair will be rinsed again, and the perm rods will be removed.
  7. Your stylist will then apply a conditioning treatment to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage.
  8. Your hair will be dried and styled as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Spiral Perms

After getting a spiral perm, it's important to follow these tips to ensure your curls stay bouncy and healthy:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a spiral perm.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your curls. If you must use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

FAQs

How long do spiral perms last?

Spiral perms can last for several months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your curls. You may need to get touch-ups every 2-3 months to maintain your curls.

Can I color my hair after getting a spiral perm?

You should wait at least two weeks after getting a spiral perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the perm process.

Can I swim with a spiral perm?

You can swim with a spiral perm, but you should avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or saltwater for extended periods. You should also rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove any chemicals or salt.

Hair Care for Spiral Perms

To keep your spiral perms looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your curl shape.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to extreme heat or cold, as this can cause frizz and dryness.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
