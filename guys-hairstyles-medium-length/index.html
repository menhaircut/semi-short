Guys Hairstyles Medium Length A Guide To Effortlessly Cool Hair In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to rock this year? Medium length hair is a versatile and popular option for guys who want to mix up their look without going too short or too long. In this guide, we'll cover how to achieve some of the coolest and most stylish guys hairstyles medium length, as well as tips on hair care and FAQs to ensure you get the perfect cut.

How to Achieve Guys Hairstyles Medium Length

The first step to achieving the perfect guys hairstyles medium length is to decide on the length you want. Generally, medium length hair falls between 2 and 4 inches in length. Some popular styles include the messy quiff, the slicked-back undercut, and the textured crop.

When getting your hair cut, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the style you want. Bring in pictures or examples to ensure you're both on the same page. If you're unsure about what style would suit you best, ask your stylist for their opinion.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Medium Length Hair

Once you've got your medium length hair cut, it's time to start styling. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect guys hairstyles medium length:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product (such as pomade or wax) to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into your desired shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair wax to hold the style in place.

Remember, the key to achieving guys hairstyles medium length is to experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Hair

Now that you've got your perfect guys hairstyles medium length, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the length in check.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid excess oil buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair (such as blow dryers or straighteners) to prevent damage.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to ensure your hair looks its best.

FAQs About Guys Hairstyles Medium Length

Here are some common questions about guys hairstyles medium length:

Q: What face shapes suit guys hairstyles medium length?
A: Guys hairstyles medium length can suit a range of face shapes, but it's important to find a style that complements your features. For example, if you have a round face, a textured crop or a messy quiff can help add definition to your face.

Q: Can guys hairstyles medium length work for curly hair?
A: Absolutely! Medium length hair can look great with curly or wavy hair. Just be sure to use products specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking defined and frizz-free.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your medium length hair looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your hair than a brush.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

There you have it – a complete guide to achieving and maintaining the coolest guys hairstyles medium length in 2023. Happy styling!
