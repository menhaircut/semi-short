Guys Hair Style Long On Top A Trendy And Low Maintenance Look For Men

Written by Luffy Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair on top is a popular trend for men's hairstyles in 2023. This style allows for versatility in length and texture on the crown while maintaining shorter sides and back for a clean and polished look. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this style, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve the Long on Top Look

To achieve the long on top look, you'll need to have at least 3-4 inches of hair on top, while keeping the sides and back shorter. You can achieve this look by visiting a hairstylist or barber and requesting a layered cut with longer hair on top. You can also use hair products to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve the long on top look:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or cream to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and separate the hair on top.
  4. Use a round brush to add more volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it into your hair, focusing on the ends to create texture and definition.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long on top hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair to avoid tugging and pulling on your hair.
  • Apply hair products sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top hairstyle:

Is the long on top style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long on top style can work for all hair types, although it may require different hair products and techniques to achieve the desired look.

Can I still have a long on top style if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still have a long on top style if you have a receding hairline. However, it's best to consult with your hairstylist or barber to determine the best length and style for your hairline.

How do I maintain my long on top hairstyle?

To maintain your long on top hairstyle, wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair. Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long on top hairstyle looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a trendy and low maintenance long on top hairstyle that will turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish. Remember to experiment with different hair products and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to consult with your hairstylist or barber for advice and guidance. Happy styling!
