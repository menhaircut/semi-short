How To Make Hair Wavy For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to achieve that effortless, beachy look with your hair? Well, you're in luck because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to make hair wavy for guys. Whether you have short or long hair, these methods will work for you. So, let's get started!

How to Make Hair Wavy: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil or dirt. Once you've washed your hair, towel dry it and comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Using heat on your hair can damage it, so it's important to use a heat protectant before you start styling. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections, depending on how thick your hair is. If you have thin hair, you can divide it into two sections. For thicker hair, you can divide it into four or more sections.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Wand

Using a curling iron or wand, wrap each section of hair around the barrel, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Hold the hair around the barrel for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 5: Repeat Until All Sections Are Curled

Repeat the previous step until all sections of your hair are curled. Make sure to curl your hair in different directions to create a more natural look.

Step 6: Use a Texturizing Spray

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Spray the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 7: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to create more texture and volume.

Step 8: Allow Your Hair to Set

Allow your hair to set for a few minutes before touching it. This will help the waves to hold.

Step 9: Optional: Add Hairspray

If you want your waves to hold for longer, you can spray some hairspray onto your hair. This will help to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Making Hair Wavy

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when making your hair wavy:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel to create loose waves.
  • Use a smaller barrel to create tighter waves.
  • Curl your hair in different directions for a more natural look.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can create a crimped look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to create more texture.
  • Allow your hair to set before touching it.
  • Use a hairspray to help your waves hold for longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will the waves last?

The length of time the waves last will depend on your hair type and how you've styled it. Generally, the waves should last for a few hours, but you can use hairspray to help them hold for longer.

Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by using a salt spray or by braiding your hair overnight.

Is it safe to use heat on my hair?

Using heat on your hair can damage it, so it's important to use a heat protectant before you start styling.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Also, try to limit the use of hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to make hair wavy for guys, you can try out these methods and see which one works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and use a heat protectant to avoid damage. Happy styling!
