How to Style Medium Length Hair No Heat: A Step by Step Guide

Are you tired of using heat styling tools on your hair? Do you want to give your hair a break from the heat and still look stylish? If yes, then this article is for you. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to style medium length hair without using any heat. So, let’s get started.

How to Style Medium Length Hair No Heat

There are many ways to style medium length hair without using any heat. Some of the most popular methods include:

  • Braids
  • Buns
  • Ponytails
  • Twists
  • Accessorizing

All of these methods are easy to do and can be done quickly without any heat. The best part is that you can use them for any occasion.

Braids

Braids are a great way to style your medium length hair without using any heat. They are easy to do and can be done in many different ways. You can do a simple braid or a more complicated one like a French braid or a Dutch braid. You can also add accessories like beads or ribbons to make it more stylish.

Buns

Buns are another great way to style your medium length hair without using any heat. You can do a messy bun or a sleek one depending on your preference. You can also add accessories like flowers or hairpins to make it look more stylish.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can do a high ponytail or a low one depending on your preference. You can also add accessories like scrunchies or bows to make it more stylish.

Twists

Twists are a great way to style your medium length hair without using any heat. You can do a simple twist or a more complicated one like a French twist or a rope twist. You can also add accessories like hairpins or flowers to make it more stylish.

Accessorizing

Accessorizing is a great way to add style to any of the above hairstyles. You can add accessories like headbands, hairpins, flowers, or scarves to make it more stylish. You can also experiment with different colors and patterns to match your outfit.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style medium length hair without using any heat:

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to do.
  3. Section your hair if necessary.
  4. Start braiding, twisting, or tying your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen.
  5. Add accessories if desired.
  6. Spray some hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

And that’s it! You now have a stylish hairstyle without using any heat.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair No Heat

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hair without using any heat:

  • Start with clean and dry hair.
  • Use hair accessories to add style to your hairstyle.
  • Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Don't overdo it with the hairspray. Too much hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How can I make my hairstyle last longer without using any heat?

A: You can use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. You can also use hair accessories like bobby pins or hairpins to secure your hairstyle.

Q: Can I style my hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without using any products. However, using hairspray can help hold your hairstyle in place for longer.

Q: Can I style my hair without using any accessories?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without using any accessories. However, adding accessories can add style to your hairstyle and make it look more fashionable.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair to prevent breakage.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Styling medium length hair without using any heat is easy and fun. With the right tools and accessories, you can create stylish hairstyles that you will love. So, try out these methods and see what works best for you.
