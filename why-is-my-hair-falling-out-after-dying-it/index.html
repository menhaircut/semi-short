Why Is My Hair Falling Out After Dying It

Written by Petter Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall after dying your hair? You are not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that many people face after a hair dye. In this article, we will discuss why hair falls out after dyeing, how to prevent hair fall, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Why Hair Fall After Dye?
  • How to Prevent Hair Fall After Dye?
  • Hair Care Tips After Dye
  • FAQ

Why Hair Fall After Dye?

Hair dye contains chemicals that can damage your hair. These chemicals can weaken your hair strands, making them prone to breakage and fall. The most common chemical found in hair dyes is ammonia. Ammonia helps to open up hair cuticles allowing the dye to penetrate deeper into the hair shaft. However, ammonia can also damage hair cuticles, making them weak and vulnerable to hair fall.

Hair dye can also cause scalp irritation, leading to hair fall. When the scalp is irritated, hair follicles become inflamed, and this can cause hair fall. Additionally, hair dye can cause allergic reactions, leading to hair fall. If you have an allergy to any of the hair dye ingredients, it can cause scalp irritation and hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall After Dye?

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall after a hair dye:

  • Choose a hair dye that is ammonia-free. Ammonia-free hair dyes are less likely to cause scalp irritation and hair fall.
  • Do a patch test before applying hair dye to your hair. A patch test will help you identify if you are allergic to any of the hair dye ingredients.
  • Don't dye your hair too often. Frequent hair dyeing can lead to hair fall.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after dyeing your hair. Deep conditioning treatments can help to restore moisture to your hair, making it less prone to breakage and hair fall.

Hair Care Tips After Dye

Here are some hair care tips to follow after dying your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfate-free shampoos are gentle on your hair, preventing hair fall.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair. Hot styling tools can damage your hair, leading to hair fall.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. A wide-tooth comb will detangle your hair without causing any breakage or hair fall.
  • Trim your hair regularly. Trimming your hair regularly will prevent split ends, making your hair less prone to breakage and hair fall.

FAQ

Q. Can hair dye cause permanent hair fall?

A. No, hair dye cannot cause permanent hair fall. Hair fall caused by hair dye is usually temporary and can be prevented by following the tips mentioned in this article.

Q. Can hair dye cause hair thinning?

A. Yes, hair dye can cause hair thinning. Hair dye can weaken your hair strands, making them thinner and prone to breakage and hair fall.

Q. Can hair dye cause baldness?

A. No, hair dye cannot cause baldness. Hair fall caused by hair dye is usually temporary and can be prevented by following the tips mentioned in this article.

Q. How long does hair fall last after dyeing?

A. Hair fall caused by hair dye is usually temporary and lasts for a few weeks. If you experience hair fall for more than a month, you should consult a dermatologist.

By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can prevent hair fall after dyeing your hair. Remember to choose a hair dye that is ammonia-free and to do a patch test before applying hair dye to your hair. Additionally, follow the hair care tips mentioned in this article to keep your hair healthy and less prone to breakage and hair fall.
