How To Look Your Best After 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our bodies and skin undergo various changes. However, it doesn't mean that we can't look our best after 60. With a few simple tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident at any age. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best ways to look your best after 60, including hair care, makeup, and skincare.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Makeup
  • Skincare
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

As we age, our hair tends to lose its shine, volume, and thickness. However, with proper hair care, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

1. Keep it short

Short haircuts are perfect for women over 60. They are easy to maintain, and they can make you look younger and more vibrant. Consider a bob, pixie, or layered haircut.

2. Add volume

Use volumizing products such as mousse or hairspray to add volume to your hair. You can also use a round brush while blow-drying your hair to add extra volume.

3. Embrace your natural color

Instead of dying your hair, embrace your natural color. Gray hair can look stunning and elegant. If you want to add some color, consider highlights or lowlights.

Makeup

Makeup can enhance your natural beauty and make you look more youthful. Here are some makeup tips for women over 60:

1. Moisturize your skin

As we age, our skin tends to become dry. Before applying makeup, make sure to moisturize your skin to create a smooth and even base.

2. Use a lightweight foundation

Heavy foundations can settle into fine lines and wrinkles, making them more visible. Instead, use a lightweight foundation or tinted moisturizer to even out your skin tone.

3. Accentuate your eyes

As we age, our eyes tend to lose their sparkle. Use a brightening concealer under your eyes to reduce dark circles and make your eyes appear brighter. You can also use eyeliner and mascara to accentuate your eyes.

Skincare

Skincare is essential to maintain healthy and youthful-looking skin. Here are some skincare tips for women over 60:

1. Use a gentle cleanser

Harsh cleansers can strip your skin of its natural oils, making it dry and dull. Use a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and makeup without drying out your skin.

2. Moisturize regularly

Moisturizing is essential to keep your skin hydrated and plump. Use a moisturizer that contains hyaluronic acid or ceramides to retain moisture in your skin.

3. Protect your skin from the sun

The sun can damage your skin and cause wrinkles and age spots. Use a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to look your best after 60:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly to stay fit and healthy
  • Wear clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape
  • Accessorize with jewelry, scarves, and hats to add style to your outfits

FAQ

1. Can I still wear bold makeup after 60?

Yes, you can still wear bold makeup after 60. However, it's best to use it sparingly and focus on one feature at a time. For example, if you're wearing bold lipstick, keep your eye makeup minimal.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

3. Should I still wear sunscreen if I don't go outside often?

Yes, you should still wear sunscreen even if you don't go outside often. UV rays can penetrate through windows, and they can cause skin damage even on cloudy days.

Conclusion

Looking your best after 60 is all about taking care of yourself and enhancing your natural beauty. By following these tips and tricks, you can feel confident and beautiful at any age. Remember to stay hydrated, eat well, exercise regularly, and take care of your hair, skin, and makeup.
