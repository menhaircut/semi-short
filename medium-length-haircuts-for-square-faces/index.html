Medium Length Haircuts For Square Faces The Best Hairstyles In 2023

Are you looking for a new haircut that flatters your square face shape? Medium length haircuts might be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we'll show you the best hairstyles for square faces, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

Description of Square Faces

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped hairline. If you have a square face, you want to choose a haircut that softens your features and creates some curves. Medium length haircuts are a great option because they can add volume and movement to your hair without overwhelming your face.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a square face, you want to focus on creating some softness and movement. Here are some medium length haircuts that work well for square faces:

  • Layered Haircuts: Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, which can create some curves around your face. Opt for long layers that start at your chin or collarbone.
  • Wavy Bob: A wavy bob is a great option for square faces because it can add volume and softness around your jawline. Keep the length around your chin or collarbone and add some loose waves for a romantic look.
  • Shoulder-Length Hair: Shoulder-length hair is a classic option for square faces because it can balance out your strong jawline. Keep the length even and add some face-framing layers to soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Haircuts

Now that you've chosen your medium length haircut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. If you want to add some waves or curls, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length haircut:

  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts for square faces:

  • Can I wear bangs with a square face? Yes, but make sure they are soft and face-framing. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are a great option for square faces.
  • What hair color works best for square faces? Any hair color can work for square faces, but highlights or balayage can add some dimension and softness to your hair.
  • What should I avoid with a square face? Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can emphasize your strong jawline. Also, avoid center parts that can make your face look even more square.

Hair Care for Medium Length Haircuts

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length haircut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, which can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and split ends.
  • Avoid tying your hair up tightly, which can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a beautiful medium length haircut that flatters your square face. Whether you opt for a layered haircut, a wavy bob, or shoulder-length hair, you can add some softness and movement to your hair with the right styling techniques and hair care routine.
