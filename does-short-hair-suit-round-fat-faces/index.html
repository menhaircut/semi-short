Does Short Hair Suit Round Fat Faces

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Short hair is one of the most versatile and convenient hairstyles for women with round and fat faces. It can help to enhance your facial features and provide an overall slimmer appearance. In this blog post, we will be discussing the question that most women with round faces ask – does short hair suit round fat faces?

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a great option for women with round and fat faces. It can help to create an illusion of slimmer facial features and enhance your natural beauty. However, it is important to choose the right style and length that complements your face shape and features.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for round and fat faces, it is important to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great option as it can help to elongate your face and create a slimmer appearance. You can also add volume on top to balance out your features.

Step by step

The following are the steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for round and fat faces:

  1. Determine your face shape and features
  2. Choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and features
  3. Consult with a hair stylist to ensure the style suits you
  4. Take proper care of your hair to maintain the style

Tips

The following are some tips to consider when choosing a short hairstyle for round and fat faces:

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face appear wider
  • Opt for layered cuts to create an illusion of length
  • Add volume on top to balance out your features
  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and features
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style

FAQ

Q: Does short hair suit round faces?
A: Yes, short hair can be a great option for women with round faces. It can help to create an illusion of length and balance out your features.

Q: Can short hair make my face appear wider?
A: It depends on the style and length of the hair. Blunt cuts can make your face appear wider, while layered cuts can create an illusion of length.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain a short hairstyle for round and fat faces. The following are some tips to consider:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for women with round and fat faces. It can help to enhance your facial features and create an overall slimmer appearance. However, it is important to choose the right style and length that complements your face shape and features. Consult with a hair stylist to find the perfect short hairstyle for you.
