How To Figure Out What Hair Type You Have

Written by William Jul 23, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to figure out what hair type you have? Don't worry; you are not alone! Many people find it challenging to determine their hair type, but it is essential to know your hair type to take proper care of it. In this article, we will guide you through the process of figuring out your hair type in relaxed English language.

Description

Hair types are classified into four categories: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each type has its unique characteristics that require different hair care routines. Knowing your hair type can help you choose the right products and styling techniques that will keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

How to

The easiest way to determine your hair type is to observe your hair's natural texture without any styling products or treatments. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with your regular shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using any styling products, such as gel, mousse, or hairspray, as they can alter your hair's natural texture.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Air Dry

After washing your hair, let it air dry naturally without using a hairdryer or towel. You want to see your hair's natural texture without any influence from external factors.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you determine your hair type:

  • Ask your hairdresser for their opinion on your hair type.
  • Take a strand of hair and stretch it. If it stretches easily and bounces back, you have healthy hair.
  • Use a magnifying glass to examine your hair's texture and shape.
  • Try different hair care products and techniques to see which ones work best for your hair.

FAQ

What is the difference between curly and coily hair?

Curly hair has loose, defined curls, while coily hair has tight, springy curls that form ringlets or coils.

Can my hair type change over time?

Yes, your hair type can change over time due to hormonal changes, medication, and aging.

Is it essential to know my hair type?

Yes, it is crucial to know your hair type to take proper care of it, choose the right products, and avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Once you determine your hair type, you can choose the right hair care routine and products that work best for your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hairdryers, straighteners, and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy, beautiful, and manageable, regardless of your hair type.
