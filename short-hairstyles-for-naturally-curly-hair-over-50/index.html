Short Hairstyles For Naturally Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Luffy Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural texture and thickness, which is why many women over 50 prefer to cut their hair short. Short hairstyles not only require less maintenance but can also make you look younger and more stylish. If you have naturally curly hair, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll share some of the best short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50.

How to Style Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a chic and elegant look. Here are some tips to help you style your short curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to damp hair.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to create defined curls.
  • Use a curling wand or iron to touch up any loose or frizzy curls.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Curly Hair

If you're considering cutting your naturally curly hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  • Start by washing and drying your hair.
  • Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  • Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut the back section of your hair to the desired length.
  • Continue cutting the side sections, taking care to blend the hair at the back.
  • Finally, cut the top section of your hair to the desired length, taking care to blend the hair at the sides.
  • Use your fingers to lift and separate your curls, making any necessary adjustments.
  • Finish with a styling product to enhance your curls and hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50?

Some of the best short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 include the pixie cut, the bob, and the curly shag.

How do I style my short curly hair without using heat styling tools?

You can style your short curly hair without using heat styling tools by applying a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to damp hair and scrunching your hair with your hands to enhance your curls.

How often should I wash my short curly hair?

You should wash your short curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent your hair from drying out.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can embrace your natural curls and look fabulous at any age. Try out these short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 and see which one works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques, and you'll have healthy and beautiful curls for years to come.
