What Hairstyle Suits Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles only to find out that they don't suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through the best hairstyles that suit round faces, along with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice. So let's get started!

  • Description
  • How To: Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, soft curves, and a width that is almost equal to the length of the face. If you have a round face, you may struggle to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face and creates the illusion of a more angular, sculpted look. In this article, we'll discuss different hairstyles that suit round faces, including long, short, curly, and straight hairstyles.

How To: Choose the Best Hairstyle for Round Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key factors to keep in mind:

  • Length: Hairstyles that add length to your face are ideal for round faces. Long hair, angled bobs, and pixie cuts are all great options.
  • Volume: To create the illusion of a more sculpted face, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head. This can be achieved with layers, curls, and waves.
  • Layers: Layered haircuts can add depth and dimension to a round face. Opt for long layers that start below the chin.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help to balance out a round face, but be careful not to choose a style that is too blunt or heavy. Wispy, side-swept bangs are a great option.

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face, long hairstyles can be a great option. Long hair can help to elongate your face and create a more angular look. Here are some long hairstyles to consider:

  • Layered: Long layers that start below the chin can help to add depth and dimension to a round face. This style works well with both straight and curly hair.
  • Straight: Straight, sleek hair can be a great option for round faces. To add volume and length, consider adding layers or subtle waves.
  • Curls: Soft curls or beachy waves can help to add volume and dimension to a round face. To avoid adding too much width, keep the curls loose and relaxed.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Short hair can also be a great option for round faces, as long as the cut is done correctly. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Pixie Cut: A pixie cut can help to elongate a round face and create a more angular look. Consider adding layers and texture to avoid a flat, one-dimensional look.
  • Angled Bob: An angled bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front can help to create the illusion of a more sculpted face.
  • Layered Bob: A layered bob with long layers that start below the chin can help to add depth and dimension to a round face.

Curly Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have curly hair and a round face, there are plenty of hairstyles that can work for you. Here are some curly hairstyles to consider:

  • Long Layers: Long layers can help to elongate a round face and add depth to curly hair. Consider adding subtle highlights to add even more dimension.
  • Curly Bob: A curly bob that hits just above the shoulders can help to create a more sculpted look for round faces.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Wispy, side-swept bangs can help to balance out a round face and add definition to curly hair.

Step-by-Step: How to Style Your Hair for a Round Face

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for round faces, it's time to learn how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. If you're using a curling iron or straightener, start by sectioning your hair. Work in small sections to ensure that each strand is properly styled.
  3. If you're curling your hair, wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron and hold for 5-10 seconds. Release the curl and let it cool before moving on to the next section.
  4. If you're straightening your hair, start at the roots and slowly work your way down to the ends. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  5. If you're adding volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots. Use a round brush to lift the hair and create volume.
  6. Once you've styled your hair, finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hairstyle looking great:

  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can help to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Don't over-wash your hair: Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different styles to see what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a round face. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face and creates the illusion of a more sculpted look.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or heavy bangs. These styles can make your face appear wider and rounder.

Q: Should I choose a hairstyle based on my age?

A: No, you should choose a hairstyle based on what suits your face shape and personal style, regardless of your age.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Round Face Hairstyle Looking Great

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for round faces, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner: Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid hot tools: Limit your use of hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A healthy diet can help to keep your hair looking great from the inside out.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and skin hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and keep it
