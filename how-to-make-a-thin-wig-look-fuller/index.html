How To Make A Thin Wig Look Fuller

Written by Oliver Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're wearing a wig, you may have noticed that it can look thin and unnatural due to its lack of volume. However, there are many ways to make a thin wig look fuller and more natural. In this post, we will discuss some of the best ways to achieve a fuller look with your wig.

Description

A thin wig can be a source of embarrassment, especially if it looks unnatural on your head. It can be challenging to style and may not hold up well under certain conditions. However, there are ways to make your wig look fuller and more natural, giving you more confidence and style options.

How to Make a Thin Wig Look Fuller

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add more body and fullness to your wig. These products are specially formulated to add volume to your hair, and they can work wonders on your wig as well.

2. Blow Dry Your Wig

Blow drying your wig is an excellent way to add volume and fullness. When blow drying, focus on the roots of the wig, as this is where you want to add the most volume. Use a round brush to lift the hair and create a fuller look.

3. Use a Root Lifter

A root lifter can also help add volume and fullness to your wig. Apply the root lifter to the roots of your wig and blow dry using a round brush to create a lifted, voluminous look.

4. Use Hair Extensions

Another way to add volume to your wig is by using hair extensions. Clip-in extensions can be added to the sides and back of your wig to create a fuller, more natural look.

5. Tease Your Wig

Teasing your wig can also help create a fuller, more voluminous look. Take small sections of hair and backcomb or tease them at the roots using a fine-toothed comb. This will create more volume and lift.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

Start by washing your wig with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Root Lifter

Apply a root lifter to the roots of your wig, focusing on the areas where you want to add the most volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Wig

Blow dry your wig using a round brush to lift the hair and create more volume. Focus on the roots and use a back and forth motion to create more lift.

Step 4: Tease Your Wig

Tease your wig using a fine-toothed comb. Take small sections of hair and backcomb at the roots to create more volume and lift.

Tips

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your wig gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Store your wig on a mannequin head to help maintain its shape and fullness.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQ

Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

It is best to use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner, as these are formulated to be gentle on wig fibers.

How often should I wash my wig?

It is recommended to wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Can I use hair products on my wig?

You can use hair products on your wig, but be sure to use products that are specifically formulated for wigs. Avoid using heavy or greasy products that can weigh down your wig.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the fullness and volume of your wig. Be sure to store your wig properly when not in use, and avoid exposing it to excessive heat or sunlight. Regular washing and conditioning will also help keep your wig looking its best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thin wig look fuller and more natural. Whether you're wearing your wig for fashion or medical reasons, a full and voluminous look can help boost your confidence and style.
