Face Shapes That Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In recent years, curtain bangs have become one of the most popular hair trends. They offer a versatile and effortless look that can suit any face shape. However, not all face shapes may look good with curtain bangs. In this blog post, we will discuss the face shapes that look good with curtain bangs and how to style them.

Table of Contents

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of hair bangs that are parted down the middle, resembling a curtain. They are longer than regular bangs and can be swept to the sides or left to frame the face. Curtain bangs work well for those who want a low-maintenance and effortless hairstyle that can be styled in many ways.

Face Shapes That Look Good with Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs can work well for most face shapes, but they are especially flattering for those with oval, heart, or square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid curtain bangs, as they can make your face appear wider. However, if you still want to try curtain bangs, make sure they are longer and swept to the sides instead of straight across.

For those with oval-shaped faces, curtain bangs can help to frame the face and add softness. Heart-shaped faces can benefit from curtain bangs, as they can balance out the forehead and narrow chin. Square-shaped faces can look great with curtain bangs that are swept to the sides, as they can soften the jawline.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Styling curtain bangs is easy, and there are many ways to wear them. You can sweep them to the side, tuck them behind your ears, or let them hang down the middle. If you want a more polished look, you can blow-dry them with a round brush or use a straightener to smooth them out.

To style curtain bangs, start with clean, dry hair. Part your hair down the middle, and clip the rest of your hair away. Take your bangs and divide them into two sections. Twist each section away from your face, and use a blow dryer to dry them. Finish off by using a round brush to give your bangs a soft curve or use a straightener to straighten them.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, you should trim them every four to six weeks. This will prevent them from getting too long and covering your eyes. You can also use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent them from getting oily.

When washing your hair, make sure to avoid getting shampoo on your bangs. This can cause them to become greasy and unmanageable. Instead, wash your bangs separately or use a shower cap to protect them from getting wet.

FAQs about Curtain Bangs

1. How long should curtain bangs be?

Curtain bangs should be longer than regular bangs, as they are meant to be swept to the sides. They should be at least chin-length or longer, depending on your face shape and hair length.

2. Can I still wear a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with curtain bangs. You can either leave your bangs out or tuck them behind your ears.

3. Can I style curtain bangs with a curling iron?

Yes, you can style curtain bangs with a curling iron. Use a small barrel curling iron to create soft waves, or use a straightener to create a sleek and smooth look.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, you should wash them every two to three days. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent them from becoming dry or damaged. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.

When blow-drying your hair, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. You should also avoid using hot tools on your bangs every day, as this can cause them to become dry and brittle. Instead, try to air-dry your hair as much as possible.

Overall, curtain bangs can be a great hairstyle for those who want a low-maintenance and effortless look. They can work well for most face shapes, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your face. With the right care and styling, you can keep your curtain bangs looking their best.
