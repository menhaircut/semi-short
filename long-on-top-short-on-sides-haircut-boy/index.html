Long On Top Short On Sides Haircut Boy The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your young boy, the long on top short on sides haircut is a great option. This versatile haircut has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among boys of all ages. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the long on top short on sides haircut boy, how to achieve it, and how to care for it.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut boy is a classic hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides. This haircut is typically achieved using clippers or scissors to create a gradual fade from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides. The long top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or even spiked. This haircut is perfect for boys who want a stylish and trendy look.

How to

To achieve the long on top short on sides haircut boy, you'll need to follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your child's hair to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  2. Using clippers or scissors, cut the hair on the sides and back of the head to the desired length. This length can vary depending on the specific style you're going for, but usually ranges from a #1 to a #4 guard.
  3. Blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides using clippers or scissors. This will create a gradual fade from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides.
  4. Cut the hair on top of the head to the desired length. This can vary depending on the specific style you're going for, but usually ranges from 2 to 4 inches.
  5. Style the hair on top using your preferred styling products.

Step by step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your child's hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup. Towel dry the hair until it's damp.
  2. Using clippers, cut the hair on the sides and back of the head to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to create a gradual fade. Make sure to blend the hair around the ears and neckline to avoid any harsh lines.
  3. Switch to scissors and blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides. Start by cutting the hair on top to the desired length, and then gradually blend the hair on the sides using scissors.
  4. Style the hair on top using your preferred styling products. For a slicked back look, apply a small amount of pomade or gel to the hair and comb it back. For a messy or textured look, use a matte paste or clay and use your fingers to style the hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut boy:

  • Choose a length that works well with your child's hair type and face shape.
  • Be patient and take your time when blending the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides.
  • Use a good quality pair of clippers and scissors to achieve a clean and precise cut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect look for your child.
  • Visit a professional stylist if you're unsure about cutting your child's hair yourself.

FAQ

What face shapes does the long on top short on sides haircut boy work well with?

This haircut works well with a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. It can help to balance out a round face by adding height to the top of the head.

How often should I cut my child's hair to maintain the long on top short on sides haircut?

It's recommended to get your child's hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Can this haircut work for curly or wavy hair?

Yes, this haircut can work well for curly or wavy hair. Just make sure to choose a length that works well with your child's natural texture.

Hair Care

Maintaining the long on top short on sides haircut is easy with a few simple steps:

  • Wash your child's hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

With these tips and tricks, your child's long on top short on sides haircut will look great and stay healthy for weeks to come!
