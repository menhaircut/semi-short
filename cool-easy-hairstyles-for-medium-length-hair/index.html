Cool Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Medium length hair is perfect for those who want the best of both worlds – not too long, not too short. But sometimes, it can be challenging to come up with new hairstyles that suit your length. In this article, we'll show you some cool and easy hairstyles that you can try on your medium length hair. Whether you're going to school, work, or a party, these hairstyles will surely make you stand out.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. From messy buns to sleek ponytails, there are plenty of hairstyles to choose from. However, we understand that it can be challenging to come up with new hairstyles every day. That's why we've curated some cool and easy hairstyles that you can try on your medium length hair.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are some things you need to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your hair is dry before styling.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories such as hair ties, bobby pins, and hair clips.

Step by Step

1. Top Knot

The top knot is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for those lazy days. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some hair strands to create a messy look.

This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out with friends.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a popular choice among medium-length hair owners. Here's how you can achieve this look:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie.
  2. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume.
  3. Secure the ponytail with bobby pins.

This hairstyle is perfect for a date night or a formal event.

3. Side Braid

The side braid is a chic hairstyle that is easy to achieve. Follow these steps:

  1. Take a section of hair from one side of your head.
  2. Start braiding the section of hair.
  3. Secure the braid with a hair tie.
  4. Pull out some hair strands to create a messy look.

This hairstyle is perfect for a day out with friends or a casual dinner.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, scarves, and hair clips.
  • Don't be afraid to try out new hairstyles.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to avoid hair breakage.

FAQ

1. Can I curl my medium length hair?

Yes, you can. Use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose curls or beachy waves.

2. How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

3. How can I make my medium length hair look thicker?

You can use volumizing products such as mousse or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair. You can also try teasing your hair at the crown to create volume.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.

With these cool and easy hairstyles for medium length hair, you're sure to turn heads wherever you go. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and don't forget to take care of your hair.
