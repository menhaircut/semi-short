Why White Hair Is Called Grey

Written by Oliver Oct 22, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’ve ever wondered why white hair is called grey, you’re not alone. Many people use the terms white and grey interchangeably when it comes to describing hair color. However, there is a reason why white hair is often called grey. In this blog post, we’ll explore why that is and provide some tips on how to care for white or grey hair.

What Causes Hair to Turn Grey?

Before we get into why white hair is called grey, let’s first understand what causes hair to turn grey. As we age, our hair follicles produce less melanin, which is the pigment that gives our hair its color. This causes our hair to gradually lose its color and turn grey or white. However, genetics and lifestyle factors can also play a role in when and how quickly our hair turns grey.

Why is White Hair Called Grey?

So, why is white hair called grey? The answer lies in the way light reflects off of hair. When hair has a lot of melanin, it reflects light in a way that appears darker. As hair loses melanin and turns grey or white, it reflects light differently and can appear to have a grey or silver hue. This is why many people refer to white hair as grey, even though technically it doesn’t have any color at all.

How to Care for White or Grey Hair

If you have white or grey hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

White and grey hair can be more delicate and prone to damage than other hair colors, so it’s important to use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip away natural oils. Look for a shampoo that’s specifically designed for white or grey hair.

Condition Regularly

Conditioner can help keep white or grey hair soft and shiny. Use a conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type and apply it after shampooing.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage fragile white or grey hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Just like skin, hair can be damaged by the sun’s UV rays. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQ

What’s the Difference Between Grey and White Hair?

Technically, there is no difference between grey and white hair. Both colors are the result of a loss of melanin in the hair follicle. However, some people use the term grey to describe hair that has a more silver or ashy hue, while others use the term white to describe hair that has lost all of its pigment.

Can Stress Cause Grey Hair?

There is some evidence to suggest that stress can cause hair to turn grey prematurely. However, more research is needed to fully understand the link between stress and grey hair.

Conclusion

Now you know why white hair is called grey and how to care for white or grey hair. Remember to use a gentle shampoo, condition regularly, avoid heat styling, and protect your hair from the sun. With proper care, your white or grey hair can look healthy and beautiful.
