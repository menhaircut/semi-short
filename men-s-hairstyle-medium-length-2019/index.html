Men s Hairstyle Medium Length 2019

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect men’s hairstyle medium length 2019? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll talk about the latest trends in medium length hairstyles for men. From how to style your hair to tips on hair care, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Medium length hair is the perfect balance between short and long hair. It gives you enough length to play with different styles, while still being easy to manage. In 2019, the trend for men’s hairstyles is all about texture, volume, and natural-looking hair. The key is to embrace your hair’s natural texture and work with it, rather than against it.

How to

The first step in achieving the perfect men’s hairstyle medium length 2019 is to find the right haircut for you. The most popular styles for medium length hair are the classic side part, messy textured hair, and the slicked back look. Talk to your stylist about which style will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Once you have the right haircut, it’s time to style your hair. For a textured, messy look, apply a small amount of texturizing product, like sea salt spray, to your damp hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, shaping it as you go. For a slicked back look, use a pomade or gel and a comb to smooth your hair back.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.

2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp.

3. Apply a small amount of texturizing product or pomade to your hair.

4. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired look.

5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

One of the best tips for men’s hairstyle medium length 2019 is to use the right products for your hair type. If you have thin hair, use a lightweight product that won’t weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, use a stronger product that will hold your hair in place.

Another tip is to use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a round brush to lift the roots of your hair while blow-drying it. This will give your hair more volume and make it easier to style.

FAQ

What is the most popular men's hairstyle for medium length hair in 2019?

The most popular men’s hairstyle for medium length hair in 2019 is the messy textured look. This style is all about embracing your hair’s natural texture and adding volume and movement to it.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every few days.

What type of product should I use for a slicked back look?

You should use a pomade or gel for a slicked back look. These products will hold your hair in place and give it a shiny finish.

Hair Care

Hair care is just as important as styling when it comes to men’s hairstyle medium length 2019. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Avoid using hot tools, like a hair straightener or curling iron, too often.

4. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Conclusion

Men’s hairstyle medium length 2019 is all about embracing your natural texture and adding volume and movement to your hair. Whether you prefer a messy textured look or a slicked back style, the key is to find the right haircut for your hair type and face shape. Use the right products and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With these tips, you’ll be sure to have the perfect men’s hairstyle medium length 2019.
