How To Style Asymmetrical Pixie Bob

Written by Luffy Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the asymmetrical pixie bob! This bold haircut combines the short length of a pixie cut with the angled lines of a bob, creating a unique and eye-catching look. In this article, we'll show you how to style your asymmetrical pixie bob for maximum impact.

Description

The asymmetrical pixie bob is a modern haircut that features short hair on one side of the head and longer hair on the other. The hair is typically cut at an angle, with the longer side falling just above the chin and the shorter side barely grazing the ear. This creates a dramatic and dynamic look that is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair.

How to Style Asymmetrical Pixie Bob

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and leave your hair feeling soft and silky.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Be careful not to rub your hair too vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, gel, or wax, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the longer side of your asymmetrical bob.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair on a low to medium heat setting. Use a brush to smooth out any tangles and create volume and movement in your hair.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to style it as desired. You can create loose waves, sleek and straight strands, or voluminous curls, depending on your mood and the occasion.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your asymmetrical pixie bob:

  • Invest in a high-quality styling product to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to play with color! An asymmetrical bob can look great with highlights or ombre coloring.

FAQ

Q: Will an asymmetrical pixie bob work for my face shape?

A: The great thing about the asymmetrical pixie bob is that it can work for a variety of face shapes, including oval, heart, and square. Talk to your stylist about how to customize the cut to flatter your features.

Q: How often should I get my asymmetrical pixie bob trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your asymmetrical bob.

Hair Care

To keep your asymmetrical pixie bob looking its best, be sure to take good care of your hair. This means washing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and products that can damage your hair. With a little TLC, your asymmetrical pixie bob will stay looking fierce and fabulous!
