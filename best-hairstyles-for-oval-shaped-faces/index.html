Best Hairstyles For Oval Shaped Faces

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with an oval-shaped face, struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that accentuates your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oval shaped faces, along with tips and tricks to maintain them.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Oval shaped faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as they have balanced features and can pull off various hairstyles. However, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and does not overpower them.

How To:

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for an oval-shaped face is to determine whether you have a long or short forehead. If you have a long forehead, you can opt for bangs that cover your forehead and make it appear shorter. If you have a short forehead, you can go for a center part or side-swept bangs.

Step By Step:

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval shaped faces:

1. Long Layers:

Long layers are perfect for oval shaped faces as they add movement and dimension to your hair. They also help in framing your face and accentuating your features. You can opt for a middle part or a side part, depending on your preference.

2. Bob:

A classic bob is a great option for oval shaped faces. It adds volume to your hair and frames your face beautifully. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob, depending on your face shape and preference.

3. Pixie Cut:

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut is a great choice for oval shaped faces. It accentuates your cheekbones and jawline and adds a touch of edginess to your look.

4. Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval shaped faces as they add volume to your hair and make it appear fuller. They also help in balancing out your features and creating a more symmetrical look.

5. Beach Waves:

Beach waves are perfect for oval shaped faces as they add texture and volume to your hair. They also give a relaxed, effortless vibe to your look. You can achieve beach waves by using a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oval-shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear too long or too short.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your features and does not overpower them.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape.

FAQ:

Q: Can oval shaped faces pull off any hairstyle?

A: Oval shaped faces are considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off various hairstyles. However, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and does not overpower them.

Q: Can oval shaped faces have bangs?

A: Yes, oval shaped faces can have bangs. If you have a long forehead, you can opt for bangs that cover your forehead and make it appear shorter. If you have a short forehead, you can go for a center part or side-swept bangs.

Hair Care:

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to ensure that your hairstyle looks its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, choosing the best hairstyle for an oval-shaped face can be a daunting task. However, by following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can find the perfect hairstyle that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.
