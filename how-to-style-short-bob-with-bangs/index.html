How To Style Short Bob With Bangs

If you have recently chopped your hair into a short bob with bangs, you may be wondering how to style it. This trendy haircut can be versatile and easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style short bob with bangs, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Short Bob with Bangs

Styling a short bob with bangs can be easy and fun. You can try different looks depending on the occasion, your mood, and your hair type. Here are some popular ways to style short bob with bangs:

  • Straight and Sleek
  • Wavy and Tousled
  • Curly and Bouncy
  • Pinned Back
  • Half-Up Half-Down

Straight and Sleek

To achieve a straight and sleek look, you will need a flat iron, a heat protectant, and a smoothing serum. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and start straightening it with a flat iron.
  4. Apply a smoothing serum to tame any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Wavy and Tousled

To achieve a wavy and tousled look, you will need a curling iron, a texturizing spray, and a sea salt spray. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and start curling it with a curling iron.
  4. Apply a sea salt spray to give your hair a beachy texture.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a tousled look.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Curly and Bouncy

To achieve a curly and bouncy look, you will need a diffuser, a curl-enhancing product, and a lightweight hair oil. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and start drying your hair on low heat.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to encourage the curls.
  5. Apply a lightweight hair oil to add shine and definition.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Pinned Back

To achieve a pinned back look, you will need bobby pins, a teasing comb, and a hairspray. Follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair on the side.
  2. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front and twist it to the back.
  4. Secure the twisted section with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

To achieve a half-up half-down look, you will need bobby pins, a teasing comb, and a hairspray. Follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair on the side.
  2. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.
  3. Take a section of hair from each side and bring it to the back.
  4. Twist the sections and secure them with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a general step-by-step guide on how to style short bob with bangs:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant, texturizing spray, or curl-enhancing product depending on your desired look.
  3. If you want to straighten your hair, use a flat iron. If you want to curl your hair, use a curling iron or a diffuser.
  4. Apply a serum, oil, or spray to add shine and definition.
  5. Style your bangs with a round brush or a straightener.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Bob with Bangs

Here are some tips for styling short bob with bangs:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Use a round brush to style your bangs for a smooth and polished look.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume and lift at the roots.
  • Don't be afraid to try different parting styles such as middle part or deep side part.
  • Accessorize with headbands, hair clips, or scarves to add a pop of color or texture.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my short bob with bangs?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you have dry hair or don't exercise much, you can wash your hair every other day or every three days.

2. Can I style my short bob with bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your short bob with bangs without heat. You can air dry your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a natural wave. You can also use a headband or hair clips to create a cute and easy updo.

3. What should I do if my bangs are too long or too short?

If your bangs are too long, you can trim them with a pair of scissors or visit a hairstylist for a professional haircut. If your bangs are too short, you can use bobby pins or hair clips to pin them back until they grow out.

Hair Care for Short Bob with Bangs

To maintain a healthy and shiny short bob with bangs, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these styling tips, you can rock your short bob with bangs with confidence and ease. Remember to have fun and try new looks to express your personality and style.
