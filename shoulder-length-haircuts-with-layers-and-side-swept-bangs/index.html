Shoulder Length Haircuts With Layers And Side Swept Bangs The Perfect Style For 2023

Written by Aprilia Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a chic and trendy hairstyle that doesn't require too much maintenance? Look no further than a shoulder length haircut with layers and side swept bangs! This style has been a go-to for many women for years, and it's not going anywhere in 2023. In this article, we'll talk about how to achieve this look, offer some tips and tricks, and provide answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Shoulder length haircuts with layers and side swept bangs are a versatile and low-maintenance option for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair without sacrificing length. The layers add movement and dimension, while the side swept bangs frame the face and add a touch of sophistication. This style works well for all face shapes and hair textures, making it a popular choice for many women.

How to

To achieve this look, start by consulting with your hairstylist to determine the best length for your face shape and hair texture. The stylist will then use a combination of layering techniques to create volume and movement throughout the hair. The side swept bangs can be customized to your preference - whether you want them long and wispy or short and blunt.

To style your hair at home, first apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Your stylist will use layering techniques to create volume and movement throughout the hair.

3. Customize your side swept bangs to your preference.

4. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

5. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to your hair.

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

3. If you have naturally curly hair, consider getting a keratin treatment to help tame frizz and make styling easier.

4. Use a texturizing spray to add even more movement and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will this style work for all face shapes?

A: Yes! This style is versatile and works well for all face shapes.

Q: How often should I get a trim to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Absolutely! This style works well both down and up, making it a great option for any occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to use quality hair care products and practice good hair care habits. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help protect your hair from damage.

2. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

5. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush.

Conclusion

Shoulder length haircuts with layers and side swept bangs are a versatile and low-maintenance style that works well for all face shapes and hair textures. Whether you want to add volume and movement to your hair or simply switch up your look, this style is sure to impress. Follow our tips and tricks for maintaining this style and your hair will look beautiful and healthy all year long!
