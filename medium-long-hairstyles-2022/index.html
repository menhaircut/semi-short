Medium Long Hairstyles 2022 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Looking for inspiration for your next hairdo? You've come to the right place. Medium long hairstyles are set to be a huge trend in 2022, and we've got all the info you need to get on board with this stylish look. From how to achieve the perfect cut to the best products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, we've got you covered.

Description

Medium long hairstyles are a versatile and flattering option for anyone looking to update their look in 2022. This style typically falls just above or below the shoulders, and can be worn with or without layers. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there's a medium long style that will work for you.

How to

If you're thinking about trying a medium long hairstyle, the first step is to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Bring in pictures of styles you like, and be open to suggestions from your stylist. Once you've got the perfect cut, you'll need to learn how to style your hair to keep it looking great.

Step by step

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and smoothness.

3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair, depending on the style you're going for.

4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place all day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect medium long hairstyle:

- Invest in a quality hairbrush to help you detangle your hair without causing damage.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.

- Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up in a ponytail with a medium long hairstyle?

A: Yes! Medium long hairstyles are versatile enough to be worn up or down, depending on your mood and the occasion.

Q: Will a medium long hairstyle work for my face shape?

A: Yes! Medium long hairstyles can be customized to flatter any face shape, whether you have a round, square, or oval face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is key to keeping your medium long hairstyle looking great. Here are a few tips to help you care for your hair:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to reduce damage.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a medium long hairstyle in 2022. Who knows, this might be the perfect look for you!
