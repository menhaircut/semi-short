How to Get Different Hair in Animal Crossing

Getting different hair in Animal Crossing is a relatively straightforward process. Here's what you need to do:

Step by Step

  1. Visit Harriet's salon in Shampoodle.
  2. Talk to Harriet and choose "Makeover."
  3. Select "Change Hair Style."
  4. Choose the hairstyle you want from the options available.
  5. Select "Yes" when Harriet asks if you're sure.
  6. Wait for Harriet to work her magic.
  7. Pay the 3,000 Bell fee.
  8. Admire your new 'do!

It's important to note that you can only change your hair once per day, so make sure you're happy with your choice before committing.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing:

  • Make sure you have enough Bells to pay for the service.
  • Consider trying on different hairstyles in the mirror before committing.
  • Remember that you can only change your hair once per day.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment - you can always change your hair again tomorrow!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing hair in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hair color?

Yes! You can change your hair color at Harriet's salon using the same process as changing your hairstyle.

Can I change my hair more than once per day?

No, you can only change your hair once per day.

How much does it cost to change my hair?

It costs 3,000 Bells to change your hair at Harriet's salon.

Hair Care

Once you've changed your hair in Animal Crossing, you'll want to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a comb or brush to keep your hair looking neat.
  • Wear a hat or headband to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Visit Harriet's salon regularly to keep your hair looking fresh.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to get different hair in Animal Crossing and keep it looking great! Happy styling!
