Best Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Christine Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair goes through changes in texture and density, making it important to choose a haircut that flatters our face shape and works well with our natural hair type. If you have thick wavy hair over 50 and are looking for a new haircut, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll explore the best haircut options for thick wavy hair over 50, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair. Let's get started!

Description

Thick wavy hair over 50 can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with, and on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. The key to a great haircut for thick wavy hair over 50 is to find a style that works with your natural texture and enhances your best features. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair Over 50

When choosing a haircut for thick wavy hair over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. A good haircut should flatter your face shape and balance any asymmetry. Second, think about your hair texture. Thick wavy hair can be heavy and bulky, so it's important to choose a haircut that thins out the hair and adds movement. Finally, consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair each day. A low-maintenance haircut may be best if you're short on time.

Step by Step Guide to the Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair Over 50

1. Short Haircut

A short haircut can be a great option for thick wavy hair over 50. A pixie cut, for example, can add volume to the hair and frame the face. To style, apply a small amount of mousse to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz, and finish with a light hairspray.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for thick wavy hair over 50. A chin-length bob can add movement and texture to the hair, while a longer bob can create a more sophisticated look. To style, apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Let air dry or diffuse with a hair dryer on low heat.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can add dimension and volume to thick wavy hair over 50. Ask your stylist for long layers that frame the face and thin out the bulk of the hair. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush. Use a curling iron to create loose waves, and finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to maintain healthy hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated

FAQ

1. What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair over 50?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair over 50 depends on your face shape and personal style. Some great options include short haircuts like pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and layered haircuts.

2. How do I style thick wavy hair over 50?

To style thick wavy hair over 50, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Let air dry or diffuse with a hair dryer on low heat. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or smooth out any frizz.

3. How can I maintain healthy hair?

To maintain healthy hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, get regular trims, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Now that you know the best haircut options for thick wavy hair over 50, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a haircut, and don't forget to maintain healthy hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments. With the right haircut and styling tips, you can rock your thick wavy hair over 50 with confidence!
