How To Match Makeup To Your Skin Tone

Written by Petter Nov 13, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to applying makeup, one of the most important things to keep in mind is your skin tone. Matching your makeup to your skin tone can make all the difference in achieving a flawless finish. In this article, we’ll be discussing how to match makeup to your skin tone and provide some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect shades for your complexion.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in matching makeup to your skin tone is determining your skin tone. There are three main categories: cool, warm, and neutral. A cool skin tone has pink or blue undertones, a warm skin tone has yellow or golden undertones, and a neutral skin tone has a mix of both.

One easy way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear blue, you likely have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you likely have a warm skin tone. If they appear somewhere in between, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Matching Foundation to Your Skin Tone

Foundation is the base of your makeup look, so it’s important to find the right shade for your skin tone. Look for foundations with descriptors like “warm”, “cool”, or “neutral” to help you find the right shade. You can also test the foundation on your jawline to see if it blends seamlessly into your skin.

Be sure to also consider the finish of the foundation you choose. If you have dry skin, a dewy finish can help add moisture and glow to your complexion. If you have oily skin, a matte finish can help control excess shine.

Choosing the Right Blush

Blush can help add a pop of color and dimension to your face, but it’s important to choose the right shade for your skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, look for blushes with pink or berry undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, look for blushes with peach or coral undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

When applying blush, be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines or patches of color.

Finding the Perfect Lipstick

Lipstick is a fun way to play with color and add personality to your makeup look. When choosing a lipstick shade, look for colors that complement your skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, look for lipsticks with blue or pink undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, look for lipsticks with orange or red undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

Don’t be afraid to try bold colors or experiment with different finishes, like matte or glossy.

Tips and Tricks for Matching Makeup to Your Skin Tone

  • When in doubt, opt for shades with neutral undertones.
  • Use color-correcting products to help neutralize any redness or discoloration in your skin.
  • Consider your hair and eye color when choosing makeup shades.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help at makeup counters or from beauty professionals.
  • Always test makeup in natural light to get the most accurate representation of the shade.

FAQ

How do I know if a foundation is the right shade for me?

Test the foundation on your jawline and blend it in. If it disappears into your skin seamlessly, it’s likely the right shade for you.

Can I wear cool and warm tones together?

Yes! Mixing cool and warm tones can create a beautiful, dimensional makeup look.

What if I can’t find a lipstick shade that works for my skin tone?

Don’t be afraid to mix and match different lipstick shades to create a custom color that works for you.

Hair Care Tips for Your Skin Tone

Just like makeup, hair color can also be matched to your skin tone. Cool skin tones can look great with ash or platinum blonde hair, while warm skin tones can look great with honey or caramel highlights. Neutral skin tones can experiment with both cool and warm hair colors.

When it comes to choosing a hair color, it’s important to consider your natural hair color and maintenance preferences. Consult with a professional stylist to help you find the perfect shade for your skin tone and lifestyle.
