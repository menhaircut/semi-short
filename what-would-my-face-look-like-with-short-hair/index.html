What Would My Face Look Like With Short Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short but wondering what it would do to your face? As we enter 2023, short hair is becoming more and more popular, but it can be intimidating to make such a big change. In this guide, we'll explore what a short hairstyle might do for your face shape, how to choose the right cut, and tips for caring for your new 'do.

Description: What Happens To Your Face When You Cut Your Hair Short?

When you cut your hair short, it can change your entire look. Your face shape may become more pronounced, and your features may stand out more. Short hair can also make your neck appear longer and create a sleek, modern silhouette. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

How To Choose The Right Short Haircut

When choosing a short haircut, consider your face shape and features. A pixie cut can work well on oval or heart-shaped faces, while a lob (long bob) can flatter square or round faces. If you have a prominent nose or chin, a side-parted bob can draw attention to your eyes and balance out your features. It's also important to consider your hair texture and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day.

Step By Step Guide: Finding The Right Short Haircut

1. Determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and note whether your face is oval, round, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped.

2. Research short hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and save pictures of styles you like.

3. Consider your hair texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, a short cut may require more styling time to maintain. If you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and texture.

4. Consult with a stylist. A professional hair stylist can help you choose a cut that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type. They can also recommend products and styling techniques to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Tips For Caring For Short Hair

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires regular upkeep to look its best. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and stylish:

  • Get regular trims. Short haircuts can lose their shape quickly, so it's important to get regular trims to maintain your style.
  • Use the right products. Choose products that are designed for your hair type and style, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with styling. Short hair can be versatile, so don't be afraid to try new styling techniques, like adding waves or curls.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools, and avoid using high heat settings.

FAQ: Common Questions About Short Haircuts

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the cut. A long bob or pixie cut can actually make your face appear slimmer, while a blunt bob may emphasize a round face. Consult with a stylist to find the right cut for your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including braids, buns, and ponytails. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut with a short style?

A: Short haircuts typically require more frequent trims than longer styles. Depending on your hair growth rate and the style of your cut, you may need to get a trim every 4-8 weeks.

Hair Care: Taking Care Of Your Short Hair

Short hair can be a fun and stylish change, but it requires some extra care. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type, and avoid over-washing, which can strip your hair of natural oils. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Protect your hair from sun and chlorine damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner. With the right care, your short hair can look and feel healthy and beautiful.

So there you have it - a guide to what your face might look like with short hair, how to choose the right cut, and tips for caring for your new style. Whether you're considering a pixie cut or a bob, take the time to find a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. And most importantly, have fun with your new look!
