How To Grow Very Short Hair Fast A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who wants to grow out their very short hair, you may feel like it's an impossible task. However, there are ways to speed up the process and get the long locks you desire. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know to grow out your very short hair fast.

Description

Before we dive into the nitty-gritty of how to grow very short hair fast, let's clarify what we mean by "very short hair." Typically, very short hair is considered to be hair that is less than an inch long. If your hair is only a few inches long but you're still struggling to grow it out, fear not - the tips in this guide will work for you, too!

How to Grow Very Short Hair Fast

1. Get Regular Trims

While it may sound counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. When you get a trim, you're getting rid of split ends and damage that can prevent your hair from growing. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use Hair Growth Products

There are a variety of hair growth products on the market that can help speed up the growth process. Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, and other ingredients that promote hair growth. Additionally, consider taking a daily multivitamin to ensure your body has all the necessary nutrients for hair growth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and prevent it from growing. If you're trying to grow out your very short hair fast, avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature as low as possible.

4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help stimulate hair growth by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

5. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet plays a big role in the health of your hair. Make sure you're eating a diet rich in protein, healthy fats, and vitamins and minerals like vitamin D, iron, and zinc.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Get Regular Trims

Schedule a trim with your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends and damage that can prevent your hair from growing.

Step 2: Use Hair Growth Products

Look for hair growth products that contain biotin, keratin, and other ingredients that promote hair growth. Consider taking a daily multivitamin as well.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature as low as possible.

Step 4: Massage Your Scalp

Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day to increase blood flow to the hair follicles and stimulate hair growth.

Step 5: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a diet rich in protein, healthy fats, and vitamins and minerals like vitamin D, iron, and zinc to promote hair growth.

Tips

- Be patient! Growing out very short hair takes time, so don't get discouraged if you don't see results right away.

- Consider switching to a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and damage to your hair while you sleep.

- Don't wash your hair every day - aim to wash it every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of natural oils.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow out very short hair?

The length of time it takes to grow out very short hair varies from person to person, but it typically takes several months to a year to grow out hair that is less than an inch long.

Can I use hair extensions to make my hair appear longer while I'm growing it out?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to make your hair appear longer while you're growing it out. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions and have them installed by a professional.

Hair Care

While you're growing out your very short hair, it's important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following the tips in this guide and taking good care of your hair, you can grow out your very short hair fast and achieve the long locks you desire.
