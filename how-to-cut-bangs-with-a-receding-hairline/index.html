How To Cut Bangs With A Receding Hairline A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with a receding hairline, but still want to rock bangs? Look no further! In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut bangs that will flatter your face, even with a receding hairline.

Description

If you have a receding hairline, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits you. However, cutting bangs can be a great way to frame your face and create a youthful look. Bangs can also help to cover up a receding hairline and give you the confidence you need. In this article, we'll show you how to cut bangs with a receding hairline, including tips on how to choose the right bangs for your face shape.

How to Cut Bangs with a Receding Hairline

Step 1: Choose the Right Bangs

The first step in cutting bangs with a receding hairline is to choose the right bangs for your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for longer, side-swept bangs that will elongate your face. If you have a square face, go for wispy, textured bangs that will soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any style of bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape, it's time to section off your hair. Use a comb to create a triangle section at the front of your head, with the top of the triangle being your hairline and the bottom of the triangle being the tip of your nose.

Step 3: Cut the Bangs

Using a pair of scissors, start cutting your bangs from the center of the triangle section and work your way outwards. Make sure to cut small sections at a time and keep checking to see if the length is right for you. Remember, it's always better to start with a longer length and cut more if needed.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, it's time to style them. You can use a blow dryer and a round brush to give your bangs some volume, or you can use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look. Whatever you choose, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and cut more if needed.
  • Use a comb to create a triangle section when cutting your bangs.
  • Choose the right bangs for your face shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your bangs.
  • When in doubt, go to a professional stylist.

FAQ

Can I still cut bangs if I have a receding hairline?

Absolutely! Bangs can actually help to cover up a receding hairline and give you a more youthful look.

How do I choose the right bangs for my face shape?

If you have a round face, opt for longer, side-swept bangs. If you have a square face, go for wispy, textured bangs. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any style of bangs.

Should I cut my bangs myself or go to a professional stylist?

If you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's always best to go to a professional stylist. However, if you're feeling adventurous, you can definitely cut your bangs yourself!

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain their look. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair. Also, don't forget to get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh!

By following these tips, you can successfully cut bangs with a receding hairline and feel confident in your new hairstyle.
