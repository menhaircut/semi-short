How To Make Curtain Bangs Look Good With A Straightener

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, curtain bangs are a great way to do it. These versatile bangs frame your face and add dimension to your hair. But how do you make them look good with a straightener? In this article, we'll go through step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect curtain bangs with a straightener.

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that are cut shorter in the middle and longer on the sides. They are called "curtain" bangs because they frame your face like a curtain. This style is versatile and can be worn with straight or curly hair.

How to

To make your curtain bangs look good with a straightener, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start straightening your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Take a section of hair at the top of your head and clip it up. This will prevent you from accidentally straightening the wrong section of hair. Next, section off your bangs by parting them down the middle.

Step 3: Straighten Your Bangs

Using a straightener, start at the roots of your bangs and slowly glide the straightener down to the ends. Make sure to keep the straightener moving to prevent any heat damage. Repeat this process until your bangs are straight and sleek.

Step 4: Add Volume

To add volume to your curtain bangs, use a round brush and roll the ends of your bangs away from your face. This will create a natural curve that frames your face. Use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs:

- Use a high-quality straightener to prevent heat damage. - Don't straighten your hair too often to prevent damage. - Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. - Use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Q: Can you curl curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can curl curtain bangs. Use a curling iron to create loose waves that frame your face.

Q: How often should you straighten your curtain bangs?

A: It's best to limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage. Try to straighten your bangs no more than once a week.

Q: Can curtain bangs be styled with curly hair?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be styled with curly hair. Use a curling iron to create loose curls that frame your face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Towel dry your hair instead of using a blow dryer. - Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools. - Limit your use of heat styling tools. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Following these tips will help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs with a straightener. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep it healthy.
