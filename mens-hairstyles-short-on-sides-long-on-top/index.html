Description of Mens Hairstyles Short on Sides Long on Top

Mens hairstyles short on sides long on top feature short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. This style can be achieved with a fade, an undercut, or simply by cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving it longer on top. The longer hair on top can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This style is perfect for men who want a trendy and edgy look that's versatile and easy to maintain.

How to Achieve This Style

The first step to achieving mens hairstyles short on sides long on top is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You'll also need to decide on the length of the hair on the sides and the length of the hair on top. Once you've decided on the length, your hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to cut your hair accordingly. If you're going for an undercut or a fade, your hairstylist will use clippers to gradually fade the hair from short to long.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving mens hairstyles short on sides long on top:

  1. Decide on the length of the hair on the sides and on top
  2. Find a skilled hairstylist
  3. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist cuts your hair
  4. Style the longer hair on top according to your preference

Tips for Maintaining This Style

Mens hairstyles short on sides long on top are relatively easy to maintain, but there are a few tips you can follow to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for hold

Here are some hair care tips for mens hairstyles short on sides long on top:
