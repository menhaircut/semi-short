Can You Get A Perm If You Have Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about getting a perm if you have wavy hair. If you're looking for a way to add more volume and texture to your hair, perming may be the answer. But can you get a perm if you already have wavy hair? The answer is yes! In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about getting a perm with wavy hair.

Description

Perming is a popular hair treatment that involves chemically altering the structure of the hair to create curls or waves. It can be a great way to add volume, texture, and definition to your hair. If you have wavy hair, you may be wondering if you can get a perm to enhance your natural waves. The good news is that it's possible, and we'll explain how.

How to Get a Perm with Wavy Hair

The process of getting a perm with wavy hair is similar to getting a perm with straight hair. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Perm

There are different types of perms to choose from, depending on the type of curls or waves you want. For wavy hair, you can opt for a loose perm, which creates soft waves and curls.

Step 2: Consult with a Professional Stylist

It's essential to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm. They will assess your hair type, condition, and the results you want to achieve. They will also recommend the best type of perm for your wavy hair.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting a perm, your hair needs to be in good condition. Your stylist may recommend a protein treatment to strengthen your hair. They will also wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that may interfere with the perm's results.

Step 4: Apply the Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, which contains chemicals that break down the hair's bonds to create curls or waves. The solution will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and the type of perm you chose.

Step 5: Rinse and Style

After the perm solution has done its job, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any chemicals. They will then apply a neutralizer to rebond your hair and set the curls or waves. Finally, they will style your hair as desired.

Tips for Getting a Perm with Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a perm with wavy hair:

  • Choose a professional stylist with experience in perming wavy hair.
  • Be honest about your hair's history, including previous chemical treatments.
  • Follow your stylist's instructions for before and aftercare to maintain your perm's health and longevity.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24-48 hours after getting a perm to allow the curls or waves to set.
  • Use products specifically formulated for permed hair to maintain its health and texture.

FAQ

Can I get a perm if I have natural waves?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have natural waves. Perms can enhance your natural waves and add more volume and texture to your hair.

How long does a perm last on wavy hair?

The lifespan of a perm on wavy hair depends on several factors, including hair type, condition, and aftercare. On average, a perm can last up to six months, but it may start to loosen after three months.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. The chemicals in hair dye can be harsh on permed hair, and coloring too soon can damage your hair's health and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

Here are some tips to keep your permed hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your permed hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to avoid pulling or breaking your curls or waves.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or use a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos or products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and dry it out.

We hope you found this article helpful in answering your question, "Can you get a perm if you have wavy hair?" Remember to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm and follow their instructions for before and aftercare. With the right care, your permed hair can look beautiful and healthy for months to come!
