Where To Find A Barber In Fable Anniversary

Written by Daniel Oct 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to Fable Anniversary, where you get to experience the world of Albion in high definition. One of the activities you can do in the game is to change your character's hairstyle. But where do you find a barber in Fable Anniversary? In this post, we'll guide you on where to look for one and some tips on hair care in the game.

How to Find a Barber in Fable Anniversary

Barbers can be found in several locations in the game, but the most common place to find them is in towns and cities. Here are some of the locations where you can find barbers:

  • Bowerstone South
  • Twinblade's Camp
  • Greatwood Caves
  • Knothole Glade

Once you reach any of these locations, look for a sign that says "Barber" or "Hairdresser". The barber shop usually has a red and white pole outside, similar to real-life barber shops.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to change your character's hairstyle in Fable Anniversary:

  1. Find a barber shop in any of the locations mentioned above.
  2. Enter the shop and approach the barber.
  3. Select the "Hairstyle" option from the dialogue menu.
  4. Choose from the available hairstyles. Some may require a certain level of renown or alignment.
  5. Confirm your selection and pay the required amount of gold.
  6. Exit the shop and admire your new hairstyle!

Tips for Hair Care in Fable Anniversary

Here are some tips on how to take care of your character's hair in the game:

  • Visit a barber regularly to keep your hair neat and tidy.
  • Avoid getting into fights or getting hit as this can mess up your hairstyle.
  • Use hair potions to change your hair color or style without visiting a barber.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your character's personality or playstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding a barber in Fable Anniversary:

Can I change my character's hair color?

Yes, you can change your character's hair color by using hair potions. These can be bought from general stores or found in chests throughout the game.

Do I need to pay for each visit to the barber?

Yes, you need to pay a certain amount of gold for each visit to the barber. The cost may vary depending on the location and the hairstyle you choose.

Can I change my hairstyle anytime?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle anytime you want by visiting a barber shop. However, some hairstyles may require a certain level of renown or alignment.

Conclusion

Now that you know where to find a barber in Fable Anniversary, you can change your character's hairstyle as often as you want. Remember to take care of your hair and visit a barber regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. Have fun exploring the world of Albion!
