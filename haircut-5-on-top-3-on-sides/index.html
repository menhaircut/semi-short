Haircut 5 On Top 3 On Sides A Stylish And Versatile Look

Written by Christine Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and versatile, the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" might just be what you're looking for. This popular hairstyle features a short buzz cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on the top. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to get this haircut, as well as some tips on how to maintain and style it.

Description

The "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" is a variation of the classic buzz cut, with longer hair on the top. The "5 on top" refers to the length of hair on the top of the head, which is typically around 5mm. The "3 on sides" refers to the length of hair on the sides and back of the head, which is typically around 3mm. This creates a sharp contrast between the shorter sides and longer top, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

How to Get the Haircut

Getting the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" is relatively easy, but it's important to go to a professional stylist to ensure that the cut is done correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get this haircut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting your haircut, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly. This will help your stylist to get a better idea of your hair's natural texture and thickness.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. The "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" typically features hair that's around 5mm in length on the top and 3mm on the sides.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Using clippers, your stylist will cut your hair to a length of around 3mm on the sides and back of your head. This will create a clean, uniform look.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors, your stylist will cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length, typically around 5mm. They may also use thinning shears to add texture and volume to the hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using a comb and styling product. You can choose to style your hair in a variety of ways, such as slicking it back or leaving it messy and textured.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling the Haircut

Here are some tips on how to maintain and style the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides":

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider getting a fade on the sides to create a more gradual transition between the shorter sides and longer top.

FAQ

Is the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my hair cut to maintain this style?

We recommend getting your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides".

Can I style the hair on the top in different ways?

Yes, you can style the hair on the top in a variety of ways, such as slicking it back, spiking it up, or leaving it messy and textured.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and nourished, we recommend using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Additionally, you should avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Finally, be sure to visit your stylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides".

Conclusion

The "haircut 5 on top 3 on sides" is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that's perfect for men of all ages. By following the tips and advice outlined in this article, you can easily get this haircut and maintain it for a sharp and polished look.
