Description

Men's hair with curly tops and short sides has become a popular hairstyle trend in recent years. This hairstyle is a blend of modern and classic styles, which creates a unique and stylish look. The curly top is left long, and the sides and back are trimmed short, creating a sharp contrast in length. The hairstyle works best for men with naturally curly hair, but it can also be achieved with the help of styling products and tools.

How to Achieve the Look

The curly top short sides hairstyle can be achieved in two ways: natural curls and styling products. Men with naturally curly hair can easily achieve the look by growing out the top and trimming the sides and back short. On the other hand, men with straight hair can use styling products such as mousse, curling irons, and gels to create curls on the top.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the curly top short sides hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to the top.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance the curls.
  4. Use a curling iron to create curls on the top if necessary.
  5. Trim the sides and back short using clippers or scissors.
  6. Style the top using your fingers or a comb.
  7. Apply a finishing product such as hairspray or pomade to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for maintaining the curly top short sides hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls to avoid breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the style.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the curly top short sides hairstyle:

Can I achieve this style with straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve the look with the help of styling products such as mousse, curling irons, and gels.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

What styling products should I use?

You can use mousse, gel, pomade, or hairspray to style your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls to avoid breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.

The curly top short sides hairstyle is a stylish and trendy look that works well for men with naturally curly hair or those who want to create curls. By following our step-by-step guide and hair care tips, you can easily achieve and maintain this hairstyle for 2023.
