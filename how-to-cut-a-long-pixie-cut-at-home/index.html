In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a long pixie cut at home. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want something short but not too short. It is also easy to maintain and style. We will provide you with tips and tricks on how to achieve a perfect long pixie cut.

How to Cut a Long Pixie Cut at Home

A long pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is longer than a traditional pixie cut. It is usually cut above the ear and can be styled in various ways. Cutting a long pixie cut at home is easy and can save you a lot of money. Here are the things that you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it completely. It is easier to cut your hair when it is dry. You can also use a water spray bottle to wet your hair if it is too dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts. Use hair clips to hold the sections in place. The first section should be from the front to the back of your head. The second section should be from the top of your ear to the back of your head. The third section should be from the other side of your head. The last section should be the crown of your head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Cut the sides of your hair first. Use the comb to comb your hair downwards. Cut the hair that is below the ear. Make sure that the hair is even on both sides. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Move to the back of your head. Comb your hair downwards and cut it in a straight line. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even. You can also use a mirror to check if the hair is even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Move to the top of your head. Comb your hair upwards and cut a little bit at a time. Do not cut too much at once. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair the way you want it. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to add some texture to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure that you get a clean cut.
  • Start cutting a little bit at a time. You can always cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check if the hair is even.
  • Watch some tutorials online to get some ideas on how to style your hair.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair if it is wet?

It is better to cut your hair when it is dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. You can cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add some shine to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut a long pixie cut at home, you can save a lot of money and time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
