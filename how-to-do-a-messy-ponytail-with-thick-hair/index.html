How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to create a stylish hairstyle that holds up throughout the day. One of the easiest and most versatile hairstyles for thick hair is the messy ponytail. In this guide, we will walk you through how to create a messy ponytail with thick hair, along with tips and tricks to make it look effortless and chic.

Table of Contents

  • How to Create a Messy Ponytail with Thick Hair
  • Tips for a Perfectly Messy Ponytail
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

How to Create a Messy Ponytail with Thick Hair

Follow these easy steps to create a messy ponytail with thick hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and grip.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, gather your hair at the crown of your head. Leave a few strands loose around your face for a more effortless look.

Step 3: Secure Your Ponytail

Using a hair tie, secure your ponytail in place. Don't worry if it's not perfect, as the messy look is what we're going for.

Step 4: Create Volume

To create volume, use your fingers to gently pull and tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will add height and texture to your ponytail.

Step 5: Mess It Up

Now for the fun part: mess up your ponytail! Use your fingers to gently pull and tug at different sections of your hair, creating a tousled, textured look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you're happy with your messy ponytail, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for a Perfectly Messy Ponytail

Here are a few tips to help you create the perfect messy ponytail:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip and volume to your hair.
  • Don't worry about making it perfect; the beauty of a messy ponytail is in its imperfection.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  • Experiment with different ponytail heights to find what works best for your face shape and hair length.
  • Accessorize your messy ponytail with a cute scrunchie or hair clip for added style.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make my ponytail look messier?

To make your ponytail look messier, use your fingers to pull and tug at different sections of your hair. You can also backcomb the hair at the crown of your head for added volume and texture.

Can I create a messy ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can still create a messy ponytail with short hair! Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways, and experiment with different ponytail heights to find what works best for your hair length.

How can I make my messy ponytail look more polished?

If you want to make your messy ponytail look more polished, try using a smoothing serum or hair oil to tame any flyaways. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair before creating your ponytail.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are a few tips to help you care for your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for thick hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a perfectly messy ponytail with your thick hair. Have fun experimenting with different styles and textures, and don't be afraid to embrace the messy look!
