Face Framing Short Haircut With Bangs And Glasses A Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you wear glasses and have been considering a short haircut, you might be wondering if it's a good idea. The good news is that a face-framing short haircut with bangs and glasses can look amazing! In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Face-Framing Short Haircut with Bangs and Glasses
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A face-framing short haircut with bangs and glasses is a versatile and stylish look. The haircut typically features short layers around the face to create a flattering shape and frame the face. Bangs can be added to the haircut to add even more dimension and style. Pairing this haircut with glasses can create a chic and sophisticated look.

How to Get a Face-Framing Short Haircut with Bangs and Glasses

The first step to getting a face-framing short haircut with bangs and glasses is to find a stylist who has experience with this type of cut. Look for a stylist who has experience working with glasses wearers, as they will be able to create a haircut that complements your frames.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps you can expect during your appointment:

  1. You will discuss your desired haircut with your stylist, including the length, shape, and whether or not you want bangs.
  2. Your stylist will wash and condition your hair, then trim the length as desired.
  3. Next, your stylist will add layers around your face to create the face-framing effect.
  4. If you opted for bangs, your stylist will cut them to the desired length and shape.
  5. Your stylist will blow dry your hair and style it to your liking.
  6. Finally, you'll put on your glasses and check out your new look!

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've got your new haircut, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Try different styling products to create different looks with your haircut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about face-framing short haircuts with bangs and glasses:

Can I wear this haircut with any type of glasses?

Yes, this haircut can be paired with any type of glasses. Your stylist can help you choose a haircut that complements your frames.

Will this haircut work with my face shape?

Yes, a face-framing short haircut with bangs can work with any face shape. Your stylist can create a haircut that flatters your features.

How often should I get my haircut trimmed?

You should visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Overall, a face-framing short haircut with bangs and glasses can be a flattering and stylish look. If you're considering this haircut, be sure to find a stylist who has experience with glasses wearers and follow these tips to keep your hair looking great!
