Brush your eyebrows upward with a spoolie brush to reveal the natural shape. This will help you see which hairs need to be removed and which ones need to be trimmed.

Step 2: Trim the Long Hairs

Use a small pair of scissors to trim the long hairs. Be careful not to cut too much or too close to the skin. Trim only the hairs that are longer than the rest.

Step 3: Remove the Unwanted Hairs

Use tweezers to remove the unwanted hairs. Pluck hairs in the direction of hair growth to avoid ingrown hairs. Be sure to only remove the hairs that are outside the natural shape of your eyebrows.

Step 4: Shape the Arch

Use your tweezers to shape the arch. The arch should be in line with the outer edge of your iris. Be sure to step back and check your progress in the mirror from time to time.

Step 5: Define the Tail

Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to define the tail of your eyebrows. The tail should be in line with the outer corner of your eye. Use a light hand and small strokes to achieve a natural look.

Step 6: Clean Up

Clean up any excess hairs or makeup around your eyebrows with a cotton swab or makeup remover.
