How To Tell If A Dress Is Too Short For Work

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you having trouble deciding whether a dress is too short for work or not? It can be tricky to determine the appropriate length, especially when dress codes can vary from workplace to workplace. In this article, we’ll give you some tips on how to tell if a dress is too short for work.

Table of Contents

  • Determining the Appropriate Length
  • Checking the Fit
  • Considering the Material
  • Accessorizing Appropriately
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Determining the Appropriate Length

The first thing you need to consider when determining if a dress is too short for work is the dress code of your workplace. Some companies have strict dress codes, while others are more relaxed. If you work in a conservative environment, it’s best to err on the side of caution and choose a dress that falls below the knee. If you work in a more casual environment, you may be able to get away with a dress that hits just above the knee.

Another factor to consider is your body type. If you’re tall, a dress that hits above the knee may be more appropriate than if you’re shorter. Similarly, if you have long legs, a shorter dress may look more modest than if you have shorter legs.

Checking the Fit

The fit of the dress is also important when determining if it’s appropriate for work. A dress that’s too tight or too loose can look unprofessional, regardless of its length. Make sure the dress fits well and isn’t too tight in the chest, waist, or hips.

It’s also important to check the neckline of the dress. A low-cut or revealing neckline can make even a longer dress look inappropriate for work.

Considering the Material

The material of the dress can also impact its appropriateness for work. A dress made of a casual material like cotton or jersey may be too casual for some workplaces, even if it’s a longer length. On the other hand, a dress made of a more formal material like silk or wool may be appropriate even if it’s shorter.

Accessorizing Appropriately

The way you accessorize a dress can also impact its appropriateness for work. If you wear a short dress with high heels and bold jewelry, it may look too flashy or provocative for work. However, if you pair the same dress with simple flats and minimal jewelry, it may look more professional.

FAQs

Q: How short is too short for a work dress?

A: It depends on the dress code of your workplace and your body type. In general, a dress that falls at or below the knee is appropriate for most conservative workplaces.

Q: Can I wear a short dress with leggings or tights to make it more appropriate for work?

A: Yes, wearing leggings or tights under a short dress can make it more appropriate for work. Just make sure the leggings or tights aren’t too sheer or patterned.

Q: Can I wear a short dress to work if I pair it with a blazer or cardigan?

A: Yes, a blazer or cardigan can make a short dress more appropriate for work. Just make sure the blazer or cardigan is a professional-looking style.

Hair Care

When wearing a dress to work, it’s important to make sure your hair looks professional as well. Avoid wearing your hair in a messy bun or ponytail, and instead opt for a sleek updo or polished blowout. If you have long hair, consider wearing it in a low ponytail or braid to keep it out of your face and looking neat.

By following these tips, you can determine whether a dress is too short for work and make sure you look professional and polished in the workplace.
