How To Haircut Yourself Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts and want to try cutting your own hair? With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to haircut yourself male.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right preparation, it can be a rewarding experience. You will need to invest in some basic tools, such as scissors, clippers, and a comb, and take the time to understand your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

How to Haircut Yourself Male: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools, including scissors, clippers, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to invest in a hair cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before starting. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results.

Step 3: Decide on Your Haircut Style

Determine the style you want to achieve, whether itâs a buzz cut, crew cut, or longer style. Look at pictures online or in magazines for inspiration and make a plan for how you will achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Start with the Clippers

Begin by using clippers to remove the bulk of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Blend

Use scissors to blend the hair on the top and sides. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create a natural-looking finish. Blend the sides and back into the top for a seamless look.

Step 6: Trim the Sideburns

Use the clippers or scissors to trim the sideburns to the desired length. Make sure they are even on both sides.

Step 7: Check the Back

Use two mirrors to check the back of your head and ensure that the cut is even. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Clean Up

Clean up any stray hairs and wash your hair again to remove any clippings.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure even results.
  • Use the scissors to blend and create texture for a natural-looking finish.
  • Donât forget to trim the sideburns and check the back of your head.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair without clippers?

While clippers can make the process easier, you can still achieve a great haircut with just scissors. However, it may take longer and require more patience.

How often should I cut my own hair?

This will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Generally, hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, itâs important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims can also help maintain healthy hair.
