How To Make Hair Really Fluffy

Written by Daniel Jun 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to achieve a fluffier and fuller look for your hair? Look no further as we have gathered the best tips and tricks to make your hair really fluffy. Read on and find out how to achieve voluminous and bouncy hair in no time.

Description

Fluffy hair has always been a trend and will continue to be so in the future. Fluffy hair is all about adding volume and texture to your hair. Whether you have naturally thin or thick hair, you can achieve a fluffy look with the right techniques and products. In this article, we will guide you on how to make hair really fluffy so you can rock a voluminous look any day of the week.

How To

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is one of the oldest tricks in the book when it comes to achieving more volume. This technique works by lifting the roots of your hair, creating more space and volume in between strands. Use a round brush to help increase the lift and smoothness of your hair while blow-drying.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner is essential to achieving a fluffy look. These products are specially formulated to add more body and texture to your hair. When choosing a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, look for products that contain ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen. These ingredients help strengthen and thicken your hair, giving you the fluffiness you desire.

3. Apply a Root Lifting Spray

Root lifting sprays are a must-have product for anyone looking to add more volume to their hair. These sprays are designed to be applied to the roots of your hair, lifting them up and adding more texture. Make sure to apply the product directly to the roots of your hair and work it in with your fingers for maximum lift.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing or backcombing your hair is another great way to add more volume and texture. Use a fine-toothed comb to gently tease the roots of your hair, working your way up towards the crown. Be careful not to be too aggressive as this can damage your hair. Once you have achieved your desired level of volume, use a soft bristle brush to smooth down the top layer of your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in achieving a fluffy look is to wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup and residue.

Step 2: Apply a Root Lifting Spray

Apply a root lifting spray directly to the roots of your hair, working it in with your fingers. Make sure to lift your hair up as you apply the product to create more space and volume in between strands.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a round brush. Focus on lifting the roots of your hair as you dry it to create more volume and texture.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Use a fine-toothed comb to tease the roots of your hair, working your way up towards the crown. Be gentle and take your time to achieve your desired level of volume.

Step 5: Finish With a Holding Spray

Finish off your look by applying a holding spray to keep your hair in place. Make sure to choose a hairspray that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

Tips

- Use a volumizing mousse or foam for added texture and hold

- Sleep with your hair in braids to create natural waves and volume

- Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh your hair down

- Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add more volume throughout the day

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, if you have dry or curly hair, you can wash it less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I achieve a fluffy look with short hair?

Absolutely! The techniques mentioned in this article can be applied to any hair length. Just adjust the amount of product and use smaller tools to achieve your desired look.

What should I do if my hair becomes too fluffy?

If your hair becomes too fluffy, you can use a smoothing serum or cream to tame it down. Apply the product to the ends of your hair and work it in with your fingers, avoiding the roots.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using quality products and avoiding excessive heat styling. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help maintain the health and thickness of your hair. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a fluffy and voluminous look without compromising the health of your hair.
