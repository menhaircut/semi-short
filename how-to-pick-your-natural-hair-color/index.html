How To Pick Your Natural Hair Color A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're going for a natural look. With so many shades and hues available, it can be overwhelming to figure out what will work best for your skin tone and hair type. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of picking your natural hair color, from determining your skin tone to choosing the right shade for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Choosing the Right Shade
  • Tips for Picking Your Natural Hair Color
  • FAQs About Choosing Your Natural Hair Color
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking your natural hair color is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. A warm skin tone has yellow or golden undertones, while a cool skin tone has pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral undertone.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when picking your natural hair color is your hair type. Different types of hair absorb color differently, so it's important to choose a shade that will work well with your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a lighter shade as it will appear brighter. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a darker shade as it will appear more vibrant. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a shade that will enhance the natural texture of your curls.

Choosing the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone and hair type, it's time to choose the right shade for you. If you have warm undertones, you may want to go for shades that have golden or caramel tones. If you have cool undertones, you may want to go for shades that have ash or platinum tones. If you have neutral undertones, you can choose a shade that has a mix of warm and cool tones. It's important to choose a shade that will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty.

Tips for Picking Your Natural Hair Color

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you have an active lifestyle or work in a professional setting, you may want to choose a shade that is low-maintenance and easy to maintain.
  • Take inspiration from nature: Look to nature for inspiration when choosing your natural hair color. Think about the colors of the seasons and how they can complement your skin tone.
  • Consult a professional: If you're not sure about what shade to choose, it's always a good idea to consult a professional hairstylist who can help you pick the right shade for you.

FAQs About Choosing Your Natural Hair Color

Q: Can I dye my hair a lighter color if I have dark hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a lighter color if you have dark hair, but it's important to go to a professional hairstylist who can do it safely and without damaging your hair.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my roots?

A: The frequency of root touch-ups depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable the roots are. Generally, root touch-ups are done every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.
  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Choosing your natural hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these steps and tips, you can pick a shade that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful.
