Causes of Hair Fall

Before we dive into the best shampoo for hair fall in India 2021, let's first understand the causes of hair fall.

Hair fall can be caused by various factors such as:

  • Stress
  • Pollution
  • Genetics
  • Medical conditions
  • Poor diet
  • Hormonal changes
  • Chemical treatments

Identifying the root cause of your hair fall can help you choose the right shampoo and hair care routine to prevent further hair fall.

How to Choose the Best Shampoo for Hair Fall

Choosing the right shampoo for hair fall can be overwhelming as there are so many options available in the market. Here are some factors to consider when choosing the best shampoo for hair fall:

  • Ingredients: Look for shampoos that contain ingredients such as biotin, keratin, caffeine, and argan oil as they are known to promote hair growth.
  • Hair type: Choose a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type, whether it's oily, dry, or normal.
  • Brand reputation: Choose a shampoo from a reputable brand that is known for its quality and effectiveness.
  • Reviews: Read reviews from other customers to know their experience with the shampoo.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Follow a healthy diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair color and bleach.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause hair fall.

FAQs

Q. How often should I use shampoo for hair fall?

A. It is recommended to use shampoo for hair fall 2-3 times a week.

Q. Can I use conditioner after using shampoo for hair fall?

A. Yes, you can use conditioner after using shampoo for hair fall. However, make sure to choose a conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Q. Can I use hair oil along with shampoo for hair fall?

A. Yes, you can use hair oil along with shampoo for hair fall. However, make sure to choose an oil that is suitable for your hair type and does not cause buildup on your scalp.

Hair Care Routine for Healthy Hair

Here is a hair care routine for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo for hair fall 2-3 times a week.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to moisturize and detangle hair.
  • Apply hair oil once a week to nourish and strengthen hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Reduce stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

In conclusion, choosing the best shampoo for hair fall in India 2021 can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Make sure to choose a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type, contains nourishing ingredients, and is from a reputable brand. Follow a healthy hair care routine and take care of your hair to prevent further hair fall.
