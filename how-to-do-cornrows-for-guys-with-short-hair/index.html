How To Do Cornrows For Guys With Short Hair

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a guy with short hair who wants to rock some cornrows? It's totally possible! Cornrows are a stylish and practical hairstyle that have been around for centuries. They're perfect for men with active lifestyles who want a low-maintenance yet eye-catching look. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of how to do cornrows for guys with short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your supplies. You'll need a comb, hair clips, a spray bottle filled with water, and hair gel or wax. You may also want to invest in a good hair oil to keep your scalp moisturized.

Next, decide on the pattern you want for your cornrows. You can do straight lines, zig-zags, or even a combination of both. Once you've decided on your pattern, section off your hair into small, even sections using your comb and hair clips. Make sure the sections are no wider than the width of your comb.

Step by Step

Step 1: Moisturize Your Scalp

Before you start braiding, it's important to moisturize your scalp to prevent dryness and itching. Use a hair oil or moisturizer to massage your scalp and ensure it's well-hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Gel or Wax

Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to each section of hair. This will help to keep your hair in place and make it easier to braid.

Step 3: Start Braiding

Starting at the front of your head, take three small sections of hair and begin braiding them together. As you braid, add a small amount of hair from each section to the braid. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat the braiding process with each section of hair until you've braided your entire head. Make sure your braids are neat and even.

Tips

  • Start with clean, detangled hair to make the braiding process easier.
  • Make sure your sections are even to ensure a neat and uniform look.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you braid.
  • Don't braid your hair too tightly, as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.
  • Consider adding beads or other accessories to your braids for a unique look.

FAQ

Can I do cornrows on short hair?

Yes, you can! While longer hair may be easier to braid, you can still achieve a great look with short hair. Just make sure your sections are small and even.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last anywhere from 2-6 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. Be sure to keep your scalp moisturized and avoid pulling on your braids.

Hair Care

Once you have your cornrows in, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Be sure to keep your scalp moisturized with a hair oil or moisturizer. You can also use a satin or silk scarf to protect your braids while you sleep. Avoid pulling on your braids or wearing tight hats or headbands, as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to confidently rock cornrows with short hair. Not only is this style practical and low-maintenance, but it's also a great way to express your personal style.
