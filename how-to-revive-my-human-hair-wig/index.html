How To Revive My Human Hair Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're wearing a human hair wig, you know that it requires proper care and maintenance to keep it looking great. But even with the best care, your wig may eventually start to lose its luster and become dull and lifeless. If you're wondering how to revive your human hair wig and bring it back to life, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to revive your wig using simple and effective techniques that you can do at home.

Description

Human hair wigs are a great investment, but they can be expensive. If you want to get the most out of your wig, you need to take care of it properly. However, even with the best care, your wig can start to look dull and lifeless over time. This can happen due to a variety of factors, such as exposure to sunlight, heat styling, and chemical treatments. If you want to revive your human hair wig and restore its natural shine and softness, you need to follow some simple steps.

How to Revive My Human Hair Wig

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

The first step in reviving your human hair wig is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for human hair wigs. Make sure to rinse your wig thoroughly and avoid rubbing or twisting it, as this can cause damage. After washing, gently pat your wig dry with a towel and let it air dry on a wig stand.

Step 2: Detangle Your Wig

After washing, your wig may be tangled and difficult to manage. To detangle your wig, use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush. Start at the ends of your wig and work your way up, gently teasing out any tangles. Avoid pulling or tugging on your wig, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Use a Hair Mask

To restore moisture and shine to your wig, use a hair mask. Apply the hair mask to your wig and leave it on for the recommended time. Rinse the mask out thoroughly and let your wig air dry on a wig stand.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Once your wig is dry, you can style it using heat tools or by using styling products. However, be careful not to use too much heat or product, as this can damage your wig. Always use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools on your wig.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your human hair wig looking great:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to keep it from getting tangled.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight, as this can cause fading.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your wig.

FAQ

Can I dye my human hair wig?

Yes, you can dye your human hair wig. However, it's best to have it done by a professional stylist who specializes in wig styling. This will ensure that your wig is properly dyed and that the color is even.

How often should I wash my human hair wig?

You should wash your human hair wig every 6-8 wears, or when it starts to look dull or dirty. Be careful not to over-wash your wig, as this can cause damage.

Can I use heat tools on my human hair wig?

Yes, you can use heat tools on your human hair wig. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using too much heat, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your human hair wig looking great, you need to take good care of it. This includes washing it regularly, detangling it gently, and using styling products and heat tools sparingly. With proper care, your human hair wig can last for years and continue to look great.
