Short Haircuts For Round Faces Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect short haircut for your round face and curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for round faces with curly hair. From how to style your hair to tips on maintaining healthy curls, we have got you covered!

Description

Round faces with curly hair can be a challenge to style. It can be difficult to find the right haircut that flatters your face shape while also working with your curls. Short haircuts for round faces curly hair can be a great solution. They can help to balance out the roundness of your face while also showcasing your beautiful curls.

How To

When it comes to styling short haircuts for round faces curly hair, it is important to work with your natural curls. Start with clean, damp hair and apply a curl-enhancing product. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut is crucial when it comes to short haircuts for round faces curly hair. A layered bob or a pixie cut can work well with curly hair and round faces. Make sure to consult with your stylist and bring in pictures of the styles you like to ensure you get the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it is important to wash and condition it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated. After washing, gently pat your hair dry with a towel, being careful not to rub your curls too vigorously.

Step 3: Apply Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair, working it through from roots to ends. A mousse or a curl defining cream can work well for curly hair. Be sure to use a product that is specifically designed for curly hair to enhance your natural curl pattern.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. Start by scrunching your curls with the diffuser, using a low heat setting. Once your hair is almost dry, switch to the cool setting to set your style.

Step 5: Define Your Curls

Use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. This will help to define your curls and give them extra bounce.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short haircuts for round faces curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated
  • Apply a curl-defining product to enhance your natural curl pattern
  • Use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls
