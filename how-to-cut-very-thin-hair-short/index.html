How To Cut Very Thin Hair Short A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 10, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have very thin hair and want to go for a short haircut, it can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to cut very thin hair short. So, let's get started.

Table of Content

  1. How to Cut Very Thin Hair Short
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Very Thin Hair Short
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care After Cutting Very Thin Hair Short

How to Cut Very Thin Hair Short

Before we dive into the step by step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a clip, and a mirror.

Now, let's start with the basics. The first thing you need to do is decide on the length of your hair. It's important to keep in mind that shorter hair tends to look fuller.

Once you've decided on the length, it's time to start cutting. It's important to cut your hair when it's dry because wet hair tends to look longer than it actually is.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Divide your hair into four sections, two in the front and two in the back.

Step 2: Cut the back

Start with the back of your head. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

Continue cutting small sections of hair, moving upward until you reach the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

Continue cutting small sections of hair, moving upward until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Trim the ends

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to trim the ends. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will give your hair a more natural look.

Tips for Cutting Very Thin Hair Short

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting very thin hair short:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts
  • Cut your hair when it's dry
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Cut your hair in layers to add volume
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair if it's very thin?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's very thin. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the step by step guide carefully.

How often should I cut my very thin hair short?

It's recommended to cut very thin hair short every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care After Cutting Very Thin Hair Short

After cutting your very thin hair short, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your hair

That's it! Now you know how to cut very thin hair short. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve a stylish and healthy haircut.
