Short Hair Styles For Oval Face Male

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an oval-faced male looking for the perfect short hair style to suit your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best short hair styles for oval face male, as well as sharing tips on how to maintain and care for your new haircut.

Description

The oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape when it comes to haircuts. With its balanced proportions and lack of sharp angles, it can pull off a variety of different hairstyles. However, not all short hair styles are created equal, and some may be more flattering than others for oval face male.

How to

When choosing a short hair style for oval face male, it's important to consider not only your face shape but also your hair texture and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short hair style:

1. Consider your face shape

While oval face male can pull off a variety of short hair styles, some may be more flattering than others. If you have a longer face, you may want to avoid very short haircuts that can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a slightly longer style that falls just above the ears or chin.

2. Take your hair texture into account

If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to go for a shorter, more textured cut that will help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable. On the other hand, if you have fine, thin hair, a shorter, more blunt cut may be more flattering.

3. Consider your personal style

Your short hair style should also reflect your personal style and personality. If you're a more laid-back, casual guy, a messy, textured cut may be perfect for you. On the other hand, if you prefer a more polished, sophisticated look, a sleek, classic cut may be more your style.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen the perfect short hair style for your oval face male, here are some tips to help you maintain and care for your new haircut:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Regular washing is essential for keeping your short hair style looking clean and fresh. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type and follow the instructions on the bottle.

2. Use styling products

Styling products like pomade, wax, or gel can help you achieve the perfect short hair style. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

3. Visit your barber regularly

Even with a short hair style, regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and style of your haircut. Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim and touch-up.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose and maintain the perfect short hair style for your oval face male:

- Avoid very short haircuts if you have a longer face - Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you - Consider your personal style and personality when choosing a short hair style - Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and style of your haircut

FAQ

What short hair styles are best for oval face male?

Some of the best short hair styles for oval face male include the classic crew cut, the textured crop, and the side-parted undercut.

How often should I wash my hair with a short hair style?

You should wash your hair with a short hair style every 2-3 days, or as often as necessary to keep it clean and fresh.

What styling products should I use for a short hair style?

The best styling products for a short hair style include pomade, wax, and gel. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

Hair Care

Maintaining and caring for your short hair style is essential for keeping it looking clean, fresh, and stylish. Here are some additional tips for taking care of your hair:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type - Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too often - Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf - Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals for healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose, style, and care for the perfect short hair style for your oval face male. Whether you prefer a messy, textured cut or a sleek, classic style, there's a short hair style out there to suit your face shape and personal style.
