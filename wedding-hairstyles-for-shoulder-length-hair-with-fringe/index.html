Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair With Fringe

Written by Petter Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a bride-to-be with shoulder length hair and a fringe, you may be wondering what kind of hairstyle would look best on your big day. You want a hairstyle that will complement your dress, makeup and overall look. In this article, we'll go over some tips, tricks and styles for wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with fringe.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

When deciding on a hairstyle for your wedding day, there are a few things to consider. First, think about the style of your dress. If you have a more classic or traditional dress, you may want to opt for a more polished hairstyle. If you have a more bohemian or relaxed dress, you may want to go for a more effortless or messy hairstyle.

Next, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to go for a style that elongates your face, like a sleek updo. If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to go for a style that adds volume to the sides of your face, like loose waves. Lastly, think about your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to go for a style that adds volume and texture, like a messy bun. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a style that tames your hair, like a sleek ponytail.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with fringe:

Low Bun

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and brush it out.
  3. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and brush it out.
  3. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back, securing it with a clear elastic band.
  4. Tease the crown of your head for more volume.
  5. Curl the bottom half of your hair with a curling iron.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips for wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with fringe:

  • Consider adding hair accessories, like a flower crown or hairpins, to elevate your look.
  • If you're doing your hair yourself, practice the style a few times before your wedding day.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for their opinion on what style would look best on you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with fringe:

What kind of fringe looks best with shoulder length hair?

Side-swept bangs or choppy fringe look best with shoulder length hair.

What kind of hair accessories can I wear with a shoulder length hairstyle?

You can wear hairpins, a flower crown, a headband or a hair comb with a shoulder length hairstyle.

How long should I book my hairstylist for on my wedding day?

You should book your hairstylist for at least 2-3 hours on your wedding day.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, make sure to take care of your hair. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. And on the day of your wedding, make sure to wash your hair the night before so it's clean but not too slippery.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find a wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair with fringe that complements your dress, makeup and overall look. Remember to practice the style a few times before your big day and don't be afraid to ask for help from a hairstylist if needed. Congratulations on your upcoming wedding!
