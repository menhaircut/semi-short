Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick wavy hair? Do you want to find a haircut that will make your life easier? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for thick wavy hair in 2021. From short to long, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's voluminous and full of life, but it can also be difficult to manage. Finding the right haircut can make all the difference. In this guide, we will showcase the best haircuts for thick wavy hair in 2021. We'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to manage your hair and keep it healthy.

How To

Before we dive into the haircuts, let's talk about how to manage thick wavy hair. First, invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that will keep your hair moisturized. Second, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet. Third, avoid using heat tools as much as possible. Fourth, get regular trims to prevent split ends. And finally, embrace your natural texture!

Step by Step

1. Short Bob

A short bob is a classic haircut that looks great on thick wavy hair. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways. Ask your stylist for a blunt cut that falls just above your shoulders. You can also add layers for more texture.

2. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, go for long layers. This haircut will add movement to your hair and prevent it from looking too heavy. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your chin and blend into the rest of your hair.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice, but it can look amazing on thick wavy hair. It's low maintenance and will highlight your natural texture. Ask your stylist for a textured cut that's longer on top and shorter on the sides.

4. Shaggy Lob

A shaggy lob is a modern take on the classic lob. It's perfect for thick wavy hair because it adds volume and texture. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your chin and are cut in a choppy, uneven way.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for thick wavy hair:

  • Ask your stylist to use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.
  • Consider getting bangs to frame your face.
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Invest in a good hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Short bobs, long layers, pixie cuts, and shaggy lobs are all great options.

How can I manage my thick wavy hair?

To manage thick wavy hair, use a good shampoo and conditioner, detangle with a wide-tooth comb, avoid heat tools, get regular trims, and embrace your natural texture.

What products should I use on my thick wavy hair?

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a hair oil, and a styling product that enhances your natural waves. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your thick wavy hair healthy, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when dry.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.

Congratulations! You're now armed with the knowledge to find the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles.
