Haircuts For Long Faces 2021 A Guide For Your Best Look Yet

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long faces in 2021. If you have a long face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Fortunately, there are haircuts that can help elongate your face and make it appear more balanced. Let's dive in!

Description

A long face is characterized by a face shape that is longer than it is wide. This means that your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, and your face has a narrow appearance. The goal of a good haircut for a long face is to create width and balance, making your face appear more proportionate.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Long Face

When choosing a haircut for your long face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid haircuts that add height to the top of your head, as this will make your face appear even longer.
  • Look for haircuts that add width to the sides of your face, as this will create the illusion of balance.
  • Consider a side part or bangs to help shorten the appearance of your forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Best Haircuts for Long Faces in 2021

Now that you know what to look for in a good haircut for a long face, here are some of the best haircuts for long faces in 2021:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add movement and dimension to a long face. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your cheekbones and gradually get longer towards the ends of your hair. This will help create volume and width around your face.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a classic haircut that works well for long faces. The side-swept bangs will help shorten the appearance of your forehead, while the bob will add width to the sides of your face.

3. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves are a great option for those with long faces. The waves will add volume and width to your hair, creating a more balanced appearance. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose waves, and finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Face Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your long face haircut:

  • Regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • To add more width to your hair, consider using a round brush when blow-drying.

FAQs

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid haircuts that add height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or a slicked-back hairstyle. These styles will only elongate your face more.

Can I still have long hair if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Long hair can look great on those with long faces. Just be sure to add layers and texture to your hair to create width and volume.

Hair Care for Long Faces

When it comes to hair care for long faces, the key is to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a texturizing spray or mousse to add texture. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and haircut ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your long face in 2021. Remember to choose haircuts that add width to your face and avoid styles that add height to the top of your head. With a little bit of maintenance and care, you can rock your long face haircut with confidence!
