How To Match Hair Color With Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add length and volume to your hair, extensions can be a great option. However, if you want your extensions to look natural, it's important to choose the right color. In this guide, we'll show you how to match your hair color with extensions so that you can achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Match Hair Color with Extensions
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Matching Hair Color with Extensions
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Extensions

How to Match Hair Color with Extensions

The first step in matching your hair color with extensions is to identify your natural hair color. This will help you choose extensions that are a close match. If you're not sure what your natural hair color is, take a look at the roots of your hair.

Once you know your natural hair color, you can start looking for extensions that match. Extensions come in a variety of colors, so it's important to choose the right shade. Look for extensions that are the same color as your natural hair, or a shade lighter or darker.

It's also important to consider the tone of your hair. Hair can be warm or cool-toned, and this can affect how the color of your extensions looks. If your hair is warm-toned, look for extensions with warm undertones. If your hair is cool-toned, look for extensions with cool undertones.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to match your hair color with extensions:

  1. Identify your natural hair color.
  2. Choose extensions that are the same color as your natural hair, or a shade lighter or darker.
  3. Consider the tone of your hair and choose extensions with a similar undertone.
  4. If you're not sure which shade to choose, ask a professional stylist for advice.
  5. Try on the extensions before you buy them to make sure they match your hair color.

Tips for Matching Hair Color with Extensions

Here are some additional tips to help you match your hair color with extensions:

  • If you have highlights or lowlights in your hair, choose extensions that match the color of the highlights or lowlights.
  • If you're not sure which shade to choose, it's always better to go lighter rather than darker. You can always darken the extensions later if needed.
  • If you have ombre or balayage hair, choose extensions that match the color of the ends of your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my extensions to match my hair color?

A: It's generally not recommended to dye extensions, as the process can damage the hair. It's better to choose extensions that are a close match to your natural hair color.

Q: How do I know if the extensions are a good match?

A: The best way to know if the extensions are a good match is to try them on. Look at the extensions in natural light to see how they look next to your hair. If they blend in seamlessly, they're a good match.

Q: How long do extensions last?

A: The lifespan of extensions depends on the type of extensions and how well they're cared for. Generally, clip-in extensions can last up to a year with proper care, while semi-permanent extensions can last up to six months.

Hair Care for Extensions

Once you've matched your hair color with extensions, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your extensions:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your extensions.
  • Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions, or use a heat protectant spray if you must.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing them.

By following these tips, you can enjoy your extensions and achieve the perfect look.
