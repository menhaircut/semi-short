Bangs Make Your Face Look Rounder How To Deal With It

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to try bangs but are afraid they will make your face look rounder? You're not alone. Bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle, but they can also have an unexpected effect on the shape of your face. In this article, we'll explore why bangs make your face look rounder and what you can do about it.

Why Bangs Make Your Face Look Rounder

Bangs have a tendency to shorten the appearance of the face, which can make it look rounder. This is because they cover up the forehead, which is one of the key areas that creates the illusion of length in the face. When the forehead is covered, the face appears shorter and wider, which can make it look rounder.

In addition, the shape of the bangs can also make a difference. Bangs that are straight across can create a horizontal line that emphasizes the roundness of the face. Bangs that are angled or swept to the side can create a more flattering shape that helps to balance out the roundness.

How to Deal with a Round Face and Bangs

If you have a round face and want to try bangs, there are a few things you can do to make sure they flatter your face shape:

  • Choose bangs that are angled or swept to the side, rather than straight across.
  • Make sure the length of the bangs is appropriate for your face shape. Longer bangs can help to elongate the face, while shorter bangs can make it look rounder.
  • Consider the thickness of the bangs. Thicker bangs can add volume to the face, while thinner bangs can help to create a more balanced look.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hairstyle

If you've decided to try bangs and want to make sure they flatter your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Choose bangs that are angled or swept to the side.
  2. Make sure the length of the bangs is appropriate for your face shape.
  3. Consider the thickness of the bangs.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.
  5. When styling your bangs, use a round brush to create volume and shape.
  6. Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  7. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or bumps.
  8. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.
  9. Trim your bangs regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  10. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs

When choosing bangs, keep these tips in mind:

  • Consider your face shape and choose bangs that will flatter it.
  • Think about your hair type and choose bangs that will work with it.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right type of bangs.
  • Be willing to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look.
  • Remember that bangs require regular maintenance, so be prepared to trim them every few weeks.

FAQ About Bangs and Face Shape

Do bangs make your face look rounder?

Yes, bangs have a tendency to shorten the appearance of the face, which can make it look rounder. However, choosing the right type of bangs can help to balance out the roundness and flatter your face shape.

What type of bangs are best for a round face?

Angled or swept to the side bangs are best for a round face, as they help to create a more flattering shape that balances out the roundness.

How do I know if bangs will look good on me?

Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you an idea of how the bangs will look with your current hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly, but be careful not to overwash them or they may become dry and brittle.
  • Dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or bumps.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

With the right type of bangs and proper care, you can enjoy this versatile hairstyle without worrying about making your face look rounder.
