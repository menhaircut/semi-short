What Does A Taper Haircut Look Like

Written by Petter Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, a taper haircut might be the perfect option for you. This haircut is characterized by a gradual decrease in hair length from the top of the head to the nape of the neck, resulting in a clean and polished look.

Description

The taper haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that can be adapted to suit a wide range of personal styles and preferences. It's characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head that gradually gets longer as it approaches the crown. The top of the hair is left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

How to Get a Taper Haircut

If you're interested in getting a taper haircut, you'll need to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this particular style. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a taper haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a taper haircut is to decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. If you're unsure, your barber or hairstylist can help you choose a length that will flatter your features.

Step 2: Determine the Fade

The next step is to determine how short you want the hair on the sides and back of your head to be. This will depend on the type of taper you want. A low taper will have longer hair on the sides and back, while a high taper will have shorter hair.

Step 3: Clean Up the Nape

The final step in getting a taper haircut is to clean up the hair at the nape of your neck. This will give your haircut a polished and finished look.

Tips for Maintaining a Taper Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your taper haircut:

- Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh. - Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair and keep it in place. - Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

1. Is a taper haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a taper haircut can be adapted to suit all hair types, from straight to curly.

2. How often do I need to get a taper haircut?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your taper haircut.

3. Can I style my taper haircut in different ways?

Yes, the top of your hair can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal style and preferences.

Hair Care for Taper Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you care for your hair after getting a taper haircut:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. - Use a hair serum or oil to nourish your hair and prevent frizz. - Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

Overall, a taper haircut is a versatile and stylish option for men who want a clean and polished look. With the right care and maintenance, this haircut can last for several weeks and look great in a variety of styles.
