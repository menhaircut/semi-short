How To Wear Shoulder Length Hair Over 50

Written by Bardi Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and more fragile. However, that doesn't mean we have to sacrifice style! Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for women over 50 because it's versatile, easy to care for, and can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we'll explore how to wear shoulder length hair over 50, including styles, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Style
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Style

There are many different ways to wear shoulder length hair over 50, depending on your personal style and preferences. Here are some ideas:

1. Layers

Adding layers to your shoulder length hair can create movement and texture. This is a great option if you have thin or fine hair, as it can add volume and body.

2. Bob

The classic bob is a timeless style that looks great on women of all ages. A shoulder length bob can be sleek and sophisticated or tousled and casual, depending on how you style it.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. Side-swept bangs can be flattering on most face shapes, while blunt bangs can add a modern edge.

4. Waves

Adding waves or curls to your shoulder length hair can create a soft and romantic look. This style works well for special occasions or just for everyday wear.

Tips

Here are some tips for wearing shoulder length hair over 50:

1. Embrace your natural texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, consider embracing your texture instead of fighting it. A shoulder length cut can be a great way to showcase your natural curls or waves.

2. Use products to add volume

If you have thin or fine hair, use volumizing products like mousse or root lift spray to add body and fullness.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

If you use heated styling tools like flat irons or curling wands, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Keep your hair healthy

Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and shiny. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

Q: What face shapes look best with shoulder length hair?

A: Shoulder length hair can be flattering on most face shapes. If you have a round face, consider adding layers to create a more angular look. If you have a square face, try a side part to soften your features.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I wear my shoulder length hair up?

A: Yes, there are many ways to wear shoulder length hair up, including ponytails, buns, and braids.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your shoulder length hair over 50:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair soft and shiny. Try using a hair mask once a week or as needed.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

4. Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and dull. Aim to wash your hair every other day or as needed.

In conclusion, wearing shoulder length hair over 50 can be a stylish and versatile choice. Whether you prefer a sleek bob or soft waves, there are many ways to wear this length of hair. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
