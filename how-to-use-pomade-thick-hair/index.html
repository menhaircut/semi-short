How To Use Pomade For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of dealing with unruly and unmanageable thick hair? Pomade can be your new best friend! This versatile styling product can give you the hold, shine, and texture you desire. But if you're new to pomade, you might be wondering how to use it properly. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to use pomade for thick hair, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Use Pomade for Thick Hair

Pomade is a versatile hair styling product that can give you a range of looks, from slick and shiny to matte and textured. But before you start using pomade, it's important to choose the right type for your hair. For thick hair, you'll want a pomade with a strong hold that can tame your unruly strands.

Here are the basic steps for using pomade on thick hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Pomade works best on dry hair, so make sure you've towel-dried your hair thoroughly before applying it.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms. The amount of pomade you'll need depends on the length and thickness of your hair. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.
  3. Apply the pomade evenly to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way up to the tips. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the pomade evenly.
  4. Style your hair as desired. Pomade can give you a range of looks, from slick and shiny to matte and textured. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  5. Finish with a hairspray if desired. This will help to set your style and keep it in place all day.

Tips for Using Pomade

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your pomade:

  • Choose the right type of pomade for your hair. For thick hair, look for a pomade with a strong hold.
  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more if needed. Too much pomade can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Pomade can give you a range of looks, from slick and shiny to matte and textured.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.
  • Finish with a hairspray if desired to set your style and keep it in place all day.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using pomade for thick hair:

Q: Can I use pomade on wet hair?

A: Pomade works best on dry hair, so it's recommended that you use it on towel-dried hair.

Q: How often should I use pomade?

A: It's up to you! Some people use pomade every day, while others only use it for special occasions.

Q: How do I remove pomade from my hair?

A: Use a clarifying shampoo or a deep-cleansing shampoo to remove pomade from your hair. You may need to shampoo your hair several times to completely remove the pomade.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for those with thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to use pomade for thick hair like a pro. Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by using moisturizing products and avoiding heat styling tools too often.
