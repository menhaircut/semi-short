What Is A Boy Cut Hairstyle

Written by Christine Jul 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, a boy cut might be the perfect option for you. This short haircut is characterized by its closely cropped sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. It's a versatile style that can be adapted to suit a range of different hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among women of all ages. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what a boy cut hairstyle is, how to achieve it, and some tips for keeping it looking great.

How to Get a Boy Cut Hairstyle?

If you're considering a boy cut hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to choose a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. They'll be able to guide you on the best length and shape for your face, and ensure that the cut is even and well-blended.

To achieve the boy cut, your hairstylist will typically start by trimming the sides and back of your hair quite short. This might involve using clippers or scissors, depending on your hair type and the desired look. They'll then gradually blend the hair on top, leaving it slightly longer than the rest. This can be styled in a variety of ways, from a sleek and smooth look to a tousled, textured style.

Step by Step Guide for Boy Cut Hairstyle

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a boy cut hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose a Skilled Hairstylist

Look for a hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair, and who can help you achieve the look you want. You might want to bring in some pictures of boy cut styles that you like, to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your stylist to see the natural texture and shape of your hair, and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Discuss the Cut with Your Stylist

Talk to your stylist about the length and shape of the cut, and any styling preferences you have. They'll be able to advise you on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Your stylist will start by trimming the sides and back of your hair quite short, using clippers or scissors. They'll then gradually blend the hair on top, leaving it slightly longer than the rest.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will show you how to style it to achieve your desired look. This might involve using styling products, like wax or pomade, to add texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining a Boy Cut Hairstyle

To keep your boy cut looking great, here are a few tips to follow:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of the cut
  • Use styling products to add texture and hold
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs About Boy Cut Hairstyles

What is the difference between a pixie cut and a boy cut?

While both pixie cuts and boy cuts are short hairstyles, there are some key differences. Pixie cuts are typically a bit longer on top, and can be styled in a range of different ways. Boy cuts, on the other hand, are generally shorter and more closely cropped, with a more masculine look.

Will a boy cut suit my face shape?

Boy cuts can be adapted to suit a range of different face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. Talk to your hairstylist about the best length and shape for your face, and they'll be able to advise you on what will work best.

How often should I get my boy cut trimmed?

To maintain the shape and length of your boy cut, it's a good idea to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and well-groomed.

Hair Care for Boy Cut Hairstyle

To keep your boy cut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray

With these tips, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance boy cut hairstyle that looks great and feels great to wear.
