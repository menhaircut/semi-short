How To Know If Baby Bangs Suit You

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle, you might be considering baby bangs. These short, wispy bangs can be a fun and playful addition to any haircut, but they’re not for everyone. So, how do you know if baby bangs will suit you? Here’s everything you need to know.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Know if Baby Bangs Suit You
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Know if Baby Bangs Suit You

Before you make the chop, it’s important to consider a few factors that can affect whether or not baby bangs will suit your face shape and hair type.

Face shape

One of the most important factors to consider is your face shape. Baby bangs tend to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces, as they can help to balance out the proportions of the face. If you have a round face, however, baby bangs can make your face appear even rounder, so you might want to consider longer, side-swept bangs instead.

Hair texture

Another important factor is your hair texture. Baby bangs tend to work best on those with fine or straight hair, as they can appear wispy and delicate. If you have thick or curly hair, baby bangs might look too heavy or bulky, so you might want to opt for a longer, more layered haircut instead.

Personal style

Finally, it’s important to consider your personal style. Baby bangs can give off a playful and youthful vibe, so they might not be the best choice if you’re looking for a more sophisticated or professional look.

Step by Step

Still not sure if baby bangs are right for you? Here are some steps you can take to help you decide:

Step 1: Look at pictures

Start by looking at pictures of people with baby bangs. Try to find pictures of people with similar face shapes and hair textures to yours, so you can get a better idea of how the style might look on you.

Step 2: Try a wig

If you’re not ready to commit to a haircut just yet, try on a wig with baby bangs. This can give you a better idea of how the style will look on you without making any permanent changes.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

If you’re still not sure, book a consultation with a stylist. They can help you determine whether or not baby bangs will suit your face shape and hair type, and can offer suggestions for other styles that might work better for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind if you’re considering baby bangs:

  • Make sure you’re prepared for the upkeep. Baby bangs require regular trimming to maintain their shape, so you’ll need to visit the salon more often than you would with longer bangs.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you’re someone who likes to pull their hair back into a ponytail or bun, baby bangs might not be the best choice, as they can be difficult to style with an updo.
  • Experiment with different lengths. Baby bangs can be cut at different lengths, so don’t be afraid to play around with the length to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off baby bangs?

While baby bangs can be a fun and playful addition to any haircut, they’re not for everyone. It’s important to consider your face shape and hair texture before making the chop.

Do baby bangs require a lot of upkeep?

Yes, baby bangs require regular trimming to maintain their shape, so you’ll need to visit the salon more often than you would with longer bangs.

Hair Care

If you do decide to go for baby bangs, it’s important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup in your bangs.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to give them a smooth and sleek look.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, like pomade or wax, to keep your bangs in place.

With these tips in mind, you’ll be able to rock your new baby bangs with confidence!
