Do Curtain Bangs Suit Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting a new hairstyle but not sure which one will suit your heart-shaped face? Curtain bangs have become a popular hairstyle trend in recent years, but do they suit heart-shaped faces? In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about curtain bangs and whether they are a good fit for your heart-shaped face.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Curtain Bangs
  3. Step by Step Guide to Get Curtain Bangs
  4. Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a trendy hairstyle that features long, side-swept bangs that frame the face. They are called "curtain" bangs because the hair parts in the middle, much like a set of curtains. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some movement and texture to their hair without committing to a full fringe.

Heart-shaped faces, on the other hand, are characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow chin. They typically have a pointy chin that gives the face a heart-like shape. People with heart-shaped faces tend to have a more prominent forehead and cheekbones, which can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're thinking about getting curtain bangs, it's important to talk to your hairstylist first. They can help you determine if this hairstyle will suit your face shape and recommend the best length and style for your hair type.

When getting curtain bangs, it's essential to consider your hair's texture and thickness. If you have thick hair, you may need to have your bangs thinned out to prevent them from looking too bulky. If you have fine hair, your hairstylist may recommend a shorter length to prevent your bangs from looking limp.

Step by Step Guide to Get Curtain Bangs

If you've decided that curtain bangs are the right hairstyle for you, here's a step-by-step guide on how to get them:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a part down the middle of your head.
  2. Take the section of hair that you want to turn into bangs and divide it into two equal parts.
  3. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the outer corner and working your way inwards.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Style your bangs as desired, sweeping them to the side or parting them in the middle.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent them from becoming too long.
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, which will help to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause your bangs to become greasy and flat.
  • Use a dry shampoo or hair powder to absorb excess oil and add texture to your bangs.

FAQs

What face shapes do curtain bangs suit?

Curtain bangs can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces. However, it's important to talk to your hairstylist to determine if this hairstyle will work for your specific face shape.

What length should curtain bangs be?

The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your hair's texture and thickness. If you have thick hair, you may need to have your bangs thinned out to prevent them from looking too bulky. If you have fine hair, your hairstylist may recommend a shorter length to prevent your bangs from looking limp.

Can I style my curtain bangs differently?

Yes, curtain bangs can be styled in a variety of ways. You can sweep them to the side, part them in the middle, or even pin them back. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

When you have curtain bangs, it's essential to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause your bangs to become misshapen.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can suit heart-shaped faces, but it's important to talk to your hairstylist to determine if this hairstyle will work for your specific face shape. With proper maintenance and care, curtain bangs can add movement and texture to your hair, giving you a fresh and trendy look.
