What Does A Low Taper Fade Look Like

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but don't know where to start? A low taper fade might be just the look you're looking for. This hairstyle is versatile and looks great on any face shape. In this article, we'll discuss what a low taper fade looks like, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A low taper fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually fades from longer to shorter towards the neck. The hair is longest at the top and gradually gets shorter towards the back and sides of the head. This haircut is known for its clean and sharp look.

How to Get a Low Taper Fade

If you're interested in getting a low taper fade, it's important to go to a professional barber or stylist. They will be able to give you the best results. Here's how to get a low taper fade:

Step 1: Consultation

During your consultation, your barber or stylist will discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine if a low taper fade is right for you. They will also determine the best length and style for your hair.

Step 2: Cutting

Your barber or stylist will use clippers to gradually cut the sides and back of your hair. They will start with a longer guard and work their way down to a shorter guard as they get closer to the neck. The top of your hair will be cut with scissors to maintain the length.

Step 3: Blending

Your barber or stylist will use a blending technique to make sure the transition from longer to shorter hair is smooth. They may use scissors or clippers to blend the hair.

Step 4: Styling

Your barber or stylist will style your hair to your desired look. They may use pomade or gel to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your low taper fade looking great:

- Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. - Style your hair with a light touch to avoid weighing it down. - Use a comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about low taper fades:

What's the difference between a low taper fade and a high taper fade?

A low taper fade starts the fade lower on the head, while a high taper fade starts the fade higher on the head.

Can I get a low taper fade with long hair?

Yes, a low taper fade can be done with longer hair. Your barber or stylist will determine the best length for your hair.

How do I style my low taper fade?

You can style your low taper fade with pomade or gel. Use a light touch to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining your low taper fade. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. - Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons. - Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Conclusion

A low taper fade is a great hairstyle for anyone looking for a clean and sharp look. It's important to go to a professional barber or stylist to get the best results. With proper care and maintenance, your low taper fade will look great for weeks to come.
