What Hair Looks Best On Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, one of the most important factors to consider is your face shape. For those with round faces, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit challenging. That's why we've put together this comprehensive guide to help you find the best hair look for your round face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair for Round Faces
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Round Face Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Face Hairstyles

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval shape. This can be achieved by adding height and volume to the crown of your head, elongating your face with long layers, or adding side-swept bangs to create angles.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Round Faces

The best haircuts for round faces are those that create the illusion of a longer, more oval shape. This can be achieved with a variety of haircuts and styles, including:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate your face and create a more oval shape. Opt for layers that start at your chin or below, as shorter layers can make your face look wider.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and draw attention away from the fullness of your cheeks. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Volume at the Crown

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help to elongate your face and create a more oval shape. This can be achieved with a volumizing mousse or by backcombing your hair at the roots.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Hair for Round Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair, making sure it's completely dry before you start styling.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair, focusing on the crown of your head.

Step 3: Backcomb your hair

Backcomb your hair at the roots to create volume at the crown of your head. Be sure to use a fine-tooth comb and be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Create side-swept bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, use a round brush to blow-dry them, sweeping them to the side as you go. If you don't have side-swept bangs, you can create them by parting your hair to the side and sweeping a section of hair across your forehead.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with a light-hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Round Face Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your round face hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create volume at the crown of your head.
  • Avoid heavy or blunt bangs, which can make your face look even rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools to avoid damage.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and straight, blunt cuts.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of a longer, more oval shape. This can be achieved with long layers, side-swept bangs, and volume at the crown of your head.

Hair Care for Round Face Hairstyles

To keep your round face hairstyle looking its best, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face shape and feel confident and beautiful every day.
