Mens Haircut Long On Top Wavy Tips And Tricks For A Stylish Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, a mens haircut long on top wavy might be the perfect fit for you. This versatile cut is characterized by longer hair on the top that is left wavy or textured, while the sides and back are trimmed short. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or are willing to use styling products to achieve the look, this hairstyle can work for any occasion and face shape. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and FAQs about how to get and care for a mens haircut long on top wavy.

Description

The mens haircut long on top wavy is a modern and stylish hairstyle that suits many men's preferences. This haircut is characterized by long hair on the top that can be styled in different ways, such as wavy, textured, or slicked back, and short sides and back that can be faded or tapered. The hair on the top can be left natural or styled with a hairdryer, a comb, or a texture spray to create volume, definition, and movement. This haircut can be adapted to any face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, making it a popular choice among men of all ages and professions.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Long on Top Wavy

If you want to get a mens haircut long on top wavy, you should follow these steps:

1. Find a good barber or hairstylist

The first step to getting a mens haircut long on top wavy is to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced and skilled in cutting men's hair. You can ask for recommendations from friends, family, or colleagues, or search online for reviews and portfolios of local barbershops and salons. Once you find a barber or hairstylist that you trust, you can book an appointment and discuss your haircut goals and preferences with them.

2. Choose the right length and texture

The second step is to choose the right length and texture for your hair. Depending on your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you may want to go for a longer or shorter top, and a more or less defined texture. You can show your barber or hairstylist a picture or a sketch of the haircut you want, or ask for their advice on what would suit you best. Generally, a mens haircut long on top wavy looks best when the hair on the top is at least 3 inches long and the sides and back are faded or tapered.

3. Clean and prepare your hair

The third step is to clean and prepare your hair for the haircut. You should shampoo and condition your hair the day before the appointment, and avoid using any heavy styling products or oils that can weigh down your hair. You should also avoid cutting your hair yourself or letting it grow too long, as this can make it harder for your barber or hairstylist to achieve the desired length and texture.

4. Communicate with your barber or hairstylist

The fourth step is to communicate with your barber or hairstylist during the haircut. You should let them know if you feel uncomfortable or if you want to make any changes to the initial plan. You should also ask them for advice on how to style and maintain your haircut at home, and what products or tools you should use. A good barber or hairstylist will listen to your needs and preferences and provide you with a customized and flattering mens haircut long on top wavy.

Tips for a Stylish Mens Haircut Long on Top Wavy

Here are some tips for achieving and maintaining a stylish mens haircut long on top wavy:

1. Use the right products

To enhance and define your wavy hair on the top, you can use a sea salt spray, a texture spray, or a curl defining cream. These products will add volume, texture, and hold to your hair, without making it look greasy or flat. You should apply the product to damp hair, scrunch it with your fingers, and let it air dry or diffuse it with a blow dryer on low heat.

2. Trim your hair regularly

To keep your mens haircut long on top wavy looking fresh and neat, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent split ends, frizz, and uneven growth, and maintain the desired length and texture. You should also ask your barber or hairstylist to trim the sides and back to the same length every time, to create a consistent and balanced look.

3. Protect your hair from heat and sun

To prevent your wavy hair on the top from getting dry, brittle, or damaged, you should protect it from heat and sun. You should avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can weaken your hair and make it prone to breakage. You should also wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're exposed to the sun for a long time, as UV rays can cause color fading and dehydration.

FAQs about Mens Haircut Long on Top Wavy

1. Can I get a mens haircut long on top wavy if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can still get a mens haircut long on top wavy if you have straight hair. You can use a hair curler or a texture spray to create waves or curls on the top, or ask your barber or hairstylist to add some layers or texture to your hair to make it more voluminous and dynamic.

2. How long does it take to style a mens haircut long on top wavy?

It depends on the length and texture of your hair, as well as the products and tools you use. Generally, it takes about 5-10 minutes to style a mens haircut long on top wavy, using a hairdryer, a sea salt spray, and your fingers. However, if you want a more defined or sleek look, you may need to spend more time and use a comb or a styling gel.

3. Can I wear a hat with a mens haircut long on top wavy?

Yes, you can wear a hat with a mens haircut long on top wavy, as long as you choose a hat that fits your head and hair well. You can opt for a beanie, a baseball cap, or a fedora, depending on your style and occasion. However, you should avoid wearing a hat for too long, as it can flatten your hair and create unwanted creases.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Long on Top Wavy

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and stylish mens haircut long on top wavy:

1. Wash your hair regularly

You should wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 days, or as needed. You should avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz. You should also rinse your hair with cool water, to seal the cuticles and make your hair shinier and smoother.

2. Brush or comb your hair gently

You should brush or comb your hair gently, especially when it's wet, to prevent breakage and tangles. You should use a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristle brush, and start from the ends and work your way up, without pulling or yanking your hair. You should also avoid using a hairbrush on your wavy hair on the top, as it can disrupt the natural texture and create static.

3. Protect your hair from chlorine

If you swim in a pool or a beach, you should protect your hair from chlorine and salt water, which can damage your hair and make it discolored or brittle. You should wear a swim cap or rinse your hair with fresh water before and after swimming, to minimize the exposure to the chemicals. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or an oil treatment, to nourish and hydrate your hair after the swim.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain a stylish and low-maintenance mens haircut long on top wavy, and feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
