How To Get A Man Bun With Short Hair In 2023

Written by Christine Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish way to rock your short hair? A man bun is a great option, but what if your hair is too short? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to get a man bun with short hair in 2023. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get a Man Bun with Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling a Man Bun with Short Hair
  4. FAQs

How to Get a Man Bun with Short Hair

The first step to getting a man bun with short hair is to grow it out to at least 6 inches, which will give you enough hair to tie up. If your hair is not yet at that length, you'll have to be patient and wait it out.

Once your hair is long enough, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil and make your hair more manageable.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to work with.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a hair gel or pomade, to your hair. This will help to hold your hair in place and make it easier to style.
  4. Use a comb or brush to pull your hair back into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.
  5. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape. Tuck the ends of your hair under the bun and secure it with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  6. Use your fingers to gently loosen the bun and create a messy, relaxed look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to keep your man bun in place.

Tips for Styling a Man Bun with Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling a man bun with short hair:

  • Invest in good-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and make it easier to style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find the look that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to seek advice from a professional stylist if you're unsure how to style your hair.
  • Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine. Growing out your hair takes time and effort, but the results are worth it!

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a man bun with short hair:

Can I get a man bun with really short hair?

No, you need at least 6 inches of hair to tie up into a man bun.

What if my hair is too thin to create a man bun?

You can try using hair extensions or a hairpiece to add volume to your hair.

How do I maintain my man bun?

Regular haircuts, conditioning treatments, and gentle styling are essential for maintaining a healthy man bun.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Man Bun

Here are some tips for keeping your man bun looking its best:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish man bun with short hair in 2023. Remember to be patient, take good care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
