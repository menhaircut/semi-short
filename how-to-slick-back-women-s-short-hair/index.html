How To Slick Back Women s Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to slick back women's short hair. Slicked-back hair has always been a classic look that exudes sophistication and glamour. Whether you're headed to a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday hairstyle, the slicked-back look is a great option. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of achieving this look on short hair. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

The slicked-back look involves combing back the hair from the forehead to the nape of the neck, creating a smooth, polished finish. This style works well on short hair, as it helps to create the illusion of length and can make your hair appear more voluminous. While the slicked-back look can seem intimidating at first, with the right products and techniques, it's actually quite easy to achieve.

How to Slick Back Women's Short Hair

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have oily hair, shampoo and condition your hair beforehand. If your hair is on the drier side, you may want to apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner or serum to help smooth down any frizz or flyaways.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. A gel or pomade works best for achieving the slicked-back look. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and the areas where you want to create the most volume.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-tooth comb, start combing your hair back from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Make sure to comb your hair in a smooth, even motion, and use your fingers to help create volume at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Secure your hair

Once you've combed your hair back, use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place. Make sure to tuck any loose strands behind your ears, and use a hairspray to set the look in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Women's Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Use a light hand when applying styling products to avoid creating a greasy, weighed-down look.
  • Don't over-comb your hair, as this can create a flat, lifeless look.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use hairspray to help set the look in place and keep any flyaways at bay.

FAQ

Can I achieve the slicked-back look on short hair?

Yes, absolutely! The slicked-back look works well on short hair, and can help to create the illusion of length and volume.

What products should I use to achieve the slicked-back look?

A gel or pomade works best for achieving the slicked-back look. Look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

How do I avoid creating a greasy, weighed-down look?

Use a light hand when applying styling products, and make sure to comb your hair in a smooth, even motion to avoid creating any clumps or build-up.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

To keep your hair looking sleek and polished, make sure to wash it regularly and use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your strands and make them look dull and lifeless. And don't forget to trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay!

That's it for our guide on how to slick back women's short hair. We hope you found this helpful, and that you're feeling confident and glamorous with your new slicked-back look!
