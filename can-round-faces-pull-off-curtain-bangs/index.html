Can Round Faces Pull Off Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for quite some time now. It is a versatile style that looks good on most face shapes. However, those with round faces often wonder if they can pull off this look. The answer is yes! Round faces can definitely rock curtain bangs. In this article, we will show you how to style curtain bangs for round faces and give you some tips on how to make it work for you.

How To

The first step in styling curtain bangs for round faces is to determine the length of the bangs. The ideal length is just above the eyebrows, as this will help to elongate the face. You can also opt for longer bangs that reach the cheekbones, depending on your preference.

Face Shape

It is important to consider your face shape when styling curtain bangs. Round faces tend to have fuller cheeks, so you want to create angles and length in your hair to balance out the roundness. This can be achieved by having longer layers around the face, which will help to create an illusion of length.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair also plays a role in how curtain bangs will look on you. If you have thin hair, you may want to opt for shorter bangs that are less voluminous. If you have thick hair, longer bangs with more volume will complement your face shape better.

Step By Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs for round faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use a clip to secure the rest of your hair out of the way.
  4. Cut your bangs straight across, just above the eyebrows.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the front of your bangs and twist it away from your face.
  6. Use a straightener to curl the twisted section away from your face.
  7. Repeat on the other side.
  8. Use a hairspray to set your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your curtain bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.
  • Use a straightener to add curls or waves to your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I style curtain bangs myself?

Yes, you can style curtain bangs yourself. However, it is recommended that you get them cut by a professional stylist first. This will ensure that they are the right length and shape for your face.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and stylish.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still rock curtain bangs. However, you may want to opt for longer bangs that blend in with the rest of your curls.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, round faces can definitely pull off curtain bangs. With the right length and styling, this hairstyle can help to elongate the face and create a flattering look. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
