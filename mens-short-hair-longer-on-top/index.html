Mens Short Hair Longer On Top A Relaxed Guide For 2023

In this article, we will be discussing the popular mens short hair longer on top hairstyle. We will cover everything from how to achieve the look, step by step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice. This hairstyle has been trending for a while now and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. So, whether you're looking for a fresh new cut or just curious about this hairstyle, keep reading to learn more.

Description

The mens short hair longer on top hairstyle is a versatile and trendy look that is perfect for any occasion. This style involves keeping the sides and back of the hair short while leaving the top longer. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it is typically 2-4 inches long. This hairstyle is great for men who want a low-maintenance look that is still stylish and modern.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the mens short hair longer on top hairstyle, you will need to visit a professional stylist or barber. They will be able to give you the perfect cut based on your hair type and face shape. It's important to communicate with your stylist about the length and style you want, as well as any specific concerns you may have.

What to Ask For

When you go to your stylist or barber, ask for a short back and sides with a longer top. Be specific about how much longer you want the top to be and how you want it styled. You can ask for a textured look, a slicked-back look, or anything in between.

Step by Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the mens short hair longer on top hairstyle:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to the hair on top, such as a pomade or styling cream.
  3. Using a comb, create a part or push the hair back for a more textured look.
  4. Use a blow dryer to dry the hair on top, using your fingers to shape it as you go.
  5. Once the hair is dry, use a small amount of product to define the style and hold it in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To keep your mens short hair longer on top looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips:

  • Visit your stylist or barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of the cut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider getting a trim every few weeks to keep the hair on top from becoming too long and unmanageable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the mens short hair longer on top hairstyle easy to maintain?

Yes, this hairstyle is relatively low-maintenance and easy to style. With the right products and tools, you can achieve a fresh and stylish look in just a few minutes each day.

Can this hairstyle work with any hair type?

Yes, the mens short hair longer on top hairstyle can work with most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist or barber can help you determine the best cut and styling options for your hair type.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant product when using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair strong and healthy.

With these tips and advice, you can achieve and maintain the mens short hair longer on top hairstyle with ease. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
