Guys Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, guys haircuts long on top short on sides might be just the thing for you. This popular haircut trend has been around for a few years now and shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

Guys haircuts long on top short on sides are characterized by longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This gives the haircut a modern and trendy look while still being relatively easy to maintain. The length of the top hair can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually between 2-4 inches.

How to Get It

The first step in getting a guys haircut long on top short on sides is to find a reputable hairstylist or barber. Once you've found someone you trust, you can start discussing the specifics of the haircut. You'll want to decide on the length of the top hair, how short the sides should be, and whether you want any fades or undercuts.

During the haircut, the stylist will typically use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair and scissors to cut the top. They may also use a razor to add texture or layers to the top hair. Once the haircut is finished, your stylist will show you how to style it and recommend products to use.

Step by Step

1. Find a reputable hairstylist or barber
2. Discuss the specifics of the haircut
3. Decide on the length of the top hair and how short the sides should be
4. Decide on any fades or undercuts
5. During the haircut, the stylist will use clippers and scissors
6. They may also use a razor to add texture or layers to the top hair
7. Once the haircut is finished, your stylist will show you how to style it
8. Use recommended products to style your hair

Tips

- Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your stylist
- Be open to the stylist's suggestions
- Use high-quality hair products
- Use a blow dryer to add volume to the top hair
- Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut

FAQ

Q: Will this haircut work for all hair types?
A: Yes, this haircut can work for most hair types, although some may require more maintenance than others.

Q: How often should I get this haircut?
A: It's recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Q: Can I style my hair differently?
A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as slicking the top hair back or adding texture with a matte paste.

Hair Care

When it comes to taking care of your guys haircut long on top short on sides, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Second, avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Finally, get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut and keep your hair looking its best.

Overall, guys haircuts long on top short on sides are a stylish and versatile choice for men of all ages. Whether you're looking for a trendy and modern look or a classic and timeless style, this haircut is sure to impress.
