How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Chubby Face

Choosing the perfect short hairstyle for your chubby face can be a challenging task. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can easily find a style that suits you. Here are some things to consider when choosing a short hairstyle for chubby face:

Face Shape

The first thing you need to consider is your face shape. If you have a chubby face, you need a hairstyle that can complement it. Short hairstyles that have volume and layers can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can make your face look rounder.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is another factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have thin hair, go for a short hairstyle that has volume and layers. This will give your hair more body and make it appear thicker. If you have thick hair, go for a short hairstyle that can help thin it out.

Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different styles, go for a short hairstyle that can be versatile. If you're someone who likes to keep it simple, go for a classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style.

Short Hairstyle Ideas for Chubby Face 2021

Now that you know how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your chubby face, let's take a look at some hairstyle ideas:

Bob Haircut

The bob is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for chubby faces. The bob can be worn in different ways, such as a blunt bob, an angled bob, or a layered bob. The bob can also be styled with bangs or without.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and edgy hairstyle that can complement a chubby face. The pixie cut can be styled in different ways, such as a messy pixie, a slicked back pixie, or a pixie with bangs.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is another short hairstyle that can help elongate a chubby face. The layers can be cut at different lengths to add volume and texture to the hair.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Chubby Face

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve the perfect short hairstyle for chubby face:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Refer to the tips and ideas mentioned above for inspiration.

Step 2: Visit a Trusted Hairstylist

Visit a trusted hairstylist who has experience with short hairstyles. Discuss your desired look and ask for their opinion on what would suit you best.

Step 3: Maintain Your Hairstyle

Once you've achieved your desired look, maintain your hairstyle by visiting your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups. Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type to keep it healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Chubby Face

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle for chubby face:

Tip 1: Use the Right Hair Care Products

Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type. This includes shampoo, conditioner, and styling products. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals as they can damage your hair.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups. This will help maintain the shape of your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for chubby face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle for chubby face. However, make sure the bangs are not too heavy as they can make your face look rounder.

Q: Can I color my hair with a short hairstyle for chubby face?

A: Yes, you can color your hair with a short hairstyle for chubby face. However, avoid drastic changes in color as they can overwhelm your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyle for Chubby Face

Here are some hair care tips for short hairstyle for chubby face:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair soft and shiny. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tip 3: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Apply the hair mask to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time before rinsing it off.
