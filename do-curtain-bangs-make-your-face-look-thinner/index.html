Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Thinner The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curtain bangs have been trending for a while now, and it's not hard to see why. They're versatile, chic, and can complement any face shape. But, one question that keeps popping up is, do curtain bangs make your face look thinner? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and give you tips on how to achieve the perfect curtain bangs look.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted in the middle, framing your face on either side. They're called curtain bangs because they resemble the opening of a drawn curtain. Curtain bangs are longer than traditional bangs, and they're usually cut at an angle to blend in with the rest of your hair. These bangs can be styled in different ways, including straight, wavy, or curly, making them a versatile choice for any hair type.

How to Achieve Curtain Bangs

Before we dive into the question of whether curtain bangs make your face look thinner, let's first discuss how to achieve the perfect curtain bangs. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving curtain bangs is to choose the right length. The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your face shape and the look you're going for. If you have a round face, longer curtain bangs will help elongate your face. If you have a square jawline, shorter curtain bangs will soften your features. A good rule of thumb is to have your bangs end at the bridge of your nose.

Step 2: Part Your Hair in the Middle

Once you've decided on the length of your curtain bangs, it's time to part your hair in the middle. Use a comb to create a straight part that runs from your forehead to the back of your head. This will create the "curtain" effect.

Step 3: Trim Your Bangs

Now that your hair is parted in the middle, it's time to trim your bangs. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure that both sides are even.

Step 4: Blend Your Bangs

Once your bangs are trimmed, it's time to blend them in with the rest of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the top of your bangs and pull it up. Cut the hair at an angle to blend it in with the rest of your hair. Repeat this process on both sides until your bangs are seamlessly blended in with the rest of your hair.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Now that you know how to achieve curtain bangs let's discuss some tips for styling them:

Tip 1: Use a Round Brush

To achieve a smooth and sleek look with your curtain bangs, use a round brush when blow-drying. This will help create volume and bounce in your bangs.

Tip 2: Add Texture

If you want to add some texture to your curtain bangs, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help create a messy and effortless look.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles with your curtain bangs. You can wear them straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your mood and the occasion.

FAQs

Q: Do curtain bangs make your face look thinner?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can make your face look thinner. They create a vertical line down the center of your face, making it appear longer and slimmer.

Q: Can curtain bangs work for any face shape?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can work for any face shape. The key is to choose the right length and style that complements your features.

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Lastly, it's essential to take care of your curtain bangs to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

When styling your curtain bangs with heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant. This will help prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep your curtain bangs clean and free of oil and dirt.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curtain bangs moisturized and healthy.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can make your face look thinner by creating a vertical line down the center of your face. With the right length and style, they can complement any face shape. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and remember to take care of your curtain bangs with proper hair care.
