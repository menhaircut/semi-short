How To Ask For The Edgar Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a fan of the iconic Edgar haircut? Do you want to know how to ask for it at your next salon appointment? Look no further, as we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to ask for the Edgar haircut.

Description: What is the Edgar Haircut?

The Edgar haircut, popularized by Mexican reggaeton artist Edgar Hernandez, is a popular style that features a short, buzzed cut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top styled forward in a messy, textured look. This style is known for its versatility and low-maintenance upkeep, making it a popular choice for many men.

How to Ask for the Edgar Haircut

When asking for the Edgar haircut, it's important to communicate clearly with your stylist to ensure you get the desired look. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Bring a Picture

Bringing a picture of the Edgar haircut to your stylist can help them understand exactly what you're looking for. You can search for pictures on social media or Google, or use a picture of Edgar Hernandez himself.

Step 2: Use the Right Terminology

When describing the Edgar haircut, use specific terminology to avoid any confusion. Ask for a "short, buzzed cut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top styled forward."

Step 3: Discuss Texture and Length

The texture and length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference. Discuss with your stylist whether you want a messy, textured look or a sleeker, more polished finish. You can also discuss the length of the hair on top, whether you want it shorter or longer.

Step 4: Ask for Maintenance Tips

The Edgar haircut is known for its low-maintenance upkeep, but it's still important to ask your stylist for tips on how to keep your hair looking its best. They may recommend specific products or styling techniques to maintain the texture and shape of your hair.

Tips for the Edgar Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the Edgar haircut:

  • Regular trims are important to maintain the shape and texture of the haircut.
  • Use a matte styling product to achieve the messy, textured look on top.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find the right look for you.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing the length and style of the haircut.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the Edgar haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the Edgar haircut can be adapted to suit different hair types and lengths.

Q: How often should I get a trim for the Edgar haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of the haircut.

Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

A: Yes, the hair on top can be styled in different ways, depending on personal preference and the occasion.

Hair Care for the Edgar Haircut

Proper hair care is important to maintain the health and appearance of the Edgar haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners, as these can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair on top hydrated and soft.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Now that you know how to ask for the Edgar haircut, it's time to book your next salon appointment and try out this iconic style for yourself. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist and experiment with different lengths and textures to find the perfect look for you.
