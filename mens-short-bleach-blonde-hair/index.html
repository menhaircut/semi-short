Mens Short Bleach Blonde Hair Tips How To And Hair Care

If you're thinking of going for a bold and daring look, you might want to try mens short bleach blonde hair. This hairstyle can be a head-turner and can make you look edgy and stylish. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about mens short bleach blonde hair, including tips, how-to, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Mens short bleach blonde hair is a hairstyle that involves bleaching your hair to a bright white or platinum blonde color. This hairstyle is popular among men who want to stand out or those who want to experiment with their looks. It's a bold and daring hairstyle that requires maintenance and upkeep.

How to

If you want to achieve mens short bleach blonde hair, you'll need to bleach your hair first. Bleaching involves using a chemical process to remove the natural color of your hair. Here's how to do it:

Step by Step

  1. Start by preparing your hair. Make sure it's clean and dry.
  2. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the bleach.
  3. Mix the bleach powder and developer according to the instructions on the packaging.
  4. Apply the bleach mixture to your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  5. Leave the bleach on your hair for the recommended amount of time. This can be anywhere from 20 to 45 minutes, depending on your hair type and the level of lightness you want to achieve.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  7. Apply a toner to your hair to remove any yellow or orange tones. Leave the toner on for 10 to 15 minutes, then rinse it out.
  8. Condition your hair to restore moisture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when going for mens short bleach blonde hair:

  • Make sure to choose a reputable hair colorist who has experience in bleaching hair.
  • Be prepared for the upkeep and maintenance that comes with this hairstyle. You'll need to touch up your roots every few weeks to maintain the color.
  • Invest in a good quality purple shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair from turning yellow.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens short bleach blonde hair:

Is it safe to bleach my hair?

Bleaching can be damaging to your hair if not done properly. It's important to have a professional do it for you to minimize the risk of damage.

Do I need to bleach my hair multiple times to achieve the desired color?

It depends on your hair type and the level of lightness you want to achieve. Some people may need to bleach their hair multiple times to achieve the desired color.

How often do I need to touch up my roots?

You'll need to touch up your roots every few weeks to maintain the color.

Hair Care

After bleaching your hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a good quality purple shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair from turning yellow.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Mens short bleach blonde hair can be a bold and daring hairstyle that can make you stand out from the crowd. Just make sure to have it done by a professional and take care of your hair after bleaching to prevent damage and breakage. With the right maintenance and care, you can rock this edgy and stylish look.
