How To Do 40S Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a vintage and glamorous hairstyle? Look no further than 40s waves! These classic waves were popular during the 1940s and can be recreated today for a timeless look. In this post, we'll guide you through how to do 40s waves in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Description

40s waves are characterized by soft, voluminous curls that frame the face. This hairstyle was a popular choice for Hollywood starlets during the 1940s and has since become a timeless classic. 40s waves can be achieved on any hair length, but work best on shoulder-length to long hair.

How to Do 40s Waves

Follow these simple steps to achieve 40s waves:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, then apply a heat protectant spray. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, then part it to one side.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head. The smaller the sections, the tighter the waves will be.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a 1-inch curling iron, start curling each section of hair. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the section of hair around the barrel, away from your face. Hold for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, use a paddle brush to gently brush out the curls. This will create soft, voluminous waves.

Step 5: Set Your Style

Finish off your look with a medium-hold hairspray to set the style in place. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a flower or headband, for an extra touch of glamour.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect 40s waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Start with small sections of hair for tighter waves
  • For looser waves, use a larger curling iron
  • Brush out your curls gently to avoid ruining the shape
  • Use a medium-hold hairspray to keep your style in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 40s waves:

Can 40s waves be achieved on short hair?

While 40s waves work best on shoulder-length to long hair, they can still be achieved on shorter hair. Use a smaller curling iron and create tighter curls to achieve the same look.

How long will 40s waves last?

With the right hair products, 40s waves can last up to 2-3 days. Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.

Can I achieve 40s waves without heat?

While heat styling is the easiest way to achieve 40s waves, you can also use foam rollers or braids to create similar waves without using heat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times per week
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Now you know how to do 40s waves! Follow these steps and tips to achieve a timeless and glamorous hairstyle.
