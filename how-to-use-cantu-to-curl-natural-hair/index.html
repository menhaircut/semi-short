How To Use Cantu To Curl Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to enhance your natural curls, Cantu products can help. Cantu is a popular brand that offers a wide range of hair care products that are perfect for natural hair. In this post, we'll show you how to use Cantu to curl natural hair.

Description

Before we dive into the steps, let's take a quick look at what Cantu is and how it works. Cantu is a line of hair care products that are designed to moisturize and nourish natural hair. Their products are made with natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and honey. When used correctly, Cantu can help enhance your natural curls, reduce frizz, and add shine to your hair.

How to

To use Cantu to curl natural hair, you'll need a few key products:

  • Cantu Coconut Curling Cream
  • Cantu Curl Activator Cream
  • Cantu Define & Shine Custard
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Small hair clips
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer

Once you have all the necessary products, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you'll need to wash and condition it. Use a gentle shampoo to cleanse your scalp and a moisturizing conditioner to hydrate your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and gently towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Cantu Coconut Curling Cream

After you've towel-dried your hair, apply a generous amount of Cantu Coconut Curling Cream to your hair. Use your fingers to work the cream through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Apply Cantu Curl Activator Cream

Once you've applied the Coconut Curling Cream, it's time to apply the Cantu Curl Activator Cream. Apply a small amount of the cream to your hair and use your fingers to work it through your hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Apply Cantu Define & Shine Custard

To further enhance your curls, apply a small amount of Cantu Define & Shine Custard to your hair. This will help add shine and hold to your curls. Use your fingers to work the custard through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 5: Comb and Clip Your Hair

Once you've applied all the products, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots. Once your hair is detangled, use small hair clips to section your hair.

Step 6: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. Gently dry your hair, using the diffuser to enhance your curls. Be sure to move the diffuser around your head to ensure that all your curls are evenly dried.

Step 7: Remove Hair Clips

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the hair clips and use your fingers to gently separate your curls.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using Cantu to curl natural hair:

  • Use a small amount of product at first and add more as needed.
  • Don't use too much heat, as this can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Experiment with different Cantu products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use Cantu products on relaxed hair?

A: Yes, Cantu products can be used on relaxed hair. However, they are specifically designed for natural hair, so you may need to use a bit less product if your hair is relaxed.

Q: How often should I use Cantu products?

A: This depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. As a general rule, it's best to use Cantu products once or twice a week.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and using Cantu products, you can keep your natural curls looking beautiful and healthy.
