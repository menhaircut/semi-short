What Haircut Suits Long Face Shape

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face shape, it can sometimes be challenging to find the perfect haircut that complements your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of options available that can help you accentuate your best features and create a balanced look. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best haircuts for long face shapes and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a long chin. If you have this face shape, you should choose a haircut that adds width to your face and makes it look more proportional. The ideal haircut should also be able to balance out your features and create a harmonious look.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long face shape, the key is to add width to the face. You can achieve this by adding layers to your hair, creating volume at the sides, or adding bangs to your hair. The ideal haircut should also be able to complement your face shape and enhance your best features.

Step by step

The first step in getting the perfect haircut for a long face shape is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are some popular haircuts for long face shapes:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for a long face shape. It adds volume to the sides of the face and creates a balanced look. The layers also help to create movement and texture in the hair.

2. Long Layers

Long layers can also work well for a long face shape. They add volume and dimension to the hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. Long layers also help to frame the face and create a more balanced look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for a long face shape. They add width to the forehead and help to balance out the face. Side-swept bangs can also help to soften the features and create a more feminine look.

Tips

When getting a haircut for a long face shape, it’s important to consider your hair type as well. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter haircut or one with more layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a longer haircut with fewer layers to avoid adding too much volume.

FAQ

What hairstyles should be avoided for a long face shape?

Hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, such as high ponytails or top knots, should be avoided for a long face shape. These hairstyles can make the face look even longer and accentuate its narrowness.

Can I wear my hair straight with a long face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a long face shape. However, it’s important to avoid hairstyles that are too flat and straight, as they can make the face look longer. Adding layers or volume to the hair can help to create a more balanced look.

Hair Care

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut for your long face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can create a beautiful look that complements your long face shape and enhances your best features.
