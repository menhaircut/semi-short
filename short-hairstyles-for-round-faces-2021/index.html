Short Hairstyles For Round Faces 2021 Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a short haircut that will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. In this blog post, we'll take a look at some of the best short hairstyles for round faces in 2021.

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. While this face shape is often associated with youthfulness and femininity, it can be difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters it. Short haircuts are a great option for round faces, as they can help to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for your round face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and shape of your hair. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

Consider the Length

Short haircuts that are chin-length or shorter are ideal for round faces, as they can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape. Avoid haircuts that fall below the chin, as they can make your face look wider and rounder.

Choose the Right Texture

If you have thin or fine hair, a textured short haircut can add volume and movement to your hair, making your face look slimmer. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can help to create a sleek, polished look.

Find the Right Shape

The shape of your haircut can also play a role in how flattering it is for your round face. A layered bob, for example, can help to create the illusion of length and volume, while a short pixie cut can accentuate your facial features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly with cool water to help seal the hair cuticle and add shine.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Use a soft, absorbent towel to gently blot your hair dry. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse or styling cream. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create volume and movement.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh and polished, it's important to get regular trims. Aim to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in shape.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat tools to style your hair, such as a curling iron or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for round faces?

Some of the best short hairstyles for round faces include layered bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical haircuts.

Can I wear bangs with a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short haircut if you have a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs, as they can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for my round face?

Avoid haircuts that fall below the chin, as they can make your face look wider and rounder. Also, avoid blunt cuts that don't have any layers or texture, as they can accentuate your facial features.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Round Faces

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly with high-quality products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Avoid Overstyling

While it's tempting to style your short haircut every day, overstyling can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling and avoid using too much product.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

If you spend a lot of time in the sun, be sure to protect your hair with a hat or scarf. You can also use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to help prevent damage from UV rays.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and flattering short haircut for your round face in 2021. Remember to choose the right length, texture, and shape for your hair, and take good care of it to keep it looking its best.
