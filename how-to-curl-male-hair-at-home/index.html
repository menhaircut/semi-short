How To Curl Male Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curling hair is not just for women. In fact, more and more men are experimenting with different hairstyles that require curls. Whether you want beachy waves or tight ringlets, you can achieve the look you want without going to a salon. In this article, we will show you how to curl male hair at home with ease.

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl Male Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curling Male Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Curl Male Hair

Before we get started, it's important to note that you will need the right tools to achieve the perfect curls. You will need a curling iron or wand, heat protectant spray, and hair styling products such as hairspray or mousse. Once you have these items, you can follow these steps to curl male hair at home.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. If you have naturally curly hair, you can skip this step. However, if your hair is straight or wavy, you will need to blow dry it to get rid of any moisture.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, focusing on the ends where the heat damage is most likely to occur.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. The size of the sections will depend on the size of the curls you want to achieve. For tighter curls, use smaller sections. For looser waves, use larger sections.

Step 4: Start Curling Your Hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Apply Hair Styling Products

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, apply hair styling products such as hairspray or mousse to hold the curls in place. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create the style you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

To recap, here is a step-by-step guide on how to curl male hair at home:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Apply heat protectant spray
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Start curling your hair
  5. Apply hair styling products

Tips for Curling Male Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with adjustable heat settings to prevent heat damage
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase it as needed
  • Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair before curling it
  • Twist the hair before wrapping it around the curling iron or wand for a more natural look
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls

FAQs

Can men use the same curling irons as women?

Yes, there is no difference between curling irons for men and women. You can use any curling iron or wand that is suitable for your hair type and desired style.

Do you need to use a specific type of hairspray for male hair?

No, any hairspray that is suitable for your hair type and desired style will work. Look for hairsprays that provide a strong hold without leaving a sticky residue.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a different approach than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and vibrant curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause heat damage

With these tips and tricks, you can easily curl male hair at home and achieve the look you want. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep your curls looking healthy and vibrant.
