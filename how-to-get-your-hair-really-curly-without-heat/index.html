How To Get Your Hair Really Curly Without Heat In 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair has always been in trend and has been considered one of the most attractive hairstyles. However, using heat to curl your hair can be damaging, and it can also be time-consuming. Luckily, there are natural ways to get your hair really curly without heat. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get your hair really curly without heat.

How to Get Your Hair Really Curly Without Heat

To get your hair really curly without heat, you need to follow these steps:

1. Use Curl Enhancing Products

Using curl-enhancing products like mousse, gel, or curl-defining cream can help you get really curly hair without heat. These products work by defining your natural curls and providing hold.

2. Twist Your Hair

Twisting your hair into small sections and securing them with bobby pins can help you achieve natural curls. Leave the twists in your hair overnight and release them in the morning for bouncy curls. You can also use a hair tie to create a bun, which will give you tighter curls.

3. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are an excellent alternative to using heat to curl your hair. Simply wrap small sections of your hair around the flexi rods and secure them in place. Leave them in overnight, and in the morning, you'll have bouncy curls.

4. Use Foam Rollers

Foam rollers are another great option for getting curly hair without heat. Roll small sections of your hair around the foam rollers and secure them in place. Leave them in overnight, and you'll wake up to bouncy curls.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to get really curly hair without heat:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help define your natural curls.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Products

Apply mousse, gel, or curl-defining cream to your wet hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Twist, Flexi Rods or Foam Rollers

Choose your preferred method, twist your hair, use flexi rods or foam rollers to curl your hair. If you're using foam rollers or flexi rods, roll your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. If you're twisting your hair, twist small sections and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 4: Leave it Overnight

Leave your hair wrapped up in your chosen method overnight. This will give your curls time to set.

Step 5: Release Your Curls

Release your curls in the morning and use your fingers to separate them. Don't brush your curls, as this will cause frizz.

Tips for Getting Really Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get really curly hair without heat:

  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.
  • Don't touch your hair: Don't touch your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on silk: Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.
  • Use a diffuser: If you're using a hairdryer, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair gently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get curly hair without using products?

While it's possible to get curly hair without using products, using curl-enhancing products can help define your curls and provide hold.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last for a few days if you take care of them properly. Use a silk pillowcase, don't touch your hair too much, and avoid using heat styling tools.

Can I use this method on relaxed hair?

Yes, you can use this method on relaxed hair. Just make sure to use products that are designed for relaxed hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Don't wash your hair too often: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to detangle your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair: Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools: Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so try to avoid using them as much as possible.

Now that you know how to get your hair really curly without heat, you can rock those bouncy curls without causing damage to your hair. Follow these tips, take care of your hair, and enjoy your new curly hairstyle!
