Best Pomade For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right hair products that work for your hair type. Pomade is a popular styling product that can help you achieve your desired hairstyle. However, not all pomades are created equal, and finding the best one for your hair can be a daunting task. In this article, we will discuss the best pomade for thick curly hair, how to use it, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Pomade for Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide on Applying Pomade

Description

Pomade is a versatile hair styling product that can help you achieve a variety of hairstyles. It is especially useful for those with thick curly hair as it can help tame frizz and define curls. However, not all pomades are created equal, and some can leave your hair feeling greasy or weighed down. When looking for the best pomade for thick curly hair, look for one that is lightweight, non-greasy, and provides a strong hold.

How to Use Pomade for Thick Curly Hair

Before using pomade, it's important to start with clean, damp hair. This will help the pomade distribute evenly throughout your hair and provide a stronger hold.

Start by taking a small amount of pomade and rubbing it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair in sections, focusing on the areas that need the most control or definition.

Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair, making sure to avoid applying too much to any one area.

Once you have achieved your desired style, allow the pomade to dry completely before touching your hair. This will help ensure that your hairstyle stays in place all day.

Step-by-Step Guide on Applying Pomade

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to apply pomade for thick curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair in sections, focusing on the areas that need the most control or definition.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Allow the pomade to dry completely before touching your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to using the right pomade, there are several tips you can follow to maintain healthy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best pomade for thick curly hair?

The best pomade for thick curly hair is one that is lightweight, non-greasy, and provides a strong hold. Look for pomades that contain natural ingredients such as beeswax or shea butter, which can help nourish and protect your hair.

How often should I use pomade on my hair?

It's best to use pomade on your hair only when necessary. Using too much pomade too often can lead to buildup and make your hair look greasy.

Can I use pomade on wet hair?

It's best to use pomade on damp or dry hair. Using it on wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Can pomade damage my hair?

Using pomade on your hair in moderation is unlikely to cause damage. However, using too much pomade too often can lead to buildup and make your hair look greasy.

In conclusion, finding the best pomade for thick curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right product and techniques, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles and maintain healthy hair. Follow the tips outlined in this article, and don't be afraid to experiment with different pomades to find the one that works best for you.
