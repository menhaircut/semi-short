How To Slick Back Hair Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're looking for a timeless hairstyle that exudes sophistication and class, you can't go wrong with a slick back hairdo. However, achieving this look can be a bit challenging, especially if you don't want to use chemical-laden hair products. That's why we've put together this guide on how to slick back hair naturally.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Hair Naturally
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

How to Slick Back Hair Naturally

To achieve a slicked-back hairstyle without using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, you'll need to use natural ingredients that provide hold and shine. Here are the ingredients you'll need:
  • Aloe vera gel
  • Coconut oil
  • Water
  • Essential oils (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your ingredients, here's a step-by-step guide on how to slick back your hair naturally:
  1. Mix 1 tablespoon of aloe vera gel, 1 tablespoon of coconut oil, and 1 tablespoon of water in a small bowl.
  2. Optional: add a few drops of your favorite essential oil for fragrance.
  3. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  4. Apply the mixture to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  5. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting at the front and working your way back.
  6. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingertips.
  7. Let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair Naturally

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:
  • Experiment with different ratios of aloe vera gel, coconut oil, and water to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before slicking it back.
  • If you have long hair, consider braiding it or putting it in a bun before applying the mixture.
  • Don't use too much of the mixture, or your hair may become greasy and weighed down.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before leaving the house to avoid a wet, slicked-back look.

FAQs

Q: Can I use other oils instead of coconut oil?
A: Yes, you can use any oil that provides hold and shine, such as argan oil or jojoba oil. Q: How often should I slick back my hair?
A: It's best to avoid slicking back your hair every day, as it can cause breakage and damage. Aim for once or twice a week at most. Q: Can I use this mixture on curly hair?
A: Yes, you can use this mixture on any hair type, but you may need to use more of it to achieve the desired hold.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best while slicked back, here are some hair care tips:
  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair ties or clips that can create dents in your slicked-back hairstyle.

Conclusion

Slicking back your hair naturally is a great way to achieve a classic, sophisticated look without exposing your hair to harsh chemicals. By following our step-by-step guide and using natural ingredients like aloe vera gel and coconut oil, you can achieve a sleek and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Just remember to take care of your hair by using gentle hair care products and avoiding damaging hot tools.
