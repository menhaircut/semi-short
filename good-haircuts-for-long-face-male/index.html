Good Haircuts For Long Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a man with a long face and having a hard time finding the perfect haircut that compliments your features? Look no further, because we've got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best haircuts for long face male that will enhance your facial structure and make you look your best. So, let's dive in!

Description

Having a long face can be a bit tricky when it comes to finding the right haircut. You'll want to choose something that will add width to your face and balance out your features. But don't worry, there are plenty of great haircuts for long face male that will do just that.

How To

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to look for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of your face. Some great options include:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well for men with long faces. It adds volume to the sides of your face and creates a balanced look. To achieve this style, simply part your hair on one side and comb it over to the other side. Use a bit of product to hold it in place.

2. The Pompadour

The pompadour is another great option for men with long faces. This style adds height to the top of your head, which balances out the length of your face. To achieve this look, sweep your hair upwards and back, using a bit of product to hold it in place.

3. The Buzz Cut

If you prefer a shorter haircut, the buzz cut is a great option for men with long faces. This style is low-maintenance and easy to maintain. It also adds volume to the sides of your face, which can help balance out your features.

Step By Step

To achieve the perfect haircut for your long face, follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to look for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of your face.

Step 2: Research Haircuts

Once you know your face shape, research different haircuts that work well for your features. Look for styles that add volume to the sides of your face and balance out your features.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're not sure which haircut is right for you, consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that complements your features and fits your personal style.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut, be sure to maintain it properly. This includes regular trims and using the right products to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect haircut for your long face:

  • Choose a style that complements your personal style
  • Consider your hair type when choosing a haircut
  • Use the right products to maintain your haircut
  • Don't be afraid to try something new

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long face male?

Some of the best haircuts for a long face male include the side part, pompadour, and buzz cut.

How do I know if I have a long face?

A long face is characterized by a high forehead and a narrow chin. If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a long face.

Can I still wear my hair long if I have a long face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have a long face. Just be sure to add layers and volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your long face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. This includes using the right products for your hair type, washing and conditioning regularly, and getting regular trims to maintain your haircut.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for a long face male can be a bit tricky, but with the right research and guidance, you can find the perfect style that complements your features and fits your personal style. Use the tips and advice in this article to help you choose the perfect haircut and keep your hair looking great.
