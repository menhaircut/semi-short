How To Cut Men s Long Curly Hair At Home

Written by Bardi Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with long curly hair, you may be wondering how to cut your hair at home. Going to the salon can be expensive, and sometimes you just want to try something new. Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a great haircut. In this article, we'll go over how to cut men's long curly hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut Men's Long Curly Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Long Curly Hair at Home
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Men's Long Curly Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather some tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Hair ties or clips

Make sure your scissors are sharp and your clippers are fully charged before you start.

How to Cut Men's Long Curly Hair at Home

Now that you have your tools, it's time to get started. Here's how to cut men's long curly hair at home:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to see the length and shape.
  2. Section your hair. Use hair ties or clips to separate your hair into manageable sections. Start with the top section and work your way down.
  3. Start cutting. Use your scissors to trim the ends of your hair. Start with small snips and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.
  4. Use your clippers. Use your clippers with a guard to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a tapered look.
  5. Blend the sides and back. Use your clippers without a guard to blend the sides and back with the longer hair on top.
  6. Check your work. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Trim the top. Use your scissors to trim the top section of your hair. Start with small snips and work your way up.
  8. Blend the top. Use your scissors to blend the top section with the sides and back.
  9. Check your work again. Use a mirror to check the overall shape and length of your haircut.
  10. Style your hair. Use a styling product to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Curly Hair at Home

Here are some tips to make cutting men's long curly hair at home easier:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors and fully charged clippers.
  • Section your hair and work in manageable sections.
  • Start with small snips and work your way up.
  • Check your work in a mirror.
  • Be patient and take your time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair if I have long curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if you have long curly hair. Follow the steps in this article to give yourself a great haircut.

How often should I cut my long curly hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

You should cut your hair dry. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to see the length and shape.

Hair Care for Men's Long Curly Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care for men's long curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like flat irons and blow dryers, too often.
  • Air dry your hair or use a diffuser on low heat.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut men's long curly hair at home and take care of your hair afterwards. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
