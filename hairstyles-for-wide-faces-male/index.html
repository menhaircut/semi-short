Best Hairstyles For Wide Faces Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

As men, we all want to look our best, and having a wide face can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. But don't worry – there are plenty of hairstyles that can complement a wide face shape. In this post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks to help you find the best hairstyles for wide faces male.

How to Determine if You Have a Wide Face

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for wide faces male, it's important to determine if you actually have a wide face. The easiest way to do this is to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, then you have a wide face.

Best Hairstyles for Wide Faces Male

Short and Sweet

Short hairstyles are a great option for men with wide faces. A simple buzz cut or crew cut can help to balance out your face shape. If you prefer something a bit longer, a textured crop or French crop can work well too. Just make sure to keep the sides short to avoid adding extra width to your face.

Slicked Back

If you're looking for a more polished look, a slicked-back hairstyle can work well for wide faces. The key is to keep the sides short and the top longer, and use a bit of styling product to slick back your hair. This will help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Side Part

A classic side part is another great option for men with wide faces. This style works well with both short and longer hair, and can help to create a more symmetrical look. Just make sure to keep the part off-center to avoid adding extra width to your face.

Fringe

If you prefer a bit of fringe, a side-swept fringe can work well for wide faces. This style adds some height to your forehead and helps to balance out your face shape. Just make sure to keep the sides short to avoid adding extra width.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality styling product to help hold your hair in place
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay
  • Use a comb or brush to help shape your hair
  • If you have longer hair, consider using a hair tie or clip to keep it off your face

Frequently Asked Questions

Can men with wide faces pull off long hair?

While shorter hairstyles are generally recommended for men with wide faces, that doesn't mean you can't have longer hair. Just make sure to keep the sides short and the top longer to help balance out your face shape.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a wide face?

Not necessarily. A side-swept fringe can actually work well for wide faces – just make sure to keep the sides short to avoid adding extra width.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on your hair type and style, but most men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their style.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair hydrated
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your wide face and keep your hair looking great. Remember, it's all about finding balance and creating a symmetrical look.
