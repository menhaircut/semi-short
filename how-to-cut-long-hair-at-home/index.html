How To Cut Long Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a salon-worthy cut at home. If you're looking to trim your long locks, we've created a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect cut. In this article, we'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the best techniques for cutting long hair. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

Tools you'll need

Before you start cutting your long hair, it's important to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water

How to cut long hair at home

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to start cutting your long hair at home. Follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using your comb, create a middle part down the back of your head. Then, section your hair into two equal parts and clip them out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the back

Unclip one section of your hair and comb it straight down your back. Hold the section between your fingers and cut straight across using your sharp haircutting scissors. Repeat this on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your fingers and cut at a slight angle, starting at the length you cut in the back and angling down towards the front. Repeat this on the other side.

Step 5: Check for evenness

Once you've cut all sections of your hair, check for evenness by combing it out and looking in the mirror. If you notice any unevenness, use your scissors to trim any longer pieces until you achieve your desired length.

Tips for cutting long hair at home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start by cutting less than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a sharp pair of haircutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you're feeling fatigued. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks as needed.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're struggling to cut the back of your hair, ask a friend or family member to help.

FAQ: Cutting long hair at home

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, it's possible to cut your own hair at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and techniques to achieve a salon-worthy cut.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go back and trim any longer pieces until you achieve your desired length.

How often should I cut my long hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. However, you can also trim your hair at home as needed.

Hair care after cutting long hair at home

After cutting your long hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful.
