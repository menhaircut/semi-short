How To Cut Hair For Oval Face A Guide In 2023

If you have an oval face, then you are in luck! This face shape is versatile and can handle any haircut style. However, there are certain haircuts that can enhance and complement the oval face shape. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut hair for oval face shape and provide some tips on hair care.

  • How to Cut Hair for Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair for Oval Face

How to Cut Hair for Oval Face

The key to cutting hair for an oval face is to focus on the features that you want to enhance. Since the oval face shape is balanced, you can experiment with different haircuts and styles. However, the following haircuts are the most flattering for oval face shape:

  • Long layers
  • Bob haircut
  • Soft bangs
  • Shoulder-length hair
  • Pixie cut

These haircuts help to accentuate the oval face shape and create a balance between the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before cutting hair for oval face shape, make sure that the hair is clean and dry. Follow these steps to cut hair for oval face shape:

  1. Start by dividing the hair into sections. The hair should be divided into three sections - the top, middle, and bottom.
  2. Start cutting the hair at the back. Cut the hair in a straight line from the bottom to the top.
  3. Move to the sides and cut the hair at an angle. The hair should be angled towards the face to frame it.
  4. Trim the front section of the hair. Cut the hair in layers to create movement and volume.
  5. Finish the haircut by trimming the ends to create a polished look.

Remember to cut the hair slowly and carefully. Always start by cutting less hair and then trim more if needed.

Tips for Cutting Hair for Oval Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair for oval face shape:

  • Avoid cutting the hair too short as it can make the face look longer.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure clean cuts.
  • When cutting layers, start at the cheekbones to create a natural look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair for oval face shape:

Q: Can oval face shape handle short haircuts?

A: Yes! Oval face shape can handle short haircuts like pixie cut, bob haircut, and layered haircut.

Q: Are bangs suitable for oval face shape?

A: Yes, soft bangs are suitable for oval face shape. They help to frame the face and create a balance between the forehead and cheekbones.

Hair Care for Oval Face

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shape:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair and make it look dry.
  • Trim the ends of the hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep the hair healthy and shiny.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep the hair healthy from the inside.

In conclusion, cutting hair for oval face shape is easy and versatile. Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for you. Remember to keep the hair healthy and maintain it regularly for a polished look.
