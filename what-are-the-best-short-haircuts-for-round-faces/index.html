What Are The Best Short Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Short haircuts are a great option as they can help to elongate the face and add definition to your features. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for round faces.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape and fuller cheeks. The key to finding the perfect haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. Short haircuts that add height and volume at the crown can help to elongate the face. Asymmetrical cuts and side-swept bangs can also help to create angles and enhance your features.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it is important to consider your hair texture and personal style. A consultation with your stylist can help to determine which cut will work best for you. Here are some of the best short haircuts for round faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for round faces. It is a low-maintenance style that can be customized to suit your face shape. A longer, textured pixie cut can help to elongate the face, while a shorter, choppy cut can add volume and definition.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a versatile short haircut that can work for a variety of face shapes, including round faces. A chin-length bob with layers can help to create angles and add definition to your features. A bob with side-swept bangs can also help to elongate the face.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a bold and edgy short haircut that can work well for round faces. The longer side of the cut can help to create the illusion of length, while the shorter side can add volume and definition.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short haircut for a round face:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine which cut will work best for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Bring pictures of short haircuts that you like to your appointment.
  3. Discuss the length, layers, and style of the cut with your stylist.
  4. Ask for side-swept bangs or an asymmetrical cut to add angles and definition.
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline as they can make the face look wider.
  • Add layers and texture to your haircut to create movement and volume.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a short haircut.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs or an asymmetrical cut to create angles and enhance your features.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and angles. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and asymmetrical cuts can all work well for round faces.

Should I get bangs with a short haircut for a round face?

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face and create angles. Consider getting bangs with your short haircut if you want to enhance your features.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage. Apply a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add volume and texture to your hair. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for round faces, you can confidently choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Consult with your stylist and consider your personal style to find the perfect short haircut for you.
