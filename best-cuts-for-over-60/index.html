Best Cuts For Over 60 A Guide To Age Appropriate Hairstyles

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best cuts for over 60! As we age, it's important to find hairstyles that are age-appropriate and complement our features. In this article, we'll go over how to choose the right cut for your face shape and hair texture, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Cut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right cut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are four main face shapes: round, square, heart, and oval. Each face shape has certain hairstyles that look best. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round faces: opt for longer layers and side-swept bangs to elongate the face.
  • Square faces: soft, wavy styles with layers around the face can soften angular features.
  • Heart faces: side-swept bangs and chin-length cuts can balance out a pointed chin.
  • Oval faces: lucky you! Most hairstyles look great on oval faces.

How to Choose the Right Cut for Your Hair Texture

In addition to your face shape, your hair texture also plays a role in determining the right cut for you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Fine hair: shorter cuts with layers can add volume and texture.
  • Thick hair: longer cuts with layers can help to thin out the hair and make it more manageable.
  • Curly hair: longer cuts with layers can help to tame curls and prevent frizz.
  • Straight hair: medium-length cuts with layers can add movement and texture.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Cut

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, here's a step by step guide to choosing the right cut:

  1. Determine your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Browse hairstyles that are recommended for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser.
  4. Ask your hairdresser for their opinion on what would look best on you.
  5. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.
  6. Choose a cut that you feel comfortable with and that complements your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've chosen the perfect cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQ About Best Cuts for Over 60

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best cuts for over 60:

What is the most flattering hairstyle for an older woman?

The most flattering hairstyle for an older woman is one that complements her features and makes her feel confident. Some popular styles for older women include bob haircuts, pixie cuts, and layered cuts.

Can older women wear long hair?

Yes, older women can certainly wear long hair! However, longer hair may require more maintenance and styling time. It's important to choose a style that works for your lifestyle and hair texture.

Should older women have bangs?

Whether or not to have bangs is a personal choice. Bangs can be a great way to frame the face and add a youthful touch, but they can also require more maintenance. If you're considering bangs, talk to your hairdresser about which type of bangs would work best for you.

Conclusion

Choosing the best cut for over 60 can be a fun and exciting process! By following these guidelines, you can find a style that complements your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair with a good hair care routine, and don't be afraid to try something new!
