How To Get 50S Style Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a fan of vintage fashion, you’ve probably admired the glamorous, bouncy curls that were all the rage in the 1950s. Fortunately, you don’t need to be a Hollywood starlet to achieve this iconic look! In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to get 50s style curls in just a few easy steps.

Description

50s style curls are defined by their tight, bouncy ringlets that frame the face and add volume to the hair. This look was popularized by stars like Marilyn Monroe and Elizabeth Taylor, and has remained a classic look for decades.

How to Get 50s Style Curls

Follow these steps to achieve perfect 50s style curls:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly, making sure it’s completely dry before you begin. It’s best to start with clean hair so that your curls will hold their shape better.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before you start curling, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. You can find heat protectant sprays at most drugstores and beauty supply stores.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Pin each section up with a clip to keep it out of the way.

Step 4: Curl each section

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron, starting at the base and working your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way up your head.

Step 5: Brush out your curls

Once all of your hair is curled, use a soft brush to gently brush out your curls. This will help to create the bouncy, voluminous look that is characteristic of 50s style curls.

Step 6: Set your curls with hairspray

Finish off your look by spraying your hair with a light hold hairspray. This will help your curls to stay in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s style curls:

  • Use a smaller curling iron to create tighter curls
  • Don’t curl your hair all the way to the roots – leave a few inches of hair uncurled at the top to create a more natural look
  • Use a soft brush to brush out your curls, rather than a comb, which can create frizz
  • Experiment with different hairsprays to find one that works best for your hair type

FAQ

Can I achieve 50s style curls on short hair?

Absolutely! You may need to use a smaller curling iron to create tighter curls, but you can definitely achieve this look on shorter hair.

Will 50s style curls work on all hair types?

Yes, but some hair types may require more prep work to achieve the desired look. If you have very fine or straight hair, you may need to use a volumizing mousse before you start curling to add texture and hold to your hair.

How long will my 50s style curls last?

With the right prep work and hairspray, your curls should last all day. However, if you plan to wear your curls for a special event, it’s a good idea to do a test run beforehand to see how long they hold up.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to use a heat protectant before you curl your hair. You should also avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage to your hair over time. Finally, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
