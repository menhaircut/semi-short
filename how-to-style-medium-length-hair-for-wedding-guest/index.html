How To Style Medium Length Hair For Wedding Guest

Attending a wedding is a joyous occasion, but figuring out what to wear and how to style your hair can be stressful. If you have medium length hair, don't worry! There are many hairstyles that can make you look stunning and elegant. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to style medium length hair for wedding guests.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Wedding Guest

There are a variety of hairstyles that can be done with medium length hair, such as braids, updos, and curls. Here are some popular hairstyles:

Braids:

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be done in many ways. You can braid your hair into a fishtail braid, a Dutch braid, or a French braid. Braids can be worn in a half-up, half-down style or in an updo. Braids are perfect for a boho or rustic wedding.

Updos:

Updos are a classic and elegant hairstyle for weddings. You can wear your hair in a bun, a chignon, or a twist. Updos are perfect for a formal wedding.

Curls:

Curls are a romantic and feminine hairstyle. You can curl your hair with a curling iron or hot rollers. You can wear your curls in a half-up, half-down style or in an updo. Curls are perfect for a beach or garden wedding.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style medium length hair for wedding guests:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. If you want to add volume to your hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse.
  3. If you want to add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  4. If you want to curl your hair, use a curling iron or hot rollers. Section your hair and curl each section away from your face.
  5. If you want to braid your hair, section your hair and braid each section. Secure each braid with a hair tie.
  6. If you want to wear an updo, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun or a chignon. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  7. If you want to wear a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down or curl it.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair for Wedding Guest

Here are some tips for styling medium length hair for wedding guests:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and the wedding theme.
  • Don't try a new hairstyle on the day of the wedding. Practice your hairstyle beforehand.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume.
  • If you have thick hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hairstyle in place.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a headband, a hair clip, or a flower crown.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair down to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair down to a wedding. You can curl your hair or add waves to create a romantic and feminine look.

Q: Can I wear a messy bun to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a messy bun to a wedding. A messy bun is a popular and effortless hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

Q: How do I keep my curls or updo in place all day?

A: Use hairspray to hold your curls or updo in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for styling medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Styling medium length hair for a wedding guest can be easy and fun. With these tips and tricks, you can create a hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and the wedding theme. Don't forget to practice your hairstyle beforehand and use hairspray to hold it in place. With these tips and tricks, you'll be the best-dressed guest at the wedding!
