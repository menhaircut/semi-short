How To Choose My Hairstyle Male A Guide For Men

Written by Oliver Jul 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task for men. With so many options out there, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. But fear not, because we’re here to help. In this guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about how to choose your hairstyle as a male. From the different types of hairstyles to consider, to tips for taking care of your hair, we’ve got you covered.

  • Choosing a Hairstyle Based on Your Face Shape

Types of Hairstyles for Men

Before you can choose a hairstyle, you need to know what your options are. Here are some of the most popular types of hairstyles for men:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a classic option for men. They’re low maintenance and easy to style. Some popular short haircuts include the buzz cut, crew cut, and the classic short back and sides.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts are a great option if you want something that’s not too short or too long. Some popular medium-length haircuts include the side part, the messy textured look, and the slicked-back undercut.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts are becoming more popular among men. They’re great for guys who want to make a statement and have a unique look. Some popular long haircuts include the man bun, the ponytail, and the shoulder-length cut.

Choosing a Hairstyle Based on Your Face Shape

One important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. Different hairstyles can complement or accentuate different face shapes. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle based on your face shape:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you’ll want a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates more angles. A short back and sides with some length on top, or a textured fringe, can work well.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you’ll want a hairstyle that softens your features. A textured crop or a side part with some length on top can work well.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck – most hairstyles will suit you. You can experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you’ll want a hairstyle that balances out your features. A textured fringe or a side-swept style can work well.

Choosing a Hairstyle Based on Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Different hairstyles work better with different hair types. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle based on your hair type:

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, you can go for a sleek and polished look. A side part or a slicked-back style can work well.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, you’ll want a hairstyle that embraces your natural texture. A messy textured look or a curly fringe can work well.

Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you’ll want a hairstyle that works with your volume. A textured crop or a messy quiff can work well.

Thin Hair

If you have thin hair, you’ll want a hairstyle that creates the illusion of volume. A side part or a swept-back style can work well.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle, you’ll need to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling different types of hairstyles:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are low maintenance, but you’ll still need to style them. Use a small amount of product to create texture and hold. Work the product through your hair and style it with your fingers.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways. Use a small amount of product to create texture and hold. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts require more maintenance, but they can be styled in a variety of ways. Use a small amount of product to create texture and hold. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Taking care of your hair is important if you want it to look its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suited to your hair type.

Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can lead to damage and breakage. Use a small amount of product and avoid heat styling as much as possible.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy. Make sure you’re getting plenty of vitamins and minerals.

FAQs

What’s the best hairstyle for men?

The best hairstyle for men depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, but this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

What’s the best product to use for styling my hair?

The best product to use for styling your hair depends on your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Congratulations, you now know how to choose your hairstyle as a male. Remember to consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a hairstyle, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a stylish and healthy head of hair.
