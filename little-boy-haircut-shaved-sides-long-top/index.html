Little Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top The Latest Trend For Your Little Ones

Written by William Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy? Look no further than the shaved sides long top haircut. This popular hairstyle is perfect for young boys who want to look trendy and stylish. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this trendy haircut, as well as tips for maintaining it.

Description

The little boy haircut shaved sides long top is a versatile and edgy hairstyle that features short sides and a long top. The sides of the head are shaved, while the hair on top is left longer and styled to create a textured, messy look. This haircut is perfect for boys who want to stand out and make a statement with their hair.

How to

To achieve the shaved sides long top haircut, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Before attempting to cut your child's hair, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best cut for your child's hair type and face shape. They can also provide tips on how to style and maintain the haircut.

Step 2: Shave the Sides

Using clippers, shave the sides of your child's head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length. Be sure to blend the hair on top with the shaved sides for a seamless look.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on top of your child's head to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a few inches to several inches, depending on your child's preference. Be sure to leave enough length to create a textured, messy look.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Using a styling product such as pomade or wax, style the hair on top of your child's head into a messy, textured look. Use your fingers to create a tousled, natural look.

Tips

To maintain the little boy haircut shaved sides long top, follow these tips:

Tip 1: Trim the Sides Regularly

To keep the shaved sides looking neat and clean, trim them every few weeks. Use clippers with a short guard to maintain the length.

Tip 2: Use a Styling Product

To keep the hair on top looking styled and textured, use a styling product such as pomade or wax. Apply a small amount to your child's hair and use your fingers to create a tousled, natural look.

Tip 3: Wash and Condition Regularly

To keep your child's hair healthy and shiny, wash and condition it regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for children.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the little boy haircut shaved sides long top:

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit all hair types, from straight to curly.

Q: How often should I take my child for a haircut?

A: It's recommended to take your child for a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

A: Yes, the hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

Hair Care

To keep your child's hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is formulated for children's hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can strip the hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner that is formulated for children's hair to keep it soft and manageable. Conditioner can also help to prevent tangles and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect from the Sun

When spending time in the sun, protect your child's hair with a hat or sunscreen. Sun exposure can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

In conclusion, the little boy haircut shaved sides long top is a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for young boys who want to stand out. With the right cut and styling products, this haircut can be adapted to suit any hair type or face shape. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your child's hair healthy and looking great.
