How To Cut Hair Into Layers At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your flat, one-length hair? Layers can add depth, movement, and texture to your locks. But, getting a professional haircut can be expensive, especially if you want to maintain the style. The good news is that you can easily cut your hair into layers at home with a few tools and techniques. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair into layers at home.

How to Cut Hair into Layers at Home

Before we dive into the cutting process, make sure you have the following tools:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips
  • A mirror

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry, as wet hair can lead to uneven cuts.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into three sections: the top, middle, and bottom. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the bottom section first. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the comb to hold the hair straight and cut the ends in a straight line.

Next, move to the middle section and repeat the same process. Cut the hair in a straight line, but this time, hold the scissors at an angle to create longer layers.

Finally, move to the top section. This time, hold the hair straight up and cut it at a 90-degree angle to create shorter layers.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

After cutting each section, comb through your hair to blend the layers. This will create a natural-looking finish.

Step 6: Check for Symmetry

Use the mirror to check for symmetry. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure both sides are even.

Step 7: Finish and Style

Once you are satisfied with your cut, style your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure accuracy.
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

FAQs

How long does it take to cut hair into layers at home?

It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can I cut my hair into layers if it is curly?

Yes, you can cut curly hair into layers. However, make sure you cut it when it is dry, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair into layers, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layers.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.

Cutting your hair into layers at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look without breaking the bank.
