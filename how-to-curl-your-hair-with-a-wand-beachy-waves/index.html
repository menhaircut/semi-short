How To Curl Your Hair With A Wand Beachy Waves

Written by Bardi Sep 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some texture to your hair? Look no further than the wand beachy waves! This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to look effortlessly chic. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this look, as well as provide some tips and FAQs. Let's get started!

How to

Before we get into the steps, let's talk about what you'll need. To achieve wand beachy waves, you'll need a curling wand, heat protectant spray, and hairspray. If you have naturally straight hair, it's best to start with second-day hair, as the natural oils will help hold the curls better.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by applying heat protectant spray to your hair, focusing on the ends. Then, brush your hair to ensure there are no tangles.

Step 2: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the wand, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends. Hold the hair on the wand for 5-10 seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 3: Add Texture

After all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more tousled, beachy look.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your curls stay in place all day, finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wand beachy waves:

  • Use a curling wand with a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • For tighter waves, use a smaller barrel.
  • Don't curl all of your hair in the same direction - alternate between wrapping the hair around the wand towards your face and away from your face.
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your curls.

FAQ

How long will the curls last?

This will depend on your hair type and the products you use. On average, wand beachy waves will last 1-2 days.

Can I achieve this look with naturally curly hair?

Yes! Start by applying a curl-defining product to your hair, then follow the same steps as above.

Can I achieve this look without a curling wand?

Yes! You can achieve similar results using a flat iron. Start by clamping a small section of hair with the flat iron, then twist the iron away from your face. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools. Also, try to limit heat styling as much as possible to avoid damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve perfect wand beachy waves in no time! Give it a try and see how effortlessly chic you can look.
