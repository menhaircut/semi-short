How To Create Wavy Hair With Curling Iron A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to have those gorgeous waves in your hair that many celebrities flaunt on the red carpet? Do you want to add more volume and texture to your hair? If so, you are in the right place. In this article, we will show you how to create wavy hair with a curling iron. You'll be surprised how easy it is!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Wavy hair is a timeless hairstyle that looks good on everyone. Whether you have short or long hair, straight or curly, you can create beautiful waves with a curling iron. A curling iron is a must-have tool for creating wavy hair. It is easy to use, and with a little practice, you can achieve the perfect waves in no time.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a curling iron, heat protectant spray, hair clips, and a comb. You may also want to use a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom section, and work your way up to the top section. This will make it easier to curl your hair.

Step 3: Heat up your curling iron

Turn on your curling iron and let it heat up. The temperature should be around 350-400°F for most hair types.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it for 10-15 seconds, and then release. Continue curling your hair in sections until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 5: Add volume

Once you have curled your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume to your hair. You can also use a comb to gently tease your hair to add more volume.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to hold the waves in place. You can also use a hair oil to add shine to your hair.

Tips

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for loose waves.
  • Use a smaller barrel for tighter waves.
  • Always use heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose waves look more natural.
  • If you have fine hair, use a lower heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Can I create wavy hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can create wavy hair without a curling iron. You can use a flat iron or hair rollers to create waves in your hair.

How long will the waves last?

The waves can last for up to 2-3 days depending on your hair type and the weather. Use a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after curling it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage. Use a heat protectant spray every time you use a curling iron or flat iron.

Creating wavy hair with a curling iron is easy and fun. With a little practice, you can achieve the perfect waves in no time. Follow the steps and tips in this article, and you'll have gorgeous wavy hair in no time!
