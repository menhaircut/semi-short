Does Shorter Hair Make Your Face Look Bigger

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair is a popular trend, and many people are opting for it. But, one of the biggest concerns that people have is whether shorter hair makes their face look bigger. This topic is widely discussed, and there are many opinions on it. In this article, we will discuss the truth behind the myth of whether shorter hair makes your face look bigger.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The answer to whether shorter hair makes your face look bigger is, it depends. There are many factors that determine whether shorter hair makes your face look bigger or not. One of the main factors is the shape of your face. Different face shapes look different with different hairstyles.

People with round faces tend to look better with longer hair. This is because longer hair can create an illusion of a longer face, which balances out the roundness. On the other hand, people with square faces look better with shorter hair. This is because shorter hair can soften the sharpness of the jawline and make the face look less angular.

How To

The first step to determining whether shorter hair makes your face look bigger is to identify your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your face shape.

People with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face look even rounder. Instead, they should opt for hairstyles that add length to the face. People with square faces should avoid hairstyles that make the face look more angular. Instead, they should opt for hairstyles that soften the jawline.

Step By Step

Step 1: Identify your face shape
Step 2: Look for hairstyles that suit your face shape
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist
Step 4: Experiment with different hairstyles

Tips

When looking for hairstyles, it is important to consider your hair type and texture. People with fine hair may not be able to pull off certain hairstyles, while people with thick hair may need to thin out their hair to achieve a certain look. It is also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on your hair. Some hairstyles require more maintenance than others.

FAQ

Q: Does shorter hair make your face look bigger?

A: It depends on your face shape. People with round faces tend to look better with longer hair, while people with square faces tend to look better with shorter hair.

Q: What face shape looks best with short hair?

A: People with square faces tend to look best with short hair, as it can soften the sharpness of the jawline.

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear short hair if you have a round face. However, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you have short or long hair, it is important to take care of your hair. This includes washing your hair regularly, using conditioner, and using heat protectant products when styling your hair. It is also important to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, whether shorter hair makes your face look bigger depends on your face shape. People with round faces tend to look better with longer hair, while people with square faces tend to look better with shorter hair. However, it is important to experiment with different hairstyles and consult with a hairstylist to find the right look for you.
