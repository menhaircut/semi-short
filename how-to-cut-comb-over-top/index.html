How To Cut Comb Over Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a comb over top. A comb over is a popular men's hairstyle that requires the hair to be combed over to one side, creating a neat and polished look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to look stylish and professional. With our step by step guide, you can easily achieve this hairstyle at home. Let's get started!

How to Cut Comb Over Top

Cutting a comb over top requires a few basic tools, including a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. Before you start cutting your hair, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You will also need to decide on the length of your hair and the direction you want your hair to be combed over.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by combing your hair to one side to determine the length you want to cut. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.

2. Use the scissors to blend the sides with the top of your hair. Comb your hair to the opposite side and repeat the process.

3. Comb your hair to the side you want your hair to be combed over. Use the scissors to cut the top of your hair to your desired length.

4. Use the comb to create a parting on the side of your hair. Use the scissors to trim the hair on the shorter side of the parting.

5. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the longer side of the parting. Make sure to blend the hair with the rest of your hair.

6. Use the scissors to blend the hair on the longer side of the parting with the top of your hair.

7. Comb your hair to the side you want your hair to be combed over. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

8. Use the clippers to create a clean and sharp hairline around your ears and neck.

Tips for Cutting Comb Over Top

1. Start with longer hair and gradually trim it down to your desired length. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer.

2. Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting your hair.

3. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create a more natural look.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my hair while it's wet?

A: It is not recommended to cut your hair while it's wet as wet hair tends to shrink when it dries, resulting in a shorter haircut than intended.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or using a straightener.

3. Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.

4. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips, you can easily cut a comb over top at home and maintain it for a polished and stylish look. Happy cutting!
