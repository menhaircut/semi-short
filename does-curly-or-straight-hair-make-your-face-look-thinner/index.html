Does Curly Or Straight Hair Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by William Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to make your face look slimmer? One simple trick that can help is changing your hairstyle. But, does curly or straight hair make your face look thinner? Let’s find out in this article.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Chosen Hairstyle

Description

The shape of your face plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle flatters you the most. Some hairstyles can make your face look rounder or wider than it actually is, while others can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Curly and straight hair both have their pros and cons when it comes to making your face look thinner.

How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with Your Hair

The key to making your face look slimmer with your hairstyle is to create balance. You want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face, creating a more elongated look.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, you can still make your face look thinner by choosing the right hairstyle. Curly hair tends to add volume to the sides of the face, which can make it look wider. To avoid this, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face.

One great option for curly hair is a layered cut. This can help create the illusion of length and add movement to your hair. You can also consider a side-swept bang, which can help create a more elongated look.

Straight Hair

Straight hair can also help create the illusion of a slimmer face. You want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length.

A great option for straight hair is a long bob or “lob.” This length can help create the illusion of a longer face. You can also consider adding layers or a side-swept bang to create more movement and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to finding the right hairstyle to make your face look thinner:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair type (curly, straight, etc.) and how it affects the shape of your face.
  4. Talk to a hairstylist about which hairstyles they recommend for your face shape and hair type.
  5. Choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle to make your face look thinner:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face.
  • Consider adding layers or a side-swept bang to create more movement and dimension.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hairstyles work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQs

Does curly hair make your face look rounder?

Curly hair can add volume to the sides of your face, which can make your face look rounder. To avoid this, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face.

Does straight hair make your face look thinner?

Straight hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. To achieve this, choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length to your face. This can include a long bob, layered cut, or side-swept bangs.

Hair Care Tips for Your Chosen Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes your face look thinner, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your chosen hairstyle.
  • Consider using hair oils or masks to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

So, does curly or straight hair make your face look thinner? The answer is both. The key to making your face look slimmer with your hairstyle is to create balance and choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face.
