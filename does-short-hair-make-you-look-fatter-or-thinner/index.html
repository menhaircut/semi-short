Does Short Hair Make You Look Fatter Or Thinner

Written by Eveline Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for years, but many people still wonder if it makes them look fatter or thinner. The answer is not cut and dried, as it depends on various factors. In this blog post, we will explore whether short hair makes you look fatter or thinner and how to get the most flattering look for your face shape and body type.

Description

As with any hairstyle, the way you wear your hair can affect the way you look. Short hair can make you look thinner or fatter, depending on how it is cut, styled, and how it complements your face shape and body type.

How to

To figure out whether short hair will make you look fatter or thinner, consider the following factors:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in how your hair looks. For example, if you have a round face, short hair can make you look wider. In contrast, if you have a long face, short hair can make your face look slimmer.

Body Type

Your body type can also play a role in how your hair looks. If you have a larger body type, short hair can make you look wider. In contrast, if you have a smaller body type, short hair can make you look more petite.

Hairstyle

The hairstyle you choose can also have an impact on how your hair looks. For example, a sleek bob can make you look slimmer, while a curly or voluminous cut can make you look wider.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you determine whether short hair will make you look fatter or thinner:

  1. Consider your face shape.
  2. Consider your body type.
  3. Research different short hairstyles.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist.
  5. Try on a wig or use a virtual hairstyle app to see how different short hairstyles look on you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most flattering look:

  • Consider adding layers to your short hairstyle to add volume and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Consider pairing your short hairstyle with statement earrings to draw attention away from your face.
  • Consider using hair products such as mousse or volumizing spray to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Does short hair make you look fatter or thinner?

It depends on various factors, including face shape, body type, and hairstyle.

What short hairstyle is best for a round face?

A sleek bob or a layered cut that falls just below the chin can be the most flattering for a round face.

Can short hair make a long face look shorter?

Yes, short hair can make a long face look shorter. A bob or a pixie cut can be the most flattering for a long face.

Hair Care

Whether you have short or long hair, it is essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, short hair can make you look fatter or thinner, depending on various factors. By considering your face shape, body type, and hairstyle, you can determine the most flattering short hairstyle for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
