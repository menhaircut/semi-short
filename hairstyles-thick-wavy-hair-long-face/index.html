Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair Long Face

Written by Luffy Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and long face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing hairstyles that are perfect for those with thick wavy hair and a long face. Whether you prefer short or long hair, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle can be tricky, especially when you have a specific face shape to consider. If you have a long face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will balance out your features. This means avoiding hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add width to your face, such as layers, bangs, or waves.

Step-by-Step Guide for Short Hairstyles

If you prefer short hair, there are plenty of options that will flatter your face shape. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect short hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or styling gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on creating volume at the crown of your head.
  4. Use a flat iron to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide for Long Hairstyles

If you prefer long hair, there are also plenty of options that will flatter your face shape. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect long hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or styling gel.
  3. Part your hair down the middle or to one side.
  4. Create loose waves or curls throughout your hair using a curling iron or flat iron.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and long face, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQs

1. What hairstyles are best for a long face?

Hairstyles that add width to the face are best for a long face. This includes styles with layers, bangs, or waves.

2. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face. However, it's important to add volume to your hair to balance out your features.

3. What styling products should I use for thick wavy hair?

Styling products that add moisture and definition to your hair are best for thick wavy hair. This includes mousse, styling gel, and curl-defining cream.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair Long Face

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to maintain, but with the right hair care routine, you can keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips for caring for your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and long face. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from. Remember to maintain your new hairstyle with a proper hair care routine to keep it looking its best.
