What Is A Medium Length Haircut

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a haircut that falls somewhere in between short and long, a medium length haircut could be the perfect solution. In this blog post, we’ll explain what a medium length haircut is, how to achieve it, and how to care for it.

Table of Contents:

  • Definition of Medium Length Haircut
  • How to Achieve a Medium Length Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Medium Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining a Medium Length Haircut
  • FAQs About Medium Length Haircuts
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Definition of Medium Length Haircut

A medium length haircut typically falls somewhere between the chin and the shoulders. It’s a versatile haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to sleek and straight.

One of the benefits of a medium length haircut is that it works well with many different face shapes and hair types. It can also be a great way to switch up your look without committing to a short or long haircut.

How to Achieve a Medium Length Haircut

If you’re starting with long hair, you’ll need to trim off several inches to achieve a medium length haircut. If you’re starting with short hair, you’ll need to grow it out for several months.

When you’re ready to get your hair cut, it’s important to communicate with your stylist about the length you want. You can bring in photos of medium length haircuts you like to help give your stylist a better idea of what you’re looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Medium Length Hair

If you’re cutting your own hair or helping a friend, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving a medium length haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, cut a straight line across the bottom of each section.
  4. Use a comb to check that the length is even.
  5. Continue cutting each section, working your way to the front of your head.
  6. When you reach the front, use a mirror to check that the length is even on both sides.
  7. Trim any layers or bangs if desired.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Medium Length Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your medium length haircut looking its best:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new styles and switch up your look.

FAQs About Medium Length Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts:

Can I still wear my hair up with a medium length haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a medium length haircut. Try a messy bun, a low ponytail, or a half-up half-down style.

Will a medium length haircut work for my face shape?

A medium length haircut can work well for many different face shapes. Talk to your stylist to find a style that flatters your features.

Can I still have layers with a medium length haircut?

Yes, layers can add texture and movement to a medium length haircut. Talk to your stylist about incorporating layers into your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a medium length haircut with confidence.
