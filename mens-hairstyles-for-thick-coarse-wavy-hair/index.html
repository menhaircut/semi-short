Mens Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

When it comes to men's hair, thick coarse wavy hair can be a challenge to style. However, with the right haircut and styling products, you can manage and embrace your hair's natural texture. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for men with thick coarse wavy hair, tips on how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be difficult to manage and style, but it's also a blessing in disguise. With the right haircut and styling products, you can make your hair look voluminous, textured, and stylish.

One of the best things about having thick coarse wavy hair is that you can experiment with different hairstyles. From short and textured to long and layered, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to

When it comes to styling your thick coarse wavy hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for thick, coarse hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair shaft and cause frizz.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, like a sea salt spray or a mousse, to enhance your hair's natural texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

Step by step

Here's how to style your thick coarse wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick, coarse hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a lightweight styling product, like a sea salt spray or a mousse, to your hair.
  4. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and enhance its natural texture.
  5. If you want a more polished look, blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  6. Finish by using a small amount of styling wax or pomade to define your hair's shape and add texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair shaft and cause frizz.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, like a sea salt spray or a mousse, to enhance your hair's natural texture.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for men with thick coarse wavy hair?

The best hairstyles for men with thick coarse wavy hair are short and textured, long and layered, or medium and messy.

What styling products should I use for my thick coarse wavy hair?

You should use a lightweight styling product, like a sea salt spray or a mousse, to enhance your hair's natural texture.

How often should I get a haircut if I have thick coarse wavy hair?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your thick coarse wavy hair looking its best, you should:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for thick, coarse hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair shaft and cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, like a sea salt spray or a mousse, to enhance your hair's natural texture.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can embrace your thick coarse wavy hair and create stylish, textured looks that will turn heads.
