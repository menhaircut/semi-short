How To Stop Hair Fall Naturally Quora Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing strands of hair on your pillow, in the shower drain, or on your brush? Hair fall can be a real problem for many people. While there are several hair care products available in the market that claim to stop hair fall, most of them are loaded with chemicals that can harm your hair in the long run. So, what's the solution? Well, the good news is that there are several natural remedies that can help you stop hair fall. In this post, we will discuss the best ways to stop hair fall naturally quora.

How to stop hair fall naturally quora?

Here are some effective ways to stop hair fall naturally quora:

1. Oil Massage

Oil massage is one of the best ways to stop hair fall. It helps to nourish your hair and scalp, improves blood circulation, and strengthens the hair roots. You can use coconut oil, almond oil, or olive oil for massaging your hair. Massage your scalp and hair with warm oil for 15-20 minutes and leave it for an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural ingredient that can help you stop hair fall. It helps to soothe your scalp, reduces inflammation, and promotes hair growth. You can apply fresh aloe vera gel on your scalp and hair and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off with water. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which helps to improve blood circulation, reduce inflammation, and promote hair growth. You can extract onion juice by blending onions and apply it on your scalp and hair. Leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off with water. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

4. Egg Mask

Egg mask is a natural remedy that can help you stop hair fall. Eggs are rich in protein, which helps to strengthen your hair and prevent hair fall. You can make an egg mask by mixing one egg with one tablespoon of olive oil and honey. Apply this on your scalp and hair and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off with water.

5. Green Tea

Green tea is rich in antioxidants, which helps to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. You can make green tea by boiling green tea leaves in water and apply it on your scalp and hair. Leave it for an hour before washing it off with water.

FAQs

1. How can I stop hair fall immediately?

While there is no immediate solution to hair fall, you can follow some natural remedies like oil massage, aloe vera, onion juice, egg mask, and green tea to stop hair fall naturally quora.

2. What foods help stop hair fall?

Foods rich in protein, iron, and vitamins can help to stop hair fall. Some of the foods that you can include in your diet are eggs, spinach, nuts, carrots, sweet potatoes, and oranges.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips that can help you stop hair fall:

1. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid heat styling tools.

3. Do not tie your hair tightly.

4. Avoid using hair color or bleach.

5. Trim your hair regularly.

In conclusion, hair fall can be a real problem for many people, but with the right hair care routine and natural remedies, you can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Follow the tips mentioned above and say goodbye to hair fall naturally quora.
