Best Haircut For Round Face Long Hair In 2023 The Ultimate Guide

If you have a round face and long hair, you might be wondering what the best haircut is for you. Luckily, there are plenty of options that can flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about choosing the best haircut for round face long hair, including how to determine your face shape, the most flattering styles, and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best haircut for your round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back so your face is fully visible. Then, use a measuring tape to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point (typically just above your eyebrows).
  • Cheekbones: Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point (usually just below the outer corner of your eyes).
  • Jawline: Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point (typically just below your ears).
  • Face length: Measure the distance from your hairline to the bottom of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape:

  • Round face: If your cheekbones and face length are roughly equal and your jawline is soft and rounded, you have a round face shape.
  • Oval face: If your face length is longer than your cheekbones and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline, you have an oval face shape.
  • Square face: If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal in width and your face length is moderate to long, you have a square face shape.
  • Heart face: If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a heart face shape.
  • Diamond face: If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your forehead and jawline are narrow, you have a diamond face shape.

Best Haircuts for Round Face Long Hair

Once you've determined that you have a round face shape, it's time to start looking for the best haircut for you. The key is to choose a style that elongates your face and creates the illusion of length. Here are some of the most flattering haircuts for round face long hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces because they add movement and texture to your hair while also creating the illusion of length. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, face-framing layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer toward the ends of your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces because they help to create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the widest part of your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the top of your head and gradually get longer toward the ends of your hair.

Blunt Cut with Face-Framing Layers

A blunt cut with face-framing layers is a great option for round faces because it gives your hair structure and definition while also elongating your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a blunt cut that falls just below your collarbone and face-framing layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer toward the ends of your hair.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a great option for round faces because it adds angles and definition to your hair while also elongating your face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a bob that's longer on one side than the other and falls just below your chin.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the best haircut for your round face long hair, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for a round face?

The most flattering haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and angles. Some great options include long layers, side-swept bangs, a blunt cut with face-framing layers, and an asymmetrical bob.

Can you wear long hair with a round face?

Absolutely! Long hair can be very flattering on a round face as long as it's styled properly. The key is to choose a cut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of length, such as long layers or an asymmetrical bob.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to getting regular trims and using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, there are a few other things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair.

With these tips and the right haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident with your round face long hair.
