Easy Hairstyles For Short To Medium Length Hair To Do At Home

Are you looking for some easy hairstyles for short to medium length hair that you can do at home? Look no further! In this article, we will cover a variety of styles that are perfect for those with shorter hair. From braids to updos, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some interest to your short to medium length hair. Here are a few styles to try:

French Braid

To create a French braid, start by sectioning off a small portion of hair at the crown of your head. Divide this section into three equal parts and begin braiding, adding in more hair as you go. Continue braiding all the way down to the ends of your hair and secure with an elastic band.

Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a great option for those with shorter hair. Simply divide your hair into two sections and take a small piece from the outside of one section and bring it over to the other section. Repeat this process, alternating sides, until you reach the end of your hair. Secure with an elastic band.

Updos

Updos are another great way to switch up your look. Here are a few easy styles to try:

Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure with bobby pins.

Twisted Updo

To create a twisted updo, start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it, pulling it over to the other side of your head. Secure with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side and continue until all of your hair is twisted and secured.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic and easy hairstyle for those with shorter hair. Here are a few variations to try:

Half-Up Ponytail

To create a half-up ponytail, start by sectioning off the top portion of your hair. Pull this section back into a ponytail and secure with an elastic band.

Braided Ponytail

To create a braided ponytail, start by braiding a small section of hair at the crown of your head. Pull the rest of your hair back into a ponytail, incorporating the braid into the ponytail. Secure with an elastic band.

Accessories

Accessories can also add some interest to your short to medium length hair. Here are a few options to try:

Headbands

Headbands are a great way to add some interest to your hair. Try a thick, cloth headband for a boho look or a thin, metallic headband for a more polished look.

Hair Clips

Hair clips are another fun accessory to try. Clip a few small clips into the side of your hair to add some interest or try a larger, statement clip to hold back your bangs.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

When styling short to medium length hair, it's important to keep a few things in mind:

  • Use smaller elastics and bobby pins to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to use texturizing products to add some volume and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I still braid my hair if it's short?

Absolutely! Try a French braid or a fishtail braid for a fun and easy style.

What accessories work best for short to medium length hair?

Headbands and hair clips are both great options to add some interest to your shorter hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

When it comes to caring for your short to medium length hair, it's important to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Don't over-wash your hair â aim for every other day or every two days.
  • Use a heat protectant when heat styling your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have a variety of easy hairstyles for short to medium length hair to do at home, it's time to get styling! Experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.
