How To Spike Your Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a new way to style your hair? Spiking your hair up is a fun and edgy option that can give you a whole new look. In this article, we’ll show you how to spike your hair up in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Your Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Your Hair Up
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Spiked Hair

How to Spike Your Hair Up

Spiking your hair up is a great way to add some edge to your look. Here’s what you’ll need to get started:

  • Hair gel or pomade
  • A comb or brush
  • A blow dryer (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. It’s important to have clean hair before you start styling. Once you’ve washed your hair, towel dry it and use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Comb or Brush to Style Your Hair

Using a comb or brush, style your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you want a more natural look, use your fingers to style your hair instead.

Step 4: Spike Your Hair Up

Now it’s time to start spiking your hair up. Using your fingers or a comb, start at the roots and work your way up. Use a twisting motion to create spikes.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your hair in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair stay spiked up all day long.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair Up

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade
  • Style your hair in the direction you want it to go
  • Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray

FAQ

How long will my spiked hair last?

Your spiked hair should last all day as long as you use enough hair gel or pomade and finish with hairspray.

Can I spike my hair up without hair gel?

While it’s possible to spike your hair up without hair gel, it’s much easier and will last longer if you use a styling product like hair gel or pomade.

Can I still spike my hair up if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still spike your hair up if you have short hair. Just use a small amount of hair gel or pomade and create small spikes.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

When you spike your hair up, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent excess drying
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Spiking your hair up is a fun and easy way to change up your look. With just a few simple steps, you can create edgy, cool spikes that will last all day.
