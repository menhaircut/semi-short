Mid Long Hairstyles 2020 Rocking Your Locks With Style

Written by Petter Aug 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for inspiration for your mid long hair, you've come to the right place. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the top mid long hairstyles of 2020 that are still going strong in 2023. From beachy waves to blunt bobs, we've got you covered.

Description

Mid long hairstyles are the perfect length for those who want a versatile look without sacrificing too much length. This length allows for a variety of styles and can be easily maintained with the right products and tools. The mid long hairstyles of 2020 were all about texture and volume, and these trends have continued to be popular in 2023.

How to

Creating a mid long hairstyle can be done in a few simple steps. First, start with clean, dry hair. Use a heat protectant spray and curling iron to add loose waves or use a straightener for a sleek look. You can also add volume by teasing the roots or using a volumizing mousse. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.
2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
3. Use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style.
4. Add volume by teasing the roots or using a volumizing mousse.
5. Finish off with hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

When it comes to mid long hairstyles, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, invest in a good quality heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Second, use the right tools for your hair type. If you have thick hair, a larger curling iron may be necessary to create loose waves. Third, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: What are some popular mid long hairstyles in 2023?

A: Some popular mid long hairstyles in 2023 include beachy waves, blunt bobs, and textured shags.

Q: How can I add volume to my mid long hair?

A: You can add volume to your mid long hair by teasing the roots or using a volumizing mousse.

Q: Can I still wear mid long hairstyles if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, mid long hairstyles can work for thin hair as well. Just be sure to use the right products and tools to create volume and texture.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your mid long hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Second, avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray. Third, get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

In conclusion, mid long hairstyles are a great option for those who want a versatile look without sacrificing too much length. Whether you prefer beachy waves or a blunt bob, there's a mid long hairstyle out there for you. By following the tips and steps outlined in this blog post, you can create a stylish and manageable mid long hairstyle that will turn heads.
