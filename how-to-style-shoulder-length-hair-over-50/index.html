How To Style Shoulder Length Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have shoulder length hair and are over 50, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that is flattering and age-appropriate. Fortunately, there are many options available that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks for styling your shoulder length hair, as well as answer some frequently asked questions about hair care.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair Over 50

The first step in styling your shoulder length hair is to find a cut that is flattering and easy to maintain. Some great options for women over 50 include a layered bob, a shag, or a long bob with side-swept bangs. These cuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from a sleek and sophisticated look to a more casual and tousled style.

Once you have your cut, there are many ways to style your hair. One popular option is to add some volume and texture with a curling iron or hot rollers. This can give your hair a more youthful and playful look, while still being age-appropriate. Another option is to use a straightening iron to create a sleek and polished look.

If you prefer a more casual look, you can try styling your hair in a messy bun or a loose braid. This can be a great option for days when you don't want to spend a lot of time styling your hair, but still want to look stylish and put-together.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling your shoulder length hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to separate the curls and create a more tousled look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling your shoulder length hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as this can help prevent breakage.

FAQ about Hair Care

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair care:

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

What should I do if my hair is damaged?

If your hair is damaged, you may need to cut off the damaged ends and use a deep conditioning treatment to help repair your hair. You can also try using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help protect your hair from further damage.

How can I prevent split ends?

To prevent split ends, you should avoid using heat styling tools too often, as well as using harsh chemicals and coloring products. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage.

What should I do if my hair is thinning?

If your hair is thinning, you may want to speak to your doctor or a hair specialist to determine the cause. They may recommend a hair growth treatment or suggest changes to your diet and lifestyle to help promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Styling shoulder length hair over 50 can be fun and easy with the right cut and techniques. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more tousled and casual style, there are many options available that can help you look and feel your best. Remember to take good care of your hair by using quality products and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
