How To Have A Diamond Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to enhance your natural beauty, achieving a diamond face shape can do wonders for your overall appearance. With the right hair and makeup techniques, you can create a more defined and angular look that flatters your features. In this article, we'll show you how to have a diamond face shape through simple steps, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve a Diamond Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Diamond Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Diamond Face Shapes

Description

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. It's a rare and unique shape that can be enhanced through makeup and hair styling techniques. By accentuating your cheekbones and creating more angles around your face, you can achieve a more defined and sophisticated look.

How to Achieve a Diamond Face Shape

There are several ways to create a diamond face shape, including makeup, hair styling, and contouring. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Use highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones and create a more angular look
  • Create a side part and add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face
  • Wear earrings that draw attention to your cheekbones
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and taper down towards your chin
  • Contour your jawline and temples to create more angles

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a diamond face shape:

  1. Start by applying a light foundation or BB cream to even out your skin tone
  2. Use a highlighter or illuminator to accentuate your cheekbones and brow bones
  3. Create a side part and add volume to the crown of your head using a volumizing mousse or spray
  4. Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or top knot
  5. Contour your jawline and temples using a contouring powder or cream
  6. Use a bronzer to add warmth to your complexion
  7. Wear earrings that draw attention to your cheekbones, such as hoops or chandelier earrings
  8. Apply mascara and eyeliner to enhance your eyes
  9. Finish with a bold lip color to make your lips stand out

Tips for Maintaining a Diamond Face Shape

To maintain your diamond face shape, here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear wider
  • Use a face mask or exfoliating scrub to keep your skin looking smooth and radiant
  • Invest in a good-quality highlighter and contouring kit to enhance your features
  • Wear sunglasses that flatter your face shape, such as cat-eye or wayfarer styles
  • Choose clothing that accentuates your waist and creates a balanced silhouette

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about achieving a diamond face shape:

What hairstyles are best for diamond face shapes?

Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and taper down towards your chin are best for diamond face shapes. This includes high ponytails, top knots, and layered haircuts.

What makeup techniques can I use to accentuate my cheekbones?

You can use a highlighter or illuminator to accentuate your cheekbones and create a more defined look. Apply the product to the highest point of your cheekbones and blend well.

Can I contour my jawline to create more angles?

Yes, contouring your jawline can help create more angles and enhance your diamond face shape. Use a contouring powder or cream and apply it underneath your jawline and along your temples.

Hair Care for Diamond Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear wider
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the top of your head
  • Invest in a good-quality hairbrush and comb to keep your hair looking smooth and tangle-free
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair

By following these simple tips and techniques, you can achieve a diamond face shape that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to have fun with your hair and makeup, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
