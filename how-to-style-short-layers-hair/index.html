How To Style Short Layers Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short layers can add volume and texture to your hair, but styling them can be a challenge. In this guide, we'll show you how to style your short layers hair in a way that looks great and is easy to maintain.

Description

Short layers hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for a while. It's a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, and it's perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. Short layers can be styled in many different ways, from messy to sleek and everything in between.

How to

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your short layers hair, try blow-drying it upside down. This will create volume and give your hair a tousled look. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves or beachy curls.

For a more polished look, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron. This will give your hair a sleek and smooth appearance. You can also use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Another option is to braid your hair while it's still damp and then let it air dry. This will create soft waves and add texture to your hair. You can also try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style for a more casual look.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are tailored to your hair type. This will leave your hair clean and moisturized, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel. This will help to hold your style in place and add volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume and texture. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and add even more volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair as desired. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling or flat iron.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short layers hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. If your hair gets oily quickly, you may need to wash it every day. If your hair is dry, you may be able to wash it every other day or every few days.

Q: Can I style my short layers hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can braid your hair while it's still damp and let it air dry for a natural, textured look. You can also try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style for a casual look.

Hair Care

To keep your short layers hair looking its best, make sure to use products that are tailored to your hair type. Avoid overusing styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Also, be sure to wash your hair regularly and use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
