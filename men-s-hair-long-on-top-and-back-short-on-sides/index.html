Men s Hair Long On Top And Back Short On Sides A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man looking for a versatile hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to maintain, look no further than the classic "long on top and back short on sides" cut. This timeless style has been popular for decades and continues to be a go-to look for many men. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about this style, from how to ask for it at the barber shop to how to care for your hair at home.

Description

The long on top and back short on sides style is characterized by longer hair on the top and back of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast in length that is both stylish and practical. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy, while the shorter sides and back make for a low-maintenance look that requires minimal styling.

How to Ask for it

When you go to the barber shop, it's important to know how to ask for the long on top and back short on sides style. Here's what to tell your barber:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Start by deciding how long you want your hair to be on top. This can vary depending on your personal preference and face shape, so take some time to consider what will look best on you. Once you've decided on a length, tell your barber exactly how many inches you want to leave on top.

Step 2: Decide on Your Fade

The short sides and back of this style are achieved with a fade, which gradually tapers the hair from longer on top to shorter on the sides and back. There are several types of fades to choose from, including a high fade, mid fade, and low fade. Decide which type of fade you prefer and let your barber know.

Step 3: Choose Your Styling

Finally, decide on how you want to style your longer hair on top. This can include anything from a classic side part to a messy, textured style. Work with your barber to determine what will look best with your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Style Long on Top and Back Short on Sides Hair

Once you've got your long on top and back short on sides cut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the longer hair on top. This can include anything from a pomade to a styling cream or gel.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your longer hair on top into your desired look. This can include anything from a slicked-back style to a messy, textured look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Long on Top and Back Short on Sides Hair

Once you've got your long on top and back short on sides cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your new style:

  • Visit your barber regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suited to your hair type
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of natural oils

FAQ: Long on Top and Back Short on Sides Hair

Q: Is long on top and back short on sides hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this style is relatively low-maintenance and requires minimal styling. However, you will need to visit your barber regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Q: Can this style be worn by men with all hair types?

A: Yes, the long on top and back short on sides style can be worn by men with all hair types, from straight to curly. However, the way you style your hair may vary depending on your hair type.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with this style. However, you may need to adjust the way you style your hair to accommodate the hat.

Hair Care for Long on Top and Back Short on Sides Hair

To keep your long on top and back short on sides hair looking great, it's important to take care of your hair on a daily basis. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Invest in a high-quality brush or comb to prevent damage and breakage
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the long on top and back short on sides style with confidence and ease. Whether you're looking for a classic, timeless look or something more modern and trendy, this versatile cut is sure to suit your needs.
