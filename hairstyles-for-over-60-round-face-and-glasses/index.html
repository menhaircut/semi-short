As we age, our hair changes along with our facial features. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for over 60 round face and glasses. We understand that it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits your age, face shape, and glasses. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

Description

Women over 60 often have round faces, which means that they have full cheeks and a rounded chin. This face shape is beautiful, but it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters it. Additionally, many women over 60 wear glasses, which can make styling their hair even more difficult.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential. Here are some tips to help you choose the right one:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is the most important factor when choosing a hairstyle. As mentioned earlier, women over 60 often have a round face shape. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length to balance it out.

Consider Your Glasses

Glasses can make or break a hairstyle. If you wear glasses, you want to choose a hairstyle that complements them.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect the hairstyle you choose. If you have thin hair, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume. If you have thick hair, you want to choose a style that thins it out.

Step by Step Guide to Hairstyles for Over 60 Round Face and Glasses

Short Pixie Cut

The short pixie cut is a trendy and chic hairstyle that suits all face shapes. It's effortless to maintain and perfect for women over 60. The short hairstyle is perfect for women who want to show off their glasses.

Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit all face shapes. The layers in the bob give it volume, making it perfect for women with thin hair.

Shoulder-Length Cut

The shoulder-length cut is a popular hairstyle that suits all face shapes. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. Women over 60 can style it with their glasses, making it a perfect choice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain your hairstyle's shape. It's recommended that you get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential to keep your hair healthy and looking great. Choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair, making it brittle and dry. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can pull off a short hairstyle if you have a round face. Just make sure that it's tailored to suit your face shape.

Q: Can I wear glasses with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can wear glasses with a short hairstyle. It's all about styling your hair in a way that complements your glasses.

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle is right for me?

A: The best way to know if a hairstyle is right for you is to try it out. If you're not sure, ask your hairstylist for their opinion.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will keep it clean and healthy. It's recommended that you wash your hair every two to three days.

Use Conditioner

Using conditioner after washing your hair will keep it soft and manageable. Make sure to choose a conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.

Avoid Over-styling

Avoid over-styling your hair, as it can damage it. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and hair products.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for over 60 round face and glasses can be challenging. But with the right tips, you can find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, glasses, and hair type. Remember to maintain your hairstyle and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
