Mens Haircut Longer Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that's easy to maintain, then the mens haircut longer top short sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. It's a versatile haircut that works well with different hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to get and maintain the mens haircut longer top short sides.

Description

The mens haircut longer top short sides is a modern and stylish haircut that's perfect for men who want to look sharp and trendy. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it suitable for different occasions and settings. The longer hair on the top of the head can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, textured, or swept to the side, while the shorter sides and back provide a neat and clean look.

How To Get The Mens Haircut Longer Top Short Sides

If you're interested in getting the mens haircut longer top short sides, you can follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Type

The mens haircut longer top short sides works well with different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the styling and maintenance of the haircut may vary depending on your hair type. If you have straight hair, you may need to use some hair products to add texture and volume to the longer hair on the top of your head. If you have wavy or curly hair, you may want to keep the longer hair on the top of your head a bit shorter to avoid frizz.

Step 2: Find A Good Hairstylist

It's essential to find a skilled hairstylist who can give you the mens haircut longer top short sides that suits your hair type and face shape. Look for a hairstylist who has experience in cutting men's hair and has good reviews from previous clients.

Step 3: Communicate With Your Hairstylist

Before the haircut, communicate with your hairstylist about the length and style you want. Bring some pictures of the mens haircut longer top short sides that you like to give your hairstylist a clear idea of what you want.

Step 4: Get The Haircut

During the haircut, your hairstylist will cut the sides and back of your hair short while leaving the top longer. The length of the longer hair on the top of your head can vary depending on your preference and face shape.

Step 5: Style The Haircut

After the haircut, you can style your hair in different ways, depending on your preference and the occasion. You can use hair products like wax, pomade, or gel to add texture and hold to the longer hair on the top of your head. You can also comb or brush your hair to create different styles.

Tips For Maintaining The Mens Haircut Longer Top Short Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining the mens haircut longer top short sides:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the haircut looking sharp and neat.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use hair products like wax or pomade to add texture and hold to the longer hair on the top of your head.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.

FAQs

1. Is the mens haircut longer top short sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens haircut longer top short sides works well with different face shapes, including round, square, and oval faces. However, the length and style of the longer hair on the top of your head may vary depending on your face shape.

2. How long should the longer hair on the top of my head be?

The length of the longer hair on the top of your head can vary depending on your preference and face shape. Generally, it can be between 2 to 4 inches long.

3. How often should I visit my hairstylist for the mens haircut longer top short sides?

It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4 to 6 weeks to keep the haircut looking sharp and neat.

Hair Care For Mens Haircut Longer Top Short Sides

Here are some hair care tips for mens haircut longer top short sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use hair products like wax or pomade to add texture and hold to the longer hair on the top of your head.
  • Brush or comb your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, the mens haircut longer top short sides is a stylish and trendy haircut that's perfect for men who want to look sharp and trendy. It's a versatile haircut that works well with different hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.
