How To Straighten Short Hair With Volume A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In today's world, hairstyles play a crucial role in defining one's personality. Many people love to have voluminous short hair, but it can be challenging to straighten them. If you are one of those individuals who struggle with straightening their short hair with volume, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to straighten short hair with volume.

Description

Short hair with volume is a popular hairstyle that is ideal for those who want to achieve a fuller look. However, straightening short hair with volume can be a bit tricky. It requires the right tools, techniques, and products to get the desired results. In this article, we will discuss how to straighten short hair with volume in detail.

How to Straighten Short Hair with Volume

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to straightening short hair with volume is to wash your hair. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. After washing your hair, use a towel to dry it.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, it is essential to apply a heat protectant. This will protect your hair from heat damage and prevent breakage. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair and comb it through.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely. Make sure to use a round brush to create volume in your hair while blow-drying it. Once your hair is completely dry, divide it into sections.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Take one section of your hair and run a straightener through it. Make sure to start at the roots and work your way to the ends. Repeat this process with all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Apply a Finishing Product

Once you have straightened your hair, apply a finishing product, such as hairspray or serum to set your hairstyle in place. This will also add shine to your hair and prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you straighten short hair with volume:

  • Invest in a good quality straightener.
  • Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  • Divide your hair into sections to ensure that you straighten all parts of your hair.
  • Apply a finishing product to set your hairstyle in place and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Can I straighten short hair with volume without using a heat protectant?

No, it is not recommended to straighten your hair without using a heat protectant. Heat can damage your hair and cause breakage.

How often should I straighten my hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair every day as it can damage your hair. Try to limit it to once or twice a week.

What type of straightener should I use?

You should use a straightener that is suitable for your hair type. If you have thin hair, use a straightener with lower heat settings, and if you have thick hair, use a straightener with higher heat settings.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips that can help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair.

Straightening short hair with volume can be challenging, but with the right tools, techniques, and products, you can achieve the desired results. Follow the steps mentioned in this article, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage.
