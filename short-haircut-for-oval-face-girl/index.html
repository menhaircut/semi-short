Short Haircut For Oval Face Girl The Perfect Style For You

Written by Frank Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try a new look? A short haircut can give you a fresh and modern appearance. If you have an oval face, you are in luck because this face shape is versatile and can pull off various short hairstyles. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect short haircut for oval face girl.

Description

An oval face has balanced proportions, with a slightly wider forehead and prominent cheekbones. The chin is slightly narrower than the forehead, which gives the face an oval shape. This face shape is considered the ideal shape because it can carry any hairstyle. However, you still need to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut for Oval Face Girl

Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect short haircut for oval face girl:

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is an essential factor in choosing a short haircut. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a textured lob can help thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be considered when choosing a short haircut. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance haircut like a pixie cut or a short bob can be perfect for you. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can opt for a choppy bob or a textured lob.

Consider Your Personality

Your personality can also influence your choice of a short haircut. If you are bold and daring, you can try a buzz cut or an asymmetrical bob. If you are more reserved, a classic bob or a layered lob can be perfect for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair for Oval Face Girl

If you have decided to cut your hair short, here is a step-by-step guide to cutting short hair for oval face girl:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils. Towel-dry your hair and comb it straight.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section securely.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Starting with the sides, comb the hair straight and cut at a 45-degree angle. Work your way towards the back, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Comb the top section straight and cut at a 90-degree angle. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the shape of your head.

Step 5: Cut the Back

Unclip the back section and comb it straight. Cut the hair at a 45-degree angle, starting from the nape of your neck.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair and style it using a flat iron or a curling iron. You can also use hair products to add volume or texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Use Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style. Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to fine hair, or a texturizing spray to add texture to thick hair.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for oval face girl?

The best short haircut for oval face girl is a layered bob or a textured lob. These haircuts can flatter your face shape and add volume and texture to your hair.

Can oval face girl pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, oval face girl can pull off a pixie cut. However, you need to choose a pixie cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

How do I know if a short haircut will look good on me?

You can use virtual hairstyle apps or consult with your hairstylist to see if a short haircut will look good on you. You can also try on wigs or hair extensions to see how you would look with a short haircut.

Hair Care for Short Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for short haircut:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray and set the heat to a lower temperature.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for oval face girl. Remember to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personality when choosing a haircut. Keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to keep your short haircut looking great.
