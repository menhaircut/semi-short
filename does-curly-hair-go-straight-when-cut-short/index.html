Does Curly Hair Go Straight When Cut Short

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is beautiful and unique, but it can also be challenging to manage. If you have curly hair, you may be wondering what happens when you cut it short. Will your curls disappear, and will your hair become straight? In this blog post, we’ll explore the answer to the question, “Does curly hair go straight when cut short?”

Description

Curly hair is a result of the shape of the hair follicle. The hair follicle of curly hair is oval-shaped, which causes the hair to curl as it grows. When curly hair is cut short, the hair is still curly, but the shape of the curls may be more defined. The length of your hair can affect the curl pattern, but it won't change the shape of the hair follicle.

How to

If you want to cut your curly hair short, it's important to find a stylist who understands how to cut curly hair. Curly hair should be cut dry, and the stylist should cut the hair in its natural curl pattern. This ensures that the curls will fall correctly and that the shape of the curls will be preserved.

Step by Step

1. Find a stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair.

2. Have a consultation with the stylist to discuss the length and style you want.

3. Wash and dry your hair before the appointment.

4. Have your hair cut dry in its natural curl pattern.

5. Maintain your curls with regular trims and a good hair care routine.

Tips

If you're unsure about cutting your curly hair short, start with a gradual cut. This means cutting off a little bit of hair each time you go to the salon until you reach your desired length. This allows you to adjust to the shorter length and make sure you're comfortable with the change.

FAQ

Will my curls disappear if I cut my hair short?

No, your curls won't disappear if you cut your hair short. The shape of the hair follicle determines the curl pattern, not the length of the hair.

How should I style my curly hair after cutting it short?

You can style your curly hair in a variety of ways after cutting it short. Some popular styles for short curly hair include a pixie cut, a bob, or a curly shag. You can also experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your curls.

Do I need to use different hair care products after cutting my curly hair short?

You may need to adjust your hair care routine after cutting your curly hair short. Shorter hair may require less product, and you may need to use different products to maintain the shape of your curls. Talk to your stylist about the best hair care products for your new style.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy, curly hair, it's important to use the right hair care products. Choose products that are designed for curly hair, and avoid products that contain sulfates and alcohol, which can dry out your hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized, and avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your curls.

In conclusion, cutting your curly hair short won't make your curls disappear or turn your hair straight. The shape of the hair follicle determines the curl pattern, and this won't change with a shorter length. If you're considering cutting your curly hair short, find a stylist who understands how to cut curly hair, and maintain your curls with regular trims and a good hair care routine.
