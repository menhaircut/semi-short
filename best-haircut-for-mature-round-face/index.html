Best Haircut For Mature Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our face shape changes and we need to update our hairstyle accordingly. If you have a round face and are looking for the best haircut to flatter your features, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll discuss the best haircut for mature round face, how to achieve it, and how to take care of your hair to maintain the style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wide forehead. The best haircut for mature round face is one that adds height and length to the face, while also creating angles to balance out the roundness. This can be achieved through layers, angles, and volume.

How to

The best way to achieve the perfect haircut for a mature round face is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your face shape and recommend the best haircut for you. However, there are a few general guidelines to keep in mind:

1. Consider Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out a round face. Long layers that start below the chin can be particularly flattering for mature women.

2. Opt for Angles

Angled cuts can create the illusion of a longer, more slender face. A long bob with angled layers can be a great option for a round face.

3. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Blunt cuts can emphasize the roundness of your face, so it’s best to avoid them. Instead, opt for cuts with layers and angles.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieving the best haircut for a mature round face:

1. Consult with a Hairstylist

The first step is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

2. Choose the Right Cut

Based on your consultation, choose a cut that adds height and length to your face while also creating angles to balance out the roundness.

3. Style Your Hair

Once you have your cut, style your hair to enhance the shape. Use volumizing products and a round brush to add height and movement.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Invest in Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that will help you achieve and maintain your desired style. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Consider Hair Extensions

If you have thin hair, hair extensions can add volume and length to your hair, which can help balance out a round face.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural texture and work with it rather than against it. If you have curly hair, for example, consider a cut that enhances your curls.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a mature round face?

The best haircut for a mature round face is one that adds height and length to the face, while also creating angles to balance out the roundness. This can be achieved through layers, angles, and volume.

How do I know if I have a round face?

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wide forehead.

Can I still have short hair with a round face?

Yes, you can still have short hair with a round face. Just make sure to choose a cut that adds height and length to your face, such as a pixie cut with textured layers.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut and keep it looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Use a High-Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to maintain your desired style.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best haircut for mature round face, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist! Remember to follow these tips and take care of your hair to maintain your new look.
