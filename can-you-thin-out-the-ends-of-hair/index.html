Can You Thin Out The Ends Of Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Thinning out the ends of your hair is a common practice that many people do to achieve a more natural and textured look. This technique involves removing some of the bulk from the ends of your hair without significantly reducing its length. If you're wondering whether you can thin out the ends of your relaxed hair, the answer is yes! In this article, we'll discuss how to thin out your relaxed hair and some tips for maintaining your hair's health.

How to Thin Out Relaxed Hair

Before you start thinning out your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, towel-dry it until it's slightly damp. It's easier to work with damp hair when you're thinning it out.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Use your comb to section off your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle, then divide it into four equal sections. Clip each section out of the way so that you can work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Start Thinning Out Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from one of the sections you've clipped. Hold the hair between your fingers, and use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle. Make sure you don't cut too much at once. Repeat this process until you've thinned out the hair to your liking.

Step 4: Repeat on the Other Sections

Once you've finished thinning out one section, unclip the next section and repeat the process. Keep going until you've thinned out all four sections of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're finished thinning out your hair, style it as you normally would. You should notice a difference in the texture and volume of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Thinned Out Hair

Now that you've thinned out your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

FAQ

Can I thin out my hair if it's already thin?

If your hair is already thin, it's not recommended to thin it out further. Thinning out your hair can make it look even thinner and can cause damage if you're not careful. Instead, try adding layers to your hair to add texture and volume.

Can I thin out my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can thin out curly hair. However, you should be careful not to thin out too much, as this can cause your curls to become limp and lifeless. Start with a small amount and gradually thin out more if necessary.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It's recommended to thin out your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and texture.

Hair Care

Thinning out the ends of your relaxed hair is a great way to add texture and volume to your style. However, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health. Use good quality hair products, avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. By following these tips, you'll be able to enjoy your thinned out hair for longer.
