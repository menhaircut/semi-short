How To Look Fashionable At 70

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, we tend to think that fashion is no longer important. However, looking fashionable is not just about keeping up with the latest trends. It's about feeling confident and comfortable in your own skin. Whether you're retired or still working, there are plenty of ways to look fashionable at 70. In this article, we will discuss some tips on how to look fashionable at 70 while still feeling comfortable and confident.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Fashionable at 70
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Fashionable Tips
  • FAQs

How to Look Fashionable at 70

Age is just a number, and you can still look fashionable at 70. Here are some tips:

Clothing

Choosing the right clothing is the first step to looking fashionable at 70. Opt for pieces that flatter your body shape, and choose fabrics that feel good against your skin. Avoid anything too tight or too revealing, and opt for classic pieces that will never go out of style.

Accessories

Accessories can take any outfit to the next level. Invest in a few statement pieces such as a scarf, necklace, or earrings. These accessories can instantly elevate any outfit and add a touch of glamour.

Makeup

Makeup can help you look and feel more confident. Focus on enhancing your best features and using products that are age-appropriate. A little bit of blush, mascara, and lipstick can go a long way.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to look fashionable at 70:

Step 1: Clean Out Your Closet

Start by cleaning out your closet and getting rid of anything that no longer fits or is outdated. This will give you a better idea of what you have and what you need to add to your wardrobe.

Step 2: Invest in Classic Pieces

Invest in classic pieces such as a tailored blazer, a stylish pair of jeans, and a little black dress. These pieces will never go out of style and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Step 3: Choose Flattering Fabrics

Choose fabrics that feel good against your skin and flatter your body shape. Avoid anything too tight or too revealing, and opt for natural fibers such as cotton, linen, and silk.

Step 4: Accessorize

Add accessories such as a scarf, belt, or statement jewelry to complete your look. These pieces can instantly elevate any outfit and add a touch of glamour.

Step 5: Wear Age-Appropriate Makeup

Enhance your best features and use products that are age-appropriate. A little bit of blush, mascara, and lipstick can go a long way.

Fashionable Tips

Here are some fashionable tips for looking stylish at 70:

Tip 1: Embrace Your Personal Style

Don't be afraid to embrace your personal style. Whether you prefer a classic or trendy look, wear what makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tip 2: Experiment with Color

Experiment with color and try something new. Don't be afraid to mix and match different colors and patterns.

Tip 3: Wear Comfortable Shoes

Wear comfortable shoes that are both stylish and practical. Opt for flats or low heels, and avoid anything too high or uncomfortable.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear jeans at 70?

A: Yes, you can still wear jeans at 70. Opt for a stylish pair of jeans that flatter your body shape and pair them with a classic blouse or sweater.

Q: What kind of jewelry should I wear at 70?

A: You can wear any kind of jewelry at 70, but it's best to choose statement pieces that add a touch of glamour to your outfit. Necklaces, earrings, and bracelets are all great options.

Hair Care

Looking fashionable at 70 also involves taking care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Keep Your Hair Healthy

Keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly and using a conditioner. If you color your hair, touch up your roots every few weeks to maintain your color.

Tip 2: Experiment with a New Hairstyle

Experiment with a new hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. A new hairstyle can instantly update your look and make you feel more confident.

Tip 3: Use Hair Accessories

Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and barrettes to add a touch of glamour to your hair. These accessories can also help keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

In conclusion, looking fashionable at 70 is all about embracing your personal style, choosing the right clothing and accessories, and taking care of your hair. With these tips, you can look and feel confident at any age.
