How To Get Wavy Curls Without Using Heat In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to get wavy curls without using heat, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll go over some easy steps you can take to achieve beautiful, natural-looking waves without damaging your hair with heat.

How to Get Wavy Curls Without Heat

There are several ways to get wavy curls without using heat. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Method 1: Braids

One of the easiest ways to get wavy curls without using heat is by braiding your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Leave the braids in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. In the morning, undo the braids and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.

This method works best on damp hair, so be sure to braid your hair while it's still slightly wet.

Method 2: Twists

Another way to get wavy curls without using heat is by twisting your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Leave the twists in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  4. In the morning, undo the twists and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.

This method works best on damp hair, so be sure to twist your hair while it's still slightly wet.

Method 3: Pin Curls

Pin curls are another great way to get wavy curls without using heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section into a tight spiral.
  3. Secure each spiral with a bobby pin.
  4. Leave the pin curls in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  5. In the morning, remove the bobby pins and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.

This method works best on damp hair, so be sure to roll your hair into pin curls while it's still slightly wet.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting wavy curls without using heat:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Choose a method for creating waves (braids, twists, or pin curls).
  3. Divide your hair into sections and start braiding, twisting, or rolling each section.
  4. Secure each section with a bobby pin or hair tie.
  5. Leave the waves in overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  6. In the morning, remove the bobby pins or hair ties and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.
  7. Apply a light styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to help hold the waves in place.

And that's it! With these simple steps, you can achieve beautiful, wavy curls without using any heat.

Tips for Getting Wavy Curls Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when creating wavy curls without using heat:

  • Start with clean, damp hair.
  • Choose a method that works best for your hair type and length.
  • Use a light styling product to help hold the waves in place.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after creating the waves. Use your fingers to gently separate and loosen the curls.
  • Experiment with different methods and techniques until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I use heat protectant spray with these methods?

A: While you don't need to use heat protectant spray when creating wavy curls without using heat, you can certainly use it if you prefer. Just be sure to apply it before braiding, twisting, or rolling your hair.

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The longevity of the waves will depend on your hair type, the method you used, and the products you used to hold the waves in place. In general, the waves should last at least a day or two.

Q: Can I sleep with wet hair to create waves?

A: It's not recommended to sleep with wet hair, as it can cause damage and breakage. Instead, try to braid, twist, or roll your hair while it's still slightly damp.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best, even when creating wavy curls without using heat.
