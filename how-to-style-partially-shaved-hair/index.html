How To Style Partially Shaved Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Partial shaved hair has been a trending hairstyle for a while now. It’s a bold and unique look that can make you stand out from the crowd. But, if you’re new to this style, you might be wondering how to style it. In this post, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about styling partially shaved hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Partially Shaved Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Partially Shaved Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Partially Shaved Hair

Description

Partially shaved hair is a hairstyle where one side of the head is shaved while the other side has hair. It’s a versatile style that can be worn in many ways, from edgy to elegant. This hairstyle is perfect for people who want to make a statement and try something new with their hair.

How to Style Partially Shaved Hair

Styling partially shaved hair is easy and fun. You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, depending on your mood and occasion. Here are some popular ways to style partially shaved hair:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and edgy haircut that is perfect for partially shaved hair. To style a pixie cut, apply some hair wax or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to style it in the desired direction. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

If you have longer hair on one side of your head, you can style it into side-swept bangs. This hairstyle is perfect for people who want a more feminine and elegant look. To style side-swept bangs, use a round brush to blow dry your hair in the desired direction.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture and dimension to your partially shaved hair. You can create different types of braids, such as French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. To style braids, divide your hair into sections and start braiding from the top of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re new to this hairstyle, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style partially shaved hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair and remove any excess water.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a styling product, such as hair wax or pomade, to your hair. Use your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair and add some volume. You can also use a round brush to create a more polished look.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair in the desired way, such as a pixie cut or side-swept bangs. You can also add some braids or other accessories to your hair.

Tips for Styling Partially Shaved Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling partially shaved hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use high-quality styling products to keep your hair in place.
  • Consider adding some accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, to your hair.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist if you’re unsure about how to style your hair.

FAQs

What should I do if I change my mind about my partially shaved hair?

If you change your mind about your partially shaved hair, you can always grow it out or shave it completely. It’s important to remember that hair grows back, so don’t be afraid to experiment and try new things.

How often should I wash my partially shaved hair?

It’s recommended to wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Partially Shaved Hair

Here are some tips to keep your partially shaved hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

Styling partially shaved hair can be a fun and exciting experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a unique and personalized look that suits your personality and style. So, go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles and enjoy your new look!
