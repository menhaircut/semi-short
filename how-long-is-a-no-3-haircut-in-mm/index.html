How Long Is A No 3 Haircut In Mm

Written by Luffy Jun 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to get a new haircut or want to learn more about different hairstyles, you might be wondering, "How long is a no 3 haircut in mm?" In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including its length in millimeters, how to get it, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

A no 3 haircut is a short hairstyle that is cut with a 3mm guard on clippers. This haircut is also known as a "number 3" or "3 guard" haircut. It leaves about 3/8 of an inch (9.5mm) of hair on the scalp, making it a popular choice for those who want a short, low-maintenance haircut that still has some length.

How To

If you want to get a no 3 haircut, you can either go to a professional barber or do it yourself at home with clippers. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Clippers

You'll need clippers with a 3mm guard to get a no 3 haircut. Make sure they're charged and have oil on the blades.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a clean, even cut.

Step 3: Trim Your Hair

Use the clippers to trim your hair all over your head, using the 3mm guard. Start at the back of your head and work your way up to the top. Make sure to go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Clean Up Your Neckline

Use a trimmer or razor to clean up your neckline, making sure the hair is straight and even.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair with a small amount of hair product, such as wax or gel, if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting and maintaining a no 3 haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality clippers for the best results.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it may look uneven or patchy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about no 3 haircuts:

How long is a no 3 haircut in inches?

A no 3 haircut leaves about 3/8 of an inch (9.5mm) of hair on the scalp.

What other haircuts are similar to a no 3?

Other haircuts that are similar to a no 3 include a no 2, no 4, and no 5 haircut, which use different guard sizes to achieve different lengths.

Is a no 3 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a no 3 haircut is suitable for all hair types, including thick, curly, and thin hair.

Hair Care

To keep your no 3 haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your haircut's shape.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

In conclusion, a no 3 haircut is a short, low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a clean, neat look without sacrificing length. Whether you go to a barber or do it yourself at home, follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect no 3 haircut.
