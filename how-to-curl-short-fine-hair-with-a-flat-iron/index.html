How To Curl Short Fine Hair With A Flat Iron

Written by Christine Nov 08, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of trying to curl your short, fine hair with a curling iron only to end up with lifeless, limp locks? Fear not, because with the right technique and tools, you can create beautiful, bouncy curls using a flat iron. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to achieve gorgeous curls that will make your short fine hair look voluminous and full.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we get started, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a flat iron, heat protectant spray, and hair clips.

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that will add body to your hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair into sections. Clip up the top section and start with the bottom layer.
  3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the roots. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down the length of your hair. Release the flat iron and let the curl cool in your hand before dropping it.
  4. Repeat this process on the rest of your hair, working your way up to the top section.
  5. For the top section, take smaller sections of hair to create tighter curls. Make sure to curl the hair away from your face.
  6. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create more volume. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Short Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.
  • Don’t clamp the flat iron too hard on your hair or you risk creating a crease.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair and hold the flat iron for a longer period of time.
  • If you have trouble curling the hair at the back of your head, use a mirror to guide you.

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: Use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying your hair to add more body. You can also use a curl-enhancing cream to help hold the curl.

Q: Can I curl my hair with a flat iron if it’s already been curled?

A: Yes, you can touch up your curls with a flat iron. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your short fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using high heat settings on your styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls that will make your short fine hair look full and voluminous. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
