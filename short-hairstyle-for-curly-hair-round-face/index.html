Short Hairstyle For Curly Hair Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Nov 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find a hairstyle that suits your curly hair and round face? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect short hairstyle for your curly hair and round face shape. From how to style your hair to tips and tricks on how to care for it, we’ve got you covered.

Description

A round face shape has a width that is almost equal to its length, with a soft jawline and cheekbones. Curly hair, on the other hand, has a tendency to create volume and body, which can make a round face look wider than it actually is. However, with the right short hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and create a flattering look that suits your unique style.

How to Find the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Curly Hair Round Face

The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your curly hair and round face is to choose a cut that balances out your features. This means creating volume at the top of your head and avoiding styles that add width to your cheeks. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect short hairstyle:

Tip 1: Consider Your Hair Texture

The first thing to consider when choosing a short hairstyle for curly hair is your hair texture. Curly hair tends to be thicker and coarser than straight hair, which means you’ll need a cut that can handle the volume and body. A layered cut that creates movement and texture is a great option for curly hair.

Tip 2: Look for Styles with Volume at the Crown

Creating volume at the crown of your head can help balance out a round face shape. A short layered cut with volume at the top can create the illusion of a longer face, which is more flattering for a round face shape.

Tip 3: Avoid Adding Width to Your Cheeks

Avoid styles that add width to your cheeks, such as a blunt bob. Instead, opt for a layered cut that frames your face and creates movement. This will help draw attention away from your cheeks and towards your eyes and cheekbones.

Tip 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a short hairstyle that suits your lifestyle. If you’re always on the go, a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling may be the best option for you. However, if you have more time to style your hair, a more intricate cut with lots of layers and texture may be a better fit.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair for a Round Face

Now that you’ve found the perfect short hairstyle for your curly hair and round face, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a product designed for curly hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl-enhancing mousse or gel. This will help define your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair. This will help create volume and body while reducing frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or wand. Create loose waves or curls, focusing on the top of your head to create volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Caring for Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair an extra boost of moisture.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing curly hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

Tip 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Curly hair is prone to tangling, so use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair after washing it.

Tip 5: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair, so try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you do use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What short hairstyles are best for a round face and curly hair?

Short layered cuts with volume at the crown are the best option for a round face and curly hair.

What styling products should I use for short curly hair?

Curl-enhancing mousse or gel can help define your curls and add volume to your hair.

How often should I wash my short curly hair?

Curly hair should be washed no more than two or three times a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Conclusion

With the right short hairstyle and proper care, you can create a flattering look that suits your curly hair and round face shape. Remember to choose a cut that balances out your features and use styling products that enhance your curls and create volume. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking a fabulous short hairstyle in no time!
