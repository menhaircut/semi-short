Best Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks For Fabulous Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best hair over 60! Aging does not mean you have to compromise on your hair. In fact, your hair can be one of your most attractive features, even as you get older. This guide will help you learn how to take care of your hair, what styles work best for you, and how to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. So, let's get started!

How to Take Care of Your Hair

When it comes to the best hair over 60, taking care of your hair is crucial. The following tips will help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove dirt, oils, and other impurities that can build up over time. However, you should avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your scalp of essential oils. Instead, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

As you get older, your hair becomes more delicate, so it's essential to use gentle products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for mature hair, as they contain ingredients that help to nourish and protect your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's best to avoid them as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat at a low setting.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts is essential for maintaining healthy hair. As you get older, your hair may become thinner or more brittle, so getting a trim every 6-8 weeks can help to prevent split ends and breakage.

What Styles Work Best for You?

When it comes to the best hair over 60, there are many styles to choose from. Here are a few that work well:

1. Short and Sassy

Short haircuts are trendy and easy to maintain. They're also a great way to show off your face and neck, which can be especially flattering as you get older.

2. Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are classic and versatile, making them a great choice for women of all ages. They can be worn straight or wavy, and you can customize the length to suit your face shape and personal style.

3. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create movement and volume. This style is great for women with thin or fine hair, as it can help to create the illusion of thickness.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Healthy and Beautiful

Here are a few additional tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for healthy hair. Be sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet, as these nutrients are vital for strong, healthy hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so it's essential to protect it from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and strengthen your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for fine, thin hair?

The best haircut for fine, thin hair is a layered bob. Layers can create the illusion of thickness and volume, making your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

What is the best hair color for women over 60?

The best hair color for women over 60 is one that complements your skin tone and personal style. Soft, natural-looking shades like blonde, brown, and grey are always a great choice.

Conclusion

With the right care and styling, your hair can look fabulous at any age. Whether you prefer short and sassy or long and layered, there is a style that will work for you. So, take care of your hair, choose a style that suits you, and enjoy your beautiful, healthy hair for years to come!
