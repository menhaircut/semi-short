Will My Face Look Good With Short Hair

Written by William Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair has been a trend for quite some time now, and many women are opting for this look. However, not everyone is confident enough to chop off their long locks. One of the primary concerns that women have is whether they will look good with short hair. In this article, we will try to answer this question and provide you with some tips on how to rock a short hairstyle that complements your face shape.

Description

Short hair is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape, but you need to choose the right haircut that will flatter your features. Whether you have a heart-shaped, oval, round, or square face, there is a short hairstyle that will suit you. However, it is essential to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a short haircut.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right short haircut for your face shape can be challenging, but it is not impossible. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a short bob will look great on you. You can also opt for a pixie cut or a short bob with side-swept bangs. These hairstyles will balance your features and draw attention away from your forehead.

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes are lucky because they can pull off almost any short hairstyle. However, a choppy bob or a messy pixie cut will look fantastic on you. These hairstyles will add volume to your hair and create a playful look.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you need to create the illusion of length. A short bob with layers will elongate your face and add dimension to your hair. You can also opt for a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you need to soften your features with a short hairstyle. A short bob with layers or a pixie cut with wispy bangs will look great on you. These hairstyles will add movement to your hair and create a feminine look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair Short

If you have decided to cut your hair short, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the length you want. You can use a ruler or a tape measure to measure the length you want to cut.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair ties or clips. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the bottom up. Use sharp scissors to cut your hair in small sections. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Check Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, check your hair in a mirror to ensure that it is even. You can also ask someone to check your hair for you.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Maintaining short hair requires some effort, but it is worth it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use volumizing products to add body to your hair. If you have curly hair, use products that define your curls.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair grows faster than long hair, so you need to trim your hair regularly to maintain your hairstyle. Visit your hairdresser every four to six weeks to keep your hair in shape.

Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Short hair is versatile, and you can experiment with different hairstyles. You can add braids, curls, or waves to your hair to create a different look.

FAQs

1. Will short hair suit me?

Short hair can suit any face shape, but you need to choose the right haircut that complements your features. Consult with your hairdresser to find the right hairstyle for you.

2. How do I know if a short hairstyle will suit me?

You can try on different short hairstyles using virtual hairstyle apps or by wearing a wig. This will give you an idea of how a short hairstyle will look on you.

3. How do I style short hair?

You can style short hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type to create the desired look.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires some hair care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Short hair tends to get oily quickly, so you need to wash it regularly to keep it clean.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged by the sun's harmful rays.

Conclusion

Short hair is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape, but you need to choose the right haircut that complements your features. Use the tips in this article to choose the right short hairstyle for you and maintain it properly. With the right haircut and hair care, you can rock a short hairstyle and look fabulous.
