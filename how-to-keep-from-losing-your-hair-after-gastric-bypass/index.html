How To Keep From Losing Your Hair After Gastric Bypass

Written by Aprilia Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you worried about losing your hair after undergoing gastric bypass surgery? You’re not alone. Many people who have had this surgery experience hair loss, but there are things you can do to prevent it. In this article, we’ll discuss how to keep from losing your hair after gastric bypass.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Keep from Losing Your Hair After Gastric Bypass
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Gastric bypass surgery is a weight loss surgery that involves reducing the size of the stomach and rerouting the small intestine. This results in weight loss, but it can also cause hair loss. This is because the body is not getting enough nutrients, including protein, which is essential for healthy hair growth.

It’s important to note that not everyone who undergoes gastric bypass surgery will experience hair loss. However, if you do, it will typically occur 3-6 months after the surgery.

How to Keep from Losing Your Hair After Gastric Bypass

The good news is that there are things you can do to prevent hair loss after gastric bypass surgery. Here are some tips:

1. Get Enough Protein

Protein is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you’re getting enough protein by eating foods such as eggs, lean meats, fish, nuts, and legumes. You may also want to consider taking a protein supplement.

2. Take Vitamins and Supplements

Make sure you’re getting enough vitamins and minerals, including vitamin D, vitamin B12, iron, and zinc. You may need to take supplements to ensure you’re getting enough of these nutrients.

3. Avoid Crash Diets

Avoid crash diets or diets that restrict certain foods or nutrients. These types of diets can cause hair loss.

4. Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as coloring or perming your hair. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and avoid tight hairstyles such as tight braids or ponytails.

5. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair loss. Find ways to manage stress, such as exercise, meditation, or deep breathing.

6. Talk to Your Doctor

If you’re experiencing hair loss after gastric bypass surgery, talk to your doctor. They may be able to recommend additional supplements or treatments to help prevent hair loss.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for preventing hair loss after gastric bypass surgery:

  1. Eat a diet rich in protein
  2. Take vitamins and supplements as recommended by your doctor
  3. Avoid crash diets and diets that restrict certain foods or nutrients
  4. Be gentle with your hair
  5. Manage stress
  6. Talk to your doctor if you’re experiencing hair loss

Tips

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair loss after gastric bypass surgery:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons
  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables to ensure you’re getting all the nutrients you need
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption

FAQ

Q. Will my hair grow back after gastric bypass surgery?

A. Yes, in most cases hair loss after gastric bypass surgery is temporary and your hair will grow back.

Q. How long will it take for my hair to grow back?

A. It can take up to a year for your hair to fully grow back.

Q. Can I dye my hair after gastric bypass surgery?

A. It’s best to avoid dying your hair for at least 6 months after surgery to allow your hair to recover.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair after gastric bypass surgery:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hair accessories that can pull on your hair
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or salt water
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat or cold

By following these tips, you can prevent hair loss after gastric bypass surgery and keep your hair looking healthy and full.
