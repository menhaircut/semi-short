Description

How to Thin Out Thick Long Hair at Home

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start thinning out your hair, wash it with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and comb it out.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by separating the top layer of your hair and clipping it away.

Step 4: Begin Thinning Out Your Hair

Take a section of your hair and hold it between your fingers. Slide your fingers down the length of your hair until you reach the point where you want to start thinning it out.

Using your scissors, carefully cut into your hair at a 45-degree angle. Make sure not to cut too much hair at once.

Continue cutting small sections of hair at a time until you have thinned out the entire section.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of your hair.
