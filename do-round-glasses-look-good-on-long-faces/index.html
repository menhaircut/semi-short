Do Round Glasses Look Good On Long Faces

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheeks. If you have a long face shape, you might be wondering if round glasses are a good fit for you. The answer is yes! Round glasses can look great on long faces, but it's important to choose the right pair. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect round glasses for your long face shape.

How To Choose the Right Round Glasses for Long Faces

When it comes to choosing the right glasses for your face shape, there are a few key things to keep in mind. Here are some tips for choosing the perfect round glasses for long faces:

1. Look for Glasses with Soft, Rounded Edges

Glasses with sharp, angular edges can make a long face shape look even longer. Instead, look for glasses with soft, rounded edges. Round glasses are a great choice, as they soften the angles of your face and create a more balanced look.

2. Choose Glasses that are the Right Size

The size of your glasses is important when it comes to getting the right look for your face shape. Glasses that are too small can make your face look longer, while glasses that are too big can overwhelm your features. Look for glasses that are the right size for your face, with frames that sit comfortably on your nose and ears.

3. Consider the Material

The material of your glasses can also make a difference in how they look on your face. If you have a long face shape, consider glasses with thinner frames made of metal or plastic. Thick, heavy frames can add weight to your face and make it look longer.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect round glasses for your long face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right glasses for your face shape is to determine your face shape. If you have a long face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will be roughly the same width, and your face will be longer than it is wide.

Step 2: Look for Glasses with Soft, Rounded Edges

As we mentioned earlier, glasses with soft, rounded edges are a great choice for long face shapes. Look for round glasses with frames that are slightly wider than your face to create a more balanced look.

Step 3: Consider the Size of the Glasses

The size of your glasses is important when it comes to getting the right look for your face shape. Look for glasses that are the right size for your face, with frames that sit comfortably on your nose and ears. Avoid glasses that are too small or too big.

Step 4: Think About the Color and Material of the Frames

When it comes to choosing the color and material of your glasses, consider your skin tone and hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, look for glasses with frames in warm colors like brown, gold, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, look for frames in cool colors like silver, black, or blue. For long face shapes, thinner frames made of metal or plastic are a good choice.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Pair of Round Glasses

Here are some additional tips for choosing the perfect pair of round glasses for your long face shape:

1. Try On Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try on different styles of glasses to see what looks best on you. Round glasses come in a variety of styles and sizes, so experiment until you find the perfect pair.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

Think about your lifestyle when choosing glasses. If you're active and need glasses that will stay in place during physical activity, look for glasses with adjustable nose pads or rubberized grips.

3. Get a Second Opinion

Bring a friend or family member with you when you're trying on glasses. They can give you an objective opinion and help you choose the perfect pair.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear other shapes of glasses if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear other shapes of glasses if you have a long face. Round glasses are a great choice, but other shapes like square, oval, and rectangle can also look good on long faces.

Q: Are there any glasses shapes I should avoid if I have a long face?

A: Glasses with sharp, angular edges can make a long face look even longer. Avoid glasses with square or rectangular frames, as well as glasses with thick, heavy frames.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for long face shapes:

1. Add Volume to the Sides of Your Hair

Adding volume to the sides of your hair can help balance out a long face shape. Try a layered haircut or add waves or curls to your hair.

2. Avoid Center Parts

Center parts can make a long face shape look even longer. Instead, try a side part or a zigzag part.

3. Experiment with Bangs

Bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long face shape. Try side-swept bangs or choppy bangs to add dimension to your hair.

Now that you know how to choose the perfect round glasses for your long face shape, you can confidently shop for your next pair of glasses. Remember to consider the size, shape, and material of the frames, as well as your lifestyle and personal style. With these tips, you'll find the perfect pair of glasses in no time!
