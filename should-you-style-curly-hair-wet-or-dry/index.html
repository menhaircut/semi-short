How to Style Curly Hair Wet or Dry

The method you choose to style your curly hair depends on various factors such as hair type, texture, and desired style. Here are some of the ways you can style curly hair wet or dry:

Wet Styling

Wet styling involves applying styling products to wet hair before styling. This method can help to define curls and reduce frizz. Here are some steps to follow when styling your curly hair wet:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your wet hair.
  3. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Dry Styling

Dry styling involves applying styling products to dry hair before styling. This method can provide more volume and texture. Here are some steps to follow when styling your curly hair dry:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curl-defining cream or gel to define curls and reduce frizz.
  5. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Q: Should I style my curly hair wet or dry?
