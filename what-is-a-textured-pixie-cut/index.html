What Is A Textured Pixie Cut

Written by Bardi Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A textured pixie cut is a trendy and edgy haircut that involves cutting the hair short at the back and sides while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. The hair is then layered and texturized to create a messy, tousled look with plenty of volume and movement.

Description

The textured pixie cut is a popular hairstyle that has been around for several years now. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The cut typically involves short hair at the back and sides, with longer hair on top that is styled into a messy and tousled look.

This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain and style. It is also a great option for those who want to showcase their facial features and bone structure, as it draws attention to the eyes, cheekbones, and jawline.

How to

If you're interested in getting a textured pixie cut, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your hairstylist to determine if a textured pixie cut is the right style for you. Your stylist will assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to ensure that the cut will suit your needs and preferences.

Step 2: Cutting

Once you've decided on the textured pixie cut, your stylist will start by cutting the hair short at the back and sides. The hair on top will be left longer, and layers will be added to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Styling

To style the textured pixie cut, you can use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to the hair. You can also use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to the hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your textured pixie cut:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to the hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of the cut.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Will a textured pixie cut suit my face shape?

A: The textured pixie cut is a versatile style that can suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if the cut will suit your face shape and features.

Q: Can I style my textured pixie cut differently?

A: Yes, you can style your textured pixie cut in different ways. You can use different products and tools to create different looks, such as a sleek and smooth look or a messy and tousled look.

Q: Is a textured pixie cut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, a textured pixie cut is a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain and style. Regular trims and using the right products can help keep the hair looking healthy and styled.

Hair Care

To keep your textured pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With the right products and styling techniques, you can maintain your textured pixie cut and keep it looking its best. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if a textured pixie cut is the right style for you.
