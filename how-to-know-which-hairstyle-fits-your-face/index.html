How To Know Which Hairstyle Fits Your Face A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you’re not sure which style will suit your face shape. The right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look amazing, while the wrong one can detract from your natural beauty. In this guide, we’ll show you how to know which hairstyle fits your face shape, so you can rock a fabulous look that’s perfect for you.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Hairstyle
  3. Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Face
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your forehead is slightly wider than your chin, and your face is longer than it is wide. The jawline is slightly rounded, and the cheekbones are the widest part of your face.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is nearly as wide as it is long, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. Your forehead and jawline are rounded, and your chin is less defined.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width. Your face is angular, with a strong, square jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed. Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower. Your chin is pointed.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight, narrow jawline. Your forehead and cheekbones are the same width.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Hairstyle

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to find the perfect hairstyle for you. Follow these steps:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that are recommended for your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on your hair.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face:

  • Consider your hair color and skin tone when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new, but also remember to stay true to your personal style.
  • Take into account any hair concerns you may have, such as thinning hair or hair loss.
  • Keep in mind the maintenance required for your chosen hairstyle.

FAQs

What hairstyle is best for a round face?

Hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as a pixie cut or a high ponytail, can elongate a round face shape. Avoid blunt bangs and chin-length cuts, which can make the face appear wider.

What hairstyle is best for a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the angular jawline, such as layered cuts and side-swept bangs, are best for a square face shape. Avoid center parts and straight, blunt cuts.

What hairstyle is best for a heart-shaped face?

Hairstyles that add volume at the chin, such as a bob or a layered cut, are best for a heart-shaped face. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s right for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape and choose the perfect hairstyle for you, it’s time to rock a fabulous new look that highlights your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and gorgeous.
