Is Short Hair Still In Style 2021

Introduction

Short hair has been in style for decades, and it's still popular in 2021. Many people love the convenience and versatility of short haircuts. However, some people may be hesitant to try a short hairstyle because they think it's outdated. So, is short hair still in style in 2021? The answer is yes! Short hair is still trendy, and there are many ways to make it work for you.

How to Style Short Hair

There are many ways to style short hair, depending on your hair type and personal style. Some popular short haircuts include the pixie cut, bob, and lob (long bob). Here are some tips for styling short hair:

Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your short hair looks and feels. Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. You may also want to use a styling product, such as mousse, gel, or wax, to add volume or texture to your hair.

Hair Accessories

Adding hair accessories can be a fun way to switch up your short hair look. Try using headbands, clips, or scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.

Hair Color

Changing your hair color can also give your short hair a new look. Consider getting highlights, lowlights, or a new hair color to freshen up your hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide for styling short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are formulated for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too hard. You may want to use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to help reduce frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

If you're using a styling product, apply it to your damp hair, being sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Add Hair Accessories (Optional)

If you want to add hair accessories, do so after you've finished blow drying your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

Get Regular Haircuts

Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer hairstyles. Be sure to get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Use Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, especially if it's short. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

Short hair can work for many different face shapes, but some styles may be more flattering than others. Talk to your hairstylist about which short haircuts will work best for your face shape.

How often should I wash my short hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days between washes. Experiment to find out what works best for you.

Can I still wear my hair up if it's short?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up even if it's short. Try using bobby pins or hair elastics to create a ponytail or bun.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny. Choose products that are formulated for your hair type.

Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling can damage your hair, especially if it's short. Be gentle when styling your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help protect your hair from damage while you sleep. You can also try using a hair cap or scarf to protect your hair.

Conclusion

Short hair is still in style in 2021, and there are many ways to make it work for you. Use the tips and tricks in this article to style and care for your short hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts and styles.
