How To Cut Your Own Hair In The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair in the back? It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will walk you through the steps on how to cut your own hair in the back.

Tools You Will Need

Before we begin, you will need to gather the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Once your hair is dry, comb it straight down.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, create a horizontal parting about an inch above the nape of your neck. Clip the top section of your hair away with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Using your clippers, start at the center of the back of your head and work your way outwards. Use a longer guard if you want to leave more length, or a shorter guard if you want a closer cut. Move the clippers upwards in a sweeping motion, following the natural curve of your head. Repeat this process on both sides of your head.

Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Using your scissors, cut the hair in a straight line. You can also use a point-cutting technique to create a softer, textured look. Hold your scissors at a diagonal angle and snip into the hair, creating small, choppy pieces.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use your mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to even out the length or fix any uneven areas.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair in the Back

  • Start with a longer guard or longer length if you are new to cutting your own hair.
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from a friend or family member.
  • Invest in a good quality mirror that allows you to see the back of your head.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair in the back with just scissors?

While it is possible to cut your own hair in the back with just scissors, it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut. Clippers can help to create a more precise, uniform cut.

What is the best way to cut my own hair in the back?

The best way to cut your own hair in the back is to start with a longer guard or length and work your way down gradually. Take your time and use a combination of scissors and clippers to achieve your desired look.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it is important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes regularly washing and conditioning your hair, using a heat protectant before using styling tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals or products that can damage your hair. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can also help to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to cut your own hair in the back and save money on expensive salon visits. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed. Happy cutting!
