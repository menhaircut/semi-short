How To Straighten Thick Hair With Flat Iron A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizz and unruly hair? Straightening your thick hair with a flat iron may seem like a daunting task, but with the right techniques and products, you can easily achieve sleek, straight locks. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to straighten thick hair with a flat iron.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Straighten Thick Hair with Flat Iron

Straightening thick hair with a flat iron may seem like a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done quickly and easily. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to help tame frizz and prepare your hair for straightening. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it until it’s damp.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before you start straightening your hair, it’s essential to apply a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by the heat of the flat iron. Spray the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair evenly and thoroughly. Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up to the top.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the roots. Slowly glide the flat iron down the hair shaft, making sure to keep the iron moving smoothly and evenly. Repeat this process for each section of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Serum

Once you’ve straightened all of your hair, apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair. This will help keep your hair looking smooth and shiny throughout the day.

Tips for Straightening Thick Hair with Flat Iron

Here are some additional tips to help you straighten your thick hair with a flat iron:

  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates, as this will help distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots
  • Choose a flat iron with adjustable temperature settings, so you can adjust the heat according to your hair type
  • Don’t use too much pressure when straightening your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before straightening it
  • Only straighten your hair when it’s completely dry, as wet hair is more prone to damage

FAQs

Q: How often can I straighten my hair with a flat iron?

A: It’s best to limit straightening your hair with a flat iron to once or twice a week, as frequent use can cause damage and breakage.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy after straightening it?

A: To prevent frizz, apply a hair serum or oil to your hair after straightening it. Avoid touching your hair too much or exposing it to humidity.

Hair Care Tips for Straightened Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your straightened hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your hair’s natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair

Now that you know how to straighten thick hair with a flat iron, you can easily achieve sleek, smooth locks. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and hydrated.
