Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman With Long Hair Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more delicate, but that doesn't mean we have to sacrifice our length. Long hair can be just as flattering and stylish on a 60 year old woman as it is on a younger one. In fact, long hair can be quite versatile, allowing for a variety of hairstyles that suit different occasions and moods.

How to Style Long Hair for a 60 Year Old Woman

When it comes to styling long hair, the key is to keep it healthy and well-nourished. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for mature hair, and avoid using too many heat-styling tools that can damage the hair. Allow your hair to air-dry as much as possible, and use a wide-toothed comb or a soft-bristled brush to detangle it.

Tips for Long Hair Care

Here are some additional tips to care for long hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Woman with Long Hair

Now that you know how to care for your long hair, let's talk about some hairstyle ideas:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, while still maintaining its length. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your jawline or cheekbones, and taper down to the ends. This will create a soft, flowing look that's perfect for everyday wear.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to add some dimension to your long hair, consider getting side-swept bangs. These can help frame your face and draw attention to your eyes, while still keeping the majority of your hair long and flowing.

3. Braids

Braids are a fun and stylish way to change up your look. Try a simple side braid, a fishtail braid, or a braided headband to add some texture to your long hair. You can also experiment with different braiding techniques to see what works best for your hair texture and face shape.

4. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a classic look that's perfect for any occasion. Simply gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, and let the rest flow freely. This style is easy to do and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

5. Updo

If you're looking for a more formal hairstyle, try an updo. This can be a sleek bun, a messy chignon, or a braided crown. Updos are versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style and face shape.

FAQs

1. Can I still have long hair in my 60s?

Absolutely! As long as your hair is healthy and well-maintained, there's no reason why you can't rock long hair in your 60s.

2. Should I avoid certain hairstyles as I age?

While there are no hard-and-fast rules when it comes to hairstyles, some styles may be more flattering than others as you age. Avoid styles that are too severe or harsh, and opt for softer, more natural looks.

3. How often should I wash my long hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can embrace your long hair and feel confident and stylish at any age.
