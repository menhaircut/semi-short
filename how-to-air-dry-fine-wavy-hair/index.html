How To Air Dry Fine Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools every day to tame your fine wavy hair? Air drying your hair is a great way to enhance your natural waves and save time in your daily routine. In this guide, we’ll share some tips on how to air dry fine wavy hair and achieve that effortless, beachy look.

How to Air Dry Fine Wavy Hair

Air drying fine wavy hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique and products, you can achieve beautiful, natural waves. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Gently blot your hair with a microfiber towel or a T-shirt to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling product

Apply a lightweight leave-in conditioner or a styling product that’s specifically designed for wavy hair. This will help define your waves and keep them frizz-free. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

3. Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into 2-3 sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Use hair clips to keep the sections separated. This will make it easier to apply products and ensure that your waves are evenly distributed.

4. Scrunch your hair

Take one section of your hair and scrunch it with your hands. Start from the bottom and work your way up, squeezing your hair gently to encourage the waves. Repeat this step with the other sections of your hair.

5. Let your hair air dry

Once you’ve scrunched your hair, let it air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process, but make sure to use it on the lowest heat setting.

Tips for Air Drying Fine Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect air-dried waves:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a T-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to enhance your waves.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I air dry my hair overnight?

A: Yes, you can air dry your hair overnight. Just make sure to sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Q: How often should I air dry my hair?

A: You can air dry your hair as often as you’d like. However, it’s important to give your hair a break from heat styling tools to prevent damage.

Hair Care Routine for Fine Wavy Hair

Aside from air drying, here are some additional tips for taking care of your fine wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Air drying your fine wavy hair can be a great way to enhance your natural waves and save time in your daily routine. Follow these steps and tips to achieve beautiful, effortless waves that will turn heads.
