How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across Back

Written by Oliver Nov 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Or maybe you're in a pinch and need to give yourself a quick trim? Whatever your reason, cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll go over how to cut your own hair straight across the back in a relaxed English language.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across Back:

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the proper tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a straight and even cut across the back of your head.

What You'll Need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1:

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will help ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles, making it easier to cut.

Step 2:

Part your hair down the middle and clip each side away from the back. This will help you see the back of your head more clearly and prevent you from accidentally cutting the wrong section of hair.

Step 3:

Stand in front of a mirror and hold another mirror behind your head so that you can see the back of your head. Make sure the mirror is positioned high enough so that you can see the entire back of your head.

Step 4:

Take a small section of hair in the middle of the back of your head and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across with your scissors. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully to ensure an even cut.

Step 5:

Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process until you have cut all of the hair at the back of your head.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair:

  • Invest in a good pair of haircutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections as you gain more confidence.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your hair too quickly can lead to uneven cuts.
  • If you're unsure about a particular section of hair, leave it and come back to it later. It's better to be safe than sorry!

FAQs:

Q: Can I cut my hair straight across the back without a mirror?

A: It is not recommended to cut your hair straight across the back without a mirror. You need to be able to see the back of your head to ensure an even cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care After Cutting:

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take proper care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Cutting your own hair can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a straight and even cut across the back of your head. Remember to take your time and be careful, and you'll have beautiful hair in no time!
