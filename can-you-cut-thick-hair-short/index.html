Can You Cut Thick Hair Short A Guide To Getting The Perfect Short Haircut

Written by Eveline Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks great when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One solution to this problem is to cut your thick hair short. But can you cut thick hair short? The answer is yes, you can! In this guide, we will show you how to get the perfect short haircut for your thick hair.

Description

Thick hair is defined as hair that has a large circumference and is dense. This type of hair can be difficult to style and manage, especially when it is long. Cutting your thick hair short can make it easier to manage and style while still looking great.

How to Cut Thick Hair Short

Cutting thick hair short can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in cutting your thick hair short is to choose the right haircut. There are many short haircuts that work well for thick hair, such as a bob, pixie, or layered cut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, you should prep it by washing and drying it. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some lift to your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separated.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back section, working your way to the front. Use sharp scissors and cut your hair in small sections. Trim the ends to remove any split ends or damaged hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, style it using a blow dryer and a round brush. Use a volumizing mousse or hairspray to add some texture and volume to your hair.

Tips for Cutting Thick Hair Short

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting thick hair short:

  • Choose a haircut that works well with your hair type and face shape.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Trim your hair in small sections to avoid any mistakes.
  • Style your hair using a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick hair short:

Can you cut thick hair short?

Yes, you can cut thick hair short. You just need to choose the right haircut and use the proper techniques.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair depends on your hair type and face shape. Some popular haircuts for thick hair include the bob, pixie, and layered cut.

How do you style short thick hair?

You can style short thick hair by using a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume. You can also use a volumizing mousse or hairspray to add texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut thick hair short, you can get the perfect short haircut for your hair type and face shape. Remember to choose the right haircut, use the proper techniques, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
