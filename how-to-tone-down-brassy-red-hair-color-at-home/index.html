How To Tone Down Brassy Red Hair Color At Home

Written by Frank Oct 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a way to tone down your brassy red hair color without heading to the salon? Toning down brassy red hair color at home is easier than you might think. In this article, we will guide you through the process of toning down brassy red hair color at home, step by step.

Description

Red hair color is bold and beautiful, but it can sometimes turn brassy over time due to factors such as sun exposure, heat styling, and hard water. Brassy hair color can make your hair look dull, and it can be challenging to style. Toning down brassy hair color can help enhance the vibrancy of your hair, making it look healthier and more manageable.

How to Tone Down Brassy Red Hair Color at Home

If you want to tone down brassy red hair color at home, you will need the following:

  • Purple shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • A hair dye in a cooler tone
  • Plastic gloves
  • A plastic mixing bowl
  • A tint brush

Here are the steps to tone down your brassy red hair color at home:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is specifically designed to neutralize brassy tones in hair. Apply the shampoo to your hair and leave it on for about 5 minutes before rinsing it off. Repeat the process once a week until you see a desired change in your hair color.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Dye in a Cooler Tone

If you want a more dramatic change in your hair color, you can apply a hair dye in a cooler tone. Choose a hair dye that is at least two shades darker than your current hair color. Mix the hair dye according to the instructions on the package, and apply it to your hair using a tint brush. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended amount of time before rinsing it off.

Step 3: Use a Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tips

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause your hair color to fade faster.
  • Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from your hair.

FAQ

Can I tone down my brassy red hair color without using hair dye?

Yes, you can use a purple shampoo to tone down your brassy red hair color without using hair dye. However, if you want a more dramatic change in your hair color, you will need to use a hair dye in a cooler tone.

What is the difference between purple shampoo and regular shampoo?

Purple shampoo contains purple pigments that neutralize brassy tones in hair, while regular shampoo does not.

How often should I use purple shampoo?

You should use purple shampoo once a week to maintain the desired tone of your hair.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to tone down brassy red hair color at home, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, and protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF. With proper care, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.
