How To Give Yourself A Short Pixie Cut With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to give yourself a bold and stylish new haircut, a short pixie cut with clippers might be just what you need. While it may seem daunting to cut your own hair, following a few simple steps and tips can help you achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of giving yourself a short pixie cut with clippers, including everything from preparation to finishing touches.

Description

A short pixie cut is a hairstyle that is characterized by short, cropped layers around the head, with longer layers on top. It's a bold and stylish look that can be customized to suit your individual style and preferences. Using clippers to achieve this cut can make the process easier and more precise, especially if you're new to cutting your own hair.

How to Give Yourself a Short Pixie Cut with Clippers: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary supplies on hand. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel or cape to protect your clothing from hair clippings.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly and allowing it to dry completely. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the final result looks neat and polished.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section your hair into four parts: two on the top of your head and two on the sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate and out of the way.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Begin cutting the sides of your hair with the clippers. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard, depending on how short you want your hair to be. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it evenly.

Step 5: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your head and repeat the process, working from the bottom up. Use the longer guard at the bottom and gradually work your way up to a shorter guard as you move higher up your head.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top of your head. Use the scissors to trim the longer layers at the top of your head to your desired length. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and in small sections, taking care not to cut too much at once.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Using the scissors, blend the shorter layers on the sides and back of your head with the longer layers on top. This will create a seamless and natural-looking finish.

Step 8: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers and scissors to clean up any stray hairs around the edges of your hairline and ears. Take your time and work carefully to achieve a polished and professional finish.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it according to your preferences. You can add texture with a styling product or keep it sleek and simple.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Short Pixie Cut with Clippers

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid cutting your hair too short.

- Take your time and work in small sections to achieve a polished finish.

- Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head as you cut.

- If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider practicing on a wig or mannequin head first.

FAQ

Can I give myself a short pixie cut with scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can use scissors to achieve a short pixie cut, but clippers can make the process easier and more precise.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair shorter or blend the layers to fix any unevenness.

How often should I trim my short pixie cut?

Your hair will grow out faster with a short pixie cut, so it's recommended that you trim it every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Cuts

To keep your short pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair. You may also want to invest in a high-quality styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add volume and texture to your hair.

With a little practice and these helpful tips, you can give yourself a stylish and on-trend short pixie cut with clippers. Enjoy your new look and the confidence that comes with it!
