How To Get Bushy Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight and lifeless hair? Do you dream of having big, bushy curls that turn heads wherever you go? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to get bushy curly hair in a few easy steps. From choosing the right products to styling your hair, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve the perfect look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Bushy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Get Bushy Curly Hair

Getting bushy curly hair requires a combination of the right products, techniques, and styling. Here are some things you need to consider:

1. Choose the Right Products

The first step in getting bushy curly hair is to choose the right products. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling creams. These products will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

You may also want to consider using a leave-in conditioner and a styling gel or mousse to help hold your curls in place. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol, as these can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

2. Use the Right Techniques

Once you have the right products, it's time to start styling your hair. Here are some techniques to try:

  • Scrunching: This involves using your hands to scrunch your hair upward, which will help define your curls and add volume.
  • Plopping: Plopping is a technique where you use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to wrap your hair, which helps to reduce frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Diffusing: A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help enhance your curls by distributing heat evenly and reducing frizz.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You can try wearing your hair down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. You can also experiment with different partings and accessories to add some extra flair to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting bushy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling gel or mousse.
  3. Scrunch your hair upward to define your curls and add volume.
  4. Plop your hair by wrapping it in a microfiber towel or t-shirt to reduce frizz.
  5. Diffuse your hair with a hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  6. Experiment with different styles, such as wearing your hair down, in a ponytail, or in a bun.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your bushy curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and keep your curls intact.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bushy curly hair:

Q: Can I get bushy curly hair if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can! You'll need to use the right products and techniques to enhance your curls, but it's definitely possible.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: What should I do if my curls look frizzy?

A: Try using a leave-in conditioner and a styling gel or mousse to help hold your curls in place. You can also try plopping your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or sulfate-based shampoos, as these can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to get bushy curly hair that looks amazing and turns heads wherever you go. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you. Good luck!
