How to Style Long Hair in an Updo

Styling long hair in an updo can seem like a daunting task, but with these easy to do updo hairstyles for long hair, you'll be able to style your hair in no time. The first step in styling your hair in an updo is to decide what type of updo you want to create. There are various types of updos, ranging from messy buns to sleek chignons.

Once you have decided on the type of updo you want to create, you should gather all the necessary tools and products. This includes bobby pins, hair ties, hairspray, and a comb. You may also want to use a curling iron or straightener to add some texture to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Creating Updo Hairstyles

Now that you've gathered all the necessary tools and products, it's time to start creating your updo hairstyle. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Decide where you want to place your updo. You can create an updo at the top of your head, at the nape of your neck, or anywhere in between.
  3. Gather your hair and twist it into a bun, securing it with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  4. If you want a messy bun, pull out a few strands of hair to create a more relaxed look.
  5. If you want a sleek chignon, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways and spray with hairspray to keep it in place.
  6. You can also add accessories, such as a headband or hair clip, to add some extra style to your updo.
  7. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep your updo in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair in an Updo

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long hair in an updo:

  • Use bobby pins and hair ties that match your hair color to create a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different types of updos to find what works best for you.
  • Use hairspray to keep your updo in place, but don't overdo it as it can make your hair look stiff.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to add some texture to your hair before creating an updo.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for long hair:

Q: Can updo hairstyles be done on all types of long hair?

A: Yes, updo hairstyles can be done on all types of long hair, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to create an updo hairstyle?

A: It depends on the type of updo you want to create and your hair type. However, most updo hairstyles can be done in under 10 minutes.

Q: Do I need to use hair products to create an updo?

A: It's not necessary, but using hair products like hairspray and dry shampoo can make it easier to create an updo and keep it in place.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep your long hair healthy:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to brush your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you have learned about easy to do updo hairstyles for long hair, you can experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your long hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
