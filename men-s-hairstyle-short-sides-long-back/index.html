Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Back - Trendy and Low Maintenance

If you're looking for a trendy and low maintenance hairstyle, consider the men's hairstyle short sides long back. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and it's easy to see why. It's versatile, easy to style, and looks great on men of all ages. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the men's hairstyle short sides long back, including how to get it, how to maintain it, and some styling tips.

Description

The men's hairstyle short sides long back is characterized by short hair on the sides and long hair on the back. This hairstyle is also known as the "mullet" and was popularized in the 1980s. However, the modern version of this hairstyle is much more subtle and stylish. The short sides provide a clean and polished look, while the long back adds a touch of edge and texture.

How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Back

The men's hairstyle short sides long back is a relatively easy hairstyle to achieve. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist or barber.
  2. Show them a picture of the men's hairstyle short sides long back that you like.
  3. Ask for a short fade on the sides and leave the hair longer on the back.
  4. Decide on the length of the back hair. You can have it as long or as short as you want.
  5. Ask your hairstylist to blend the back hair with the sides for a seamless transition.
  6. Style your new hairstyle with some hair product.

Step by Step Guide to Style the Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Back

Styling the men's hairstyle short sides long back is also easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair product to your palm.
  4. Rub your palms together to distribute the product evenly.
  5. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the longer back hair.
  6. Use your fingers to style your hair. You can keep it messy or slick it back for a more polished look.
  7. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Back

The men's hairstyle short sides long back is a low maintenance hairstyle, but there are still some tips you should follow to keep it looking great:

  • Visit your hairstylist or barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

FAQ

1. Who can wear the men's hairstyle short sides long back?

The men's hairstyle short sides long back looks great on men of all ages and hair types. However, it's best suited for men with thick hair.

2. Can I style the men's hairstyle short sides long back in different ways?

Yes, you can style the men's hairstyle short sides long back in different ways. You can slick it back for a polished look, keep it messy for a casual look, or add some texture for a more edgy look.

3. Is the men's hairstyle short sides long back professional?

The men's hairstyle short sides long back can be professional if styled properly. You can slick it back and use a hair product to keep it in place for a more polished look.

Hair Care

Good hair care is essential for maintaining the men's hairstyle short sides long back. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a healthy diet and exercise regularly to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for trims.

The men's hairstyle short sides long back is a trendy and low maintenance hairstyle that looks great on men of all ages. Follow the steps in this article to get the hairstyle, style it, and maintain it properly. With the right care, this hairstyle can last for weeks and keep you looking stylish and confident.
