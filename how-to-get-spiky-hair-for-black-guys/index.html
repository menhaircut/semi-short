How To Get Spiky Hair For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Spiky hair is a popular hairstyle for black guys. It's edgy, stylish, and easy to maintain. If you're looking to switch up your hair game, spiky hair might be the perfect option for you. In this article, we'll show you how to get spiky hair for black guys in a relaxed English language. From the tools you'll need to the steps to follow, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Spiky hair is a hairstyle where the hair is styled to stick out in multiple directions, creating a spiky effect. It's a popular hairstyle for black guys because it looks good on short hair, and it's easy to style. To achieve this look, you'll need hair gel, a comb, and a hairdryer. With these tools, you can create a spiky hairstyle that will turn heads.

How to Get Spiky Hair for Black Guys

Getting spiky hair for black guys is an easy process that doesn't require a lot of time. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Clean Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you'll need to clean it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup. Once you've cleaned your hair, towel-dry it until it's slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel to your hair. You don't want to use too much gel, or your hair will look greasy. Start with a dime-sized amount and add more if needed. Rub the gel between your palms, then apply it to your hair. Make sure you distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a hairdryer to dry your hair while shaping it. Use a comb to lift your hair up and blow it in the direction you want it to go. For spiky hair, blow the hair upwards to create volume. Keep shaping your hair until it's dry and holds the desired shape.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish it off with a hairspray that provides a strong hold. Hold the hairspray can a few inches away from your head and spray it evenly over your hair. This will help your hairstyle last longer.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect spiky hair:

  • Use a hair gel that provides a strong hold.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a hairspray that provides a strong hold.
  • Don't use too much product, or your hair will look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting spiky hair for black guys:

Can I get spiky hair with long hair?

Yes, you can get spiky hair with long hair. However, it's easier to style short hair into a spiky hairstyle.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type.

What if I don't have a hairdryer?

If you don't have a hairdryer, you can air-dry your hair. However, it will take longer to dry, and you won't be able to shape it as easily.

Hair Care

To maintain your spiky hairstyle, you'll need to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for black guys:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

That's it! You now know how to get spiky hair for black guys. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and edgy hairstyle that will turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your spiky hairstyle.
