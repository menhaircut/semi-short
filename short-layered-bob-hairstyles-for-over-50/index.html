Short Layered Bob Hairstyles For Over 50 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Robby Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a chic and modern hairstyle, a short layered bob is a great option. Not only is it low maintenance, but it also adds volume to thinning hair and frames the face beautifully. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about short layered bob hairstyles for over 50, from how to style them to hair care tips.

Description

A short layered bob is a hairstyle that is cut to the jawline or above and has layers that add texture and volume. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For women over 50, a short layered bob can make you look younger and more stylish while still being appropriate for your age.

How to Style

Styling a short layered bob is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a towel to gently dry your hair, but don't rub it too hard as this can cause damage.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help to add volume and hold your style in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair while pulling it upwards to add volume. Use a hair dryer with a concentrator attachment to direct the air flow and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten your hair if desired. You can also add curls or waves using a curling iron or hot rollers. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short layered bob:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer to speed up your styling time and prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as a messy bob or a textured bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layered bob hairstyles for over 50:

Q: Will a short layered bob suit my face shape?

A: Yes, a short layered bob can be customized to suit your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best style for you.

Q: Is a short layered bob high maintenance?

A: No, a short layered bob is low maintenance and can be styled quickly and easily.

Q: Can I still have a short layered bob if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, a short layered bob can add volume and texture to thinning hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your short layered bob:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Conclusion

A short layered bob is a stylish and low maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for women over 50. With the right styling tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and modern look that will make you feel confident and fabulous. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your short layered bob looking its best.
