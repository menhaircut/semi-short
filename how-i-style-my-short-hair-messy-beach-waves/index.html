How I Style My Short Hair Messy Beach Waves

Written by Aprilia Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an effortless hairstyle that looks chic and stylish? One that doesn't take hours to complete and still looks fabulous? Well, you're in luck because I'm about to share with you how I style my short hair messy beach waves. In this article, I'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look and share some tips and tricks to make it last all day. So, let's get started!

Description

Messy beach waves are a popular hairstyle that gives you a relaxed and effortless look. This hairstyle is perfect for short hair because it adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. The best part is that it's easy to achieve, and you don't need any fancy tools or products. All you need is a curling iron, some hair spray, and a few minutes of your time.

How to

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it first. Here's how to style your short hair messy beach waves:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage. Then, apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add some grip and hold. This will help your waves last longer.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Take small sections of your hair and wrap them around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron vertically to create loose waves. Hold the curl for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 3: Mess It Up

Once you've curled your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a messy look. Don't be afraid to pull on the ends of your hair to create more texture and volume.

Step 4: Set It

To make your waves last all day, use a hairspray to set the style. Spray it all over your hair, then scrunch it up with your hands to create more texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy beach waves:

  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose curls will give you a more natural and effortless look.
  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for bigger waves.
  • Don't be afraid to mess up your hair. The messier, the better!
  • Use a texturizing spray to add more grip and hold to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to set your style and make it last longer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair messy beach waves:

Can you do messy beach waves on short hair?

Yes, you can. Short hair is perfect for messy beach waves because it adds volume and texture to your hair.

What products do I need to achieve messy beach waves?

All you need is a curling iron, a heat protectant spray, a texturizing spray, and a hairspray.

How long will my messy beach waves last?

Your waves should last all day with the help of a hairspray to set the style. However, if your hair is prone to falling flat, you may need to touch it up throughout the day.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Avoid using too many products in your hair, as they can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to style your short hair messy beach waves, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to have fun with it and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
