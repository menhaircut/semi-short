How To Decide Which Hairstyle Suits Me A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which one will suit you the best. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you decide which hairstyle suits you. We'll also answer some of the most frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Decide Which Hairstyle Suits Me
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Hair Styling Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Decide Which Hairstyle Suits Me

Before you decide which hairstyle suits you, you need to consider a few factors. The first factor is your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes. The second factor is your hair type. Some hairstyles work better with certain hair types than others. Lastly, you need to consider your lifestyle. Your hairstyle should complement your lifestyle and be easy to maintain.

Face Shape

The first step to determining which hairstyle suits you is to figure out your face shape. Your face shape can be one of the following: round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped. Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements it.

Hair Type

The second factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has hairstyles that work better than others. For example, curly hair looks great with a layered cut, while straight hair looks great with a blunt cut.

Lifestyle

Your hairstyle should complement your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have a lot of free time, you may want to choose a more intricate hairstyle that requires more styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's go through a step-by-step guide to help you decide which hairstyle suits you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror. Your face shape will fall into one of the following categories:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. As mentioned earlier, there are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has different hairstyles that work better than others. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered cut to enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, you may want to consider a blunt cut to add volume.

Step 3: Determine Your Lifestyle

The last step is to determine your lifestyle. Your hairstyle should complement your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have a lot of free time, you may want to choose a more intricate hairstyle that requires more styling.

Hair Styling Tips

Now that you've decided on a hairstyle, here are some tips to help you style it:

  • Invest in good quality hair products. Good quality products will help you achieve your desired hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.
  • Use heat protection products when styling your hair with heat tools. Heat can damage your hair, so it's important to protect it.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to try new things!

FAQs

Q: What hairstyle suits a round face?

A: Hairstyles that suit a round face include a long bob, a pixie cut, or a side-swept bang.

Q: What hairstyle suits an oval face?

A: An oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape, so most hairstyles will suit it. However, a shoulder-length cut or a long layered cut look great.

Q: What hairstyle suits a square face?

A: Hairstyles that suit a square face include a long bob with layers, a side-swept bang, or a shaggy cut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to choose a hairstyle that suits you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Happy hairstyling!
