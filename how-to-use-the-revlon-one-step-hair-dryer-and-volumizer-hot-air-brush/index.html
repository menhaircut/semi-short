How To Use The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer Hot Air Brush

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using multiple hair styling tools to achieve the perfect look? The Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush is a game changer, combining the power of a hair dryer and the styling ability of a round brush in one tool. In this article, we will guide you through how to use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush for effortless, salon-worthy hair.

Description

The Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush is an innovative hair styling tool that helps you achieve a perfect blowout at home. It has a unique oval brush design with nylon and boar bristles that detangle, smooth, and volumize your hair, while the ionic technology reduces frizz and adds shine. The brush has two heat and speed settings, and a cool setting to set your style.

How to Use

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. For extra volume, you can also apply a volumizing mousse or spray.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 3: Start styling

Turn on the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush and choose the desired heat and speed setting. Place the brush at the root of your hair and glide it through the section towards the ends, twisting the brush as you go to create volume and waves. Repeat this step with each section of your hair.

Step 4: Set your style

Once you have styled all sections of your hair, use the cool setting to set your style. This will help your hair hold its shape and reduce frizz.

Tips

  • Use the low heat and speed setting for fine hair and the high setting for thick or coarse hair.
  • For extra volume, hold the brush at the root of your hair for a few seconds before gliding it through the section.
  • For a sleeker look, glide the brush through your hair without twisting it.
  • Clean the brush after each use to prevent buildup of hair products and maintain its performance.

FAQ

Can I use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush on wet hair?

No, it is recommended to use the tool on towel-dried hair for the best results.

How long does it take to dry and style my hair with the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush?

It depends on the length and thickness of your hair, but on average it takes about 30-40 minutes to dry and style your hair.

Hair Care

Using the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush can cause heat damage to your hair if not used properly. To keep your hair healthy, it is important to use a heat protectant spray, to not use the tool on wet hair, and to not use it every day. In addition, regular hair trims and deep conditioning treatments can help prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home with the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer Hot Air Brush.
