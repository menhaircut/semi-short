Is Braids Unprofessional

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Braids are a popular and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. However, there has been some debate in recent years about whether or not braids are considered unprofessional. In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with some tips on how to wear braids in a professional setting.

Description

Braids are a hairstyle that involves weaving together strands of hair. They can be worn in a variety of styles, including cornrows, box braids, and twists. Braids have a long history and cultural significance, particularly in African and African American communities. In recent years, there has been some controversy over whether or not braids are considered unprofessional in certain settings.

How to

When it comes to wearing braids in a professional setting, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, it's important to consider the type of braids you're wearing. Some styles, such as brightly colored or oversized braids, may be seen as too flashy or distracting in a professional setting. Stick to more understated styles, such as traditional cornrows or small box braids.

Another thing to consider is how neat and tidy your braids look. If your braids are messy or unkempt, they may be seen as unprofessional. Make sure to take the time to properly maintain your braids, including washing and conditioning them regularly.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when wearing braids in a professional setting:

Step 1: Choose an understated style

Stick to traditional styles, such as cornrows or small box braids. Avoid brightly colored or oversized braids.

Step 2: Keep your braids neat and tidy

Make sure to properly maintain your braids, including washing and conditioning them regularly.

Step 3: Consider your workplace culture

Depending on your workplace culture, braids may or may not be seen as unprofessional. Consider the expectations of your workplace and dress accordingly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing braids in a professional setting:

  • Keep your hair out of your face
  • Avoid over-accessorizing
  • Keep your makeup and clothing simple and understated
  • Consider wearing your braids in an updo or bun

FAQ

Are braids unprofessional in all workplaces?

No, the perception of braids as unprofessional can vary depending on the workplace culture. It's important to consider the expectations of your workplace before deciding to wear braids.

Can I wear brightly colored braids in a professional setting?

It's generally best to avoid brightly colored or oversized braids in a professional setting. Stick to more understated styles.

How can I make sure my braids look neat and tidy?

Make sure to properly maintain your braids, including washing and conditioning them regularly. You may also want to consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your braids looking shiny and healthy.

Hair Care

When wearing braids, it's important to take proper care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while wearing braids:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids
  • Sleep with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to prevent frizz

Conclusion

In conclusion, braids can be a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different settings, including professional ones. However, it's important to consider the expectations of your workplace and dress accordingly. Stick to more understated styles and make sure to properly maintain your braids to keep them looking neat and tidy.
