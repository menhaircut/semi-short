Which Hairstyle Suits For Long Face Female A Guide For The Perfect Look

Are you a long-faced woman struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further, as we have got you covered! In this article, we will guide you on which hairstyle suits for long face female, including how to style and maintain it. Read on and discover the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

Long-faced women have an elongated face shape with a narrow width and high forehead. The perfect hairstyles for long face female should balance out the length of the face and create the illusion of width. A great hairstyle can flatter your face shape and enhance your features, while the wrong one can make your face look even longer.

When choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Long-faced women can opt for a variety of hairstyles, from short to long, straight to curly, and everything in between. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of the face, creating the illusion of width.

How to

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for long face female can be overwhelming, but with the following tips, you can find the perfect one for you:

Consider Your Hair Type

Long-faced women can opt for a variety of hairstyles, but it's essential to consider your hair type. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid long hairstyles as they can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for shorter hairstyles with layers that add volume and texture to your hair.

If you have thick hair, you can go for longer hairstyles that add weight to your hair. Layers can also help to add volume to your hair, but avoid too many layers as they can make your face look longer.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair plays a significant role in how it flatters your face shape. Long-faced women can opt for both short and long hairstyles, but it's essential to choose the right length. If you have a long face and want to go for a short hairstyle, choose a length that falls between your chin and shoulders. This length adds volume to the sides of your face, creating the illusion of width.

If you prefer longer hairstyles, opt for hairstyles that fall below your shoulders. Avoid hairstyles that fall above your shoulders as they can make your face look longer.

Add Volume to the Sides

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for long face female is to add volume to the sides of the face. This can be achieved through hairstyles with layers, curls, waves, and volume-adding products. Avoid hairstyles that are flat and straight as they can make your face look even longer.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect hairstyle for long face female:

  1. Choose the right haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  2. Use volume-adding products such as mousse or hairspray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying to enhance your curls.
  5. If you prefer a straight hairstyle, use a flat iron to straighten your hair, and finish with a shine spray.
  6. Accessorize with hair accessories such as headbands, clips, or barrettes to add extra volume to your hairstyle.

Tips

Consider these tips when choosing the perfect hairstyle for long face female:

  • Opt for hairstyles with layers and volume to add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are flat and straight as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Consider hair accessories such as headbands, clips, or barrettes to add extra volume to your hairstyle.
  • Use volume-adding products such as mousse or hairspray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

What hairstyles suit a long face?

Hairstyles with layers, curls, waves, and volume are perfect for long-faced women. Short hairstyles that fall between the chin and shoulders and long hairstyles that fall below the shoulders are great options for long face female.

What hairstyles should be avoided for long face female?

Avoid hairstyles that are flat and straight as they can make your face look even longer. Hairstyles that fall above the shoulders should also be avoided as they can make your face look longer.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieve the perfect hairstyle for long face female. Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, including blow-drying and flat ironing.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits for long face female, you can confidently choose the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. Remember to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle, and always consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect one for you. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and achieve the perfect look.
