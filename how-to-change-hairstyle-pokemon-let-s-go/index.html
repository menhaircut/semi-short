How to Change Hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go - Tips and FAQ

If you're tired of your Pokemon trainer's hairstyle, you'll be happy to know that you can change it in Pokemon Let's Go. In this article, we'll show you how to change your hairstyle, provide some tips for choosing the right style, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Change Hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go is easy. Here are the steps:

  1. Go to any Pokemon Center in the game.
  2. Approach the mirror on the right side of the room.
  3. Interact with the mirror to bring up the hairstyle options.
  4. Choose the hairstyle you want and confirm your selection.
  5. Pay the fee to change your hairstyle.

That's it! Your new hairstyle will be applied immediately.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know how to change your hairstyle, it's important to choose the right one for your trainer. Here are some tips:

  • Consider your trainer's personality. If your trainer is bold and adventurous, a spiky hairstyle might be a good choice. If your trainer is more reserved, a sleek and simple style might be better.
  • Think about your trainer's outfit. Your hairstyle should complement your trainer's overall look.
  • Try out different hairstyles before committing. You can preview each hairstyle at the mirror before you make your final decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my hairstyle multiple times?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want. However, there is a fee for each change.

Do I need any special items to change my hairstyle?

No, you don't need any special items to change your hairstyle. Just visit a Pokemon Center and use the mirror to access the hairstyle options.

Can I change my hair color?

No, you can't change your hair color in Pokemon Let's Go. However, you can choose from a variety of different hairstyles.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your trainer's hair, especially if you want to keep your new hairstyle looking fresh. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your trainer's hair neat and tidy.
  • Wash your trainer's hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a styling product to help your new hairstyle stay in place.

That's all you need to know about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go. We hope this article has been helpful. Happy styling!
