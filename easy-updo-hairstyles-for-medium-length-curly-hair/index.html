Easy Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Nov 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be tricky to style, especially when you have medium length hair. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you some easy updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair that you can do at home. Whether you're going to a special occasion or just want to switch up your everyday look, these hairstyles are perfect for you.

Description

Updo hairstyles are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck. They're also perfect for special occasions like weddings, proms, and other formal events. However, updos can be tricky when you have curly hair. That's why we've compiled a list of easy updo hairstyles specifically for medium length curly hair. These hairstyles are simple, elegant, and perfect for any occasion.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Apply a styling product to help define your curls
  • Use bobby pins and hair ties that match your hair color
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the hairstyles and make them your own

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that works well with curly hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy look. You can also leave some curls out around your face for a softer look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their curls but still keep their hair out of their face. To create this look, take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back. Secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins. You can leave the rest of your hair down or pull it back into a low ponytail.

3. Braided Updo

The braided updo is a great way to add some texture and detail to your hair. To create this look, start by braiding a section of hair on one side of your head. Once the braid reaches the back of your head, twist it into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side of your head and you're done!

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the braided updo:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it
  3. Once the braid reaches the back of your head, twist it into a bun
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your updo hairstyles:

  • Use a hairspray or hair gel to help hold your style in place
  • Experiment with different bobby pin placements to find the most secure hold
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories like flowers or hairpins to your updo
  • Practice makes perfect – don't be discouraged if your first attempt doesn't turn out perfectly

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair:

1. Can I do these hairstyles with short curly hair?

These hairstyles are designed for medium length hair, but you may be able to adapt them for shorter hair by using more bobby pins and hair ties.

2. How long will these hairstyles last?

These hairstyles should last for several hours, but you may need to touch up the style throughout the day.

3. Do I need to use any special products for these hairstyles?

It's a good idea to use a styling product that will define your curls and help hold your style in place, but you don't need any special products.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry – use a wide-toothed comb instead
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often – let your hair air-dry whenever possible

With these easy updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair, you'll never have to worry about a bad hair day again. Try out these styles and see which ones work best for you. Remember, practice makes perfect!
