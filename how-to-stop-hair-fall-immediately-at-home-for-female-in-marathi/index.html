How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately At Home For Female In Marathi

Written by Daniel Nov 24, 2023 · 3 min read

If you are a woman experiencing hair fall, you are not alone. Hair fall is a common problem for women, and it can be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, and genetics. In this article, we will provide you with some tips on how to stop hair fall immediately at home for female in Marathi.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some effective steps you can take to stop hair fall immediately:

Step 1: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation, which in turn can stimulate hair growth. Use warm oil to massage your scalp in circular motions for 5-10 minutes. You can use coconut oil, almond oil, or olive oil for this purpose.

Step 2: Use Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel is known for its healing properties and can help soothe an itchy scalp. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with water.

Step 3: Apply Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which can help stimulate hair growth. Grind an onion and extract its juice. Apply the juice to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with water.

Step 4: Use Egg White

Egg white is a rich source of protein, which can help strengthen hair roots. Beat an egg white and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with water.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and bleaches.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to detangle your hair.

FAQs on Hair Fall

Q. What causes hair fall in women?

A. Hair fall in women can be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, and genetics.

Q. Can hair fall be reversed?

A. Yes, hair fall can be reversed with the right treatment and hair care routine.

Q. How long does it take to see results?

A. It may take a few weeks or months to see results, depending on the severity of your hair fall.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a hair care routine you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo at least twice a week.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing your hair to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Avoid going to bed with wet hair, as it can cause hair breakage.

By following these steps and tips, you can stop hair fall immediately at home for female in Marathi. Remember to be patient and consistent in your hair care routine, and you will see results in no time.
