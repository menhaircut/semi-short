Cutting Men s Hair Top Of Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the barbershop to maintain your hair? Do you want to learn how to cut your hair at home? Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and time. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut men's hair top of head.

Description

The top of the head is a crucial part of a man's haircut. It is the part that most people see first when they look at you. Cutting this part of the hair requires precision and attention to detail. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's hair top of head.

How to Cut Men's Hair Top of Head

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before cutting your hair, you will need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a pair of scissors. Make sure that your hair clippers are sharp and have a variety of guard sizes to choose from.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly before cutting it. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that may be in your hair. It will also make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair in the direction that it naturally falls. This will help you to see any uneven spots in your hair that need to be trimmed. It will also make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Choose Your Guard Size

Choose the guard size that you want to use. If you want a shorter haircut, use a smaller guard size. If you want a longer haircut, use a larger guard size. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller one if necessary.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair at the back of your head. Use the clipper to trim your hair in an upward motion. Move the clipper slowly and evenly across your head. Continue cutting your hair until you reach the front of your head.

Step 6: Trim the Sides

Trim the sides of your hair with the clipper. Use a smaller guard size than you used on the top of your head. Start at the bottom of your hairline and move the clipper up to the top of your head. Repeat this process until you have trimmed both sides evenly.

Step 7: Blend the Top and Sides

Blend the top and sides of your hair using scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair on the top of your head and trim any uneven spots. Use the scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair together. This will create a natural look.

Step 8: Check Your Hair

Check your hair in the mirror to make sure that it is even. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them with the scissors. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Tips

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller one if necessary.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair on the top of your head and trim any uneven spots.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

FAQ

1. How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a healthy and attractive look.

2. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors to ensure that you get a clean cut and don't damage your hair.

3. What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim your hair a little shorter to fix the mistake. Alternatively, you can go to a professional barber to fix the mistake.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, be sure to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Cutting men's hair top of head can be a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Follow the steps and tips in this article to cut your hair with confidence.
