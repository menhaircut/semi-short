How To Cut The Back Of Your Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the barbershop to get your hair cut? If you're a guy looking to save some time and money, then learning how to cut the back of your hair yourself can be a game-changer. Cutting the back of your hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and stylish look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut the back of your hair guys in a relaxed and easy-to-understand manner.

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Guys: Step-by-Step Guide

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools for the job:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Handheld mirror
  • Hairspray or gel

Follow these steps to cut the back of your hair:

Step 1: Clean and Dampen Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's slightly damp. This will make it easier to cut your hair and prevent any tugging or pulling.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to make sure your hair is tangle-free and easy to work with.

Step 3: Use Clippers or Scissors

Decide whether you want to use clippers or scissors to cut your hair. Clippers are great for a shorter, more uniform cut while scissors are better for a layered or textured look.

Step 4: Start at the Nape of the Neck

Begin cutting at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the handheld mirror to see the back of your head and make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 5: Cut in Small Sections

Don't try to cut too much hair at once. Cut in small, even sections to ensure you're getting a clean and precise cut.

Step 6: Use the Scissors Over Comb Technique

If you're using scissors, you can use the scissors over comb technique to achieve an even cut. Comb the hair and then cut along the comb.

Step 7: Check Your Progress

Use the handheld mirror to check your progress and make sure you're happy with the length and shape of your hair.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting, style your hair with some hairspray or gel to keep it in place.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of your hair:

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Make sure your scissors or clippers are sharp to avoid any tugging or pulling.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure you're cutting evenly.
  • Start with a longer length and trim as needed to avoid cutting too much off.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Cut the Back of My Hair Myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your hair yourself with the right tools and techniques.

How Often Should I Cut the Back of My Hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good timeframe.

Hair Care for Guys

Cutting your own hair is just one part of maintaining a healthy and stylish look. Here are some additional tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Learning how to cut the back of your hair guys can save you time and money, and give you more control over your hair style. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a clean and stylish cut.
