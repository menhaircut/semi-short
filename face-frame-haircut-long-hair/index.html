Face Frame Haircut Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old long hair look? Do you want to add some dimension and shape to your locks? Look no further than the face frame haircut for long hair. This trendy style has been seen on celebrities and influencers alike, and it's not hard to see why. In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on how to get the perfect face frame haircut for long hair.

Description

The face frame haircut is a technique used to add layers and angles to long hair. It's called a face frame haircut because the layers are strategically placed around the face to highlight your features. This style is perfect for those who want to add some movement and dimension to their long locks without sacrificing length.

How to

Step 1: Consultation

Before you get started, it's important to have a consultation with your hairstylist. They can help determine the best placement for the layers based on your face shape and hair type. It's also a good idea to bring in pictures of the style you're looking for to ensure you're both on the same page.

Step 2: Sectioning

Once you're in the chair, your hairstylist will start by sectioning your hair. They'll typically start at the back and work their way forward, clipping away the sections they're not working on.

Step 3: Cutting

Next, your hairstylist will start cutting the layers. They'll typically use a technique called point cutting, which involves cutting into the hair at an angle to create a softer, more natural look. The layers will be cut at different lengths to create the face-framing effect.

Step 4: Finishing

Once the layers are in place, your hairstylist will finish the haircut by blending the layers together and adding any additional texture or shaping.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind before and after your face frame haircut:

  • Make sure to use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Ask your hairstylist for product recommendations to help maintain your new style.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for adjustments if you're not happy with the initial cut.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a face frame haircut?

A face frame haircut can work for any face shape, but the placement of the layers may vary. For example, those with a round face may want more layers around the chin to create a slimming effect.

How often do I need to get a face frame haircut?

This can vary depending on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, you may want to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Can I still wear my hair up with a face frame haircut?

Absolutely! The layers may add some texture and movement to your updo, but it can still be easily styled and worn up.

Hair Care

To keep your face frame haircut looking its best, make sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. It's also a good idea to use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools too often and use a heat protectant when you do. Lastly, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know everything about the face frame haircut for long hair, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist. Get ready to turn heads with your new, dimensional locks!
