How To Have A Messy Hair Look For Guys A Guide To Effortlessly Cool Hair In 2023

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of perfectly coiffed hair and looking for a more relaxed, effortless style? Look no further than the messy hair look for guys. This popular style has been around for years and shows no signs of going out of fashion. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect messy hair look for guys, along with tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Messy Hair Look for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for the Messy Hair Look

How to Achieve the Messy Hair Look for Guys

The messy hair look for guys is all about looking effortless and cool. It's the perfect style for those who don't want to spend hours in front of the mirror but still want to look put together. Here are a few key elements to keep in mind when aiming for the perfect messy hair look:

  • Texture: Messy hair is all about texture. You want your hair to look tousled and slightly unkempt, so avoid using too much product or combing your hair too much.
  • Length: While you can achieve a messy look with any hair length, longer hair tends to work better for this style.
  • Layers: Layers can add to the messy look, giving your hair more movement and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to achieve the perfect messy hair look? Follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too clean, it can be harder to achieve the messy look. A little bit of natural oil can help add texture and hold.
  2. Add a small amount of product. A texturizing spray or salt spray is a great option for creating messy hair. Apply it to your hair and use your hands to scrunch and tousle your hair.
  3. If you have longer hair, consider adding some layers for more texture and movement.
  4. Use your hands to style your hair. Avoid using a comb or brush, as this can make your hair look too neat and tidy.
  5. If you want a more defined messy look, you can add some wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it into your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your hair in place without making it look too stiff or shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Hair

Now that you've achieved the perfect messy hair look, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips for maintaining your style:

  • Don't wash your hair too often. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of natural oils, making it harder to achieve the messy look.
  • Avoid using too much product. While a little bit of product can help create texture, too much can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Get regular haircuts. Keeping your hair trimmed and well-maintained can help maintain the messy look without letting it become too unruly.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions about the messy hair look for guys? Here are a few answers to some commonly asked questions:

  • Can I achieve the messy hair look with short hair?
  • Yes, you can achieve the messy hair look with short hair. Just be sure to use a texturizing product and focus on creating texture with your hands rather than a comb.
  • How do I keep my hair from looking too greasy?
  • Avoid using too much product, and be sure to wash your hair regularly. If you find that your hair looks too greasy, try using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil.
  • Can I achieve the messy hair look with curly hair?
  • Absolutely! Curly hair can be a great fit for the messy hair look. Just be sure to use a product that helps define your curls while adding texture.

Hair Care for the Messy Hair Look

Maintaining the perfect messy hair look requires a little bit of effort, but it's worth it for the effortless cool factor. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve the messy look.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and avoid split ends.
  • Invest in a high-quality texturizing spray or other product to help achieve the perfect messy look.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving the perfect messy hair look for guys. So go ahead, embrace the effortless cool of messy hair and enjoy a low-maintenance, high-style look that's perfect for any occasion.
