How To Reduce Hair Fall For Oily Scalp Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oily scalp, you might be prone to hair fall. The excess oil produced by your scalp can clog your hair follicles and lead to hair fall. But don't worry, there are several ways to reduce hair fall for oily scalp. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you combat hair fall and maintain healthy hair.

  • How to Reduce Hair Fall for Oily Scalp
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Reduce Hair Fall for Oily Scalp

Here are some ways to reduce hair fall if you have an oily scalp:

Cleanse Your Scalp Regularly

Cleansing your scalp regularly is crucial if you have an oily scalp. Use a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for oily hair. Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip away the natural oils from your scalp and make it even oilier.

Avoid Using Heavy Hair Products

Avoid using heavy hair products such as hair serums, oils, and gels. These products can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Instead, use lightweight hair products that are specifically designed for oily hair.

Use a Clarifying Shampoo

A clarifying shampoo can help remove buildup from your hair and scalp. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to keep your scalp clean and healthy.

Avoid Over-brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair too much can stimulate your scalp and lead to more oil production. Avoid over-brushing your hair and use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to reducing hair fall for oily scalp:

  1. Cleanse your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  2. Avoid using heavy hair products.
  3. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week.
  4. Avoid over-brushing your hair.
  5. Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  6. Drink plenty of water to keep your scalp hydrated.
  7. Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair.
  8. Get enough sleep to reduce stress levels.
  9. Exercise regularly to improve blood circulation.
  10. Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and scalp.

Tips to Reduce Hair Fall for Oily Scalp

Here are some additional tips to reduce hair fall if you have an oily scalp:

  • Avoid using too much conditioner as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Avoid touching your hair too often as it can transfer oil from your hands to your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your scalp.
  • Consider using dry shampoo between washes to absorb excess oil from your scalp.
  • Consult a dermatologist if you have severe hair fall or scalp issues.

FAQs

Q: Can an oily scalp cause hair fall?

A: Yes, an oily scalp can clog your hair follicles and lead to hair fall.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have an oily scalp?

A: You should wash your hair every other day if you have an oily scalp.

Q: Can a healthy diet reduce hair fall?

A: Yes, a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can promote healthy hair growth and reduce hair fall.

Hair Care Routine for Oily Scalp

Here's a simple hair care routine for oily scalp:

  • Cleanse your scalp with a gentle shampoo every other day.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair.
  • Get enough sleep to reduce stress levels.
  • Exercise regularly to improve blood circulation.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and scalp.

Reducing hair fall for oily scalp requires a consistent hair care routine and a healthy lifestyle. By following these tips and tricks, you can maintain healthy hair and reduce hair fall. If you have severe hair fall or scalp issues, don't hesitate to consult a dermatologist.
