Will Short Hair Make My Face Look Fatter Tips How To And Faq

Written by Eveline Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a popular trend nowadays, but people often hesitate to cut their hair short because they’re afraid it will make their face look fatter. This is a common misconception, and the answer is not as simple as a yes or no. In this blog post, we will discuss whether short hair makes your face look fatter or not, and we will give you tips on how to find the right hairstyle that will suit your face shape.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description:

Short hair can make your face look fatter or slimmer depending on the shape of your face, the length of your hair, and the way it is styled. In general, shorter hair tends to highlight the features of your face, so it is important to find a style that complements your face shape and draws attention to your best features.

How To:

The first step in finding the right short hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and triangle. Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

Round Face:

If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for a round face.

Square Face:

If you have a square face, you should choose a hairstyle that softens your angular features. Soft layers or a side-swept bangs can be a great option for a square face. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles with too much volume on the sides.

Oval Face:

If you have an oval face, you can choose almost any short hairstyle. A bob, a pixie cut, or a layered cut can all work well with an oval face.

Heart Face:

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead and jawline. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for a heart-shaped face.

Diamond Face:

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you should choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for a diamond-shaped face.

Triangle Face:

If you have a triangle-shaped face, you should choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head. A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top can be a great option for a triangle-shaped face.

Step-by-Step:

If you’re still unsure about what hairstyle will suit your face shape, here are some steps you can take to find the right hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Look for hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  4. Take into account your lifestyle and daily routine
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Don’t be afraid to try something new
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Choose a style that is easy to maintain
  • Use hair products to add volume or texture to your hair
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or clips

FAQ:

Q: Will short hair make my face look fatter?

A: Short hair can make your face look fatter or slimmer depending on the length of your hair and the way it is styled. It is important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention to your best features.

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

A: A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for a round face.

Q: Can I style my short hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your short hair differently for different occasions. Use hair products to add volume or texture to your hair, and accessorize with headbands or clips.

Hair Care:

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape
  • Use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture

In conclusion, short hair can make your face look fatter or slimmer depending on the length of your hair and the way it is styled. It is important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention to your best features. Don’t be afraid to try something new, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
