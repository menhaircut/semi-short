How To Tell If Short Hair Will Suit You A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking of making a drastic change to your hair by chopping it all off? Before you take the plunge and go for a short hairdo, you might want to consider if it will suit you. Short hair can look fantastic on some people, but it's not a one-size-fits-all solution. In this article, we'll go through how to tell if short hair will suit you, step-by-step, so you can make an informed decision.

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if short hair will suit you is to figure out your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has certain hairstyles that will suit it best. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face. Look at the outline of your face and compare it to the six face shapes. You can also use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face to help narrow down your face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you. If you have thick, curly hair, a short haircut may require more maintenance and styling than you're used to. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, a short haircut can add volume and texture.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider. If you're constantly on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a short haircut may be a good option for you. Short hair is low maintenance and can be styled quickly and easily. However, if you work in a more conservative environment, you may want to consider a longer hairstyle.

Consider Your Personality

Your personality is also an important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you. Short hair can be edgy and bold, so if you have a more conservative personality, a short haircut may not be the best fit for you.

Think About Your Hair Color

Your hair color is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you. Some hair colors look better with short hair than others. For example, if you have a bold hair color like red, a short haircut can make your hair color stand out even more.

Consider Your Current Hair Length

Your current hair length is another important factor to consider. If you have long hair and want to make a drastic change, a short haircut may be a good option for you. However, if you already have a shorter haircut, you may want to consider a longer hairstyle to switch things up.

Look at Your Hair History

Your hair history is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you. If you've had short hair before and loved it, it may be a good option for you again. On the other hand, if you've never had short hair before, you may want to start with a longer hairstyle and work your way up to a shorter haircut.

Consider Your Face Features

Your face features are another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you. If you have prominent cheekbones or a strong jawline, a short haircut can help to accentuate these features. On the other hand, if you have a smaller face or delicate features, a longer hairstyle may be a better fit.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

If you decide to go for a short haircut, there are a few hair care tips to keep in mind. Short hair requires more frequent trims to maintain its shape, so be prepared to visit the salon every four to six weeks. You may also need to switch up your hair care routine to accommodate your new hairstyle. For example, you may need to use different styling products or wash your hair more frequently.

FAQs About Short Hair

1. Will short hair suit me if I have a round face?

Yes, short hair can look great on round faces. A short bob with layers can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

2. Will short hair suit me if I have thick, curly hair?

Short hair can look great on thick, curly hair, but it may require more maintenance and styling than you're used to.

3. How often do I need to trim my short hair?

You should visit the salon every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of your short haircut.

4. Can I still style short hair?

Yes, short hair can be styled in a variety of ways. Talk to your stylist about the best styling products and techniques for your hair type and texture.

Now that you know how to tell if short hair will suit you, you can make an informed decision about your next haircut. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, personality, hair color, current hair length, hair history, and face features before making a decision. With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a bold and stylish look.
