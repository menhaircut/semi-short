Best Short Hair For 50 Year Olds A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a woman over 50, you may be wondering what the best short hairstyle is for you. Short hair can be a great option for mature women, as it's easy to maintain and can help you look younger and more stylish. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best short haircuts for women over 50, as well as tips for caring for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and lose volume. This is why many women choose to cut their hair shorter, as it can create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. Short haircuts can also be more flattering for mature women, as they can help to frame the face and draw attention to your best features.

How To

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A good stylist can help you find a cut that suits your individual features and lifestyle. Here are some popular short haircuts for women over 50:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can be tailored to suit your face shape and hair texture. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down, and it's a great option if you have fine hair.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a versatile haircut that can be worn straight or wavy, and it can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. It's a great option if you want a low-maintenance style that's easy to maintain.

3. Shaggy Cut

The shaggy cut is a layered haircut that can add volume and texture to your hair. It's a great option if you have thin or fine hair, as it can help to create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by choosing a short haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

2. Work with your stylist to customize the cut to suit your individual features and lifestyle.

3. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Consider using a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

5. Use a round brush to style your hair, and consider adding some layers to create movement and texture.

Tips

1. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

2. Work with a stylist who has experience with short haircuts for mature women.

3. Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

5. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short haircut can actually make you look younger and more stylish.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: Short haircuts require more frequent trims than longer haircuts, so you should aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

When caring for your short hair, it's important to use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering short haircut, you can look and feel your best at any age.
