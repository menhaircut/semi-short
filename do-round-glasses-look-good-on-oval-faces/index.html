Do Round Glasses Look Good On Oval Faces

Written by William Sep 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you an oval-faced individual with a love for round glasses? If so, you might be wondering if this eyewear style suits your face shape. In this article, we'll answer the question, "do round glasses look good on oval faces?", and provide you with tips on how to choose the best frames for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval faces are characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a slightly rounded chin. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can wear almost any eyewear style. However, choosing the right frames can enhance your features and complement your look.

Round glasses have become an iconic eyewear style, popularized by famous figures like John Lennon and Harry Potter. This style features circular lenses and a curved frame that follows the shape of the lens. Round glasses can offer a unique and trendy look, but does it suit oval faces?

How To

To determine if round glasses suit oval faces, take a look at the proportions of your face. Oval faces have balanced proportions, so choosing frames that maintain this balance is crucial. If the frames are too small or too large, it can throw off the symmetry of your face.

When choosing round glasses, consider the size and shape of the frame. Round frames come in various sizes, so it's essential to choose one that complements your facial features. If you have a small face, opt for smaller frames, and if you have a larger face, choose larger frames.

The shape of the frame is also essential. Oval faces have soft curves, so choosing frames with sharp angles or geometric shapes can create a harsh look. Round frames with a slight cat-eye or squared-off edges can balance the softness of your face while adding a touch of edge.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape.

Step 2: Choose round glasses that complement your facial features.

Step 3: Consider the size and shape of the frame.

Step 4: Try on different frames to see which one suits you best.

Tips

  • Consider the color of the frame to complement your skin tone and hair color.
  • Opt for frames with adjustable nose pads for a comfortable fit.
  • Choose frames that match your personal style.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles and shapes to find the perfect pair.

FAQ

Q: Can oval faces wear any eyewear style?
A: Yes, oval faces can wear almost any eyewear style, but it's essential to choose frames that maintain balance and complement your facial features.

Q: What other eyewear styles suit oval faces?
A: Square, rectangular, and cat-eye frames are also great options for oval faces.

Hair Care

When wearing round glasses, it's essential to consider your hairstyle. Avoid hairstyles that cover your face or compete with the frames. Instead, opt for hairstyles that complement the frames and enhance your facial features.

In conclusion, round glasses can look good on oval faces with the right frame size and shape. Consider your facial features, try on different frames, and choose a style that matches your personal taste. With these tips, you'll find the perfect pair of round glasses for your oval face.
