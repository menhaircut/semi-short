How To Keep Your Hair Out Of Your Face While Surfing

Written by Luffy Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Surfing is a wonderful sport that allows you to connect with nature and enjoy the beauty of the ocean. But it can be quite challenging to keep your hair out of your face while surfing, especially if you have long hair. Fortunately, there are various ways to keep your hair under control so that you can enjoy your surfing experience without any distractions. In this article, we will provide you with some easy tips and tricks to keep your hair out of your face while surfing.

  3. Tips and Tricks

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some easy steps to follow to keep your hair out of your face while surfing:

Step 1: Tie Your Hair

The easiest way to keep your hair out of your face while surfing is to tie it back. Use a hair tie or a scrunchie to secure your hair in a ponytail or a bun. This will keep your hair away from your face and prevent it from obstructing your vision.

Step 2: Wear a Headband

If you have shorter hair or bangs that tend to fall into your face, wearing a headband can be a great option. Choose a headband that is made of a non-slip material so that it stays in place while you surf. You can also wear a bandana to keep your hair out of your face.

Step 3: Use Hair Clips

If you have layers or shorter hair that tends to fly around, hair clips can be a lifesaver. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair away from your face. You can also use clips to create small braids or twists that will keep your hair under control.

Step 4: Try a Surf Hat

If you're surfing in sunny conditions, wearing a surf hat can protect your face and keep your hair under control. Choose a hat that has a chin strap so that it stays in place while you surf.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep your hair out of your face while surfing:

Tip 1: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Before you head out to surf, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to tame any frizz and make your hair more manageable. It will also help to protect your hair from the sun and saltwater.

Tip 2: Use a Water-Resistant Hairspray

If you have particularly unruly hair, using a water-resistant hairspray can help to keep your hair in place while you surf. Look for a hairspray that is specifically designed for use in the water.

Tip 3: Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair can be an effective way to keep it under control while you surf. Try a French braid or a fishtail braid to keep your hair out of your face. You can also use small braids to create a boho-inspired look.

FAQs

Q: Can I surf with my hair down?

A: While it is technically possible to surf with your hair down, it is not recommended. Your hair can get in your face, obstruct your vision, and even get tangled in your surfboard. It's best to tie your hair back or use one of the other methods mentioned in this article to keep your hair under control.

Q: What should I do if my hair gets wet while surfing?

A: If your hair gets wet while surfing, it's important to rinse it with fresh water as soon as possible. Saltwater and chlorine can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. You should also use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Surfing can be tough on your hair, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Hydrating Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner will help to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Look for products that are specifically designed for use after exposure to saltwater or chlorine.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners as much as possible, and let your hair air-dry whenever you can.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, or as needed.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair out of your face while surfing and keep your hair healthy and strong. Have fun out there!
