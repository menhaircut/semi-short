How To Cut Curly Men s Hair With Scissors Only

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting curly men's hair can be a challenge, especially if you're new to it. But with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself or someone else a great haircut. In this article, you'll learn how to cut curly hair using only scissors. We'll cover everything from how to prepare the hair to how to finish the cut. So let's get started.

Description

Curly hair requires special attention when it comes to cutting. Unlike straight hair, curly hair tends to shrink when it's cut, which means you need to cut it longer than you want it to be. Additionally, curly hair can be difficult to manage, which means you need to use the right techniques to get the best results.

How to Cut Curly Men's Hair with Scissors Only

Step 1: Wash the Hair

The first step in cutting curly hair is to wash it. This will help to remove any dirt or debris that may be in the hair. It will also make the hair easier to work with. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair. This will help to keep the hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Once the hair is clean, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots. This will help to remove any knots or tangles in the hair. It will also help to prevent breakage.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Next, you'll need to section the hair. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly. To do this, use the end of the comb to create a part in the hair. Then, clip the hair above the part out of the way. This will leave a section of hair that you can work with.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Now it's time to start cutting the hair. Start by taking a small section of hair and holding it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair, making sure to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be. Remember that curly hair shrinks when it's cut, so it's important to cut it longer than you want it to be.

Continue to cut the hair in small sections, moving from one side of the head to the other. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much off at once. If you're not sure how much to cut, start with a small amount and work your way up.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all of the hair, it's time to blend the hair. To do this, use the scissors to cut into the hair at an angle. This will help to blend the hair and create a more natural look.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

Finally, it's time to finish the cut. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven ends. You can also use a razor to create texture in the hair. Just be careful not to overdo it, as this can cause the hair to become frizzy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors - dull scissors can damage the hair
  • Cut the hair slightly longer than you want it to be
  • Work in small sections to ensure even cutting
  • Use a razor to create texture in the hair
  • Be patient - cutting curly hair takes time and practice

FAQ

Can I cut curly hair with clippers?

While it's possible to cut curly hair with clippers, it's not recommended. Clippers can cause the hair to become frizzy and can also create an uneven cut.

How often should I cut curly hair?

This will depend on how fast the hair grows and how long you want it to be. As a general rule, it's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping curly hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair
  • Avoid brushing curly hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage the hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized

By following these tips, you can keep curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
