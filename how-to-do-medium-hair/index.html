How To Do Medium Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium hair is a versatile length that offers endless styling possibilities. Whether you prefer sleek and straight or tousled and textured, there are plenty of ways to switch up your look. In this blog post, we'll walk you through how to do medium hair with our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Do Medium Hair

To start, you will need the proper tools and products. A good quality blow dryer, flat iron, curling iron, and styling products such as mousse, hairspray, and heat protectant are essential.

Now, let's get started on how to do medium hair:

1. Blow Dry

Begin by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.

2. Straighten or Curl

If you want to straighten your hair, use a flat iron in small sections, starting at the roots and working towards the ends. For curls or waves, use a curling iron and wrap small sections around the barrel.

3. Add Texture

For added texture, apply a small amount of texturizing spray or mousse to the roots and mid-lengths of your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a natural-looking wave.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing down your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide on how to do medium hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  4. Straighten or curl your hair with a flat iron or curling iron.
  5. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or mousse to the roots and mid-lengths of your hair.
  6. Tousle your hair with your fingers to create a natural-looking wave.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Medium Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling medium hair:

  • Experiment with different partings to switch up your look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a quality hair dryer with multiple heat and speed settings.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQs

What hair type is best for medium hair?

Medium hair works well on most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I wash my medium hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently than someone with dry hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it looking and feeling its best.

What is the best way to add volume to medium hair?

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can add volume and body to medium hair. You can also use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray for added lift.

Hair Care Advice for Medium Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your medium hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your medium hair with ease. Experiment with different looks and find what works best for you.
