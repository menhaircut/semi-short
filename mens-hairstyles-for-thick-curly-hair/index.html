Best Men s Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying to tame your thick curly hair? Do you feel like you're running out of options when it comes to hairstyles? Fear not, because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best men's hairstyles for thick curly hair. From short to long, we've got something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Description

Thick curly hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you choose to style it. While it can be difficult to manage, it's also incredibly versatile. With the right haircut and styling products, you can create a look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance style or something more high-maintenance, there's a hairstyle out there for you.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to understand how to care for your thick curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair

Step by Step

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are a great option for men with thick curly hair. Not only are they low-maintenance, but they also showcase your natural texture. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's also a great option for men with thick curly hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short, all-over cut. You can also add a fade to the sides for a more modern look.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another timeless hairstyle that works well for thick curly hair. This style is longer on top and shorter on the sides. You can choose to keep the top textured or slick it back for a more polished look.

Medium Length Hairstyles

If you prefer a bit more length, medium length hairstyles are a great option. They allow you to showcase your natural texture while still being easy to manage. Here are some medium length hairstyles to consider:

The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for thick curly hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a tapered cut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. Use a comb to create a deep side part and style with a pomade or wax.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern hairstyle that's perfect for thick curly hair. This style is all about embracing your natural texture. Ask your barber for a layered cut with plenty of texture on top. Use a sea salt spray or texturizing cream to enhance your curls.

Long Hairstyles

If you're feeling adventurous, long hairstyles can be a great way to showcase your thick curly hair. They're also a great way to make a statement. Here are some long hairstyles to consider:

The Man Bun

The man bun is a popular hairstyle that works well for men with thick curly hair. To achieve this look, grow your hair out to shoulder length and tie it into a bun at the back of your head. Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure the bun in place.

The Shoulder Length Cut

The shoulder length cut is a great option for men who want to show off their thick curly hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a layered cut that falls just below your shoulders. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls defined and moisturized.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your thick curly hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice on styling your hair
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help tame frizz and make your hair more manageable
  • Invest in a good hairbrush or comb to help distribute product evenly

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles for thick curly hair:

What is the best haircut for thick curly hair?

The best haircut for thick curly hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Short hairstyles like the buzz cut and crew cut are great options for low-maintenance looks, while medium length hairstyles like the side part and textured crop allow you to showcase your natural texture. Long hairstyles like the man bun and shoulder length cut are perfect for making a statement.

How do I style my thick curly hair?

To style your thick curly hair, start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls defined and moisturized. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair. Use a hairbrush or comb to distribute product evenly and style your hair.

How do I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your thick curly hair, avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to minimize damage. Consider getting a keratin treatment to help tame frizz and make your hair more manageable. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Hair Care

Caring for your thick curly hair is essential to maintaining healthy and manageable hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help tame frizz and make your hair more manageable

With these tips and hairstyle options, you're sure to find a look that suits your thick curly hair. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!
