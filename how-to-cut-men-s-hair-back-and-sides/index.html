How To Cut Men s Hair Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own hair at home, you're not alone. With the rise of DIY haircuts, it's important to know how to cut men's hair back and sides. In this step-by-step guide, we'll walk you through the process to achieve a professional-looking cut.

Description

The back and sides of men's hair are often the most challenging areas to cut, but with a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve a great result. This guide will cover everything you need to know to cut men's hair back and sides, including how to section the hair, choose the right clippers, and blend the hair for a seamless finish.

How to Cut Men's Hair Back and Sides: Step by Step

Step 1: Section the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's essential to section the hair to make the process easier. Separate the top of the hair from the back and sides by creating a parting from the crown of the head to the ears. Use clips to secure the top section of the hair out of the way.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

Choosing the right clippers is crucial to achieving a good cut. Medium to heavy-duty clippers with a range of blade guards are ideal for men's hair. Start with the longest guard and work down to shorter guards as needed to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Back and Sides

Starting at the nape of the neck, use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Work your way up to the crown of the head, using a mirror to check your progress. Use a shorter guard around the edges of the hairline to achieve a clean, polished look.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the back and sides, it's time to blend the hair for a seamless finish. Use a clipper with a shorter guard to blend the hair between the back and sides and the top section. Take your time and use a light touch to avoid creating gaps or lines.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Back and Sides

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a great cut:

  • Use a comb to guide the clippers for a more even cut.
  • Work in small sections to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take breaks if you get tired or need to adjust your position.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and style, but most men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their look.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim a little more hair to even out the cut. If you're really unhappy with the result, you can always visit a professional stylist to fix the problem.

Hair Care After Cutting Men's Hair Back and Sides

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair in good condition and maintain the style.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair back and sides, you can save money and achieve a great look at home. With a little practice and patience, you can become a pro at cutting men's hair.
