How To Style Guys Hair Long On Top A Step By Step Guide

Long hair on top is a popular hairstyle for guys these days, and for good reason. This style allows for versatility in terms of styling, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. If you're considering trying out this trend, but aren't sure where to start, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Guys Hair Long on Top

The key to styling long hair on top is to keep it well-groomed and styled to suit your face shape and hair type. There are several ways to style long hair on top, including:

  • Side part
  • Slick back
  • Messy textured
  • Undercut
  • Pompadour

Each of these styles can be tailored to suit your individual preferences and hair type. To achieve any of these looks, you'll need to start with clean, dry hair that has been styled using a good quality styling product.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your long hair on top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style, whether that's a side part, slick back, messy textured look, undercut, or pompadour.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling products. Use a small amount to start with, and add more as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top

Long hair on top requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Invest in a quality styling product that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Can I style my hair long on top if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Long hair on top can look great on curly hair, and there are plenty of styling products available that can help you achieve your desired look.

What kind of styling product should I use?

This will depend on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you. Pomade, clay, and wax are all popular choices for styling long hair on top.

Hair Care for Long Hair on Top

In addition to regular trims and a good quality shampoo and conditioner, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With a little bit of care and attention, you can rock the long hair on top look with confidence and style.
