How To Properly Apply Hair Serum A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of frizzy and unmanageable hair? Do you want to achieve a sleek and smooth hairstyle? If your answer is yes, then hair serum might be the solution for you. Hair serum is a popular hair care product that can help tame frizz, add shine, and protect your hair from damage. However, applying hair serum can be tricky, especially if you're new to the game. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to properly apply hair serum to achieve the best results.

  1. How to Apply Hair Serum
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

  4. FAQs About Hair Serum

How to Apply Hair Serum

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first discuss the basics of hair serum. Hair serum is a leave-in hair product that is applied to damp or dry hair to protect it from damage and add shine. It's important to note that hair serum is not a styling product, but rather a hair care product that helps improve the overall health and appearance of your hair.

When applying hair serum, it's important to use the right amount and apply it evenly throughout your hair. Using too much hair serum can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy, while using too little may not provide the desired results. It's also important to choose the right hair serum for your hair type and concerns.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of hair serum, let's get into the step-by-step guide on how to apply it properly:

Step 1: Wash and towel-dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Dispense the hair serum

Dispense a pea-sized amount of hair serum onto your palm. Remember, less is more when it comes to hair serum.

Step 3: Rub the hair serum between your palms

Rub your palms together to evenly distribute the hair serum.

Step 4: Apply the hair serum to your hair

Apply the hair serum to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up towards the roots. Make sure to distribute the hair serum evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Comb your hair

Comb your hair to evenly distribute the hair serum and detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush as it can cause breakage.

Step 6: Style your hair as desired

You can now style your hair as desired. Hair serum can help protect your hair from heat damage, so feel free to use heat styling tools if needed.

Tips for Applying Hair Serum

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your hair serum:

  • Start with a small amount of hair serum and add more if needed.
  • Choose a hair serum that is formulated for your hair type and concerns.
  • Avoid applying hair serum to your roots as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after applying hair serum.
  • Apply hair serum to damp hair to help with absorption.

FAQs About Hair Serum

What does hair serum do?

Hair serum helps tame frizz, add shine, and protect your hair from damage.

Can hair serum be used on all hair types?

Yes, there are hair serums formulated for all hair types, from fine to thick and curly to straight.

How often should I use hair serum?

You can use hair serum daily or as needed. It's a leave-in hair product, so you don't need to rinse it out.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.

By following these simple tips and using hair serum properly, you can achieve healthy, shiny, and beautiful hair. Remember, hair care is a journey, and it takes time and effort to see results. But with patience and consistency, you can achieve your hair goals.
