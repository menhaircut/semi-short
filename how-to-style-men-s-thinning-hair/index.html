How To Style Men s Thinning Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem that many men face as they age. It can be challenging to style thinning hair, but with the right techniques, you can create a stylish and modern look that suits your personality. In this article, we will discuss how to style men's thinning hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Style Men's Thinning Hair

When it comes to styling thinning hair, the key is to create volume and texture. Avoid slick hairstyles that will highlight your thinning hair, and instead opt for a more natural and tousled look. Here are some popular styles that work well for men with thinning hair:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that is perfect for men with thinning hair. This cut is low maintenance and requires minimal styling. It also has the added benefit of making your hair look thicker and fuller. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short buzz cut with clippers, leaving about 1/8 inch of hair.

The Side Part

The side part is a versatile hairstyle that can work for any occasion. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume, then apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to the roots and comb your hair to one side.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and stylish hairstyle that works well for men with thinning hair. This cut features short layers and a messy texture, which helps to create the illusion of thicker hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short crop with choppy layers, then apply a small amount of texturizing paste to your hair and tousle it with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you style your thinning hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to the roots.
  4. Comb your hair to one side for a side part, or tousle it for a textured crop.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your thinning hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that will weigh down your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider using hair fibers or a hairpiece to add volume and thickness to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thinning hair. However, it's important to keep your hair healthy and avoid using heavy styling products that will weigh it down. Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Q: What are some common mistakes to avoid when styling thinning hair?

A: Some common mistakes to avoid when styling thinning hair include using too much product, slicking your hair back, and using a comb to create a part. These styles can highlight your thinning hair and make it look more noticeable.

Hair Care Advice for Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thinning hair healthy and strong:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking supplements like biotin or vitamin D to support healthy hair growth.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a stylish and modern look that works for your thinning hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you. With a little effort and care, you can achieve a confident and stylish look that makes you feel great.
