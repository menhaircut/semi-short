Shoulder Length Hairstyles With Long Side Bangs The Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and versatile, then shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs might just be what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to go for a fresh and stylish look without sacrificing the length of their hair.

What are Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs?

Shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs are hairstyles that feature hair that falls just above the shoulders and long bangs that are swept to one side of the face. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to frame their face and add some texture to their hair. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down and can be worn by women of all ages.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs

To achieve shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs, you'll need to have hair that's at least shoulder length. If your hair is shorter than this, you might need to grow it out or use hair extensions to achieve the desired length. Once you have the desired length, you can then add long side bangs to your hair.

To add long side bangs, you'll need to section off the front part of your hair and cut it to the desired length. You can then style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or straightener to add some texture and volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  3. Section off the front part of your hair that you want to use for your bangs.
  4. Cut your bangs to the desired length.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a curling iron or straightener.
  6. Use a hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain the desired length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a curling iron or straightener to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh between washes.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs

Q: Will shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs can work for all face shapes. However, the length and thickness of the bangs can be adjusted to suit different face shapes.

Q: Can I style shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs are versatile and can be styled differently for different occasions. You can wear your hair straight and sleek for a formal occasion or add some curls or waves for a more casual look.

Q: Are shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs easy to maintain?

A: Yes, shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs are easy to maintain. Regular trims and conditioning treatments are all that's needed to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Side Bangs

When it comes to hair care for shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs, it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, look for products that are designed to add moisture and repair damage.

In addition to using the right products, it's also important to avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you need to use a curling iron or straightener, use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the highest heat setting.

Finally, don't forget to trim your hair regularly to maintain the desired length and shape. With the right care and maintenance, shoulder length hairstyles with long side bangs can be a stylish and versatile look that's perfect for any occasion.
