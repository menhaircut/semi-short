What Glasses To Wear With Grey Hair A Guide For The Fashion Forward

Written by Luffy Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair naturally starts to grey. While some people embrace their grey hair, others prefer to cover it up. Regardless of which camp you fall into, one thing is for sure: you need to update your eyewear collection to complement your new look. In this article, we'll show you what glasses to wear with grey hair so you can look your best.

How to Choose Glasses for Grey Hair

Choosing the right glasses for grey hair can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you choose:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is an important factor when choosing glasses. If you have cool-toned skin, you'll want to choose glasses with blue or purple undertones. If you have warm-toned skin, you'll want to choose glasses with yellow or orange undertones.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is another important factor when choosing glasses. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose glasses with sharp angles to balance out your features. If you have a square face, you'll want to choose glasses with rounded edges to soften your features.

Consider Your Hair Color

Your hair color is also an important factor when choosing glasses. If you have grey hair with cool undertones, you'll want to choose glasses with blue or purple undertones to complement your hair. If you have grey hair with warm undertones, you'll want to choose glasses with yellow or orange undertones to complement your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose glasses for grey hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they're blue, you have cool-toned skin. If they're green, you have warm-toned skin.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Look in the mirror and determine your face shape. If you're not sure, ask a friend to help you.

Step 3: Determine Your Hair Color

Look at your hair in natural light and determine if it has cool or warm undertones.

Step 4: Choose Glasses

Based on your skin tone, face shape, and hair color, choose glasses that complement your features. If you're still unsure, visit an eyewear store and try on different styles.

Tips for Choosing Glasses for Grey Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you choose glasses for grey hair:

Choose Glasses That Make a Statement

Grey hair can be a statement in itself, so don't be afraid to choose glasses that make a statement as well. Choose glasses with bold colors or unique shapes.

Choose Glasses That Reflect Your Personality

Your glasses should reflect your personality. If you're outgoing, choose glasses with bold colors or patterns. If you're more reserved, choose glasses with subtle details.

Choose Glasses That Fit Properly

Your glasses should fit properly to ensure maximum comfort and style. Make sure the glasses rest comfortably on your nose and ears, and that they don't slide down your nose.

FAQ About Glasses for Grey Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about glasses for grey hair:

Q: Can I wear any color glasses with grey hair?

A: No, you should choose glasses with undertones that complement your skin tone and hair color.

Q: Can I wear glasses with cool undertones if I have warm-toned skin?

A: No, you should choose glasses with undertones that complement your skin tone.

Q: Can I wear glasses with warm undertones if I have cool-toned skin?

A: No, you should choose glasses with undertones that complement your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for Grey Hair

Here are some hair care tips for grey hair:

Use a Purple Shampoo

Use a purple shampoo once a week to keep your grey hair looking bright and shiny.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what glasses to wear with grey hair, you can update your eyewear collection and look your best. Remember to choose glasses that complement your skin tone, face shape, and hair color, and that fit properly. And don't forget to take care of your grey hair with regular trims and purple shampoo.
