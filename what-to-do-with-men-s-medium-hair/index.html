What To Do With Men s Medium Hair A Guide For Relaxed Styling

Written by Christine Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with medium-length hair, you may be wondering what to do with it. Perhaps you've been wearing the same style for years, or maybe you're looking for something new. Whatever your situation, there are plenty of options for styling men's medium hair. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve a relaxed, stylish look that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Medium Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Men's Medium Hair
  • FAQs About Men's Medium Hair
  • Hair Care for Men's Medium Hair

How to Style Men's Medium Hair

There are many different styles you can choose from when it comes to men's medium hair. Some popular options include:

  • Side part
  • Slick back
  • Textured crop
  • Quiff
  • Surfer hair

The key to choosing the right style for you is to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. You may want to consult with a hairstylist or barber to get their input on what would work best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Medium Hair

Once you've chosen a style, the next step is to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling men's medium hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a towel to dry it off before styling.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. The type of product you use will depend on your chosen style. For example, if you're going for a slicked-back look, you may want to use a pomade or wax. If you're going for a textured crop, you may want to use a sea salt spray.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  4. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your hands or a brush. This will help to set the style in place.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Remember, practice makes perfect when it comes to styling men's medium hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques until you find a style that works for you.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Medium Hair

Once you've styled your hair, you'll want to make sure it stays looking good throughout the day. Here are some tips for maintaining men's medium hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much. This can cause it to become greasy or lose its shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil between washes.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs About Men's Medium Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's medium hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some men may need to wash their hair every day, while others may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

What should I do if my hair gets frizzy?

Try using a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz. You can also try blow-drying your hair with a round brush to smooth it out.

Can I still wear a hat with styled hair?

Absolutely! Just be gentle when you take your hat off to avoid messing up your style.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Hair

In addition to styling and maintaining your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for men's medium hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support hair growth and health.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement if you're experiencing hair loss or thinning.

By following these tips, you can achieve a stylish, relaxed look with your men's medium hair that's both easy to maintain and healthy.
