How To Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently At Home

Written by Christine Oct 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch things up with a permanent curly hairstyle? Look no further as we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on how to make straight hair curly permanently at home. You no longer have to spend countless hours and money at the salon for a curly hairdo. Follow these simple and easy steps to achieve the perfect curly hair look that you desire.

Description

Permanent hair curling is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly or wavy. This process may sound intimidating but it is a simple procedure that can be done at home with the right products and tools. The process involves the use of a perm solution that breaks down the bonds in your hair, followed by a neutralizer that reforms the bonds in the new shape.

How To

Before starting the process, make sure to read the instructions on the perm kit carefully. The kit will contain a perm solution, neutralizer, gloves, and rods. Follow these easy steps to achieve permanent curly hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Do not use conditioner as it may interfere with the perm process. Dry your hair completely before starting the process.

Step 2: Protect Your Skin

Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline, ears, and neck to protect your skin from the chemicals in the perm solution.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts using hair clips. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be.

Step 4: Roll Your Hair

Using the rods provided in the kit, roll your hair tightly around the rod. Start at the ends of your hair and roll upwards towards your scalp. Secure the rod in place with the clips provided in the kit.

Step 5: Apply Perm Solution

Put on the gloves provided in the kit and apply the perm solution to each rod. Make sure to saturate each rod completely with the solution.

Step 6: Wait

Leave the perm solution on your hair for the recommended time mentioned in the kit instructions. Do not leave the solution on for longer as it may damage your hair.

Step 7: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water, making sure to remove all the perm solution. Do not shampoo your hair at this stage.

Step 8: Apply Neutralizer

Apply the neutralizer to each rod, making sure to saturate each one completely. Leave the neutralizer on for the recommended time mentioned in the kit instructions.

Step 9: Remove Rods

Remove the rods from your hair gently, making sure not to tug or pull at your hair.

Step 10: Rinse and Style

Rinse your hair with lukewarm water and apply a deep conditioning treatment. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out. Style your hair as desired using a diffuser or air-dry it for a natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a permanent hair curl:

  • Choose the right size of the rod depending on the type of curl you want. The smaller the rod, the tighter the curl will be.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after the perm process as it takes time for the curls to set in.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as it may damage your curls and make them frizzy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the health of your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about permanent hair curling:

Can I do a permanent hair curl at home?

Yes, you can do a permanent hair curl at home using a perm kit. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and take all the necessary precautions.

Will a permanent hair curl damage my hair?

The perm process involves the use of chemicals that may damage your hair if not done correctly. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and use a neutralizer to reform the bonds in your hair.

How long does a permanent hair curl last?

A permanent hair curl can last up to 6 months depending on your hair type and the aftercare you give it.

Hair Care

After getting a permanent hair curl, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the health and longevity of your curls. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as it may damage your curls.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it may cause breakage.

Now that you know how to make straight hair curly permanently at home, it is time to switch up your look and rock those curls. Follow these easy steps and tips to achieve the perfect curly hairdo.
