How To Cut Guys Long Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, or if you have a friend or family member with long hair, you may be wondering how to cut it with scissors. Cutting long hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut guys long hair with scissors in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself. Whether you're looking to save money or just want to try something new, cutting your long hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience.

How To Cut Guys Long Hair with Scissors

Before you begin cutting your long hair, you'll need to gather a few supplies. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror

Once you have your supplies ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in cutting your long hair is to wash and dry it. This will help you to see the natural flow of your hair and will make it easier to cut. Once your hair is dry, comb it out so that it's tangle-free.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Next, you'll want to divide your hair into sections. Use your comb to create a part down the center of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, create two more parts on each side of your head, dividing your hair into four sections. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom section, use your comb to separate a small section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut off the desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all of the hair in that section. Then, move on to the next section and repeat the process.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all of the sections of your hair, it's time to blend the layers. To do this, comb your hair straight up and use your scissors to cut into the ends of your hair. This will create a more natural and blended look.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to check for evenness. Use your mirror to check the back of your head and make sure that your hair is even on both sides. Make any necessary adjustments by cutting small sections of hair until you're satisfied with the length.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you cut your long hair with scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut a little bit at a time.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create a softer and more natural look.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear longer when it's dry.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to mist your hair as you cut, as this will help you to see the natural shape of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting guys long hair with scissors:

Can I cut my long hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically cut your long hair with regular scissors, it's best to use sharp hair-cutting scissors. Regular scissors can dull quickly and may damage your hair.

How often should I cut my long hair?

It's recommended to cut your long hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and free from split ends.

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Cutting curly hair can be more challenging than cutting straight hair, but it's still possible to do it yourself. Just be sure to cut your hair when it's dry and use the point-cutting technique to create a natural look.

Hair Care

After you've cut your long hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your guys long hair with scissors at home. Remember to take your time, start with small sections, and check for evenness to achieve a great result. Happy cutting!
