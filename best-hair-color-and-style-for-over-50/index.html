Best Hair Color And Style For Over 50

Written by Eveline Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Getting older doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style! In fact, many women over 50 find themselves embracing new hair colors and styles that flatter their changing features. In this article, we'll explore the best hair color and style options for women over 50, with tips for caring for your locks and maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hair Color and Style for Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your New Look
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color and Style
  • FAQ About Hair Color and Style for Women Over 50
  • Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

How to Choose the Best Hair Color and Style for Over 50

When it comes to choosing a new hair color and style, there are a few factors to consider. First, think about your skin tone and eye color. Warm skin tones tend to look best with red, golden, and honey tones, while cooler skin tones pair well with ash or platinum shades. You should also consider the texture and thickness of your hair, as certain styles may not work well with fine or thinning locks.

Another consideration is your personal style and lifestyle. Do you prefer a low-maintenance look, or are you willing to spend more time styling your hair? Are you comfortable with dramatic changes, or do you want a more subtle update?

Finally, consider your face shape and features. A skilled stylist can recommend styles that flatter your bone structure and complement your best features. For example, a layered bob can add volume and movement to thinner hair, while long layers can soften angular features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your New Look

Once you've chosen a new hair color and style, it's time to make it a reality. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving your new look:

  1. Find a reputable stylist who specializes in hair color and cuts for women over 50.
  2. Bring photos or examples of the hair color and style you want.
  3. Work with your stylist to choose the right shade and tone for your skin and eye color.
  4. Discuss any concerns you have about your hair texture or face shape.
  5. Trust your stylist's expertise and let them work their magic!

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color and Style

Once you've achieved your new look, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to avoid fading.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Consider a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep locks hydrated.
  • Trim regularly to maintain shape and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ About Hair Color and Style for Women Over 50

Q: Is it okay to color my hair if I have gray or white hair?

A: Yes! In fact, many women find that adding highlights or lowlights can add depth and dimension to their gray or white hair.

Q: What hair color is best for women with fair skin?

A: Women with fair skin tend to look best with cooler hair colors, such as ash blonde, platinum, or caramel. Avoid warm tones, which can wash out fair skin.

Q: Can I still wear long hair after 50?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can be flattering at any age, as long as it's well-maintained and styled to flatter your features.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

In addition to caring for your hair color and style, there are some general tips for keeping your locks healthy and beautiful as you age:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip natural oils and lead to dryness.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent damage while you sleep.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair health, such as biotin and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Stay hydrated to keep hair and skin looking their best.
  • Consider taking supplements or using topical treatments to support hair growth and thickness.

With the right hair color and style, as well as proper care, women over 50 can look stylish and confident as they age. Whether you opt for a bold new color or a subtle update to your current style, trust your instincts and work with a skilled stylist to achieve your desired look.
