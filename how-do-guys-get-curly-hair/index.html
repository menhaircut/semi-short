How Do Guys Get Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair has been a popular trend for men in recent years. Many guys want to achieve that curly hair look, but not everyone is born with it. Fortunately, there are several ways to get curly hair, whether you have straight or wavy hair. In this article, we will discuss the different methods on how do guys get curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair

There are several ways on how do guys get curly hair. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Perming

Perming is a chemical process that involves applying a solution to the hair to create curls. This method is permanent and can last for several months. Perming can be done on any hair type, but it is best suited for men with straight or wavy hair. It is important to note that perming can damage the hair if not done correctly.

2. Curling Iron

A curling iron is a tool that can create curls in the hair. This method is temporary and can last for a day or two. To use a curling iron, you need to wrap sections of your hair around the barrel of the iron and hold it for a few seconds. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Twist Out

A twist out is a method of creating curls in natural hair. You can achieve this look by twisting small sections of your hair and leaving them overnight. In the morning, you can unravel the twists and use a pick to create volume. This method is temporary and can last for a few days.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how do guys get curly hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for the styling process.

2. Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to define your curls and hold them in place.

3. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and create volume in your curls.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or twist out method. You can also use a comb or pick to create volume in your curls.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a hairspray to hold your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or pick to detangle your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I get curly hair naturally?

Yes, some men are born with naturally curly hair. However, if you have straight or wavy hair, you can use the methods mentioned above to achieve curly hair.

2. Can perming damage my hair?

Yes, perming can damage your hair if not done correctly. It is important to go to a professional stylist who has experience with perming.

3. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip away natural oils and cause your hair to become dry and frizzy.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips specifically for curly hair:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently pat your hair dry.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or pick when your hair is wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how do guys get curly hair, you can try out these methods to achieve the curly hair look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated. With the right products and techniques, you can have a head full of bouncy curls!
