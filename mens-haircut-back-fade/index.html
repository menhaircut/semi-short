Mens Haircut Back Fade How To Get The Look

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and stylish haircut? Look no further than the mens haircut back fade. This popular hairstyle is characterized by short hair at the sides and back of the head that gradually fades into longer hair on top. It's a versatile look that can be tailored to your personal style, from a slick and polished finish to a more casual and tousled vibe. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect mens haircut back fade, including tips for hair care and styling.

How to Get the Look

If you're ready to try out the mens haircut back fade, here's what you'll need to do:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

The first step in getting a mens haircut back fade is choosing your fade. There are several types of fades to choose from, including:

  • Low fade: The hair gradually fades from short to long, starting just above the ears.
  • Mid fade: The hair fades halfway up the sides and back of the head.
  • High fade: The hair fades all the way up to the top of the head.

Choose the fade that best suits your personal style and face shape.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Once you've chosen your fade, it's time to cut the sides and back of your hair. This is typically done with clippers, starting with a longer guard and gradually working down to a shorter guard as you move up the head. Your stylist will work with you to determine the best length for your hair and your desired fade.

Step 3: Blend the Top

After the sides and back are cut, it's time to blend the top of the hair. This is typically done with scissors, cutting the hair to the desired length and blending it into the fade. Your stylist will work with you to determine the best length and style for your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, it's time to style your hair. The mens haircut back fade can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal style and the occasion. Use a quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Back Fade

Once you've gotten your mens haircut back fade, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair and style.

FAQs About Mens Haircut Back Fade

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut back fade:

What is a back fade?

A back fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually fades from short to long at the back of the head.

What is the difference between a fade and a taper?

A fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually fades from short to long, while a taper is a type of haircut where the hair gradually tapers from the top of the head to the sides and back.

Is the mens haircut back fade suitable for all hair types?

The mens haircut back fade can be adapted to suit all hair types, from straight and fine hair to thick and curly hair.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Back Fade

To keep your mens haircut back fade looking sharp, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair and style, and visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the mens haircut back fade with confidence and style.
