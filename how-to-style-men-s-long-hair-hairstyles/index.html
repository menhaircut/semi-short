How To Style Men s Long Hair Hairstyles Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair is a popular hairstyle choice for men. However, it can be challenging to style long hair, especially if you’re new to it. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to style men's long hair hairstyles with ease. Here’s what we’ll cover:

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Men's Long Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Style Men's Long Hair

When it comes to styling men's long hair, the possibilities are endless. But before you start, you need to know your hair type and the style that suits your face shape. Here are some popular long hair hairstyles for men:

  • Man Bun
  • Ponytail
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Beach Waves
  • Slicked Back
  • Curly Long Hair

Once you’ve chosen your desired hairstyle, it’s time to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to style men's long hair hairstyles:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Long hair needs more nourishment, so make sure to use a product that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a hair oil or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair or let it air dry. If you’re going for a straight hairstyle, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  4. For a man bun, gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps.
  5. For a ponytail, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it with a hair tie. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps.
  6. For a half-up half-down hairstyle, gather the top section of your hair and tie it with a hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down.
  7. For beach waves, use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose curls. Use a sea salt spray to add texture and hold.
  8. For a slicked-back hairstyle, apply a hair gel or pomade to your hair and use a comb to slick it back.
  9. For curly long hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Apply a curl defining cream to enhance your curls.
  10. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips and Tricks

Styling men's long hair hairstyles can take time and effort. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality brush and comb to help you style your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's long hair hairstyles:

Q: Can I style my long hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can style your long hair without using any products. However, using a hair oil or serum can help protect your hair from heat damage and add shine.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Q: How do I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair needs extra care and attention to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style men's long hair hairstyles and keep your hair healthy and shiny. So go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best!
