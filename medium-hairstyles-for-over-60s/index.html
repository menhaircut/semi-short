Medium Hairstyles For Over 60S A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Luffy Nov 25, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, it's important to find a hairstyle that not only suits our changing face and hair texture but also makes us feel confident and beautiful. Medium hairstyles are an excellent choice for those over 60 as they offer versatility and can be styled in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll explore some of the best medium hairstyles for over 60s and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Medium hairstyles are typically defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This length is perfect for those over 60 as it's long enough to be styled in a variety of ways but short enough to be low maintenance. Medium hairstyles can also be layered or textured, which can add volume and movement to thinning hair.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect medium hairstyle is to consider your face shape. Oval faces can pull off almost any style, while square faces benefit from layers and softening of the jawline. Round faces look best with angles and height on top, while heart-shaped faces suit hairstyles that frame the face.

Next, consider your hair type. Thick hair can handle more layers and texture, while fine hair benefits from blunt cuts and volume-boosting products. Curly hair looks great with layers and shaping, while straight hair can benefit from a choppy, textured cut.

Once you've decided on a hairstyle, it's important to find a stylist who specializes in cutting and styling hair for older women. They can offer advice on which styles will work best for your hair type and face shape and can also provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Divide hair into sections and clip away the top layers.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry the bottom layers, creating volume and movement.

4. Unclip the top layers and blow-dry, using the brush to create lift at the roots.

5. Finish with a light hairspray or styling product.

Tips

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to thinning hair.

- Avoid heavy products that can weigh down hair.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

- Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools.

- Consider trying a wig or hairpiece if you're looking for a temporary change.

FAQ

Q: What are some good medium hairstyles for over 60s with thinning hair?

A: A layered, textured bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers can add volume and movement to thinning hair.

Q: Can I wear my hair long over 60?

A: Of course! However, longer hair may require more maintenance and styling to keep it looking healthy and youthful.

Q: Should I avoid bangs over 60?

A: Not necessarily! Bangs can be a great way to frame the face and add a youthful touch. However, consider softer, side-swept bangs rather than blunt bangs.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is essential, especially as we age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping natural oils.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons every day.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

- Consider using hair masks and deep conditioning treatments to keep hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and medium hairstyle ideas, you'll be sure to find a look that works for you and makes you feel confident and beautiful at any age.
