Hairstyles For Fat Faces Male Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Having a round or chubby face can be a challenge when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The goal is to create a balance and slimming effect that enhances your features. In this article, we will share some tips and ideas on hairstyles for fat faces male that you can try.

Description

A fat or round face can be defined by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded jawline. The key to choosing the right hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. This can be achieved by adding height or volume on top of the head, keeping the sides short, or creating angles around the jawline.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same, you have a round or chubby face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Look for hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by asking your barber or stylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Consult a Professional

It's always a good idea to consult a professional before making a drastic change to your hairstyle. They can advise you on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a fat face:

  • Choose a style that adds height or volume on top of the head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  • Create angles around the jawline to add definition.
  • Try a side-parted hairstyle to create asymmetry.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for fat faces male:

What is the best hairstyle for a fat face?

The best hairstyle for a fat face is one that adds height or volume on top of the head and keeps the sides short. This creates the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Can a chubby man have long hair?

Yes, a chubby man can have long hair, but it's important to choose a style that adds volume on top and keeps the sides trimmed. A messy or layered look can also help create a slimming effect.

What hair products should I use for a fat face?

Use products that add volume or texture to your hair, such as mousse, sea salt spray, or wax. Avoid products that make your hair look flat or greasy.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips and ideas, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Remember to consult a professional and take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look.
