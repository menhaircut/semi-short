How To Know The Right Eyebrow Shape For Your Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly shaping your eyebrows only to find out that it doesn't suit your face? Fear not! In this article, we'll be discussing how to know the right eyebrow shape for your face. We'll be providing you with easy tips and step-by-step guides to achieve the perfect brow shape that complements your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Knowing the right eyebrow shape for your face is crucial as it can enhance your overall appearance. It can also make you look more youthful and awake. The perfect brow shape should complement your natural features and enhance your face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing various factors that contribute to finding the right eyebrow shape for your face.

How to

Before diving into the different eyebrow shapes, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you identify the right brow shape that will complement your features. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Once you've determined your face shape, you can then identify the ideal eyebrow shape.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're lucky as most brow shapes will suit you. Your face shape is naturally balanced, so you don't need to create an arch. Instead, go for a soft angled brow shape that complements your natural features.

Round Face Shape

For those with a round face shape, it's important to create an arch to add definition to your face. Go for a high arch shape that elongates your face. Avoid round brow shapes as it can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, create a soft arch to balance out your features. Avoid angular brow shapes as it can make your face appear harsher. Instead, opt for a curved brow shape that softens your features.

Heart Face Shape

For those with a heart face shape, go for a low arch shape that compliments your natural features. Avoid high arch shapes as it can make your forehead appear wider. Instead, create a soft brow shape that balances out your face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, go for a curved brow shape that softens your features. Avoid high arch shapes as it can make your forehead appear wider. Instead, create a soft brow shape that balances out your face.

Step-by-Step

Now that you've determined your face shape and the ideal brow shape, it's time to shape your eyebrows. Follow these easy steps to achieve the perfect brow shape:

  1. Brush your eyebrows upwards using a spoolie brush.
  2. Use a brow pencil to mark the beginning, arch, and end of your eyebrow.
  3. Pluck any stray hairs outside the marked areas.
  4. Use a brow gel to set your hairs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaping your eyebrows:

  • Always use a sharp and clean pair of tweezers.
  • Don't overpluck your eyebrows as it can take months to grow back.
  • Use small and quick plucking motions to avoid pain.
  • Choose a brow pencil that matches your natural hair color.

FAQ

How often should I pluck my eyebrows?

You should only pluck your eyebrows once every two weeks to avoid overplucking and damaging your hair follicles.

Can I shape my eyebrows at home?

Yes, you can shape your eyebrows at home by following the steps provided in this article. However, if you're not confident, it's best to visit a professional.

What if I accidentally overpluck my eyebrows?

If you accidentally overpluck your eyebrows, don't panic. Apply castor oil to your eyebrows every night to promote hair growth. It may take a few weeks to see results, but your eyebrows will eventually grow back.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your eyebrows to promote healthy growth. Apply castor oil to your eyebrows every night to nourish your hair follicles. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your eyebrows and always remove your makeup before going to bed.

Knowing the right eyebrow shape for your face is crucial as it can enhance your overall appearance. By following the tips and step-by-step guides provided in this article, you can achieve the perfect brow shape that complements your natural features. Remember to take care of your eyebrows to promote healthy growth and avoid overplucking. Happy shaping!
