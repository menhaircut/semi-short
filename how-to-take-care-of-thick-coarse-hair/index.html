How To Take Care Of Thick Coarse Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick and coarse hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right care and attention, it can look beautiful and healthy. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to take care of thick coarse hair.

  • How to Take Care of Thick Coarse Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

  • FAQs About Thick Coarse Hair

How to Take Care of Thick Coarse Hair

Thick and coarse hair requires extra attention and care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Thick and coarse hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so you don’t need to wash it as often. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and make it even drier. Try washing your hair once or twice a week instead of every day.

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

When you do wash your hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for thick and coarse hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil, which can help nourish and hydrate your hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Thick and coarse hair can be prone to damage from heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use them at a lower temperature setting. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Brush Your Hair Regularly

Thick and coarse hair can be prone to tangling, so it’s important to brush your hair regularly. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with boar bristles to gently detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to take care of your thick coarse hair:

  1. Wash your hair once or twice a week with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, or use them at a lower temperature setting.
  3. Brush your hair regularly with a wide-tooth comb or a brush with boar bristles.
  4. Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy.
  5. Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Tips for Managing Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing your thick coarse hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage, like tight braids or ponytails.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangling while you sleep.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your hair and scalp nourished.

FAQs About Thick Coarse Hair

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my thick coarse hair?

A: It’s best to use a shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for thick and coarse hair, as these products will contain ingredients that can help nourish and hydrate your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my thick coarse hair?

A: Thick and coarse hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so you don’t need to wash it as often. Try washing your hair once or twice a week instead of every day.

Q: How can I prevent my thick coarse hair from tangling?

A: Brush your hair regularly with a wide-tooth comb or a brush with boar bristles to prevent tangling. You can also use a detangling spray or a leave-in conditioner to help make your hair more manageable.
