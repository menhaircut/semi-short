Older Men s Hair Long Top Short Sides A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In recent years, older men's hair with a long top and short sides has become a popular trend. This style is versatile, easy to maintain, and works well for men of all ages. Whether you're looking to create a bold new look or simply update your current hairstyle, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know about older men's hair long top short sides.

Description

Older men's hair long top short sides is a hairstyle where the hair on the top of the head is left long while the sides and back are cut short. This style is often paired with a fade or undercut, which creates a gradual transition from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides. The length of the top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How To

To achieve the older men's hair long top short sides look, follow these steps:

  1. Decide on the length of the top and the type of fade or undercut you want.
  2. Visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style.
  3. Show them pictures of the style you want and communicate any specific requests or concerns.
  4. Once the sides and back are trimmed, the top will be cut and styled to your desired length and texture.
  5. Use styling products like pomade or wax to create your preferred look.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in getting older men's hair long top short sides:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

The first step is to decide on the length and style of the top, as well as the type of fade or undercut you want. This will depend on your personal preferences and the shape of your face.

Step 2: Find a Skilled Barber or Hairstylist

It's important to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Ask for recommendations from friends or do some research online to find someone who has a portfolio of similar hairstyles.

Step 3: Communicate Your Preferences

Show your barber or hairstylist pictures of the style you want and communicate any specific requests or concerns. They should be able to offer advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you're both on the same page, the barber or hairstylist will start by trimming the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. They may use clippers or scissors, depending on the style you've chosen.

Step 5: Cut and Style the Top

Next, the barber or hairstylist will cut and style the top of your hair to your desired length and texture. This may involve using scissors or a razor to create layers and texture.

Step 6: Use Styling Products

Finally, you can use styling products like pomade, wax, or gel to create your preferred look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting older men's hair long top short sides:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Find a skilled barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style.
  • Communicate your preferences clearly and don't be afraid to ask questions.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain your style with regular trims and touch-ups.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about older men's hair long top short sides:

Is this style suitable for all ages?

Yes, this style works well for men of all ages. It can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How often do I need to get it trimmed?

You'll need to get regular trims and touch-ups to maintain the style. This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how neat you want the sides and back to look.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of styling products to create your preferred look, including pomade, wax, gel, and hairspray. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the older men's hair long top short sides style with confidence and ease. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or simply update your look, this versatile hairstyle is sure to impress.
