Men s Hairstyle Long On Top Medium Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In recent years, the men's hairstyle long on top medium sides has become increasingly popular. This versatile hairstyle can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types, making it a go-to choice for many men. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at how to achieve this style, as well as some tips for keeping it looking great.

Description

The men's hairstyle long on top medium sides is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, which is typically styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, textured or curly. The sides and back, meanwhile, are kept shorter, usually around a medium length. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the more tapered sides, resulting in a stylish and modern look.

How to Achieve the Men's Hairstyle Long on Top Medium Sides

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's hairstyle long on top medium sides:

Step 1: Grow out the hair on top

The first step is to grow out the hair on top of your head to a suitable length. This can take several months, depending on your hair type and how fast it grows. Aim for a length of around 4-6 inches, so you have plenty of hair to work with when styling.

Step 2: Trim the sides and back

Once the hair on top has reached the desired length, it's time to trim the sides and back. Ask your barber for a medium-length cut, usually around a 2-3 on the clipper guard. This will create a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter sides, which is the hallmark of this hairstyle.

Step 3: Style the hair on top

Now it's time to style the hair on top. This can be done in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preference and the occasion. Slick it back for a more formal look, or add some texture for a more relaxed, casual vibe. You can also experiment with different products, such as pomade, wax or clay, to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Keeping the Men's Hairstyle Long on Top Medium Sides Looking Great

Here are some tips for keeping your men's hairstyle long on top medium sides looking great:

Tip 1: Invest in quality hair products

Using quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner and styling products, can make a big difference in the overall look and health of your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

Tip 2: Regularly trim the sides and back

To maintain the contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter sides, it's important to regularly trim the sides and back. This will keep the overall shape of the hairstyle looking sharp and neat.

Tip 3: Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques and products to find the look that works best for you. This is a versatile hairstyle, so there are plenty of options to choose from.

FAQ

Q: Can the men's hairstyle long on top medium sides work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy and curly hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended to trim the sides and back every 4-6 weeks to maintain the overall shape of the hairstyle.

Hair Care

Maintaining a healthy hair care routine is important for keeping your men's hairstyle long on top medium sides looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner can help keep it clean, healthy and looking great.

Tip 2: Use a heat protectant

If you frequently use heat styling tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener, it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Avoid over-styling

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage, so it's important to avoid excessive heat styling, harsh chemicals and tight hairstyles.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve and maintain a stylish and modern men's hairstyle long on top medium sides that works for you.
