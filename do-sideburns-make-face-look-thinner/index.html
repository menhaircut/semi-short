Do Sideburns Make Face Look Thinner

Written by Oliver Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are someone who wants to make your face look thinner, then sideburns could be the answer. It is said that sideburns can create an illusion of a thinner face, but is there any truth to this statement? Let's dive into this topic and find out.

  • Step by step

Description

Sideburns are the hair that grows in front of the ears and extends to the jawline. They can be long, short, or even non-existent, depending on the individual's preference. Some people believe that having sideburns can make their face look thinner, while others believe the opposite. But what is the truth?

The answer to this question is that it depends on the individual's face shape. Sideburns can make the face look thinner or wider, depending on the length and thickness of the hair. For example, if you have a round face, shorter and thinner sideburns can create an illusion of a slimmer face. On the other hand, if you have a long face, longer and thicker sideburns can balance out the proportions of your face and make it look wider.

How to

If you want to try out sideburns to make your face look thinner, here are some tips to follow:

1. Determine your face shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing it to the different face shapes such as round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can decide on the length and thickness of your sideburns.

2. Choose the right length and thickness

The length and thickness of your sideburns should be based on your face shape. As a general rule of thumb, shorter and thinner sideburns work well for round faces, while longer and thicker sideburns work well for long faces.

3. Trim regularly

Make sure to trim your sideburns regularly to maintain the desired length and thickness. Overgrown sideburns can make your face look wider, so it's important to keep them in check.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your sideburns:

1. Wash your face

Before styling your sideburns, make sure to wash your face with a gentle cleanser and pat it dry.

2. Comb your hair

Comb your hair in the direction of your sideburns to make sure they are lying flat against your skin.

3. Trim the excess hair

Use a pair of scissors to trim any excess hair that is sticking out. Make sure to trim slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.

4. Style your sideburns

Use a hair gel or wax to style your sideburns in the desired shape. You can use a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Experiment with different lengths and thicknesses to find the perfect look for your face shape.
  • Use a razor to create a more precise look.
  • Avoid over-trimming your sideburns, as this can make your face look wider.

FAQ

Do sideburns make your face look thinner?

Yes, sideburns can make your face look thinner or wider, depending on the length and thickness of the hair and your face shape.

What face shape suits sideburns?

Shorter and thinner sideburns work well for round faces, while longer and thicker sideburns work well for long faces.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your sideburns to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your sideburns regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your sideburns to keep them soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel or wax, as this can cause buildup and make your sideburns look greasy.

In conclusion, sideburns can make your face look thinner or wider, depending on the length and thickness of the hair and your face shape. It's important to experiment with different looks and find the perfect style for your face shape. With the right care and maintenance, your sideburns can be a great addition to your overall look.
