How To Fake Curtain Bangs No Heat

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to try something new? Curtain bangs are a great way to change up your look, but not everyone is ready to commit to a full-on haircut. Luckily, there is a way to fake curtain bangs without using any heat! In this article, we will show you how to achieve this trendy hairstyle without damaging your hair.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that frame the face and create a soft, effortless look. They are called âcurtainâ bangs because they are parted in the middle, just like a pair of curtains. How To

Here's how to fake curtain bangs no heat:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head, right above your forehead.
  3. Twist the section of hair away from your face.
  4. Using a bobby pin, secure the twisted section of hair to the side of your head.
  5. Repeat the same steps on the other side of your head.
  6. Fluff out the twisted sections of hair to create the curtain bang effect.

And that's it! You now have fake curtain bangs without using any heat.

Step by Step

For those who need a more detailed step-by-step guide, here it is:

  1. Brush your hair and create a middle part.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head, right above your forehead.
  3. Twist the section of hair away from your face.
  4. Hold the twisted section of hair with one hand and use the other hand to push the twisted section towards your scalp.
  5. Using a bobby pin, secure the twisted section of hair to the side of your head.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 on the other side of your head.
  7. Fluff out the twisted sections of hair to create the curtain bang effect.

It may take some practice to get the twists and placement just right, but once you get the hang of it, it's a quick and easy hairstyle to achieve.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect fake curtain bangs:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Experiment with the thickness of the twisted sections to find what works best for you.
  • If you have long hair, tuck the ends of the twisted sections behind your ears to create a more polished look.
  • Add some hairspray to keep the curtain bangs in place all day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about faking curtain bangs:

Can I use a curling iron or straightener to create curtain bangs?

While using a curling iron or straightener can create the curtain bang look, it can also damage your hair with the heat. That's why we recommend the no-heat method.

How long will the fake curtain bangs last?

The fake curtain bangs will last until you take them out. You can even sleep with them in and wake up with the same look!

Will this work on all hair types?

Yes, this method can work on all hair types. However, longer hair may require more bobby pins to secure the twisted sections in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, even when faking curtain bangs. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips, you can achieve the perfect fake curtain bangs without damaging your hair. Give it a try and see how you like the look!
