How to Choose the Right Haircut

Before you choose a haircut for your oblong face, it's important to understand what an oblong face shape looks like. An oblong face is longer than it is wide, and it has a straight forehead and jawline. To balance out an oblong face, you need a haircut that will add width to the face.

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your oblong face:

  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as long straight hair.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and volume.
  • Try a side-swept fringe to add width to your forehead.
  • Consider a bob or lob haircut that ends at or above your shoulders.
  • Avoid center parts and instead opt for a side part to create width.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut for your oblong face, here are some step-by-step instructions for getting the perfect cut:

  1. Start by finding a hairstylist who has experience working with oblong face shapes.
  2. Show your hairstylist pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss which ones will work best for your face shape.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair to create volume and movement.
  4. Consider adding a side-swept fringe to add width to your forehead.
  5. A bob or lob haircut that ends at or above your shoulders can also work well for an oblong face.
  6. Ask your hairstylist to create a side part instead of a center part to add width to your face.
  7. Once your haircut is complete, style your hair with volumizing products to create even more width and volume.

Tips for the Perfect Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for your oblong face:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Try a messy, textured look to create volume and movement.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create extra volume and hold.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut to create width and balance.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as long straight hair or center parts. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your face.

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that adds volume and width to the face. Consider a bob or lob haircut that ends at or above your shoulders, with layers and a side-swept fringe.

Should I add bangs to my haircut if I have an oblong face?

Adding bangs to your haircut can help create width and balance on an oblong face. Consider a side-swept fringe that adds volume and width to your forehead.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume and body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect haircut for your oblong face in 2021. Remember, a good haircut can do wonders for your confidence and self-esteem, so don't be afraid to experiment and try new styles!
