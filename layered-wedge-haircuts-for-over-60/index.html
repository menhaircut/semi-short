Layered Wedge Haircuts For Over 60 The Perfect Style For You

Written by Oliver Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new haircut, layered wedge haircuts might be the perfect style for you. This stylish and versatile haircut can give you a fresh look while still maintaining a classic vibe. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting a layered wedge haircut and how to take care of it. Keep reading to find out more!

Description

A layered wedge haircut is a short, layered haircut that features a graduated shape. The hair at the back is cut short and gradually gets longer towards the front. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the wedge shape creates a flattering frame for your face. This haircut is perfect for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to

To get a layered wedge haircut, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and your face shape. A shorter length can give you a more youthful appearance, while a longer length can add more volume to your hair. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for you.

Step 2: Decide on the layers

The layers in a layered wedge haircut can be soft or sharp, depending on your preference. Soft layers can create a more natural and relaxed look, while sharp layers can add more texture to your hair. Discuss with your hairstylist to decide on the layers that would best suit your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Cut the hair

Your hairstylist will start by cutting the hair at the back of your head short and gradually longer towards the front. They will then add layers to create volume and texture. Finally, they will shape the hair into the wedge shape by cutting the hair at an angle towards the front.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a layered wedge haircut:

- Bring a photo of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist. - Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair. - Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair for a more polished look. - Use a texturizing spray to add more texture and hold to your hair. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Will a layered wedge haircut suit my face shape?

A: Layered wedge haircuts are versatile and can suit most face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

Q: How often should I get a layered wedge haircut?

A: You should get a layered wedge haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I style my layered wedge haircut differently?

A: Yes, you can style your layered wedge haircut in different ways, such as adding curls or waves for a more textured look.

Hair Care

To keep your layered wedge haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner for your hair type. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Protect your hair from the sun and UV rays by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen. - Brush your hair gently to avoid breaking the hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance layered wedge haircut at any age. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you and enjoy your new look!
