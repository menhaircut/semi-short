How To Style Wavy Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 · 4 min read

As a guy with wavy hair, it can be difficult to know how to style it. Wavy hair can be tricky to work with, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a great style that looks effortless. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style wavy hair for guys, step-by-step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Wavy Hair for Guys
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Wavy hair is a combination of curly and straight hair, with S-shaped curls that are loose and flowy. Wavy hair can be thick or thin, and can be styled in many different ways. However, it can also be difficult to manage, as it can frizz easily and lose its shape.

When it comes to styling wavy hair for guys, the key is to work with your natural texture and find products that enhance your waves without weighing them down. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a great style that looks effortless and natural.

How to Style Wavy Hair for Guys

There are many different ways to style wavy hair for guys, depending on your hair length and personal style. Here are some popular styles for wavy hair:

  • Messy Waves: This style is perfect for guys with long or medium-length wavy hair. Simply apply a texturizing product to your hair and scrunch it up to create a messy, beachy look.
  • Side Part: This classic style works well for guys with short or medium-length wavy hair. Use a comb to create a deep side part, and apply a styling product to keep your waves in place.
  • Undercut: This edgy style is great for guys with thick, wavy hair. Keep the sides and back of your hair short, and leave the top longer to show off your waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style wavy hair for guys:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing spray or mousse.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair up, creating waves and volume.
  4. If you have longer hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your waves.
  5. For a messy look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a natural, beachy texture.
  6. For a more polished look, use a comb to create a part and smooth down any flyaways.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for styling wavy hair for guys:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.
  • Don’t be afraid to embrace your natural texture and let your waves do their thing!

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wavy hair for guys:

  • Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair? A: Yes, you can use a flat iron or straightening brush to straighten your hair, but be sure to use heat protectant and not do it too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair? A: It depends on your hair type and styling routine, but generally 2-3 times a week is recommended to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Q: Can I use gel on my wavy hair? A: Yes, but be sure to use a lightweight, non-greasy gel and apply it sparingly to avoid weighing down your waves.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your wavy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style your wavy hair and look great every day. Whether you prefer a messy, beachy look or a polished, classic style, there’s a perfect look for you.
