Revlon Pro Collection Salon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer How To Use

Written by Daniel Aug 07, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking for a hair styling tool that can help you achieve salon-quality results at home, look no further than the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer. This innovative tool combines a hair dryer and volumizer in one, making it easy to achieve voluminous, shiny hair without having to use multiple tools.

Description

The Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer features a unique oval brush design that helps to create volume and shine while drying your hair. The brush is made with ionic technology, which helps to reduce frizz and static, and features multiple heat and speed settings to suit your hair type and styling preferences.

How to Use

Using the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is easy, even if you're not a professional stylist. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water, then apply a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage from the heat of the dryer.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use hair clips to secure each section out of the way while you work on the others.

Step 3: Start Drying

Turn on the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer and select the heat and speed settings that work best for your hair type. Take a section of hair and place the brush underneath it, close to the roots. Slowly glide the brush down the section of hair, using your other hand to guide the brush through the ends.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way up from the bottom. Use the brush to create volume and lift at the roots, and to smooth and straighten the ends.

Step 5: Finish and Style

Once you've dried and styled all of your hair, use a finishing spray or serum to add shine and hold. Style your hair as desired, and enjoy your voluminous, shiny locks!

Tips

  • For best results, use the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on damp, not soaking wet, hair.
  • Use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair type to reduce the risk of damage.
  • Experiment with different brush sizes and shapes to achieve different styles and levels of volume.
  • Clean the brush regularly to remove any hair or product buildup.

FAQ

Can the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer be used on all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be used on all hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

Can the brush be removed for cleaning?

No, the brush on the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer cannot be removed. However, it can be cleaned by wiping it down with a damp cloth or using a cleaning brush to remove any buildup.

Is the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer safe for daily use?

Yes, the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is safe for daily use. However, to reduce the risk of damage, it's best to use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair type and to apply a heat protectant spray before styling.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding excessive heat styling and chemical treatments.

In addition to using the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, try incorporating other hair styling tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, to create a variety of styles. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and using the Revlon Pro Collection Salon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, you can achieve salon-quality results at home and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
