What Hairstyles Suit Diamond Face Shape A Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a diamond face shape, then you have a narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones. This unique face shape can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut, you can enhance your features and look your best. In this article, we'll share with you some hairstyles that suit diamond face shapes.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The diamond face shape is quite unique, and it's essential to find a haircut that complements your features. It's all about creating balance and highlighting your best features. The right hairstyle can soften your cheekbones and jawline, and make your forehead appear wider. The wrong hairstyle, on the other hand, can make your face look too narrow or elongated.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for diamond face shape, it's essential to focus on creating width at the forehead and chin. Layers, bangs, and volume are your best friends. You can also play with textures and lengths, such as waves or curls, to create balance and soften your features.

Step by step

Here are some hairstyles that suit diamond face shapes:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and texture to your hair, creating balance and softening your cheekbones. You can add face-framing layers around your chin to create width and highlight your jawline.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a wider forehead and balance out your cheekbones. You can also add layers around your face to create softness and texture.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and edgy choice for diamond face shapes. It can create balance by adding volume at the crown and highlighting your cheekbones. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a softer look.

4. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers can create volume and texture, making your face appear wider. You can choose a chin-length bob or a longer bob that grazes your shoulders. Adding side-swept bangs can also create a softer look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that create too much volume at the crown, as they can make your face look too narrow.
  • Choose hairstyles that create width at the forehead and chin.
  • Play with layers, bangs, and textures to create balance and softness.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate your face.
  • Choose hairstyles that highlight your best features, such as your cheekbones or jawline.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for diamond face shape:

What hairstyles should I avoid with a diamond face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that create too much volume at the crown or are too long and straight. These hairstyles can make your face appear too narrow or elongated.

What hairstyles suit diamond face shape?

Hairstyles that create width at the forehead and chin, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and bobs with layers, suit diamond face shapes. Playing with textures and lengths can also create balance and softness.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for diamond face shape can be a challenge, but with the right haircut, you can enhance your features and look your best. Focus on creating width at the forehead and chin, and play with layers, bangs, and textures to create balance and softness. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
