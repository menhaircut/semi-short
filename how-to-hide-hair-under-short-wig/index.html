How To Hide Hair Under Short Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to hide your natural hair under a short wig? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. Many people find it difficult to hide their natural hair under a wig, especially if the wig is short. But with the right techniques and tips, you can easily hide your hair and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. In this article, we’ll show you how to hide hair under a short wig, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Hair Under Short Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hiding Hair Under a Short Wig
  • FAQs

How to Hide Hair Under Short Wig

The first step to hiding your natural hair under a short wig is to prepare your hair. You want to make sure that your hair is as flat and smooth as possible, so that it doesn’t create any bumps or lumps under the wig. Here’s what you need to do:

  • Wash your hair and blow-dry it straight
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to make your hair more manageable
  • Apply a light oil or serum to add shine and smoothness
  • Brush your hair back and create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck
  • Secure the ponytail with a hair tie

Now that your hair is prepared, you’re ready to put on the wig.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to hide your hair under a short wig:

  • Put on a wig cap that matches your skin tone
  • Place the wig on your head, aligning it with your natural hairline
  • Secure the wig in place with the combs or clips that are attached to the wig
  • Use a rat-tail comb to pull out a small section of hair from the wig and comb it forward
  • Take the small section of hair and tuck it under the hair tie that’s holding your natural hair in the ponytail
  • Repeat this process, working your way around the wig, until all of the hair is tucked under the hair tie
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs or flyaways
  • Style the wig as desired

That’s it! Your natural hair is now hidden under a short wig.

Tips for Hiding Hair Under a Short Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you hide your hair under a short wig:

  • Choose a wig that fits properly – a wig that is too tight or too loose can make it difficult to hide your natural hair
  • Use a wig cap to help keep your natural hair in place
  • Consider using a wig grip or band to help keep the wig in place
  • Trim any excess lace from the front of the wig to create a more natural-looking hairline
  • Use a wig brush or comb to style the wig

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a short wig if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short wig even if you have long hair. Just make sure to prepare your hair properly, as outlined above, and tuck it all under the hair tie before putting on the wig.

Q: Can I wear a short wig if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short wig if you have thick hair. However, you may need to use more bobby pins to keep your hair in place, and you may need to adjust the wig more frequently throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

It’s important to take care of your natural hair, even when you’re wearing a wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your natural hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

By following these tips, you can keep your natural hair healthy and looking great, even when you’re wearing a short wig.

Now that you know how to hide hair under a short wig, you can confidently wear your wig and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. With the right preparation, technique, and care, you can enjoy wearing your wig and feel great about your hair.
