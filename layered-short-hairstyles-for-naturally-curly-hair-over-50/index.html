Layered Short Hairstyles For Naturally Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Christine Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50 and have naturally curly hair, you might be wondering what hairstyles are suitable for you. Layered short hairstyles are a great option for those with curly hair as they add volume, texture, and shape to your hair. In this article, we'll provide you with a guide on how to achieve layered short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50.

Description

Layered short hairstyles involve cutting your hair into different lengths, creating layers. This technique adds movement and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and voluminous. Layered short hairstyles also work well for those with naturally curly hair as the layers help the curls fall naturally, creating a relaxed and effortless look.

How to Achieve Layered Short Hairstyles for Naturally Curly Hair Over 50

Step 1: Consultation

The first step to achieving layered short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best layered short hairstyle for you.

Step 2: Choosing the Right Length

After your consultation, your hairstylist will determine the right length for your hair. Layered short hairstyles can range from a pixie cut to a bob cut, depending on your preference and face shape. Your hairstylist will also consider your hair's natural curl pattern and adjust the layers accordingly.

Step 3: Cutting the Layers

Once the length is determined, your hairstylist will start cutting the layers. They will start cutting the layers from the top of your head, working their way down to the bottom. The layers will be cut at different lengths, creating a textured look.

Step 4: Styling the Hair

After the layers are cut, your hairstylist will style your hair. They will apply styling products to enhance the curls and add volume to your hair. Your hairstylist may also use a diffuser to dry your hair, which helps to define the curls and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Short Hairstyles for Naturally Curly Hair Over 50

Now that you have achieved the perfect layered short hairstyle, here are some tips on how to maintain it:

  • Regular trims: Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help to maintain the shape and texture of your layered short hairstyle.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for curly hair to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so avoid using hot tools as much as possible.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase: Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and maintain your curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve layered short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 at home?

A: We recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist to achieve the perfect layered short hairstyle for your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain the natural oils in your hair.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair, but we recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist to ensure the color complements your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hairstyles for Naturally Curly Hair Over 50

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Moisturize your hair: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as sulfates, which can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, layered short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 50 are a great option for those looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle. Consult with a professional hairstylist to achieve the perfect layered short hairstyle for your hair type and face shape, and follow our tips for maintaining your hair's health and beauty.
