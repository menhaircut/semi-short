Description

Round faces are characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may be wondering what the best hairstyles are to flatter your features. In this post, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for round faces in 2023, and provide tips for maintaining and styling your hair.

How To Choose the Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider the length and volume of your hair. Generally, hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head and length to the face can help to elongate a round face. Layered hairstyles can also help to add depth and texture, while avoiding styles that accentuate the fullness of the cheeks.

Some popular hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Pixie cuts
  • Updos
  • High ponytails

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate a round face by adding volume and depth to the hair. This hairstyle works particularly well with wavy or curly hair, as the layers can help to define and enhance natural texture.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance the proportions of a round face by drawing attention to the eyes and framing the face. This hairstyle can be paired with a variety of lengths and textures, and can be styled straight or wavy.

Bob Cuts

Bob cuts can be a great option for round faces, particularly when paired with longer layers or side-swept bangs. This hairstyle can help to add length to the face, while also providing volume and texture.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts can be a bold and stylish choice for round faces, particularly when paired with wispy bangs or textured layers. This hairstyle can help to accentuate the cheekbones and elongate the face.

Updos

Updos can be a great option for special occasions, particularly when paired with voluminous curls or braids. This hairstyle can help to add height to the crown of the head, while also showcasing the face.

High Ponytails

High ponytails can help to elongate a round face by drawing the eye upward and creating the illusion of a longer face. This hairstyle can be paired with a variety of textures, and can be worn sleek and straight or curly and voluminous.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face

Follow these steps to style your hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Choose an appropriate hairstyle, such as long layers or side-swept bangs
  3. Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair
  4. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair before using hot tools
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired texture
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Your Round Face Hairstyle

Follow these tips to keep your round face hairstyle looking great:

  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Use a heat protectant product before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair
  • Visit a stylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking its best
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find the look that works best for you
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, and other accessories to add interest and variety to your style

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the fullness of the cheeks, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. Also avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as straight, one-length cuts.

Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, short hair can be a great option for round faces, particularly when paired with textured layers or side-swept bangs.

What is the best way to add volume to my hair?

Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add lift and body to your hair.

Hair Care for Round Faces

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a heat protectant product before using hot tools
  • Visit a stylist regularly to trim and maintain your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and hairstyles in mind, you can find the perfect look to flatter your round face in 2023.
