How to Take the Quiz

To take the "what haircut would suit me quiz," simply click on the start button and answer the questions as honestly as possible. Make sure to select the answer that best reflects your natural hair texture and face shape, rather than what you wish was true. Tips for Taking the Quiz

Here are some tips to help you get the most accurate results from the "what haircut would suit me quiz":

  • Answer the questions honestly and to the best of your knowledge.
  • Consider your natural hair texture and face shape when selecting your answers.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a friend or family member for their opinion on your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your new haircut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
