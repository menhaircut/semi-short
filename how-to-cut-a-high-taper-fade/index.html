How To Cut A High Taper Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, a high taper fade might be just what you need. This haircut involves cutting the hair gradually shorter towards the neck, creating a fade effect. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a high taper fade in easy-to-understand steps.

How to Cut a High Taper Fade

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, a pair of scissors, and a cape to protect your client's clothing.

Step 2: Start with the Top

Begin by cutting the top of the hair to the desired length using scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 3: Choose the Guard Size

Next, you'll need to choose a guard size for the clippers. The size of the guard will determine how short the hair will be cut. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller guard size as you move towards the neckline.

Step 4: Start the Fade

Using the clippers with the chosen guard size, start cutting the hair at the sides and back of the head. Cut the hair in an upward motion towards the top of the head.

Step 5: Gradually Decrease Guard Size

Gradually decrease the guard size as you move towards the neckline. This will create a fade effect. Make sure to blend the hair evenly to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 6: Define the Hairline

Once you've finished fading the hair, it's time to define the hairline. Use the clippers or scissors to trim the hairline around the ears and the neckline.

Step 7: Clean Up the Hair

Use the clippers to clean up any stray hairs and make sure the haircut is even.

Step 8: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair to the desired look. Use hair products such as pomade or gel to add texture and hold.

Tips

- Make sure to use the right guard size to avoid cutting the hair too short.

- Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair takes practice and skill.

- Use a mirror to check the haircut from different angles.

FAQ

How long does a high taper fade last?

A high taper fade typically lasts 2-4 weeks before it needs to be touched up.

Can you cut a high taper fade on curly hair?

Yes, a high taper fade can be done on curly hair. Make sure to cut the hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises when it dries.

Hair Care

To maintain the high taper fade, it's important to keep the hair clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on the hair and use products that protect the hair from damage.

Cutting a high taper fade is a great way to update your look and stay stylish. With these easy-to-follow steps, you'll be able to achieve the perfect high taper fade every time.
