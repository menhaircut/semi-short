How To Cut Balding Men s Hair With Clippers And Scissors

Written by William Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to cut your own or someone else's hair at home? If you or someone you know is balding, it can be a bit tricky to figure out the best approach to cutting hair. In this article, we will discuss how to cut balding men's hair with clippers and scissors, step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Balding Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Balding Men

How to Cut Balding Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

Before we get started, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you don't have any of these tools, you can purchase them at your local beauty supply store or online.

When cutting hair, it's important to have a plan. Take some time to decide how short you want the hair to be and what style you want to achieve. Once you have a plan in mind, it's time to get started.

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, so make sure the hair is completely dry.

Step 2: Use the clippers to create a fade. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion.

Step 3: Use the scissors to blend the hair. Once you've created a fade with the clippers, use the scissors to blend the hair even more. Start at the top of the fade and work your way down, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to cut the hair in a downward motion.

Step 4: Cut the top of the hair. Use the scissors to cut the top of the hair to the desired length. Cut small sections of hair at a time and take breaks to check the length and make sure it's even.

Step 5: Trim the sides and back. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs on the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to cut the hair in a downward motion.

Tips to Keep in Mind:

  • Take breaks often to check the length and make sure it's even.
  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and make it easier to cut.
  • When using the scissors, hold them at an angle to create a more natural look.

FAQs:

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It's recommended to cut hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers and scissors. Just make sure to take your time and follow the steps carefully.

Hair Care for Balding Men:

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're balding. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Consider using a hair growth product to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and steps, you can cut balding men's hair with clippers and scissors at home. Remember to take your time and have a plan in mind before you start cutting. Happy cutting!
