How To Style Medium Length Hair 2021 Tips And Tricks

Medium length hair can be versatile and trendy with the right styling techniques. Whether you have a lob, shoulder-length, or collarbone-length hair, there are plenty of ways to style it for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll cover how to style medium length hair 2021, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

How to Style Medium Length Hair 2021

First things first, decide on the look you want to achieve. Do you want to add volume, create waves, or straighten your hair? Once you know what you're going for, you can start prepping your hair for styling.

Before styling, it's essential to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools. Once your hair is prepped, you can start styling it using your preferred method.

1. Straightening

If you want a sleek and polished look, straightening your hair is the way to go. Using a flat iron, you can achieve straight, shiny hair in just a few minutes.

Start by sectioning your hair and working on one section at a time. Apply a heat protectant spray and run the flat iron through your hair from root to tip. Make sure to keep the iron moving to avoid burning your hair.

2. Adding Waves

If you want to add some texture and volume to your hair, adding waves is an excellent option. You can achieve this look using a curling iron or a hair straightener.

Start by sectioning your hair and working on one section at a time. Apply a heat protectant spray and wrap a small section of hair around the curling iron or hair straightener. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until all sections of your hair are styled.

3. Creating Volume

If you have flat or fine hair, creating volume can be a game-changer. You can add volume using a blow dryer and a round brush.

Start by sectioning your hair and working on one section at a time. Blow-dry the roots of your hair using a round brush to lift and create volume. Repeat until all sections of your hair are styled.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your medium length hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair and start styling using your preferred method (straightening, adding waves, or creating volume).
  4. Once you've finished styling, apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and tame any frizz or flyaways.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling your medium length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Section your hair to make styling easier and more manageable.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and tame any frizz or flyaways.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my medium length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Is it okay to use heat styling tools on my hair?

A: Yes, but it's essential to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. It's also a good idea to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to avoid excessive damage.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair looking its best:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or braids that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your medium length hair 2021 with confidence. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a more textured and voluminous style, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant spray, hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling.
