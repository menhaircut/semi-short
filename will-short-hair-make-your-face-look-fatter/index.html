Description

Many people have the misconception that short hair can make the face look fatter. However, this is not necessarily true. The way a hairstyle can affect the appearance of the face depends on various factors such as face shape, hair texture, and styling techniques. In this blog post, we will discuss whether short hair can make your face look fatter and provide some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

How to

To determine whether short hair will make your face look fatter, you need to consider your face shape. Certain hairstyles can accentuate or minimize certain facial features. For example, a short bob can make a round face look wider, while a pixie cut can elongate a round face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Research hairstyles that are suitable for your face shape.

3. Consider your hair texture and how it will work with the hairstyle.

4. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

- If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown.

- If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as layered bobs or wavy pixie cuts.

- If you have a heart-shaped face, consider a short bob or a pixie cut with bangs.

- If you have an oval face, lucky you! Most short hairstyles will suit you.

FAQ

Q: Will a short pixie cut make my face look fatter?
A: No, a pixie cut can actually elongate a round face.

Q: Can I still have a short hairstyle if I have a double chin?
A: Yes, but you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can accentuate the chin area.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

- Consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

- Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your hair in shape.

In conclusion, short hair can make your face look fatter depending on your face shape and the hairstyle you choose. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and styling techniques, you can choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to also take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
