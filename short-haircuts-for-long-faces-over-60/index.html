Short Haircuts For Long Faces Over 60

Table of Content:Description, How to, Step by step, Tips, FAQ, Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair often becomes thinner and more delicate. For women over 60 with long faces, finding the perfect short haircut can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of options that can work well for this face shape. Short haircuts can help to balance out a long face and highlight your best features. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircuts for long faces over 60.

How to

When selecting a short haircut, it is important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. If you have a long face, choose a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of your face. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option. If you have thin hair, consider adding layers or texture to create the illusion of more volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can work well.

Step by step

1. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with mature women.

2. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when selecting a short haircut.

3. Choose a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of your face.

4. If you have thin hair, consider adding layers or texture to create the illusion of more volume.

5. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can work well.

Tips

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create lift at the roots.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and round brush to help create volume and shape.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! A new haircut can be a great way to refresh your look.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a long face?

A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for a long face. These haircuts help to add width and volume to the sides of the face, balancing out the length.

What hair texture works best with short haircuts for long faces over 60?

Thin hair can benefit from layers or texture to create the illusion of more volume. Thick hair can work well with a blunt cut.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Be sure to schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist for trims and touch-ups. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and shine. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can cause damage to your hair. Finally, be sure to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
