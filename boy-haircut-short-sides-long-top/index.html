Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Trending Hairstyle Of 2023

Are you looking for a stylish haircut for your little boy? Well, boy haircut short sides long top is the trending hairstyle that you should consider. This popular hairstyle is loved by many boys and their parents due to its versatility and edgy look. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve this look and give you tips on how to take care of your boy's hair.

Description

Boy haircut short sides long top is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut short while the top is left longer. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in different ways such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part. The hairstyle is suitable for boys of all ages and can be customized to suit their hair type and face shape.

How to Achieve the Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

To achieve the boy haircut short sides long top, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. The stylist will help you choose the right haircut for your boy's hair type and face shape. The stylist will also help you determine the length of the top and the sides. The haircut can be achieved using different techniques such as clippers, scissors, or a combination of both.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve the boy haircut short sides long top:

  1. Wash your boy's hair and dry it with a towel
  2. Section the hair by parting it in the middle from the forehead to the nape of the neck
  3. Clip the top section of the hair and leave it to the desired length
  4. Using a clipper, cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length
  5. Blend the top and the sides using scissors or clippers
  6. Style the hair to your desired look

Tips to Maintain the Look

To maintain the boy haircut short sides long top, you will need to follow these tips:

  • Visit a professional hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to wash your boy's hair
  • Apply a styling product such as gel, wax, or pomade to style the hair
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to keep it neat and tidy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the boy haircut short sides long top:

Q: Is the boy haircut short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types.

Q: Can I style the hair in different ways?

A: Yes, the hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in different ways.

Q: How often should I visit a hairstylist?

A: You should visit a hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your boy's hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to wash the hair
  • Avoid using hot water to wash the hair as it can damage the hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair when styling
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips, you can achieve the boy haircut short sides long top and keep your boy's hair looking stylish and healthy.
