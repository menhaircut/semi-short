Best Haircuts For Round Faces Over 40

Written by Luffy Sep 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. As you age, your hair and face may change, which means you need to find a haircut that flatters your features. In this article, we will discuss the top 10 haircuts for round faces over 40, as well as provide tips on how to choose the right style, how to care for your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut, there are several things to consider, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For round faces, it is important to choose a style that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some tips:

1. Length

Longer hair can help to elongate the face, while short hair can make it appear more round. If you prefer short hair, consider a pixie cut with longer layers on top to add height.

2. Layers

Layered hair can help to add volume and movement, which can be flattering for round faces. However, be careful not to add too much volume around the cheeks, as this can make the face appear wider.

3. Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline, while straight across bangs can make the face appear more round. Consider a long, side-swept fringe that blends into the rest of your hair.

4. Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create the illusion of a more angular face. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair, or using a texturizing spray to add volume and movement.

Top 10 Haircuts for Round Faces over 40

Here are our top 10 haircuts for round faces over 40:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Consider adding subtle highlights to add dimension to your hair.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob with layers can be a flattering option for round faces, as it creates the illusion of length. Consider a side part and soft waves to add volume and movement.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with longer layers on top can be a great option for those who prefer short hair. Consider adding texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

4. Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob with longer layers on one side can help to create the illusion of a more angular face. Consider adding volume to the crown of your head to elongate the face.

5. Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) with a side part can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Consider adding subtle highlights to add dimension to your hair.

6. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Consider a long, side-swept fringe that blends into the rest of your hair.

7. Shaggy Layers

Shaggy layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can be flattering for round faces. Consider adding texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

8. Blunt Bob

A blunt bob with no layers can be a great option for those with fine hair, as it can help to create the illusion of thickness. Consider adding subtle highlights to add dimension to your hair.

9. Curly Bob

A curly bob can be a flattering option for round faces, as it adds volume and movement. Consider using a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

10. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can be a great option for those who prefer short hair. Consider adding texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free from sulfates and harsh chemicals, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Get regular trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces over 40:

1. Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still wear short hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a style with longer layers on top to add height.

2. Is it better to have straight or curly hair if I have a round face?

Either straight or curly hair can be flattering for round faces. If you have curly hair, consider a curly bob to add volume and movement. If you have straight hair, consider adding texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

3. Should I get bangs if I have a round face?

Side-swept bangs can be flattering for round faces, as they help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Consider a long, side-swept fringe that blends into the rest of your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for a round face can be challenging, but with the right style and care, you can flatter your features and look your best. Consider the top 10 haircuts for round faces over 40, and remember to choose a style that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. With the right care, your hair will look healthy and beautiful at any age.
