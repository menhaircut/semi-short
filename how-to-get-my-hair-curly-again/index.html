How To Get My Hair Curly Again A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 04, 2023 · 4 min read
Are you tired of your straight hair and longing for your curls back? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people who have chemically relaxed their hair, or have naturally straight hair, crave curls. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get your hair curly again.

Description

This blog post will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get your hair curly again. We will go through the different methods and tips on how to achieve those luscious curls you've been dreaming of.

How to

There are several ways to get your hair curly again. Below are some of the methods you can try:

Method 1: Use Rollers or Curling Irons

Rollers and curling irons are great tools to get those curls back. First, start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's damp. Then, section your hair and start using the rollers or curling irons to curl your hair. Once you're done, use a hairspray to set the curls in place.

Method 2: Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another way to get curly hair. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's damp. Then, divide your hair into sections and braid each section. Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight. Once you take out the braids, you'll have soft curls.

Method 3: Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool to use if you have naturally curly hair. First, start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's damp. Then, attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and start drying your hair on low heat. Once your hair is dry, use a serum or oil to define your curls.

Step-by-Step

Now that you know the different methods, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to get your hair curly again.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry until it's damp. Do not use a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz.

Step 3: Choose Your Method

Decide which method you want to use to get your hair curly again. You can choose from using rollers, braiding your hair, or using a diffuser.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to curl or braid.

Step 5: Curl or Braid Your Hair

Start curling your hair with rollers or a curling iron, or braid your hair. Leave the curls or braids in for a few hours or overnight.

Step 6: Take Out the Curls or Braids

Once you've left the curls or braids in, take them out gently. Do not use a brush or comb as it can cause frizz.

Step 7: Use a Serum or Oil

Use a serum or oil to define your curls or braids.

Step 8: Set Your Curls

Use a hairspray to set your curls in place.

Tips

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. - Don't use a towel to dry your hair, use a cotton t-shirt instead. - Use a diffuser on low heat to avoid damaging your hair. - Use a serum or oil to define your curls. - Use a hairspray to set your curls in place.

FAQ

How long will my curls last?

It depends on the method you use and how you take care of your curls. If you use a hairspray to set your curls, they can last up to a few days.

Can I get my curls back permanently?

Yes, you can get your curls back permanently by getting a perm. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to consult with a professional before getting one.

Hair Care

To keep your curls healthy and hydrated, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a diffuser on low heat if you need to use a hair dryer. Use a serum or oil to define your curls and a hairspray to set them in place.
In conclusion, there are several ways to get your hair curly again. Whether you use rollers, braid your hair, or use a diffuser, make sure to take care of your curls to keep them healthy and hydrated. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to get those luscious curls you've been dreaming of.
