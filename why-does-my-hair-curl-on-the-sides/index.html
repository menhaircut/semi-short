Why Does My Hair Curl On The Sides

Written by Bardi Sep 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're someone who has noticed that your hair tends to curl on the sides, you're not alone. This is a common issue that many people face, and it can be frustrating to deal with on a daily basis. In this blog post, we'll discuss why your hair might be curling on the sides and what you can do to manage it.

Description

Curly hair is generally caused by genetics, but there are other factors that can contribute to curls, such as humidity, heat, and products. When it comes to hair that curls on the sides, the cause is often related to the way that you sleep or the way that you style your hair.

How to

If you're looking to manage your curly hair, there are a few things that you can try. One option is to use a straightening iron to smooth out the curls on the sides of your head. Another option is to use a styling product that will help to tame the curls and keep them in place.

Step by Step

Here are some steps that you can take to manage your curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp.
  3. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair, using a diffuser attachment to help tame the curls.
  4. If you're using a straightening iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  5. Gently run the straightening iron over the curls on the sides of your head, working in small sections.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's designed for curly hair, as this can help to manage the curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair that curls on the sides:

Q: Why does my hair curl on the sides?

A: There are a few reasons that your hair might be curling on the sides, including genetics, the way that you sleep, and the way that you style your hair.

Q: How can I manage my curly hair?

A: You can manage your curly hair by using a straightening iron, using styling products, and getting a haircut that's designed for curly hair.

Q: Is it possible to permanently straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, there are treatments available that can permanently straighten curly hair, but these treatments can be expensive and can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care

When it comes to managing curly hair, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using styling products that are designed for curly hair, and avoiding using too much heat on your hair. You should also consider getting regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

Conclusion

If you're someone who struggles with hair that curls on the sides, there are a few things that you can do to manage it. By using styling products, straightening irons, and getting regular haircuts, you can keep your hair looking its best. Just remember to take good care of your hair and avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals that can damage it.
