Hairstyles For Big Forehead And Round Face Male A Guide

Are you a male with a big forehead and a round face? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further as we have curated a list of hairstyles that will suit your face shape and accentuate your best features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Men with big foreheads and round faces may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements their features. The key is to opt for a hairstyle that balances the roundness of the face and offsets the size of the forehead. The right haircut can make a significant difference in how a person looks and feels. With the right hairstyle, you can create an illusion of sharpness and angles, making your face appear longer and less round.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for a big forehead and round face male is to determine the shape of the face. A round face has a circular shape with a width that is almost equal to the length. The forehead and jaw are typically rounded, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that creates an illusion of length and angles.

Step by step

The following are some hairstyles that big forehead and round face male can try:

1. Side-swept Bangs

This hairstyle works well for a round face and big forehead because it creates an illusion of length. The side-swept bangs should be long enough to cover the forehead and taper towards the sides. This style also adds volume to the hair, making the face appear less round.

2. Short Quiff

A short quiff is an excellent choice for a round face and big forehead. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and a longer top that is styled upwards. The height of the quiff creates an illusion of length, making the face appear less round.

3. Fauxhawk

The fauxhawk is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that works well for a round face with a big forehead. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and a longer top that is styled upwards. The height of the fauxhawk creates an illusion of length and angles, making the face appear less round.

4. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a versatile hairstyle that works well for a round face with a big forehead. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and a textured top that is styled upwards. The textured top adds volume to the hair and creates an illusion of length and angles.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a big forehead and round face male:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as a bowl cut or a buzz cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  • Choose a hairstyle that creates an illusion of length and angles.
  • Consider adding volume to the hair to balance the roundness of the face.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a big forehead and round face male?

The best haircut for a big forehead and round face male is one that creates an illusion of length and angles. Hairstyles that add volume to the hair and taper towards the sides work well for this face shape.

Can a big forehead be hidden with a hairstyle?

A big forehead can be hidden with a hairstyle that features bangs or a fringe. Bangs that are side-swept or tapered towards the sides work well to cover a big forehead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair and a flattering hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as it can cause breakage and damage.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, big forehead and round face male can look their best and feel confident. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
