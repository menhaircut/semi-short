How To Fix Super Dry Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have super dry and frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be. No matter what you do, your hair seems to have a life of its own, and it can be difficult to style or manage. But don't worry – there are solutions to this problem. In this article, we'll go over how to fix super dry frizzy hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Super Dry Frizzy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Dryness and Frizz
  • FAQ

How to Fix Super Dry Frizzy Hair

The first step to fixing super dry frizzy hair is to understand what causes it. Dryness and frizz are usually caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. This can be due to a variety of factors, such as over-washing, using hot tools too often, and exposure to harsh weather conditions.

The good news is that there are ways to combat dryness and frizz. By following the tips in this article, you can restore moisture to your hair and reduce frizz, leaving you with healthy, manageable hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils, such as coconut or argan oil, as these will help to nourish and hydrate your hair. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly after washing to remove any residue.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Once or twice a week, apply a hair mask to your hair. Look for a mask that is specifically designed for dry and frizzy hair. Leave the mask on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be very damaging to your hair, especially if it's already dry and frizzy. Try to avoid using hot tools as much as possible. If you must use a blow dryer or straightener, use a heat protectant spray beforehand and keep the heat setting on low.

Step 4: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. This will also help to reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable. Look for a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down.

Step 5: Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it even more dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use dry shampoo in between washes to help absorb excess oil.

Tips to Prevent Dryness and Frizz

Here are a few additional tips to help prevent dryness and frizz:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will be gentler than a brush
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair than cotton

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair no more than every other day to prevent dryness and frizz.

What should I do if my hair is still dry and frizzy after following these steps?

If your hair is still dry and frizzy after following these steps, you may need to see a hair stylist for a professional treatment.

Hair Care Products for Dry and Frizzy Hair

If you're looking for hair care products to help with dryness and frizz, here are a few recommendations:

  • Coconut Oil Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Argan Oil Hair Mask
  • Leave-In Conditioner Spray
  • Heat Protectant Spray

Remember, everyone's hair is different, so you may need to experiment with different products to find what works best for you. With a little patience and some TLC, you can fix super dry frizzy hair and have healthy, beautiful locks.
