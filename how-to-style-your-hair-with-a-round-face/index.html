Description

Having a round face shape can be challenging when it comes to finding the right hairstyle that suits you. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your best features and create a more balanced look. In this blog post, we will guide you through how to style your hair with a round face shape and offer tips and advice to help you achieve your desired look.

How to Style Your Hair with a Round Face

When it comes to styling your hair with a round face, the key is to create angles and volume to elongate your face. Here are some popular hairstyles that flatter a round face shape:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add movement and dimension to your hair, which helps to create a more elongated look. Avoid blunt cuts and opt for layers that start from the cheekbones and continue down to the ends of your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create an illusion of length and balance out your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear wider.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume can help to create angles and elongate your face shape. Opt for a layered bob with volume at the crown of your head to create more height and length.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for those with a round face shape. Choose a style that has volume at the crown of your head and avoid styles that are too short or flat.

Step by Step

Here are some steps on how to style your hair with a round face:

1. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair.

2. Add Texture

Add texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help to create volume and make your hair easier to style.

3. Create Waves

Create waves with a curling iron or hot rollers to add more dimension and length to your hair. Curl your hair away from your face to create a more open and elongated look.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair with a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or flat as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face shape.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to create a more balanced and elongated look.
  • Avoid blunt cuts and opt for layers that start from the cheekbones and continue down to the ends of your hair.
  • Use texturizing products to add volume and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style your hair with a round face:

Q: Can I still wear a bob with a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear a bob with a round face. Opt for a layered bob with volume at the crown of your head to create more height and length.

Q: Can I wear blunt bangs with a round face?

A: It's best to avoid blunt bangs with a round face as they can make your face appear wider. Side-swept bangs are a better option as they can help to create a more balanced and elongated look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair with a round face:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a hairstyle that flatters your round face shape and enhances your best features. Experiment with different cuts and styles to find the one that works best for you.
