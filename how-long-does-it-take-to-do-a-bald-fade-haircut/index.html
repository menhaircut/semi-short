How Long Does It Take To Do A Bald Fade Haircut

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're considering getting a bald fade haircut, you may be wondering how long it takes to achieve this stylish and trendy look. In this blog post, we'll answer this question and provide you with some helpful tips and information to ensure that your bald fade haircut looks great and lasts as long as possible.

Description

A bald fade haircut is a popular hairstyle that involves trimming the hair on the sides of the head very short and gradually blending it into longer hair on the top of the head. This creates a fade effect that is subtle and stylish. The hair on the top of the head can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked-back, messy, or textured.

How to

If you're interested in getting a bald fade haircut, it's important to find a skilled and experienced hairstylist who can achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a bald fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a bald fade haircut is to choose the right length for your hair. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your head. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right length based on your preferences and the look you're trying to achieve.

Step 2: Trim the Hair on the Sides

The next step is to trim the hair on the sides of your head very short. This is usually done using clippers with a guard attachment. Your hairstylist will gradually reduce the length of the hair as they move up the sides of your head.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Once the hair on the sides of your head has been trimmed, your hairstylist will use scissors or clippers to blend the hair into the longer hair on the top of your head. This creates the fade effect that is characteristic of a bald fade haircut.

Step 4: Style the Hair on Top

Finally, your hairstylist will style the hair on top of your head to create the look you want. This can involve using products like pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to ensure that your bald fade haircut looks great and lasts as long as possible:

  • Visit a skilled and experienced hairstylist
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims
  • Use high-quality hair products to style your hair
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about bald fade haircuts:

How long does it take to do a bald fade haircut?

The amount of time it takes to do a bald fade haircut will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the skill and experience of your hairstylist. On average, a bald fade haircut can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

How often do I need to get a bald fade haircut?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style changes. In general, it's a good idea to get a bald fade haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care

To keep your bald fade haircut looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes using high-quality hair products, protecting your hair from heat and sun damage, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut. With proper care, your bald fade haircut can look great for weeks.
