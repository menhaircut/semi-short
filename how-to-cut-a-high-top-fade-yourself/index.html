How to Cut a High Top Fade Yourself: Step-by-Step Guide

If you're looking to achieve a trendy and stylish look, a high top fade is the perfect haircut for you. A high top fade is a cut where the hair on the sides is faded while the hair on the top is left long and styled. This haircut has been popularized by celebrities and athletes alike.

How to Cut a High Top Fade Yourself

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a high top fade at home. Here are some things you'll need:

  • Clippers with various guards
  • Shears
  • Comb
  • Hair products (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with a Clean and Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting to cut it. This will make it easier to see the length and texture of your hair, and it will also prevent your clippers from getting clogged with hair.

Step 2: Determine Your Desired Length

Decide on the length you want for the hair on top of your head. Use the longest guard on your clippers to cut the hair to that length.

Step 3: Create a Fade

Use a shorter guard on your clippers to create a fade on the sides and back of your head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest, blending the hair from the top to the sides.

Step 4: Blend the Top with the Sides

Use the shears to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides. You can also use the clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the perimeter of your hairline.

Step 5: Style as Desired

Apply hair products as desired to style your high top fade. You can use a pomade or gel to create a sleek and polished look, or you can use a mousse or leave-in conditioner for a more natural and textured look.

Tips for Cutting a High Top Fade

  • Always start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create a gradual fade.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure your fade is even.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get better.

FAQs

Can I cut a high top fade without clippers?

Technically, you can cut a high top fade without clippers, but it will be much more difficult and time-consuming. Clippers make it easier to create a fade and blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top.

What length should I cut the hair on top?

The length of the hair on top depends on your personal style and preference. Generally, the hair on top should be at least 3-4 inches long to achieve a high top fade.

Hair Care for High Top Fade

Maintaining a high top fade requires regular trims and styling. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type to keep it healthy and strong. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

When styling your high top fade, use products that are appropriate for your hair type and desired look. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a high top fade at home and maintain it with ease. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll have a stylish and trendy haircut in no time!
