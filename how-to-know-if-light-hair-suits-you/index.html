How To Know If Light Hair Suits You

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair color to a lighter shade but not sure if it will suit you? Light hair can be a refreshing change, but it’s important to make sure it complements your skin tone and features. In this article, we’ll share with you how to know if light hair suits you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Think About Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 4: Try a Temporary Hair Color
  • Tips for Maintaining Light Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Light Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have warm skin tones, such as yellow or golden undertones, light hair colors with warm tones like honey blonde or caramel may suit you best. On the other hand, if you have cool skin tones, such as pink or blue undertones, ash blonde or platinum may complement your complexion better.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also have an impact on how well light hair suits you. If you have blue or green eyes, a cooler blonde shade may make your eyes stand out more. If you have brown or hazel eyes, a warmer blonde tone may be more flattering.

Step 3: Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you determine if light hair would suit you. If you have naturally light hair, going a few shades lighter may enhance your features. However, if you have dark hair, a drastic change to light hair may not be the best option. Instead, consider gradually lightening your hair with highlights or balayage.

Step 4: Try a Temporary Hair Color

If you’re still unsure if light hair suits you, try a temporary hair color before committing to a permanent change. This will allow you to see how the color looks on your skin tone and how it makes you feel.

Tips for Maintaining Light Hair

Once you’ve decided to go for a lighter hair color, here are some tips to help maintain your new look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQs

Q: Is it okay to dye my hair at home?

A: While it’s possible to dye your hair at home, it’s recommended to visit a professional for the best results. They can help you choose the right shade and ensure that the color is applied evenly.

Q: Will light hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Light hair can actually make you look younger and more refreshed, as long as the color complements your skin tone and features.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on the rate at which your hair grows and the contrast between your natural hair color and the light shade. It’s recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Light Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your light hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a purple shampoo to prevent brassy tones from developing in your hair.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated pools, as this can cause your hair to turn green.

Now that you know how to determine if light hair suits you, you can confidently make a change to your hair color. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
