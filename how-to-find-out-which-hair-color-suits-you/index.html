How To Find Out Which Hair Color Suits You

Written by Christine Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair color but not sure what will suit you best? With so many options available in the market, finding the perfect hair color can be overwhelming. But don't worry, we have got you covered! In this article, we will guide you on how to find out which hair color suits you best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before you decide on a hair color, it is essential to know your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. These factors play a crucial role in determining the perfect hair color for you. Here are some steps you can follow to find out which hair color will suit you best:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining the hair color that will suit you best. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green or olive, you have a warm undertone. If you are unsure, you may have a neutral undertone, which means you can pull off both cool and warm hair colors.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine the perfect hair color for you. Cool-toned hair colors such as platinum blonde or ash brown look great on people with blue, green, or grey eyes. Warm-toned hair colors such as golden blonde or auburn look great on people with brown or hazel eyes.

3. Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you decide which hair color will suit you best. If you have dark hair, going too light may not look great on you. It is best to stick to shades that are no more than two shades lighter than your natural hair color.

Step by Step

Now that you know the factors that play a crucial role in determining the perfect hair color for you let's take a look at the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist to determine if you have a cool, warm, or neutral undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Choose a hair color that complements your eye color.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Choose a hair color that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.

Step 4: Try On Wigs or Hair Extensions

If you are unsure about a hair color, try on wigs or hair extensions that are similar to the color you are considering.

Step 5: Consult a Professional

If you are still unsure about which hair color will suit you best, consult a professional hair stylist. They will be able to recommend the perfect hair color based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Tips

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.
  • Try on wigs or hair extensions before committing to a hair color.
  • Consult a professional hair stylist if you are unsure about which hair color will suit you best.

FAQ

What hair color looks best on cool-toned skin?

Cool-toned skin looks great with cool-toned hair colors such as platinum blonde or ash brown.

What hair color looks best on warm-toned skin?

Warm-toned skin looks great with warm-toned hair colors such as golden blonde or auburn.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain its shine and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to find out which hair color suits you best, go ahead and experiment with different colors to find the perfect one for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine.
