How To Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's easy to style and can look incredibly chic. On the other hand, it can be difficult to tame and maintain. Whether you're looking to chop off your curls for a new look or simply want to keep them short and sweet, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of shortening your curly locks, from how to cut them to how to care for them afterward.

Description

Short curly hair is a trendy and chic style that can be worn by anyone. Whether you have naturally curly hair or you've curled your hair with a curling iron, this style is perfect for those who want to switch up their look. Short curly hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from a pixie cut to a bob, and it's a great way to show off your natural curls.

How to Short Curly Hair

Step 1: Choose Your Cut

The first step in shortening your curly hair is to choose your cut. There are a variety of styles to choose from, including a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob. Once you've decided on your cut, it's important to find a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you cut your hair, it's important to wash and condition it. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair to ensure that your curls stay healthy and bouncy.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, it's time to dry it. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while keeping your curls intact. This will help to maintain the shape of your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the front, the back, and the sides. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 5: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the back section, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way through each section, cutting your hair evenly as you go.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose curls or waves. Alternatively, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair for a sleek and chic look.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls before cutting them.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as this can make it difficult to style.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

How often should I cut my short curly hair?

You should aim to get your curly hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I dye my short curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your short curly hair. However, it's important to use a dye that's specifically designed for curly hair to prevent damage and maintain the health of your curls.

Hair Care

After you've cut your curly hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and bouncy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. In addition, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls and a leave-in conditioner to keep them hydrated and healthy.
