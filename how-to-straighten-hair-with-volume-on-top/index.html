How To Straighten Hair With Volume On Top

Written by Luffy Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may find yourself wishing for straight, sleek locks. However, if you also want to keep volume on top of your head, you might feel like it's impossible to achieve both looks at the same time. But fear not! With the right tools and techniques, you can straighten your hair while keeping volume on top. In this article, we'll show you how.

Description

Straightening hair with volume on top can be tricky, but it's achievable with the right tools and techniques. This article will guide you through the steps to achieve a straight, sleek look while still maintaining volume on top of your head.

How to Straighten Hair with Volume on Top: Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving a straight, voluminous hairstyle is to start with clean hair. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would, making sure to focus on the roots and scalp to remove any buildup that could weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are particularly prone to breakage.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

To achieve volume on top of your head, you'll want to blow dry your hair upside down. This will help lift the roots and create volume. Use a round brush to help smooth out any frizz or kinks as you blow dry.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, section it into manageable pieces. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way, and start straightening the bottom section with a flat iron.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small, manageable sections. Use a comb to help guide the hair through the flat iron, and be sure to clamp down firmly to ensure a smooth, straight finish.

Step 6: Release the Top Section and Repeat

Once you've straightened the bottom section of your hair, release the top section and repeat the process. Be sure to focus on the roots to achieve volume on top.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

To help your style hold throughout the day, finish with a light misting of hairspray. Choose a hairspray that's lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Tips for Straightening Hair with Volume on Top

  • Use a flat iron with adjustable heat settings to avoid damaging your hair with too much heat.
  • Be patient and work in small sections to ensure a smooth, straight finish.
  • Add volume to your roots by teasing or backcombing before straightening.
  • Use a high-quality heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Choose a hairspray that's lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair without a flat iron?

While a flat iron is the most effective tool for straightening hair, you can also achieve a similar look with a blow dryer and round brush. Blow dry your hair upside down, using the round brush to smooth out any kinks or frizz.

Will straightening my hair damage it?

Straightening your hair can cause damage if you don't take proper precautions. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream to protect your hair from heat damage, and avoid using a flat iron on wet or damp hair.

How often should I straighten my hair?

Straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week, and use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Straightened Hair with Volume on Top

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week. Avoid using hot tools on wet or damp hair, and use a heat protectant spray or cream every time you straighten your hair. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Straightening your hair with volume on top is a great way to achieve a sleek, sophisticated look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect balance between straight, smooth hair and voluminous roots. Follow these tips and tricks to get the look you want while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
