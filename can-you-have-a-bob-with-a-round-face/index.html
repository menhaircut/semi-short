Can You Have A Bob With A Round Face

Written by Robby Nov 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone with a round face and wondering if you can pull off a bob haircut? The answer is yes, you absolutely can! The key is finding the right type of bob that will flatter your face shape. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about getting a bob with a round face.

Description

A bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. A bob typically features hair that is cut to chin-length or just above the shoulders. It can be straight, wavy or curly, and can be styled in many different ways.

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to choose a bob that will elongate your face and create the illusion of angles. This means avoiding bobs that are too short or too round, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

How to

When getting a bob with a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Length

The length of your bob is crucial when it comes to flattery. You want to avoid bobs that are too short, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders. This will help to elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.

2. Angles

Creating angles in your bob is key to elongating your face. Ask your stylist to cut your hair so that it is longer in the front and shorter in the back. This will create a diagonal line that will draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of length.

3. Layers

Adding layers to your bob can also help to create angles and add volume. Ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair to create movement and texture. This will help to break up the roundness of your face and create a more flattering shape.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a bob with a round face:

1. Consult with your stylist

Before getting a bob, it's important to consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. Bring in pictures of bobs you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

2. Choose the right length

Opt for a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders. This will help to elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.

3. Create angles

Ask your stylist to cut your hair so that it is longer in the front and shorter in the back. This will create a diagonal line that will draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of length.

4. Add layers

Adding long layers throughout your hair can help to create movement and texture. This will break up the roundness of your face and create a more flattering shape.

5. Style your bob

There are many ways to style a bob, depending on your hair type and personal preference. You can wear it straight, wavy or curly, and can add accessories like headbands or hair clips to make it more interesting.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a bob with a round face:

  • Choose a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders
  • Avoid bobs that are too short or too round
  • Create angles in your bob by cutting it longer in the front and shorter in the back
  • Add layers to create movement and texture
  • Style your bob to suit your hair type and personal preference

FAQ

1. Will a bob make my face look rounder?

If you choose the right type of bob, it can actually elongate your face and create the illusion of angles. Avoid bobs that are too short or too round, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

2. Can I wear a bob if I have thick hair?

A bob can work well for thick hair, as long as it is layered to create movement and texture. Talk to your stylist about the best type of bob for your hair type.

3. How often do I need to get my bob trimmed?

You should get your bob trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your bob
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair

In conclusion, a bob can be a great haircut choice for those with a round face, as long as it is customized to flatter your face shape. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for you, and follow these tips to keep your bob looking its best.
