How To Get Animal Crossing Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Animal Crossing is a popular video game that has captured the hearts of many players around the world. One of the game's best features is the ability to customize your character's appearance, including their hairstyle. In this article, we'll show you how to get Animal Crossing hairstyles so you can create the perfect look for your character.

How to Get Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Getting Animal Crossing hairstyles is a simple process that involves visiting the in-game salon and choosing the hairstyle you want. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Unlock the Salon

The salon becomes available once you've upgraded your house to include the second floor. To upgrade your house, you'll need to pay off your initial loan, which can be done by catching fish, bugs, and selling items to Tom Nook.

Step 2: Visit Harriet the Stylist

Once the salon is unlocked, visit Harriet the Stylist, who will be waiting for you on the second floor. Talk to her, and she'll give you the option to change your hairstyle.

Step 3: Choose Your Hairstyle

Harriet will show you all of the available hairstyles. Choose the one you want, and she'll ask you a few questions to make sure you're happy with your choice. Once you've confirmed your decision, Harriet will give you your new hairstyle.

Tips for Getting Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Animal Crossing hairstyle:

Tip 1: Experiment

Animal Crossing has a wide variety of hairstyles to choose from, so don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect one for your character.

Tip 2: Save Your Favorite Hairstyles

If you find a hairstyle you love, save it so you can switch back to it later if you change your mind.

Tip 3: Customize Your Hair Color

Harriet can also change your hair color, so consider changing it to match your character's personality or outfit.

FAQ

Q: Can I Change My Hairstyle Whenever I Want?

A: Yes, you can visit Harriet at any time to change your hairstyle or hair color.

Q: Are There Any Hairstyles That Are Only Available During Certain Seasons?

A: Yes, some hairstyles are only available during certain seasons or events, so keep an eye out for special offers.

Q: Can I Change My Character's Gender or Skin Color?

A: No, you can't change your character's gender or skin color in Animal Crossing.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

In Animal Crossing, your character's hair will grow over time, so you'll need to visit Harriet regularly to keep it looking its best. You can also use a mirror in your home to check your hairstyle and make sure it's still looking good.

That's all you need to know about getting Animal Crossing hairstyles. With this guide, you'll be able to create the perfect look for your character and stand out in the world of Animal Crossing.
