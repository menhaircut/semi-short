How To Tie Scarf On Short Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles for your short natural hair? Do you want to add some flair to your look? A scarf can be the perfect accessory to spice up your hair game. However, tying a scarf on short natural hair can be a challenge. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to tie a scarf on short natural hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Scarf on Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Tying Scarf on Short Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Natural Hair

How to Tie Scarf on Short Natural Hair

Tying a scarf on short natural hair can be challenging, but it is not impossible. The first thing you need to do is to choose the right type of scarf. You want a scarf that is long enough to tie around your head but not too thick that it will add bulk to your hair. Here are the steps to tie a scarf on short natural hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by brushing your hair and smoothing it down.
  2. Fold the scarf in half to create a triangle.
  3. Place the scarf on your head with the long side of the triangle at the back of your head and the point of the triangle at the front.
  4. Take the two corners of the scarf at the back of your head and tie them together in a knot.
  5. Take the two corners of the scarf at the front of your head and tie them together in a knot on top of the first knot.
  6. Tuck the loose ends of the scarf under the knot to hide them.
  7. Adjust the scarf to your liking and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Tying Scarf on Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips to make tying a scarf on short natural hair easier:

  • Use a satin scarf to prevent breakage and protect your hair.
  • Choose a scarf that complements your outfit and personal style.
  • Experiment with different scarf tying techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place if needed.

FAQs

Q: Can I tie a scarf on short natural hair without damaging it?

A: Yes, you can tie a scarf on short natural hair without damaging it if you use a satin scarf and tie it loosely.

Q: How do I keep my scarf from slipping off?

A: You can use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place or tie the knot tighter.

Hair Care Tips for Short Natural Hair

Tying a scarf on short natural hair is a great way to add style and protect your hair, but it is also important to take care of your hair underneath. Here are some hair care tips for short natural hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and opt for protective styles instead.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide on how to tie a scarf on short natural hair, you can add some pizzazz to your hairstyle and protect your hair at the same time. Happy styling!
