Can You Get A Perm If You Have Thick Hair

Written by William Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you may be wondering if you can get a perm. The answer is yes, you can! However, there are some things you should consider before getting a perm, such as the type of perm you want and the condition of your hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to get a perm if you have thick hair, as well as some tips and FAQs for maintaining your perm.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair. It involves wrapping your hair around perm rods and applying a chemical solution that breaks down the bonds in your hair. Once the bonds are broken, the hair can be reshaped into curls or waves. Perms can last anywhere from a few months to a year, depending on the type of perm and how well you take care of your hair.

How to Get a Perm if You Have Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you'll need to make sure that your hair can handle the perm. Thick hair can be more resistant to chemical treatments, so you'll need to choose the right perm solution for your hair. Here are some steps to follow when getting a perm with thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Perm Solution

There are two types of perm solutions: alkaline and acid. Alkaline solutions are stronger and are better suited for thick hair. Acid solutions are milder and are better suited for fine or damaged hair. Make sure to consult with your stylist to determine which solution is best for your hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting a perm, you'll need to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any buildup or residue from your hair, which can interfere with the perm solution. You should also avoid using any hair products on the day of your perm.

Step 3: Wrap Your Hair

Your stylist will wrap your hair around perm rods, which will determine the size and shape of your curls. Make sure to communicate with your stylist about the type of curls you want, as well as any concerns you have about your hair.

Step 4: Apply the Perm Solution

Once your hair is wrapped around the perm rods, your stylist will apply the perm solution. The solution will break down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into curls. The solution will need to sit on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and the perm solution.

Step 5: Rinse and Style

After the perm solution has been applied, your stylist will rinse your hair with water and apply a neutralizer. The neutralizer will restore the bonds in your hair and set the curls. Once your hair has been neutralized, your stylist will style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Perm

Once you've gotten a perm, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your curls. Here are some tips for maintaining your perm:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause your curls to loosen.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to keep your curls looking their best.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I get a perm if I have colored hair?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have colored hair. However, you should wait at least two weeks after coloring your hair before getting a perm.

Can I get a perm if I have damaged hair?

If you have damaged hair, you may not be a good candidate for a perm. Perms can further damage your hair, so it's important to consult with your stylist to determine if a perm is right for you.

How long will my perm last?

Perms can last anywhere from a few months to a year, depending on the type of perm and how well you take care of your hair.

Hair Care

Perming your hair can be a fun way to change up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your curls. By following the tips in this article and consulting with your stylist, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls that last.
