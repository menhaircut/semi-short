Best Medium Length Haircuts For Round Faces In 2023

Written by Eveline Oct 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the best medium length haircuts for round faces? You have come to the right place! In this article, we will discuss the most popular and trendy haircuts that will complement your round face shape. Keep reading to discover how to choose the perfect haircut, how to style it, and how to take care of your hair.

How to Choose the Perfect Haircut for Round Faces

Choosing the perfect haircut for a round face can be challenging. The key is to create the illusion of length and angles. The best haircuts for round faces are those that elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin area.

When choosing a haircut, consider the length, layers, and texture. A medium length haircut with layers and texture will add dimension and create a slimming effect. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic and timeless haircut that is perfect for round faces. A chin-length bob with layers and texture will create the illusion of length and angles. The layers will add volume and movement, while the texture will create a slimming effect.

Shoulder-Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut with layers is another great option for round faces. The layers will create movement and volume, while the length will elongate the face. You can also add texture to the ends of the hair to create a slimming effect.

Layered Cut

A layered cut with textured ends is perfect for round faces. The layers will add volume and movement, while the texture will create a slimming effect. You can also add side-swept bangs to create angles and draw attention away from the chin area.

Updo Haircut

If you prefer to wear your hair up, an updo haircut with layers and texture is perfect for round faces. The layers will add volume and movement, while the texture will create a slimming effect. You can also add braids or twists to create a more intricate look.

How to Style Your Haircut

Styling your haircut is just as important as choosing the right one. The key is to create volume and movement to elongate the face. Here are some styling tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the roots
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create movement
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture
  • Finish with a texturizing spray to create a slimming effect

Hair Care for Round Faces

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends

FAQs

Can round faces pull off bangs?

Yes, round faces can pull off bangs, but it is important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are the best options for round faces, as they create angles and draw attention away from the chin area.

What is the best hair color for round faces?

The best hair color for round faces is one that creates contrast and dimension. Highlights or balayage can create a slimming effect and add dimension to the hair.

Can I wear my hair straight with a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a round face, but it is important to add volume and movement. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create movement, and finish with a texturizing spray to add volume.

Now that you know the best medium length haircuts for round faces, it's time to book your next appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a haircut that creates length and angles, and style it to add volume and movement. With proper hair care, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair that complements your round face shape.
