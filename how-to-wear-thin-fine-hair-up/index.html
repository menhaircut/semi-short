How To Wear Thin Fine Hair Up Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thin and fine hair, you might think that wearing your hair up is impossible. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of updos that will make your hair look fuller and thicker. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear thin fine hair up in different styles and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the most out of your hair.

How to Wear Thin Fine Hair Up

When it comes to wearing thin fine hair up, the key is to create volume and texture. Here are some ways to wear your hair up:

Braids

Braids are a great way to create the illusion of volume and thickness. You can opt for a classic French braid or a fishtail braid. To make your braid look fuller, gently pull the strands apart after braiding.

Ponytail

A ponytail is a simple and classic updo that works well for thin fine hair. To create a fuller look, tease the crown of your hair and tie your ponytail higher on your head. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the elastic to hide it.

Bun

A bun is another easy and elegant updo for thin fine hair. You can create a messy bun or a sleek bun, depending on the occasion. To add volume, tease your hair before creating the bun and use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Updos for Thin Fine Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to creating a simple updo for thin fine hair:

  1. Tease the crown of your hair to create volume.
  2. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail and secure it with an elastic.
  3. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks for Wearing Thin Fine Hair Up

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your thin fine hair look fuller and thicker when worn up:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and make your hair look thinner.
  • Invest in good quality hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair.
  • Experiment with different updos to find the ones that work best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add texture and volume to your hair.

FAQs about Wearing Thin Fine Hair Up

Q. Can I wear my hair up if it’s thin and fine?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair up if it’s thin and fine. The key is to create volume and texture to make your hair look fuller.

Q. What are the best updos for thin fine hair?

A. Some of the best updos for thin fine hair include braids, ponytails, and buns. These styles create the illusion of volume and thickness.

Q. How do I make my hair look fuller when wearing it up?

A. To make your hair look fuller when wearing it up, you can use volumizing products, tease your hair, and experiment with different updos. You can also consider getting hair extensions.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Fine Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have thin fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear your thin fine hair up in a variety of styles. Experiment with different updos and find the ones that work best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
