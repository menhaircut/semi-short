The best product to make wavy hair curly is a curl defining cream. This type of product is specifically designed to enhance and define your curls without weighing your hair down. Curl defining creams work by providing moisture to your hair, which helps to eliminate frizz and create definition in your curls.

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly

If you have wavy hair and want to make it curly, there are a few different methods you can try. The most popular methods include using a curling iron, using rollers, or using a curl defining cream.

Curling Iron

Using a curling iron is a quick and easy way to create curls in your hair. To use a curling iron, simply section your hair and wrap each section around the iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Rollers

Rollers are another popular method for creating curls in your hair. To use rollers, simply section your hair and roll each section onto a roller, securing with a clip. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight before removing.

Curl Defining Cream

The best way to make wavy hair curly is by using a curl defining cream. Simply apply the cream to your hair, scrunching as you go, and let your hair air dry.

Step by Step Guide

To make wavy hair curly using a curl defining cream, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing excess moisture.
  3. Apply a quarter-sized amount of curl defining cream to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  4. Scrunch your hair as you apply the cream to help define your curls.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your curls and create more definition.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help your curls stay in place.

FAQ

Can I use a curl defining cream on straight hair?

Yes, you can use a curl defining cream on straight hair to create curls. However, the results may not be as defined as they would be on wavy or curly hair.

Can I use a curl defining cream every day?

Yes, you can use a curl defining cream every day. Just be sure not to over-apply the cream, as this can weigh your hair down.

Can I use a curl defining cream on color-treated hair?

Yes, you can use a curl defining cream on color-treated hair. Just be sure to choose a cream that is safe for color-treated hair.
