How To Cut Curly Hair With Scissors And Comb

Written by Bardi Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that cutting it can be a daunting task. You don't want to end up with a bad haircut that leaves you with frizzy, unmanageable hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut curly hair with scissors and comb and achieve beautiful results. In this article, we'll show you how to cut curly hair with scissors and comb in a relaxed English language.

How to Cut Curly Hair with Scissors and Comb

To cut curly hair with scissors and comb, you'll need the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Spray bottle
  • Sectioning clips

Here's how to cut curly hair with scissors and comb:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle the Hair

Start by washing and conditioning the hair. After washing, detangle the hair with a wide-tooth comb to remove any knots and tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using sectioning clips, divide the hair into smaller sections. This will make it easier to cut the hair and ensure that you don't miss any parts.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using your wide-tooth comb, comb through each section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim the ends with your scissors. Work your way through each section, cutting off the desired amount of hair.

Step 4: Check for Balance

After you've finished cutting the hair, check for balance. Make sure that both sides of the hair are even and that there are no uneven sections.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once you've finished cutting and checking for balance, style the hair as desired. You can use a diffuser to dry the hair and enhance the curls.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curly hair with scissors and comb:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Work on small sections of hair to ensure that you don't miss any parts.
  • Trim the hair slowly and gradually to avoid cutting off too much.
  • Check for balance as you go along to avoid uneven sections.
  • Use a diffuser to dry the hair and enhance the curls.

FAQs

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended that you cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut curly hair when it's dry?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's wet because it's easier to see the shape and length of the hair. However, if you need to trim your hair when it's dry, make sure to use sharp scissors and work slowly.

Should I cut my curly hair myself or go to a salon?

If you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's best to go to a salon and have a professional cut your hair. However, if you have experience cutting hair and feel comfortable doing it yourself, you can cut your curly hair at home with scissors and comb.

Hair Care after Cutting Curly Hair

After cutting your curly hair with scissors and comb, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Protect the hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut curly hair with scissors and comb and achieve beautiful results. Remember to take your time, work on small sections of hair, and check for balance as you go along. Happy cutting!
