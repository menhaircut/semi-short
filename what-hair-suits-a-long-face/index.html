What Hair Suits A Long Face

Written by Petter Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on what hair suits a long face. We'll cover everything from the best haircuts to hairstyle options to maintain your long face's balance. Read on to find out more.

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow jawline. Hairstyles that add width to the face and create volume at the sides are ideal for balancing the length of a long face. On the other hand, hairstyles that add length to the face or are too flat and straight can make a long face appear even longer.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your long face is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose your hairstyle. Here are some options:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face can be a great option for long faces. Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts can add texture and volume to the hair, creating a fuller appearance.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts can also work well for long faces. Shoulder-length cuts with layers or waves can add volume and movement to the hair, balancing the length of the face. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make the face appear longer.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can be challenging for long faces, but it's not impossible. Layered cuts with waves or curls can add volume and texture to the hair, creating a balanced look. Avoid straight, flat hair that can make the face look longer.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape using a measuring tape.

2. Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of the face.

3. Avoid straight, flat hairstyles that can make the face appear longer.

4. Experiment with layers, waves, and curls to add texture and movement to the hair.

5. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face.

Tips

1. Consider getting bangs to add width to the face.

2. Avoid center parts that can make the face look longer.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

4. Use styling products to add volume and texture to the hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that add length to the face, such as straight, flat hair or styles with a center part.

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume to the sides of the face, such as a layered cut with waves or curls.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a balanced hairstyle for a long face. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Regular trims can also help keep hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a long face can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following our tips and tricks, you can find a hairstyle that balances your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.
