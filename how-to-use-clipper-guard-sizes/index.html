How To Use Clipper Guard Sizes A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Clipper guard sizes are an essential tool for anyone who wants to cut their hair at home. These guards come in different sizes, and each size corresponds to a specific length of hair that the clipper will cut. In this article, we will discuss how to use clipper guard sizes and provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect haircut.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Use Clipper Guard Sizes
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After the Haircut

How to Use Clipper Guard Sizes

Clipper guard sizes are numbered, and each number corresponds to a specific length of hair that the clipper will cut. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. For example, a #1 guard will cut hair to 1/8 of an inch, while a #8 guard will cut hair to 1 inch.

Before using clipper guard sizes, it is crucial to ensure that your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can cause the clipper blades to rust, and it can also make it difficult to achieve an even haircut.

Choosing the Right Clipper Guard Size

Choosing the right clipper guard size depends on the length of hair you want to achieve. If you want a shorter haircut, choose a guard with a lower number. If you want a longer haircut, choose a guard with a higher number. It is also essential to consider the shape of your head and the style that you want to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to use clipper guard sizes:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Attach the clipper guard to your clipper.
  3. Turn on the clipper and start cutting your hair.
  4. Start at the back of your head and work your way to the front.
  5. Use a mirror to ensure that your haircut is even.
  6. Change the clipper guard size as needed to achieve the desired length.
  7. Trim the hair around your ears and neckline using a smaller clipper guard or without a guard.
  8. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to add texture to your haircut.
  9. Shower to remove any loose hair from your head and body.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair before cutting it with the clipper.
  • Use a smaller clipper guard or no guard at all to trim the hair around your ears and neckline.
  • Use scissors to add texture to your haircut or to trim any stray hairs.
  • Keep the clipper blades oiled to ensure a smooth and even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check if your haircut is even from all angles.

FAQs

1. Can I use clipper guard sizes on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use clipper guard sizes on wet hair, as it can cause the clipper blades to rust and make it difficult to achieve an even haircut.

2. How often should I change my clipper blades?

It is recommended to change your clipper blades every six months to ensure a smooth and even cut.

3. Can I use clipper guard sizes to cut someone else's hair?

Yes, you can use clipper guard sizes to cut someone else's hair. However, make sure that you have the right clipper guard size, and always ask for their preference before cutting their hair.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, it is essential to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hairdryers and straighteners.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Using clipper guard sizes is an easy and convenient way to achieve a perfect haircut at home. By following the steps and tips provided in this guide, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your house. Remember to take care of your hair after the haircut to maintain its health and shine.
