Description

Thinning scissors are a type of hair-cutting tool that has teeth on one blade and a straight edge on the other. The teeth are used to cut small sections of hair, while the straight edge is used to blend the cut sections with the rest of the hair. Thinning scissors are commonly used in hair cutting to remove weight and create texture in the hair.
