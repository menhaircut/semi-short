How To Style Short Layered Pixie Cut A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? A short layered pixie cut might be the answer you’re looking for! Not only is it a trendy hairstyle, but it’s also low maintenance and easy to style. In this article, we’ll show you how to style short layered pixie cut in various ways, and we’ll also give you tips on how to care for your hair. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Layered Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Layered Pixie Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Pixie Cut

How to Style Short Layered Pixie Cut

If you’re new to the short hair world, styling a short layered pixie cut can seem intimidating. But don’t worry, it’s actually quite easy! The key is to experiment with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you. Here are some popular ways to style a short layered pixie cut:

1. Sleek and Straight

If you’re looking for a classic and chic look, you can’t go wrong with a sleek and straight style. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and then use a flat iron to straighten any remaining kinks. Finish off with a shine serum to add some gloss.

2. Messy and Textured

If you prefer a more casual and carefree look, try a messy and textured style. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair, and then use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair. You can also use a pomade or wax to add definition and hold to your style.

3. Pixie Cut with Bangs

If you want to add some dimension to your pixie cut, consider adding bangs. There are many different types of bangs to choose from, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. Talk to your stylist to find out which type of bangs will best suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some popular ways to style a short layered pixie cut, let’s walk through the process step-by-step:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry. Don’t rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can damage your hair and create frizz.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as a mousse, gel, or texturizing spray. This will help to add volume and hold to your style.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to lift your hair at the roots to create volume.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a flat iron, curling iron, or texturizing spray to create your desired style. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Once you’re happy with your style, finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Short Layered Pixie Cut

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short layered pixie cut:

  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer and flat iron to make styling easier.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from styling tools.
  • Try different types of styling products to find what works best for your hair texture and style.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, and scarves, to add some flair to your style.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my short layered pixie cut trimmed?

It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short layered pixie cut?

Yes, but you might need to use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place.

Will a short layered pixie cut suit my face shape?

Short layered pixie cuts can suit a variety of face shapes, but it’s best to consult with a stylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Pixie Cut

Short hair requires different hair care techniques than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short layered pixie cut looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to style short layered pixie cut, it’s time to try it out for yourself! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles and techniques until you find what works best for you. Happy styling!
