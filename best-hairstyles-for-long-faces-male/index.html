Best Hairstyles For Long Faces Male

Written by Frank Nov 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a man with a long face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that will suit your face shape? Look no further because in this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for long faces male. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, we have got you covered.

Description

Having a long face can be challenging when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. The goal is to pick a hairstyle that will balance out the length of your face and make it appear shorter. The right hairstyle can make a significant impact on your overall appearance, so it is essential to choose the right one that suits your face shape.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, it is essential to keep in mind that you want to create the illusion of width. This can be achieved by adding volume on the sides of your head, avoiding hairstyles that are too long and flat, and choosing hairstyles that have a lot of texture.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
2. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat.
3. Choose hairstyles that have a lot of texture and volume on the sides of your head.
4. Consider getting a fringe or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.
5. Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits your face shape the best.

Tips

1. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what hairstyle would suit your face shape.
2. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
3. Use hair products such as mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture to your hair.
4. Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or too tight as they can elongate your face.

FAQ

Q: What are the best hairstyles for long faces male?
A: The best hairstyles for long faces male are hairstyles that have volume on the sides of the head and texture. Some examples include side-swept bangs, messy hairstyles, and layered haircuts.
Q: Can men with long faces have long hair?
A: Men with long faces can have long hair, but it is important to add volume and texture to the hair to create the illusion of width.
Q: What should men with long faces avoid when it comes to hairstyles?
A: Men with long faces should avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat, slicked-back hairstyles, and hairstyles that are too tight.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
- Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
- Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curlers too often as they can damage your hair.
- Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
- Use hair products such as mousse, hairspray, and gel to style your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, men with long faces can look their best and feel confident.
