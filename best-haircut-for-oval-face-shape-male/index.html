Best Haircut For Oval Face Shape Male In 2023

Written by Bardi Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape for men. It is because almost any haircut will look good on an oval face shape. However, it is essential to choose the best haircut that complements your features and enhances your overall appearance. In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircut for oval face shape male in 2023.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

An oval face shape has a balanced proportion and is slightly longer than it is wide. The forehead is wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are prominent. Men with oval face shape have the advantage of being able to experiment with different haircuts and styles. The best haircut for oval face shape male is one that does not add volume to the top of the head and is not too short on the sides. The perfect haircut for oval face shape male should complement the face shape and enhance the overall appearance.

How to

The first step in choosing the best haircut for oval face shape male is to determine the face shape. One can do this by measuring the width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have determined that you have an oval face shape, you can start exploring different haircuts and styles.

Step by step

The following are the best haircuts for oval face shape male in 2023:

1. Undercut

The undercut is a popular haircut for men with oval face shape. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top hair is left longer. The undercut accentuates the cheekbones and balances the face shape.

2. Quiff

The quiff is a timeless haircut that works well for men with oval face shape. This haircut features short hair on the sides and long hair on the top, styled upwards and back. The quiff adds volume to the top of the head, making the face appear more balanced.

3. Slicked-back hair

Slicked-back hair is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with oval face shape. This hairstyle features combed-back hair that is held in place with gel or pomade. The slicked-back hair adds volume to the top of the head and makes the face appear more balanced.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for oval face shape male:

  • Choose a haircut that complements the face shape and enhances the overall appearance.
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the top of the head or are too short on the sides.
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the perfect one.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their expert opinion on what haircut will work best for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if I have an oval face shape?

A: An oval face shape has a balanced proportion and is slightly longer than it is wide. The forehead is wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are prominent.

Q: What is the best haircut for oval face shape male?

A: The best haircut for oval face shape male is one that does not add volume to the top of the head and is not too short on the sides. The perfect haircut for oval face shape male should complement the face shape and enhance the overall appearance.

Hair Care

To maintain the best haircut for oval face shape male, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and well-groomed.

In conclusion, the best haircut for oval face shape male in 2023 is one that complements the face shape and enhances the overall appearance. The undercut, quiff, and slicked-back hair are some of the best haircuts for oval face shape male. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the perfect haircut.
