Which Hairstyle Suit For Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit challenging. You want a style that will flatter your features, but not make your face appear even rounder. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about which hairstyle suits a round face. From the best cuts to styling tips, we have got you covered.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape, with the width and length being almost equal. The cheeks are usually the widest part of a round face, and the forehead and jawline tend to be less defined. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined angles.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round face, there are several things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can impact the overall look of your hairstyle. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that will help to balance out the volume of your hair.

Choose a Style that Adds Height

Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head can help to elongate a round face. This can be achieved with styles like a messy bun or a high ponytail. However, be careful not to add too much height, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

Avoid Styles that Add Volume to the Sides of Your Face

As mentioned above, adding too much volume to the sides of your face can make your face appear wider. To avoid this, you may want to avoid styles like blunt bobs or heavy bangs. Instead, opt for styles that are more layered and have movement.

Consider Your Face Shape When Choosing Bangs

While bangs can be a great addition to a round face, you want to make sure you choose the right style. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs, as these can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that help to create more angles in your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle for your round face, it is important to know how to style it properly. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, while those with thick hair may want to choose a smoothing formula.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Next, apply any styling products that you may need. This could include a heat protectant spray, a volumizing mousse, or a smoothing serum, depending on your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. If you want to add volume, flip your head upside down while blow drying.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair as desired. If you are going for a straight look, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves. If you want to add curls or waves, use a curling iron or wand to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it is important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important for maintaining the shape and style of your hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in the appearance and health of your hair. Choose products that are appropriate for your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair over time. To protect your hair, use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and avoid using them on the highest heat setting.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or heavy bangs. Instead, opt for styles that are more layered and have movement.

Can I wear bangs if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great addition to a round face, but you want to make sure you choose the right style. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs, and opt for side-swept or wispy bangs instead.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. However, you may want to add some volume to the crown of your head to help elongate your face.

Hair Care for a Round Face

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove dirt and oil that can build up on your scalp. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type.

Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner helps to moisturize and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and shiny. Choose a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair over time. To protect your hair, use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and avoid using them on the highest heat setting.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits a round face, go ahead and try out some new styles! Remember to choose a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
