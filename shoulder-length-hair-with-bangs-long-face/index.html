Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs Long Face The Perfect Hairstyle

If you have a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and brings out your best features. One hairstyle that works great for long faces is shoulder length hair with bangs. In this article, we will discuss how to style shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face, hair care tips, and more.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for a Long Face

The first thing to consider when styling shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face is the length of the bangs. Bangs that are too short can make your face look even longer, while bangs that are too long can hide your features. The ideal length for bangs for a long face is just above the eyebrows.

Another thing to consider is the type of bangs. Side-swept bangs work well for long faces as they help to shorten the face. Blunt bangs can also work, but make sure they are not too short. Wispy bangs are not recommended for long faces as they can make the face look even longer.

Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs Long Face

Shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face can be styled in various ways, including:

  • Loose Waves: Add some loose waves to your shoulder-length hair for a relaxed and effortless look.
  • Sleek and Straight: Straighten your hair for a sleek and sophisticated look.
  • Half-Up Half-Down: Pull half of your shoulder-length hair up into a ponytail or bun for a chic and stylish look.
  • Braids: Braid your shoulder-length hair for a fun and playful look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for a Long Face

Here is a step by step guide to styling shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Straighten your hair using a flat iron.
  4. Create a side parting and section off your bangs.
  5. Curl your bangs using a curling iron.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for a Long Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or straightening your hair to prevent damage.
  • Avoid cutting blunt bangs too short as they can make your face look longer.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs to help shorten your face.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.
  • Don't overuse hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can shoulder length hair with bangs be styled in an updo?

Yes, shoulder length hair with bangs can be styled in an updo. Half-up half-down styles work well, as do low buns and ponytails.

What type of hair suits shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face?

Shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face works best for those with straight or wavy hair. Curly hair may not work as well as it can add volume to the face.

Can shoulder length hair with bangs be styled with layers?

Yes, shoulder length hair with bangs can be styled with layers. Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, but make sure they are not too short as they can make the face look longer.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for a Long Face

Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down.

Shoulder length hair with bangs for a long face is a great hairstyle that can flatter your face shape and bring out your best features. By following the styling, tips and hair care tips in this article, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish look that suits you.
