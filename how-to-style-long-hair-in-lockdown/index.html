How To Style Long Hair In Lockdown

Written by Frank Sep 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

With the current global pandemic, many of us are finding ourselves stuck at home with limited access to hair salons and stylists. This has resulted in many people growing out their hair, which can be both a blessing and a curse. While long hair is beautiful and versatile, it can also be overwhelming to style, especially when you're in lockdown and can't visit your hairdresser. But fear not! In this article, we will guide you on how to style long hair in lockdown.

How to Style Long Hair in Lockdown

Styling long hair can be a challenge, especially when you're in lockdown and have limited access to hair salons and products. However, with a little creativity and patience, you can create stunning hairstyles for your long locks. Here are a few ideas:

1. Braids

Braids are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. There are various types of braids you can try, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. Braids are easy to do and can be worn in a variety of ways, such as a simple side braid, a braided ponytail, or a braided crown.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around, and secure it with bobby pins. Leave a few strands of hair loose to create a messy look. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a scrunchie or a headband, to jazz up your messy bun.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your long locks while keeping them out of your face. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or a clip. You can leave the bottom half of your hair loose or add some curls or waves for a more polished look.

4. Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can create a sleek and polished look by smoothing down your hair and tying it with a hair tie. Or, you can create a more casual look by leaving a few strands of hair loose and pulling out a few pieces around your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair in lockdown:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan to use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or a straightener, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Choose a hairstyle from the list above and start styling your hair. Remember to be patient and take your time.

Step 5: Finish With Hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair in Lockdown

Here are a few tips to help you style your long hair in lockdown:

1. Use Hair Accessories

Add some flair to your hairstyles by using hair accessories such as scrunchies, headbands, and hair clips.

2. Experiment With Different Hair Products

Try out different hair products, such as mousse, hair serum, or hair oil, to see which ones work best for your hair type.

3. Don't Overdo It With Heat Styling Tools

While heat styling tools can be great for creating different hairstyles, try not to overdo it as this can cause damage to your hair.

4. Embrace Your Natural Hair Texture

Don't be afraid to embrace your natural hair texture. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, there are plenty of hairstyles that can show off your natural texture.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. However, if you have dry hair, you may want to wash it less frequently to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a hair dye that is suitable for your hair type.

Q: How do I prevent split ends?

A: To prevent split ends, make sure to trim your hair regularly, avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your long locks healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as tight ponytails or braids, as these can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

3. Protect Your Hair From the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains UV protection to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as biotin, vitamin C, and iron, to promote healthy hair growth.

There you have it - a complete guide on how to style long hair in lockdown. With these tips and ideas, you can create stunning hairstyles for your long locks, even when you're stuck at home. Remember to take care of your hair and be patient when styling it. Happy styling!
