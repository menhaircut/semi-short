How To Grow Out Thick Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to grow out your thick hair? You’re not alone! Many guys with thick hair struggle to find the right hairstyle that suits them. But fear not, because in this article, we’ll guide you through the process of growing out thick hair.

Table of Content

  • How to Grow Out Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Out Thick Hair
  • FAQs

How to Grow Out Thick Hair

Growing out thick hair requires patience and a bit of effort. It’s not as simple as just letting it grow. Here are a few tips to help you grow out your hair:

Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals is crucial for hair growth. Make sure to include foods that are high in protein, such as eggs, fish, and lean meats. Also, consume plenty of fruits and vegetables to provide your hair with the necessary nutrients.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and slow down its growth. It’s best to avoid using blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts are essential for growing out thick hair. Trimming the split ends will prevent the hair from breaking and promote healthy growth. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to grow out thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before you start growing out your hair, decide on the hairstyle you want. This will give you a clear idea of how long you need to grow your hair and what style to aim for.

Step 2: Be Patient

Growing out hair takes time, so be patient. Your hair will grow approximately half an inch per month. Don’t get discouraged if your hair takes longer to grow than expected.

Step 3: Take Care of Your Hair

Take care of your hair by washing it regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your scalp of natural oils. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

Step 4: Experiment with Hairstyles

As your hair grows, experiment with different hairstyles. Try out different lengths, partings, and styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Growing Out Thick Hair

Here are a few tips to help you grow out your thick hair:

Tip 1: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp increases blood flow to the hair follicles, promoting growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes each day.

Tip 2: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are less harsh on your hair than cotton ones. They reduce friction and prevent your hair from tangling and breaking while you sleep.

Tip 3: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid pulling or tugging at your hair, as it can cause breakage. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair when growing it out?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days when growing it out. Over-washing can strip your scalp of natural oils, which can slow down hair growth.

Q: How can I prevent split ends?

A: Prevent split ends by getting regular trims every 8-12 weeks. Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair.

Q: Can I use hair growth products to speed up hair growth?

A: Hair growth products can help promote growth, but they’re not a magic solution. They work best when combined with a healthy diet and hair care routine.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to help you grow out your thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and promote growth. Apply the treatment to your hair after shampooing, leave it on for 5-10 minutes, and rinse it out thoroughly.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and slow down its growth. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF protection when spending time outdoors.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and slow down growth. Avoid using excessive heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair.

By following these tips and being patient, you can successfully grow out your thick hair. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best!
