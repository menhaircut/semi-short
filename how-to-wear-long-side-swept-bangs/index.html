How To Wear Long Side Swept Bangs

Long side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of sophistication and style to your look. If you're considering getting them, but aren't sure how to wear them, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to wear long side swept bangs, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Wear Long Side Swept Bangs

Long side swept bangs can be worn in a variety of ways, depending on your personal style and face shape. Here are some popular ways to wear them:

  • Straight and sleek: If you have straight hair, you can wear your bangs straight and sleek. This look is perfect for those who want a polished and sophisticated look.
  • Messy and textured: For a more relaxed look, you can add some texture to your bangs. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, tousled look.
  • Swept to the side: The classic way to wear long side swept bangs is to sweep them to one side. This look is flattering on most face shapes and can be worn with any hair texture.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you're not sure how to style your long side swept bangs, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs from the rest of your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  4. When your bangs are almost dry, switch to a flat brush and continue to blow dry them to the side.
  5. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Wearing Long Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips to help you wear long side swept bangs:

  • Choose the right length: If you have a high forehead, longer bangs can help to balance out your features. If you have a smaller forehead, go for shorter bangs.
  • Consider your face shape: Bangs can help to balance out your features, so consider your face shape when choosing the length and style of your bangs.
  • Use the right products: Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture to your bangs. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  • Trim your bangs regularly: To keep your bangs looking their best, trim them every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about long side swept bangs:

  • Can I wear long side swept bangs with curly hair? Yes! Long side swept bangs can look great with curly hair. Just make sure to use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • How do I know if long side swept bangs will look good on me? Long side swept bangs can be flattering on most face shapes. However, if you're not sure, consult with your hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape and hair texture.
  • How do I style my bangs if I'm growing them out? Use bobby pins or hair clips to pin your bangs back while they're growing out. You can also try a side part for a different look.

Hair Care for Long Side Swept Bangs

To keep your long side swept bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying your scalp.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your bangs.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear long side swept bangs with confidence and style!
