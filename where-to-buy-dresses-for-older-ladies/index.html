Where To Buy Dresses For Older Ladies The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're an older lady looking for stylish dresses that suit your taste and age, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you where to buy dresses for older ladies that are fashionable, comfortable, and affordable. We'll also give you tips on how to choose the right dress for your body type and occasion, as well as how to care for your hair when wearing dresses. So, let's get started!

Description

As we age, our fashion sense may change, but that doesn't mean we can't still look fabulous in dresses. However, finding dresses that suit our style and body type can be a challenge, especially if we don't know where to look. That's why we've compiled a list of the best places to buy dresses for older ladies, both online and offline, so you can find the perfect dress for any occasion.

How to Choose the Right Dress for Your Body Type

Before we dive into where to buy dresses for older ladies, let's talk about how to choose the right dress for your body type. Here are some tips:

1. Know Your Body Shape

Before you start shopping, it's important to know your body shape. Are you pear-shaped, apple-shaped, hourglass-shaped, or straight-shaped? Knowing your body shape will help you choose dresses that flatter your figure and make you feel confident.

2. Look for Dresses that Accentuate Your Best Features

Whether you have a great pair of legs, a slim waist, or a beautiful décolletage, look for dresses that highlight your best features. For example, if you have toned arms, choose a sleeveless dress that shows them off.

3. Choose the Right Length

The length of your dress can make a big difference in how it flatters your figure. If you're petite, choose a dress that hits above the knee to elongate your legs. If you're taller, choose a longer dress that accentuates your height.

Where to Buy Dresses for Older Ladies: Online Stores

Online shopping is a great option for older ladies who prefer the comfort of their own homes. Here are some online stores that offer stylish dresses for older ladies:

1. Macy's

Macy's offers a wide range of dresses for all ages, including older ladies. They have a variety of styles, from cocktail dresses to maxi dresses, and carry popular brands like Calvin Klein, Jones New York, and Lauren Ralph Lauren. Macy's also offers free shipping on orders over $25.

2. Nordstrom

Nordstrom is another great online store for older ladies who want to stay fashionable. They have a vast selection of dresses from popular brands like Eileen Fisher, Vince Camuto, and Eliza J. Nordstrom also offers free shipping and returns on all orders.

3. Chico's

Chico's specializes in clothing for women over 40, so you can be sure they have dresses that suit your taste and age. They have a range of styles, from casual to dressy, and offer free shipping on orders over $125.

Where to Buy Dresses for Older Ladies: Brick and Mortar Stores

If you prefer to shop in person, there are plenty of brick and mortar stores that offer dresses for older ladies. Here are some options:

1. Talbots

Talbots is a popular store for older ladies who want classic, timeless dresses. They offer a variety of styles and sizes, from petite to plus, and have a range of accessories to complete your look.

2. J.Crew

J.Crew may be known for its preppy, youthful styles, but they also offer dresses that suit older ladies. They have a variety of prints, colors, and silhouettes to choose from, and offer free shipping on orders over $150.

3. Ann Taylor

Ann Taylor is another store that offers dresses for older ladies who want to stay fashionable. They have a range of styles, from shift dresses to wrap dresses, and carry sizes from petite to tall.

Tips for Wearing Dresses as an Older Lady

Now that you know where to buy dresses for older ladies, here are some tips for wearing them:

1. Choose Comfortable Fabrics

As we age, our skin becomes more sensitive, so it's important to choose dresses made from comfortable fabrics like cotton, linen, or silk. Avoid fabrics that are too tight, scratchy, or heavy.

2. Accessorize Wisely

Accessorizing can make or break an outfit, so choose wisely. Opt for simple, classic accessories like a statement necklace, hoop earrings, or a clutch bag. Avoid anything too trendy or flashy.

3. Wear the Right Undergarments

Wearing the right undergarments can make a big difference in how a dress looks and feels. Choose undergarments that smooth and support your figure, like a well-fitting bra or shapewear. Avoid undergarments that create bulges or lines.

FAQ

Q: What type of dress is best for an older lady?

A: The best type of dress for an older lady depends on her body type and personal style. However, classic styles like shift dresses, wrap dresses, and A-line dresses are always a safe bet.

Q: How can I make a dress look more youthful?

A: You can make a dress look more youthful by accessorizing with trendy pieces like statement jewelry, scarves, or shoes. You can also choose dresses with bold prints or bright colors.

Q: How can I care for my hair when wearing dresses?

A: When wearing dresses, it's important to style your hair in a way that complements your outfit. If you're wearing a dress with a high neckline, consider wearing your hair up to show off your neck. If you're wearing a dress with a low neckline, wear your hair down to frame your face.

We hope this guide has helped you find where to buy dresses for older ladies. Remember, fashion is for everyone, no matter your age or body type. With the right dress and accessories, you can look and feel fabulous at any age.
