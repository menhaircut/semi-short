Fade Haircut With Long Top A Trendy Style For Men In 2023

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish, modern haircut that's easy to maintain, the fade haircut with long top might be just what you need. This popular hairstyle combines a classic fade with longer hair on top, creating a versatile look that can be styled in many different ways.

Description

The fade haircut with long top is a popular style that involves fading the hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a contrast between the short, faded hair and the longer, textured hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it typically ranges from 2-4 inches.

How to Get a Fade Haircut with Long Top

If you're interested in getting a fade haircut with long top, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

Find a barber who has experience with fade haircuts and can help you achieve the look you want. Look for a barber who has a portfolio of previous clients, and check out reviews online to see what other people have to say about their services.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you might want to keep your hair shorter on top, while if you have a longer face, you can go for a longer length.

Step 3: Choose the Type of Fade

There are several types of fades to choose from, including a low fade, mid fade, and high fade. The type of fade you choose will depend on how much contrast you want between the top of your hair and the sides and back.

Step 4: Get the Haircut

Once you've chosen your barber, decided on the length of your hair, and chosen the type of fade, it's time to get the haircut. Sit back and relax as your barber works their magic, creating a fade that suits your face shape and personal style.

Tips for Styling a Fade Haircut with Long Top

Once you've got your fade haircut with long top, here are some tips for styling it:

- Use a matte hair product for a natural, textured look. - Comb your hair to one side for a classic, gentlemanly style. - Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair. - Experiment with different styles, such as a messy, textured look or a slicked-back style.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a fade haircut with long top?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, you should get a trim every 2-4 weeks to maintain the fade and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways with a fade haircut with long top?

A: Absolutely! The great thing about this style is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care for a Fade Haircut with Long Top

To keep your fade haircut with long top looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade. - Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a fade haircut with long top with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a classic, timeless look or a modern, edgy style, this haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
