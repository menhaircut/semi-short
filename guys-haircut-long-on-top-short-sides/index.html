Guys Haircut Long On Top Short Sides A Trending Style In 2023

Description

The guys haircut long on top short sides is a popular hairstyle among men for the past few years. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways, making it a perfect choice for guys who want to switch up their look. This style involves keeping the hair on top longer while the sides and back are trimmed short. It is a trendy and fashionable look that suits all face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you want to achieve the guys haircut long on top short sides, it is recommended to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut that suits your face shape and hair type. However, if you want to try it at home, here are the steps:

Step by Step

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short. Use a clipper with a short guard to achieve the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides with the top by using a pair of scissors or clippers with a longer guard.
  4. Leave the hair on top longer and style it according to your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a guys haircut long on top short sides:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Use styling products such as wax or pomade to achieve different looks.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

FAQ

Q: Does the guys haircut long on top short sides suit all face shapes?

A: Yes, this haircut suits all face shapes. Your hairstylist will adjust the length and shape of the haircut to suit your face.

Q: How do I style the hair on top?

A: You can style the hair on top in many ways, such as slicking it back, creating a quiff, or leaving it messy and textured. Use styling products such as wax or pomade to achieve different looks.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after getting a guys haircut long on top short sides. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.
  • Use styling products such as wax or pomade to achieve different looks.

In conclusion, the guys haircut long on top short sides is a trendy and fashionable hairstyle that suits all face shapes and hair types. It is a versatile style that can be styled in many ways, making it a perfect choice for guys who want to switch up their look. Remember to visit a professional hairstylist or follow the step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect cut. Take care of your hair by using good quality hair care products and visiting your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.
