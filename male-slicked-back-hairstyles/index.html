Male Slicked Back Hairstyles A Timeless Look

Written by Petter Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic, stylish look that never goes out of fashion, male slicked back hairstyles are the way to go. Whether you're going for a slicked back undercut or a more classic look, this style is perfect for any occasion.

Description

The slicked back hairstyle is characterized by hair that has been combed back and held in place with some sort of product. This style can be worn by men of any age, and it works well with a variety of hair types.

How To

To achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle, you'll need a few key tools and products:

  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair dryer (optional)

Here's how to get the look:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your palms and work it through your hair.
  3. Use a comb to comb your hair back, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and combed back smoothly.
  4. If you want to add extra volume, you can use a hair dryer on low heat to blow dry your hair back while you comb it.
  5. Once your hair is in place, use a little bit of additional product to hold it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly, making sure to remove any excess dirt or product buildup. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your palms and rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Starting at the front of your hairline, use a comb to slick your hair back. Comb your hair all the way back to the crown of your head, making sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles along the way.
  4. Once your hair is slicked back, use your hands to smooth out any remaining bumps or flyaways. You can also use a little bit of additional product to hold your hair in place.
  5. If you want extra volume, use a hair dryer on low heat to blow your hair back while you comb it. Use your hands to shape your hair as you blow dry it.
  6. Once your hair is in place, use a little bit of hairspray or additional product to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality hair product. A good hair gel or pomade will help you achieve the perfect slicked back look.
  • Experiment with different styles. There are many different variations of the slicked back hairstyle, so don't be afraid to try out different looks until you find one that works for you.
  • Maintain your hairstyle. To keep your hairstyle looking its best, you'll need to touch it up every day. Use a little bit of product to re-slick your hair back each morning.

FAQ

Here are a few common questions about male slicked back hairstyles:

Q: Can anyone wear a slicked back hairstyle?

A: Yes, the slicked back hairstyle works well with a variety of hair types and face shapes, so anyone can wear this style.

Q: How do I choose the right hair product?

A: Look for a high-quality hair gel or pomade that provides strong hold without making your hair look greasy or heavy.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hairstyle?

A: You'll need to touch up your hairstyle every day to keep it looking its best. Use a little bit of product to re-slick your hair back each morning.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and excess oil.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle in no time. So why not give it a try and see how this classic look can work for you?
