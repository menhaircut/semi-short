How To Make Thick Wavy Hair Lay Flat

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Having thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can be voluminous and beautiful, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One of the biggest challenges that people with thick wavy hair face is getting it to lay flat. Fortunately, there are several techniques that you can use to make your hair look sleek and smooth. Here are some tips on how to make thick wavy hair lay flat.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to style, especially if you want it to lay flat. While there are many techniques that you can use to achieve this look, it's important to start with the right products and tools. You'll need a good shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, as well as a flat iron or straightening brush. With a little bit of patience and practice, you can achieve a sleek and smooth look that will turn heads.

How to

If you're wondering how to make thick wavy hair lay flat, here are some tips that you can try:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type to help smooth out your strands. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant

When using heat tools on your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant first. This will help to protect your strands from damage and keep them looking healthy. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use a Flat Iron or Straightening Brush

One of the most effective ways to make thick wavy hair lay flat is to use a flat iron or straightening brush. These tools can help to smooth out your strands and create a sleek look. Be sure to work in small sections, and use a comb to help guide the hair through the tool.

4. Finish with a Shine Spray

Once you've straightened your hair, finish with a shine spray to help add some extra gloss and smoothness. This will help to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny throughout the day.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make thick wavy hair lay flat:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. This will help to smooth out your strands and prepare them for styling.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant throughout your hair to help protect it from damage. This is especially important if you're using a flat iron or straightening brush.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to help smooth out your strands. Focus on the roots to create volume, and use the brush to guide your hair in the direction that you want it to lay.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron or Straightening Brush

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or straightening brush to create a sleek look. Work in small sections, and use a comb to help guide your hair through the tool.

Step 5: Finish with a Shine Spray

Finish with a shine spray to help add some extra gloss and smoothness. This will help to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make thick wavy hair lay flat:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When working with thick wavy hair, it's important to use a wide-tooth comb to help detangle your strands. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Don't Overdo It with Products

Avoid using too many products on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy. Stick to a few key products, such as a heat protectant and shine spray.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for your hair. You may need to try a few different tools or products before you find the perfect combination.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my thick wavy hair lay flat without using heat?

A: While heat tools are one of the most effective ways to make thick wavy hair lay flat, you can also try using a smoothing serum or cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, and then use a round brush to blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to lay.

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: The frequency with which you should wash your hair will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people with thick wavy hair may find that they need to wash it every other day, while others may be able to go longer between washes. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your thick wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This includes using products that are designed for your hair type, avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy. With a little bit of TLC, you can keep your hair looking sleek and smooth.
