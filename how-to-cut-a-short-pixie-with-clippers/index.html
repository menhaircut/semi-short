How To Cut A Short Pixie With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to cut your hair into a short pixie style, using clippers can be a quick and easy way to achieve the look. With the right tools and technique, you can create a chic and stylish haircut that is both low-maintenance and versatile. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut a short pixie with clippers, as well as share tips and hair care advice to make the most of your new style.

Description

A short pixie haircut is a classic style that is both bold and feminine. It is characterized by its short length, typically no longer than two inches, and its layered and textured appearance. By using clippers to cut your hair, you can achieve an even and precise cut that is perfect for this style. Clippers also make it easier to create the layers and texture that are essential to a short pixie haircut.

How to Cut a Short Pixie with Clippers

Before you begin cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. You'll need a set of clippers with adjustable guards, a comb, and hair cutting shears. You may also want to have a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head as you cut.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. You can blow dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start at the top of your head and create a section that is about one inch wide. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Choose your guard size

Select the guard size that you want to use for your clippers. The size you choose will depend on how short you want your hair to be. For a shorter pixie cut, use a smaller guard size. For a longer pixie cut, use a larger guard size.

Step 4: Start cutting

Starting at the back of your head, use the clippers to cut your hair using an upward motion. Move the clippers in the opposite direction of the hair growth, so that you are cutting against the grain. Work your way up to the crown of your head, cutting each section of hair as you go.

Step 5: Blend the sides and top

Once you have cut the back of your head, move on to the sides and top. Use the same upward motion to cut the hair, but be sure to blend the sides and top so that they flow seamlessly together. Use the comb to lift sections of hair and cut any stray hairs with the shears.

Step 6: Trim the bangs

If you want to include bangs in your pixie cut, use the shears to trim them to the desired length. You can create blunt or textured bangs, depending on your preference. Be sure to cut the bangs slightly longer than the desired length, as they will shrink up slightly when they dry.

Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie with Clippers

When cutting a short pixie with clippers, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure the best results:

  • Use a smaller guard size for a shorter cut, and a larger guard size for a longer cut.
  • Work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Be sure to blend the sides and top for a seamless look.
  • Trim the bangs slightly longer than the desired length, as they will shrink up when they dry.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.

FAQ

How often should I cut my short pixie haircut?

A short pixie cut typically requires more frequent trims than longer hairstyles. You should plan to get your hair trimmed every four to six weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Can I cut a short pixie with clippers at home?

Yes, cutting a short pixie with clippers can be done at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you are unsure about how to cut your hair, it is always best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care for a Short Pixie Cut

Once you have cut your hair into a short pixie style, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips for a short pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush that can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut a short pixie with clippers and enjoy a chic and stylish new look. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking its best, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to make your haircut your own.
