How To Get A Haircut In Fallout New Vegas

Written by Luffy Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you playing Fallout New Vegas and wondering how to get a haircut for your character? Look no further! This article will guide you through the process step by step.

Description

In Fallout New Vegas, your character can get a haircut to change their appearance. This can be done at several locations throughout the game. However, some locations may require you to complete certain quests or have a certain reputation with factions before they will offer their services.

How to

To get a haircut in Fallout New Vegas, follow these simple steps:

  1. Find a barber shop. Some locations include: Mick & Ralph's in Freeside, New Vegas Medical Clinic, Atomic Wrangler Casino, and the Tops Casino.
  2. Enter the barber shop and speak to the barber.
  3. Select the haircut you want from the menu. There are several options to choose from.
  4. Confirm your selection and pay the fee. Haircuts typically cost between 20-50 caps.
  5. Enjoy your new haircut!

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed step by step guide:

Step 1: Find a Barber Shop

Barber shops can be found in several locations throughout the game. Mick & Ralph's in Freeside is one of the easiest to find. It is located near the entrance to Freeside and is marked on your map. Other locations include the New Vegas Medical Clinic, Atomic Wrangler Casino, and the Tops Casino.

Step 2: Speak to the Barber

Enter the barber shop and speak to the barber. They will greet you and offer their services. Select the option to get a haircut.

Step 3: Select Your Haircut

You will be presented with a menu of different haircuts to choose from. Select the one you want. Keep in mind that some options may not be available depending on your character's gender or race.

Step 4: Confirm and Pay

Confirm your selection and pay the fee. Haircuts typically cost between 20-50 caps. Once you have paid, your character will receive their new haircut.

Step 5: Enjoy Your New Look

Exit the barber shop and enjoy your new haircut. Your character's appearance will be permanently changed until you get another haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut in Fallout New Vegas:

  • Make sure you have enough caps to pay for the haircut.
  • Some locations may require you to complete certain quests before they will offer their services.
  • Check the menu carefully before selecting your haircut. Some options may not be available depending on your character's gender or race.
  • Your new haircut will be visible in cutscenes and on your character's profile picture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a haircut in Fallout New Vegas:

Can I change my haircut later?

Yes, you can get another haircut at any time by visiting a barber shop.

Do haircuts affect gameplay?

No, haircuts are purely cosmetic and do not affect gameplay in any way.

How much do haircuts cost?

Haircuts typically cost between 20-50 caps.

Hair Care

Your character's hair will grow over time, but there is no need to worry about it getting too long. Haircuts can be obtained at any time to keep your character looking fresh.

That's it! You now know how to get a haircut in Fallout New Vegas. Have fun customizing your character's appearance!
