How To Cut A Man s Hair With Clippers Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting a man's hair with clippers can be a challenging task, especially if you are new to it. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of practice, you can easily achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut a man's hair with clippers using Youtube videos as a reference.

Description

Cutting hair with clippers is one of the most popular and efficient ways to cut men's hair. Clippers are easy to use and can be adjusted to different lengths, making them ideal for various hairstyles. Youtube is an excellent resource for learning how to cut hair with clippers, as there are many tutorials available that can guide you through the process.

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Clippers Youtube - Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a set of clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a cape or towel to cover the person's shoulders. Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in and have the appropriate guards for the desired length of hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before starting the haircut, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wash the hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel or blow-dryer. Wet hair can be challenging to cut, and it can also damage the clippers.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

Start by using the clippers with the longest guard to create a guideline. Begin at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up to the crown of the head. Use the clippers in an upward motion, following the natural direction of the hair growth.

Step 4: Use the Scissors

Once you have created the guideline with the clippers, switch to the scissors. Comb the hair straight up and cut across the top to create a layered effect. Trim the sides and back to match the length of the top.

Step 5: Create the Sideburns

Create the sideburns by trimming the hair in front of the ear with scissors. Use the clippers to taper the sideburns and blend them into the hair.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Clean up the neckline by removing any stray hairs with the clippers. Use a comb to create a straight line and trim any hair below it.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style the hair as desired. Use gel, pomade, or hairspray to hold the hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you achieve a better result when cutting hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers in a straight line.
  • Take breaks and step back to evaluate your progress.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.

FAQ

What is the Best Type of Clippers to Use?

The best type of clippers to use for cutting hair is a professional-grade set with adjustable blades and guards. Wahl, Andis, and Oster are some of the most popular brands.

How Do You Clean and Maintain Your Clippers?

To clean and maintain your clippers, you should remove the blades and clean them with a brush and oil. You should also lubricate the blades regularly and replace them when they become dull.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it is essential to take care of the hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims can also help keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut a man's hair with clippers using Youtube, you can save time and money by doing it at home. With practice, you can become an expert and achieve salon-quality results.
