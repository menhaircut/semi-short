How to Figure Out Which Hairstyle Suits You

The first step to finding your perfect hairstyle is figuring out your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. You can also use a tape measure to measure the length and width of your face.

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement it. For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, you'll want to look for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a side-swept bang.

Round Face

Women with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of their face. Instead, they should look for hairstyles that elongate their face, such as:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Messy buns

Oval Face

Women with oval faces have a balanced face shape, so they can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Some popular options include:

  • Long layers
  • Bob cuts
  • Pixie cuts
  • Side-swept bangs

Square Face

Women with square faces have a strong jawline, so they should look for hairstyles that soften their features. Some great options include:

  • Soft waves
  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts

Heart Face

Women with heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrow chin, so they should look for hairstyles that balance out their features. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Updos with volume

Diamond Face

Women with diamond-shaped faces have a narrow forehead and chin with wider cheekbones, so they should look for hairstyles that balance out their features. Some great options include:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Bob cuts
  • Updos with volume

Oblong Face

Women with oblong faces have a longer face shape, so they should look for hairstyles that add width to their face. Some great options include:

  • Bangs
  • Bob cuts
  • Soft waves
  • Voluminous updos

Where to Find Hairstyle Inspiration Online

Now that you know your face shape and the types of hairstyles that suit you best, it's time to find some inspiration. Here are some great places to look online:

  • Pinterest: This social media platform is a great place to find hairstyle inspiration. You can search for specific keywords, such as "long layers" or "pixie cut," and browse through thousands of images.
  • Instagram: Many hairstylists and hair bloggers post their work on Instagram, so it's a great place to find real-life examples of different hairstyles.
  • Magazine websites: Many magazines have online versions of their publications, which often include hairstyle inspiration and tutorials.
  • YouTube: There are countless hair tutorials on YouTube, ranging from simple styles to more complex ones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle Online

Ready to find your perfect hairstyle online? Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Figure out your face shape using the method outlined above.
  2. Make a list of the types of hairstyles that suit your face shape.
  3. Browse through Pinterest, Instagram, and magazine websites to find examples of the hairstyles on your list.
  4. Screenshot or save the images of the hairstyles you like best.
  5. Make a new list of the hairstyles you saved, along with any notes or comments.
  6. Take your list to your hairstylist and discuss which styles would work best for your hair type and lifestyle.
  7. Try out your new hairstyle!

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle Online

Here are some tips to keep in mind when browsing for hairstyles online:

  • Consider your hair type: Some hairstyles work better on certain hair types, so make sure to take your hair type into account when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair in the morning, you'll want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Be realistic: If you have thin hair, you might not be able to achieve the same volume as someone with thick hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: If you see a hairstyle you love but aren't sure if it will suit you, try it out! Hair grows back, and you might be pleasantly surprised.

FAQ About Finding a Hairstyle Online

What if I'm not sure what my face shape is?

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are plenty of online resources that can help. You can also ask your hairstylist to help you determine your face shape.

What if I can't find any hairstyles I like online?

If you're having trouble finding hairstyles online, consider making an appointment with a hairstylist. They can help you find a hairstyle that suits you and your lifestyle.

How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

The best way to know if a hairstyle will suit you is to try it out. Bring a photo of the hairstyle to your hairstylist and ask for their opinion. They can help you determine if the hairstyle will work with your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, you'll want to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding your perfect hairstyle online. Happy browsing!
