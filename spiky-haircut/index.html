The Spiky Haircut A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut, you might want to consider the spiky haircut. This style has been popular for many years and has evolved over time to suit different hair types, face shapes, and personal preferences. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the spiky haircut, including how to get it, style it, and take care of it.

Description

The spiky haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that is styled into spikes using hair gel or wax. The length and shape of the spikes can vary depending on the individual's hair texture and desired look. Some people prefer a messy and tousled look, while others opt for a more defined and structured style.

How to

To get a spiky haircut, you'll need to visit a hairstylist or barber who is familiar with this style. They will use clippers or scissors to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving more length on top. The length of the top hair can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually around 2-3 inches.

Once your hair is cut, you can style it into spikes using a hair gel or wax. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up. Use your fingers to shape the spikes into the desired style, and add more product as needed to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

1. Visit a hairstylist or barber who is familiar with the spiky haircut.

2. Communicate your desired length and style to the stylist.

3. Sit back and let the stylist work their magic.

4. Once your hair is cut, apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips.

5. Work the product into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.

6. Use your fingers to shape the spikes into the desired style.

7. Add more product as needed to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the one that suits you best.

- Use a high-quality hair gel or wax to ensure your spikes stay in place all day.

- Consider using a hairspray or setting spray to lock in your style.

- Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on the best products and techniques to use.

FAQ

Q: Can anyone get a spiky haircut?

A: Yes, the spiky haircut can be adapted to suit different hair types, face shapes, and personal preferences.

Q: How often do I need to get a spiky haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style starts to lose its shape. Most people get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the spiky look.

Q: Can I style my spiky haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as a messy, tousled look or a more defined and structured style.

Hair Care

To keep your spiky haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or straighteners.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock a spiky haircut with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a bold and edgy look or a more refined and classic style, the spiky haircut has something to offer. So why not give it a try?
