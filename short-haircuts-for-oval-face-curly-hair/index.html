Short Haircuts For Oval Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. If you have an oval face, you are already lucky, as this face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, finding the perfect short haircut for oval face curly hair can be a daunting task. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the right haircut, how to style it, and how to take care of your curls.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircuts for Oval Face Curly Hair

The first step in choosing the right short haircut for oval face curly hair is to determine your face shape. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle, but some haircuts look better than others. The key is to find a haircut that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

For oval face curly hair, short haircuts that add volume and texture work best. Layers and bangs can also enhance your natural curls and add dimension to your hair. Here are some short haircuts that work well for oval face curly hair:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered cut
  • Curly shag
  • Asymmetrical cut

When choosing a short haircut, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find a haircut that best suits you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Oval Face Curly Hair

Styling short haircuts for oval face curly hair can be easy if you follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Dry your hair in sections, scrunching your hair as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Remember to avoid brushing or combing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and damage to your curls.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Oval Face Curly Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to keep your short haircut for oval face curly hair looking its best:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Instead, air dry your hair whenever possible.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs about Short Haircuts for Oval Face Curly Hair

1. Can I wear a pixie cut with curly hair?

Yes, you can. A pixie cut can look great on curly hair, especially if you have an oval face. Just make sure to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and showcases your curls.

2. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

3. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, and use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

In addition to the tips mentioned above, here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut for oval face curly hair with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun with your new hairstyle!
