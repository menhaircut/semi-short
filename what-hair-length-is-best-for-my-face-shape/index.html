What Hair Length Is Best For My Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hair length can be a daunting task, especially if you're unsure of which style best suits your face shape. A haircut that complements your facial features can enhance your appearance, while the wrong cut can make you look unflattering. In this article, we'll explore how to determine your face shape and which hair length is best for each shape.

What is Face Shape?

Face shape refers to the overall shape of your face, which can be determined by measuring the length and width of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin. There are six main face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead from one eyebrow arch to the other.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Measure the widest part of your cheekbones, which is usually just below the outer corner of your eyes.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Measure the distance from the tip of your chin to the point below your earlobe where your jawline angles upward.

Step 4: Determine Your Face Shape

Use the measurements to determine your face shape.

  • Oval: Forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face tapers to a narrow chin.
  • Round: The width and length of the face are equal, with soft, rounded edges.
  • Square: The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are equal, with a strong and angular jawline.
  • Heart: The forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face tapers to a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: The face is widest at the cheekbones, and the forehead and jawline are narrow.
  • Oblong: The face is longer than it is wide, with a straight, elongated jawline.

Best Hair Length for Each Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Oval

An oval face shape is versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. If you have an oval face, consider a long layered cut or a shoulder-length bob.

Round

To elongate a round face, opt for a hairstyle that adds height at the crown and lengthens the face. A long, layered cut or a side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face appear wider.

Square

A square face shape benefits from hairstyles that soften the strong jawline. Consider a layered cut that falls below the chin or a long, wavy style. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can emphasize the square shape.

Heart

For a heart-shaped face, choose a style that balances the width of the forehead and cheekbones with the narrow chin. A chin-length bob or a layered cut that falls just below the chin can work well. Avoid styles that add volume to the crown, as they can make the forehead appear wider.

Diamond

A diamond face shape benefits from hairstyles that add fullness to the forehead and chin while minimizing the cheekbones. Consider a chin-length bob or a layered cut that falls below the jawline. Avoid styles that add volume to the cheekbones, as they can make the face appear wider.

Oblong

To balance an oblong face shape, opt for a style that adds width at the sides and volume at the crown. Consider a layered cut that falls just above the shoulders or a wavy shoulder-length style. Avoid styles that add volume at the crown, as they can elongate the face further.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Regardless of your face shape, it's essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for your face shape:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Protect your hair from the sun's harmful UV rays with a hat or a leave-in conditioner that contains SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that best suits your face shape and personality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's essential to choose a style that adds height at the crown and lengthens the face. Consider a pixie cut with side-swept bangs or a layered bob that falls just below the chin.

Q: Can I wear a center part if I have a square face?

A: Yes, but it's essential to soften the square shape with a layered cut or a wavy style. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can emphasize the square shape.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Not necessarily, but it's essential to choose a style that balances the width of the forehead and cheekbones with the narrow chin. Consider a side-swept bang or a wispy fringe that falls just above the eyebrows.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair length for your face shape can be a game-changer, enhancing your appearance and boosting your confidence. By determining your face shape and choosing a style that flatters your features, you can create a signature look that's uniquely you.
