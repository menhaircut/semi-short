Will A Beard Suit My Face Everything You Need To Know

Written by Petter Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering growing a beard but unsure if it will suit your face shape? It's a common concern for many men. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about whether a beard will suit your face or not. From how to determine your face shape to the best beard styles for your face, we've got you covered. Let's dive in.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if a beard will suit your face is to identify your face shape. Here are the most common face shapes and how to identify them:

1. Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face will be longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead. This is considered the most versatile face shape, and most beard styles will suit it.

2. Round Face Shape

A round face shape has a width and length that are almost equal, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones. To balance out the roundness, choose a beard style that adds length to your face, such as a goatee or chin strap.

3. Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong, angular jawline and a square forehead. To soften the sharp angles, choose a beard style that adds length to your face, such as a longer goatee or a full beard with rounded edges.

4. Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wider forehead and a narrow chin. To balance out the proportions, choose a beard style that adds volume to the chin area, such as a goatee or a chinstrap beard.

5. Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. To add width to your chin and balance out your face, choose a beard style that has more hair on the chin than the cheeks, such as a goatee or a soul patch.

Best Beard Styles for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the best beard style that will suit your face. Here are some beard styles to consider:

1. Full Beard

A full beard is a classic look that works well for most face shapes, especially if you have a square or oval face. It adds volume to your face and can help balance out your features.

2. Goatee

A goatee is a versatile beard style that can work for most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with round or heart-shaped faces. It adds length to your face and can make your chin appear more defined.

3. Stubble

Stubble is a low-maintenance beard style that works well for men with a square or oval face shape. It adds a rugged, masculine look to your face and can help balance out your features.

4. Chin Strap

A chin strap beard is a thin line of hair that runs along your jawline. It works well for men with a round or heart-shaped face and can help add definition to your jawline.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Once you've chosen the best beard style for your face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a beard trimmer to keep your beard at the desired length.
  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  • Comb or brush your beard regularly to detangle and style it.
  • Visit a barber regularly to shape your beard and keep it looking sharp.

FAQ

Q: Can I grow a beard if I have patchy facial hair?

A: Yes, you can still grow a beard even if you have patchy facial hair. Consider growing a shorter beard or stubble to help mask the patches.

Q: Will a beard make me look older?

A: It depends on the beard style you choose. A full beard can make you look older, while a shorter beard or stubble can make you look younger.

Q: Can I dye my beard?

A: Yes, you can dye your beard if you want to change the color. However, be sure to use a beard-specific dye and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your beard.

Conclusion

In conclusion, whether a beard will suit your face or not depends on your face shape and the beard style you choose. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can find the best beard style that will suit your face and maintain it properly. Happy bearding!
