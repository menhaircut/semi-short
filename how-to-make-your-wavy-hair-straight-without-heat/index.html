How to Make Your Wavy Hair Straight Without Heat

If you are tired of using heat tools to straighten your wavy hair, then this article is for you. Here, we will be sharing some tips and tricks on how to make your wavy hair straight without using any heat.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we begin, let us first understand why it is important to avoid using heat tools on our hair. Heat tools not only damage our hair but also make it dry and brittle, which leads to breakage and split ends. Therefore, it is essential to use heat-free methods to straighten our wavy hair.

Here are some ways to make your wavy hair straight without heat:

1. Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can help straighten your hair without using any heat. Start by washing your hair, then towel-dry it. Apply a heat protectant spray and comb your hair. Take a round brush and start blow drying your hair from the roots to the ends. Repeat until your hair is straight.

2. Hair Bands

Another way to straighten your hair without using any heat is by using hair bands. Start by washing your hair, then towel-dry it. Divide your hair into two sections and make two ponytails. Take a hair band and wrap it around the base of the ponytail. Repeat on the other side. Leave the hair bands on overnight and remove them in the morning. You will wake up with straight hair.

Step by Step

Blow Drying:

  1. Wash your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  4. Comb your hair.
  5. Take a round brush and start blow drying your hair from the roots to the ends.
  6. Repeat until your hair is straight.

Hair Bands:

  1. Wash your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into two sections and make two ponytails.
  4. Take a hair band and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Leave the hair bands on overnight and remove them in the morning.

Tips

  • Use a good quality heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Comb your hair before blow drying it to avoid tangles.
  • Use hair bands that are not too tight to avoid breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal parts as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

1. How long will my hair stay straight using hair bands?

Your hair will stay straight for a day or two. However, it may not be as long-lasting as using heat tools.

2. Can I use hair bands on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use hair bands on wet hair as it can cause breakage.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and straight hair, it is essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat tools frequently.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet.
