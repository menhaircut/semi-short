How To Make Wavy Hair Dry Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking the same every day? Do you want to switch things up and try a new style? If you have wavy hair, you may be wondering how to make it dry curly. Fortunately, with a few simple steps and the right products, you can achieve the curly look you desire. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make wavy hair dry curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Wavy Hair Dry Curly
  • Tips for Achieving Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Wavy Hair Dry Curly

To make wavy hair dry curly, you will need a few essential products:

  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Curl-enhancing product (gel, mousse, or cream)
  • Diffuser attachment for your hair dryer

Once you have these products, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. Use a generous amount, but be careful not to use too much, as this can weigh down your curls.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands. This will help to define your curls and distribute the product evenly.
  4. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer.
  5. Flip your head upside down and use the diffuser to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, scrunching your hair as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your hands to separate your curls and give them more volume.

Voila! You now have curly hair.

Tips for Achieving Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve curly hair:

  • Use the right products for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight gel or mousse. If you have thick hair, use a heavier cream.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Try plopping your hair. This involves wrapping your hair in a t-shirt or microfiber towel to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wavy hair dry curly:

  • Can I make my hair curly without using heat? Yes, you can try using hair rollers or braiding your hair while it is wet to help create curls without using heat.
  • How long will my curly hair last? This will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curly hair can last for a few days before it starts to lose its shape.
  • What should I do if my curls fall flat? Try refreshing your curls with a curl-enhancing spray or reapplying a curl-defining product.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a different type of hair care than straight hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape.

We hope this guide has helped you learn how to make wavy hair dry curly. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve the curly hair of your dreams.
