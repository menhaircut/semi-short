Why Do An Undercut

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's stylish, low-maintenance, and versatile, then you might want to consider getting an undercut. An undercut is a popular hairstyle that involves shaving or trimming the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer. This hairstyle is perfect for both men and women and can be customized to suit your unique style and personality.

Description

The undercut hairstyle has been around for a long time, but it has recently become more popular among men and women. This hairstyle is characterized by short or shaved sides and longer hair on top. The length and style of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference, but the undercut typically features a noticeable contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

How To

If you're interested in getting an undercut, you can do it yourself or go to a professional stylist. Here are some steps to follow if you want to do it yourself:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You will need a set of clippers or trimmers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Determine how short you want your sides to be and adjust your clippers or trimmers accordingly.

Step 3: Start Shaving

Begin shaving the sides and back of your head with the clippers or trimmers, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to keep the hair in place.

Step 4: Trim the Top

Once you're done with the sides and back, trim the hair on top to your desired length and style.

Step 5: Clean Up

Clean up any loose hairs and make sure your hair looks even and tidy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering getting an undercut:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and features.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles on top to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a fade or taper on the sides for a more gradual transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.
  • Maintain your undercut by trimming the sides and back regularly to keep the contrast between the lengths clear.
  • Use hair products like pomade or wax to style your hair on top and keep it in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

Is an undercut suitable for all hair types?

An undercut can work for most hair types, but it may require different styling techniques depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Can women get undercuts?

Absolutely! Undercuts are a popular hairstyle for women and can be customized to suit their unique style and personality.

How often do I need to trim my undercut?

You should trim the sides and back of your undercut every few weeks to maintain the contrast between the lengths.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking its best, you should take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Overall, an undercut can be a great hairstyle choice if you're looking for something stylish, low-maintenance, and versatile. With the right styling and hair care techniques, you can rock this look with confidence.
