Hairstyle For Square Face Indian Female Tips And Ideas

If you have a square face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyles that suit you. But don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some hairstyle ideas and tips for square face Indian females. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and provide some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyle ideas, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

  • Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  • Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  • Compare the measurements to determine which part of your face is the widest.
  • If the measurements are almost equal, you have a square face shape.

Now that you know your face shape let's move on to hairstyle ideas.

Hairstyle Ideas for Square Face Indian Female

Here are some hairstyle ideas for square face Indian females:

Long Layers

Long layers can soften the angular features of a square face. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start below your chin and blend in with the rest of your hair. Avoid layers that start at your cheekbones as they can make your face look wider.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the squareness of your face. Ask your hairstylist to cut bangs that start from the side of your forehead and angle down towards your cheekbones. Avoid blunt-cut bangs as they can make your face look boxy.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be flattering for square face Indian females. Ask your hairstylist to cut a chin-length bob with soft layers. Avoid blunt-cut bobs as they can make your face look wider.

Soft Waves

Soft waves can add volume to your hair and soften the angular features of your face. Use a curling iron to create loose waves that start below your chin. Avoid tight curls as they can make your face look wider.

Updo Hairstyles

Updo hairstyles can be a great way to show off your square face shape. Try a messy bun or a braided updo that starts at the nape of your neck. Avoid updos that sit high on your head as they can make your face look wider.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for square face Indian females:

  • Avoid hairstyles that sit at your cheekbones as they can make your face look wider.
  • Avoid straight-cut bangs as they can make your face look boxy.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to your hair as they can help soften the angular features of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that sit high on your head as they can make your face look wider.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyle for a square face is one that softens the angular features of your face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, bob haircuts, soft waves, and updo hairstyles are all great options.

Can square face Indian females wear short hair?

Yes, square face Indian females can wear short hair. However, it's essential to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape. A chin-length bob with soft layers can be a flattering option.

Should I avoid center-parted hairstyles if I have a square face?

Center-parted hairstyles can be challenging to pull off if you have a square face. However, if you want to try a center part, opt for a hairstyle with soft waves or layers that start below your chin.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a hair conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these hairstyle ideas, tips, and hair care tips, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your square face Indian female. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
