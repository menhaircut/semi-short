How To Make Big Hair Bows For Babies

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair accessories for your little ones? Why not try making big hair bows for babies instead? Not only are they adorable, but they are also a great way to add a pop of color and personality to any outfit. In this article, we'll show you how to make big hair bows for babies step by step, and provide some tips and tricks along the way.

Description

Big hair bows are a timeless accessory that can be worn by babies of all ages. These bows come in a variety of colors, patterns, and fabrics, making them the perfect accessory to match any outfit. Whether you're looking for a bow to wear for a special occasion or just to add a little extra flair to your baby's everyday look, making your own big hair bow is a fun and easy project that anyone can do.

How to

Materials Needed:

  • Grosgrain ribbon (2 1/2 inches wide)
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread
  • Hot glue gun
  • Alligator hair clip
  • Embellishments (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Cut a piece of ribbon to the desired length (we recommend 24 inches).
  2. Fold the ribbon in half and crease it in the middle to mark the center.
  3. Take the left side of the ribbon and fold it in half, making a loop. Pinch the loop in the middle and secure it with a needle and thread.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the right side of the ribbon, making sure the loops are the same size and shape.
  5. Take the two loops and cross them over each other in the center, creating an "X" shape.
  6. Pinch the center of the "X" together and secure with needle and thread.
  7. Wrap a small piece of ribbon around the center of the bow and secure it with hot glue.
  8. Glue an alligator clip to the back of the bow.
  9. Add embellishments (if desired) to the center of the bow.
  10. Your big hair bow for babies is complete!

Tips

  • Use high-quality grosgrain ribbon for the best results.
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns to create unique looks.
  • Try adding embellishments such as rhinestones, pearls, or buttons for added charm.
  • Make sure the alligator clip is securely attached to the back of the bow.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be afraid to try the steps a few times to get the hang of it.

FAQ

What type of ribbon should I use?

We recommend using grosgrain ribbon that is 2 1/2 inches wide.

Can I use a different type of hair clip?

Yes, you can use any type of hair clip you prefer. However, we recommend using an alligator clip for the best hold.

Are big hair bows safe for babies?

As with any hair accessory, it is important to supervise your baby while they are wearing a big hair bow to ensure their safety.

Hair Care

When wearing big hair bows for babies, it is important to make sure their hair is pulled back and out of their face. We recommend using a gentle baby brush to detangle their hair and using a light hold hairspray to keep the bow in place.

Overall, making big hair bows for babies is a fun and easy project that anyone can do. With a little bit of practice and creativity, you can create adorable hair accessories that will make your little ones look and feel their best. Give it a try and see what kind of bows you can come up with!
