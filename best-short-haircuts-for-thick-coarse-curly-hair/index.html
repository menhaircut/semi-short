Best Short Haircuts For Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Written by Christine Oct 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick, coarse, and curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for thick coarse curly hair that will frame your face and bring out the beauty of your natural curls.

Description

Thick, coarse, and curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right haircut, you can make the most of your natural texture. Short haircuts are a popular choice for those with thick, coarse, curly hair as they are easy to maintain and can help to reduce the appearance of volume.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for thick coarse curly hair, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A layered haircut can help to reduce the appearance of volume, while a bob can help to frame your face and accentuate your curls.

If you have a round face, a short layered bob can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your best features. A long pixie cut is another popular choice for those with thick, coarse, curly hair as it can help to reduce the appearance of volume and keep your curls under control.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Wash and condition your hair using products designed for thick, coarse, curly hair.

Step 3: Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining product to enhance your natural curls.

Step 4: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for thick coarse curly hair, it is important to choose a style that works with your natural texture. Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of heat styling or products as these can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut and maintenance routine for your hair type.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for thick coarse curly hair?

The best short haircut for thick coarse curly hair depends on your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob or long pixie cut are popular choices for those with thick, coarse, curly hair.

How do I style my short curly hair?

To style short curly hair, apply a curl-defining product to damp hair and scrunch your curls as you dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.

How often should I trim my short hair?

It is recommended to trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Regular hair care is important to keep your short thick coarse curly hair healthy and looking its best. Use products designed for thick, coarse, curly hair to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and when you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can make the most of your natural curls and embrace your unique beauty.
