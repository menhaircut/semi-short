Men s Style Back Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut? Look no further than the men's style back haircut. This classic cut has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect men's style back haircut.

Description

The men's style back haircut is a versatile cut that can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair texture. The cut features short sides and back with longer hair on top that is styled back. This cut is ideal for men with thick hair as it allows for plenty of volume and texture.

How To

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you head to the barber, it's important to decide on the style you want. There are several variations of the men's style back haircut, including the slicked-back look, the messy look, and the textured look. Choose the style that best suits your personality and hair type.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before your haircut, it's important to prep your hair. Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup. This will ensure that your hair is clean and ready for the cut.

Step 3: Discuss Your Cut with Your Barber

When you arrive at the barbershop, discuss your desired cut with your barber. They will be able to recommend the best cut for your face shape and hair type. Be sure to communicate any specific requests or concerns you may have.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

The barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. They will use clippers to create a clean, short cut. The length of the sides and back will depend on your personal preference.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Once the sides and back are cut, the barber will move on to the top of your hair. They will use scissors to create texture and volume. The length of the top will also depend on your personal preference.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Apply a small amount of pomade or styling product to your hair and use a comb to style it back. You can choose to slick it back for a more polished look or leave it messy for a more casual look.

Tips

  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your cut.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that best suits you.

FAQ

What face shapes are best suited for the men's style back haircut?

The men's style back haircut is versatile and can work with any face shape.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your cut.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! The men's style back haircut is versatile and can be styled differently for different occasions.

Hair Care

To keep your men's style back haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

The men's style back haircut is a classic look that will never go out of style. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect men's style back haircut.
