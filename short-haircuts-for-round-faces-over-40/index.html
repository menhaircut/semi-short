Short Haircuts For Round Faces Over 40

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 40 and have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features, while also being easy to maintain. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces over 40, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Short haircuts are a great option for women over 40 with round faces. They can help to elongate the face and create a more youthful appearance. However, not all short haircuts are created equal. The key is to find a style that complements your features and works with your hair type.

How To

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are several factors to consider. The first is the length of the cut. Shorter styles tend to work better for round faces, as they help to create the illusion of length. The second factor is the shape of the cut. Styles that are longer in the front and shorter in the back can help to elongate the face. Finally, the texture of the hair is also important. Fine hair may benefit from layers, while thick hair may need to be thinned out.

Step By Step

1. Determine your face shape - This will help you to choose a style that flatters your features. 2. Consider your hair type - Different hair types require different cuts and styles. 3. Research different short haircuts - Look for styles that are suitable for round faces and that you like. 4. Consult with a stylist - A professional stylist can help you to choose the right cut and style for your face shape and hair type. 5. Maintain your haircut - Regular trims and proper hair care can help to keep your short haircut looking its best.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short haircut:

  • Use a volumizing product to add body and lift to your hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs, which can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools to help you achieve your desired look.

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for round faces over 40?

Some of the best short haircuts for round faces over 40 include pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical cuts.

What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for a round face?

Avoid styles that are too blunt or heavy, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Also, avoid styles that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, shiny, and beautiful short haircut. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to break or split.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a styling product with SPF.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right short haircut and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age. Whether you opt for a pixie cut or a layered bob, make sure to choose a style that works with your face shape and hair type. By following these tips, you can achieve a beautiful, low-maintenance hairstyle that flatters your features and boosts your confidence.
