How To Get Natural Messy Hair For Guys

Written by Aprilia Aug 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you want to achieve that effortlessly cool and relaxed look with your hair? Natural messy hair is the answer! It’s a style that does not require a lot of maintenance and is perfect for guys who want a casual and laid-back look. In this article, we will guide you on how to get natural messy hair for guys. Read on to find out more.

Description

Natural messy hair is a style that looks like you just rolled out of bed. It’s a popular hairstyle for guys because it’s low maintenance and easy to achieve. It’s a style that works with any hair type and length, and it can be worn for any occasion. Whether you’re going to work or hanging out with friends, natural messy hair is the perfect style to give you that cool and relaxed vibe.

How to

Here are the steps on how to get natural messy hair for guys:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt and excess oils from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve washed your hair, use a blow dryer to dry it. Use your fingers to comb through your hair while blow drying it to add volume and texture.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a wax or clay-based product for a more natural look. Rub the product between your palms and work it through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends.

Step 4: Mess It Up

Now it’s time to mess it up! Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look. Don’t worry about making it perfect – the key to natural messy hair is imperfection.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect natural messy hair:

  • Use a matte finish product for a more natural look.
  • Don’t use too much product – a little goes a long way.
  • Try different techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tangles and breakage.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about natural messy hair for guys:

What hair types work best for natural messy hair?

Natural messy hair works with any hair type – straight, wavy, or curly.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I achieve natural messy hair with long hair?

Yes, you can still achieve natural messy hair with long hair. Just use more product and focus on adding texture and volume.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect natural messy hair for guys. Remember to embrace imperfection and have fun with your style!
