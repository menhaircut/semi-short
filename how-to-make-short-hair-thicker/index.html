How To Make Short Hair Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair can be a challenge to style, especially if it's thin and lacking volume. But don't worry, there are plenty of ways to make your short hair look thicker and more voluminous. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make short hair thicker. Let's get started!

What Causes Thin Hair?

Before we dive into the tips, let's first understand what causes thin hair. Genetics plays a significant role in determining the thickness of your hair. Other factors that can contribute to thinning hair include stress, hormonal imbalances, and a poor diet. Certain hair products and styling techniques can also damage your hair, making it look thin and limp.

How to Make Short Hair Thicker

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help remove split ends and prevent breakage, which can make your hair look thinner. Aim to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and voluminous.

2. Use Volumizing Products

Using volumizing products can help add body and fullness to your hair. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically designed for fine or thin hair. These products contain ingredients that can plump up your hair strands and give you a fuller look.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help add volume and lift to your roots. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow-dry your hair from the roots to the tips. Use a round brush to add even more volume.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can help create the illusion of thickness and fullness. Use a comb to backcomb your hair at the roots, then gently brush the top layer to smooth out the look. Don't go overboard with teasing, as it can damage your hair over time.

5. Try Hair Extensions

If you want an instant boost of volume, consider trying hair extensions. Clip-in extensions are easy to use and can give you a full, voluminous look in minutes. Just make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for the most natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

1. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it look thinner over time. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help promote hair growth and thickness. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair strong and healthy.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help prevent hair breakage and tangling, which can make your hair look thinner. Silk is also gentle on your hair, reducing friction and preventing damage.

FAQs

1. Can I make my hair thicker naturally?

Yes, you can make your hair thicker naturally by eating a healthy diet, avoiding heat styling, and using natural hair care products that promote hair growth.

2. What haircuts are best for thin hair?

Short haircuts with layers or blunt cuts can help create the illusion of thickness and fullness. Ask your hairstylist for recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Now that you know how to make short hair thicker, it's important to take care of your new voluminous locks. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when wet. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the thick, voluminous hair of your dreams!
