How To Make Your Afro Look Bigger Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly or coily hair, you may find it challenging to style your afro to make it look bigger. Don't worry; there are several ways to achieve a bigger afro without damaging your hair or spending hours in front of the mirror. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your afro look bigger.

  1. How to Make Your Afro Look Bigger
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ

How to Make Your Afro Look Bigger

The key to making your afro look bigger is to stretch your curls and coils. When your hair is stretched, it appears longer, giving the illusion of a bigger afro. Here are some ways to stretch your hair:

  • Braiding or twisting your hair before bed
  • Using hair rollers or flexi rods
  • Stretching your hair with a blow dryer
  • Using a hairband to create a pineapple style

Aside from stretching your hair, you can also use products to add volume and texture. Here are some products you can use to make your afro look bigger:

  • Hair mousse
  • Hair gel
  • Dry shampoo
  • Hair powder

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your afro look bigger:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup and product residue.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  3. Section your hair and braid or twist it before bed.
  4. Undo the braids or twists in the morning and fluff your hair with your fingers.
  5. Apply a hair mousse or gel to add volume and texture.
  6. Use hair rollers or flexi rods to stretch your hair further.
  7. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to stretch it even more.
  8. Remove the hair rollers or flexi rods and fluff your hair with your fingers.
  9. Apply a dry shampoo or hair powder to add more volume and texture.
  10. Use a hairband to create a pineapple style and secure it with bobby pins.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make your afro look bigger:

  • Don't comb or brush your hair too much, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your afro look bigger:

Can I make my afro look bigger without using heat?

Yes, you can stretch your hair without using heat by braiding or twisting it before bed. You can also use hair rollers or flexi rods to stretch your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your hair every 7-10 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What should I do if my hair is too short to create a big afro?

You can still make your hair look bigger by stretching it with braids, twists, or hair rollers. You can also use hair extensions to add length and volume.

Hair Care for Big Afro

Here are some tips on how to take care of your big afro:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk bonnet or using a satin or silk pillowcase.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your afro look bigger and better than ever before. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
