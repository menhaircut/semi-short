How To Cut A Flat Top Haircut Video Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish haircut that is easy to maintain and gives you a sharp and clean look? Then a flat top haircut might be just what you need. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to cut a flat top haircut video step-by-step.

Description

A flat top haircut is a classic style that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile haircut that can be adjusted to suit any face shape or hair type. The cut is characterized by a straight horizontal line on the top of the head, giving it a flat appearance. It is a great option for those who want a low maintenance haircut that is still stylish and trendy.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, and a pair of scissors. It is also important to have a clear idea of the length and style you want to achieve.

Step 1: Preparation

Start by washing and drying the hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides

Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly. Make sure to blend the hair on the sides and back with the top.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head. Start at the front and work your way back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to keep the hair at the back of the top slightly longer than the front.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Use the clippers to create a straight horizontal line on the top of the head. Make sure it is even and straight. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a clean and sharp look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect flat top haircut:

  • Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.
  • Use a longer guard when cutting the sides and back, and a shorter guard when cutting the top.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.
  • Blend the hair on the sides and back with the top to create a seamless look.
  • Keep the hair at the back of the top slightly longer than the front to create a natural slope.

FAQ

Q: How long does a flat top haircut last?

A: A flat top haircut can last anywhere from 2-6 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I cut a flat top haircut myself?

A: It is possible to cut a flat top haircut yourself, but it is recommended to go to a professional barber or hairstylist to achieve the best results.

Q: Is a flat top haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: A flat top haircut can be adjusted to suit any hair type or face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your flat top haircut, it is important to keep it trimmed regularly. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any stray hairs with scissors. You can also use hair products such as wax or gel to style the hair and keep it in place.

Congratulations, now you know how to cut a flat top haircut video step-by-step. With a little practice, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that is easy to maintain.
