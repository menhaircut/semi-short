How To Style Curly Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly thick hair can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve stunning looks that will turn heads. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style your curly thick hair with ease and confidence. Here’s what we’ll cover:

  • Description
  • How to Style Curly Thick Hair
  • Step-by-step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curly Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Thick Hair

Description

Curly thick hair is characterized by tight curls and a lot of volume. It can be a challenge to style because it tends to be frizzy and difficult to manage. However, with the right techniques and products, curly thick hair can look stunning and beautiful.

How to Style Curly Thick Hair

The key to styling curly thick hair is to find the right products that work for your hair type. Here are some products that you should consider:

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil.

2. Leave-in Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to tame frizz and define your curls. Apply it to damp hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Curl Cream

Curl cream helps to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply it to damp hair, and use your fingers to scrunch your curls upwards.

4. Hair Oil

A hair oil can help to add shine and moisture to your curls. Apply a small amount to your ends, and work your way up towards your roots.

Step-by-step Guide

Now that you know what products to use, here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your curly thick hair:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly, and gently pat your hair dry with a towel. Don’t rub your hair, as this can cause frizz.

2. Apply Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly. This will help to detangle your hair and reduce frizz.

3. Apply Curl Cream

Apply a curl cream to your hair, and use your fingers to scrunch your curls upwards. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

4. Diffuse or Air-dry

You can either diffuse your hair with a hairdryer or let it air-dry. If you’re using a hairdryer, use a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz. If you’re air-drying your hair, avoid touching your curls while they dry.

Tips for Styling Curly Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your curly thick hair:

1. Use a Wide-tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush. This will help to prevent breakage and minimize frizz.

2. Don’t Touch Your Curls While They’re Drying

Avoid touching your curls while they’re drying, as this can cause frizz. Let them air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair and won’t cause as much friction as cotton.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly thick hair:

1. Can I Straighten My Curly Thick Hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly thick hair, but it’s important to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat tools too often. Straightening can cause damage to your hair, so it’s best to limit how often you do it.

2. How Often Should I Wash My Curly Thick Hair?

It’s best to wash your curly thick hair every 2-3 days, as washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. How Do I Revive Second-day Curls?

You can revive second-day curls by spritzing them with water and applying a small amount of curl cream. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls upwards, and let them air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer.

Hair Care for Curly Thick Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your curly thick hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant before using any heat tools on your hair, and avoid using heat tools too often.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Styling curly thick hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls. Use this guide to help you style your hair with ease and confidence.
