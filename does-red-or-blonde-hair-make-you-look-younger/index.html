Does Red Or Blonde Hair Make You Look Younger

Written by Oliver Nov 04, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, we often look for ways to maintain our youthful appearance. One popular method is through hair color. But, does red or blonde hair make you look younger? In this article, we’ll explore the answer to this question and provide tips for maintaining your hair color.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that red hair makes you look younger because it adds a pop of color to your face, making it appear brighter and more youthful. On the other hand, blonde hair is often associated with youthfulness and innocence, which is why many women opt for this color as they age.

However, the truth is that neither red nor blonde hair is guaranteed to make you look younger. The key is finding the right shade that complements your skin tone and features.

How to

The first step in determining whether red or blonde hair will make you look younger is to consider your skin tone. If you have cool undertones, blonde hair may be a better option for you. If you have warm undertones, red hair may be more flattering.

It’s also important to consider your natural hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, going blonde may require more maintenance and upkeep. If you have naturally light hair, going red may be easier to achieve.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone.

2. Consider your natural hair color.

3. Choose the right shade of red or blonde that complements your skin tone and features.

4. Consult with a professional stylist to achieve the desired look.

Tips

- Use hair color products that are gentle and nourishing to prevent damage.

- Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.

- Avoid overexposure to the sun, as it can cause your hair color to fade.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair for dimension and depth.

FAQ

Q: Will going blonde or red make me look younger?

A: It depends on the shade that complements your skin tone and features.

Q: Is it safe to color my hair at home?

A: It’s best to consult with a professional stylist to avoid damage and achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of color and how fast your hair grows. Consult with a professional stylist for guidance.

Hair Care

Maintaining your hair color requires proper care and attention. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for color-treated hair. Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage and fade your color. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. And, don’t forget to schedule regular appointments with your stylist to touch up your color and maintain your style.

In conclusion, whether red or blonde hair makes you look younger depends on the shade that complements your skin tone and features. Consult with a professional stylist to achieve the desired look and maintain your hair color with proper care and attention.
