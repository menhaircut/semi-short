Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2021

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face or a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can help you look your best. In this article, we will explore the best short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2021.

Description

A round face or a double chin can make you feel self-conscious about your appearance. However, choosing the right hairstyle can help you highlight your best features and minimize any areas you're not comfortable with. Short hairstyles are a great option for women with round faces and double chins because they can make your face look slimmer and more defined.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the features you want to highlight. A good hairstylist can help you find the perfect haircut that complements your face shape and personal style. Here are some popular short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2021:

Pixie Cut

The Pixie Cut is a popular short hairstyle that can make your face look slimmer and more defined. This haircut is ideal for women with round faces and double chins because it draws attention to your cheekbones and eyes. The Pixie Cut is also low maintenance and easy to style, making it a great choice for busy women.

Bob Cut

The Bob Cut is another popular short hairstyle that can flatter a round face or a double chin. This haircut can be customized to suit your face shape and features. For example, a chin-length Bob Cut can make your face look slimmer, while a longer Bob Cut can draw attention to your jawline.

Layered Cut

The Layered Cut is a versatile short hairstyle that can add volume and texture to your hair. This haircut can also make your face look slimmer by creating the illusion of length. The Layered Cut can be worn straight or curled, making it a great choice for any occasion.

Step by Step

To achieve the perfect short hairstyle, it's important to follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style.
  2. Find a reputable hairstylist who understands your hair type and goals.
  3. Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist.
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your hairstylist.
  5. Maintain your short hairstyle with regular trims and styling.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a hair gel or mousse, to help you achieve the perfect look.
  • Blow dry your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips or headbands to add a touch of glamour.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins:

What is the best short hairstyle for a fat face?

The best short hairstyle for a fat face is one that highlights your best features and minimizes any areas you're not comfortable with. Popular short hairstyles for fat faces include the Pixie Cut, Bob Cut, and Layered Cut.

What is the best way to style a short hairstyle?

The best way to style a short hairstyle is to use a high-quality styling product, such as a hair gel or mousse, and blow dry your hair to add volume and texture. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles.

How often should I get my short hairstyle trimmed?

You should get your short hairstyle trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your short hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your short hairstyle's shape and style.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, there are plenty of short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2021 that can help you look your best. By choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style, and maintaining it with regular trims and styling, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
