How Do You Change Your Hair Color In Animal Crossing

Written by Petter Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of the same old hairstyle in Animal Crossing, changing your hair color can be a fun way to switch up your look. Luckily, it's a simple process that can be done from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to change your hair color in Animal Crossing, step by step.

How to Change Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Before we get started, it's important to note that you'll need to have unlocked the Able Sisters shop in your town to change your hair color. Once you've done that, follow these steps:

Step 1: Visit the Able Sisters Shop

The Able Sisters Shop is located on the main street of your town. Once you're inside, head over to the mirror on the right-hand side of the store.

Step 2: Choose "Change My Look"

When you interact with the mirror, you'll be given a few different options. Choose "Change My Look" to access the hair salon.

Step 3: Select "Hair Color"

Once you're in the hair salon, choose the "Hair Color" option. This will bring up a menu of all the different hair colors you can choose from.

Step 4: Choose Your New Hair Color

Scroll through the menu of hair colors and choose the one you want. If you're not sure which color you want, you can always try on different colors using the "Try It On" feature.

Step 5: Confirm Your Selection

Once you've chosen your new hair color, confirm your selection. The game will ask you if you're sure you want to change your hair color, so make sure you're happy with your choice before you confirm.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair color in Animal Crossing:

  • Try on different colors before you commit to one. You might be surprised by what looks good on you!
  • Consider your character's skin tone when choosing a new hair color. Some colors might clash with certain skin tones.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment! Changing your hair color is a low-risk way to try out a new look.

FAQ: Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Can I change my hair color as many times as I want?

Yes! You can change your hair color as many times as you want once you've unlocked the Able Sisters shop.

Do I need to pay to change my hair color?

Yes, there is a fee to change your hair color. The fee varies depending on the color you choose, but it's usually around 3,000 bells.

Can I change my hair style and color at the same time?

No, you'll need to choose either a new hair style or a new hair color. If you want to change both, you'll need to make two separate trips to the hair salon.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing is a fun way to switch up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair too! Here are a few tips:

  • Visit Harriet at the Shampoodle salon to get a haircut or style your hair.
  • Use a net to catch fish and bugs, rather than running through them. This will help prevent your hair from getting messed up.
  • Wear a hat or headband to keep your hair out of your face on windy days.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to change your hair color in Animal Crossing with confidence. Have fun experimenting with different colors and styles!
