Mens Hairstyles For Long Thick Wavy Hair A Guide For 2023

If you're a man with long, thick, and wavy hair, you might be struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your hair type. Fortunately, there are many mens hairstyles for long thick wavy hair that you can try. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best hairstyles for men with long, thick, and wavy hair. We'll also be sharing some tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it healthy.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for your long, thick, and wavy hair can be challenging. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape: Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will look the best on you. If you have a round face, you might want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.
  • Think about your hair texture: Wavy hair tends to be more voluminous than straight hair, so you'll want to choose a hairstyle that takes this into account. Look for hairstyles that work with your natural texture.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you have an active lifestyle, you might want to choose a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your long, thick, and wavy hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for thick and wavy hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, but don't rub it vigorously. Instead, gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or hair gel, to your damp hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  6. Apply a finishing product, such as a hairspray or pomade, to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long, Thick, and Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long, thick, and wavy hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQs

What are some hairstyles for men with long, thick, and wavy hair?

Some popular hairstyles for men with long, thick, and wavy hair include the messy bun, the slicked-back undercut, and the long layered cut.

How do I style my long, thick, and wavy hair?

You can style your long, thick, and wavy hair by washing and conditioning it regularly, using styling products, blow-drying it with a diffuser attachment, and using a finishing product to hold your style in place.

How do I keep my long, thick, and wavy hair healthy?

You can keep your long, thick, and wavy hair healthy by trimming it regularly, using deep conditioning treatments, avoiding hot tools, using gentle shampoos, and protecting it from the sun.

Hair Care for Long, Thick, and Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with long, thick, and wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to help tame frizz and add shine to your hair.

In conclusion, there are many mens hairstyles for long thick wavy hair that you can try. By choosing the right hairstyle and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and healthy look that suits your hair type. Follow the tips and tricks outlined in this guide to maintain your long, thick, and wavy hair and keep it looking its best.
