How To Cut Bangs So They Curl Under

Written by Luffy Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight bangs always falling in your face? If you want to try something new, why not try cutting your bangs so they curl under? This style is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. In this article, we will show you how to cut bangs so they curl under in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Bangs So They Curl Under
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Bangs
  • FAQs

How to Cut Bangs So They Curl Under

The first step in cutting bangs that curl under is to determine the length you want. It's essential to keep in mind that the shorter your bangs are, the more they will curl under. If you're not sure what length you want, start longer and gradually cut more if needed.

When you're ready to cut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut, and it will be difficult to see the final result.

Before cutting your bangs, section them off from the rest of your hair. Start by combing your bangs straight down and parting them in the middle. Then, comb them out to the sides to create a triangular section.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to cut your bangs so they curl under:

  1. Comb your bangs straight down and part them in the middle.
  2. Comb your bangs out to the sides to create a triangular section.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, making sure to hold it tightly.
  4. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut your bangs at an angle, starting at the center and working your way outward.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Check to make sure your bangs are even.
  7. Take a small section of hair from the front of your bangs and twist it away from your face.
  8. Hold the twisted section of hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut the ends at an angle.
  9. Repeat on the other side.
  10. Use a curling iron or round brush to curl your bangs under.

Tips for Cutting Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your bangs:

  • Start longer and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Hold your hair tightly while cutting to avoid any mistakes.
  • Check your bangs frequently to make sure they're even.
  • Use a curling iron or round brush to curl your bangs under for the best results.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs so they curl under:

Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?

No, it's best to cut your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair is harder to cut, and you won't be able to see the final result.

What length should I cut my bangs?

The length you cut your bangs depends on how much curl you want. The shorter your bangs are, the more they will curl under.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake while cutting your bangs, you can try to fix it by cutting a little bit more to even it out. If it's a significant mistake, you may need to go to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know how to cut bangs so they curl under, here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant before using a curling iron or other heated styling tools.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.

Now that you know how to cut bangs so they curl under, you can try this fun and stylish look for yourself. Follow our step-by-step guide, and don't forget to use our tips to keep your hair looking great. Happy styling!
