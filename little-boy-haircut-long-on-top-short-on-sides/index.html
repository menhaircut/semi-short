Little Boy Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides A Popular Style For Boys In 2023

Written by William Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a stylish and trendy haircut for your little boy, then the "long on top short on sides" style might be just what you need. This haircut features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides, creating a neat and modern look that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will provide a detailed guide on how to achieve this haircut, as well as some tips and advice on how to take care of your little boy's hair.

How to Achieve the Little Boy Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides

Before you start cutting your little boy's hair, it is important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your little boy's hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a more even cut.

Step 2: Create a Guide Line

Using a comb, create a guide line on the top of your little boy's head. This guide line will help you determine how much hair to cut and where to cut it.

Step 3: Use Clippers to Cut the Sides

Using the clippers, start cutting the sides of your little boy's hair. Use a shorter guard for a more dramatic effect or a longer guard for a more subtle look.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Using the scissors, blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair on top using a comb or your fingers. You can choose to leave it messy or slick it back for a more formal look.

Tips for Maintaining the Little Boy Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides

Now that you have achieved the perfect little boy haircut long on top short on sides, it is important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and length
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and shiny
  • Apply a styling product to keep the hair in place throughout the day
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair

FAQ

What age is the little boy haircut long on top short on sides suitable for?

This haircut is suitable for boys of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers.

What face shape is best for the little boy haircut long on top short on sides?

This haircut works well for boys with oval, round, or square face shapes.

Can I style the hair in different ways?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as parting the hair to the side or spiking it up.

Hair Care for Little Boys

It is important to take care of your little boy's hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for children's hair
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out the scalp and hair
  • Comb the hair gently to avoid tangles and breakage
  • Avoid using styling products that contain harsh chemicals

The little boy haircut long on top short on sides is a popular choice for boys in 2023. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this stylish and trendy haircut at home. Remember to maintain the hair properly and follow good hair care practices to keep it healthy and shiny.
