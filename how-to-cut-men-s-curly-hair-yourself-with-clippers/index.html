How To Cut Men s Curly Hair Yourself With Clippers

Curly hair can be a bit tricky to cut, especially if you're doing it yourself. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking cut without having to go to a salon. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's curly hair yourself with clippers, step by step.

  • Step by Step

Description

If you're tired of spending money on haircuts or just want to try something new, cutting your own hair can be a great option. It can be especially helpful if you have curly hair, which can be more difficult to cut than straight hair. With clippers, you can achieve a neat and tidy cut that looks great and is easy to maintain.

How to Cut Men's Curly Hair Yourself with Clippers

To cut men's curly hair yourself with clippers, you'll need a few things:

  • Clippers with guard attachments
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • A towel

Once you have these tools, you're ready to start cutting your hair.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to cut your hair with clippers:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly before you begin.
  2. Choose the right guard attachment. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the right length.
  3. Trim the sides and back. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head, using the guard attachment you've chosen.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top. Comb the hair on top of your head straight up and trim any longer hairs with scissors.
  5. Blend the top and sides. Use the clippers to blend the shorter hair on the sides with the longer hair on top.
  6. Trim the edges. Use the clippers without a guard attachment to trim around your ears and the back of your neck.
  7. Check your work. Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head to make sure everything looks even.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to find the right length. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut too much at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go. It can be hard to see the back of your head, so use a hand mirror or ask someone to help.
  • Take your time. Cutting your own hair can be nerve-wracking, but it's important to go slow and be patient.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting men's curly hair with clippers:

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it?

No, it's best to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may result in uneven lengths.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and trim a little more hair. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut too much at once.

How often do I need to cut my hair?

How often you need to cut your hair depends on how quickly it grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, most people need to cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help reduce frizz and keep your curls looking great.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your own curly hair with clippers and achieve a great-looking cut that's easy to maintain. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll be sure to get great results.
