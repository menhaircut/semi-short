How To Slick Back Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to style. Slicking back your hair is a classic look that can help you achieve a polished and put-together appearance. In this guide, we’ll show you how to slick back thick hair with ease.

Description

Slicking back your hair involves combing your hair back and away from your face using a styling product. This style is perfect for formal events, job interviews, or any occasion where you want to look sharp and professional. However, if you have thick hair, you may find that it’s challenging to achieve this look. Thick hair tends to be coarse, unruly, and resistant to styling. But don’t worry, with the right technique and products, you can slick back your thick hair effortlessly.

How to Slick Back Thick Hair

Follow these simple steps to slick back your thick hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add body and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. You can use a gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your preference. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair back and away from your face. Start from the hairline and work your way back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you want a slicker look, comb your hair straight back. If you want a more casual look, you can comb your hair to one side.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve combed your hair, use a hairspray to set it in place. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly. This will help your hair stay in place all day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a comb with fine teeth to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid slicking your hair straight back.
  • If you’re going for a more casual look, you can leave some hair loose around your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back thick hair:

Can I slick back my hair if I have a lot of hair?

Yes, you can slick back your hair even if you have a lot of hair. Just make sure to use the right products and comb your hair in the direction you want it to go.

What styling product should I use?

You can use a gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your preference. Gel will give you a slicker look, while pomade and wax will give you more texture and hold.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Thick hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Take a break from styling products to give your hair a chance to breathe.

Now that you know how to slick back your thick hair, you can rock this classic look with confidence. With the right products and technique, you’ll have a polished and put-together appearance in no time.
