Best Way To Cut Medium Length Curly Hair A Complete Guide

If you have medium length curly hair, you know just how tricky it can be to cut it properly without losing its natural shape and texture. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we will be sharing the best way to cut medium length curly hair, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to ensure your curls look their best.

  • How To Cut Medium Length Curly Hair

Description

Medium length curly hair can be challenging to cut because of its natural texture and shape. If cut improperly, it can look frizzy and uneven. But with the right technique, you can achieve a great haircut that complements your curls.

How To Cut Medium Length Curly Hair

The best way to cut medium length curly hair is by using the curly girl method. This method involves cutting the hair when it is dry, in its natural state, and without using any heat tools or combs. This ensures that the curls are not stretched out, and the shape of the hair is maintained.

Step By Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when cutting medium length curly hair:

1. Wash your hair

Before cutting your hair, wash it with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and free of tangles.

2. Detangle your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help to remove any knots and tangles so that you can cut your hair with ease.

3. Cut your hair when it is dry

Once your hair is detangled, let it air dry or use a diffuser to dry it. Do not use any heat tools or combs. Once your hair is dry, cut it in its natural state, ensuring that you cut each curl individually.

4. Cut your hair in sections

Divide your hair into sections and cut each section individually. This will help to ensure that you cut each curl to the same length and shape.

5. Trim the ends

Once you have cut your hair, take a pair of scissors and trim any split ends. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Tips for Cutting Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting medium length curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Do not use any heat tools or combs when cutting your hair
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure that you cut each curl individually
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Consider getting your hair cut by a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair

FAQs

Can I cut my curly hair when it is wet?

No, it is best to cut your curly hair when it is dry and in its natural state. This ensures that you cut each curl individually and maintain the shape of your hair.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

It is recommended that you trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my curly hair?

No, it is best to use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can cause uneven cuts and damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser when drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and damage to your curls
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy

By following these tips and the curly girl method, you can achieve a great haircut that complements your natural curls. Remember to trim your hair regularly and take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best.
