How To Cut A Short Pixie Cut Step By Step

Written by Petter Oct 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're trying to achieve a short pixie cut. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can create a chic and stylish look in no time. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to cut a short pixie cut that's perfect for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short pixie cut is a popular hairstyle that features short, cropped hair that's typically cut around the ears and nape of the neck. This look is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that's chic and stylish. Pixie cuts can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type, making it a versatile option for anyone looking to switch up their look.

How to

If you're looking to cut a short pixie cut, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to keep sections of hair in place. It's also important to start with clean, dry hair to ensure an even cut.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to cut a short pixie cut:

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four sections: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the back section

Starting at the nape of the neck, use your scissors to cut the back section of your hair. Cut upward, using the comb to guide your scissors as you go. Be sure to cut in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Use your comb to lift sections of hair and cut upward, following the shape of your head. Be sure to cut in small sections to ensure an even cut on both sides.

Step 4: Cut the top section

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Use your scissors to cut the hair in small sections, using your fingers to create texture and movement. Be sure to cut in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting a short pixie cut:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually cut more to ensure you don't cut too much off at once.
  • Use the comb to guide your scissors and ensure an even cut.
  • Once you're finished cutting, use a razor to create texture and movement in your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my short pixie cut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

Q: Can I style my short pixie cut in different ways?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to messy and textured. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after cutting a short pixie cut. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture and shine.

Cutting a short pixie cut may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve this stylish look. Follow these steps and tips to create a look that's perfect for you.
