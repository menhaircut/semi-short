How To Keep Long Hair Out Of Face Guys

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it out of your face. Whether you're playing sports or just going about your day-to-day activities, long hair can be a nuisance. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to keep your long hair out of your face with some simple tips and tricks.

Description

Long hair can be a great look for guys, but it can also be a challenge to manage. If you're tired of constantly having to brush your hair out of your face, this article is for you. We'll cover everything from hairstyles to accessories that can help you keep your hair under control.

How to

The first step in keeping your long hair out of your face is to find a hairstyle that works for you. There are a variety of options that can work, depending on your hair type and personal style. Some popular options include:

1. Man Bun

A man bun is a popular hairstyle that involves pulling your hair back into a bun at the top of your head. This style works best for guys with longer hair, but can be adapted for shorter hair as well. To create a man bun, start by brushing your hair back and gathering it at the crown of your head. Once you have all of your hair in one place, twist it into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.

2. Ponytail

A ponytail is another classic hairstyle that can work well for guys with long hair. To create a ponytail, simply gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can position the ponytail higher or lower on your head depending on your preference.

3. Braid

A braid can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face while also adding some style. There are a variety of braiding techniques you can use, from a simple three-strand braid to more complex styles like a fishtail or French braid.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the popular hairstyles mentioned above:

Man Bun

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie.

Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  2. Secure the hair with a hair tie.

Braid

  1. Choose a braiding technique (such as a three-strand braid).
  2. Gather your hair and divide it into three sections.
  3. Begin braiding your hair, incorporating more hair as you go.
  4. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your long hair out of your face:

  • Use hair accessories like headbands or bandanas to keep your hair in place.
  • Apply a styling product like gel or mousse to help hold your hair in place.
  • Consider getting a trim every few months to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping long hair out of your face:

1. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's a good idea to get a trim every few months to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. What type of hair accessories work best for keeping hair out of your face?

Headbands and bandanas can be effective at keeping hair out of your face, but you can also experiment with other accessories like hair clips or bobby pins.

3. What type of styling product should I use to keep my hair in place?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some guys prefer a strong-hold gel, while others prefer a lighter mousse.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your long hair to keep it looking and feeling its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your long hair out of your face and looking great.
