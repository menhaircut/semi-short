Slicked Back Hair How To Achieve The Perfect Look

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile look that can be worn for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or a casual day out. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect slicked back look, give you some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Perfect Slicked Back Hair Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Achieve the Perfect Slicked Back Hair Look

The first step to achieving the perfect slicked back hair look is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Slicked back hair works best on medium to long hair, but it can also be achieved on shorter hair. You'll also need a good quality hair gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your hair type and the level of hold you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect slicked back hair look:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly
  2. Apply your chosen product evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way back
  4. If you want a more polished look, use a fine-toothed comb to create a defined part in your hair
  5. Smooth down any flyaways or frizz with a small amount of product
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place all day

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hair look:

  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a lightweight product to avoid weighing it down
  • If you have thick or curly hair, use a stronger hold product to keep it in place
  • Experiment with different parts to find the one that suits your face shape best
  • If you have a receding hairline, try a slicked back undercut or fade to create the illusion of fuller hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked back hair:

Is slicked back hair suitable for all hair types?

Yes, slicked back hair can be achieved on all hair types, but the level of hold required may vary depending on your hair type.

What products should I use for slicked back hair?

You can use a hair gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your hair type and the level of hold you want. Look for products that offer a strong hold without weighing down your hair.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Use a small amount of product and avoid applying it to your roots. You can also use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back hair look in no time. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and radiant.
