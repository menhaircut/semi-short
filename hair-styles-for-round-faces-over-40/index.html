Best Hair Styles For Round Faces Over 40

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 40 and have a round face, choosing the right hairstyle can be a bit tricky. Your face shape may be the same as when you were younger, but your hair texture and skin tone may have changed. You need a hairstyle that not only flatters your face shape but also suits your age and style. In this blog post, we will give you tips and ideas for hair styles for round faces over 40.

Description

Round faces have softer features with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The goal of a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face shape and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. As we age, our skin loses elasticity, and our hair may thin, so a hairstyle that adds volume and texture can be beneficial.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Opt for a hairstyle that has layers to add volume and texture.
  • Choose a style that elongates your face shape, such as a long bob or layers that fall below the chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a style that works with it.
  • Think about your lifestyle and choose a style that is low maintenance and easy to style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a hairstyle that flatters your round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture.
  4. If you have thick or curly hair, use a smoothing serum to tame frizz and add shine.
  5. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.
  6. If you prefer a straight style, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.
  • Choose a style that elongates your face shape.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a style that works with it.
  • Think about your lifestyle and choose a style that is low maintenance and easy to style.
  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What are the best hair styles for round faces over 40?

The best hairstyles for round faces over 40 are those that elongate the face shape and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Some examples include long bobs, layered cuts, and hairstyles with side-swept bangs.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and hairstyles with too much volume on the sides. These styles can make your face appear rounder and wider.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, or using a curling iron or flat iron to create texture.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have some ideas for hair styles for round faces over 40, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. With the right style, you can look and feel your best at any age.
