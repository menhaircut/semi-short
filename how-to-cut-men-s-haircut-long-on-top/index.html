How To Cut Men s Haircut Long On Top A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men? The long on top haircut is the perfect choice for you. This haircut involves keeping the hair on the top of your head long while trimming the sides and back short. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways, from slicked-back to messy and tousled.

Description

The long on top haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that involves keeping the hair on the top of the head long while trimming the sides and back short. This style is versatile and can be styled in many ways, making it a trendy choice for men of all ages.

How to Cut Men's Haircut Long on Top

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's haircut long on top:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to wash it thoroughly with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, and product buildup and make it easier for you to work with.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair by parting it down the middle and dividing it into two equal parts. Clip the sides and back of your hair so that they're out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the sides and back

Using a clipper, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length. Make sure to blend the sides and back with the top of your hair for a seamless look.

Step 4: Cut the top

Using sharp scissors, cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. Start by cutting the hair at the front of your head and work your way back. Make sure to leave the hair longer towards the front and shorter towards the back for a gradient effect.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you've cut your hair, style it in your desired way. You can use a comb, brush, or your fingers to style your hair. Apply some hair product to hold your hair in place, such as wax, gel, or pomade.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long on top haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to cut your hair for a clean and precise cut.
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Blend the sides and back with the top of your hair for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's long on top haircuts:

What face shapes suit the long on top haircut?

The long on top haircut suits most face shapes, including oval, square, and round faces. However, if you have a long or narrow face, you may want to avoid this style as it can make your face look even longer.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

What hair products should I use?

You can use a variety of hair products to style your long on top haircut, including wax, gel, pomade, and hairspray. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Hair Care

To keep your long on top haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with too much product, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to cut men's haircut long on top, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect long on top haircut that suits your style and personality. Good luck!
