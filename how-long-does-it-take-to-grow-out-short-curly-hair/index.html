How Long Does It Take to Grow Out Short Curly Hair?

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short curly hair can be challenging to grow out, but it is possible with patience and care. The process of growing out short curly hair can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years, depending on various factors such as your hair growth rate, hair length, and hair care routine. In this article, we will discuss how to grow out short curly hair and provide tips and tricks to make the process as smooth as possible.

How to

The first step to growing out short curly hair is to stop cutting it. While this may seem obvious, it can be tempting to trim your hair to keep it neat and tidy. However, trimming your hair will only slow down the growing process. Instead, allow your hair to grow naturally and embrace the messy look.

Step by step

Step 1: Stop cutting your hair.

Step 2: Embrace the messy look.

Step 3: Use a wide-tooth comb or finger detangle to avoid breakage.

Step 4: Moisturize your hair regularly with a deep conditioner.

Step 5: Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk bonnet.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you grow out short curly hair:

  • Be patient. Growing out short curly hair takes time.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair and slow down the growing process.
  • Trim your hair only when necessary to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair growth.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates, silicones, and alcohol as they can dry out your hair and cause breakage.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow out short curly hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow out short curly hair, depending on various factors such as your hair growth rate, hair length, and hair care routine.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to speed up the process of growing out short curly hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can help you achieve longer hair while you wait for your natural hair to grow out. However, it is important to choose high-quality extensions that match your hair texture and color to avoid damage and breakage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is crucial to growing out short curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or finger detangle to avoid breakage.

With these tips and tricks, growing out short curly hair can be a rewarding and enjoyable experience. Remember to be patient and embrace the messy look, and soon enough, you will have long, beautiful curls!
