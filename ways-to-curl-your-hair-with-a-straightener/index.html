Ways To Curl Your Hair With A Straightener

Written by Christine Dec 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of using the same curling iron over and over again? Look no further than your trusty straightener! There are many ways to use a straightener to curl your hair, and we’ve got you covered with all the tips and tricks you need.

Description

Curling your hair with a straightener is a great way to achieve a variety of looks, from loose beachy waves to tight ringlets. Not only is it a versatile tool, but it also saves time and money by eliminating the need for a separate curling iron. However, it can be intimidating to use a straightener for curls if you’ve never done it before. That’s why we’ve created this step-by-step guide to help you master the technique.

How To

The first step to curling your hair with a straightener is to prep your hair with a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Next, section your hair and clip it up so that you can work on one section at a time. Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener near the roots, then twist the straightener away from your face as you glide it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process on each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step by Step

1. Prep your hair with a heat protectant spray.

2. Section your hair and clip it up.

3. Take a small section of hair.

4. Clamp the straightener near the roots.

5. Twist the straightener away from your face.

6. Glide it down the length of your hair.

7. Repeat on each section of hair.

8. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a straightener with rounded edges to avoid kinks in your curls.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair.
  • For looser waves, use larger sections of hair and twist the straightener less.
  • Experiment with different angles to achieve different looks.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl my hair with any straightener?

A: Ideally, you want to use a straightener with rounded edges to avoid kinks in your curls. However, you can still use a straightener with flat edges if that’s all you have.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type and how well you prep your hair. Generally, you can expect your curls to last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Can I curl my hair with a straightener if it’s already curly?

A: Yes! Curling your hair with a straightener is a great way to touch up your curls or create a different look.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair before and after using heat tools. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Don’t use heat tools on wet hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a straightener, you can experiment with different looks and styles. Remember to always use a heat protectant spray and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
