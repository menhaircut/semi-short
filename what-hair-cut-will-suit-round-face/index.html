What Hair Cut Will Suit Round Face

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts but none of them seem to suit your round face? Worry no more as we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the different haircuts that will suit your round face. So, let’s get started!

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft and circular shape with no prominent angles. The width and length of the face are almost equal, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. To complement this face shape, it’s essential to choose a hair cut that adds length and angles to your face.

How to Choose the Right Hair Cut

Choosing the right hair cut can enhance your features and give you a flattering look. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair cut:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Identify your face shape. A round face is characterized by a soft and circular shape with no prominent angles.
  2. Choose a hair cut that adds length and angles to your face. Long layered hair, angled bob, and pixie cut are some of the haircuts that will suit your face shape.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs as they will make your face look rounder.
  4. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  5. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair cut for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair cut:

  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair cut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant.

FAQ

What is the best hair cut for a round face?

The best hair cut for a round face is one that adds length and angles to your face. Long layered hair, angled bob, and pixie cut are some of the haircuts that will suit your face shape.

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs as they will make your face look rounder.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and beauty of your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know what hair cut will suit your round face, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get the perfect hair cut for your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
