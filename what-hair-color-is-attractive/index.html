What Hair Color Is Attractive A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Nov 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, you may be wondering what hair color is the most attractive. With so many hair colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide on the perfect shade. In this guide, we'll break down what hair color is attractive and provide tips on how to find the right shade for you.

What Hair Colors are Attractive?

While beauty is subjective, there are certain hair colors that are commonly considered attractive. These include:

  • Blonde
  • Brunette
  • Red
  • Black

Each of these hair colors has its own unique appeal and can be worn in a variety of shades.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color for You

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Here are some tips on how to choose the right hair color for you:

  • If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde and chestnut brown.
  • If you have cool undertones, opt for cool hair colors like platinum blonde and ash brown.
  • If you have a neutral undertone, you can wear a variety of hair colors.
  • If you have green or blue eyes, opt for hair colors that will make your eyes pop like red and blonde.
  • If you have brown eyes, opt for hair colors that complement your skin tone like warm browns and dark blonde.
  • If you have black or dark brown hair, consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair color.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you've decided on a hair color, here's a step-by-step guide to coloring your hair:

  1. Choose a hair dye that is the shade you want and is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Do a patch test to ensure you're not allergic to the hair dye.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Follow the instructions on the hair dye packaging for mixing and applying the dye.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse your hair with cool water and apply the conditioner that comes with the hair dye.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Wash your hair in cool water to prevent the color from bleeding.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage colored hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs

What hair color looks good on everyone?

While there's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, brown hair is a versatile hair color that can look good on many people.

Is it better to dye your hair at home or at a salon?

It's generally safer to dye your hair at a salon as they have professional-grade products and experienced stylists. However, at-home hair dye kits can be a more affordable option.

How often should you touch up your hair color?

It depends on the hair color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in your overall look. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can find a hair color that is both attractive and suits you. Remember to take care of your colored hair with the right products and regular trims to keep it looking its best.
