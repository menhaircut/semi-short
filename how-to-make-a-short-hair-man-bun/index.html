How To Make A Short Hair Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a man with short hair who wants to rock a stylish man bun? Look no further! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to make a short hair man bun. So, grab your hair ties and let's get started.

Description

A man bun is a popular hairstyle that involves pulling the hair back into a bun on the top of the head. While it is usually associated with long hair, men with short hair can still achieve the look with a few simple steps. Making a man bun with short hair can be a bit trickier, but with the right technique and some styling products, you can have a stylish hairdo that will turn heads.

How to

Step 1: Grow your hair out

The first step to making a man bun with short hair is to grow out your hair. While it may take some time, you will need at least 3-4 inches of hair to create a man bun. In the meantime, you can experiment with other hairstyles, such as a slicked-back look or a side part.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Use a dime-sized amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair. This will make it easier to mold your hair into a bun.

Step 3: Pull your hair back

Using a comb or your fingers, pull your hair back into a ponytail on the top of your head. Make sure it is tight, but not too tight that it causes discomfort. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 4: Twist and wrap your hair

Take the hair in the ponytail and twist it into a tight rope. Then, wrap the twisted hair around the hair tie to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To keep your man bun in place, finish with a spritz of hairspray. This will also help to tame any frizz or flyaways.

Tips

- Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

- Don't be afraid to play around with the size and placement of your man bun to find the look that suits you best.

FAQ

Can you make a man bun with short hair?

Yes, you can make a man bun with short hair. However, you will need at least 3-4 inches of hair to create a bun.

What products should I use to make a man bun with short hair?

You can use hair wax, pomade, or styling cream to add texture and hold to your hair.

How do I keep my man bun in place?

Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail and bobby pins to secure any loose strands. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.

- Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make a stylish man bun with short hair that will turn heads.
