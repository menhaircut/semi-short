Hairstyles For Long Face Male 2023 Ultimate Guide

Hairstyles for Long Face Male - 2023 Ultimate Guide

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. Fortunately, there are a variety of hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you look your best. In this ultimate guide, we'll explore the best hairstyles for long face male, how to style them, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Long Face Male
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • Hair Care Advice
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for long face male, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, look in the mirror and note the following:

  • Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all relatively the same width, you have a square face shape.
  • If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your chin comes to a point, you have a heart-shaped face.
  • If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all relatively the same width, you have an oval face shape.
  • If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead and jawline are approximately the same width, you have a long face shape.



Best Hairstyles for Long Face Male

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's explore the best hairstyles for long face male:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can help balance out a long face by adding width to the sides of the head. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Side-parted undercut
  • Textured crop


Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts can also flatter a long face by adding width to the sides. Here are some medium-length hairstyles to consider:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wavy shag
  • Layered cut
  • Quiff


Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can work for long face male as long as they add volume and width to the sides of the head. Here are some long hairstyles to consider:

  • Wavy or curly hair with layers
  • Man bun or ponytail
  • Side-swept bangs with layers
  • Half-up, half-down hairstyle



Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your long face, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair while using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your head.
  • Add texture to your hair with a texturizing paste or spray.
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it back in a bun or ponytail to add volume to the sides of your head.



Hair Care Advice

Proper hair care is important for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long face male:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.



Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid center-parted hairstyles, as they can elongate your face.

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, consider getting a short haircut with layers to add volume to your hair.

Can I still wear a beard if I have a long face?

A well-groomed beard can actually help balance out a long face. Consider a short, trimmed beard or a goatee.



With these tips and hairstyle suggestions, you're sure to find a look that flatters your long face and makes you feel confident.
