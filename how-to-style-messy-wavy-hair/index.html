How To Style Messy Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of trying to tame your messy wavy hair? Do you want to embrace your natural texture and look effortlessly chic? Look no further, because we've got you covered with some easy tips and tricks on how to style messy wavy hair.

How to Style Messy Wavy Hair

The key to styling messy wavy hair is to enhance its natural texture and volume while keeping it under control. Here are some basic steps to follow:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and lengths.
  • Scrunch your hair gently with your fingers to encourage the waves and curls.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat and low speed. Use your fingers to lift and separate the curls while drying.
  • Once your hair is dry, apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add definition and hold.
  • Gently tousle your hair with your fingers for a messy, undone look.
  • Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide on how to style messy wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or product residue. Rinse thoroughly.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and lengths. Comb through with a wide-tooth comb to distribute evenly.
  3. Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it with your fingers to encourage the waves and curls.
  4. Flip your hair back and divide it into sections. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and set it on low heat and low speed.
  5. Start with the bottom sections and lift them up towards your scalp, placing them on the diffuser. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  6. Repeat with the remaining sections, using your fingers to lift and separate the curls while drying.
  7. Once your hair is dry, apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add definition and hold. Scrunch your hair gently with your fingers to distribute the product.
  8. Gently tousle your hair with your fingers for a messy, undone look. Avoid using a brush or comb, as it will break up the curls and create frizz.
  9. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your head and spray evenly.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style messy wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and disrupt the natural texture.
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain the waves and curls overnight.
  • Add some hair accessories like clips or headbands to dress up your messy wavy hair.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace the frizz and flyaways â they add to the natural and effortless look of messy wavy hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling messy wavy hair:

  • Can I style my messy wavy hair without heat? Yes, you can air-dry your hair and use styling products like mousse or gel to enhance the waves and curls.
  • How do I control the frizz in my messy wavy hair? Use a hydrating hair mask or conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and reduce frizz. You can also apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to smooth out the frizz.
  • Can I wear my messy wavy hair to formal events? Yes, you can dress up your messy wavy hair by adding some elegant accessories like a hairpin or a sparkly headband.

Hair Care for Messy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of your messy wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask or deep conditioner to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can embrace your messy wavy hair and look effortlessly chic every day.
