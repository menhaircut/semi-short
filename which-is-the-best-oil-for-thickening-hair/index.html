Which Is The Best Oil For Thickening Hair

Written by Petter Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick and voluminous hair is a dream for many women, and even men. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally thick hair. Some have to put in extra effort to achieve it. One way to do so is to use hair oil. But which is the best oil for thickening hair? In this article, we will discuss the top oils that can help you achieve thicker hair.

Why Use Oil to Thicken Hair?

Hair oils have been used for centuries to nourish and protect hair. They contain essential fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals that can help to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. When applied regularly, hair oils can also stimulate hair growth and increase hair volume.

Top Oils for Thickening Hair

1. Castor Oil

Castor oil is a thick and viscous oil that is rich in fatty acids and vitamin E. It has been used for centuries to promote hair growth and thickness. Castor oil is also known for its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help to soothe an itchy and flaky scalp. To use castor oil, warm it up and massage it onto your scalp and hair. Leave it on for at least an hour before washing it off.

2. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a lightweight oil that is easily absorbed by hair. It contains lauric acid, which can penetrate hair shafts and strengthen them from within. Coconut oil is also a great moisturizer and can help to prevent hair breakage. To use coconut oil, warm it up and apply it to your hair and scalp. Leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it off.

3. Argan Oil

Argan oil is a lightweight oil that is rich in vitamin E and essential fatty acids. It can help to moisturize and strengthen hair, and also protect it from heat damage. Argan oil is also great for taming frizz and adding shine to hair. To use argan oil, apply a small amount to your hair and scalp, and leave it on overnight.

How to Use Hair Oil

Using hair oil is easy. Simply warm up the oil and apply it to your hair and scalp. Massage it in for a few minutes, and then leave it on for at least 30 minutes. You can also leave it on overnight for maximum benefits. To wash it off, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Tips for Thickening Hair

Using hair oil is just one way to thicken hair. Here are some additional tips:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQ

Can hair oil really thicken hair?

Yes, hair oil can help to thicken hair by nourishing and strengthening it from within. It can also stimulate hair growth and prevent breakage, which can lead to thicker hair.

How often should I use hair oil?

You can use hair oil once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and needs. If you have oily hair, you may want to use it less often.

Can I use any oil to thicken my hair?

No, not all oils are suitable for hair. Some oils can actually make your hair greasy and weigh it down. Stick to oils that are lightweight and easily absorbed by hair, such as coconut oil, castor oil, and argan oil.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important if you want it to be thick and healthy. Use hair oils, eat a healthy diet, and avoid using too many styling products. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve the thick and voluminous hair you've always wanted.
