How To Curl Your Hair With A Ghd Straightener

Written by William Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for an easy and quick way to curl your hair, a ghd straightener may be just what you need. With its high-quality plates and temperature control, the ghd straightener is perfect for creating curls that last all day. In this post, we'll show you how to curl your hair with a ghd straightener step by step, as well as provide some tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Curl Your Hair with a ghd Straightener

Before we get started, it's important to note that you should always use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray and brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Adjust the Temperature

Turn on your ghd straightener and adjust the temperature to the level that's suitable for your hair. If you have fine or thin hair, use a lower temperature setting, while those with thick or coarse hair can use a higher setting.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the ghd straightener around it, about halfway down the section. Twist the straightener away from your face and pull it down to the end of the section. Release the hair from the straightener and repeat on the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled all of your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Curling Your Hair with a ghd Straightener

- Start with clean, dry hair

- Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage

- Section your hair into small sections

- Adjust the temperature to suit your hair type

- Twist the straightener away from your face for natural-looking curls

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I curl my hair with a ghd straightener?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a ghd straightener.

How do I use a ghd straightener to curl my hair?

Follow the steps outlined in this post to curl your hair with a ghd straightener.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last all day if you use a heat protectant spray and finish with hairspray.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a ghd straightener, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls in just minutes. Follow these tips and steps, and you'll be on your way to a great hair day every day.
