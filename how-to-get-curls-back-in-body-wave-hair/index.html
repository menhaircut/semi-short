How To Get Curls Back In Body Wave Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have body wave hair that has lost its curls over time? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with wavy hair struggle to maintain their curls. However, there are ways to get them back without spending a fortune on expensive products or treatments.

Description

Body wave hair is a type of hair that has a subtle wave pattern. It's not as curly as spiral or ringlet curls, but it's also not completely straight. Over time, body wave hair can lose its curls due to heat damage, over-styling, or simply just gravity.

How to Get Curls Back in Body Wave Hair

There are several ways to get curls back in body wave hair. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Curling Iron

If you want to get instant curls, a curling iron is the way to go. Start by sectioning your hair and curling it with a medium-sized curling iron. Make sure to curl away from your face to create a natural-looking curl pattern. Once you've curled your entire head, use your fingers to separate and fluff up the curls.

2. Try a Perm

If you're looking for a longer-lasting solution, a perm might be the answer. A perm involves applying a chemical solution to your hair to create curls. It's important to note that a perm can be damaging to your hair, so it's crucial to choose a reputable stylist and take good care of your hair afterwards.

3. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great way to create curls without using heat. Simply wrap sections of your hair around the flexi rods and leave them in overnight. In the morning, remove the rods and separate the curls with your fingers.

4. Apply Mousse

Mousse is a great product for enhancing curls. Apply a small amount of mousse to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while preserving your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get curls back in body wave hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to help restore moisture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product such as mousse or curl cream to damp hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces using hair clips or elastics.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or flexi rods

If you're using a curling iron, start by curling the bottom layers of your hair first. Use a medium-sized curling iron and curl away from your face. If you're using flexi rods, wrap sections of your hair around the rods and leave them in overnight.

Step 5: Remove the curling iron or flexi rods

Once you've curled your entire head or left the flexi rods in overnight, remove them and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get curls back in body wave hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on the method you use and how well you take care of your hair afterwards. Curls created with a curling iron or flexi rods can last up to a few days, while a perm can last several months.

Can I get curls back in body wave hair without using heat?

Yes! Flexi rods and curl-enhancing products like mousse can help you achieve curls without having to use heat.

Is it safe to get a perm?

A perm can be damaging to your hair if not done properly. It's important to choose a reputable stylist and follow their aftercare instructions carefully.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is crucial to maintaining healthy curls. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in good condition:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can get curls back in your body wave hair and enjoy a beautiful, bouncy hairstyle once again!
