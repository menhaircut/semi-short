High Bald Fade Haircut For Black Men

Written by William Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A high bald fade haircut for black men is a trendy and popular hairstyle that has been around for a while. It is a clean and stylish look that is perfect for guys who prefer short haircuts. This haircut is characterized by a gradual taper on the sides and back of the head, leaving a bald or skin fade at the top. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of getting a high bald fade haircut, provide tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description of High Bald Fade Haircut for Black Men

A high bald fade haircut involves cutting the hair very short on the sides and back of the head, with a gradual taper that blends into the hair on top. The fade can start high up on the head, just above the ears or even higher, depending on the desired look. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as a short buzz cut, a crew cut, or a textured crop. The high bald fade haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a High Bald Fade Haircut for Black Men

If you want to get a high bald fade haircut, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose a Style

Decide on the style you want for the hair on top of your head. You can bring a picture of the style you like to show your barber or stylist.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair before you go to the barber or stylist. This will make it easier to cut and style your hair.

Step 3: Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Talk to your barber or stylist about the style you want and ask for their advice on how to achieve it. They will also be able to recommend the best fade for your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your barber or stylist will start by cutting your hair on top to the desired length. They will then use clippers to create the fade, starting at the desired length and gradually tapering down to the skin or bald fade.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the fade is complete, your barber or stylist will style the hair on top according to your preferences.

Tips for Maintaining a High Bald Fade Haircut for Black Men

Here are some tips to help you maintain your high bald fade haircut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep the fade looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a styling product to keep the hair on top in place, such as a pomade, wax or gel.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and cold weather by wearing a hat or beanie.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or blow dryer, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ About High Bald Fade Haircut for Black Men

What face shape is best for a high bald fade haircut?

A high bald fade haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and round. Your barber or stylist can recommend the best fade for your face shape.

How often should I get a high bald fade haircut?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 2-4 weeks to keep your fade looking fresh and clean.

Is a high bald fade haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, a high bald fade haircut is easy to maintain with regular visits to your barber or stylist and the use of good quality hair care products.

Can I style my hair differently with a high bald fade haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair on top in various ways, such as a short buzz cut, a crew cut, or a textured crop.

Hair Care for High Bald Fade Haircut for Black Men

To keep your hair looking healthy and moisturized, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair. Protect your hair from the sun and cold weather by wearing a hat or beanie. Be sure to visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep your fade looking fresh and clean.

In conclusion, the high bald fade haircut for black men is a trendy and stylish look that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. With regular maintenance and good hair care practices, you can keep your fade looking fresh and clean.
