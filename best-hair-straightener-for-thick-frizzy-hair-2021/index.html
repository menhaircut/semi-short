Best Hair Straightener For Thick Frizzy Hair 2021

If you have thick and frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to tame your mane. But with the right hair straightener, you can achieve sleek and straight locks that will last all day. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hair straighteners for thick frizzy hair in 2021.

How to Choose the Right Hair Straightener

When shopping for a hair straightener, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips for choosing the right hair straightener for thick frizzy hair:

  • Look for a straightener with adjustable temperature settings. Thick hair requires a higher temperature to straighten effectively.
  • Choose a straightener with ceramic or titanium plates. These materials distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Consider the width of the plates. For thick hair, wider plates (1.5 inches or more) are more efficient.
  • Choose a straightener with a high heat capacity. Look for a straightener with a heat capacity of at least 400 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step-by-Step Guide for Straightening Thick Frizzy Hair

Now that you have the right hair straightener, it's time to straighten your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to reduce frizz and create volume.
  4. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  5. Starting at the root, clamp a section of hair between the plates of the straightener.
  6. Gently glide the straightener down the length of the hair, being careful not to snag or pull.
  7. Repeat on each section of hair until all of your hair is straightened.
  8. Apply a small amount of serum or oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Straightening Thick Frizzy Hair

Straightening thick frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with these tips, you'll achieve sleek and shiny locks:

  • Use a high-quality hair straightener with ceramic or titanium plates.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Blow dry your hair before straightening to reduce frizz.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that each strand is straightened.
  • Use a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I straighten my hair every day?

A: It's not recommended to straighten your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling to two or three times a week.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: To prevent frizz, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools, and use a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on wet hair?

A: No, it's not recommended to use a hair straightener on wet hair. Always make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tools.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Frizzy Hair

To keep your thick frizzy hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With the right hair straightener and hair care routine, you can achieve sleek and straight locks that will last all day. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to healthy, shiny hair.
