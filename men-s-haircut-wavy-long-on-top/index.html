Men s Haircut Wavy Long On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Wavy hair has been a popular choice for men's hairstyles for years, and it's no wonder why. The texture adds dimension and movement to the hair, making it look effortlessly stylish. One of the most popular styles for wavy hair is the "long on top" look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about getting a men's haircut wavy long on top, including how to achieve the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips.

How to Achieve the Men's Haircut Wavy Long on Top Look

Achieving the men's haircut wavy long on top look requires a few key steps. First, you will need to decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Once you have decided on the length, you will need to make sure that your hair is properly washed and conditioned.

Next, you will need to apply a styling product to your hair. A pomade or wax will work well for this style, as it will help to define the waves and hold the hair in place. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair from root to tip.

Once your hair is styled, you can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style. Alternatively, you can let your hair air dry for a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Instructions for the Men's Haircut Wavy Long on Top

If you're looking to get a men's haircut wavy long on top, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the look.

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a men's haircut wavy long on top is to decide on the length you want your hair to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Make sure your hair is properly washed and conditioned before getting a haircut. This will ensure that your hair is healthy and clean, and will make styling easier.

Step 3: Consult with Your Barber

Before getting a haircut, it's important to consult with your barber. They will be able to advise you on the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it using a pomade or wax to define the waves and hold the hair in place. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair from root to tip.

Step 5: Set Your Style

You can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style, or let your hair air dry for a more natural look.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Haircut Wavy Long on Top Look

To keep your men's haircut wavy long on top looking its best, there are a few tips to keep in mind.

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to maintaining the men's haircut wavy long on top look. Choose a pomade or wax that works well with your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it more difficult to style. Try to wash your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts will help to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the men's haircut wavy long on top suitable for all hair types?

A: The men's haircut wavy long on top can work well for most hair types, but it may require some additional styling for those with straight or fine hair.

Q: How long does the men's haircut wavy long on top typically last?

A: The length of time the men's haircut wavy long on top lasts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, there are a few hair care tips to keep in mind.

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy or curly hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Try to avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, causing it to dry out and become brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your men's haircut wavy long on top looking stylish and healthy.
