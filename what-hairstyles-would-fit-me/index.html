What Hairstyles Would Fit Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In 2023, there are so many different hairstyles to choose from that it can be overwhelming to decide what would look best on you. However, with the right guidance, you can find a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we'll give you tips on how to determine what hairstyles would fit you, as well as provide some popular options to consider.

How to Determine What Hairstyles Would Fit You

Before you start exploring different hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape and hair texture. This will help you narrow down your options and find a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here's how to figure out what would work for you:

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding a hairstyle that fits you is to determine your face shape. The four most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Examine the outline of your face and compare it to the descriptions above. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features.

Determine Your Hair Texture

The next step is to determine your hair texture. Hair texture can be categorized as:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

To determine your hair texture, simply examine your hair and see how it naturally falls. Once you know your hair texture, you can choose a hairstyle that works with it.

Popular Hairstyles to Consider

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, you can start exploring different hairstyles. Here are some popular options to consider:

Short Bob

A short bob is a timeless hairstyle that works well for all face shapes. It's a low-maintenance option that's perfect if you're always on the go. It's also a great way to show off your jawline and neck.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding some layers to give it more movement and volume. Long layers work well for all face shapes and hair textures. They're also versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a dramatic change, consider a pixie cut. This short hairstyle is perfect for oval and heart-shaped faces. It's also a great option if you have fine hair, as it can make it appear fuller.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found a hairstyle that works for you, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

What if I have a unique face shape or hair texture?

If you have a unique face shape or hair texture, don't worry! There are many different hairstyles to choose from, and a professional stylist can help you find the perfect one for you.

What if I want to try a trendier hairstyle?

If you want to try a trendier hairstyle, go for it! Just make sure it complements your face shape and hair texture. A professional stylist can help you achieve the look you want while keeping your hair healthy.

What if I'm not sure what hairstyle I want?

If you're not sure what hairstyle you want, start by looking at pictures online or in magazines. You can also ask your stylist for suggestions based on your face shape and hair texture.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. By determining your face shape and hair texture, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your personal style. Whether you choose a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, make sure to maintain your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments.
