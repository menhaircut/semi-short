Description

Medium length haircuts for round faces have been trending since 2020. These haircuts are perfect for those with round faces as they give an illusion of a longer face shape. Additionally, medium length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in various ways.

How to

Before getting a medium length haircut, it is important to decide on the specific style that suits your face shape. Some popular medium length haircuts for round faces include:

Bob Cut

This is a classic haircut that looks great on round faces. The bob cut is usually shoulder-length and can be styled with a side part or straight bangs.

Layered Cut

A layered cut adds volume and texture to the hair. It is perfect for those with thin hair and can be styled with loose waves or straight.

Lob

The lob is a longer version of the bob cut. It is usually shoulder-length or slightly longer and can be styled with loose waves or straight.

Step by Step

When getting a medium length haircut, follow these steps:

  1. Decide on the specific haircut style that suits your face shape
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your hair
  3. Wash and dry your hair before the haircut
  4. Sit comfortably in the hairstylist's chair and let them work their magic
  5. Once the haircut is done, style your hair according to your preference

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a medium length haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the desired haircut to show your hairstylist
  • Consider the maintenance required for the specific haircut style
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the haircut shape

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts for round faces:

Q: Will a medium length haircut make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, a medium length haircut can give an illusion of a slimmer face shape.

Q: Can I style a medium length haircut in various ways?

A: Yes, medium length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in various ways.

Q: Is a layered haircut suitable for thin hair?

A: Yes, a layered haircut can add volume and texture to thin hair.

Hair Care

After getting a medium length haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply hair serum or oil to keep your hair shiny and healthy
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the haircut shape


Medium length haircuts for round faces are perfect for those who want a versatile and flattering hairstyle. By following the tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain the haircut style and achieve a confident and stylish look.
