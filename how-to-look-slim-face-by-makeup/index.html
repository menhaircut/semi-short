How To Look Slim Face By Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by William Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to appear slimmer and more defined in your face, makeup can be a great tool to help you achieve your desired look. With the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of a more chiseled jawline, higher cheekbones, and a more defined facial structure. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to look slim face by makeup.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Look Slim Face by Makeup
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Look Slim Face by Makeup:

There are a few key steps to follow to achieve a slimmer looking face with makeup. These include:

Contouring:

Contouring is the process of using makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face, which can help create the illusion of a more defined bone structure. To contour your face, use a matte powder or cream product that is a few shades darker than your natural skin tone. Apply it in the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and around your hairline, blending it out with a brush or sponge.

Highlighting:

Highlighting is the process of using a lighter shade of makeup to bring out the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone. Use a cream or powder product that is a few shades lighter than your natural skin tone and apply it to the tops of your cheekbones, down the center of your nose, and on your brow bone.

Blush:

Adding a pop of color to your cheeks can help create a more youthful and defined look. Choose a blush that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks, blending it out towards your temples.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to achieve a slim face makeup look:

  1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
  2. Apply your foundation as you normally would.
  3. Use a contouring product to create shadows on the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and around your hairline.
  4. Use a highlighting product to bring out the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone.
  5. Add a pop of color to your cheeks with blush.
  6. Blend everything out with a brush or sponge to create a seamless look.
  7. Finish with a setting spray to keep your makeup in place all day.

Tips and Tricks:

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve a slim face makeup look:

  • Choose a contouring product that is a few shades darker than your natural skin tone for the most natural look.
  • Use a small, fluffy brush to apply your contouring and highlighting products for more precise application.
  • Blend everything out well to avoid any harsh lines or streaks.
  • Experiment with different blush shades to find the perfect color for your skin tone.
  • Apply your makeup in natural light to ensure you're achieving the most natural look possible.

FAQs:

Q: Can makeup really make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes! With the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of a more defined and chiseled bone structure.

Q: What products do I need to achieve a slim face makeup look?

A: You'll need a contouring product, a highlighting product, and a blush.

Q: What's the best way to apply contouring and highlighting products?

A: Use a small, fluffy brush to apply the products in the desired areas, and blend everything out well to avoid any harsh lines or streaks.

Hair Care:

In addition to makeup, your hairstyle can also help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Try a sleek ponytail or an updo to pull your hair away from your face and create a more defined jawline. You can also try adding volume to the crown of your head to create a more elongated facial shape.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a slimmer looking face with makeup. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and have fun with it!
