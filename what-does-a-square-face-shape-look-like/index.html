What Does A Square Face Shape Look Like

Written by Eveline Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you unsure of what your face shape is? Do you think you might have a square face shape? If so, this blog post is for you. We will be discussing what a square face shape looks like, how to determine if you have one, and tips for hair care.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped chin. The length and width of the face are usually equal, and the cheekbones are also prominent. This face shape can sometimes give off a more masculine appearance, but with the right styling, it can also look very feminine.

How to Determine if You Have a Square Face Shape

There are a few ways to determine if you have a square face shape. The easiest way is to look at your face in a mirror and see if it resembles the description above. You can also measure your face to see if the length and width are equal. Another way is to take a photo of yourself and draw lines from the hairline to the jawline and from the widest part of the cheekbones to the other side. If the lines form a square, you likely have a square face shape.

Step by Step

To determine if you have a square face shape:

  1. Look at your face in a mirror and see if it resembles the description above.
  2. Measure your face to see if the length and width are equal.
  3. Take a photo of yourself and draw lines from the hairline to the jawline and from the widest part of the cheekbones to the other side. If the lines form a square, you likely have a square face shape.

Tips

If you have a square face shape, there are a few tips to keep in mind when it comes to hair care:

  • Try to avoid hairstyles that draw attention to the jawline, such as blunt bobs or center parts.
  • Soft, layered hairstyles can help soften the strong features of a square face shape.
  • A side part can also help balance out the face.
  • Long, side-swept bangs can also help soften the look of a square face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about square face shapes:

Q: Can makeup help soften the look of a square face shape?

A: Yes, contouring with makeup can help soften the look of a square face shape. By shading the jawline and temples, you can create a more oval-shaped face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that should be avoided if you have a square face shape?

A: Yes, hairstyles that draw attention to the jawline, such as blunt bobs or center parts, should be avoided if you have a square face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for square face shapes, it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Soft, layered hairstyles can help soften the look of a square face shape, while a side part can help balance out the face. Long, side-swept bangs can also help soften the look of a square face shape. Avoid hairstyles that draw attention to the jawline, such as blunt bobs or center parts.

Contouring with makeup can also help soften the look of a square face shape. By shading the jawline and temples, you can create a more oval-shaped face. When it comes to hair care products, look for volumizing products that can help add volume to the top of the head, which can help balance out the face.


In conclusion, a square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped chin. If you have a square face shape, try to avoid hairstyles that draw attention to the jawline and instead opt for soft, layered hairstyles or a side part. Contouring with makeup can also help soften the look of a square face shape. With the right styling and hair care, you can enhance your natural features and look your best.
