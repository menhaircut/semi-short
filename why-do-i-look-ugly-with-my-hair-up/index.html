Why Do I Look Ugly With My Hair Up

Written by Bardi Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are someone who has ever asked this question, you are not alone. Many people feel that they don't look their best with their hair up, and it can be frustrating to try and figure out why. In this blog post, we will explore some of the reasons why you might feel this way and what you can do to feel more confident with your hair up.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

There are a few different reasons why you might feel that you look ugly with your hair up. One of the most common reasons is that you simply don't like the way your face looks with your hair pulled back. This can be because you feel that your face looks too wide or too narrow, or that your forehead looks too big without your hair to cover it.

Another reason why you might feel that you look ugly with your hair up is that you are not used to seeing yourself with this hairstyle. If you are used to wearing your hair down all the time, it can be a shock to see yourself with your hair up, and it may take some time to get used to the new look.

How to

If you want to feel more confident with your hair up, there are a few things that you can try.

Experiment with different updos

There are many different updo styles that you can try, and some may be more flattering for your face shape than others. Play around with different styles and see which ones you like best.

Accessorize

Adding accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scarves can help to distract from any features that you don't like about your face.

Practice

The more you wear your hair up, the more comfortable you will become with the look. Practice wearing your hair up at home or on days when you don't have any important events to attend.

Step by Step

If you are new to wearing your hair up, here is a simple step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Brush your hair back and secure it into a low ponytail with an elastic band
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins
  4. Use hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you feel more confident with your hair up:

  • Try to embrace your unique features rather than hiding them
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a stylist or friend
  • Remember that confidence comes from within

FAQ

Q: Is there a certain face shape that looks better with hair up?

A: While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, many people with heart-shaped faces find that they look great with their hair up.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have short hair?

A: Yes, there are plenty of updo styles that work well on short hair. Talk to your stylist for recommendations.

Hair Care

When wearing your hair up, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage.

Use a gentle hair tie

Avoid using elastic bands that can pull and break your hair. Instead, use a soft, fabric hair tie.

Don't wear your hair up too tight

Pulling your hair back too tightly can cause tension headaches and damage to your hair.

Take breaks

Give your hair a break from being up all day by taking it down for a few hours each day.

Conclusion

Feeling ugly with your hair up is a common struggle, but there are things that you can do to feel more confident. Experiment with different updo styles, accessorize, and practice wearing your hair up. Remember that confidence comes from within, and embracing your unique features is key to feeling beautiful.
