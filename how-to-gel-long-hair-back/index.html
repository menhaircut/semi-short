How To Gel Long Hair Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Gelling long hair back is a classic hairstyle that can look sleek and stylish. However, achieving this look can be challenging if you’re not familiar with the process. In this guide, we’ll show you how to gel long hair back step by step. We’ll also provide tips on how to maintain the style and answer some frequently asked questions about hair gelling.

Table of Contents

  • How to Gel Long Hair Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Gelling Long Hair Back
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Gel Long Hair Back

Gelling long hair back requires a few key tools and products. You’ll need:

  • Gel (preferably a strong hold gel)
  • Comb or brush
  • Hairdryer (optional)

Once you have these tools, you can start the process of gelling your hair back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt or oil from your scalp and hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it’s damp. You can also use a hairdryer if you prefer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start applying gel.

Step 3: Apply Gel

Take a small amount of gel (about the size of a quarter) and rub it between your palms. Apply the gel evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the front and top sections.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb or brush, comb your hair back starting from the front. Make sure you comb your hair evenly, so there are no clumps of gel. If you have long hair, you may need to separate it into sections as you comb it back.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is combed back, you can style it as desired. If you want a sleek look, use your hands to smooth down any flyaways. You can also use a hairdryer on a low setting to help set the gel.

Tips for Gelling Long Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect gelled back hairstyle:

  • Use a strong hold gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Comb your hair back in sections if you have long hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low setting to help set the gel.
  • Use a small amount of gel to avoid clumps.
  • Apply gel evenly throughout your hair.
  • Smooth down flyaways with your hands for a sleek look.

FAQ

Can I Use Gel on Wet Hair?

It’s best to use gel on damp hair rather than wet hair. This will help the gel to spread evenly throughout your hair and prevent clumps.

How Long Will the Style Last?

The longevity of your gelled back hairstyle depends on the type of gel you use and your hair type. Generally, gelled hair can last up to 8 hours or more.

How Do I Remove Gel from My Hair?

To remove gel from your hair, simply wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure you rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all the gel.

Hair Care Tips

Using gel on your hair can be drying, so it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using gel on a daily basis.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and stylish gelled back hairstyle that will turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and promote healthy hair growth.
