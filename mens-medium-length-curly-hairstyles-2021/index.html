Men s Medium Length Curly Hairstyles 2021 A Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you're in luck! Curly hair is in style and there are many great options for men's medium length curly hairstyles in 2021. Whether you're looking for something classic or trendy, there's a medium length curly hairstyle that will suit you. In this guide, we'll go over how to choose the right hairstyle, tips for styling and maintenance, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Men's Medium Length Curly Hairstyle

When choosing a medium length curly hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are a few examples:

The Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that works well for men with curly hair. To achieve this look, grow out your hair to medium length and part it on the side. This style works well for men with oval or oblong face shapes.

The Messy Curls

If you have thick, voluminous curls, try a messy look. This style is easy to maintain and looks great with a beard. To achieve this look, keep your hair at medium length and use a curl defining product to enhance your natural curls.

The Afro

The afro is a bold and iconic hairstyle that works well for men with curly hair. To achieve this look, grow out your hair to medium length and use a pick to shape your curls into a round shape. This style works well for men with round face shapes.

How to Style Men's Medium Length Curly Hair

Styling men's medium length curly hair can be tricky, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Here are a few tips:

Use a Curl Defining Product

To enhance your natural curls, use a curl defining product like mousse, gel, or cream. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your curls to create texture and volume.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your curly hair can cause frizz and damage. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and shape your curls.

Apply Product Sparingly

When using styling products, less is more. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and add more as needed.

FAQs About Men's Medium Length Curly Hairstyles

What is the Best Haircut for Men with Curly Hair?

The best haircut for men with curly hair is one that complements your face shape and hair type. Men with oval or oblong face shapes can try a classic side part or messy curls, while men with round face shapes can try an afro or textured crop.

How Often Should I Trim My Curly Hair?

It's recommended that you trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

How Do I Prevent Frizz?

To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair and use a curl defining product to enhance your natural curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Men's Medium Length Curly Hair Care Tips

Shampoo Less

Curly hair tends to be dry, so shampooing less often can help retain moisture. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

Condition Often

Conditioning your hair is important for maintaining its health and moisture. Use a conditioner specifically designed for curly hair and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage curly hair. Try air drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer instead.

With the right hairstyle, styling techniques, and maintenance routine, men with curly hair can achieve a great look. Try out some of our tips and see what works best for you!
