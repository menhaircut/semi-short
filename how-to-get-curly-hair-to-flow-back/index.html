How To Get Curly Hair To Flow Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how hard it can be to style it in a way that looks good and stays put. One of the most popular styles for curly hair is to have it flow back, but achieving this look can be tricky. In this guide, we will show you how to get curly hair to flow back in a way that is easy and effective.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair to Flow Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain the Style
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair to Flow Back

The first step in getting your curly hair to flow back is to start with clean, wet hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and personal preference.

Next, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb through your hair and distribute the product evenly. Then, flip your hair upside down and scrunch it gently to encourage the curls to form.

Once you have scrunched your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and move it around to dry all areas of your head. Make sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, wet hair.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly.

4. Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it gently.

5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.

6. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and move it around to dry all areas of your head.

7. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips to Maintain the Style

To maintain the flow-back style, make sure to use a silk or satin pillowcase when you sleep. This will reduce friction and prevent your hair from becoming frizzy. You can also use a silk or satin hair wrap to protect your hair while you sleep.

Another tip is to avoid brushing your hair when it is dry. This can cause your curls to become frizzy and lose their shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair when it is wet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

A: No, using a regular towel can cause your hair to become frizzy. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently dry your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the techniques outlined in this guide, you can achieve a beautiful flow-back style for your curly hair. Remember to be patient and experiment to find what works best for your hair type and personal style.
