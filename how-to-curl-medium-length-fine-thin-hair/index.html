How To Curl Medium Length Fine Thin Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length fine thin hair, you might think that curling your hair is impossible. But fear not, with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day. In this article, we will guide you through the process of curling your medium length fine thin hair, from preparation to finishing touches.

Preparation

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture to your hair. If your hair tends to get oily quickly, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and give your hair some grip.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron. It's also a good idea to apply a styling mousse or gel to give your hair some hold.

Tools

The right tools can make all the difference when it comes to curling your hair. For medium length fine thin hair, we recommend using a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or less. A smaller barrel size will create tighter curls that will hold better in fine hair.

You should also invest in a good quality heat protectant spray, styling mousse or gel, and hairspray to keep your curls in place.

How to Curl Medium Length Fine Thin Hair

Follow these simple steps to curl your medium length fine thin hair:

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back. Use a clip or hair tie to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 2: Start curling

Take a small section of hair, about 1 inch wide, and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold for 10-15 seconds and release. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 3: Set your curls

Once all sections of your hair are curled, use a light hold hairspray to set your curls in place.

Step 4: Finish with a shine spray

Finish your look with a shine spray to add some extra shine to your curls.

Tips for Curling Medium Length Fine Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you curl your medium length fine thin hair:

  • Use a smaller barrel size curling iron for tighter curls that will hold better in fine hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  • Apply a styling mousse or gel to give your hair some hold.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to set your curls in place.
  • Finish your look with a shine spray to add some extra shine to your curls.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on a few factors, such as the texture of your hair and the humidity in the air. On average, you can expect your curls to last for 6-8 hours.

Q: Can I curl my hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can curl your hair without using heat by using methods such as braiding or twisting your hair and leaving it overnight.

Hair Care for Medium Length Fine Thin Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length fine thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture to your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and give your hair some grip.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray whenever you use heat styling tools.

Now that you know how to curl your medium length fine thin hair, go ahead and give it a try. With the right techniques and tools, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day.
