What Lip Colour Suits My Skin Tone

Written by William Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right lip colour can be tricky, especially if you are not sure what suits your skin tone. Different skin tones have different undertones that can make certain shades look great or not so great. In this article, we will help you find the perfect lip colour for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

First and foremost, it is important to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have warm undertones, your skin has a yellow or golden hue. If you have cool undertones, your skin has a pink or blue hue. If you have neutral undertones, your skin has a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can start looking for lip colours that suit you best. Generally, warm skin tones suit warmer shades like reds, oranges, and browns, while cool skin tones suit cooler shades like pinks, purples, and blues. Neutral skin tones can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

How to

The easiest way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear a mix of both, you have neutral undertones.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewellery you wear. If you look better in gold jewellery, you have warm undertones. If you look better in silver jewellery, you have cool undertones. If you look good in both, you have neutral undertones.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your skin tone.

Step 2: Look for lip colours that suit your skin tone.

Step 3: Experiment with different shades to find your perfect match.

Tips

1. If you have warm undertones, look for lip colours with warm undertones like reds, oranges, and browns.

2. If you have cool undertones, look for lip colours with cool undertones like pinks, purples, and blues.

3. If you have neutral undertones, experiment with both warm and cool shades to find your perfect match.

4. When trying on different lip colours, make sure to apply them to your lips and not just your hand. The colour can look different on your lips than it does on your hand.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear any lip colour with any skin tone?

A: While you can wear any lip colour you want, certain shades may look better on certain skin tones than others.

Q: Are there any lip colours that suit all skin tones?

A: Yes, neutral shades like nude, beige, and light pink can suit all skin tones.

Q: How do I know if a lip colour is too bold for my skin tone?

A: If a lip colour looks too bright or overpowering on your skin, it may be too bold for your skin tone.

Hair Care

Remember that your hair colour can also affect how lip colours look on your skin. If you have warm undertones and blonde or red hair, warmer shades like reds and oranges can look great. If you have cool undertones and dark hair, cooler shades like purples and blues can look great. Experiment with different shades to find what suits you best.

Choosing the right lip colour for your skin tone can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident. Use these tips and tricks to find your perfect match.
