What s Taper Fade A Complete Guide

Written by William Nov 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're into grooming and keeping your hair looking fresh and stylish, you've probably heard of the term "taper fade." Taper fade is a popular hairstyle that has been around for many years, and it's still trending in 2023. In this guide, we'll explain everything you need to know about taper fade, including what it is, how to get it, tips, and more.

What's Taper Fade?

Taper fade is a men's haircut that involves gradually decreasing hair length from the top down to the neck. The hair on the top of the head is left longer than the hair on the sides and back, which gradually fades to a shorter length. The result is a clean, polished look that can be customized to suit any style or preference.

How to Get Taper Fade

Getting a taper fade haircut can be done at a barbershop or salon. However, it's crucial to find a skilled stylist who has experience in working with different hair types and textures. Here are the steps involved in getting a taper fade:

1. Discuss your desired look with your stylist

Before your barber or stylist starts cutting your hair, it's essential to discuss the look you want to achieve. You can show them pictures or describe the style you have in mind. They can then advise you on the best taper fade style for your hair type and face shape.

2. Determine the length of hair to keep on top

The next step is to decide how much hair you want to keep on top of your head. This will depend on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve. The stylist will use a comb and scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length.

3. Decide on the fade type

There are different types of fades, including low fade, high fade, and mid fade. The type of fade you choose will depend on your style preference and the shape of your head. The stylist will use clippers to gradually taper the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.

4. Blend the hair

The final step is to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. This involves using scissors or clippers to create a smooth transition between the different hair lengths. The stylist will then use a razor or trimmer to clean up the edges and create a sharp, polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Taper Fade

Once you have your taper fade, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help you keep your taper fade looking great:

1. Visit your barber regularly

To keep your taper fade looking sharp, it's important to get it touched up regularly. Visit your barber every 2-4 weeks for a trim and clean up the edges.

2. Use the right hair products

Using the right hair products can help you style and maintain your taper fade. Use a high-quality hair gel, pomade, or wax to style your hair and keep it looking neat and polished.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause it to dry out and break. Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners and blow dryers too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can taper fade work for all hair types?

Yes, taper fade can work for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. However, the style and length of the fade may vary depending on your hair type and texture.

2. How long does taper fade last?

Taper fade can last for several weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. It's recommended to get a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep your taper fade looking sharp.

3. Can I style my taper fade differently?

Yes, you can style your taper fade differently based on your personal preference and the occasion. You can use hair gel, pomade, or wax to create different looks and add some texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Taper Fade

Proper hair care is essential to keep your taper fade looking fresh and stylish. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your taper fade:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair at least twice a week to keep it clean and healthy. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

2. Use hair products that suit your hair type

Using the right hair products can help you style and maintain your taper fade. Choose hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair to dry out and become damaged. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF protection when you're out in the sun for an extended period.

Conclusion

Taper fade is a timeless hairstyle that can elevate your grooming game. Whether you're going for a low fade, high fade, or mid fade, it's essential to find a skilled stylist who can create the look you want. With proper maintenance and care, your taper fade can last for weeks and keep you looking sharp and stylish.
