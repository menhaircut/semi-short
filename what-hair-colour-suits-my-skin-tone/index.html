What Hair Colour Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to try something new? Choosing the right hair colour can be a bit tricky, especially if you're not sure what suits your skin tone. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair colour for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Colour
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Hair Colour
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Choosing the right hair colour for your skin tone can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. Hair colour that complements your skin tone can brighten your complexion, while the wrong hair colour can make your skin look dull and washed out. In general, people with warm skin tones should opt for warm hair colours, while people with cool skin tones should choose cool hair colours.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Before you decide on a new hair colour, it's important to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tone: warm and cool. People with warm skin tones have golden or yellow undertones, while people with cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair colour that complements it. If you have warm skin tones, opt for hair colours like golden blonde, honey blonde, caramel, copper, or auburn. If you have cool skin tones, go for hair colours like platinum blonde, ash blonde, ash brown, jet black, or cool reds like burgundy or cherry.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right hair colour for your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone: warm or cool.
  2. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair colour and eye colour when selecting a new hair colour.
  4. Consult with a hair stylist to determine the best hair colour for you.
  5. Do a patch test before dyeing your hair to ensure that you're not allergic to the hair dye.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Colour

Once you've found the perfect hair colour for your skin tone, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining hair colour:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for colour-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the colour to fade.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as heat can also cause the colour to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your hair colour.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour:

  • Can I dye my hair a different colour than my skin tone? Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like, regardless of your skin tone. However, choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone can make a big difference in your overall appearance.
  • What hair colours should I avoid if I have warm skin tones? If you have warm skin tones, avoid hair colours with cool undertones, like platinum blonde or ash brown.
  • How often should I touch up my hair colour? It depends on the individual and the hair colour. Some hair colours require more frequent touch-ups than others. Consult with your hair stylist to determine how often you should touch up your hair colour.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or relaxers.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Choosing the right hair colour for your skin tone can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hair colour to complement your complexion. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
