Best Haircut For Small Long Face A Comprehensive Guide

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your small long face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with this face shape find it challenging to select a hairstyle that complements their features. In this article, we will guide you through the best haircut for small long face, including a description of the face shape, how-to tips, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

Description

A small long face is defined as having a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. It is a beautiful and unique face shape that requires a specific haircut style to enhance its features. The key is to add volume and width to the crown and sides while keeping the length below the chin.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Small Long Face

Choosing the right haircut for small long face can be a challenge, but with these tips, you can find the perfect style:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Make sure to choose a haircut that complements your face shape. For small long face, avoid styles that add length to the face, such as a long and straight cut. Instead, opt for a layered style with volume on the sides.

2. Look for Volume

Adding volume to the crown and sides of your hair can balance out the narrow forehead and prominent cheekbones. A layered cut or a side-swept fringe can achieve this effect.

3. Keep Length Below the Chin

Short bobs or pixie cuts can make a small long face appear even longer. Keep the length below the chin to balance out your features and create a more proportionate look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best haircut for small long face:

1. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Ask your hairstylist for recommendations on the best haircut for your small long face. They can recommend a style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

2. Choose a Layered Cut

A layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it appear fuller and wider. Ask your hairstylist for long layers that start at the cheekbones and taper down to the ends.

3. Add Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften the forehead and add volume to the sides. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the cheekbones and tapering down to the ends.

4. Style with Volume

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown and sides to create volume.

Tips for Styling Small Long Face Hair

Here are some tips for styling your small long face hair:

1. Add Layers

Adding layers can create volume and texture in your hair, making it appear fuller and wider.

2. Use a Volumizing Product

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Apply the product to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush to create volume.

3. Avoid Straightening

Avoid straightening your hair, as this can make a small long face appear even longer. Instead, embrace your natural texture and add volume with a layered cut and volumizing product.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for a small long face?

The best haircuts for a small long face are layered cuts with volume on the sides and a length below the chin. Side-swept bangs can also add volume and balance out the face.

Can I wear a pixie cut with a small long face?

A pixie cut can make a small long face appear even longer, so it's not the best choice. Instead, choose a layered cut with volume on the sides and a length below the chin.

Hair Care for Small Long Face

Here are some hair care tips for small long face:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can lead to dryness and damage.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. Avoid using hot tools on a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

In conclusion, the best haircut for small long face is a layered cut with volume on the sides and a length below the chin. Side-swept bangs can also add volume and balance out the face. Use a volumizing product and follow proper hair care to keep your hair healthy and voluminous. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations on the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. With these tips, you can find the perfect haircut to enhance your features and create a proportionate look.
