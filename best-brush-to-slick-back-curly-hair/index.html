Best Brush To Slick Back Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially when you want to slick it back. Finding the right brush is key to achieving the sleek, polished look you desire. In this article, we'll discuss the best brushes to use for slicking back curly hair, how to properly use them, and provide some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Slick Back Curly Hair
  • Step-by-step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

Description

The best brush to use for slicking back curly hair is a paddle brush with wide, flat bristles. This type of brush is designed to detangle and smooth hair, making it ideal for creating a sleek, polished look. It's important to choose a brush with bristles that are gentle on your hair, as curly hair can be prone to breakage and damage.

How To Slick Back Curly Hair

Slicking back curly hair requires a bit of effort and the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Paddle brush with wide, flat bristles
  • Styling gel or pomade
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair tie or clip

Step-by-step Guide

Follow these steps to slick back your curly hair:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling gel or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots and the back of your head.
  4. Using your paddle brush, begin brushing your hair back from your forehead towards the crown of your head.
  5. Continue brushing your hair back until it's smooth and slicked back.
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  7. If necessary, use a hair tie or clip to secure your hair in place.
  8. Finish off with a light spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

What is the best brush to use for slicking back curly hair?

A paddle brush with wide, flat bristles is the best brush for slicking back curly hair. It's designed to detangle and smooth hair, making it ideal for creating a sleek, polished look.

Can I use a regular brush to slick back my curly hair?

No, a regular brush is not recommended for slicking back curly hair. It can cause breakage and damage, especially if your hair is prone to tangles and knots. Use a paddle brush with wide, flat bristles instead.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry or curly hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and using the right brush, you can achieve a sleek, polished look for your curly hair. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and always use high-quality products to keep it healthy and shiny.
