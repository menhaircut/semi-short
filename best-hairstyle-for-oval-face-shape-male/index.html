Best Hairstyles For Oval Face Shape Male A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Nov 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you an oval-faced man looking for the best hairstyle to suit your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oval face shape male, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your new look. Let's get started!

Description:

The oval face shape is often considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. This face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion of features, including a well-defined jawline, high cheekbones, and a slightly rounded forehead. The key to finding the best hairstyle for oval face shape male is to accentuate your best features while minimizing any imperfections.

How to:

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for oval face shape male is to determine the length and texture of your hair. From there, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your natural features. Here are some popular hairstyles for oval face shape male:

1. Pompadour:

The pompadour is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval face shape male. This style involves slicking back the sides of your hair and creating a large, voluminous quiff on top. This style is perfect for those with thick, wavy hair and can be achieved with a pomade or styling cream.

2. Short Buzz Cut:

The short buzz cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for oval face shape male with a more masculine appearance. This style involves cutting your hair short all over and can be achieved with a pair of clippers. This style is perfect for those with thinning hair or receding hairlines.

3. Side Part:

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval face shape male with medium-length hair. This style involves parting your hair to one side and combing it neatly to create a sleek, sophisticated look. This style can be achieved with a pomade or styling cream.

Step by Step:

Now that you know some popular hairstyles for oval face shape male, let's go over how to achieve them:

1. Pompadour:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair
Step 2: Apply a pomade or styling cream to your hair
Step 3: Use a comb to create a side part
Step 4: Comb the sides of your hair back and the top forward
Step 5: Use a blow dryer to create volume in the front
Step 6: Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

2. Short Buzz Cut:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair
Step 2: Use a pair of clippers to cut your hair short all over
Step 3: Use a comb to clean up any loose hairs
Step 4: Finish with a trimmer to create clean lines around the ears and neckline

3. Side Part:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair
Step 2: Apply a pomade or styling cream to your hair
Step 3: Use a comb to create a side part
Step 4: Comb your hair to one side and neaten up any stray hairs
Step 5: Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips:

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

1. Invest in quality hair products:

Using high-quality hair products can make a big difference in how your hairstyle looks and lasts throughout the day. Look for products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture.

2. Get regular trims:

Even if you have a short hairstyle, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

3. Protect your hair from heat:

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ:

Q: Can oval-faced men pull off any hairstyle?

A: Yes, oval-faced men have a lot of versatility when it comes to hairstyles. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your best features.

Q: Can I achieve these hairstyles at home?

A: Yes, these hairstyles can be achieved at home with the right tools and products. However, if you're unsure about how to achieve a certain style, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist.

Q: How do I know if I have an oval face shape?

A: Oval face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion of features, including a well-defined jawline, high cheekbones, and a slightly rounded forehead. If you're unsure about your face shape, consult with a professional stylist.

Hair Care:

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving any hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly:

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. Use a conditioner:

Using a conditioner after shampooing helps to add moisture back into your hair, preventing it from becoming dry and brittle.

3. Protect your hair from the sun:

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when spending time outdoors.

In conclusion, finding the best hairstyle for oval face shape male is all about accentuating your best features and choosing a style that complements your face shape. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look that suits your unique features.
