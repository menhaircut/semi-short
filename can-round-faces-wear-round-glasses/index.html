Can Round Faces Wear Round Glasses

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and a circular shape. If you have a round face, you may be wondering if you can pull off round glasses. The answer is yes! Round glasses can look great on round faces, as long as you choose the right style.

How to Choose Round Glasses for Round Faces

When choosing round glasses for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Look for Glasses with Angular Lines

To add some definition to your face, look for glasses with angular lines. This can include glasses with rectangular or square frames, or glasses with sharp angles at the top or bottom of the frame.

2. Choose Glasses with Thin Frames

Glasses with thick frames can overwhelm a round face, so it's best to choose glasses with thin frames. This will help to balance out the proportions of your face.

3. Consider Glasses with a Bridge

Glasses with a bridge can help to create the illusion of a longer, more defined nose. This can be especially helpful for those with round faces, as it can help to balance out the proportions of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Choose Round Glasses for Round Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect round glasses for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you're not sure what your face shape is, take a look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a dry-erase marker. Then, step back and take a look at the shape you've traced. If your face is circular and soft, you likely have a round face.

Step 2: Look for Glasses with Angular Lines

Once you've determined your face shape, start looking for glasses with angular lines. This can include glasses with rectangular or square frames, or glasses with sharp angles at the top or bottom of the frame.

Step 3: Choose Glasses with Thin Frames

Next, choose glasses with thin frames. This will help to balance out the proportions of your face and prevent the glasses from overwhelming your features.

Step 4: Consider Glasses with a Bridge

If you're still not sure which glasses to choose, consider glasses with a bridge. This can help to create the illusion of a longer, more defined nose, which can be especially helpful for those with round faces.

Tips for Wearing Round Glasses with a Round Face

Here are a few tips to help you rock your round glasses:

1. Keep Your Hair in Check

When wearing round glasses with a round face, it's important to keep your hair in check. Avoid adding volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

2. Choose the Right Makeup

When wearing round glasses, it's important to choose the right makeup. Avoid heavy eye makeup, as this can make your eyes appear smaller. Instead, opt for light, natural-looking makeup that emphasizes your features.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles of round glasses! Try on different frames until you find the perfect pair that flatters your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can Round Faces Wear Circular Glasses?

Yes, round faces can wear circular glasses! However, it's important to choose the right style of circular glasses. Look for glasses with angular lines and thin frames to balance out the proportions of your face.

2. What Glasses Should I Avoid if I Have a Round Face?

If you have a round face, it's best to avoid glasses with round frames, as these can make your face appear even rounder. It's also best to avoid glasses with thick frames, as these can overwhelm your features.

3. How Do I Know if Glasses Look Good on My Round Face?

The best way to know if glasses look good on your round face is to try them on! Experiment with different styles and frames until you find the perfect pair that flatters your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are a few hair care tips to help you flatter your round face:

1. Add Volume to the Crown

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help to elongate your face and balance out the proportions of your features.

2. Avoid Adding Volume to the Sides

Avoid adding volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

3. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles! Try out different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look that flatters your face shape.

Overall, round faces can definitely wear round glasses! Just be sure to choose the right style and frame to flatter your features. Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect pair that makes you feel confident and stylish.
