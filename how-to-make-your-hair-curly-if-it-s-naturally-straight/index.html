How To Make Your Hair Curly If It s Naturally Straight

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are someone with naturally straight hair, you may have always wanted to change things up and try out curly hair. While some people may be blessed with naturally curly hair, others have to make an effort to get those luscious curls. Thankfully, there are several ways to make your hair curly if it's naturally straight. In this article, we will discuss some of the most effective ways to achieve curly hair and tips on how to maintain it.

Description

There are several methods to make your hair curly, whether it be with the use of heat styling tools or natural methods. The key to achieving curly hair is to find the right method that works for you and your hair type. It's important to keep in mind that frequent heat styling can damage your hair, so it's best to use protective products before applying heat to your hair.

How to Make Your Hair Curly if it's Naturally Straight

1. Use a Curling Iron

One of the most common ways to achieve curly hair is by using a curling iron. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair and section it off. Take small sections of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Remember to curl in different directions for a natural-looking finish.

2. Use a Curling Wand

A curling wand is similar to a curling iron but doesn't have a clamp. This tool allows you to create more natural-looking curls. Start by sectioning off your hair and wrapping small sections around the wand. Hold for a few seconds before releasing.

3. Use Hot Rollers

Hot rollers are a great tool to use if you want to achieve loose, bouncy curls. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair and section it off. Wrap each section around the hot rollers and leave them in for about 20-30 minutes.

4. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great alternative to heat styling tools. Start by applying a styling product to damp hair and section it off. Take small sections of hair and wrap it around the flexi rod, securing it in place. Leave them in overnight or until your hair is fully dry.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to achieve curly hair using a curling iron:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before styling it with heat. This will help prevent damage to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, so it's easier to style with the curling iron.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve and maintain curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair before styling.
  • Don't brush your curls out, use your fingers to separate and define them.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.

FAQ

How long will my curls last?

How long your curls last will depend on your hair type, the method you used to curl your hair, and how well you maintain your curls. If you want your curls to last longer, try using a setting spray or sleeping in a silk scarf to preserve your curls.

Can I curl my hair without heat?

Yes, you can curl your hair without heat. Some natural methods include using flexi rods or setting your hair in braids or twists.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're applying heat to it frequently. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

In conclusion, there are several ways to make your hair curly if it's naturally straight. Whether you use heat styling tools or natural methods, the key is to find the right method that works for you and your hair type. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain your curls.
