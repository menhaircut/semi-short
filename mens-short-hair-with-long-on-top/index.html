Mens Short Hair With Long On Top A Guide To Achieving The Look

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man looking for a hairstyle that strikes the perfect balance between short and long, then the mens short hair with long on top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been a popular trend for several years now and continues to be in style in 2023. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to achieve this look, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Achieve the Look

The mens short hair with long on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual style and face shape. To achieve this look, you will need to have short hair on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a contrast between the short and long hair, giving you an edgy and modern look.

Tools You Will Need

To achieve this look, you will need the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or wax

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for achieving the mens short hair with long on top:

  1. Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head short. Use clippers with a 1 or 2 guard for a clean and even cut.
  2. Next, use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. You can either leave the hair long and tousled or style it into a slicked-back look.
  3. Use a comb to blend the short and long hair together, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to the hair on top of your head to add texture and hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To keep your mens short hair with long on top looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair on the sides and back of your head short and neat.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Is the mens short hair with long on top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a shorter cut on top to create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, go for a longer cut on top to soften your features.

Q: How do I style my mens short hair with long on top?

A: Use a small amount of hair gel or wax to add texture and hold the style in place. Style the hair on top of your head to the desired look, whether it be slicked-back or tousled.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your mens short hair with long on top looking healthy and stylish, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote strong and healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the mens short hair with long on top is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape and style. By following these tips and instructions, you can easily achieve this look and keep it looking fresh and stylish. So why not try this modern and edgy hairstyle for yourself?
