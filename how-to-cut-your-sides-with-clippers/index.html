How to Cut Your Sides with Clippers

Learn how to get a fresh haircut at home with clippers. Follow our step-by-step guide to cut your sides with clippers.

Description

Cutting your own hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be an easy and cost-effective way to maintain your hairstyle. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your sides with clippers, one of the most popular hair cutting tools. Whether you're looking to trim your hair or create a new style, we've got you covered.


How To

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right clippers and attachments. You'll need a set of clippers with adjustable guards, which allow you to change the length of the cut. Attach a guard to the clippers based on the length of hair you want to cut. Here's how to cut your sides with clippers:


Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting. If you have long hair, consider tying it up or using clips to keep it out of the way. This will help you see what you're doing and prevent any hair from getting in the clippers.


Step 2: Choose Your Guard

Select the clipper guard based on the length you want to cut your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.


Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair by starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the clippers in an upward motion against the grain of your hair. Move the clippers in a straight line, starting at the base of your hairline and moving towards the top of your head. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair on the side.


Step 4: Repeat on the Other Side

After you've finished cutting one side, move on to the other side and repeat the process. Make sure to keep the length and style consistent on both sides.


Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure both sides are even.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and keep the hair in place.
  • Clean your clippers after each use to keep them in good condition.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It just takes practice and the right technique.


How long should I leave my hair with clippers?

The length you leave your hair with clippers depends on your personal preference. Use a longer guard for a shorter cut, and a shorter guard for a longer cut.


Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a styling product if desired. If you're unsure about how to care for your hair, consult a stylist.


Conclusion

Cutting your sides with clippers is an easy and affordable way to maintain your hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh haircut at home. Follow our guide and you'll be a pro in no time.
