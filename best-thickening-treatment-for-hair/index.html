Best Thickening Treatment For Hair

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of thin, limp hair? Do you dream of having thick, voluminous locks? Look no further, because we’ve compiled a list of the best thickening treatments for hair that will leave your hair looking full and fabulous! In this article, we’ll cover everything from how to thicken hair, the best products to use, and tips for maintaining your luscious locks. So, let’s get started!

How to Thicken Hair

Before we dive into the best thickening treatments, let’s discuss how to thicken hair naturally. Here are some tips:

Eat a Balanced Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Eating a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals can promote hair growth and thickness. Some foods to include in your diet are eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and salmon.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can stimulate hair growth and increase blood flow to the hair follicles. Use your fingers to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair and make it look thinner. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat on a low setting.

The Best Thickening Treatments for Hair

Now, let’s talk about the best thickening treatments for hair. These products can give your hair the appearance of thickness and volume.

Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the easiest ways to thicken your hair is to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Hair Thickening Spray

Hair thickening sprays are a great option for adding volume to your hair. These sprays contain polymers that coat the hair shaft and make it appear thicker. Simply spray the product onto your roots and massage it in.

Root Lifting Powder

If you have thin, fine hair, root lifting powder can be a game-changer. This powder can be sprinkled onto your roots to add volume and lift. Use your fingers to massage the powder into your roots.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Thickening Treatments

Now that you know the best thickening treatments for hair, let’s go over how to use them.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply Thickening Spray

If you’re using a thickening spray, apply it to your roots and massage it in.

Step 3: Sprinkle Root Lifting Powder

If you’re using root lifting powder, sprinkle it onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add even more volume.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

Now that you’ve achieved thick, voluminous hair, it’s important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look thinner. Aim to wash your hair every other day.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause breakage and make your hair look thinner. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can put stress on your hair and make it look thinner. Opt for looser hairstyles instead.

FAQ

What Causes Thin Hair?

Thin hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, age, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions.

Can You Thicken Hair Naturally?

Yes, you can thicken hair naturally by eating a balanced diet, massaging your scalp, and avoiding heat styling tools.

Do Thickening Treatments Damage Hair?

No, thickening treatments do not damage hair. They simply coat the hair shaft to make it appear thicker.

Hair Care is Key

Now that you know the best thickening treatments for hair, it’s time to get started on your journey to thick, voluminous locks. Remember to take care of your hair by eating a balanced diet, avoiding heat styling tools, and using the right products. With a little bit of effort, you can have the thick, beautiful hair of your dreams!
