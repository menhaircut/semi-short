Short Haircuts For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that will flatter both your age and glasses? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for over 60 with glasses, along with tips on how to style and maintain them.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose volume. This is why short haircuts are perfect for women over 60. They not only make you look younger, but they are also easy to maintain and style. If you wear glasses, your haircut should complement your frames and accentuate your facial features.

How to

The first step in choosing a short haircut for over 60 with glasses is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a haircut that will flatter your features. If you have a round face, opt for a short pixie cut or a bob with layers. If you have a square face, go for a short bob or a layered cut that will soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short haircut.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that will complement your glasses. If you have thick frames, go for a short haircut that will balance out your features. If you have thin frames, opt for a layered haircut that will add volume to your hair. If you wear rimless glasses, you can pull off almost any short haircut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a haircut that will complement your glasses
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist
Step 4: Style your hair according to your face shape and glasses
Step 5: Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims

Tips

When styling your short haircut, use volumizing products that will add thickness to your hair. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and lift. Use a texturizing spray to add texture and definition to your hair.

When choosing glasses, opt for frames that will complement your haircut. If you have a short pixie cut, go for round or square frames that will balance out your features. If you have a short bob, go for rectangular or oval frames that will elongate your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have thin hair?
A: Yes! Short haircuts are perfect for women with thin hair. They can add volume and thickness to your hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my haircut?
A: It's recommended to get your haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

To maintain your short haircut, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that will not strip your hair of its natural oils. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for over 60 with glasses, it's time to schedule a consultation with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a haircut that will flatter your face shape and complement your glasses. With the right haircut and styling products, you'll look and feel younger and more confident!
