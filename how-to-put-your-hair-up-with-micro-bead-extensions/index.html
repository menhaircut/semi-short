How To Put Your Hair Up With Micro Bead Extensions

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have micro bead extensions, you know how versatile they can be. Not only can you wear your hair down and let your extensions blend seamlessly with your natural hair, but you can also put your hair up in a variety of styles. In this blog post, we'll go over how to put your hair up with micro bead extensions, step-by-step.

How To

Before we get started, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need a brush, some bobby pins, and a hair tie. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Make sure your hair is free of tangles and knots by brushing it from root to tip.

Step 2: Create a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail, making sure to include your extensions. Use your brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Twist Your Ponytail

Take your ponytail and twist it until it begins to coil. Keep twisting until your hair forms a bun.

Step 4: Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place. Make sure to hide the pins so they're not visible.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Use a hair tie to secure any loose pieces of hair. You can also add a hair accessory, like a headband or hair clip, to add some extra flair to your updo.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect updo with your micro bead extensions:

  • Use hairspray to help hold your style in place
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don't pull too tightly on your extensions, as this can cause damage
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be afraid to try different styles until you find the perfect one for you

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about putting their hair up with micro bead extensions:

Can I put my hair up if I have short hair?

Yes! Even if you have short hair, you can still put your hair up in a variety of styles with micro bead extensions.

Can I use a hair tie with micro bead extensions?

Yes, just be sure to avoid pulling too tightly on your extensions.

How do I keep my extensions from showing when I put my hair up?

Make sure your extensions are evenly distributed throughout your hair so they blend seamlessly. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces in place.

Hair Care

Taking care of your micro bead extensions is important to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips to keep your extensions looking their best:

  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging your extensions
  • Avoid using hot tools on your extensions
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put your hair up with micro bead extensions in no time. Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find what works best for you.
