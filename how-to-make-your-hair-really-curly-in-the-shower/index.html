How To Make Your Hair Really Curly In The Shower

Written by Eveline Sep 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of using heat styling tools to get curly hair, there's a solution that's gentler on your locks: getting curly hair in the shower. Not only is it less damaging, but it's also an easy way to create voluminous curls that will last all day. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair really curly in the shower, step by step. We'll also provide some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice.

  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips

Step by Step

Follow these steps to get curly hair in the shower:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a curl-defining cream or gel. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair while it's still wet. This will ensure that the curl-enhancing product is evenly distributed throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls to form. Do this gently, so you don't disrupt the curl pattern.

Step 5: Plop Your Hair

Plop your hair by wrapping it in a microfiber towel or t-shirt. This will help to absorb excess water and encourage your curls to form.

Step 6: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to set the curls in place and prevent frizz.

Step 7: Finish with a Hair Spray

Finish with a hair spray to hold your curls in place. Choose a spray that's lightweight and won't weigh down your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it's still wet.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair while it's still wet.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls to form.
  • Wrap your hair in a microfiber towel or t-shirt to absorb excess water.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  • Finish with a hair spray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

Can I use this method on any hair type?

Yes, this method works on all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

How long will the curls last?

The curls should last all day, but you can refresh them with a curl revitalizing spray if needed.

Can I use a regular towel instead of a microfiber towel or t-shirt?

No, regular towels can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern. Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt instead.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair while it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair really curly in the shower, it's time to try it out for yourself. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips, and take care of your hair to keep your curls looking their best.
