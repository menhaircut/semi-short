Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task for men, but the long on top short sides hairstyle has become a popular choice among men in recent years. This hairstyle is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about mens hairstyles long on top short sides.

Description

The long on top short sides hairstyle is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This hairstyle is also known as the undercut or disconnected undercut. The contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides creates a bold and edgy look.

How to

The long on top short sides hairstyle can be achieved in a few simple steps:

  1. Start by growing out the hair on top of your head to the desired length.
  2. Visit a trusted barber or stylist and ask for an undercut with a fade on the sides and back.
  3. Maintain the length of the hair on top by getting regular trims and styling it with a pomade or wax.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your long on top short sides haircut:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of pomade or wax to the hair on top.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to the hair on top.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top into your desired shape.
  5. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to the sides and back of your hair and use a comb to smooth it down.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your long on top short sides hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length of the hair on top and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Try different hairstyles with your long on top short sides haircut, such as a slick back or a messy quiff.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short sides hairstyle:

What face shape is best suited for the long on top short sides hairstyle?

The long on top short sides hairstyle is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes. However, it looks best on men with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

How often should I get a trim for my long on top short sides haircut?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length of the hair on top and prevent split ends.

What styling products work best for the long on top short sides hairstyle?

Pomade and wax are the best styling products for the long on top short sides hairstyle. They provide hold and texture without making the hair look greasy or weighed down.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish long on top short sides hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make the hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length of the hair on top and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and versatile long on top short sides hairstyle. Remember to experiment with different styling products and hairstyles to find the look that suits you best.
