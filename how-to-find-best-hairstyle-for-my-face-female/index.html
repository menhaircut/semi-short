How To Find The Best Hairstyle For Your Face A Guide For Women

Written by Christine Sep 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of always wearing the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the best hairstyle for your face. We'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to which hairstyles are most flattering for your features. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Your Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by standing in front of a mirror and examining the shape of your face. There are several different face shapes, including:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Once you have determined your face shape, you can move on to finding the best hairstyles for your features.

Best Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the best hairstyles for your features. Here are some general guidelines:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape looks great with almost any hairstyle. Consider trying a long layered cut, a blunt bob, or some soft waves.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and lengthens your face. Consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a high ponytail, or a messy updo.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that has rounded edges. Consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a bob with soft waves, or a messy bun.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances your features. Consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a bob with soft waves, or a messy updo.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features. Consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a bob with soft waves, or a messy updo.

Triangle Face Shape

If you have a triangle face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances your features. Consider a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a bob with soft waves, or a messy updo.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage

FAQ

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, focus on the dominant features of your face when choosing a hairstyle.

Can I still wear my hair in the same style if it's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions, and ultimately you should wear your hair in whatever style makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.


Now that you know how to find the best hairstyle for your face, it's time to try something new! Remember, these guidelines are just suggestions, and ultimately you should wear your hair in whatever style makes you feel confident and beautiful. Take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find your perfect look!
