Medium Length Spiky Haircuts For Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Christine Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a hairstyle that's edgy and trendy, you might want to consider a medium length spiky haircut. This style is versatile, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect medium length spiky haircut and offer some tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking fabulous.

Description: What is a Medium Length Spiky Haircut?

A medium length spiky haircut is a style that features hair that is cut to a medium length and styled into short, sharp spikes. This style is often achieved by using a styling product, such as gel or mousse, to create texture and hold the hair in place. The result is a look that is edgy, modern, and youthful.

How to: Getting a Medium Length Spiky Haircut

If you're interested in getting a medium length spiky haircut, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step to getting a medium length spiky haircut is to choose the right length for your hair. Your stylist can help you determine the best length based on your face shape and personal style. Generally, a medium length haircut falls just above or below the shoulders.

Step 2: Ask for Layers

Layers are an essential element of a spiky haircut. They add texture and movement to the hair, making it easier to style. Ask your stylist to add layers to your haircut, focusing on the crown and top of the head.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it into spikes. Apply a styling product, such as gel or mousse, to your hair and use your fingers to create short, sharp spikes. You can also use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style in place.

Tips: How to Care for Your Medium Length Spiky Haircut

Here are some tips on how to care for your medium length spiky haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair, as this is gentler on your hair than a brush

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get a medium length spiky haircut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a medium length spiky haircut if you have thin hair. Your stylist can add layers to your haircut to create texture and volume.

Q: Will a medium length spiky haircut make me look younger?

A: A medium length spiky haircut can help you look younger and more modern. The edgy style is a great way to update your look and stay up-to-date with current trends.

Q: How often do I need to style my medium length spiky haircut?

A: You should style your medium length spiky haircut every day to keep it looking its best. However, once you've mastered the technique, it should only take a few minutes each day.

Hair Care: Conclusion

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle, a medium length spiky haircut might be just what you need. This versatile style is easy to maintain and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. By following these tips on how to care for your hair, you can keep your medium length spiky haircut looking fabulous for years to come.
