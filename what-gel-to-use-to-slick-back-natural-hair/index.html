What Gel To Use To Slick Back Natural Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will share some tips on what gel to use to slick back natural hair. Slicking back your hair is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It gives a clean and polished look that is perfect for any occasion. However, finding the right gel to achieve this look can be challenging, especially if you have natural hair. Don't worry, we've got you covered!

Description

Slicking back natural hair can be a challenge, especially if you don't have the right products. Using the wrong gel can leave your hair looking greasy or flaky. The key to achieving a sleek look is to find a gel that is lightweight, non-greasy, and provides a strong hold.

How to Slick Back Natural Hair

Before we dive into what gel to use, let's first go over how to slick back natural hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a small amount of gel to your fingertips
  3. Smooth the gel through your hair, starting from the front and working your way back
  4. Use a fine-tooth comb to comb your hair back, starting from the hairline and working your way back
  5. Continue combing until your hair is slicked back to your liking
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on low heat to set the style

What Gel to Use

Now that you know how to slick back your hair, let's talk about what gel to use. Here are some of the best gels for slicking back natural hair:

1. Eco Styler Gel

Eco Styler Gel is a popular choice for natural hair. It is lightweight, non-greasy, and provides a strong hold. It also comes in different strengths, so you can choose the one that works best for your hair type.

2. Mielle Organics Honey & Ginger Styling Gel

This gel is made with natural ingredients and is perfect for slicking back natural hair. It provides a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or flaky.

3. Aunt Jackie's Flaxseed Elongating Curling Gel

While this gel is marketed towards curly hair, it also works well for slicking back natural hair. It provides a strong hold and leaves your hair looking shiny and moisturized.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when slicking back natural hair:

  • Use a small amount of gel to avoid leaving your hair looking greasy or flaky
  • Don't apply gel to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair and cause it to look flat
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to achieve a smooth and sleek look
  • Experiment with different gels to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back natural hair:

Can I slick back my hair if I have natural hair?

Yes, you can slick back natural hair. However, it can be more challenging than slicking back straight hair. It's important to use a gel that provides a strong hold and won't leave your hair feeling greasy or flaky.

What is the best gel for slicking back natural hair?

The best gel for slicking back natural hair is one that is lightweight, non-greasy, and provides a strong hold. Some of the best gels for natural hair include Eco Styler Gel, Mielle Organics Honey & Ginger Styling Gel, and Aunt Jackie's Flaxseed Elongating Curling Gel.

Can I slick back my hair every day?

While slicking back your hair every day is possible, it's important to give your hair a break from time to time. Slicking back your hair too often can lead to breakage and damage. Try to limit slicking back your hair to a few times a week.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair even when slicking it back. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Don't forget to deep condition your hair regularly to prevent breakage and promote growth.

Slicking back natural hair can be a challenge, but with the right gel and technique, you can achieve a sleek and polished look. Experiment with different gels and don't be afraid to try new styles. Happy styling!
