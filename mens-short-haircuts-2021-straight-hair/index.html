How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Straight Hair

Before you go ahead and chop off your locks, it's important to choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are some of the best mens short haircuts 2021 straight hair:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that is easy to maintain and suits all face shapes. It involves cutting the hair to the same length all around the head using clippers. This is a great option if you want a low-maintenance haircut.

2. Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic short haircut that is popular among men. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving it slightly longer on top. This haircut is great if you have a round face as it helps to elongate the face.

3. Undercut

The undercut involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving the top long. This is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways. It's great if you have a square or oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Mens Short Haircuts 2021 Straight Hair

Now that you've chosen the right short haircut, it's time to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will help to remove any buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

2. Towel dry your hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's slightly damp. Don't rub your hair vigorously with the towel as this can cause damage.

3. Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. This can be a pomade, wax, or gel depending on your preference. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair evenly.

4. Use a comb or brush

Use a comb or brush to style your hair in the desired direction. For a buzz cut, you can skip this step.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look by applying a small amount of hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Short Haircuts 2021 Straight Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens short haircuts 2021 straight hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to help style your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for straight hair?

The best short haircut for straight hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Some popular options include the buzz cut, crew cut, and undercut.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Can I style my short haircuts in different ways?

Yes! Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find a style that suits you.

Hair Care Products for Mens Short Haircuts 2021 Straight Hair

Here are some hair care products that are perfect for mens short haircuts 2021 straight hair:

  • Dry shampoo
  • Hairspray
  • Leave-in conditioner
