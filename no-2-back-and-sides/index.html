Description

The No 2 Back and Sides haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that features a close-cut on the sides and back of the head, usually with a #2 clipper guard. The hair on top of the head can be styled in a variety of ways, from short and textured to longer and slicked back.

This haircut is a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance style that still looks sharp and put-together. It's also a versatile haircut that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

How to Get the No 2 Back and Sides Haircut

Getting a No 2 Back and Sides haircut is relatively easy and can be done at home with a set of clippers and a steady hand. However, it's always best to visit a professional barber or stylist for the best results.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair so that it's clean and free of any product.
  2. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top of your head. This can vary depending on your personal style and the shape of your face.
  3. Attach a #2 clipper guard to your clippers and use it to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using short, even strokes.
  4. Blend the hair on top of your head with the hair on the sides and back, using scissors or clippers with a longer guard.
  5. Use a comb and scissors to trim and shape the hair on top of your head, creating the desired style.
  6. Finish with a styling product, if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your No 2 Back and Sides Haircut

Keeping your No 2 Back and Sides haircut looking its best requires a little bit of maintenance. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking sharp:

  • Visit a barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
