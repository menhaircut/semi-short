How To Relax Curls Into Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you may sometimes want to change it up and go for a more relaxed, wavy look. But how do you go from tight curls to loose waves? In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for relaxing curls into waves.

Description

Relaxing curls into waves involves using a technique that loosens the curl pattern of your hair. This can be done using chemicals or heat, but both methods come with risks. Chemical relaxers can damage your hair if not used properly, and heat styling can lead to heat damage over time. It's important to take proper precautions and use the right products to protect your hair.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to relax curls into waves:

Step 1: Cleanse

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. This will help the relaxer penetrate your hair more effectively.

Step 2: Protect

Apply a protective barrier to your scalp and hairline using petroleum jelly or a similar product. This will prevent the relaxer from touching your skin and causing irritation.

Step 3: Apply the Relaxer

Follow the instructions on your relaxer kit to mix and apply the product to your hair. Be sure to apply it evenly and avoid overlapping the relaxer on previously relaxed hair.

Step 4: Wait

Leave the relaxer on your hair for the amount of time specified in the instructions. It's important not to leave it on for too long, as this can cause damage.

Step 5: Neutralize

Once the time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and apply the neutralizing shampoo included in your kit. This will stop the chemical process and prevent any further damage.

Step 6: Condition

After rinsing out the neutralizing shampoo, apply a deep conditioner to your hair to restore moisture and prevent breakage. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing thoroughly.

Step 7: Style

Now that your hair is relaxed, you can style it into loose waves using a curling iron or other heat styling tool. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when relaxing curls into waves:

  • Choose a relaxer formula that's appropriate for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't scratch your scalp before or after applying the relaxer, as this can cause irritation.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before and after applying the relaxer.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair, as relaxed hair is more fragile and prone to breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing curls into waves:

Is it safe to relax curls into waves?

When done correctly, relaxing curls into waves can be safe. However, both chemical and heat-based methods come with risks, so it's important to take proper precautions and follow the instructions carefully.

How long does a relaxer last?

The effects of a relaxer can last for several weeks to several months, depending on your hair's texture and how fast it grows.

Can I relax my hair at home?

Yes, you can relax your hair at home using a relaxer kit. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and take proper precautions to avoid damage. If you're unsure, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After relaxing your curls into waves, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to restore moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can lead to damage over time.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and keep it healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can relax your curls into beautiful, bouncy waves. Just remember to take proper precautions and give your hair the care it needs to stay healthy and strong.
