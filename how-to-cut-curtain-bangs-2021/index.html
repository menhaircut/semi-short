How To Cut Curtain Bangs 2021 A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your regular hairstyle and want to try something new? Curtain bangs are a great option in 2021! This style is popular among celebrities and influencers, and it can add a soft and feminine touch to your look. In this article, we will guide you through how to cut curtain bangs 2021 style, step-by-step.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs 2021

Before we get started, make sure you have the right tools: a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-tooth comb, and hair clips.

Step 1: Decide on Your Bangs' Width

Start by deciding how wide you want your bangs to be. Using the fine-tooth comb, part your hair down the middle and pull the hair you want to cut forward.

Step 2: Cut the Center of Your Bangs

Hold your hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut the center of your bangs straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 3: Cut the Sides of Your Bangs

Divide your bangs into two sections on each side of your face. Hold each section between your fingers and cut at a slight angle. The angle should start from the center of your bangs and end at the outer corner of your eyes.

Step 4: Blend Your Bangs

Blend the two sides of your bangs by holding them together and cutting at a slight angle. This will create a natural curve that frames your face.

Step 5: Check Your Bangs

After cutting your bangs, comb them down and check if they are even. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair accordingly.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs 2021

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs:

  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair and gradually increase if necessary.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry, as wet hair tends to look longer than it is.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. You can always trim them later, but you can't add hair that's already been cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows, but generally, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can! However, if you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Now that you have your new curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and oil-free.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and bounce.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as it can damage them.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum to your bangs to keep them smooth and shiny.

Conclusion

Cutting your own curtain bangs can be challenging, but with this step-by-step guide, you can achieve the perfect look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't forget to take care of your new hairstyle. With these tips, you'll be rocking your new curtain bangs in no time!
