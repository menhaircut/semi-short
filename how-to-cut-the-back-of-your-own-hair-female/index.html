How To Cut The Back Of Your Own Hair Female

Written by Daniel Jun 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the hair salon for a simple trim? Do you want to save time and money by cutting your own hair? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut the back of your own hair female in a relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything from the tools you’ll need to the step-by-step process of cutting your hair. Let’s get started!

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you’ll need to gather a few tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips
  • Handheld mirror or full-length mirror
  • Haircutting cape or old towel

Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid any uneven cuts. You can find haircutting capes online or use an old towel to protect your clothing from hair clippings.

How to Cut the Back of Your Own Hair Female

Cutting the back of your own hair can be a bit tricky, but with some practice and patience, you can master the technique. Here’s what you need to do:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Comb or brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Clip the top section of your hair away from the back of your head. This will make it easier to focus on the back of your hair.
  3. Use a handheld mirror or full-length mirror to check the back of your head. This will help you see any uneven areas that need trimming.
  4. Take a small section of hair in the middle of the back of your head. Comb it straight and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  5. Trim the ends of the hair with your scissors. Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  6. Repeat step 4 and 5 with the rest of the sections in the back of your head.
  7. Release the top section of your hair and blend it in with the back. Use a comb to blend any uneven areas.
  8. Check the back of your head in the mirror to make sure it looks even.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with these tips, you’ll feel confident in your skills:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a comb to blend any uneven areas.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring.
  • Be patient. It may take a few tries to get the hang of it.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it’s best to use sharp haircutting scissors to avoid any uneven cuts.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always visit a hair salon to fix any uneven areas.

Hair Care After Your Haircut

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water for healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut the back of your own hair female, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to be patient and start with small cuts. With practice, you’ll become a pro in no time!
