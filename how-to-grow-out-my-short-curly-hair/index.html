How To Grow Out My Short Curly Hair

Written by Frank Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you may be wondering how to grow it out into longer, more manageable locks. Fortunately, there are several steps you can take to encourage healthy hair growth and make the process as smooth as possible. In this article, we'll explore some key tips and tricks for growing out short curly hair, as well as answer some common questions about the process.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Out Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs

How to Grow Out Short Curly Hair

When it comes to growing out short curly hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Be patient. Hair growth takes time, especially if you're starting from a very short cut.
  • Maintain a healthy scalp. A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth, so make sure you're taking care of your scalp with regular washing and moisturizing.
  • Protect your hair from damage. Curly hair is prone to damage from heat styling, chemical treatments, and other factors. To promote healthy growth, avoid harsh treatments that can damage the hair shaft.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to start growing out your short curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Start with a healthy foundation. Make sure your hair is clean, moisturized, and free of damage before you start growing it out.
  2. Trim regularly. As your hair grows, it's important to trim it regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  3. Avoid harsh treatments. Chemical treatments like relaxers and perms can damage curly hair and slow down growth. Stick to gentle, natural treatments instead.
  4. Protect your hair while you sleep. Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and tangling while you sleep.
  5. Be gentle with your hair. Avoid rough brushing or combing, and be gentle when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  6. Experiment with protective styles. Braids, twists, and other protective styles can help protect your hair and promote healthy growth.
  7. Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  8. Eat a healthy diet. Your hair needs essential nutrients like protein, vitamins, and minerals to grow strong and healthy.

Tips for Healthy Hair Growth

In addition to the steps outlined above, there are several other tips and tricks you can use to encourage healthy hair growth:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask regularly to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause damage to curly hair.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement or using a topical treatment to encourage healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about growing out short curly hair:

How long does it take to grow out short curly hair?

The length of time it takes to grow out short curly hair depends on a variety of factors, including your genetics, diet, and hair care routine. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it may take several months or even years to grow out very short hair.

Can I still style my hair while I'm growing it out?

Yes! There are plenty of styling options for short curly hair, from braids and twists to updos and accessories. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind as you grow out your short curly hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics, as these can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind with a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can grow out your short curly hair into longer, healthier locks. Just remember to be patient, take care of your hair and scalp, and avoid harsh treatments that can damage your hair. With time and dedication, you'll have the beautiful, healthy hair you've always wanted!
