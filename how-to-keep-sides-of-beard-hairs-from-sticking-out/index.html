How To Keep Sides Of Beard Hairs From Sticking Out A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of your beard looking like a wild bush? Do you struggle to keep the sides of your beard hairs from sticking out? If so, you're not alone! Many men face this common problem when growing out their beards. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to keep the sides of beard hairs from sticking out.

How to Keep Sides of Beard Hairs from Sticking Out

There are a few things you can do to keep the sides of your beard hairs from sticking out. These include:

  • Using a beard oil
  • Brushing your beard regularly
  • Trimming your beard regularly
  • Using a beard balm
  • Using a beard comb

We'll go into more detail on each of these methods in the following sections.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to keep the sides of beard hairs from sticking out:

Step 1: Use a Beard Oil

Beard oil is a product that is designed to hydrate and moisturize your beard. It can also help to tame unruly beard hairs. To use it, simply apply a few drops to your beard and massage it in.

Step 2: Brush Your Beard Regularly

Brushing your beard regularly can help to train your beard hairs to lay flat. Use a beard brush to gently brush your beard in the direction of hair growth.

Step 3: Trim Your Beard Regularly

Trimming your beard regularly can help to keep it looking neat and tidy. Use a pair of beard scissors or a beard trimmer to trim the sides of your beard to your desired length.

Step 4: Use a Beard Balm

Beard balm is similar to beard oil, but it also contains beeswax or shea butter to provide hold. To use it, apply a small amount to your beard and massage it in.

Step 5: Use a Beard Comb

A beard comb can help to distribute product evenly throughout your beard and remove any tangles or knots. Use a wide-toothed beard comb to gently comb through your beard.

Tips for Keeping Sides of Beard Hairs from Sticking Out

Here are some additional tips for keeping the sides of your beard hairs from sticking out:

  • Use a boar bristle brush to help distribute your beard oil or balm
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your beard
  • Be patient - it takes time for your beard hairs to grow and train them
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I use beard oil?

A: You can use beard oil daily, or as often as you like.

Q: Can I use a regular comb on my beard?

A: It's best to use a beard-specific comb, as these are designed to be gentler on your beard hairs.

Q: How often should I trim my beard?

A: This depends on how quickly your beard grows and your desired length. As a general rule, aim to trim your beard every 2-4 weeks.

Beard Hair Care

In addition to following the tips above, it's important to take good care of your beard hairs. This means:

  • Washing your beard regularly with a beard-specific shampoo
  • Conditioning your beard to keep it soft and hydrated
  • Avoiding heat styling tools, as these can damage your beard hairs
  • Protecting your beard from the elements, such as wind and sun

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to keep the sides of your beard hairs from sticking out and achieve a neat, well-groomed beard. Happy bearding!
