How To Make Big Hair Bows For Toddlers

Written by Daniel Sep 01, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a toddler girl, then you know how cute big hair bows can look on her. They can add a pop of color to any outfit and make her stand out in a crowd. In this article, we'll show you how to make big hair bows for toddlers that are easy, fun, and budget-friendly.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Big hair bows for toddlers are a great way to accessorize their outfits and make them look even cuter. They can be made in any color or pattern, and you can even customize them with your toddler's name or initials.

How to

To make big hair bows for toddlers, you'll need a few supplies:

  • Grosgrain ribbon (1 ½ inch width)
  • Alligator clips
  • Hot glue gun
  • Scissors

Step by Step

Follow these steps to make big hair bows for toddlers:

  1. Cut a piece of ribbon to 18 inches in length.
  2. Fold the ribbon in half and make a crease in the center.
  3. Open the ribbon and fold each end towards the center crease.
  4. Overlap the ends in the center and glue them together with hot glue.
  5. Pinch the center of the bow and wrap a small piece of ribbon around it to secure it.
  6. Glue an alligator clip to the back of the bow.
  7. Trim the ends of the ribbon at an angle.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process of making big hair bows for toddlers easier:

  • Use a ruler to measure the ribbon for accuracy.
  • Use a lighter to seal the ends of the ribbon to prevent fraying.
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns of ribbon to create unique hair bows.
  • Add embellishments like pearls or rhinestones to the center of the bow for an extra touch of glam.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making big hair bows for toddlers:

Q: How long does it take to make a big hair bow for a toddler?

A: It can take anywhere from 10 to 20 minutes to make a big hair bow for a toddler.

Q: Can I use other types of ribbon besides grosgrain?

A: Yes, you can use other types of ribbon like satin, velvet, or organza.

Q: How do I attach the hair bow to my toddler's hair?

A: You can simply clip the alligator clip onto a section of your toddler's hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your toddler's hair when using hair accessories like big hair bows. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using tight hair elastics or hair accessories that pull on the hair.
  • Use a soft-bristled brush to detangle your toddler's hair.
  • Wash your toddler's hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners on your toddler's hair.

Now that you know how to make big hair bows for toddlers, you can create a variety of cute and unique bows for your little one. Have fun experimenting with different colors, patterns, and embellishments!
