Does Short Hair Look Better On Skinny Faces

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hairstyles are always in trend, no matter what face shape or body type you have. However, there is a common misconception that short hair looks better on skinny faces. But is it really true?

Description

If you are a skinny-faced person, then you might have heard from your friends or family members that short hair will suit you the best. But the truth is, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. Short hair can look great on any face shape, including skinny faces. It all depends on the style and cut of the hair, and how well it complements your facial features.

How to

If you are considering a short haircut, it is essential to consult with your hairdresser to see what looks best on you. Your hairdresser will be able to recommend a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personality. You can also try different hairstyles virtually using online apps.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you choose the right short haircut for your skinny face:

  1. Know your face shape - Determine if you have an oval, round, square, or heart-shaped face.
  2. Research - Look for inspiration online or in magazines for hairstyles that suit your face shape.
  3. Consult with your hairdresser - Ask your hairdresser for recommendations on hairstyles that will enhance your facial features.
  4. Try different styles - Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one that suits your face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut:

  • Consider your hair type - Make sure the haircut suits your hair type and texture.
  • Choose a style that complements your facial features - If you have a sharp jawline, choose a haircut that highlights it.
  • Accessorize - Use accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some style to your hair.

FAQ

Q. Can short hair make my face look skinnier?

A. Yes, short hair can make your face look skinnier if the haircut is done correctly and complements your facial features.

Q. What are the best short haircuts for skinny faces?

A. The best short haircuts for skinny faces are pixie cuts, bob cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.

Q. Will short hair make my face look too thin?

A. No, if the haircut is done correctly, it will enhance your facial features and make your face look more defined.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it is essential to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damage to your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to avoid damage to your hair. Finally, embrace your short hair and rock it with confidence!
