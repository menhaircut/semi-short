What s The Most Attractive Face Shape In 2023

Written by Daniel Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to physical attractiveness, there are a number of factors that can influence our perception of beauty. One of the most important of these factors is the shape of the face. In this article, we'll explore what's considered to be the most attractive face shape, as well as provide tips for hair care and more.

Table of Contents

  1. What's the Most Attractive Face Shape?
  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Face Shape
  4. Tips for Enhancing Your Face Shape
  5. FAQs About Face Shapes
  6. Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

What's the Most Attractive Face Shape?

While beauty is certainly in the eye of the beholder, there are certain face shapes that are generally considered to be more attractive than others. According to research, the most attractive face shape is the oval face shape. This is because the oval face shape is symmetrical and balanced, with a forehead that isn't too large or too small, and a chin that isn't too pointed or too round.

Furthermore, the oval face shape is versatile and can be paired with a variety of hairstyles and makeup looks. It's also a shape that's easy to maintain, as it doesn't require any drastic changes or modifications to look its best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're unsure about your face shape, there are a few key characteristics to look for that can help you determine which shape you have. These include:

  • The width of your forehead
  • The length of your face
  • The shape of your jawline
  • The prominence of your cheekbones

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Face Shape

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine your face shape:

  1. Take a photo of your face, making sure that you're looking straight ahead and that your hair is pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo and use a ruler to measure the width of your forehead, the length of your face, and the width of your jawline.
  3. Compare your measurements to the following criteria:
    • Oval face shape: forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width
    • Round face shape: forehead and jawline are roughly the same width, and the length of the face is about the same as the width
    • Square face shape: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, and the length of the face is about the same as the width
    • Heart face shape: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointed
    • Diamond face shape: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower

Tips for Enhancing Your Face Shape

Regardless of your face shape, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to enhance your natural beauty:

  • Choose hairstyles that complement your face shape
  • Use makeup to highlight your best features
  • Wear clothing that flatters your body type
  • Maintain healthy skin and hair with a good skincare and hair care routine

FAQs About Face Shapes

What's the rarest face shape?

The rarest face shape is the diamond face shape, which accounts for only 1% of the population.

Can face shape change over time?

Yes, face shape can change over time due to factors such as aging, weight gain or loss, and changes in bone structure.

Can you change your face shape with makeup?

You can use makeup to contour and highlight certain areas of your face to create the illusion of a different shape, but you can't permanently change your face shape with makeup alone.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Oval face shape: most hairstyles will work for this shape, but avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of the head
  • Round face shape: choose haircuts that elongate the face, such as long layers or an angled bob
  • Square face shape: opt for hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as a long, layered style or a side-swept fringe
  • Heart face shape: try a chin-length bob or a layered style that adds volume to the bottom of the hair
  • Diamond face shape: balance out the widest part of the face (the cheekbones) with a haircut that adds volume at the top and bottom of the head

By following these tips and embracing your natural beauty, you can enhance your face shape and feel confident and beautiful every day.
