Types of Swimsuits for Large Busts

When it comes to swimsuits for large busts, there are a few different styles to choose from:

Bra-sized Swimsuits

Bra-sized swimsuits are designed to fit like a bra, with cups that correspond to your bra size. These swimsuits offer the best support for large busts and are perfect for women who want a comfortable and secure fit.

One-Piece Swimsuits

One-piece swimsuits are a great option for women with large busts because they offer more coverage and support than bikinis. Look for one-piece swimsuits with adjustable straps and built-in bras for added support.

Bikinis

Bikinis can be tricky for women with large busts, but don't let that stop you from wearing one! Look for bikinis with underwire or built-in bras for the best support. High-waisted bottoms can also be a good option for balancing out your silhouette.

Fitting Tips

When trying on swimsuits, pay attention to the fit in the following areas:

Bust

The most important area to consider when choosing a swimsuit for a large bust is the bust itself. Look for swimsuits with adjustable straps, underwire, or built-in bras for added support. Make sure the cups fit properly and don't create any spillage or gaps.

Shoulders

Make sure the straps of your swimsuit fit comfortably on your shoulders without digging in or slipping off. Adjustable straps are a must for a customized fit.

Waist

If you have a larger bust, you may also have a larger waist. Look for swimsuits with tummy control panels or high-waisted bottoms to create a smooth silhouette.

Tips for Shopping

When shopping for a swimsuit for a large bust, keep the following tips in mind:

Shop at Specialty Stores

Specialty stores that cater to women with larger busts will have a wider range of sizes and styles to choose from. Look for stores that carry bra-sized swimsuits for the best fit.

Try Before You Buy

It's always best to try on swimsuits before purchasing, especially if you're buying online. Make sure to read the size chart carefully and take your measurements to ensure the best fit.

Don't Be Afraid to Mix and Match

If you have a larger bust, you may need a different size top and bottom. Don't be afraid to mix and match sizes to get the best fit.

Swimsuit Care Tips

Proper care is essential for extending the life of your swimsuit:

Rinse After Use

Rinse your swimsuit with cool water after each use to remove chlorine, salt, and sand. Avoid wringing out your swimsuit, as this can damage the fabric.

Hand Wash Only

Hand wash your swimsuit with a gentle detergent and cool water. Avoid using hot water, as this can damage the fabric and cause the color to fade.

Avoid Sunscreen Stains

To avoid sunscreen stains on your swimsuit, apply sunscreen at least 30 minutes before putting on your swimsuit. Avoid sitting on rough surfaces, as this can also cause damage to the fabric.

FAQs

What is the best swimsuit for a large bust?

The best swimsuit for a large bust is one that offers adequate support and fits well. Look for swimsuits with adjustable straps, underwire, or built-in bras for added support.

Can I wear a bikini with a large bust?

Yes, you can wear a bikini with a large bust! Look for bikinis with underwire or built-in bras for the best support. High-waisted bottoms can also be a good option for balancing out your silhouette.

How do I choose the right size swimsuit?

When choosing a swimsuit, make sure to read the size chart carefully and take your measurements. It's always best to try on swimsuits before purchasing, especially if you're buying online.


Choosing the best swimsuit for a large bust doesn't have to be a stressful experience. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a swimsuit that fits well and makes you feel confident and comfortable. Remember to take proper care of your swimsuit to extend its life and always have fun in the sun!
