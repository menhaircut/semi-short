How To Braid Your Own Hair Cornrows Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle among men, and learning how to braid your own hair can be a great skill to have. Not only will it save you money on trips to the barbershop, but it can also be a fun way to experiment with different hairstyles. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps of how to braid your own hair cornrows male style.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Braid Your Own Hair Cornrows Male

Before we get started, it’s important to have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a fine-tooth comb, hair clips, and hair elastics. You may also want to have some hair gel or pomade to help smooth your hair as you braid.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your cornrows to be. You can use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 2: Start Braiding

Begin at the front of your head, taking a small section of hair and dividing it into three strands. Cross the right strand over the middle strand, then cross the left strand over the new middle strand. Continue this pattern, gradually adding more hair to each strand as you move back towards your scalp.

Step 3: Add Hair as You Braid

As you continue braiding, you’ll want to add more hair to each strand. This will help create the classic cornrow look. To do this, simply pick up a small section of hair from the section next to the one you’re braiding and add it to the strand you’re working with.

Step 4: Finish the Braid

Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section. Secure the braid with a hair elastic. Repeat this process on the remaining sections of hair until you’ve completed all of your cornrows.

Step-by-Step Guide to Braid Your Own Hair Cornrows Male

To make things easier, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Section your hair
  2. Start braiding at the front of your head
  3. Add more hair to each strand as you braid
  4. Finish the braid and secure it with a hair elastic
  5. Repeat on the remaining sections of hair

Tips for Braiding Your Own Hair Cornrows Male

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cornrows:

  • Use hair gel or pomade to help smooth your hair as you braid
  • Practice makes perfect – don’t be discouraged if your first few attempts don’t turn out perfectly
  • Take breaks as needed – braiding your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks if you need to

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to braid your own hair cornrows male style?

The time it takes to braid your own hair will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as how many cornrows you want to create. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours.

Can I braid my hair if it’s short?

Yes, you can still braid your hair if it’s short. However, you may need to use smaller sections to create the cornrows.

How long will my cornrows last?

With proper care, your cornrows can last anywhere from two weeks to a month. To help them last longer, avoid pulling or tugging on your hair and keep your scalp moisturized.

Hair Care for Cornrows

Once you’ve braided your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep your scalp healthy and your cornrows looking fresh. Here are a few tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with a hair oil or moisturizer
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Cover your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf to prevent frizz and breakage

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to braid your own hair cornrows male style in no time. Remember to take your time and be patient – the end result will be worth it!
