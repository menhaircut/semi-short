How To Take Care Of Wavy Hair In The Morning A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it looking its best. Wavy hair is prone to frizz and can be difficult to manage, especially in the morning. But with the right care and styling techniques, you can have beautiful, healthy waves every day. In this article, we will show you how to take care of wavy hair in the morning, from washing to styling.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in taking care of wavy hair in the morning is to wash it. Choose a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free, as sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz. Wet your hair thoroughly, then apply the shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

After shampooing, apply a conditioner to your hair. Be sure to focus on the mid-lengths and ends, as these are the areas that tend to be the driest. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

To keep your wavy hair looking healthy and hydrated throughout the day, apply a leave-in conditioner after you have washed and conditioned your hair. This will help to seal in moisture and prevent frizz. Apply the leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 4: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. This will help to distribute the conditioner evenly and prevent breakage.

Step 5: Apply a Styling Product

To enhance your waves and keep them looking defined, apply a styling product to your hair. Choose a product that is specifically designed for wavy hair, such as a curl cream or a mousse. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends, then scrunch your hair upward to encourage the waves.

Step 6: Diffuse Your Hair

If you have time, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and prevent frizz. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting. Gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser as you dry it.

Step 7: Finish with a Hair Spray

To lock in your style and prevent frizz, finish with a light-hold hair spray. Hold the spray about 6 inches away from your head and mist it over your hair. Be careful not to use too much, as this can weigh down your waves.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your waves.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out regularly, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If your hair is dry, you may be able to wash it less frequently.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

A: No. Regular towels can cause frizz and damage your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair.

Q: How can I revive my waves if they start to fall flat during the day?

A: Spritz your hair with a wave-enhancing spray or a mixture of water and leave-in conditioner, then scrunch your hair upward to revive your waves.
