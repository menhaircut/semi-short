How To Get A Slicked Back Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to get a slicked back hairstyle. This classic look is perfect for any occasion, from a formal event to a night out with friends. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this timeless hairstyle in no time.

Description

A slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that has been popular for decades. This hairstyle involves combing the hair back and away from the face, creating a sleek and polished appearance. It can be worn with short or long hair and is perfect for any hair type.

How to

Getting a slicked back hairstyle requires a few key tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair spray (optional)

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Starting at the front of your hairline, use a comb to comb your hair back and away from your face.
  4. Continue combing your hair back until you reach the crown of your head.
  5. Use your hands to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.
  6. If desired, spray your hair with hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to getting a slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it before styling.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Using your fingers, apply the gel or pomade evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Take a comb and start at the front of your hairline. Comb your hair straight back and away from your face.
  5. Continue combing your hair back, using the same motion as in step 4. Make sure to comb all of your hair back and away from your face.
  6. As you reach the crown of your head, use your hands to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.
  7. If desired, spray your hair with hairspray to help hold the style in place.
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 until you have achieved the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of hair gel or pomade and add more as needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb for a sleeker look or a wide-toothed comb for a more textured look.
  • Experiment with different types of hair gel or pomade to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have longer hair, try tying it back into a low ponytail or bun for a more polished look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a slicked back hairstyle:

  • Can I get a slicked back hairstyle with curly hair?
  • Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back hairstyle with curly hair. Use a hair gel or pomade that is designed for curly hair and use a wide-toothed comb to comb your hair back.
  • How do I maintain a slicked back hairstyle?
  • To maintain your slicked back hairstyle, use a small amount of hair gel or pomade each day to keep your hair in place. You can also use a dry shampoo to help absorb any excess oil.
  • Can I wear a hat with a slicked back hairstyle?
  • Yes, you can wear a hat with a slicked back hairstyle. Just be sure to smooth down any flyaways or bumps before putting on the hat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors, such as wind and cold weather.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle. Whether you're going for a formal look or a casual one, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
