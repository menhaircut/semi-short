Short Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hairstyles have been a popular trend for a while now and it's not going away anytime soon. Women of all ages and ethnicities are embracing the short hair movement and experimenting with different styles. If you're considering getting a short haircut, then you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about short hairstyles, from how to style them to the best products to use.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles are defined as haircuts that are above the shoulders and usually fall between a pixie cut and a bob. They are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personality. Some popular short hairstyles include the pixie cut, bob, lob, and shag. The great thing about short hairstyles is that they can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to messy and curly.

How to

If you're considering getting a short haircut, here are some tips to help you prepare:

  • Research different short hairstyles and find inspiration from celebrities and social media influencers.
  • Talk to your hairstylist about what short hairstyles will suit your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in short hairstyles, as they may require more frequent trims and styling.
  • Invest in quality hair products that will help you style and maintain your short hair.

Step by step

Here's how to style some popular short hairstyles:

Pixie Cut:

  1. Apply a styling product to damp hair, such as mousse or gel.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Bob:

  1. Apply a heat protectant to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a flat iron to add subtle waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Lob:

  1. Apply a styling product to damp hair, such as mousse or gel.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Shag:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using your fingers to create messy texture.
  3. Use a flat iron to add subtle waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Invest in a quality haircut from a professional hairstylist.
  • Use heat protectant products when using hot tools.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Accessorize short hairstyles with headbands, clips, or scarves.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit my face shape?

A: Short hairstyles can be customized to suit any face shape, but it's important to consult with a hairstylist to find the best cut for you.

Q: How often should I get my short hair cut?

A: Short hairstyles usually require more frequent trims to maintain their shape, so it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if it's naturally curly?

A: Yes, short curly hair can be styled in many different ways, from defined curls to messy waves. It's important to use products that will enhance your natural curl pattern and provide hold.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles require minimal hair care, but it's important to use products that will keep your hair healthy and nourished. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you have all the information you need about short hairstyles, you're ready to embrace this trendy and versatile haircut. Whether you opt for a pixie cut or a bob, remember to have fun and experiment with different styles and products. Happy styling!
