How To Make Your Hair Curly Overnight Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to try something new? Curly hair can add a lot of volume and texture to your look. However, it can take a lot of time and effort to curl your hair every day. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair curly overnight, so you can wake up with perfectly styled hair. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair curly overnight guys.

How to Make Your Hair Curly Overnight Guys

There are several methods to make your hair curly overnight, but we will focus on the two most popular methods: using braids and using buns.

Braids

Braiding your hair is a simple and effective way to create natural-looking curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many braids you want.
  3. Braid each section of hair tightly and secure the ends with a hair tie.
  4. Sleep with your braids in overnight.
  5. Take out the braids in the morning and finger-comb your hair to separate the curls.

Buns

Buns can create tighter curls than braids and are perfect for shorter hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many buns you want.
  3. Twist each section of hair tightly and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  5. Sleep with your buns in overnight.
  6. Take out the buns in the morning and finger-comb your hair to separate the curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly overnight using braids:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's no longer dripping wet.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many braids you want.
  4. Braid each section of hair tightly and secure the ends with a hair tie.
  5. Sleep with your braids in overnight.
  6. Take out the braids in the morning and finger-comb your hair to separate the curls.
  7. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your curls to hold their shape.

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly overnight using buns:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's no longer dripping wet.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many buns you want.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  5. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  6. Sleep with your buns in overnight.
  7. Take out the buns in the morning and finger-comb your hair to separate the curls.
  8. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your curls to hold their shape.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly Overnight

Here are some tips to help you make your hair curly overnight:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Dirty or oily hair can weigh down your curls and make them fall flat.
  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls.
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your curls before you go to bed to help hold their shape.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose ends or flyaways.
  • Wrap a silk or satin scarf around your hair to help protect your curls while you sleep.

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls should last all day if you apply a curl-defining cream or gel after you take out your braids or buns.

Q: Will this work on all hair types?

A: Yes, these methods will work on all hair types, but the results may vary depending on your natural hair texture and thickness.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer or heat tools to speed up the process?

A: It's best to avoid using heat tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and frizz. Letting your hair dry naturally overnight is the safest and healthiest option.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to help lock in moisture.
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can cause damage and frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair curly overnight and enjoy a new hairstyle without spending hours in front of the mirror. Experiment with different methods and find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!
