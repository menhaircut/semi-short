Description

Curly hair has always been in fashion, but in 2020, it has taken the world by storm. Celebrities, models, and influencers are rocking their natural curls, and it has become a trend that is here to stay. Whether you have loose waves or tight coils, there is a curly hair trend that will work for you.

Curly hair is all about embracing your natural texture and working with it, rather than against it. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to style your curly hair in 2020, provide you with step-by-step instructions, and give you tips for maintaining your curls.

How to Style Curly Hair in 2020

The key to styling curly hair in 2020 is to let your natural texture shine. This means using products that enhance your curls, rather than weighing them down or making them frizzy. Here are some tips for styling your curly hair in 2020:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their shape.
  • Finish with a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your curly hair in 2020:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair, working it through from roots to ends.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  5. Flip your head upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls, scrunching them as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair can be challenging, but it's essential for keeping your curls healthy and defined. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to bed to protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

  • Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?
    A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use heat-protectant products and not to do it too often, as it can damage your curls.
  • Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?
    A: It depends on your hair type, but most people with curly hair only need to wash it once or twice a week.
  • Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?
    A: It's best to use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner on curly hair, as sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a specific hair care routine to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to define your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to bed to protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, curly hair is definitely in fashion in 2020, and it's a trend that is here to stay. Whether you have loose waves or tight coils, there is a curly hair trend that will work for you. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can keep your curls healthy, defined, and on-trend.
