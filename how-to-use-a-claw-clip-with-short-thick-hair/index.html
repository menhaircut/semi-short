How To Use A Claw Clip With Short Thick Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short thick hair, you might think that claw clips are not for you. However, with the right technique, you can use claw clips to create chic hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will show you how to use a claw clip with short thick hair.

Description

Claw clips are a popular hair accessory that helps to create quick and easy hairstyles. They are perfect for those with short thick hair, as they can hold hair securely in place without slipping. Claw clips come in various sizes and designs, making them suitable for different hair types and styles.

How to

Here's how to use a claw clip with short thick hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before using a claw clip, you need to prepare your hair. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and apply some hair oil or serum to make it manageable.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb or your fingers. You can create a middle or side part, or leave your hair as it is.

Step 3: Gather your hair

Gather the section of hair you want to put in the claw clip. Hold it firmly in one hand and use the other hand to open the claw clip.

Step 4: Position the claw clip

Place the claw clip at the base of the section of hair and slide it up towards the scalp. The teeth of the clip should be facing downwards, towards your scalp.

Step 5: Secure the claw clip

Once you have positioned the claw clip, press the top part of the clip down to secure it in place. Make sure the clip is holding your hair securely without causing any discomfort.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you use a claw clip with short thick hair:

  • Choose a claw clip that is suitable for your hair length and thickness.
  • Use a hair tie to secure your hair before using a claw clip, especially if you have very thick hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles using claw clips, such as half up, half down, or a messy bun.
  • Use hair accessories such as bobby pins or hair clips to add more texture and volume to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using a claw clip with short thick hair:

Can I use a claw clip for a formal occasion?

Yes, you can use a claw clip for a formal occasion. Choose a claw clip with a sleek design and add some accessories such as a hairpin or a headband to create an elegant look.

Can I use a claw clip for sports or outdoor activities?

Yes, you can use a claw clip for sports or outdoor activities. Choose a claw clip that is made of durable materials and can hold your hair securely in place.

How often should I clean my claw clip?

You should clean your claw clip regularly to avoid buildup of dirt and oil. Use a damp cloth or a toothbrush to remove any debris from the clip, and let it air dry.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it is important to follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and lifestyle. Here are some tips for taking care of short thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or a leave-in conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain its shape.

By following these tips, you can keep your short thick hair healthy and beautiful, and create stunning hairstyles using a claw clip.
