How To Cut Men s Short Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your hair at home, you're not alone. With the rise of YouTube tutorials and affordable hair clippers, many men are taking matters into their own hands. But if you're new to cutting hair, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll walk you through the basics of how to cut men's short hair using YouTube tutorials.

Description

The first step to cutting men's short hair is to understand what you're working with. Short hair is typically defined as hair that is less than three inches long. This can include buzz cuts, fades, and textured crops. When cutting short hair, it's important to pay attention to the natural growth patterns and texture of the hair. This will help you achieve a more natural-looking cut.

How to Cut Men's Short Hair Youtube

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's short hair using YouTube tutorials:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather your tools. This includes a set of hair clippers with guards, scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to invest in a cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Choose Your Tutorial

There are countless YouTube tutorials on how to cut men's short hair. Take some time to browse through different channels and find a tutorial that matches the style you're going for. Some popular channels include Alpha M, Beardbrand, and Cut and Grind.

Step 3: Watch the Tutorial

Before you start cutting, watch the tutorial all the way through. Pay attention to the techniques used and the tools required. If you're unsure about anything, rewind and watch it again.

Step 4: Prep the Hair

Before you start cutting, wash and dry the hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a more even cut. Once the hair is dry, comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 5: Section the Hair

Using hair clips, section off the hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure you don't miss any spots.

Step 6: Start Cutting

Following the techniques outlined in the tutorial, start cutting the hair. Work in small sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use the clippers with guards to create an even length, and scissors to trim any longer pieces. Be sure to blend the hair at the sides and back for a seamless look.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, take a step back and check your work. Look for any uneven spots or areas that need to be touched up. You may also want to ask a friend or family member to take a look and offer feedback.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you're happy with the cut, it's time to clean up. Use a vacuum or broom to sweep up any hair clippings, and wash any tools that need cleaning.

Tips

Here are some tips to help ensure a successful haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. This will help avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the sides and back of the hair.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's easier to take off more hair than to add it back on.
  • Invest in quality tools. Cheap clippers can snag or pull the hair, which can be painful and result in an uneven cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's short hair using YouTube tutorials:

Is it hard to cut men's short hair?

It can be challenging if you're new to cutting hair. However, with practice and the right tools, it's possible to achieve a great cut at home.

Can I cut my hair without a tutorial?

If you're new to cutting hair, it's recommended to use a tutorial as a guide. This will help ensure a more even and professional-looking cut.

How often should I cut my short hair?

Short hair typically needs to be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using a quality styling product, and getting regular trims. Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut men's short hair using YouTube tutorials with confidence. Remember to take your time, follow the tutorial closely, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
