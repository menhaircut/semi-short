Mens Hair Short Back And Sides Long On Top A Relaxed Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's both trendy and practical, then the mens hair short back and sides long on top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is also known as the "undercut" and has been popular for several years now. In this guide, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve this hairstyle, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve the Mens Hair Short Back and Sides Long on Top

The mens hair short back and sides long on top hairstyle is characterized by having a shorter length on the sides and back of the head, while the top section is left longer. Here are the steps to achieve this hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or build-up on your hair and make it easier to style.
  2. Using a pair of clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. This can range from a short buzz cut to a longer length, depending on your preference.
  3. Next, use scissors to cut the top section of your hair. This is where you can get creative and decide on the length and style that you want. You can choose to leave it long and messy or slick it back for a more formal look.
  4. Once you've cut your hair, apply a styling product such as wax or pomade to help hold the style in place. You can also use a blow dryer to help set the style.

Remember, this hairstyle works best on hair that is thick and has some texture. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to consider a different hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hair Short Back and Sides Long on Top

To keep your mens hair short back and sides long on top looking its best, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you're going for a more formal look, consider using a comb to help style your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs about Mens Hair Short Back and Sides Long on Top

Q: Can I achieve this hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add some extra texture and volume to this hairstyle.

Q: How often do I need to visit my barber for touch-ups?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style starts to lose its shape. As a general rule, we recommend visiting your barber every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I still achieve this hairstyle if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can still achieve this hairstyle even if you have a receding hairline. However, you may want to keep the sides and back slightly longer to help create the illusion of more volume on the top.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hair Short Back and Sides Long on Top

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dye, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the mens hair short back and sides long on top hairstyle with ease. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make a statement. So go ahead and give it a try!
