How To Cut Bangs For Round Face Step By Step

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your round face and want to change your hairstyle? Well, cutting bangs can be an excellent solution for you. Bangs can help frame your face and make it appear slimmer. In this article, we will guide you through how to cut bangs for round face step by step.

How to:

1. Determine the Type of Bangs

Before you start cutting, you should decide on the type of bangs you want. There are different types of bangs, such as side-swept, blunt, or choppy. If you have a round face, side-swept or asymmetrical bangs will be the best option for you. They will help create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

2. Prepare Your Hair and Tools

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Comb your hair and remove all the tangles. To cut your bangs, you will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

3. Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be at the center of your forehead, and the base should be at the top of your head.

4. Wet Your Bangs

Use a spray bottle to wet your bangs. Wetting your hair will make it easier to cut and will prevent split ends.

5. Cut Your Bangs

Hold your hair between your index and middle finger and cut it in a straight line. Start cutting from the center of your forehead and work your way towards the sides. Cut your bangs a little longer than you want them to be. You can always trim them later.

6. Trim Your Bangs

After cutting, comb your bangs and trim any uneven ends. Use small, sharp scissors to trim your bangs.

7. Style Your Bangs

Blow-dry your bangs and style them as you wish. You can use a hair straightener or a curling iron to create different styles.

8. Check the Length

Check the length of your bangs by pulling them down. If they are too long, trim them a little bit more.

9. Finish with Hair Spray

Finish your hairstyle with a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips:

  • Start cutting your bangs a little longer than you want them to be. You can always trim them later.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Wet your hair before cutting to make it easier to cut.
  • Comb your hair and remove all the tangles before cutting.
  • Use a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.

FAQ:

1. Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your bangs yourself. Just follow the steps mentioned above carefully.

2. What type of bangs is best for a round face?

Side-swept or asymmetrical bangs are the best option for a round face. They help create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

3. Can I style my bangs differently?

Yes, you can style your bangs differently. You can use a hair straightener or a curling iron to create different styles.

Hair Care:

After cutting your bangs, take care of them to avoid split ends. Use a hair conditioner regularly and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your bangs every 6-8 weeks to maintain their length.

Cutting bangs can be a fun and easy way to change your look. Follow the steps mentioned above carefully to get the perfect bangs for your round face.
