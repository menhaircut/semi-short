What Hair Colors Go With My Skin Tone A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Petter Nov 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about changing up your hair color, it's important to consider your skin tone. The right hair color can enhance your features and make you look radiant, while the wrong color can wash you out or create a harsh contrast. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to determine your skin tone and suggest hair colors that complement it.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. Here are three methods you can use:

Method 1: Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell or they appear blue-green, you likely have neutral undertones.

Method 2: Consider Your Sunburn and Tan Patterns

Do you burn easily in the sun and rarely tan? You likely have cool undertones. Do you tan easily and rarely burn? You likely have warm undertones. If you burn and tan evenly, you likely have neutral undertones.

Method 3: Try on Jewelry

Hold up a silver piece of jewelry and a gold piece of jewelry to your skin. If the silver looks more flattering, you likely have cool undertones. If the gold looks more flattering, you likely have warm undertones. If both look good, you likely have neutral undertones.

Hair Colors for Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, your skin has a yellow or golden cast. Here are some hair colors that complement warm undertones:

  • Golden blonde
  • Honey blonde
  • Caramel
  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Warm brown

These colors will enhance your warmth and give you a sun-kissed glow.

Hair Colors for Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, your skin has a pink or blue cast. Here are some hair colors that complement cool undertones:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Silver
  • Jet black
  • Cool brown
  • Burgundy

These colors will enhance your coolness and give you a sophisticated look.

Hair Colors for Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, your skin has a balance of warm and cool tones. Here are some hair colors that complement neutral undertones:

  • Neutral blonde
  • Beige blonde
  • Chocolate brown
  • Mahogany
  • Chestnut
  • Soft black

These colors will complement your balanced undertones and give you a natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've found your perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat styling
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair a color that doesn't complement my skin tone?

A: Of course! Your hair color should ultimately make you happy. While certain colors may be more flattering, it's up to you to decide what looks best.

Q: Can I dye my hair a color that complements my skin tone but doesn't match my natural hair color?

A: Absolutely! Experimenting with hair color can be fun and liberating. Just make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

The Bottom Line

Choosing the right hair color can be a game-changer. By determining your skin tone and choosing a complementary shade, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your new look. Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun!
