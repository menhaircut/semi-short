Best Hairstyles For Circle Face Shape A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a circle face shape, you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. A circle face shape is characterized by a round jawline and cheekbones, and a width that is similar to the length of your face. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for circle face shape, including how to style them and some tips on hair care.

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Circle Face Shape
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for circle face shape, it's important to determine if you actually have a circle face shape. Here's how you can do it:

Step 1: Take a picture of yourself facing straight ahead.

Step 2: Trace the outline of your face shape using a photo editing app or a piece of paper.

Step 3: Compare the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same width, then you have a circle face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Circle Face Shape

Now that you know you have a circle face shape, let's look at some of the best hairstyles that will flatter your features:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair, while also elongating your face shape. Make sure to avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear rounder.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are perfect for circle face shapes, as they create the illusion of a longer face shape. Make sure to keep them wispy and layered, as thick bangs can add more width to your face.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can work well for circle face shapes. Opt for a longer, choppy pixie cut with layers around the crown of your head to add height and elongate your face shape.

4. Lob

A long bob, or lob, is a versatile hairstyle that can work well for circle face shapes. Keep the length of your hair just above your shoulders, and add layers around your face to create movement and volume.

5. Half Up, Half Down

A half up, half down hairstyle can be a great option for circle face shapes, as it allows you to show off your features while also creating the illusion of a longer face shape. Keep the top section of your hair voluminous, and avoid slicking it back tightly.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for circle face shape, here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

1. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair will help to elongate your face shape and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray, and blow dry your hair upside down to add more lift.

2. Avoid Center Parts

Center parts can make your face appear rounder, so it's best to avoid them if you have a circle face shape. Opt for a side part instead, and add volume to the crown of your head to balance out your features.

3. Embrace Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create movement and dimension, which can be especially flattering for circle face shapes. Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray, and scrunch your hair with your hands to create a tousled, beachy look.

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a circle face shape?

Yes, you can definitely wear your hair straight if you have a circle face shape! Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

2. Should I avoid short hairstyles if I have a circle face shape?

Not necessarily! Short hairstyles can work well for circle face shapes, as long as they are layered and textured to create movement and volume.

Hair Care for Circle Face Shape

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful if you have a circle face shape:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage. Opt for a sulfate-free shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, which is especially important if you have a circle face shape. Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

Heat styling tools can cause damage and breakage, so it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying, straightening, or curling your hair.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find the perfect look for your circle face shape!
