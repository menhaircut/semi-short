How To Style Hair For Square Face

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle. This is because square faces have a strong jawline and equally wide forehead and cheekbones. The key is to soften your features and create balance with the right hairstyle. In this article, we’ll give you some tips on how to style hair for square face shape.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and equally wide cheekbones. It is also characterized by a straight hairline and a square chin. The goal of styling hair for a square face is to soften the angles and create balance.

How to Style Hair for Square Face

When it comes to styling hair for a square face, you should focus on creating softness and volume around the face. Here are some tips:

1. Add Soft Layers

Adding soft layers around the face can help to soften the angles of a square face. This can be achieved with a layered haircut or by adding face-framing layers to your existing haircut.

2. Avoid Straight Bangs

Straight bangs can make a square face look even more angular. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or layered bangs that are shorter in the middle and longer on the sides.

3. Go for Soft Waves

Soft waves can help to create volume and soften the angles of a square face. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by using rollers.

4. Try a Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention away from the jawline. Experiment with different parting positions to find the most flattering one for your face shape.

5. Add Height at the Crown

Adding height at the crown can help to elongate a square face. You can achieve this look by teasing the hair at the crown or by using a volumizing spray.

Step by Step

Here’s how you can style your hair for a square face shape:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Choose a styling method that works best for you. You can opt for soft waves, a side part, or a volumized crown.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling hair for a square face shape:

1. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don’t be afraid to try different hairstyles to find the most flattering one for your face shape.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves can help to add interest to your hairstyle and draw attention away from the jawline.

3. Consult with a Hairstylist

If you’re unsure about which hairstyle to choose, consult with a hairstylist who can give you personalized advice.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for a square face?

The best hairstyles for a square face are those that create softness and volume around the face. These include layered haircuts, soft waves, and side-swept bangs.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a square face. However, avoid pulling your hair back tightly as this can accentuate the jawline.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and suitable for your hair type.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often as this can damage your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when you’re out in the sun to protect your hair from damage.

In conclusion, styling hair for a square face shape is all about creating softness and volume around the face. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can accentuate your best features and create balance. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
