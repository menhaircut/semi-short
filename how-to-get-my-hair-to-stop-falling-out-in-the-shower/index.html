How To Get My Hair To Stop Falling Out In The Shower Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of seeing clumps of hair in the shower drain? Hair loss can be a frustrating and stressful experience, but there are steps you can take to combat it. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for preventing hair loss in the shower. Read on to learn more.

Why Does Hair Fall Out in the Shower?

Before we dive into our tips, it's important to understand why hair falls out in the shower in the first place. There are a few reasons why this might happen:

  • Water temperature: Hot water can be damaging to the hair and cause it to fall out more easily.
  • Shampoo: Certain shampoos can be harsh on the hair and scalp, leading to breakage and shedding.
  • Brushing: If you brush your hair while it's wet, it can be more prone to breakage and fall out.

How to Prevent Hair Loss in the Shower

Now that we know what causes hair loss in the shower, let's talk about how to prevent it. Here are some tips:

Use Lukewarm Water

As we mentioned earlier, hot water can be damaging to the hair. Instead, use lukewarm water to wash your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and shedding.

Choose a Gentle Shampoo

When selecting a shampoo, look for one that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause it to become brittle.

Massage Your Scalp

When washing your hair, take the time to massage your scalp gently. This will help to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

Avoid Brushing Wet Hair

Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so avoid brushing it when it's wet. Instead, wait until it's dry or use a wide-toothed comb to detangle it.

Use a Conditioner

Conditioner can help to strengthen the hair and prevent breakage. Be sure to apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

How much hair loss is normal?

It's normal to lose between 50-100 hairs a day. If you're losing more than that, it may be a sign of a larger issue.

Can stress cause hair loss?

Yes, stress can be a contributing factor to hair loss. Try to manage your stress levels through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Is it normal for hair to fall out in the shower?

Yes, it's normal to see some hair fall out in the shower. However, if you're noticing excessive shedding, it may be time to take action.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

Aside from the tips we've already discussed, there are some other things you can do to prevent hair loss:

  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid harsh treatments: Chemical treatments like perms, relaxers, and coloring can be damaging to the hair. If possible, avoid these treatments or opt for gentler alternatives.

Conclusion

Dealing with hair loss can be frustrating, but by taking the right steps, you can prevent it from getting worse. Remember to use lukewarm water, choose a gentle shampoo, massage your scalp, and avoid brushing wet hair. With these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and strong.
