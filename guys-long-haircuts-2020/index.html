Guys Long Haircuts 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair has been a trend for men in recent years and it is here to stay. Guys who want to sport long haircuts in 2020 have several options to choose from. In this guide, we will discuss the latest trends in guys long haircuts 2020, how to style them, tips to maintain them, and answer some frequently asked questions about long haircuts for men.

  1. Latest Trends in Guys Long Haircuts 2020
  2. How to Style Guys Long Haircuts 2020
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cut Guys Long Haircuts 2020

  5. FAQ about Guys Long Haircuts 2020

Latest Trends in Guys Long Haircuts 2020

The latest trends in guys long haircuts 2020 include messy waves, slicked-back hair, and braids. Messy waves are perfect for guys who want a casual and relaxed look. Slicked-back hair is ideal for formal occasions or for guys who want a clean and polished appearance. Braids are great for guys who want to add some texture and dimension to their long haircuts.

Another trend in guys long haircuts 2020 is the "curtain haircut". This style features long hair parted down the middle, with the hair covering the forehead on either side. This style was made popular by celebrities like Timothée Chalamet and Harry Styles.

How to Style Guys Long Haircuts 2020

The styling method for guys long haircuts 2020 depends on the specific cut and style. For messy waves, apply some sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. For slicked-back hair, use a styling gel or wax and comb the hair back. For braids, section the hair and braid it in the desired style. For the curtain haircut, add some texture to the hair with a texturizing spray and part it down the middle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cut Guys Long Haircuts 2020

The first step in cutting guys long haircuts 2020 is to determine the desired length and style. Next, section the hair and comb it thoroughly. Use sharp scissors to trim the ends and create layers if necessary. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides. For the curtain haircut, part the hair down the middle and trim any stray hairs.

Tips to Maintain Guys Long Haircuts 2020

Maintaining guys long haircuts 2020 requires regular washing and conditioning. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for long hair. Avoid using hot water and excessive heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle the hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. Trim the hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

FAQ about Guys Long Haircuts 2020

Q: How long does it take for guys to grow long hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years for guys to grow long hair, depending on the desired length and texture.

Q: Who can wear guys long haircuts 2020?

A: Guys of all ages and hair types can wear long haircuts in 2020, as long as the style suits their face shape and personal style.

Q: Can guys with curly hair wear long haircuts?

A: Yes, guys with curly hair can wear long haircuts, but they may need to use specific hair products to maintain their curls and prevent frizz.

Hair Care for Guys Long Haircuts 2020

Proper hair care is essential for guys long haircuts 2020. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair hydrated and smooth. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when necessary. Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

Overall, guys long haircuts 2020 offer a wide range of styles and options for men who want to express their personal style. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care routine, guys can achieve a long and luscious mane that is both trendy and stylish.
