Best Hair Color For 70 Year Old Woman

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a 70-year-old woman looking for the perfect hair color to enhance your natural beauty? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hair color options for 70-year-old women. Whether you want to cover up gray hairs or add some dimension to your hair, we have got you covered.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural color and becomes more brittle and thin. Choosing the right hair color can not only help you look younger but also boost your confidence. It is important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and facial features.

How To Choose the Right Hair Color?

Choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, especially if you are not sure which color will suit you the best. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair color:

Tip 1: Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair color will look the best on you. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for warm shades like golden brown, honey blonde, and copper. If you have a cool skin tone, choose cool shades like ash brown, platinum blonde, and burgundy.

Tip 2: Choose a Low-Maintenance Color

As you age, you may not have the time or energy to maintain high-maintenance hair colors. Choose a hair color that requires minimal upkeep, such as highlights or lowlights.

Tip 3: Don't Go Too Dark

Going too dark can make you look older and wash out your complexion. Instead, choose a hair color that is one or two shades lighter than your natural hair color.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you have decided to color your hair at home, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Color

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. Read the instructions carefully and make sure you have all the necessary tools.

Step 2: Do a Patch Test

Before applying the hair color, do a patch test to make sure you are not allergic to the product. Apply a small amount of the hair color on your skin and wait for 24 hours. If there is no reaction, you can proceed with the hair color.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo and dry it completely. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the color from staining your skin.

Step 4: Apply the Hair Color

Follow the instructions on the box and apply the hair color evenly. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water. Apply the conditioner that came with the hair color and leave it on for a few minutes. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the cuticles.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair color:

Tip 1: Use Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for color-treated hair. Avoid using hot water as it can strip the color from your hair.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause your hair color to fade quickly. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help maintain the health of your hair and prevent split ends, which can make your hair color look dull.

FAQs

Q1. Can I dye my hair if it is thinning?

A1. Yes, you can dye your hair if it is thinning. However, it is recommended to choose a hair color that is gentle on your hair and doesn't cause further damage.

Q2. Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A2. If you have sensitive skin, it is recommended to do a patch test before coloring your hair. Choose a hair color that is gentle on your scalp and doesn't cause any irritation.

Q3. How often should I color my hair?

A3. It depends on the type of hair color you choose. If you opt for a permanent hair color, you will need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks. If you choose a semi-permanent hair color, you will need to touch up your hair every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

Now that you have colored your hair, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make your hair color look dull.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet can promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Now that you know the best hair color options for 70-year-old women, go ahead and try out a new hair color. Remember to choose a color that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy coloring!
