What Haircut Suits A Rectangle Face

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering what haircut suits a rectangle face? If you have a rectangular face shape, you might find it challenging to find the right haircut that complements your features. However, there are many hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and bring out your best features. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the best haircuts for a rectangle face.

Description

A rectangle face shape has a strong jawline, high forehead, and a long, straight cheekbone line. This face shape is longer than it is wide and can often look angular. The goal of a good haircut for a rectangle face is to soften the angles and create more width in the face to balance out the length.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your rectangle face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face from the hairline to the chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your features.

When choosing a haircut, you want to create volume and width in the areas that need it the most. This means adding layers, curls, or waves to the hair. You can also try side-swept bangs or a deep side part to create the illusion of width in the forehead and cheekbones.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a haircut for a rectangle face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Look for hairstyles that add volume and width
  3. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the length of your face
  4. Consider adding layers, curls, or waves to your hair
  5. Try side-swept bangs or a deep side part

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a rectangle face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume and width to your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your strong jawline
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one
  • Consider getting a consultation with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for a rectangle face:

What is the best haircut for a rectangle face?

The best haircut for a rectangle face is one that adds volume and width to your face. This can include layered cuts, waves, curls, side-swept bangs, and deep side parts.

Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a rectangle face?

Short haircuts can work for a rectangle face if they add volume and width to the face. However, you should avoid very short haircuts that accentuate the length of your face.

Can I have straight hair if I have a rectangle face?

Yes, you can have straight hair if you have a rectangle face. However, you should consider adding layers or waves to create volume and width in the face.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut for your rectangle face, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after shampooing
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Overall, finding the right haircut for a rectangle face takes some time and experimentation. However, by following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and brings out your best self.
