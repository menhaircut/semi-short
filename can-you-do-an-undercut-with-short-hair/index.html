Can You Do An Undercut With Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair and want to try something new? An undercut might be the perfect solution for you! But, can you do an undercut with short hair? The answer is yes, you can! In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve the perfect undercut with short hair.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially among women. Undercuts can be done on any hair length, including short hair.

How to

Here's how you can achieve the perfect undercut with short hair:

Step 1: Decide on the length of your undercut

The first thing you need to do is decide on the length of your undercut. For short hair, we recommend going for a shorter undercut, as this will create a more dramatic look. You can also opt for a subtle undercut, where only a small section of hair is shaved or cut short.

Step 2: Choose the right clipper size

Choosing the right clipper size is crucial when doing an undercut. For short hair, we recommend using a clipper with a size between 1 to 3, depending on how short you want your hair to be. Make sure to test the clipper on a small section of hair first to see if it's the right size.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair by separating the top section from the sides and back. Use hair clips to secure the top section and make it easier for you to work on the sides and back.

Step 4: Start shaving or cutting

Start shaving or cutting the sides and back of your hair using the clipper. Make sure to follow the natural curve of your head and use a mirror to check if you're doing it evenly. You can also use scissors to clean up any stray hairs.

Step 5: Blend the top section with the undercut

Once you're done with the sides and back, unclip the top section and blend it with the undercut. You can use scissors or a razor to create a seamless transition between the top and the undercut.

Step 6: Style your hair

Now that you have your perfect undercut, it's time to style your hair. You can use a hair gel or wax to create a slicked-back look, or you can leave it natural for a more casual look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing an undercut with short hair:

  • Choose a clipper with a good quality blade to avoid any nicks or cuts.
  • Start with a longer clipper size and gradually go shorter until you achieve your desired length.
  • Make sure to clean your clipper after every use to maintain its quality.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about doing an undercut on your own.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing an undercut with short hair:

  • Can I do an undercut with curly hair? Yes, you can! However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair to avoid any damage.
  • How often do I need to maintain my undercut? It depends on how fast your hair grows. We recommend maintaining it every 4-6 weeks.
  • Can I dye my undercut? Yes, you can! Dyeing your undercut can add a pop of color to your hair and make it more unique.

Hair Care

After doing an undercut with short hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and quality. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Now that you know that you can do an undercut with short hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect undercut and rock a new look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and quality.
