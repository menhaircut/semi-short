How To Curl The Right Side Of Your Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to curl the right side of your hair? Do you find yourself constantly frustrated with uneven curls and kinks? Well, fear not, because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing our top tips and tricks for curling the right side of your hair, so you can achieve flawless, bouncy curls every time. From the tools you need to the techniques you should try, we'll cover everything you need to know. So, let's get started!

Description

Curling your hair can be a tricky process, especially when you're trying to create even curls on both sides of your head. The right side of your hair can be particularly difficult to curl, as it requires using your non-dominant hand and arm. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of practice, you can curl the right side of your hair like a pro.

How to Curl the Right Side of Your Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, it's important to prep your hair properly. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Blow-dry your hair thoroughly, making sure it's completely dry before you start curling.

Step 2: Choose Your Curling Iron

Choosing the right curling iron is key to creating the perfect curls. For the right side of your hair, we recommend using a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or smaller. This will help you create tight, defined curls that will hold their shape throughout the day.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections, starting with the right side. Clip the left side of your hair out of the way, so you can focus on curling the right side. You may want to use hair clips or hair ties to keep each section of hair separate.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling. Take a small section of hair and clamp it into your curling iron, starting at the root. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until you've curled all the sections on the right side of your head.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

Once you've curled the right side of your hair, it's time to finish your look. You can use a brush or your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural look. You may also want to apply a hairspray or styling product to help hold the curls in place throughout the day.

Tips for Curling the Right Side of Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you curl the right side of your hair like a pro:

  • Practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if your first few attempts aren't perfect.
  • Use a mirror to help you see what you're doing on the right side of your head.
  • Consider using a curling wand instead of a curling iron, as it can be easier to maneuver with your non-dominant hand.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as wrapping the hair around the curling iron or using the clamp to create curls.

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: To prevent your curls from falling flat, make sure you're using a heat protectant spray before you curl your hair. You may also want to use a stronger hold hairspray or styling product to help hold the curls in place throughout the day.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It's best to avoid curling your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, try to limit curling to once or twice a week, and use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Hair Care for Curled Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair after you've curled it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and preserve your curls while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl the right side of your hair like a pro in no time. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to switch up your everyday look, curled hair is a timeless and versatile style that never goes out of fashion. So, grab your curling iron and get ready to rock those bouncy, beautiful curls!
