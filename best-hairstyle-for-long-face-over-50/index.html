Best Hairstyle For Long Face Over 50

Written by Luffy Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for long face over 50. We will cover everything from how to choose the right style to tips on how to care for your hair. So let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and more brittle. This can make finding the right hairstyle even more challenging. If you have a long face, it's important to find a style that will complement your features and make you look your best. The right hairstyle can also help to give your hair more volume and thickness.

How to Choose the Right Style

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your long face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length of your face from your forehead to your chin. If this measurement is longer than the width of your face, then you have a long face.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. Some of the best hairstyles for long face over 50 include layered cuts, bobs, and pixie cuts. These styles can help to add volume and thickness to your hair while also framing your face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length of your face from your forehead to your chin.
2. Look for hairstyles that will complement your features, such as layered cuts, bobs, and pixie cuts.
3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which style will work best for your hair type and face shape.
4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
5. Invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face appear even longer.
2. Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to help shorten the length of your face.
3. Use volumizing products to add thickness and body to your hair.
4. Experiment with different styles and hair accessories to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a long face?
A: Yes, you can wear your hair long, but it's important to add layers and volume to help balance out the length of your face.

Q: What is the best haircut for a long face over 50?
A: Some of the best haircuts for a long face over 50 include layered cuts, bobs, and pixie cuts.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair?
A: You can add volume to your hair by using volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, and by blow-drying your hair upside down.

Q: What can I do to keep my hair healthy?
A: To keep your hair healthy, it's important to use quality hair care products, such as shampoo and conditioner, and to avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to invest in quality hair care products. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and that contain natural ingredients. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and be sure to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for long face over 50, it's time to schedule an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one!
