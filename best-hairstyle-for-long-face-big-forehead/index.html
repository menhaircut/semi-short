Best Hairstyle For Long Face Big Forehead

Written by Robby Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a long face and a big forehead? Have you ever wondered which hairstyle would suit you the best? Well, you're in luck because we've created a guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and forehead size. Read on to discover the best hairstyle for a long face and a big forehead.

Description

A long face and a big forehead can be a challenging combination when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances out the proportions of your face and forehead. You want to create the illusion of a shorter face and a smaller forehead. The right hairstyle can make a huge difference, and it can boost your confidence and enhance your overall look.

How to

The first step to finding the best hairstyle for a long face and a big forehead is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face, and then comparing the two measurements. If your face is longer than it is wide, then you have a long face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement it.

Step 1: Bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out a big forehead. They can be blunt, side-swept, or textured, depending on your preference. Blunt bangs are ideal for someone with a high forehead, while side-swept bangs can help to break up the length of a long face. Textured bangs are perfect for someone who wants something a little more low-maintenance.

Step 2: Layers

Layers can add volume and dimension to a long face. They can be short or long, depending on your preference. Short layers can add volume to the crown of your head, while long layers can help to soften the length of your face.

Step 3: Curls

Curls are a great way to add volume to a long face. They can be tight or loose, depending on your preference. Tight curls can add volume and dimension to a long face, while loose curls can help to soften the length of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for a long face and a big forehead:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight, as they can accentuate the length of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will work best for your face shape and forehead size.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a long face and a big forehead?

The best hairstyle for a long face and a big forehead is one that balances out the proportions of your face and forehead. Bangs, layers, and curls are all great options to consider.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face and a big forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight, as well as hairstyles that are too long and straight. These styles can accentuate the length of your face and forehead.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your long face and big forehead, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and forehead size, you can look and feel your best every day.
