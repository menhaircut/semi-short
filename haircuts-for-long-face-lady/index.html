Haircuts For Long Face Lady A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Are you a long-faced lady who's struggling to find the perfect haircut? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best haircuts for long face ladies that will flatter your features and make you look fabulous. So, let's get started.

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, prominent chin, and narrow cheeks. The goal of a good haircut for a long-faced lady is to create the illusion of width and balance out the features.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long face, you want to avoid styles that make your face appear longer. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and width to your face. Here are some haircuts that are perfect for long-faced ladies.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long-faced ladies as they add volume and movement to the hair. This style works best when the layers are cut starting from the chin downwards. This helps to create the illusion of width around the cheeks and jawline.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to shorten the length of the face. They create the illusion of width and draw attention away from the high forehead. Be sure to ask your stylist to cut the bangs in a way that will flatter your face shape.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic style that works well for long-faced ladies. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers or curls to add volume and width to your face. This style works well with side-swept bangs to balance out the face.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your stylist about your face shape and hair type. 2. Choose a haircut that adds volume and width to your face. 3. Consider adding layers, curls, or side-swept bangs to your haircut. 4. Ask your stylist to cut your hair to flatter your face shape. 5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and a good hair care routine.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer. 2. Add volume and width to your hair with layers, curls, or side-swept bangs. 3. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. 4. Consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a haircut. 5. Invest in a good hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q1. What is the best haircut for a long-faced lady? A. The best haircut for a long-faced lady is one that adds volume and width to the face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, or a bob haircut. Q2. Will a short haircut make my face look longer? A. It depends on the style and cut of the haircut. Generally, shorter hairstyles tend to make the face appear longer, so it's best to opt for a longer style if you have a long face shape. Q3. How often should I get my haircut? A. It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it's important to invest in a good hair care routine. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools frequently, as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools. And finally, protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf when you're out in the sun. In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for a long-faced lady can be a challenge, but with the right style and cut, you can flatter your features and look fabulous. Use the tips and advice in this article to choose a haircut that works best for you, and maintain it with a good hair care routine. Happy styling!
