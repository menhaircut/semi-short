How To Do A Cute Messy Bun With Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same boring hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further than the cute messy bun! This hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair and can be dressed up or down for any occasion. Here's how to achieve the perfect messy bun in just a few simple steps.

How to:

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, using a hair tie, gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head.

Step 2: Twist your hair around the hair tie, creating a bun shape. Make sure to leave some pieces of hair out for a messier look.

Step 3: Using bobby pins, secure the bun in place. You can use as many or as few as you need to make sure the bun feels secure.

Step 4: Gently pull on the sides of the bun to create a more relaxed, messy look.

Step 5: Finish the look with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips:

- Experiment with different placements of the bun (higher up on the head or lower down) to see what looks best on you.

- Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo before starting to add some grip to your hair.

- Don't stress about making the bun perfect - the messier it looks, the better!

FAQ:

Q: Will this hairstyle work on short hair?

A: It may be a bit more difficult to achieve the perfect bun on shorter hair, but it's definitely worth a try!

Q: Can I use a hair donut to make the bun look fuller?

A: Yes! A hair donut can be a great tool to add some volume to your bun.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling. Make sure to use a heat protectant if you plan on using any hot tools, and use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Now that you know how to do a cute messy bun with medium length hair, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy bun every time.
