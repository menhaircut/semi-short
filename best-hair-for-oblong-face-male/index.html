Best Hair For Oblong Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by William Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, choosing the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that complements your face shape and highlights your best features. In this guide, we’ll help you find the best hair for oblong face male. We’ll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for oblong face males. Let’s get started!

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hair for oblong face male is to determine your face shape. Here’s how:

  1. Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the tip of your chin.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point.
  4. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead and jawline, you have an oblong face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Oblong Face Male

Now that you know your face shape, let’s take a look at the best hairstyles for oblong face male:

1. Short and Textured

A short and textured haircut can help balance out the length of your face. Keep the sides and back short, and add texture to the top with layers or a choppy cut. This will add volume and dimension to your hair, which will help make your face appear wider.

2. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a great option for oblong face males who want a low-maintenance, masculine look. This haircut will draw attention away from the length of your face and focus it on your facial features. Keep the sides and back short, and leave a little length on top for a textured look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the length of your face and add balance. Keep the sides and back short, and add some length to the top. Sweep your bangs to one side, and use a styling product to add texture and hold.

4. Messy Quiff

A messy quiff is a great option for oblong face males who want a stylish, trendy look. Keep the sides and back short, and add volume to the top with a messy, textured quiff. This will add width to your face and draw attention to your hairstyle.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve found the best hair for oblong face male, it’s important to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or flat, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should oblong face males avoid?

Oblong face males should avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or flat, as they can make your face appear even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the top of your head, as they can make your face appear even more narrow.

How often should oblong face males get a haircut?

It’s recommended that oblong face males get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

What styling products are best for oblong face males?

Styling products that add texture and hold are best for oblong face males. Look for products like pomades, waxes, and clays.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Male

Finally, here are some hair care tips for oblong face males:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’re well on your way to finding the best hair for oblong face male. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you. Good luck!
