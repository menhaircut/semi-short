Can Square Faces Wear Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a square-faced beauty wondering if you can wear bangs? You're not alone! Bangs are a timeless hairstyle that can transform your look. However, not all hairstyles suit every face shape. If you have a square face shape, you may wonder if bangs will suit you. In this article, we'll answer the question, "Can square faces wear bangs?" and provide you with tips on how to rock this look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Wear Bangs for Square Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs for Square Faces
  • Tips for Wearing Bangs with a Square Face Shape
  • FAQs About Square Faces and Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs and Square Faces

Description

A square face shape has a strong jawline and forehead, with the width of the forehead and jawline being almost the same. If you have a square face shape, you might feel like your face is angular and boxy. However, don't worry; bangs can soften your features and add a touch of femininity to your look.

How to Wear Bangs for Square Faces

The key to wearing bangs with a square face shape is to choose a style that doesn't accentuate your strong jawline. Here are some bang styles that can suit square faces:

  • Side-swept bangs: These bangs are longer on one side, and they create an angled look that softens your jawline.
  • Curtain bangs: These bangs are parted in the middle and fall to the sides, framing your face and creating a softer look.
  • Soft, wispy bangs: These bangs are feathery and light, creating a soft and feminine look that balances out your angular features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs for Square Faces

If you want to cut your own bangs, follow these steps:

  1. Start with dry, styled hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Comb the sectioned hair forward, so it's resting on your forehead.
  4. Decide on the length of your bangs and cut them with sharp scissors.
  5. Repeat until you've achieved your desired length.
  6. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Wearing Bangs with a Square Face Shape

Here are some tips for wearing bangs with a square face shape:

  • Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as they can accentuate your strong jawline.
  • Choose a bang style that is longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, as this will create a softening effect on your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair, as this will create movement and balance out your features.
  • Experiment with different bang styles to see what works best for you.

FAQs About Square Faces and Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about square faces and bangs:

  • Can a square face shape wear bangs? Yes, a square face shape can wear bangs. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features.
  • What kind of bangs should I get for a square face shape? Side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and soft, wispy bangs can all suit a square face shape.
  • Should I avoid bangs if I have a square face shape? No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a square face shape. However, it's important to choose a style that suits your features.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs and Square Faces

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your bangs and square face looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Consider using a hair serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

In conclusion, square faces can definitely wear bangs, as long as you choose a style that flatters your features. Side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and soft, wispy bangs can all suit a square face shape. Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
