Can A Perm Make Your Hair Thicker

Written by Aprilia Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having thin and flat hair? Have you been considering getting a perm to add some volume and texture to your locks? If so, you may be wondering if a perm can actually make your hair thicker. In this article, we'll explore whether a perm can give you fuller-looking hair and provide some tips on how to care for permed hair to keep it healthy and bouncy.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair, creating curls or waves that last for several months. The process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that hold the hair in its natural shape. Once the bonds are broken, the hair is wrapped around rods and another solution is applied to set the new shape. The result is bouncy, textured hair that can add volume and movement to flat locks.

How to Get a Perm

If you're interested in getting a perm, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Before getting a perm, it's important to consult with a professional stylist who can assess the condition of your hair and determine whether it's healthy enough for a chemical treatment. They can also recommend the best type of perm for your hair type and desired look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before the perm, you'll need to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup of products or oil. You should also avoid using any styling products or heat styling tools on your hair for a few days before the perm.

Step 3: Get the Perm

During the perm, your stylist will apply the solution to your hair and wrap it around rods of different sizes, depending on the type of curl or wave you want. The solution will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, and then a neutralizer will be applied to set the new shape.

Step 4: Care for Your Permed Hair

After getting a perm, it's important to care for your hair properly to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are some tips:

  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about perms:

Can a perm make your hair thicker?

A perm can give the illusion of thicker hair by adding volume and texture to your locks. However, it doesn't actually change the thickness or density of your hair.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on your hair type, the type of perm you get, and how well you care for your hair after the treatment.

Can you color your hair after a perm?

You should wait at least a week before coloring your hair after a perm to avoid damaging your locks. It's also best to consult with your stylist before coloring your permed hair to ensure that it's done safely and without causing damage.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you have permed hair or not, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some general hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

Conclusion

A perm can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. However, it doesn't actually change the thickness or density of your hair. If you're considering getting a perm, be sure to consult with a professional stylist and follow the proper care instructions to keep your hair healthy and bouncy. And remember, whether you have permed hair or not, taking good care of your locks is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair.
