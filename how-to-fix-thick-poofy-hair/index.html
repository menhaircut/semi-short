How To Fix Thick Poofy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have thick, poofy hair that is difficult to manage? You are not alone. Many people with this hair type struggle to find the right products and techniques to tame their locks. However, with the right approach, you can get your hair under control and achieve the sleek, smooth look you desire. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to fix thick poofy hair, as well as tips and tricks to maintain your new look.

How to Fix Thick Poofy Hair

Before we dive into the steps to fix thick poofy hair, it is important to understand why your hair behaves this way. Thick hair tends to be heavy and can pull down on the roots, causing poofiness. Additionally, hair that is dry or damaged can become frizzy and poofy. By following these steps, you can address these issues and transform your hair.

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in fixing thick poofy hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for thick hair and contain ingredients like keratin and argan oil. These ingredients will help to smooth and nourish your hair, making it more manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Once a week, apply a hair mask to your hair. Look for a mask that contains hydrating ingredients like coconut oil and shea butter. Leave the mask on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out. This will help to nourish your hair and reduce poofiness.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a blow dryer to dry it. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using a round brush to smooth out your hair. This will help to reduce poofiness and give your hair a sleek look.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

If you still have some poofiness after blow drying, use a flat iron to smooth out your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with a Smoothing Serum

Once your hair is dry and styled, apply a smoothing serum to your hair. This will help to tame any remaining frizz and give your hair a glossy finish.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Now that you have fixed your thick poofy hair, it is important to maintain your new look. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking sleek and smooth:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still use styling products on my hair?

A: Yes, you can still use styling products on your hair. However, be sure to use them sparingly and choose products that are designed for your hair type.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days. However, if you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care

Fixing thick poofy hair requires a bit of effort, but the results are worth it. By following these steps and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can transform your hair and feel confident in your appearance. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for you.
