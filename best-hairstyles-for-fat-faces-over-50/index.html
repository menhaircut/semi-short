Best Hairstyles For Fat Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have a round or chubby face, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, there are plenty of options to choose from that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for fat faces over 50, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Description

As we age, our face shape may change, and we may gain weight in our face and neck area. This can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements our features and highlights our best assets. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles to choose from that can help you look and feel your best, regardless of your face shape or age.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

When selecting a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. If you have a round or chubby face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head, elongating your face and creating a more balanced look.

Layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts are all excellent options for those with a round or chubby face. These styles add dimension and movement to your hair, creating a flattering and youthful appearance.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hairstyles for Fat Faces Over 50

To achieve the best hairstyles for fat faces over 50, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a flat iron, curling iron, and hair dryer.
  3. Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add height and lift to your hair.
  4. Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest and texture to your style.
  5. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth to your look.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

When styling your hair, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs that frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyles for fat faces over 50:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the top of your head, elongating your face and creating a more balanced look. Layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts are all excellent options for those with a round face.

What is the best hairstyle for a chubby face?

The best hairstyle for a chubby face is one that adds height and volume to the top of your head while framing your face and drawing attention to your best features. Layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts are all excellent options for those with a chubby face.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles to choose from that can help you look and feel your best, regardless of your face shape or age. By selecting a hairstyle that complements your features and personal style, following these styling and hair care tips, and consulting with your hairstylist, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look that enhances your natural beauty.
