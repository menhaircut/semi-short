Easy Updos For Long Hair Wedding A Guide To Achieving A Beautiful Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're getting married soon and have long hair, you're probably looking for an easy updo that will make you look stunning on your big day. Fortunately, there are many easy updos for long hair wedding that you can choose from. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a beautiful look with these hairstyles. We'll also give you some tips on how to take care of your hair before and after the wedding.

Description

The first thing you need to do is decide on the style you want. There are many easy updos for long hair wedding that you can choose from, including braids, buns, and twists. You can opt for a sleek and sophisticated look, or go for a more relaxed and bohemian style. Whatever your preference, make sure to choose a style that suits your dress, your face shape, and your personality.

How to

Once you've decided on the style you want, it's time to start preparing your hair. This is crucial if you want your updo to last throughout the day. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Then, apply a hair mask to nourish and hydrate your strands. After rinsing off the mask, apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

Next, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or curls. This will add dimension to your updo and make it look more interesting.

Now it's time to start creating your updo. You can start by sectioning your hair and creating a loose braid or a messy bun. You can also create twists or knots, depending on the style you want. Use bobby pins and hair ties to secure your hair in place, and make sure to spray some hairspray to keep your updo in place.

Step-by-step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create an easy updo for long hair wedding:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a hair mask to nourish and hydrate your strands.
  3. Rinse off the mask and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Create loose waves or curls using a curling iron or a straightener.
  6. Section your hair and create a loose braid or a messy bun.
  7. Use bobby pins and hair ties to secure your hair in place.
  8. Spray some hairspray to keep your updo in place.
  9. Add some accessories, such as flowers or pins, to enhance your updo.
  10. Finally, check your updo in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect easy updo for long hair wedding:

  • Start preparing your hair a few weeks before the wedding to ensure it's in good condition.
  • Choose a style that complements your dress and your face shape.
  • Use high-quality products, such as a hair mask and a leave-in conditioner, to nourish and protect your hair.
  • Don't use too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different accessories, such as flowers or pins, to add some sparkle to your updo.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy updos for long hair wedding:

What is the easiest updo for long hair?

The easiest updo for long hair is a messy bun. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it into a bun, and secure it with bobby pins and hair ties. Pull out a few strands to frame your face, and you're done!

How do I make my updo last all day?

To make your updo last all day, start by preparing your hair with a hair mask and a leave-in conditioner. Use high-quality products, such as bobby pins and hairspray, to secure your hair in place. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, and use a touch of hairspray to freshen it up if necessary.

What accessories can I use to enhance my updo?

You can use a variety of accessories to enhance your updo, such as flowers, pins, and headbands. Choose accessories that complement your dress and your hairstyle, and use them sparingly to avoid overwhelming your look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful before and after the wedding, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask once a week to nourish your strands, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. You should also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage, and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.

Conclusion

Easy updos for long hair wedding can be a beautiful and effortless way to look stunning on your big day. By following our guide and tips, you can achieve a stunning updo that complements your dress and your personality. Remember to take care of your hair before and after the wedding to keep it healthy and beautiful!
