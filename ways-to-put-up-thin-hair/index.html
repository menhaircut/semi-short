Ways To Put Up Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you might struggle with styling it because it can be hard to create volume and texture. But don't worry, there are many ways to put up thin hair that will make it look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style thin hair, step by step tutorials, and hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

How to Put Up Thin Hair

There are various ways to put up thin hair, depending on your preference and the occasion. Here are some popular options:

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well with thin hair. To create volume, tease the crown of your head with a fine-tooth comb and then gather your hair into a high ponytail. Secure it with a hair tie and use a bobby pin to pin the ends under the ponytail to create a fuller look.

Bun

A bun is another great option for thin hair. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and then twist it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun and create a fuller look. You can also add a hair accessory like a ribbon or a scrunchie to elevate the look.

Braid

A braid can add texture and volume to thin hair. French braids or fishtail braids are especially great for thin hair because they create an illusion of thickness. Start by dividing your hair into three sections and then braid as usual. Use a hair tie to secure the end of the braid.

Step by Step Tutorials

Here are some step by step tutorials on how to put up thin hair:

Ponytail Tutorial

  1. Tease the crown of your head with a fine-tooth comb.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  3. Secure it with a hair tie.
  4. Use a bobby pin to pin the ends under the ponytail to create a fuller look.

Bun Tutorial

  1. Pull your hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist it into a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun and create a fuller look.
  4. Add a hair accessory like a ribbon or a scrunchie if desired.

Braid Tutorial

  1. Divide your hair into three sections.
  2. Braid as usual.
  3. Use a hair tie to secure the end of the braid.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you put up thin hair:

  • Use volumizing products like mousse or dry shampoo to create texture and volume.
  • Tease the crown of your head with a fine-tooth comb to add height.
  • Add a hair accessory like a headband or a scarf to elevate the look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

FAQ

Can I put up thin hair without damaging it?

Yes, you can put up thin hair without damaging it by using gentle hair ties and avoiding heat styling tools.

What is the best way to add volume to thin hair?

The best way to add volume to thin hair is by using volumizing products like mousse or dry shampoo and teasing the crown of your head with a fine-tooth comb.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have thin hair?

You should avoid hairstyles that pull your hair tight, as they can make your hair look even thinner. Avoid tight braids, high buns, and tight ponytails.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thin hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Now that you know how to put up thin hair, experiment with different hairstyles and have fun styling your locks!
