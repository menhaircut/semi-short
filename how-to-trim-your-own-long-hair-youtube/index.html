How To Trim Your Own Long Hair Youtube Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of spending money on haircuts and want to learn how to trim your own long hair, then YouTube is the perfect platform for you. With thousands of tutorials available, it's easy to find a video that suits your needs and preferences. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim your own long hair YouTube, as well as some tips and FAQ to help you achieve the best results.

Description

Trimming your own long hair can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. By watching YouTube tutorials and following our guide, you'll be able to trim your own long hair with ease and save money in the process.

How to Trim Your Own Long Hair YouTube

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

The first step in trimming your own long hair is to gather the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair ties or clips to section your hair. Make sure that your scissors are specifically designed for hair cutting and are sharp enough to prevent split ends.

Step 2: Choose the Right YouTube Tutorial

Once you have your tools, it's time to choose a YouTube tutorial that suits your needs. Look for videos that focus on trimming long hair and have positive reviews and comments from viewers. Some popular hair YouTubers include Brad Mondo, Kayley Melissa, and Milabu.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start trimming your hair, it's important to wash and dry it thoroughly. This will help you see the natural texture and length of your hair and make it easier to cut. You can use your regular shampoo and conditioner and let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to section it. Use hair ties or clips to divide your hair into smaller sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. This will make it easier to trim each section evenly and prevent any uneven layers.

Step 5: Start Trimming

Now it's time to start trimming your hair. Follow the YouTube tutorial you chose and start cutting from the bottom of each section. Make small cuts and avoid cutting too much at once, as this can lead to uneven results. Remember to keep your hair comb straight and avoid pulling it too hard.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished trimming your hair, it's important to check for evenness. Use a mirror and comb to check each section and make sure that they are all the same length. If you notice any uneven areas, you can make small adjustments with your scissors.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've trimmed your hair and checked for evenness, it's time to style it. You can use your regular styling products or try something new to give your hair a fresh look. Experiment with different hairstyles and enjoy your new haircut!

Tips

- Start with small cuts and avoid cutting too much at once
- Use sharp hair cutting scissors
- Watch multiple YouTube tutorials before starting
- Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed
- Don't be afraid to ask for help

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my long hair?
A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep it healthy.

Q: Can I trim my own hair if it's curly?
A: Yes, you can trim your own curly hair by following the same steps and using the right tools.

Hair Care

Trimming your own long hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain its health. Remember to use a deep conditioner once a week, avoid using hot tools too often, and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
