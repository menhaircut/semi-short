Mens Hairstyles 2020 Skin Fade The Trending Look For Men

Written by Oliver Dec 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old haircut and want to try something new? Look no further than the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade! This haircut has been all the rage since its inception in 2016 and has remained a popular choice for men. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips on maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade is characterized by a gradual fade in hair length from the top to the sides and back of the head. The fade is achieved by using clippers to taper the length of the hair. The fade can be high, low, or mid, depending on personal preference. The top of the hair can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or a pompadour.

How to Achieve the Mens Hairstyles 2020 Skin Fade

The mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade is best achieved by going to a professional barber or hairstylist. However, if you are confident in your skills, you can attempt it at home. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair or use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Choose Your Fade Length

Decide on the length of the fade you want. You can choose a high, low, or mid fade. Use clippers with the appropriate guard size to start tapering the hair length.

Step 3: Gradually Fade the Hair

Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up, gradually fading the hair length. Blend the fade as you go along to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Style the Top of the Hair

After achieving the desired fade, style the top of the hair as desired. You can use a hair gel, pomade, or wax to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hairstyles 2020 Skin Fade

Here are some tips to keep your mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade looking fresh:

- Get regular trims to maintain the fade. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. - Use a hair styling product that suits your hair type and the desired look. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair when styling.

FAQ

What is a skin fade?

A skin fade is a haircut that gradually fades the hair length to the skin on the sides and back of the head.

Is the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade can be achieved on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can I achieve the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade at home?

While it is recommended to go to a professional hairstylist or barber, you can attempt the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade at home if you are confident in your skills.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain the mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade. Here are some tips:

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair. - Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. - Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. - Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve the trendy mens hairstyles 2020 skin fade. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products and techniques to achieve your desired look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine.
