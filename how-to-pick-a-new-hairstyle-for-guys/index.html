How To Pick A New Hairstyle For Guys

Written by Robby Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle but don't know where to start, you've come to the right place! Picking a new hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with a few simple tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect look in no time.

Description

Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style is essential. A new hairstyle can enhance your confidence, change your overall appearance, and make you feel like a new person. However, with so many different haircuts and styles to choose from, it can be challenging to determine which one is right for you.

How to

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in picking a new hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. Knowing your face shape will help you narrow down your options and select a haircut that compliments your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top. If you have thin or fine hair, a shorter haircut may be more flattering.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also play a role in the hairstyle you choose. If you prefer a more classic look, a short, tapered cut may be the way to go. If you're more adventurous, you may want to try a longer, textured cut or even a bold hair color.

Tips

- Bring photos of hairstyles you like to your stylist for inspiration

- Consider the maintenance required for your new hairstyle

- Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their opinion

FAQ

What if I don't know my face shape?

If you're unsure about your face shape, try taking a selfie and drawing an outline around your face. This will give you a better idea of your face shape and help you choose a flattering haircut.

Do I have to stick to a specific haircut based on my face shape?

No, you don't have to stick to a specific haircut based on your face shape. However, certain haircuts may be more flattering than others, so it's worth considering when choosing a new hairstyle.

Hair Care

Once you've picked your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its look. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type, and consider using styling products to enhance your new haircut. Regular trims will also help keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut that suits you. Don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles until you find your perfect look.
