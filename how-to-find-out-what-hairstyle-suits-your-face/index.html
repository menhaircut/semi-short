How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right hairstyle:

What is the best hairstyle for round faces?

The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add volume and height to the crown of the head. This can be achieved with hairstyles that have layers or those that are longer on top.

What is the best hairstyle for square faces?

The best hairstyles for square faces are those that soften the angles of the face. This can be achieved with hairstyles that have layers around the face or those that are longer on the sides.

What is the best hairstyle for oval faces?

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, hairstyles that frame the face or add volume to the crown of the head are particularly flattering.
