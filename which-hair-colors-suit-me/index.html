Which Hair Colors Suit Me

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which shades will suit you. In this article, we'll explore the different factors that can influence which hair colors suit you best, and provide some tips and tricks to help you make the right choice.

Factors that Influence Which Hair Colors Suit You

There are several factors that can influence which hair colors will look best on you, including your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Other factors to consider include your personality, lifestyle, and the season or occasion.

How Skin Tone Affects Hair Color

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hair color. Generally, people with warm undertones look best in warm hair colors like honey blonde, copper, and auburn, while those with cool undertones look best in cool shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy. Neutral undertones can generally pull off any hair color.

If you're not sure what your skin undertone is, there are several ways to find out. One easy way is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.

How Eye Color Affects Hair Color

Your eye color can also play a role in which hair colors will look best on you. For example, if you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy can complement your eyes and make them pop. If you have warm brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors like honey blonde, copper, and auburn can bring out the warmth in your eyes.

How Your Natural Hair Color Affects Your Choice

Your natural hair color can also influence which shades will suit you best. For example, if you have naturally dark hair, going too light can be a drastic change and may not suit your skin tone. On the other hand, if you have naturally light hair, going too dark can also be a drastic change and may not suit your skin tone. It's important to consider your natural hair color when choosing a new shade.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color
  • Think about your personality and lifestyle
  • Consider the season or occasion
  • Take inspiration from celebrities or social media influencers
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist

FAQs

What hair color suits me if I have fair skin?

If you have fair skin with cool undertones, try cool hair colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy. If you have fair skin with warm undertones, try warm hair colors like honey blonde, copper, and auburn.

What hair color suits me if I have dark skin?

If you have dark skin with cool undertones, try cool hair colors like jet black, espresso brown, and cool burgundy. If you have dark skin with warm undertones, try warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel brown, and warm burgundy.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for caring for colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use heat protectant when styling with heat tools
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience, and with these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find a shade that suits you perfectly!
