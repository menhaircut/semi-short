Best Long Hair For Over 60 A Guide For Women

Written by Petter Oct 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As women age, their hair changes as well. It becomes thinner, drier, and prone to breakage. However, this does not mean that they can't have long hair anymore. In fact, long hair can be very flattering for women over 60, as long as they choose the right style and take good care of their hair. In this guide, we will share the best long hair for over 60, as well as tips and tricks to keep it healthy and beautiful.

Best Long Hair Styles for Over 60

The best long hair styles for women over 60 are those that are low-maintenance, youthful, and flattering to their face shape. Here are some ideas:

1. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut adds volume and texture to thinning hair, making it look fuller and more youthful. It also frames the face nicely, drawing attention to the cheekbones and eyes. This style works well for women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help soften a square or angular face, and also add a playful touch to long hair. They can be worn straight or curled, and require minimal styling. This style works best for women with straight or wavy hair.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a classic look that never goes out of style. They add texture and movement to long hair, and can be achieved with a curling iron or a salt spray. This style works well for women with round or square faces.

4. Braids and Twists

Braids and twists are a fun and easy way to add interest to long hair. They can be worn loose or tight, and can be styled in different ways depending on the occasion. This style works well for women with any face shape.

How to Take Care of Long Hair Over 60

Taking care of long hair over 60 requires a bit of extra effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

As we age, our hair becomes more delicate and prone to damage. Therefore, it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip the hair of its natural oils. Look for a product that is sulfate-free and contains nourishing ingredients like argan oil or keratin.

2. Condition Regularly

Conditioner is a must for long hair over 60, as it helps to moisturize and detangle the hair. Apply conditioner from mid-length to ends, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing. If your hair is particularly dry, you may want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle. To avoid this, use a heat protectant spray before styling, and try to limit your use of heat tools to once a week or less.

4. Trim Regularly

Even if you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks, or whenever your hair feels dry or frizzy.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair Over 60

Styling long hair over 60 doesn't have to be complicated. Here's a simple guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo, and let it air dry or blow dry it on low heat. Avoid using a high heat setting, as this can damage the hair.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Spray a heat protectant onto your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help prevent damage from heat styling tools.

3. Curl or Straighten

If you want to add some texture to your hair, use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or sleek strands. Be sure to use a low heat setting, and don't hold the tool on your hair for too long.

4. Add Some Volume

If your hair is thinning, you may want to add some volume at the crown. Use a teasing comb or a volumizing spray to create lift.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Spray some hairspray onto your hair to help your style last all day. Choose a flexible hold formula that won't make your hair look stiff or crunchy.

Tips and Tricks for Long Hair Over 60

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you look your best with long hair over 60:

1. Experiment with Accessories

Scarves, headbands, and hair clips can add a pop of color or a touch of sparkle to your hair. Try different styles to see what works best for you.

2. Don't Overdo It with Products

Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use a light hand when applying styling products, and stick to just a few key items.

3. Choose the Right Color

If you're considering coloring your hair, choose a shade that complements your skin tone and doesn't require too much upkeep. Highlights or lowlights can also add dimension to your hair without being too dramatic.

FAQs about Long Hair for Over 60

1. Is long hair suitable for women over 60?

Yes, long hair can be very flattering for women over 60, as long as they choose the right style and take good care of their hair.

2. What are the best long hair styles for women over 60?

The best long hair styles for women over 60 are those that are low-maintenance, youthful, and flattering to their face shape. Some ideas include layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, beachy waves, and braids.

3. How do I keep my long hair healthy over 60?

To keep your long hair healthy over 60, use a gentle shampoo, condition regularly, protect your hair from heat, and trim it regularly. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
