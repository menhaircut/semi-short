How To Curl Your Hair With A Square Straightener

If you want to add some curls to your hair but don't have a curling iron on hand, don't worry! You can still achieve bouncy and voluminous curls with a square straightener. In this article, we'll walk you through how to curl your hair with a square straightener in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Square Straightener

Curling your hair with a square straightener is easier than you might think. Here's what you'll need:

  • Square straightener
  • Hairbrush
  • Hairspray

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl your hair with a square straightener:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Brush it out to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener down near the roots.
  3. Twist the straightener away from your face, and slowly glide it down the length of your hair.
  4. Release the straightener and let the curl fall naturally.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 on the remaining sections of your hair until you've curled all of it.
  6. Once you've finished curling your hair, spray it lightly with hairspray to set the curls.

And that's it! You now have beautiful, bouncy curls.

Tips for Curling Your Hair with a Square Straightener

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when curling your hair with a square straightener:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure that the curls are more defined.
  • If you want looser curls, use larger sections of hair.
  • If you want tighter curls, hold the straightener in place for a few extra seconds before releasing it.
  • Don't forget to spray your hair with hairspray after curling it to help hold the curls in place.

FAQs

Can you curl your hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a straightener. In fact, using a straightener to curl your hair can give you more defined and longer-lasting curls.

What is the best type of straightener to use for curling hair?

A square straightener is ideal for curling hair because it has straight edges that can help create defined curls.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Curls Looking Great

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a square straightener, here are a few tips to keep your curls looking great:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve your curls overnight.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a square straightener, you can achieve beautiful curls whenever you want. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
