Facial Hair Can Make You Look Thinner How To Achieve The Look You Want

Written by Bardi Sep 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your weight or your face shape? Do you wish there was an easy way to slim down your appearance without having to go on a diet or hit the gym? Believe it or not, the solution may be as simple as growing out some facial hair! In this article, we'll dive into the details about how facial hair can make you look thinner, as well as some tips and tricks for achieving the look you want.

The Science Behind the Look

Before we dive into the details about how to grow facial hair to flatter your face shape, let's take a quick look at why facial hair can make you look thinner in the first place. Essentially, facial hair can help to create the illusion of a more angular and defined face, which can help to balance out roundness or softness in your features. By adding structure and depth to your face, facial hair can help to create sharper lines and angles, which can make you look slimmer and more chiseled.

How to Grow Facial Hair to Flatter Your Face Shape

Of course, not all facial hair is created equal - and if you want to use your beard or mustache to create a thinner appearance, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips for growing facial hair that will help to make you look thinner:

  • If you have a round face, try growing a beard that is longer on the chin and shorter on the sides. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline.
  • If you have a square face, consider growing a full beard that is evenly trimmed and well-groomed. This can help to soften the sharp angles of your face and create a more balanced appearance.
  • If you have an oval face, you're in luck - almost any beard style will likely flatter your features! Consider experimenting with different shapes and lengths to find the look that suits you best.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, try growing a beard that is fuller on the chin and tapered at the sides. This can help to balance out the width of your forehead and create a more harmonious appearance.

Beard Styles That Can Help You Look Thinner

When it comes to beard styles that can help you look thinner, there are a few key options to consider. Here are some of the most popular beard styles for creating a slimming effect:

The Goatee

A classic goatee involves growing hair on the chin and keeping the cheeks and neck clean-shaven. This can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. To make the most of this style, be sure to keep your goatee neatly trimmed and well-shaped.

The Van Dyke

Similar to a goatee, a Van Dyke beard involves growing hair on the chin and mustache, while keeping the cheeks and neck clean-shaven. However, a Van Dyke typically features a more pointed and stylized shape, with the mustache and goatee hair forming a distinct "V" shape. This style can be a great option for creating a more angular and defined appearance.

The Anchor

An anchor beard involves growing hair on the chin and mustache, as well as a small patch of hair just below the lower lip. This style can help to create a more sculpted and angular look, particularly when paired with a well-groomed mustache.

Mustache Styles That Can Help You Look Thinner

If you're not interested in growing a full beard, a well-groomed mustache can also be an effective way to create a thinner appearance. Here are some mustache styles to consider:

The Chevron

A classic chevron mustache involves growing a thick, bushy mustache that extends past the corners of the mouth. This style can help to add volume and depth to the upper lip area, which can create the illusion of a fuller and more defined jawline.

The Handlebar

A handlebar mustache involves growing long, curled tips at the ends of the mustache, which can create a dramatic and stylized look. This style can be particularly effective for creating a more chiseled appearance, as the curls can help to add definition to the upper lip and cheek area.

Sideburn and Jawline Styles That Can Help You Look Thinner

In addition to beards and mustaches, sideburns and jawline styles can also be effective for creating a thinner appearance. Here are some options to consider:

The Extended Sideburn

Extending your sideburns down past your ear can help to create a more elongated and defined appearance, particularly if you keep the rest of your facial hair neatly trimmed. This style can be a great option for those with a round or soft face shape.

The Chin Strap

A chin strap involves growing a thin line of hair along the jawline, which can help to create a more angular and defined appearance. This style can be a great option for those with a square or heart-shaped face, as it can help to balance out the width of the face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

No matter which facial hair style you choose, it's important to keep your hair well-groomed and maintained in order to achieve the best possible results. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in quality grooming tools, such as a high-quality razor, beard trimmer, or mustache wax.
  • Trim your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and well-groomed.
  • Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer to keep your skin healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different styles and shapes to find the look that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions About Facial Hair and Looking Thinner

Q: How long does it take to grow a beard or mustache?

A: The timeline for growing facial hair can vary depending on your genetics, age, and other factors. In general, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to grow a full beard or mustache.

Q: Can facial hair really make you look thinner?

A: Yes! By creating more defined angles and adding structure to your face, facial hair can help to create the illusion of a slimmer and more chiseled appearance.

Q: What if I don't like the way my facial hair looks?

A: If you're not happy with your facial hair style, don't be afraid to try something new! Remember that facial hair is a versatile and adaptable accessory, and there are countless styles and shapes to choose from. Experiment with different looks until you find the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Final Thoughts: Embrace Your Natural Look

At the end of the day, the most important thing is to embrace your natural look - whether that means growing out a full beard, keeping a clean-shaven face, or somewhere in between. Remember that facial hair can be a powerful tool for enhancing your appearance and boosting your confidence, but it's not a magical solution to all your problems. By taking care of your skin, grooming your hair regularly, and embracing your unique features, you can create a look that is truly your own.
