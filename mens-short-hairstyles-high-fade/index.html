Mens Short Hairstyles High Fade

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to our guide on mens short hairstyles high fade! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle. From how to style it to tips for maintenance, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Description

The high fade is a popular hairstyle for men that involves the hair on the sides and back of the head being cut short and tapered upwards to create a fade effect. This hairstyle is often paired with short hair on top, creating a stylish and modern look.

How to

If you’re looking to get a high fade haircut, here’s what you need to do:

  1. Choose the length of the fade you want – high, mid, or low.
  2. Decide on the length of hair you want on top – short, medium, or long.
  3. Visit a barber or hairstylist and show them a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hair is cut and styled.

Step by Step

If you’re looking to style your high fade haircut at home, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil for added shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your high fade:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to ensure your hair stays in place all day.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles on top.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the high fade:

What should I tell my barber when getting a high fade?

Show your barber a picture of the hairstyle you want and specify the length of the fade and the hair on top.

How often should I get a high fade?

We recommend getting a touch-up every 3-4 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I style my high fade differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! You can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks.

Hair Care

To keep your high fade looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

We hope this guide on mens short hairstyles high fade has been helpful! Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you.
