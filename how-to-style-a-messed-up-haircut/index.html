How To Style A Messed Up Haircut Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you frustrated with a recent haircut gone wrong? Don't worry, you are not alone. Many people have experienced the same issue at some point in their lives. But the good news is that there are some tips and tricks that can help you style your messed up haircut and make it look great. Keep reading to learn more.

How to Style a Messed Up Haircut: Step by Step

Step 1: Identify the issues with your haircut. Is it too short, uneven, or does it have a strange shape? Knowing the issues will help you determine the best way to style it.

Step 2: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add some volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Use a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. If your hair is too short, use a round brush to add some volume and shape to it.

Step 4: Apply a styling product, such as a pomade, wax, or gel, to your hair. This will help hold your hair in place and give it some texture. Use a small amount of product, as you don't want to overdo it.

Step 5: Style your hair using your fingers or a comb. You can create a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished look, depending on your preference.

Tips for Styling a Messed Up Haircut

Tip 1: Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You may find a new look that you love.

Tip 2: Use a hair accessory, such as a headband or hair clip, to help hold your hair in place.

Tip 3: Consider getting a trim or haircut from a different stylist to fix any major issues with your haircut.

Tip 4: Embrace your natural hair texture. Sometimes, a messed up haircut can lead to a new and improved style.

FAQ: Styling a Messed Up Haircut

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's too short?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair even if it's too short. Use a round brush to add some volume and shape, and apply a styling product to hold your hair in place.

Q: How do I style my hair if it's uneven?

A: If your hair is uneven, try to create a textured, messy look. Use a styling product to hold your hair in place and add some texture.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Great Style

Tip 1: Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tip 3: Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Tip 4: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your messed up haircut and make it look great. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products, and embrace your natural hair texture. With a little bit of practice, you can create a new and improved look that you love.
