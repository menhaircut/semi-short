How to Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs Wikihow

Wikihow is a popular website that offers step-by-step guides on various topics, including cutting your own hair. Here's how to cut your own curtain bangs using Wikihow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your own curtain bangs, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips or bobby pins to section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to wash and dry your hair before cutting your own curtain bangs. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and avoid any mistakes. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair into three parts: the top section, the middle section, and the bottom section. Secure each section with hair clips or bobby pins.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Section

Start with the middle section and comb it straight down. Use the hair cutting scissors to cut the middle section to the desired length. It's important to cut your hair slowly and carefully, making small snips instead of big cuts.

Step 5: Cut the Bottom Section

Repeat the same process with the bottom section, combing it straight down and cutting it to the same length as the middle section.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

Now it's time to cut the top section, which will become your curtain bangs. Comb the top section forward and cut it to the desired length. It's important to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle and angling down towards the sides.

Step 7: Check Your Bangs

After you've finished cutting your curtain bangs, check them in the mirror to make sure they're even. If you notice any unevenness, fix it by snipping small sections of hair until they're even.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you prefer a more detailed step-by-step guide, here's how to cut your own curtain bangs using Wikihow:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair into three parts: the top section, the middle section, and the bottom section
  3. Clip the top and middle sections out of the way
  4. Comb the bottom section straight down and cut it to the desired length using hair cutting scissors
  5. Release the middle section and comb it straight down, cutting it to the same length as the bottom section
  6. Comb the top section forward and cut it to the desired length, making sure to cut at an angle from the middle towards the sides
  7. Check your bangs in the mirror to ensure they're even
  8. Trim any uneven sections until your bangs are even
  9. Style your new curtain bangs as desired

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Start with small snips instead of big cuts
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends
  • Cut your bangs at an angle for a natural look
  • Check your bangs in the mirror frequently to ensure they're even

FAQs

Can I cut my own curtain bangs with regular scissors?

No, it's not recommended to cut your own curtain bangs with regular scissors. Hair cutting scissors are specifically designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly, while regular scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.

What length should I cut my curtain bangs?

The length of your curtain bangs depends on your face shape and personal preference. Generally, curtain bangs should hit just above your eyebrows or in the middle of your forehead.

Should I cut my curtain bangs when my hair is wet or dry?

You should always cut your curtain bangs when your hair is dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer, which can lead to uneven cuts.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs

After cutting your own curtain bangs, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your bangs

With these tips and the step-by-step guide on how to cut your own curtain bangs using Wikihow, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and trendy look without having to leave your house.
