How To Buzz Cut Fade Your Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that's easy to maintain, a buzz cut fade may be just what you need. This style is particularly popular among men, but it can also look great on women. In this post, we'll show you how to buzz cut fade your own hair in the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Buzz Cut Fade Your Own Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Buzz Cut Fade
  • FAQs

How to Buzz Cut Fade Your Own Hair

A buzz cut fade is a haircut that starts with a short buzzed top and gradually fades into a longer length on the sides and back. You can achieve this look using clippers with different guard sizes. Here's a quick overview of the steps:

  1. Choose the right clippers
  2. Decide on the length of the top and sides
  3. Start with the top
  4. Blend the sides and back
  5. Clean up the neckline and sideburns

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

You'll need a good set of clippers with different guard sizes to achieve a buzz cut fade. We recommend the Wahl Elite Pro High-Performance Haircut Kit, which comes with 10 guide combs and a powerful motor to cut through thick hair. Make sure your clippers are fully charged before you start.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of the Top and Sides

Before you start cutting, decide on the length of the top and sides. You can use different guard sizes to achieve a gradual fade. For example, you can start with a #3 guard on the top and blend it into a #2 guard on the sides. You can also choose to leave a little more length on top if you prefer a textured look.

Step 3: Start with the Top

Attach your chosen guard size to your clippers and start at the top of your head. Work in small sections, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Use an upward motion to lift the hair and clip it evenly. Repeat until you've clipped the entire top section.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Next, you'll need to blend the sides and back into the top section. Attach a shorter guard size (e.g., #2 or #1) to your clippers and start at the bottom of your head. Use an upward motion to clip the hair, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. Repeat on both sides and the back until you achieve the desired fade.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline and Sideburns

Finally, use a trimmer or razor to clean up the neckline and sideburns. You can choose to leave them natural or shape them to your liking. Make sure to check your work in a mirror to catch any stray hairs.

Tips for a Perfect Buzz Cut Fade

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to achieve a more gradual fade.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Don't rush – take your time to avoid mistakes.
  • Use hair clippers with a cord for consistent power.
  • Clean your clippers after each use to maintain their performance.

FAQs

1. Can I achieve a buzz cut fade with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve a buzz cut fade with curly hair. However, we recommend going to a professional stylist who has experience working with curly hair to ensure a clean and even cut.

2. How often do I need to get a buzz cut fade?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the look. We recommend getting a buzz cut fade every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

3. Can I use a razor instead of clippers?

You can use a razor to achieve a buzz cut fade, but it's more difficult to control the length and blend. We recommend using clippers for the best results.

Hair Care for a Buzz Cut Fade

A buzz cut fade is a low-maintenance hairstyle, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a moisturizing hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your scalp and hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can achieve a perfect buzz cut fade at home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and practice good hair care to keep your look fresh and stylish.
