Medium Length Hair With Side Bangs And Layers A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, why not try medium length hair with side bangs and layers? This look is versatile, trendy, and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about achieving the perfect medium length hair with side bangs and layers.

Description

Medium length hair with side bangs and layers is a hairstyle that features hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. The hair is typically cut into layers, with shorter layers framing the face and longer layers added throughout the length of the hair. Side bangs are also added to the hairstyle, which frame the face and add dimension to the overall look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a chic and trendy look that is easy to maintain.

How to Achieve the Look

Before you can achieve the perfect medium length hair with side bangs and layers, you'll need to visit a hair salon. A professional stylist will be able to cut and style your hair to achieve the desired look. Here are the steps to achieve the perfect medium length hair with side bangs and layers:

Step 1: Consultation

Before the stylist begins cutting your hair, they will consult with you to understand your desired look. They will also examine your hair type and face shape to determine the most flattering cut for you.

Step 2: Cutting the Hair

Once the consultation is complete, the stylist will begin cutting your hair. They will start by adding layers throughout the length of your hair, which will add volume and texture. Next, they will cut the side bangs to frame your face. The length and thickness of the bangs will depend on your personal preference and face shape.

Step 3: Styling

After the hair is cut, the stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer and styling products. They may also use a curling iron or straightener to add additional texture to the hair.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair with Side Bangs and Layers

Once you've achieved the perfect medium length hair with side bangs and layers, it's important to maintain the look. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the hairstyle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

Absolutely! Medium length hair with side bangs and layers looks great on all hair types, including curly hair. Your stylist can cut the layers and bangs to complement your natural curls.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

To maintain the shape of the hairstyle and prevent split ends, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Can I still wear my hair up with this hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this hairstyle. A messy bun or braided updo looks great with medium length hair with side bangs and layers.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as this is less damaging than a brush.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and maintaining your new medium length hair with side bangs and layers, you'll be able to enjoy a chic and trendy hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion.
