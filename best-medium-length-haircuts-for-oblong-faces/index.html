Best Medium Length Haircuts For Oblong Faces

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, then you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want to find a haircut that will flatter your face shape and enhance your features. Medium length haircuts are a great choice for oblong faces because they help to balance out the length of your face. In this article, we will be discussing the best medium length haircuts for oblong faces.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. The length of your face is longer than the width, and your chin is usually slightly pointed. The goal of a good haircut for an oblong face is to create width and balance out the length of your face.

How to

When choosing a medium length haircut for an oblong face, you want to focus on adding volume and width to the sides of your face. This can be achieved through layers, bangs, and curls. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for layers that start at your cheekbones or chin to add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider adding bangs to break up the length of your face and create the illusion of width.
  • Curls and waves can also help to add width and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can elongate your face even further.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best medium length haircuts for oblong faces:

The Lob

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Using a curling iron, curl the ends of your hair inwards to create a soft, bouncy effect.
  3. Apply some texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

The Layered Bob

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Ask your stylist to add layers starting at your chin to add volume and width to your face.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

The Wavy Shag

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Using a curling iron, create loose waves throughout your hair.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers to create texture and volume.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium length haircut for an oblong face:

  • Consider side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Experiment with different partings to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts for oblong faces:

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that adds width and volume to the sides of your face. Medium length haircuts with layers, bangs, and curls are a great choice.

Should I avoid long hair if I have an oblong face?

Long hair can elongate your face even further, so it's best to avoid hairstyles that are too long if you have an oblong face. Medium length haircuts are a great choice.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and ideas for medium length haircuts, you can find the perfect style to flatter your oblong face shape. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the best cut for you and your hair type.
