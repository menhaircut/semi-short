Description

If you're looking for a bold and edgy new hairstyle, look no further than the pixie cut. This short and sassy look is perfect for ladies who want to showcase their confidence and individuality. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, there's a pixie cut out there that will suit your style.


In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about pixie cut ladies short hair styles in 2023. From how to choose the right cut for your face shape to tips on maintaining your new look, we've got you covered.

How to

Choose the Right Pixie Cut for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing a pixie cut, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you'll want to consider your face shape. Different pixie cuts will suit different face shapes, so it's important to find one that flatters your features.


If you have a round face, for example, you'll want to choose a pixie cut with more height on top to elongate your face. If you have a square face, on the other hand, you'll want to choose a pixie cut with softer, more rounded edges to balance out your features.

Find Inspiration for Your New Look

If you're not sure where to start when it comes to choosing a pixie cut, don't worry! There are plenty of places to find inspiration. Check out celebrity hairstyles, browse Pinterest and Instagram, or talk to your hairstylist for ideas.

Step by Step

Getting the Perfect Pixie Cut

The first step to getting a pixie cut is finding a hairstylist who specializes in short hair styles. Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's time to book your appointment.


Before your appointment, it's a good idea to gather some inspiration photos to show your stylist. This will give them a better idea of what you're looking for and help ensure you get the perfect cut.

Styling Your Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is easy and low-maintenance. Simply apply a small amount of styling product, like a wax or pomade, to your hair and use your fingers to shape it into your desired style. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to add some texture and volume.

Tips

Keep Your Pixie Cut Looking Fresh

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to get regular trims. Short hair styles like the pixie cut require frequent trims to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.


You can also experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks. A matte wax will give your hair a more textured, tousled look, while a shiny pomade will give it a sleek, polished finish.

FAQ

Will a Pixie Cut Suit Me?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as everyone's face shape and hair type is different. However, there are pixie cuts out there that will suit just about anyone. Talk to your hairstylist to find out what cut will work best for you.


Can I Still Style My Hair with a Pixie Cut?

Absolutely! While a pixie cut is a short hair style, there are still plenty of ways to style it. Experiment with different products, tools, and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair Care

Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and avoid over-washing or using too much heat on your hair.


You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
