How To Cut Women s Hair With Short Hair Clippers

Written by Bardi Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a woman with short hair or a stylist looking to learn a new technique, cutting hair with short hair clippers can be a game-changer. It's a quick and easy way to give your hair a fresh, clean look without having to spend hours in the salon. In this article, we'll be discussing how to cut women's hair with short hair clippers, step by step.

How to Cut Women's Hair with Short Hair Clippers

Step 1: Prepare the Hair and Clippers

Before you begin, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and ensure that it's free of any knots. Then, prepare your clippers by attaching the guard size that you want to use.

Step 2: Start with the Back and Sides

Begin by starting at the back of the head and work your way up to the crown. Hold the clippers vertically and use them to create a straight line. Then, continue to the sides of the head, using the same technique to create a straight line.

Step 3: Texturize the Hair

To add texture to the hair, switch to a smaller guard size and use the clippers to create a choppy, layered effect. Start at the top of the head and work your way down to the sides and back.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair, switch to a larger guard size and use the clippers to create a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a natural-looking blend.

Step 5: Finish with Detailing

Finally, use a pair of scissors to trim any stray hairs or to create a more precise look. Use your fingers to check for any uneven spots and make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting Women's Hair with Short Hair Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting women's hair with short hair clippers:

  • Always use a guard size that is appropriate for the length of hair you're cutting.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a more precise cut.
  • Take your time and make small adjustments as you go to avoid making any drastic mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from other stylists or professionals.

FAQs about Cutting Women's Hair with Short Hair Clippers

Can you use short hair clippers on long hair?

No, short hair clippers are designed for cutting short hair only. Using them on long hair can result in a choppy, uneven cut.

Can you cut your own hair with short hair clippers?

It's possible to cut your own hair with short hair clippers, but it can be difficult to achieve a precise cut. It's best to have a professional stylist do it for you or have someone help you.

How often should you cut your hair with short hair clippers?

It depends on the length and style of your hair, but generally, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind if you have short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of any buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Cutting women's hair with short hair clippers can be a fun and easy way to give your hair a fresh new look. With the right technique and some practice, you can achieve a salon-quality cut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, use the right guard size, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
