Best Thickening Products For Fine Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how difficult it can be to create volume and thickness. Luckily, there are many products on the market that can help you achieve the full, luscious locks you've always wanted. In this article, we'll explore the best thickening products for fine hair, and provide tips and tricks for achieving maximum volume.

Why do people with fine hair need thickening products?

People with fine hair often struggle to create volume and body in their hair. This is because fine hair strands are smaller in diameter and have less density than other hair types. Thickening products can help to add volume and thickness to fine hair, making it easier to style and creating a fuller, more voluminous look.

How to choose the best thickening products for fine hair

When choosing a thickening product for fine hair, look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. Avoid heavy oils and serums, as these can make your hair look greasy and flat. Instead, opt for products that contain volumizing ingredients like keratin, biotin, and collagen.

Top 5 Best Thickening Products for Fine Hair

1. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray

The Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray is a fan favorite for a reason. This lightweight spray creates volume and thickness without weighing your hair down, and it also protects against heat damage. Simply spray onto damp hair before blow drying for best results.

2. Living Proof Full Shampoo

The Living Proof Full Shampoo is designed specifically for fine, flat hair. This shampoo contains a patented molecule that creates volume and thickness, while also protecting against humidity and frizz.

3. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

The Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray is a cult favorite among hairstylists. This spray creates volume and thickness while also providing heat protection and UV protection. Apply to damp hair before blow drying for best results.

4. Aveda Thickening Tonic

The Aveda Thickening Tonic is a natural option for those with fine hair. This tonic contains organic aloe vera and wheat protein to create volume and thickness, while also nourishing and strengthening your hair.

5. Garnier Fructis Full & Plush Mega Full Thickening Lotion

The Garnier Fructis Full & Plush Mega Full Thickening Lotion is an affordable option that delivers great results. This lotion creates volume and thickness while also providing hold and frizz control. Apply to damp hair before blow drying for best results.

Tips for achieving maximum volume

1. Blow dry your hair upside down to create extra lift at the roots.

2. Use a round brush to create a smooth, voluminous finish.

3. Apply thickening products to damp hair for best results.

4. Use a volumizing mousse or foam for extra hold.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can thickening products damage my hair?

A: No, as long as you use them properly, thickening products should not damage your hair. Avoid applying too much product, and make sure to wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup.

Q: Can thickening products make my hair look greasy?

A: Yes, if you apply too much product or use the wrong product for your hair type. Look for lightweight formulas and avoid heavy oils and serums.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

In addition to using thickening products, there are several things you can do to care for your fine hair. Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. Finally, make sure to get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right products and care, you can achieve the full, voluminous hair you've always wanted. Try out some of the best thickening products for fine hair listed above, and see which ones work best for you!
