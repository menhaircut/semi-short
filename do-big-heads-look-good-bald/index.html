Do Big Heads Look Good Bald A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you contemplating shaving your head but worried that your big head might not look good bald? Rest assured, you are not alone. Many people with larger heads have wondered the same thing. In this article, we will explore whether big heads look good bald, how to shave your head, and tips to care for your bald scalp.

Does Having a Big Head Affect Baldness?

Having a big head does not necessarily affect how good you will look bald. Baldness is largely determined by genetics and hormonal factors, not head size. The shape of your head, however, may influence how baldness appears on you. Those with rounder heads may have a smoother appearance when bald, while those with more angular heads may have a more defined look.

How to Shave Your Head

Shaving your head is a simple process that can be done at home. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

Before shaving your head, trim your hair as short as possible using electric clippers. This will make the shaving process easier and prevent clogging of the razor.

Step 2: Apply Shaving Cream

Apply shaving cream or gel to your head. Use a product designed specifically for shaving, as they provide a smoother shave and reduce irritation. Leave the cream on for a few minutes to soften the hair.

Step 3: Shave Your Head

Using a razor, shave your head in the direction of hair growth. Start at the top of your head and work your way down. Rinse the razor frequently to prevent clogging. After shaving, rinse your head with cool water to remove any remaining shaving cream.

Step 4: Moisturize Your Scalp

After shaving, apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to your scalp to prevent dryness and irritation. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your scalp.

Tips for Caring for Your Bald Scalp

Once you have shaved your head, it is important to take care of your bald scalp to prevent irritation and dryness. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Moisturize Daily

Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to your scalp daily to prevent dryness and irritation. This will also help your scalp maintain its natural oils.

Tip 2: Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Without hair to protect your scalp from the sun, it is important to wear a hat or apply sunscreen to your scalp when spending time outdoors.

Tip 3: Exfoliate Your Scalp

Exfoliating your scalp once a week can help remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs. Use a gentle exfoliating scrub or a soft-bristled brush.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will My Head Look Bigger When Bald?

Having a bald head may make your head appear slightly larger, but this is largely dependent on your facial features and head shape.

Do Women Find Bald Men Attractive?

Many women find bald men attractive. In fact, baldness can be seen as a sign of confidence and masculinity.

Will Shaving My Head Cause Hair to Grow Back Thicker?

No, shaving your head does not cause hair to grow back thicker. This is a common myth.

Conclusion

Whether you have a big head or not, shaving your head can be a liberating and refreshing experience. With the right care, your bald scalp can look and feel great. Follow the tips and steps outlined in this guide to achieve a smooth and stylish bald look.
