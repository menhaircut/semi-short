Quick And Easy Updo Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have shoulder-length hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! There are plenty of quick and easy updo hairstyles that you can try to change up your look. Here are some of our favorites.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you're looking for a quick and easy style for everyday wear or something more elegant for a special occasion, there are plenty of updos that you can try. These styles are perfect for when you're short on time but still want to look polished and put together.

How to

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite quick and easy updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair.

Low Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

Half-Up Bun

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair down or curl it for a more polished look.

Messy Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Tease the crown of your head for some volume.
  3. Mess up the ponytail by pulling out some pieces and loosening it up.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure your updo looks great:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair for some extra volume.
  • Use hairspray to secure your style in place.

FAQ

Can I do these styles on curly hair?

Absolutely! These styles work well on both straight and curly hair.

Do I need any special tools?

No, you just need some bobby pins, hairspray, and maybe a hair tie for the half-up bun.

How long do these styles take?

They should only take a few minutes each, making them perfect for when you're short on time.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly. Use a heat protectant when using hot tools and avoid using too much product to prevent buildup. Also, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these quick and easy updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you can change up your look in no time. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, these styles are sure to impress. Give them a try and see which one works best for you!
