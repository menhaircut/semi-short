How To Roller Set Medium Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools on your hair? Roller setting is a heatless styling technique that can give you beautiful, bouncy curls. In this guide, we’ll show you how to roller set medium length hair in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare Your Hair
  • How to Choose the Right Rollers
  • How to Roll Your Hair
  • How to Dry Your Hair
  • Tips for a Perfect Roller Set
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Roller Setting

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before starting the roller setting process, it’s essential to prepare your hair correctly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp, but not soaking wet.

Next, apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the curls. A mousse or setting lotion works great for this purpose. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

How to Choose the Right Rollers

Choosing the right rollers can make or break the success of your roller set. Medium-sized rollers work best for medium length hair. You can choose from different types of rollers, such as foam, magnetic, or velcro rollers.

If you want tighter curls, use smaller rollers. If you prefer looser curls, use larger rollers.

How to Roll Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Take one section of hair and comb it through. Place a roller at the end of the hair and roll it up towards the roots.

Secure the roller in place with a clip or pin. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair until it’s entirely rolled up.

How to Dry Your Hair

Allow your hair to air dry naturally, or use a hairdryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process. Avoid using high heat, as this can damage your hair and ruin the roller set.

Once your hair is dry, carefully remove the rollers. Use your fingers to style your curls and create your desired look.

Tips for a Perfect Roller Set

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect roller set:

  • Use a styling product to help hold the curls in place.
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Choose the right size rollers for your hair length and desired curl pattern.
  • Allow your hair to dry completely before removing the rollers.
  • Use your fingers to style your curls and create volume.

FAQ

How long should I leave the rollers in my hair?

It’s best to leave the rollers in your hair until it’s completely dry. This can take anywhere from a few hours to overnight, depending on your hair type and the rollers you use.

Can I roller set my hair if it’s already dry?

It’s best to roller set your hair when it’s damp. If your hair is already dry, dampen it with water or a hair mist before starting the roller set process.

Hair Care After Roller Setting

After roller setting your hair, it’s essential to take care of it properly to maintain the curls. Use a light hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold to your curls.

Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and ruin your roller set. Instead, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair, or let it air dry naturally.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful roller set on your medium length hair. Give it a try and see how easy it is to get bouncy, voluminous curls without using heat tools.
