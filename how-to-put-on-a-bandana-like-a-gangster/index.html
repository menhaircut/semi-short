How To Put On A Bandana Like A Gangster

Written by Bardi Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you want to rock the gangster style, one accessory you need to master is the bandana. Worn around the head, neck, or wrist, a bandana adds a touch of toughness to any outfit. In this article, we'll show you how to put on a bandana like a gangster, step by step.

How To

Putting on a bandana like a gangster is easy with these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Bandana

The first step is to choose your bandana. You can find bandanas in a variety of colors and patterns. Some popular colors for gangsters include red, blue, black, and white. You can also choose a pattern that fits your style, such as paisley or camo.

Step 2: Fold Your Bandana

Once you have your bandana, fold it in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.

Step 3: Place the Bandana on Your Head

Place the folded bandana on your head with the longest side of the triangle at the back of your head and the tip of the triangle pointing forward. The bandana should sit on your forehead just above your eyebrows.

Step 4: Tie the Bandana

Take the two ends of the bandana and tie them at the back of your head. Make sure it's secure, but not too tight. You don't want to give yourself a headache.

Step 5: Adjust the Bandana

Adjust the bandana so that it sits comfortably on your head. You can pull it down over your ears or leave it up higher on your head. You can also adjust the knot in the back to make it tighter or looser.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you perfect your gangster style:

Tip 1: Coordinate Your Bandana

Coordinate your bandana with the rest of your outfit. If you're wearing a red shirt, for example, a red bandana will tie your look together.

Tip 2: Experiment with Placement

Try wearing your bandana around your neck or wrist instead of your head for a different look.

Tip 3: Use a Hair Tie

If you have long hair, use a hair tie to pull it back before putting on the bandana. This will make it easier to tie the bandana and keep it in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting on a bandana like a gangster:

Q: Can I wear a bandana in any color?

A: While gangsters often wear red or blue bandanas, you can wear a bandana in any color that fits your style.

Q: Do I have to tie the bandana in the back?

A: No, you can tie the bandana in the front or on the side for a different look.

Q: Can I wear a bandana if I'm not a gangster?

A: Yes, anyone can wear a bandana as an accessory. It's a versatile accessory that can add some edge to any outfit.

Hair Care

Wearing a bandana can be tough on your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Silk Scarf

Wrap your hair in a silk scarf before putting on the bandana. This will help protect your hair from damage and keep it looking healthy.

Tip 2: Don't Tie it Too Tight

Make sure the bandana isn't tied too tight. If it's too tight, it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. This will help prevent breakage and damage from wearing a bandana.

Now that you know how to put on a bandana like a gangster, you can rock the look with confidence. Whether you're going for a classic gangster style or putting your own spin on it, a bandana is a versatile accessory that can add some edge to any outfit.
