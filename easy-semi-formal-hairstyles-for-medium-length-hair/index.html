How to Style Semi Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

When it comes to styling your hair for a semi-formal event, you want to keep it simple yet elegant. You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, ranging from updos to half-up styles. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Accessorize your hair with hairpins, headbands, or clips

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
