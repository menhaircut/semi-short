Easy Semi Formal Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair In 2023

Written by Petter Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for some easy semi formal hairstyles for your medium length hair? You’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we’ll go over some of the best hairstyles that are perfect for any semi-formal event. From elegant updos to chic half-up styles, we’ve got you covered. Plus, we’ll also share some tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Semi Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Easy Semi Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Semi Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

When it comes to styling your hair for a semi-formal event, you want to keep it simple yet elegant. You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, ranging from updos to half-up styles. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your outfit and face shape
  • Accessorize your hair with hairpins, headbands, or clips
  • Finish your style with a hairspray to keep everything in place

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Easy Semi Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

1. Sleek Low Ponytail

This hairstyle is perfect for a semi-formal event. It’s simple yet elegant, and it works well with any outfit. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and pulling it back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band, securing it with a bobby pin.
  4. Finish the hairstyle with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

2. Half-Up Twist

This hairstyle is perfect if you want to keep your hair down but still want to look put together. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Start by taking a section of hair from the crown of your head and twisting it.
  2. Secure the twist with a bobby pin at the back of your head.
  3. Take another section of hair from the other side of your head and twist it.
  4. Secure the twist with a bobby pin, crossing it over the first twist.
  5. Finish the hairstyle with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

3. Braided Updo

If you want a more intricate hairstyle, try this braided updo. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Start by creating a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Divide the ponytail into three sections and create a braid.
  3. Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Finish the hairstyle with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQs

What are some easy semi formal hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy semi formal hairstyles for medium length hair include a sleek low ponytail, half-up twist, and braided updo.

How do I keep my hair healthy?

To keep your hair healthy, wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too often, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Try to air dry your hair as much as possible to avoid using hot tools.

With these easy semi formal hairstyles for medium length hair, you’ll be ready for any event. Whether you’re attending a wedding, prom, or another special occasion, these hairstyles are perfect for any semi-formal event. Remember to take care of your hair by following our tips, and your hair will look healthy and shiny in no time.
