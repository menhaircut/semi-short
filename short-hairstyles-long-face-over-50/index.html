Short Hairstyles For Long Face Over 50

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you in your 50s and have a long face? Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and make you look younger and more stylish? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for long faces over 50, along with tips on how to style and take care of your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its volume, and our face shape may change, becoming longer and more angular. This is why it's important to choose the right hairstyle that will enhance your features and complement your face shape. Short hairstyles are perfect for women over 50, as they are low-maintenance and can make you look younger and more vibrant.

How to

The first step in choosing the right short hairstyle for your long face is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the length of your face from the forehead to the chin, and compare it to the width of your face at the cheekbones. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle. The best short hairstyles for long faces over 50 are those that add volume and width to the sides of your face, while keeping the top and crown area flat. Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles are all great options.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are short, cropped hairstyles that are perfect for women over 50 with long faces. They are low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and textured. Pixie cuts add volume and width to the sides of your face, making your face appear shorter and more balanced.

Bobs

Bobs are another great short hairstyle for long faces over 50. They come in a variety of lengths and styles, from chin-length bobs to asymmetrical bobs. Bobs add volume to the sides of your face, while keeping the top and crown area flat. They are also versatile and can be styled with bangs or without.

Layered Hairstyles

Layered hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 with long faces, as they add volume and texture to the sides of your face, while keeping the top and crown area flat. Layered hairstyles can be short or long, and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

Step by Step

Now that you've chosen the right short hairstyle for your long face, it's time to style it. Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your short hair.

Pixie Cuts

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, or use a curling iron to add texture.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Bobs

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling your short hairstyle.

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift to the roots.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension to your hair.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for long faces over 50?

The best short hairstyles for long faces over 50 are pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles. These styles add volume and width to the sides of your face, while keeping the top and crown area flat.

How do I style my short hair?

To style your short hair, start by washing and conditioning your hair with volumizing products. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension to your hair. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for short hair.

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and cause dryness.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style.

With the right short hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best in your 50s and beyond. So go ahead and try out a pixie cut, bob, or layered hairstyle, and embrace your long face shape with confidence!
