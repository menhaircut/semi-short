How To Grow Your Hair Out Without Looking Stupid

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

In this day and age, long hair is in. However, growing your hair out can be a bit of a challenge, especially if you're not used to having long hair. It can be a bit of an awkward phase, where you're not quite sure what to do with your hair, and you may feel like you look a bit silly. But fear not! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to grow your hair out without looking stupid.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Grow Your Hair Out
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Help You Along the Way
  4. FAQ

How to Grow Your Hair Out

First and foremost, you need to be patient. Growing your hair out takes time, and there's no way around it. You can't force your hair to grow faster, so you'll just have to wait it out. That being said, there are some things you can do to help your hair grow healthier and stronger.

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Make sure you're eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals, especially those that are known to promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin B, and vitamin C.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure you use a heat protectant spray and don't use them too often.

3. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in the health of your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and that are free from harsh chemicals.

4. Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow longer. Trimming the ends of your hair can help prevent split ends, which can cause your hair to break off and prevent it from growing longer.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know some of the basics of growing your hair out, let's go over a step by step guide to help you along the way.

1. Decide on Your Goal Length

Before you start growing your hair out, you should decide on your goal length. This will help you stay motivated and focused throughout the process.

2. Be Patient

As mentioned before, growing your hair out takes time. Be patient and don't get discouraged if your hair doesn't seem to be growing as quickly as you'd like.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

While you're growing your hair out, you may go through an awkward phase where your hair doesn't quite know what to do. Experiment with different styles, such as braids or updos, to help you get through this phase.

4. Get Regular Trims

As mentioned before, getting regular trims can help your hair grow longer. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or whenever you notice split ends.

Tips to Help You Along the Way

Here are some additional tips to help you grow your hair out without looking stupid:

1. Wear Your Hair Up

Wearing your hair up in a ponytail or bun can help you get through the awkward phase where your hair is growing out.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Using hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, can help you style your hair in a way that looks intentional and put together.

3. Experiment with Hats

Wearing hats can be a great way to cover up any awkward hair growth stages and still look fashionable.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow hair out?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. However, the rate of hair growth can vary depending on factors such as age, genetics, and diet.

Should I cut my hair while I'm growing it out?

Yes, getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can inhibit hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you grow your hair out:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Don't Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can grow your hair out without looking stupid. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair along the way. Good luck!
