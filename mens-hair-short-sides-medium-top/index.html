Mens Hair Short Sides Medium Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Aug 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and low maintenance, then the mens hair short sides medium top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years, and it's easy to see why. It's versatile, easy to style, and looks great on just about anyone. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, give you some tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions about mens hair short sides medium top. Let's get started!

Description

The mens hair short sides medium top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and a medium-length top. This hairstyle is perfect for anyone who wants a low maintenance look that still looks stylish. The length of the top can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually around 2-3 inches long. The sides are usually buzzed or faded, but you can also choose to have a more gradual taper if that's what you prefer. This hairstyle is great for any face shape and can be customized to suit your individual style.

How To

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in achieving the mens hair short sides medium top is to choose the length you want for your hair. The top should be around 2-3 inches long, but you can choose to go shorter or longer depending on your preference. The sides should be short, either buzzed or faded, but you can also choose to have a more gradual taper if that's what you prefer.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Once you've decided on the length you want, it's time to cut your hair. If you're doing it yourself, start by cutting the sides first. Use a clipper with a guard to get the desired length, and work your way up to the top. Once you've finished the sides, move on to the top. Use scissors to cut the top to the desired length, and make sure to blend it into the sides so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. The mens hair short sides medium top is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. You can choose to slick it back for a more formal look, or you can leave it messy for a more casual look. Use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair, and experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your mens hair short sides medium top:

  • Use a good quality pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, depending on the look you're going for.
  • If you're going for a more formal look, make sure to slick back the sides as well as the top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hair short sides medium top:

Is the mens hair short sides medium top easy to maintain?

Yes, the mens hair short sides medium top is a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. A simple comb or your fingers is all you need to style it, and you won't need to visit the salon as often as you would with a more complicated hairstyle.

What face shape is best suited for mens hair short sides medium top?

The mens hair short sides medium top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape. It's a great choice for men with oval, square, or heart-shaped faces.

What products should I use to style my mens hair short sides medium top?

You should use a good quality pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair, and avoid products that are too heavy or greasy.

Hair Care

To keep your mens hair short sides medium top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your mens hair short sides medium top looks great and stays healthy.

Conclusion

The mens hair short sides medium top is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for anyone who wants a low maintenance look. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve this look and experiment with different styles until you find one that you like. Whether you're going for a formal or casual look, the mens hair short sides medium top is a great choice that's sure to turn heads.
