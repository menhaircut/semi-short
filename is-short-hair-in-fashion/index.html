Is Short Hair In Fashion

Written by Frank Oct 22, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

In recent years, short hair has become increasingly popular among women. From celebrities to the everyday person, more and more ladies are opting for a shorter, edgier look. But is short hair really in fashion or is it just a passing trend? In this article, we'll explore the current state of short hair in the fashion world and provide some tips for rocking a shorter 'do.

  2. How to Cut Short Hair

How to Cut Short Hair

If you're considering a shorter haircut, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. A good stylist will take into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when creating a short haircut that works for you.

Some popular short haircuts include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob). The pixie cut is a very short, boyish haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways. The bob is a classic short haircut that falls just above the shoulders, while the lob is a longer version of the bob that falls just below the shoulders.

When getting a short haircut, it's important to communicate with your stylist about the length you want and any specific details you would like, such as bangs or layers. It's also a good idea to bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to give your stylist a better idea of what you're looking for.

Styling Short Hair

One of the benefits of having short hair is that it's typically easier to style than longer hair. Depending on your haircut, you may be able to simply wash and go or add a bit of product for a more polished look.

If you do want to style your short hair, there are a variety of products you can use to achieve different looks. For a messy, textured look, try a sea salt spray or texturizing paste. For a sleek, polished style, use a smoothing cream or pomade.

When styling your short hair, it's important to use products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to use a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down.

Tips for Rocking Short Hair

If you're new to the world of short hair, it can be a bit intimidating at first. Here are some tips for rocking a shorter 'do:

  • Experiment with different styles and products until you find what works for you.
  • Accessorize with earrings, headbands, and scarves to add interest to your look.
  • Don't be afraid to go bold with your makeup or clothing choices to balance out your shorter hair.
  • Be confident! Short hair can be a statement look that sets you apart from the crowd.

FAQ About Short Hair

Here are some common questions people have about short hair:

Will short hair look good on me?

Short hair can look good on anyone, but it's important to find a haircut that works for your face shape and hair texture. Consult with a stylist to find a short haircut that flatters your features.

Do I need to style my short hair every day?

Not necessarily. Some short haircuts can be washed and left to air dry for a more natural, tousled look. Other haircuts may require a bit of styling to look their best. It's up to you to decide how much time and effort you want to put into styling your short hair.

Can I still wear extensions with short hair?

Yes! There are a variety of clip-in and tape-in extensions that can be used to add length and volume to short hair. Talk to your stylist about the best options for your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires different care than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Final Thoughts: Is Short Hair Here to Stay?

Short hair has been in and out of fashion for decades, but it seems to be having a moment right now. Whether you're a longtime fan of short hair or just considering a chop, there's no denying that it can be a stylish and versatile look. With the right cut and styling, short hair can be a statement look that sets you apart from the crowd.
