How To Do A Easy Messy Bun For Long Hair A Relaxed Guide

Written by Petter Nov 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it every day. Sometimes, you just want to throw it up into a messy bun and be done with it. But how do you create that effortless look without it looking like a complete mess?

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to:

Creating a messy bun is actually easier than you might think. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1:

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to work with.

Step 2:

Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, but don't pull it too tight. Leave some slack so that the bun will be loose and messy.

Step 3:

Twist the ponytail around itself until it starts to coil into a bun shape.

Step 4:

Wrap a hair tie around the bun to secure it in place. You can also use bobby pins to hold any loose pieces in place.

Step by Step:

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1:

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make it easier to work with.

Step 2:

Pull your hair back into a high ponytail. You can use your hands or a brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 3:

Use your fingers to twist the ponytail around itself. Keep twisting until it starts to coil into a bun shape.

Step 4:

Wrap a hair tie around the bun to secure it in place. Make sure to leave some slack so that the bun will be loose and messy.

Step 5:

Use bobby pins to hold any loose pieces in place. Pull out a few strands around your face to create a more relaxed look.

Tips:

Here are some tips to make your messy bun look even better:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't worry about making your bun too perfect. The whole point is to look messy and effortless.
  • If you have shorter layers around your face, use bobby pins to secure them back into the bun.

FAQ:

Q: Can I do a messy bun with curly hair?

A: Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can make a messy bun look even better. Just be sure to use a hair tie that won't damage your curls and avoid brushing your hair too much.

Q: How do I make my messy bun last all day?

A: Use a strong hair tie and bobby pins to secure your bun in place. You can also use hairspray to keep it from falling apart.

Hair Care:

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup and keep it clean.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often. They can damage your hair and cause split ends.

With these tips, you can create an easy messy bun for long hair that looks effortlessly chic. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
