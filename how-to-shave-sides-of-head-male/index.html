How To Shave Sides Of Head Male Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read
Are you looking for a fresh and clean look? Shaving the sides of your head might just be the solution. This hairstyle has been popular for years and is still a trend for men in 2023. However, shaving the sides of your head can be challenging, especially if you've never done it before. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of how to shave sides of head male in a step-by-step guide.

Description

Shaving the sides of your head means cutting the hair close to the scalp or even bald. This style is most commonly seen in men and is also referred to as a "fade" or "undercut." The shaved sides can be blended into longer hair on top, creating a chic and modern look. This hairstyle is low maintenance and can be easily styled with hair gel or wax.

How to Shave Sides of Head Male

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are a few things you will need: - Clippers or trimmers - Mirror - Comb - Scissors - Shaving cream - Razor

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil from your hair. Dry your hair completely before starting the shaving process.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want to shave your hair. If you're new to shaving your hair, start with a longer length and work your way to a shorter length.

Step 3: Trim the Hair

Use clippers or trimmers to trim the hair on the sides of your head to the desired length. Start with the longest clipper setting and work your way to a shorter length until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Create a Fade

Create a fade by using a shorter clipper setting to blend the hair from the sides to the top. Use the longer clipper settings to create a smooth transition between the hair lengths.

Step 5: Define the Hairline

Use scissors to define the hairline around your ears and neck. This will create a clean and sharp look.

Step 6: Apply Shaving Cream

Apply shaving cream to the shaved areas of your head. This will help reduce irritation and razor burn.

Step 7: Shave the Sides

Use a razor to shave the sides of your head. Start at the top and work your way down, using short strokes. Be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this can cause razor burn.

Step 8: Rinse and Dry

After shaving, rinse your head with warm water and dry it with a towel. Be sure to pat your head gently to avoid irritation.

Tips

- Start with a longer clipper setting and work your way to a shorter length. - Take your time when shaving to avoid nicks and cuts. - Use a mirror to check the back of your head. - Apply aftershave lotion to reduce irritation.

FAQ

How often should I shave the sides of my head?

It depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. Generally, you should shave the sides of your head every two to three weeks to maintain the look.

What if I make a mistake while shaving?

If you make a mistake while shaving, don't panic. You can always trim the hair to a longer length and start over.

Can I shave my head on my own?

Yes, you can shave your head on your own. However, it may be easier to have someone help you, especially when shaving the back of your head.

Hair Care

To maintain your shaved hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips: - Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. - Apply hair oil or serum to keep your hair shiny and healthy. - Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair. - Visit a professional barber every few weeks to maintain the look. Shaving the sides of your head can be a bold and stylish look for men. With this step-by-step guide, you can achieve this look on your own. Remember to take your time and be careful when shaving to avoid nicks and cuts. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain the look.
