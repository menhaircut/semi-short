How To Shave A Dog With A Double Coat

Written by Petter Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Shaving a dog with a double coat can be a daunting task, but it is necessary during the hot summer months to keep your furry friend cool and comfortable. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to shave a dog with a double coat. We will also give you some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about double-coated dogs.

Description

Dogs with double coats have two layers of fur: a soft undercoat and a coarser outer coat. Breeds such as German Shepherds, Huskies, and Golden Retrievers have double coats. These coats help regulate the dog's body temperature in all weather conditions, keeping them warm in the winter and cool in the summer. However, during the hot summer months, double coats can make your dog uncomfortable and overheated, leading to heatstroke. That's why it's essential to know how to shave your dog's double coat safely.

How to

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start shaving your dog, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a good quality clipper, a pair of scissors, a slicker brush, and a comb. Also, make sure your dog's coat is clean and dry before you start. Brush your dog's coat thoroughly to remove any tangles or mats.

Step 2: Start Shaving

Start shaving your dog from the neck down to the tail. Use a clipper with a blade guard to avoid cutting your dog's skin. Shave with the direction of the hair growth, and use a comb to lift the hair and check for any missed spots. Don't forget to shave the belly, armpits, and groin area.

Step 3: Trim the Legs

Use scissors to trim the hair around your dog's legs, feet, and toes. Be careful not to cut the skin or the fur too short. It's better to leave the fur a little longer to protect your dog's skin from the hot pavement.

Step 4: Clean Up

After you finish shaving your dog, give them a bath to remove any loose hair and debris. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for dogs with sensitive skin. Dry your dog thoroughly with a towel or a blow dryer on a low setting.

Tips

  • Always use a clipper with a blade guard to avoid cutting your dog's skin.
  • Start shaving from the neck down to the tail.
  • Use a slicker brush to remove any tangles or mats before you start shaving.
  • Trim the hair around your dog's ears, eyes, and mouth carefully.
  • Don't shave your dog's double coat too short, as it can damage the hair follicles and affect the coat's regrowth.

FAQ

Can I shave my double-coated dog completely?

No, you should never shave a double-coated dog completely. The undercoat provides insulation and protects your dog's skin from the sun's harmful rays. Shaving the fur too short can also expose your dog's skin to hot pavement and cause sunburn.

How often should I shave my double-coated dog?

You should only shave your double-coated dog during the hot summer months to keep them cool and comfortable. You don't need to shave your dog's coat in the winter as the undercoat provides insulation and keeps them warm.

Can I use human clippers to shave my dog?

No, you should never use human clippers to shave your dog. Human clippers are not designed for the thick and coarse hair of dogs, and they can cause skin irritation and injury. Always use a clipper designed specifically for dogs.

Hair Care

After you shave your dog's double coat, you need to take care of their skin and fur. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for dogs with sensitive skin to avoid irritation and dryness. Brush your dog's coat regularly to prevent tangles and mats. Provide your dog with a comfortable and cool environment during the hot summer months to prevent overheating.

Shaving a dog with a double coat can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it safely and effectively. Remember to follow the tips and tricks we provided to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about double-coated dogs. Take care of your furry friend, and they will love you unconditionally.
