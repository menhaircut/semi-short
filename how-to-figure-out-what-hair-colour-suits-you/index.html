How To Figure Out What Hair Colour Suits You

Are you tired of your current hair colour and ready for a change? Choosing a new hair colour can be daunting, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect shade for you. In this article, we will discuss how to figure out what hair colour suits you and provide some helpful tips to make the process easier.

How to Figure Out What Hair Colour Suits You

The first step in figuring out what hair colour suits you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural colour of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair colour that complements it. If you have a cool skin tone, choose hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown. If you have a warm skin tone, choose hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, copper, or warm brown. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose hair colours with both warm and cool undertones.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to figure out what hair colour suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.
  2. Choose a hair colour with undertones that complement your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair colour and how much maintenance you are willing to do.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and how your hair colour will fit in.
  5. Consult with a professional hair stylist for advice.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Consider your eye colour. Your eye colour can also play a role in choosing the right hair colour.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colours look on you.
  • Consider your skin undertones. If you have pink undertones, choose a cooler hair colour. If you have yellow undertones, choose a warmer hair colour.
  • Don't be afraid to start with a subtle change. You can always go bolder later.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour:

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different colour?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a completely different colour, but keep in mind that it may require more maintenance and upkeep.

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preferences. Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Choosing a new hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and consulting with a professional hairstylist, you can find the perfect shade for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
