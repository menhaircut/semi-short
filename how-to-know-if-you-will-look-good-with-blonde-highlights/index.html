How To Know If You Will Look Good With Blonde Highlights

Written by Bardi Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking of getting blonde highlights but you're not sure if they'll suit you, you're not alone. Choosing the right hair color can be difficult, especially if you're not used to changing your look. But don't worry, in this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know to determine whether blonde highlights are the right choice for you.

Description

Blonde highlights are a popular hair color trend that involves adding blonde streaks to your hair. These highlights can be subtle or bold, depending on your preference. Blonde highlights can add dimension and depth to your hair and can brighten up your overall look. However, not everyone can pull off blonde highlights. You need to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style to determine if blonde highlights will suit you.

How to Determine if Blonde Highlights Will Suit You

Step 1: Consider Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining if blonde highlights will suit you is to consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow or peach, blonde highlights with warm tones, such as honey or caramel, will complement your skin. If you have cool undertones, such as pink or blue, blonde highlights with cool tones, such as platinum or ash, will be more flattering.

Step 2: Assess Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color is an important factor in determining if blonde highlights will suit you. If you have dark hair, blonde highlights will create a bold contrast that can be striking. If you have light hair, blonde highlights can add depth and dimension to your hair.

Step 3: Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also important in determining if blonde highlights will suit you. If you have a classic style, subtle blonde highlights will be more appropriate. If you have a more edgy style, bold blonde highlights will complement your look.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Highlights

Once you've decided to get blonde highlights, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain their vibrancy. Here are some tips to keep your blonde highlights looking their best:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

- Avoid hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ

What if I'm not sure about getting blonde highlights?

If you're not sure about getting blonde highlights, you can try a temporary hair color to test the waters. This will give you an idea of how blonde highlights will look on you without committing to a permanent change.

What if I have dyed hair?

If you have dyed hair, it's important to consider the condition of your hair before getting blonde highlights. Bleaching your hair can be damaging, so you should consult with a hair stylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough for the process.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain the health and vibrancy of your blonde highlights. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

- Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too frequently.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Blonde highlights can be a great way to add dimension and depth to your hair, but they're not for everyone. By considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style, you can determine if blonde highlights will suit you. With proper care and maintenance, your blonde highlights can look beautiful and vibrant for weeks to come.
