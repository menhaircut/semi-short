How To Style Long Hair Around Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can be incredibly versatile and fun to style, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and can quickly become overwhelming. One of the biggest challenges when it comes to styling long hair is figuring out how to frame your face in a flattering way. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks for styling long hair around your face.

How to Style Long Hair Around Face

When it comes to styling long hair around your face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Face shape: Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to consider your face shape when choosing a style.
  • Texture: The texture of your hair can also impact the way it falls around your face, so it's important to factor this in as well.
  • Length: The length of your hair will obviously impact the way it frames your face, so it's important to take this into account when choosing a style.

With these things in mind, let's take a look at some popular styles for framing your face with long hair.

Layers

Layers are a great way to frame your face and add some movement and dimension to your hair. If you have long hair, consider getting some long layers cut into it to help soften your features and add some shape.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great way to frame your face and add some interest to your hair. They're also a good option if you're not ready to commit to a full fringe. Consider getting some long, side-swept bangs cut into your hair to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers are exactly what they sound like - layers that are designed to frame your face. They can be a good option if you have a round face, as they can help to elongate your features. Consider getting some shorter layers cut around your face to draw attention to your cheekbones and jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some ideas for styles that will frame your face, let's take a look at how to achieve these looks:

Layers

  1. Wash and dry your hair as normal.
  2. Section your hair into four parts - two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the top of your head.
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  6. Blend the layers together by cutting into them at a slight angle.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and dry your hair as normal.
  2. Section your hair into four parts - two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Starting at the front of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length, angling downwards towards your cheekbone.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by cutting into them at a slight angle.

Face-Framing Layers

  1. Wash and dry your hair as normal.
  2. Section your hair into four parts - two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Starting at the front of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length, angling downwards towards your cheekbone.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Blend the layers into the rest of your hair by cutting into them at a slight angle.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Styling long hair can be tricky, but these tips should help make the process a little easier:

  • Invest in good haircare products - this will help keep your hair healthy and make it easier to style.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairdresser for advice - they're the experts, after all.
  • Consider using styling tools like curling irons, straighteners, and hot rollers to add some variety to your look.

FAQs

How often should I wash my long hair?

This will depend on your hair type and lifestyle, but as a general rule, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

How can I prevent my long hair from getting tangled?

Regular brushing and using a detangler can help prevent tangles. You should also try to avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can make it more prone to tangling.

What's the best way to add volume to long hair?

You can add volume to long hair by blow-drying it upside down, using a volumizing mousse or spray, or backcombing the roots.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy is essential if you want it to look its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain long, healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Try to avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach and peroxide.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you should be well on your way to styling your long hair around your face in a way that flatters your features and suits your personal style.
