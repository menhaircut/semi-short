How To Do Locs With Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 18, 2023 췅 4 min read

If you've been considering locs for your natural hair, you're in luck! Locs are a versatile, low-maintenance style that can be done with any hair type. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieving beautiful, healthy locs with your natural hair. We'll also provide tips for maintaining and caring for your locs once they're established. Let's get started!

How to Do Locs with Natural Hair

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

The first step to creating locs with natural hair is to cleanse your hair thoroughly. This will remove any product buildup or debris that may be on your scalp. Use a clarifying shampoo or apple cider vinegar rinse to thoroughly cleanse your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is clean and detangled, it's time to section it into small, equal-sized parts. Use a comb to part your hair into small sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up to the crown of your head. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Next, twist each section of hair tightly from root to tip. Use a twisting cream or gel to help hold the twists in place. Once twisted, use a small rubber band to secure the ends of each twist.

Step 4: Palm Roll Your Twists

After twisting and securing each section, it's time to palm roll your twists. This process helps to smooth and tighten your twists, creating the foundation for your locs. Use your palms to firmly roll each twist in a clockwise direction, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Maintain Your Locs

Once your locs are established, it's important to maintain them properly. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and oiling to keep your scalp and hair healthy. You should also avoid using heavy products, excessive heat, or tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your locs.

Tips for Creating and Maintaining Locs with Natural Hair

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to cleanse your hair before starting the loc process.
  • Section your hair into small, equal-sized parts for best results.
  • Twist your hair tightly and use a twisting cream or gel to help hold the twists in place.
  • Palm roll your twists to smooth and tighten them, creating the foundation for your locs.
  • Avoid using heavy products, excessive heat, or tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your locs.
  • Regularly wash and condition your hair to keep your scalp and hair healthy.
  • Use a light oil to moisturize your locs and scalp, preventing dryness and breakage.

FAQ About Locs with Natural Hair

How long does it take to create locs with natural hair?

The length of time it takes to create locs with natural hair can vary depending on your hair type, length, and the method you use. On average, it can take anywhere from 6 months to a year or more to fully establish locs.

Can you create locs with short hair?

Yes, you can create locs with short hair. However, it may take longer to establish them and you may need to use a different method than someone with longer hair.

How often should you wash your locs?

You should wash your locs at least once a week to keep your scalp and hair healthy. However, some people may need to wash their locs more frequently depending on their lifestyle and hair type.

Can you color your locs?

Yes, you can color your locs. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye and to avoid excessive heat or lightening that can cause damage to your hair.

Final Thoughts

Creating locs with natural hair is a great way to embrace your natural texture and achieve a low-maintenance, versatile style. By following these steps and tips, you can create beautiful, healthy locs that will last for years to come. Remember to be patient, take care of your hair, and enjoy the journey!
