Mens Haircut Long On Top Shorter On Sides The Perfect Style For Men In 2023

Written by Oliver Oct 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle that will never go out of style, then the mens haircut long on top shorter on sides is the perfect choice. This classic cut has been popular for decades and is still a favorite amongst men of all ages. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to achieve this timeless style, tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The mens haircut long on top shorter on sides is a style that features longer hair on the top of the head while the sides and back are shorter. This style can be achieved with various hair types, textures, and lengths, making it a versatile style for men of all ages. The style is typically achieved with a fade or undercut on the sides, leaving the hair longer on top. This style can be styled in various ways, depending on the individual's preferences, making it a popular choice for men who want a versatile and easy-to-maintain look.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Long on Top Shorter on Sides

To achieve the mens haircut long on top shorter on sides, you will need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with Your Barber or Hairstylist

Before getting this style, it's essential to consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine if this style will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also give you advice on the best products to use to maintain your new hairstyle.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Once you have discussed the style with your barber or hairstylist, you need to decide on the length of your hair. The length of your hair will depend on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. If you have a square face shape, shorter hair on top and longer on the sides will look great. Those with rounder faces can opt for longer hair on top and shorter on the sides to elongate their face.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

Once you have decided on the length of your hair, it's time to get the haircut. Your barber or hairstylist will use clippers to create the fade or undercut on the sides and back of your head. They will then give you the desired length on top, which can be styled in various ways.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to style your hair. You can use various products such as pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair in various ways. If you want a sleek and polished look, use pomade. If you want a messy and textured look, use wax. Gel is perfect for a wet look.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Haircut Long on Top Shorter on Sides

To maintain your mens haircut long on top shorter on sides, follow these tips:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to keep your hair looking fresh and tidy. Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking its best.

Tip 2: Use Quality Hair Products

Using quality hair products is essential to maintain your hairstyle. Using the right products will keep your hair healthy, shiny, and easy to style. Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's essential to protect your hair from heat. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools such as a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the mens haircut long on top shorter on sides with thin hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve this style with thin hair. However, you may need to use products to add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: Is the mens haircut long on top shorter on sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: This style is suitable for most face shapes, but it's essential to consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your mens haircut long on top shorter on sides. Follow these tips for healthy hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, so it's essential to protect your hair from the sun. Use a hat or hair products that contain UV protection.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for healthy hair. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains.

The mens haircut long on top shorter on sides is a timeless style that will never go out of fashion. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and suitable for men of all ages. Follow our guide to achieve this style and maintain it with proper hair care.
