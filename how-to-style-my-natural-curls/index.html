How To Style My Natural Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who has naturally curly hair, you know that styling it can be a bit of a challenge. It's not always easy to achieve the look you want, and it can take a lot of trial and error to figure out what works best for your hair. In this post, we'll be sharing our best tips and tricks for how to style your natural curls, so you can rock your curls with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: How to Prep Your Hair for Styling
  • Section 2: How to Style Your Curls
  • Section 3: Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • Section 4: FAQ
  • Section 5: Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Section 1: How to Prep Your Hair for Styling

Before you start styling your natural curls, it's important to prep your hair properly. This will ensure that your curls look their best and that your style lasts as long as possible. Here's how to prep your hair for styling:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair. This will help to hydrate your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls. This will help to prevent breakage and make it easier to style your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help hydrate your curls and prevent frizz. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Curl Defining Product

Apply a curl defining product to your hair to help enhance your curls and give them definition. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Section 2: How to Style Your Curls

Now that your hair is prepped and ready, it's time to start styling your curls. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold your curls in place. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the style you're going for.

Step 2: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Curls

Use your hands to scrunch your curls as you dry them. This will help to enhance your curls and give them definition.

Step 4: Finish with a Hair Oil

Finish by applying a hair oil to your curls to help hydrate and add shine. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Section 3: Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Now that you've styled your curls, it's important to know how to maintain them. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Satin Pillowcase

Use a satin pillowcase instead of a cotton one to help prevent frizz and breakage.

Tip 2: Refresh Your Curls

Use a spray bottle filled with water and a leave-in conditioner to refresh your curls throughout the day.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other elements by wearing a hat or scarf.

Section 4: FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others need to wash their hair more frequently.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curls?

A: One of the best ways to prevent frizz is to use a styling product that is specifically designed for curly hair. You can also try using a diffuser when you blow dry your hair, and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Section 5: Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your curls, as this will help to prevent breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair. If you do use heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking their best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect style for your natural curls. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
