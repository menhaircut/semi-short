Does Thick Hair Look Bad Short

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone with thick hair, you may have wondered whether short hair would suit you or not. It's a common misconception that thick hair can't be styled in shorter haircuts. However, the truth is that thick hair can look great in short haircuts, as long as it's done properly. In this article, we'll discuss whether thick hair looks bad short and give you some tips on how to style it.

Description

Thick hair is a blessing and a curse. While it can be voluminous and luscious, it can also be unruly and difficult to manage. When it comes to short haircuts, many people believe that thick hair won't look good. But the truth is that it all depends on the cut and how it's styled.

How To

The key to making thick hair look good in a short haircut is to find a style that works for you. A good stylist can help you choose a cut that suits your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short haircuts for thick hair include a bob, pixie cut, and layered cut.

When styling your hair, it's important to use the right products. Thick hair can be heavy, so you want to avoid using heavy products that will weigh it down. Instead, opt for lightweight products that will add volume and texture. A good volumizing mousse or spray can work wonders for thick hair.

Step by Step

1. Choose a short haircut that works for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for thick hair.

3. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.

4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume.

5. Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener, depending on the look you want to achieve.

6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your thick hair look good in a short haircut:

  • Avoid heavy products that will weigh your hair down.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Don't overdo it with heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

1. Can thick hair be styled in a short haircut?

Yes, absolutely. Thick hair can look great in a short haircut, as long as it's done properly.

2. What are some popular short haircuts for thick hair?

Some popular short haircuts for thick hair include a bob, pixie cut, and layered cut.

3. What products should I use to style my thick hair?

Use lightweight products that will add volume and texture, such as a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your thick hair, it's important to use products that are suitable for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for thick hair, as these will help to nourish and strengthen your locks. You should also avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Regular trims are also important for keeping your thick hair healthy and manageable. Get a trim every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. And always use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools, to prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Thick hair can look great in a short haircut, as long as it's done properly. By choosing the right cut, using the right products, and following these tips, you can make your thick hair look amazing in a short hairstyle. So don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
