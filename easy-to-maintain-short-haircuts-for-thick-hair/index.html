Easy To Maintain Short Haircuts For Thick Hair

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have lots of hair to work with, it can also be a challenge to maintain. Short haircuts can be a great solution for those with thick hair who want to make their hair more manageable. In this article, we'll discuss some easy to maintain short haircuts for thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts for thick hair can be both functional and stylish. They can help reduce the weight of your hair and make it easier to style. Short haircuts can also make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. Some popular short haircuts for thick hair include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for thick hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A pixie cut can be a great option for those with an oval or heart-shaped face and fine hair. A bob or lob can work well for those with a round or square face and thick hair.

Step by step

To achieve a short haircut for thick hair, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash and condition your hair.
  3. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for thick hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick hair:

1. How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

2. Can I still style my hair if it's short?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

3. Will a short haircut make my hair look thinner?

No, a short haircut can actually make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut for thick hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat with a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut for your thick hair, you can have a stylish and easy to maintain look that you'll love.
