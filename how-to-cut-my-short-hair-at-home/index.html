How To Cut My Short Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying big bucks to get your short hair trimmed at the salon? With the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your short hair at home. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to cut your short hair at home like a pro.

How to Cut Your Short Hair at Home

Cutting your short hair at home may seem daunting at first, but with a little practice, you’ll get the hang of it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your short hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clipper with guards
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Spray bottle with water

Make sure your tools are clean and sanitized before you start cutting your hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry. Comb your hair to remove tangles and knots. Divide your hair into sections and tie each section with a hair tie. This will help you focus on one section at a time.

Step 3: Start Cutting Your Hair

Begin cutting your hair with the clipper. Start with the longest guard and gradually work your way down to the shortest guard. Use the comb to guide the clipper and make sure the hair is even.

Once you’ve finished using the clipper, use the scissors to trim any uneven parts. Start with the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to guide the scissors and make sure the hair is even.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make sure your hair is even and there are no uneven parts. Trim any parts that need to be fixed.

Tips for Cutting Your Short Hair at Home

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your short hair at home:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use the comb to guide the clipper and scissors.
  • Trim small sections at a time.
  • Check your work in the mirror frequently.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short.

FAQs

Can I cut my short hair at home?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your short hair at home.

What tools do I need to cut my short hair at home?

You’ll need sharp scissors, a clipper with guards, a comb, hair ties, and a spray bottle with water.

How do I prepare my hair before cutting it?

Wash your hair and let it air dry. Comb your hair to remove tangles and knots. Divide your hair into sections and tie each section with a hair tie.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Short Hair at Home

After cutting your short hair at home, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

Now that you know how to cut your short hair at home, you can save money on salon visits and maintain your hair between appointments. Happy cutting!
