What Do Guys Like More Straight Or Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to hair, everyone has their own preferences. Some guys like straight hair while others prefer curly hair. But what do guys really like more: straight or curly hair? Let's find out.

Description

It's a common question among women, especially when it comes to dating and attracting men. While some believe that straight hair is more attractive, others think that curly hair is more alluring. In this article, we'll explore what guys like more and why.

How to

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question as everyone has their own preferences. However, we can gather some insights from surveys and studies conducted on the topic. According to a survey conducted by Glamour Magazine, 43% of men prefer women with straight hair while 33% prefer women with curly hair. The remaining 24% said they have no preference.

Step by Step

So, what does this mean? It means that there's no clear winner when it comes to straight vs curly hair. Some guys like one over the other, and some don't really care. The most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin and hair.

Tips

If you're still wondering whether to straighten or curl your hair for a date, event, or just for everyday wear, here are some tips to help you decide:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture. Certain hairstyles may complement your features better than others.
  • Think about the occasion. Some events may call for a more polished look, while others may allow for a more relaxed, natural look.
  • Experiment with different styles. You never know what you might like until you try it.
  • Don't worry too much about what guys like. It's important to feel good about yourself first and foremost.

FAQ

What do guys like more, straight or curly hair?

As mentioned, there's no clear winner. Some guys prefer straight hair while others like curly hair. It ultimately comes down to personal preference.

Does hair texture matter to guys?

Yes, hair texture can play a role in attractiveness to some guys. However, it's not the only factor and some guys may not have a strong preference either way.

Is it better to have natural or styled hair?

It depends on the occasion and your personal preferences. Some guys may prefer a natural, effortless look while others may be drawn to a more polished, styled look.

Hair Care

Whether you have straight or curly hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage and dryness.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to nourish your hair from the inside out.

So, what do guys like more: straight or curly hair? The answer is that it varies from person to person. The most important thing is to embrace your natural hair texture and style it in a way that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
