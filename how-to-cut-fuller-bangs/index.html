How To Cut Fuller Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, cutting fuller bangs can be a great way to freshen up your look. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut fuller bangs from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Fuller Bangs

What You'll Need

Before you start cutting your bangs, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Wide-toothed comb
  • Clips to hold back hair
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect fuller bangs:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to have clean, dry hair before you start cutting.
  2. Using the wide-toothed comb, separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip back the rest of your hair so it's out of the way.
  3. Decide how long you want your bangs to be. Use the comb to pull your hair straight down and then cut across at the desired length.
  4. If you want your bangs to be thicker, take small sections of hair and cut at an angle. This will create layers and give your bangs a fuller look.
  5. Once you've cut your hair to the desired length, use the scissors to create a slight curve in the middle. This will give your bangs a natural look.
  6. Take down the rest of your hair and make sure your bangs are even on both sides.
  7. Use the comb to style your bangs to your liking.

Tips for Cutting Fuller Bangs

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect fuller bangs:

  • Start by cutting your bangs a little longer than you want them. You can always go back and cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use a light hand when cutting. It's better to take small snips than to cut off too much hair at once.
  • Don't cut your bangs when they're wet. Hair shrinks when it's wet, so you may end up cutting your bangs too short if you cut them while they're still wet.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider going to a professional hair stylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting fuller bangs:

Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your bangs yourself. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I cut my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look unkempt. As a general rule, it's a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Will cutting my bangs make them thicker?

Yes, cutting your bangs at an angle can create layers and give them a fuller look.

Hair Care

After cutting your bangs, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips for caring for your bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to give them a smooth look.
  • Use a hair serum to tame any frizz or flyaways.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect fuller bangs and keep them looking great. Happy cutting!
