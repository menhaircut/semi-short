How To Grow Emo Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle and go for a more emo look? Emo hair for guys is all about long, choppy layers and a lot of texture. This style has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to grow emo hair for guys, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Grow Emo Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Grow Emo Hair for Guys

If you're starting from scratch, growing out your hair may take some time. The key to emo hair for guys is to have long, choppy layers that can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Let Your Hair Grow

The first step to emo hair for guys is to let your hair grow out. This will take time, but it's essential to achieve the look you're going for. You'll want your hair to be long enough to create choppy layers, so aim for at least shoulder-length hair.

Step 2: Get a Haircut

Once your hair has grown out enough, it's time to get a haircut. It's best to go to a stylist who has experience with emo hairstyles. They'll be able to give you the choppy layers you need to achieve the look. You can also ask for side-swept bangs, which are a staple of emo hair for guys.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right cut, it's time to style your hair. Emo hair for guys is all about texture and volume. You can achieve this by using a texturizing spray or pomade. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to create the choppy layers. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to grow emo hair for guys:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before you start growing out your hair, it's a good idea to find some inspiration. Look for pictures of emo hairstyles that you like and show them to your stylist.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Grow

As mentioned, you'll need to let your hair grow out to achieve the choppy layers of emo hair for guys. This may take anywhere from six months to a year, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Step 3: Get a Haircut

Once your hair has grown out, it's time to get a haircut. Tell your stylist that you want long, choppy layers and side-swept bangs.

Step 4: Use Products to Style Your Hair

After your haircut, you'll need to style your hair to achieve the emo look. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair. Use your fingers to create the choppy layers and style your bangs to the side.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

Once you've mastered the basic emo hairstyle, you can experiment with different styles. Try wearing your hair up in a messy bun or ponytail, or add some color with highlights or lowlights.

Tips for Maintaining Emo Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your emo hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing emo hair for guys:

Can I grow emo hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still grow emo hair if you have curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve the choppy layers. Talk to your stylist about the best way to achieve the look with your hair type.

Do I need to dye my hair to have emo hair?

No, you don't need to dye your hair to have emo hair. However, adding some color can enhance the look. Talk to your stylist about adding highlights or lowlights.

How do I prevent my hair from becoming too greasy?

Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too greasy. You can also use dry shampoo in between washes to absorb oil.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your emo hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot water when you shower, as it can dry out your hair
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

By following these tips and steps, you can grow emo hair for guys and achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient, as it may take time to achieve the right length and texture. With the right care and styling, you can rock this popular hairstyle.
