What Does It Mean When Your Dog Loses Hair Around The Eyes

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a pet parent, you may have noticed that your furry friend is losing hair around their eyes. This can be a cause for concern, as it may indicate an underlying health issue. In this article, we will discuss what it means when your dog loses hair around the eyes, possible causes, and tips for proper hair care.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. Causes
  3. How to Check for Hair Loss
  4. Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Care
  5. Tips for Hair Care

Description

Hair loss around the eyes in dogs is not uncommon. It can be a sign of a medical condition or simply a result of aging. The skin around the eyes is thin and sensitive, making it more prone to hair loss. The loss of hair may be gradual or sudden, and can affect one or both eyes. It can also be accompanied by other symptoms, such as itching, redness, or discharge.

Causes

There are several possible causes of hair loss around the eyes in dogs. These include:

  • Allergies
  • Mites or fleas
  • Eye infections
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Autoimmune diseases
  • Stress or anxiety
  • Injury or trauma

If you notice hair loss around your dog's eyes, it's important to take them to the vet for a proper diagnosis. Your vet may perform tests, such as a skin scraping or blood work, to determine the underlying cause of the hair loss.

How to Check for Hair Loss

To check for hair loss around your dog's eyes, gently lift the fur and examine the skin. If there is hair loss, you may see redness, bumps, or scabs on the skin. If you notice any of these symptoms, it's important to take your dog to the vet for further examination.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Care

To care for your dog's hair around the eyes, follow these steps:

  1. Clean the area around the eyes with a damp cloth or cotton ball
  2. Trim any long hairs around the eyes to prevent them from irritating the skin
  3. Apply a gentle moisturizer to the skin to soothe any irritation
  4. Avoid using harsh chemicals or shampoos on the area around the eyes

It's important to be gentle when caring for the area around your dog's eyes, as the skin is sensitive and can easily become irritated. If you notice any signs of infection or inflammation, take your dog to the vet for further treatment.

Tips for Hair Care

To prevent hair loss around the eyes in dogs, it's important to practice good hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Feed your dog a healthy diet to promote healthy skin and hair
  • Brush your dog regularly to remove loose hair and prevent matting
  • Use a gentle shampoo when bathing your dog
  • Trim any long hairs around the eyes to prevent them from irritating the skin
  • Take your dog to the vet for regular check-ups to catch any health issues early

FAQs

Can stress cause hair loss in dogs?

Yes, stress can be a cause of hair loss in dogs. If you notice that your dog is losing hair around their eyes and is also exhibiting signs of stress or anxiety, it's important to address the underlying cause of the stress.

Can allergies cause hair loss in dogs?

Yes, allergies can be a cause of hair loss in dogs. If your dog is experiencing hair loss around their eyes and is also exhibiting signs of allergies, such as itching or redness, it's important to take them to the vet for proper diagnosis and treatment.

How can I prevent hair loss around my dog's eyes?

To prevent hair loss around your dog's eyes, practice good hair care, feed your dog a healthy diet, and take them to the vet for regular check-ups. If you notice any signs of hair loss or skin irritation, take your dog to the vet for further examination.

In conclusion, hair loss around the eyes in dogs can be a sign of an underlying health issue. It's important to take your dog to the vet for proper diagnosis and treatment. With proper care and attention, you can help prevent hair loss and promote healthy skin and hair in your furry friend.
