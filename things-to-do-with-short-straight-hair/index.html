Things To Do With Short Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to explore different ways to style your short straight hair? Look no further because we have compiled a list of things to do with short straight hair that will help you switch up your look. From braids to buns, we have got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Short straight hair is a popular hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. However, it can become boring to wear the same style every day. That's why we have created this guide to help you explore different hairstyles that you can do with your short straight hair.

How To

1. Braids

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in different ways. For short straight hair, you can try a French braid, Dutch braid or a plain braid. To do a French braid, divide your hair into three sections and start braiding from the top of your head. Keep adding more hair to the braid as you go down. Secure the end with a hair tie. You can also try a half-up half-down braid for a different look.

2. Buns

Buns are another easy hairstyle that can be done with short straight hair. You can try a messy bun or a sleek bun depending on your preference. To do a messy bun, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it around. Secure it with bobby pins. To do a sleek bun, brush your hair back and gather it into a ponytail. Twist it around and secure it with bobby pins. Use a hairspray to keep it in place.

3. Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can be done with short straight hair. You can try a high ponytail or a low ponytail depending on your mood. To do a high ponytail, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie. To do a low ponytail, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. You can also try a side ponytail for a different look.

Step by Step

1. French Braid

  1. Divide your hair into three sections.
  2. Start braiding from the top of your head.
  3. Add more hair to the braid as you go down.
  4. Secure the end with a hair tie.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Twist it around.
  3. Secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use a hairspray to keep it in place.

3. High Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair at the top of your head.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie.

Tips

  • Use a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Accessorize your hair with hairpins, headbands or clips.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl my short straight hair?

A: Yes, you can curl your short straight hair using a curling iron or a flat iron. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short straight hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally, you can wash your short straight hair every 2-3 days. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

To keep your short straight hair healthy and shiny, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush that can cause breakage. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote hair growth.

Now that you have a guide on things to do with short straight hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles and switch up your look. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with it!
