How to Change Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go

Before we dive into the steps, let's discuss the requirements for changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go. To change your hairstyle, you need to have reached at least Level 5 in the game. Additionally, you must have a Pokemon Go avatar that is visible on your screen.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  1. Open the Pokemon Go app on your device.
  2. Tap on your avatar icon located at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  3. Tap on the "Style" icon located on the bottom right corner of your screen.
  4. Scroll down to the "Hair" section and select "Change".
  5. Select the hairstyle you want for your avatar.
  6. Once you have selected your hairstyle, tap on the "Confirm" button.

And just like that, you have changed your hairstyle in Pokemon Go!

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see which one you like best.
  • Don't be afraid to change your hairstyle frequently to keep things fresh and exciting.
  • Consider the weather when choosing a hairstyle. Some hairstyles may be more suitable for hot or cold weather.
  • Have fun with it! Changing your hairstyle is a great way to express your creativity and individuality.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing hairstyles in Pokemon Go:

Can I change my hairstyle more than once?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want in Pokemon Go.

Do I need to pay to change my hairstyle?

No, changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is completely free.

Can I change my avatar's gender?

Yes, you can change your avatar's gender in Pokemon Go. However, this will reset all of your progress and you will have to start over from Level 1.

Hair Care in Pokemon Go

While changing your hairstyle is fun and exciting, it's also important to take care of your avatar's hair. Here are some tips for keeping your avatar's hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your avatar's hair regularly to prevent buildup and damage.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your avatar's hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your avatar's hair as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask to nourish and strengthen your avatar's hair.
