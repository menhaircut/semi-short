How To Tell If Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Petter Nov 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of always getting a haircut that doesn't seem to suit you? Well, worry no more! In this article, we will give you tips on how to tell if a hairstyle suits you or not. We will also provide you with helpful information on how to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking good. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell if a Hairstyle Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

How to Tell if a Hairstyle Suits You

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but there are a few things that you should consider when making your decision.

Face Shape

Your face shape is an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features and requires a different hairstyle to complement it.

For example, if you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to your crown, such as a high ponytail or updo. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much get away with any hairstyle, but a side-swept bang or layered cut will complement your features well.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to avoid short, layered cuts that can make your hair look frizzy. Instead, opt for long, layered cuts that will help to weigh down your hair and prevent frizz.

If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume, such as a blunt bob. Instead, opt for a layered haircut that adds movement and texture to your hair.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be taken into consideration when choosing a hairstyle. If you're always on the go and don't have time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle, such as a messy bun or a simple ponytail. If you have a more formal job, you may want to opt for a sleek, polished hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's go through the steps to determine if a hairstyle suits you or not.

Step 1: Look in the Mirror

Start by looking in the mirror and taking note of your face shape, hair texture, and features. This will help you narrow down your hairstyle options.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Next, consider your lifestyle and the amount of time you're willing to spend on your hair. This will help you determine if you should opt for a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hairstyle.

Step 3: Try on Wigs or Use Virtual Try-On Tools

If you're still unsure about which hairstyle to choose, try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hairstyles look on you. This can help you visualize how a certain hairstyle will look before committing to it.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're still unsure about which hairstyle to choose, consult with a hairstylist. They can provide you with recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.
  • Take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQs

Q: What if I have multiple face shapes?

A: If you have multiple face shapes, choose a hairstyle that complements the most prominent feature of your face.

Q: Can I pull off a bob haircut?

A: Bobs can be flattering on most face shapes, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've chosen the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can achieve a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
