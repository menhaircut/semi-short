How to Change Your Hair Color Permanently - Tips and Tricks

Are you tired of your hair color and want to make a permanent change? Changing your hair color can be a big decision, but it can also be a fun way to switch up your look. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about how to change your hair color permanently.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Your Hair Color Permanently
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Hair Color Change
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Permanent Hair Color Change

How to Change Your Hair Color Permanently

Permanent hair color is the most dramatic way to change your hair color. It involves using chemicals to penetrate the hair shaft and change the color from the inside out. Permanent hair color will last until the hair grows out or is cut.

Before making a permanent hair color change, it is important to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Consider consulting with a professional hair stylist to help you choose the right shade for your complexion.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow if you want to change your hair color permanently:

  1. Choose your desired hair color.
  2. Purchase a permanent hair color kit.
  3. Do a patch test to ensure that you are not allergic to the hair color.
  4. Read the instructions carefully and follow them closely.
  5. Apply the hair color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time, usually between 20 to 45 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear.
  8. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair color kit.
  9. Rinse your hair with cool water to close the hair cuticle and lock in the color.

Tips for a Successful Hair Color Change

Here are some tips to keep in mind for a successful permanent hair color change:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  • Consider going to a professional hair stylist for your first permanent hair color change.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about permanent hair color:

  1. Is permanent hair color safe?

    2. Yes, permanent hair color is safe when used as directed. However, it is important to do a patch test before using the product to ensure that you are not allergic to any of the ingredients.

  2. Can I change my hair color from dark to light with permanent hair color?

    3. It is possible to lighten your hair with permanent hair color, but it can be a difficult process. It is best to consult with a professional hair stylist for this type of color change.

  3. How often should I touch up my permanent hair color?

    4. It is recommended to touch up permanent hair color every 4 to 6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care After Permanent Hair Color Change

After making a permanent hair color change, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage the hair and fade the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Changing your hair color permanently can be a fun way to switch up your look. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a successful hair color change that will last until your next touch-up.
