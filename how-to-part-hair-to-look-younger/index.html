How To Part Your Hair To Look Younger

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to look younger, changing your hair part can make all the difference. With just a few simple steps, you can create a new part that helps to lift your face and give you a more youthful appearance. In this article, we'll show you how to part your hair to look younger, including tips and tricks for getting the perfect part every time.

How to Part Your Hair to Look Younger

The first step to creating a youthful hair part is to find the right spot on your head. You want to avoid a center part, as this can draw attention to any wrinkles or fine lines on your face. Instead, try a side part that's slightly off-center. This will help to create volume and lift on one side of your face, which can help to smooth out any imperfections.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you to see where your natural part is and make it easier to create a new one. You can also use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some extra lift and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Use a Comb to Create Your Part

Take a fine-tooth comb and use it to create a part on one side of your head. Start at the crown of your head and comb your hair forward and to one side, creating a part that's slightly off-center. You can use a mirror to help you see where the part looks best.

Step 3: Add Some Volume

Once you've created your part, you can add some extra volume to the top of your head to help lift your face. You can use a round brush and a blow dryer to create some volume at the roots, or you can use a volumizing powder or spray to add some texture and lift.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Part

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect part every time:

  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a precise part
  • Experiment with different part locations to find the one that works best for your face shape
  • Add some volume to the top of your head to create lift and balance out your features
  • Use a hairspray or styling cream to help hold your part in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to part your hair to look younger:

Q: How do I know where to create my part?

A: Start at the crown of your head and comb your hair forward and to one side, creating a part that's slightly off-center. You can use a mirror to help you see where the part looks best.

Q: Can I create a youthful part with short hair?

A: Yes! You can create a side part with almost any length of hair. Just follow the same steps and use a comb to create a part that's slightly off-center.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to create my part?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to create a part if you prefer. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and youthful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

With these tips and tricks, you can create a youthful hair part that helps to lift and smooth out your features. Remember to experiment with different part locations to find the one that works best for your face shape. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant!
