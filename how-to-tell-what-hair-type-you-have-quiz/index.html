How To Tell What Hair Type You Have Quiz

Written by Daniel Oct 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you confused about what type of hair you have? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. With so many different hair textures and types, it can be difficult to determine which one you have. Fortunately, there is a simple quiz you can take to help you figure it out. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps of taking a hair type quiz and provide some tips on how to care for your hair based on your results.

What is a Hair Type Quiz?

A hair type quiz is a set of questions designed to help you determine your hair type. The quiz typically asks about the texture, thickness, and curl pattern of your hair, as well as any issues you may be experiencing, such as dryness or breakage. Based on your answers, the quiz will assign you a hair type, such as straight, wavy, curly, or coily.

How to Take a Hair Type Quiz

There are many hair type quizzes available online, but we recommend the one provided by NaturallyCurly.com. Here are the steps to take the quiz:

  1. Visit the NaturallyCurly website and click on the “Hair Type Quiz” link.
  2. Answer the questions about your hair texture, thickness, and curl pattern.
  3. Indicate any issues you may be experiencing with your hair.
  4. Submit your answers and receive your hair type results.

Once you have your hair type results, you can use that information to determine the best hair care routine for your hair.

Tips for Caring for Your Hair Type

Every hair type is unique and requires a specific hair care routine. Here are some tips for caring for each hair type:

Straight Hair

Straight hair tends to be oily and can become weighed down easily. Use a gentle, clarifying shampoo and avoid heavy conditioners. Blow-dry your hair on a low setting to prevent damage.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can be prone to frizz and dryness. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your waves.

Curly Hair

Curly hair is often dry and prone to breakage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner to moisturize your hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry and use a diffuser when blow-drying.

Coily Hair

Coily hair is the most fragile hair type and requires the most moisture. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using heat on your hair and use protective styles to prevent breakage.

FAQs

What if my hair doesn’t fit neatly into one hair type category?

It’s common for hair to have multiple textures and types. If your hair doesn’t fit neatly into one category, use the hair type that best describes the majority of your hair.

Can my hair type change over time?

Yes, your hair type can change over time due to hormonal changes, aging, or changes in your hair care routine. It’s important to reassess your hair type periodically to ensure that you’re using the right products and techniques.

Hair Care Summary

Determining your hair type is the first step to achieving healthy, beautiful hair. By taking a hair type quiz and following the tips for caring for your hair type, you can create a hair care routine that will keep your hair looking its best.
