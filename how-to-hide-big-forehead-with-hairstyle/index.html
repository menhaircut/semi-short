How To Hide Big Forehead With Hairstyle Tips And Tricks

Are you struggling with a big forehead and feeling conscious about it? Don't worry; there are several hairstyles that can help you conceal your forehead and give you a confident look. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles to hide a big forehead, along with some tips and tricks to make the most of your look.

How to Hide Big Forehead with Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a huge difference in your appearance. Here are some popular hairstyles that can help you hide your big forehead:

1. Bangs

Bangs are the most popular hairstyle for concealing a big forehead. They cover a significant portion of the forehead and create an illusion of a smaller forehead. You can opt for side-swept bangs, blunt bangs, or wispy bangs, depending on your face shape.

2. Layers

Layers are another great option to cover a big forehead. They add volume to your hair and draw attention away from your forehead. You can opt for long layers or short layers, depending on your preference.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a versatile hairstyle that can help you hide your forehead. You can choose a chin-length bob or a shoulder-length bob, depending on your face shape. You can also add layers or bangs to your bob cut to enhance your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle to hide your big forehead:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Step 2: Choose the right hairstyle

Once you determine your face shape, you can choose the right hairstyle that suits your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you can opt for a long bob with layers or a side-swept fringe.

Step 3: Consult a hairstylist

If you are not sure which hairstyle to choose, you can consult a hairstylist. They can suggest the best hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make the most of your hairstyle:

1. Use hair accessories

You can use hair accessories like headbands, hairpins, and hair clips to add an element of style to your hairstyle. They can also help you keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling on your face.

2. Try different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. You can try braids, ponytails, and updos to add variety to your look.

3. Use volumizing products

You can use volumizing products like mousse, hairspray, and dry shampoo to add volume to your hair. They can help you create a fuller look and make your hair appear thicker.

FAQ

Q1. Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big forehead?

A1. Yes, you can still wear your hair up if you have a big forehead. You can opt for a high ponytail or a messy bun to create a chic look.

Q2. How do I know if bangs will suit me?

A2. You can determine if bangs will suit you by considering your face shape and hair type. You can also consult a hairstylist for their opinion.

Q3. How often should I wash my hair?

A3. You should wash your hair 2-3 times a week, depending on your hair type and scalp condition.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

You should use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out and becoming brittle.

2. Use a conditioner

You should use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming frizzy.

3. Avoid heat styling

You should avoid heat styling your hair too often as it can damage your hair and make it brittle.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle can help you conceal your big forehead and boost your confidence. You can try different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat styling too often.
