How To Cut Men s Long Hair With Scissors Step By Step

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair can be a challenge to manage, especially for men. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut men's long hair with scissors step by step at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process, from preparation to aftercare.

Preparation

Before cutting men's long hair, you need to gather the necessary tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You should also wash and dry the hair to ensure it is clean and free of tangles.

It is also important to have a clear idea of the desired hairstyle and length. You can use pictures or videos as a reference to guide you during the cutting process.

Sectioning the Hair

Sectioning the hair is essential to ensure an even cut. Start by dividing the hair into four equal sections: two at the front and two at the back. Secure each section with hair clips.

Next, take a small section of hair from one of the front sections and comb it straight. Hold it between your fingers and trim off the desired length using the scissors. Repeat this process for each section, working from the front to the back.

Trimming the Hair

Once you have cut the initial length, you can begin trimming the rest of the hair. Take a small section of hair from one of the back sections and comb it straight. Hold it between your fingers and cut off a small amount of hair at a time, working your way down the length of the hair.

Continue this process for each section, making sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides. Take breaks as needed to rest your hands and assess your progress.

Blending the Hair

After trimming the hair, you need to blend it to create a seamless transition between the different sections. To do this, take a small section of hair from one of the front sections and comb it straight. Hold it between your fingers and cut off a small amount of hair at a time, working your way towards the back.

Repeat this process for each section, making sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides. Use the comb to blend any uneven areas and ensure a smooth transition between the sections.

Final Touches

Once you have blended the hair, you can make any final touches to the length and style. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven sections. You can also use thinning shears to add texture and volume to the hair.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take breaks as needed to rest your hands and assess your progress
  • Use reference pictures or videos to guide your cutting
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once
  • Blend the hair to create a seamless transition between sections

FAQs

How often should I cut my long hair?

The frequency of haircuts depends on the desired length and style. For long hair, it is typically recommended to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Can I cut men's long hair with regular scissors?

While it is possible to cut men's long hair with regular scissors, it is recommended to use hair cutting scissors for a clean and even cut.

How do I know if I have cut too much hair?

If you have cut too much hair, it will be noticeable in the overall length and style. Take breaks as needed to assess your progress and make small cuts to avoid cutting too much at once.

Hair Care after Cutting Men's Long Hair

After cutting men's long hair, it is important to maintain healthy hair with proper hair care. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using hair products to protect and nourish the hair.

You can also schedule regular trims to maintain the desired length and style. With the right tools and techniques, you can cut men's long hair with scissors step by step and achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.
