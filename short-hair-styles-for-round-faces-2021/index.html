Short Hair Styles For Round Faces 2021 A Guide

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle that doesn't flatter your round face shape? If so, it's time to switch things up with a short hair style that accentuates your features. In this guide, we'll explore the top short hair styles for round faces in 2021. From the classic bob to the trendy pixie cut, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style for Your Round Face

When it comes to choosing a short hair style for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to create the illusion of length in your face. This means avoiding styles that add width or volume to the sides of your head.

One of the most important factors to consider is the length of the cut. Generally speaking, shorter hair styles work better for round faces. This is because longer styles tend to drag down the face and make it look wider.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of the cut. You want to look for styles that have some height or volume at the crown of the head, as this will help elongate your face. Avoid styles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face appear wider.

Top Short Hair Styles for Round Faces in 2021

Now that you know what to look for in a short hair style for a round face, let's take a look at some of the top styles for 2021:

  • The Bob: The classic bob is a great choice for round faces. Just be sure to keep the length above your chin and add some layers to create volume at the crown of your head.
  • The Pixie Cut: This trendy style is perfect for round faces, as it creates the illusion of length and height. Consider adding some choppy layers to create texture and volume.
  • The Shag: A shaggy cut is a great way to add some edge to your look. Ask your stylist to add some layers and choppy bangs to create a messy, textured look.
  • The Lob: If you're not quite ready to go super short, a lob (long bob) is a great choice. Just be sure to keep the length above your shoulders and add some layers to create volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Now that you've chosen your perfect short hair style for your round face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair from the roots to the ends.
  4. Add some volumizing spray to the crown of your head to create height.
  5. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head.
  6. Use a light hold hairspray to set the style.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

Short hair styles require regular maintenance to stay looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to keep your hair straight and sleek.
  • Use a volumizing spray at the roots of your hair to create height and volume.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs About Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

What is the best short hair style for a round face?

The best short hair style for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length. The classic bob, pixie cut, shag, and lob are all great choices for round faces.

Can I add bangs to a short hair style for a round face?

Yes, bangs can be a great addition to a short hair style for a round face. Consider choppy, textured bangs to create a messy, edgy look.

How often should I get my short hair cut?

You should get your short hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

Short hair styles require some extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short hair style that flatters your round face shape in 2021. Whether you choose a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, the key is to create the illusion of length and height in your face. With a little bit of maintenance and care, your short hair style will look great all year round.
