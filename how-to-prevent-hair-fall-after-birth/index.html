How To Prevent Hair Fall After Birth Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after giving birth? This is a common concern among new moms and can be caused by hormonal changes, stress, and lack of sleep. If you're tired of finding strands of hair all over your clothes and floor, don't worry! In this article, we'll discuss how to prevent hair fall after birth with some simple and effective tips.

  • How to Prevent Hair Fall After Birth
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Prevent Hair Fall After Birth

Here are some steps you can take to prevent hair fall after giving birth:

1. Eat a Nutritious Diet

Eating a balanced diet can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of proteins, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens are great for your hair.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is essential for overall health, including healthy hair. Aim for at least eight glasses of water a day to keep your scalp and hair hydrated.

3. Manage Stress

Stress can negatively impact your hair growth. Try to manage stress through relaxation techniques like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises. Get enough sleep and take breaks when needed.

4. Take Care of Your Scalp

A healthy scalp is the foundation for healthy hair. Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by washing your hair regularly and using a nourishing conditioner. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to prevent hair fall after birth:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on wet hair, as it can lead to breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

3. Don't Tie Your Hair Too Tight

Tight hairstyles like braids, ponytails, or buns can lead to hair breakage. Try to wear your hair down or in loose hairstyles as much as possible.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage. It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Satin pillowcases can help prevent hair breakage and tangling, as they are gentler on your hair than cotton pillowcases.

2. Avoid Using Hot Tools

Heat styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your hair. Try to avoid using them as much as possible or use them on the lowest heat setting.

3. Protect Your Hair from UV Rays

Exposure to UV rays can damage your hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

1. Is Hair Fall After Birth Normal?

Yes, it's normal to experience hair fall after giving birth due to hormonal changes. However, if you're experiencing excessive hair loss, it's important to speak to your doctor as it could be a sign of an underlying condition.

2. How Long Does Hair Fall Last After Giving Birth?

Hair fall can last up to six months after giving birth. However, it should gradually improve over time.

3. Can Breastfeeding Cause Hair Loss?

Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair loss due to hormonal changes. However, this is usually temporary and should improve after you stop breastfeeding.

Hair Care Products to Try

Here are some hair care products that can help prevent hair fall:

1. Nioxin System Kit

The Nioxin System Kit is a three-step system that helps promote healthy hair growth. It includes a cleanser, conditioner, and scalp treatment.

2. Biotin Shampoo and Conditioner

Biotin is a vitamin that promotes healthy hair growth. Try using a biotin shampoo and conditioner to help prevent hair fall.

3. Hair Growth Vitamins

There are several hair growth vitamins available that can help promote healthy hair growth. Look for vitamins that contain biotin, vitamin E, and vitamin C.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can prevent hair fall after giving birth. Remember to be patient, as it can take time for your hair to recover. If you're experiencing excessive hair loss, speak to your doctor for advice.
