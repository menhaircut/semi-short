Does Blow Drying Hair Ruin Curls The Truth Behind The Myth

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As someone with curly hair, you've probably heard the age-old advice to let your hair air dry to avoid ruining your curls. But is this really true? In this article, we'll explore the question of whether blow drying hair ruins curls and give you tips on how to blow dry your hair without damaging your curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Blow Dry Curly Hair
  • Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Blow Drying Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair

The key to blow drying curly hair without ruining your curls is to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. A diffuser works by spreading out the air flow, which helps to prevent frizz and maintain the natural shape of your curls.

Before blow drying, you should start with clean, damp hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. Then, use your fingers to gently separate your curls and lift them up towards your scalp. This will help to create volume and prevent your curls from clumping together.

Next, attach the diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to a low or medium heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around in circular motions to dry your hair. Avoid using a high heat setting, as this can cause damage to your hair and make your curls frizzy.

Once your hair is about 80% dry, you can switch to a cool setting on your blow dryer to set your curls in place. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls up towards your scalp as you blow dry, which will help to enhance their shape and definition.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you blow dry your curly hair without damaging your curls:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair before blow drying. These materials are gentler on your hair than a regular towel and can help to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair before blow drying to help define your curls and keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair while blow drying, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural shape of your curls.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help to ensure that all of your curls are evenly dried.

FAQs About Blow Drying Curly Hair

1. Does blow drying hair ruin curls?

Blow drying hair can potentially ruin curls if you use a high heat setting or don't use a diffuser attachment. However, if you use a diffuser on a low or medium heat setting and follow the proper techniques, you can blow dry your hair without damaging your curls.

2. What is the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is to let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural shape of your curls.

3. How often should you blow dry curly hair?

Blow drying curly hair too often can cause damage and dryness. It's best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week and let your hair air dry the rest of the time.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Aside from blow drying, here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, blow drying hair does not have to ruin curls if done properly. By using a diffuser attachment and following the proper techniques, you can achieve defined, frizz-free curls without damaging your hair. Remember to also take care of your hair with the right products and techniques to keep it healthy and looking its best.
