How To Achieve Messy Hair Look A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Messy hair look is a trend that has been around for a while and it is not going away anytime soon. This effortless style can make you look chic and stylish, and it is perfect for those days when you don’t have time to style your hair. In this article, we will show you how to achieve messy hair look in easy steps. We will also give you tips on how to maintain this style and answer some of the frequently asked questions about messy hair look.

Table of Content

  1. How to Achieve Messy Hair Look
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair Look
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair Look

How to Achieve Messy Hair Look

To achieve the perfect messy hair look, you need to keep in mind that the key is to make it look effortless. The goal is to create an undone appearance that looks natural and messy, not a greasy or unkempt one. Here are some ways to achieve the messy hair look:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add volume and lift.
  • Loosely curl your hair with a curling iron or wand to create waves.
  • Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create texture and messiness.
  • Use a sea salt spray to add texture and create a beachy look.

These are just a few ways to achieve the messy hair look. You can experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect messy hair look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to your roots to add volume and texture.
  3. Tease your hair at the crown to add volume and lift.
  4. Loosely curl your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create texture and messiness.
  6. Use a sea salt spray to add texture and create a beachy look.
  7. Flip your head upside down and spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.
  8. Flip your head back up and use your fingers to gently tousle your hair.
  9. Finish with a spritz of hair oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

Remember, the key is to keep it effortless and natural-looking. Don’t worry about making it perfect; the imperfections are what make it look great.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hair look:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day. This will help maintain the texture and volume.
  • Use dry shampoo or texturizing spray to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have naturally straight hair, try sleeping in braids to create waves and texture.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, apply a styling cream to define and enhance your curls.

These tips will help you maintain your messy hair look and keep it looking great for days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy hair look:

Is messy hair look suitable for all hair types?

Yes, messy hair look can work for all hair types. The key is to find the right products and techniques that work for your hair type.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Also, try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair.

Can I achieve messy hair look without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve messy hair look without using heat. Try sleeping in braids or using a styling cream to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair Look

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your messy hair look:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips, you can maintain your messy hair look and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

In conclusion, achieving messy hair look is easy with the right products and techniques. Follow the steps in this article to achieve the perfect messy hair look, and use the tips and hair care advice to keep it looking great for days. Remember, the key is to keep it effortless and natural-looking, so don’t worry about making it perfect.
