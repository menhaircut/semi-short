What Colour Hair Dye Will Suit Me

Written by Eveline Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on what colour hair dye will suit you. Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit your skin tone, eye colour, and overall look. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hair colour for your next dye job.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the tips, it's important to understand how hair colours work. Hair colours are typically classified into warm and cool tones, and the right one for you will depend on your skin tone and eye colour. Warm tones include shades of red, orange, and yellow, while cool tones include shades of blue, green, and purple.

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your natural hair colour as well. If you have dark hair, going for a lighter shade may require bleaching, which can damage your hair. On the other hand, if you have light hair, going for a darker shade may require multiple dye jobs to achieve the desired result.

How To

Now that you have an idea of how hair colours work, let's move on to some tips on how to choose the right one for you.

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is a crucial factor in choosing the right hair colour. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow or olive, warm hair colours like red, copper, and honey blonde will complement your complexion. If you have cool undertones, like pink or blue, cool hair colours like platinum blonde, ash brown, and black will work best.

Look at Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also influence your hair colour choice. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours like golden blonde and copper can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colours like dark ash blonde and espresso can complement your eye colour.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the right hair colour. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, going for a natural-looking hair colour that doesn't require frequent touch-ups may be the best choice. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with your hair and have the time and budget for frequent salon visits, you can opt for a more daring hair colour.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone and eye colour.
  2. Consider your natural hair colour and how drastic of a change you're willing to make.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do.
  4. Research different hair colours and shades that suit your skin tone and eye colour.
  5. Consult with a professional hair stylist to get their expert opinion.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your wardrobe and choose a hair colour that complements your favourite clothing colours.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover apps to visualize different hair colours on yourself before committing to a dye job.
  • Choose a hair colour that expresses your personality and makes you feel confident.

FAQ

What hair colour suits fair skin?

Cool hair colours like platinum blonde, ash brown, and black work best for fair skin with cool undertones. Warm hair colours like golden blonde and copper can also complement fair skin with warm undertones.

What hair colour suits dark skin?

Warm hair colours like red, copper, and honey blonde can complement dark skin with warm undertones. Cool hair colours like espresso and dark ash blonde can also work well with dark skin.

Hair Care

Whichever hair colour you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and health. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent colour fading and damage, and use hair masks and oils to keep your hair moisturized and shiny. Avoid using hot tools frequently and protect your hair from the sun with hats or hair products with UV protection.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hair colour that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
