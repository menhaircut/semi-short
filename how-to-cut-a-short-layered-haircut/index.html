How To Cut A Short Layered Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to try something new with your hair? A short layered haircut might just be the change you need! Not only does it add volume and texture to your hair, but it's also easy to maintain. In this post, we'll walk you through the steps to cut a short layered haircut. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced stylist, this guide will help you achieve the perfect cut. Let's get started!

How to Cut a Short Layered Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle

Once you have all your tools, it's time to move on to the next step.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

The first thing you need to do is section your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, use hair clips to separate the top section from the bottom section. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair in layers.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting! Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the ends at an angle. This will create a layered effect. Repeat this process for all the sections in the bottom layer.

Once you're done with the bottom layer, unclip the top layer and repeat the process. Make sure you cut the top layer slightly shorter than the bottom layer to create more volume.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

After you've cut all the layers, it's time to blend them. Use the scissors to cut any harsh lines or uneven edges. You want your layers to blend seamlessly into each other.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with the length and layers of your haircut, it's time to style your hair. You can blow-dry your hair for a sleek look or let it air-dry for a more natural look. You can also add some styling products to give your hair more texture and volume.

Tips for Cutting a Short Layered Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short layered haircut:

  • Start with dry hair. It's easier to cut hair when it's dry, as wet hair tends to shrink.
  • Take your time. Cutting hair is a slow process, especially if you're doing it for the first time.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's always better to start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress. It's important to see how your haircut looks from different angles.
  • Be patient. It might take a few tries to get the perfect short layered haircut.

FAQ about Cutting a Short Layered Haircut

Q: Can I cut my own short layered haircut?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure you have all the necessary tools and take your time.

Q: How often do I need to get a short layered haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers.

Q: Can I style my short layered haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can! A short layered haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can straighten it, curl it, or add some waves.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short layered haircut looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your layers.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Conclusion

Cutting a short layered haircut might seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's easy to do. Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and blend your layers seamlessly. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect short layered haircut in no time!
