What Is A Taper Haircut Look Like

Written by Luffy Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A taper haircut is a popular men's haircut that features hair that gradually shortens towards the neck and ears. The hair on top of the head is longer than the hair on the sides and back, creating a tapered effect. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types.

Description of a Taper Haircut

A taper haircut is a classic men's haircut that has been popular for decades. It is a low-maintenance haircut that looks great on most men. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, gradually getting longer towards the top of the head. The hair on top is left longer than the hair on the sides and back, creating a tapered effect. The hair is typically cut with scissors and clippers to create a clean, smooth look.

How to Get a Taper Haircut

If you're interested in getting a taper haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist. Look for someone who has experience cutting men's hair and can give you the look you want. Bring in a picture of the style you're interested in to give the stylist a clear idea of what you want.

Once you're in the chair, the stylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back with clippers. They will then use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. The stylist will then blend the hair so there are no harsh lines or edges. The final result should be a smooth, tapered look.

Step by Step Guide to Taper Haircut

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting a taper haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier for the stylist to cut your hair and will give you a better result.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Decide on the style you want before you go to the barber. Bring in a picture or show the stylist a photo on your phone to give them a clear idea of what you want.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

The stylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back with clippers. They will use different guard sizes to create a gradual taper.

Step 4: Cut the Top

The stylist will then use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. They will blend the hair so there are no harsh lines or edges.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style your hair using your favorite products. You can use gel, pomade, or wax to create a variety of looks.

Tips for Maintaining a Taper Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your taper haircut:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair and remove any tangles.
  • Consider using a hair dryer to help you style your hair.

FAQs

1. How long does a taper haircut last?

A taper haircut can last anywhere from two to four weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. It's a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling, making it a popular choice for busy men.

2. Can I style a taper haircut in different ways?

Yes, a taper haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can use gel, pomade, or wax to create different looks, depending on your mood and the occasion.

Hair Care for Taper Haircut

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dull. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.
