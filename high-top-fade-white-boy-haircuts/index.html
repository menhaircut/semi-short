High Top Fade White Boy Haircuts A Guide To The Trendy Hairstyle

Written by Petter Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and edgy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the high top fade white boy haircut. This trendy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's a versatile look that can be customized to fit your personal style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the high top fade white boy haircut, from how to get it to how to care for it.

Description

The high top fade white boy haircut is a modern take on the classic high top fade. It's characterized by a short buzz cut on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that's styled into a flat top. This haircut is often paired with a shaved or faded design on the sides for added flair. It's a bold and daring look that's perfect for guys who want to make a statement with their hair.

How to Get a High Top Fade White Boy Haircut

If you're interested in getting a high top fade white boy haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber who's experienced with this style. Bring in a picture of the look you're going for, and be sure to communicate your preferences clearly. The barber will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head short, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. They may also add in a shaved or faded design if you desire.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a high top fade white boy haircut:

  1. Find a skilled barber who's experienced with this style
  2. Show them a picture of the look you're going for
  3. Communicate your preferences clearly
  4. The barber will cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short
  5. They'll gradually blend it into the longer hair on top
  6. If desired, they'll add in a shaved or faded design

Tips for Maintaining a High Top Fade White Boy Haircut

Once you've got your high top fade white boy haircut, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut
  • Use a good quality styling product to keep the hair on top in place
  • Wash your hair regularly, but don't overdo it as this can dry out the hair and scalp
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners as these can damage the hair
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and healthy

FAQ

What face shape is best for a high top fade white boy haircut?

The high top fade white boy haircut can work well for a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for guys with square or oval faces. It can help to balance out a strong jawline and add some height to the face.

What hair types work best for a high top fade white boy haircut?

The high top fade white boy haircut can work well for a variety of hair types, but it's best suited for guys with straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have very curly or kinky hair, it may be more difficult to achieve this look.

How often do I need to get a trim?

To keep your high top fade white boy haircut looking sharp, it's best to get a trim every 3-4 weeks. This will help to maintain the shape of the haircut and prevent the hair on top from becoming too long and difficult to style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a high top fade white boy haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners as these can damage the hair
  • Use a good quality styling product to keep the hair on top in place
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a high top fade white boy haircut with confidence. Whether you're looking for a bold and daring style or a more subtle variation, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
