Does Short Hair Suit An Oval Face

Written by William Nov 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair is a bold and stylish choice for any woman, but many women with oval faces wonder whether short hair suits their face shape. If you're considering a short haircut and you have an oval face, there are several things to consider to determine whether it will be a good fit. In this article, we'll explore the question of whether short hair suits an oval face, as well as tips for styling and caring for short hair.

Description

Oval faces are considered one of the most versatile face shapes, as they can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, when it comes to short hair, there are certain factors to consider. One of the most important considerations is the length of the hair. Depending on your face shape, some short hairstyles may work better than others. Additionally, the texture and thickness of your hair can also impact how well a short hairstyle suits your oval face.

How to Determine Whether Short Hair Suits an Oval Face

The best way to determine whether short hair suits your oval face is to consider the proportions of your face. Oval faces are longer than they are wide, with a forehead that is slightly wider than the jawline. If you have an oval face, you can generally pull off a wide range of hairstyles, including short haircuts.

However, when it comes to short haircuts, there are a few things to keep in mind. One of the most important factors is the length of the haircut. Short haircuts that are too short can make an oval face appear rounder, which may not be the most flattering look. On the other hand, a short haircut that is slightly longer can help to elongate the face and highlight its natural shape.

Another factor to consider is the texture and thickness of your hair. If you have thin or fine hair, a short haircut may not be the best choice, as it can make your hair appear even thinner. However, if you have thick hair, a short haircut can help to lighten the weight of your hair and make it more manageable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oval Faces

If you've decided that short hair is the right choice for your oval face, there are several steps you can take to ensure that your haircut looks its best:

  1. Choose the right length: As we mentioned earlier, the length of your haircut is one of the most important factors to consider. Work with your stylist to find a length that flatters your face shape.
  2. Consider the texture of your hair: Depending on the texture of your hair, certain haircuts may work better than others. For example, if you have curly hair, a short haircut may require more maintenance to keep it looking its best.
  3. Choose a style that suits your personality: Short hair can be edgy, sophisticated, or playful. Think about your personal style and choose a haircut that reflects it.
  4. Maintain your haircut: Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Oval Faces

Once you've chosen a short haircut, there are several tips you can follow to keep it looking its best:

  • Invest in quality hair products: Short haircuts require products that can help to add volume, texture, and hold to your hair. Look for high-quality products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Use heat styling tools sparingly: Heat can damage your hair, so use heat styling tools sparingly. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Experiment with different styles: Short haircuts are versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find one that works for you.

FAQs About Short Hair and Oval Faces

Q: Can oval faces pull off pixie cuts?

A: Yes, oval faces can generally pull off pixie cuts. However, it's important to consider the length of the haircut and the texture of your hair when choosing a pixie cut.

Q: What is the best short haircut for oval faces?

A: The best short haircut for oval faces depends on your personal style and the texture of your hair. However, a bob or lob (long bob) is a classic choice that can flatter many oval faces.

Q: Does short hair make an oval face look rounder?

A: Short haircuts that are too short can make an oval face appear rounder. However, if the haircut is slightly longer, it can help to elongate the face and highlight its natural shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires different care than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days: Short hair can get oily more quickly than longer hair, so it's important to wash it regularly.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo: Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, so look for a sulfate-free shampoo that is gentle on your hair.
  • Condition your hair regularly: Short hair can be prone to dryness, so use a conditioner to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair: Heat styling tools and products can damage your hair, so use them sparingly.

In conclusion, short hair can suit an oval face, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your face shape and hair texture. Follow these tips for styling and caring for short hair to keep it looking its best.
