How To Make Curtain Bangs Look Good A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curtain bangs are having a moment, and for good reason. They're a low-maintenance way to add a little something special to your hairstyle. But, it can be tough to make curtain bangs look good. Here's how to do it.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that frames your face. They're typically parted down the middle, which creates a "curtain" effect. Curtain bangs can work on most hair types, but they're especially flattering on those with longer hair.

How to Make Curtain Bangs Look Good

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your curtain bangs, make sure your hair is clean. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair and your bangs straight. Use a round brush to add volume and a bit of curl to the ends of your hair. Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Part your hair down the middle and separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use clips to keep your hair in place while you work on your bangs.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs

Use sharp scissors to trim your bangs. Start by cutting a little bit at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut. Cut your bangs straight across, and then use the point of your scissors to create a little bit of texture.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Use a comb to style your bangs. If you want a little more volume, use a round brush to curl the ends of your bangs. You can also use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you make your curtain bangs look good:

  • Start with longer bangs â you can always cut more off later
  • Use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your bangs
  • Be patient â it can take a little while to get your bangs looking just right

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

What face shapes do curtain bangs work best on?

Curtain bangs work well on most face shapes, but they're especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially if you're styling your hair frequently. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips, you can make your curtain bangs look good and keep your hair healthy.
