What Is A Good Holding Hairspray

Written by Petter Aug 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Looking for a hairspray that will keep your hair in place all day long? With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your hair type and styling needs. In this article, we'll break down what a good holding hairspray is, what to look for when shopping for one, and our top picks for the best hairsprays out there.

What is a Good Holding Hairspray?

A good holding hairspray is a styling product that is designed to keep your hair in place and maintain its shape throughout the day. It provides a firm hold without making your hair feel stiff or sticky. A good hairspray should also be easy to brush out and not leave any residue or flakes behind.

What to Look for When Shopping for a Hairspray

When shopping for a hairspray, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Hold strength: Make sure to choose a hairspray with the right hold strength for your hair type and styling needs.
  • Ingredients: Look for hairsprays that are free of harsh chemicals and contain nourishing ingredients like vitamins and antioxidants.
  • Finish: Decide whether you want a hairspray that provides a matte or shiny finish.
  • Scent: Choose a hairspray with a scent that you enjoy.

How to Use Hairspray

Here are some tips on how to use hairspray:

  1. Shake the can well before use.
  2. Hold the can about 8-10 inches away from your hair.
  3. Spray the hairspray evenly over your hair, focusing on the areas that need the most hold.
  4. Let the hairspray dry for a few seconds before touching your hair.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Hairspray

Here are some additional tips for using hairspray:

  • Use hairspray sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Apply hairspray in layers, letting each layer dry before applying more.
  • For a more natural look, spray the hairspray onto your hands and then apply it to your hair using your fingers.
  • Don't use hairspray on wet hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Brush your hair before applying hairspray to remove any tangles or knots.

Top Picks for the Best Hairsprays

Here are our top picks for the best hairsprays:

  1. L'Oreal Elnett Satin Hairspray - This iconic hairspray provides a strong hold without making your hair feel stiff or crunchy. It also brushes out easily and doesn't leave any residue behind.
  2. Kenra Volume Spray 25 - This hairspray provides a firm hold that lasts up to 24 hours. It also contains a humidity-resistant formula that helps to prevent frizz and flyaways.
  3. Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray - This hairspray provides a flexible hold that allows you to brush and style your hair without it feeling sticky or stiff. It also contains argan oil, which helps to nourish and protect your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairspray:

  • Q: Can hairspray damage your hair?
  • A: Overusing hairspray can cause damage and breakage, but using it in moderation and following the instructions on the can should not cause any harm to your hair.
  • Q: How do I remove hairspray from my hair?
  • A: To remove hairspray from your hair, simply brush it out or wash your hair with shampoo.
  • Q: Can I use hairspray on curly hair?
  • A: Yes, you can use hairspray on curly hair to help define and hold your curls in place. Just make sure to choose a hairspray with a flexible hold to avoid making your curls feel stiff or crunchy.

Hair Care

Using a good holding hairspray is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. To keep your hair looking and feeling its best, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote hair health, such as biotin, vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With the right hairspray and a good hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle that stays in place all day long.
