Description

Blow drying hair is a common practice among women, but is it weird for guys to do the same? The answer is no! Men can also benefit from blow drying their hair, whether they have short or long hair. Blow drying can add volume, help with styling, and make your hair look healthier. In this blog post, we will discuss how to blow dry hair, step by step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Blow Dry Hair

Before we get into the steps, it's important to mention that using a heat protectant spray is crucial to prevent damage to your hair. You can also use a volumizing mousse to add extra volume to your hair.

Step 1: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to gently dry your hair. Don't rub your hair vigorously with the towel as it can cause damage.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray and Volumizing Mousse

Spray the heat protectant spray on your hair and apply the volumizing mousse. Distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair and ensure that all sections of your hair are dried properly.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair using a medium heat setting. Hold the blow dryer at least 6 inches away from your hair and move it around to dry your hair evenly. Use a round brush to add volume and style your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Cold Air

Once your hair is almost dry, switch to the cool air setting to lock in the style and add shine to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Towel dry your hair
  2. Apply heat protectant spray and volumizing mousse
  3. Section your hair
  4. Start blow drying your hair
  5. Finish with cold air

Tips for Blow Drying Hair

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Section your hair to ensure that all sections are dried properly
  • Use a round brush to add volume and style your hair
  • Finish with cold air to lock in the style and add shine to your hair

FAQs

Q: Is it weird for guys to blow dry their hair?

A: No, it's not weird for guys to blow dry their hair. Blow drying can add volume, help with styling, and make your hair look healthier.

Q: Can blow drying damage my hair?

A: Yes, blow drying can damage your hair if you don't use a heat protectant spray or use too much heat. It's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Hair Care

Blow drying your hair can be a great way to add volume and style your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Use a heat protectant spray, avoid using high heat settings, and use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, blow drying your hair is not weird for guys. It can be a great way to add volume, style your hair, and make it look healthier. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this blog post to blow dry your hair like a pro.
