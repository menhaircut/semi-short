How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a simple haircut? With a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can easily cut the top of your hair yourself at home. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have more control over your hairstyle. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut the top of men's hair yourself.

Description

Cutting the top of your hair can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're new to it. However, with the right technique and a steady hand, you can achieve a great-looking haircut in no time. This guide will cover everything you need to know, from what tools you'll need to the best techniques for cutting the top of men's hair.

How to Cut Top of Men's Hair Yourself: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you get started, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • A pair of hair clippers
  • A comb
  • A pair of scissors
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cape to catch hair clippings

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your desired hairstyle. Comb your hair forward and down so that it's easier to cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your hair clippers, start cutting your hair from the back to the front. Use a higher guard setting for the top of your head and a lower setting for the sides and back. Be sure to keep the clippers level and move them in an upward motion against the direction of hair growth.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've finished cutting the top of your hair, switch to a lower guard setting and blend the sides and top. Use your comb to guide the clippers and make sure that the transition is smooth and even.

Step 6: Trim the Top with Scissors

Next, use your scissors to trim any longer hairs on the top of your head. Take small sections of hair and hold them between your fingers, then trim the ends with your scissors.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any necessary touch-ups to ensure that your hair is even and well-blended.

Tips for Cutting Top of Men's Hair Yourself

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut the top of your hair:

  • Start with a higher guard setting and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use your comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to go slow and get it right than rush and make mistakes.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time with your scissors to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles and make any necessary touch-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut the top of my hair myself?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can easily cut the top of your hair yourself.

What tools do I need to cut the top of my hair?

You'll need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, scissors, a mirror, and a towel or cape to catch hair clippings.

What guard setting should I use for the top of my hair?

Start with a higher guard setting and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.

Hair Care After Cutting Top of Men's Hair Yourself

After cutting the top of your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut the top of your hair yourself, you can save money and have more control over your hairstyle. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve a great-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
