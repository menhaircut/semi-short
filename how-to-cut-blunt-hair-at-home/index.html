How To Cut Blunt Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to give your hair a fresh new look, cutting it blunt is a great option. Not only is it trendy, but it's also a low-maintenance cut that suits almost everyone. The good news is that you don't have to go to a salon to get this look. You can easily cut your hair blunt at home with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to cut blunt hair at home in a step-by-step guide.

How to Cut Blunt Hair at Home

Cutting blunt hair at home is easier than you might think. All you need is a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut, and it will also be easier to see the length of your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts. Two at the front and two at the back. This will help you get an even cut and make the process easier.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the ends of your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all the hair at the back.

Next, move to the sides of your head. Take a small section of hair from one side and comb it straight down. Hold the ends of your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you've cut your hair, check for evenness. Comb your hair straight down and check if the length is even. If you notice any unevenness, trim the longer strands until you achieve an even length.

Tips for Cutting Blunt Hair at Home

Cutting blunt hair at home can be a bit tricky, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve a perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and frayed edges.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid cutting it too short.
  • Check for evenness regularly to avoid any mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting blunt hair:

Q: Can I cut my hair blunt if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can cut your curly hair blunt. However, it's recommended that you cut it when it's dry, so you can see the length of your hair better.

Q: How often should I cut my blunt hair?

A: It's recommended that you cut your blunt hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care after Cutting Blunt Hair at Home

After you've cut your hair blunt, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to cut blunt hair at home, you can give yourself a fresh new look without spending a lot of money. Just remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and check for evenness regularly. Happy cutting!
