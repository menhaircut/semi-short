Description

Enlarging a commercial sewing pattern is a necessary step for those who want to create clothing that fits them perfectly. Many commercial sewing patterns come in a range of sizes, but not everyone will fit into those sizes. By enlarging a sewing pattern, you can make sure that your clothes fit comfortably and look great.

How to Enlarge a Commercial Sewing Pattern

Enlarging a commercial sewing pattern is not as difficult as you might think. All you need is some basic sewing knowledge and a few tools. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose the pattern you want to enlarge. Make sure it's a pattern that you love and that you want to wear.
  2. Measure yourself carefully. You will need to know your bust, waist, and hip measurements. Write these measurements down on a piece of paper.
  3. Look at the back of the pattern envelope. There should be a measurement chart that tells you which size to use based on your measurements. Choose the size that is closest to your measurements.
  4. Take the pattern pieces out of the envelope and lay them on a flat surface. You will need to make copies of these pieces so that you can enlarge them.
  5. Decide how much you want to enlarge the pattern. You may want to enlarge it by one size, two sizes, or more. Make a note of how much you want to enlarge the pattern by.
  6. Use a photocopier to enlarge the pattern pieces. Enlarge them by the amount you decided on in step 5. Make sure that you enlarge all of the pieces by the same amount.
  7. Cut out the enlarged pattern pieces. Double-check that you have cut them all out correctly.
  8. Follow the pattern instructions as you would for a regular-sized pattern. The only difference is that you will be using the enlarged pattern pieces.
  9. Try on your garment as you sew it. This will help you make sure that it fits correctly.
  10. Enjoy your new clothing!

Tips for Enlarging a Commercial Sewing Pattern

Here are some tips to keep in mind when enlarging a commercial sewing pattern:

  • Make sure that you choose a pattern that has a simple design. The more complicated the design, the more difficult it will be to enlarge.
  • If you are unsure about how much to enlarge the pattern by, start with a small amount. You can always make it larger later.
  • Use a ruler or measuring tape to make sure that you are enlarging the pattern pieces by the correct amount.
  • When you are cutting out the pattern pieces, use a sharp pair of scissors. This will help you cut out the pieces accurately.
  • Always try on your garment as you are sewing it. This will help you make any necessary adjustments.

FAQ About Enlarging a Commercial Sewing Pattern

Here are some frequently asked questions about enlarging a commercial sewing pattern:

  • Can I enlarge a pattern by more than one size? Yes, you can enlarge a pattern by as much as you want. Just make sure that you enlarge all of the pieces by the same amount.
  • Do I need special tools to enlarge a pattern? No, you don't need any special tools. You just need a photocopier, a ruler or measuring tape, and a sharp pair of scissors.
  • What if I make a mistake when enlarging the pattern? If you make a mistake, don't worry. Just go back and start again. It's better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.

Hair Care While Enlarging a Commercial Sewing Pattern

While you are enlarging a commercial sewing pattern, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wear a hair tie or headband to keep your hair out of your face.
  • Wash your hair before you start sewing. This will help prevent any stray hairs from getting on your fabric.
  • Brush your hair before you start sewing. This will also help prevent any stray hairs from getting on your fabric.

Enlarging a commercial sewing pattern is a great way to ensure that your clothes fit you perfectly. By following these simple steps, you can create clothing that looks great and feels comfortable. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and enjoy the process!
