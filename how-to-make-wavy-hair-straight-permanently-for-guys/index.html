How To Make Wavy Hair Straight Permanently For Guys

Written by Robby Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing or a curse for guys. While some people love their natural waves, others may find them difficult to manage. If you're someone who wants to make your wavy hair straight permanently, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve straight hair for guys that lasts.

Description

Wavy hair can be hard to control, and it can be especially frustrating when you want a sleek, straight look. While there are many temporary options for straightening hair, like flat irons and chemical treatments, they don't offer a permanent solution. This article will cover a permanent solution for guys who want to transform their naturally wavy hair into straight locks.

How to

The best way to make wavy hair straight permanently for guys is through a chemical process called hair relaxing. This process uses chemicals to break down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into a straight style. It's important to note that this is a permanent process, so you won't be able to go back to your natural wavy hair once it's relaxed.

Step by Step

Here's how to make wavy hair straight permanently for guys through hair relaxing:

  1. Find a reputable salon that specializes in hair relaxing.
  2. Consult with a stylist to determine if your hair is suitable for hair relaxing.
  3. Wash your hair thoroughly before the appointment, but don't apply any products.
  4. The stylist will apply a hair relaxing solution to your hair, which will break down the bonds and make it easier to straighten.
  5. After the solution has been applied, the stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly.
  6. Your hair will then be blow-dried and flat ironed to create a sleek, straight look.
  7. The stylist will apply a neutralizer to your hair to restore the bonds and lock in the straight shape.
  8. Your hair will be rinsed again and styled as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting your hair relaxed:

  • Make sure to find a stylist who is experienced in hair relaxing to ensure the best results.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after the treatment to allow the bonds to fully set.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the straight style.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and undo the relaxing treatment.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wavy hair straight permanently for guys:

Is hair relaxing safe?

When done by a professional, hair relaxing is generally considered safe. However, it's important to note that the chemicals used can be harsh, so it's important to find a reputable salon and stylist.

How long does hair relaxing last?

Hair relaxing is a permanent process, so your hair will remain straight until it grows out. However, you will need to get touch-ups as your hair grows to maintain the straight style.

Can I get my hair relaxed if it's been previously chemically treated?

It depends on the type of treatment you've had. Your stylist will be able to determine if your hair is suitable for hair relaxing, even if it's been previously treated.

Hair Care

After getting your hair relaxed, it's important to take good care of it to maintain the straight style. Here are some tips for caring for relaxed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the straight style and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can cause damage and undo the relaxing treatment.
  • Get regular touch-ups as your hair grows to maintain the straight style.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking sleek and straight for months to come.
