Male Blue Eyes Short Blonde Hair Anime A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you a fan of male characters in anime with blue eyes and short blonde hair? You're not alone! This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, not only in anime but also in real life. In this article, we'll dive into the world of male blue eyes short blonde hair anime, providing you with tips, steps, and hair care advice to achieve this look. So, let's get started!

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks

Description

Male characters in anime with blue eyes and short blonde hair are often depicted as confident, outgoing, and adventurous. This hairstyle has become iconic in the anime world, with characters like Naruto, Ichigo, and Yato rocking this look. It's no wonder that many anime fans have been inspired to try it out for themselves.

To achieve this look, you will need to have short blonde hair and blue contact lenses if you don't already have blue eyes. While this hairstyle may seem simple, there are a few key elements that you should keep in mind to make it look authentic.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the male blue eyes short blonde hair anime look, you will need to follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by dyeing your hair blonde. If your natural hair color is not blonde, you will need to bleach it first to achieve the desired shade.
  2. Once your hair is blonde, cut it short. You can choose to have a fade, undercut, or buzz cut, depending on your preference.
  3. If you don't have blue eyes, wear blue contact lenses to complete the look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the male blue eyes short blonde hair anime look:

  • Use a blue-tinted shampoo to enhance the color of your blonde hair and make it look brighter.
  • Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the short length and shape.
  • Use hair gel or wax to style your hair into the desired look, whether it's spiky, messy, or slicked back.
  • Accessorize with a headband or bandana to add an extra touch of style to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the male blue eyes short blonde hair anime look:

Q: Can I achieve this look if I have naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve this look if you have naturally curly hair. However, you may need to straighten your hair first before dyeing it blonde to make the process easier.

Q: Do I need to wear blue contact lenses if I already have blue eyes?

A: No, if you already have blue eyes, you don't need to wear contact lenses.

Q: How often do I need to bleach my hair to maintain the blonde color?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you wash it. Generally, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain the blonde color.

Hair Care Advice

To maintain the male blue eyes short blonde hair anime look, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out and becoming brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the male blue eyes short blonde hair anime look while keeping your hair healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and embrace your inner anime character!
