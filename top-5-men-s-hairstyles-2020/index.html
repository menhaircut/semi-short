Top 5 Men s Hairstyles 2020

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try in 2020, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the top 5 men's hairstyles of 2020. From the classic buzz cut to the trendy disconnected undercut, we have something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Description

The top 5 men's hairstyles of 2020 have been chosen based on their popularity, versatility, and overall style. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to work or heading out for a night on the town. They're also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal taste.

How To

To achieve these hairstyles, you'll need to visit your local barber or stylist. They will be able to give you a professional cut that is tailored to your hair type and face shape. You can also bring in a photo of the style you want to help guide the process.

Step by Step

1. Buzz Cut - This classic style is achieved by using clippers to cut your hair very short all over. It's low maintenance and perfect for those who want a simple, no-fuss look.

2. French Crop - This style features short hair on the sides and back with a longer, textured top. It's a great option for those with straight or wavy hair.

3. Side Part - This timeless style features a clean side part with short hair on the sides and a longer, slicked-back top. It's perfect for a professional look.

4. Quiff - This style features short hair on the sides and a longer, voluminous top that is styled upwards and back. It's a trendy option for those who want to stand out.

5. Disconnected Undercut - This style features short hair on the sides and back with a longer, disconnected top that is slicked back. It's a bold choice for those who want to make a statement.

Tips

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh, be sure to visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. You should also invest in quality hair products, such as a good shampoo, conditioner, and styling product. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

FAQ

Q: Are these hairstyles suitable for all hair types?
A: Yes, these hairstyles can be adapted to suit any hair type. Your barber or stylist will be able to recommend the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

Q: How can I style these haircuts?
A: These haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal taste. You can use a pomade or wax to add texture, or a gel to create a slicked-back look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to wash it regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally and use a comb to style it.

Now that you know the top 5 men's hairstyles of 2020, it's time to book your appointment with your barber or stylist. Whether you go for a classic buzz cut or a trendy disconnected undercut, you're sure to turn heads with your new look.
