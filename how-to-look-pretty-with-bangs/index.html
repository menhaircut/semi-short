How To Look Pretty With Bangs Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to change up your hairstyle, adding bangs can do just that. Not only can bangs frame your face and enhance your features, but they can also make you look pretty and stylish. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to look pretty with bangs, including how to style and care for them.

How to Get the Perfect Bangs

Before we dive into the styling tips, let's talk about how to get the perfect bangs. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to visit a salon and have a professional do it. However, if you want to cut your own bangs, here are some tips:

Step 1: Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

Not all bangs are created equal, and it's important to choose the right style for your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, go for longer, side-swept bangs. If you have a heart-shaped face, go for wispy, layered bangs.

Step 2: Use Sharp Scissors

Don't use regular household scissors to cut your bangs. Use sharp, professional scissors to avoid any unevenness or damage to your hair.

Step 3: Start Small

It's always better to start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back on!

Step 4: Cut at an Angle

To avoid blunt lines, cut your bangs at a slight angle. This will give them a natural, effortless look.

Tips for Styling Your Bangs

Now that you have the perfect bangs, it's time to style them! Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Blow Dryer

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your bangs. This will give them volume and shape.

Tip 2: Use a Flat Iron

If you want a sleeker, more polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.

Tip 3: Experiment with Products

There are many products on the market that can help you style your bangs, such as hairspray, mousse, and gel. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Tip 4: Pin Them Back

If you're having a bad hair day or just want to switch things up, try pinning your bangs back with bobby pins or hair clips.

FAQ

How Often Should I Trim My Bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

What Should I Do if I Don't Like How My Bangs Turned Out?

If you're not happy with how your bangs turned out, don't panic! You can always go to a salon and have them fixed. In the meantime, try pinning them back or styling them differently.

Can I Wear Bangs with Curly Hair?

Absolutely! Bangs can look great with curly hair. Just make sure to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

It's important to take care of your bangs to keep them looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Them Regularly

Wash your bangs regularly to avoid any oil buildup or greasiness. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause heat damage and split ends.

Tip 3: Trim Them Regularly

As mentioned earlier, trim your bangs regularly to avoid any split ends or damage.

Tip 4: Protect Them from the Sun

Just like the rest of your hair, your bangs can be damaged by the sun's rays. Protect them by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your bangs and look pretty and stylish. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
