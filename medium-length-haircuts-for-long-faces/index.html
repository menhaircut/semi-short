Medium Length Haircuts for Long Faces - A Complete Guide

If you have a long face, you know that finding the right haircut can be a real challenge. You want something that will flatter your face shape without making it look even longer. That's where medium length haircuts come in. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about medium length haircuts for long faces.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Perfect Medium Length Haircut for Long Faces

Description

Medium length haircuts are perfect for long faces because they create the illusion of width. They also provide enough length to create volume and movement, which is important for balancing out a long face. There are many different types of medium length haircuts to choose from, including layered cuts, bob cuts, and shag cuts.

How to Get the Perfect Medium Length Haircut for Long Faces

The first step in getting a medium length haircut for a long face is to find a stylist who understands your face shape and can recommend a cut that will flatter it. You'll want to choose a cut that adds volume to the sides of your face and avoids lengthening it further. A layered cut or a bob with layers will typically work well.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Haircuts for Long Faces

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

2. Towel-dry your hair until it is damp but not dripping.

3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.

4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement.

6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Haircut for Long Faces

1. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

3. Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage.

4. Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a medium length haircut for a long face?

A: Yes, you can. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style to keep your hair out of your face without sacrificing style.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go several days between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair every day.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

2. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

4. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect medium length haircut for your long face. Whether you opt for a layered cut, a bob, or a shag, you'll love the way your new style flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.
