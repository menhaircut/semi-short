Haircut For Square Face Indian Girl Flattering Styles And Tips

Written by Aprilia Oct 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face shape, finding the right haircut can be a daunting task. With a broad forehead, strong jawline, and angular features, it's important to choose a style that softens your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we will share some of the best haircuts for square face Indian girls, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

  • Best Haircuts for Square Face Indian Girl

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the haircuts, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheeks and the width and length of the face are almost equal.
  • Square: Prominent jaw and forehead, with the width of the face being almost equal to the length.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow jawline.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones.

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that softens your jawline and forehead while adding volume to the top of your head.

Best Haircuts for Square Face Indian Girl

Now that you know your face shape, let's explore some of the best haircuts for square face Indian girls:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for square face Indian girls as they soften the jawline and add movement to the hair. Ask your stylist to cut long layers that start below your chin and frame your face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for square face Indian girls. They draw attention to the eyes and soften the forehead. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the crown of your head and sweeping down to the side.

3. Bob with Soft Layers

A bob with soft layers is a chic and stylish option for square face Indian girls. The layers add movement and volume to the hair, while the length of the bob softens the jawline. Ask your stylist to cut soft layers that start at the chin and frame your face.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for square face Indian girls. The short length draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while the volume at the top of your head softens your forehead. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a textured pixie cut with volume at the top.

Styling Tips for Square Face Indian Girl

Now that you have the perfect haircut, here are some styling tips to enhance your features:

  • Add volume to the top of your head: Adding volume to the top of your head will soften your forehead and balance out your features. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  • Experiment with different parts: Changing your part can help to balance out your features. Try a deep side part or a middle part to see what works best for you.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make your features appear more angular. Stick to soft layers or textured cuts to soften your features.
  • Accessorize: Accessories like headbands, scarves, and earrings can draw attention to your best features and distract from areas you're not as confident about.

FAQs

1. Can square face Indian girls wear short hair?

Absolutely! Square face Indian girls can wear short hair, but it's important to choose a style that adds volume at the top of your head and softens your features.

2. Should I avoid straight hair if I have a square face?

Straight hair can work for square face Indian girls, but it's important to add volume at the top of your head and soften your features with layers or a textured cut.

3. What hair colors work best for square face Indian girls?

Warm, rich colors like caramel, honey, and chestnut can complement square face Indian girls. Avoid harsh, cool tones like platinum blonde or jet black that can make your features appear more angular.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Indian Girl

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day: Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Try washing your hair every other day or using a dry shampoo in between washes.
  • Use a heat protectant: If you use heat styling tools like a flat iron or curling wand, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.
  • Deep condition once a week: Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and hydrate your hair. Try using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these haircuts, styling tips, and hair care tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
