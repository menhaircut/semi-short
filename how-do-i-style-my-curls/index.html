How Do I Style My Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to style your curls! Whether you have tight coils or loose waves, we've got you covered with tips and tricks for achieving your best curls yet. Ready to get started?

Table of Contents:

  1. Hair Care
  2. How To Style Your Curls
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  5. FAQs

Hair Care:

Before we dive into styling, it's important to note that the foundation of great curls is healthy hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curls in tip-top shape:

1. Use a gentle shampoo:

Curls tend to be dryer than straight hair, so it's important to use a shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo, as sulfates can be harsh on curls.

2. Deep condition regularly:

Curls need extra moisture, so be sure to deep condition your hair once a week. You can use a store-bought deep conditioner or make your own with ingredients like honey, olive oil, and avocado.

3. Avoid heat styling:

While it can be tempting to straighten or blow-dry your curls, heat styling can cause damage and frizz. If you must use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

How To Style Your Curls:

Now that your hair is healthy and hydrated, it's time to style your curls. Here are a few different techniques to try:

1. Wash and go:

If you're short on time, the wash and go method is a great option. After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner and gel or mousse, then scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser.

2. Twist out:

To achieve a defined twist out, divide your hair into sections and apply a twisting cream or butter. Twist each section tightly, then let your hair air dry or use a hooded dryer. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists and fluff your hair to create volume.

3. Braid out:

Similar to the twist out, the braid out involves braiding your hair while wet or damp and letting it air dry. Once your hair is dry, unravel the braids and fluff your hair to create volume.

Step by Step Guide:

Ready to style your curls? Follow these step-by-step instructions:

1. Start with clean, hydrated hair:

Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and deep conditioner. Be sure to thoroughly rinse out the conditioner.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner:

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide extra moisture and help detangle your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to work the conditioner through your hair.

3. Choose your styling product:

Depending on the look you're going for, choose a styling product like gel, mousse, or cream. Apply a small amount to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

4. Scrunch your hair:

Use your hands to scrunch your hair, encouraging curl formation. Be gentle and avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

5. Dry your hair:

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting to avoid damage.

6. Fluff your hair:

Once your hair is completely dry, use your fingers to fluff your hair and create volume. Avoid using a brush, as this can disrupt your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls:

Now that your curls are styled, here are a few tips for maintaining them:

1. Sleep on a satin pillowcase:

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and frizz, so opt for a satin or silk pillowcase instead.

2. Refresh your curls:

If your curls are looking a little flat, you can refresh them by spritzing your hair with water and applying a bit of leave-in conditioner or styling product.

3. Protect your hair:

When swimming or in extreme weather conditions, protect your curls with a swim cap or hat.

FAQs:

Q: How often should I wash my curls?

A: This varies depending on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others only wash once a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I use a hairbrush on my curls?

A: It's best to avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

And there you have it – our ultimate guide on how to style your curls! With a little patience and practice, you'll be rocking your best curls yet.
