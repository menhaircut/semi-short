Can You Get Locs With Relaxed Hair

Written by Frank Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have relaxed hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to get locs. The answer is yes, it is possible to get locs with relaxed hair. However, there are a few things that you need to consider before you make the decision to get locs. In this blog post, we will explore the process of getting locs with relaxed hair, including how to do it, tips, and hair care.

Description

Locs are a popular hairstyle that involves twisting or matting hair into rope-like strands. The process of getting locs with relaxed hair involves allowing your hair to grow out and then twisting it into locs. This process can take several months to a year, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

How to Get Locs with Relaxed Hair

The first step in getting locs with relaxed hair is to stop relaxing your hair. This means that you will need to grow out your hair until it is long enough to twist into locs. You should also consider trimming your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Once your hair is long enough, you can begin the process of twisting your hair into locs. There are several methods that you can use to twist your hair, including palm rolling, two-strand twisting, and interlocking. It's important to choose a method that works best for your hair type and texture.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting locs with relaxed hair:

  1. Stop relaxing your hair and allow it to grow out.
  2. Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  3. Choose a twisting method that works best for your hair type and texture.
  4. Twist small sections of hair at a time, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Secure the ends of your twists with rubber bands or clips.
  6. Repeat the twisting process every few weeks to maintain your locs.

Tips for Getting Locs with Relaxed Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get locs with relaxed hair:

  • Be patient. Getting locs with relaxed hair can take several months to a year.
  • Choose a twisting method that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting locs with relaxed hair:

Can you get locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, it is possible to get locs with relaxed hair. However, you will need to stop relaxing your hair and allow it to grow out before you can twist it into locs.

How long does it take to get locs with relaxed hair?

The process of getting locs with relaxed hair can take several months to a year, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

What is the best method for twisting hair into locs?

There are several methods that you can use to twist your hair into locs, including palm rolling, two-strand twisting, and interlocking. It's important to choose a method that works best for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Locs with Relaxed Hair

Once you have locs with relaxed hair, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your locs regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, getting locs with relaxed hair is possible, but it requires patience, dedication, and proper hair care. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this blog post, you can achieve beautiful, healthy locs with relaxed hair.
