Long Curtain Bangs For Straight Hair And Round Face

Written by Christine Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long curtain bangs are a popular hair trend that can work well for those with straight hair and a round face shape. This style features longer bangs that frame the face and can be parted in the middle or swept to the side. In this blog post, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve long curtain bangs, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance. Let's get started!

How to Achieve Long Curtain Bangs

The first step in achieving long curtain bangs is to start with clean, dry hair. Use a hairbrush to part your hair down the middle or to the side, depending on your preference. Then, take a section of hair at the front of your head and comb it forward. This will be the section you'll cut to create your bangs.

Next, use a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors to cut your bangs to the desired length. It's important to cut slowly and carefully, making small snips rather than large cuts. This will help you achieve a more precise and natural-looking result. If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider visiting a professional stylist to help you achieve the look you want.

Once you've cut your bangs, you can style them by blow-drying them with a round brush. This will help give them volume and shape. You can also use a flat iron to smooth them out and create a sleeker look.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curtain Bangs

Long curtain bangs can be a bit high-maintenance, but with the right care, you can keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

Trim Your Bangs Regularly

To keep your long curtain bangs looking fresh, it's important to trim them regularly. This will help prevent split ends and keep them from getting too long and heavy.

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help keep your bangs looking great. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, as well as a styling product like a texturizing spray or hair wax to help shape and define your bangs.

Avoid Touching Your Bangs Too Much

Touching your bangs too much can cause them to become oily and lose their shape. Try to avoid touching them throughout the day, and use a blotting paper or dry shampoo to soak up any excess oil.

FAQ

Can Long Curtain Bangs Work for Curly Hair?

While long curtain bangs are typically best for straight hair, they can work for curly hair as well. However, it's important to keep in mind that curly hair can be harder to cut and style, so it may be best to visit a professional stylist for help.

Do Long Curtain Bangs Work for All Face Shapes?

Long curtain bangs can work for a variety of face shapes, but they tend to be most flattering on those with a round face. This is because the longer bangs help elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or as needed depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair soft, shiny, and hydrated. Look for a mask that's tailored to your hair type and needs.

Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Heat styling can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to avoid doing it too often. Consider air-drying your hair whenever possible, or using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

Conclusion

Long curtain bangs can be a fun and stylish way to update your look. By following the steps and tips outlined in this blog post, you can achieve this trendy hairstyle while keeping your hair healthy and looking great. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and don't be afraid to visit a professional stylist if you need help achieving the perfect look!
