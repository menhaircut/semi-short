Mens Long Hair Low Fade A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, a mens long hair low fade might be the perfect option for you. This trendy hairstyle combines the length of long hair with the sleekness of a fade, creating a look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the mens long hair low fade, from how to get the look to how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Description

A mens long hair low fade is a haircut that features long hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The fade is gradual, starting with longer hair at the top of the head and gradually getting shorter as it reaches the nape of the neck. This creates a sleek and stylish look that works well for many different hair types and face shapes.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a mens long hair low fade, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut. They'll be able to guide you through the process and help you choose the right length and style for your hair. Here are the basic steps involved in getting a mens long hair low fade:

Step 1: Choose the Length

The first step is to determine how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the overall look you're going for. You can choose to keep your hair shoulder-length or longer, or you can go for a shorter style that falls just above the ears.

Step 2: Decide on the Fade

Next, you'll need to decide on the type of fade you want. A low fade starts just above the ears and gradually gets shorter as it reaches the nape of the neck. This is a popular choice for the mens long hair low fade because it creates a sleek and polished look.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Once you've decided on the length and style, your hairstylist will begin cutting your hair. They'll start by trimming the hair on top to the desired length, then move on to the sides and back to create the fade. The end result should be a clean and polished look that's perfect for any occasion.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Long Hair Low Fade

Once you've got your mens long hair low fade, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and style, such as a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for long hair. You may also want to use a styling product to help keep your hair in place and add some extra shine.

Tip 2: Schedule Regular Trims

To keep your mens long hair low fade looking its best, it's important to schedule regular trims. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends from forming. Depending on the length of your hair, you may need to schedule trims every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat tools like a blow dryer or flat iron on your hair, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair to help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a mens long hair low fade work for my hair type?

A: Mens long hair low fade can work for many different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to talk to your hairstylist about your hair type and texture to determine if this haircut is right for you.

Q: How long does a mens long hair low fade take to grow out?

A: The length of time it takes for a mens long hair low fade to grow out will depend on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. On average, it may take several months to grow out a mens long hair low fade.

Q: Can I style my mens long hair low fade in different ways?

A: Yes! Mens long hair low fade is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. You can wear your hair straight and sleek, or you can add some texture and volume for a more casual look.

Hair Care for Mens Long Hair Low Fade

Taking care of your hair is essential to keeping your mens long hair low fade looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important to remove dirt and oil that can build up on your scalp. However, you don't want to wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair after shampooing can help keep it soft and manageable. Look for a conditioner that's formulated for your hair type and apply it to the ends of your hair. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week for added moisture.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair, wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF when you're outside for extended periods of time.

In conclusion, a mens long hair low fade is a stylish and versatile haircut that can work for many different hair types and face shapes. By following the tips and advice in this article, you'll be able to maintain healthy and beautiful hair that's perfect for any occasion.
