Short Hairstyles For Over 60S Ladies

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a fresh new hairstyle? Short haircuts are a great option for women over 60 as they are easy to maintain and can make you look younger. In this article, we will guide you through the best short hairstyles for over 60s ladies.

Description

As women age, their hair tends to lose volume and thickness. Short hairstyles can add volume and create a youthful appearance. They are also low maintenance and can save you time and money.

How to

If you are considering a short haircut, it is important to find the right style that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle:

Step 1: Know your face shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to know your face shape. The four main face shapes are round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, so it is important to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair type

Short hairstyles can work with any hair type, but some styles may be better suited to certain hair types. For example, if you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a pixie cut can help to thin it out.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it is important to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Short Hairstyles for Over 60s Ladies

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for over 60s ladies:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for women of all ages. It is low maintenance and can be styled in many different ways. It is also a great way to show off your facial features.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. It can be layered or blunt, and can be styled straight or wavy. It is a great option for women with fine hair as it can add volume and texture.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for women with thick hair. It can help to thin out the hair and add volume and texture. It is also a great way to add movement and dimension to your hair.

4. Short Shag Cut

A short shag cut is a great option for women with curly or wavy hair. It can help to define your curls and add texture to your hair. It is also a great option for women with thin hair as it can add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your short hairstyle:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools. - Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. - Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

FAQ

1. Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

No, a short hairstyle can actually make you look younger. It can add volume and create a youthful appearance.

2. What short hairstyle is best for fine hair?

A layered bob is a great option for women with fine hair. It can add volume and texture to your hair.

3. How often should I visit my hairstylist?

It is recommended to visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for trims and touch-ups.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis. - Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are a great option for women over 60. They are easy to maintain and can make you look younger. It is important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and to consult with a hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
