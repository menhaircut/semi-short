What Type Of Hairstyles Do Guys Like

If you're looking for a new hairstyle and wondering what type of hairstyles guys like, you've come to the right place. Many men have different preferences when it comes to hairstyles, but there are a few styles that most guys find attractive. In this article, we'll go over some of the most popular hairstyles that guys like, how to achieve them, and some tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

When it comes to hairstyles, men are often drawn to styles that are low maintenance, stylish, and easy to manage. Here are some of the most popular hairstyles that guys like:

  • Short haircuts, such as buzz cuts or crew cuts
  • Medium-length haircuts, such as messy textured cuts or slicked-back styles
  • Long haircuts, such as man buns or flowing locks

No matter what your hair type or face shape is, there is a hairstyle that will work for you. Keep reading to learn how to achieve some of the most popular styles that guys like.

How to Achieve Popular Hairstyles

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to choose a haircut that works for your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips:

  • For round faces, try a haircut that adds height to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • For square faces, try a textured haircut that adds volume to the sides, such as a messy crop or side-swept fringe.
  • For oval faces, try a haircut that accentuates your natural features, such as a classic side part or slicked-back style.
  • For curly hair, try a haircut that shows off your natural texture, such as a curly undercut or curly afro.

Once you've chosen a haircut, it's time to start styling. Here are some popular hairstyles that guys like:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are low maintenance and easy to style. Here are some popular short haircuts:

  • Buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • High and tight
  • Textured crop

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some popular medium-length haircuts:

  • Messy textured cut
  • Slicked-back style
  • Side part
  • Quiff

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts require more maintenance, but can be very attractive. Here are some popular long haircuts:

  • Man bun
  • Flowing locks
  • Surfer hair
  • Undercut

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Popular Hairstyles

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving some of the most popular hairstyles:

Buzz Cut

  1. Choose the length you want for your buzz cut
  2. Use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length
  3. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs around the ears and neckline
  4. Use a comb to remove any loose hairs
  5. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade for a polished look

Messy Textured Cut

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair
  3. Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look
  4. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting for added volume

Man Bun

  1. Gather your hair at the back of your head
  2. Twist your hair into a bun
  3. Secure the bun with a hair elastic
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product for a polished look

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for you

FAQs About Men's Hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles:

Q: What haircut looks good on a round face?

A: A pompadour or quiff can add height to the top of your head and balance out a round face.

Q: What haircut looks good on a square face?

A: A textured crop or side-swept fringe can add volume to the sides of your face and soften a square jawline.

Q: How do I style my hair for a formal event?

A: For a formal event, try a slicked-back style or a classic side part.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for you

Now that you know what type of hairstyles guys like, it's time to choose a style that works for you. Remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair type, and experiment with different hair products to find what works best for you. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can have a hairstyle that guys will love.
