How To Pick Hairstyle For Your Face In 2023

Written by Petter Oct 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you are not sure what would suit your face shape. A hairstyle that looks good on your friend might not necessarily look great on you. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to pick the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape. Below is the table of content that highlights the steps and tips we will cover:

How to Pick Hairstyle for Your Face

The first step to picking the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, square, round, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will enhance your best features and camouflage any flaws. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because most hairstyles will suit you. You can try a pixie cut, a bob, or long layers. Avoid hairstyles that cover your face too much.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, go for hairstyles that soften your jawline. You can try a layered bob, a side-swept bang, or loose curls. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, go for hairstyles that elongate your face. You can try a long bob, a side-parted hairstyle, or an updo. Avoid blunt, chin-length cuts.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, go for hairstyles that balance your forehead and chin. You can try a long bob, side-swept bangs, or a layered cut. Avoid short, full hairstyles.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, go for hairstyles that widen your forehead and jawline. You can try a side-swept bang, a bob, or a voluminous updo. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, go for hairstyles that add width to your face. You can try a wavy bob, a side-swept bang, or a layered cut. Avoid long, straight hairstyles.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here is a step-by-step guide on how to pick the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Find inspiration: Look for hairstyle inspiration in magazines, social media, or consult with your stylist.
  2. Determine your hair texture: Do you have curly, wavy, or straight hair? This will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your natural texture.
  3. Consider your lifestyle: Are you a busy mom who needs a low-maintenance hairstyle? Or do you have time to style your hair every day? Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle.
  4. Consult with your stylist: Your stylist can give you personalized advice on what hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair type.
  5. Try it out: Before committing to a new hairstyle, try it out with a wig or extensions to see if you like it.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look wider or shorter.
  • Highlight your best features with your hairstyle. For example, if you have beautiful eyes, go for a hairstyle that shows them off.
  • Consider your hair volume. If you have thin hair, avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look flat.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. If you've had the same hairstyle for years, it might be time to switch it up.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the right hairstyle:

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It is possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, go for a hairstyle that complements your dominant face shape.

Can I pull off a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts look great on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a square or round face shape, consider a longer pixie cut.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back! You can always try a different hairstyle or go back to your old one.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have picked the perfect hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular hair trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products. Use only what is necessary.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can seem overwhelming, but with these tips and guidelines, you can find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
