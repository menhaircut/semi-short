Do Fat Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you might be wondering if short hair is a good look for you. After all, it's widely believed that long hair helps to elongate the face and create a more flattering shape. However, there's no hard and fast rule about which hairstyle is best for which face shape. In this article, we'll explore the question "do fat faces look good with short hair?" and provide some tips and tricks for finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

First, let's talk about what we mean by "fat face." While there's no one-size-fits-all definition, a fat face generally refers to a round, full face with soft features. This can be due to genetics, weight gain, or other factors. If you have a fat face, you might feel self-conscious about your appearance and wonder if short hair is a good option for you.

How to

If you're considering a short hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to ensure that it flatters your face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Consider your hair texture

Short hair looks best on people with thick, wavy hair. If your hair is thin or fine, a short cut might make your face look even rounder. If you have curly hair, you might want to opt for a longer style that will show off your natural texture.

2. Look for hairstyles with volume on top

Avoid flat, sleek styles that will make your face look wider. Instead, look for hairstyles that have volume on top and are tapered at the sides. This will help to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face shape.

3. Consider a side-swept fringe

If you're not ready to commit to a full-on short cut, consider a side-swept fringe. This will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones and create a more flattering shape for your face.

Step by step

Still not sure if short hair is the right choice for you? Here's a step-by-step guide to help you decide:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Take a good look at your face in the mirror and determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you might want to avoid short hair that ends at your chin. If you have an oval face, you can pull off just about any short hairstyle.

Step 2: Look for inspiration

Search online for photos of people with similar face shapes and short hairstyles. This will give you an idea of what styles might look best on you.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, consult with a professional stylist. They can give you advice on which styles will flatter your face shape and hair texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering a short hairstyle:

1. Experiment with different lengths

Short hair doesn't have to mean super short. Consider a mid-length bob or a shoulder-length cut that will still flatter your face shape.

2. Embrace layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a more dynamic look. Just be sure to avoid layers that end at your chin, as this can make your face look wider.

3. Play with styling products

Styling products can help you achieve the perfect look for your face shape. Try volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add body and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a pixie cut if I have a fat face?

A: Yes! A pixie cut can look great on people with round faces. Just be sure to add volume on top and keep the sides tapered.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a fat face?

A: Not necessarily. Bangs can actually help to create a more flattering shape for your face. Just be sure to opt for side-swept bangs rather than straight-across bangs.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should plan on getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils. Opt for a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo instead.

2. Condition regularly

Conditioner helps to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

3. Protect your hair from heat

If you use hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray first. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Plan on getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.
