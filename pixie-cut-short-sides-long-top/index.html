Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top A Trendy Haircut For 2023

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle for 2023, consider getting a pixie cut with short sides and a long top. This haircut is perfect for those who want to make a statement and show off their unique sense of style. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the pixie cut short sides long top, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

How to Get a Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top

The pixie cut short sides long top is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. To get this haircut, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience with pixie cuts. Here's what you can expect during your appointment:

  • Your hairstylist will begin by washing your hair and sectioning it into parts.
  • They will then use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head short, leaving the hair on top longer.
  • You can choose how long you want the hair on top to be, but it's usually around 2-3 inches.
  • Your hairstylist will then blend the hair on the sides and top together to create a seamless look.
  • Finally, they will style your hair using a blow dryer and styling products to create the desired look.

It's important to communicate with your hairstylist about the specific style you want and any concerns you may have. They can provide recommendations based on your hair type and face shape to ensure you get the best possible result.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top

Styling a pixie cut short sides long top is relatively easy and can be done in just a few steps. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. You can use a blow dryer to dry your hair or let it air dry.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as hair wax or pomade, to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your fingertips together to warm up the product.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair, focusing on the longer hair on top. Style it in the direction you want it to go.
  5. You can create texture and volume by using your fingers to lift the hair at the roots.
  6. Use a comb or brush to smooth down the sides and back of your hair.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, the pixie cut short sides long top is a versatile haircut, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques and products to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top

Keeping your pixie cut short sides long top looking its best requires some maintenance and care. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides short and maintain the shape.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ About Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top

1. Is the pixie cut short sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the pixie cut short sides long top can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist can provide recommendations based on your unique features to ensure the most flattering result.

2. Can I still style my hair in different ways with a pixie cut short sides long top?

Yes, despite its short length, the pixie cut short sides long top is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways. You can experiment with different products and techniques to create different looks.

3. Will a pixie cut short sides long top require a lot of maintenance?

While the pixie cut short sides long top does require some maintenance, it's relatively low maintenance compared to longer hairstyles. Regular trims and proper hair care can help keep it looking its best.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut Short Sides Long Top

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your pixie cut short sides long top looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and properly caring for your hair, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance pixie cut short sides long top that suits your unique sense of style.
