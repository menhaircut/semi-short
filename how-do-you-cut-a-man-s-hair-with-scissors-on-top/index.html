How To Cut A Man s Hair With Scissors On Top

Written by Christine Jul 30, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to cutting a man's hair, there are various techniques that you can use. However, one of the most popular methods is using scissors on top. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a man's hair with scissors on top, step-by-step, while sharing some tips and hair care advice along the way.

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Scissors on Top

Before you start cutting, make sure that you have all the necessary tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to cut a man's hair with scissors on top:

1. Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Wash the hair and dry it completely before you start cutting. Wet hair is harder to cut and may result in uneven lengths.

2. Part the Hair

Use the comb to create a part in the hair where you want to start cutting. This will give you a clear guideline to follow when cutting.

3. Cut the Hair in Sections

Starting from the back of the head, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle using the scissors. Continue moving forward until you have cut all the hair on the top of the head.

4. Blend the Hair

Using the comb, blend the hair on top of the head with the hair on the sides and back by making small cuts at an angle. This will create a natural-looking transition between the different lengths of hair.

5. Check for Evenness

After you have finished cutting, check to make sure that the hair is even on both sides. If you notice any unevenness, make small adjustments with the scissors until the hair looks symmetrical.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Scissors on Top

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting a man's hair with scissors on top:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. These will be sharper and more comfortable to use than regular scissors.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take small sections of hair and make multiple cuts than to try to cut a large amount of hair in one go.
  • Use the comb to create a natural-looking blend between different lengths of hair.
  • When cutting, keep the scissors parallel to the direction of the hair growth. This will help you achieve a more even cut.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, it's a good idea to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not sharp enough to cut hair cleanly and may result in split ends and uneven cuts.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. Try to make small adjustments with the scissors to even out the hair. If the mistake is more significant, it's best to seek the help of a professional hairdresser.

Hair Care after Cutting

After you have finished cutting, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting a man's hair with scissors on top is a simple and effective way to achieve a stylish and professional look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve great results at home. Remember to take care of your hair after cutting to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
