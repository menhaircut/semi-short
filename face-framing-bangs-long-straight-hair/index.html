Face Framing Bangs For Long Straight Hair A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Long straight hair can be beautiful, but sometimes it can feel a bit plain. One way to add some flair to your long locks is by getting face framing bangs. Not only do they add dimension and shape to your hair, but they also highlight your best features. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect face framing bangs for your long straight hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Face Framing Bangs for Long Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Face Framing Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Face Framing Bangs
  • FAQs About Face Framing Bangs for Long Straight Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Straight Hair with Bangs

How to Get Face Framing Bangs for Long Straight Hair

Before you cut your bangs, you need to decide what kind of face framing bangs you want. There are several different styles of face framing bangs, including:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Straight-across bangs
  • Curly bangs
  • Layered bangs

Once you’ve decided on a style, it’s time to cut your bangs. While you can cut your own bangs at home, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure that your bangs are even and properly shaped.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Face Framing Bangs

If you’re feeling confident and want to cut your own bangs, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to cut face framing bangs for long straight hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair straight down and make sure it’s free of tangles.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section of hair at the front of your head. The point of the triangle should be at the center of your forehead.
  3. Pin back the rest of your hair so it doesn’t get in the way.
  4. Decide how long you want your bangs to be. Use your fingers to measure the length and cut straight across. Remember, it’s better to start with longer bangs and trim them later.
  5. Take small sections of hair and cut them at a slight angle. This creates a layered effect and gives your bangs more texture.
  6. Repeat the previous step until you’ve cut all of your bangs.
  7. Use a comb to check that your bangs are even. If they’re not, trim any uneven pieces.
  8. Style your bangs as desired. You can blow dry them straight, curl them, or add some texture with a hair straightener.

Tips for Maintaining Your Face Framing Bangs

Once you’ve cut your face framing bangs, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your face framing bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Wash your bangs often to prevent oily buildup.
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go to maintain their shape.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy and flat.

FAQs About Face Framing Bangs for Long Straight Hair

What face shape is best for face framing bangs?

Face framing bangs work well for most face shapes, but they’re especially flattering for those with heart-shaped or oval faces.

What kind of hair works best for face framing bangs?

Face framing bangs work well for most hair types, but they’re especially flattering for those with long straight hair.

Can I cut my own face framing bangs?

While it’s possible to cut your own face framing bangs, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure that your bangs are even and properly shaped.

Hair Care Tips for Long Straight Hair with Bangs

Long straight hair with bangs requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for long straight hair with bangs:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect face framing bangs for your long straight hair. Whether you’re looking for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, face framing bangs are a great way to add some personality to your hair.
