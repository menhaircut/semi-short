How To Slick Back Your Hair Into A Ponytail

Written by Daniel Nov 13, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

Are you looking for a chic and polished hairstyle that is easy to create? Look no further than the slicked-back ponytail! This sleek style is perfect for any occasion, whether you're heading to work or hitting the town with friends. Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect slicked-back ponytail.

Description

The slicked-back ponytail is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that has been popular for many years. It involves pulling your hair back tightly and securing it with an elastic band. This style works well for most hair types and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Slick Back Your Hair into a Ponytail

Follow these simple steps to create a slicked-back ponytail:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before beginning, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help the style stay in place and prevent any unwanted frizz or flyaways.

Step 2: Apply Gel or Pomade

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to smooth down any frizz or flyaways and give your hair a sleek, polished look.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a wide-tooth comb or brush, brush your hair back away from your face. Make sure to brush it back tightly to create a smooth, sleek look.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair with an Elastic Band

Once your hair is brushed back, secure it with an elastic band. Make sure to pull it tight to keep the hair in place and prevent any bumps or lumps.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your style in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold the hair in place and prevent any unwanted flyaways.

Tips

  • Use a strong-hold gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Try using a toothbrush or small brush to smooth down any flyaways or baby hairs.
  • For a more polished look, wrap a small piece of hair around the elastic band and secure it with a bobby pin.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I create a slicked-back ponytail on curly or wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can! Follow the same steps but use a curl-enhancing gel or mousse to help define your curls or waves.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting damaged from frequent styling?

A: Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools, and try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot styling tools too frequently. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these simple steps, you can create a chic and polished slicked-back ponytail that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're heading to work or hitting the town with friends, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
