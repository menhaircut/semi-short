How Long Does It Take For Eyebrows To Heal After Microblading

Written by Eveline Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Microblading is a popular cosmetic procedure that involves tattooing tiny strokes onto the eyebrows to create a fuller, natural-looking brow. While the results can be stunning, many people wonder how long it takes for eyebrows to heal after microblading. In this article, we'll answer that question and provide some tips for optimal healing.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare for Microblading
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Microblading
  • Tips for Optimal Healing
  • FAQs About Microblading
  • Hair Care After Microblading

How to Prepare for Microblading

Before undergoing microblading, there are a few things you should do to prepare. Firstly, avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for at least 24 hours before the procedure. Secondly, avoid sun exposure and tanning for at least two weeks before the procedure. Lastly, avoid waxing, threading, or tweezing your eyebrows for at least a week before the procedure.

Step-by-Step Guide to Microblading

The microblading procedure typically takes around two hours to complete. Here's a step-by-step guide to what you can expect during the procedure:

  1. The technician will clean your eyebrows and apply a numbing cream.
  2. The technician will use a small blade to make tiny strokes onto your eyebrows.
  3. The technician will apply pigment to your eyebrows.
  4. You will be given aftercare instructions.

After the procedure, your eyebrows will appear darker and more defined than you may be used to. This is normal and will fade over time.

Tips for Optimal Healing

After microblading, it's essential to take proper care of your eyebrows to ensure optimal healing. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid getting your eyebrows wet for at least a week after the procedure.
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning for at least two weeks after the procedure.
  • Avoid applying makeup to your eyebrows for at least a week after the procedure.
  • Avoid picking at scabs or flakes that may appear on your eyebrows.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for at least a week after the procedure.

FAQs About Microblading

How long does it take for eyebrows to heal after microblading?

It typically takes around four to six weeks for eyebrows to heal after microblading.

Will my eyebrows scab after microblading?

Yes, it's normal for your eyebrows to scab after microblading. However, it's important not to pick at the scabs and let them fall off naturally.

Can I wear makeup after microblading?

Avoid applying makeup to your eyebrows for at least a week after the procedure.

Hair Care After Microblading

After microblading, it's important to take proper care of your eyebrows to ensure optimal healing. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Avoid getting your eyebrows wet for at least a week after the procedure.
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning for at least two weeks after the procedure.
  • Avoid applying makeup to your eyebrows for at least a week after the procedure.
  • Avoid picking at scabs or flakes that may appear on your eyebrows.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for at least a week after the procedure.

Overall, microblading is a safe and effective way to achieve fuller, natural-looking eyebrows. By following proper aftercare instructions and taking care of your eyebrows, you can ensure optimal healing and long-lasting results.
