How To Make Your Face Oval Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having a round or square-shaped face? Do you want to achieve a more oval-shaped face? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to make your face oval. Whether you have a round, square, or heart-shaped face, these tips will help you achieve a more oval-shaped face. So, let's get started!

How to Make Your Face Oval

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in making your face oval is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror.

If your face is longer than it is wide and your jawline is slightly rounded, you have an oval face shape. If your face is as wide as it is long and your jawline is rounded, you have a round face shape. If your face is squared off at the jawline and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width, you have a square face shape. If your face is wider at the forehead and narrows at the jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you have a diamond-shaped face. And if your face is longer than it is wide and your jawline is square, you have an oblong face shape.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

The next step in making your face oval is to contour your face. Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using dark and light shades to create shadows and highlights on your face. To contour your face, you will need a contour kit or a bronzer that is two shades darker than your skin tone.

Start by applying the darker shade to the areas of your face that you want to minimize. This includes your temples, the sides of your forehead, your cheeks, and your jawline. Use a blending brush to blend the contour out, making sure there are no harsh lines.

Next, apply the lighter shade to the areas of your face that you want to highlight. This includes the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, your cheekbones, and your cupid's bow. Blend the highlight out, making sure there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

The third step in making your face oval is to style your hair. The right hairstyle can make a big difference in the shape of your face. If you have a round face, opt for a long, layered haircut that falls below your chin. This will help elongate your face and make it appear more oval. If you have a square face, opt for a hairstyle with soft layers and waves. This will soften your features and make your face appear more oval.

For a heart-shaped face, opt for a side-swept bang that falls below your cheekbones. This will help balance out your forehead and jawline. For a diamond-shaped face, opt for a layered haircut with volume at the crown. This will help balance out your cheekbones and jawline. And for an oblong face, opt for a short hairstyle with volume at the sides. This will help widen your face and make it appear more oval.

Step 4: Dress for Your Shape

The final step in making your face oval is to dress for your shape. The right clothes can help balance out your features and make your face appear more oval. If you have a round face, opt for clothes with vertical lines or patterns. This will help elongate your body and make your face appear more oval. If you have a square face, opt for clothes with soft, flowing fabrics. This will soften your features and make your face appear more oval.

For a heart-shaped face, opt for clothes that draw attention to your shoulders and neckline. This will help balance out your forehead and jawline. For a diamond-shaped face, opt for clothes with a defined waistline. This will help balance out your cheekbones and jawline. And for an oblong face, opt for clothes with horizontal lines or patterns. This will help widen your body and make your face appear more oval.

Tips for Maintaining an Oval Face Shape

Now that you know how to make your face oval, here are some tips for maintaining an oval face shape:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Get enough sleep to reduce puffiness and dark circles under your eyes.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage your skin and make your face appear puffy.
  • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight and reduce puffiness in your face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my face oval without contouring?

Yes, you can make your face oval without contouring. Simply follow the other steps in this article, such as styling your hair and dressing for your shape, to achieve a more oval-shaped face.

How long does it take to make my face oval?

The time it takes to make your face oval depends on your current face shape and the techniques you use. It may take several weeks or months to achieve a more oval-shaped face.

Can I make my face oval permanently?

No, you cannot make your face oval permanently. Your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which cannot be changed.

Hair Care for Oval Faces

Now that you know how to make your face oval, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some tips for hair care for oval faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, which can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more oval-shaped face and maintain your new look with proper hair care. Remember to embrace your unique features and rock your new oval shape with confidence!
