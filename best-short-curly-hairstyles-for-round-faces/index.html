Best Short Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and curly hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing the best short curly hairstyles for round faces. Whether you have natural curls or prefer to use a curling iron, these hairstyles will flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful. Keep reading for tips, how-to guides, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved features and lack of sharp angles. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline. Short curly hairstyles are a great option for round faces because they add volume and texture to your hair, which can balance out the roundness of your face. In this article, we will be sharing the best short curly hairstyles for round faces, including pixie cuts, bobs, and curly bangs.

How To

The key to finding the perfect short curly hairstyle for your round face is to choose a cut that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have natural curls, you can enhance them with a curling cream or gel. If you prefer to use a curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant and curl your hair away from your face to create more volume.

Step By Step

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that is perfect for round faces. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a styling cream or gel to your hair
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the crown of your head to create volume
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways
  5. Use a texturizing spray to add texture and body to your hair

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for round faces. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair
  3. Use a curling iron to curl your hair away from your face
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth the ends of your hair
  5. Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place

Curly Bangs

Curly bangs can add a playful and flirty touch to your look. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a styling cream or gel to your hair
  3. Use a curling iron to curl your bangs away from your face
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways
  5. Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place

Tips

When choosing a short curly hairstyle for your round face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head to elongate your face
  • Opt for layers to add texture and movement to your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short curly hairstyles for round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best short curly hairstyles for round faces are ones that add height and volume to the crown of your head, such as pixie cuts, bobs, and curly bangs.

Can I wear blunt bangs with a round face?

Blunt bangs can make a round face appear wider, so it is best to avoid them if you have a round face. Instead, opt for side-swept or curly bangs.

How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your curly hair, use a curling cream or gel and avoid brushing your hair when it is dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hairstyle looking its best, make sure to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Final Thoughts

Short curly hairstyles can be flattering and stylish for round faces. By choosing a cut that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, you can create a more balanced look that elongates your face. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, bob, or curly bangs, these hairstyles will make you feel confident and beautiful.
