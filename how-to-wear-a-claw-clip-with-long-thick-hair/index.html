How To Wear A Claw Clip With Long Thick Hair

Written by Frank Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Claw clips are a versatile hair accessory that can help you create different looks with ease. They are perfect for long thick hair, as they can hold a large amount of hair in place. In this article, we will guide you through how to wear a claw clip with long thick hair. We will cover everything from how to choose the right clip to how to style your hair using a claw clip. So, let's get started!

Choosing the Right Claw Clip

When it comes to choosing a claw clip for long thick hair, you need to look for one that is large and sturdy. The clip should be able to hold a large amount of hair without slipping or sliding out. Look for clips that have teeth on the inside, as they will grip your hair better and prevent it from slipping out.

You can choose from various shapes and sizes of claw clips, depending on your preference. Some popular options include round, oval, and rectangular clips. Choose the one that suits your hair length and thickness best.

Preparing Your Hair

Before putting in a claw clip, you need to prepare your hair. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. Apply some hair spray or texturizing spray to add some grip to your hair. If you have very thick hair, you may need to section it off and work with smaller sections.

Putting in the Claw Clip

To put in a claw clip, follow these simple steps:

  1. Gather your hair at the back of your head or at the crown, depending on the style you want to create.
  2. Hold the clip with the teeth facing downwards and the hinge facing up.
  3. Place the clip over your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  4. Once the clip is in place, press down on the hinge to secure it in place.
  5. Adjust the clip as needed to make sure it is holding your hair securely.

Styling Your Hair with a Claw Clip

Claw clips are perfect for creating a variety of hairstyles, from messy buns to half-up styles. Here are some ideas:

  • Messy bun: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a messy bun. Secure the bun with a large claw clip.
  • Half-up style: Gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a claw clip at the crown. You can leave the rest of your hair down or style it in waves or curls.
  • Low ponytail: Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a claw clip. This is a great option for a sleek and simple look.

Tips for Wearing a Claw Clip with Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to make wearing a claw clip with long thick hair easier:

  • Choose a clip that is large enough to hold all of your hair.
  • Apply some hair spray or texturizing spray to add some grip to your hair.
  • Section off your hair if it is very thick to make it easier to work with.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your face shape and hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear a claw clip with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a claw clip with short hair. Look for smaller clips that can hold your hair securely without slipping out.

Q: Can I wear a claw clip to the gym?

A: Yes, you can wear a claw clip to the gym. Look for a clip that is large and sturdy enough to hold your hair in place during your workout.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep your long thick hair healthy and shiny:

  • Brush your hair regularly to distribute the natural oils and prevent tangles.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to wear a claw clip with long thick hair, you can try out different styles and experiment with different clips to create the perfect look. Whether you are going for a casual or formal look, a claw clip can help you achieve it with ease.
