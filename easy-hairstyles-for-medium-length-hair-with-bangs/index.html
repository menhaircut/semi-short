Description

Medium length hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle among women. It's versatile and easy to style. If you're looking for some easy hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you some simple hairstyles that you can do at home. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event.


How to

Before we get started, here are some things you'll need:

  • Hairbrush
  • Hairspray
  • Bobby pins
  • Elastic bands
  • Straightening or curling iron (optional)

Now, let's get started!


Step by step

1. Half up, half down

Start by brushing your hair and separating the top half from the bottom half. Secure the top half with an elastic band. You can leave some hair out to frame your face. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out.

2. Low ponytail with a twist

Brush your hair and gather it at the nape of your neck. Secure it with an elastic band. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it. Secure it with a bobby pin. You can curl the ends of your hair for a softer look. This hairstyle is perfect for a formal event.

3. Side braid

Part your hair on one side and brush it to the opposite side. Take a small section of hair near your part and braid it. Secure the braid with a bobby pin. Gather the rest of your hair and braid it to the side. Secure the braid with an elastic band. You can also curl the ends of your hair for a softer look. This hairstyle is perfect for a romantic date.

4. Messy bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Twist your hair and wrap it around the elastic band to form a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Pull out some hair near your face to frame it. This hairstyle is perfect for a lazy day at home.


Tips

  • Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or hair clips.
  • If you have straight hair, use a curling iron to add some texture to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, use a straightening iron to straighten your bangs.

FAQ

What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs?

Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs are half up, half down, low ponytail with a twist, side braid, and messy bun.

What do I need to style my hair?

You'll need a hairbrush, hairspray, bobby pins, elastic bands, and a straightening or curling iron (optional).

How do I keep my hairstyle in place?

Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.


Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs, you can change up your look in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a hairstyle for every occasion. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
