How To Wear A Wig Topper For Thinning Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you suffer from thinning hair or hair loss? A wig topper can be an excellent solution to add volume and thickness to your hair. However, if you're new to wearing wigs, you may feel a little intimidated about how to wear a wig topper for thinning hair. In this post, we'll guide you through the process step by step and provide you with some tips to make the experience as comfortable and natural-looking as possible.

Description: What is a Wig Topper?

A wig topper is a hairpiece that is designed to be worn on top of your head to add volume and thickness to your hair. It's typically made from synthetic or natural hair and can be attached to your existing hair using clips, combs, or adhesive tape. A wig topper is an excellent solution for people with thinning hair or hair loss, and it can help you achieve the look of a full head of hair.

How to Wear a Wig Topper for Thinning Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Wig Topper

The first step in wearing a wig topper for thinning hair is to choose the right one. You'll want to consider the color, length, texture, and style of the wig topper to ensure that it matches your existing hair and looks natural. You may also want to consider the material, as synthetic wigs tend to be more affordable, while natural hair wigs offer a more realistic look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you can attach your wig topper, you'll need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. If you have long hair, you may want to tie it up in a bun or a ponytail to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Attach the Wig Topper

Once your hair is prepared, it's time to attach the wig topper. To do this, simply place the wig topper on top of your head and secure it in place using the clips, combs, or adhesive tape. Be sure to position the wig topper correctly so that it blends in with your natural hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

With the wig topper in place, you can now style your hair as desired. You can use a hairbrush or comb to blend your natural hair with the wig topper and create a seamless look. You may also want to use hair products such as hairspray, gel, or mousse to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Wearing a Wig Topper for Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to help you wear a wig topper for thinning hair comfortably and confidently:

  • Choose a wig topper that matches your natural hair color and texture as closely as possible.
  • Trim the wig topper as needed to ensure that it fits your head comfortably and securely.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that looks best with your wig topper.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid damaging the wig topper.
  • Consider using a wig cap to help keep your natural hair in place and prevent it from showing through the wig topper.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Wearing a Wig Topper for Thinning Hair

Q: How long can I wear a wig topper?

A: You can wear a wig topper for as long as you like, but it's important to remove it before going to bed to avoid damaging the wig or your natural hair.

Q: Can I swim or shower with a wig topper?

A: Yes, you can swim or shower with a wig topper, but it's important to take proper care of the wig topper afterward to ensure that it stays in good condition.

Q: How do I clean and maintain my wig topper?

A: To clean your wig topper, gently wash it with a mild shampoo and cold water. Rinse it thoroughly and allow it to air dry. Avoid using hot water or heat styling tools on your wig topper, as this can damage the fibers or cause it to lose its shape.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

In addition to wearing a wig topper, there are several other things you can do to care for your thinning hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair growth.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Consider using hair growth products such as minoxidil or biotin supplements to help support hair growth.

By following these tips and wearing a wig topper, you can enjoy the look of a full head of hair and feel confident and beautiful every day.
