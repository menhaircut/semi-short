Medium Length Hair Long Side Bangs How To Achieve The Perfect Look

Written by Daniel Nov 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, medium length hair with long side bangs might be just what you need. This style is suitable for all hair types, face shapes, and ages, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look step by step, provide tips and tricks for maintaining it, and answer some common questions about this hairstyle. Let's get started!

Description

Medium length hair with long side bangs is a hairstyle that features hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone, with longer layers framing the face on one side. The side bangs can be styled to sweep across the forehead or tucked behind the ear, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is ideal for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair, while still keeping it at a manageable length.

How to Achieve the Look

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect medium length hair with long side bangs:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair type, and provide tips for maintaining the look.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Make sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any build-up that can weigh down your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom. Clip the top and middle sections out of the way, leaving the bottom section loose.

Step 5: Cut the Bottom Layer

Starting with the bottom layer, cut your hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut straight across and avoid any uneven lines.

Step 6: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the middle section and cut it to the same length as the bottom layer. This layer should be slightly shorter than the top layer to create a layered effect.

Step 7: Cut the Top Layer

Release the top section and cut it to the desired length. This layer should be the longest of the three layers.

Step 8: Cut the Side Bangs

Section your hair on one side and cut your bangs to the desired length. Make sure to cut them at an angle to create a natural-looking curve.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tools as desired. Sweep your side bangs across your forehead or tuck them behind your ear, depending on your preference.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length hair with long side bangs:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools to prevent damage
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils

FAQ

Here are some common questions about medium length hair with long side bangs:

Q: Will this hairstyle work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your specific face shape.

Q: Can I style my side bangs differently?

A: Yes, you can style your side bangs to sweep across your forehead or tuck them behind your ear, depending on your preference.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair with long side bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect medium length hair with long side bangs that suits your personality and style. Don't forget to consult with your stylist and maintain your hair regularly for the best results. Happy styling!
