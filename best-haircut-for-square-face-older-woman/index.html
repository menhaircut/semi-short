Best Haircut For Square Face Older Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are an older woman with a square face, you may be wondering what the best haircut for your face shape is. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, forehead, and cheekbones, which can make it challenging to find a flattering haircut. However, with the right styling and cut, you can enhance your features and achieve a stunning look. In this guide, we will explore the best haircuts for square face older women, how to style them, and essential hair care tips.

Description

A square face has a strong, angular jawline, a broad forehead, and wide cheekbones. The length and width of the face are almost equal, giving it a boxy shape. The aim of a haircut for a square face is to soften the angles and create a more oval or round shape. The right cut can highlight your best features while minimizing the less desirable ones.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Square Face Older Woman

When choosing a haircut, there are a few things to consider:

Face shape

As an older woman with a square face, you should look for haircuts that elongate your face and soften the angles. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face as they can make your face appear wider.

Hair texture

Your hair texture will also determine the best haircut for you. If you have thin hair, a short bob or pixie cut can add volume and make your hair appear fuller. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help manage the volume and achieve a flattering shape.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you are an active person and need a low-maintenance style, a short haircut may be the best option. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, a longer cut with layers can offer more versatility.

Best Haircuts for Square Face Older Women

Here are some of the best haircuts for square face older women:

Short Bob

A short bob is a classic haircut that works well for square face older women. It can add volume to thin hair and create a more oval shape. A layered bob with side-swept bangs can also soften the angles of the face.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance haircut that can flatter a square face. It can create a more elongated shape and highlight your cheekbones. If you have thick hair, a textured pixie cut can add dimension and movement.

Long Layers

Long layers can help soften the angles of a square face and create a more oval shape. Layers can add volume and movement to thick hair and create a flattering frame around the face. If you have fine hair, long layers can add texture and body.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right haircut, styling is essential to achieve the desired look. Here are some tips:

Blow-dry your hair

Blow-drying your hair can add volume and create a more polished look. Use a round brush to create a gentle curve at the ends of your hair.

Use a curling iron

A curling iron can add dimension and create a more feminine look. Use a large-barrel curling iron to create loose waves.

Apply a volumizing product

Using a volumizing product can add body and hold to your hair. Apply it to the roots of your hair and blow-dry with a round brush.

Hair Care Tips

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair and a flattering style. Here are some tips:

Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Use a heat protectant

Using a heat protectant can prevent damage from hot styling tools and maintain the health of your hair.

Use a deep conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

FAQ

Can I wear bangs with a square face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a square face. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs can soften the angles of the face.

Can I wear a middle part with a square face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a square face. However, a side part can create a more flattering shape.

Can I wear a short haircut with a square face?

Yes, a short haircut can be a great option for a square face. A short bob or pixie cut can elongate the face and create a more feminine look.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for a square face can be challenging, but with the right cut and styling, you can achieve a flattering look. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut, and don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and nourishing products.
