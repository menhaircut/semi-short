How Do I Use My Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

Written by Robby Oct 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Animal Crossing is a popular simulation game where players can create their own character and live a virtual life in a village filled with anthropomorphic animals. One of the most exciting aspects of the game is the ability to change your character's appearance, including their hairstyle. In this article, we will explore how to use hairstyles in Animal Crossing by providing step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Your Hairstyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  • FAQs about Animal Crossing Hairstyles
  • Hair Care Advice for Your Virtual Character

How to Change Your Hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process. First, you need to unlock the hair salon in your village by spending 10,000 bells at the Able Sisters' shop. Once you have unlocked the salon, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the salon and sit in the chair.
  2. Select "Change hairstyle" from the menu.
  3. Choose from the available hairstyles and colors.
  4. Confirm your selection and pay the fee.

That's it! Your character's new hairstyle will be immediately applied.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

With so many hairstyle options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect one for your character. Here are some tips to help you make the best decision:

  • Consider your character's personality and style. Are they more laid-back or avant-garde?
  • Think about the color options as well. Does your character have a favorite color or a particular color scheme?
  • Look at your character's face shape and features. Certain hairstyles may complement or accentuate their features better than others.
  • Try on different hairstyles before committing to a new one. The salon offers a preview option, so take advantage of it!

Remember, you can always change your hairstyle again if you aren't happy with the first choice.

FAQs about Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my character's hairstyle after leaving the salon?

No, you cannot change your hairstyle once you leave the salon. You will need to return to the salon and pay the fee again to change it.

Can I unlock more hairstyles?

Yes, you can unlock additional hairstyles by using amiibo cards or by visiting players who have unlocked different hairstyles in their salon.

Do hairstyles affect gameplay?

No, hairstyles do not affect gameplay. They are purely cosmetic.

Hair Care Advice for Your Virtual Character

While your character's hair may not require as much maintenance as your own, it's still important to keep it looking healthy and styled. Here are some tips for virtual hair care:

  • Visit the salon regularly to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a virtual hairbrush or comb to keep your hair detangled and smooth.
  • Accessorize with virtual hair ties, headbands, or hats for a different look.
  • Try out different hair colors for a fun change.

By following these tips, your virtual character will have fabulous hair in no time!

Conclusion

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a fun and easy way to switch up your character's appearance. By following our step-by-step instructions, considering our tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle, and taking care of your virtual hair, you'll have the most stylish character in the village.
