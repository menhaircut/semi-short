How To Get A Messy Hair Look Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a hairstyle that is effortless and chic, then the messy hair look is perfect for you. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular over the years, and it's not hard to see why. It's easy to achieve, low-maintenance, and perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we will show you how to achieve that perfect messy hair look in just a few simple steps.

  • How to Get a Messy Hair Look
  • Step by Step Guide

Description

The messy hair look is a hairstyle that looks natural, effortless, and unpolished. It's perfect for those who want to achieve a laid-back, bohemian look without putting in too much effort. This hairstyle can be achieved by using various hair products and techniques, and it's suitable for all hair types, lengths, and textures.

How to Get a Messy Hair Look

Getting the perfect messy hair look requires some effort, but it's not as difficult as you might think. Here are some steps you can take to achieve this hairstyle:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it using a diffuser attachment to add volume and texture.

  2. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair. This will add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair, creating a messy, undone look. You can also use a comb or a brush to create more defined waves or curls.

  4. If you have straight hair, use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves or curls. Don't make them too perfect; the goal is to create an effortless, messy look.

  5. Add some dry shampoo to your roots to absorb excess oil and add volume.

  6. Use your fingers to tease your hair at the crown. This will add height and volume to your hair.

  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. Choose a flexible hold hairspray that won't make your hair feel stiff or crunchy.

Tips to Achieve the Perfect Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hair look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine.

  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to create natural-looking waves and curls.

  • Don't overdo it with the hair products. Use a small amount of product at a time and build up as needed.

  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse to add body and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the messy hair look:

  • Can I achieve the messy hair look with short hair?
    Yes, you can achieve the messy hair look with short hair. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture, and use your fingers to tousle your hair.

  • Do I need to use heat tools to achieve the messy hair look?
    No, you don't necessarily need to use heat tools to achieve the messy hair look. You can use a texturizing spray or mousse and your fingers to tousle your hair.

  • Can I achieve the messy hair look with curly hair?
    Yes, you can achieve the messy hair look with curly hair. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curls, and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Hair Look

The messy hair look requires minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your messy hair look:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid drying it out.

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day; this can cause damage and breakage.

  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

  • Avoid using too much product on your hair; this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

By following these tips, you can maintain your messy hair look and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
