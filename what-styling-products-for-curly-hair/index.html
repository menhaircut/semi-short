What Styling Products For Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On good days, your curls look bouncy and gorgeous, but on bad days, your hair can be frizzy, unmanageable, and downright frustrating. Fortunately, there are styling products that can help you achieve the perfect curly look every time. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best styling products for curly hair, how to use them, and some tips to keep your curls looking their best.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Use Styling Products for Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair
  4. Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Description

There are many different types of styling products for curly hair, but the most common are:

  • Curl defining cream
  • Curl enhancing mousse
  • Curl refreshing spray
  • Curl holding gel
  • Curl nourishing oil

Each of these products has a different purpose and can help you achieve different looks. Curl defining cream is great for defining and moisturizing curls, while curl enhancing mousse can add volume and bounce. Curl refreshing spray can give your curls a boost throughout the day, and curl holding gel can keep your curls in place all day long. Curl nourishing oil can help keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

How to Use Styling Products for Curly Hair

The best way to use styling products for curly hair is to start with clean, damp hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends and any areas that tend to get frizzy. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls and create definition. If you're using a curl holding gel, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls while keeping their shape.

General Tips:

  • Use a small amount of product at first, and add more as needed
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Step 1: Cleanse and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply your chosen styling product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends and any areas that tend to get frizzy.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Curls

Use your fingers to scrunch your curls and create definition.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

If you're using a curl holding gel, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls while keeping their shape. Otherwise, you can let your hair air dry or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, as a brush can cause frizz
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can also cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry
  • Protect your curls from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using a hair dryer or flat iron
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: Can I use multiple styling products at once?

A: Yes, you can use multiple styling products at once, but be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Q: Can I use styling products on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can use styling products on dry hair, but it's best to start with damp hair to ensure even distribution of the product.

Q: Can I use the same styling products for different curl types?

A: While some products may work for multiple curl types, it's best to choose products that are specifically formulated for your curl type for the best results.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

  • Wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping your curls of their natural oils
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your curls. Instead, use lukewarm or cool water
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls

By following these tips and using the right styling products for your curl type, you can achieve the perfect curly look every time. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
