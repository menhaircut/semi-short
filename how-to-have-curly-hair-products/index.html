How To Have Curly Hair Products A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having straight, boring hair? Do you want to spice things up and embrace your natural curls? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to have curly hair products that will leave your locks looking fabulous. From choosing the right products to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. Let’s dive in!

  2. Step 2: Choose the Right Products

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you begin your curly hair journey, it’s important to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and styling techniques that work best for your hair. There are four types of curly hair:

Type 2:

Wavy hair that is slightly curly and has an “S” shape.

Type 3:

Curly hair that is springy and has a defined “S” or “Z” shape.

Type 4:

Tightly coiled hair that forms a “Z” or “S” shape when stretched.

Once you’ve determined your hair type, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Choose the Right Products

Choosing the right products for your curly hair is crucial. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This includes shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. Some popular curly hair products include:

  • Curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner
  • Curl defining cream
  • Curl refreshing spray
  • Gel or mousse

When choosing products, be sure to read the labels and look for ingredients that are beneficial for curly hair. Some important ingredients include:

  • Glycerin
  • Coconut oil
  • Shea butter
  • Jojoba oil

These ingredients help to moisturize and define curls, leaving your hair looking healthy and bouncy.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair Properly

Washing your curly hair properly is important to prevent frizz and maintain your curls. Here’s how to do it:

  • Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage gently.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the scalp.
  • Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, then rinse with cool water.

Remember to be gentle when washing your hair to avoid tangling and breakage.

Step 4: Apply Products Correctly

Now that your hair is clean, it’s time to apply your styling products. Here’s how:

  • Start with damp hair.
  • Apply a small amount of curl defining cream or gel to your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Scrunch your hair gently to encourage curl formation.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, or let it air dry.

Remember not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair Properly

Drying your curly hair properly is important to prevent frizz and maintain your curls. Here’s how to do it:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Scrunch your hair gently with the towel or t-shirt to remove excess water.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, or let it air dry.

Remember not to rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage your curls.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

A: No, it’s best to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go up to a week without washing, while others may need to wash every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to use heat protectant products and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls.

Q: How can I control frizz in my curly hair?

A: Use products that are specifically designed to control frizz, such as curl refreshing spray or anti-frizz serum. Also, avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

And there you have it, a comprehensive guide on how to have curly hair products that will leave your locks looking fabulous. Follow these steps and tips to embrace your natural curls and achieve the look you’ve always wanted!
