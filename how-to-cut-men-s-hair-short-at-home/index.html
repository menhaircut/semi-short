How To Cut Men s Hair Short At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a great haircut at home. If you're looking to cut your hair short, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll take you through the process of cutting men's hair short at home, step-by-step.

Description

Cutting men's hair short at home is a great way to save money and time. It's also a useful skill to have in case you can't make it to the barbershop or hair salon. However, it's important to note that cutting your hair at home may not always produce the same results as a professional haircut. It's also important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid any mishaps.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a clipper with different guards. It's also a good idea to have a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it's clean and easy to work with. Dry your hair completely before you start cutting.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a clipper with a guard to make it easier to achieve an even cut. Use the comb to lift a section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair on the sides and back of your head.

Next, move on to the top of your head. Use the hair cutting scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift a section of hair and cut it straight across. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair on the top of your head.

Step 5: Blend and Check

Use the clipper without a guard to blend the sides and back of your hair with the top. Check the mirror to ensure that the hair is even and there are no uneven spots.

Step 6: Trim the Edges

Use the hair cutting scissors to trim the edges around your ears and the back of your neck. This will give your haircut a clean and polished look.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.

- Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.

- Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can take longer than getting it done professionally.

FAQ

Q: Can I use any type of scissors to cut my hair?
A: No, you should use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant when using styling tools. Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain the style.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short at home, you can give it a try and see how it turns out. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't hesitate to ask for help if you're unsure about anything.
