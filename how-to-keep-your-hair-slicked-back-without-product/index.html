How To Keep Your Hair Slicked Back Without Product A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using hair products to keep your hair slicked back? Do you want to achieve a sleek and polished look without the use of any products? In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to keep your hair slicked back without product. Read on to learn more.

Description

Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile look that can be worn on any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. The traditional way of achieving this look is by using hair products such as gel, pomade, or wax. However, these products can leave your hair feeling heavy, greasy, and sticky. If you want to avoid using products, you can still achieve a slicked back look with a few simple steps.

How To

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving a slicked back look without product is to wash your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up or residue from previous products. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is towel-dried, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle any knots or tangles. This will ensure that your hair is smooth and easy to work with.

Step 3: Use a Blow Dryer

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while combing it back. Make sure to use the cool setting to avoid damaging your hair. This will help to set your hair in place and give it a sleek look.

Step 4: Use a Hair Tie

Once your hair is dry, use a hair tie to secure it in a ponytail at the back of your head. Make sure to tie it tightly to keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Twist Your Hair

Take the ponytail and twist it tightly. Once it's twisted, wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins to keep it in place.

Step 6: Use Hairspray

If you want to make sure your hair stays in place all day, you can use a light-hold hairspray. Spray it over your hair to keep it sleek and polished.

Tips

- If you have long hair, you may need to use more bobby pins to keep your bun in place.

- You can also use a headband to keep any flyaways in place.

- If you have curly hair, you can still achieve a slicked back look by straightening your hair before following the above steps.

FAQ

Can I achieve a slicked back look without product?

Yes, you can achieve a slicked back look without product by following the above steps.

How long will the slicked back look last without product?

The slicked back look will last as long as you keep your hair in place with a hair tie and bobby pins. You may need to touch up your hair throughout the day to keep it looking polished.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any build-up from products. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. And finally, get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these steps, you can achieve a sleek and polished look without using any products. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, slicked back hair is a timeless style that will never go out of fashion. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
