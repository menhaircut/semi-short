How To Pick A New Haircut Male A Guide For Men

Written by Eveline Nov 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle and wondering how to pick a new haircut male? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide on a new look. In this guide, we’ll break down the process step by step and offer some tips to help you find the perfect haircut for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick a New Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing a Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Pick a New Haircut

The first step in choosing a new haircut is to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles will suit different face shapes better, so it’s important to take this into account when deciding on a new look. Some common face shapes include:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Oval

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start to look for hairstyles that will complement your features. Consider your hair texture and thickness as well, as this can also impact which styles will work best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to help you pick a new haircut:

  1. Consider your face shape and features.
  2. Research different hairstyles that would suit your face shape and hair type.
  3. Take inspiration from celebrities or other men with similar face shapes to you.
  4. Consult with your barber or hairstylist to get their opinion and advice.
  5. Ask for a recommendation based on your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences.
  6. Bring in photos or examples of haircuts you like to show your barber or hairstylist.
  7. Discuss the maintenance and styling required for your chosen haircut.
  8. Decide on a haircut and let your barber or hairstylist work their magic!
  9. Don’t be afraid to make small adjustments or tweaks to the haircut during the appointment if needed.
  10. Ask for styling tips and product recommendations to help maintain your new haircut at home.

Tips for Choosing a Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will suit you and your features.
  • Find a barber or hairstylist you trust and who understands your hair type and preferences.
  • Be open to feedback and suggestions from your barber or hairstylist.
  • Take into account any upcoming events or occasions you may need to style your hair for.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about picking a new haircut:

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. On average, most men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their style.

What if I don’t like my new haircut?

Don’t panic! Hair grows back, and there are ways to style or adjust the haircut to make it work better for you. Talk to your barber or hairstylist about any concerns you have and see if they can make any changes or recommendations.

How can I maintain my new haircut?

Follow the styling and maintenance tips provided by your barber or hairstylist, and invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a natural bristle brush to gently detangle your hair and distribute natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to pick a new haircut male, it’s time to book that appointment with your barber or hairstylist and try out a new look! Remember to take into account your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences when choosing a style, and don’t be afraid to ask for advice and recommendations along the way.
