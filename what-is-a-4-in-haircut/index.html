What Is A 4 In Haircut

Written by Eveline Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're new to getting haircuts or simply don't know what a 4 in haircut is, don't worry, you're not alone! In this article, we'll explain all you need to know about this popular haircut style.

Description

A 4 in haircut refers to the length of hair that is left on the head after it has been cut. Specifically, it means that the hair is left at a length of 4/8 inch or 1/2 inch. This length is considered to be relatively short, but not as short as a buzz cut or crew cut.

How to

The 4 in haircut can be achieved with clippers or scissors. To do it yourself with clippers, start with the longest guard (usually an 8), then work your way down to a 4. Use the clippers to go against the grain of your hair, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up to the crown. Make sure to go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.

If you're going to a barber or hairstylist, simply ask for a 4 in haircut. They'll take care of the rest.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your hair with clippers at a 4 length:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Attach the #4 guard to your clippers.
  3. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head, going against the grain of your hair.
  4. Go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.
  5. Blend the sides and back with scissors or clippers, depending on your preference.
  6. Finish with styling products, if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting or maintaining a 4 in haircut:

  • Make sure to use sharp clippers or scissors for the cleanest cut.
  • Keep the length in mind when choosing a 4 in haircut - it may not be suitable for all face shapes or hair types.
  • Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will help maintain the length and shape of the cut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

What hair types are suitable for a 4 in haircut?

A 4 in haircut can work well on most hair types, but may not be suitable for those with very curly or thick hair.

Can I style a 4 in haircut?

Yes, a 4 in haircut can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, spiked up, or even with a part.

How often should I get a 4 in haircut?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the cut.

Hair Care

To keep your 4 in haircut looking its best, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat styling or chemical treatments, as this can damage the hair and affect the cut's overall appearance.

Overall, a 4 in haircut is a popular choice for those who want a short, low-maintenance cut that still has some length to it. With the right tools and techniques, it's easy to achieve and maintain this style. Just remember to keep the length and shape in mind when choosing this haircut and to keep up with regular trims to keep it looking its best.
