Best Haircuts For Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Oliver Sep 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new haircut that flatters your face shape and is easy to maintain? Look no further than this guide on the best haircuts for over 60! From short and sassy to long and flowing, there is a haircut for every mature woman. Read on to discover our top picks and learn how to care for your new hairstyle.

Best Haircuts for Over 60: Description

As we age, our hair changes in texture, color, and thickness. Many women over 60 find that their hair becomes thinner and drier, making it more difficult to style. However, with the right haircut and hair care routine, mature women can still look stylish and beautiful. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance pixie cut or a sophisticated bob, there is a haircut that will make you feel confident and chic.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for You

Before heading to the salon, consider these factors when choosing a haircut:

Face Shape

The shape of your face plays a big role in determining which haircut will flatter you the most. If you have a round face, opt for a bob or a layered cut that frames your face. If you have a square face, a long bob or a shaggy cut will soften your features. If you have an oval face, you're lucky – almost any haircut will suit you!

Hair Texture

Your hair texture will also impact which haircut will work best for you. If you have fine hair, a short, layered cut will add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a long bob or a shaggy cut will help to thin it out. If you have curly hair, a layered cut will help to define your curls.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, opt for a low-maintenance cut like a pixie or a bob. If you're more of a fashionista and love to experiment with different hairstyles, a long, layered cut will give you plenty of styling options.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut

Once you've chosen your ideal haircut, follow these steps to get the perfect style:

Step 1: Research

Before heading to the salon, do some research on the haircut you want. Look up pictures of celebrities or models with the same haircut, and bring these to your stylist for reference.

Step 2: Consultation

During your consultation, discuss your desired haircut with your stylist. They can offer advice on whether the cut will work for your hair texture and face shape, and can suggest any modifications to make the cut more flattering.

Step 3: Wash and Condition

Before cutting your hair, your stylist will wash and condition it. This will help them to see your natural hair texture and ensure a clean cut.

Step 4: Cut and Style

Using sharp scissors, your stylist will cut your hair according to your desired style. They will then blow-dry and style your hair, giving you a polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've got your new haircut, follow these tips to keep it looking great:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair

To prevent damage to your hair, avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. When you do use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, get a trim every 6-8 weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

A bob or a layered cut that frames your face is the most flattering for a round face.

What is the best haircut for fine hair?

A short, layered cut will add volume and movement to fine hair.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

A long bob or a shaggy cut will help to thin out thick hair.

Hair Care for Over 60

As we age, it's important to take extra care of our hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and shiny:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

As we age, our hair becomes drier, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Shampooing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so try to limit your shampooing to once or twice a week.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner will help to keep your hair soft and smooth, and can also help to detangle it.

With these tips and suggestions for the best haircuts for over 60, you'll be sure to find a cut that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair with the right products and regular trims, and you'll have healthy, beautiful hair for years to come.
