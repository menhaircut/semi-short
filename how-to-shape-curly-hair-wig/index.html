How To Shape Curly Hair Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

For those who love to switch up their hairstyle often, wigs are a great way to do so without damaging your natural hair. If you have a curly hair wig, it's important to know how to shape it properly to ensure it looks natural and beautiful. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to shape your curly hair wig.

  • How to Shape Curly Hair Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Shape Curly Hair Wig

Shaping a curly hair wig can be intimidating, but it's not as difficult as it may seem. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

The first step to shaping your curly hair wig is to wash it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep the curls intact. Rinse thoroughly to remove all the product.

Step 2: Detangle Your Wig

After washing your wig, gently detangle it using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid damaging the curls.

Step 3: Style Your Wig

Now, you can style your wig. Use a curl-defining cream or gel to enhance the curls. Scrunch your hair to create more defined curls.

Step 4: Air Dry Your Wig

Allow your wig to air dry. Do not use a blow dryer or other heat styling tools as they can damage the curls. If you need to speed up the drying process, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Step 5: Trim Your Wig

If you need to trim your wig, do so after it's dry. Use sharp scissors and cut small sections at a time. Be careful not to cut too much or you may ruin the shape of the curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide for shaping your curly hair wig:

Step 1: Prepare Your Wig

Before you start shaping your wig, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a wig stand or wig head to hold your wig in place while you work on it.

Step 2: Section Your Wig

Section your wig into manageable parts. Secure each section with a clip or hair tie.

Step 3: Shape Your Wig

Starting at the bottom section, use your fingers to shape the curls. Twist small sections of hair to create more defined curls. Use a curl-defining cream or gel to help hold the shape. Repeat this process for each section.

Step 4: Trim Your Wig

If you need to trim your wig, do so after you've shaped it. Use sharp scissors and cut small sections at a time. Be careful not to cut too much or you may ruin the shape of the curls.

Step 5: Shake Your Wig

After you've shaped and trimmed your wig, shake it gently to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Tips for Shaping Curly Hair Wig

Here are some tips to help you shape your curly hair wig:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep the curls intact.
  • Detangle your wig gently to avoid damaging the curls.
  • Use a curl-defining cream or gel to enhance the curls.
  • Air dry your wig to avoid damaging the curls.
  • Trim your wig carefully to avoid ruining the shape of the curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I shape my curly hair wig?

You should shape your curly hair wig as often as needed. If you notice the curls starting to lose their shape, it's time to reshape your wig.

Can I use heat styling tools on my curly hair wig?

No, you should avoid using heat styling tools on your curly hair wig as they can damage the curls.

How can I maintain the shape of my curly hair wig?

To maintain the shape of your curly hair wig, store it on a wig stand or wig head when you're not wearing it. Avoid pulling or tugging on the curls, and use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig gently.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Wig

To keep your curly hair wig looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your wig with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Detangle your wig gently using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Air dry your wig to avoid damaging the curls.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or wig head when you're not wearing it.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig.

Shaping a curly hair wig is easy with the right tools and techniques. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips to keep your wig looking its best. With a little practice, you'll be able to shape your wig like a pro!
