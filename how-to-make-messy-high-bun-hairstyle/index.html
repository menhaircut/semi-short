How To Make Messy High Bun Hairstyle

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for an easy and effortless hairstyle that you can rock on any occasion? Look no further than the messy high bun! This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still look chic and stylish. In this blog post, we will show you how to make a messy high bun hairstyle in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Messy High Bun Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Messy High Bun Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to make a messy high bun hairstyle:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start, gather all the supplies you will need. You will need a hairbrush, hair elastic, bobby pins, and hairspray.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. This will make it easier to create a smooth and sleek high bun.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair in a High Ponytail

Gather all your hair at the crown of your head and tie it in a high ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Create a Messy Bun

Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a messy bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to create a messy and effortless look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place. This will also help to tame any flyaways and frizz.

Tips for Making Messy High Bun Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you make the perfect messy high bun hairstyle:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Create a loose and messy bun for a more relaxed and effortless look.
  • Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to create a messy and undone look.
  • Accessorize your bun with hair accessories such as pins and headbands.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make the bun look more natural.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to make a messy high bun hairstyle?

A: It takes less than 5 minutes to make a messy high bun hairstyle.

Q: Can I make a messy high bun with short hair?

A: Yes, you can make a messy high bun with short hair. Use a hair extension or a hair donut to add volume and length to your hair.

Q: How do I make sure my bun stays in place?

A: Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place. You can also use a hair elastic to tie your hair in a ponytail before creating the bun.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Now that you know how to make a messy high bun hairstyle, it's time to try it out for yourself! This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and will make you look effortlessly chic and stylish.
