How To Dry Fine Hair For Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your fine hair falling flat and lacking volume? Drying your hair the right way can make all the difference. In this article, we’ll share our top tips and tricks for how to dry fine hair for volume.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Dry Fine Hair for Volume
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Drying Fine Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Fine Hair

How to Dry Fine Hair for Volume

The key to drying fine hair for volume is to avoid using too much heat or roughing up the hair too much. Here are our top tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume before you even start drying your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to distribute heat evenly and avoid frizz.
  • Finish by using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to drying fine hair for volume:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Flip your hair upside down and begin blow-drying on a low heat setting. Use your fingers to lift and separate your hair at the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is about 80% dry, switch to a medium heat setting and attach a diffuser to your hair dryer. Use the diffuser to dry the rest of your hair, scrunching it gently as you go to enhance any curls or waves.
  5. Finally, use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Roll the brush up to the roots, then blow-dry on a low heat setting for a few seconds before releasing.

Tips for Drying Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you dry your fine hair for maximum volume:

  • Avoid using a brush on wet hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot excess water from your hair, rather than rubbing it vigorously with a regular towel.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to wet hair before blow-drying.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer with adjustable heat and speed settings, as well as a diffuser attachment.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much while drying it, as this can cause frizz and break up any volume you’ve created.

FAQs

Here are some answers to common questions about drying fine hair for volume:

Can I use a straightening iron to add volume to my fine hair?

No, using a straightening iron can actually flatten your hair even more. Stick to blow-drying with a diffuser and a round brush to add volume.

Should I use a leave-in conditioner on my fine hair?

It depends on the conditioner. Look for a lightweight, volumizing leave-in conditioner that won’t weigh down your hair.

How often should I wash my fine hair?

It’s best to wash fine hair every other day, as washing it too often can strip away natural oils and make it look even flatter.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

In addition to drying your hair the right way, there are a few other things you can do to care for your fine hair:

  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to dry your fine hair for maximum volume and body. Give them a try and see the difference it can make!
