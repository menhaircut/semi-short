Mens Slicked Back Undercut Haircut The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Sep 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then the mens slicked back undercut is a great option. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in different ways and suits different face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll give you all the information you need to know about the mens slicked back undercut haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The mens slicked back undercut is a haircut that features short sides and a longer top that is slicked back. This hairstyle is inspired by the classic 1920s look and has recently made a comeback in popularity. The undercut refers to the shaved or closely trimmed sides, while the slicked back top can be styled in different ways, such as a pompadour, quiff, or messy look. The slicked back undercut is a low-maintenance and modern hairstyle that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions.

How to Get a Mens Slicked Back Undercut Haircut

To get a mens slicked back undercut haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. It's important to bring a photo or have a clear idea of the hairstyle you want, as the length and style of the top can vary. The undercut can be tailored to your preference, whether you want a skin fade, tapered cut, or disconnected undercut. The stylist will use clippers and scissors to create the undercut, while leaving the top longer. The hair on the top can be cut and styled according to your preference, whether you want a slicked back or messy look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style a mens slicked back undercut haircut:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. It's important to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and easy to manage.
  2. Apply a styling product to the hair on top. This can be a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on the hold and shine you want. Work the product evenly through the hair, starting from the roots to the tips.
  3. Use a comb to slick back the hair. Start from the front and comb back the hair, making sure it's evenly distributed and smooth. You can also create a side part if you prefer.
  4. Use your fingers to style the hair on top. This can be to create a messy or textured look, or to add volume and height to a pompadour or quiff.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or hair tonic to set the style and add shine.

Tips for Styling a Mens Slicked Back Undercut Haircut

Here are some tips to help you style a mens slicked back undercut haircut:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type and the style you want. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  • For a more natural and textured look, use your fingers to style your hair instead of a comb.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing the length and style of the top. A longer top can add height and balance to a round face, while a shorter top can suit a more angular face.

FAQs

How long does my hair need to be for a mens slicked back undercut?

The length of the hair on top can vary, but generally it should be at least 3-4 inches long. The sides are usually shorter, with a tapered or faded cut.

What face shapes suit a mens slicked back undercut?

The mens slicked back undercut can suit different face shapes, but it's important to choose the length and style of the top that suits your face. A longer top can add height and balance to a round face, while a shorter top can suit a more angular face.

What products should I use to style my mens slicked back undercut?

You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to style your mens slicked back undercut, depending on the hold and shine you want. It's important to choose a high-quality product that suits your hair type and the style you want.

Hair Care Tips for a Mens Slicked Back Undercut Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens slicked back undercut looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it in shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can build up and make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

With these tips and advice, you'll be able to rock a mens slicked back undercut haircut with confidence and style. It's a versatile and modern hairstyle that can suit different occasions and outfits, whether you're going to work, a party, or a casual day out. Remember to take care of your hair and choose the products that work best for you, and you'll have a sleek and stylish haircut that will turn heads.
