How Much Does A Long Hair Perm Cost

Written by Oliver Oct 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some bounce and curl to your long locks, a perm is a great option. But before you book an appointment at your local hair salon, you may be wondering how much a long hair perm will cost. In this article, we'll break down the average cost of a long hair perm, as well as some tips on how to care for your permed hair.

Description

Perming is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair, creating curls or waves. Long hair perms are a popular option for those with shoulder-length or longer hair, as they can add volume and texture to your locks. However, getting a perm can be a time-consuming process that requires some maintenance to keep your curls looking their best.

How To

If you're considering getting a long hair perm, the first step is to find a hair salon that specializes in perms. Look for reviews online and ask friends for recommendations. Once you've found a salon you're comfortable with, book a consultation with a stylist to discuss your desired look and the cost of the service.

The cost of a long hair perm can vary depending on a few factors, such as your location, the salon you choose, and the length and thickness of your hair. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $150 to $300 for a long hair perm.

During the perm process, your stylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its shape. They will then wrap your hair around perm rods and apply a neutralizing solution to set the curls in place. The entire process can take a few hours, so be prepared to spend some time at the salon.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of what to expect during a long hair perm:

Step 1: Consultation

During your consultation, your stylist will assess your hair and discuss the look you want to achieve. They may also do a strand test to ensure that your hair can handle the perm solution.

Step 2: Prepping Your Hair

Your stylist will shampoo your hair and dry it partially, leaving it slightly damp. They may also apply a protective product to your hair to prevent damage from the perm solution.

Step 3: Applying the Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, using a comb to distribute it evenly. They will then wrap your hair around perm rods, securing them in place with clips.

Step 4: Neutralizing Solution

After the perm solution has set, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a neutralizing solution to set the curls in place. They may also apply a conditioning treatment to your hair to restore moisture.

Step 5: Styling and Finishing

Your stylist will remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired. They may also recommend products to help maintain your new curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your permed hair looking its best:

  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a perm.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.

FAQs

How long does a long hair perm last?

A long hair perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how well you take care of your curls and how quickly your hair grows.

Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

You should wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm, as the chemicals can be harsh on your hair. It's also a good idea to consult with your stylist before making any major changes to your hair.

Can you perm your hair if it's already been colored?

Yes, you can perm your hair if it's been colored, but you should wait at least 2 weeks after coloring before getting a perm. It's also important to let your stylist know that your hair has been colored, as this can affect how the perm solution reacts with your hair.

Hair Care

Getting a long hair perm can be a great way to add some bounce and volume to your locks. However, it's important to take care of your permed hair to keep it looking its best. Follow these tips for healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and taking good care of your permed hair, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls for months to come.
