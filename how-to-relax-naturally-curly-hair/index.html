How To Relax Naturally Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you may sometimes find yourself wishing for straighter locks. While there are chemical treatments available to permanently relax your hair, these can be damaging and expensive. Fortunately, there are natural methods you can use to temporarily relax your curls and achieve a sleeker look. In this article, we will discuss how to relax naturally curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Relax Naturally Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Relax Naturally Curly Hair

The first step in relaxing your naturally curly hair is to wash and condition it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help your hair retain moisture and prevent damage.

After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to your hair. This will help to further moisturize your hair and make it easier to detangle.

Once your hair is detangled, you can begin to blow dry it. Use a round brush to pull your hair straight as you blow dry. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

After blow drying, you can use a flat iron to further straighten your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to ensure that each section is properly straightened.

Finally, use a serum or hair oil to add shine and tame any remaining frizz.

Step by Step

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  4. Detangle your hair
  5. Blow dry your hair with a round brush
  6. Straighten your hair with a flat iron
  7. Apply serum or hair oil for shine and frizz control

Tips

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to detangle your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or flat ironing your hair
  • Work in small sections when using a flat iron
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage

FAQ

Can I relax my curly hair permanently?

Yes, there are chemical treatments available to permanently relax your hair. However, these treatments can be damaging and expensive.

Will relaxing my hair damage it?

Temporary methods of relaxing your hair, such as blow drying and flat ironing, can cause damage if not done properly. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to prevent damage.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go longer between washes. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy, beautiful hair. Be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and deep condition your hair once a week. Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to detangle your hair, and avoid using hot tools too often. Finally, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and techniques, you can relax your naturally curly hair and achieve a sleek, straight look without damaging your hair or breaking the bank.
