Are Round Brushes Good For Curly Hair

Written by Robby Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly haired folks have been asking this question for years! While some swear by round brushes for creating the perfect blowout, others say they're a big no-no for curly hair. So, what's the verdict? Let's put this debate to rest once and for all.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Use Round Brushes on Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Round Brushes on Curly Hair
  • FAQ

Description

First things first, let's talk about what a round brush is. A round brush is a type of hairbrush with a circular barrel that can be made from various materials such as ceramic, metal, or plastic. Round brushes are commonly used for blowouts as they help add volume, smoothness, and shape to hair. However, using a round brush on curly hair can be a bit tricky.

How To Use Round Brushes on Curly Hair

Using a round brush on curly hair requires some skill and patience. Here are some tips to get you started:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your curls from heat damage.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  3. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness and length of your hair.
  4. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the round brush, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds and then gently pull the brush through the hair while blowing hot air on it.
  6. Repeat until you've gone through all the sections of your hair.
  7. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Tips for Using Round Brushes on Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of using a round brush on curly hair:

  • Choose the right size of the brush for your hair length and thickness. A smaller barrel is best for shorter hair, while a larger barrel is better for longer hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality brush made from natural bristles. These are gentler on curly hair and help distribute natural oils throughout the hair shaft.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your curls from heat damage.
  • Take your time and be patient. It may take a few tries to get the hang of using a round brush on curly hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using round brushes on curly hair:

Q. Will using a round brush on curly hair damage my curls?

A. Using a round brush on curly hair can cause damage if it's not done properly. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum and avoid using high heat settings on your blow dryer.

Q. Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

A. It's best to use a round brush on hair that is at least 80% dry. Using a round brush on wet hair can cause breakage and damage to your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your curls.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

So, are round brushes good for curly hair? The answer is yes, but with some caution and careful technique. With the right tools, products, and patience, you can achieve a beautiful blowout on your curly hair.
