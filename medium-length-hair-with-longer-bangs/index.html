Medium Length Hair With Longer Bangs The Perfect Hairstyle For 2023

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both chic and versatile, then medium length hair with longer bangs might be the perfect fit for you. Not only does this hairstyle work for various face shapes and hair textures, but it's also easy to maintain and style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about rocking medium length hair with longer bangs, including how to achieve it, tips for styling, and hair care.

Description

Medium length hair with longer bangs is a hairstyle that features hair that falls just above the shoulders to mid-back, with bangs that extend past the eyebrows. This hairstyle can be customized with layers, curls, waves, or straightened hair, making it a versatile look for any occasion. Additionally, the longer bangs add a touch of edginess and sophistication to the overall style.

How to Achieve Medium Length Hair with Longer Bangs

If you're considering getting medium length hair with longer bangs, it's important to start by finding a skilled hairstylist who can create the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type. Once you've found the right stylist, follow these steps to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the right length

Determine the length you want for your hair and the bangs. Medium-length hair is typically considered hair that falls between the collarbone and shoulder blades, while longer bangs can be anywhere from chin to eyebrow length.

Step 2: Cut your hair

Ask your stylist to cut your hair to the desired length, including the longer bangs. To create the perfect look, your stylist may add layers to your hair to create more volume and depth.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. You can use a curling iron, straightener or hair dryer to achieve the desired look. To add more volume, use a volumizing spray or mousse.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair with Longer Bangs

Now that you have achieved medium length hair with longer bangs, here are some tips for styling:

Tip 1: Use the right products

When styling your hair, make sure to use the right products. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair, and opt for lighter products like hairspray or mousse to add volume and hold your style.

Tip 2: Experiment with different styles

One of the best things about medium length hair with longer bangs is that it's incredibly versatile. Experiment with different styles, like beachy waves, straight and sleek, or curly locks, to find the perfect look for you.

Tip 3: Accessorize

Adding hair accessories like barrettes, headbands, or hairpins can elevate your hairstyle and add a touch of glamour to your look.

FAQ

What face shapes work best with medium length hair with longer bangs?

Medium length hair with longer bangs works well with various face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces.

How often should I trim my hair to maintain the style?

To maintain medium length hair with longer bangs, it's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks.

Can I style medium length hair with longer bangs for formal occasions?

Yes, medium length hair with longer bangs can be styled for formal occasions. You can add curls or waves to your hair and accessorize with hairpins or a tiara to add a touch of glam.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair with longer bangs looking its best, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can cause dryness and irritation.

Tip 2: Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so it's essential to limit your use of hot tools. If you must use a curling iron, straightener or hair dryer, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair, so it's essential to protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, medium length hair with longer bangs is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for 2023. Whether you're headed to the office, a casual outing or a formal event, this hairstyle can work for any occasion. By following our tips for achieving the perfect look, styling and hair care, you're sure to turn heads with your chic and edgy hairstyle.
