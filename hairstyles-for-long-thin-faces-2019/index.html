Description

Long thin faces can be both a blessing and a curse when it comes to hairstyles. While they tend to be versatile and can pull off a variety of different looks, finding the right style can be tricky. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for long thin faces in 2019, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Thin Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a long thin face, it is important to consider the overall shape of your face. Your goal should be to create a look that adds width and volume to your face without making it look longer. Some of the best styles for long thin faces include:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with long thin faces. This style adds volume and texture to your hair while creating the illusion of width. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for those with long thin faces. These bangs help to create the illusion of width and can be worn with a variety of different hairstyles. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, sweeping them to one side of your face.

Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are a classic style that can work well for those with long thin faces. This style adds volume and texture to your hair while creating the illusion of width. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob with layers throughout.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Long Thin Face

To achieve the perfect style for your long thin face, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will help to ensure that your style holds throughout the day.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing product

To add volume to your hair, apply a volumizing product to your roots. This will help to create the illusion of thickness and fullness.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron

To add texture and volume to your hair, use a curling iron to create loose waves. This will help to create the illusion of thickness and fullness.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To ensure that your style holds throughout the day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Maintaining your hairstyle is key to ensuring that it looks great day in and day out. Here are some tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking its best:

Tip 1: Use the right products

Using the right products is key to keeping your hair looking its best. Look for volumizing products that will help to add thickness and fullness to your hair.

Tip 2: Get regular trims

Getting regular trims is important for maintaining the shape and style of your hair. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid over-styling

Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Stick to simple styles that are easy to maintain and won't require a lot of heat styling.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a long thin face?

The best haircut for a long thin face is one that adds volume and texture to your hair, while creating the illusion of width. Some of the best styles for long thin faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and bob haircuts.

What is the best way to style long thin hair?

The best way to style long thin hair is to add volume and texture. This can be achieved through the use of volumizing products, blow-drying, and curling irons.

How can I prevent my hair from falling flat?

To prevent your hair from falling flat, use a volumizing product at the roots and finish with a light mist of hairspray. Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Long Thin Faces

Keeping your hair healthy and looking its best is key to maintaining any hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Tip 2: Condition regularly

Regular conditioning is key to keeping your hair soft, smooth, and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

Heat can cause damage and breakage, so it's important to protect your hair from heat styling tools. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoid using them too frequently.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your long thin face in 2019. With the right style and proper maintenance, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful day in and day out.
