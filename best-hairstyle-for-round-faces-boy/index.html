The Best Hairstyle For Round Faces Boy A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Sep 16, 2023 · 3 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, it’s important to choose one that compliments your face shape. If you’re a boy with a round face, you may find it difficult to find the perfect hairstyle. However, with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best features and create a sharp, stylish look. In this article, we’ll provide a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for round faces boys.

Description:

A round face is characterized by its soft, curved edges and a lack of sharp angles. The width and length of a round face are usually equal, and the jawline is less defined. To create a more angular appearance, it’s important to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of the head while keeping the sides short.

How to:

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to identify your face shape. If you have a round face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head while keeping the sides short. This will help elongate your face and create a more angular appearance.

Step by Step:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your face. If they’re equal, you have a round face.

Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head

Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or a messy quiff, are great choices for boys with round faces.

Step 3: Keep the sides short

To create a more angular appearance, keep the sides short. A buzz cut or a fade are great options.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Use hair products such as pomade or wax to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new!

FAQ:

What hairstyles should boys with round faces avoid?

Boys with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of their heads, such as a bowl cut or a mushroom cut.

Can boys with round faces have long hair?

Yes, boys with round faces can have long hair, but it’s important to keep it layered and textured to create a more angular appearance.

How often should boys with round faces get a haircut?

It’s recommended that boys with round faces get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their hairstyle’s shape and structure.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use hair products in moderation to avoid buildup and weigh down your hair. Regular trims are also essential for maintaining healthy hair.

With the right hairstyle, boys with round faces can look sharp and stylish. By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style.
