How To Get Dark Red Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair color? If you're considering a bold and stunning look, dark red hair might be the perfect choice for you. This rich and vibrant shade can complement a variety of skin tones and hair types. In this article, we'll show you how to get dark red hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Dark Red Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Dark Red Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Dark Red Hair

How to Get Dark Red Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about what you'll need to achieve dark red hair:

  • Dark red hair dye
  • Developer
  • Conditioner
  • Gloves
  • Applicator brush
  • Bowl

Now that you have your supplies ready, let's get started on achieving that bold and beautiful dark red hair color.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepping Your Hair

Before applying any hair dye, make sure to wash and dry your hair thoroughly. This will ensure that the dye sticks to your hair evenly. You should also apply a thin layer of petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent any staining on your skin.

Step 2: Mixing the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to mix the dye and developer together in a bowl. Make sure to wear gloves when handling the dye to prevent any staining on your hands.

Step 3: Applying the Dye

Using an applicator brush, apply the hair dye to your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate your hair evenly with the dye.

Step 4: Waiting Time

Leave the hair dye on your hair according to the instructions on the box. This usually ranges from 20-40 minutes depending on the brand and shade.

Step 5: Rinsing Your Hair

After the waiting time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear. Apply a conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 6: Drying Your Hair

Pat your hair dry with a towel and avoid using any heat styling tools right away. Let your hair air dry or use a cool setting on your hair dryer.

Tips for Maintaining Dark Red Hair

After achieving your desired dark red hair color, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent the color from fading
  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often

FAQ

Can I achieve dark red hair on dark hair?

Yes, you can achieve dark red hair on dark hair. However, you may need to bleach your hair first to allow the color to show up more vibrantly.

How often do I need to touch up my dark red hair?

Touch-up frequency depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the color fades. On average, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Dark Red Hair

It's important to take care of your hair after dyeing it to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips for dark red hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use hair products specifically formulated for colored hair
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorinated water
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid over-washing your hair

With these tips and the step-by-step guide, you can achieve beautiful and bold dark red hair. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
