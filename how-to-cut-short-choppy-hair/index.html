How To Cut Short Choppy Hair Step By Step Guide And Tips

Written by Aprilia Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choppy haircuts have been trendy for years and are loved for their effortless and edgy look. Short choppy hair, in particular, is perfect for women who want to add some texture and volume to their hair. However, getting the perfect choppy haircut can be challenging, especially if you're doing it yourself. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut short choppy hair step by step and give you some tips to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Cut Short Choppy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here's a list of things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut. Once your hair is dry, comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the back section, take a small section of hair, and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut small, choppy pieces of hair at a time. Make sure you cut at an angle to create texture and movement. Repeat this process on the other sections of your hair.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections, blend the layers together by taking small sections of hair and cutting at a slight angle. This will create a seamless transition between the layers and give your hair a natural look.

Step 5: Check the Length

After you've cut your hair, check the length to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven parts, trim them to achieve the desired length.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using your favorite styling products. Short choppy hair looks great when styled in messy waves or with a bit of texture.

Tips for Cutting Short Choppy Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short choppy haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to get clean cuts and avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time to achieve the desired texture.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and angles to achieve the perfect look.

FAQ

Can I cut my short choppy hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your short choppy hair yourself, but it's recommended to get a professional haircut at least once to learn the basics and get some tips.

What face shape suits short choppy hair?

Short choppy hair suits a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care for Short Choppy Hair

Short choppy hair requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking healthy and textured. Here are some hair care tips for short choppy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly and use a leave-in conditioner to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can achieve the perfect short choppy haircut at home. Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the best look for you, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and textured.
