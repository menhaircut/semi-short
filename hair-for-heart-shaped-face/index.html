Hair For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. But fear not, because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you a comprehensive guide on hair for heart shaped faces, including how to choose the right haircut, styling tips, and hair care. Read on to learn more.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of choosing the right hairstyle is to balance out these features and create a more proportionate look. This means choosing a haircut that adds volume to the lower half of your face and softens your forehead.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for a heart-shaped face, there are a few key things to keep in mind.

Length

Medium to long hair is ideal for heart-shaped faces, as it helps to balance out the wider forehead and narrow chin. If you have short hair, consider adding layers to create the illusion of length.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great option for heart-shaped faces, as they help to soften the forehead and draw attention to the eyes. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal, as they create a diagonal line that helps to balance out the wider forehead.

Layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out a narrow chin. However, be careful not to add too much volume around the crown of your head, as this can make your forehead look even wider.

Step by Step: How to Style Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's time to think about styling. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your hair hold its shape and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

If you're going to be using heat tools to style your hair, it's important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before you start styling.

Step 3: Use a round brush

If you want to add volume and bounce to your hair, use a round brush while blow-drying. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift your hair away from your scalp.

Step 4: Add waves or curls

Adding waves or curls to your hair can help balance out a narrow chin and add volume to the lower half of your face. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves, or try a beachy wave spray for a more natural look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To help your style hold its shape, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important no matter what your face shape, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best if you have a heart-shaped face.

Use a deep conditioning treatment

Heart-shaped faces tend to have fine hair, which can be prone to damage. Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Avoid harsh chemicals

Harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dye can damage your hair, making it more prone to breakage and split ends. If you do want to color your hair, opt for a gentle, ammonia-free formula.

Be gentle when brushing

Fine hair can be easily damaged by rough brushing, so be gentle when detangling your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush specifically designed for detangling, and start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart-shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume around the crown of your head, as this can make your forehead look even wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can emphasize the narrowness of your chin.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a heart-shaped face. Just be sure to add volume to the lower half of your face to balance out your forehead.

What's the best way to add volume to my hair if I have a heart-shaped face?

Adding layers and using a round brush while blow-drying can both help add volume to your hair. You can also try using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Conclusion

Finding the right hairstyle for a heart-shaped face can be challenging, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a balanced, flattering look. Remember to choose a length and bangs that complement your face shape, add volume to the lower half of your face, and take care of your hair with regular conditioning and gentle brushing.
