How To Get Big Hair Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for ways to add volume to your hair, you're not alone. Many people struggle with flat, lifeless hair that lacks body and bounce. Fortunately, there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to add volume to your hair and achieve the big, beautiful look you've always wanted. In this guide, we'll show you how to get big hair volume in a few easy steps.

1. Start with the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to achieving big hair volume is to start with the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume and body to your hair. These products will typically contain ingredients like wheat protein, which helps to strengthen your hair and add thickness, and panthenol, which helps to moisturize and condition your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair is to blow dry it upside down. This technique helps to lift your roots and create more volume at the crown of your head. To do this, simply flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to lift your roots as you go. Once your hair is dry, flip it back over and style as desired.

3. Use a Root-Lifting Spray

If you're looking for an extra boost of volume, try using a root-lifting spray. These products are designed to be applied directly to your roots and help to lift and volumize your hair. Simply spray the product onto your roots, then use your fingers or a comb to work it in.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is another great way to add volume and texture. To do this, take a section of hair and backcomb it by brushing it downwards towards your roots. Once you've teased your hair, smooth it out on top with a brush or your fingers to create a more polished look.

5. Add Volume with Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers are a great tool for adding volume to your hair. Simply roll sections of your hair onto the rollers and leave them in for a few minutes before removing them. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots, giving you a big, beautiful look.

6. Try a Volumizing Mousse

Volumizing mousse is another great product for adding body and bounce to your hair. Apply a small amount of the product to your roots, then work it through your hair using your fingers or a comb. This will help to create volume and texture throughout your hair, giving you a fuller, more voluminous look.

7. Use a Round Brush to Curl Your Hair

If you're looking to add curls to your hair, try using a round brush to curl your hair while blow drying. Start by wrapping a section of hair around the brush, then use your blow dryer to heat the hair while you gently pull the brush through the ends. This will help to create soft, bouncy curls that add volume and texture to your hair.

8. Finish with Hair Spray

Once you've achieved your desired look, finish it off with a light mist of hair spray. This will help to hold your hair in place and add extra volume and texture. Look for a hair spray that is specifically designed for volumizing and adds shine to your hair.

9. Tips for Maintaining Volume

To maintain volume throughout the day, avoid touching your hair too much. The oils from your hands can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. You can also try using dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add extra volume.

10. FAQs

Q: Can I still achieve big hair volume if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve big hair volume if you have fine hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for fine hair, and try blow drying your hair upside down and using a root-lifting spray. You can also try using velcro rollers or teasing your hair to add extra volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. However, if you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to add volume?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume to your hair. Look for extensions that are specifically designed for volume, and choose a color that matches your natural hair color for a seamless blend.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from falling flat?

A: To prevent your hair from falling flat, avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair. Instead, opt for lightweight products that are specifically designed for volume. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain volume overnight.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve big hair volume and add body and bounce to your hair. Remember to start with the right shampoo and conditioner, blow dry your hair upside down, use a root-lifting spray, tease your hair, add volume with velcro rollers or a volumizing mousse, curl your hair with a round brush, finish with hair spray, and maintain volume throughout the day with dry shampoo and by avoiding touching your hair too much. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the big, beautiful look you've always wanted.
