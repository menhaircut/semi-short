How To Touch Up Frontal Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to wearing wigs, or you're simply looking for ways to keep your frontal wig looking fresh and natural, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll show you how to touch up your frontal wig so that it looks great every time you wear it.

Description

A frontal wig is a type of wig that features a lace or silk closure that covers the entire front of the wig. This closure gives the wig a natural-looking hairline and makes it easy to style the hair away from the face. While frontal wigs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without having to commit to a permanent change, they do require some maintenance to keep them looking their best.

How to Touch Up Frontal Wig

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

To touch up your frontal wig, you'll need the following supplies:

  • A wig stand or mannequin head
  • A comb or brush
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A small amount of wig glue or adhesive (optional)
  • A pair of scissors (optional)

Step 2: Detangle Your Wig

Before you begin touching up your wig, you'll need to detangle it to make sure that the hair is smooth and easy to work with. Use a comb or brush to gently remove any knots or tangles from the hair.

Step 3: Wet Your Wig

Once your wig is detangled, use the spray bottle to wet the hair. This will make it easier to work with and will help to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Now that your wig is wet, you can style it as desired. Use a comb or brush to create the look that you want, and use the wig glue or adhesive to secure the hair in place if necessary. If you need to trim any stray hairs, use a pair of scissors to carefully snip them away.

Step 5: Allow Your Wig to Dry

Once you've styled your wig, allow it to air dry completely before wearing it. This will ensure that the hair sets in the desired style and that the wig looks natural and polished.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when touching up your frontal wig:

  • Use a gentle touch when detangling your wig to avoid damaging the hair
  • Don't use too much wig glue or adhesive, as this can make the hair look stiff or unnatural
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig, as this can cause damage to the hair
  • Store your wig on a stand or mannequin head when you're not wearing it to help it maintain its shape

FAQ

How often should I touch up my frontal wig?

The frequency with which you touch up your wig will depend on how often you wear it and how much styling you do. As a general rule, you should touch up your wig every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking fresh and natural.

Can I wash my wig before touching it up?

Yes, you can wash your wig before touching it up if it's dirty or has product buildup. However, be sure to allow the wig to air dry completely before styling it.

Hair Care

To keep your wig looking great between touch-ups, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for wigs
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your wig, as this can damage the hair
  • Allow your wig to air dry completely before storing it
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat, as this can cause the hair to fade or become damaged

By following these tips and techniques, you can touch up your frontal wig and keep it looking fresh and natural for weeks to come.
