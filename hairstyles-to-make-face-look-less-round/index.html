Hairstyles To Make Your Face Look Less Round

If you have a round face and want to create the illusion of a more angular look, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles to make your face look less round, along with tips for caring for your hair and keeping your new style looking great.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to understand how to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to avoid styles that add too much volume or width to the sides of your face.

Instead, look for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, or that create angles and lines that help to elongate your face. It's also a good idea to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Some general tips for choosing a hairstyle for a round face include:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides
  • Look for styles that add height to the top of your head
  • Choose hairstyles that create angles and lines that elongate your face
  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long

Angled Bob

The angled bob is a classic hairstyle that can help to create the illusion of a more angular face. This style features longer hair in the front that gradually gets shorter towards the back, creating a tapered effect.

To make this style work for a round face, ask your stylist to keep the length of the hair in the front just above your chin, and to angle the cut so that it's shorter in the back. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of sharper angles.

Long Layers

Long layers can also be a great choice for those with round faces, as they help to create movement and texture that can make your face appear longer and more angular.

When getting long layers, ask your stylist to concentrate the layers around your face, as this will help to create a more flattering shape. You can also add some gentle waves or curls to the ends of your hair, which will further elongate your face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for those with round faces, as they help to create diagonal lines that can make your face appear longer and slimmer.

When getting side-swept bangs, ask your stylist to cut them so that they fall just below your cheekbones. This will help to create a more flattering shape and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Pixie Cut

While it's generally a good idea to avoid shorter hairstyles if you have a round face, a pixie cut can actually be quite flattering if done correctly.

To make a pixie cut work for a round face, ask your stylist to keep the hair on top of your head longer, and to taper the sides and back. This will help to create the illusion of more height and angles, which can make your face appear less round.

Updo

If you're looking for a more formal hairstyle that can help to elongate your face, an updo can be a great choice.

When getting an updo, ask your stylist to create some height at the top of your head, and to avoid styles that are too tight or pulled back. You can also add some loose curls or waves to the ends of your hair, which will help to create a more flattering shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found a hairstyle that flatters your round face, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Consider using styling products like mousse or gel to add volume and texture to your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just be sure to avoid styles that are too flat or that add too much volume to the sides of your face. Instead, aim for a sleek, straight look that adds height to the top of your head.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should definitely avoid if I have a round face?

A: Yes, there are a few hairstyles that tend to be less flattering for those with round faces. These include styles that are too short, too long, or too voluminous at the sides. You should also avoid styles that are too symmetrical or that don't create any angles or lines to help elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too frequently as this can strip your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can cause heat damage
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help prevent damage
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

With these tips and hairstyle ideas in mind, you'll be well on your way to finding a flattering look that helps to make your face appear less round. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
