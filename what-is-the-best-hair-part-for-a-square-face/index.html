What Is The Best Hair Part For A Square Face

Written by William Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what is the best hair part for a square face? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair part for your square-shaped face. From the description to the tips and hair care, we have got you covered!

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. The key to finding the best hair part for a square face is to soften the angles of the face and create an illusion of length. A well-chosen hair part can help to balance the face and highlight your best features.

How to Choose the Best Hair Part for a Square Face

Choosing the best hair part for a square face requires a bit of experimentation. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect hair part:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the best hair part for a square face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is square-shaped, you will have a prominent jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin.

Step 2: Experiment with Different Parts

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to experiment with different hair parts. Try a center part, a side part, or a deep side part to see which one flatters your face the most. You can also try a zigzag part or a diagonal part to create the illusion of length and soften the angles of your face.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

Your hair texture and length can also affect the best hair part for your square face. If you have thick hair, a deep side part can help to balance your face. If you have fine hair, a center part can create the illusion of fullness. If you have short hair, a side part can help to elongate your face.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Part for a Square Face

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best hair part for your square face:

  • Opt for a soft, tousled hairstyle to soften the angles of your face.
  • Avoid sleek, straight hairstyles that can accentuate the squareness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and volume.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a center part with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear a center part with a square face. However, a deep side part or a diagonal part may be more flattering.

Q: Can I wear a side part with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a side part with short hair. A side part can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of length.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid with a square face?

A: Avoid sleek, straight hairstyles that can accentuate the squareness of your face. Instead, opt for soft, tousled hairstyles with layers to create movement and volume.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Now that you know the best hair part for a square face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair part for your square face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
