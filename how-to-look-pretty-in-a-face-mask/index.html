How to Look Pretty in a Face Mask - Tips and Tricks 2023

Wearing a face mask has become a part of our daily routine due to the ongoing pandemic. However, it is still possible to look pretty while wearing a mask. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to look pretty in a face mask.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Look Pretty in a Face Mask
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

Hair Care

When wearing a face mask, your hair can either make or break your look. Make sure your hair is styled and neat. You can opt for a simple hairstyle such as a ponytail or bun. You can also add a hair accessory such as a headband or hair clip to add some style to your look.

How to Look Pretty in a Face Mask

Here are some ways on how to look pretty in a face mask:

Choose the Right Face Mask

The first step in looking pretty in a face mask is choosing the right one. Choose a face mask that complements your style and personality. You can choose from different designs, colors, and patterns. Make sure the face mask fits your face properly and is comfortable to wear.

Focus on Your Eyes

Since your face mask covers most of your face, you can focus on your eyes to make them stand out. Wear mascara, eyeliner, and eyeshadow to enhance your eyes. You can also use false eyelashes to add some drama to your look.

Use Highlighter

Highlighter can help brighten up your face and make you look more awake. Apply highlighter on the high points of your face such as your cheekbones, cupid's bow, and the bridge of your nose.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to look pretty in a face mask:

Step 1: Cleanse and Moisturize Your Face

Before putting on your face mask, make sure to cleanse and moisturize your face. This will help keep your skin hydrated and prevent breakouts.

Step 2: Apply Makeup

Apply makeup to enhance your features. Focus on your eyes and use highlighter to brighten up your face.

Step 3: Put on Your Face Mask

Put on your face mask and make sure it fits your face properly. Adjust the straps if necessary.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in a neat and simple way. You can add a hair accessory to add some style to your look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks:

  • Choose a face mask that complements your outfit
  • Use a lip balm to keep your lips hydrated
  • Wear statement earrings to add some style to your look
  • Use a setting spray to keep your makeup in place

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent my glasses from fogging up while wearing a face mask?

A: You can prevent your glasses from fogging up by placing a tissue or piece of cloth inside your mask where it sits on your nose. This will absorb the moisture and prevent it from fogging up your glasses.

Q: How often should I wash my face mask?

A: You should wash your face mask after every use to prevent the spread of germs and bacteria.

Q: Can I wear makeup under my face mask?

A: Yes, you can wear makeup under your face mask. However, make sure to use products that are lightweight and won't clog your pores.

Q: Can I wear a face mask with a beard?

A: Yes, you can wear a face mask with a beard. Make sure the face mask fits your face properly and covers your nose and mouth.


By following these tips and tricks, you can look pretty in a face mask. Remember to choose a face mask that complements your style and personality, focus on your eyes, and use highlighter to brighten up your face. Stay safe and stay pretty!
