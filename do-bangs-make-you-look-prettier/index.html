Do Bangs Make You Look Prettier The Truth Revealed

Written by Aprilia Nov 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, you're probably wondering if they'll make you look prettier. Bangs have been a popular hairstyle for decades, and they continue to be a go-to for many women. But, do they actually make you look more attractive? In this article, we'll explore the truth behind the myth and give you some tips on how to rock bangs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs at Home
  • Tips for Styling Bangs
  • FAQs about Bangs

How to Get Bangs

Before we dive into whether bangs make you look prettier, let's talk about how to get them. The best way to get bangs is to go to a professional hairstylist. They'll be able to assess your face shape and recommend the best type of bangs for you. However, if you're feeling daring, you can cut your own bangs at home.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs at Home

If you're going to cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  3. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  4. Cut the hair straight across.
  5. Trim any long pieces or unevenness.
  6. Style your bangs as desired.

Remember, cutting your own bangs can be risky, so proceed with caution. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's always best to leave it to the professionals.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Now that you have your bangs, it's time to style them. Here are some tips to make your bangs look their best:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume.
  • Straighten your bangs with a flat iron for a sleek look.
  • Use a curling iron to curl your bangs away from your face for a flirty look.
  • Try different hairstyles with your bangs, like a messy bun or ponytail.
  • Don't forget to trim your bangs every few weeks to keep them looking fresh.

FAQs about Bangs

Do bangs make you look younger?

Yes, bangs can make you look younger. They cover wrinkles on the forehead and soften facial features.

Do bangs make your face look rounder?

It depends on the type of bangs you get. Blunt bangs can make your face look rounder, while side-swept bangs can elongate your face.

Do bangs make your hair greasy?

Not necessarily. However, if you have oily skin, your bangs may collect oil and become greasy more quickly.

Hair Care for Bangs

Bangs require a little extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs every other day to prevent them from getting too oily.
  • Blow-dry your bangs immediately after washing to prevent them from air-drying in a weird position.
  • Use a dry shampoo on your bangs to absorb any excess oil.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum to tame any frizz or flyaways.

So, do bangs make you look prettier? The answer is subjective. Bangs can enhance your features and give you a fresh new look, but they may not be for everyone. Ultimately, the decision to get bangs should be based on your personal style and preferences. If you're considering bangs, consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you.
