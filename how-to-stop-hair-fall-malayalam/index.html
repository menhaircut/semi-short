How To Stop Hair Fall Malayalam Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing clumps of hair on your shower floor or comb every day? Hair fall is a common problem faced by many people, and it can be caused by various factors such as stress, poor diet, hormonal changes, and genetics. But don't worry, there are ways to prevent and stop hair fall. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall Malayalam and provide some useful tips and tricks. Let's get started!

How to Stop Hair Fall Malayalam: The Basics

Before we dive into the tips and tricks to stop hair fall, let's first understand the basics of hair growth and fall. Typically, a person loses around 50 to 100 strands of hair per day, which is considered normal. However, if you notice excessive hair fall or a receding hairline, it's time to take action. Here are some simple steps you can take to prevent hair fall:

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

Your hair needs proper nutrition to grow strong and healthy. Make sure you include foods rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and biotin in your diet. Some examples of such foods are spinach, eggs, nuts, and fish. Also, drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes, hair sprays, and gels can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Try to limit the use of such products and opt for natural alternatives instead.

3. Be Gentle While Washing and Brushing Your Hair

Washing and brushing your hair too aggressively can cause damage and lead to hair fall. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and avoid rubbing your hair vigorously while drying it. Also, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

How to Stop Hair Fall Malayalam: Tips and Tricks

Now that you know the basics of preventing hair fall, let's look at some tips and tricks to stop hair fall Malayalam:

1. Apply Onion Juice

Onion juice is known to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Extract the juice from an onion and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Do this once or twice a week for best results.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for 5-10 minutes every day.

3. Use Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel is rich in vitamins and minerals that can nourish your hair and prevent hair fall. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off.

4. Try Essential Oils

Essential oils such as lavender, peppermint, and rosemary are known to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil or olive oil and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for an hour before washing it off.

FAQ: How to Stop Hair Fall Malayalam

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress is one of the major causes of hair fall. Try to manage your stress levels by practicing meditation, yoga, or any other relaxation technique.

Q. Can genetics cause hair fall?

A. Yes, genetics can play a role in hair fall. If you have a family history of baldness or hair thinning, you are more likely to experience hair fall.

Q. How long does it take to see results?

A. It may take a few weeks or months to see visible results. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Hair Care: Conclusion

So, there you have it, some useful tips and tricks on how to stop hair fall Malayalam. Remember, the key to preventing hair fall is to maintain a healthy diet, avoid harsh chemicals, and be gentle with your hair. Try out these tips and see which ones work best for you. If you notice excessive hair fall even after trying these tips, it's best to consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist. Happy hair care!
