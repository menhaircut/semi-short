Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles For Men With Thin Hair

Are you a man with an oblong face shape and thin hair? If so, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. You want to create balance and symmetry with your facial features while also adding volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and hairstyle ideas that will flatter your oblong face shape and make the most of your thin hair.

Description: Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles for Men with Thin Hair

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow shape with prominent cheekbones and a pointed chin. Men with this face shape often have a prominent forehead and a straight jawline. When it comes to thin hair, the lack of volume and texture can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that suits their face shape. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, men with oblong face shapes and thin hair can achieve a stylish and flattering look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your oblong face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine which parts of your face are the widest and narrowest.
  4. Use the measurements to determine your face shape, whether it's oval, round, square, heart, or oblong.

Step by Step Guide: Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles for Men with Thin Hair

Now that you have determined your face shape, let's take a look at some hairstyle ideas that will flatter your oblong face shape and thin hair.

1. Short and Textured Haircut

A short and textured haircut is perfect for men with oblong face shapes and thin hair. This style adds volume and texture to your hair while also creating balance with your facial features. Ask your stylist to cut the sides and back short while leaving the top longer and textured. You can style it with a matte finish product for a casual and effortless look.

2. Side Parted Hairstyle

A side-parted hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that works well with an oblong face shape. This style adds volume and texture to your hair while creating balance with your facial features. Ask your stylist to cut the sides and back short while keeping the top longer and side-parted. You can style it with a pomade for a sleek and polished finish.

3. Messy Hairstyle

A messy hairstyle is a great way to add volume and texture to your thin hair while also creating a relaxed and casual look. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in layers and add texture to the top. You can style it with a texturizing spray for a messy and effortless finish.

Tips for Maintaining Oblog Face Shape Hairstyles for Men with Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions: Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles for Men with Thin Hair

What hairstyles are best for men with oblong face shapes and thin hair?

Short and textured haircuts, side-parted hairstyles, and messy hairstyles are great options for men with oblong face shapes and thin hair.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline and comparing the measurements to determine which parts of your face are the widest and narrowest.

How do I maintain my oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thin hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-dry your hair with a round brush, avoid using heavy products, and visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shape Hairstyles for Men with Thin Hair

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining your oblong face shape hairstyles for men with thin hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thinning hair to prevent breakage and damage. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause heat damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools on your hair. And finally, eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, men with oblong face shapes and thin hair can achieve a stylish and flattering look.
