Description

Mens haircuts skin fade long on top is a popular trend nowadays. This type of hairstyle features a short fade on the sides and back that gradually blends into longer hair on top. The skin fade is a modern take on the classic taper fade, and it involves shaving the sides and back of the head extremely close to the skin. This creates a sharp contrast between the short hair and the longer hair on top, which can be styled in various ways depending on personal preference.

How To

If you are interested in getting a mens haircut skin fade long on top, here are some tips on how to achieve the look:

1. Find a good barber

It is important to find a barber who is experienced in cutting mens haircuts skin fade long on top. Look for someone who has a portfolio of similar haircuts and can give you advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

2. Decide on your desired length

You can choose how long you want your hair to be on top. Some prefer a shorter length, while others opt for a longer style. It is important to communicate with your barber on what you want, so they can create the perfect haircut for you.

3. Choose the right fade

There are different types of fades to choose from, such as low, mid, and high fades. The skin fade is the most popular and creates a sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer top.

4. Style your hair

Once your haircut is complete, you can style your hair in various ways. You can slick it back, leave it messy, or add some texture with a styling product.
