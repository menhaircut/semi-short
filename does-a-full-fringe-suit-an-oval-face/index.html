Does A Full Fringe Suit An Oval Face

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle with a full fringe but unsure if it will suit your oval face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the benefits of a full fringe for oval faces, how to style it, and some tips and tricks to keep your new hairstyle looking fresh.

Table of Contents

  • Benefits of a Full Fringe for Oval Faces
  • How to Style a Full Fringe for Oval Faces
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Full Fringe

Benefits of a Full Fringe for Oval Faces

Contrary to popular belief, oval faces can benefit greatly from a full fringe. The oval face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off many different hairstyles, including a full fringe. A full fringe can complement your oval face shape by:

  • Softening angular features
  • Accentuating your eyes
  • Creating a youthful look
  • Adding dimension to your face

A full fringe can also help balance out your facial features if you have a prominent forehead or a long face.

How to Style a Full Fringe for Oval Faces

Styling a full fringe for oval faces is relatively easy and can be customized to your liking. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start by blow-drying your fringe from side to side with a round brush to create volume.
  2. Once dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  3. To add texture, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo and scrunch your fringe with your fingers.
  4. If you want a more polished look, use a small amount of hair serum to smooth out any flyaways.

Remember to always use heat protectant when using hot tools and avoid washing your fringe every day to prevent it from becoming greasy.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Full Fringe

Here are some tips and tricks to keep your full fringe looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your fringe at the desired length.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your fringe looking fresh between washes.
  • Avoid touching your fringe throughout the day to prevent it from becoming oily.
  • Experiment with different partings to switch up your look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will a full fringe suit all oval face shapes?

While a full fringe can complement most oval face shapes, it may not be suitable for those with very short foreheads or long faces. It's best to consult with your hairstylist to determine if a full fringe is right for you.

Can I style my full fringe differently?

Yes! You can experiment with different partings, lengths, and textures to create a custom look that suits you.

How often should I wash my fringe?

It's best to avoid washing your fringe every day to prevent it from becoming greasy. Aim to wash it every 2-3 days or as needed.

Hair Care for Your Full Fringe

Proper hair care is essential to keep your full fringe looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your fringe from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your fringe every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your fringe moisturized.
  • Protect your fringe from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a full fringe on your oval face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
