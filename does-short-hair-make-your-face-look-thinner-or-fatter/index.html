Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Thinner Or Fatter

Written by William Sep 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, but many people wonder if it makes their face look thinner or fatter. The answer is not straightforward, as it depends on several factors such as face shape, hair texture, and style. In this blog post, we will explore how short hair can affect the appearance of your face, and provide tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Short hair can make your face look thinner or fatter, depending on various factors such as face shape, hair texture, and style. It can also enhance your facial features and make you look more youthful and stylish. However, choosing the wrong hairstyle can have the opposite effect, making your face look wider or heavier than it actually is.

How To

Before deciding on a short hairstyle, you should consider your face shape. There are several face shapes such as round, oval, square, heart, and diamond, and each requires a different hairstyle to flatter it. For example, a round face looks best with a hairstyle that adds height and volume on top, while an oval face can pull off almost any short hairstyle.

You should also consider your hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you may need a haircut that adds volume and texture, while thick hair may require a haircut that thins out the bulk. Additionally, you should choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle, as well as the occasion you will be attending.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to determine the width and length of your face, and compare it to the following face shapes:

  • Round: width and length are roughly the same, with soft curves and no angles.
  • Oval: length is longer than width, with a gently rounded jawline and forehead.
  • Square: width and length are roughly the same, with a strong jawline and forehead.
  • Heart: wider forehead and narrower jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: widest at the cheekbones, with a pointed chin and forehead.

Step 2: Choose Your Hairstyle

Based on your face shape, choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features:

  • Round: choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume on top, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.
  • Oval: almost any short hairstyle works for this face shape, from a blunt bob to a shaggy pixie.
  • Square: choose a hairstyle that softens your features, such as a wavy bob or a layered pixie.
  • Heart: a chin-length bob or a side-swept pixie can balance out your features.
  • Diamond: a short bob with blunt bangs or a textured pixie can enhance your cheekbones.

Step 3: Maintain Your Hairstyle

Once you have chosen your short hairstyle, it's important to maintain it regularly to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim, and use hair products that suit your hair type and style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture before choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personality and lifestyle.
  • Maintain your hairstyle regularly to keep it looking fresh and stylish.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair and face shape:

Q: Does short hair make your face look thinner or fatter?

A: It depends on several factors such as face shape, hair texture, and style. Short hair can make your face look thinner if it adds height and volume on top, or wider if it emphasizes the width of your face.

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

A: A pixie cut or a short bob with layers can add height and volume on top, making your face look thinner.

Q: Can short hair suit any face shape?

A: Yes, short hair can suit any face shape if you choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: You should trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and style, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking healthy and stylish, and enhance your facial features.
