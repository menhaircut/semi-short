How To Make Your Hair Curly For Black Males A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to add some curls to your look? Well, you're in luck! This guide will show you how to make your hair curly for black males. We'll cover everything from how to prep your hair before styling to tips on how to maintain your curls once you've achieved them.

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Tools You'll Need
  • Curling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel, but don't rub it aggressively. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron. This is especially important for black males because our hair can be more prone to dryness and breakage.

Tools You'll Need

To make your hair curly, you'll need a few tools:

  • Curling iron
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hair gel or mousse (optional)

Curling Your Hair

Now that your hair is prepped and you have your tools, it's time to start curling. Here's how:

  1. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  2. Starting with the bottom section, take a small piece of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, holding it for 5-10 seconds. Be careful not to burn yourself!
  3. Release the hair from the curling iron and repeat until all sections are curled.
  4. Once all sections are curled, use a wide-tooth comb to gently separate the curls.
  5. If you want a more defined look, apply hair gel or mousse to your curls and scrunch them with your hands.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Now that you have your curls, here are a few tips to keep them looking great:

  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can dry out your curls.
  • If you do wash your hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair curly:

Can I use a diffuser instead of a curling iron?

Yes! A diffuser attachment on a blow dryer can be used to create curls as well. Simply scrunch your hair with the diffuser while blow drying on a low heat setting.

How long will my curls last?

This depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your curls. Generally, your curls will last for a few days to a week.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or relaxers on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your hair curly and keep it looking great. Have fun experimenting with different styles and remember to take care of your hair!
