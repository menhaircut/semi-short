How To Know What Hair Color Fits You Best A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Oct 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair color and want to try something new? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll explain how to know what hair color fits you best and provide tips on how to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know What Hair Color Fits You Best
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

How to Know What Hair Color Fits You Best

There are a few factors to consider when choosing the right hair color:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone is the most important factor in determining the right hair color for you. If you have warm undertones, choose warm colors like honey blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colors like ash blonde or burgundy. Neutral undertones can pull off both warm and cool colors.

Eye Color

Your eye color can also help determine what hair color fits you best. If you have blue or green eyes, choose cool colors like platinum blonde or black. If you have brown eyes, warm colors like caramel or chocolate brown will complement your eyes.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have dark hair, going too light can make you look washed out. If you have light hair, going too dark can look too drastic.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Identify your eye color.
  3. Consider your natural hair color.
  4. Choose a shade within two shades of your natural color.
  5. Consult with a professional stylist.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time for maintenance, choose a color that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.
  • Think about your wardrobe. Make sure the color complements your clothing.
  • Start with a subtle change. If you're not sure, try a semi-permanent color first.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for advice. Your stylist can recommend a color that will work best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the type of color you choose. Permanent color lasts up to eight weeks, while semi-permanent color lasts up to six weeks. Root touch-ups can be done every four to six weeks.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist. They can ensure the color is applied evenly and can provide guidance on how to maintain your new color.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Here are some tips to help maintain your new hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Limit heat styling as much as possible.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater as they can cause color to fade quickly.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your color looking fresh.

Now that you know how to choose the right hair color and how to maintain it, go ahead and book that appointment with your stylist. A new hair color can be just what you need to boost your confidence and change up your look.
