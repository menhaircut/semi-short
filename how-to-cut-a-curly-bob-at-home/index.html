How To Cut A Curly Bob At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, a curly bob might be the perfect choice for you. Not only is it chic and stylish, but it's also easy to maintain. The best part? You can cut it yourself at home! In this article, we'll show you how to cut a curly bob step by step.

How to Cut a Curly Bob at Home

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. Here's how to cut a curly bob:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid any jagged cuts.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. This will ensure that your curls are defined and easy to work with.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, create a middle part and then divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with one of the front sections, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your jawline. Repeat this process for the other front section.

Next, move on to the back sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers, pulling it straight down. Use your scissors to cut the hair straight across. Repeat this process for the other back section.

Step 5: Check Your Layers

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check to make sure your layers are even. If you notice any unevenness, go back and make small adjustments until you're happy with the results.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Apply your favorite styling product and scrunch your curls to enhance their shape and definition. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Tips for Cutting a Curly Bob at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curly bob:

  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to make small cuts and adjust as needed.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create softer, more natural-looking layers.
  • Make sure your hair is damp, but not soaking wet, before you start cutting.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your hair can be time-consuming, but the results are worth it!

FAQ

Can I cut a curly bob with straight hair?

While it's possible to cut a curly bob with straight hair, it's important to keep in mind that the final result will look different. Straight hair doesn't have the same texture and bounce as curly hair, so your bob may not have as much volume or movement.

How often should I cut my curly bob?

It's recommended that you get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your curly bob looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care for Curly Bobs

Now that you've cut your curly bob, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut a curly bob at home and keep it looking great. Happy cutting!
