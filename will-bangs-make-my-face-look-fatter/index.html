Will Bangs Make My Face Look Fatter

Written by Luffy Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried that they might make your face look rounder or fuller? This is a common concern for many people, especially those with round or chubby faces. In this article, we'll explore whether or not bangs will really make your face look fatter and provide some tips and tricks to help you make the best decision for your hair and face shape.

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle choice for many people, but they're not always the best choice for everyone. People with round or chubby faces often worry that bangs will make their faces look fuller or wider, while others believe that bangs can actually help to hide their facial features and create a more balanced look.

How to

If you're considering getting bangs but you're not sure if they're the right choice for your face shape, there are a few things you can do to help you make the best decision.

Step 1: Consider your face shape

The first step is to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that will help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to avoid heavy or straight bangs as they can make your face look boxy. Instead, opt for soft, curved bangs that will help to soften your features.

Step 2: Try on different styles

If you're still not sure which bangs will work best for your face shape, try on different styles. You can do this by using a virtual hairstyle app or by trying on wigs or clip-in bangs. This will help you get a better idea of what different styles will look like on your face and which ones will be the most flattering.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist

If you're still unsure, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape and provide you with tips and tricks for maintaining your new bangs.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting bangs:

  • Opt for side-swept or wispy bangs if you have a round face
  • Choose soft, curved bangs if you have a square face
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing bangs
  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to prevent them from becoming too long or heavy
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs and create volume

FAQ

Will bangs make my face look fatter?

Not necessarily. The key is to choose the right style of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept or wispy bangs can help to elongate a round face, while soft, curved bangs can help to soften a square face.

How do I know if bangs are right for me?

The best way to know if bangs are right for you is to try them on. Use a virtual hairstyle app or try on clip-in bangs to see what different styles will look like on your face. You can also consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

How do I maintain my bangs?

Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to prevent them from getting too long or heavy. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs and create volume. You can also use dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh.

Hair Care

When getting bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently choose the right style of bangs for your face shape and maintain healthy, beautiful hair. So go ahead and give bangs a try - you might just love them!
