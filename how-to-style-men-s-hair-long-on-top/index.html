How To Style Men s Hair Long On Top

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man with long hair on top, you might be wondering how to style it to keep it looking sharp and stylish. Long hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that will suit your face shape and personal style. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to style men's hair long on top.

  • How to Style Men's Hair Long on Top
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind

How to Style Men's Hair Long on Top

There are many ways to style long hair on top, depending on your personal preference and the occasion. Here are some popular styles to try:

1. Slicked-Back

The slicked-back style is a classic look that works well for both formal and casual occasions. To achieve this style, you will need a strong-hold pomade or gel. Apply the product to damp hair and comb it back, using your fingers to create a natural-looking part. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.

2. Undercut

The undercut is a trendy style that involves shaving the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top long. This style can be worn straight back or combed to the side. To maintain the style, you will need to get regular trims to keep the sides and back short.

3. Messy Textured

The messy textured style is a relaxed, low-maintenance look that works well for everyday wear. To achieve this style, you will need a texturizing spray or sea salt spray. Apply the product to dry hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

4. Top Knot

The top knot is a popular style for men with long hair on top. To achieve this look, tie your hair into a bun at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the bun in place. This style works well for both casual and formal occasions.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style men's hair long on top:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, leaving it clean and fresh.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing, towel dry your hair until it is damp but not soaking wet. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply your chosen styling product to your hair, using a small amount at first and adding more if needed. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a comb to spread it evenly.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired, using a blow dryer to set the style in place. For a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create a tousled effect.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style by applying a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place. This will help to keep your hair looking neat and styled throughout the day.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling men's hair long on top:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Use quality styling products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and styled.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and brush to help you achieve your desired style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best product to use for styling men's hair long on top?

The best product to use will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For a slicked-back look, use a strong-hold pomade or gel. For a messy, textured look, use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

Can I style my hair long on top for a formal occasion?

Yes, you can style your hair long on top for a formal occasion. The slicked-back style is a classic look that works well for black-tie events and weddings.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair looking healthy and styled:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your long hair looking stylish and fashionable. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.
