Curtain Bangs For Round Face Shape The Perfect Hairstyle For 2023

Written by Petter Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face shape, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. You want something that will flatter your face shape, but also be trendy and stylish. If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle for 2023, curtain bangs for round face shape might be just what you need. In this blog post, we'll be exploring everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round face shape, from how to get them to how to care for them.

Description: What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a hairstyle that frames the face with long, wispy bangs that are parted in the middle, just like a curtain. This hairstyle has been popular for several years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making them perfect for any occasion.

How to Get Curtain Bangs for Round Face Shape

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs for round face shape, there are a few things you should consider. First, you'll need to find a hairstylist who has experience working with round face shapes. They'll be able to advise you on the best length and style for your face shape. Once you've found a hairstylist, book a consultation appointment to discuss your options.

During your consultation, your hairstylist will assess your face shape and recommend a length and style that will flatter your features. They may suggest a shorter length if you have a smaller forehead, or a longer length if you have a larger forehead. They'll also take into account your hair texture and lifestyle when recommending a style.

Step by Step: How to Style Curtain Bangs for Round Face Shape

Styling curtain bangs for round face shape is relatively simple. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your bangs in the middle.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush, pulling them to each side as you go.
  4. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs for Round Face Shape

Like any hairstyle, curtain bangs require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your new bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your bangs with hot tools.
  • Wash your bangs every other day to avoid over-washing and drying them out.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Curtain Bangs for Round Face Shape

Q: Will curtain bangs work on my round face shape?

A: Yes! Curtain bangs are a great option for round face shapes because they help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Q: How often should I get my curtain bangs trimmed?

A: You should aim to get your curtain bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs in different ways?

A: Yes! Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear them straight and sleek or tousled and wavy.

Hair Care: Products to Use for Curtain Bangs for Round Face Shape

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, you'll need to invest in some quality hair care products. Here are a few that we recommend:

  • A heat protectant spray
  • A dry shampoo
  • A light hairspray
  • A round brush
  • A flat iron

Conclusion

Curtain bangs for round face shape are a trendy and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for 2023. With the right length and style, curtain bangs can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. If you're interested in getting curtain bangs for round face shape, be sure to find a hairstylist who has experience working with this face shape. And don't forget to take care of your new bangs with regular trims and quality hair care products.
