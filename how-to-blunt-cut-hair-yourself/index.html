How To Blunt Cut Hair Yourself

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Blunt cuts are a popular hairstyle that never goes out of style. This type of cut can be done on any hair type or texture and is easy to maintain. Blunt cuts are also versatile and can be worn in a variety of styles. If you're looking to save money on salon trips or just prefer to cut your hair at home, you can learn how to blunt cut hair yourself. In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieve a blunt cut, tips for success, and how to care for your hair after the cut.

Description

A blunt cut is a hairstyle that is cut straight across, typically at the ends. This creates a sharp, clean look that is easy to maintain. Blunt cuts can be done on long or short hair and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture. This type of cut is also great for people who want to achieve a polished, professional look.

How to Blunt Cut Hair Yourself

If you're ready to try blunt cutting your hair yourself, follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather your tools

You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will ensure that you can see the natural shape of your hair and avoid any surprises once you've cut it.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: front, back, and two sides. Clip each section of hair to keep it out of the way while you work.

Step 4: Start cutting

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut in a straight line.

Step 5: Check your work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even and straight.

Tips for Success

Cutting your hair at home can be nerve-wracking, but with these tips, you can achieve a great blunt cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Check your work frequently to ensure that your hair is even.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about blunt cutting hair at home:

Can I blunt cut curly hair?

Yes, you can blunt cut curly hair. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises once it dries.

Can I blunt cut my own bangs?

Yes, you can blunt cut your own bangs. Just make sure to use small, sharp scissors and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.

How often should I blunt cut my hair?

How often you cut your hair depends on how fast it grows and how well you maintain it. Generally, hair should be trimmed every 6 to 8 weeks to keep it healthy.

Hair Care

After blunt cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your blunt cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioner to your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to keep it healthy.

Now that you know how to blunt cut hair yourself, you can save money on salon trips and maintain your own hairstyle at home. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a great blunt cut that will make you feel confident and stylish.
