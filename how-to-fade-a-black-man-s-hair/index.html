How To Fade A Black Man s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a black man, you may be looking for a way to change up your hairstyle. One popular option is to get a fade. A fade is a haircut where the hair gradually gets shorter from the top of the head to the neck. It's a stylish and versatile look that can be worn in many different ways. In this article, we'll show you how to fade a black man's hair in easy-to-follow steps.

How To

Before we get started, it's important to note that fading hair is a skill that takes practice. If you're unsure about doing it yourself, consider going to a professional barber. But if you're feeling confident, here's how to fade a black man's hair:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a few things before you get started:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Barber cape or towel
  • Mirror

Make sure your clippers are fully charged and your scissors are sharp.

Step 2: Decide on Your Fade Style

There are many different types of fades, such as a low fade, high fade, or skin fade. Decide which style you want before you begin.

Step 3: Start with a Longer Guard

Start with a longer guard on your clippers, such as a #4 or #5. This will leave more hair on the top of your head. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the top longer.

Step 4: Gradually Shorten the Guard

Switch to a shorter guard, such as a #2 or #3. Use the clippers to fade the hair from the top down to the sides and back. Be sure to blend the hair so it looks natural.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Blend

Use scissors to blend any sections of hair that need it. You can also use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the hair on the back of your neck. You can either create a straight line or a rounded neckline.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Use a mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Now that your fade is complete, you can style your hair however you like. You can use hair products like gel, pomade, or wax to create a specific look.

Tips

  • Take your time. Fading hair is a skill that takes practice.
  • Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use scissors to blend any sections of hair that need it.
  • Check for evenness using a mirror.
  • Consider going to a professional barber if you're unsure about doing it yourself.

FAQ

How often should I get a fade?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your fade grows out. A good rule of thumb is to get a fade every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

What's the difference between a low fade and a high fade?

A low fade starts at the bottom of the hairline and gradually fades up. A high fade starts higher up on the head and fades down towards the neck.

Can I fade my hair with scissors?

It's possible, but it's much easier to use clippers. Scissors can be used to blend any sections of hair that need it, but you'll need clippers to create the actual fade.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Visit a barber regularly to keep your fade looking fresh.

By following these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can achieve a stylish and modern fade haircut.
