How To Style Short Curly Hair Without Heat

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you may be wondering how to style it without damaging it with heat. Fortunately, there are many ways to style short curly hair without using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners. In this article, we will go over how to style short curly hair without heat, step by step. We will also provide tips for maintaining and caring for your curly hair.

How to Style Short Curly Hair Without Heat

The key to styling short curly hair without heat is to work with your natural curl pattern. Here are some ways to do this:

1. Use Hair Products

Hair products are essential for styling short curly hair without heat. Here are some products you can use:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl cream
  • Defining gel
  • Hair oil

These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply them to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural curl pattern.

2. Twist or Braid Your Hair

You can also style your short curly hair without heat by twisting or braiding it. Here's how:

  1. Divide your hair into sections.
  2. Twist or braid each section.
  3. Leave the twists or braids in overnight.
  4. Take out the twists or braids in the morning.

You will have beautiful, defined curls without using any heat tools.

3. Pineapple Your Hair

Pineappling is a technique for preserving your curls overnight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair on top of your head, as if you were making a high ponytail.
  2. Secure your hair with a scrunchie or hair tie.
  3. Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase.

This will help to preserve your curls and prevent them from getting flattened while you sleep.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling short curly hair without heat:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, without rubbing or twisting.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, working it through with your fingers.
  4. Apply a curl cream or defining gel to your hair, scrunching it with your hands to enhance your natural curl pattern.
  5. If you want to twist or braid your hair, divide it into sections and twist or braid each section. Leave the twists or braids in overnight.
  6. If you want to pineapple your hair, gather your hair on top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie. Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase.
  7. In the morning, take out the twists or braids and scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short curly hair without heat:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • If you want to add volume to your hair, flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair with your hands while it's drying.

FAQ

Can I use heat on my curly hair?

While you can use heat tools on your curly hair, it's best to avoid them as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle and dry.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your curly hair no more than once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy.

What should I do if my curls are flat?

If your curls are flat, try flipping your head upside down and scrunching your hair with your hands to add volume. You can also try using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your short curly hair without heat, it's important to take care of your hair overall. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or hair dye.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your short curly hair healthy and beautiful, without resorting to heat tools.
