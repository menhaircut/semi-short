Best Haircut To Suit Heart Shaped Face

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you have a wide forehead and a narrow chin. This shape can be a little challenging when it comes to choosing the right haircut. You need a hairstyle that will balance your features and make your face appear more symmetrical. So, if you're wondering what haircut to suit heart shaped face, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll help you find the perfect haircut that will flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Heart Shaped Face

Choosing the right haircut for heart shaped face can be a little tricky, but there are a few things you can keep in mind to make the process easier. Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for your heart-shaped face:

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

First things first, you need to make sure that you have a heart-shaped face. Look in the mirror and check if you have a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you do, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Step 2: Look for Inspiration

Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or even on social media. Find some celebrities or models who have a similar face shape to yours and see what hairstyles work for them.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is an important factor when choosing a haircut. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers as they can make your hair look limp. If you have thick hair, you may want to add more layers to reduce the weight.

Step 4: Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a haircut, you also need to consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance cut that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you can opt for a more intricate style.

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut for Heart Shaped Face

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for your heart-shaped face, here are some tips to help you find the best style:

Tip 1: Opt for Bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out a heart-shaped face. If you have a wide forehead, opt for side-swept bangs that will draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes.

Tip 2: Add Layers

Layers can add movement and volume to your hair, making it look more balanced. If you have long hair, add layers around your face to soften your features.

Tip 3: Avoid Heavy Volume

While layers can add volume to your hair, you want to avoid adding too much volume at the crown of your head. This can make your face look even wider.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Best Haircut for Heart Shaped Face?

The best haircut for heart shaped face is one that balances your features. You can opt for a shoulder-length bob, long layers, or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Can I Wear Short Hair with a Heart Shaped Face?

Yes, you can wear short hair with a heart shaped face. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your features. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs or a layered bob can look great on a heart-shaped face.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for heart shaped face doesn't have to be difficult. Just remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a style. And don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts until you find one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
