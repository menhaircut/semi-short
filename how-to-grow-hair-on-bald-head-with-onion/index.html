How To Grow Hair On Bald Head With Onion

Are you struggling with hair loss and baldness? Do you want to regrow your hair naturally without relying on expensive treatments and products? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss how to grow hair on bald head with onion. Onion is known for its hair growth properties and has been used for centuries to treat hair loss and baldness. How does onion help in hair growth?

Onion contains sulfur, which is essential for the production of collagen. Collagen is a protein that helps in the production of new hair cells. It also contains antioxidants, which help in fighting free radicals that can damage hair follicles. Onion juice also has antibacterial properties, which can help in preventing scalp infections that can lead to hair loss.

How to use onion for hair growth?

There are several ways to use onion for hair growth. The most popular method is to extract onion juice and apply it to the scalp. You can also mix onion juice with other natural ingredients like coconut oil, honey, and lemon juice for added benefits. Another way is to include onions in your diet. Eating onions regularly can provide your body with the essential nutrients that are needed for healthy hair growth.

Step by step guide to use onion for hair growth

Here is a step by step guide on how to use onion for hair growth:

  • Peel and chop one medium-sized onion.
  • Blend the onion pieces in a blender or food processor.
  • Squeeze the onion pulp to extract the juice.
  • Apply the onion juice to your scalp using a cotton ball or brush.
  • Massage your scalp gently for 5-10 minutes.
  • Leave the onion juice on your scalp for 30-45 minutes.
  • Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo and water.

Tips for using onion for hair growth

  • Always do a patch test before applying onion juice to your scalp to check for any allergic reactions.
  • Use fresh onion juice for best results.
  • Do not leave the onion juice on your scalp for too long as it can cause irritation.
  • Use a mild shampoo to wash off the onion juice.
  • Repeat this process 2-3 times a week for best results.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. Can onion juice cure baldness?

A. Onion juice cannot cure baldness completely, but it can help in regrowing hair on bald spots.

Q. How long does it take for onion juice to show results?

A. It can take anywhere from 4-6 weeks to see visible results.

Q. Can onion juice be used on all hair types?

A. Yes, onion juice can be used on all hair types.

Hair Care Tips for Better Results

Here are some additional hair care tips that can help in improving hair growth:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and styling products on your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels as they can affect hair growth.

With these tips and the use of onion juice, you can effectively regrow hair on bald spots and improve overall hair health. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine for best results.
