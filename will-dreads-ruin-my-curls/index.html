Will Dreads Ruin My Curls A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting dreadlocks but worried it will ruin your curly hair? It’s a common concern, especially for those who have spent years perfecting their natural curls. In this article, we’ll dive into the question “will dreads ruin my curls?” and provide you with tips on how to maintain your hair’s health and beauty while having dreadlocks.

Description

Dreadlocks, or dreads for short, are a hairstyle that involves the matting and knotting of hair into rope-like strands. They have been around for centuries and are popular among different cultures worldwide. However, some people are hesitant to get dreads because they worry that it will ruin their natural curls. In this article, we’ll answer the question, “will dreads ruin my curls?” and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair while having dreads.

How to

The process of getting dreads involves sectioning your hair and twisting each section into a tight coil. Over time, the hair will lock and form a solid dread. This process can take several months, and during this time, you’ll need to care for your hair differently than you would with loose curls. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair while having dreads:

Step 1: Keep your scalp clean

It’s essential to keep your scalp clean while having dreads because dirt and buildup can cause your hair to smell and become itchy. You can wash your scalp with a residue-free shampoo and use a clarifying treatment once a month to remove any buildup.

Step 2: Moisturize your hair

Dreads can be drying to your hair, so it’s essential to moisturize your hair regularly. You can use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Protect your hair while sleeping

While sleeping, your hair can become tangled and frizzy, which can cause damage to your dreads. You can protect your hair by sleeping with a satin or silk pillowcase or wrapping your hair in a scarf or bonnet.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to maintain your hair’s health and beauty while having dreads:

1. Avoid using heavy products

Heavy products can cause buildup and prevent your hair from locking properly. Use lightweight products that won’t weigh down your hair.

2. Don’t over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair once a week or every two weeks.

3. Be gentle when detangling

Detangling your hair can be challenging with dreads, but it’s essential to be gentle to prevent breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your hair.

FAQ

1. Will dreads ruin my curls?

No, dreads will not ruin your curls. However, they will change the texture of your hair and require different maintenance than loose curls.

2. Can I still wear my hair curly with dreads?

Yes, you can still wear your hair curly with dreads. Once your dreads have matured, you can style your hair in different ways, including wearing it curly.

3. How long does it take for dreads to mature?

It can take anywhere from six months to a year for dreads to mature and become fully locked.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair while having dreads is essential to prevent damage and breakage. Make sure to keep your scalp clean, moisturize your hair, and protect it while sleeping. Also, be gentle when detangling and avoid using heavy products. With proper care, you can have healthy and beautiful hair with dreads.
