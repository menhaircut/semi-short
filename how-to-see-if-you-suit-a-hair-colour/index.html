How To See If You Suit A Hair Colour

Written by Eveline Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the perfect hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you have never coloured your hair before. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which shade will suit you best. In this article, we will provide you with some tips on how to see if you suit a hair colour.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine If You Suit a Hair Colour
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Determine If You Suit a Hair Colour

Before choosing a hair colour, you need to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone will help you decide which hair colour will suit you best. There are two types of skin tones: warm and cool.

People with warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. If you have warm skin tones, you will look best with hair colours that have warm undertones. These include shades of brown, red, and blonde, with golden or caramel highlights.

On the other hand, people with cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. If you have a cool skin tone, you will look best with hair colours that have cool undertones. These include shades of brown, red, and blonde, with ash or platinum highlights.

Another factor to consider is your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours will complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colours will make your eyes pop.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to see if you suit a hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye colour
  3. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual hair colour apps to see how the colour will look on you
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best hair colour for you

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you are willing to do
  • Start with a semi-permanent hair colour before committing to a permanent one
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your natural hair colour
  • Consider your wardrobe and how the hair colour will look with your clothing
  • Take into account any skin conditions or allergies you may have

FAQs

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it is essential to choose a hair dye that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for hair dyes that are specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

How often should I touch up my roots?

Root touch-ups will depend on the type of hair colour you choose. Some hair colours require touch-ups every four to six weeks, while others can last up to eight weeks. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best touch-up schedule for your hair colour.

Will my hair colour fade over time?

All hair colours will fade over time, but some will fade faster than others. To prevent fading, use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight.

Hair Care Tips

After colouring your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair colour looks great and lasts as long as possible.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and other factors, you can determine which hair colour will suit you best. Remember to take care of your hair after colouring it to maintain the colour and prevent damage.
