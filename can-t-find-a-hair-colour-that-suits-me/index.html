Can t Find A Hair Colour That Suits Me Here s What You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who has been struggling to find a hair colour that suits you, you're not alone. Many people go through this struggle, and it can be frustrating trying to figure out what works for you. In this article, we will discuss the reasons why you might be having trouble finding a hair colour that suits you and what you can do to fix it.

Why Can't You Find a Hair Colour That Suits You?

There are several reasons why you might be having trouble finding a hair colour that suits you. Some of the most common reasons include:

  • You're not considering your skin tone
  • You're not taking your eye colour into account
  • You're not considering your natural hair colour
  • You're not considering your lifestyle

It's important to consider all of these factors when choosing a hair colour. If you're not taking them into account, you might end up with a colour that doesn't complement you.

How to Find a Hair Colour That Suits You

Now that you know why you might be having trouble finding a hair colour that suits you, let's talk about what you can do to fix it. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect hair colour:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a big role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to go for cooler hair colours like ash blonde or platinum. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to go for warmer hair colours like golden blonde or copper.

Take Your Eye Colour into Account

Your eye colour can also help you determine which hair colour will suit you. If you have blue eyes, you'll want to go for cooler hair colours like ash blonde or light brown. If you have brown eyes, you'll want to go for warmer hair colours like caramel or honey.

Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

It's important to consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new hair colour. If you're going for a drastic change, you'll want to make sure the colour you choose complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have a busy lifestyle, you might want to go for a low-maintenance hair colour that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

FAQ About Finding a Hair Colour That Suits You

Q: Can I dye my hair a different colour if I don't like it?

A: Yes, you can always dye your hair a different colour if you don't like it. However, it's important to keep in mind that constantly dyeing your hair can cause damage.

Q: Should I go to a professional to dye my hair?

A: If you're unsure about dyeing your hair, it's best to go to a professional. They can help you choose the right colour and ensure that the dye is applied correctly.

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's been previously dyed?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if it's been previously dyed. However, it's important to make sure you're using the right products to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found a hair colour that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for coloured hair
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your new hair colour for longer.

Conclusion

If you're struggling to find a hair colour that suits you, it's important to consider your skin tone, eye colour, natural hair colour, and lifestyle. By taking all of these factors into account, you can find a hair colour that complements you and makes you feel confident. And remember, once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
