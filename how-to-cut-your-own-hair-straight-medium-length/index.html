How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Medium Length

Written by Eveline Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on trips to the hair salon? Do you want to save time and money by cutting your own hair at home? Look no further than this step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair straight medium length. With a few simple tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut from the comfort of your own home.

How To

To begin, gather the necessary tools: a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting.

Next, part your hair down the middle and clip one side up. Using your comb, grab a small section of hair from the unclipped side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across, making sure to keep your fingers at a 90-degree angle. This will ensure an even cut.

Continue this process, working your way up and around the head. Make sure to keep the sections even in thickness and length.

Once you have finished one side, unclip the other side and repeat the process. After you have cut both sides, check for any uneven spots and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair straight medium length:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut too much and regret it.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Dull scissors can damage hair and make it harder to achieve a smooth cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair straight medium length if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight medium length even if it's curly. Keep in mind that the length will appear shorter when the hair is dry and curly, so you may want to cut it a little longer than you initially planned.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends and promote hair growth.

What if I mess up while cutting my hair?

If you mess up while cutting your hair, don't panic. It's just hair, and it will grow back. You can always make an appointment with a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair straight medium length, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage hair and cause split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and professional-looking cut without leaving your home. Happy cutting!
