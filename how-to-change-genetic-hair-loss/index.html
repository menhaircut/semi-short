How To Change Genetic Hair Loss Tips Steps And Faqs

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling with genetic hair loss? It can be frustrating and stressful to lose your hair due to factors beyond your control. But don't worry, there are ways to change genetic hair loss and keep your hair healthy and full. In this article, we'll share tips, steps, and FAQs to help you regain your confidence and keep your hair looking great.

How to Change Genetic Hair Loss

Genetic hair loss, also known as male or female pattern baldness, is a genetic condition that affects millions of people worldwide. While there is no cure for genetic hair loss, there are ways to slow down or stop the progression of hair loss and even regrow hair.

The most effective solutions for changing genetic hair loss include:

  • Medications like minoxidil and finasteride
  • Hair transplant surgery
  • Low-level laser therapy

These treatments have been shown to be effective in clinical studies and can help you keep your hair and regain your confidence.

Medications

Minoxidil and finasteride are two medications that have been approved by the FDA for the treatment of genetic hair loss.

Minoxidil is a topical medication that is applied to the scalp twice a day. It works by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles and stimulating hair growth. Finasteride is an oral medication that works by blocking the production of the hormone dihydrotestosterone (DHT), which is responsible for causing hair loss.

Both medications have been shown to be effective in clinical studies, with minoxidil showing improvement in hair count and thickness, and finasteride showing improvement in hair growth and slowing hair loss.

Hair Transplant Surgery

Hair transplant surgery is a surgical procedure that involves taking hair follicles from one area of the scalp and transplanting them to another area where hair is thinning or balding.

The procedure can be done using two methods, follicular unit transplantation (FUT) and follicular unit extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of skin from the back of the scalp and harvesting hair follicles from it. FUE involves harvesting individual hair follicles from the scalp using a small punch tool.

Hair transplant surgery is a highly effective solution for changing genetic hair loss, with a success rate of up to 95%. However, it can be expensive and may require multiple sessions to achieve the desired results.

Low-Level Laser Therapy

Low-level laser therapy (LLLT) is a non-invasive treatment that uses red light to stimulate hair growth. The therapy works by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles and stimulating cellular activity.

LLLT can be done at home using a handheld device or in a clinical setting using a larger device. It is a painless and safe treatment that has been shown to be effective in clinical studies.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're considering changing genetic hair loss, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Consult with a dermatologist or hair restoration specialist to determine the best course of treatment for your specific condition.
  2. If medication is prescribed, follow the instructions carefully and be patient. It can take several months to see results.
  3. If hair transplant surgery is recommended, choose a reputable and experienced surgeon and follow post-operative care instructions carefully.
  4. If using low-level laser therapy, follow the instructions carefully and be patient. It can take several months to see results.
  5. Practice good hair care habits, including washing regularly, avoiding harsh chemicals, and using a wide-tooth comb.
  6. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as vitamin C, iron, and biotin.
  7. Reduce stress through exercise, meditation, or other stress-reducing activities.
  8. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as braids or ponytails.
  9. Avoid smoking, which can reduce blood flow to the hair follicles and contribute to hair loss.
  10. Be patient and consistent with your chosen treatment. It can take time to see results, but with persistence and dedication, you can change genetic hair loss.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

While genetic hair loss is often beyond our control, there are steps we can take to prevent hair loss and keep our hair healthy:

  • Practice good hair care habits, like washing regularly and avoiding harsh chemicals.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair.
  • Avoid smoking.
  • Reduce stress through exercise, meditation, or other stress-reducing activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can genetic hair loss be reversed?

While there is no cure for genetic hair loss, there are treatments available that can slow down or stop the progression of hair loss and even regrow hair.

Is hair transplant surgery painful?

Hair transplant surgery is performed under local anesthesia, so you will not feel any pain during the procedure. You may experience some discomfort or soreness after the procedure, but this can be managed with pain medication.

How long does it take to see results from medication or low-level laser therapy?

It can take several months to see results from medication or low-level laser therapy. It's important to be patient and consistent with your chosen treatment.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and full:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

Changing genetic hair loss can be a challenging process, but with the right treatment and care, you can keep your hair healthy and full. Consult with a dermatologist or hair restoration specialist to determine the best course of treatment for your specific condition, and be patient and consistent with your chosen treatment. With persistence and dedication, you can regain your confidence and keep your hair looking great.
