Curtain Bangs For My Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Oct 04, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, you may want to consider getting curtain bangs. These versatile bangs can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, heart, and square. In this article, we'll explore how to get curtain bangs for your face shape, step by step. We'll also provide tips on how to style and care for your new bangs. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs for My Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They are called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the drapes that hang on either side of a window. Curtain bangs can add texture and movement to your hairstyle, and they can also help to soften your features. They work well on a variety of face shapes, including oval, heart, and square. If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that can add some interest to your look, curtain bangs may be the perfect choice for you.

How to Get Curtain Bangs for My Face Shape

The first step in getting curtain bangs is to determine your face shape. This will help you to choose the right style of bangs that will complement your features. Here are some tips for choosing the right curtain bangs for your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, and can pull off a variety of bang styles. For curtain bangs, you can choose a wispy, textured style that falls just below your eyebrows. This will help to frame your face and highlight your features.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin. To balance out your features, choose curtain bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes.

Square Face Shape

Square faces have a strong jawline and a square forehead. To soften your features, choose curtain bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to frame your face and add some texture to your hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you've determined your face shape and chosen the right style of curtain bangs, it's time to get them cut! Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curtain bangs:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Your hair should be clean and free of any styling products.
  2. Section off the front part of your hair, from temple to temple.
  3. Separate the section of hair into two parts, one on either side of your head.
  4. Using scissors, cut each section of hair to your desired length. Remember to cut your bangs longer than you think you want them, as you can always go back and trim them later.
  5. Once you've cut your bangs, use a comb to part them down the middle.
  6. Style your new curtain bangs as desired. You can blow dry them straight or use a curling iron to add some texture.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Now that you have your new curtain bangs, you may be wondering how to style them. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new look:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs. This will help to add volume and texture.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs and create a sleek look.
  • Try using a texturizing spray to add some movement and definition to your bangs.
  • If your bangs are too long, don't be afraid to trim them yourself. Just remember to cut them a little bit at a time to avoid any mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

Curtain bangs can take anywhere from 2-6 months to grow out, depending on how fast your hair grows. If you're ready for a change, but don't want to commit to bangs long-term, curtain bangs can be a great option.

Can I wear my curtain bangs with a ponytail?

Absolutely! Curtain bangs can be styled with a variety of hairstyles, including ponytails, braids, and updos.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs are relatively low-maintenance, but they do require some upkeep. You may need to trim them every few weeks to keep them looking fresh, and you'll want to style them with the right products to keep them looking their best.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • If you're going to be out in the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your new curtain bangs with confidence. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a bold new look, curtain bangs can be a great option for any face shape. So go ahead and give them a try!
