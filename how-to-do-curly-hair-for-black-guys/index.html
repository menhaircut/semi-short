How To Do Curly Hair For Black Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a black guy with curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep your hair looking its best. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a great-looking style that will turn heads. In this guide, we'll show you how to do curly hair for black guys in easy-to-follow steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in styling your curly hair is to understand your hair type. Not all curly hair is the same, and different types of curls require different products and techniques. Here are some tips to help you figure out your hair type:

  • Check the curl pattern: Is your hair tightly coiled or loosely curled?
  • Check the density: Is your hair thick or thin?
  • Check the porosity: Does your hair absorb moisture easily or does it repel it?

Once you've figured out your hair type, you can start using the right products and techniques to style your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your curly hair:

  1. Start with clean hair. Shampoo and condition your hair to remove any buildup and to moisturize your curls.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide moisture and to help define your curls.
  3. Apply a curl cream or gel to your hair to help define your curls and to provide hold.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to minimize frizz and to enhance your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a pick to shape your curls and to add volume.
  6. Apply a light hold hairspray to set your style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your curly hair:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair for black guys:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 3 to 4 days to prevent your curls from drying out.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Q: What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

A: Try using a leave-in conditioner or a curl-defining cream to help tame your curls.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a pick to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers or straightening treatments.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking style for your curly hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type, and don't be afraid to try something new. With a little effort, you can have healthy, beautiful curls that will turn heads.
