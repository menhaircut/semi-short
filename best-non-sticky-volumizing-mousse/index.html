The Best Non Sticky Volumizing Mousse For Perfect Hair In 2023

Are you looking for a volumizing mousse that won't leave your hair sticky and stiff? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best non-sticky volumizing mousses that will give you the perfect lift without the unpleasant residue.

Why Use a Volumizing Mousse?

Volumizing mousse is a great hair styling product that helps add volume and fullness to your hair. It can be used on all hair types, whether you have fine, thin, or thick hair. Volumizing mousse works by adding body to your hair strands, making them look fuller and thicker.

How to Use Volumizing Mousse?

Using volumizing mousse is easy and simple. Follow these steps to get the perfect lift:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Take a small amount of volumizing mousse and spread it evenly on your palms.
  4. Apply the mousse to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

It's important to note that you should use a small amount of mousse to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Using Volumizing Mousse

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your volumizing mousse:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance the effects of the mousse.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance the natural curls in your hair.
  • Choose a mousse that is suitable for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I use volumizing mousse on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can apply volumizing mousse on wet hair. It's actually recommended to apply mousse on damp hair for better absorption.

Q: Can volumizing mousse cause hair damage?

A: No, volumizing mousse does not cause hair damage. It is a lightweight product that is designed to add volume and fullness to your hair without causing any harm.

Q: Can I use volumizing mousse on colored hair?

A: Yes, you can use volumizing mousse on colored hair. However, it's important to choose a mousse that is specifically formulated for color-treated hair to avoid fading or damage.

2. John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse

The John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse is a lightweight formula that adds fullness and body to your hair without leaving any residue. It is perfect for those with fine or thin hair, as it provides a natural-looking lift.

3. Kenra Volume Mousse Extra

The Kenra Volume Mousse Extra is a high-performance formula that provides maximum volume and hold without leaving any sticky residue. It is perfect for those with thick or heavy hair, as it provides a long-lasting lift.

Hair Care Tips

Using volumizing mousse is a great way to add volume and fullness to your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip away the natural oils that protect your scalp and hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to avoid damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair spray.

Conclusion

Choosing the right volumizing mousse is important for achieving the perfect lift and volume. The TRESemme 24 Hour Body Foaming Mousse, John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse, and Kenra Volume Mousse Extra are all great options for adding fullness and body to your hair without leaving any sticky residue. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
