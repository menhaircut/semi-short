How To Get Straight Bangs With Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and want to rock straight bangs, you may think it's impossible. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve sleek, straight bangs that perfectly frame your face. In this post, we'll show you step-by-step how to get straight bangs with curly hair, along with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

Straight bangs can be a fun way to switch up your look, but they can be a challenge for those with curly hair. Curly hair tends to have a mind of its own, making it difficult to achieve a sleek, straight look. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can straighten your bangs and keep them looking great throughout the day.

How to Get Straight Bangs with Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide for getting straight bangs with curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is important when using heat tools. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Before using any heat tools, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair straight, focusing on your bangs. Make sure to dry your hair completely, as any dampness can cause your hair to revert back to its natural curl.

Step 4: Straighten your bangs

Using a flat iron, straighten your bangs by taking small sections and running the flat iron through them. Make sure to keep the flat iron moving, so you don't burn your hair. Repeat this process until your bangs are completely straight.

Step 5: Apply finishing products

Finish off your look by applying a frizz-fighting serum or oil to your hair. This will help to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when straightening your bangs:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with adjustable heat settings.
  • Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair in small sections, using a round brush to help smooth out your hair.
  • Apply a light hairspray to your bangs to help keep them in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting straight bangs with curly hair:

Can I straighten my bangs without a flat iron?

While a flat iron is the most effective tool for straightening your bangs, you can also use a round brush and blow dryer. This method may take longer, but it can still give you great results.

How often should I straighten my bangs?

It's best to limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. Try to straighten your bangs no more than once a week.

How can I keep my straight bangs looking good throughout the day?

Apply a light hairspray or finishing serum to your bangs to help keep them in place. You can also use a dry shampoo to help absorb any excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve straight bangs with curly hair and rock a new look that's both stylish and fun. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong, and enjoy your new hairstyle!
