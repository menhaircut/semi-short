How To Cut Long Male Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of having long hair and want to switch to a shorter, more manageable style? Cutting your long hair short is not as daunting as it may seem. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and practical haircut that suits your face shape and lifestyle. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting long male hair short, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Prepare for a Haircut

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to prepare your tools and workspace. Here's what you will need:

  • Haircutting scissors or clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Hair clips or ties

Make sure your workspace is well-lit and clean. It's also a good idea to wear a shirt that you don't mind getting hair on.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

There are many short haircuts to choose from, such as the buzz cut, crew cut, French crop, and undercut. When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. You can also ask your barber for recommendations.

For example, if you have a round face, a short haircut with height on top and short sides can balance out your face shape. If you have curly hair, a short haircut with texture and layers can enhance your natural curls.

How to Cut Short Hair with Scissors

If you prefer a more traditional haircut, you can use scissors to cut your hair. Here's how:

  1. Wet your hair with a spray bottle or wash it with shampoo and towel-dry it.
  2. Comb your hair to remove tangles and knots.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section separate.
  4. Start with the top section and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it with scissors, following the length guide you want.
  5. Move to the side sections and comb the hair straight out. Cut the hair with scissors, following the length guide and angle you want.
  6. Repeat the same process for the back section.
  7. Blend the haircut by combing the hair between sections and cutting any uneven parts.
  8. Check the haircut in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

Remember to cut less hair than you think you need, as you can always trim more later.

How to Cut Short Hair with Clippers

If you prefer a shorter, more uniform haircut, you can use clippers to cut your hair. Here's how:

  1. Attach the desired guard size to the clippers. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  2. Turn on the clippers and comb your hair to remove tangles and knots.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section separate.
  4. Start with the top section and comb it straight up. Hold the clippers against your head and move it against the hair growth, following the length guide you want.
  5. Move to the side sections and comb the hair straight out. Use the clippers to cut the hair, following the length guide and angle you want.
  6. Repeat the same process for the back section.
  7. Blend the haircut by combing the hair between sections and using a shorter guard size on the clippers to cut any uneven parts.
  8. Check the haircut in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

Remember to clean the clippers regularly and oil the blades to maintain their performance.

How to Blend the Haircut

To blend the haircut and prevent any harsh lines, you can use scissors or clippers over comb technique. Here's how:

  1. Choose either scissors or clippers over comb technique.
  2. Comb the hair between sections and hold it at a 45-degree angle.
  3. Use scissors or clippers to cut the hair at the angle, blending the shorter and longer parts together.
  4. Repeat the same process for all sections, blending the hair together.
  5. Check the haircut in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

How to Style Short Hair

Once you have cut your hair, you can style it in various ways, depending on the haircut and your personal preference. Here are some styling tips:

  • Use a hair product, such as wax, pomade, or gel, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.
  • Comb your hair to a side part for a classic, formal look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but you still need to take care of it to keep it looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some FAQs about cutting long male hair short:

  1. How much hair should I cut off?

    2. It depends on the length and style you want. It's always better to start with less hair and gradually cut more if needed.

  2. Can I cut my hair myself or should I go to a barber?

    3. You can cut your hair yourself if you have the right tools and skills. However, if you're not confident or want a more professional haircut, it's better to go to a barber.

  3. How do I know which short haircut suits me?

    4. You can ask your barber for recommendations or use a virtual hairstyle app to try on different haircuts. You can also consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

  4. How do I prevent my short hair from looking flat or boring?

    5. You can add texture and volume to your hair by using a hair product, blow-drying with a round brush, or creating a messy, textured look with your fingers.

  5. How often should I wash my short hair?

    6. You can wash your hair every other day or as needed, depending on your scalp's oiliness and your hair type.

Conclusion

Cutting long male hair short is a simple and rewarding process that can transform your look and make your hair more manageable. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can achieve a stylish and practical haircut that suits your face shape and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair by washing, styling, and trimming it regularly, and enjoy your new short hair!
