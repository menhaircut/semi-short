Short Layered Bob With Fringe Over 50 The Perfect Hairstyle For Mature Women

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a stylish and youthful hairstyle, you might want to consider getting a short layered bob with fringe. This classic haircut is versatile, flattering, and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice among women of all ages and hair types. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect short layered bob with fringe over 50, including tips on how to style and care for your new 'do. Let's dive in!

Description: What is a Short Layered Bob with Fringe?

A short layered bob with fringe is a modern and trendy haircut that features layers of different lengths throughout the hair, creating a textured and voluminous look. The fringe or bangs are cut straight across the forehead, framing the face and adding a youthful touch to the overall style. This hairstyle is particularly flattering for women over 50 who want to soften their features, conceal fine lines, and add some fullness and movement to their hair.

How to Get a Short Layered Bob with Fringe Over 50?

If you're interested in getting a short layered bob with fringe over 50, here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Find the Right Inspiration

Before you book your appointment with a hairstylist, you should spend some time browsing pictures of short layered bobs with fringe that you like. You can check out websites, magazines, social media, or even ask your friends for recommendations. Make sure to save some images that suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step 2: Consult with a Professional

Once you have a few ideas of how you want your short layered bob with fringe to look like, it's time to schedule a consultation with a trusted hairstylist. They will assess your hair's condition, discuss your goals, and recommend the best cut and styling techniques for your needs. Don't be afraid to ask questions, show your inspiration pictures, and voice any concerns or preferences you have.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

When you're ready to get your short layered bob with fringe, make sure to bring your inspiration pictures and communicate clearly with your hairstylist. They will start by washing and drying your hair, then section it and cut it into layers of different lengths, using scissors or a razor. They will also cut the fringe to the desired length and thickness, depending on your face shape and hairline. After the cut, they might add some texturizing or thinning techniques to create more movement and volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your short layered bob with fringe over 50, you can style it in various ways, depending on the occasion and your mood. You can blow-dry it with a round brush to create a sleek and polished look, or let it air-dry for a more natural and tousled effect. You can also use a curling iron or a straightener to add waves or curls, or apply some volumizing or texturizing products to enhance the layers and the fringe. Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray and a hairspray to keep your style in place and protect your hair from damage.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Layered Bob with Fringe Over 50

Now that you have your short layered bob with fringe, you need to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bob.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid overusing heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or a scarf.
  • Consider using hair masks or oil treatments once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Short Layered Bob with Fringe Over 50

Here are some common questions and answers about short layered bob with fringe over 50:

Q: Will a short layered bob with fringe suit my face shape?

A: Short layered bobs with fringe can flatter most face shapes, as long as the length and the layers are adjusted to complement your features. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best cut for you.

Q: Can I style my short layered bob with fringe differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your short layered bob with fringe in various ways, from sleek and straight to wavy and tousled, depending on the event and your mood.

Q: How often should I wash my short layered bob with fringe?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, you can wash your hair every 2-3 days, or even less frequently if you have dry or curly hair. Use dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and absorb excess oil.

Hair Care for Short Layered Bob with Fringe Over 50

Finally, here are some hair care products that can enhance and maintain your short layered bob with fringe over 50:

  • Texturizing spray or mousse
  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray or hair wax
  • Hair mask or oil treatment

Remember to choose products that suit your hair type, needs, and preferences, and apply them sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair or making it greasy. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a short layered bob with fringe over 50 and feel confident and stylish every day!
