How Do You Style Short Shaved Hair

Written by Robby Sep 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short shaved hair is a bold and stylish look that has become increasingly popular in recent years. Whether you have a full buzz cut or a faded undercut, there are many ways to style short hair to create a unique and trendy look. In this article, we will explore some popular styling options for short shaved hair, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your hair's health and appearance.

Description

Short shaved hair is a low maintenance hairstyle that is easy to care for and style. It typically involves cutting the hair close to the scalp or shaving it entirely, leaving little to no length. This look can be achieved with a variety of cutting techniques, including clippers, scissors, or a straight razor. Short shaved hair is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your individual preferences and personality.

How to Style Short Shaved Hair

There are many ways to style short shaved hair, depending on your hair type, length, and texture. Here are some popular styling options to try:

1. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a great option for those with slightly longer hair. This style involves leaving some length on top and using a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to create a messy, textured look. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a natural, effortless style.

2. High and Tight

The high and tight is a classic military-inspired hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving a slightly longer strip of hair on top. This style is great for those with a more angular face shape, as it emphasizes the cheekbones and jawline.

3. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a simple yet stylish option for those who prefer a low maintenance hairstyle. This involves cutting the hair close to the scalp with clippers, leaving little to no length. The buzz cut can be customized to suit your individual preferences, with variations such as the full buzz or the faded buzz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to styling short shaved hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to cleanse your scalp and remove any buildup or residue.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too harshly. You can also use a blow dryer to speed up the drying process.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as wax or pomade, to your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 4: Style your hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair into the desired shape. You can create a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style depending on your preferences.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to help hold it in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Short Shaved Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining the health and appearance of your short shaved hair:

1. Keep it clean

Regularly wash your hair with a gentle shampoo to keep your scalp and hair clean and free of buildup.

2. Moisturize your scalp

Short shaved hair can sometimes lead to a dry scalp, so be sure to moisturize your scalp regularly with a lightweight oil or moisturizer.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair and scalp, so be sure to wear a hat or use a protective hair product when spending time outside.

4. Trim regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your short shaved hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

Q. Is short shaved hair suitable for all face shapes?

A. Short shaved hair can be customized to suit any face shape, but some styles may be more flattering on certain face shapes than others. Consult with your stylist to find the best style for you.

Q. Will my hair grow back differently after shaving it?

A. Shaving your hair will not change the texture or thickness of your hair, but it may appear thicker or coarser as it grows back.

Q. Can I color my short shaved hair?

A. Yes, you can color your short shaved hair. However, keep in mind that frequent coloring can damage your hair, so be sure to use a quality hair color and follow proper hair care practices.

Hair Care for Short Shaved Hair

Short shaved hair requires minimal maintenance, but it is still important to care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short shaved hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and free of buildup.

2. Protect your scalp from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your scalp, so be sure to wear a hat or use a protective hair product when spending time outside.

3. Moisturize your scalp

Short shaved hair can sometimes lead to a dry scalp, so be sure to moisturize your scalp regularly with a lightweight oil or moisturizer.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your short shaved hair looking neat and tidy, and prevent split ends and breakage.

5. Use a quality styling product

Using a quality styling product can help to create the desired look for your short shaved hair, and keep it in place throughout the day.

In conclusion, short shaved hair is a stylish and versatile look that can be customized to suit your individual preferences and personality. With the right styling techniques and hair care practices, you can maintain a healthy and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go.
