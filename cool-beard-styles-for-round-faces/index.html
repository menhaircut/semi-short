Cool Beard Styles For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same old beard style for your round face? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing some cool beard styles that are perfect for round faces. From the classic goatee to the trendy stubble, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by having a wide, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. When it comes to beard styles, it’s important to choose one that will complement your face shape, rather than accentuate it. The right beard style can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How To

Before choosing a beard style, it’s important to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is by taking a picture of your face and tracing the outline with a marker. Once you’ve determined that you have a round face, you can start exploring different beard styles that will suit your features.

Choosing the Right Beard Style

When it comes to beard styles for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that will make your face appear even rounder. This includes full beards that are too wide or too long. Instead, opt for styles that will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Some cool beard styles for round faces include:

  • The Goatee: This classic beard style is perfect for round faces. It involves growing a small, pointed beard on the chin, while keeping the rest of the face clean-shaven. This style helps to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.
  • The Stubble: If you prefer a more low-maintenance look, the stubble is a great option. This style involves growing a short, scruffy beard all over the face. It can help to add definition to a round face and create a more rugged look.
  • The Anchor: This beard style is a combination of a mustache, soul patch, and chin strap. It’s a great option for round faces because it helps to create a more angular look.
  • The Chinstrap: This style involves growing a thin beard along the jawline, connecting to a mustache. It can help to add definition to a round face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Step by Step

Once you’ve chosen a beard style that suits your round face, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  2. Wash your beard with a gentle cleanser to keep it clean and free from bacteria.
  3. Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  4. Use a comb or brush to style your beard in the desired shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a beard style for a round face:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your face shape best.
  • Avoid styles that are too wide or too long, as they can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Consider consulting with a barber or stylist to help you choose the right style for your face shape.

FAQ

What beard styles are best for round faces?

Some of the best beard styles for round faces include the goatee, stubble, anchor, and chinstrap.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by taking a picture of your face and tracing the outline with a marker. Once you’ve done this, you can compare your face shape to different face shape templates to determine which one you have.

Hair Care

In addition to maintaining your beard, it’s important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage it and cause split ends.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

By following these tips and choosing the right beard style for your round face, you can create a stylish and flattering look that will make you feel confident and attractive.
