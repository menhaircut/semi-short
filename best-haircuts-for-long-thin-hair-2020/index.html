Best Haircuts For Long Thin Hair 2020 A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Long thin hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right haircut, you can create volume and movement that will transform your look. In this guide, we’ll go over the best haircuts for long thin hair 2020 and provide tips on how to care for and style your hair to keep it looking its best in 2023.

Description

Long thin hair can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut, you can add volume, movement, and shape to your hair. The best haircuts for long thin hair 2020 include layers, choppy cuts, and blunt cuts that add volume and texture to your hair. These haircuts will create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair, and they’re perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their long and thin hair.

How to Get the Best Haircuts for Long Thin Hair

If you have long thin hair, the first step to getting the best haircut is to find a stylist who specializes in working with thin hair. They will be able to recommend the best haircut for your hair type and face shape. Some of the best haircuts for long thin hair 2020 include:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add volume and movement to long thin hair. They create a soft and feminine look and are perfect for those who want to keep their hair long.

Choppy Layers

Choppy layers are a more edgy and modern take on long layers. They add texture and volume to long thin hair and are perfect for those who want a more playful and fun look.

Blunt Cut

A blunt cut is a great option for those who want to create the illusion of thicker hair. It’s a simple and classic haircut that adds volume and shape to long thin hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Thin Hair

Once you have the right haircut for your long thin hair, it’s important to style it correctly to keep it looking its best. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling long thin hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Root Lifter

Apply a volumizing mousse or root lifter to your hair to add volume and texture. Apply the product to the roots of your hair and work it through to the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help create volume and movement in your hair. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Flat Iron

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair. Curling your hair will create more volume and movement, while using a flat iron will create a sleek and modern look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thin Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining long thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy styling products
  • Avoid hot tools as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair

FAQs about Long Thin Hair

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if it’s thin?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long if it’s thin. The key is to add volume and texture to your hair with the right haircut and styling products.

Q: Will layers make my thin hair look thinner?

A: No, layers can actually make thin hair look thicker by adding volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

Here are some tips for caring for long thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools as much as possible
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full
  • Avoid using heavy styling products

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can transform your long thin hair into a voluminous and full look. Use these tips to keep your hair looking its best in 2023 and beyond!
