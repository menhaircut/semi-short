How to Get the Top 8 Cool Hairstyles in Animal Crossing

The top 8 cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing can be obtained by visiting Harriet's hair salon in the Able Sisters store. Here are the hairstyles you can choose from:

  1. Short Bob
  2. Top Knot Bun
  3. Long Wavy Hair
  4. Ponytail with Bangs
  5. Messy Updo
  6. Curly Bob
  7. Twin Braids
  8. Spiky Hair

Once you've unlocked Harriet's hair salon, you can select any of these hairstyles for your character.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using the Top 8 Cool Hairstyles in Animal Crossing

Using the top 8 cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing is easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Visit Harriet's hair salon in the Able Sisters store.
  2. Talk to Harriet and select the "Change My Hairstyle" option.
  3. Select the hairstyle you want to use from the menu.
  4. Harriet will ask you if you're sure you want to use that hairstyle. Confirm your selection.
  5. Your character's hair will then be updated with the new hairstyle.

And that's it! You can switch between any of the top 8 cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing whenever you want by visiting Harriet's hair salon again.

Tips for Using the Top 8 Cool Hairstyles in Animal Crossing

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of the top 8 cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  • Try out different hairstyles to see which one you like best.
  • Consider matching your character's hair color to their clothing for a cohesive look.
  • Accessorize your character's outfit with hats or headbands that complement their hairstyle.
  • Take screenshots of your character with different hairstyles to share with friends or on social media.

FAQs About Using the Top 8 Cool Hairstyles in Animal Crossing

Can I change my character's hair color?

Yes! You can change your character's hair color by using the mirror item in your house or by visiting Harriet's hair salon and selecting the "Change My Hair Color" option.

Can I use the top 8 cool hairstyles on my villager characters?

No, the top 8 cool hairstyles can only be used on your player character.

Are there any other hairstyles available in Animal Crossing?

Yes! There are many other hairstyles available in Animal Crossing, including some that can only be obtained through special events or by scanning specific amiibo figures.

Hair Care Tips for Your Animal Crossing Character

Just like in real life, your Animal Crossing character's hair requires some care to stay looking its best. Here are some tips to help keep your character's hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your character's hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much product on your character's hair, as it can weigh it down.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your character's hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your character's hair too often, as it can damage it.
  • Consider using a hair mask to keep your character's hair moisturized.

With these tips and the top 8 cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing, your character is sure to look their best!
