How To Tie Long Hair In Summer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 25, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Summer is the perfect time to let your hair down and enjoy the warm weather. However, with long hair, it can be a challenge to keep it out of your face and off your neck. That's why we've put together this guide on how to tie long hair in summer. From braids to buns, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Long Hair in Summer
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Tying Long Hair in Summer
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair in Summer

How to Tie Long Hair in Summer

There are many ways to tie long hair in summer, but some of the most popular styles include braids, buns, and ponytails. These styles are easy to do and will keep your hair off your neck and out of your face.

Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair off your face while still looking stylish. There are many different types of braids you can try, including:

  • Fishtail braid
  • Dutch braid
  • French braid
  • Waterfall braid

To create a braid, start by brushing your hair and dividing it into three sections. Then, cross the right section over the middle section, followed by the left section over the middle section. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair.

Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. They're perfect for keeping your hair off your neck and out of your face. There are many different types of buns you can try, including:

  • Messy bun
  • Sock bun
  • Top knot
  • Low bun

To create a bun, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun. Secure with bobby pins.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a quick and easy way to tie long hair in summer. They're perfect for when you're on-the-go or don't have a lot of time to style your hair. To create a ponytail, simply pull your hair back and secure it with a hair tie.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to tie long hair in summer:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide which hairstyle you want to try (braid, bun, or ponytail).
  3. Follow the steps for your chosen hairstyle (see above).
  4. Secure your hairstyle with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Tips for Tying Long Hair in Summer

Here are some tips to help you tie long hair in summer:

  • Use hair ties that won't damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories, such as clips and headbands, to add some flair to your hairstyle.
  • Don't tie your hair too tightly, as this can cause headaches and hair breakage.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair in summer?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day in summer, as sweat and oil can build up quickly on your scalp.

Q: Can I wear my hair down in summer?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair down in summer, but it's important to keep it off your face and neck to stay cool.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair in Summer

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair healthy and looking great in summer:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun's rays.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can easily tie your long hair in summer and keep it looking great all season long.
