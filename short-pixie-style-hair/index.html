Short Pixie Style Hair A Bold And Chic Look

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and chic hairstyle that's easy to maintain, short pixie style hair may be the perfect fit for you. This trendy hairstyle has gained popularity in recent years and continues to be a popular choice for women of all ages. In this article, we'll provide a complete guide on how to get the perfect short pixie style hair, including the steps, tips, and hair care.

Description

Short pixie style hair is a trendy haircut that's characterized by short layers on the top and sides, with longer layers in the back. This hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type, making it a versatile choice for women of all ages. Short pixie style hair is perfect for those who are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

How to Get Short Pixie Style Hair

Getting short pixie style hair is a simple process that can be done at any hair salon. Follow these steps to get the perfect short pixie style hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Start by choosing the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short pixie style hair that fits your needs.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, ensure that it's clean and dry. Remove any tangles or knots by brushing it gently.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

The hairstylist will cut your hair according to the chosen hairstyle. Short pixie style hair typically involves cutting the top and sides shorter than the back.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style your hair using a styling product such as gel or mousse. You can use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look or use a comb for a more polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining Short Pixie Style Hair

Short pixie style hair is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal upkeep. Here are some tips for maintaining your short pixie style hair:

  • Regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying your scalp.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as a flat iron or curling iron to prevent heat damage to your hair.

FAQ

What Face Shapes Suit Short Pixie Style Hair?

Short pixie style hair suits a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short pixie style hair for your face shape.

What Hair Types Suit Short Pixie Style Hair?

Short pixie style hair is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. The haircut can be customized to suit your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Style Hair

Short pixie style hair requires minimal hair care, but it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some hair care tips for short pixie style hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent over-drying your scalp.
  • Use a conditioner to add moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

Short pixie style hair is a bold and chic hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. Follow the steps, tips, and hair care guide above to get the perfect short pixie style hair that suits your needs and style.
