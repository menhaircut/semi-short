Best Short Haircuts For Long Face Female

Written by William Nov 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the best short haircuts for long face female? If you have a long face, you need to choose a haircut that will help balance out your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this post, we’ll share some of the best short haircuts for long face female that will flatter your face shape and make you look your best.

Description

Long faces are characterized by an elongated shape that can make your face look narrow and sometimes even gaunt. To counteract this, you need a haircut that will add width and volume to your face. The right haircut can also help to balance out your features and highlight your best features.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Long Face Female

When choosing a short haircut for long face female, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a haircut that adds width to your face
  • Avoid haircuts that accentuate your face length
  • Opt for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look even longer

Best Short Haircuts for Long Face Female

Now that you know what to look for, here are some of the best short haircuts for long face female:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for long face female. This haircut adds volume to the sides of your face, making it look wider and more balanced. The pixie cut also highlights your cheekbones and jawline, making your face look more defined.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for long face female. This haircut adds volume to the sides of your face and frames your face beautifully. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. This haircut works well for long face female because it adds volume to the sides of your face and creates a more balanced look. You can opt for a layered bob or a layered pixie cut.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add volume and width to your face. This haircut works well for long face female because it creates a diagonal line across your face, making it look wider and more balanced. You can opt for long side-swept bangs or short side-swept bangs.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the best short haircut for long face female:

  1. Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face
  2. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape
  4. Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist
  5. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your haircut to create volume and texture
  6. Consider adding side-swept bangs to your haircut to add width and balance to your face
  7. Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair
  8. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  9. Enjoy your new haircut and newfound confidence!

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long face female:

1. Will short haircuts make my face look even longer?

No, as long as you choose a short haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face and avoids haircuts that accentuate your face length.

2. Can I still style my short haircut?

Yes, you can still style your short haircut using styling products and tools. Talk to your hairstylist about the best products and tools to use for your hair.

3. How often should I visit my hairstylist for trims?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups.

Conclusion

Now that you know the best short haircuts for long face female, it’s time to choose the one that works best for you. Remember to choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face, avoids haircuts that accentuate your face length, and consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape. With the right haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and beautiful every day!
