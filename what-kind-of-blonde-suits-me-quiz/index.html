What Kind Of Blonde Suits Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Daniel Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of going blonde but not sure which shade would suit you? Look no further than the "What Kind of Blonde Suits Me Quiz." This quiz is designed to help you find your perfect blonde shade based on your skin tone, hair type, and personal preferences. In this article, we'll explain how to take the quiz, give you tips on hair care and answer some frequently asked questions about going blonde.

How to Take the Quiz

The "What Kind of Blonde Suits Me Quiz" is a simple online quiz that takes less than 10 minutes to complete. To take the quiz, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the website that offers the quiz
  2. Provide your name and email address
  3. Answer a series of questions about your skin tone, hair type, and personal preferences
  4. Submit your answers and wait for the result

Once you receive the quiz result, you'll know which blonde shade is perfect for you.

Step-by-Step Guide: Finding Your Perfect Blonde Shade

The "What Kind of Blonde Suits Me Quiz" is a great starting point to find your perfect blonde shade. But there are other factors you should consider when choosing a shade. Follow these steps to find your perfect blonde shade:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding your perfect blonde shade is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If your veins appear blue, you have cool undertones. If your veins appear both green and blue, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

The next step is to consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it may take several sessions to achieve your desired blonde shade. If you have light hair, you may be able to achieve your desired shade in one session.

Step 3: Choose Your Blonde Shade

Now that you know your skin tone and natural hair color, it's time to choose your blonde shade. Here are some suggestions:

  • Warm skin tones: honey blonde, golden blonde, caramel blonde
  • Cool skin tones: platinum blonde, ash blonde, silver blonde
  • Neutral skin tones: champagne blonde, beige blonde, buttery blonde

Remember to consider your personal preferences, lifestyle, and maintenance when choosing a shade.

Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your blonde hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for blonde hair
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider using a purple shampoo to prevent brassiness

Frequently Asked Questions About Going Blonde

1. Will going blonde damage my hair?

Going blonde can cause some damage to your hair, especially if you have dark hair. However, with proper care and maintenance, you can minimize damage and keep your hair healthy.

2. How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on your natural hair color and how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

3. Can I go blonde if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can go blonde if you have curly hair. However, it's important to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair to ensure you get the best results.

4. How do I prevent my blonde hair from turning brassy?

To prevent your blonde hair from turning brassy, use a purple shampoo once a week. This will neutralize any yellow or orange tones in your hair.

5. How do I choose the right stylist?

When choosing a stylist, look for someone who has experience working with blonde hair. Ask to see before and after photos of their previous work and read reviews from other clients. It's also important to communicate your expectations and preferences with your stylist.

By taking the "What Kind of Blonde Suits Me Quiz" and following these tips, you can find your perfect blonde shade and maintain healthy, vibrant hair. Happy blonding!
