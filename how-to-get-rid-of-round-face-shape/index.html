How To Get Rid Of Round Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of having a round face shape? Do you want to achieve a more defined and angular look? If yes, then this article is for you. We will provide you with tips, tricks, and hair care techniques to help you get rid of your round face shape. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wider forehead. It is a common face shape among many people, but it can be frustrating for some who want to achieve a more angular look. There are several ways to get rid of round face shape, including changing your hairstyle, makeup, and facial exercises.

How To

Getting rid of round face shape requires a combination of lifestyle changes, beauty techniques, and hair care. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Change Your Hairstyle

Your hairstyle plays a significant role in the appearance of your face shape. To create the illusion of a more angular face, consider getting a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and creates a more defined jawline. A short bob or pixie cut can work wonders for a round face shape.

2. Use Makeup to Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that uses shading to create the illusion of a more defined face shape. To contour your face, use a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline to create a more angular look.

3. Do Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and create a more defined jawline. Some exercises to try include puckering your lips and holding for 5-10 seconds, smiling while pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth, and chewing gum to strengthen your jaw muscles.

Step by Step

Step 1:

Consult a hairstylist to find a haircut that flatters your face shape. Consider getting a short bob or pixie cut to create a more defined jawline.

Step 2:

Invest in a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Use it to shade the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline.

Step 3:

Try facial exercises to tone your facial muscles and create a more defined jawline. Incorporate exercises like puckering your lips, smiling while pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth, and chewing gum regularly.

Tips

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and create a more defined jawline.
  • Use contouring makeup to shade the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline.
  • Try facial exercises to tone your facial muscles and create a more defined jawline.
  • Eat a healthy diet and exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight and reduce facial fat.
  • Consider getting a professional facial to help contour your face and reduce puffiness.

FAQ

Q: Can I get rid of round face shape without surgery?

A: Yes, there are several non-surgical techniques that can help you get rid of round face shape, including changing your hairstyle, makeup, and facial exercises.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It varies from person to person, but you can typically see results within a few weeks if you do facial exercises regularly.

Hair Care

In addition to changing your hairstyle, you should also take care of your hair to maintain a healthy, shiny appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can get rid of your round face shape and achieve a more defined and angular look. Remember to be patient and consistent in your efforts, and you will see results in no time!
