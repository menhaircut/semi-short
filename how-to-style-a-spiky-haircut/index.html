How To Style A Spiky Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Nov 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to try something new? A spiky haircut may be just what you need. This edgy and stylish hairdo can add a touch of attitude to your look, but it can be tricky to style. In this article, we will guide you on how to style a spiky haircut and share some tips on how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Spiky Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Spiky Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Spiky Haircuts

How to Style a Spiky Haircut

Before you start styling your spiky haircut, you need to prepare your hair. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling gel or pomade to hold the spikes in place.

Tools You Will Need

To style a spiky haircut, you will need the following tools:

  • Styling gel or pomade
  • Blow dryer
  • Hairbrush
  • Flat iron (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style a spiky haircut:

  1. Apply styling gel or pomade to your hair, starting from the roots to the tips.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a hairbrush to add volume and texture.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair if necessary.
  4. Using your fingers or a comb, create spikes on the top of your head by lifting your hair upwards.
  5. Apply more styling gel or pomade to keep the spikes in place.
  6. Use a hairdryer on a low setting to set the spikes in place.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the spikes in place and add shine to your hair.

Tips for Styling a Spiky Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling a spiky haircut:

  • Use a high-quality styling gel or pomade that can hold your hair in place without making it look greasy.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low setting to avoid damaging it.
  • Don't overuse styling products as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different spike lengths and angles to find the best look for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I style my spiky haircut?

You can style your spiky haircut every day or every other day, depending on how long you want the spikes to last. However, try not to style it too often as it can damage your hair.

2. Can I style a spiky haircut with curly hair?

Yes, you can style a spiky haircut with curly hair. However, you may need to straighten your hair first using a flat iron or blow dryer to create the spikes.

3. How do I maintain my spiky haircut?

To maintain your spiky haircut, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair. Trim your hair regularly to keep the spikes looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips for Spiky Haircuts

Here are some hair care tips for spiky haircuts:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

Now that you know how to style a spiky haircut, go ahead and try this edgy and stylish hairdo. With a little practice, you can rock this look like a pro.
