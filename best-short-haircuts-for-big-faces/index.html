Best Short Haircuts For Big Faces Flattering Styles For Your Features

Are you looking for a short haircut that complements your big face? You're in luck because we've rounded up the best short haircuts for big faces to help you find a style that flatters your features. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are plenty of options to choose from that will make you look and feel your best. Read on to discover how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, how to style it, and how to care for your hair.

Determining Your Face Shape

Before deciding on a short haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheeks and the face is about as wide as it is long.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a short haircut that flatters your features.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are short and sweet, making them a great option for big faces. A pixie cut can help elongate the face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, a pixie cut will look particularly flattering.

When styling a pixie cut, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to shape your hair and add texture. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some volume with a curling iron.

Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are another great option for big faces. Bobs can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face. There are many different types of bobs, including blunt bobs, layered bobs, and asymmetrical bobs.

To style a bob haircut, use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add volume. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some texture with a curling iron.

Long Bob Haircuts

A long bob, or lob, is a slightly longer version of a bob haircut. Lobs are a great option for big faces because they can help elongate the face and draw attention to your neck and collarbone. Lobs work particularly well for oval and heart-shaped faces.

To style a lob, use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some texture with a curling iron. You can also add some volume by using a round brush to blow dry your hair.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut for big faces. Bangs can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face. There are many different types of bangs, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and wispy bangs.

To style bangs, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to shape your hair and add texture. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some volume with a curling iron.

Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts can add volume and texture to a short haircut for big faces. Layers can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face. There are many different types of layered haircuts, including choppy layers, face-framing layers, and long layers.

To style layered hair, use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add volume. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some texture with a curling iron.

Asymmetrical Haircuts

Asymmetrical haircuts can add some edge to a short haircut for big faces. Asymmetrical haircuts can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face. There are many different types of asymmetrical haircuts, including long on one side and short on the other, or angled layers.

To style an asymmetrical haircut, use a flat iron to create a sleek look or add some texture with a curling iron. You can also use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to shape your hair and add texture.

Curls

If you have naturally curly hair and a big face, don't be afraid to embrace your curls! Curly hair can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face. You can choose a short haircut that complements your curls, such as a curly pixie cut or a curly bob.

To style curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product and let your hair air dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define your curls.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of which short haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face is a bob or a lob. These haircuts can help balance out the features of a round face and make it appear more elongated.

What is the best short haircut for a square face?

The best short haircut for a square face is a layered bob or an asymmetrical haircut. These haircuts can help soften the angular features of a square face.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a big face?

Yes, you can definitely pull off a pixie cut with a big face! A pixie cut can help elongate the face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Should I get bangs with a big face?

Yes, bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut for big faces. Bangs can help balance out the features of a round or square face, and can also help elongate the face.
