Best Product To Slick Back Women s Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect product to slick back your hair? The slick back hairstyle has been around for decades and is still a popular choice for both men and women. However, finding the right product to achieve the perfect slick back look can be challenging, especially for women. In this article, we'll explore the best product to slick back women's hair and provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back Women's Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Description

The best product to slick back women's hair is pomade. Pomade is a type of hair product that provides a strong hold and a shiny finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look, which is exactly what you want when you're trying to slick back your hair.

Pomade is available in different formulas, including water-based and oil-based. Water-based pomade is easier to wash out and doesn't leave a greasy residue, making it a great option for women who don't want to damage their hair. Oil-based pomade, on the other hand, provides a stronger hold and is perfect for women with thicker hair.

How to Slick Back Women's Hair

Slicking back women's hair is easy, but it requires a bit of practice to get it right. Here's what you'll need:

  • Pomade
  • Comb
  • Hair Tie

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slick back look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Using a comb, slick your hair back towards the crown of your head. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Secure your hair in a low ponytail or bun at the back of your head.
  6. Apply a small amount of pomade to the top of your hair to create a shiny finish.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slick back look:

  • Use a small amount of pomade to avoid a greasy finish.
  • Avoid using too much pomade, as this can make your hair look oily and weigh it down.
  • If you have thin hair, use a water-based pomade to avoid damaging your hair.
  • If you have thick hair, use an oil-based pomade for a stronger hold.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a high ponytail or bun, to switch up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back women's hair:

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can still slick back your hair if it's curly. However, you may need to use more product to achieve the desired hold.

Q: Will slicking back my hair damage it?

A: Slicking back your hair won't damage it if you use the right products and techniques. Avoid using too much product, and wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.

Q: How long will the slick back look last?

A: The slick back look can last all day if you use the right products and techniques. However, if you have fine hair or if it's windy outside, the style may not hold as well.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when slicking it back. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, the best product to slick back women's hair is pomade. It provides a strong hold and a shiny finish, making it perfect for creating a sleek and polished look. Follow our tips and tricks to achieve the perfect slick back look, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
