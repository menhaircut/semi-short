How to Get a Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

The mens haircut short sides longer top is a simple but striking haircut that can be achieved with just a few basic tools. To get this haircut, you'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, and a comb. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Use the clippers to shave the sides of your head, leaving about an inch of hair on top.
  3. Blend the sides into the longer hair on top using scissors, creating a gradual fade.
  4. Trim the top of your hair to your desired length, leaving it longer at the front and shorter at the back.
  5. Style your hair using a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your hair on top will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the top of your hair shorter to create a more angular look. If you have a longer face, you can afford to keep the top of your hair longer to balance out your proportions.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Styling a mens haircut short sides longer top is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here's how:

  1. Start by towel-drying your hair until it's slightly damp.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Run your hands through your hair, starting at the front and working your way back.
  4. Use your fingers to tousle your hair, creating a messy, textured look.
  5. Finish by using a comb to style your hair into your desired shape.

Choosing the Right Hair Product

The type of hair product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you're trying to achieve. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to use a strong-hold pomade or wax to keep your hair in place. If you have fine, thin hair, a lighter styling cream or mousse may be more suitable.

Tips for Maintaining a Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Maintaining a mens haircut short sides longer top is relatively easy, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily or greasy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions About Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Is a mens haircut short sides longer top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a mens haircut short sides longer top can be customized to suit any face shape. Your barber can adjust the length and shape of your haircut to flatter your features and balance out your proportions.

Can I style a mens haircut short sides longer top in different ways?

Yes, a mens haircut short sides longer top is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. You can slick your hair back for a more polished look, or leave it messy and textured for a more casual vibe.

How often should I get a trim for my mens haircut short sides longer top?

You should get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your haircut looking its best. Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

To keep your mens haircut short sides longer top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry or damaged.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Pat your hair dry with a towel instead of rubbing it vigorously, which can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a mens haircut short sides longer top with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement. So why not give it a try?
