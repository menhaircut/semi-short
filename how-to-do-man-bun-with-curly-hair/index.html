How To Do Man Bun With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair and want to rock a man bun, you might think it's impossible. But fear not, curly-haired folks! With a few simple steps and some helpful tips, you can achieve a stylish man bun that will turn heads. In this article, we'll show you how to do a man bun with curly hair, including everything from preparation to styling. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Preparation
  2. Styling the Bun
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Man Bun
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

Preparation

The first step in achieving a man bun with curly hair is to make sure your hair is clean and moisturized. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.

Next, you'll want to detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to style your hair. If you have particularly stubborn tangles, you can use a detangling spray or cream.

Once your hair is detangled, you can apply a styling product. For a man bun, you'll want to use a strong hold product like a styling cream or gel. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to get it all the way to the roots. This will help keep your bun in place.

Styling the Bun

Now it's time to create your man bun! Here's how:

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head, making sure to leave some hair loose at the front.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun shape and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Adjust the bun so that it sits comfortably on your head. You can also pull out some strands at the front for a more relaxed look.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

And there you have it – a stylish man bun with curly hair!

Tips for Maintaining Your Man Bun

Here are some tips for keeping your man bun looking great:

  • Don't wash your hair too often – curly hair doesn't need to be washed as frequently as straight hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage curly hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some common questions people have about man buns and curly hair:

Q: Can I still do a man bun if my hair is really short?

A: It might be difficult to create a full man bun with very short hair, but you can still do a half bun or a top knot.

Q: Will a man bun damage my hair?

A: As long as you're not pulling your hair too tight, a man bun shouldn't cause any damage. Just make sure to take breaks from wearing your hair up to prevent breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your curls.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish man bun with your curly hair. So why not give it a try?
