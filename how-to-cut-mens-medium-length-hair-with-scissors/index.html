How To Cut Men s Medium Length Hair With Scissors

Written by Robby Jun 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair cut at a salon? Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have medium length hair. But fear not, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your own hair at home with scissors. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's medium length hair with scissors.

Description

Medium length hair refers to hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This length is popular among men because it is versatile and easy to maintain. Cutting your own hair with scissors can save you time and money, but it requires practice and patience. You will need the following tools: scissors, a comb, a mirror, and hair clips.

How to Cut Men's Medium Length Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and pat your hair dry with a towel. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the middle or on one side. This will help you create an even cut and avoid any lopsidedness. Use hair clips to separate the top section of your hair from the bottom section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Hold a small section of your hair between your fingers and cut the ends with scissors. Start with a small amount and repeat the process until you have achieved the desired length. Take small sections of hair and work your way around your head. Make sure to cut both sides evenly.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs

If you have bangs, use scissors to trim them. Comb your bangs straight down and hold them between your fingers. Cut the ends at an angle to create a natural look.

Step 5: Check Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, check it in the mirror to make sure it is even. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take breaks to avoid fatigue and to check your progress.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your own hair takes practice.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair when it is wet?

It is not recommended to cut your hair when it is wet as it can lead to uneven results. It is best to cut your hair when it is dry.

Can I use any type of scissors?

No. It is recommended to use hair cutting scissors as they are sharp and designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length and style.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and use a comb to detangle your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair. Take care of your hair and it will look great!
