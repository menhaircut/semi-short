Description:

Clip in hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair without the commitment of permanent extensions. However, sometimes the extensions may come in one length which can look unnatural when blended with your natural hair. Cutting the clip in extensions into layers can help to create a seamless and natural looking blend. In this article, we will go over how to cut clip in extensions into layers.

How to Cut Clip In Extensions into Layers:

Cutting clip in extensions into layers can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step by Step Process:

Step 1: First, start by washing and drying your clip in extensions to get rid of any product buildup or dirt.

Step 2: Next, separate your extensions into sections that match the sections on your head where you want to clip them in.

Step 3: Take one section at a time and clip it onto a mannequin head or a wig stand.

Step 4: Use a comb to separate the hair into sections.

Step 5: Take a small section of hair from the bottom and cut it to the desired length. This will be your guide for the rest of the layers.

Step 6: Continue cutting small sections of hair, making sure to follow the guide you created in step 5.

Step 7: Repeat this process for all the sections of your clip in extensions.

Step 8: Once you have finished cutting all the sections, brush through the hair to make sure all the layers are blended.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting clip in extensions into layers:

  • Always start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much.
  • Make sure to cut the hair while it is clipped onto a mannequin head or wig stand to avoid uneven cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors designed for cutting hair.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure a natural and seamless blend.

FAQ:

Q: Can I cut my clip in extensions while they are clipped onto my head?

A: No, it is not recommended to cut clip in extensions while they are clipped onto your head as it can result in uneven cutting and damage to your natural hair.

Q: Can I still style my clip in extensions after cutting them?

A: Yes, you can still style your clip in extensions after cutting them. However, make sure to use heat protectant before using any hot tools to avoid damaging the hair.

Hair Care:

Proper hair care is important to ensure that your clip in extensions last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your clip in extensions gently with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your clip in extensions too often to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Store your clip in extensions in a cool, dry place to prevent tangling and damage.
  • Use a detangling brush or comb to gently brush through your clip in extensions to prevent tangling.

By following these tips and steps, you can easily cut your clip in extensions into layers for a natural and seamless blend with your natural hair.
