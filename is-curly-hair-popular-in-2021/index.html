Is Curly Hair Popular In 2021

Curly hair has been popular for ages, and it doesn't seem to be going out of style anytime soon. In 2021, curly hair is more popular than ever before. Many people are embracing their natural curls, and others are opting for perms, twists, and other styles to create curly or wavy hair.

How to Get Curly Hair

If you have straight hair and want to get curly hair, there are several methods you can try. Here are a few:

1. Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that adds curls or waves to your hair. The process involves applying a solution to your hair, wrapping it in rods, and then rinsing the solution out after a certain amount of time. The result is curly hair that lasts for several months.

2. Twist Out

A twist out is a natural way to create curly hair without heat or chemicals. The process involves twisting small sections of your hair after applying a styling product, and then letting the twists dry before unraveling them. The result is defined curls that can last for several days.

3. Curling Iron

Using a curling iron is a quick and easy way to create curly hair. Simply wrap small sections of your hair around the barrel of the curling iron, hold for a few seconds, and then release. The result is bouncy, defined curls that can last for several hours.

Step by Step Guide

If you're new to styling curly hair, here's a step by step guide to help you:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair. This will help to hydrate and define your curls.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

4. Finish with a hairspray

Finish off by spraying a light hairspray over your hair to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining curly hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. This will help to avoid breakage and preserve your curls.

2. Sleep on a satin pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and keep your curls intact.

3. Avoid heat styling

To avoid damaging your curls, try to avoid heat styling as much as possible. If you do use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.

FAQs About Curly Hair

1. How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and how quickly it gets greasy.

2. Can I brush my curly hair?

You should avoid brushing your curly hair, as this can disrupt your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

3. How can I define my curls?

You can define your curls by using a styling product that's designed for curly hair, such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Apply the product to your damp hair, and then scrunch your hair upwards to encourage your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are a few hair care tips for curly hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Using a sulfate-free shampoo can help to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid over-styling

To avoid damaging your curls, try to avoid over-styling your hair. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use styling products to enhance them.

In conclusion, curly hair is definitely popular in 2021, and it's a trend that's not going away anytime soon. Whether you have natural curls or opt for a perm or twist out, there are plenty of ways to embrace your curly hair and make it look its best.
