How To Spike Natural Black Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to add some edge to your natural black hair, spiking it up is a great way to do so. While this style may seem intimidating, it's actually quite easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of spiking natural black hair, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Natural Black Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Natural Black Hair
  • FAQs about Spiking Natural Black Hair
  • Hair Care for Spiked Natural Black Hair

How to Spike Natural Black Hair

Spiking natural black hair involves creating small, defined spikes throughout your hair. This style works best with short to medium length hair, and can be achieved with a variety of hair products.

What You'll Need:

  • Strong hold hair gel
  • Hair spray
  • Comb
  • Hair dryer

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to spike your natural black hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of strong hold hair gel to your fingertips.
  3. Take a small section of hair and pull it upwards.
  4. Use your fingers to shape the hair into a small spike.
  5. Continue creating spikes throughout your hair, working in small sections.
  6. Use a comb to smooth any flyaways and create definition.
  7. Once your hair is spiked to your liking, use hair spray to hold the style in place.
  8. Use a hair dryer on a low setting to set the spikes and dry any excess hair spray.

Tips for Spiking Natural Black Hair

Follow these tips to ensure the best results when spiking your natural black hair:

  • Start with a small amount of hair gel, and add more as needed.
  • Work in small sections to ensure even spikes throughout your hair.
  • Use a strong hold hair gel to ensure your spikes stay in place.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and shapes to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs about Spiking Natural Black Hair

How long will my spiked hair last?

With the right hair products and technique, your spiked hair should last all day. However, you may need to touch up your spikes throughout the day if they start to lose their shape.

Can I spike my hair if it's relaxed?

Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's relaxed. Just be sure to use a strong hold hair gel to ensure your spikes stay in place.

Do I need to use a specific type of comb for spiking my hair?

No, you can use any comb to smooth your hair and create definition. However, a fine-toothed comb may work best for creating small, defined spikes.

Hair Care for Spiked Natural Black Hair

To keep your spiked natural black hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to spike.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily spike your natural black hair for a bold and edgy look. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.
