How Do You Cut Short Side Bangs

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to switch things up with a cute and trendy short side bang? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to cut short side bangs in the comfort of your own home. From the tools you need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You Will Need
  • How To Cut Short Side Bangs
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Side Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Side Bangs

Tools You Will Need

Before we start, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or ponytail holder
  • Water spray bottle

Make sure your scissors are sharp so that you have a clean and precise cut. A comb will help you section your hair, while a clip or ponytail holder will keep the rest of your hair out of the way. A water spray bottle will help keep your hair damp and easier to cut.

How To Cut Short Side Bangs

Cutting short side bangs may seem intimidating, but with these easy steps, you'll be a pro in no time!

Step-By-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Comb your hair straight down and identify where you want your bangs to start. Use the arch of your eyebrow as a guide.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down. If your hair is thick, you may want to use a water spray bottle to dampen the section.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle finger, and use your other hand to hold the scissors.
  5. Point your scissors upwards and start cutting at a 45-degree angle. Cut small sections at a time until you reach your desired length.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side.
  7. Blend the bangs by taking small sections from both sides and cutting them at the same length.
  8. Check for any uneven pieces and trim them accordingly.
  9. Style your bangs by blow-drying them or letting them air dry.

Tips for Cutting Short Side Bangs

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind while cutting your short side bangs:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter. You can always cut more, but you can't add length back.
  • Keep your scissors pointed upwards to create a softer, more natural look.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven pieces.
  • Don't cut your bangs when they're wet as they will appear shorter when they dry.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own bangs, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short side bangs:

  • Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair?

    • Yes! However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you may want to cut your bangs longer than your desired length.

  • What do I do if I cut my bangs too short?

    • Don't panic! You can always style your bangs to the side or pin them back until they grow out.

  • How often should I trim my bangs?

    • It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. On average, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Short Side Bangs

Now that you've cut your short side bangs, it's important to take care of them to maintain their shape and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent oils from your hands transferring onto them.
  • Use a small amount of hair product, such as wax or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their length and shape.

Cutting short side bangs may seem daunting, but with these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a trendy and chic look. Remember to take your time, start with a longer length, and use sharp scissors for a clean cut. Happy cutting!
