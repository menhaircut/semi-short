How To Determine Which Hair Colour Suits You

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which shade to go for. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of determining which hair colour suits you. From determining your skin tone to considering your lifestyle, we'll cover everything you need to know to make an informed decision. Read on to learn more!

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair colour suits you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to hold up a piece of silver fabric and a piece of gold fabric to your face. If the silver fabric makes your skin look bright and healthy, you have a cool skin tone. If the gold fabric makes your skin look bright and healthy, you have a warm skin tone. If both fabrics make your skin look bright and healthy, you have a neutral skin tone.

Considering Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair colour. If you work in a conservative environment, you may want to choose a natural-looking hair colour.

If you're adventurous and like to try new things, you may want to choose a bold hair colour. If you're not sure which hair colour to choose, start with a subtle change and work your way up to a bolder colour.

Identifying Your Natural Hair Colour

Identifying your natural hair colour is important when choosing a new hair colour. Your natural hair colour can help you determine which shades will look best on you. If you're not sure what your natural hair colour is, look at the roots of your hair. Your roots will give you a good idea of what your natural hair colour is.

It's important to note that your natural hair colour may have changed over time due to factors such as age and exposure to the sun. If you're not sure what your natural hair colour is, consult with a stylist.

Matching Your Hair Colour to Your Eyes

Matching your hair colour to your eyes can help enhance your natural beauty. If you have blue eyes, you may want to choose a hair colour that has warm tones such as golden blonde. If you have green eyes, you may want to choose a hair colour that has cool tones such as ash brown.

If you have brown eyes, you have a lot of options when it comes to hair colour. Warm shades such as caramel and honey will complement your eyes, while cool shades such as espresso and dark chocolate will provide a nice contrast.

Choosing the Right Hair Colour for Your Age

As we age, our skin tone and hair texture change. It's important to choose a hair colour that complements your age. If you're in your 20s, you can get away with bold and bright hair colours. If you're in your 30s or 40s, you may want to choose a hair colour that has more natural-looking tones.

If you're in your 50s or older, you may want to choose a hair colour that is lighter and has warm tones. This will help brighten your complexion and give you a more youthful appearance.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've chosen a hair colour, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair every day
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. Look for hair dyes that are designed for sensitive skin or consult with a stylist.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This depends on the colour and texture of your hair. Consult with a stylist to determine how often you should touch up your roots.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and considering your skin tone, lifestyle, natural hair colour, eye colour, and age, you can determine which hair colour suits you best. Remember to take steps to maintain your hair colour and care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
