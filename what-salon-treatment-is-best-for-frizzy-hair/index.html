What Salon Treatment Is Best For Frizzy Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nightmare to deal with. It can be stubborn, unmanageable, and just downright frustrating. Luckily, there are salon treatments that can help tame frizz and leave your hair looking smooth and shiny. In this article, we’ll discuss the best salon treatments for frizzy hair and how they work.

Table of Contents

  • Keratin Treatment
  • Hair Smoothing Treatment
  • Olaplex Treatment
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Keratin Treatment

Keratin treatments are one of the most popular salon treatments for frizzy hair. This treatment involves applying a keratin protein to the hair, which helps to smooth out the cuticle and reduce frizz. The treatment can last for several weeks, depending on how often you wash your hair and how much heat styling you do.

During a keratin treatment, your stylist will apply the keratin to your hair and then use a flat iron to seal it in. The treatment can take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair. After the treatment, you’ll need to avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours to give the keratin time to fully penetrate the hair shaft.

Hair Smoothing Treatment

A hair smoothing treatment is similar to a keratin treatment, but it uses different ingredients to achieve a smooth, frizz-free look. These treatments usually involve a combination of amino acids, collagen, and other proteins that help to strengthen the hair and improve its texture.

Like keratin treatments, hair smoothing treatments can last for several weeks and require some downtime after the treatment. You’ll want to avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours to allow the treatment to set in.

Olaplex Treatment

Olaplex is a relatively new treatment that has gained popularity in recent years. This treatment is designed to repair damaged hair and prevent future damage by strengthening the hair bonds. Olaplex can be used as a standalone treatment or added to a color or bleach service to prevent damage.

While Olaplex isn’t specifically designed to control frizz, it can help to improve the overall texture of the hair, making it easier to manage and style. If you have damaged hair that is prone to frizz, an Olaplex treatment may be a good option for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a salon treatment for frizzy hair?

The frequency of salon treatments for frizzy hair will depend on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may only need a treatment every few months, while others may need one every 4-6 weeks. Talk to your stylist about the best treatment plan for your hair.

Can I get a salon treatment if I have color-treated hair?

Yes, you can get a salon treatment if you have color-treated hair. However, you should talk to your stylist about which treatments are safe for your specific hair type and color.

Will a salon treatment make my hair completely straight?

No, a salon treatment will not make your hair completely straight. However, it will help to reduce frizz and make your hair easier to manage.

Hair Care Tips

While salon treatments can help to tame frizzy hair, it’s important to take care of your hair at home as well. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid overwashing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.

By following these tips and getting regular salon treatments, you can keep your frizzy hair under control and achieve the smooth, shiny look you’ve always wanted.
