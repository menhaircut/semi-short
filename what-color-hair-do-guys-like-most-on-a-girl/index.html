What Color Hair Do Guys Like Most On a Girl? Tips and FAQ

Are you wondering what hair color guys like most on a girl? You're not alone! Many women are curious about what men prefer when it comes to hair color. In this article, we'll explore the topic in detail, from the most popular hair colors to tips for taking care of your hair. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Most Popular Hair Colors Among Men
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Hair Color

Most Popular Hair Colors Among Men

According to a survey conducted by a popular dating app, men tend to prefer women with natural hair colors. The top three most attractive hair colors for women, according to the survey, are:

  1. Blonde
  2. Brunette
  3. Redhead

Of course, this doesn't mean that men don't find other hair colors attractive. It's important to remember that everyone has their own preferences, and there's no one "right" hair color.

Blonde Hair

Blonde hair is often associated with youthfulness and fun. It's a popular choice among women who want to lighten their natural hair color or add highlights. If you're considering going blonde, make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Brunette Hair

Brunette hair is timeless and sophisticated. It's a great choice for women who want a low-maintenance hair color that looks great year-round. If you have dark hair and are considering going brunette, make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Red Hair

Red hair is bold and striking. It's a great choice for women who want to make a statement with their hair color. If you're considering going red, make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When it comes to choosing the right hair color, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Consider your skin tone. Your hair color should complement your skin tone, not clash with it.
  2. Think about your natural hair color. If you're considering a drastic change, make sure to choose a color that will look natural with your skin tone.
  3. Consider your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, choose a low-maintenance color.

Hair Care Tips for Your Hair Color

No matter what hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  3. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  4. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Hair Color and Attraction

Q: Do guys really care about hair color?

A: Some guys do care about hair color, but it's not the most important factor when it comes to attraction. It's more important to be confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Q: What hair color do guys find least attractive?

A: According to the same survey mentioned earlier, men find unnatural hair colors (such as pink or purple) the least attractive.

Q: Can changing my hair color improve my dating life?

A: While changing your hair color won't magically improve your dating life, it can make you feel more confident and attractive. Confidence is key when it comes to attracting a partner!


In conclusion, there's no one "right" hair color that guys prefer on a girl. It's more important to choose a color that makes you feel confident and comfortable. No matter what color you choose, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. And remember, confidence is the most attractive quality of all!
