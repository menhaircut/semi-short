Long Weave Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Robby Sep 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be tricky. Fortunately, long weave hairstyles can flatter round faces by adding length and creating the illusion of a more elongated face shape. In this article, we'll share some of our favorite long weave hairstyles for round faces, along with tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

A long weave hairstyle involves adding hair extensions to natural hair to create a longer, fuller look. This style is perfect for those with round faces as it can help to elongate the face shape and create a more balanced appearance. With the right styling and maintenance, long weave hairstyles for round faces can be both stylish and practical.

How to

The first step in achieving a long weave hairstyle is to choose the right type of hair extensions. Synthetic hair extensions can be less expensive, but they may not look as natural or last as long as human hair extensions. Human hair extensions can be more expensive, but they can be styled and maintained just like natural hair.

Once you have the right hair extensions, you'll need to have them installed by a professional stylist. This typically involves braiding the natural hair and then sewing or gluing the extensions onto the braids. The stylist will then cut and style the hair to create the desired look.

Step by Step

1. Choose the right hair extensions

2. Have the extensions installed by a professional stylist

3. Cut and style the hair to create the desired look

4. Maintain the hair with regular washing, conditioning, and styling

Tips

1. Choose a style that adds length to the face, such as loose waves or a side-swept bang

2. Use a heat protectant spray when styling the hair with heat tools

3. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and create a greasy look

4. Schedule regular appointments with a stylist to maintain the extensions and keep the hair healthy

FAQ

What type of hair extensions are best for long weave hairstyles?

Human hair extensions are typically the best option for long weave hairstyles, as they can be styled and maintained just like natural hair.

How often do I need to wash my hair with a long weave hairstyle?

You should aim to wash your hair every 1-2 weeks, depending on your hair type and how often you use styling products.

How do I maintain my hair with a long weave hairstyle?

You can maintain your hair by regularly washing and conditioning it, using a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools, and avoiding too much product.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle, but it's especially important for long weave hairstyles. To keep your hair healthy, be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling with heat tools. It's also a good idea to schedule regular trims to get rid of split ends and keep the hair looking healthy and full.

With the right styling and maintenance, long weave hairstyles for round faces can be a great option for anyone looking for a stylish and practical hairstyle. Whether you choose loose waves, a side-swept bang, or another style, be sure to work with a professional stylist to achieve the best results.
