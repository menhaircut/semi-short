Face Framing Short Hairstyles With Bangs The Perfect Look For Every Occasion

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and looking for a change? Do you want to try something new that will make you look younger and more stylish? Then, face framing short hairstyles with bangs are the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is trendy, versatile, and suits all face shapes. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, tips, and hair care to maintain the style.

How to Achieve Face Framing Short Hairstyles with Bangs

Face framing short hairstyles with bangs are easy to achieve with the right haircut and styling techniques. The first step is to find a good hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut. You can also show them pictures of the hairstyle you want to achieve to get a better idea of what you are looking for.

Choose the Right Bangs

When choosing bangs, you need to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, go for long and layered bangs that will make your face look slimmer. If you have a square face, choose side-swept bangs that will soften your features. For a heart-shaped face, go for wispy bangs that will balance out your forehead and chin. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with any type of bangs.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair also matters when choosing face framing short hairstyles with bangs. If you have short hair, go for short bangs that will complement your hairstyle. For medium-length hair, choose long and layered bangs that will add dimension to your hair. If you have long hair, you can go for blunt or wispy bangs that will frame your face perfectly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving face framing short hairstyles with bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a styling mousse or gel to your hair to add volume.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Section your hair and cut the bangs according to your face shape.
  6. Style the bangs using a blow dryer and a round brush.
  7. Apply a hairspray to set the style.

Tips to Maintain the Style

Here are some tips to maintain face framing short hairstyles with bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q. Who can wear face framing short hairstyles with bangs?

Anyone can wear this hairstyle regardless of their age or face shape. It is a versatile hairstyle that suits all hair types and face shapes.

Q. Can I style my bangs differently?

Yes, you can style your bangs differently depending on your mood or occasion. You can style them straight, side-swept, or curly.

Q. How long do face framing short hairstyles with bangs last?

The hairstyle can last up to 6 weeks with proper maintenance and regular trims.

Hair Care for Face Framing Short Hairstyles with Bangs

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

In conclusion, face framing short hairstyles with bangs are a trendy and stylish choice for anyone looking for a change. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care, you can achieve the perfect look for every occasion.
