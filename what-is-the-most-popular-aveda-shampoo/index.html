The most popular Aveda shampoo is the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Shampoo. How to Use

To use the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Shampoo, wet your hair and massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair. Rinse thoroughly with water and repeat if necessary. For best results, follow up with the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Conditioner.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Shampoo:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with water.
  2. Pour a small amount of shampoo into your hand.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, working up a lather.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.
  5. Repeat if necessary.
  6. Follow up with the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Conditioner.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to get the most out of your Aveda Shampure Nurturing Shampoo:

  • Use a small amount of shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Massage the shampoo into your scalp to promote blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with water to remove all traces of shampoo.
  • Follow up with the Aveda Shampure Nurturing Conditioner for best results.

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:
