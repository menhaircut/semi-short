How To Make Your Hair Lay Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you struggle with getting your hair to lay back? Whether you’re going for a sleek and sophisticated look or just want to keep your hair out of your face, learning how to style your hair to lay back is a must. In this guide, we’ll show you how to make your hair lay back in just a few simple steps.

Description

When it comes to styling your hair, there are countless options to choose from. However, if you’re looking for a style that’s both classy and practical, laying your hair back is the way to go. Not only does this style keep your hair out of your face, but it also gives you a sleek and professional look that’s perfect for any occasion.

How to Make Your Hair Lay Back

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect laid-back hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. If your hair is wet or oily, it will be more difficult to style and may not hold its shape as well. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

To help your hair stay in place, apply a styling product such as hair gel or pomade. These products will help hold your hair in place and give it a sleek, polished look. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the roots and tips.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb or brush, comb your hair straight back from your forehead. Make sure to comb your hair evenly to avoid any clumps or tangles. If you have longer hair, you may need to use a hair tie or clip to keep it in place.

Step 4: Use a Blow Dryer

To help your hair stay in place, use a blow dryer on a cool setting to set your style. Hold the dryer about six inches away from your head and blow your hair back for a few seconds. This will help lock in your style and give you a sleek, polished finish.

Step 5: Use a Hairspray

To make sure your hair stays in place all day, finish off your style with a light hairspray. Spray the hairspray evenly over your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. This will help keep your hair in place and give it a sleek, polished finish.

Tips for Making Your Hair Lay Back

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect laid-back hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have shorter hair, use a comb with finer teeth to help control your style.
  • If you have longer hair, use a hair tie or clip to keep your hair in place while you’re styling it.
  • Blow dry your hair on a cool setting to help set your style without damaging your hair.
  • Use a light hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

FAQ

Here are a few common questions that people have about making their hair lay back:

How do I keep my hair from falling forward?

To keep your hair from falling forward, use a styling product such as hair gel or pomade to help hold your hair in place. You can also use a hair tie or clip to keep your hair back while you’re styling it.

What kind of comb should I use?

If you have shorter hair, use a comb with finer teeth to help control your style. If you have longer hair, use a comb with wider teeth to help detangle your hair before you start styling it.

How do I prevent my hair from getting oily?

To prevent your hair from getting oily, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. You can also use a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb any excess oil.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote strong, healthy hair.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect laid-back hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and strong.
