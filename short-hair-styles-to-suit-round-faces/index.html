Short Hair Styles To Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 2 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect short hair style can be a daunting task. You want a style that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Luckily, there are many short hair styles that suit round faces. In this article, we'll explore some of the best options and provide tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair styles can help elongate a round face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. It's important to choose a style that complements your facial features and highlights your best features.

When choosing a short hair style, there are several factors to consider. These include your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good stylist can help you find the perfect style that suits your needs.

How To

The first step in finding a short hair style that suits your round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you likely have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start to explore different short hair styles. Some of the best options for round faces include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped style that can help elongate a round face. This style works well for those with straight or wavy hair, and can be customized to suit your personal style.

Bob

A bob is a classic short hair style that can be tailored to suit round faces. A chin-length bob can help elongate the face, while a layered bob can add volume and texture.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a trendy and edgy short hair style that can work well for round faces. This style features one side of the hair longer than the other, creating an angular effect that can help elongate the face.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a short hair style that suits your round face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or wax.
  4. Style your hair using your fingers or a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling wand.

Tips

When choosing a short hair style for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, opt for a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, choose a style that thins out the hair and creates a sleeker look.

It's also important to consider the shape of your face when choosing a short hair style. Those with round faces should avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or heavy layers around the cheeks.

FAQ

What are the best short hair styles for round faces?

Some of the best short hair styles for round faces include the pixie cut, bob, and asymmetrical cut.

How do I style short hair?

To style short hair, start by washing and blow drying your hair. Apply a styling product and use your fingers or a styling tool to shape your hair. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair style looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes regular trims to maintain the shape of your style, as well as using high-quality hair care products that are suited to your hair type.

It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can maintain a short hair style that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident.
