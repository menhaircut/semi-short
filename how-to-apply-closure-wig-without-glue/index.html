How To Apply Closure Wig Without Glue

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Applying a closure wig without glue is a great way to protect your hairline and avoid damage to your natural hair. This method is also good for those who are allergic to adhesives or simply do not want to use them. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of applying a closure wig without glue.

Description

A closure wig is a type of wig that has a piece of lace or silk material at the front, allowing you to part the hair and create a natural-looking hairline. Traditionally, closure wigs are applied using glue or tape, but this can cause damage to the natural hairline and scalp. Applying a closure wig without glue is a healthier alternative that allows you to protect your hair and style your wig with ease.

How to Apply Closure Wig Without Glue: Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step to applying a closure wig without glue is to prepare your hair. If you have long hair, it is recommended that you braid it or put it in a bun to ensure that it is flat and does not interfere with the wig. You can also use a wig cap to keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Attach the Wig Clips

Next, attach the wig clips to the wig. Wig clips are small combs that are sewn onto the wig cap and help keep the wig in place. Place the wig on your head and position it where you want it to sit. Then, mark the position of the wig clips on the wig cap using a marker or chalk.

Step 3: Sew the Wig Clips

Once you have marked the position of the wig clips, remove the wig and sew them onto the wig cap. Use a needle and thread to sew the clips onto the cap, making sure they are secure and tight.

Step 4: Adjust the Wig

After you have sewn the wig clips onto the wig cap, put the wig back on and adjust it to make sure it sits comfortably and securely on your head. You can use a mirror to help you position the wig correctly.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once the wig is in place, you can style it as desired. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles and looks. Make sure to use heat protectant before using any hot tools on the wig.

Tips

- Make sure to measure your head before purchasing a closure wig to ensure a proper fit.

- Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and protect it from damage.

- Be gentle when combing or brushing the wig to avoid tangling or shedding.

FAQ

Can I wear a closure wig without glue?

Yes, you can wear a closure wig without glue by using wig clips to secure it in place.

How long does a closure wig last?

A closure wig can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

How do I clean my closure wig?

You can clean your closure wig by washing it with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse thoroughly and air dry.

Hair Care

To keep your closure wig looking its best, it is important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

- Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the wig.

- Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.

- Avoid using heat on the wig too often, as this can damage the hair fibers.

By following these steps and tips, you can confidently wear a closure wig without glue and protect your natural hairline while enjoying a natural-looking hairstyle.
