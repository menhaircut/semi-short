Does Short Hair Make You Look Bigger

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are someone who has always had long hair, you may have wondered if getting a short haircut will make you look bigger. This is a common concern for many people, especially those who have a round face or are on the heavier side. In this blog post, we will explore this question in detail and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle for your body type.

Description

Short hair is a popular hairstyle choice for many women, especially those who lead busy lives and want a low-maintenance hairstyle. However, there is a common belief that short hair can make you look bigger than you actually are. This is because short hair draws attention to your face and neck, which can make your body appear shorter and wider. Additionally, if you have a round face or are on the heavier side, a short haircut can highlight these features.

How to

If you are considering getting a short haircut but are worried about looking bigger, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your hairstyle flatters your body type:

1. Consider your face shape:

When choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous on top. Instead, opt for a hairstyle that is longer on top and shorter on the sides.

2. Choose the right length:

The length of your hair can also affect how it looks on your body. If you have a short neck, for example, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too close to your head. Instead, opt for a hairstyle that falls just below your chin or longer.

3. Add volume:

If you have thin or fine hair, adding volume to your hairstyle can help balance out your body proportions. Consider adding layers or using volumizing products to create a fuller, more textured look.

Step by Step

If you are still unsure about whether short hair will make you look bigger, here are some steps you can take to determine what length and style will flatter your body type:

1. Research hairstyles:

Before getting a haircut, do some research on different hairstyles that flatter your face shape and body type. Look for pictures of women with similar features to yours and take note of the hairstyles that look best on them.

2. Consult with a stylist:

A professional stylist can help you determine what length and style will flatter your body type. They can also offer advice on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

3. Try on wigs:

If you are still unsure about getting a short haircut, consider trying on wigs in different lengths and styles. This will give you a better idea of how you will look with shorter hair without committing to a permanent haircut.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering a short hairstyle:

1. Embrace your body:

Remember that your body is beautiful, no matter what size or shape it is. Don't be afraid to flaunt your curves and embrace your unique features.

2. Experiment with different styles:

Short hair offers a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. Experiment with different textures, lengths, and colors to find a style that suits your personality and lifestyle.

3. Take care of your hair:

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use styling products that are designed for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short hair can actually make you look more youthful and fresh, especially if you choose the right style for your face shape and body type.

Q: Can a short haircut be styled for formal events?

A: Absolutely. Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and sophisticated to textured and tousled. Talk to your stylist about different formal hairstyles that will work with your short haircut.

Q: How often do I need to get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It depends on your hair growth and the style of your haircut. Generally, you should get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

1. Wash and condition regularly:

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid buildup.

2. Use styling products:

Use styling products that are designed for your hair type to create your desired look. Be sure to apply products evenly and comb through to distribute.

3. Protect your hair:

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage. Additionally, wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

4. Get regular trims:

Get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and stylish. This will also prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, getting a short haircut does not necessarily make you look bigger. By choosing the right length and style for your body type, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features. Remember to take care of your hair and embrace your unique beauty!
