How To Keep Long Bangs Out Of Your Face

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long bangs can add a touch of glamour to any hairstyle, but they can be a real pain when they keep falling in your face. Whether you're working or playing, it's important to keep your hair out of your eyes so you can focus on what you're doing. In this blog post, we'll share some tips on how to keep long bangs out of your face, so you can look your best and stay productive.

How to Keep Long Bangs Out of Your Face

There are several ways to keep long bangs out of your face. Here are some options:

Hair Clips

Hair clips are an easy and functional way to keep your bangs out of your face. Simply clip the hair back off your forehead, and you're good to go. You can use a single clip or multiple clips, depending on the thickness of your hair and the style you're going for.

Bobby Pins

Bobby pins are another great option for keeping your bangs out of your face. These small pins can be used to secure your hair in place, while still maintaining a natural look. You can use bobby pins to create a side-swept look or to pin your bangs back in a half-up, half-down style.

Headbands

Headbands are not only trendy, but they can also be a great way to keep your bangs out of your face. You can choose from a wide range of styles, from sporty to elegant, depending on the occasion. Headbands are ideal for those with thicker hair, as they can help to keep your hair in place for longer periods of time.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep your long bangs out of your face:

  1. Start by washing and styling your hair as usual.
  2. Choose your preferred method of keeping your bangs out of your face, such as hair clips, bobby pins, or headbands.
  3. Take a small section of your bangs and pull it back off your forehead.
  4. Secure the hair in place using your chosen method.
  5. Repeat this process with any remaining sections of your bangs, until all the hair is secured off your face.
  6. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's secure and looks the way you want it to.
  7. Carry your chosen hair accessory with you throughout the day, in case you need to re-secure your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your long bangs out of your face:

  • Use hairspray to help keep your hair in place.
  • Choose hair accessories that match your outfit or personal style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the best way to keep your bangs out of your face.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to help absorb oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Can I use gel to keep my bangs out of my face?

A: Yes, you can use gel to keep your bangs in place. However, be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have long bangs?

A: It's recommended that you wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to follow a healthy hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your long bangs out of your face and look your best every day. Whether you're at work or at play, you can stay focused and confident with your hair under control. Remember to take good care of your hair, and it will reward you with healthy, beautiful locks for years to come.
