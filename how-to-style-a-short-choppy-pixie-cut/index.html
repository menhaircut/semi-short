How To Style A Short Choppy Pixie Cut

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have recently cut your hair into a short choppy pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style it. This trendy haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years and is a great way to show off your features while making a bold statement. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions for styling your short choppy pixie cut, as well as tips and advice for maintaining it.

Description

A short choppy pixie cut is a haircut that is typically very short on the sides and back, with longer layers on the top. This allows for a lot of versatility in styling, as you can choose to keep your hair slicked back, tousled, or even add a bit of volume for a more dramatic look. This haircut is perfect for those who want to make a statement and show off their unique style.

How to Style a Short Choppy Pixie Cut

Styling a short choppy pixie cut is actually quite simple, and can be achieved in just a few minutes. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in styling your short choppy pixie cut is to wash and condition your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and be sure to rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Next, use a towel to gently dry your hair, being careful not to rub too hard or cause any damage. You want to leave your hair slightly damp for the next step.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing spray or a styling cream. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 4: Use a Blow Dryer

Using a blow dryer, use your fingers to comb and shape your hair into the desired style. You can choose to add volume by blow-drying your hair in an upward direction, or keep it sleek by blow-drying it straight back.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Finally, add any finishing touches to your hair, such as hairspray or a shine serum. This will help to keep your hair in place and add a bit of shine for a polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Choppy Pixie Cut

Now that you know how to style your short choppy pixie cut, it is important to know how to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short choppy pixie cuts:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.

Can I still style my hair if it is very short?

Absolutely! Even if your hair is very short, you can still experiment with different styles and products to achieve a unique look.

Is a short choppy pixie cut easy to maintain?

Yes, a short choppy pixie cut is actually quite easy to maintain. With regular trims and the right products, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling its best. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Now that you know how to style and maintain your short choppy pixie cut, you can rock this trendy haircut with confidence. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you.
