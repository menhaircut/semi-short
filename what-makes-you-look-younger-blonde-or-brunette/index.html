What Makes You Look Younger Blonde Or Brunette

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to take a few years off your appearance, changing your hair color can be a great way to do it. But what makes you look younger: blonde or brunette? In this article, we'll explore the different factors that can affect how youthful you look with either hair color. We'll also provide some tips on how to care for your hair, regardless of which color you choose.

Table of Contents

  • Factors that Affect How Youthful You Look
  • Blonde Hair and Youthfulness
  • Brunette Hair and Youthfulness
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Factors that Affect How Youthful You Look

There are a few different factors that can affect how youthful you look with either blonde or brunette hair:

  • Skin tone: Your skin tone can play a big role in how well blonde or brunette hair suits you. Generally, those with cooler undertones will look better with blonde hair, while those with warmer undertones will look better with brunette hair.
  • Eye color: Your eye color can also affect how well blonde or brunette hair suits you. Those with blue or green eyes tend to look better with blonde hair, while those with brown or hazel eyes tend to look better with brunette hair.
  • Age: Your age can also play a role in how youthful you look with different hair colors. Generally, lighter hair colors like blonde can make you look younger, while darker hair colors like brunette can make you look more mature.

Blonde Hair and Youthfulness

Blonde hair can be a great way to make you look younger. Here are a few reasons why:

  • It brightens your complexion: Blonde hair can help brighten your complexion, which can make you look more youthful and radiant.
  • It can make you look more carefree: Blonde hair has long been associated with a carefree, playful attitude. If you're looking to convey a more youthful vibe, blonde hair can be a great choice.
  • It can soften your features: If you have sharp or angular features, blonde hair can help soften them and make you look more youthful.

Brunette Hair and Youthfulness

While blonde hair can be a great choice for those looking to look younger, brunette hair can also have its benefits:

  • It can make you look more sophisticated: If you're looking to convey a more mature, sophisticated vibe, brunette hair can be a great choice.
  • It can make your eyes stand out: Brunette hair can help make your eye color pop, which can be a great way to draw attention to your youthful features.
  • It can make your hair look thicker: Generally, darker hair colors like brunette can make your hair look thicker and more voluminous, which can be a great way to look more youthful.

Hair Care Tips

No matter which hair color you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and youthful. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo: Sulfates can be harsh on your hair, so it's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Deep condition regularly: Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair soft, shiny, and healthy.
  • Limit heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Try to limit your use of these tools, or use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can help keep split ends at bay and keep your hair looking healthy and youthful.

FAQs

Q: Will blonde hair always make me look younger?

A: Not necessarily. How youthful you look with blonde hair will depend on a variety of factors, including your skin tone, eye color, and age.

Q: Can I dye my hair blonde or brunette at home?

A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it's generally best to leave it to the professionals. A professional stylist can help ensure that your hair color looks natural and flattering.

Q: How can I prevent my hair color from fading?

A: To prevent your hair color from fading, try to limit your use of heat styling tools, use a sulfate-free shampoo, and deep condition your hair regularly.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's generally a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

So, what makes you look younger: blonde or brunette? Ultimately, the answer will depend on your individual features and preferences. But by considering factors like skin tone, eye color, and age, you can choose the hair color that will make you look and feel your best.
