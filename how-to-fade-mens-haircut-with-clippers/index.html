How To Fade Men s Haircut With Clippers A Step By Step Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve a stylish haircut for men, the fade is a popular choice that never goes out of style. With the help of a good set of clippers, you can easily achieve this look at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to fade men's haircut with clippers in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Fade Men's Haircut with Clippers

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, and a pair of scissors.

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard

To begin, decide on the length of the fade you want. This will determine the guard size you'll need to start with. If you want a shorter fade, start with a smaller guard size, like a number two. For a longer fade, start with a larger guard size, like a number four.

Step 2: Create the Base

Start by creating a base with the chosen guard size. Begin at the bottom of the head and move the clippers upward in a straight line. Repeat this process around the head, making sure to keep the line straight.

Step 3: Change the Guard

Once you've created the base, it's time to switch to a smaller guard size. Gradually move the clippers upward, blending the shorter hair to the longer hair. Repeat this process with progressively smaller guard sizes until you've achieved the desired fade.

Step 4: Define the Hairline

Use scissors to trim any excess hair around the hairline and ears. This will help to create a clean, polished look.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you've finished fading the hair, use a comb to check for any uneven spots or stray hairs. Trim as needed to create a clean, polished look.

Tips for Fading Men's Haircut with Clippers

While fading men's haircut with clippers is a relatively simple process, there are a few tips that can help you achieve the best results.

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing the process can result in uneven fades.
  • Use scissors to create clean and defined lines around the hairline and ears.
  • Experiment with different guard sizes to achieve different fade lengths.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I fade my own hair at home?

A: Yes, fading men's haircut with clippers can be done at home with the right tools and techniques.

Q: What guard size should I use to start a fade?

A: The guard size you start with will depend on the length of the fade you want. Generally, a number two guard is a good starting point for shorter fades, while a number four is better for longer fades.

Q: How often should I fade my hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, haircuts every four to six weeks are recommended to maintain a fresh and polished look.

Hair Care for Faded Haircuts

To maintain a faded haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Regular washing and conditioning, as well as occasional deep conditioning treatments, can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Additionally, using a hair oil or serum can help to add shine and prevent dryness. Avoid using excessive heat styling tools, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and polished faded haircut at home. Experiment with different guard sizes and techniques to find the perfect fade for your style.
