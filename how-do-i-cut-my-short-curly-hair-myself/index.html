How Do I Cut My Short Curly Hair Myself

Written by Frank Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to trim your short curly hair at home, you've come to the right place. Cutting your own hair may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut your short curly hair at home, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Short Curly Hair Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair

How to Cut Short Curly Hair Yourself

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, hair clips, a comb, and a spray bottle with water. Once you have these tools, follow the steps below to cut your short curly hair at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Once your hair is clean and damp, use a comb to part your hair where you want to cut it.

Step 3: Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair, leaving only the section you want to cut loose.

Step 4: Use your comb to comb through the section of hair you want to cut. This will help you see any uneven areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 5: Take your scissors and start cutting small sections of hair at a time. Cut in a downward motion, starting with the ends of your hair and working your way up.

Step 6: Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've reached your desired length. Remember to trim both sides of your hair evenly.

Step 7: Once you've finished cutting, use your comb to style your hair as desired. You can also use a diffuser or hair dryer to dry and style your hair.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your short curly hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to help you see the back of your head while cutting.
  • Trim your hair while it's damp to avoid cutting off too much length.
  • Don't cut your hair too often. It's best to wait 6-8 weeks between haircuts to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short curly hair at home:

Can I cut my curly hair when it's dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's damp to avoid cutting off too much length. However, if you need to trim your hair when it's dry, make sure to cut small sections at a time.

What type of scissors should I use to cut my hair?

Use a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors. Don't use regular household scissors, as they can cause split ends and damage your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Curly Hair

After cutting your short curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut your short curly hair at home, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time and be patient, and soon you'll have beautiful, healthy curls.
