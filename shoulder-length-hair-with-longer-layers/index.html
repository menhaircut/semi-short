Shoulder Length Hair With Longer Layers A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Eveline Nov 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, then shoulder length hair with longer layers might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been trending since 2021 and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, provide step-by-step instructions, share some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice.

Description

Shoulder length hair with longer layers is a hairstyle that features layers that are longer than the shoulder length. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume and movement to their hair, while keeping it at a manageable length. The layers are cut in a way that creates a seamless blend from the roots to the ends, giving the hair a natural look.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Longer Layers

To achieve this hairstyle, you'll need to visit a professional stylist. They will be able to cut the layers in a way that complements your face shape and hair texture. You can bring in pictures of the style you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve shoulder length hair with longer layers:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  5. Divide your hair into sections
  6. Cut the layers using a point cutting technique
  7. Blend the layers by using a razor or scissors
  8. Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener
  9. Apply a hair serum to add shine
  10. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling shoulder length hair with longer layers:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or curls
  • Apply a hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz
  • Use a hairspray to hold the style in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair with longer layers:

1. Is shoulder length hair with longer layers suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed to maintain this style?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the layers.

3. Can I wear my hair up with this style?

Yes, you can wear your hair up with this style. You can create a messy bun or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair with longer layers healthy and shiny, you should follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

Overall, shoulder length hair with longer layers is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain. By following our tips and advice, you can achieve this look and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
