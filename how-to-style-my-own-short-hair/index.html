How To Style My Own Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every time you need a new hairstyle? Do you want to learn how to style your own short hair at home? Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your own short hair, tips on how to make your hairstyle last longer, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style My Own Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style My Own Short Hair

Styling your own short hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Flat iron
  • Curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Texture spray
  • Styling cream
  • Comb or brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your own short hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If you have time, let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer to avoid heat damage.

Step 2: Apply styling cream

Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair to help control frizz and add shine. Use your fingers to distribute the cream evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Create texture

Use a texture spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling iron

Depending on the look you’re going for, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair. Use the flat iron to straighten your hair or create waves, or use the curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place. Use a comb or brush to smooth any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your own short hair:

  • Invest in high-quality styling tools.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new hairstyles.
  • Use heat protectant products to minimize heat damage.
  • Take care of your hair by using a nourishing shampoo and conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles are best for short hair?

Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

How often should I wash my short hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What products should I use to style my short hair?

Use a combination of styling cream, texture spray, and hairspray to create your desired hairstyle.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it’s still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Now that you know how to style your own short hair, you can save time and money by styling your hair at home. With a little practice, you’ll be a pro in no time!
