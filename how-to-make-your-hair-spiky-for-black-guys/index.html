How To Make Your Hair Spiky For Black Guys

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a black guy looking to add some edge to your hairstyle? Spiky hair is a popular and trendy look that can be achieved with a few simple steps. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair spiky for black guys, along with some helpful tips and FAQs. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Spiky
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiky Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Spiky Hair

How to Make Your Hair Spiky

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first discuss what you'll need to make your hair spiky. Here are some essential tools:

  • Styling gel or wax
  • Comb
  • Hair dryer

With these tools and a bit of patience, you can achieve a spiky hairstyle that will turn heads. Let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair spiky:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a towel to dry it thoroughly.
  2. Squeeze a small amount of styling gel or wax onto your fingertips.
  3. Apply the gel or wax to your hair, focusing on the roots and the tips of your hair.
  4. Use a comb to create the desired spikes. For short hair, use the comb to push your hair upwards. For longer hair, use the comb to twist and shape your hair into spikes.
  5. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the spikes. Hold the hair dryer about six inches away from your head and blow dry your hair for a few minutes.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to further shape and style the spikes.
  7. Apply a small amount of hairspray to hold the spikes in place.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can achieve a spiky hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd.

Tips for Spiky Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making your hair spiky:

  • Start with a small amount of styling gel or wax and add more as needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Experiment with different types of gel or wax to find the one that works best for your hair texture and style.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the shape and height of your spikes. Spiky hair is all about creativity and individuality.

FAQs

1. Can spiky hair work for all hair types?

Yes! Spiky hair can be achieved with any hair type, whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair.

2. Will spiky hair damage my hair?

As long as you use the proper hair products and don't over-style your hair, spiky hair should not cause damage.

3. How long will my spiky hair last?

This depends on the type of hair products you use and how well you maintain your hair. Generally, spiky hair can last for several hours to a full day.

Hair Care for Spiky Hair

After making your hair spiky, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your spikes.
  • Avoid touching or playing with your hair too much, as this can cause the spikes to fall out of shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain a stylish and edgy spiky hairstyle. Have fun experimenting with different products and styles to find the one that works best for you!
