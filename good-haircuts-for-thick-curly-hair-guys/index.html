Good Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair Guys A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy with thick, curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find a haircut that works for you. Too many barbers and stylists don't know how to handle your hair type, leaving you with a look that's more frizzy than fabulous. But fear not! With the right cut and styling techniques, you can showcase your curls in all their glory. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for thick curly hair guys, along with tips for keeping your locks healthy and vibrant.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to a great haircut is finding a stylist who understands your hair type. Look for a salon or barber that specializes in curly hair, or ask for recommendations from friends with similar locks. Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's time to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and showcases your curls. Here are some options to consider:

Short and Textured

A short, textured cut can look great on guys with thick, curly hair. Ask for a choppy, layered style that's shorter on the sides and longer on top, with plenty of texture to enhance your curls. This cut works well for men with round or square faces, as it adds height and balances out facial features.

Long and Layered

If you're willing to grow your hair out, a long, layered cut can be a great option for thick curly hair. This style works well for men with oval or heart-shaped faces, as it adds volume and movement to the hair. Ask for layers that start below the chin and taper down towards the ends, with longer layers on top for added height.

Tapered Fade

A tapered fade is a classic cut that works well for guys with thick, curly hair. This style features short, buzzed sides that fade into longer curls on top. It's a great option for men with square or rectangular faces, as it adds definition and structure to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Once you've got the right cut, it's important to know how to style your curls for maximum impact. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or cream, from root to tip.
  2. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage curl formation.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on low heat. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around to dry your curls evenly.
  4. If desired, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need extra definition.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to lock in your style.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Shiny Curls

Healthy hair is key to a great haircut, so it's important to take care of your locks on a daily basis. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can lead to frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle while your hair is damp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and shiny.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as these can damage your hair and disrupt curl formation.
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf in cold weather, and using a UV protectant spray in the summer.

FAQ: Answers to Your Burning Hair Questions

Here are some common questions guys with thick curly hair may have:

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your individual hair growth rate and the style you're going for. As a general rule, aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Should I use a diffuser or air-dry my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have very tight curls, a diffuser can help enhance curl formation and reduce frizz. If you have looser curls, air-drying may give you a more natural look.

Q: Can I dye my curly hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a dye that's specifically formulated for curly hair, as these tend to be more gentle and less likely to cause damage or dryness.

Hair Care Products for Thick Curly Hair Guys

Here are some products to consider adding to your hair care routine:

  • Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner: Look for sulfate-free formulas that are designed to enhance curl formation and reduce frizz.
  • Curl Enhancing Cream or Mousse: Apply to damp hair to enhance curl formation and reduce frizz.
  • Deep Conditioning Treatment: Use once a week to keep your curls hydrated and shiny.
  • UV Protectant Spray: Use in the summer to protect your hair from sun damage.

With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can showcase your thick, curly hair in all its glory. Whether you opt for a short, textured cut or a long, layered style, embrace your curls and rock your unique look with confidence.
