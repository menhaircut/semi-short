Easy Hairstyles to Try on Yourself

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to try something new? Don't worry, we have got you covered! In this article, we will be sharing some easy hairstyles that you can try on yourself. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's go over some basic tips to ensure that your hair is ready for styling:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Blow dry your hair or let it air dry completely
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for a casual day out or a lazy day at home. Here's how you can achieve it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail
  3. Secure with bobby pins
  4. Pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create a messy look

2. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is another easy yet stylish option. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hairband
  2. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume
  3. You can also curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look

3. Braided Headband

The braided headband is a great option if you want to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to create it:

  1. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it
  2. Bring the braid across your head like a headband and secure it with bobby pins
  3. Repeat on the other side
  4. You can also tuck the ends of the braids behind your ears for a neater look

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like hairbands, clips, and scrunchies
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair with a sea salt spray or texturizing powder
  • Practice makes perfect, so keep trying different hairstyles until you find one that you love

FAQ

1. How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently brush your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help smooth out any knots.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days. If your hair feels greasy or dirty before that, then it's okay to wash it more often.


Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein and vitamins
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

With these easy hairstyles and hair care tips, you can switch up your look and keep your hair healthy at the same time. Have fun experimenting and don't forget to take some selfies to show off your new hairdo!
