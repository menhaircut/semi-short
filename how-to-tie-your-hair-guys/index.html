How To Tie Your Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 14, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you might be wondering how to tie it up. Whether you're going for a casual look or need your hair out of your face for work or sports, there are plenty of ways to tie your hair back. In this article, we'll go over some popular methods for tying your hair guys, along with tips for hair care and FAQ.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Your Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Tie Your Hair Guys

There are many different ways to tie your hair guys, depending on your hair length, thickness, and personal style. Here are some popular methods:

  • Ponytail
  • Top Knot
  • Bun
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Braid

Ponytail

The ponytail is the easiest and most common way to tie your hair guys. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it with an elastic band. You can position the ponytail high or low, depending on your preference. This style is great for keeping your hair out of your face.

Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy and stylish way to tie your hair guys. To create this look, gather your hair at the top of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie. This style is perfect for a night out or a casual day look.

Bun

The bun is a classic and versatile way to tie your hair guys. To create a bun, gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a coil. Wrap the coil around itself to form a bun, and secure it with bobby pins or an elastic band. This style is great for formal events or a sleek, professional look.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a popular choice for guys with longer hair. To create this look, gather the top half of your hair and tie it with an elastic band. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose. This style is great for a casual, laid-back look.

Braid

The braid is a fun and unique way to tie your hair guys. To create a braid, divide your hair into three sections and weave them together. You can braid your hair all the way down or stop halfway for a half-braid look. This style is perfect for a festival or a bohemian-inspired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for tying your hair guys:

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove tangles and knots.
  2. Decide on the style you want to create (ponytail, top knot, bun, half-up half-down, or braid).
  3. Gather your hair into the desired position with your hands.
  4. Secure your hair with an elastic band or hair tie.
  5. If you're creating a bun or top knot, twist your hair into a coil and wrap it around itself.
  6. Secure the bun or top knot with bobby pins or another elastic band.
  7. If you're creating a braid, weave your hair together in three sections.
  8. Secure the end of the braid with an elastic band or hair tie.
  9. Adjust your hair and style as needed.
  10. Spray your hair with hairspray or a styling product to keep it in place.

Tips for Tying Your Hair

Here are some tips for tying your hair guys:

  • Use an elastic band or hair tie that won't damage your hair, such as a soft scrunchie or a fabric hair tie.
  • Don't tie your hair too tightly, as this can cause headaches and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and positions to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, hats, or hair clips for a unique look.
  • Take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using conditioner, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ about Tying Your Hair

Can I tie my hair guys if it's short?

Yes, you can still tie your hair guys if it's short. Try a half-up half-down style or a small top knot at the back of your head.

What's the best way to tie my hair guys if I'm exercising?

A high ponytail or bun is the best way to tie your hair guys if you're exercising. This will keep your hair out of your face and prevent it from getting tangled or sweaty.

Do I need to use hair products when tying my hair guys?

You don't need to use hair products when tying your hair guys, but they can help keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways. Use a light hairspray or styling cream if needed.

Hair Care for Guys with Long Hair

Guys with long hair need to take extra care of their hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and methods for tying your hair guys, you're ready to rock any hairstyle you want. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy for a sleek, stylish look.
