How To Make Fake Curtain Bangs With A Straightener

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle but don't want to commit to a permanent change, fake curtain bangs might be the perfect solution. Not only are they easy to create, but they're also a stylish addition to any hair length or texture. In this blog post, we'll show you how to make fake curtain bangs with a straightener in just a few simple steps.

Description

Fake curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle trend that involves creating the illusion of bangs by parting your hair in the middle and styling the front sections to frame your face. This style is inspired by the classic curtain bangs, which are shorter layers of hair that are cut to frame the face and parted in the middle. By using a straightener to create the shape of the bangs, you can achieve this look without committing to a haircut.

How to

Here's how to make fake curtain bangs with a straightener:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you begin styling. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it with a flat iron or blow dryer beforehand.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into two equal parts by creating a center part down the middle of your head. Use a comb to make sure the part is straight and even.

Step 3: Create the bangs shape

Take a small section of hair from one side of your part, and twist it away from your face. Clamp the straightener down on the twisted section and slowly glide it down towards the ends. This will create a soft, rounded shape that frames your face. Repeat this process on the other side of your part.

Step 4: Blend the bangs

Once you've created the shape of your bangs, use the straightener to blend them into the rest of your hair. Take small sections of hair from the sides of your face and gently run the straightener over them to create a seamless transition from your bangs to the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Style and finish

To finish off the look, style your hair as desired. You can leave it straight, add some loose waves, or pull it back into a ponytail or bun. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your fake curtain bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Experiment with different sizes and shapes of curls to create different looks
  • Try adding a little texture to your bangs by lightly teasing the roots with a comb or brush

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fake curtain bangs:

Can I create fake curtain bangs with a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to create fake curtain bangs. Simply twist small sections of hair away from your face and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release and gently pull the curl down to create a softer shape.

How long do fake curtain bangs last?

Fake curtain bangs can last until you wash your hair, so they're perfect for a one-day hair change. If you want to make them last longer, use a little hairspray to hold the shape in place.

Will fake curtain bangs look good on me?

Fake curtain bangs can be flattering on any face shape. They're a great way to add some dimension and framing to your face without committing to a haircut.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Avoid using high heat settings on your tools
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools to a few times a week
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished

Now that you know how to make fake curtain bangs with a straightener, it's time to give this trendy hairstyle a try. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a stylish new look in no time.
