How To Train Your Hair To Be Slicked Back

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a classic, sophisticated hairstyle that never goes out of style, you may want to consider slicking your hair back. A slicked back hairstyle is versatile and can be worn for various occasions, from a formal event to a casual night out with friends. However, not everyone can pull off the look, and it may take some patience and practice to train your hair to stay slicked back. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to train your hair to be slicked back.

Description

Slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It is a hairstyle where the hair is combed back and held in place with a product, such as gel or pomade. The slicked back hair look is often associated with businessmen, celebrities, and other influential people.

How to Train Your Hair to be Slicked Back

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start training your hair to be slicked back, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent product buildup. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and then dry it completely.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of hair product, such as gel or pomade, to your hair. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use your fingers or a comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a fine-tooth comb to comb your hair back. Start at the front of your head and work your way back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you are not sure which direction to comb your hair, try combing it straight back first.

Step 4: Train Your Hair

Repeat steps 2 and 3 every day, gradually training your hair to stay slicked back. You may need to experiment with different hair products and combs to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

Tips

- Be patient. It may take a few weeks or even months to train your hair to stay slicked back.

- Use a high-quality hair product that is designed for your hair type and style.

- Experiment with different combs to find the one that works best for you.

- Use a hair dryer to help set your hair in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I slick back short hair?

A: Yes, you can slick back short hair. However, you may need to use more product and a smaller comb to achieve the desired look.

Q: Will slicking back my hair damage it?

A: Slicking back your hair should not damage it as long as you use high-quality hair products and comb your hair gently.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it is curly?

A: Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and a larger comb to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

Slicking back your hair can be a great way to achieve a classic, sophisticated look. However, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain its health. Be sure to shampoo and condition your hair regularly, and use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and style. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can cause damage and breakage.
