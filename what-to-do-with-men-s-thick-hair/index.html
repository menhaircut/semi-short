What To Do With Men s Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it provides volume and texture, it can also be difficult to style and manage. In this guide, we'll explore the best hairstyles, products, and tips for men with thick hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Manage Men's Thick Hair
  • Step by Step Guide for Styling Thick Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

How to Manage Men's Thick Hair

Managing thick hair starts with finding the right haircut. Shorter hairstyles can help keep thick hair under control, but longer styles can work as well with proper maintenance. Layered cuts can also help reduce bulk and add dimension to thick hair.

Once you have the right cut, it's important to use the right products for your hair type. Thick hair requires more moisture than other hair types, so look for shampoos and conditioners that provide hydration. Leave-in conditioners and hair oils can also help keep thick hair healthy and manageable.

Best Hairstyles for Men with Thick Hair

There are a variety of hairstyles that work well for men with thick hair. Here are some popular options:

  • Crew cut
  • Short back and sides
  • Side part
  • Slicked back
  • Textured crop
  • Quiff
  • Long and layered

Step by Step Guide for Styling Thick Hair

Styling thick hair can be a challenge, but the right products and techniques can make a big difference. Here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Blow dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting, using your hands or a brush to create the desired style.
  4. Finish with a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling thick hair. Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

Maintaining thick hair requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it for healthy, manageable hair. Here are some tips:

  • Don't shampoo your hair every day. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet. This helps prevent breakage and damage.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

FAQs about Men's Thick Hair

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean without stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Is it better to blow dry or air dry thick hair?

A: Blow drying can help add volume and texture to thick hair, but air drying is also a good option if you're short on time or want a more natural look.

Q: Can I use hair gel on my thick hair?

A: Yes, hair gel can work well on thick hair to add hold and texture. Just be sure to use a small amount and avoid using it every day to prevent build-up.

Hair Care Products for Thick Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for men with thick hair:

  • Moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Styling wax or pomade

With the right haircut, products, and styling techniques, men with thick hair can achieve a variety of stylish looks. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
