Best Hairstyle For Rectangle Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a rectangle face shape, choosing the right hairstyle can make a world of difference in how you look and feel. A rectangle face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, high forehead, and straight sides. The best hairstyle for rectangle face shapes should soften the angles of the face and add width to the forehead and cheeks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for rectangle face shapes, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror and examine the shape of your face.
  3. Look at the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin.

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, and your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a rectangle face shape.

The Best Hairstyles for Rectangle Face Shapes

The best hairstyles for rectangle face shapes are those that add width to the forehead and cheeks while softening the angles of the jawline. Here are some of the best options:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are an excellent option for rectangle face shapes because they add volume and width to the forehead, which helps balance out the strong jawline. You can wear them long or short, depending on your preference.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option because it adds volume to the sides of the face, which helps soften the angles of the jawline. You can wear it straight or with a slight curl for added texture.

3. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for rectangle face shapes because they add volume to the sides of the face and create movement, which helps soften the angles of the jawline. You can wear your hair straight or with loose waves for added texture.

4. Soft Waves

Soft, loose waves are an excellent option for rectangle face shapes because they add volume to the sides of the face while softening the angles of the jawline. You can create soft waves using a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Style Your Hair for a Rectangle Face Shape

Now that you know the best hairstyles for rectangle face shapes let's talk about how to style your hair to achieve these looks.

1. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs to one side.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling cream to keep your bangs in place.

2. Layered Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair from root to tip.
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a curling iron, create loose waves in your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and keep your hair in place.

4. Soft Waves

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and braid each section overnight.
  3. Untangle the braids in the morning, and use your fingers to create soft waves.
  4. Apply a small amount of hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have the perfect hairstyle for your rectangle face shape, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage when using styling tools.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a rectangle face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a rectangle face shape. However, it's best to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the strong jawline. You can achieve this by adding soft waves or wearing side-swept bangs.

2. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a rectangle face shape?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a rectangle face shape. However, it's best to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the strong jawline. You can achieve this by adding layers or wearing side-swept bangs.

3. What hair colors look best on rectangle face shapes?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It depends on your skin tone and personal preference. However, highlights and lowlights can help add depth and dimension to your hair, which can be flattering on rectangle face shapes.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for your rectangle face shape can make a world of difference in how you look and feel. The best hairstyles for rectangle face shapes add volume to the forehead and cheeks while softening the angles of the jawline. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there's a perfect hairstyle out there for you. Use these tips to help you find and maintain the perfect hairstyle for your rectangle face shape.
