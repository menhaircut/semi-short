Best Straight Iron For Thick Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Description

If you have thick curly hair, you know the struggle of trying to straighten it. It can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have the right tools. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best straight iron for thick curly hair. We've researched and tested a variety of straight irons to bring you the top picks for achieving sleek, straight hair.

How to Choose the Best Straight Iron for Thick Curly Hair

Before we dive into the top picks, let's talk about what to look for when choosing a straight iron for thick curly hair. Here are some things to consider: 1. Plate Material: Look for a straight iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates. These materials distribute heat evenly and won't damage your hair. 2. Plate Size: For thick curly hair, you'll want a straight iron with wider plates. This will allow you to straighten more hair at once and make the process quicker. 3. Heat Settings: Look for a straight iron with adjustable heat settings. Thick curly hair requires higher heat to straighten, but you don't want to damage your hair in the process. Adjustable heat settings allow you to find the perfect temperature for your hair.

Top Picks

Now that you know what to look for, let's take a look at the top picks for the best straight iron for thick curly hair: 1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Straightening Iron: This straight iron has 1.75-inch plates, making it perfect for thick curly hair. The titanium plates heat up quickly and evenly, and the adjustable heat settings allow you to find the perfect temperature for your hair. 2. CHI G2 Ceramic and Titanium Straightening Iron: The CHI G2 has 1.25-inch plates that are perfect for thick curly hair. HSI Professional Glider Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron: This straight iron has 1-inch plates, making it perfect for shorter hair or touch-ups. Step by Step Guide

Now that you have your straight iron, it's time to straighten your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving sleek, straight hair: 1. Wash and condition your hair. 2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. 3. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry. 4. Section your hair into manageable sections. 5. Turn on your straight iron and adjust the heat settings to the desired temperature. 6. Starting at the roots, clamp the straight iron down on a small section of hair. 7. Slowly glide the straight iron down the length of your hair. 8. Repeat steps 6 and 7 for each section of hair. 9. Once you've straightened all of your hair, apply a smoothing serum to tame any flyaways.

Tips

- Always use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair. - Don't straighten your hair when it's wet. This can cause damage to your hair. - Don't use a straight iron on the same section of hair multiple times. This can cause damage to your hair. - Use a smoothing serum to tame any flyaways.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a straight iron on wet hair? A: No, you should never use a straight iron on wet hair. This can cause damage to your hair. Q: How often should I straighten my hair? A: It's best to limit straightening to once a week to prevent damage to your hair. Q: How do I clean my straight iron? A: Use a damp cloth to wipe down the plates of your straight iron after each use. For a deeper clean, use rubbing alcohol on a cloth to clean the plates.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while straightening, it's important to care for it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair: - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. - Limit heat styling to once a week. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. - Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Conclusion

With the right straight iron and proper hair care, you can achieve sleek, straight hair even with thick curly hair. Remember to look for a straight iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates, adjustable heat settings, and wider plates for easier straightening. Follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
