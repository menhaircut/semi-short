How To Cut The Top Of Guys Hair With Scissors

Written by Eveline Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money on haircuts, or just want to give your significant other a trim, cutting the top of guys hair with scissors can be a great option. While it may seem intimidating at first, with a bit of practice and the right technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut the top of guys hair with scissors.

Description

Cutting the top of guys hair with scissors involves trimming the hair on the crown of the head. This area is usually longer than the sides and back, and is where most of the volume and texture of the hairstyle comes from. By cutting the top of the hair, you can create a variety of different styles, from a classic crew cut to a messy, textured look.

How to Cut the Top of Guys Hair with Scissors

Before you start cutting, you'll need the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to cut the top of guys hair with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of the hair, and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Hair

Using your comb, section the hair into 1-2 inch sections. This will help you work more methodically and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Cut Hair

Starting at the front of the head, hold a section of hair between your fingers and use your scissors to trim off any excess length. Work your way back towards the crown of the head, taking care to keep your cuts even and consistent. Remember to cut less hair than you think you need to, as you can always go back and take off more if necessary.

Step 4: Blend Hair

Once you've cut all the hair on the top of the head, use your scissors to blend the hair at the sides and back. This will help create a seamless transition between the different lengths.

Step 5: Check for Balance

Check the hair on both sides of the head to make sure it's balanced and even. Make any necessary adjustments by trimming small sections of hair until you achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting the top of guys hair with scissors:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to lift up sections of hair and create a guide for your scissors to follow.
  • Take breaks to step back and assess the overall look of the cut.
  • If you're unsure about a particular section, err on the side of caution and cut less hair.

FAQ

Can I Cut the Top of Guys Hair with Clippers?

While clippers can be a good option for cutting the sides and back of the head, they're not ideal for cutting the top. Scissors allow for more precision and control, and can help create a more textured, natural-looking cut.

Do I Need to Use Thinning Shears?

Thinning shears can be helpful for creating texture and removing bulk, but they're not necessary for cutting the top of guys hair with scissors. If you're just starting out, stick to regular scissors and focus on achieving a clean, even cut.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting the top of guys hair with scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain the look. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy, and consider using a styling product to add texture and hold. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can help keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
