How To Hide A Bad Hair Color Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you unhappy with your hair color? Did your latest dye job leave you with an unflattering shade? Don't worry, you can still hide a bad hair color without resorting to drastic measures such as cutting or shaving your hair. In this blog post, we will discuss some easy and effective ways to hide a bad hair color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide a Bad Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Hide a Bad Hair Color

The first step to hiding a bad hair color is to choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color. If you have no idea what your natural hair color is, look at old photos or ask someone who knows. You can also use a color chart to determine your natural hair color.

If your hair is too dark, you can lighten it with a clarifying shampoo or a hair color remover. If your hair is too light, you can darken it with a semi-permanent or demi-permanent hair color. These types of hair color will not damage your hair as much as permanent hair color.

If you are not confident in coloring your hair at home, you can always go to a salon and have a professional do it for you. They can also give you advice on what color to choose.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to hide a bad hair color:

  1. Choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color.
  2. If your hair is too dark, lighten it with a clarifying shampoo or a hair color remover.
  3. If your hair is too light, darken it with a semi-permanent or demi-permanent hair color.
  4. Apply the new hair color according to the instructions.
  5. Leave the color on for the recommended time.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  7. Apply a deep conditioner or hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  8. Style your hair as usual.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you hide a bad hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new hair color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color and make it fade faster.
  • Wear a hat or a scarf if you are not happy with your new hair color and want to hide it temporarily.
  • If you don't want to dye your hair, you can use a temporary hair color spray or a hair chalk to add some color to your hair.
  • Consult a hairstylist if you are not sure which hair color to choose or how to apply it.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding a bad hair color:

Q: Can I use a permanent hair color to hide a bad hair color?

A: You can, but it is not recommended, as it can be harsh on your hair and cause damage.

Q: How often can I color my hair?

A: It is recommended to wait at least four weeks between hair coloring sessions to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair color remover on colored hair?

A: Yes, but you should be careful not to overdo it, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can cause heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips and tricks, you can hide a bad hair color and achieve a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
