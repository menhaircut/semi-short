What Is The Second Rarest Hair And Eye Color Combination

Written by Christine Nov 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you curious about the rarest hair and eye color combination? It’s red hair and blue eyes. But what about the second rarest? The answer may surprise you. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

The second rarest hair and eye color combination is blonde hair and green eyes. This combination is estimated to occur in only 2% of the world population. While it may not be as rare as red hair and blue eyes, it is still a unique and beautiful combination.

How to Identify Blonde Hair and Green Eyes

Blonde hair and green eyes are fairly easy to identify. Blonde hair can range from very light, almost white, to a darker honey or golden color. Green eyes can range from a light, almost yellowish green, to a darker emerald green. The combination of blonde hair and green eyes is often striking and memorable.

Step by Step Guide to Enhancing Blonde Hair and Green Eyes

If you have blonde hair and green eyes, you may want to enhance your natural features. Here are some steps you can take:

1. Choose the Right Hair Color

If you have naturally blonde hair, you may want to enhance it with highlights or lowlights. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and brings out the green in your eyes.

2. Wear Complementary Colors

Wearing colors that complement your natural features can make them stand out even more. Greens, blues, and earth tones are great choices for those with blonde hair and green eyes.

3. Use Makeup to Enhance Your Eyes

Green eyes are already beautiful, but you can make them even more striking with the right makeup. Use shades of purple, bronze, and gold to bring out the green in your eyes.

Tips for Taking Care of Blonde Hair and Green Eyes

Blonde hair and green eyes can be a winning combination, but they also require special care. Here are some tips:

1. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

Blonde hair is more susceptible to sun damage, so be sure to wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF when you’re spending time outdoors.

2. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Blonde hair can be prone to dryness and damage, so use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQ

1. Are blonde hair and green eyes really that rare?

Yes, blonde hair and green eyes are estimated to occur in only 2% of the world population.

2. Can I dye my hair blonde if I have green eyes?

Yes, anyone can dye their hair blonde regardless of their eye color. Just be sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

3. What makeup shades look best with green eyes?

Purple, bronze, and gold shades are great for bringing out the green in your eyes.

Conclusion

Blonde hair and green eyes may not be as rare as red hair and blue eyes, but they are still a unique and beautiful combination. Whether you have this rare combination or simply admire it, use these tips to enhance your natural features and take care of your hair and eyes.
