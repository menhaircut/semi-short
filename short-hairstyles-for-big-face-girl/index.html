Are you tired of dealing with long hair that doesn't flatter your big face? Short hairstyles may be the solution you're looking for. Not only are they stylish and easy to maintain, but they can also complement your features and make your face look more proportionate. In this article, we'll share some of the best short hairstyles for big face girls, along with tips on how to style and care for them.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Big Face

Before you go ahead and chop off your locks, it's essential to choose a short hairstyle that complements your big face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

Big faces come in different shapes, such as round, square, oblong, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Each shape requires a specific haircut to create balance and proportion. For instance, if you have a round face, you can opt for a bob that falls just below the chin to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you can go for a layered bob to soften your jawline.

Think About Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can affect how a short hairstyle looks on you. If you have thin hair, for example, a pixie cut may not be the best option as it can make your face look even bigger. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a layered bob can add volume and dimension to your locks.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Short hairstyles require regular upkeep and styling, so it's essential to choose a cut that fits your lifestyle. If you're always on-the-go, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that doesn't require too much fuss.

Bob Hairstyles for Big Face Girls

Bob hairstyles are a classic and versatile option that can suit different face shapes, including big faces. Here are some bob hairstyles to consider:

Chin-Length Bob

A chin-length bob is a great option for big face girls as it creates a vertical line that elongates the face. You can add layers to create movement and texture.

Angled Bob

An angled bob is another option that can flatter big faces. The longer length in the front and shorter length in the back can create an illusion of a slimmer face.

Pixie Cut for Big Face Girls

A pixie cut can be a bold and edgy option for big face girls. However, it's essential to choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture. Here are some ideas:

Side-Swept Pixie Cut

A side-swept pixie cut can add dimension and movement to your hair while framing your face. It can also create an illusion of a slimmer face.

Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut with layers can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and more balanced.

Layered Bob for Big Face Girls

A layered bob can be a great option for big face girls as it can add volume and movement to your hair while creating a more balanced look. Here are some ideas:

Shaggy Layered Bob

A shaggy layered bob can create a messy and effortless look that's perfect for casual occasions. You can add bangs to frame your face.

Asymmetrical Layered Bob

An asymmetrical layered bob can add a touch of edge and sophistication to your look while creating an illusion of a slimmer face.

Faux Hawk for Big Face Girls

If you're looking for a bold and edgy style, a faux hawk can be a great option for big face girls. Here's how to style it:

Short Faux Hawk

A short faux hawk can be achieved by cutting the hair on the sides and back short while leaving the hair on the top longer. You can style the hair on top to create a spiky look or a messy texture.

Tips for Styling Short Hairstyles for Big Face Girls

Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create more volume.
  • Use a pomade or wax to add texture and definition to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to create a different look.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add a pop of color or texture.

FAQs about Short Hairstyles for Big Face Girls

What short hairstyles are best for big face girls?

Short hairstyles that create vertical lines or elongate the face can be flattering for big face girls. Examples include chin-length bobs, angled bobs, side-swept pixie cuts, and layered bobs.

Can big face girls wear a pixie cut?

Yes, big face girls can wear a pixie cut, but it's essential to choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture. For example, a side-swept pixie cut or a textured pixie cut can be flattering for big face girls.

How do I style my short hair?

You can style your short hair using a volumizing shampoo, blow-drying it upside down, and using a pomade or wax to add texture and definition. You can also experiment with different partings and accessorize with headbands or clips.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles for Big Face Girls

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know some of the best short hairstyles for big face girls, it's time to find the one that suits you best. Experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
