How To Put Long Hair In A Beanie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read
If you have long hair, you know how challenging it can be to wear a beanie without feeling like you have a giant lump on top of your head. But fear not! With a little bit of practice and some helpful tips, you can learn how to put long hair in a beanie and look stylish and comfortable at the same time.

Description

In this blog post, we will teach you how to put long hair in a beanie in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, helpful tips, and answers to frequently asked questions to make sure that you can pull off a beanie with long hair like a pro.

How to Put Long Hair in a Beanie: Step-by-Step

1. Brush your hair - Start by brushing your hair to remove tangles and knots. This will make it easier to put your hair in a beanie without any bumps or lumps. 2. Put the beanie on - Place the beanie on your head, making sure that it covers your ears and forehead. If you prefer a slouchy look, pull the beanie back slightly so that it sits on the back of your head. 3. Create a low ponytail - Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure that it is tight enough to stay in place but not so tight that it gives you a headache. 4. Twist the ponytail - Twist the ponytail tightly until it forms a coil. You can also braid your hair if you prefer a more intricate look. 5. Tuck the ponytail inside the beanie - Carefully tuck the twisted ponytail or braid inside the beanie, making sure that it is evenly distributed and doesn't create any bumps or lumps. 6. Adjust the beanie - Once your hair is tucked inside the beanie, adjust it to fit comfortably on your head. You can pull the beanie up slightly to create a slouchy look or keep it pulled down for a more snug fit.

Tips for Putting Long Hair in a Beanie

- Use a silk scarf or headband to protect your hair from static and frizz. - Choose a beanie with a looser fit if you have thick or curly hair. - Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a side braid, to create a unique look. - Invest in a high-quality beanie made of natural fibers like wool or cashmere to keep your head warm and comfortable.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a beanie with long hair without looking messy? A: Yes, you can! By following our step-by-step guide and using some of our helpful tips, you can wear a beanie with long hair and still look stylish and put-together. Q: What type of beanie should I choose for long hair? A: Look for a beanie with a looser fit and made of natural fibers like wool or cashmere. Avoid synthetic materials that can cause static and frizz.

Hair Care for Long Hair

To keep your long hair healthy and beautiful, make sure to: - Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. - Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind with a hat or scarf. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to put long hair in a beanie, you can rock this stylish accessory all winter long. By following our step-by-step guide and using some of our helpful tips, you'll be able to create a look that is both comfortable and chic. So go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles and beanie styles until you find the perfect combination for you!
