How To Put Your Hair Up In A High Ponytail By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 · 3 min read
Are you tired of your hair falling in your face or sticking to your neck on hot summer days? A high ponytail is a quick and easy hairstyle that can help keep you cool and stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to put your hair up in a high ponytail by yourself. Whether you're getting ready for work, school, or a night out, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Prepare for a High Ponytail
  2. How to Section Your Hair
  3. How to Tie Your Hair into a Ponytail
  4. How to Secure Your Ponytail

  6. Tips for a Sleek High Ponytail

How to Prepare for a High Ponytail

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have curly hair, consider using a straightener to smooth out any frizz. Brush your hair using a paddle brush to remove any tangles and knots.

How to Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections: the top and the bottom. Use a hair tie to secure the bottom section, leaving the top section loose.

How to Tie Your Hair into a Ponytail

Gather the top section of your hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps. Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail.

How to Secure Your Ponytail

To keep your ponytail in place, wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin. This will help hide the hair tie and create a more polished look.

How to Style Your Ponytail

If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, gently tease the hair at the crown of your head using a comb. You can also use hairspray to hold your ponytail in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips for a Sleek High Ponytail

- Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to blend in with your ponytail - Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can create a stiff and unnatural look - For a more dramatic look, add hair extensions to your ponytail

FAQs

Q: Can I put my hair up in a high ponytail if I have short hair? A: Yes, you can still put your hair up in a high ponytail with short hair. You may need to use bobby pins to secure any loose strands. Q: How do I prevent my hair tie from slipping out of my hair? A: Use a hair tie that has a good grip and make sure your hair is pulled tightly. You can also try wrapping your hair tie around your ponytail twice for extra hold.

Hair Care Tips

- Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style - Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair

Final Thoughts

Putting your hair up in a high ponytail is a quick and easy hairstyle that anyone can do. With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to rock a sleek and stylish high ponytail in no time. Experiment with different variations, such as a messy ponytail or a side ponytail, to switch up your look. Happy styling!
