How To Get Big Hair Naturally In 2023 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair that refuses to hold a style? Do you dream of voluminous, bouncy locks that turn heads wherever you go? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to get big hair naturally, without relying on harsh chemicals or damaging heat tools. From simple hair care tips to styling tricks, we've got everything you need to achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

The first step to getting big hair naturally is to take good care of your hair. This means using gentle, nourishing products that don't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are free from sulfates and other harsh chemicals. You should also avoid washing your hair too often, as this can dry out your scalp and make your hair look flat.

In addition to using the right products, you should also invest in regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. And don't forget to nourish your hair from the inside out by eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals. Foods like salmon, avocados, and nuts are all great for promoting healthy hair growth.

How To

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let's talk about how to style it for maximum volume. One of the easiest ways to get big hair naturally is to air-dry your hair rather than using a blow dryer. This will help your hair maintain its natural texture and prevent it from looking flat and lifeless.

You can also try using a volumizing mousse or foam to add texture and body to your hair. Apply it to damp hair, then scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage it to dry in a natural, voluminous style.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting big hair naturally:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Pat your hair dry with a towel, then apply a volumizing mousse or foam.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands, then let it air-dry.
  4. If your hair is still damp after a few hours, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a teasing comb to add extra volume at the roots.

Tips

Here are a few extra tips for getting big hair naturally:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Try flipping your hair upside down while you blow dry it to add extra volume.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute your hair's natural oils and add shine.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting big hair naturally:

Q: Can I get big hair naturally if I have fine hair?

A: Yes! Even if you have fine hair, you can still achieve big, voluminous hair by using the right products and styling techniques. Look for lightweight mousses and foams that won't weigh your hair down, and try backcombing your hair at the roots to add extra volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to get big hair naturally?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look flat and lifeless.

Q: Can I use heat tools to get big hair naturally?

A: While heat tools can help you achieve big, voluminous hair, they can also damage your hair over time. If you do use heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low to prevent damage. Alternatively, try using foam rollers or a volumizing diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to achieve a natural, voluminous look.

Hair Care is Key to Big Hair Naturally

Getting big hair naturally doesn't have to be a challenge. By taking good care of your hair and using the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve the voluminous, bouncy locks you've always dreamed of. So why wait? Start experimenting with these tips and tricks today and get ready to turn heads wherever you go!
