Is A Round Face Considered Attractive

Written by Eveline Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering if it's considered attractive. The truth is, beauty is subjective and what one person finds attractive may not be the same for someone else. However, there are certain features that are generally considered attractive, including symmetry, clear skin, and a healthy glow. In this article, we'll explore whether or not a round face is considered attractive and provide some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. People with round faces often have softer, more youthful features and tend to look more approachable and friendly. However, some people may feel self-conscious about their round face and wish to make it appear more angular or defined.

How to Enhance a Round Face

If you have a round face and want to enhance your natural beauty, there are a few things you can do. Here are some tips:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that uses dark and light shades to create the illusion of shadows and highlights. By using contouring, you can create the appearance of a more angular face. Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and around your forehead. Then, use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, nose, and forehead.

2. Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can also help enhance a round face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or bobs that end at your cheeks. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head or create the illusion of length, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.

3. Accessorize

Accessories can also help enhance a round face. Choose earrings and necklaces that are long and angular, as they can create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Avoid round or hoop earrings, as they can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to enhance a round face:

  1. Start by applying foundation and concealer to create a smooth, even base.
  2. Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and around your forehead.
  3. Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, nose, and forehead.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head or creates the illusion of length.
  5. Accessorize with long, angular earrings and necklaces.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for enhancing a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Choose sunglasses with angular frames.
  • Wear clothing with V-necks or scoop necks to elongate your neck.
  • Practice good skincare habits to keep your skin looking clear and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Is a round face considered unattractive?

A: No, a round face is not considered unattractive. Beauty is subjective and what one person finds attractive may not be the same for someone else. However, there are certain features that are generally considered attractive, including symmetry, clear skin, and a healthy glow.

Q: Can you change the shape of your face?

A: No, you cannot change the shape of your face without surgery. However, you can enhance your features with makeup, hairstyles, and accessories.

Q: What hairstyles are best for a round face?

A: Hairstyles that add height to the top of your head or create the illusion of length are best for a round face. Examples include long layers, a side-swept fringe, or a high ponytail.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to choose products that are gentle and nourishing. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and leave it feeling dry and brittle. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

In conclusion, a round face can be just as attractive as any other face shape. With the right makeup, hairstyles, and accessories, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin. Remember, beauty is subjective, and what matters most is how you feel about yourself.
