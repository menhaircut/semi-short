How Do I Choose The Right Hairstyle For My Face

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit your face shape. However, with a little bit of know-how, you can easily find the perfect hairstyle for your face. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks to help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are seven basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, oblong, and triangle. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck as most hairstyles will suit you. You can go for a short, medium, or long hairstyle, and experiment with different textures and finishes. The only thing to keep in mind is to avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead, as it can make your face look shorter.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, go for hairstyles that create the illusion of length. Long, layered haircuts with side-swept bangs or a deep side part will elongate your face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, soften your jawline with hairstyles that add volume to the crown or top of your head. Soft, layered hairstyles with side-swept bangs or a deep side part will flatter your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too angular or blunt, as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, go for hairstyles that balance your features. Soft, layered hairstyles with side-swept bangs or a deep side part will flatter your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too top-heavy, such as a short, voluminous hairstyle.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and jawline. Soft, layered hairstyles with bangs or a deep side part will flatter your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too narrow or too wide, as they can make your face look imbalanced.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, go for hairstyles that add width to your face. Soft, layered hairstyles with bangs or a deep side part will flatter your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as they can make your face look even longer.

Triangle Face Shape

If you have a triangle face shape, go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and temples. Soft, layered hairstyles with bangs or a deep side part will flatter your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too narrow or too voluminous at the chin, as they can make your face look bottom-heavy.

Step by Step

Now that you know your face shape and what hairstyles suit it, here are some steps to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type and texture.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair.
  4. Look for inspiration in magazines or online.
  5. Consult with your hairstylist and ask for their opinion.
  6. Try out different hairstyles using virtual makeover tools or by trying on wigs.
  7. Take into account your personal style and preferences.
  8. Consider the maintenance of the hairstyle and how often you'll need to trim or color your hair.
  9. Be open to trying something new and stepping out of your comfort zone.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Highlight your best features with the right hairstyle.
  • Use accessories such as headbands or hair clips to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to enhance your complexion.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing your hair parting.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for their opinion.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

What if I have multiple face shapes?

If you have multiple face shapes, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent features of your face.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Yes, you can still wear a certain hairstyle if it doesn't suit your face shape, but keep in mind that it may not be as flattering as a hairstyle that complements your features.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle, but it's good to switch things up every once in a while to keep your look fresh.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Invest in good quality hair products that suit your hair type and texture.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personal style.
