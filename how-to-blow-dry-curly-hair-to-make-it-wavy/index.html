How To Blow Dry Curly Hair To Make It Wavy

Curly hair can be hard to style, but with the right technique, you can blow dry it to make it wavy. In this article, we will discuss how to blow dry curly hair to make it wavy, step by step. We will also provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you advice on how to care for your hair.

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair to Make it Wavy

The first step to blow drying curly hair to make it wavy is to start with wet hair. Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry, but don't rub it vigorously.

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent it from getting damaged during the blow dry process. You can use a spray or a cream, and make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Then, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will make it easier to blow dry and prevent any knots or tangles from forming.

Now it's time to start blow drying your hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls and create waves. Start by drying the roots of your hair, and then move to the ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it, which will help create the waves.

Continue to dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. Then, switch to a round brush and use it to smooth out your hair and create more defined waves. Finish by using the cool shot button on your blow dryer to help set the waves in place.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide to blow drying curly hair to make it wavy:

  1. Start with wet hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant
  3. Detangle your hair
  4. Use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair
  5. Scrunch your hair as you dry it
  6. Dry your hair until it is 80% dry
  7. Switch to a round brush
  8. Smooth out your hair and create waves
  9. Use the cool shot button to set the waves in place

Tips

Here are some tips to help you blow dry curly hair to make it wavy:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer
  • Apply a heat protectant to prevent damage
  • Detangle your hair before blow drying
  • Scrunch your hair as you dry it to create waves
  • Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and create more defined waves
  • Use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to set the waves in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying curly hair to make it wavy:

Can I blow dry curly hair without a diffuser attachment?

Yes, you can, but using a diffuser attachment will help enhance your curls and create waves.

Do I need to apply a heat protectant to my hair?

Yes, it's important to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

What type of brush should I use?

Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and create more defined waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to nourish your hair from the inside out

By following these tips and using the right technique, you can blow dry curly hair to make it wavy and achieve the look you want.
