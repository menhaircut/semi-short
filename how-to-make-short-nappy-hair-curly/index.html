How To Make Short Nappy Hair Curly

Written by Eveline Oct 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your short nappy hair and want to add some curls to your hair? If so, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to make short nappy hair curly. We will also provide you with tips on hair care to maintain your curls.

Description

Short nappy hair can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques, you can add curls and texture to your hair. You can use various methods to make your hair curly, including natural methods, heat styling, and chemical treatments.

How to Make Short Nappy Hair Curly

There are several ways to make short nappy hair curly. Here are some methods you can try:

Method 1: Twist Out

Twist out is a popular method used to make short nappy hair curly. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your hair.
  3. Part your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely before unraveling the twists.
  5. Fluff and style your hair to achieve the desired look.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are also an effective method to make short nappy hair curly. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a moisturizing cream or styling gel to your hair.
  3. Part your hair into sections and twist each section into a small knot.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely before unraveling the knots.
  5. Fluff and style your hair to achieve the desired look.

Method 3: Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a heatless way to make short nappy hair curly. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your hair.
  3. Part your hair into sections and wrap each section around a flexi rod.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely before removing the rods.
  5. Fluff and style your hair to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from harsh weather conditions.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making short nappy hair curly:

Can I use heat styling tools to make my hair curly?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools such as curling irons and flat irons to make your hair curly. However, excessive use of these tools can cause damage to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Can I use natural oils on my hair?

Yes, you can use natural oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, and jojoba oil to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and curly hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from direct sunlight and heat.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Visit a hair stylist regularly to trim your hair and maintain its shape.

By following these tips and methods, you can make your short nappy hair curly and maintain its health and texture. Experiment with different techniques and find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
