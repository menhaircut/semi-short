How Do Salons Do Eyelash Extensions

Written by Daniel Sep 06, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to enhance your natural beauty, eyelash extensions are a great option. Salons offer this service, and it's becoming more and more popular. In this article, we'll go over the process of how salons do eyelash extensions, as well as tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the process, it's important to understand what eyelash extensions are. Eyelash extensions are individual lashes that are glued to your natural lashes to make them look longer, fuller, and darker. The process of getting eyelash extensions done at a salon typically takes 1-2 hours.

Choosing Your Lashes

The first step is choosing the type of lashes you want. The salon will typically offer different lengths, thicknesses, and curls to choose from. Your stylist will also take into consideration the shape of your eyes, the length of your natural lashes, and your desired look.

Preparing Your Lashes

Once you've chosen your lashes, your stylist will prepare your natural lashes for the extensions. This involves cleaning them with a special solution and using a small tool to separate them.

Step by Step

Now that your lashes are prepped, your stylist will start the process of applying the extensions.

Step 1: Applying Adhesive

The stylist will apply a small amount of adhesive to the base of the extension and then carefully attach it to your natural lash. This process is repeated until all of your lashes are covered.

Step 2: Drying Time

After all of the extensions are applied, you'll need to wait for the adhesive to dry. This typically takes around 24 hours, so it's important to avoid getting your lashes wet during this time.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that your eyelash extensions last as long as possible:

  • Avoid getting your lashes wet for 24 hours after the application
  • Avoid rubbing your eyes
  • Avoid using oil-based products near your eyes
  • Don't use a lash curler on your extensions
  • Brush your lashes regularly with a clean spoolie brush

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about eyelash extensions:

How long do eyelash extensions last?

Eyelash extensions typically last 4-6 weeks, but this can vary depending on how well you take care of them.

Are eyelash extensions safe?

Yes, eyelash extensions are safe when they are applied properly by a trained professional.

Do eyelash extensions damage your natural lashes?

No, eyelash extensions should not damage your natural lashes if they are applied properly and you take care of them.

Hair Care

When it comes to taking care of your eyelash extensions, it's important to also take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Now that you know how salons do eyelash extensions, you can decide if this is a service you want to try. Remember to choose a reputable salon and take good care of your lashes to ensure they last as long as possible.
