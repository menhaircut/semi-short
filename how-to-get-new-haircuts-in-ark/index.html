How To Get New Haircuts In Ark

Written by Petter Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle in Ark and want to switch things up? Look no further! In this article, we will walk you through step by step on how to get new haircuts in Ark. From where to find the necessary tools to the best tips for maintaining your new look, we have got you covered.

How to

Getting a new haircut in Ark is a simple process. All you need is a pair of scissors and a mirror. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Find the Scissors

The first thing you need to do is find a pair of scissors. You can find them in the crafting section of your inventory. Once you have found them, equip them by dragging them to the hotbar at the bottom of the screen.

Step 2: Find a Mirror

Next, you need to find a mirror. You can craft one using 5 metal ingots and 10 crystal. Once you have crafted it, place it in your base where you can easily access it.

Step 3: Sit in Front of the Mirror

Once you have found a mirror, sit in front of it. This will allow you to see your character's hairstyle in real-time.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Use the scissors to trim your hair to your desired length. Be careful not to cut too much or you may end up with a hairstyle you don't like.

Step 5: Finish Up

Once you are happy with your new haircut, you can stop cutting. Congratulations! You have successfully gotten a new haircut in Ark.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results with your new haircut:

  • Take your time. Cutting too quickly can lead to mistakes.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what looks best on your character.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for feedback from other players.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting new haircuts in Ark:

How often can I change my hairstyle?

There is no limit to how often you can change your hairstyle in Ark.

Can I dye my hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair in Ark. You will need to craft the appropriate dye and apply it to your hair.

Will my hair grow back if I cut it too short?

No, your hair will not grow back if you cut it too short. You will need to wait until it grows back naturally.

Hair Care

Now that you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Regularly wash your hair to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

Congratulations on getting a new haircut in Ark! With these tips and tricks, you can keep your new look looking great for as long as you want. Happy styling!
