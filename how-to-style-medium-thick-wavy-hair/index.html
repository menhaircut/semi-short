How To Style Medium Thick Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

If you have medium thick wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style it just right. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles that showcase your natural waves. In this blog post, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to style medium thick wavy hair. Let's get started!

How to Style Medium Thick Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the styling tips, it's important to note that the foundation of any great hairstyle is healthy hair. Make sure to keep your hair hydrated and nourished with regular washing and conditioning. Also, consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now, let's talk about how to style medium thick wavy hair. The first step is to determine the type of waves you have. Are they loose or tight? Do you have more volume on top or at the bottom? Understanding your hair type will help you choose the right styling products and tools.

For loose waves, you can use a curling wand or iron to create more defined curls. For tighter waves, consider using a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture. You can also use a styling cream or mousse to add volume and hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style medium thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves. Focus on the roots to add volume.
  3. Apply a styling cream or mousse to add hold and texture.
  4. Use a curling wand or iron to create more defined curls if desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to set the style.

Tips for Styling Medium Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling medium thick wavy hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Don't be afraid to use hair accessories like clips or headbands to add flair to your style.

FAQs About Styling Medium Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium thick wavy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every few days. Use your judgement and listen to your hair's needs.

Hair Care for Medium Thick Wavy Hair

Finally, let's talk about hair care for medium thick wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week for added hydration.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your medium thick wavy hair with confidence. Remember to listen to your hair's needs and have fun experimenting with different styles!
