Women s Short Layered Haircuts 2021 A Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short layered haircuts have been trending among women for years now. And in 2021, this hairstyle is still going strong. Layered haircuts give volume and movement to your hair, making it a great choice for those who have thin hair. In this article, we'll talk about the different types of short layered haircuts for women, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Types of Short Layered Haircuts

When it comes to short layered haircuts, there are several styles to choose from. Here are some of the most popular ones:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and layered haircut that's perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. It's also a great choice if you have round or heart-shaped face.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic and versatile haircut that can be worn in many different ways. It can be short or long, straight or wavy, and can be layered to add volume and movement to your hair.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that's great for those who have thick and wavy hair. It gives a messy and effortless look that's perfect for those who want to look edgy and cool.

How to Style Short Layered Haircuts

Styling short layered haircuts can be easy and fun. Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair in different ways.
  • Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add definition and texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Layered Hair

If you're planning to cut your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair and tie it up.
  3. Cut the first layer shorter than the rest of your hair.
  4. Continue cutting the layers, making sure to blend them well.
  5. Use a razor to add texture and movement to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Layered Haircuts

Here are some tips on how to keep your short layered haircut looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shape is best for a short layered haircut?

A short layered haircut can look great on any face shape. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine which style suits you best.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I style my short layered haircut in different ways?

Yes, short layered haircuts can be styled in many different ways. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create different looks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Haircuts

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short layered haircut looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Short layered haircuts are a great choice for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and maintain. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob cut, or shag cut, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can rock a short layered haircut with confidence!
