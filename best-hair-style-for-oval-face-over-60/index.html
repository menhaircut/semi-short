Best Hair Style For Oval Face Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose its natural shine. However, with the right hair style, you can still look stylish and youthful. If you have an oval face and are over 60, it can be challenging to find a hair style that flatters your facial features. In this article, we will discuss the best hair style for oval face over 60, along with some useful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Oval Face Over 60
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Aging Hair

Description

An oval face is considered the most balanced face shape, which means you can experiment with a variety of hair styles. However, as you age, your hair texture and density change, and you need to choose a hair style that flatters your face shape and takes into account your hair's changing needs.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Oval Face Over 60

When choosing a hair style for an oval face over 60, keep the following factors in mind:

  • Short hair: Short hair styles work well for oval faces as they highlight your facial features. However, make sure the length is not too short, as it can make you look older.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which is essential as your hair thins with age.
  • Bangs: If you want to look younger, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer in the middle and shorter on the sides.
  • Highlights: Highlights can add depth and dimension to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.
  • Face-framing layers: Face-framing layers can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, giving you a youthful and fresh look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for an oval face over 60:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side with a small round brush.
  5. If you have layers, use a curling iron to add curls to the ends for a bouncy look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair style:

  • Visit your hair stylist every six to eight weeks for a trim to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • Use a root lifter to add volume to the roots.
  • Apply a hair serum to the ends to prevent split ends and add shine.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

FAQs

Q. Can I still have long hair if I have an oval face over 60?

A. Yes, you can still have long hair, but make sure it is layered and styled in a way that flatters your face shape.

Q. Should I avoid bangs if I have an oval face over 60?

A. No, you can still have bangs, but opt for side-swept bangs that are longer in the middle and shorter on the sides.

Hair Care Tips for Aging Hair

As we age, our hair undergoes several changes, such as thinning, graying, and becoming brittle. Here are some hair care tips for aging hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip away the natural oils.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as perms and relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as they can cause hair breakage and thinning.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair style for an oval face over 60 can be challenging, but by following the tips and advice in this article, you can find a hair style that flatters your facial features and makes you look youthful and stylish.
