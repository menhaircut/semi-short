Best Holding Hairspray For Thick Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Are you tired of your hair falling flat and losing its hold halfway through the day? If you have thick hair, finding the right hairspray can be a challenge. That's why we've put together this ultimate guide to help you find the best holding hairspray for thick hair. In this guide, we'll cover everything from the top products to tips for applying and caring for your hair.

Top Products

When it comes to finding the best holding hairspray for thick hair, you want to look for products that offer strong hold and long-lasting results. Here are some of the top hairsprays on the market that are perfect for thick hair:

1. L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray

This iconic hairspray is a favorite of hairstylists and celebrities alike. It offers a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling stiff or crunchy. Plus, it brushes out easily for a flexible finish.

2. Kenra Professional Volume Spray

If you're looking for maximum hold, Kenra Professional Volume Spray is the way to go. It offers a humidity-resistant hold that lasts for up to 120 hours. Plus, it adds volume and shine to your hair.

3. TRESemme Compressed Micro Mist Hairspray

This lightweight hairspray offers a strong hold that lasts all day. It's infused with a humidity-resistant formula that keeps your hair in place even in the most humid conditions.

How to Apply Hairspray for Thick Hair

Applying hairspray for thick hair requires a slightly different technique than for other hair types. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before applying hairspray, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Section Your Hair

To ensure that all of your hair is evenly coated with hairspray, section it off into smaller sections. This will also help you get to hard-to-reach areas.

3. Hold the Hairspray 8-10 Inches Away

Hold the hairspray can 8-10 inches away from your head and spray evenly over each section of hair.

4. Use a Comb or Brush to Distribute

After spraying each section, use a comb or brush to distribute the hairspray evenly. This will help to avoid any clumps or crunchy areas.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to applying hairspray for thick hair:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. If you have naturally thick hair, you may want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into smaller sections to ensure that each area is evenly coated with hairspray.

3. Spray the Hairspray

Hold the hairspray can 8-10 inches away from your head and spray each section of hair evenly.

4. Comb or Brush Your Hair

After spraying each section, use a comb or brush to distribute the hairspray evenly.

5. Finish with a Final Spray

Once you've finished styling your hair, use a final spray of hairspray to lock everything in place.

Tips for Using Hairspray on Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your hairspray for thick hair:

1. Don't Overdo It

While you want to use enough hairspray to hold your hair in place, don't overdo it. Too much hairspray can leave your hair feeling stiff and crunchy.

2. Choose the Right Formula

Look for hairsprays that are specifically formulated for thick hair. These will offer a stronger hold than regular hairspray.

3. Use a Lightweight Formula

If you have fine or thin hair, avoid using heavy hairsprays that can weigh your hair down. Instead, opt for lightweight formulas that offer a flexible hold.

FAQs

1. Can hairspray damage thick hair?

While hairspray itself won't necessarily damage your hair, using it too often or using a formula that is too heavy can cause breakage and split ends over time.

2. How often should I use hairspray on thick hair?

It's best to use hairspray sparingly to avoid buildup and damage. Use it only when you need to hold a specific style in place.

3. How do I remove hairspray from thick hair?

To remove hairspray from your hair, simply wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. You may need to use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair healthy and strong, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Use a Nourishing Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for thick hair. These will help to nourish and strengthen your hair.

2. Avoid Over-Washing

While it's important to keep your hair clean, washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

To keep your hair hydrated and healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to repair any damage and keep your hair looking shiny and strong.

With these tips and product recommendations, you're sure to find the best holding hairspray for your thick hair. Remember to apply it sparingly and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and strong.
