How To Find The Right Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Eveline Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly changing hairstyles and still not getting the look you desire? The problem might not be the haircuts, but rather the fact that they do not suit your face shape. Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is crucial in achieving a flattering and stylish look. In this article, we will guide you on how to find the right hairstyle for your face and provide tips on hair care to maintain your new look.

Table of Content

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Research Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in front of a mirror. Observe the outline of your face and compare it to the following descriptions:

  • Oval face: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin and the length of the face is roughly one and a half times the width.
  • Round face: Prominent cheeks and the width and length of the face are roughly equal.
  • Square face: Prominent jawline and forehead, and the width of the forehead and jawline are roughly equal.
  • Heart face: Wider forehead and a narrow jawline and chin.
  • Diamond face: Narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones.
  • Oblong face: Longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Identifying your face shape is important as it will help you determine the best hairstyles that will complement your features and balance your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, the next step is to research hairstyles that suit your face shape. There are many resources, such as magazines and online articles, that can provide ideas for hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for hairstyles that highlight your best features and complement your face shape.

Step 3: Consult a Hairstylist

Consulting a hairstylist is the final step in finding the right hairstyle for your face. A professional hairstylist can provide expert advice on which hairstyles will flatter your face shape and enhance your features. They can also recommend haircuts that suit your hair type, texture, and lifestyle. Be open to their suggestions and ask questions if you have any concerns.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle for your face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head if you have a round face.
  • Soft waves and layers can soften a square face shape.
  • Heart-shaped faces look great with chin-length bobs and side-swept bangs.
  • Long, layered hairstyles are ideal for oblong face shapes.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short if you have a diamond-shaped face.
  • Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that does not match my face shape?

A: Yes, you can. However, it might not be as flattering as a hairstyle that complements your face shape. If you choose to wear a hairstyle that does not match your face shape, make sure it highlights your best features and fits your personal style.

Q: Can hair color affect how a hairstyle looks on me?

A: Yes, hair color can affect how a hairstyle looks on you. It can enhance or detract from your features and impact how the hairstyle frames your face. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hair color for your skin tone and face shape.

Hair Care Tips

After finding the right hairstyle for your face, it is essential to maintain it with proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and remove split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or hair products that contain UV protection.
  • Use hair products that match your hair type and style, such as hair gel, mousse, or hairspray.

By following these tips and finding the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that enhances your features and boosts your confidence.
