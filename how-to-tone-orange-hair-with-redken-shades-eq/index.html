The Basics of Toning

Toning is a process of neutralizing unwanted tones in your hair, such as yellow, orange, or red. It is often used after bleaching or coloring to achieve a more natural-looking hair color. Toning works by using opposite colors on the color wheel to cancel out the unwanted tones in your hair. For example, purple cancels out yellow, and blue cancels out orange.

There are various types of toners available, such as semi-permanent, demi-permanent, and permanent. Semi-permanent toners, like Redken Shades EQ, are the most popular choice as they provide a more natural-looking result and are less damaging to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Tone Orange Hair with Redken Shades EQ

Follow these steps to tone your orange hair with Redken Shades EQ:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup and open up your hair cuticles.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply Redken Shades EQ using a bowl and brush. It is recommended to use a 1:1 ratio of Shades EQ color and Shades EQ processing solution.
  3. Apply the toner from roots to ends and make sure to saturate every strand evenly.
  4. Leave the toner on for 20 minutes, or as recommended by your stylist.
  5. Rinse your hair with cool water until the water runs clear.
  6. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair with cool water and style as desired.

Tips for Toning Orange Hair with Redken Shades EQ

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when toning your orange hair with Redken Shades EQ:

  • Do a strand test before applying the toner to your entire head to avoid any unwanted results.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your toned hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair for the first few days after toning to prevent fading.
  • If you have extremely orange hair, you may need to use a higher volume developer, such as 20 or 30, to achieve your desired result.

FAQs about Toning Orange Hair with Redken Shades EQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about toning orange hair with Redken Shades EQ:

Q. Can Redken Shades EQ be used on previously colored hair?

A. Yes, Redken Shades EQ can be used on previously colored hair. However, the results may vary depending on the previous color and condition of your hair.

Q. How often should I tone my hair with Redken Shades EQ?

A. It depends on your hair type, color, and the amount of maintenance you do. Generally, toning your hair every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain your desired color.

Q. Can I mix Shades EQ shades to create a custom color?

A. Yes, you can mix different Shades EQ shades to create a custom color. However, it is recommended to do a strand test before applying the mixture to your entire head.

Hair Care Tips after Toning with Redken Shades EQ

After toning your hair with Redken Shades EQ, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent the toner from fading.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair for at least two weeks after toning.

That's it! Now you know how to tone orange hair with Redken Shades EQ. Remember to follow these steps and take care of your hair to achieve the best results.
