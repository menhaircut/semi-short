Female Hairstyles Short Back Long Front A Stylish Look For 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new and trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out in 2023? Look no further than the short back long front hairstyle! This edgy and chic look has been popular for years and shows no signs of going out of style any time soon. In this blog post, we will guide you through how to achieve this stunning look, hair care tips, and answer some frequently asked questions!

Description

The short back long front hairstyle features a shorter length at the back of the head, gradually increasing in length towards the front. The front section of the hair is left longer and can be styled in various ways such as sleek and straight, wavy, or textured. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some edge and dimension to their hair, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How To Achieve the Short Back Long Front Look

There are different ways to achieve the short back long front look, depending on your hair type and desired outcome. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before getting a short back long front hairstyle, consult with your hairstylist to ensure that it will suit your face shape and hair type. They can also suggest different variations of the hairstyle and give you tips on how to maintain it.

Step 2: Cut the back section of your hair

The first step in achieving the short back long front look is to cut the back section of your hair to the desired length. This can be a blunt cut or layered, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Gradually increase the length towards the front

As you move towards the front of your head, gradually increase the length of your hair. This can be done through layering or a gradual cut.

Step 4: Style the front section

The front section of the hair is left longer and can be styled in different ways. You can opt for a sleek and straight look, add some waves or texture, or even braid it. The possibilities are endless!

Tips for Maintaining the Short Back Long Front Hairstyle

To keep your short back long front hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim the back section of your hair to maintain the length and shape
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage
  • Experiment with different styling products such as mousse, hairspray, or texturizing spray to achieve your desired look
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

Is the short back long front hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the short back long front hairstyle can be customized to suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best variation for your face shape.

What hair types are suitable for the short back long front hairstyle?

The short back long front hairstyle can work for most hair types, but it is best suited for those with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it may require more maintenance to keep the length and shape.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short back long front hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short back long front hairstyle. Experiment with different updo styles such as a messy bun or a half-up half-down look.

Hair Care

To keep your short back long front hairstyle looking its best, it's essential to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as it can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf

Conclusion

The short back long front hairstyle is a versatile and stylish look that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. With these tips and tricks, you can maintain this edgy and chic hairstyle and keep turning heads in 2023!
