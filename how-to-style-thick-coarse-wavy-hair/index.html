How To Style Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

If you have thick, coarse, and wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style your hair effortlessly.

How to Style Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

First, determine the type of waves you have. Are they loose or tight? Thick or thin? This will help you determine the right styling products and tools to use.

When it comes to styling thick coarse wavy hair, you want to keep the hair hydrated and nourished. This will help minimize frizz and make your hair more manageable.

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using products with harsh chemicals, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help detangle your hair and add moisture. This will also help protect your hair from heat damage when using styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your thick coarse wavy hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to detangle and add moisture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to define your curls or waves. Choose a barrel size that matches the size of your waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your hair with ease:

  • Avoid using a brush on wet hair as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves and minimize frizz.
  • Choose a curling iron or wand that matches the size of your waves to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy-looking hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick coarse wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage and keep your hair hydrated.

Q: How often should I wash my thick coarse wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, if you have dry hair, you can wash it less often to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Aside from styling your hair, it's essential to take care of it to keep it healthy and manageable.

Here are some tips on how to care for your thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your thick coarse wavy hair with confidence. Remember, healthy hair is beautiful hair!
