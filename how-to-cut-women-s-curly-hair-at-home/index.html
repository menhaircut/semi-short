How To Cut Women s Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your curly hair cut at a salon? With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair at home can be a great way to save money and achieve the perfect style. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut women's curly hair at home, step by step. From preparing your hair to making the final cuts, we've got you covered. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare Your Hair
  • How to Section Your Hair
  • How to Cut Your Hair
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Here's what you need to do:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to soften your curls and make them easier to cut.
  • Towel-dry your hair until it's damp. Don't use a blow dryer, as this can make your hair too dry and difficult to cut.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling product to your hair. This will help to define your curls and make them more manageable.

Once your hair is prepared, you're ready to start cutting!

How to Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important when cutting curly hair, as it helps you to see what you're doing and ensures that you cut each section evenly. Here's how to do it:

  • Divide your hair into four sections: top, back, and two sides.
  • Clip each section up with a hair clip.
  • Starting with the back section, take a 1-inch section of hair and twist it tightly. This will help you to see the individual curls and make it easier to cut them.
  • Clip the rest of the back section out of the way.
  • Repeat this process with each section of hair, working from back to front.

How to Cut Your Hair

Now it's time to start cutting your hair! Here's what you need to do:

  • Start with the back section of your hair.
  • Hold your hair between your fingers and pull it straight up. This will help you to see the length of each curl.
  • Using sharp scissors, cut the ends of your hair straight across. Don't cut too much at once, as this can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Continue cutting each section of hair, working from back to front.
  • Once you've finished cutting your hair, shake it out and check for any uneven sections. If you find any, go back and trim them.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be deceptive and lead to uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take small sections and cut them gradually.
  • Use the twist method to see the individual curls and ensure even cuts.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member, especially for hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair at home:

Q: Can I cut my curly hair when it's wet?

A: It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be deceptive and lead to uneven cuts.

Q: What type of scissors should I use?

A: Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts.

Q: How much should I cut off?

A: It's better to take small sections and cut them gradually. Don't cut too much at once, as this can lead to uneven cuts.

Hair Care After Cutting

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here's what you can do:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling product to your hair to define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut your own curly hair at home with confidence. Happy cutting!
