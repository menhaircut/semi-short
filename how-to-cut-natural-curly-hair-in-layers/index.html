How to Cut Natural Curly Hair in Layers - Step by Step Guide

In this step by step guide, we will show you how to cut natural curly hair in layers. Layered haircuts are perfect for curly hair as they help to add volume and dimension to your curls. Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect layered haircut for your natural curly hair.

How to Cut Natural Curly Hair in Layers - Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Divide your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into four sections - two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut straight across. This will create a base for your layered haircut.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Take the middle section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the bottom layer and working your way up. This will create a layered effect.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Take the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the bottom layer and working your way up. This will create more layers and volume in your hair.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Use a pair of thinning shears to blend the layers together. This will help to create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 7: Check for any Unevenness

Check your hair for any unevenness or areas that need to be trimmed. Use your scissors to make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Style your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls and add volume to your hair.

Tips for Cutting Natural Curly Hair in Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting natural curly hair in layers:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent any split ends.
  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to prevent any drastic changes.
  • Take your time and be patient.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own natural curly hair in layers?

Yes, you can cut your own natural curly hair in layers by following these simple steps. However, it is always recommended to seek the help of a professional stylist for best results.

How often should I cut my natural curly hair?

It is recommended to cut your natural curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent any split ends.

Hair Care for Natural Curly Hair

Here are some tips to take care of your natural curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent any dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Conclusion

Cutting natural curly hair in layers can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect layered haircut for your curls. Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve a natural and voluminous look.
