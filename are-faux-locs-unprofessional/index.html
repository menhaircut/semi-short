Are Faux Locs Unprofessional

Written by Daniel Aug 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Faux locs have become increasingly popular in recent years, with many people opting for this protective style as a way to switch up their look. However, there is still a common misconception that faux locs are unprofessional. In this article, we will explore whether or not this is true and provide some tips on how to style and care for your faux locs.

Description

Faux locs are a type of protective style that involves creating locs using synthetic hair or yarn. This style is popular among Black women and has become a staple in the natural hair community. Faux locs can be done in various lengths and thicknesses, and can even be customized with different colors and embellishments.

How To

To get faux locs, you can either do them yourself or go to a professional stylist. If you're doing it yourself, you'll need a crochet hook, synthetic hair or yarn, and some patience. Here are the steps:

Step 1:

Section off your hair into small parts and braid each section to the end.

Step 2:

Using a crochet hook, loop the synthetic hair or yarn onto the braid and pull it through. Then, tie a knot at the end to secure it in place.

Step 3:

Repeat the process until all of your hair is covered in faux locs.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and caring for your faux locs:

Tip 1:

Use a light oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your scalp moisturized.

Tip 2:

Tie your locs up at night to prevent tangling.

Tip 3:

Experiment with different styles, such as braids or twists, to switch up your look.

FAQ

Q: Are faux locs unprofessional?

A: No, faux locs are not unprofessional. They are a protective style that can be worn in a variety of settings.

Q: How long do faux locs last?

A: Faux locs can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Q: Can I wash my faux locs?

A: Yes, you can wash your faux locs using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. However, be sure to let them dry completely to prevent mildew.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, faux locs require some extra attention. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping the synthetic hair or yarn.

Tip 2:

Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your locs.

Tip 3:

Be gentle when detangling your locs to avoid pulling on your natural hair.

In conclusion, faux locs are not unprofessional. They are a versatile and stylish protective style that can be worn in various settings. With proper care and maintenance, faux locs can last for weeks and keep your natural hair healthy and protected.
