What Do Guys Think Of Box Braids

Written by Luffy Oct 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Box braids have been a popular hairstyle for women of all ages and ethnicities for many years. This protective style can be worn in various lengths and sizes, and it's easy to maintain. But what do guys think of box braids? In this article, we will explore the male perspective on box braids and give you some tips on how to care for them.

Description

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that consists of braiding natural hair into small, box-shaped sections. This style can be achieved using synthetic or natural hair extensions, and it's a great way to protect your natural hair from damage caused by heat styling and environmental factors.

How to

Box braids can be achieved by following these simple steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair or let it air dry.
  3. Divide your hair into small, square sections.
  4. Braid each section from the root to the tip.
  5. Secure the ends of the braids with rubber bands or hair ties.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create box braids:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair or let it air dry. Make sure your hair is completely dry before starting the braiding process.
  3. Divide your hair into small, square sections using a rat-tail comb. The sections should be no larger than the width of your finger.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair oil or leave-in conditioner to each section to help prevent breakage and make the braiding process easier.
  5. Start braiding each section from the root to the tip using the three-strand braid method. Make sure the braid is tight but not too tight to avoid causing tension on your scalp.
  6. Continue braiding each section until your entire head is braided.
  7. Secure the ends of the braids with rubber bands or hair ties.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your box braids:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized by applying a natural oil, such as coconut or jojoba oil, to your scalp daily.
  • Wash your box braids every 2-3 weeks using a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your box braids, as they can weigh them down and cause breakage.
  • Wrap your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf to help prevent frizz and preserve your style.
  • Avoid wearing your box braids for more than 8 weeks to prevent damage to your natural hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about box braids:

Are box braids suitable for all hair types?

Yes, box braids can be worn by women of all hair types. However, if you have fine or fragile hair, you may want to avoid using heavy extensions or wearing your braids for too long to prevent breakage.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last for up to 8 weeks if properly cared for. However, if you notice any signs of breakage or damage, it's best to remove them sooner.

Can I swim with box braids?

Yes, you can swim with box braids. However, it's best to wear a swim cap to protect your braids from chlorine and saltwater damage.

Hair Care

Box braids are a great way to protect your natural hair and experiment with different styles. However, it's important to care for your braids properly to prevent breakage and damage to your natural hair. Follow the tips and guidelines outlined in this article to keep your box braids looking healthy and beautiful.
