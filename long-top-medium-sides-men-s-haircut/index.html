Long Top Medium Sides Men s Haircut A Relaxed Guide

Written by William Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish but low-maintenance haircut, the long top medium sides men's haircut might be the perfect choice for you. This versatile haircut can work for any hair type and face shape, and it's a great option for guys who want to keep their hair out of their face without going too short.

Description

The long top medium sides men's haircut features longer hair on the top of the head, typically between 3 and 6 inches, with shorter hair on the sides and back, usually around 1 or 2 inches. This creates a contrast between the longer and shorter sections of hair, and allows for plenty of styling options.

How To

If you're interested in getting a long top medium sides men's haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Make sure to bring in a photo or two of the style you're aiming for, and be clear about how much length you want on the top and how short you want the sides to be.

Once you're in the chair, your barber or stylist will likely start by cutting the sides and back with clippers, gradually working up to the desired length. They may also use scissors to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter sides, creating a seamless transition. Finally, they will style your hair using a product of your choice, such as pomade or wax.

Step by Step

  1. Find a skilled barber or stylist
  2. Bring in a photo of the desired style
  3. Be clear about length and style preferences
  4. Cut the sides and back with clippers
  5. Blend the longer hair on top with the shorter sides using scissors
  6. Style hair using a product of your choice

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long top medium sides men's haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look
  • Consider adding some texture or layers to your haircut for added dimension and interest

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long top medium sides men's haircut:

What face shape does this haircut work best for?

The long top medium sides men's haircut can work for any face shape, but it's especially flattering for guys with oval or square faces.

Can I still style my hair if I have a receding hairline?

Absolutely! Your barber or stylist can work with your hairline to create a flattering and stylish look, and you can use styling products to add volume and texture to the hair on top.

Do I need to use a specific product to style my hair?

No, you can use any styling product that works for your hair type and desired look. Some popular options include pomade, wax, gel, and clay.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is key to maintaining a great long top medium sides men's haircut. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair to help detangle and moisturize
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture and shine

With proper care and maintenance, your long top medium sides men's haircut can look great for weeks or even months at a time. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
