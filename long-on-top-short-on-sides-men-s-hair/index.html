Long On Top Short On Sides Men s Hair The Ultimate Guide

Are you considering getting the trendy long on top, short on sides men's haircut? This hairstyle has been popular for several years now and is still going strong. It's a versatile haircut that can suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll go through everything you need to know about this haircut, from how to get it, to styling tips and hair care.

  • Description
  • How to Get the Long on Top, Short on Sides Haircut

Description

The long on top, short on sides hairstyle is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head while keeping the sides and back short. The contrast between the two lengths creates a visually appealing look that's both modern and stylish. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, from slicked back to messy and textured, depending on your preference and hair type.

How to Get the Long on Top, Short on Sides Haircut

The long on top, short on sides haircut can be achieved by asking your barber or stylist for a fade or undercut. The fade is a gradual transition from short to long hair, while the undercut involves shaving the sides and back and leaving the top longer. Both styles are popular options for this haircut and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for getting the long on top, short on sides haircut:

  1. Decide on the length of hair you want on top and the type of fade or undercut you prefer.
  2. Show your barber or stylist a picture of the hairstyle you want, or describe it clearly.
  3. Sit back and let your barber work their magic.
  4. Once the haircut is done, style your hair using your preferred products.

Styling Tips

Here are some tips for styling your long on top, short on sides hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.
  • Don't be afraid to use a blow dryer to create volume and shape.
  • Consider getting a trim every few weeks to maintain the style and keep the hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top, short on sides haircut:

What face shape suits this haircut?

This haircut can suit different face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

What hair type is best for this haircut?

This haircut can work for different hair types, from straight to curly. However, it's easier to style if you have thick hair.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every four to six weeks to maintain the style and keep the hair looking neat.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your long on top, short on sides hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, the long on top, short on sides haircut is a stylish and versatile option for men who want a modern look. With the right styling and hair care, this haircut can suit different face shapes and hair types, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.
