The How To Style My Hair While Growing Out My Bangs For Short Hair

Written by William Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read
Table of Contents: - Introduction - How to Style Your Hair While Growing Out Your Bangs - Step by Step Guide - Tips for Styling Bangs in Transition - FAQ - Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Bangs - Conclusion Introduction Growing out bangs can be a frustrating process, especially when you're trying to style your hair in the meantime. But fear not! There are plenty of ways to style your hair while growing out your bangs that will keep you looking stylish and feeling confident. In this article, we'll discuss how to style your hair while growing out your bangs, step by step, with helpful tips and tricks along the way. How to Style Your Hair While Growing Out Your Bangs The key to styling your hair while growing out your bangs is to work with what you have. Instead of trying to fight your hair, embrace its natural texture and find hairstyles that work with your current length and style. Here are some ideas: 1. Side-swept bangs: Sweep your bangs to the side and pin them in place with bobby pins. This will create a stylish look that keeps your bangs out of your face. 2. Braids: Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking chic. Try a side braid or a fishtail braid. 3. Top knot: Pull your hair up into a top knot and leave your bangs out. This will give you a sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion. 4. Headbands: Headbands are a great way to keep your hair back and add some style to your look. Choose a headband that matches your outfit and wear it with confidence. Step by Step Guide 1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. 2. Comb your hair out and towel dry it gently. 3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. 4. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume and shape. 5. Style your hair using one of the above techniques, depending on your preference. 6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place. Tips for Styling Bangs in Transition 1. Use hair accessories: Headbands, hair clips, and bobby pins can be your best friend when growing out bangs. They can help keep your hair out of your face and add some style to your look. 2. Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out new hairstyles while growing out your bangs. You might discover a new look that you love! 3. Consider getting a trim: While growing out your bangs, it's important to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends. Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best. FAQ Q: How long does it take to grow out bangs? A: The length of time it takes to grow out bangs varies depending on the individual. On average, it can take anywhere from 4-6 months for bangs to grow out completely. Q: Can I still style my hair if I have short bangs? A: Absolutely! Even if your bangs are short, you can still style your hair using the techniques mentioned above. Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Bangs 1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. 2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. 3. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Conclusion Growing out your bangs can be a challenge, but with the right styling techniques and hair care tips, you can still look fabulous while your bangs are in transition. Remember to work with what you have, experiment with different styles, and keep your hair healthy and moisturized. With these tips, you'll be able to rock any hairstyle while growing out your bangs!
