Men s Hairstyle Long On Top Short Back And Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, the men's hairstyle long on top short back and sides is the perfect choice. This haircut is a classic and timeless look that has been popular for decades. It's a great option for men of all ages and can be customized to suit your individual style. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get this haircut, as well as some tips on how to style and care for it.

Description

The men's hairstyle long on top short back and sides is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the back and sides. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference, but it's typically anywhere from 2-4 inches. The back and sides are usually cut with clippers to a shorter length, anywhere from a 1-3 guard.

How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides

To get this haircut, you'll need to visit a skilled barber or hairstylist. Here's a step-by-step guide on what to expect:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in getting any haircut is a consultation with your barber or stylist. This is where you'll discuss your desired length, style, and any specific requests you may have. Your barber or stylist will also assess the texture and thickness of your hair to determine the best approach for your cut.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides and Back

Next, your barber or stylist will start by cutting the back and sides of your hair with clippers. They'll use a guard to achieve the desired length, and may blend the hair on the sides and back for a seamless look. This part of the cut is usually quick and straightforward.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

Once the sides and back are done, your barber or stylist will move on to the top of your hair. They'll use scissors to cut the length, working in sections to ensure an even and balanced look. This is where you can customize the length and shape of the cut to suit your style.

Step 4: Styling

Finally, your barber or stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, comb, and styling products. They'll show you how to style your hair at home, so you can maintain your new look between haircuts.

Tips for Styling the Men's Hairstyle Long on Top Short Back and Sides

Here are some tips for styling your men's hairstyle long on top short back and sides:

  • Use a matte pomade or wax to style your hair for a natural, textured look.
  • If you want a sleeker look, use a high-shine pomade or gel.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction for added volume and texture.

FAQ

How often should I get this haircut?

It's recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the cut.

Can this haircut work for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can I still wear a hat with this haircut?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this haircut. Just make sure to style your hair first, and avoid wearing tight-fitting hats that may flatten your hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your men's hairstyle long on top short back and sides looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

The men's hairstyle long on top short back and sides is a classic and versatile haircut that never goes out of style. With a little maintenance and styling, you can keep this look fresh and modern. Whether you prefer a messy, textured style or a sleek and polished look, this haircut can be customized to suit your individual taste. So why not give it a try?
