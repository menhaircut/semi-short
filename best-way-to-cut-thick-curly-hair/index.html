The Best Way To Cut Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be both beautiful and challenging to maintain. When it comes to cutting it, finding the right technique is crucial. In this article, we’ll show you the best way to cut thick curly hair, along with some tips and tricks for keeping it healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Description

Thick curly hair is typically coarser and more voluminous than other hair types. It can be challenging to manage, but the right haircut can make a significant difference in how it looks and feels. The best way to cut thick curly hair is to use a layering technique that removes weight and adds shape. It’s essential to work with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair, as they will know how to work with the texture and avoid cutting too much off.

How to Cut Thick Curly Hair

The best way to cut thick curly hair is to use a layering technique that removes weight and adds shape. This technique involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create a natural-looking, textured effect. It’s important to use the right tools, such as sharp scissors and a comb, and to work with dry hair to get an accurate sense of how it will look when styled.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to cutting thick curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. It’s essential to work with dry hair to get an accurate sense of how it will look when styled.
  2. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and separate it into sections. Use clips to hold the sections in place.
  3. Start with the bottom section of hair and work your way up. Use the comb to lift a small section of hair and cut it at a slight angle, removing the weight from the hair. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way up to the top.
  4. Once you’ve cut all of the sections, use the scissors to trim any stray strands or uneven bits. Be careful not to cut too much off, as this can disrupt the natural shape of the hair.
  5. If desired, add some additional layers to the top section of hair to add more volume and texture.
  6. Finish by styling the hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the best results when cutting thick curly hair:

  • Work with dry hair to get an accurate sense of how it will look when styled.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean, precise cuts.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to the top.
  • Be careful not to cut too much off, as this can disrupt the natural shape of the hair.
  • Consider adding some layers to the top section of hair to add more volume and texture.
  • Work with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair to ensure the best results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick curly hair:

How often should I cut my thick curly hair?

It’s recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

What’s the best way to style thick curly hair?

The best way to style thick curly hair is to use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining product. Scrunch the hair gently with your hands and let it air dry, or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.

Can I cut my own thick curly hair?

While it’s possible to cut your own hair, it’s recommended to work with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair to ensure the best results.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your thick curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

By following these tips and using the right cutting techniques, you can keep your thick curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
