Why Do Some Chinese Have Curly Hair

Written by Frank Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair is often associated with people of African descent, but it is not uncommon to see Chinese people with curly hair. In fact, there are many Chinese people who have naturally curly hair. This article will explore the reasons why some Chinese people have curly hair.

What Causes Curly Hair?

Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. Straight hair has a round hair follicle, while curly hair has an oval-shaped hair follicle. The more oval-shaped the hair follicle, the curlier the hair. Additionally, curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, which can lead to more breakage and damage.

Genetics

One of the primary reasons why some Chinese people have curly hair is genetics. Just like how some people are born with blue eyes and others with brown eyes, some people are born with straight hair and others with curly hair. It is believed that curly hair is an inherited trait that is passed down from generation to generation.

Mixed Ethnicity

Another reason why some Chinese people have curly hair is because of their mixed ethnicity. China is a diverse country with many different ethnic groups. Some of these ethnic groups have curly hair as a common trait. For example, the Hui people, who are a Muslim minority group in China, often have curly hair. If a Chinese person has ancestry from one of these ethnic groups, they may also have curly hair as a result.

How to Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it is important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it is important to use a shampoo that is moisturizing. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage curly hair. If you must use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray first and keep the temperature low.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling curly hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. Brushes can cause breakage and damage to curly hair.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Harsh chemicals like bleach and relaxers can damage curly hair. If you must color your hair, opt for a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye.

FAQ

Is it rare for Chinese people to have curly hair?

No, it is not rare for Chinese people to have curly hair. While straight hair is more common in Chinese people, there are many Chinese people who have naturally curly hair.

Can Chinese people have naturally curly hair or is it always the result of styling?

Chinese people can have naturally curly hair. While some people may use styling tools to enhance their curls, others are born with naturally curly hair.

The Bottom Line

Curly hair is not exclusive to any particular race or ethnicity. There are many Chinese people who have naturally curly hair, and the reasons for this can vary from genetics to mixed ethnicity. If you have curly hair, it is important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage.
