How To Get Rid Of A Round Face Shape Surgery A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your round face shape and looking for a way to get rid of it? Surgery may be an option for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to get rid of a round face shape surgery, including how to prepare for it, the procedure, and aftercare.

How to Get Rid of a Round Face Shape Surgery

Getting rid of a round face shape through surgery involves a procedure known as facial liposuction. The procedure removes excess fat from the face, giving it a more defined and angular appearance.

Facial liposuction is a minimally invasive procedure that is performed under local anesthesia. The procedure involves making small incisions in the face and using a cannula to remove the excess fat from the targeted areas.

Preparation

Before undergoing facial liposuction, it is essential to prepare yourself both physically and mentally. Here are some things to consider:

  • Consult with a board-certified plastic surgeon to determine if you are a good candidate for the procedure.
  • Quit smoking at least four weeks before the surgery to reduce the risk of complications.
  • Avoid taking any blood-thinning medications, such as aspirin or ibuprofen, for at least two weeks before the surgery.
  • Arrange for someone to drive you home after the surgery.

Procedure

The facial liposuction procedure typically takes about one to two hours to complete. Here is what you can expect during the procedure:

  • The surgeon will administer local anesthesia to numb the targeted areas.
  • The surgeon will make small incisions in the face and insert a cannula to remove the excess fat.
  • The surgeon will then close the incisions with sutures.
  • You will be monitored in the recovery room for a few hours before being allowed to go home.

Aftercare

After the surgery, it is essential to follow the surgeon's instructions to ensure proper healing. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid touching your face or getting it wet for at least 24 hours after the surgery.
  • Avoid strenuous activities, such as exercise, for at least one week after the surgery.
  • Take any prescribed medications as directed by the surgeon.
  • Attend all follow-up appointments with the surgeon to monitor your progress.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your facial liposuction:

  • Follow a healthy diet and exercise regularly to maintain your new facial contour.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure, which can cause skin damage and premature aging.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and avoiding alcohol and caffeine.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people ask about getting rid of a round face shape surgery:

  • Is facial liposuction safe?
  • What is the recovery time for facial liposuction?
  • How much does facial liposuction cost?
  • Can facial liposuction be combined with other procedures?

Hair Care

After facial liposuction, it is essential to take care of your hair to avoid any irritation or infection. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, which can dry out the skin and cause irritation.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, which can cause tension on the incisions.
  • Gently wash your hair and avoid rubbing the incision areas.

Getting rid of a round face shape through surgery can give you the confidence boost you need. Follow these tips and guidelines to ensure a safe and successful procedure.
