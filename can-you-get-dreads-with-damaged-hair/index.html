Can You Get Dreads With Damaged Hair

Written by William Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have damaged hair, you may be wondering if you can still get dreadlocks. The answer is yes, but there are some things you need to consider before making the decision to lock your hair. In this article, we will discuss the steps you can take to get dreadlocks with damaged hair, as well as tips for maintaining healthy dreadlocks.

Description

Dreadlocks are a popular hairstyle that involves allowing hair to mat and form into long, rope-like strands. While dreadlocks can be achieved with any hair type, it is important to note that the process can be more challenging for those with damaged hair. Damaged hair is hair that has been weakened due to chemical treatments, heat styling, or other factors that can cause breakage and split ends.

How to Get Dreads with Damaged Hair

If you have damaged hair and want to get dreadlocks, there are a few steps you can take to ensure that your hair stays healthy throughout the process:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

The first step in getting dreadlocks with damaged hair is to consult with a professional stylist who specializes in dreadlocks. They will be able to assess the condition of your hair and determine if it is strong enough to withstand the locking process.

Step 2: Repair Damaged Hair

Before starting the locking process, it is important to repair any damage that may be present in your hair. This can be done through regular deep conditioning treatments, using protein-rich products, and avoiding heat styling and chemical treatments.

Step 3: Start with Smaller Sections

When starting the locking process, it is important to start with smaller sections of hair. This will help to distribute the weight of the locks evenly and prevent breakage.

Step 4: Use a Gentle Locking Method

When locking your hair, it is important to use a gentle method that will not cause further damage. Some popular locking methods include the backcombing method, the twist and rip method, and the interlocking method.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Dreadlocks

Once you have achieved your dreadlocks, it is important to take proper care of them to ensure that they stay healthy:

Tip 1: Keep Your Scalp Clean

Keeping your scalp clean is important for preventing buildup that can lead to itchiness and dandruff. Use a gentle shampoo and be sure to rinse thoroughly.

Tip 2: Moisturize Your Locks

Dreadlocks can become dry and brittle if they are not moisturized regularly. Use a moisturizing spray or oil to keep your locks hydrated.

Tip 3: Avoid Overwashing

While it is important to keep your scalp clean, overwashing can strip your locks of their natural oils. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week.

FAQ

Q: Can I get dreadlocks if my hair is severely damaged?

A: It is possible to get dreadlocks with severely damaged hair, but it may be more difficult to maintain their health. Consult with a professional stylist before starting the locking process.

Q: Will getting dreadlocks cause further damage to my hair?

A: If done correctly, getting dreadlocks should not cause further damage to your hair. It is important to use a gentle locking method and take proper care of your locks after they have been formed.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for maintaining its health, whether you have dreadlocks or not. Be sure to use gentle products, avoid heat styling and chemical treatments, and keep your hair moisturized.
