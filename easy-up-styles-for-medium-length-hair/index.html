Easy Up Styles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 16, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have medium length hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! There are many easy up styles for medium length hair that you can try to switch up your look. In this article, we'll go through some of the best up styles for medium length hair that you can try at home.

Description

Medium length hair is perfect for trying out up styles, as it's long enough to create a variety of looks, but not so long that you have to spend hours styling it. Up styles are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck, especially during the summer months. They're also perfect for special occasions, such as weddings or proms, as they add a touch of elegance to any outfit.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. You can apply some styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to help hold your style in place. Here are some easy up styles for medium length hair that you can try:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic up style that's perfect for medium length hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun, and secure it in place with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a great way to show off your medium length hair. To create this look, gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, leaving the bottom half loose. You can add some texture to your hair by curling it with a curling iron or adding some braids.

3. French Twist

The French twist is a sophisticated up style that's perfect for special occasions. To create this look, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it upwards. Secure the twist with bobby pins, and tuck the ends of your hair into the twist. You can add some accessories, such as flowers or jeweled pins, to make the style even more glamorous.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create the messy bun up style:

  1. Gather your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun in place with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Finish the look with hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect up style for your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different accessories, such as headbands or jeweled pins, to add some sparkle to your up style.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different textures, such as braids or curls, to add some dimension to your hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help hide them.
  • Don't worry if your up style isn't perfect – a messy, effortless look can be just as stylish!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about up styles for medium length hair:

1. Will up styles damage my hair?

Up styles are generally safe for your hair, as long as you don't pull your hair too tightly or use too many hair accessories that could damage your hair. Make sure to use bobby pins that match your hair color to help prevent breakage.

2. Can I create up styles for medium length hair myself?

Yes, you can definitely create up styles for medium length hair yourself! With a bit of practice and some styling products, you can create a variety of up styles that are perfect for any occasion.

3. How long will my up style last?

The longevity of your up style will depend on the type of style you choose and how well you secure it in place. You can use hairspray or other styling products to help hold your style in place for longer.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling it. Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these easy up styles for medium length hair, you can switch up your look and add some variety to your styling routine. Whether you're going for a casual or a formal look, there's an up style that's perfect for you!
