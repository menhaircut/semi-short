How To Get A Haircut In Animal Crossing New Leaf

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get a haircut in Animal Crossing: New Leaf! In this game, you can customize your character's appearance in a variety of ways, including getting a new haircut. Changing your hairstyle can be a fun way to switch up your look and express your creativity. Here's everything you need to know about how to get a haircut in New Leaf.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get a Haircut

In Animal Crossing: New Leaf, there are two ways to get a haircut. You can either visit the salon or use a mirror in your home. The salon is located in the city, and the mirror can be purchased from the furniture store once it becomes available.

Using the Salon

To get a haircut at the salon, head to the city and look for a building with a pair of scissors on the sign. This is the salon, run by Harriet the poodle. Inside, you'll be able to choose from a variety of hairstyles and hair colors. Simply sit down in the chair and let Harriet work her magic.

Using a Mirror

If you prefer to get a haircut in the comfort of your own home, you can purchase a mirror from the furniture store. Once you have the mirror, place it in your house and interact with it to access the hairstyle options. You can choose from the same hairstyles and colors as the salon.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a haircut in Animal Crossing: New Leaf:

  1. Visit the salon in the city or purchase a mirror from the furniture store.
  2. Select the option to get a haircut.
  3. Choose your desired hairstyle and color.
  4. Sit down in the chair (if at the salon) or interact with the mirror (if at home).
  5. Wait for the stylist to finish your new haircut.
  6. Enjoy your fresh new look!

Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect haircut in Animal Crossing: New Leaf:

  • Take your time choosing your hairstyle. There are plenty of options to choose from, so don't rush the decision.
  • Consider the season and weather when choosing your hairstyle. A shorter haircut may be more comfortable during the summer, while a longer style may be better for the winter.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to find the perfect shade for your character.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! You can always change your hairstyle again if you don't like it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a haircut in Animal Crossing: New Leaf:

Can I change my hairstyle after getting a haircut?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want. Simply visit the salon or use the mirror again to choose a new hairstyle.

Do I need to pay for a haircut?

Yes, getting a haircut at the salon or using the mirror at home requires payment. The cost varies depending on the hairstyle and color you choose.

Can I customize my character's hair in other ways?

Yes, in addition to getting a new haircut, you can also wear hats or accessories that change your character's hairstyle. Some clothing items may also have hairstyles built in.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've gotten your new haircut, it's important to take care of your character's hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for Animal Crossing: New Leaf:

  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your character's hair tangle-free.
  • Avoid getting your character's hair wet, as this can cause it to lose its shape.
  • Consider using hats or accessories to protect your character's hair from the elements.
  • If your character's hair does get messed up, simply visit the salon or use the mirror to fix it.

That's everything you need to know about how to get a haircut in Animal Crossing: New Leaf. We hope this guide has been helpful in customizing your character's appearance. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and colors!
