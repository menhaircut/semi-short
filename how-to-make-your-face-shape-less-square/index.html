How To Make Your Face Shape Less Square

Written by Robby Nov 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may be looking for ways to soften your features and make your face appear less angular. Fortunately, there are a variety of techniques and styling tips that can help you achieve a more balanced and symmetrical look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to make your face shape less square, including haircuts, makeup, and skincare.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular cheekbones. While this shape can be striking and powerful, it can also be challenging to style and balance. If you're looking to soften your features and create a more balanced look, there are several techniques you can use to make your face shape less square.

How to

There are several ways to make your face shape less square, including:

1. Choose the right haircut

The right haircut can make a big difference in how your face looks. If you have a square face shape, you may want to consider a style that adds volume and softness to your jawline and forehead. Some good options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves or curls
  • A bob with angled ends

These styles can help create a more balanced and symmetrical look, and draw attention away from your angular features.

2. Use makeup to contour your face

Makeup can be a powerful tool for shaping and defining your features. To make your face shape less square, you can use contouring techniques to create the illusion of softer angles and curves. Some tips to keep in mind include:

  • Use a warm-toned bronzer to contour your cheekbones and jawline
  • Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add softness and warmth
  • Highlight the center of your forehead and chin to create the illusion of length

These techniques can help create a more balanced and symmetrical look, and draw attention to your best features.

3. Focus on skincare

Great skincare can also help make your face shape less square. By keeping your skin healthy and hydrated, you can create a smoother, more radiant complexion that complements your features. Some tips to keep in mind include:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated
  • Use a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil without stripping your skin
  • Apply a moisturizer with SPF to protect your skin from sun damage
  • Use a facial serum or oil to nourish and hydrate your skin

These techniques can help keep your skin looking its best, and create a more balanced and symmetrical appearance.

Step by step

1. Choose the right haircut

To choose the right haircut for your square face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Consider your hair texture and length
  2. Look for styles that add volume and softness to your jawline and forehead
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you

2. Use makeup to contour your face

To use makeup to contour your face, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a warm-toned bronzer and a blush that complements your skin tone
  2. Apply the bronzer to the hollows of your cheeks and your jawline, blending well
  3. Apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending well
  4. Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead and chin, blending well

3. Focus on skincare

To focus on skincare, follow these steps:

  1. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated
  2. Use a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil
  3. Apply a moisturizer with SPF to protect your skin from sun damage
  4. Use a facial serum or oil to nourish and hydrate your skin

Tips

Some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your face shape less square include:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or severe, as they can accentuate your angular features
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques to find the ones that work best for you
  • Stay consistent with your skincare routine to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant

FAQ

What is a square face shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular cheekbones.

What is the best haircut for a square face shape?

The best haircut for a square face shape is one that adds volume and softness to your jawline and forehead, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, soft waves or curls, or a bob with angled ends.

Can makeup help make my face shape less square?

Yes, makeup can be a powerful tool for shaping and defining your features. By using contouring techniques, you can create the illusion of softer angles and curves.

Hair Care

When trying to make your face shape less square, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes:

  • Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type
  • Avoiding heat styling tools that can damage your hair
  • Getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant
  • Using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

By taking good care of your hair, you can create a more balanced and symmetrical look that complements your features.
