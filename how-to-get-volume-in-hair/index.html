How To Get Volume In Hair Tips Tricks And Products

Written by Robby Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about how to get volume in hair, including tips, tricks, and product recommendations.

How to Get Volume in Hair: Step by Step

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray from roots to ends.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush. This will help lift the roots and add volume.

3. Once your hair is dry, flip it back and use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves. This will help create the illusion of more volume.

4. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold the style in place and add extra lift.

Tips for Getting Volume in Hair

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body to your hair.

- Avoid heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

- Experiment with different hairstyles and partings to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

- Don't be afraid to tease your roots for extra lift, but be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Q: What products are best for adding volume to hair?

A: Look for volumizing mousses, sprays, and powders that are specifically designed to add body to hair. Avoid heavy oils and serums, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Q: Can I add volume to my hair without heat styling?

A: Yes! Try using a volumizing foam or spray and blow-drying your hair upside down using just your fingers. You can also try air-drying your hair with braids or twists to create natural waves and texture.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

To keep your voluminous hair healthy and shiny, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid over-styling your hair and use heat protectant products when using hot tools. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy hair in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find what works best for you. Happy styling!
