5 On Top 2 On Sides Fade A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that can give you a bold and edgy look, then the 5 on top 2 on sides fade is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has gained immense popularity in recent years, and it's not hard to understand why. It's versatile, low-maintenance, and suits almost every face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know about the 5 on top 2 on sides fade haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, hair care, and more. So, let's get started!

Description: What is a 5 on Top 2 on Sides Fade?

The 5 on top 2 on sides fade haircut, also known as the low fade, is a style where the hair is cut short on the sides and back of the head, gradually blending into longer hair on the top. The number 5 refers to the length of the hair on the top, while the number 2 refers to the length on the sides and back. The fade is a gradual transition from short to longer hair, creating a clean and sophisticated look.

How to Get a 5 on Top 2 on Sides Fade?

The 5 on top 2 on sides fade is a popular haircut that can be achieved with the help of a professional hairstylist or barber. Here's how to get it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

It's essential to choose a barber who has experience in cutting fades. Look for someone who has a good reputation and can understand your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

You need to decide on the length you want for your hair on the top and sides. The standard length for the top is around 5mm, while for the sides, it's 2mm. However, you can adjust the length according to your preferences.

Step 3: The Fade

The barber will use clippers to create the fade, starting with a higher guard and gradually moving to a lower guard. This will create a gradient effect that blends the short hair on the sides with the longer hair on the top.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair with your preferred hair products. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Styling a 5 on Top 2 on Sides Fade

Here are some styling tips that can help you rock your 5 on top 2 on sides fade:

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Products

There are various hair products available in the market that can help you achieve different looks. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Tip 2: Use a Comb

Using a comb can help you achieve a more polished and refined look. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to go, and use a product to hold it in place.

Tip 3: Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape and length of your 5 on top 2 on sides fade. Visit your barber every 3-4 weeks for a trim.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is the 5 on top 2 on sides fade suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the 5 on top 2 on sides fade is suitable for almost all face shapes. It can create a more defined jawline and enhance your facial features.

Q2: Can I style my 5 on top 2 on sides fade differently?

A: Yes, you can style your 5 on top 2 on sides fade differently according to your preferences. You can create a slicked-back look, a messy look, or a textured look.

Q3: Will a 5 on top 2 on sides fade suit my hair type?

A: Yes, a 5 on top 2 on sides fade can suit various hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Hair Care for 5 on Top 2 on Sides Fade

A 5 on top 2 on sides fade is a low-maintenance haircut, but it still requires some hair care. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using products with harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Tip 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently to avoid damaging the hair follicles. Avoid using a hairdryer unless necessary.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other elements. Use a hat or a scarf to cover your head when you're outside.

That's everything you need to know about the 5 on top 2 on sides fade haircut. It's a trendy and stylish haircut that can give you a bold and edgy look. With the right styling, you can rock this haircut on any occasion. So, go ahead and try it out!
