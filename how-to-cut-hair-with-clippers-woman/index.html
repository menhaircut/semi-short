How To Cut Hair With Clippers Woman Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 05, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re wondering how to cut hair with clippers woman, you’re not alone. Many women have started to take matters into their own hands, especially during the pandemic when hair salons were closed. However, cutting your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you’ve never done it before. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Cutting hair with clippers can be a great way to maintain a short hairstyle. It’s quick, easy, and economical. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting your hair with clippers, step-by-step. We’ll also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect haircut.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers Woman – Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first thing you need to do is gather your supplies. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Make sure your clippers have different guard sizes, so you can adjust the length of your hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Size

Choose the guard size depending on the length you want for your hair. If you want a shorter haircut, start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller one. If you want a longer haircut, start with a smaller guard and work your way up to a larger one.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting it. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, and it can also cause the clippers to slip. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair by placing the clippers at the nape of your neck. Move the clippers up towards the crown of your head, following the natural curve of your head. Repeat this process until you’ve cut all the hair at the back of your head.

Step 6: Move to the Sides

Once you’ve cut the hair at the back of your head, move to the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the same technique as before. Make sure you’re cutting the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 7: Cut the Top

Now it’s time to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, using the same technique as before. Make sure you’re cutting the hair evenly and that it’s the same length as the sides and the back.

Step 8: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them separately. Comb them down and cut them to the desired length. Make sure they’re even and straight.

Step 9: Check for Any Uneven Spots

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check for any uneven spots. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or to even out any spots that look uneven.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Once you’re satisfied with the length of your hair, style it as desired. You can use a hair dryer, hair gel, or other styling products to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller one to achieve a shorter haircut.
  • Start with a smaller guard and work your way up to a larger one to achieve a longer haircut.
  • Use the comb to hold your hair in place while cutting it.
  • Use the scissors to trim any uneven spots or to achieve a more textured look.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don’t worry if your first attempt isn’t perfect. Keep practicing and you’ll get better.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with clippers if I have long hair?

Cutting your long hair with clippers can be difficult, especially if you’ve never done it before. It’s best to start with shorter hair and work your way up to longer hair.

Do I need to oil my clippers?

Yes, it’s important to oil your clippers before and after each use to keep them in good condition.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

You can use regular scissors to trim your hair, but they’re not ideal for cutting large amounts of hair. Hair clippers are designed specifically for cutting hair and will make the process much easier.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Cutting your hair with clippers can be a great way to maintain a short hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time and practice, and you’ll soon become an expert at cutting your hair with clippers.
