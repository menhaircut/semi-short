How To Wear Pixie Cut A Guide To Effortlessly Styling Short Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short haircuts have been a popular trend for many years now, and one of the most iconic short hairstyles is the pixie cut. With its low maintenance and effortless style, the pixie cut is a great way to show off your features and make a bold statement. In this guide, we will show you how to wear a pixie cut with confidence and style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Pixie Cut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cut

How to Wear Pixie Cut

Firstly, let's start with the basics of how to wear a pixie cut. It is important to note that the pixie cut is a very short haircut, and it requires minimal styling. To wear a pixie cut, you will need to keep the sides and back of your hair short, while leaving the top section slightly longer. This will give your hair some texture and volume, and create a more feminine look.

When it comes to styling your pixie cut, you can opt for a side-swept fringe or a messy, textured look. You can also add some volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down, or use a small amount of styling product to create a sleek, polished look. The key is to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to wear a pixie cut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it gently.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing spray or mousse.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair, if desired.
  5. Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you wear your pixie cut with confidence:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some flair to your look.
  • If you have a round face shape, opt for a side-swept fringe or add some height to your hair to elongate your face.
  • If you have a square or angular face shape, soften your features by adding some texture to your hair or going for a messy, tousled look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a pixie cut:

Q: Is a pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can be styled to suit all face shapes. It is all about finding the right style and length for your features.

Q: Can I still style my pixie cut if my hair is very thin or fine?

A: Yes, you can still style your pixie cut if you have thin or fine hair. Try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, or use a texturizing spray to add some volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut

When it comes to hair care for your pixie cut, it is important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot styling tools too frequently. You can also use a weekly hair mask to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

In conclusion, wearing a pixie cut can be both chic and effortless. With the right styling techniques and tips, you can rock this short hairstyle with confidence and style.
