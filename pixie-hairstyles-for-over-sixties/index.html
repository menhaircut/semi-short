Pixie Hairstyles For Over Sixties

Written by Christine Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are over sixty and looking for a new hairstyle, consider the pixie cut. This short and chic hairstyle is perfect for mature women who want a low-maintenance look that is still stylish and modern. In this article, we will provide you with a guide on how to achieve the perfect pixie hairstyle for over sixties.

Description

The pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is cut close to the head. It is a low-maintenance style that is perfect for women who want to save time on their hair routine. The pixie cut can be styled in many different ways, including with bangs, layered, or tapered.

How to Achieve the Perfect Pixie Cut

Step 1: Choose a Style

Before you get your hair cut, decide on the style you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines to find a style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.

Step 2: Find a Good Stylist

Find a stylist who is experienced in cutting pixie hairstyles. Look for reviews online or ask for recommendations from friends. A good stylist can help you choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Get Your Hair Cut

When you go to the salon, communicate with your stylist about the style you want. They will cut your hair to the desired length and shape. If you want to add layers or bangs, let your stylist know.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your hair is cut, you can style it in many different ways. Use a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to create curls or waves.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking great, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your hair
  • Use a styling product to add texture and volume
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair

FAQ

Can I still get a pixie cut if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still get a pixie cut if you have thinning hair. Talk to your stylist about adding layers to create volume and texture.

Will a pixie cut flatter my face shape?

There is a pixie cut that will flatter every face shape. Talk to your stylist about finding a style that suits your face shape.

How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

To maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut, get it trimmed every four to six weeks.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your hair
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils

Conclusion

The pixie cut is a low-maintenance and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for women over sixty. By following our guide, you can achieve the perfect pixie cut for your face shape and hair texture. Remember to maintain your hair with regular trims and gentle hair care products to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
