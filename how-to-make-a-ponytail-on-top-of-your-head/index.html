How To Make A Ponytail On Top Of Your Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old low ponytail? Why not switch things up and try a high ponytail on top of your head? This hairstyle is not only trendy but also practical for keeping your hair out of your face. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create a perfect ponytail on top of your head.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have the following essentials: a hairbrush, hair elastic, and hair spray.

Step 1:

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2:

Next, gather all your hair on top of your head with your dominant hand. Make sure it's smooth and even.

Step 3:

With your other hand, secure the hair into a ponytail with a hair elastic. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 4:

Use a hair spray to keep any flyaway hairs down and to add shine to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making a ponytail on top of your head:

  • Make sure your hair is free from any tangles or knots before making a ponytail.
  • Use a hair elastic that matches the color of your hair to make it less visible.
  • For a sleek look, use a comb or brush to smooth any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  • If you have thin hair, try teasing the crown of your hair to add volume to the ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a ponytail on top of your head:

Can I make a ponytail on top of my head if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still make a ponytail on top of your head if you have short hair. It will just be a smaller ponytail.

How do I make my ponytail stay in place all day?

Use a hair elastic that is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight to cause discomfort. You can also use a hair spray to add extra hold.

Can I wear a ponytail on top of my head to formal events?

Yes, you can definitely wear a ponytail on top of your head to formal events. Just make sure it's sleek and polished. You can also add accessories like a hair clip or headband to dress it up.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after making a ponytail on top of your head. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid making a ponytail on wet hair as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to make a ponytail on top of your head, it's time to try it out and see how it looks on you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles.
