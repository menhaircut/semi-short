Short Bob Haircut With Undercut A Modern Twist On A Classic Style

Written by Luffy Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy haircut, look no further than the short bob haircut with undercut. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a great option for those who want to add some edge to their look. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

What is a Short Bob Haircut with Undercut?

A short bob haircut with undercut is a variation of the classic bob haircut, which features hair that is cut to just above the shoulders. The undercut involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and/or back of the head very short or even bald, creating a contrasting look with the longer hair on top. This style is often worn with a side or center part and can be styled straight or with waves or curls.

How to Get a Short Bob Haircut with Undercut

Getting a short bob haircut with undercut is best done by a professional hairstylist. Here are the basic steps involved in getting this haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting any haircut, it's important to have a consultation with your stylist to discuss the look you want and to make sure it will work with your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Bring pictures of the style you're interested in and be open to your stylist's suggestions.

Step 2: Cutting the Bob

The first step in getting a short bob haircut with undercut is to cut the longer hair on top into a bob shape. Your stylist will use scissors or a razor to create a blunt or textured cut, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 3: Shaving or Cutting the Undercut

Next, your stylist will shave or cut the hair on the sides and/or back of your head to create the undercut. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the length and texture of your hair.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron, depending on the look you want to achieve. They may also apply products such as hair gel, mousse, or hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling a Short Bob Haircut with Undercut

Here are some tips for styling your short bob haircut with undercut:

  • Experiment with different parts to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Try adding texture to your hair by using a sea salt spray or texturizing spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQ: Short Bob Haircut with Undercut

Q: Who can pull off a short bob haircut with undercut?

A: This haircut can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes, but it's best suited for those with straight or wavy hair and oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Is the undercut difficult to maintain?

A: The undercut requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp, as the hair will grow back quickly. This may involve frequent trips to the salon or learning how to use clippers at home.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with an undercut. However, you may need to use more hairpins or bobby pins to secure the shorter hair, and the undercut may be visible depending on how you style your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Bob Haircut with Undercut

To keep your short bob haircut with undercut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every few days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short bob haircut with undercut like a pro. Whether you're going for a bold and edgy look or a more subtle style, this haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
