Great Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Petter Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of experimenting with different hairstyles that don't seem to suit your heart-shaped face? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will be discussing some of the best haircuts for heart shaped faces that will enhance your natural beauty and complement your facial features. Keep reading to find out more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of a great haircut for a heart-shaped face is to balance out these features and create the illusion of a more proportional face shape. The right haircut can also accentuate your best features and draw attention away from any areas you are less confident about.

How to

The key to finding the perfect haircut for a heart-shaped face is to choose a style that adds width to the lower half of your face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves. You should also consider a hairstyle that frames your face and draws attention to your eyes.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best haircuts for heart-shaped faces:

Long Layers

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Create long layers starting at your chin and working your way down.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Shoulder-Length Bob

  1. Begin by washing your hair and applying a smoothing serum to the ends.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight using a paddle brush.
  3. Cut your hair into a shoulder-length bob with long layers around your face.
  4. Finish with a shine serum to add extra luster to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a haircut for a heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or a center part, as these can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or soft layers around your face to balance out your features.
  • Experiment with different lengths to find the perfect style for you.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your unique face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for heart-shaped faces:

What is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face?

The best haircut for a heart-shaped face is one that adds width to the lower half of the face and frames the face.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off short haircuts?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off short haircuts, but it's important to choose a style that adds width to the lower half of the face.

What hairstyles should heart-shaped faces avoid?

Heart-shaped faces should avoid blunt bangs, center parts, and hairstyles that add volume to the forehead.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote strong, healthy hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect haircut for your heart-shaped face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for you, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
