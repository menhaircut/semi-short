Boy Hair Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Are you looking for a trendy hairstyle for your little boy? Look no further than the classic "boy hair long on top short on sides" cut. This style has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this cut, including how to achieve it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve the Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides Cut

The boy hair long on top short on sides cut is relatively easy to achieve, even if you're not a professional hairstylist. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Lengths

The key to this cut is having longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides. Talk to your stylist about what lengths will work best for your child's hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. You can choose a fade or a more blunt cut, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can keep it all one length or add layers for more texture.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use scissors or clippers to blend the layers on top with the shorter hair on the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Style as Desired

Now that the cut is complete, it's time to style it. Read on for our top styling tips.

Styling Tips

The boy hair long on top short on sides cut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

The right products can make all the difference in achieving the desired look. For a sleek, shiny finish, use a pomade or wax. For a more textured look, try a matte paste or clay.

Tip 2: Experiment with Parting

You can create different looks by parting the hair on top to one side or the other, or even down the middle.

Tip 3: Add Accessories

Accessories like hats or headbands can add some extra flair to the cut.

Hair Care Advice

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the boy hair long on top short on sides cut. Here's what you need to know:

Tip 1: Wash Regularly

Regular washing will keep the hair clean and healthy. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Tip 2: Trim as Needed

To keep the cut looking sharp, trim the hair every 4-6 weeks. You can do this at home or visit a stylist.

Tip 3: Protect from Heat

If using heat tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q: How long should the hair be on top?

A: This varies depending on personal preference and hair type, but a good rule of thumb is to aim for at least 2-3 inches of length on top.

Q: Can this cut work for curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair and can take its natural texture into account.

Q: Is this cut appropriate for all ages?

A: Yes! The boy hair long on top short on sides cut can work for toddlers, kids, and even teenagers.

With this guide, you're ready to give your little boy the trendy and stylish boy hair long on top short on sides cut. Just remember to choose the right lengths, style as desired, and take good care of the hair. Happy styling!
