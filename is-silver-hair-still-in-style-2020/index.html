Is Silver Hair Still In Style 2020

Description

Silver hair has been a popular trend in recent years, and it is still going strong in 2020. This hair color is perfect for those who want to add a touch of sophistication and edginess to their look. It is also a great way to embrace your natural grays and make them look chic and stylish.

Many celebrities and influencers have been embracing this trend, including Kim Kardashian, Lady Gaga, and Rihanna. They have shown that silver hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

Whether you want to fully commit to silver hair or just add some highlights to your natural hair color, this trend is still in style and can be a great way to switch up your look.

How to Achieve Silver Hair

There are a few ways to achieve silver hair, depending on your starting hair color and how much of a change you want to make.

If you have naturally light hair, such as blonde or light brown, you can achieve silver hair by using a toner or hair dye with a silver or ash tone. This will neutralize any yellow or brassy tones in your hair and give it a silver hue.

If you have darker hair, you may need to bleach your hair first to achieve a silver color. This can be damaging to your hair, so it is important to use a high-quality bleach and follow proper hair care techniques.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving silver hair:

  1. Consult with a professional hair stylist to determine the best way to achieve silver hair based on your starting hair color and hair type.
  2. If necessary, bleach your hair to a light blonde shade.
  3. Apply a silver or ash toner or hair dye to your hair, making sure to follow the instructions carefully.
  4. Wait for the dye to process, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Silver Hair

Once you have achieved your desired silver hair color, it is important to take proper care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage.

Here are some tips for maintaining silver hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

FAQs

Q: Will silver hair look good on all skin tones?

A: Silver hair can look great on many different skin tones, but it is important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade for you.

Q: Can I achieve silver hair at home?

A: It is possible to achieve silver hair at home, but it is recommended to consult with a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my silver hair?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the color. It is recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep the color looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Silver Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for keeping your silver hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a soft-bristled brush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Protect your hair from heat by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Overall, silver hair is still in style in 2020 and can be a great way to switch up your look and embrace your natural grays. Just make sure to take proper care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage.
