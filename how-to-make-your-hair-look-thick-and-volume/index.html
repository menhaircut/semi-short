How To Make Your Hair Look Thick And Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having thin, lifeless hair? Do you want to give your hair a voluminous, thick look? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to make your hair look thick and volume. From hair care tips to styling techniques, we have got everything covered. So, let’s dive in!

How to Make Your Hair Look Thick and Volume – A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial when it comes to making your hair look thick and volume. Look for products that are specifically designed for fine or thin hair. These products contain ingredients that add volume to your hair, making it look thicker.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Mousse

After washing your hair, apply a volumizing mousse from roots to tips. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it look thicker.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and thickness to your hair. Use a round brush while blow drying to create more volume.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an easy way to add volume and thickness. Take a small section of hair and backcomb it gently. Repeat this process throughout your hair to create more volume.

Step 5: Use Hair Extensions

If you want an instant thick and voluminous look, then consider using hair extensions. There are many different types of hair extensions available, such as clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins. Choose the one that suits your hair type and desired look.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Thick and Volume

Here are some additional tips that can help you to make your hair look thick and volume:

Tip 1: Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can help to add volume to your hair by absorbing excess oil and dirt. Spray dry shampoo on your roots and massage it in. This will instantly lift your hair and make it look thicker.

Tip 2: Avoid Heavy Hair Products

Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair. Instead, use lightweight products that add volume and texture.

Tip 3: Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it look flat and lifeless. Wash your hair every other day or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can I make my hair look thicker without using extensions?

Yes, you can make your hair look thicker without using extensions. Follow the steps mentioned above, use volumizing products, and avoid heavy hair products.

Q2: How often should I wash my hair?

Wash your hair every other day or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Q3: Can I use a volumizing mousse on dry hair?

No, it’s best to use a volumizing mousse on damp hair.

Hair Care

Good hair care is essential when it comes to making your hair look thick and volume. Here are some tips that can help you to maintain healthy and thick hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair tools that can damage your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

By following these tips, you can make your hair look thick and volume. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the ones that work best for you.
