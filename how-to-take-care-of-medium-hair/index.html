How To Take Care Of Medium Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Medium hair can be tricky to take care of. It’s not too long, not too short, and can easily become frizzy or dry. But with the right care, you can keep your medium hair healthy and shiny. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to take care of medium hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Take Care of Medium Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Medium Hair Care
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Medium Hair

How to Take Care of Medium Hair

Medium hair is usually shoulder length or a little bit longer. It’s important to keep it healthy so it doesn’t become dry or damaged. Here are some steps you can take to keep your medium hair looking its best:

1. Wash your hair regularly.

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

2. Use a deep conditioner.

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it moisturized and healthy. Apply the conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out. You can also use a leave-in conditioner for extra moisture.

3. Avoid heat styling.

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair.

4. Trim your hair regularly.

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or whenever you notice your ends starting to look frayed.

5. Protect your hair from the sun.

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to help protect your hair from the sun.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to take care of your medium hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and rinse it out thoroughly. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Deep condition your hair

Once a week, apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out. This will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Step 3: Avoid heat styling

Try to avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair.

Step 4: Trim your hair

Get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or whenever you notice your ends starting to look frayed. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Step 5: Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to help protect your hair from the sun.

Tips for Medium Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your medium hair:

1. Use a wide-toothed comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, especially when it’s wet. This will help prevent breakage and damage.

2. Don’t brush your hair when it’s wet

Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so avoid brushing it when it’s wet.

3. Use a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentler on your hair and can help prevent breakage.

4. Use a clarifying shampoo once a month

Clarifying shampoos can help remove buildup from styling products or hard water, which can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my medium hair?

A: It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Should I use a leave-in conditioner?

A: Yes, a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of a deep conditioner?

A: Yes, a hair mask can provide similar benefits to a deep conditioner.

Hair Care Products for Medium Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for medium hair:

1. Shampoo and conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

2. Deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Leave-in conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner for extra moisture and protection.

4. Heat protectant spray

Use a heat protectant spray if you use heat styling tools.

5. Hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week for extra hydration and nourishment.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your medium hair healthy and shiny. Remember to wash your hair regularly, deep condition it once a week, avoid heat styling, and protect it from the sun. With a little bit of care, your medium hair will look its best.
