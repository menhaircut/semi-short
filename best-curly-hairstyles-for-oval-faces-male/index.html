Best Curly Hairstyles For Oval Faces Male

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face and curly hair, then you have hit the genetic jackpot. With your perfectly symmetrical face and curly locks, there are a variety of hairstyles that will flatter your features. In this article, we will explore the best curly hairstyles for oval faces male. Whether you prefer short or long hair, we have got you covered.

Description

An oval-shaped face is considered to be the ideal face shape, as it is perfectly symmetrical with well-balanced proportions. If you have an oval face, then you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, when it comes to curly hair, you need to be careful with your hairstyle choice as it can either enhance your features or make them look disproportionate.

How to

Choosing the right hairstyle for your oval face and curly hair can be a daunting task. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your hair type. Curly hair can be categorized into three types: wavy, curly, and coily. Determine your hair type to find the best hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, then you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

Step 3: Find Inspiration

Find inspiration from celebrities or social media influencers who have an oval face and curly hair. Look for hairstyles that flatter their face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. They can provide you with professional advice on which hairstyle will flatter your features the most.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best curly hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out the symmetry of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head as it can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and movement to your curls.
  • Opt for a hairstyle that complements your personal style and personality.

FAQ

What are the best curly hairstyles for oval faces male?

The best curly hairstyles for oval faces male include the quiff, pompadour, curly fringe, messy curls, and short afro.

Can I pull off long curly hair with an oval face?

Yes, you can pull off long curly hair with an oval face. However, you need to ensure that the length and volume of your hair are balanced to avoid making your face look disproportionate.

How do I style my curly hair?

Styling curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair. Use a diffuser to dry your hair to enhance your curls. Apply a styling product such as mousse or curl cream to define your curls and add shine.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls.

There you have it, the best curly hairstyles for oval faces male. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for you. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can rock your curly locks with confidence.
