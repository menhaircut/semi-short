How To Style Your Hair When Growing It Out

Written by William Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're growing out your hair, you may find yourself stuck in a rut when it comes to styling. But fear not, there are plenty of ways to keep your locks looking fabulous during the growing process. In this post, we'll cover how to style your hair when growing it out, as well as some tips for hair care along the way.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Hair When Growing it Out
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Hair While Growing it Out

How to Style Your Hair When Growing it Out

The key to styling your hair while growing it out is to work with the length you have. Depending on your hair type, you may find some styles work better than others. Here are a few options to consider:

1. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair under control while it grows out. They're also versatile, allowing you to create a range of different looks. Try a simple three-strand braid or mix things up with a fishtail or Dutch braid.

2. Top Knot

A top knot can be a lifesaver when you're in a hurry. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it into a bun, and secure with a hair tie. You can also add a hair accessory like a scrunchie or hairpin for extra style.

3. Half Up, Half Down

If you're looking for a style that's both practical and pretty, try a half-up, half-down look. Pull the top section of your hair up and secure with a clip or hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down for a soft, romantic vibe.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a half-up, half-down style:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair and secure with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Fluff up the top section to create volume.
  4. Smooth down any flyaways with a little hairspray.
  5. Leave the bottom section of your hair down, or add some loose curls with a curling iron for extra style.

Tips for Styling Hair While Growing it Out

When it comes to styling your hair while growing it out, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Invest in some good hair accessories. From hair ties to clips to headbands, having the right tools on hand can make all the difference.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!
  • Stay patient. Growing out your hair can be a slow process, but it's worth it in the end.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still use heat styling tools while growing out my hair?

Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

How often should I get my hair trimmed while growing it out?

It's a good idea to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to keep split ends at bay.

What if my hair is too short to style?

If your hair is still too short to style, try using a headband or scarf to add some flair.

Hair Care Tips While Growing it Out

While styling your hair is important, it's also crucial to take care of it during the growing process. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using excessive heat styling tools.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients to support healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your hair with confidence as it grows out. Remember to stay patient, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair along the way.
