How To Stop Hair Fall Home Remedies In Tamil

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 · 3 min read

In this article, we will discuss the most effective home remedies to stop hair fall in Tamil. Hair fall is a common problem faced by both men and women. There can be several factors that cause hair fall such as poor diet, stress, hormonal changes, genetics, and environmental factors. However, with the help of some natural remedies, you can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some effective home remedies that can help prevent hair fall:

1. Coconut Oil Massage

Coconut oil is an excellent natural remedy for hair fall. It nourishes the hair follicles and strengthens the roots. Massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it on for an hour. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur which helps in promoting hair growth. It also helps in strengthening hair follicles and preventing hair fall. Extract the juice of an onion and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this once a week.

3. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural conditioner and is rich in proteolytic enzymes which help in repairing dead skin cells on the scalp. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this twice a week.

4. Curry Leaves

Curry leaves are rich in antioxidants which help in preventing hair fall. Boil a handful of curry leaves in coconut oil and let it cool down. Apply this oil to your scalp and hair and leave it on for an hour. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this twice a week.

5. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein and nicotinic acid which help in promoting hair growth. Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply this paste to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo. Repeat this once a week.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips that can help prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Reduce stress levels
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair
  • Get enough sleep

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you are stressed, your body releases cortisol which can damage hair follicles and cause hair fall.

Q. Can genetics cause hair fall?

A. Yes, genetics can play a role in hair fall. If your parents or grandparents have a history of hair fall, you are more likely to experience it too.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips that can help prevent hair fall:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair
  • Do not comb or brush your hair when it is wet
  • Avoid using hair dryers and straighteners
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these home remedies and hair care tips, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember, consistency is key when it comes to natural remedies, so be patient and persistent.
