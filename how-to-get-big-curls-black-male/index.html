How To Get Big Curls Black Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a black male with relaxed hair, you might be wondering how to achieve big, voluminous curls. Fortunately, with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to get the look you want. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to get big curls as a black male with relaxed hair. We'll also offer some tips on hair care and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Big Curls Black Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Big Curls
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Get Big Curls Black Male

The first step in achieving big curls as a black male is to choose the right products. Look for hair products that are specifically designed for relaxed hair and that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil. You'll also need a good quality curling wand or curling iron.

Once you have your products and tools, you can start the process of creating big curls. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prepare it for styling. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and breakage. Spray the heat protectant evenly over your hair and comb it through.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you curl each section evenly.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand or iron. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds and then release. Continue curling each section until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Loosen Your Curls

Once you've curled your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls. This will give them a more natural, voluminous look.

Step 6: Apply a Light Hold Hairspray

To help your curls hold their shape, apply a light hold hairspray. Hold the can about 8-10 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Big Curls

Once you've achieved big curls, you'll want to make sure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to help prevent frizz and maintain your curls.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get big curls with natural hair?

Yes, it's possible to get big curls with natural hair. However, the process will be different than with relaxed hair. You'll need to use different products and techniques, such as twist-outs or braid-outs.

How often should I curl my hair?

You should avoid curling your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Aim to curl your hair no more than once a week.

What should I do if my curls start to fall flat?

If your curls start to fall flat, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray to give them a boost. You can also try re-curling the sections that have fallen flat.

Hair Care for Black Males

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for black males:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers or perms.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve big, voluminous curls as a black male with relaxed hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong, and to avoid over-styling or using too much heat. Happy styling!
