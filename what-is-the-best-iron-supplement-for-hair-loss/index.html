What Is The Best Iron Supplement For Hair Loss In 2023

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Iron is one of the essential minerals that our body needs to function properly. It helps to produce hemoglobin, which carries oxygen throughout the body. Iron deficiency can lead to anemia, fatigue, and hair loss. Hair loss is a common problem faced by many people, and iron deficiency is one of the leading causes of it. In this article, we will discuss what is the best iron supplement for hair loss in 2023.

What are the Symptoms of Iron Deficiency?

Iron deficiency can lead to various symptoms, including:

  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Pale skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Headaches
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Brittle nails
  • Craving for ice or dirt
  • Hair loss

How to Diagnose Iron Deficiency?

If you experience any of the above symptoms, you should consult a doctor. A blood test can diagnose iron deficiency. The doctor will check your hemoglobin and ferritin levels to determine if you are deficient in iron.

What is the Best Iron Supplement for Hair Loss?

There are various iron supplements available in the market, but not all of them are suitable for hair loss. The best iron supplement for hair loss is the one that contains ferrous sulfate. Ferrous sulfate is the most commonly used iron supplement, and it is highly absorbable by the body.

How to Choose the Right Iron Supplement?

When choosing an iron supplement, you should look for the following:

  • Ferrous sulfate as the main ingredient
  • Low dose of iron (around 18 mg per day)
  • No added fillers or binders
  • No added flavors or colors
  • Gluten-free and vegetarian-friendly

How to Take Iron Supplement?

You should take iron supplement with vitamin C to increase its absorption. You should also avoid taking it with calcium, dairy products, or antacids as they can interfere with iron absorption. You should take iron supplement on an empty stomach, preferably in the morning.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss Due to Iron Deficiency

Here are some tips to prevent hair loss due to iron deficiency:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in iron, such as red meat, spinach, lentils, and fortified cereals
  • Avoid drinking tea or coffee with meals as they can reduce iron absorption
  • Take iron supplement as per your doctor's recommendation
  • Manage stress levels as stress can lead to hair loss
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can Iron Deficiency Cause Hair Loss?

Yes, iron deficiency can cause hair loss. Iron is essential for hair growth, and its deficiency can lead to hair loss.

What is the Recommended Daily Dose of Iron?

The recommended daily dose of iron is around 18 mg per day for adult women and 8 mg per day for adult men.

Can I Take Iron Supplement with Multivitamins?

Yes, you can take iron supplement with multivitamins, but you should consult your doctor first.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from taking iron supplements, it's also important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using tight hairstyles that can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf

In conclusion, iron deficiency can lead to hair loss, but it can be prevented by taking iron supplements and following a healthy diet. The best iron supplement for hair loss is the one that contains ferrous sulfate. You should also take care of your hair by following the hair care tips mentioned above. If you experience any severe symptoms, you should consult a doctor immediately.
