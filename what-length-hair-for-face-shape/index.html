What Length Hair For Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, it's important to find a length that complements your face shape. Not all hairstyles are created equal, and what looks great on one person may not be as flattering on another. In this guide, we'll go over what length hair is best for each face shape and provide tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle. If you have an oval face shape, you can experiment with different lengths and styles without worrying about whether it will suit your face shape. However, if you want to emphasize your features, a shoulder-length or longer hairstyle can help draw attention to your cheekbones and jawline.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles that elongate your face. A shoulder-length or longer hairstyle with layers can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and angular features. If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head. A layered bob or a shoulder-length hairstyle with layers can help balance out your face and soften your features. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, as they can accentuate your angular features.

Heart Face Shape

A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your forehead appear wider. Instead, opt for a chin-length or longer hairstyle with layers. Soft waves can also help balance out your face shape.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is characterized by high cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to balance out your features with a hairstyle that adds volume to the top and bottom of your head. A layered bob or a chin-length hairstyle with side-swept bangs can help soften your features and create balance.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as they can make your face appear longer. Instead, opt for a shoulder-length or shorter hairstyle with layers. Soft waves can also help create the illusion of width in your face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Length Hair

  • Consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure what length hair is best for your face shape.
  • Take into account your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a short hairstyle if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can still wear a short hairstyle if you have a round face shape. However, it's important to choose a style that doesn't add width to your face. A pixie cut with longer layers can help elongate your face.

Q: What if I have a combination of face shapes?

A: If you have a combination of face shapes, focus on the features you want to emphasize. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face with a wide forehead, you can choose a chin-length hairstyle with side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.

Choosing the right length hair for your face shape can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful!
